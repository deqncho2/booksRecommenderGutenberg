Synopsis: 
In the ancient Greek tragedy "Medea of Euripides," we are introduced to a woman named Medea, who is both captivating and terrifying. Medea, a foreigner in Corinth, finds herself caught in a web of betrayal and heartbreak when her husband, Jason, abandons her for a younger woman. Consumed by rage and a thirst for revenge, Medea embarks on a path of darkness, unleashing a series of shocking and devastating events that will leave the audience in awe.

Genres: 
Tragedy, Drama

Themes: 
Betrayal, Revenge, Gender Roles, Love and Passion
