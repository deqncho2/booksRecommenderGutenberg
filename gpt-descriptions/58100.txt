Synopsis: In 'Words; Their Use and Abuse' by William Mathews, embark on a captivating exploration of the power and impact of language. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, Mathews delves into the intricate ways in which words shape our lives, relationships, and society. From the subtle nuances of communication to the profound consequences of miscommunication, this book unveils the hidden depths of language and challenges readers to reconsider their own words and their impact on others. With a blend of wit, wisdom, and literary references, Mathews invites readers on a journey that will forever change the way they perceive and utilize words.

Genres: Non-fiction, Linguistics, Philosophy

Themes: Language and communication, Power of words, Miscommunication, Social impact of language
