Synopsis: In 'The Prelude to Adventure' by Hugh Walpole, a young man named David sets off on a journey that will forever change his life. Leaving behind the comforts of his home, he embarks on a thrilling and perilous adventure, encountering mysterious characters, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected challenges along the way. As David navigates through the unknown, he discovers hidden truths about himself and the world around him, ultimately leading him to question his own identity and purpose. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into David's quest, eagerly anticipating what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Exploration
