Synopsis: 
In 'Through Finland in Carts', Mrs. Alec-Tweedie takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Finland. With her vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, she recounts her adventures traveling through the country in traditional Finnish carts, immersing herself in the local culture and encountering a variety of fascinating characters along the way. From breathtaking natural wonders to charming villages and bustling cities, this travelogue offers a unique and intimate glimpse into the heart of Finland.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Natural beauty
