Synopsis: In 'A New Species of Pocket Gopher (Genus Pappogeomys) From Jalisco, México', Russell, Robert J. takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of scientific discovery as he unveils a remarkable new species of pocket gopher. Set in the diverse landscapes of Jalisco, México, this intriguing study delves into the intricacies of taxonomy, ecology, and evolutionary biology, offering a fascinating glimpse into the hidden wonders of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Scientific Literature, Natural History

Themes: Biodiversity, Species Discovery, Conservation
