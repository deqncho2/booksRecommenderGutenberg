Synopsis: In 'The Brothers' War', two brothers find themselves on opposite sides of a conflict that threatens to tear their family apart. As they navigate the treacherous waters of war, they must confront their own personal demons and make difficult choices that will shape their destinies. With betrayal, love, and sacrifice at every turn, this gripping tale explores the complexities of loyalty and the bonds that hold us together, even in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, family drama, war

Themes: Loyalty, betrayal, family bonds, personal growth
