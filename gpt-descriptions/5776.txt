Synopsis: In '100%: The Story of a Patriot', Upton Sinclair takes readers on a gripping journey through the life of Peter Gudge, a young man who becomes entangled in a web of political corruption and social injustice. As Peter navigates the treacherous world of power and greed, he must confront his own beliefs and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this novel explores the dark underbelly of society and the lengths one man will go to fight for justice.

Genres: Political fiction, Historical fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Corruption, Social inequality, Political activism, Personal transformation
