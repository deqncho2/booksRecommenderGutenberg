Synopsis: In 'Peter Binney: A Novel' by Marshall, Archibald, readers are introduced to the enigmatic Peter Binney, a man with a mysterious past and an uncanny ability to navigate the complexities of life. Set against the backdrop of a bustling city, the story follows Peter as he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. As his past catches up with him, Peter must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his future. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Deception
