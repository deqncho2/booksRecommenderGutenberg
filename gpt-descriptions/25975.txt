Synopsis: In "The Deluge in the Light of Modern Science: A Discourse," Denton, William takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysteries of the great flood. Drawing upon scientific discoveries and ancient texts, Denton weaves together a compelling narrative that challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on this legendary event. As he delves into the geological, archaeological, and historical evidence, the author uncovers surprising connections and raises thought-provoking questions that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Prepare to be enthralled by Denton's meticulous research and his ability to shed new light on one of history's most enduring enigmas.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Biblical narratives, Geology, Archaeology, Ancient civilizations, Mythology
