Synopsis: In 'The Seats of the Mighty, Volume 2' by Parker Gilbert, the gripping tale continues as the protagonist, Robert Moray, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century Quebec, Moray must navigate treacherous waters as he becomes embroiled in a dangerous mission that could change the course of history. With his loyalty tested and his life hanging in the balance, Moray must summon all his courage and wit to survive in a world where betrayal lurks at every turn.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Redemption
