Synopsis: 

'Creation of the Teton Landscape: The Geologic Story of Grand Teton National Park' takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and evolution of one of America's most stunning natural landscapes. Through vivid descriptions and detailed analysis, the book unravels the geological history of Grand Teton National Park, revealing the fascinating forces that shaped its majestic peaks, deep valleys, and breathtaking scenery. From ancient volcanic eruptions to the sculpting power of glaciers, the authors provide a comprehensive account of the park's geological wonders, offering readers a deeper understanding and appreciation of this remarkable natural treasure.

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Natural History

Themes: Geological processes, Natural landscapes, Environmental conservation
