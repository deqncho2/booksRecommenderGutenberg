Synopsis: 
'Old-World Japan: Legends of the Land of the Gods' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and enchanting tales of ancient Japan. From mythical creatures and heroic warriors to magical landscapes and divine interventions, this book unveils the hidden wonders of a bygone era. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a world where honor, bravery, and the supernatural intertwine, leaving them spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Legend and Myth, Honor and Bravery, Supernatural and Divine Intervention
