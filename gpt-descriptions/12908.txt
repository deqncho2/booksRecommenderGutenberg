Synopsis: 
In 'Missing' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman mysteriously disappears, leaving her family and friends desperate for answers. As they delve into her past, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden motives that lead them on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Disappearance, Secrets, Betrayal
