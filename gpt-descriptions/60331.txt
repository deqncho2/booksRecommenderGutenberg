Synopsis: In 'The Man on the Other Side' by Ada Barnett, a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious portal that allows her to communicate with a man from a parallel universe. As their connection deepens, Lily becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger, forcing her to question her own reality and make choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With each interaction, the line between truth and deception blurs, leaving Lily to navigate a thrilling and unpredictable journey that will challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Identity, Deception, Choices
