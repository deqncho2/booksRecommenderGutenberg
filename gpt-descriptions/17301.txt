Synopsis: 
In 'On With Torchy' by Ford, Sewell, follow the adventures of Torchy Blane, a feisty and quick-witted newspaper reporter who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When a prominent politician is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Torchy's determination to uncover the truth leads her down a thrilling path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With her sharp intellect and fearless attitude, Torchy must navigate through a world of deceit and corruption to bring justice to light.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Investigation, Journalism, Courage
