Synopsis: In 'The Gentlemen's Book of Etiquette and Manual of Politeness', Cecil B. Hartley presents a comprehensive guide for gentlemen to navigate society with grace and charm. From proper dress and grooming to conversation skills and social etiquette, this book offers invaluable advice on how to conduct oneself in various social situations. With wit and wisdom, Hartley explores the intricacies of gentlemanly behavior, providing practical tips and timeless principles that are as relevant today as they were when the book was first published. Whether you are attending a formal dinner, engaging in business dealings, or simply interacting with others, this manual is an essential companion for any gentleman seeking to master the art of politeness and refinement.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Non-fiction

Themes: Social conduct, Politeness, Refinement, Personal development
