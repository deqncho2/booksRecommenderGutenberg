Synopsis: In 'The Night Side of London', James Ewing Ritchie takes readers on a captivating journey through the dark underbelly of the city. From the eerie gas-lit streets to the mysterious characters lurking in the shadows, this gripping narrative explores the hidden secrets and sinister activities that unfold after sunset. As the protagonist delves deeper into the night side of London, they uncover a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a world where nothing is as it seems, and where the line between good and evil becomes blurred. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Darkness and light, Deception and secrets, Urban exploration
