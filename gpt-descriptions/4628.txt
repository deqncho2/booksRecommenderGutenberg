Synopsis: In 'The Iron Rule; Or, Tyranny in the Household' by Arthur, T. S., a seemingly perfect family is torn apart by the oppressive rule of a domineering patriarch. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the characters are forced to confront the consequences of their actions. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this novel explores the dark side of power and the destructive impact it can have on those closest to us.

Genres: Domestic fiction, Drama, Psychological thriller

Themes: Power dynamics, Family relationships, Secrets and betrayal
