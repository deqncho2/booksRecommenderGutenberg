Synopsis: In 'Project Cyclops' by Thomas Hoover, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and intrigue, where the line between science and fiction blurs. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Consequences of scientific advancements, Power and control
