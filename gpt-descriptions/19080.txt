Synopsis: 
'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 12: Little Journeys to the Homes of Great Scientists' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and homes of some of the most brilliant scientific minds in history. From the laboratories of Marie Curie to the study of Charles Darwin, this volume offers a unique glimpse into the personal lives and inspirations of these great scientists. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the book explores the environments that shaped their groundbreaking discoveries and sheds light on the human side of their genius. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Scientific discovery, Personal lives of scientists, Influence of environment on creativity
