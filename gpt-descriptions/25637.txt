Synopsis: In 'The Dark House: A Knot Unravelled' by George Manville Fenn, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a secluded mansion from a distant relative. As she explores the eerie house, she uncovers a series of cryptic clues that lead her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth about her family's dark past. With each revelation, Emily becomes more determined to unravel the secrets hidden within the walls of the dark house, even if it means putting her own life at risk.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Identity, Danger
