Synopsis: 

'Unwritten Literature of Hawaii: The Sacred Songs of the Hula' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural heritage of Hawaii, exploring the hidden world of the hula and its sacred songs. Nathaniel Bright Emerson delves into the heart of Hawaiian traditions, revealing the profound significance of the hula as a form of storytelling and spiritual expression. Through his meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Emerson unveils the captivating beauty and deep-rooted spirituality of this ancient art form, offering readers a rare glimpse into a world that has remained largely unknown to outsiders.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cultural Studies, Anthropology

Themes: Hawaiian culture, Traditional arts, Spirituality, Oral traditions
