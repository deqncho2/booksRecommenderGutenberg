Synopsis: In 'A Woman of Yesterday' by Caroline Atwater Mason, a young woman named Emily finds herself transported back in time to the early 1900s. As she navigates this unfamiliar world, she must confront the challenges and expectations placed upon women of that era. With her modern mindset, Emily must decide whether to conform to societal norms or forge her own path, all while grappling with the complexities of love and self-discovery. This captivating novel takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the timeless themes of identity, feminism, and the power of choice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Time Travel, Romance

Themes: Identity, Feminism, Love, Self-Discovery
