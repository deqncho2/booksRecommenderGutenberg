Synopsis: 
In 'Finger-ring lore: historical, legendary, anecdotal', William Jones, F.S.A. takes us on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of finger rings. From ancient times to the present day, this book unveils the hidden stories behind these small but significant pieces of jewelry. Delving into historical events, legendary tales, and intriguing anecdotes, Jones uncovers the secrets and symbolism that have surrounded finger rings throughout history. From royal treasures to love tokens, each ring holds a unique story waiting to be discovered. Prepare to be enchanted by the allure of finger-ring lore!

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Anecdotal

Themes: Jewelry, Symbolism, History
