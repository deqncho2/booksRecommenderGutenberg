Synopsis: In "Birds useful and birds harmful," Herman and Owen take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of avian creatures. From the majestic and awe-inspiring to the cunning and mischievous, this book explores the diverse roles that birds play in our lives. Delve into the intriguing tales of birds that bring joy and beauty, as well as those that pose challenges and create chaos. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the intricate relationship between humans and birds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Environmental Impact, Human-Animal Interaction
