Synopsis: 
"The Sea-beach at Ebb-tide: A Guide to the Study of the Seaweeds and the Lower Animal Life Found Between Tide-marks" takes readers on a captivating journey along the seashore, unveiling the hidden wonders of the marine world. Augusta Foote Arnold's guidebook offers a comprehensive exploration of the diverse seaweeds and fascinating lower animal life that thrive in the intertidal zone. From colorful algae to intricate marine creatures, this book provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for those curious about the captivating ecosystem found between the tides.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Marine Biology

Themes: Marine life, Seashore exploration, Environmental awareness
