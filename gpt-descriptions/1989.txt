Synopsis: 
'The Foolish Dictionary' is an entertaining and unconventional work of reference that explores the fascinating world of uncertain English words. Written by the enigmatic author Wurdz, Gideon, this exhaustive dictionary delves into the origin, meaning, and legitimate/illegitimate use of these words, all while accompanied by a collection of humorous and thought-provoking illustrations. Although the pictures are not included, they add an extra layer of intrigue to the already captivating content. 

Genres: Humor, Reference, Language

Themes: Language exploration, Wordplay, Satire
