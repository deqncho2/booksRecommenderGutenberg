Synopsis: 
In 'The Witness' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Ruth is thrust into a dangerous situation when she becomes the sole witness to a crime. As she grapples with the decision to come forward and testify, she finds herself caught between loyalty to her family and the pursuit of justice. With her life on the line, Ruth must navigate a web of secrets and deceit, all while discovering the strength within herself to stand up for what is right.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Courage
