Synopsis: In "A Prince of Sinners," the enigmatic and charismatic Prince Maiyo finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, scandal, and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous waters of high society, he must confront his own dark past and make choices that will shape his future. With its twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what lies ahead for the captivating Prince Maiyo.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Power
