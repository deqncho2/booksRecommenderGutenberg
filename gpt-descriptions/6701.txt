Synopsis: In the second volume of "Sidonia, the Sorceress," the gripping tale of power, betrayal, and dark magic continues. As the reign of the Ducal House of Pomerania hangs in the balance, Sidonia's true nature as a sorceress is revealed, leaving those around her in awe and fear. With her powers growing stronger, Sidonia becomes a force to be reckoned with, but her actions have consequences that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the kingdom. As alliances shift and secrets are unveiled, the fate of Pomerania rests on the shoulders of this enigmatic sorceress.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Power and its corrupting influence, Betrayal, Magic and sorcery, Fate and destiny
