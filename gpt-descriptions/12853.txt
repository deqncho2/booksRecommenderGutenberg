Synopsis: In "The "Wearing of the Green," or The Prosecuted Funeral Procession," a small Irish community is thrown into chaos when a funeral procession becomes the target of a mysterious and dangerous group. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople must come together to uncover the truth and protect their way of life. This thrilling and suspenseful tale explores the power of community, the consequences of prejudice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Community, Prejudice, Resilience
