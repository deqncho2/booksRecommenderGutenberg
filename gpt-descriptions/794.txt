Synopsis: In 'The Wouldbegoods: Being the Further Adventures of the Treasure Seekers', the Bastable children are back with more mischievous escapades and imaginative schemes. This time, they find themselves in a new country house, where they form a society called the "Wouldbegoods" and embark on a series of well-intentioned but often disastrous endeavors. From staging a play to attempting to be helpful, their adventures are filled with humor, mishaps, and unexpected consequences. Will their good intentions prevail, or will their efforts lead to even more chaos?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Consequences of actions
