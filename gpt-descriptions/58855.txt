Synopsis: In 'The Little Room, and Other Stories' by Madeline Yale Wynne, readers are transported to a world where ordinary lives intersect with the extraordinary. From a mysterious room that holds a secret to a haunting encounter with a ghostly figure, each story in this collection offers a glimpse into the unexpected and the unexplained. As the characters navigate through their own fears and desires, they are forced to confront the unknown and question the boundaries of reality. With a touch of suspense and a dash of the supernatural, these captivating tales will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Fear, Reality vs. Illusion
