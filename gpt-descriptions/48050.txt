Synopsis: In "Needlework Economies: A Book of Mending and Making with Oddments and Scraps," embark on a captivating journey through the art of needlework, where the author unveils a treasure trove of techniques and ideas for transforming ordinary materials into extraordinary creations. From intricate embroidery to patchwork quilting, this book celebrates the beauty and resourcefulness of working with oddments and scraps, inspiring readers to embrace the joy of mending and making.

Genres: Craft, DIY, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Sustainability, Resourcefulness
