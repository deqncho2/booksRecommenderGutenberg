Synopsis: In "The Truth About Port Arthur," Nozhin takes readers on a gripping journey through the mysterious events surrounding the infamous Port Arthur massacre. As the protagonist delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets and hidden agendas that challenge everything he thought he knew. With each revelation, the tension escalates, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this tragic event.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Truth vs. Perception
