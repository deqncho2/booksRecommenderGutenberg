Synopsis: 
In 'The Diamond Master' by Jacques Futrelle, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a thrilling cat-and-mouse game with a cunning criminal known as the Diamond Master. As the stakes rise and the chase intensifies, the detective must rely on his wit and expertise to outsmart the elusive thief. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Pursuit of justice, Deception and disguise
