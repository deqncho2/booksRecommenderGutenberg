Synopsis: In "Substance of a Sermon on the Bible Society," Cunningham, Francis delivers a powerful sermon that captivates the audience in Beccles and St. Mary's Church Bungay. With eloquence and conviction, he explores the significance of the Bible Society and its impact on society. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and biblical references, Cunningham urges his listeners to embrace the power of the Bible and its ability to transform lives. This sermon serves as a call to action, inspiring individuals to support the Bible Society and spread its message of hope and salvation.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: The importance of the Bible, the transformative power of faith, the role of the Bible Society in spreading the message of Christianity.
