Synopsis: 
"The Voyages of Pedro Fernandez de Quiros, 1595 to 1606. Volume 1" takes readers on a captivating journey through the uncharted waters of the Pacific Ocean. Join Pedro Fernandez de Quiros, a skilled navigator and explorer, as he embarks on a series of daring expeditions in search of new lands and treasures. From encounters with indigenous peoples to the challenges of navigating treacherous seas, this volume offers a thrilling account of Quiros' adventures and the mysteries that await him.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, exploration, adventure

Themes: 
Exploration and discovery, cultural encounters, the quest for new lands and knowledge
