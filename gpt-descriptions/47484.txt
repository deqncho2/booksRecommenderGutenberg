Synopsis: In 'America's Black and White Book: One Hundred Pictured Reasons Why We Are At War', author W. A. Rogers presents a powerful collection of illustrations that vividly depict the complex issues and conflicts that have led America into war. Through thought-provoking imagery, Rogers explores the racial tensions, political divisions, and social injustices that have shaped the nation's history and continue to fuel its conflicts. This visually captivating book offers a unique perspective on the underlying reasons behind America's ongoing struggles, inviting readers to reflect on the past and consider the path towards a more united future.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Commentary

Themes: Racial tensions, Political divisions, Social injustices, National identity
