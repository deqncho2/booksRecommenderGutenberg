Synopsis: In "Blood and Iron: Origin of German Empire As Revealed by Character of Its Founder, Bismarck," author John Hubert Greusel delves into the captivating life and political career of Otto von Bismarck, the influential statesman who played a pivotal role in the formation of the German Empire. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Greusel unveils the complex personality and strategic genius of Bismarck, exploring the events and decisions that shaped his rise to power. From his cunning diplomatic maneuvers to his unyielding determination, Bismarck's character becomes a lens through which the origins of the German Empire are revealed, offering readers a fascinating exploration of one of history's most influential figures.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Science

Themes: Power and ambition, Diplomacy and strategy, Nation-building
