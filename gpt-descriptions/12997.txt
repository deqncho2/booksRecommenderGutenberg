Synopsis: 
In "The Pony Rider Boys in the Grand Canyon; Or, The Mystery of Bright Angel Gulch," the adventurous group of young riders embarks on a thrilling journey through the majestic Grand Canyon. As they navigate treacherous trails and encounter unexpected challenges, the boys find themselves entangled in a mysterious secret that lies within the depths of Bright Angel Gulch. With danger lurking at every turn, the Pony Rider Boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to unravel the enigma and emerge victorious.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage, Mystery-solving
