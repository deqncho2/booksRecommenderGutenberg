Synopsis: 
In 'Waysiders, Stories of Connacht' by Seumas O'Kelly, readers are transported to the rugged landscapes of Connacht, Ireland, where they encounter a diverse cast of characters and witness their extraordinary tales unfold. From the humble fisherman to the cunning trickster, each story offers a glimpse into the lives of the people who call this enchanting region home. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, O'Kelly weaves together a tapestry of human experiences, exploring themes of love, loss, resilience, and the enduring spirit of the Connacht people.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Irish Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Resilience, Irish Culture, Community
