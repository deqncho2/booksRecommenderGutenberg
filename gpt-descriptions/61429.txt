Synopsis: In 'The Heart of a Dog' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young doctor stumbles upon a stray dog on the streets of New York City. Intrigued by the dog's intelligence and loyalty, he decides to take him in and conduct a groundbreaking experiment to transform him into a human. As the dog's transformation progresses, the doctor is faced with unexpected challenges and moral dilemmas that force him to question the boundaries of science and the essence of humanity.

Genres: Fiction, Science Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Identity, Morality, Science and Ethics
