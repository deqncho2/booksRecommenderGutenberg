Synopsis: 
'Boy Life: Stories and Readings Selected From The Works of William Dean Howells' is a captivating collection of stories that delve into the experiences and adventures of young boys. From the bustling streets of Boston to the idyllic countryside, these tales offer a glimpse into the joys, challenges, and growth of boyhood. Through vivid storytelling and relatable characters, William Dean Howells paints a vivid picture of the trials and triumphs that shape the lives of these young protagonists. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Short stories

Themes: Childhood, Adventure, Friendship, Moral development
