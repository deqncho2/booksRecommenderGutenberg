Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid of Massachusetts Colony' by Alice Turner Curtis, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Priscilla as she navigates the challenges and adventures of life in the Massachusetts Colony during the 17th century. From encounters with Native Americans to unexpected friendships, Priscilla's courage and determination will inspire readers as she learns the importance of loyalty, resilience, and embracing new beginnings.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Cultural exchange
