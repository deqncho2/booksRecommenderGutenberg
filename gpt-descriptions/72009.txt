Synopsis: 

In "Scaffolding: A Treatise on the Design & Erection of Scaffolds, Gantries, and Stagings," author A. G. H. Thatcher delves into the intricate world of construction scaffolding, providing a comprehensive guide for contractors, builders, and clerks of works. This informative work not only explores the design and erection of scaffolds but also delves into the legal aspects surrounding their use. With a focus on the various appliances used in connection with scaffolding, Thatcher offers valuable insights into this essential aspect of construction. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Technical Manual

Themes: Construction, Design, Legal Considerations
