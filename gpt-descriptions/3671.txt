Synopsis: In "Christie Johnstone: A Novel," Charles Reade takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two individuals from different social classes who find themselves unexpectedly drawn to each other. The story revolves around the charming and vivacious Christie Johnstone, a young fisherwoman, and Archibald Carlyle, a wealthy lawyer. As their paths cross, a series of events unfold, testing their loyalty, challenging societal norms, and ultimately forcing them to confront their own desires and prejudices. With its intricate plot, vivid characters, and a touch of romance, this novel explores the complexities of love, class divisions, and the power of personal growth.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Social Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Class Conflict, Personal Growth
