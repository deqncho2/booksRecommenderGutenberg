Synopsis: 
In 'Edith Lyle: A Novel', we are introduced to the enigmatic Edith Lyle, a woman with a mysterious past and an uncanny ability to uncover secrets. Set in a small coastal town, the story follows Edith as she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and deception, where nothing is as it seems. As she delves deeper into the town's dark underbelly, Edith must confront her own demons and navigate a dangerous game of trust and betrayal. With its gripping plot twists and complex characters, 'Edith Lyle: A Novel' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Trust, Betrayal
