Synopsis: In 'The Rhizoid Kill' by Jack Bradley, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious organism with the power to alter human behavior. As the world falls into chaos, a group of unlikely heroes must race against time to unravel the secrets of the rhizoid and find a way to save humanity. With mind-bending twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping science fiction thriller will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human nature, Manipulation, Survival
