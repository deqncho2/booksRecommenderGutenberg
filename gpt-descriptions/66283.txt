Synopsis: In 'The Disembodied Man' by Jardine, Jack Owen, a renowned scientist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind only his disembodied consciousness. As his mind floats in a state of limbo, he embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind his own disappearance and the dark forces at play. With the help of an unlikely ally, he must navigate a treacherous world of secrets, betrayal, and mind-bending technology, all while racing against time to save himself and those he holds dear. This gripping tale of mystery and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Technology, Betrayal, Redemption
