Synopsis: In 'The Hungry Tiger of Oz' by Ruth Plumly Thompson, join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to save the magical land of Oz from a ravenous tiger with an insatiable appetite. As they encounter whimsical creatures and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their wit and bravery to outsmart the hungry tiger and restore peace to the beloved land of Oz.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Overcoming Challenges
