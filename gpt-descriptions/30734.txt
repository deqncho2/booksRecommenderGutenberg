Synopsis: 

In "Mushrooms of America, Edible and Poisonous," embark on a captivating journey through the diverse world of mushrooms that will leave you both fascinated and cautious. This comprehensive guide takes you deep into the forests, revealing the hidden secrets of these mysterious fungi. From the delectable delicacies that can elevate any culinary experience to the treacherous species that can bring about a deadly outcome, this book unveils the captivating stories behind each mushroom. With stunning visuals and expert insights, "Mushrooms of America, Edible and Poisonous" is a captivating exploration that will forever change the way you view these extraordinary organisms.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Reference

Themes: Exploration, Caution, Biodiversity
