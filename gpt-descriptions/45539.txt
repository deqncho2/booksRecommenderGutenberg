Synopsis: 
In 'The Compleat Bachelor' by Oliver Onions, we are introduced to the enigmatic character of Richard, a man who seems to have it all - wealth, charm, and a string of successful relationships. However, beneath his polished exterior lies a dark secret that threatens to unravel his carefully constructed facade. As Richard navigates the treacherous waters of love and desire, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true nature of Richard's intentions and the depths of his obsession. Will he be able to maintain his bachelor lifestyle, or will his past catch up with him, exposing the truth?

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Romance

Themes: Deception, Obsession, Identity
