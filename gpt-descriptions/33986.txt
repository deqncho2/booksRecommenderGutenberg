Synopsis: In 'Comic Tragedies: Written by 'Jo' and 'Meg' and Acted by The 'Little Women', Louisa May Alcott and Anna Bronson Alcott Pratt bring to life a collection of entertaining and thought-provoking plays. Join the beloved March sisters as they explore the realms of comedy and tragedy, showcasing their wit, talent, and passion for the stage. From humorous misunderstandings to heart-wrenching dilemmas, these plays offer a captivating glimpse into the lives of the Little Women and their imaginative storytelling. 

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Sisterhood, Creativity, Identity, Love, Ambition
