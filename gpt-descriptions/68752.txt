Synopsis: 

In 'Lives and exploits of the most noted highwaymen, robbers and murderers of all nations: Drawn from the most authentic sources and brought down to the present time', Charles Whitehead takes us on a thrilling journey through the lives of infamous criminals from various nations. From daring highwaymen to cunning robbers and ruthless murderers, this book delves into their captivating stories, revealing the dark underbelly of society. With each chapter, the reader is transported to a different time and place, encountering these notorious figures and their audacious exploits. 

Genres: True Crime, Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Morality, Society's fascination with outlaws
