Synopsis: 
'Love in Excess; or, the Fatal Enquiry: A Novel in Three Parts' by Eliza Fowler Haywood is a captivating tale of forbidden love, scandal, and intrigue. The story follows the lives of several characters as they navigate the complexities of love and desire in a society bound by strict social conventions. Amidst the backdrop of lavish parties and secret rendezvous, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of passion and betrayal, where the pursuit of love comes at a high cost. As the plot unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, hearts are broken, and the consequences of one's actions become increasingly dire. With its richly drawn characters and suspenseful narrative, 'Love in Excess' is a gripping exploration of the human heart and the lengths people will go to in the name of love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Social conventions, Betrayal, Desire, Consequences
