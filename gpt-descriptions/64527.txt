Synopsis: In 'The Bloodhounds of Zirth' by Palmer Lloyd, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. When a mysterious artifact is stolen, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mythical creatures, and solve cryptic puzzles to retrieve it. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they discover that the artifact holds the key to a power that could change the fate of their world forever. With time running out, they must race against a cunning enemy and their own inner demons to save their land from impending doom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Quest for knowledge, Friendship and loyalty, Overcoming adversity
