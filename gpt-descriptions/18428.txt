Synopsis: In the small village of Solhoug, a dark secret threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As tensions rise and passions ignite, a web of deceit and betrayal is spun, leading to a climactic feast that will forever change the fate of those involved. With its intricate plot and complex characters, 'The Feast at Solhoug' is a gripping tale of love, power, and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Psychological

Themes: Love and desire, Power and manipulation, Morality and ethics
