Synopsis: In 'Friends I Have Made' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure when he befriends a mysterious stranger. Together, they uncover hidden secrets and face dangerous challenges that will test their courage and loyalty. As Tom navigates through unexpected twists and turns, he learns the true meaning of friendship and the power of trust. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for Tom and his newfound companions.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Courage
