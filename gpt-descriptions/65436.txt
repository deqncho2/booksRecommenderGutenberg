Synopsis: 

In 'The Rebirth of Turkey' by Price, Clair, a young journalist named Emma finds herself immersed in a thrilling journey through the vibrant streets of Istanbul. Drawn into a web of political intrigue and cultural clashes, Emma uncovers a hidden world of power struggles and ancient secrets that could reshape the destiny of Turkey. As she delves deeper into the heart of the country, Emma must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own personal demons, all while trying to uncover the truth that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Political intrigue, Cultural clashes, Personal transformation
