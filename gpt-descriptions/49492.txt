Synopsis: 
In "Under Orders: The story of a young reporter" by Munroe Kirk, we follow the journey of a determined and ambitious young reporter named Emma as she navigates the cutthroat world of journalism. Assigned to cover a high-profile murder case, Emma finds herself entangled in a web of corruption, deceit, and danger. As she digs deeper into the investigation, Emma uncovers shocking secrets that could not only jeopardize her career but also put her life at risk. With time running out and the truth hanging in the balance, Emma must make difficult choices that will test her integrity and courage. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Ambition, Corruption, Truth
