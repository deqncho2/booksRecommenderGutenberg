Synopsis: In "The Huey Long Murder Case," author Hermann B. Deutsch takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of politics and power. Set in the 1930s, the story follows a determined detective as he investigates the mysterious assassination of the charismatic and controversial Louisiana governor, Huey Long. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of corruption, secrets, and betrayal that threatens to shake the very foundations of the nation. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking truth is finally revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Political intrigue, Betrayal, Justice and truth
