Synopsis: 
In "Hanky Panky: A book of conjuring tricks" by Frikell, Wiljalba, prepare to be amazed and bewildered as you delve into the world of magic and illusion. This captivating book unveils a collection of mind-boggling tricks and illusions that will leave you questioning reality. From disappearing objects to mind-reading feats, Frikell takes readers on a journey through the secrets of the magician's craft. With step-by-step instructions and detailed illustrations, this book is not only a source of entertainment but also an invitation to explore the art of deception and wonder.

Genres: 
Magic, Non-fiction, Entertainment

Themes: 
Illusion, Deception, Wonder
