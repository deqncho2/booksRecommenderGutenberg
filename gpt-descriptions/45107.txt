Synopsis: In "Frey and His Wife" by Maurice Hewlett, a young knight named Frey embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his beloved wife, who has been captured by a powerful sorcerer. As Frey faces numerous challenges and encounters mystical creatures, he must confront his own inner demons and make sacrifices to save his wife. This captivating tale of love, adventure, and magic will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and devotion, Heroism, Redemption
