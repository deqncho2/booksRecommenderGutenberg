Synopsis: In 'The Torch Bearer' by Reina Melcher Marquis, a young woman named Amelia finds herself thrust into a world of ancient secrets and hidden powers when she inherits a mysterious artifact. As she embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind her inheritance, Amelia must navigate treacherous alliances, face dangerous adversaries, and confront her own inner demons. With each step, she discovers that the fate of not only her own life but also the fate of an entire realm rests in her hands. Will Amelia embrace her newfound destiny and become the torch bearer her world desperately needs?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Destiny, Power, Identity, Redemption
