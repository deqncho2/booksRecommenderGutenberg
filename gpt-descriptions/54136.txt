Synopsis: 

'Ocean Steamships: A popular account of their construction, development, management and appliances' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of ocean steamships. Written by a team of esteemed authors, this book delves into the construction, development, management, and innovative appliances that have shaped these magnificent vessels. From the early days of steamship technology to the modern advancements that have revolutionized the industry, this account offers a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the subject. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Maritime history, Technological advancements, Transportation
