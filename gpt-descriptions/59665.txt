Synopsis: On the distant planet of Zenob, a young dentist named Dr. Amelia finds herself in the midst of a mysterious toothache epidemic that threatens the entire population. As she delves deeper into the cause of this strange affliction, she uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies that could change the fate of Zenob forever. With time running out, Dr. Amelia must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own fears to save her patients and unravel the truth behind the toothache on Zenob.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Medical ethics, Conspiracy, Identity
