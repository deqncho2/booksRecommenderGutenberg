Synopsis: 

"The 'Patriotes' of '37: A Chronicle of the Lower Canada Rebellion" by Alfred D. DeCelles is a gripping historical novel set during the tumultuous times of the Lower Canada Rebellion in 1837. The story follows a group of passionate and idealistic individuals who become embroiled in a dangerous struggle for freedom and justice against a repressive government. As tensions rise and the rebellion gains momentum, the characters must navigate treacherous alliances, personal sacrifices, and the harsh realities of war. Through their experiences, the novel explores the complexities of loyalty, the power of collective action, and the enduring spirit of resistance.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Adventure

Themes: Rebellion, Freedom, Loyalty, Justice
