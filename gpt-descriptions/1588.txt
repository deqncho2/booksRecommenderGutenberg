Synopsis: 
'A Rogue's Life' by Wilkie Collins is a captivating tale that follows the adventures of a young man named Frank Softly. Tired of his mundane existence, Frank decides to abandon his respectable life and embark on a journey filled with deception, disguise, and unexpected encounters. From the bustling streets of London to the mysterious corners of the continent, Frank's life takes a thrilling turn as he navigates through a world of secrets and hidden identities. As he delves deeper into his new rogue persona, Frank must confront the consequences of his choices and question the true nature of his own character.

Genres: 
Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Deception, Morality
