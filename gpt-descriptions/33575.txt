Synopsis: In 'The Comedies of Carlo Goldoni', readers are transported to the vibrant world of 18th-century Venice, where the renowned playwright Carlo Goldoni weaves together a collection of delightful and witty comedies. From mistaken identities and romantic entanglements to social satire and clever wordplay, Goldoni's plays offer a captivating glimpse into the lives of the Venetian upper class and the common people. With a keen eye for human nature and a sharp sense of humor, Goldoni's characters navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, creating a tapestry of laughter and entertainment.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Theatre

Themes: Love and romance, Social class and hierarchy, Identity and deception
