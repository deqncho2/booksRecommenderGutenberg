Synopsis: In "Richard I: Makers of History" by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of England's most legendary kings. Follow Richard the Lionheart as he navigates treacherous political landscapes, engages in epic battles, and faces personal challenges that will test his courage and leadership. From his early days as a young prince to his reign as a powerful monarch, this enthralling biography unveils the triumphs and tribulations of a man who became a symbol of chivalry and heroism. Prepare to be transported to the medieval world of knights, castles, and intrigue as you discover the remarkable story of Richard I.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Courage, Medieval Society
