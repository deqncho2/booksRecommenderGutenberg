Synopsis: In 'An Enchanted Garden: Fairy Stories' by Molesworth, Mrs., readers are transported to a magical world where fairies, talking animals, and enchanting adventures await. From a mischievous fairy who grants wishes to a brave young girl who befriends a magical creature, each story in this collection is filled with wonder and imagination. As the characters navigate through challenges and discover the power of friendship and bravery, readers will be captivated by the enchanting tales that unfold.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Bravery
