Synopsis: 

'Excursions in the County of Cornwall' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes, rich history, and intriguing landmarks of Cornwall. Frederick Wilton Litchfield Stockdale provides a concise yet comprehensive exploration of the principal towns and villages, offering vivid descriptions of the residences of the nobility and gentry, as well as the remains of antiquity and other fascinating objects of curiosity. From ancient ruins to stunning natural wonders, this book unveils the hidden gems and captivating stories that make Cornwall a truly remarkable destination.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Heritage, Cultural Significance
