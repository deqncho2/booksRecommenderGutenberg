Synopsis: 

'A Century of Christian Service: Kensington Congregational Church, 1793-1893' by Horne, C. Silvester takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of Kensington Congregational Church, spanning a hundred years of unwavering devotion and service. From its humble beginnings in 1793 to its growth and impact on the community by 1893, this book delves into the trials, triumphs, and transformative moments that shaped the church and its congregation. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Horne paints a vivid picture of the church's evolution, highlighting the individuals who played pivotal roles in its development and the challenges they faced along the way. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Religious History

Themes: Faith and Devotion, Community Impact, Historical Transformation
