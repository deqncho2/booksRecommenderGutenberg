Synopsis: 
When Abby is unexpectedly tasked with delivering a vintage camper van to its new owner in time for Christmas, she embarks on a heartwarming journey that will change her life forever. Along the way, she encounters a charming stranger who helps her rediscover the true meaning of the holiday season. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, 'Christmas on Wheels' is a delightful tale of love, friendship, and the magic of Christmas.

Genres: Romance, Holiday, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Love, Friendship, Self-discovery, Holiday Spirit
