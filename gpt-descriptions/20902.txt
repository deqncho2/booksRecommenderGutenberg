Synopsis: In "The Clyde Mystery: a Study in Forgeries and Folklore," Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting world of Scotland's Clyde Valley. As the author delves into the history and legends surrounding the area, he uncovers a web of forgeries, deceit, and hidden truths. From ancient artifacts to local folklore, Lang skillfully weaves together a tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what is real and what is merely a clever fabrication. With each turn of the page, the mystery deepens, leading to a surprising and thought-provoking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Deception, Identity, History and Mythology
