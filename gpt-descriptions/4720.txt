Synopsis: In 'Wacousta: a tale of the Pontiac conspiracy - Volume 2', the thrilling continuation of the epic historical novel, Major Richardson takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of early Canada. As tensions rise between the British and the Native American tribes, a web of deceit, revenge, and forbidden love unfolds. Amidst the backdrop of the Pontiac Conspiracy, a mysterious figure known as Wacousta emerges, wielding a dangerous power that threatens to unravel the fragile peace. With lives hanging in the balance, the fate of a nation rests on the choices made by a courageous few.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Betrayal, Revenge, Cultural Conflict, Forbidden Love
