Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Maid of Honour: A Tale of the Dark Days of France," the gripping story continues as the protagonist, a young maid named Marie, finds herself caught in the midst of the tumultuous French Revolution. As the revolutionaries grow more radical and the Reign of Terror looms, Marie must navigate treacherous alliances, dangerous secrets, and the ever-present threat of betrayal. With her life hanging in the balance, Marie's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test, as she fights to survive in a world consumed by chaos and violence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Survival, Betrayal
