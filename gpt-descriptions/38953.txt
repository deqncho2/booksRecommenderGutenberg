Synopsis: 

In 'British Manufacturing Industries: Pottery, Glass and Silicates, Furniture and Woodwork,' authors Arnoux, Barff, and Pollen take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of British craftsmanship. From the intricate artistry of pottery and glassmaking to the timeless beauty of furniture and woodwork, this book delves into the rich history, techniques, and innovations that have shaped these industries. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors shed light on the remarkable individuals and companies that have contributed to the success and reputation of British manufacturing. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art and Design

Themes: Craftsmanship, Innovation, Industrial History
