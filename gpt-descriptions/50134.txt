Synopsis: 

In "Running the Blockade: A Personal Narrative of Adventures, Risks, and Escapes During the American Civil War," Thomas E. Taylor takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dangerous waters of the American Civil War. As a blockade runner, Taylor shares his firsthand experiences of evading Union ships, navigating treacherous waters, and facing the constant threat of capture. From high-stakes smuggling operations to heart-pounding escapes, this personal narrative offers a gripping account of the risks and adventures faced by those involved in the clandestine world of blockade running.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Espionage, War
