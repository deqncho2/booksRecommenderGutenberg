Synopsis: In "The Shotgun Princess" by William Merriam Rouse, a young princess finds herself thrust into a dangerous world of adventure and intrigue when her kingdom is threatened. Armed with her trusty shotgun, she must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable foes, and uncover the truth behind the impending danger. As she embarks on this thrilling journey, the princess discovers hidden strengths within herself and learns the true meaning of courage and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Heroism, Self-discovery
