Synopsis: In "The Walls" by Keith Laumer, a renowned architect finds himself trapped in a mysterious and seemingly impenetrable structure. As he navigates the labyrinthine corridors, he encounters bizarre creatures and uncovers dark secrets that challenge his sanity and perception of reality. With each step, the architect must unravel the enigma of the walls and find a way to escape before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Isolation, Identity, Perception of Reality
