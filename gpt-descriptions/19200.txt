Synopsis: In "The History of a Lie: 'The Protocols of the Wise Men of Zion'," Herman Bernstein delves into the origins and impact of one of the most notorious and influential conspiracy theories in history. Unraveling the web of deception, Bernstein explores the mysterious origins of "The Protocols of the Wise Men of Zion," a fabricated document that falsely claimed to reveal a global Jewish conspiracy for world domination. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Bernstein exposes the dangerous consequences of this malicious lie, which has fueled anti-Semitism and hatred for over a century.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Conspiracy theories, Anti-Semitism, Propaganda, Historical impact
