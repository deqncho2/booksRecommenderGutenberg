Synopsis: In 'The Case of the Pool of Blood in the Pastor's Study' by Auguste Groner, a seemingly ordinary murder investigation takes a chilling turn when Detective Joseph Müller discovers a pool of blood in the study of a respected pastor. As he delves deeper into the case, Müller uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that lead him down a dark and twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective finds himself questioning the true nature of the people he thought he knew. As the tension rises and the body count increases, Müller must race against time to solve the case before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
