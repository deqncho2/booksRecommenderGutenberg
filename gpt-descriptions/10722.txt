Synopsis: 
'Plays' by Aleksandr Nikolaevich Ostrovsky is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. From tales of forbidden love to stories of ambition and deceit, Ostrovsky's plays offer a glimpse into the lives of diverse characters navigating the challenges of 19th-century Russian society. Each play presents a unique narrative filled with intrigue, humor, and unexpected twists, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the final curtain falls.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Social class and hierarchy, Morality and ethics
