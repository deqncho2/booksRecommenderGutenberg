Synopsis: In 'The Outrage' by Vivanti, Annie, a young woman with a mysterious past, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Annie must navigate a dangerous world of betrayal and unexpected alliances, where nothing is as it seems. With her life on the line, Annie must uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
