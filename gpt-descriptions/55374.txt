Synopsis: In "Frank Reade, Jr., Fighting the Terror of the Coast" by Luis Senarens, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Frank Reade Jr., a brilliant inventor, faces a formidable enemy lurking along the coast. With his ingenious inventions and unwavering determination, Frank must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart his adversaries, and protect the innocent from the clutches of terror. As danger looms, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover how Frank will overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Ingenuity, Heroism, Perseverance
