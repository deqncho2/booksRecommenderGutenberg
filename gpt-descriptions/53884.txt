Synopsis: 
"A Biblical and Theological Dictionary" by Richard Watson is a captivating exploration of the history, manners, and customs of the Jews and their neighboring nations. This comprehensive dictionary delves into the rich tapestry of biblical and theological knowledge, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the ancient world and its significance in religious texts. From the intriguing rituals and traditions of the Jewish people to the fascinating interactions between different cultures, this dictionary offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of the Bible. 

Genres: 
Religious, Historical, Reference

Themes: 
Biblical history, Theology, Cultural customs
