Synopsis: In 'The City of Fire' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected romance. Will she be able to unravel the mystery before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Love and sacrifice
