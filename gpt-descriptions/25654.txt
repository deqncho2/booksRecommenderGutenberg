Synopsis: In 'Stories of King Arthur's Knights, Told to the Children', Mary Macgregor weaves a captivating tapestry of adventure, chivalry, and honor. Through a collection of enchanting tales, young readers are transported to the legendary realm of King Arthur and his valiant knights. From the noble Sir Lancelot to the brave Sir Gawain, these stories bring to life the trials and triumphs of these legendary heroes. With each page, readers will be captivated by the timeless tales of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of truth that define the Arthurian legend.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Loyalty, Quest for Truth
