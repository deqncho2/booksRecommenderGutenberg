Synopsis: In the distant future, on a distant planet, a prospector named Jack Holloway stumbles upon a fascinating discovery - a small, furry creature that seems to possess intelligence and the ability to communicate. As Jack forms a bond with these endearing creatures, he becomes embroiled in a battle between powerful corporations and the rights of these newfound friends. With the fate of the fuzzy creatures hanging in the balance, Jack must navigate a treacherous path to protect them and uncover the truth about their origins.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ethics, Corporate Power, Sentience
