Synopsis: In the final volume of 'The King's Own Borderers: A Military Romance', the captivating story of love, loyalty, and sacrifice reaches its climax. As the Borderers face their most dangerous mission yet, Lieutenant Colonel Lindsay finds himself torn between duty and his forbidden love for Lady Edith. With the fate of their regiment hanging in the balance, they must navigate treacherous battles, political intrigue, and personal demons. Will their love survive the chaos of war, or will it be lost forever?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Duty and Honor, Forbidden Relationships
