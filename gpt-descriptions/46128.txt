Synopsis: 

'Perseverance Island; Or, The Robinson Crusoe of the Nineteenth Century' by Frazar, Douglas is a thrilling adventure novel set in the 19th century. The story follows the protagonist, who finds himself stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. Alone and with limited resources, he must navigate the challenges of survival, battling the harsh elements and discovering unexpected dangers lurking in the wilderness. As he strives to persevere and find a way back to civilization, he uncovers remarkable secrets about the island and himself, leading to a gripping and unpredictable journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Mystery

Themes: Perseverance, Survival, Self-discovery
