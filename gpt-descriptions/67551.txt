Synopsis: 

'With Grenfell on the Labrador' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and unforgiving landscapes of Labrador, Canada. The story follows the experiences of the author, Fullerton Leonard Waldo, as he accompanies the renowned medical missionary, Sir Wilfred Grenfell, on his mission to provide healthcare and support to the isolated communities of the region. From treacherous sea voyages to heartwarming encounters with the locals, this gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of life in the Labrador.

Genres: Adventure, Biography, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Humanitarianism, Resilience
