Synopsis: 

"The Story of an Ancient Parish: Breage with Germoe: With Some Account of Its Armigers, Worthies and Unworthies, Smugglers and Wreckers, Its Traditions and Superstitions" takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of a fascinating parish. From the tales of armigers and worthies to the dark underworld of smugglers and wreckers, this book unveils the hidden secrets and intriguing characters that have shaped the parish of Breage with Germoe. Delving into the traditions and superstitions that have been passed down through generations, the author paints a vivid picture of a community steeped in rich history and folklore. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History, Folklore

Themes: History, Community, Tradition, Superstition
