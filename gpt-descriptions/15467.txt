Synopsis: In "The First Soprano" by Mary Hitchcock, a talented young opera singer named Amelia finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she is chosen to perform the lead role in a renowned opera house. As Amelia delves deeper into the secrets of the opera house and its inhabitants, she uncovers a dark history that threatens not only her career but also her life. With her voice as her only weapon, Amelia must navigate the treacherous world of opera and confront the sinister forces that seek to silence her forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Identity, Power
