Synopsis: 
In 'The Lesser Bourgeoisie', Balzac takes us into the lives of the Bargeton family, a middle-class family living in a provincial town. The story revolves around the ambitious and charismatic Madame Bargeton, who dreams of rising above her social status and becoming a prominent figure in Parisian society. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations, Madame Bargeton's desires clash with the realities of her life, leading to unexpected consequences for herself and those around her.

Genres: Realism, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Social climbing, Ambition, Love and Marriage, Class and Society
