Synopsis: 

In "Records of the Spanish Inquisition, Translated from the Original Manuscripts," Andrew Dickson White delves into the secretive and controversial history of the Spanish Inquisition. Through meticulous research and translation of primary sources, White uncovers the shocking and often gruesome details of this dark period in Spanish history. From the testimonies of the accused to the methods of torture employed by the Inquisitors, this book offers a chilling glimpse into the depths of religious persecution and the abuse of power. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Religious history

Themes: Religious intolerance, Abuse of power, Historical injustice
