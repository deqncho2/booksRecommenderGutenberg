Synopsis: 

In "Narrative of a Journey Down the Ohio and Mississippi in 1789-90," Samuel S. Forman takes readers on a captivating adventure through the untamed American frontier. Set in the late 18th century, this gripping travelogue follows Forman's perilous expedition down the Ohio and Mississippi rivers, as he encounters a diverse array of characters, navigates treacherous waters, and witnesses the awe-inspiring beauty of the American wilderness. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Forman's narrative offers a unique glimpse into the challenges and wonders of exploring the uncharted territories of early America.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
