Synopsis: 
'From the Angle of Seventeen' by Eden Phillpotts is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the journey of a young protagonist as they navigate the complexities of adolescence and the challenges of growing up. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque English countryside, the story delves into the protagonist's experiences, emotions, and relationships, offering a poignant exploration of the universal struggles faced during the transition from youth to adulthood. With unexpected twists and turns, the narrative beautifully captures the essence of self-discovery, identity, and the bittersweet nature of leaving behind the innocence of youth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Drama

Themes: Adolescence, Self-discovery, Identity, Transition, Relationships
