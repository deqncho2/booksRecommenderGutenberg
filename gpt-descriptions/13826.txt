Synopsis: In "A Gunner Aboard the "Yankee": From the Diary of Number Five of the After Port Gun," Russell Doubleday takes us on a thrilling journey through the Spanish-American War from the perspective of a naval reserve gunner. Through the pages of Number Five's diary, we witness the intense battles, the camaraderie among the crew, and the challenges faced by the naval reserves as they navigate the treacherous waters of war. As the story unfolds, we are drawn into the heart-pounding action and the personal struggles of the characters, making us eager to discover the outcome of their courageous fight.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Bravery, Camaraderie
