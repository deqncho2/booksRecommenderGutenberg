Synopsis: 
When a young couple's dream honeymoon takes an unexpected turn, they find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected romance. As they navigate through exotic locations and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, they must unravel the secrets of their accidental honeymoon and discover the truth behind a hidden treasure. With each twist and turn, their love is put to the test, and they must rely on their wits and each other to survive. Will they find their way back home, or will their accidental honeymoon become the journey of a lifetime?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love and relationships, Discovery and exploration, Unexpected journeys
