Synopsis: 
"A Pair of Schoolgirls: A Story of School Days" follows the lives of two young girls, Mary and Elsie, as they navigate the ups and downs of their school days. From their first meeting as strangers to the development of a deep friendship, the story takes readers on a journey filled with laughter, tears, and unexpected twists. As they face various challenges and encounter new experiences, Mary and Elsie learn valuable lessons about loyalty, resilience, and the power of friendship.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: 
Friendship, Growth, Resilience, School life
