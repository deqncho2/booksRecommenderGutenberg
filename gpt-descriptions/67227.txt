Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'The King's Own Borderers: A Military Romance', the captivating story continues as the lives of the brave soldiers of the King's Own Borderers Regiment unfold amidst the backdrop of war and love. As the characters face new challenges and unexpected twists, their loyalties and relationships are put to the ultimate test. With gripping action, heart-wrenching emotions, and a touch of mystery, this volume delves deeper into the lives of the soldiers and the women who capture their hearts. Will love conquer all, or will the harsh realities of war tear them apart?

Genres: Military Fiction, Historical Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Brotherhood, War and its Consequences
