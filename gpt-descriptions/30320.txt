Synopsis: In 'Trail Tales' by James David Gillilan, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness as a group of adventurers face perilous challenges, mysterious encounters, and unexpected twists. From treacherous mountain peaks to dense forests and raging rivers, their quest for survival becomes a test of courage, resilience, and the power of friendship. As they navigate the unknown, secrets are unveiled, and the true nature of their mission is revealed, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Discovery
