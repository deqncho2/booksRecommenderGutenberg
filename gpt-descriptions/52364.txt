Synopsis: In "The Romance of a Princess: A Comedy; and Other Poems," Amy Redpath Roddick takes readers on a delightful journey through the whimsical world of a princess seeking love and adventure. This collection of poems explores the trials and triumphs of the princess's romantic escapades, filled with humor, wit, and unexpected twists. From encounters with charming suitors to unexpected challenges, the princess's journey is sure to captivate readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Poetry, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and Romance, Adventure and Exploration, Humor and Satire
