Synopsis: 
In 'The Broken Road' by Mason, A. E. W., a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, setting off a chain of events that will uncover long-buried secrets and challenge the residents' perceptions of their own lives. As the stranger's true intentions become increasingly unclear, the villagers find themselves caught in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the broken road.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Redemption
