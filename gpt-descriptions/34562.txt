Synopsis: In "John Deere's Steel Plow" by Edward C. Kendall, embark on a captivating journey through the life of John Deere, a blacksmith turned inventor who revolutionized farming in the 19th century. This enthralling biography explores the challenges, triumphs, and innovations that led John Deere to create the iconic steel plow, forever changing the landscape of agriculture. Discover the untold story behind the man who transformed farming practices and left an indelible mark on American history.

Genres: Biography, History, Innovation

Themes: Invention, Entrepreneurship, Industrial Revolution
