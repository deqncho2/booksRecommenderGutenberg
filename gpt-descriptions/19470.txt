Synopsis: 
'Yorkshire Lyrics: Poems written in the Dialect as Spoken in the West Riding of Yorkshire. To which are added a Selection of Fugitive Verses not in the Dialect' by John Hartley is a captivating collection of poems that beautifully capture the essence of the West Riding of Yorkshire. Through the use of the local dialect, Hartley transports readers to the heart of Yorkshire, immersing them in its unique culture, landscapes, and people. This collection also includes a selection of additional verses that further showcase Hartley's poetic talent and versatility. 

Genres: Poetry, Regional Literature

Themes: Yorkshire culture, Local dialect, Landscape, People
