Synopsis: In 'Last Verses' by Susan Coolidge, a young woman named Rose embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience after a life-altering event. As she navigates through the challenges of love, loss, and personal growth, Rose finds solace in the power of poetry and the transformative nature of art. With each verse she writes, Rose uncovers hidden truths about herself and the world around her, ultimately leading her to a profound understanding of life's complexities and the beauty that can be found within them.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Poetry

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Love, Loss, Personal growth, Art
