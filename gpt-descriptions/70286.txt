Synopsis: 

From flag to flag: A woman's adventures and experiences in the South during the war, in Mexico, and in Cuba takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War, as well as the vibrant landscapes of Mexico and Cuba. Through the eyes of the courageous protagonist, Eliza Ripley, we witness her remarkable adventures, encounters with danger, and the profound impact these experiences have on her life. From the battlefields of the South to the exotic cultures of Mexico and Cuba, this gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the historical events of the era, as well as the personal growth and resilience of a remarkable woman.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Exploration and discovery, Female empowerment
