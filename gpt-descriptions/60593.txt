Synopsis: In 'The Cinder Buggy: A Fable in Iron and Steel', Garrett, Garet takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of 19th-century industrialization. Set in the heart of Pennsylvania, this gripping tale follows the ambitious and determined protagonist as he strives to revolutionize the transportation industry. Filled with intrigue, romance, and the relentless pursuit of progress, this novel explores the triumphs and sacrifices of those who dared to challenge the status quo.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Industrialization, Ambition, Progress, Love and Sacrifice
