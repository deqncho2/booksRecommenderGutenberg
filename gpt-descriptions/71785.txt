Synopsis: In "The Steps of Life: Further Essays on Happiness," Karl Hilty takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the complexities of human existence. With a blend of philosophical insights and personal anecdotes, Hilty explores the various steps we take in our pursuit of happiness, delving into the profound questions that arise along the way. From the pursuit of success and the meaning of love to the role of self-reflection and the importance of embracing change, Hilty's essays offer a captivating exploration of the human experience and the paths we navigate in our quest for fulfillment.

Genres: Philosophy, Self-help, Essay collection

Themes: Happiness, Meaning of life, Self-reflection, Success, Love, Change
