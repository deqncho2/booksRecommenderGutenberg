Synopsis: In 'The Deserted Yacht: Madge Sterling Series, #2', Madge Sterling finds herself embroiled in a thrilling mystery when she stumbles upon a seemingly abandoned yacht while on vacation. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the yacht, Madge uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With her quick wit and determination, Madge must navigate treacherous waters to solve the puzzle and uncover the truth behind the deserted yacht.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Secrets, Perseverance
