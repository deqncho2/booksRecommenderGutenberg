Synopsis: 
'A Practical Treatise on the Manufacture of Perfumery' is a comprehensive guide that unveils the secrets behind creating various perfumes, sachet powders, fumigating materials, dentrifices, cosmetics, and more. Written by the esteemed author Deite, this book delves into the world of volatile oils, balsams, resins, and other natural and artificial perfume-substances. It even explores the intriguing process of manufacturing fruit ethers and provides tests to determine their purity. With step-by-step directions and a wealth of knowledge, this treatise is a must-read for anyone interested in the art of perfumery.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Craft

Themes: Perfumery, Manufacturing, Aromatics, Chemistry
