Synopsis: In 'The Seven Great Monarchies Of The Ancient Eastern World, Vol 7: The Sassanian or New Persian Empire', George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, geography, and antiquities of the ancient Eastern world. Focusing on Chaldaea, Assyria, Babylon, Media, Persia, Parthia, and the Sassanian Empire, Rawlinson weaves together a rich tapestry of cultures, dynasties, and empires that once flourished in this region. With the aid of maps and illustrations, the author brings to life the grandeur and complexity of these ancient civilizations, shedding light on their rise and fall, their achievements and struggles. This volume, dedicated to the Sassanian Empire, offers a fascinating exploration of a powerful dynasty that left an indelible mark on the history of the Eastern world.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Geography

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Empires, Cultural heritage
