Synopsis: In 'The Duke's Daughter; and, The Fugitives; vol. 2/3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the captivating story continues as Lady Jane, the Duke's daughter, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. As she navigates the treacherous world of high society, Lady Jane must uncover the truth behind her family's mysterious past and confront the unexpected challenges that arise. With her wit and determination, she sets out on a thrilling journey that will test her loyalty, love, and resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Social class, Identity, Love and loyalty
