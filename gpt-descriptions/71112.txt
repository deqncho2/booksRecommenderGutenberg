Synopsis: In "Far above rubies," the first volume of a captivating trilogy, author J. H. Riddell introduces readers to a world of mystery and intrigue. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the life of a young woman named Ruby, who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As Ruby navigates through the complexities of society, she discovers hidden truths about her own identity and the dark forces that threaten to tear her world apart. With its richly detailed setting and compelling characters, this novel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie far above rubies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance
Themes: Identity, Secrets, Society, Forbidden Desires
