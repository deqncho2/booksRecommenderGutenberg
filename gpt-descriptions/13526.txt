Synopsis: In the Clutch of the War-God by Hastings, Milo is a thrilling adventure that takes readers on a perilous journey through the heart of war-torn Europe. When a young American journalist finds himself caught in the midst of a conflict between two powerful nations, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, evade enemy forces, and uncover the truth behind a mysterious weapon that could change the course of history. As he races against time, he discovers that the true battle lies not only on the battlefield, but within the depths of his own soul.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: War, Courage, Identity, Morality
