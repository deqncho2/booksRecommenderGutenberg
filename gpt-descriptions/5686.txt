Synopsis: 
'Four Months in a Sneak-Box: A Boat Voyage of 2600 Miles Down the Ohio and Mississippi Rivers, and Along the Gulf of Mexico' takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as Nathaniel H. Bishop embarks on a daring solo journey down the mighty rivers of America. With only a small, unconventional boat called a sneak-box as his vessel, Bishop faces treacherous waters, encounters with wildlife, and unexpected encounters with the diverse people who inhabit the riverbanks. As he navigates through the heart of America, Bishop's captivating narrative paints a vivid picture of the landscapes, cultures, and challenges he encounters along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Human Resilience
