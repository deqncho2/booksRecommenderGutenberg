Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts in the Great Flood' by Robert Shaler, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves caught in the midst of a catastrophic flood. As they navigate treacherous waters and face numerous challenges, they must rely on their survival skills, teamwork, and resourcefulness to overcome the dangers that lie ahead. With their courage and determination, the Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will test their abilities and bond them together in ways they never imagined.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Survival

Themes: Courage, Teamwork, Resilience, Survival
