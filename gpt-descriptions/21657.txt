Synopsis: In 'Deep Furrows' by Moorhouse, Herbert Joseph, a young farmer named John finds himself caught in a web of secrets and mysteries when he inherits his family's struggling farm. As he delves deeper into the history of the land, he uncovers a hidden past that intertwines with his own present, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and redemption. With each revelation, John must confront the ghosts of the past and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of a rural community, this captivating tale explores the power of family, the resilience of the human spirit, and the enduring bonds that connect us all.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Identity, Redemption, Secrets, Rural life
