Synopsis: In 'The Co-Citizens' by Harris, Corra, a group of strangers find themselves inexplicably connected through a mysterious event that alters their lives forever. As they navigate the complexities of their newfound bond, they uncover dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to tear them apart. With each character holding a key to the truth, they must decide whether to trust one another or succumb to the forces that seek to control them. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Trust, Power
