Synopsis: In 'Library Work with Children', Alice Isabel Hazeltine takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of children's librarianship. Through her insightful anecdotes and practical advice, Hazeltine explores the challenges and joys of working with young readers, offering valuable insights into fostering a love for books and literacy. From organizing engaging programs to selecting age-appropriate materials, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for anyone passionate about connecting children with the magic of libraries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Library Science

Themes: Children's literature, Literacy promotion, Library programming, Youth engagement
