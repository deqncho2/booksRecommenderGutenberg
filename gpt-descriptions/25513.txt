Synopsis: 

'Edmund Dulac's Fairy-Book: Fairy Tales of the Allied Nations' is a captivating collection of enchanting fairy tales from various countries, beautifully illustrated by the renowned artist Edmund Dulac. From the mystical lands of France, Russia, Japan, and more, these timeless tales transport readers to a world filled with magic, adventure, and wonder. Each story unveils unique characters, mythical creatures, and extraordinary quests that will leave readers spellbound. With Dulac's exquisite illustrations bringing these tales to life, this book is a treasure trove of imagination and a delightful journey into the realm of fairy tales.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Art

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Cultural diversity
