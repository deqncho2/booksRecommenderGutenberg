Synopsis: In 'The Poor Man' by Stella Benson, a young man named Richard finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he inherits a dilapidated house in the countryside. As he delves into the secrets of the house and its previous occupants, Richard uncovers a hidden world filled with eccentric characters, supernatural occurrences, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, Richard's perception of reality is challenged, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Supernatural, Transformation
