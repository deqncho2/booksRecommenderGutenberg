Synopsis: In "Bought and Paid For; From the Play of George Broadhurst," the lives of a wealthy businessman and a young woman from a humble background become intertwined in a captivating tale of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As their paths cross, they are faced with difficult choices that challenge their values and force them to confront the consequences of their actions. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century New York City, this gripping story explores the complexities of power, desire, and the price one is willing to pay for success.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Social Class and Ambition, Morality and Ethics
