Synopsis: In the small coastal village of Boscastle, a mysterious shipwreck brings with it a series of inexplicable events that shake the community to its core. As the villagers grapple with the aftermath of the wreck, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, superstitions, and ancient legends. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named Hilda must uncover the truth behind the shipwreck and confront the dark forces that threaten to consume the village. With its haunting atmosphere and gripping suspense, "In the Roar of the Sea" takes readers on a thrilling journey into the depths of the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Superstition, Secrets, Community, Identity
