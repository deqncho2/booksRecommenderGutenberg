Synopsis: In 'Mabel: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 3)', we are introduced to the captivating world of Mabel, a young woman whose life takes an unexpected turn when she inherits a mysterious estate. As she delves into the secrets of her newfound inheritance, Mabel uncovers a web of intrigue, romance, and danger that will test her courage and resilience. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Mabel's extraordinary destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Identity, Love and Betrayal
