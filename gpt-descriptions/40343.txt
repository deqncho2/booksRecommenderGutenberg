Synopsis: 
In 'Lilian' by Arnold Bennett, the reader is introduced to the captivating and enigmatic character of Lilian, a young woman who possesses an irresistible charm and an unconventional approach to life. As the story unfolds, Lilian's journey takes her through various encounters and experiences, revealing the complexities of her personality and the impact she has on those around her. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Lilian' delves into the depths of human emotions, exploring themes of love, identity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Identity, Happiness
