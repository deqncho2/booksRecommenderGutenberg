Synopsis: In the remote Isle of Man, a young man named Ewan is caught in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and a haunting curse that spans generations. As he navigates the treacherous path of power and betrayal, Ewan must confront the dark legacy of his ancestors and make a choice that will determine the fate of his own soul. Set against the backdrop of a rugged and mysterious landscape, 'The Deemster' is a gripping tale of love, redemption, and the timeless struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Power and betrayal, Redemption, Good vs. Evil
