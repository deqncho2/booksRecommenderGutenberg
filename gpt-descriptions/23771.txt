Synopsis: 
In the small town of Flat Creek, Indiana, a young boy named Tom is about to embark on a journey that will shape his character and challenge his beliefs. As he navigates the trials and tribulations of school life, Tom finds himself caught between the influence of his mischievous classmates and the guidance of his wise and compassionate teacher, Ralph Hartsook. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the power of education. With its vivid portrayal of rural life in the late 19th century, 'The Hoosier School-boy' is a heartwarming coming-of-age tale that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Education, Friendship, Morality, Rural Life
