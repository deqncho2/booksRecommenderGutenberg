Synopsis: In 'The Revolt of Man' by Besant, Walter, a mysterious event triggers a worldwide upheaval, leading to a complete reversal of power dynamics between men and women. As society grapples with this unprecedented shift, a young man finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous struggle for control and liberation. With unexpected alliances and shocking revelations, this gripping tale explores the consequences of a world turned upside down, where the battle for dominance takes on a whole new meaning.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Gender Roles, Power Dynamics, Social Change
