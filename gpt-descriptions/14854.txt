Synopsis: 
In 'Martha By-the-Day' by Julie M. Lippmann, readers are introduced to Martha, a young woman who takes on the role of a live-in nanny for a wealthy family in New York City during the early 1900s. As Martha navigates the challenges of her new position, she becomes entangled in the complex dynamics of the household, uncovering secrets and facing unexpected obstacles. With her determination and resilience, Martha must find a way to protect herself and those she cares about, all while discovering her own strength and independence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Social class, Family dynamics, Self-discovery
