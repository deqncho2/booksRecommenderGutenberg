Synopsis: 
In 'Miss Numè of Japan: A Japanese-American Romance' by Watanna, Onoto, readers are transported to the vibrant world of early 20th century Japan and America. The story revolves around the captivating Miss Numè, a young Japanese woman who finds herself torn between her traditional upbringing and the allure of the modern world. As she navigates the complexities of love, cultural identity, and societal expectations, Miss Numè's journey takes unexpected turns, leading to a poignant and thought-provoking climax. This captivating tale of romance and self-discovery will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the fate of Miss Numè.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Love and Relationships, Cultural Identity, Tradition versus Modernity
