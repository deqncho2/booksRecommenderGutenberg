Synopsis: In "A Noble Woman: The Life-Story of Edith Cavell," Ernest Protheroe delves into the captivating life of Edith Cavell, a remarkable woman whose courage and selflessness left an indelible mark on history. From her humble beginnings as a governess to her pivotal role as a nurse during World War I, Cavell's unwavering dedication to saving lives and her unwavering resolve in the face of adversity will leave readers in awe. As her story unfolds, Protheroe paints a vivid portrait of a woman who defied societal norms and risked everything for the greater good. With its gripping narrative and profound exploration of sacrifice and heroism, "A Noble Woman" is a compelling tribute to an extraordinary individual.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Heroism
