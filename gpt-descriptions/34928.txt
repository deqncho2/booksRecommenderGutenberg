Synopsis: In 'The Ranch Girls at Home Again' by Margaret Vandercook, the adventurous and spirited Ranch Girls return home to their beloved ranch after a thrilling summer abroad. But their homecoming is not without its challenges, as they find themselves caught up in a mysterious series of events that test their courage and loyalty. As they navigate through unexpected twists and turns, the Ranch Girls must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to uncover the truth and protect their family's legacy. Will they be able to solve the mystery and restore peace to their ranch?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Family, Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
