Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls at the End of the Trail' by Margaret Vandercook, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey to the rugged wilderness of the Pacific Northwest. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the girls must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and the teachings of the Camp Fire Girls organization to overcome obstacles and find their way back home. This captivating tale of friendship, resilience, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Self-discovery
