Synopsis: In 'On Strike Till 3' by Balfour, Grant, a group of unlikely individuals find themselves united in an unexpected strike that will challenge their beliefs, test their resilience, and ultimately redefine their lives. As they navigate the complexities of their personal struggles and the larger societal issues at play, they discover the power of solidarity and the potential for profound change. With humor, heart, and a touch of mystery, this captivating story explores the transformative journey of these individuals as they stand up for what they believe in.

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Humor

Themes: Solidarity, Personal growth, Social change
