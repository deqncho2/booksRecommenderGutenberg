Synopsis: 

Trinity Site: 1945-1995 takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of one of the most significant events of the 20th century. This book, written by the White Sands Missile Range Public Affairs Office, unveils the secrets and stories behind the Trinity Site, a National Historic Landmark located in White Sands Missile Range, New Mexico. From the momentous detonation of the world's first atomic bomb to the subsequent developments and transformations that occurred over five decades, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the site's historical significance and its impact on the world.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Science history

Themes: Atomic weapons, World War II, Scientific advancements, Historical preservation
