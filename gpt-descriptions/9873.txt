Synopsis: In 'Till the Clock Stops', a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of bizarre murders occur in a small, seemingly idyllic town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that challenge his own beliefs and force him to confront his own demons. With time ticking away, he must race against the clock to unravel the truth before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Moral ambiguity
