Synopsis: In 'Tom Slade, Motorcycle Dispatch Bearer' by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, follow the thrilling adventures of Tom Slade as he becomes a motorcycle dispatch bearer during World War I. From dodging enemy fire to delivering crucial messages, Tom's bravery and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. As he navigates the treacherous war-torn landscapes, Tom's determination to fulfill his duty and protect his comrades shines through. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that come his way and emerge as a true hero?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Bravery, Duty, Camaraderie
