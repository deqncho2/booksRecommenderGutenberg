Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Patch' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Peggy discovers a mysterious patch of crimson fabric hidden in her family's attic. As she delves into the history of the patch, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that spans generations. With the help of her friends, Peggy embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the patch and its connection to her own family. As the mystery deepens, Peggy must navigate through hidden clues, unexpected twists, and a race against time to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Friendship, Discovery
