Synopsis: In 'A Son of Hagar: A Romance of Our Time' by Hall Caine, Sir, a young man named Ishmael falls in love with a mysterious woman named Hagar, whose past is shrouded in secrets. As their passionate romance unfolds, Ishmael finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and the search for true identity. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, the power of forgiveness, and the enduring quest for belonging.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and redemption
