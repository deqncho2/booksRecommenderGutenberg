Synopsis: In "The Vote That Made the President" by David Dudley Field, a gripping tale unfolds as a pivotal election hangs in the balance. With the fate of the nation at stake, a small group of influential individuals must navigate a treacherous political landscape, facing corruption, betrayal, and personal sacrifices. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the power of a single vote becomes the ultimate weapon in determining the destiny of the presidency.

Genres: Political thriller, Historical fiction, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Political manipulation
