Synopsis: In 'The Yellow Streak' by Valentine Williams, a mysterious and elusive criminal known as "The Yellow Streak" terrorizes the city, leaving a trail of fear and chaos in his wake. As the police struggle to catch him, a young detective finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will the detective be able to outsmart the cunning Yellow Streak, or will he become the next victim in this thrilling and suspenseful tale?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Pursuit of Justice
