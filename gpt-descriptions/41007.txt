Synopsis: In 'The Ku Klux Klan' by Annie Cooper Burton, a small town is shaken by the arrival of a mysterious group that spreads fear and hatred. As tensions rise, a courageous young woman finds herself caught in the middle of a dangerous battle for justice and equality. With secrets lurking in every corner, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear. This gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of society and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of bigotry and oppression.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Racism, Social Justice, Courage
