Synopsis: 

In 'Building with Logs', Fickes and Groben take readers on an immersive journey into the world of log construction, revealing the secrets and techniques behind this timeless building method. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, the authors explore the art of log building, from selecting the perfect logs to mastering the intricate joinery required for a sturdy and beautiful structure. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover the rich history and cultural significance of log construction, as well as the practical knowledge needed to embark on their own log building adventure. Whether you are a seasoned builder or a curious enthusiast, 'Building with Logs' is sure to ignite your passion for this traditional craft and inspire you to create your own log masterpiece.

Genres: Non-fiction, Home Improvement, Crafts

Themes: Traditional craftsmanship, Sustainable building, Cultural heritage
