Synopsis:
'Old Mole: Being the Surprising Adventures in England of Herbert Jocelyn Beenham, M.A., Sometime Sixth-Form Master at Thrigsby Grammar School in the County of Lancaster' follows the unexpected journey of Herbert Jocelyn Beenham, a former schoolmaster, as he embarks on a series of thrilling and unconventional adventures across England. From encounters with eccentric characters to uncovering hidden secrets, Herbert's life takes an exhilarating turn, leading him to question his own identity and purpose. As he navigates through the twists and turns of his newfound existence, Herbert discovers that life's greatest surprises often lie just beneath the surface.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Identity, Exploration, Transformation
