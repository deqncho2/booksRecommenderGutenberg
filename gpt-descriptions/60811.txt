Synopsis: In Great Waters: Four Stories takes readers on a captivating journey through four interconnected tales that span across time and space. From the depths of the ocean to the heights of the sky, this collection explores the lives of extraordinary individuals who navigate the complexities of power, love, and destiny. As their paths intertwine, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the boundaries between reality and myth blur. With vivid storytelling and richly developed characters, Thomas A. Janvier weaves a tapestry of intrigue and wonder that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Relationships, Fate and Destiny
