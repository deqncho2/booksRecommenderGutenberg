Synopsis: In 'Prejudices, fifth series', H. L. Mencken takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through his sharp and unapologetic observations of society, politics, and culture. With his signature wit and biting humor, Mencken challenges conventional wisdom and exposes the absurdities of human behavior. From dissecting the follies of democracy to critiquing the hypocrisy of the American Dream, this collection of essays offers a refreshing and often controversial perspective on the world we live in.

Genres: Essay collection, Satire, Social commentary

Themes: Prejudice, Society and Culture, Politics, American Dream
