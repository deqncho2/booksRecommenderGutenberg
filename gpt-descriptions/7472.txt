Synopsis: In the midst of the tumultuous Shays' Rebellion, a young man named Richard Vining finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his love for a mysterious woman. As the rebellion escalates and danger looms, Richard must navigate treacherous paths, secret alliances, and unexpected revelations that will test his courage and reshape his destiny. Set against the backdrop of a nation in turmoil, 'The Duke of Stockbridge' is a captivating romance that explores the complexities of love, honor, and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Rebellion, Love and Sacrifice
