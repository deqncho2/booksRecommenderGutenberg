Synopsis: In 'The Altar Fire' by Arthur Christopher Benson, a young man named Stephen embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Drawn to the mystical allure of an ancient altar fire, Stephen finds himself caught between the realms of the physical and the metaphysical. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the fire, he encounters a series of enigmatic characters and experiences profound moments of introspection. With each encounter, Stephen's perception of reality becomes increasingly blurred, leading him to question the nature of existence and the boundaries of the human spirit. This captivating novel takes readers on a thought-provoking exploration of the unseen forces that shape our lives and the transformative power of self-reflection.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Spirituality, Metaphysics
