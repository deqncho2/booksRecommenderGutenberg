Synopsis: In 'If You Was a Moklin' by Leinster, Murray, a young scientist stumbles upon a remarkable discovery that allows him to experience life from a completely different perspective. As he delves deeper into this newfound ability, he uncovers a hidden world filled with unexpected challenges and thrilling adventures. With each transformation, he must navigate the complexities of identity, loyalty, and the true nature of humanity. This captivating tale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and exploration, where the boundaries of reality are pushed to their limits.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Exploration
