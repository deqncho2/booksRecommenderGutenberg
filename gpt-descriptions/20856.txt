Synopsis: In 'Ten From Infinity' by Paul W. Fairman, a group of ten strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious and isolated mansion. As they struggle to uncover the truth behind their confinement, they soon realize that their lives are at stake and that they must work together to unravel the secrets of the mansion before it's too late. With each passing moment, tension rises, alliances are tested, and the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred. Will they be able to escape their captor's twisted game, or will they succumb to the darkness that surrounds them?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Survival, Trust, Psychological Manipulation
