Synopsis:
'Eric, or Little by Little' by Farrar, F. W. is a gripping coming-of-age novel that follows the journey of Eric Williams, a young boy who enters a prestigious boarding school. As Eric navigates the challenges of adolescence, he finds himself caught between the pressures of conformity and the allure of rebellion. With friendships tested, secrets revealed, and moral dilemmas faced, Eric's choices will shape his future and determine the kind of person he becomes. This captivating tale explores the complexities of youth, the power of peer influence, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Drama, Bildungsroman

Themes:
Adolescence, Peer pressure, Morality, Identity
