Synopsis: In 'An Englishwoman's Home' by Annie S. Swan, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires when she inherits a beautiful country estate. As she navigates the challenges of managing the property and the expectations of society, Alice discovers a hidden secret that threatens to upend everything she holds dear. With the help of unexpected allies, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Independence, Society's Expectations, Secrets
