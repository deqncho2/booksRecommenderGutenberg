Synopsis: 

In 'Celebrated Claimants from Perkin Warbeck to Arthur Orton' by Anonymous, embark on a captivating journey through history as you delve into the lives of some of the most intriguing impostors and pretenders the world has ever known. From the enigmatic Perkin Warbeck, who claimed to be the lost prince of England, to the audacious Arthur Orton, who attempted to inherit a fortune by impersonating a long-lost heir, this book uncovers the fascinating tales of those who sought to deceive and claim what was not rightfully theirs. With each chapter presenting a new and captivating story, you will be left questioning the boundaries of truth and the lengths people will go to in order to attain power, wealth, or simply a new identity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, True crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power, Ambition
