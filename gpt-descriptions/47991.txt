Synopsis: 
In "Erchie, My Droll Friend" by Neil Munro, we are introduced to the charming and witty character of Erchie MacPherson, a lovable rogue with a knack for getting into humorous and often absurd situations. Through a series of entertaining anecdotes, we follow Erchie's misadventures and encounters with a colorful cast of characters, from his fellow Glaswegians to the upper echelons of society. With his quick wit and infectious humor, Erchie's escapades will keep readers laughing and eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Friendship, Social class, Scottish culture
