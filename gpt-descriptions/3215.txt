Synopsis: In 'Mr. Honey's Insurance Dictionary (English-German)', author Winfried Honig takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of insurance terminology. This comprehensive dictionary not only provides translations of insurance terms from English to German and vice versa, but also delves into the fascinating stories and anecdotes behind these terms. From the origins of insurance practices to the evolution of industry jargon, Honig's work offers a unique blend of linguistic exploration and historical context. Whether you are an insurance professional, a language enthusiast, or simply curious about the hidden stories behind everyday words, this dictionary is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Non-fiction, Language, Reference

Themes: Insurance, Linguistics, History
