Synopsis: In 'Tired Church Members' by Warner, Anna Bartlett, a small town congregation finds themselves grappling with a crisis that threatens to tear their community apart. As the church members struggle to navigate their own personal challenges, they must also confront the deep-rooted issues that have plagued their church for years. With tensions rising and faith wavering, the fate of the church hangs in the balance, forcing the characters to confront their own beliefs and redefine their sense of community.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Religious

Themes: Faith, Community, Redemption, Personal Struggles
