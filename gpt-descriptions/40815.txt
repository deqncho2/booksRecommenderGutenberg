Synopsis: In "Prices of Books: An Inquiry into the Changes in the Price of Books which have occurred in England at different Periods," Henry B. Wheatley takes readers on a fascinating journey through the history of book prices in England. From the humble beginnings of the printing press to the rise of the publishing industry, Wheatley explores the factors that have influenced the fluctuating costs of books over time. Delving into the economic, social, and cultural aspects of the book trade, this inquiry sheds light on the fascinating evolution of book pricing in England.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Economics

Themes: Book trade, Economic history, Cultural evolution
