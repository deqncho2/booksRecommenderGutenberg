Synopsis: In "The Blue Man: From "Mackinac And Lake Stories", 1899," Mary Hartwell Catherwood weaves a captivating tale set in the enchanting world of Mackinac Island. The story follows a young woman who becomes entangled in a mysterious legend surrounding a blue-skinned man who haunts the island. As she delves deeper into the island's history and the secrets it holds, she discovers a connection that will change her life forever. With its blend of folklore, romance, and suspense, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Legends and folklore, Identity and self-discovery
