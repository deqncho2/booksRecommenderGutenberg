Synopsis: 
In 'The Collected Works of Ambrose Bierce, Volume 10: The Opinionator', Ambrose Bierce presents a collection of thought-provoking and satirical essays that delve into various aspects of society and human nature. With his signature wit and biting humor, Bierce tackles topics such as politics, religion, and the absurdities of everyday life. Through his sharp observations and clever commentary, he challenges readers to question their own beliefs and assumptions, leaving them both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Satire, Essay, Humor

Themes: Society, Human nature, Critique
