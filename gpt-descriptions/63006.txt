Synopsis: 
'By-ways on Service: Notes from an Australian Journal' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast and diverse landscapes of Australia. Written by Hector Dinning, this journal offers a unique perspective on the author's experiences and encounters while traveling across the country. From the bustling cities to the remote outback, Dinning's vivid descriptions and insightful observations paint a vivid picture of the Australian way of life. As he delves into the heart of the land, the author uncovers hidden gems, encounters fascinating individuals, and explores the rich cultural tapestry that makes Australia so captivating. Through his engaging storytelling, Dinning invites readers to join him on this remarkable adventure, leaving them eager to discover the wonders of Australia for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Personal growth
