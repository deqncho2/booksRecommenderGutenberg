Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Family' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a small English village becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, secrets, and the complexities of family dynamics. Dr. Edward Rider, a respected physician, finds his life turned upside down when a mysterious woman arrives in town, stirring up long-buried emotions and challenging the stability of his family. As the secrets of the past unravel, the characters are forced to confront their own desires, loyalties, and the consequences of their actions. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, 'The Doctor's Family' is a compelling exploration of human relationships and the choices we make.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Love and desire, Secrets and revelations
