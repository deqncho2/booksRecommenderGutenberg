Synopsis: In 'Sonnets of Shakespeare's Ghost' by Thornton Gregory, a mysterious encounter with the ghost of William Shakespeare sets off a chain of events that plunges a struggling writer into a world of intrigue, romance, and literary secrets. As the protagonist delves deeper into the ghost's cryptic messages, they uncover a hidden society of writers who are willing to do anything to protect the legacy of the Bard. With danger lurking at every turn, the writer must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and preserve the literary treasures of the past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Legacy, Literature, Secrets, Identity
