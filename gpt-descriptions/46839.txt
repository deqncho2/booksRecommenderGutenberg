Synopsis: In 'The Heart of Wessex' by Sidney Heath, a young woman named Emma finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced to choose between two very different suitors. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque English countryside, Emma's journey takes her through a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, Emma must ultimately confront her own heart and make a decision that will shape her future.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and Duty, Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
