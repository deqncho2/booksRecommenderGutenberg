Synopsis: 
In 'Beyond These Voices' by M. E. Braddon, a young woman named Isabel is haunted by a mysterious voice that only she can hear. As she navigates her way through a web of secrets and deception, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of love and betrayal. With each passing day, the voice grows stronger, leading Isabel down a path she never could have imagined. Will she uncover the truth behind the voice, or will it consume her completely?

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Madness, Deception, Love and Betrayal
