Synopsis: In 'The Ruinous Face' by Maurice Hewlett, a mysterious and alluring woman arrives in a small village, captivating the hearts and minds of its inhabitants. As her presence begins to unravel the secrets and desires hidden within the community, a web of love, betrayal, and tragedy is spun, leading to unexpected consequences. With vivid descriptions and a touch of the supernatural, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the power of temptation.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and deception, Supernatural elements
