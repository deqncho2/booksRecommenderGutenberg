Synopsis: 

In 'Medicina Flagellata; Or, The Doctor Scarify'd' by Anonymous, we are introduced to a peculiar doctor who practices medicine in a rather unconventional manner. This satirical and humorous tale takes us on a journey through the doctor's bizarre treatments and the unexpected consequences that follow. As the story unfolds, we witness the doctor's encounters with various patients, each with their own unique ailments and eccentricities. With a blend of wit, irony, and absurdity, this anonymous work offers a hilarious critique of the medical profession and the human condition.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Critique of the medical profession, Human folly, Irony
