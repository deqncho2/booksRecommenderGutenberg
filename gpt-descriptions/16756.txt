Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, are thrilled to attend the County Fair, where they encounter a mysterious fortune teller, a daring trapeze act, and a missing prize-winning cow. As they navigate through the excitement and chaos of the fair, the twins find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure that tests their detective skills and brings them closer to uncovering the truth. With their quick thinking and determination, the Bobbsey Twins prove that even at the fair, there's always a mystery to solve.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
