Synopsis: 
'Letters from a Son to His Self-Made Father: Being the Replies to Letters from a Self-Made Merchant to His Son' is a captivating and thought-provoking novel that follows the correspondence between a son and his self-made father. As the son navigates his own journey through life, he shares his experiences, challenges, and aspirations with his father, who responds with wisdom and guidance. Through these heartfelt letters, the reader is taken on a poignant exploration of ambition, family dynamics, and the pursuit of success in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes: 
Ambition, Family relationships, Success, Personal growth
