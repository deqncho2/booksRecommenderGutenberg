Synopsis: In 'Tatlings' by Sydney Tremayne, a small town is rocked by a scandalous secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its residents. As rumors spread like wildfire, alliances are tested, friendships are shattered, and buried secrets are brought to light. With each chapter, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the town's web of deceit. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Gossip, Small-town dynamics
