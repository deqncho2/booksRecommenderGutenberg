Synopsis: In "Innocent: Her Fancy and His Fact" by Marie Corelli, a young woman named Innocent finds herself caught between the allure of her vivid imagination and the harsh realities of the world around her. As she navigates through love, society, and the pursuit of her dreams, Innocent must confront the clash between her fanciful ideals and the cold, hard truths that threaten to shatter her illusions. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating storyline, this novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the delicate balance between fantasy and reality.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Imagination vs. Reality, Love and Illusion, Self-Discovery
