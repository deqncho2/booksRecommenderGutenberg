Synopsis: In the remote and enigmatic Seal Islands, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a hidden secret that has been buried for centuries. As she delves deeper into the island's history, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and ancient legends. With each discovery, the line between reality and myth blurs, and she must unravel the truth before it consumes her. The Mystery of Seal Islands is a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of legends and myths, the blurred line between reality and imagination
