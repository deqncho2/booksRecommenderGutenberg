Synopsis: In "The Man Who Talked Too Much" by Roy Norton, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of secrets and danger after stumbling upon a mysterious conversation. As he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, he must navigate a treacherous world where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Betrayal
