Synopsis: In 'The Mythologies of Ancient Mexico and Peru', Lewis Spence takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich mythologies of two ancient civilizations. From the Aztecs to the Incas, Spence delves into the fascinating tales of gods, heroes, and creation myths that shaped the beliefs and rituals of these ancient cultures. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, he brings to life the awe-inspiring deities and epic legends that have endured for centuries. This book is a treasure trove of ancient wisdom and a testament to the enduring power of myth.

Genres: Mythology, History, Anthropology

Themes: Creation, Religion, Cultural Beliefs
