Synopsis: 
In the July 1886 issue of 'St. Nicholas: An Illustrated Magazine for Young Folks,' embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting stories, delightful poems, and captivating illustrations. Join the young readers of the late 19th century as they explore tales of adventure, mystery, and imagination. From thrilling adventures in far-off lands to heartwarming tales of friendship and bravery, this issue of 'St. Nicholas' is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery, Poetry

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Imagination, Bravery
