Synopsis: 

In 'Judith Lynn: A Story of the Sea' by Annie Hamilton Donnell, readers are transported to the enchanting world of the sea through the eyes of the spirited and adventurous Judith Lynn. Set against the backdrop of a coastal town, this captivating tale follows Judith as she embarks on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected discoveries. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the sea, Judith's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her to uncover secrets that will forever change her life.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Self-discovery
