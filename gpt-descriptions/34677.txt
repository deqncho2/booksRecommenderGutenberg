Synopsis: In 'Red Eagle and the Wars With the Creek Indians of Alabama,' George Cary Eggleston takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous conflicts between the Creek Indians and the settlers in Alabama. The story follows the life of Red Eagle, a Creek warrior torn between his loyalty to his people and his desire for peace. As tensions rise and battles ensue, Red Eagle finds himself facing difficult choices that will shape the destiny of his tribe and the future of the land they call home.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Native American literature

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Conflict, Cultural clashes
