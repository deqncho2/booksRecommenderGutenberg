Synopsis: In 'The Last Ditch' by Will Levington Comfort, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must navigate treacherous alliances, face unexpected betrayals, and confront her own inner demons. With time running out, Alice must make a choice that could determine the fate of not only her own life but also the world as she knows it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Betrayal, Redemption, Personal growth
