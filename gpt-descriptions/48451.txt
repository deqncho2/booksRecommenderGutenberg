Synopsis:
In Cassell's History of England, Vol. 1 (of 8): From the Roman Invasion to the Wars of the Roses, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the early history of England. From the arrival of the Romans to the tumultuous Wars of the Roses, this volume delves into the fascinating events, characters, and conflicts that shaped the nation. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the anonymous author brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and betrayals that defined England's formative years. From the rise and fall of kingdoms to the clash of cultures, this volume sets the stage for the epic saga that is England's history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, War

Themes: Power and politics, Nation-building, Conflict and warfare
