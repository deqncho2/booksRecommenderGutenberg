Synopsis: In "When a Man's a Man" by Harold Bell Wright, a young man named Jack is determined to prove himself as a true man in the rugged and unforgiving American West. Facing numerous challenges and temptations, Jack must navigate the complexities of love, honor, and self-discovery. As he embarks on a journey of personal growth, Jack's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading to unexpected revelations and life-altering decisions.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Masculinity, Self-Identity, Morality, Love and Relationships
