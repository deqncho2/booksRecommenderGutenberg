Synopsis: In "The Comic History of the United States," Sherwood takes readers on a hilarious and satirical journey through the history of America, starting from a time before its discovery and continuing long after the present. From the antics of early explorers to the absurdities of political figures, Sherwood's witty storytelling and comical illustrations bring to life the often overlooked and humorous aspects of American history. Prepare to be entertained and enlightened as you discover the unexpected twists and turns that have shaped the nation we know today.

Genres: Satire, History, Comedy

Themes: American history, Humor, Satire
