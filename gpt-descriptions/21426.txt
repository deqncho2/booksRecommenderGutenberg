Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Daddy Longlegs: Tuck-Me-In Tales' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the enchanting adventures of Daddy Longlegs as he embarks on a journey to help a young girl named Betty find her lost doll. Along the way, Daddy Longlegs encounters a host of delightful characters and faces unexpected challenges that test his courage and resourcefulness. This heartwarming tale is filled with whimsy, friendship, and the power of determination, making it a perfect bedtime story for children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
