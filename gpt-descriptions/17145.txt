Synopsis: In 'Hallowe'en at Merryvale' by Alice Hale Burnett, a group of friends gathers at the mysterious Merryvale estate for a thrilling Halloween celebration. As the night unfolds, they encounter strange occurrences and uncover long-held secrets that test their courage and friendship. With suspense and intrigue, this captivating tale takes readers on a haunting journey through the shadows of Merryvale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Courage
