Synopsis: In the not-too-distant future, space travel has become a reality, and a group of young men are chosen to embark on a dangerous mission to mine precious minerals from asteroids. As they face the perils of space and the challenges of their own personal demons, they must navigate through a treacherous journey that will test their courage, loyalty, and determination. With the fate of their mission hanging in the balance, they must confront the ultimate question: is the pursuit of wealth worth risking their lives?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Sacrifice
