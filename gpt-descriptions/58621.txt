Synopsis: In 'War-Time Breads and Cakes', Amy L. Handy takes readers on a mouthwatering journey through the challenges and creativity of baking during times of war. From rationing ingredients to finding innovative substitutes, Handy shares her secrets for creating delicious breads and cakes that bring comfort and joy even in the midst of hardship. With recipes that have stood the test of time, this book is a delightful blend of history, culinary expertise, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Cookbook, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Food and cooking during wartime, resourcefulness, resilience
