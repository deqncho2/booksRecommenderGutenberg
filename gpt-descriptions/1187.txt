Synopsis: In 'War of the Classes' by Jack London, a young man from the working class finds himself thrust into the world of the upper class, where he must navigate the complexities of social hierarchy and power dynamics. As he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ambition, he is forced to question his own identity and the society that has shaped him. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, London explores the clash between the classes and the consequences of societal divisions.

Genres: Fiction, Social Commentary, Adventure

Themes: Class struggle, Identity, Love and betrayal
