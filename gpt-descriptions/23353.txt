Synopsis: In 'A Horse Book' by Mary Tourtel, a young girl named Lucy discovers a hidden world of adventure and friendship when she encounters a mysterious horse. As Lucy embarks on a thrilling journey, she learns valuable lessons about courage, loyalty, and the power of dreams. With captivating illustrations and an enchanting narrative, this book will transport readers into a world where anything is possible.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
