Synopsis: In "Straight America, a call to national service," Frances Kellor presents a compelling exploration of the challenges faced by the United States and proposes a thought-provoking solution. Through a combination of personal anecdotes, historical analysis, and social commentary, Kellor delves into the heart of America's societal issues, urging its citizens to embrace a sense of duty and service to their nation. With a keen eye for detail and a passionate voice, the author paints a vivid picture of the struggles and potential triumphs that lie ahead for a country in need of unity and collective action.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Commentary, Political Science

Themes: National service, Civic duty, Social responsibility, Unity, American identity
