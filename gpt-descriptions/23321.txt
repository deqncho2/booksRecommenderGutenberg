Synopsis: In "The Choctaw Freedmen and the Story of Oak Hill Industrial Academy," Robert Elliott Flickinger delves into the fascinating history of the Choctaw Freedmen and their journey at the Oak Hill Industrial Academy. This captivating narrative explores the struggles, triumphs, and resilience of a community fighting for their rights and education in the face of adversity. Flickinger's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the untold story of the Choctaw Freedmen and their remarkable legacy at Oak Hill.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, African American Studies

Themes: Civil rights, Education, Identity, Resilience
