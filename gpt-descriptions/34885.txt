Synopsis: In 'Garden Ornaments' by Mary Harrod Northend, a seemingly ordinary garden becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of mystery and intrigue. When a young woman moves into a new house with a neglected garden, she discovers a hidden world of secrets and forgotten treasures. As she delves deeper into the history of the garden, she uncovers a web of past events that intertwine with her own life, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected connections. With each garden ornament she uncovers, the protagonist unravels a new layer of the garden's enigmatic past, ultimately revealing a truth that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of nature and gardens, the interconnectedness of past and present
