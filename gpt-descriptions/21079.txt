Synopsis: 
In the small fishing village of Rathmoye, a mysterious trawler arrives, bringing with it a sense of foreboding and unease. As the villagers become entangled in the trawler's dark secrets, they are forced to confront their own fears and desires, leading to a series of shocking events that will forever change their lives. With its atmospheric setting and complex characters, 'The Trawler' is a gripping tale of suspense and mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the impact of the unknown, the fragility of human nature
