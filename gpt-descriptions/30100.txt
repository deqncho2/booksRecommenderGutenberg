Synopsis:
Marion Fay: A Novel by Anthony Trollope follows the life of Marion Fay, a young woman with dreams and ambitions that defy the societal norms of her time. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and career, Marion finds herself torn between duty and desire, challenging the expectations placed upon her as a woman in Victorian England. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists and turns, Marion Fay takes readers on a captivating journey through the triumphs and tribulations of a woman determined to forge her own path.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Victorian Literature

Themes:
Gender roles, Ambition, Love and Marriage
