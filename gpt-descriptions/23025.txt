Synopsis: In 'The Book of Business Etiquette', Nella Braddy Henney takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of professional conduct and social graces. With a blend of practical advice and entertaining anecdotes, Henney explores the dos and don'ts of navigating the business world, from proper introductions and correspondence to appropriate attire and dining etiquette. Through her insightful observations, she sheds light on the importance of cultivating a polished image and building strong relationships in order to succeed in the competitive realm of business. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, this book is a valuable resource for anyone looking to enhance their professional presence and make a lasting impression.

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Self-help

Themes: Professionalism, Social skills, Personal branding, Networking
