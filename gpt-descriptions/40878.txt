Synopsis: In 'Turner' by W. Cosmo Monkhouse, a mysterious and enigmatic artist named Turner captures the attention of a young art enthusiast, leading her on a journey through the vibrant and captivating world of art. As she delves deeper into Turner's life and works, she uncovers secrets and hidden truths that challenge her perception of art and reality. With each revelation, the line between imagination and truth becomes blurred, leaving her questioning the very essence of artistic expression.

Genres: Mystery, Art, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Artistic interpretation, Perception, Identity, Truth vs. Illusion
