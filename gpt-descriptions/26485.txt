Synopsis: 
"The Making of Bobby Burnit: Being a Record of the Adventures of a Live American Young Man" follows the captivating journey of Bobby Burnit, a spirited and ambitious young man in early 20th century America. Born into a wealthy family, Bobby sets out to make a name for himself in the business world, facing numerous challenges and unexpected twists along the way. From navigating the complexities of love and relationships to encountering the cutthroat nature of corporate America, Bobby's adventures take readers on a thrilling ride filled with humor, romance, and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Comedy, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Ambition, Love, Social class, Self-identity
