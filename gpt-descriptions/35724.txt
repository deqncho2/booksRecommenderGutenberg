Synopsis: 

In '13 Days: The Chronicle of an Escape from a German Prison' by John Alan Lyde Caunter, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the harrowing experiences of a group of prisoners attempting to escape from a German prison during World War II. As the clock ticks and tensions rise, the prisoners must navigate through a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances in their desperate bid for freedom. With each passing day, the stakes become higher, and the question of who can be trusted becomes increasingly uncertain. Will they succeed in their daring escape, or will they fall victim to the relentless pursuit of their captors?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Betrayal
