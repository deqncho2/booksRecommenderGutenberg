Synopsis: In 'The Lumley Autograph' by Susan Fenimore Cooper, a mysterious and coveted autograph collection becomes the center of a thrilling quest that spans continents and centuries. As a young scholar delves into the secrets behind the Lumley Autograph, she uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected connections that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each turn of the page, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic revelation that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Obsession, Legacy, Identity
