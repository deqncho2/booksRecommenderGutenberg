Synopsis: 
In 'True Stories of Crime From the District Attorney's Office', Arthur Cheney Train takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of the criminal justice system. From high-profile murder cases to intricate fraud schemes, Train presents a collection of gripping true crime stories that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. With meticulous attention to detail and a keen understanding of human nature, Train delves into the minds of both criminals and those seeking justice, revealing the complex web of motives and consequences that drive these captivating tales. Prepare to be captivated by the shocking twists and turns that unfold within the pages of this riveting collection.

Genres: True Crime, Mystery, Legal Thriller

Themes: Justice and Injustice, Morality, Human Nature
