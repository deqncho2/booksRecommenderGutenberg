Synopsis: In "Spiritual Reformers in the 16th & 17th Centuries," Rufus M. Jones delves into the lives and teachings of influential spiritual leaders who emerged during a time of great religious upheaval. From the radical mystics to the passionate preachers, Jones uncovers the fascinating stories of these reformers and their profound impact on the spiritual landscape of their era. Through vivid narratives and insightful analysis, the book offers a captivating exploration of the diverse paths taken by these individuals as they sought to transform the religious practices and beliefs of their time.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Biography

Themes: Religious reform, Mysticism, Spiritual awakening, Religious tolerance
