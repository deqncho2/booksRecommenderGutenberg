Synopsis: In "Army of the Cumberland and the Battle of Stone's River," author G. C. Kniffin takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most significant battles of the American Civil War. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Kniffin brings to life the harrowing experiences of the soldiers of the Army of the Cumberland as they face the brutal realities of war and fight for their cause on the battlefield of Stone's River. With its compelling narrative and rich historical detail, this book offers a captivating exploration of the courage, sacrifice, and resilience of those who fought in this pivotal conflict.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American Civil War

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Battle strategies, Historical significance
