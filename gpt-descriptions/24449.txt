Synopsis:
In "Queen Summer; Or, The Tourney of the Lily and the Rose," Walter Crane takes readers on a captivating journey into a magical world where the seasons come alive. The story follows the enchanting Queen Summer as she presides over a grand tournament between the Lily and the Rose, two noble flowers vying for supremacy. As the tournament unfolds, readers are introduced to a host of whimsical characters, each representing different aspects of nature and the changing seasons. With stunning illustrations and lyrical prose, Crane weaves a tale of beauty, rivalry, and the eternal cycle of life.

Genres:
Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Children's Literature

Themes:
Nature, Seasons, Beauty, Rivalry, Transformation
