Synopsis: In 'This House to Let' by William Le Queux, a mysterious advertisement for a house to let catches the attention of four strangers. As they move into the peculiar house, they soon discover that it holds more secrets than they could have ever imagined. With each passing day, the house reveals its dark past and the occupants find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. As they strive to uncover the truth, they must confront their own fears and confront the sinister forces that threaten their very lives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Intrigue and deception, Survival and courage
