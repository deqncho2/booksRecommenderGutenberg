Synopsis: In 'A Little Question in Ladies' Rights' by Fillmore Parker, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires for independence. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and personal freedom, Emily must confront the limitations placed upon women in the 19th century. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, Emily's world is turned upside down, forcing her to question everything she thought she knew about herself and the role she is expected to play in society. This captivating tale explores the struggles and triumphs of a woman determined to challenge the status quo and find her own path in a world that seeks to confine her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Love and Marriage, Societal expectations
