Synopsis: In "The Parenticide Club" by Ambrose Bierce, a group of individuals gathers in a secret society with a sinister purpose: to discuss and plan the murder of their own parents. As the members share their chilling stories, the reader is drawn into a world of dark secrets, twisted motives, and unexpected consequences. With each tale, the boundaries of morality and the depths of human nature are explored, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of love, loyalty, and the lengths one would go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Morality, Betrayal, Family, Guilt, Manipulation
