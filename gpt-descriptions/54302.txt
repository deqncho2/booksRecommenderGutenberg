Synopsis: In 'College Men Without Money' by Riddle, Carl Brown, follow the captivating journey of a group of college students who find themselves in a desperate situation. Struggling to make ends meet, they embark on a daring adventure that will test their friendships, challenge their beliefs, and push them to their limits. As they navigate through the complexities of life, love, and the pursuit of their dreams, they discover the true meaning of resilience and the power of unity. Will they overcome their financial hardships and find a way to succeed against all odds?

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Socioeconomic struggles
