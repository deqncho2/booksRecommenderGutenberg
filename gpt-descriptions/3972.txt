Synopsis:
In 'The Ink-Stain (Tache d'encre) — Volume 1' by Bazin, René, we are introduced to the intriguing world of journalism through the eyes of a young writer named Jacques. As he navigates the bustling newsroom of a Parisian newspaper, Jacques becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, personal rivalries, and ethical dilemmas. With his determination to uncover the truth, Jacques finds himself caught in a dangerous game where the line between fact and fiction becomes blurred. As he delves deeper into his investigations, he discovers that the ink-stain of journalism can leave a lasting mark on both the page and the soul.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Journalism, Truth and Deception, Ambition, Morality
