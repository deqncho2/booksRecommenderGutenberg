Synopsis: In 'John Corwell, Sailor and Miner; and, Poisonous Fish: 1901' by Louis Becke, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of John Corwell, a sailor turned miner. From the treacherous seas to the dangerous depths of the mines, Corwell faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected adventures. However, his life takes an intriguing turn when he stumbles upon a mysterious case involving poisonous fish. As Corwell delves deeper into the investigation, he finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where his survival skills are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Intrigue
