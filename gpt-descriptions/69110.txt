Synopsis: In 'The Magic Christian', a mysterious billionaire and his adopted son embark on a series of outrageous and absurd pranks, testing the limits of human greed and morality. From staging bizarre events to offering extravagant rewards, they expose the true nature of society and the lengths people will go for money. As their antics escalate, the line between satire and reality becomes blurred, leading to unexpected consequences and a shocking climax.

Genres: Satire, Black Comedy, Absurdist Fiction

Themes: Greed, Morality, Corruption, Consumerism
