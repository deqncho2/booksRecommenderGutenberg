Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious old woman arrives, bringing with her a collection of ancient songs that have the power to unlock forgotten memories and hidden secrets. As the townspeople become enthralled by the haunting melodies, they soon realize that these songs hold the key to their own pasts and the future of their community. With each note, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leading to unexpected revelations and life-altering choices. 'Old Songs' is a captivating tale that explores the power of music to heal, transform, and connect people across generations and time.

Genres: Magical realism, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Memory, Identity, Community, Transformation
