Synopsis: 
In the remote and hauntingly beautiful landscape of Dartmoor, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn into a mysterious and dangerous world. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the moor, she uncovers a hidden history that intertwines with her own family's past. With each step she takes, the line between reality and myth becomes blurred, and Emily must confront the darkness that lurks beneath the surface. Dartmoor is a gripping tale of suspense and discovery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Myth and folklore, Identity, Nature vs. civilization
