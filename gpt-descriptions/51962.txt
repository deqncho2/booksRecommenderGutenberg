Synopsis: In 'Bill Nye's Sparks', the renowned scientist and TV personality, Bill Nye, takes readers on an electrifying journey through the fascinating world of electricity. With his signature wit and enthusiasm, Nye explores the history, science, and practical applications of this powerful force, from the discovery of static electricity to the invention of the light bulb. Along the way, he shares captivating anecdotes, conducts thrilling experiments, and reveals the surprising ways in which electricity shapes our daily lives. Prepare to be amazed and enlightened as Nye sparks your curiosity and ignites your passion for the electrifying wonders of the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Memoir

Themes: Electricity, Science Education, Curiosity
