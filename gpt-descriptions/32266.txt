Synopsis: In 'Sugar Plum' by Reginald Bretnor, a seemingly ordinary man named George discovers a mysterious object that grants him an extraordinary ability. As he navigates the consequences of this newfound power, George finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Sugar Plum' takes readers on a thrilling journey that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery
