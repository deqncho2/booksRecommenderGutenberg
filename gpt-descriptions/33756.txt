Synopsis: 

'Village Life in America 1852-1872, Including the Period of the American Civil War: As Told in the Diary of a School-Girl' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a young school-girl living in a small American village during a tumultuous period in history. Through her diary entries, we witness the daily struggles, joys, and challenges faced by the villagers, as well as the impact of the American Civil War on their lives. As the school-girl navigates her own personal growth and the changing dynamics of her community, readers are transported to a bygone era filled with vivid descriptions and heartfelt emotions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Coming-of-Age

Themes: American History, Community, Personal Growth, Resilience
