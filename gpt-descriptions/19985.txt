Synopsis: In 'The Dyeing of Woollen Fabrics' by Beech and Franklin, embark on a captivating journey into the world of textile dyeing. From the vibrant hues of ancient civilizations to the intricate techniques of modern times, this book unveils the secrets behind the art of dyeing wool. Discover the fascinating history, innovative methods, and stunning results that have shaped the world of fashion and design. With detailed instructions and inspiring examples, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to explore the colorful realm of textile dyeing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft and Hobbies, History

Themes: Textile art, Historical techniques, Color and design
