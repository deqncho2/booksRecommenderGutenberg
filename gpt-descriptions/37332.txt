Synopsis: In "A Little Princess: Being the whole story of Sara Crewe now told for the first time," Frances Hodgson Burnett takes readers on a captivating journey into the life of Sara Crewe, a young girl with a vivid imagination and a heart full of resilience. From her privileged upbringing in India to her unexpected circumstances at Miss Minchin's boarding school in London, Sara's world is turned upside down. As she faces adversity and loss, Sara's unwavering spirit and kindness shine through, inspiring those around her and proving that true strength comes from within.

Genres: Children's literature, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Resilience, Kindness, Friendship, Social class
