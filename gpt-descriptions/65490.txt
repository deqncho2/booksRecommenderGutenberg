Synopsis: In 'Magic London' by Netta Syrett, a young woman named Alice discovers a hidden world of enchantment and mystery within the bustling streets of London. As she navigates through secret societies, magical creatures, and unexpected alliances, Alice must uncover the truth about her own heritage and the power she possesses. With danger lurking at every corner, she embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and determination. 

Genres: Fantasy, Urban Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Discovery
