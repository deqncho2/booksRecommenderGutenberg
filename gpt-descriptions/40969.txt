Synopsis: In 'The Mating of the Moons' by Walton, Bryce, a young astronomer stumbles upon a celestial phenomenon that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient prophecies, forbidden love, and a race against time to prevent a catastrophic event. With the fate of both Earth and the moon hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own inner demons to save the world as he knows it.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Fate and destiny, Forbidden love, Sacrifice, The power of knowledge
