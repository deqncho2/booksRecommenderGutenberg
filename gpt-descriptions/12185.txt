Synopsis: In 'My Home in the Field of Honor' by Frances Wilson Huard, the author takes us on a captivating journey through her experiences as a nurse during World War I. From the chaos of the battlefield to the intimate moments of caring for wounded soldiers, Huard's vivid and heartfelt storytelling immerses readers in the harrowing realities of war. With a unique perspective as a woman on the front lines, she offers a poignant and gripping account that will leave readers both moved and inspired.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Gender Roles, Resilience
