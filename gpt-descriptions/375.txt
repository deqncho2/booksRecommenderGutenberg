Synopsis: A man finds himself on the brink of death as he awaits execution on Owl Creek Bridge. In the moments leading up to his demise, his mind races with vivid memories and desperate hopes for escape. As the story unfolds, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is truly happening. 

Genres: Psychological fiction, Suspense, War fiction

Themes: Perception of time, Illusion vs. reality, The human will to survive
