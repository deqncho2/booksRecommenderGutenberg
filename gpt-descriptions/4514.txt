Synopsis: 
In 'Tales of Men and Ghosts' by Edith Wharton, readers are transported to a world where the line between the living and the supernatural is blurred. This collection of haunting and mysterious tales takes us on a journey through the lives of various characters, each grappling with their own encounters with the otherworldly. From eerie apparitions to unexplained phenomena, these stories delve into the depths of human emotions and the unexplored realms of the spirit world. As the characters navigate their way through these uncanny experiences, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately leading to unexpected and often chilling outcomes.

Genres: 
Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, psychological fiction

Themes: 
The supernatural, the human psyche, fear and desire, the boundaries of reality
