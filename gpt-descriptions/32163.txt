Synopsis: Elsie on the Hudson follows the captivating journey of Elsie Dinsmore as she embarks on a summer adventure along the picturesque Hudson River. Amidst breathtaking landscapes and encounters with intriguing characters, Elsie finds herself facing unexpected challenges that test her resilience and strength. As she navigates through the twists and turns of this enchanting tale, Elsie discovers the power of friendship, the importance of family bonds, and the beauty of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Family, Friendship, Self-discovery
