Synopsis: In "Pebbles on the Shore" by A. G. Gardiner, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of secrets, they are forced to confront their own hidden desires and buried pasts. With each passing day, the stranger's influence grows stronger, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of those who dare to cross his path.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
