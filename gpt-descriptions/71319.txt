Synopsis:
In 'Clarissa Dormer: or, The Advantages of Good Instruction' by Anonymous, we are introduced to the young and spirited Clarissa Dormer. Raised in a sheltered environment, Clarissa's life takes an unexpected turn when she is sent away to receive a proper education. As she navigates the challenges of a new school and the complexities of societal expectations, Clarissa discovers the power of knowledge and the importance of self-discovery. Along the way, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters who shape her journey and challenge her beliefs. Will Clarissa find her true path and embrace the advantages of good instruction?

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Education, Self-discovery, Societal Expectations
