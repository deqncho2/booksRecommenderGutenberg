Synopsis:
In 'Bread' by Charles G. Norris, a young and ambitious baker named John Chapman sets out to revolutionize the bread industry in early 20th century America. As he navigates the cutthroat world of business, he encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely alliances. With his determination and innovative ideas, John strives to create a bread that not only satisfies hunger but also nourishes the soul. This captivating tale explores the power of passion, the pursuit of success, and the transformative nature of a simple loaf of bread.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Business, Drama

Themes:
Ambition, Innovation, Success, Transformation
