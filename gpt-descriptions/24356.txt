Synopsis: 
In "Sixty years with Plymouth Church," Stephen Morrell Griswold takes us on a captivating journey through the history of one of America's most influential churches. From its humble beginnings to its pivotal role in the abolitionist movement, Griswold's personal anecdotes and vivid descriptions bring to life the struggles, triumphs, and controversies that shaped Plymouth Church over six decades. As he delves into the lives of the church's charismatic leaders and passionate congregants, Griswold uncovers a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. 

Genres: 
Biography, History, Religion

Themes: 
Faith and spirituality, Social justice, Leadership
