Synopsis: In "Etiquette Made Easy" by Edward Summers Squier, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into high society, where she must navigate the treacherous waters of proper behavior and social expectations. With the help of a mysterious mentor, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, learning the secrets of etiquette and the power it holds in shaping one's destiny. As Emily delves deeper into this world of elegance and refinement, she uncovers hidden truths and unearths long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. Will she be able to master the art of etiquette and find her place in this complex world, or will she succumb to the pressures and lose herself in the process?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Etiquette, Social class, Identity, Self-discovery
