Synopsis: In "Magnum Bonum; Or, Mother Carey's Brood," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Carey family. As the story unfolds, secrets, mysteries, and unexpected events intertwine, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this novel explores the complexities of family dynamics, the power of love, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Family, Love, Secrets, Resilience
