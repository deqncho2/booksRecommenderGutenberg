Synopsis: In 'The Ghost Ship' by Richard Middleton, a young man named John wanders into a mysterious ship that appears out of thin air. As he explores the vessel, he encounters a crew of eccentric characters who seem to be trapped in a perpetual state of haunting. John becomes entangled in their eerie world, questioning his own reality and the nature of life and death. With each passing moment, the ship reveals its secrets, leading John on a journey of self-discovery and existential contemplation.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological

Themes: Existentialism, Identity, Perception, Life and Death
