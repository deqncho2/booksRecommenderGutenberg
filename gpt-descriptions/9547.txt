Synopsis: In "The Cruise of the Dry Dock," a group of friends embark on a seemingly ordinary sailing trip, only to find themselves caught in a web of mystery and danger. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, they must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive. With each twist and turn, the true nature of their journey is revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Deception
