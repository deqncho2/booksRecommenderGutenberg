Synopsis: In 'The Lost Road' by Davis, Richard Harding, a young adventurer named Jack embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he encounters unexpected allies, treacherous obstacles, and uncovers a hidden world filled with secrets and danger. With time running out, Jack must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own fears to solve the puzzle and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Betrayal, Discovery
