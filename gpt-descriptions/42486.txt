Synopsis: In "The Two Magics: The Turn of the Screw, Covering End" by Henry James, a young governess is hired to care for two orphaned children in a remote English country house. As she settles into her new role, she begins to experience strange and unsettling occurrences that challenge her perception of reality. Is she truly witnessing supernatural events or is her imagination playing tricks on her? With each passing day, the governess becomes increasingly obsessed with protecting the children from the mysterious forces that surround them, leading to a chilling climax that will leave readers questioning what is real and what is imagined.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Perception vs. reality, the supernatural, innocence and corruption
