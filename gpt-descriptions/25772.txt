Synopsis: In 'Little Lost Sister' by Virginia Brooks, a young woman named Emma embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her long-lost sister. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Emma finds herself entangled in a web of mystery, betrayal, and unexpected connections. With each clue she uncovers, Emma's determination to find her sister grows stronger, leading her down a path she never could have imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Identity, Secrets and lies
