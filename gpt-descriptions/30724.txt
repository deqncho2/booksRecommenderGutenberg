Synopsis: In 'Absolution' by Clara Viebig, a small German village is shaken by a scandalous event that threatens to expose long-held secrets and ignite a web of betrayal and redemption. As the truth slowly unravels, the lives of the villagers become entangled in a complex dance of guilt, forgiveness, and the search for absolution. With vivid characters and a gripping narrative, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Guilt, Betrayal, Redemption, Secrets
