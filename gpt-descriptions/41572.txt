Synopsis: In 'The Golden Bough: A Study in Magic and Religion', James George Frazer delves into the fascinating world of ancient beliefs and rituals. This third edition, Volume 04 of 12, takes readers on a captivating journey through various cultures and civilizations, exploring the intricate connections between magic, religion, and the human psyche. From the mystical practices of ancient priests to the superstitions of everyday people, Frazer uncovers the hidden threads that bind humanity's spiritual past. As he weaves together myths, legends, and historical accounts, the reader is left pondering the profound impact of these ancient beliefs on our modern world.

Genres: Anthropology, History, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Religion, Cultural Beliefs, Human Psychology
