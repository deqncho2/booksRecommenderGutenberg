Synopsis: 

'Indian Ghost Stories: Second Edition' by Mukerji, S. is a captivating collection of chilling tales that will transport readers into the mysterious realm of the supernatural. From haunted houses to vengeful spirits, this anthology delves into the eerie and spine-tingling experiences of individuals who encounter the paranormal in various forms. Each story is intricately woven with suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats as they navigate through the twists and turns of these haunting narratives. 

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Fiction

Themes: Ghosts, Fear, Mystery, Indian folklore
