Synopsis: In 'A Humble Enterprise' by Cambridge, Ada, a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience as she navigates the challenges of running her own business in Victorian England. With determination and a touch of audacity, Lucy faces societal expectations, financial struggles, and unexpected alliances, all while striving to maintain her integrity and independence. As her humble enterprise grows, Lucy finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, forcing her to confront the true nature of those around her. This captivating tale of ambition, love, and the pursuit of dreams will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Female Empowerment, Social Class, Entrepreneurship
