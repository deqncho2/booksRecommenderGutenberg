Synopsis: 

'Government Documents in Small Libraries: Reprinted from Report of Board of Library Commissioners of Ohio for the Year ending November 15, 1909' by Charles Wells Reeder is a fascinating exploration of the challenges faced by small libraries in managing government documents. Through detailed analysis and practical examples, Reeder sheds light on the importance of these documents and offers valuable insights into their organization and accessibility. This informative work provides a unique perspective on the role of small libraries in preserving and disseminating government information.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Library management, Government information, Access to information
