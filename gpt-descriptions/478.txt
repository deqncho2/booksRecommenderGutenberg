Synopsis: 
In 'The Cost' by David Graham Phillips, a young and ambitious lawyer named John Strickland finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he takes on a high-profile case involving a powerful and influential family. As he delves deeper into the case, Strickland uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to destroy not only his career but also his personal life. With his reputation on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path and make difficult choices that will have far-reaching consequences. 

Genres: Legal thriller, suspense, drama

Themes: Corruption, power, morality, personal sacrifice
