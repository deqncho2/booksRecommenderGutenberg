Synopsis: In 'Sir Mortimer: A Novel' by Mary Johnston, readers are transported to the enchanting world of medieval England, where the charismatic and enigmatic Sir Mortimer captures the hearts and imaginations of all who encounter him. As the story unfolds, we follow the journey of a young woman named Eleanor, who becomes entangled in a web of secrets, intrigue, and forbidden love, all revolving around the mysterious Sir Mortimer. With each twist and turn, Eleanor must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, honor, and betrayal, as she discovers the true nature of the man who has captured her heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
