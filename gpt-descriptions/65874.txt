Synopsis: In 'This World is Ours!' by Emil Petaja, a mysterious artifact is discovered, setting off a thrilling adventure that spans across time and space. As a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a quest to unlock the artifact's secrets, they encounter ancient civilizations, mythical creatures, and face the ultimate test of their courage and loyalty. With each step, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving the reader captivated until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Destiny, Friendship, Mythology
