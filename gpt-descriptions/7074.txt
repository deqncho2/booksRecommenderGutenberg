Synopsis: In 'Beauty and the Beast' by Leprince de Beaumont, Jeanne-Marie, a young woman finds herself in a mysterious castle, where she encounters a fearsome beast. As she spends more time with the beast, she begins to see beyond his exterior and discovers the true nature of love and beauty. This enchanting tale takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and the transformative power of compassion.

Genres: Fairy tale, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Beauty and appearance, Love and compassion, Inner beauty, Transformation
