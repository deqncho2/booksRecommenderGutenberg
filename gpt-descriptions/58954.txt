Synopsis: 
In 'The Enchanted Burro: And Other Stories as I Have Known Them from Maine to Chile and California', Charles Fletcher Lummis takes readers on a captivating journey through his adventurous life, filled with fascinating encounters and remarkable tales. From his experiences in the rugged landscapes of Maine to the vibrant cultures of Chile and California, Lummis weaves together a collection of enchanting stories that will leave readers spellbound. Through his vivid storytelling, he introduces us to a cast of colorful characters, both human and animal, and invites us to explore the beauty and mystery of the world around us.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Connection with nature
