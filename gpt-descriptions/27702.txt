Synopsis: In "Mr. Stubbs's Brother: A Sequel to 'Toby Tyler'" by James Otis, we follow the adventures of Toby Tyler's mischievous monkey, Mr. Stubbs, as he embarks on a thrilling journey to find his long-lost brother. Along the way, Mr. Stubbs encounters a colorful cast of characters, faces unexpected challenges, and learns valuable lessons about family, loyalty, and the true meaning of home. This heartwarming sequel is filled with excitement, humor, and a touch of nostalgia that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Animal Fiction

Themes: Family, Loyalty, Home, Adventure
