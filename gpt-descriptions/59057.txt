Synopsis: 
'Original Plays [First Series]' by Gilbert, W. S. is a collection of witty and satirical plays that will transport readers into a world of clever wordplay and humorous situations. From a hilarious courtroom drama to a comical exploration of love and marriage, each play presents a unique and entertaining story that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With Gilbert's signature wit and sharp social commentary, this collection is sure to delight fans of comedic theater.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Love and Marriage, Social Commentary
