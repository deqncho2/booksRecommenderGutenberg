Synopsis: Marcy, the Refugee by Castlemon, Harry is a gripping tale that follows the journey of Marcy, a young girl forced to flee her war-torn homeland and seek refuge in a foreign land. As she navigates the challenges of adapting to a new culture and language, Marcy's resilience and determination are put to the test. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, faces heart-wrenching choices, and discovers the true meaning of home. This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover Marcy's fate.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Friendship, Home
