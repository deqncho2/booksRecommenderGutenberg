Synopsis: In 'The Discovery of Witches' by Matthew Hopkins, a young historian stumbles upon an ancient manuscript that reveals a hidden world of magic and supernatural beings. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of power struggles, forbidden love, and dark secrets. With the fate of both humans and creatures hanging in the balance, she must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own hidden powers to uncover the truth and save them all.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Magic, Identity, Power, Love, Good vs. Evil
