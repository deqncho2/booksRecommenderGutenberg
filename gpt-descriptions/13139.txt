Synopsis: In Volume 2 of 'Lives of John Donne, Henry Wotton, Rich'd Hooker, George Herbert, &c', Izaak Walton delves into the fascinating lives of several influential figures from the 17th century. From the complex and passionate John Donne, to the witty and diplomatic Henry Wotton, and the brilliant theologian Richard Hooker, Walton presents a captivating exploration of their lives, achievements, and the impact they had on their contemporaries. Additionally, he delves into the life of the renowned poet George Herbert, shedding light on his spiritual journey and poetic genius. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Walton brings these remarkable individuals to life, offering readers a glimpse into the vibrant world of 17th-century England.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Religion, Literature, Intellectualism
