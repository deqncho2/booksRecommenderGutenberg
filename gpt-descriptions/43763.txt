Synopsis: In 'Snap-Dragons; Old Father Christmas' by Juliana Horatia Ewing, a group of children gather around the fireplace on Christmas Eve to play a traditional game called Snap-Dragons. As they delve into the game, they are transported to a magical world where they encounter Old Father Christmas himself. With enchanting adventures and heartwarming moments, this story captures the essence of Christmas joy and the wonder of childhood imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Christmas

Themes: Christmas spirit, Imagination, Tradition
