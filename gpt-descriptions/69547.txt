Synopsis: In "Never the twain shall meet," Peter B. Kyne takes readers on a captivating journey through the contrasting worlds of the East and the West. The story follows two individuals from vastly different backgrounds who find themselves thrown together in a series of unexpected events. As they navigate cultural differences, societal expectations, and personal ambitions, their lives become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Kyne explores the complexities of human connection and the power of bridging divides.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural clash, Personal growth, Love and friendship
