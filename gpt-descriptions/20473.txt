Synopsis: 
In 'Grace Harlowe's Third Year at Overton College', Grace Harlowe returns to Overton College for her junior year, ready to face new challenges and adventures. As she navigates her way through academic pressures, social dynamics, and personal growth, Grace finds herself caught up in a mysterious secret society that threatens to disrupt the harmony of the college. With her friends by her side, Grace must unravel the secrets and protect the values she holds dear, all while discovering the true meaning of loyalty and friendship.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Personal Growth, Academic Pressure, Social Dynamics
