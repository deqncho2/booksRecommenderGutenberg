Synopsis: In "Shiloh" as Seen by a Private Soldier: With Some Personal Reminiscences, Olney Warren takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing Battle of Shiloh during the American Civil War. Through the eyes of a private soldier, we witness the chaos, fear, and camaraderie that defined this pivotal moment in history. As the battle unfolds, Warren's personal reminiscences provide a unique perspective on the human experience amidst the horrors of war. This powerful account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of the soldiers and the impact of this historic battle.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Survival
