Synopsis: In 'Mr. Jacobs: A Tale of the Drummer, the Reporter, and the Prestidigitateur' by Bates, Arlo, a mysterious drummer arrives in a small town, capturing the attention of a curious reporter and a skilled magician. As they delve into the enigmatic past of Mr. Jacobs, they uncover a web of secrets and illusions that will challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving the reader captivated until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Identity, Redemption
