Synopsis: In 'The Governess' by Julie M. Lippmann, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into the role of governess for a wealthy family in a grand estate. As she navigates the complexities of her new position, Emily becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden desires, and hidden agendas. With each passing day, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel the very fabric of her existence. As Emily delves deeper into the mysteries of the household, she must confront her own past and make choices that will forever alter her future.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Identity and self-discovery
