Synopsis: 

In "Old Time Wall Papers: An Account of the Pictorial Papers on Our Forefathers' Walls with a Study of the Historical Development of Wall Paper Making and Decoration," Kate Sanborn takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of wall papers. From the intricate designs adorning the walls of our ancestors to the evolution of wall paper making and decoration, Sanborn delves into the rich history and artistry behind this often overlooked aspect of interior design. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, she uncovers the stories behind the patterns, the techniques employed by craftsmen, and the cultural significance of wall papers throughout different eras. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Interior design

Themes: History of interior design, Artistic craftsmanship, Cultural significance of decorative arts
