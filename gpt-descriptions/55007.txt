Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 21, September 11, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in the heart of a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human nature is revealed. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Secrets and deception, Human nature
