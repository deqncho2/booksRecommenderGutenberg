Synopsis: In "The Legends of King Arthur and His Knights," Sir Thomas Malory and Sir James Knowles bring to life the timeless tales of King Arthur and his noble knights. From the enchanting sword in the stone to the epic quest for the Holy Grail, this collection of stories takes readers on a captivating journey through the mythical world of Camelot. Filled with chivalry, honor, and the pursuit of justice, these legends explore the triumphs and tragedies of Arthur and his valiant companions as they navigate love, loyalty, and the ultimate test of their knighthood.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Loyalty, Quest for Truth, Fate and Destiny
