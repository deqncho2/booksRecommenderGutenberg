Synopsis: 
In 'Betelguese: A Trip Through Hell' by de Esque, Jean, readers are taken on a mind-bending journey through a dark and twisted underworld. The story follows a group of unlikely companions who find themselves trapped in a nightmarish realm filled with unimaginable horrors. As they navigate through the treacherous landscapes and encounter bizarre creatures, they must confront their deepest fears and inner demons. With each step they take, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers questioning what is truly lurking in the shadows. 

Genres: Horror, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Fear, Identity, Redemption
