Synopsis: In 'Hard Cash' by Charles Reade, a young woman named Grace discovers a shocking secret about her family's wealth, leading her on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, deception, and unexpected alliances. As she delves deeper into the truth, Grace must navigate a treacherous world of greed, betrayal, and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With her life hanging in the balance, she must uncover the hard cash that holds the key to her survival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wealth and deception, Family secrets, Survival
