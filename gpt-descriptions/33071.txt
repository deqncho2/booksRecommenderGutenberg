Synopsis: 
In 'Harper's Round Table, July 23, 1895', a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey that takes them to uncharted territories. As they encounter mysterious creatures and face unexpected challenges, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of these intrepid explorers, eagerly anticipating what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Friendship
