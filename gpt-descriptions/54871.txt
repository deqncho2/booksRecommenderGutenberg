Synopsis: In 'The Great Push: An Episode of the Great War' by Patrick MacGill, readers are transported to the harrowing trenches of World War I. Through the eyes of a young Irish soldier, we witness the brutal realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the indomitable human spirit that persists amidst unimaginable horrors. As the protagonist faces the ultimate test of courage and resilience, the story unfolds with gripping intensity, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, the strength of the human spirit, camaraderie and friendship in the face of adversity.
