Synopsis: In 'Darkness and Dawn' by George Allan England, a catastrophic event plunges the world into darkness, leaving only a few survivors to navigate a desolate and dangerous landscape. As they struggle to rebuild society, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the ultimate test of human resilience. Amidst the ruins, a glimmer of hope emerges, leading them on a thrilling journey of discovery and redemption. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Post-Apocalyptic, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Redemption
