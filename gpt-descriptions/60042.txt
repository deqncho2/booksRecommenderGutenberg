Synopsis: In 'The Moon Princess: A Fairy Tale' by Harrison, Edith Ogden, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world filled with enchantment and mystery. As she embarks on a magical journey, she encounters talking animals, mythical creatures, and a moon princess with a secret. With the fate of the moon kingdom hanging in the balance, Lily must uncover her own hidden powers and make a choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Fairy Tale

Themes: Coming of age, Self-discovery, Magic, Friendship
