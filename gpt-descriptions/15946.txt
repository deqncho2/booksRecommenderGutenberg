Synopsis: 
'The Original Fables of La Fontaine: Rendered into English Prose by Fredk. Colin Tilney' is a collection of timeless fables written by Jean de La Fontaine, translated into English prose by Fredk. Colin Tilney. This captivating compilation brings to life the enchanting tales of animals and humans, exploring their relationships, follies, and moral lessons. From the cleverness of the fox to the wisdom of the owl, each fable presents a unique story that both entertains and enlightens readers of all ages. 

Genres: 
Fiction, Fables, Classic Literature

Themes: 
Morality, Human Nature, Animal Behavior
