Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Chums on a Houseboat; Or, The Rivals of the Mississippi," a group of adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey down the mighty Mississippi River. When they encounter a rival group with mysterious intentions, the Outdoor Chums find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events and dangerous situations. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and courage to outwit their rivals and uncover the truth. Will they succeed in their mission and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Competition, Bravery
