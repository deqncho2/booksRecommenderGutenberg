Synopsis: 
"The Chronicle of the Discovery and Conquest of Guinea. Vol. I" by Zurara, Gomes Eannes de is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey of exploration and conquest. Set in the 15th century, the story follows a group of Portuguese navigators as they embark on a daring expedition to the mysterious lands of Guinea. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unknown cultures, the explorers face numerous challenges and dangers that test their courage and determination. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, the chronicle offers a fascinating glimpse into the early days of European exploration and the impact it had on the world.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, adventure, exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, cultural encounters, bravery and resilience
