Synopsis: 
'Unto This Last, and Other Essays on Political Economy' by John Ruskin is a collection of thought-provoking essays that challenge conventional economic theories and explore the relationship between wealth, labor, and social justice. Through a series of powerful arguments and vivid examples, Ruskin presents a compelling case for a more humane and equitable economic system. From critiquing the dehumanizing effects of industrialization to advocating for the importance of fair wages and the value of craftsmanship, Ruskin's essays offer a fresh perspective on the intersection of economics and morality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Social Criticism

Themes: Wealth inequality, Labor rights, Social justice, Critique of industrialization
