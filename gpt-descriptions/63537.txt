Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Fun; Or, Fearless and True', Frank Merriwell, the charismatic and talented college athlete, finds himself facing a series of unexpected challenges that test his courage and integrity. From solving a mysterious disappearance to uncovering a dangerous secret society, Frank must rely on his wit, physical prowess, and unwavering determination to overcome these obstacles. As he navigates through thrilling adventures and encounters with both friends and foes, Frank's true character shines through, proving that he is not only fearless but also true to his values.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Integrity, Friendship
