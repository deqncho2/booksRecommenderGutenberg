Synopsis: In "Galileo and His Judges" by F. R. Wegg-Prosser, embark on a captivating journey through the life of the renowned scientist Galileo Galilei as he faces the daunting challenge of defending his revolutionary ideas against the powerful forces of the Inquisition. Set in 17th century Italy, this gripping narrative explores the intense intellectual and moral struggle between Galileo and his judges, revealing the profound impact of his discoveries on the world and the personal sacrifices he made in the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Drama

Themes: Scientific Revolution, Conflict between Science and Religion, Intellectual Freedom, Personal Sacrifice
