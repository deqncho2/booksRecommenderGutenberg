Synopsis: In 'The Last American: A Fragment from The Journal of Khan-li, Prince of Dimph-Yoo-Chur and Admiral in the Persian Navy' by John Ames Mitchell, readers are transported to a future world where the United States has vanished, and the last remaining American is discovered in a hidden underground city. As the enigmatic Khan-li recounts his extraordinary journey, filled with adventure, romance, and political intrigue, readers are taken on a thrilling ride through a post-apocalyptic landscape. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Survival, Identity, Exploration, Political Intrigue
