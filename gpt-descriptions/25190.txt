Synopsis: In 'A Tramp's Notebook', Roberts, Morley takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a wandering tramp. Through vivid descriptions and poignant observations, the tramp's notebook becomes a window into a world filled with unexpected encounters, hidden beauty, and the struggles of the marginalized. As the tramp navigates the harsh realities of life on the fringes of society, he discovers the power of human connection and the resilience of the human spirit. With each page, readers are drawn deeper into the tramp's compelling narrative, leaving them both moved and inspired.

Genres: Fiction, Travelogue, Social Commentary

Themes: Homelessness, Human Connection, Resilience, Marginalization
