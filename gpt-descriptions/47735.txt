Synopsis: In 'The Bull Calf, and Other Tales' by A. B. Frost, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of charming and humorous stories. From mischievous animals to clever tricksters, each tale is filled with delightful surprises and unexpected twists. With Frost's vivid illustrations bringing the characters to life, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of whimsy and laughter. Whether it's the adventures of a cunning fox or the antics of a misbehaving bull calf, this collection is sure to entertain and enchant readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Animal behavior, Trickery and deception, Morality, Adventure
