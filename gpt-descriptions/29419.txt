Synopsis: 
'The Book of Anecdotes and Budget of Fun' is a delightful compilation of over one thousand hilarious sayings and jokes from renowned wits and humorists. From witty one-liners to amusing anecdotes, this book promises to keep readers entertained and laughing throughout. Whether you're in need of a good chuckle or simply enjoy the art of humor, this collection is sure to bring a smile to your face.

Genres: Humor, Comedy, Anthology

Themes: Wit, Laughter, Entertainment
