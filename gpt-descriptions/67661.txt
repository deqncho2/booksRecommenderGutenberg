Synopsis: 
In "Gallipoli Diary" by John Graham Gillam, embark on a gripping and intimate account of one soldier's experiences during the infamous Gallipoli campaign of World War I. Through the eyes of the author, we witness the harrowing realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the indomitable spirit that emerges amidst unimaginable adversity. From the initial landing to the relentless battles that follow, this diary offers a raw and unfiltered perspective on the horrors and heroism of Gallipoli.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, War Memoir, Military History

Themes: 
War, Sacrifice, Resilience, Brotherhood
