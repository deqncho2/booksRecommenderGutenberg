Synopsis: In 'The Chevalier d'Auriac' by Levett Yeats, S. (Sidney), readers are transported to 17th century France, where the charming and enigmatic Chevalier d'Auriac finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As he navigates the treacherous world of the French court, the Chevalier must rely on his wit, swordsmanship, and unwavering loyalty to protect those he holds dear. With its captivating blend of romance, suspense, and historical detail, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Intrigue, Love and Sacrifice
