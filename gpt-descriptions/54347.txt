Synopsis: 

In "Autobiography of Sir John Rennie, F.R.S., Past President of the Institute of Civil Engineers," Sir John Rennie takes us on a captivating journey through his remarkable professional life as one of the most influential civil engineers of his time. From the beginning of the century to the present day, Rennie shares his experiences, achievements, and the challenges he faced in shaping the world we live in today. With a wealth of reminiscences and anecdotes, this autobiography offers a fascinating insight into the life of a visionary engineer who left an indelible mark on the world.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, History

Themes: Engineering, Innovation, Professional Growth, Historical Context
