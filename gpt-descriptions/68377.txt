Synopsis: In 'Let's Get Together' by Isaac Asimov, a renowned scientist is invited to a secret gathering where a group of brilliant minds from various fields come together to discuss a groundbreaking discovery. As the night unfolds, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. This gripping tale of intellectual intrigue and moral dilemmas will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ethics, Collaboration, Human potential
