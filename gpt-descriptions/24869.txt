Synopsis: 
In this captivating translation of 'The Rámáyan of Válmíki', the ancient Indian epic comes to life in mesmerizing English verse. Follow the heroic journey of Prince Rama as he embarks on a quest to rescue his beloved wife, Sita, who has been abducted by a sinister demon king. Along the way, Rama encounters a host of fascinating characters, including a loyal monkey warrior named Hanuman, and faces numerous trials and tribulations that test his courage and devotion. Filled with epic battles, divine interventions, and profound moral dilemmas, this timeless tale of love, honor, and sacrifice will transport readers to a world of enchantment and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Epic Poetry, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Love and Devotion, Heroism, Good vs. Evil
