"Yanks : $b A.E.F. verse" is a captivating collection of poetry originally published in "The Stars and Stripes," the official newspaper of the American expeditionary forces during World War I. This anthology offers a unique glimpse into the experiences, emotions, and camaraderie of the American soldiers serving overseas. Through poignant verses, the poems explore the triumphs, struggles, and sacrifices of these brave men, providing a powerful and heartfelt tribute to their service.

Genres: Poetry, War Literature
