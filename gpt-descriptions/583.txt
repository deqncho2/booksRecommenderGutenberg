Synopsis:
The Woman in White by Wilkie Collins is a gripping Victorian mystery that follows the lives of two half-sisters, Laura Fairlie and Marian Halcombe. When Laura becomes engaged to Sir Percival Glyde, a mysterious woman dressed in white appears and warns Laura against the marriage. As Laura's life takes a dark turn, Marian becomes determined to uncover the truth behind the woman in white and protect her sister from danger. Filled with secrets, deception, and unexpected twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes:
Identity, Deception, Social class, Madness
