Synopsis:
'British Popular Customs, Present and Past: Illustrating the Social and Domestic Manners of the People. Arranged According to the Calendar of the Year' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of British customs and traditions. From ancient rituals to modern-day celebrations, this book unveils the fascinating social and domestic practices that have shaped the lives of the British people. Each chapter corresponds to a month of the year, offering a comprehensive exploration of the customs observed during that time. Delve into the intriguing world of folklore, superstitions, festivals, and ceremonies that have been passed down through generations, shedding light on the unique character of British society.

Genres:
Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes:
Customs and traditions, Social history, Folklore, Festivals, Cultural identity
