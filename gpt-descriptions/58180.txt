Synopsis: In 'My Knitting Book (Second Series)', Lambert, Miss (F.) takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of knitting, offering a delightful collection of patterns, techniques, and inspiration. From intricate lace shawls to cozy winter accessories, this book is a treasure trove for knitters of all skill levels. With detailed instructions and beautiful illustrations, Lambert's second series is a must-have for anyone passionate about the art of knitting. 

Genres: Craft, Non-fiction, How-to

Themes: Creativity, Self-expression, Skill-building
