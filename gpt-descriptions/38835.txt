Synopsis: 

In 'Black-Eyed Susan' by Ethel Calvert Phillips, a young woman named Susan finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Susan must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her life hanging in the balance, Susan must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity
