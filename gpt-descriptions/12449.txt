Synopsis: In 'A Reputed Changeling; Or, Three Seventh Years Two Centuries Ago', Charlotte M. Yonge takes us on a captivating journey to a small English village in the 17th century. The story revolves around the lives of three young women who are mysteriously connected by a dark secret. As their paths intertwine, they must navigate the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, all while facing the haunting presence of the supernatural. With its rich historical backdrop and intriguing characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the reputed changeling.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and Identity, Love and Relationships, Superstition and Belief
