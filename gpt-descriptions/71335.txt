Synopsis: 
'Kaffir folk-lore' takes readers on a captivating journey into the traditional tales of the people living on the eastern border of the Cape Colony. George McCall Theal presents a selection of these enchanting stories, accompanied by copious explanatory notes that shed light on their cultural significance. From mythical creatures to heroic quests, this collection offers a glimpse into a rich and vibrant folklore that has been passed down through generations. Delve into the captivating world of Kaffir folk-lore and discover the magic and wisdom hidden within these timeless tales.

Genres: Folklore, Cultural Studies, Anthropology

Themes: Mythology, Tradition, Cultural Heritage
