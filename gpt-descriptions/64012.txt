Synopsis: In "Puck's Broom: The wonderful adventures of George Henry & his dog Alexander who went to seek their fortunes in the Once upon a time land," George Henry and his loyal dog Alexander embark on a thrilling journey to the enchanting Once upon a time land. As they navigate through magical realms and encounter peculiar creatures, they must rely on their wit and courage to overcome challenges and find their fortunes. This whimsical tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage, Imagination
