Synopsis: In 'Love and Marriage' by Ellen Key, a young woman finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires when it comes to love and marriage. As she navigates the complexities of relationships and the pressures of society, she must confront the question of whether true love can truly coexist with the institution of marriage. With unexpected twists and emotional depth, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Marriage, Individuality, Societal Expectations
