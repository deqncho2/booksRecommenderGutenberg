Synopsis: In 'The Scarlet Banner' by Felix Dahn, a young noblewoman finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during the tumultuous times of ancient Rome. As she navigates the treacherous world of power and deception, she must make choices that will not only determine her own fate but also shape the destiny of an empire. With its captivating blend of romance, suspense, and historical detail, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Identity
