Synopsis: In 'Sermons for the Times', Charles Kingsley delivers a collection of thought-provoking sermons that tackle the pressing issues of his era. From social inequality to the challenges of modernity, Kingsley's powerful words resonate with timeless relevance, urging readers to reflect on their own lives and the world around them. With a blend of passion, intellect, and compassion, these sermons offer a compelling exploration of faith, morality, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Social Commentary

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Social justice, Morality, Modernity, Inequality
