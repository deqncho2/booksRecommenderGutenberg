Synopsis: 

'Motion pictures and filmstrips, January-June 1972' is a captivating catalog that takes readers on a journey through the world of cinema during the first half of 1972. Filled with a diverse range of motion pictures and filmstrips, this volume showcases the creativity and innovation of filmmakers during this period. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming dramas, this catalog offers a glimpse into the captivating stories that captivated audiences during this time. 

Genres: Catalog, Film History, Reference

Themes: Creativity, Innovation, Storytelling
