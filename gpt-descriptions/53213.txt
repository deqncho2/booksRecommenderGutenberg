Synopsis: 
In 'Marjorie Dean, Marvelous Manager' by Josephine Chase, Marjorie Dean finds herself thrust into a new role as a manager, facing challenges and obstacles she never expected. As she navigates the world of business, Marjorie must learn to balance her personal life with her professional ambitions, all while discovering the true meaning of leadership and the power of determination. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see how Marjorie's journey unfolds.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Business

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Personal growth
