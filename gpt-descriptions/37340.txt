Synopsis: 
'The Clan Fraser in Canada: Souvenir of the First Annual Gathering' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and traditions of the Fraser clan in Canada. Through a collection of stories, anecdotes, and photographs, Alexander Fraser invites readers to immerse themselves in the rich heritage of the Fraser family and their contributions to Canadian society. From tales of resilience and adventure to heartwarming accounts of family bonds, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the Frasers and their enduring legacy in Canada.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Genealogy

Themes: Heritage, Family, Canadian history
