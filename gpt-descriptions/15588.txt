Synopsis: In 'The Pilot and His Wife' by Lie, Jonas, a seemingly perfect couple's life takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious secret from the past resurfaces, threatening to unravel their idyllic existence. As the pilot and his wife navigate through a web of deception, betrayal, and hidden desires, they are forced to confront the consequences of their actions and make choices that will forever alter their lives. With gripping suspense and intricate character dynamics, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the complexities of love and loyalty.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Consequences of actions, Love and loyalty
