Synopsis: In the heart of the Adaca Forest, a legendary figure known as the Forest King emerges, a mysterious and enigmatic hunter with unparalleled skills. As rumors spread of his existence, a young adventurer named Ethan embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the Forest King's powers and the secrets hidden within the depths of the forest. Along the way, he encounters mythical creatures, faces treacherous challenges, and discovers a connection between himself and the Forest King that will change his life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Nature vs. Civilization, Destiny
