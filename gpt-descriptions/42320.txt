Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of a Sin' by Charlotte M. Brame, a mysterious and alluring woman enters the lives of two brothers, causing a rift between them and igniting a series of events that will test their loyalty, love, and integrity. As secrets unravel and dark pasts resurface, the brothers find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, passion, and danger. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who can be trusted and what consequences their choices will bring.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Family dynamics, Love and desire
