Synopsis:
'Marm Lisa' by Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin is a captivating novella that follows the life of Lisa, a young girl growing up in a small New England town. Lisa's journey is filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected twists, and a cast of memorable characters. As she navigates the challenges of family, friendship, and self-discovery, Lisa's resilience and determination shine through, making her an inspiring protagonist. With its delightful storytelling and charming setting, 'Marm Lisa' is a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes:
Family, Friendship, Self-discovery
