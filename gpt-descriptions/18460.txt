Synopsis: In 'Flight From Tomorrow' by H. Beam Piper, a brilliant scientist invents a time machine that allows him to travel to the future. However, he soon discovers that his actions in the future have unexpected consequences in the present. As he desperately tries to fix the timeline, he becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure filled with danger, intrigue, and a race against time. Will he be able to set things right before it's too late?

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Consequences of Actions, Paradoxes, Ethical Dilemmas
