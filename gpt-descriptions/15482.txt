Synopsis: In 'The Primrose Ring' by Ruth Sawyer, a young woman named Priscilla finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the past, Priscilla uncovers a long-lost love story that spans generations, leading her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and forbidden romance. With each clue she uncovers, Priscilla becomes more entangled in the enigmatic world of her ancestors, where secrets have the power to change lives forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
