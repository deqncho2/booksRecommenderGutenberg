Synopsis: In 'Song-waves' by Theodore H. Rand, a mysterious melody captivates the residents of a small coastal town, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. As the haunting song weaves its way through their lives, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the power of music becomes a force that cannot be ignored. This enchanting tale explores the profound impact of art on the human spirit and the interconnectedness of individuals in a community.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Mystery

Themes: Music, Identity, Community
