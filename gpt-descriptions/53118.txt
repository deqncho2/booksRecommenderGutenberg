Synopsis: In 'Excursions in Victorian Bibliography', Michael Sadleir takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of Victorian literature. With meticulous research and a passion for the subject, Sadleir uncovers hidden gems, explores the lives of forgotten authors, and delves into the fascinating stories behind the creation of beloved classics. From scandalous scandals to literary rivalries, this book offers a behind-the-scenes look at the Victorian literary scene, shedding light on the intriguing lives and works of the era's most influential writers. Prepare to be enthralled by the secrets and mysteries that lie within the pages of this bibliophile's delight.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Biography, History

Themes: Victorian literature, Forgotten authors, Literary history
