Synopsis: 

In "India Under British Rule from the Foundation of the East India Company," James Talboys Wheeler takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of India during the era of British colonization. From the establishment of the East India Company to the subsequent expansion of British influence, Wheeler delves into the complex dynamics between the colonizers and the colonized, shedding light on the political, economic, and cultural transformations that shaped the subcontinent.

Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Wheeler unveils the intricate web of power struggles, alliances, and betrayals that unfolded during this pivotal period. From the rise of the British Raj to the resistance movements that emerged, the author paints a vivid picture of the clash between two worlds and the lasting impact it had on India's identity.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Power dynamics, Cultural assimilation, Resistance, Identity
