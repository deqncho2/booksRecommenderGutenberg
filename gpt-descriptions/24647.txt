Synopsis: 
'The Standard Operaglass: Detailed Plots of One Hundred and Fifty-one Celebrated Operas' by Charles Annesley, pseudonymously written, is a captivating guide that delves into the intricate plots of 151 renowned operas. From the passionate love affairs to the dramatic twists and turns, this book offers a tantalizing glimpse into the world of opera, providing readers with a comprehensive understanding of the stories behind these beloved musical masterpieces. Whether you are a seasoned opera enthusiast or a curious newcomer, this book promises to transport you into the enchanting realm of opera, where emotions run high and music tells stories like no other art form.

Genres: Music, Performing Arts, Reference

Themes: Love and passion, Tragedy and drama, Human emotions and relationships
