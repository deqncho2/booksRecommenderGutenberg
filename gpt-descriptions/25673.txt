Synopsis: In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 11 (of 15), Roman', Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient Roman Empire. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the political intrigue of the Senate, this volume delves into the fascinating lives of emperors, gladiators, and everyday citizens. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research, Morris brings to life the triumphs and tribulations of one of the greatest civilizations in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Cultural Identity, Human Triumphs and Tragedies
