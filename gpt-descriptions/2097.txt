Synopsis: In "The Sign of the Four," Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson are presented with a perplexing case involving a stolen treasure, a mysterious note, and a beautiful woman seeking their help. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead them on a thrilling adventure through the dark underbelly of Victorian London. With Holmes' brilliant deductions and Watson's unwavering support, they must race against time to solve the puzzle and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Loyalty and Friendship, The Power of Deduction
