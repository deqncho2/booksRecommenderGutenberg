Synopsis: 

'The Medicinal Plants of the Philippines' by Pardo de Tavera is a captivating exploration of the rich botanical heritage of the Philippines. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a journey through the diverse landscapes of the archipelago, unveiling the hidden treasures of its medicinal plants. From the lush rainforests to the remote mountain regions, the author delves into the traditional uses, healing properties, and cultural significance of each plant, providing a fascinating glimpse into the country's indigenous knowledge and practices. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in the healing power of nature and the unique flora of the Philippines.

Genres: Non-fiction, Botany, Ethnobotany

Themes: Traditional medicine, Cultural heritage, Biodiversity
