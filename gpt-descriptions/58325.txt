Synopsis: In "Through One Administration" by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young woman named Rosalie Vanderpoel finds herself thrust into the world of politics when her father becomes the President of the United States. As she navigates the complexities of Washington society, Rosalie must learn to balance her personal desires with the responsibilities that come with her new position. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey through the corridors of power and the challenges of love and loyalty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Identity and Self-discovery, Love and Sacrifice
