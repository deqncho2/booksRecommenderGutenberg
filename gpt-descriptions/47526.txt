Synopsis: In 'Precious Memories', follow the captivating journey of a young Latter-day Saint as they navigate the challenges and triumphs of their faith. From unexpected friendships to life-altering decisions, this sixteenth book in the Faith Promoting Series will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the protagonist's remarkable experiences. As they face adversity and discover the power of their beliefs, readers will be inspired to reflect on their own spiritual journey and find strength in their own precious memories.

Genres: Fiction, Religious, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Faith, Friendship, Personal Growth
