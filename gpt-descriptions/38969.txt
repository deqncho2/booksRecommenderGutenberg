Synopsis: In 'Ralph, the Train Dispatcher; Or, The Mystery of the Pay Car' by Chapman, Allen, a young train dispatcher named Ralph finds himself caught up in a thrilling mystery when the pay car carrying a large sum of money goes missing. As Ralph delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, Ralph must use his wit and resourcefulness to solve the mystery and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Crime and investigation, Loyalty and trust
