Synopsis: In "Where the World is Quiet" by Henry Kuttner, a renowned scientist discovers a hidden world beyond our own, where time stands still and the laws of physics are turned upside down. As he delves deeper into this mysterious realm, he uncovers ancient secrets and encounters strange beings that challenge his understanding of reality. With each step, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the line between dreams and nightmares blurs. This thrilling journey will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of existence and the true nature of the world we inhabit.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of alternate realities, Perception of time and space, Quest for knowledge and truth
