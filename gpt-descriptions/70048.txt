Synopsis: In the small village of Skene, a mysterious event unfolds, leaving the townspeople bewildered and intrigued. When a renowned detective arrives to investigate, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. As the truth slowly unravels, the Great Skene Mystery takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, deception, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Intrigue
