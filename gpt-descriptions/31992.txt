Synopsis: 
In 'Titanic' by Young, Filson, a young woman finds herself aboard the grandest ship ever built, the RMS Titanic. As she embarks on this luxurious journey, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. Amidst the opulence and glamour, a forbidden romance blossoms, threatening to unravel the delicate balance of the ship. But as fate would have it, tragedy strikes, and the Titanic faces a perilous fate that will test the strength and resilience of everyone on board.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Class and social hierarchy, Human resilience in the face of adversity
