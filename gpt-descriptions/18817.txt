Synopsis: In 'Ralestone Luck' by Andre Norton, the Ralestone siblings, Kerlam and Loyse, find themselves caught in a web of mystery and danger when they inherit a fortune from their long-lost uncle. As they delve into their family's past, they uncover a dark secret that threatens their lives and forces them to confront their own courage and resourcefulness. With the help of a few loyal allies, they must navigate treacherous paths and outsmart their enemies to uncover the truth and reclaim their rightful inheritance.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Courage, Identity
