Synopsis: In "The Splash of a Drop," Worthington takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of fluid dynamics, exploring the mesmerizing behavior of liquids and the intricate patterns they create. Through a series of fascinating experiments and observations, the author unveils the hidden secrets behind the splash of a drop, revealing the beauty and complexity that lies within the seemingly simple act of liquid motion. From the graceful dance of water droplets to the explosive impact of a falling liquid, this book offers a captivating exploration of the wonders of fluid dynamics.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Fluid dynamics, Physics, Nature's beauty
