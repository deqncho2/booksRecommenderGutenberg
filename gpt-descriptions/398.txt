Synopsis: In 'The First Book of Adam and Eve' by Platt, Rutherford Hayes, embark on a captivating journey back to the beginning of humanity. Follow Adam and Eve as they navigate the wonders and challenges of the Garden of Eden, encountering mysterious creatures, divine beings, and the ultimate test of their faith. As their story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the consequences of their choices become increasingly profound. This enthralling tale explores the depths of human nature, the pursuit of knowledge, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Biblical fiction, fantasy, adventure

Themes: Creation, temptation, faith, knowledge, morality
