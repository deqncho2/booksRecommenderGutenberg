Synopsis: In 'Hocus Pocus; or The Whole Art of Legerdemain, in Perfection,' Dean Henry presents a captivating guide that unveils the secrets of magic tricks and illusions. This comprehensive manual is designed to empower even the most inexperienced individuals to perform astonishing feats of magic without the need for a teacher. From card tricks to disappearing acts, readers will be enthralled by the step-by-step instructions and the mastery they can achieve in the world of legerdemain. As the secrets of various instruments are revealed, the reader is invited to explore the fascinating realm of magic and become a skilled magician themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Entertainment

Themes: Magic, Illusion, Skill acquisition
