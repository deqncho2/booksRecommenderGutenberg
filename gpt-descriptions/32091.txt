Synopsis: 
In 'Down-Adown-Derry: A Book of Fairy Poems', Walter De la Mare takes readers on a whimsical journey through the enchanting world of fairies. From mischievous sprites to ethereal creatures, each poem unveils a unique and captivating tale. Delight in the magic of moonlit dances, secret meetings, and the hidden wonders of nature. With lyrical verses and vivid imagery, De la Mare invites readers to explore the mystical realm of fairies and experience the joy and wonder that they bring.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic, Nature, Imagination
