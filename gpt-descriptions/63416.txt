Synopsis: 

'Rust, Smut, Mildew, & Mould: An Introduction to the Study of Microscopic Fungi' takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden world of microscopic fungi. Written by renowned mycologist Mordecai Cubitt Cooke, this book delves into the fascinating realm of these often overlooked organisms, exploring their diverse forms, life cycles, and ecological significance. From the destructive rusts and smuts that plague crops to the mysterious molds and mildews that thrive in our homes, Cooke unravels the secrets of these tiny yet powerful organisms, shedding light on their impact on our daily lives. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book is a captivating introduction to the captivating world of microscopic fungi.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Microbiology, Ecology, Environmental Impact
