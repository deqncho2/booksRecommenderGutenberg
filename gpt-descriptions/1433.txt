Synopsis: 
In the remote countryside of Brittany, a group of travelers seek refuge at an isolated inn known as The Red Inn. Little do they know that this seemingly ordinary inn holds dark secrets and hidden dangers. As the night unfolds, the guests find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, suspense, and unexpected events that will test their courage and challenge their beliefs. With each passing hour, the true nature of The Red Inn is revealed, leaving the guests questioning their own sanity and the motives of those around them. Will they be able to escape the clutches of this enigmatic inn, or will they become forever trapped in its sinister grip?

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Isolation, Fear, Madness
