Synopsis: In 'Those Brewster Children' by Florence Morse Kingsley, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Brewster, capturing the attention of the curious Brewster children. As they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the stranger's secrets, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each clue they uncover, the Brewster children must rely on their wit and bravery to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Curiosity, Bravery, Family bonds
