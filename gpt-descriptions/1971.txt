Synopsis: 
In 'Erewhon Revisited Twenty Years Later, Both by the Original Discoverer of the Country and by His Son', Samuel Butler takes us back to the mysterious land of Erewhon, two decades after the original discovery. The son of the original explorer returns to the utopian society, only to find that things have drastically changed. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Erewhon, he uncovers a web of intrigue, deception, and hidden truths that threaten to shatter the very foundations of this once-perfect civilization. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this sequel explores the consequences of progress and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Utopian Fiction, Satire

Themes: Utopia and Dystopia, Progress and its Consequences, Human Nature and Society
