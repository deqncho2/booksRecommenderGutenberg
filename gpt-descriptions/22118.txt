Synopsis: In 'Official Report of the Niger Valley Exploring Party', Delany takes readers on a thrilling expedition through the uncharted Niger Valley. Led by the charismatic and enigmatic Captain James Falconer, the diverse group of explorers faces treacherous landscapes, encounters with indigenous tribes, and the constant threat of danger. As they delve deeper into the heart of Africa, the party uncovers ancient mysteries and uncanny phenomena that challenge their beliefs and push the boundaries of human understanding. Delany's vivid descriptions and captivating narrative keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the Niger Valley.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Supernatural Phenomena
