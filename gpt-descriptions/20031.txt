Synopsis: In 'A Final Reckoning: A Tale of Bush Life in Australia', follow the thrilling adventures of young Frank Norris as he embarks on a perilous journey through the rugged Australian outback. Faced with danger, treachery, and the harsh realities of bush life, Frank must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, he encounters indigenous tribes, gold prospectors, and bushrangers, all while striving to uncover the truth behind a mysterious family secret. Will Frank's determination and resilience be enough to overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Identity, Family Secrets
