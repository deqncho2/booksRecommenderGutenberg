Synopsis: In 'The Lamps of the Angels' by Sabia Richard, a young woman named Amelia discovers a hidden world of magic and mystery when she stumbles upon a forgotten attic filled with ancient lamps. As she begins to unravel the secrets behind these enchanted objects, Amelia finds herself caught in a dangerous battle between light and darkness, where the fate of both the human and supernatural realms hangs in the balance. With the help of a charismatic stranger and a group of unlikely allies, Amelia must navigate through treacherous obstacles and make difficult choices that will determine the ultimate outcome. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
