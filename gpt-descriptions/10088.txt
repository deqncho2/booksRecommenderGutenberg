Synopsis: 
'Thaumaturgia; Or, Elucidations of the Marvellous' by Oxonian is a captivating collection of tales that explores the boundaries between reality and the supernatural. From ancient myths to modern-day wonders, this book takes readers on a journey through extraordinary events and unexplained phenomena. Each story delves into the mysteries of the unknown, leaving readers questioning the limits of human understanding. With its rich storytelling and thought-provoking narratives, 'Thaumaturgia' is sure to captivate and intrigue readers who are drawn to the enigmatic and fantastical.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: The supernatural, the unknown, the power of belief, the limits of human understanding
