Synopsis: 

In "Pioneers of the Old South: A Chronicle of English Colonial Beginnings," Mary Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey through the early days of English colonization in the southern region of America. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research, Johnston brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and conflicts faced by the pioneers who laid the foundation for the Old South. From the first encounters with Native American tribes to the establishment of thriving settlements, this chronicle delves into the lives of the courageous men and women who shaped the destiny of a new land.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural clashes, Survival and resilience
