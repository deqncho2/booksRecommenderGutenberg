Synopsis: In "John Knox" by William M. Taylor, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of Scotland's most influential figures. This gripping biography delves into the trials and triumphs of John Knox, a man whose unwavering faith and determination shaped the course of history. From his humble beginnings to his pivotal role in the Scottish Reformation, follow Knox as he navigates political intrigue, religious persecution, and personal struggles. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Taylor brings to life the indomitable spirit of a man who fearlessly challenged the status quo and fought for religious freedom. 

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Religious Freedom
