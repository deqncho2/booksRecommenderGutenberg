Synopsis: In "Poganuc People: Their Loves and Lives," Harriet Beecher Stowe takes readers on a captivating journey to the small New England town of Poganuc. Through the eyes of the lively and spirited characters, we witness the joys, struggles, and triumphs of everyday life in this close-knit community. From the humorous anecdotes to the heartwarming tales of love and friendship, Stowe paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, immersing readers in the rich tapestry of Poganuc's history and its unforgettable inhabitants.

Genres: Historical fiction, Domestic fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Community, Family, Love, Social change, Gender roles
