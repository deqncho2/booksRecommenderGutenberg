Synopsis: In "Roundabout to Boston," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two young lovers, as they navigate the complexities of society and the pursuit of their dreams. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century Boston, this novel explores the intertwining paths of ambition, love, and self-discovery. As the characters embark on a roundabout journey, filled with unexpected twists and turns, readers are drawn into a world of secrets, desires, and the ever-present question of what truly matters in life.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Ambition, Love, Society, Self-discovery
