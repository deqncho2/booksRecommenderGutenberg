Synopsis: In the small town of Ramrod, a group of young men find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden love. As they navigate the complexities of their relationships and confront the dark underbelly of their community, they must make choices that will forever change their lives. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, 'The Ramrodders: A Novel' explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and lies, Friendship, Love and desire, Morality
