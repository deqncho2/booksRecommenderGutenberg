Synopsis: In 'One Third Off' by Irvin S. Cobb, a young man named Mr. Richard Smith embarks on a journey that takes him from a small town to the bustling city of New York. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, gets entangled in unexpected situations, and finds himself in the midst of hilarious and often absurd adventures. As Mr. Smith navigates the challenges of city life, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, love, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Social commentary, Self-discovery
