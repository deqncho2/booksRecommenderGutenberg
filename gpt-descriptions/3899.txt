Synopsis: 
In 'Memoirs of the Court of St. Cloud', a gentleman in Paris writes secret letters to a nobleman in London, revealing scandalous and intriguing details about the inner workings of the French court. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a world of political intrigue, forbidden love affairs, and the struggle for power. With each letter, the gentleman uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous waters of the court and protect his own life? This gripping tale of deception and betrayal will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel, Political thriller

Themes: Power struggles, Love and desire, Deception and betrayal
