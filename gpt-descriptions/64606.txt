Synopsis: In 'A China cup, and other stories for children', Feliks Volkhovskii takes young readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales. From a magical china cup that grants wishes to a mischievous cat who embarks on a thrilling adventure, each story is filled with wonder, imagination, and valuable life lessons. With delightful characters and unexpected twists, these stories will transport children to a world where anything is possible.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Morality, Magic
