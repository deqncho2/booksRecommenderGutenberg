Synopsis: In 'The Black Cross' by Olive M. Briggs, a mysterious artifact with a dark history is discovered, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of human courage and unravel secrets long buried. As a group of unlikely allies embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth, they must confront their own fears and confront the malevolent forces that seek to possess the power of the Black Cross. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of the world hangs in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Power and corruption, Redemption, Sacrifice
