Synopsis:
'Due West; Or, Round the World in Ten Months' takes readers on an exhilarating journey across the globe as the protagonist embarks on a daring adventure. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the Far East, the story unfolds with captivating encounters, unexpected twists, and heart-stopping moments. As the protagonist navigates through various cultures and landscapes, they discover the true meaning of friendship, the power of resilience, and the beauty of embracing the unknown. This thrilling tale will leave readers yearning for their own grand expedition.

Genres:
Adventure, Travel, Fiction

Themes:
Friendship, Resilience, Exploration
