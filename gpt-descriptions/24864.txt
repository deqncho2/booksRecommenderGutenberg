Synopsis: In 'The Great Potlatch Riots' by Lang, Allen Kim, a small coastal town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives and introduces the locals to the extravagant world of potlatch ceremonies. As the town becomes consumed by the allure of these lavish events, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the community is torn apart. With a cast of eccentric characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the power of tradition, the consequences of greed, and the lengths people will go to protect their way of life.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Greed, Community
