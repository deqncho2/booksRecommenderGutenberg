Synopsis:
'Told in the Coffee House: Turkish Tales' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and mysterious world of Ottoman Turkey. Set in the bustling coffee houses of Istanbul, this collection of tales weaves together stories of love, betrayal, adventure, and the supernatural. From the enchanting tales of beautiful princesses and cunning thieves to the haunting legends of jinn and sorcery, each story unfolds with rich cultural details and unexpected twists. As the characters gather in the coffee house, their narratives intertwine, revealing the power of storytelling and the enduring impact of these tales on the lives of those who hear them.

Genres:
Fiction, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes:
Love and Desire, Betrayal and Deception, Adventure and Exploration, Supernatural and Magic
