Synopsis: 
'We of the Never-Never' by Jeannie Gunn is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a remarkable journey into the heart of the Australian outback. Set in the early 1900s, the story follows Jeannie, a young woman who leaves her comfortable city life to join her husband on a remote cattle station in the Northern Territory. As she adapts to the harsh and unforgiving environment, Jeannie encounters a diverse cast of characters, navigates cultural differences, and faces unexpected challenges. Through her vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, Gunn paints a vivid picture of the rugged beauty and untamed spirit of the Australian wilderness.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, resilience, the power of nature, personal growth
