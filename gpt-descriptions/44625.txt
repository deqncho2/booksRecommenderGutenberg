Synopsis: 
In 'True Ghost Stories' by Carrington, Hereward, prepare to be captivated by a collection of spine-chilling tales that will leave you questioning the existence of the supernatural. From haunted houses to restless spirits, this book delves into the eerie encounters of individuals who have come face-to-face with the unknown. As you journey through these haunting narratives, you will be drawn into a world where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred, and where the unexplainable becomes a terrifying reality.

Genres: 
Horror, Paranormal, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Ghosts, Supernatural, Fear, Mystery
