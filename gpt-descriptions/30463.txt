Synopsis: 
In 'John Whopper: The Newsboy' by Clark, Thomas M., follow the captivating journey of a young newsboy named John Whopper as he navigates the bustling streets of New York City in the late 19th century. From the moment he sets foot in the city, John finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, uncovering secrets that could change his life forever. As he encounters a colorful cast of characters, including a charismatic detective and a cunning street gang, John must rely on his wit and determination to solve a perplexing puzzle that reaches far beyond the newsstands. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Perseverance, Social inequality
