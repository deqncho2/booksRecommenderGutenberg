Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Sophomore Year at High School: The Record of the Girl Chums in Work and Athletics', Grace Harlowe and her friends embark on a thrilling journey through their second year of high school. As they navigate the challenges of academics, extracurricular activities, and personal growth, the girls find themselves facing unexpected obstacles and forming unbreakable bonds. With determination, resilience, and a touch of mischief, Grace and her chums prove that friendship and teamwork can conquer any hurdle that comes their way.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Growth, Teamwork
