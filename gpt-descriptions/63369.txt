Synopsis: In 'The Sense of the Past' by Henry James, a young American named Spencer Brydon finds himself transported back in time to 19th-century England. As he navigates this unfamiliar world, he becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and the haunting presence of his ancestors. With each passing day, Spencer must grapple with the question of whether he can truly belong in this bygone era or if he is destined to be forever caught between two worlds.

Genres: Historical fiction, supernatural fiction, romance

Themes: Time travel, identity, the clash of cultures, the weight of the past
