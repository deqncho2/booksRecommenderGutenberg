Synopsis: In 'A Crooked Path: A Novel' by Alexander, Mrs., a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic history, Emma must navigate through a labyrinth of hidden motives, unexpected alliances, and dangerous encounters. With each step she takes, the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred, and Emma must rely on her wit and courage to unravel the twisted path that lies before her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
