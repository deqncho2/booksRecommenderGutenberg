Synopsis: In the small village of Rodmoor, a mysterious stranger arrives, captivating the townspeople with his enigmatic charm. As the villagers become entangled in his web, secrets are unveiled, passions ignited, and lives forever changed. Set against the backdrop of the rugged English countryside, 'Rodmoor: A Romance' is a captivating tale of love, desire, and the dark forces that lurk beneath the surface of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and deception, The duality of human nature
