Synopsis: 
'A Primary Reader: Old-time Stories, Fairy Tales and Myths Retold by Children' by Smythe, E. Louise is a captivating collection of classic stories, fairy tales, and myths that have been reimagined by children. From the adventures of brave heroes and heroines to the enchanting world of magical creatures, this book takes readers on a journey through the imaginative minds of young storytellers. Each tale is filled with wonder, excitement, and valuable life lessons, making it a delightful read for both children and adults alike.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Fairy Tales, Mythology

Themes: 
Imagination, Adventure, Morality, Life Lessons
