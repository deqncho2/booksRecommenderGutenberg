Synopsis: In "Regina, or the Sins of the Fathers" by Hermann Sudermann, a young woman named Regina finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies as she uncovers the truth about her family's past. Set in a small German village, Regina's journey takes her through a maze of forbidden desires, hidden identities, and the consequences of her father's actions. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Regina must confront the sins of the past and make difficult choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Morality, Redemption
