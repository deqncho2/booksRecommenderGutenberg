Synopsis: 
'The Luck of Thirteen: Wanderings and Flight through Montenegro and Serbia' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of Montenegro and Serbia. Written by Cora and Jan Gordon, this captivating travelogue follows their daring escapades as they navigate through unfamiliar territories, encountering both breathtaking beauty and heart-pounding danger. From encounters with local tribes to unexpected twists of fate, the Gordons' gripping narrative keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies around the next corner. 

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural immersion
