Synopsis: In "An Englishman's View of the Battle between the Alabama and the Kearsarge," Frederick Milnes Edge provides a gripping account of a historic naval engagement that took place in the British Channel. Set on Sunday, June 19th, 1864, this thrilling narrative explores the intense battle between two formidable warships, the Alabama and the Kearsarge. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the strategic maneuvers, the bravery of the sailors, and the unexpected twists that shape the outcome of this epic confrontation. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Naval warfare

Themes: Courage and bravery, Strategy and tactics, Historical significance
