Synopsis: 
In 'The Story of the House: Being Some Suggestions in Brickwork from the Catalogue of O. W. Ketcham', Orman Wesley Ketcham takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of brickwork. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and vivid descriptions, Ketcham explores the art and craftsmanship of building houses with bricks, offering unique insights and suggestions along the way. From the historical significance of different brick patterns to the innovative techniques employed by skilled bricklayers, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for both enthusiasts and professionals in the field. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover the hidden stories behind each brick, uncovering the secrets that make a house truly remarkable.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Home Improvement

Themes: Craftsmanship, History, Design
