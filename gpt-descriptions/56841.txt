Synopsis: In 'The Disappearing Eye' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a priceless artifact vanishes without a trace. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous world of hidden motives and dangerous adversaries to solve the mystery and recover the missing eye before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
