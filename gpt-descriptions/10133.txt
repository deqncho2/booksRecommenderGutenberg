Synopsis: 
"The Vigil of Venus and Other Poems by 'Q'" is a captivating collection of poetry by Arthur Quiller-Couch, known by his pen name "Q." This anthology takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences, exploring themes of love, loss, nature, and the human condition. From heartfelt verses that delve into the depths of the soul to vivid descriptions of the natural world, each poem in this collection is a testament to the power of language and the beauty of poetic expression. With its lyrical and evocative verses, "The Vigil of Venus and Other Poems by 'Q'" is a must-read for poetry enthusiasts and those seeking to be moved by the written word.

Genres: Poetry, Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Human Condition
