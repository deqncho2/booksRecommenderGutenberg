Synopsis: In 'How to Catalogue a Library' by Henry B. Wheatley, embark on a captivating journey through the intricate world of library cataloguing. Follow the protagonist as they navigate the challenges and discoveries that come with organizing a vast collection of books. From hidden treasures to unexpected connections, this book unveils the secrets and wonders that lie within the pages of a library. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Memoir

Themes: Organization, Knowledge, Discovery
