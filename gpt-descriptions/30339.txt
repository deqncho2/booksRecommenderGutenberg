Synopsis: In 'Status Quo' by Reynolds, Mack, a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception when he stumbles upon a mysterious object that grants him extraordinary powers. As he grapples with his newfound abilities, John becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With his life hanging in the balance, John must navigate a treacherous world of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic object. Will he be able to maintain the status quo or will he be consumed by the power he possesses?

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and trust
