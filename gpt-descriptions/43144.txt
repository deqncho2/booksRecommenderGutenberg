Synopsis: In 'Scamp and I: A Story of City By-Ways' by Meade, L. T., follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Scamp as she navigates the bustling streets of the city. With her loyal canine companion by her side, Scamp encounters a world filled with unexpected adventures, mysterious characters, and hidden secrets. As she delves deeper into the city's underbelly, Scamp discovers that there is more to her surroundings than meets the eye. Will she uncover the truth and find her place in this urban landscape? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-Age Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Urban exploration
