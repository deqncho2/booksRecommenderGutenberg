Synopsis: In 'New Nick Carter weekly no. 197: The little glass vial; or A beautiful blackmailer brought to bay', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when a captivating blackmailer emerges, threatening to expose secrets that could ruin lives. As Carter races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his keen intellect and relentless determination, Carter must outwit his adversaries and bring the beautiful blackmailer to justice before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
