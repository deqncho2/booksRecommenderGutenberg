Synopsis: In 'Melbourne House' by Susan Warner, a young woman named Ruth is faced with a life-altering decision that will challenge her beliefs, test her resilience, and lead her on a journey of self-discovery. Set in the picturesque backdrop of Melbourne House, Ruth's story unfolds as she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams. As secrets unravel and unexpected twists arise, Ruth must confront her past and confront the choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Family and relationships
