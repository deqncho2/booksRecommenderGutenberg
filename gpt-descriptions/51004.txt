Synopsis: In 'My Monks of Vagabondia' by Floyd Andress, a young artist named Ethan embarks on a transformative journey when he encounters a group of mysterious monks who live a nomadic existence. Drawn to their unconventional way of life, Ethan becomes entangled in their world, discovering ancient secrets, facing personal challenges, and questioning the boundaries of art and spirituality. As he delves deeper into the monks' teachings, Ethan's perception of reality begins to shift, leading him on a path of self-discovery and enlightenment.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Coming-of-age

Themes: Art and creativity, Spirituality, Self-discovery, Transformation
