Synopsis: In "A Little Florida Lady" by Dorothy C. Paine, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Penelope as she navigates the enchanting landscapes and unique challenges of Florida in the late 1800s. From her encounters with fascinating wildlife to her unexpected friendships with locals, Penelope's adventures will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, discovery, and the power of resilience. As she embraces the beauty of nature and learns valuable life lessons, Penelope's spirit and determination will inspire readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming-of-age, Nature and Wildlife, Friendship, Resilience
