Synopsis: In the small village of Dartmoor, a mysterious group of women known as "The Spinners" hold a secret that has been passed down through generations. When a young woman named Mary arrives in the village, she becomes entangled in their web of secrets and must navigate the blurred lines between reality and folklore. As Mary delves deeper into the world of The Spinners, she discovers that their ancient craft holds the power to change lives, but also comes with a heavy price. With each thread spun, the boundaries between truth and illusion become increasingly blurred, leaving Mary questioning her own sanity and the true nature of The Spinners.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Tradition and heritage, the power of storytelling, the thin line between reality and fantasy
