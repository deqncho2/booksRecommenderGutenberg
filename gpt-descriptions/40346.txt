Synopsis: In 'The Spanish Brothers: A Tale of the Sixteenth Century' by Deborah Alcock, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Spanish Inquisition. The story follows the lives of two brothers, Carlos and Julian, who find themselves on opposite sides of the religious divide. As they navigate the treacherous waters of faith, love, and loyalty, their paths intertwine with historical figures and events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Will their bond withstand the trials and tribulations of this dark period in history?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Religious conflict, Loyalty, Love and Sacrifice
