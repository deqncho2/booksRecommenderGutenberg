Synopsis: In 'Our Pilgrim Forefathers: Thanksgiving Studies' by Nelson, Loveday A., embark on a captivating journey through history as the author delves into the lives of the Pilgrim Forefathers and their impact on the Thanksgiving tradition. From their perilous voyage on the Mayflower to their struggles in the New World, this book uncovers the untold stories and sacrifices of these brave pioneers. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Nelson brings to life the hardships, triumphs, and enduring spirit of the Pilgrims, shedding new light on the origins of Thanksgiving.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, Cultural Studies

Themes: Exploration and Adventure, Cultural Identity, Gratitude and Thanksgiving
