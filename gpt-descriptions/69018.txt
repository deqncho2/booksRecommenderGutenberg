Synopsis: 
In 'Geographical etymology: a dictionary of place-names giving their derivations', Christina Blackie takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and meanings behind various place-names. From the ancient cities of Greece to the remote islands of the Pacific, this comprehensive dictionary unveils the fascinating stories and historical significance hidden within each name. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Blackie invites readers to explore the rich tapestry of human history and culture that is woven into the very fabric of our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Linguistics, Cultural Heritage, Historical Geography
