Synopsis: In the vast and treacherous Arizona desert, a young boy named Davey befriends a mysterious and enigmatic camel driver named Hi Jolly. As Davey learns about the fascinating history of camels in America and the hardships faced by Hi Jolly, he becomes determined to help his new friend overcome the challenges that lie ahead. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that will test their courage, loyalty, and the power of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Cultural Identity
