Synopsis: In 'A Watch-dog of the North Sea: A Naval Story of the Great War', author Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the North Sea during World War I. The story follows a young naval officer who finds himself at the forefront of a dangerous mission to protect his country's shores from enemy submarines. As he battles the elements and faces the constant threat of attack, the protagonist must navigate the complexities of war and make difficult decisions that will test his courage and loyalty. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping naval tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Naval Warfare
