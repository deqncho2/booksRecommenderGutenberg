Synopsis: 
In 'Lolóma, or two years in cannibal-land: A story of old Fiji', Henry Britton takes readers on a thrilling journey to the mysterious and dangerous world of old Fiji. The story follows the adventures of a young Englishman who finds himself stranded on a remote island inhabited by cannibals. As he navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters the island's inhabitants, he must rely on his wit, courage, and the help of unexpected allies to survive. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, 'Lolóma' immerses readers in a captivating tale of survival, cultural clashes, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Survival, Cultural Clash, Resilience
