Synopsis: In "L.P.M.: The End of the Great War," author Barney J. Stewart takes readers on a thrilling journey through the final days of World War I. Amidst the chaos and devastation, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, each with their own secrets and motivations. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn Europe, they must confront their pasts, forge new alliances, and make unimaginable sacrifices. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, the characters find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Redemption, Loyalty, Betrayal
