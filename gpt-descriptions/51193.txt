Synopsis: In 'Pictures Don't Lie' by Katherine MacLean, a renowned photographer stumbles upon a mysterious camera that has the ability to capture images of events that have not yet occurred. As she delves deeper into the camera's secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of danger and deception, where the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With each photograph she takes, she uncovers shocking truths that could change the course of her own life and the lives of those around her. As she races against time to unravel the camera's enigma, she must confront her own fears and confront the consequences of meddling with fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Fate and Free Will, Consequences of Actions
