Synopsis: In 'The Boy Volunteers on the Belgian Front' by Kenneth Ward, a group of courageous young boys find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I as they join the Belgian army to defend their country. With their unwavering determination and resourcefulness, they navigate the treacherous landscapes of war, facing unimaginable challenges and forming unbreakable bonds along the way. As they witness the horrors of battle and experience the true meaning of sacrifice, these brave boys prove that age is no barrier to heroism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
