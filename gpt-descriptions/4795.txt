Synopsis: In "The Circassian Slave, or, the Sultan's favorite: a story of Constantinople and the Caucasus," readers are transported to the exotic world of Constantinople and the Caucasus. The story revolves around a captivating Circassian slave who becomes the favorite of the powerful Sultan. As she navigates the treacherous world of the harem, she must confront her own desires, the politics of the Ottoman Empire, and the complexities of love and power. With its richly detailed setting and compelling characters, this novel offers a mesmerizing tale of intrigue, passion, and the struggle for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Desire, Freedom and Oppression
