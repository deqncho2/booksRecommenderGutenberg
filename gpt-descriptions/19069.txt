Synopsis: 
'The Silent House' by Fergus Hume is a gripping mystery novel that follows the lives of the inhabitants of a seemingly ordinary house. When a shocking crime takes place within its walls, the residents find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists. As the investigation unfolds, the true nature of the house and its occupants is gradually revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Betrayal, Identity
