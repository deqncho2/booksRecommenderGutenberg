Synopsis: In 'A Tenderfoot Bride: Tales from an Old Ranch' by Clarice E. Richards, a young woman embarks on a journey to a remote ranch in the American West, leaving behind her comfortable city life. As she navigates the challenges of adapting to her new surroundings, she encounters a cast of colorful characters, faces unexpected dangers, and discovers the true meaning of resilience and love. Through her captivating storytelling, Richards takes readers on a captivating adventure that explores the triumphs and tribulations of life on the ranch.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Transformation, Survival, Love and Relationships
