Synopsis: In 'The Safety First Club fights fire' by William Theophilus Nichols, a group of young friends find themselves facing a dangerous and unexpected challenge when a fire breaks out in their town. As members of the Safety First Club, they must use their wits, teamwork, and bravery to combat the flames and protect their community. With adrenaline-pumping action and heartwarming moments, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Community
