Synopsis: 
In 'Seven Poems and a Fragment', W.B. Yeats takes readers on a captivating journey through his poetic imagination. Each poem is a window into the depths of human emotions, exploring themes of love, loss, and the complexities of the human experience. From ethereal landscapes to poignant reflections on life's fleeting moments, Yeats weaves a tapestry of words that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Symbolism, Romanticism

Themes: Love, Loss, Transience, Nature, Spirituality
