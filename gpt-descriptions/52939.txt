Synopsis: In the fifth edition of "Life of John Knox," Thomas M'Crie takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of the Reformation in Scotland. This volume, the first of two, delves into the life and experiences of John Knox, a key figure in the Scottish Reformation. From his early struggles and encounters with religious persecution to his pivotal role in shaping the religious landscape of Scotland, M'Crie's vivid illustrations bring to life the challenges and triumphs of this influential reformer. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, "Life of John Knox" offers a compelling exploration of the Reformation in Scotland and the indomitable spirit of one of its most prominent leaders.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious

Themes: Reformation, Religious persecution, Leadership, Faith
