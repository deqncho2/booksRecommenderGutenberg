Synopsis: In "The Boys' Life of Edison" by Wm. H. Meadowcroft, embark on an extraordinary journey through the life of one of the greatest inventors in history, Thomas Edison. Follow young Edison's adventures as he overcomes numerous obstacles, faces failures, and discovers his passion for innovation. From his humble beginnings to his groundbreaking inventions, this captivating biography unveils the remarkable story of a curious and determined boy who would change the world.

Genres: Biography, Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Curiosity, Innovation
