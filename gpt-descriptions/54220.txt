Synopsis: 

'OXFORD: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic city of Oxford, showcasing its iconic landmarks, hidden corners, and vibrant atmosphere. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of Fred Richards, the reader is transported into the heart of this renowned academic hub, where ancient architecture meets modern life. From the grandeur of the university buildings to the quaint charm of the city's streets, this sketch-book captures the essence of Oxford in a truly unique and captivating way.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Architecture, History, Culture, Beauty
