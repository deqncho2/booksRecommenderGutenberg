Synopsis: In 'A Dream of John Ball; and, A King's Lesson' by Morris, William, a man named John Ball finds himself transported back in time to the tumultuous period of the Peasants' Revolt in 14th century England. As he navigates this unfamiliar world, he encounters various historical figures and witnesses the struggles of the oppressed peasants against their feudal lords. Alongside this dream-like journey, the story also delves into the lessons learned by a king who must confront the consequences of his actions. This thought-provoking tale intertwines history, social justice, and the power of dreams to create a captivating narrative.

Genres: Historical fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Social inequality, Revolution, Time travel
