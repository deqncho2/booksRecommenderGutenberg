Synopsis: 
"The Birth of the War-God: A Poem by Kálidása" is an epic tale that follows the extraordinary journey of a divine child destined for greatness. Born to a celestial couple, the child faces numerous challenges and adventures as he grows up, ultimately leading to a climactic battle between good and evil. With captivating characters, mythical creatures, and a richly woven narrative, this poem takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of love, destiny, and the eternal struggle between light and darkness.

Genres: Epic poetry, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Destiny, Good vs. Evil, Love and Sacrifice
