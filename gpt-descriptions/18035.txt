Synopsis: In the charming seaside town of Seacote, Marjorie finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and romance when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the town, Marjorie must navigate a world of hidden identities, unexpected alliances, and dangerous adversaries. With the help of a dashing stranger, she embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and unravel the truth behind the treasure. Will Marjorie uncover the hidden riches, or will she become entangled in a treacherous game of deceit?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Treasure hunting, Identity, Love and betrayal
