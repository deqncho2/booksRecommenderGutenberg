Synopsis: In "The Coming Night: A Sermon Preached in Cromer Church," Edward Hoare delivers a powerful sermon on the occasion of Anna Gurney's death. Through his words, he explores the mysteries of life and death, delving into the profound questions that arise when faced with mortality. With eloquence and deep reflection, Hoare invites the reader to contemplate the significance of the coming night and the eternal hope that lies beyond it.

Genres: Sermon, Religious, Spiritual

Themes: Life and Death, Mortality, Hope, Faith
