Synopsis: In 'Kalitan, Our Little Alaskan Cousin', we follow the adventures of Kalitan, a young Inuit boy living in the vast wilderness of Alaska. From hunting with his father to encountering wild animals and experiencing the beauty of the Northern Lights, Kalitan's life is filled with excitement and wonder. As he navigates the challenges of his unique environment, Kalitan learns important lessons about family, tradition, and the importance of preserving his cultural heritage.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Family, Tradition, Cultural Heritage
