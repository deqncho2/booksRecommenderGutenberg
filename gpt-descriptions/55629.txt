Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Sixth, or Royal First Warwickshire Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the renowned regiment from 1674 to 1838. From the regiment's humble beginnings to its involvement in significant historical events, Cannon's account offers a fascinating insight into the lives of the soldiers who served in this esteemed unit. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the book delves into the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the regiment, painting a vivid picture of their experiences on the battlefield and beyond.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events, Soldier's experiences
