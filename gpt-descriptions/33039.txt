Synopsis: In 'The Orphan' by Clarence Edward Mulford, a young boy named Jim is left alone in the world after a tragic event. As he navigates through life's challenges, he discovers a hidden talent that sets him on a path of adventure and self-discovery. From the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the West, Jim's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Loss and resilience, Identity and self-discovery, The power of determination
