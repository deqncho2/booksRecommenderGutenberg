Synopsis: In 'The Manxman: A Novel - 1895', Sir Hall Caine takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two childhood friends, Pete and Philip, who find themselves entangled in a complex love triangle with a woman named Kate. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Isle of Man, this gripping tale explores the depths of love, loyalty, and the consequences of choices made in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Love and betrayal, Friendship, Social expectations
