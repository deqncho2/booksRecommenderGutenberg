Synopsis: 
In 'Disappeared From Her Home: A Novel' by Catherine Louisa Pirkis, a young woman mysteriously vanishes from her home, leaving behind a perplexing trail of clues. As her family and friends desperately search for answers, they uncover a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to shatter their lives. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever. Will they be able to unravel the enigma and find her before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Disappearance, Family Secrets, Deception
