Synopsis: In 'The Cruise of the Noah's Ark', a group of adventurous children embark on a thrilling journey aboard a homemade ark. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive. With a mix of danger, humor, and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
