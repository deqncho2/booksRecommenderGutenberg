Synopsis: In the midst of a bitter custody battle, young Maisie finds herself caught in the crossfire between her self-absorbed parents. As she navigates their tumultuous lives, Maisie becomes an unwitting witness to their unraveling relationships and discovers unexpected allies in the most unlikely places. With her innocence and resilience, Maisie's journey unveils a poignant tale of love, betrayal, and the enduring power of family bonds.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family dynamics, Childhood innocence, Parental neglect, Resilience, Emotional growth
