Synopsis: In 'My Japanese Wife: A Japanese Idyl' by Clive Holland, a young British man named Clive embarks on a life-changing journey when he falls in love with a captivating Japanese woman named Yuki. As their relationship blossoms, Clive finds himself immersed in the rich culture and traditions of Japan, navigating the complexities of intercultural marriage. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene countryside, Clive's experiences offer a unique perspective on love, identity, and the challenges of blending two worlds together.

Genres: Memoir, Romance, Travel

Themes: Intercultural relationships, Identity, Cultural immersion
