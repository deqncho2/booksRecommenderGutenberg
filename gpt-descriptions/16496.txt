Synopsis: Chronicles (1 of 6): The Historie of England (1 of 8): From the Time That It Was First Inhabited, Vntill the Time That It Was Last Conquered: Wherein the Sundrie Alterations of the State Vnder Forren People Is Declared; And Other Manifold Observations Remembred by Holinshed, Raphael is an epic historical account that takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and tumultuous history of England. From its earliest days of habitation to its final conquest, the book explores the various shifts in power and the impact of foreign influences on the nation. With a wealth of fascinating observations and details, this chronicle offers a comprehensive and engrossing exploration of England's past.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural exchange and influence, National identity
