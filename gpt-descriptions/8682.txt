Synopsis: 
In "The Naturalist on the Thames," join the adventurous Charles John Cornish as he embarks on a captivating journey along the iconic Thames River. From the bustling city of London to the tranquil countryside, Cornish's keen observations and vivid descriptions bring the diverse flora and fauna of the river to life. As he delves deeper into the hidden world beneath the water's surface, he uncovers fascinating secrets and encounters unexpected creatures. With each page, readers will be transported into the enchanting realm of the Thames, where nature's wonders await at every turn.

Genres:
Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes:
Exploration of nature, Environmentalism, Appreciation of wildlife
