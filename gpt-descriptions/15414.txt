Synopsis: In "The Littlest Rebel" by Edward Peple, a spirited young girl named Virginia finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. With her father fighting for the Confederacy, Virginia's loyalty is tested as she navigates the challenges and dangers of war-torn Virginia. Determined to protect her family and preserve her innocence, Virginia's resilience and bravery shine through in the face of adversity. As she encounters unexpected friendships and experiences heart-wrenching moments, Virginia's journey becomes a testament to the power of hope and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, War

Themes: Loyalty, Resilience, Innocence, Friendship
