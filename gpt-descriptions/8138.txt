Synopsis: In 'War-time Silhouettes' by Stephen Hudson, a young artist finds himself caught in the chaos of World War II, where he must navigate the treacherous landscapes of love, loss, and survival. As he witnesses the horrors of war and the resilience of the human spirit, he discovers the transformative power of art and the indomitable strength of the human heart. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Loss, Art and Creativity, Resilience and Survival
