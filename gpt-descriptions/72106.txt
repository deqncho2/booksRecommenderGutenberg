Synopsis:
In 'Married or Single?, Vol. 1', Bithia Mary Croker weaves a captivating tale of love, secrets, and societal expectations. Set in the vibrant city of London, the story follows the lives of two sisters, Violet and Blanche, as they navigate the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of happiness. As their paths diverge, each sister must confront the consequences of their choices, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and Marriage, Sisterhood, Society's Expectations
