Synopsis: In the enchanting coastal village of Lyonesse, a young and mysterious girl named Armorel lives a seemingly idyllic life. But beneath the surface of this picturesque setting lies a tale of hidden secrets, forbidden love, and the power of destiny. As Armorel navigates the complexities of her past and the challenges of the present, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that will test her courage and resilience. With its captivating blend of romance, mystery, and adventure, 'Armorel of Lyonesse: A Romance of To-day' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twists and turns of fate.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Secrets, Love, Destiny
