Synopsis: In 'The Hunt Ball Mystery' by William Magnay, Sir, a seemingly innocent and joyous hunt ball takes a dark turn when a shocking murder occurs. As the guests become suspects and secrets unravel, a determined detective must navigate through a web of lies and deceit to uncover the truth. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Investigation
