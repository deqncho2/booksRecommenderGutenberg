Synopsis: In 'Brainchild' by Henry Slesar, a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking invention that has the power to change the world. However, as the experiment progresses, the line between genius and madness becomes blurred, leading to unexpected and thrilling consequences. This gripping tale explores the limits of human intelligence and the ethical dilemmas that arise when playing with the boundaries of science.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Intelligence, Morality, Ethical Dilemmas
