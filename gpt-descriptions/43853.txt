Synopsis: In 'The Galloping Ghost: A Mystery Story for Boys', a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a mysterious ghostly figure that haunts the local woods. Determined to uncover the truth behind the ghostly apparition, they embark on a thrilling journey filled with clues, hidden secrets, and unexpected twists. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they soon realize that the ghost may be connected to a larger, more dangerous plot that puts their lives at risk. Will they be able to solve the mystery and unmask the Galloping Ghost before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets, Suspense
