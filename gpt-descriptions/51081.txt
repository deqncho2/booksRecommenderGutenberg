Synopsis: In 'The Amateurs' by Alan Cogan, a group of ordinary individuals with a shared passion for solving mysteries come together to investigate a cold case that has haunted their small town for years. As they delve deeper into the secrets and lies surrounding the case, they soon realize that their amateur detective skills may not be enough to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who can truly be trusted.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Deception, Redemption
