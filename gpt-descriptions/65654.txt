Synopsis: 

'Brave British soldiers and the Victoria Cross: a general account of the regiments and men of the British Army, and stories of the brave deeds which won the prize "for valour"' takes readers on a captivating journey through the heroic actions of British soldiers. From the battlefields of World War I to the conflicts of the present day, this book delves into the extraordinary stories of the men who earned the prestigious Victoria Cross for their acts of bravery. With gripping narratives and vivid descriptions, it offers a glimpse into the courage, sacrifice, and unwavering determination of these remarkable individuals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Sacrifice, Military Service
