Synopsis: In Silk Attire: A Novel by Black, William takes readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous world of high society in Victorian England. The story revolves around the enigmatic Lady Euphemia Marjoribanks, a woman with a mysterious past and a talent for manipulating those around her. As she navigates the intricate web of social politics and romantic entanglements, Lady Euphemia's true intentions remain shrouded in secrecy. With scandal, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Power dynamics
