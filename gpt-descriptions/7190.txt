Synopsis: 
In 'The Biography of a Rabbit' by Benson, Roy, follow the extraordinary journey of a seemingly ordinary rabbit named Benjamin. From his humble beginnings in a small burrow to his encounters with various animal friends and foes, Benjamin's life takes unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates through the challenges of survival, love, and self-discovery, readers will be captivated by Benjamin's resilience and determination. This heartwarming tale explores the depths of friendship, the power of perseverance, and the beauty of embracing one's true nature.

Genres: 
Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Friendship, Resilience, Self-Discovery
