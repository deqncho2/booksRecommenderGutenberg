Synopsis: In 'Shafting, Pulleys, Belting and Rope Transmission', Hubert E. Collins takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of mechanical power transmission. From the intricate workings of shafts and pulleys to the ingenious use of belts and ropes, this book unveils the secrets behind the machinery that drives our modern world. With a blend of historical anecdotes, practical examples, and insightful explanations, Collins brings to life the evolution of transmission systems and their impact on industries and everyday life. Whether you're a curious reader or an aspiring engineer, this book will leave you in awe of the ingenuity and complexity behind these seemingly simple mechanisms.

Genres: Engineering, Technology, Non-fiction

Themes: Mechanical power transmission, Industrial revolution, Innovation
