Synopsis: 
In "My Strange Rescue, and Other Stories of Sport and Adventure in Canada," J. Macdonald Oxley takes readers on a thrilling journey through the wild and untamed landscapes of Canada. From heart-stopping rescues in treacherous mountain ranges to exhilarating encounters with wildlife, this collection of stories will leave readers on the edge of their seats. Join the author as he recounts his daring adventures, showcasing the beauty and danger of the Canadian wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Nature, Courage
