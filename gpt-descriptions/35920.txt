Synopsis: In the coastal town of Littlestone, a mysterious woman emerges from the sea, captivating the locals with her ethereal beauty and enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become enamored with her, they soon discover that she is no ordinary woman, but a creature from the depths of the ocean. As her true nature is revealed, the community is faced with a choice that will challenge their beliefs and redefine their understanding of love, acceptance, and the boundaries of the human world.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Otherness, Love and Acceptance
