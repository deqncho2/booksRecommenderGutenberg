Synopsis: In 'Ted Strong's Motor Car: Or, Fast and Furious' by Edward C. Taylor, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Ted Strong, a young and ambitious inventor. When Ted creates a revolutionary motor car, he finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue. As he races against time to protect his invention, Ted must navigate treacherous obstacles and outsmart his adversaries. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Invention and innovation, Perseverance, Courage
