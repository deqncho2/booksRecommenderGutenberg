Synopsis: In "Old Ticonderoga, a Picture of the Past," Hawthorne takes readers on a captivating journey to the historical battleground of Ticonderoga. Through vivid descriptions and intriguing characters, he unveils a tale that intertwines the present with the past, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysteries of this ancient place, secrets are unearthed, and the past comes alive in unexpected ways. This mesmerizing story will leave readers yearning to uncover the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: History and its impact on the present, the power of imagination, the blurred boundaries between reality and fantasy
