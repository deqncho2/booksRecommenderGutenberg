Synopsis: In the bustling streets of London, a mysterious golden butterfly captures the attention of a young artist named Austin. Intrigued by its beauty and symbolism, Austin embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets behind this enigmatic creature. As he delves deeper into the world of art, love, and intrigue, Austin finds himself entangled in a web of deception and danger. With each clue he uncovers, the mystery surrounding the golden butterfly becomes more complex, leading him on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and challenge his perceptions of reality.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Art and symbolism, Love and passion, Deception and intrigue
