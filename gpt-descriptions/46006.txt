Synopsis: In 'The Black Tortoise: Being the Strange Story of Old Frick's Diamond' by Sparre, Christian, a mysterious diamond with a dark past falls into the hands of an unsuspecting protagonist. As he delves deeper into the diamond's history, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the protagonist's life is forever changed, leading him on a thrilling journey that explores the depths of human greed, the power of redemption, and the enduring legacy of a cursed gem.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Greed, Redemption, Fate, Legacy
