Synopsis: In 'Blind Time' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking device that allows people to experience time at an accelerated rate. However, as the technology becomes more widespread, unforeseen consequences arise, leading to a thrilling race against time to prevent a catastrophic event. This gripping science fiction novel explores the ethical implications of manipulating time and the power of human ingenuity in the face of impending disaster.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Ethical dilemmas, Human resilience
