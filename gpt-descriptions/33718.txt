Synopsis: 

"Reminiscences of service with the Twelfth Rhode Island Volunteers, and a memorial of Col. George H. Browne" is a captivating memoir written by Pardon Elisha Tillinghast. The book takes readers on a remarkable journey through the author's experiences as a soldier in the Twelfth Rhode Island Volunteers during the American Civil War. Tillinghast vividly recounts the trials and triumphs of his comrades, offering a unique perspective on the war and the sacrifices made by those who fought.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, War Experiences
