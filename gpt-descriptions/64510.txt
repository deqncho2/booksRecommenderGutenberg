Synopsis: In 'A Daughter of Witches: A Romance', follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Eliza as she discovers her hidden lineage and embarks on a quest to uncover the truth about her family's magical past. As Eliza delves deeper into the world of witches, she encounters a mysterious stranger who seems to hold the key to her destiny. Filled with enchantment, secrets, and a touch of forbidden love, this spellbinding tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Destiny, Magic
