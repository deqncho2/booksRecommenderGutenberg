Synopsis: 

'From Libau to Tsushima: A narrative of the voyage of Admiral Rojdestvensky's fleet to eastern seas, including a detailed account of the Dogger Bank incident' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the high seas as Admiral Rojdestvensky's fleet embarks on a perilous mission. The narrative delves into the dramatic events that unfold during the voyage, including a gripping account of the Dogger Bank incident. As the fleet faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected obstacles, the story unfolds with suspense and intrigue, keeping readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Naval warfare

Themes: Courage and bravery, Leadership and strategy, Consequences of war
