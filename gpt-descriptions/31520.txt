Synopsis: In 'Books Condemned to be Burnt' by James Anson Farrer, a mysterious collection of forbidden books is discovered, leading a young scholar on a perilous journey to uncover their secrets. As he delves deeper into the hidden world of these condemned texts, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden knowledge that could change the course of history. With each page turned, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of intellectual rebellion and the power of ideas.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Censorship, Intellectual Freedom, Knowledge vs. Ignorance
