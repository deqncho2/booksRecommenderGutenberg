Synopsis: In 'The Night Horseman' by Max Brand, a mysterious horseman arrives in a small western town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge, the horseman's true identity and purpose slowly unravel, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love and Betrayal, Revenge
