Synopsis: 
'Across the Spanish Main: A Tale of the Sea in the Days of Queen Bess' follows the thrilling adventures of a young sailor named Ned Hearne. Set in the 16th century, Ned finds himself embarking on a perilous journey across the treacherous Spanish Main, facing dangerous pirates, cunning enemies, and the wrath of the Spanish Armada. As he navigates through the high seas, Ned's loyalty, courage, and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival, Exploration
