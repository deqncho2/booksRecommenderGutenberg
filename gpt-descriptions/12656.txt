Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies at Liège; Or, Through Lines of Steel', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside two young American boys who find themselves caught in the midst of the chaos and danger of World War I. As they navigate through the treacherous battlefields of Liège, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
