Synopsis: In "With Ethan Allen at Ticonderoga," follow the thrilling adventures of a young boy named Ned as he joins the Green Mountain Boys in their daring mission to capture the formidable Fort Ticonderoga from the British during the American Revolution. As Ned navigates the treacherous wilderness, encounters dangerous enemies, and faces the challenges of war, he learns the true meaning of courage, loyalty, and sacrifice. Will Ned and the Green Mountain Boys succeed in their audacious quest? Find out in this action-packed historical tale that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice
