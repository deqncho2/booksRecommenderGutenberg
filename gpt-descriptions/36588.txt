Synopsis: In 'The Red Derelict' by Mitford, Bertram, a mysterious shipwreck is discovered off the coast of a small fishing village, captivating the attention of the locals. As they delve into the secrets of the derelict, they uncover a web of intrigue, danger, and hidden treasures. With each revelation, the stakes rise, and the villagers find themselves caught in a thrilling race against time. Will they be able to unlock the truth behind the red derelict before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden treasures, Intrigue and danger
