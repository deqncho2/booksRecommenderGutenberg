Synopsis: In "In self-defense" by W. C. Tuttle, a small-town sheriff finds himself caught in a web of deception and danger when a mysterious stranger arrives in town. As the sheriff investigates a series of crimes, he uncovers dark secrets that threaten to tear the community apart. With his own life on the line, the sheriff must navigate a treacherous path of betrayal and self-preservation to bring justice to his town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
