Synopsis: In "Moral Principles in Education," John Dewey explores the profound impact of moral principles on the educational system. Through thought-provoking insights and practical examples, Dewey delves into the crucial role of ethics in shaping the minds and characters of students. As he challenges traditional notions of education, Dewey offers a compelling vision for a more holistic and morally grounded approach to teaching and learning.

Genres: Philosophy, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Moral education, Ethical development, Progressive education, Character formation
