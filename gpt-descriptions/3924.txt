Synopsis: In 'Monsieur, Madame, and Bébé — Volume 02', the delightful trio of Monsieur, Madame, and their mischievous baby Bébé return for more hilarious adventures. From unexpected encounters with eccentric neighbors to comical misunderstandings at the local market, this volume is filled with laughter and heartwarming moments. As Monsieur and Madame navigate the challenges of parenthood, their love for each other and their adorable Bébé shines through, creating a charming and endearing tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Family

Themes: Parenthood, Love, Comedy
