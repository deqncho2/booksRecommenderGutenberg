Synopsis: In 'Whiteladies' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lucy is drawn into the mysterious world of a grand estate called Whiteladies. As she becomes entangled in the lives of its inhabitants, Lucy uncovers dark secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the estate. With its haunting atmosphere and complex characters, 'Whiteladies' takes readers on a suspenseful journey through love, betrayal, and the power of the past.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, the supernatural, the influence of the past
