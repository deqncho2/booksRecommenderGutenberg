Synopsis: 

In 'Food Poisoning' by Jordan, Edwin O., a renowned chef finds himself at the center of a mysterious outbreak that threatens to destroy his career and reputation. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and betrayal that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the chef to question who he can trust and how far he is willing to go to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Redemption
