Synopsis:
In the second volume of the captivating Harry Joscelyn series, the eponymous protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue as he navigates the treacherous world of high society in Victorian England. As Harry delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his own past, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of power and deception, where loyalties are tested and alliances are shattered. With his quick wit and unwavering determination, Harry must uncover the truth before it consumes him and those he holds dear. Will he emerge unscathed or succumb to the darkness that lurks beneath the surface?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
