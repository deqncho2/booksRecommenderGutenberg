Synopsis: In 'The Yellow Phantom: A Judy Bolton Mystery' by Margaret Sutton, Judy Bolton finds herself entangled in a thrilling mystery when a mysterious yellow figure begins haunting the town of Farringdon. As Judy delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the Yellow Phantom, she uncovers a web of deception, danger, and hidden identities. With her quick wit and determination, Judy must navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic figure and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Courage, Friendship
