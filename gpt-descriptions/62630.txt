Synopsis: In 'The Ancient Rows of Great Yarmouth: Their names, why so constructed, and what visitors have written about them, also a descriptive sketch of Yarmouth Beach', Edward John Lupson takes readers on a captivating journey through the historic rows of Great Yarmouth. Exploring the origins and significance of each row's name, Lupson delves into the intriguing stories behind their construction. Additionally, he shares the fascinating accounts of visitors who have been captivated by these ancient streets. Alongside this exploration, Lupson provides a vivid and detailed description of Yarmouth Beach, painting a picture of its beauty and allure. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Local history, Architecture, Tourism
