Synopsis: In "The Eternal Boy: Being the Story of the Prodigious Hickey" by Johnson, Owen, we are introduced to a young boy named Hickey who possesses an extraordinary ability. Hickey's unique gift allows him to remain eternally youthful, never aging a day beyond his initial appearance. As Hickey navigates through life, he encounters both the joys and challenges that come with his eternal youth. However, his extraordinary nature also attracts the attention of those who wish to exploit his gift for their own gain. Hickey must learn to navigate a world that both admires and fears his eternal boyhood, all while trying to uncover the true purpose behind his remarkable existence.

Genres: Fantasy, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Purpose, Power, Society's perception of youth
