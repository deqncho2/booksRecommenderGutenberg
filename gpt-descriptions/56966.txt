Synopsis: 
'Motion Pictures, 1894-1912: Identified from the Records of the United States Copyright Office' by Howard Lamarr Walls takes readers on a captivating journey through the early years of cinema. Delving into the extensive records of the United States Copyright Office, Walls uncovers a treasure trove of forgotten films from the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From the birth of motion pictures to the emergence of the silent film era, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lost world of early cinema, revealing the stories behind these forgotten films and the pioneers who brought them to life.

Genres: Film history, Non-fiction

Themes: Early cinema, Film preservation, Cultural heritage
