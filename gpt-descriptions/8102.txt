Synopsis: 
"The Forme of Cury: A Roll of Ancient English Cookery Compiled, about A.D. 1390" takes readers on a fascinating journey into the culinary world of medieval England. This ancient cookbook, compiled in the late 14th century, offers a captivating glimpse into the tastes, techniques, and ingredients of the time. From elaborate feasts fit for kings to humble dishes enjoyed by commoners, the recipes within this book provide a window into the rich culinary heritage of the past. With its unique blend of historical context and mouthwatering recipes, "The Forme of Cury" invites readers to savor the flavors of a bygone era.

Genres: 
Cookbook, Historical, Cultural

Themes: 
Culinary heritage, Medieval England, Food culture
