Synopsis: 
In "Pax mundi: A concise account of the progress of the movement for peace by means of arbitration, neutralization, international law and disarmament," Klas Pontus Arnoldson takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the peace movement. From the early efforts of individuals to the establishment of international organizations, Arnoldson explores the triumphs and challenges faced by those dedicated to achieving peace through diplomacy and disarmament. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, he sheds light on the remarkable progress made in the pursuit of global harmony.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Peace, Diplomacy, International Relations, Disarmament
