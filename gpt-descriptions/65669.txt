Synopsis: In 'Compete or Die!' by Mark Reinsberg, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a deadly game where the stakes are high and the consequences are fatal. As they navigate through a series of twisted challenges, they must confront their darkest fears and make ruthless decisions to survive. With each passing test, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, leaving them questioning who they can trust. As the clock ticks and the pressure mounts, the group must tap into their inner strength and cunning to outwit their captor and escape the game before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Psychological suspense

Themes: Survival, Trust, Morality, Manipulation
