Synopsis: 
In 'Options' by Henry, O., a young woman finds herself at a crossroads in life, torn between the expectations of society and her own desires. As she navigates through a series of choices, each with its own set of consequences, she discovers the power of self-discovery and the courage to forge her own path. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the complexities of identity, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Choices, Society's expectations, Love
