Synopsis: In 'Pushbutton War' by Joseph Paul Martino, a group of young boys stumble upon a mysterious device that has the power to change the world. As they navigate the ethical dilemmas and consequences of their newfound power, they are forced to confront the true nature of humanity and the devastating effects of war. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking moments, this gripping novel explores the fine line between power and responsibility, leaving readers questioning the choices we make and the impact they have on our world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Morality, Consequences of Actions
