Synopsis:
'Letters of Edward FitzGerald to Fanny Kemble (1871-1883)' is a collection of intimate and heartfelt correspondence between the renowned poet Edward FitzGerald and the celebrated actress Fanny Kemble. Spanning over a decade, these letters offer a captivating glimpse into their complex and passionate relationship, filled with longing, admiration, and unspoken desires. As their connection evolves through the written word, the reader is drawn into a world of unrequited love, artistic pursuits, and the struggles of two souls yearning for connection amidst societal constraints. 

Genres:
Biography, Epistolary, Romance

Themes:
Love and longing, Artistic expression, Societal constraints
