Synopsis: In 'Friendship and Folly: A Novel' by Maria Louise Pool, readers are transported to the charming town of Maplewood, where the lives of two best friends, Emma and Lucy, take an unexpected turn. As they navigate the complexities of friendship, love, and societal expectations, their bond is tested in ways they never imagined. With a cast of colorful characters and a backdrop of small-town charm, this captivating novel explores the power of friendship and the consequences of folly.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Love, Self-discovery, Society's expectations
