Synopsis: In 'The Twins: A Domestic Novel' by Tupper, Martin Farquhar, the lives of two identical twin sisters take an unexpected turn when they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and betrayal. As they navigate through the complexities of love, family, and identity, the twins must confront their past and make choices that will shape their future. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Domestic fiction, Mystery, Psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, Family dynamics, Deception, Secrets
