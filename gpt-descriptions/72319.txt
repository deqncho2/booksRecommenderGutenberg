Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the Australian Desert' by Ernest Favenc, a group of explorers embarks on a treacherous journey into the heart of the Australian desert. As they navigate the harsh and unforgiving landscape, they stumble upon a hidden secret that has been buried for centuries. With danger lurking at every turn, they must unravel the mystery and confront the ancient forces that guard it. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they delve into the unknown depths of the Australian desert.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Ancient Mysteries
