Synopsis: In the midst of World War I, Kurt Dorn, a young farmer, finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he battles against a mysterious enemy threatening the wheat fields of the Pacific Northwest. As he navigates through the treacherous landscape of espionage and sabotage, Kurt must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of his land and loved ones.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Survival
