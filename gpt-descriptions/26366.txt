Synopsis: In 'The Electric Bath' by George M. Schweig, a renowned scientist invents a revolutionary device that promises to change the world. However, when a mysterious accident occurs during a demonstration, the scientist's life takes an unexpected turn. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his invention, he uncovers a web of intrigue and danger that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of humanity. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Consequences of technology, Intrigue and suspense
