Synopsis: In "Santa Claus' Daughter: A Musical Christmas Burlesque in Two Acts," the enchanting tale unfolds as Santa Claus' daughter, a spirited and independent young woman, embarks on a journey to discover her true identity and purpose. Set against the backdrop of a whimsical North Pole, she encounters a colorful cast of characters, including mischievous elves, a charismatic reindeer, and a mysterious stranger who holds the key to her past. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and heartwarming moments, she learns the power of love, self-discovery, and the magic of Christmas.

Genres: Musical, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Family, Love, Self-discovery, Christmas Spirit
