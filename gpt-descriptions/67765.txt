Synopsis: In 'Fighting King George' by McIntyre, John T., a young and ambitious boxer named George finds himself caught in a web of corruption and deceit when he is offered the chance of a lifetime to fight in a high-stakes championship match. As George rises through the ranks, he must navigate the treacherous world of professional boxing, facing not only formidable opponents in the ring but also the dark forces that threaten to destroy his dreams. With his determination and resilience, George must fight not only for victory but also for his own survival.

Genres: Sports, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Corruption, Perseverance
