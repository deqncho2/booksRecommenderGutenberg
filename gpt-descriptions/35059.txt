Synopsis: In 'Familiar Faces' by Harry Graham, a renowned artist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a series of haunting portraits that seem to come to life. As a young art enthusiast delves into the enigmatic world of these paintings, she uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities. With each brushstroke, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the artist's vanishing act.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Artistic obsession, Identity, Perception
