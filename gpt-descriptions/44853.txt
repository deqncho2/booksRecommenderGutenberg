Synopsis: 
'Letters from an Old Railway Official to His Son, a Division Superintendent' is a collection of heartfelt letters written by an experienced railway official to his son, who is also working in the same industry. Through these letters, the father imparts wisdom, shares anecdotes, and offers guidance on navigating the challenges and complexities of their shared profession. As the son delves into his father's letters, he discovers not only valuable insights into the railway world but also a deeper understanding of his father's life and the sacrifices he made. This poignant correspondence explores the bond between father and son, the passing down of knowledge, and the timeless lessons that can be learned from the experiences of those who came before.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Family, Legacy, Professional growth, Wisdom
