Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Volume 156, Jan. 8, 1919', readers are transported to the vibrant world of early 20th-century London, where wit, satire, and humor reign supreme. This volume presents a collection of clever and entertaining articles, cartoons, and illustrations that offer a humorous commentary on various aspects of society, politics, and culture. From amusing anecdotes to sharp social critiques, this edition of Punch promises to captivate readers with its delightful blend of wit and satire.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Society, Politics, Culture, Humor
