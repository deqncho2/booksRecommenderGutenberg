Synopsis: In 'The Mushroom, Edible and Otherwise: Its Habitat and its Time of Growth', author Hard, Miron Elisha takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of mushrooms. From the enchanting forests to the hidden corners of our own backyards, this book unveils the secrets of these fascinating fungi. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the author explores the diverse habitats and the specific conditions that allow mushrooms to thrive. Delving into the time of growth, readers will discover the intricate relationship between mushrooms and the changing seasons. As the pages turn, a sense of wonder and curiosity will be ignited, leaving readers eager to explore the world of mushrooms for themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Exploration, Biodiversity, Environmental Awareness
