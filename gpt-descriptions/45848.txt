Synopsis: 

'Up the Orinoco and down the Magdalena' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the heart of South America, as the author, J.A. Zahm, embarks on a daring expedition to explore the untamed landscapes of the Orinoco and Magdalena rivers. From encountering indigenous tribes and navigating treacherous rapids to uncovering hidden archaeological wonders, Zahm's captivating narrative transports readers to a world of adventure and discovery. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of these majestic rivers, the author's encounters with nature, culture, and the unknown will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover what lies around the next bend.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Indigenous cultures
