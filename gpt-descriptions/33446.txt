Synopsis: In "Lace Curtain Cleaning: A Successful Home Business," Leigh, Sarah Ann takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of a seemingly ordinary home business with a twist. As the protagonist delves into the intricacies of cleaning lace curtains, she uncovers hidden secrets, unexpected challenges, and a web of connections that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each curtain she cleans, the protagonist unravels a mystery that will change her life forever. This gripping tale explores the power of determination, the allure of the unknown, and the transformative nature of unexpected opportunities.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Transformation and personal growth, Pursuit of success and fulfillment
