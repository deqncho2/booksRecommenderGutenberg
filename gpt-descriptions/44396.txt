Synopsis: In "Child Labor in City Streets" by Clopper, Edward Nicholas, a young journalist uncovers a hidden world of exploitation and hardship as he delves into the lives of child laborers in the bustling city streets. Through his encounters with these resilient children, he uncovers shocking secrets and forms unexpected alliances, ultimately leading him on a quest for justice that will challenge his own beliefs and ignite a powerful movement.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Social Justice, Mystery

Themes: Child labor, Social inequality, Courage, Redemption
