Synopsis: 

'Pittsburgh in 1816: Compiled by the Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh on the One Hundredth Anniversary of the Granting of the City Charter' takes readers on a captivating journey back in time to the vibrant city of Pittsburgh in the year 1816. Through a collection of historical documents, photographs, and personal accounts, this book paints a vivid picture of the city's early days, exploring its growth, challenges, and triumphs. From the bustling streets and bustling industries to the diverse communities and influential figures that shaped its destiny, this compilation offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich history of Pittsburgh.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History

Themes: City Development, Industrialization, Community Building, Historical Significance
