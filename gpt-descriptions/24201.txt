Synopsis:
Dr. John Thorndyke, a brilliant forensic scientist, finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a young woman seeks his help to uncover the truth behind her father's sudden death. As Thorndyke delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden identities, and a mysterious artifact known as the Eye of Osiris. With each clue he uncovers, the danger intensifies, and Thorndyke must race against time to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes:
Forensic science, Identity, Deception
