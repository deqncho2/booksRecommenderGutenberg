Synopsis: 
In the small town of Milltown, Janice Day is a young girl with big dreams and a determination to make them come true. As she navigates the challenges of growing up, Janice discovers the power of friendship, the importance of family, and the resilience of the human spirit. From her mischievous adventures to her heartfelt moments of self-discovery, Janice's journey will captivate readers and leave them rooting for her every step of the way.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Family

Themes: 
Friendship, Family, Self-discovery
