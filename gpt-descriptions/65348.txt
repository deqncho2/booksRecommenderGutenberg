Synopsis: In "Barnstormer" by Tom W. Harris, a young pilot named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the small town of Willow Creek to the dazzling world of barnstorming in the 1920s. With his trusty biplane, Jack becomes part of a daring group of aviators who captivate audiences with their death-defying stunts and aerial acrobatics. As he navigates the skies and encounters unexpected challenges, Jack discovers the true meaning of courage, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Pursuit of Dreams
