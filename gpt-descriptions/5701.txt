Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Fatty Coon' by Arthur Scott Bailey, readers are introduced to a mischievous and cunning raccoon named Fatty. Fatty's insatiable appetite leads him on a series of adventures as he navigates the dangers of the forest and outsmarts his fellow woodland creatures. From daring escapes to clever tricks, Fatty's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what he will do next.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Resourcefulness
