Synopsis: In 'Asteroid Justice' by Thiessen, V. E., a group of unlikely heroes find themselves on a thrilling mission to save humanity from an impending asteroid collision. As they navigate through treacherous space, they must confront their own fears, forge unexpected alliances, and make heart-wrenching sacrifices. With time running out, the fate of the world rests in their hands, and they must summon the courage to face the ultimate challenge.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Survival
