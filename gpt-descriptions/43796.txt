Synopsis: 
In 'Tom Fairfield's Schooldays; or, The Chums of Elmwood Hall', Tom Fairfield embarks on a thrilling adventure as he enters Elmwood Hall, a prestigious boarding school. Alongside his new friends, Tom navigates the challenges of school life, from academic competitions to sports rivalries. However, when a mysterious series of events unfolds, Tom and his chums find themselves caught up in a dangerous secret that could jeopardize their friendships and the entire school. With courage and determination, they must unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
