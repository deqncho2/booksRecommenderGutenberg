Synopsis: In "The Governess; Or, The Little Female Academy," Sarah Fielding takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of young girls at a boarding school. As the girls navigate the challenges of friendship, love, and societal expectations, they find themselves caught in a web of secrets and hidden desires. With a mysterious governess at the center of it all, this tale of innocence and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the academy's walls.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Education, Female empowerment, Deception, Identity
