Synopsis: In 'Freudian Slip' by Abel Franklin, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and deception when a patient's Freudian slip leads to a shocking revelation. As he delves deeper into the patient's subconscious, he uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel his own sanity. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of truth and the power of the human mind.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Subconscious desires, Deception, Sanity vs. insanity
