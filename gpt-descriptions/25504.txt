Synopsis: In 'Khartoum Campaign, 1898; or the Re-Conquest of the Soudan', Burleigh Bennet takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Africa during a pivotal moment in history. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this gripping account follows the British-led expedition to reclaim the Soudan, culminating in the dramatic siege of Khartoum. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Bennet brings to life the heroic figures, the treacherous landscapes, and the intense battles that shaped the destiny of nations.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Heroism, Exploration, Warfare
