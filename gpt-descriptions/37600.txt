Synopsis: In "The Fight for Constantinople: A Story of the Gallipoli Peninsula," Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intense battles and strategic maneuvers that unfolded during the Gallipoli Campaign of World War I. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Gallipoli Peninsula, this gripping tale follows a group of young soldiers as they navigate the treacherous terrain, face formidable enemies, and grapple with the harsh realities of war. As the fight for Constantinople reaches its climax, the characters must summon their courage and resilience to overcome seemingly insurmountable odds. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Westerman brings to life the bravery and sacrifice of those who fought in this pivotal moment of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The horrors of war, Strategic warfare
