Synopsis: 

In 'Horton Genealogy: or Chronicles of the Descendants of Barnabas Horton, of Southold, L. I., 1640,' George Firman Horton delves into the fascinating history of the Horton family, tracing their lineage back to the arrival of Barnabas Horton in Southold, Long Island, in 1640. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Horton uncovers the triumphs, trials, and secrets that have shaped this remarkable family over the centuries. From their early struggles in a new land to their contributions to the growth of America, this genealogical journey is filled with surprising discoveries and heartwarming tales of resilience and perseverance. 

Genres: Genealogy, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Family history, Immigration, American heritage
