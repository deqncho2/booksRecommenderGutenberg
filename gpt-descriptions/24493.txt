Synopsis: In "The Boy Who Knew What the Birds Said" by Padraic Colum, a young boy named Fergus possesses a remarkable ability to understand the language of birds. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of this extraordinary gift, Fergus finds himself entangled in a world of enchantment, danger, and unexpected friendships. With the fate of both humans and birds hanging in the balance, Fergus must navigate a treacherous path to protect the harmony between the two realms.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Nature, Communication, Friendship, Identity
