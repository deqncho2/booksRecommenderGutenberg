Synopsis: 
In 'Novice' by James H. Schmitz, a young woman named Telzey Amberdon discovers she possesses a unique psychic ability that allows her to read minds. As she navigates a dangerous world filled with political intrigue and hidden agendas, Telzey must learn to control her powers and uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy. With her quick wit and determination, Telzey becomes a force to be reckoned with, challenging the powerful forces that seek to control her and those she cares about.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Psychic abilities, Power and control, Conspiracy
