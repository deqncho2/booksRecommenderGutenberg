Synopsis: In 'Out of Time's Abyss' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a group of adventurers find themselves stranded in a mysterious land filled with prehistoric creatures and savage tribes. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they uncover ancient secrets and face unimaginable dangers. With each passing moment, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Time Travel
