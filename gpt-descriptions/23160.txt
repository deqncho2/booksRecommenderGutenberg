Synopsis: In 'Solomon's Orbit' by Carroll, William, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Solomon discovers a mysterious anomaly in space that defies all known laws of physics. As he embarks on a perilous journey to unravel the secrets of this enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Dr. Solomon must navigate through treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals to uncover the truth behind this cosmic anomaly.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Consequences of scientific discovery, Betrayal and trust
