Synopsis: In 'A Horse's Tale' by Mark Twain, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a horse named Soldier Boy. From his early days as a wild mustang to his adventures as a cavalry horse during the Indian Wars, Soldier Boy's unique perspective offers a fresh and insightful look into the human-animal bond. As he encounters various characters and navigates the challenges of war, Soldier Boy's loyalty, courage, and determination shine through, making this a heartwarming and thought-provoking tale.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Human-animal bond
