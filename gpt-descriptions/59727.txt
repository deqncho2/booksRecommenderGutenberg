Synopsis: 

'An Old City Company: A Sketch of the History and Conditions of the Skinners' Company of London' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and intriguing conditions of the Skinners' Company, one of London's oldest and most influential guilds. From its humble beginnings to its rise as a powerful force in the city, this book delves into the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that have shaped the company over the centuries. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Sebastian, Lewis Boyd paints a vivid picture of the Skinners' Company, revealing the hidden world behind its grand facade.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, Cultural History

Themes: Power and Influence, Tradition and Legacy, Social Hierarchies
