Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The History of Chivalry; Or, Knighthood and Its Times,' Charles Mills delves deeper into the captivating world of knights and their noble code of conduct. From the legendary tales of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table to the epic battles fought by valiant warriors, this volume explores the rich tapestry of chivalry throughout history. Mills uncovers the intricate rituals, the romantic ideals, and the fierce loyalty that defined the knights' way of life. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, he brings to life the captivating stories of honor, bravery, and courtly love that have enthralled readers for centuries.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Romance, Warfare
