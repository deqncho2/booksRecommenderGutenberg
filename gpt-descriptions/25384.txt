Synopsis: In 'Ben's Nugget; Or, A Boy's Search For Fortune' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are taken on an exciting journey alongside young Ben as he embarks on a quest to find his fortune. Leaving behind his humble beginnings, Ben sets out to strike it rich in the gold mines of California. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, faces numerous challenges, and learns valuable life lessons about perseverance, determination, and the true meaning of wealth. Will Ben's search for fortune lead him to more than just gold?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Pursuit of wealth, Self-discovery, Resilience
