Synopsis: In 'The Book of Stories for the Story-teller' by Fanny E. Coe, readers are transported to a world filled with enchanting tales and captivating characters. From mythical creatures to ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances, this collection of stories is sure to ignite the imagination and leave readers yearning for more. Each story is carefully crafted to evoke a range of emotions, from laughter to tears, and to challenge the reader's perception of reality. With its rich storytelling and unexpected twists, 'The Book of Stories for the Story-teller' is a literary treasure that will keep readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Human nature
