Synopsis: 
In 'Jed, the Poorhouse Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Jed, a young boy who finds himself in the direst of circumstances. After being abandoned by his family and left to fend for himself, Jed is forced to navigate the harsh realities of life in the poorhouse. Determined to escape his bleak existence, Jed embarks on a courageous adventure that will test his resilience, strength, and ultimately, his ability to rise above his circumstances. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, rooting for Jed every step of the way.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Perseverance, Social class, Self-determination
