Synopsis: In 'Young Knights of the Empire: Their Code, and Further Scout Yarns', Baden-Powell of Gilwell takes readers on an exciting journey through the adventures of young knights who embody the principles of scouting. From daring rescues to solving mysteries, these young heroes face challenges that test their courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty. As they navigate through thrilling escapades, they learn valuable life lessons and the importance of honor, teamwork, and selflessness. This collection of scout yarns will captivate readers with its thrilling tales and inspire them to embrace the scout code in their own lives.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Honor, Teamwork, Selflessness, Scouting
