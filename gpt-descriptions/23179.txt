Synopsis: In "Frictional Electricity: From The Saturday Evening Post," Charles Heber Clark takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of electricity, exploring its mysterious and awe-inspiring properties. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and scientific explanations, Clark delves into the fascinating realm of frictional electricity, revealing its potential for both wonder and danger. From electrifying experiments to shocking discoveries, this work offers a thrilling exploration of the power that lies within the unseen forces of nature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Electricity, Scientific discovery, Natural forces
