Synopsis: In 'Under Canvas; or, The Hunt for the Cartaret Ghost' by Douglas, Alan, Captain, a group of friends embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind a mysterious ghost haunting the Cartaret estate. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the past, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, danger, and supernatural occurrences. With each clue they uncover, the stakes rise, and they must race against time to solve the haunting mystery before it consumes them all.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Friendship, Secrets and Betrayal, the Power of the Unknown
