Synopsis: In 'A Face Illumined' by Edward Payson Roe, a young woman named Ruth Thorne finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. Set in the picturesque countryside of New England, Ruth's journey takes her through a series of unexpected twists and turns, as she navigates the complexities of family, societal expectations, and the pursuit of true happiness. As she grapples with her own desires and the consequences of her choices, Ruth must ultimately confront the power of forgiveness and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Duty vs Desire, Family dynamics, Forgiveness
