Synopsis: In 'The Song of the Wolf' by Mayer, Frank, a young musician named Ethan embarks on a journey to discover the truth behind a haunting melody that has been passed down through generations. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. With each step, Ethan uncovers the hidden connections between the song, his own family history, and the enigmatic figure of the wolf. As he races against time, he must confront his own fears and make a choice that will change his life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Music and its power, Love and sacrifice
