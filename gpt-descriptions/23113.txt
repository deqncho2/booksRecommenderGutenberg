Synopsis: 
In 'To My Younger Brethren: Chapters on Pastoral Life and Work', H. C. G. Moule offers a heartfelt and insightful guide to the challenges and joys of pastoral ministry. Drawing from his own experiences as a bishop, Moule provides practical advice and spiritual encouragement for those called to shepherd God's flock. Through personal anecdotes and biblical wisdom, he explores the importance of prayer, the power of preaching, the art of shepherding, and the beauty of serving God's people. With warmth and wisdom, Moule's words resonate with pastors and ministers, offering guidance and inspiration for their vital role in the church.

Genres: Christian literature, Pastoral theology, Spiritual guidance

Themes: Pastoral ministry, Prayer, Preaching, Shepherding, Service to God's people
