Synopsis: In the bustling fishing town of Cordova, Alaska, a group of determined fishermen set out on a perilous journey to strike it rich in the treacherous waters of the North Pacific. As they face the challenges of the unpredictable sea, fierce competition, and personal conflicts, they must navigate the fine line between ambition and survival. With their livelihoods and dreams on the line, the silver horde will stop at nothing to achieve their goals.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Survival, Competition, Camaraderie
