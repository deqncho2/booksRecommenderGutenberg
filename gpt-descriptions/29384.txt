Synopsis: In 'Disowned' by Victor A. Endersby, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and betrayal when she discovers a hidden family history that threatens to unravel her entire existence. As she delves deeper into the past, Emily must confront the dark truths that have been kept from her, leading her on a dangerous journey of self-discovery and redemption. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a complex web of lies and deceit, forcing her to question everything she thought she knew about her identity and the people she loves. 'Disowned' is a gripping tale of mystery and suspense that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
