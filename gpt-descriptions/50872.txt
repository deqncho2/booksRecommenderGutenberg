Synopsis: In 'Not Fit for Children' by Smith, Evelyn E., a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious events that seem to be connected to a long-forgotten secret. As the townspeople try to uncover the truth, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Trust and betrayal
