Synopsis: In 'Problematic Characters: A Novel' by Friedrich Spielhagen, a diverse group of individuals find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and moral dilemmas. From a cunning seductress to a tormented artist, each character grapples with their own inner demons, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As their lives intertwine, the boundaries between right and wrong blur, leaving the reader questioning the nature of morality and the complexities of human behavior.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Human nature, Consequences of actions
