Synopsis: In 'Traffic in Souls: A Novel of Crime and Its Cure', Eustace Hale Ball takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of society, where crime and corruption thrive. Set in the bustling city streets of early 20th century New York, the story follows the lives of several individuals whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. As the characters navigate the treacherous world of human trafficking, they are forced to confront their own moral compasses and make choices that will shape their destinies. With its intricate plot and vivid portrayal of the era, this novel delves into the depths of human nature, exploring the themes of redemption, justice, and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Crime fiction, Historical fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Crime and punishment, Morality, Social justice
