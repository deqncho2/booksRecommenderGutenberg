Synopsis: In "Tea Tray in the Sky" by Evelyn E. Smith, a young woman named Jane finds herself in a peculiar situation when she discovers a mysterious tea tray that has the power to transport her to different worlds. With each journey, Jane encounters strange and fascinating characters, embarks on thrilling adventures, and learns valuable lessons about herself and the world around her. As she navigates through these extraordinary realms, Jane must confront her fears, make difficult choices, and ultimately discover the true meaning of home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Courage, Coming of Age
