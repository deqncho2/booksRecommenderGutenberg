Synopsis: In 'Human Origins' by S. Laing, embark on a captivating journey through time and explore the fascinating story of how humanity came to be. From the earliest hominids to the emergence of Homo sapiens, this book delves into the mysteries of our evolutionary past, unveiling surprising discoveries and challenging long-held beliefs. Through a blend of scientific research, archaeological evidence, and thought-provoking theories, Laing presents a compelling narrative that will leave readers pondering the profound questions surrounding our existence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Anthropology

Themes: Evolution, Human history, Archaeology, Origins of civilization
