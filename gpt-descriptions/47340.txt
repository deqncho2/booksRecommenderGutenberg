Synopsis: 

In 'Earthquakes', authors Pakiser and Shedlock take readers on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of seismic activity. From the devastating earthquakes that have shaped the Earth's landscape to the cutting-edge technology used to study and predict these natural disasters, this book offers a captivating exploration of the science behind earthquakes. Through vivid descriptions and real-life examples, the authors delve into the causes, effects, and aftermath of earthquakes, shedding light on the immense power and unpredictability of these geological phenomena. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural Disasters

Themes: Earth's geology, Natural disasters, Scientific exploration
