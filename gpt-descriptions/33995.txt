Synopsis:
'Where the Path Breaks' is a gripping and mysterious novel that follows the lives of two young scientists, Ruth and Lloyd, as they embark on a groundbreaking experiment in the remote wilderness of Canada. As they delve deeper into their research, they uncover a hidden world of secrets and danger that threatens to consume them both. With their lives hanging in the balance, Ruth and Lloyd must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will test their courage and resilience. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic path they have chosen to follow.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Exploration, Scientific discovery, Survival
