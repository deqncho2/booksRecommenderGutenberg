Synopsis: In 'Little Wolf: A Tale of the Western Frontier' by Mary Ann Mann Cornelius, readers are transported to the untamed wilderness of the Western Frontier. The story follows Little Wolf, a young Native American boy, as he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters both friends and foes, Little Wolf must navigate the complexities of the frontier while staying true to his heritage and finding his place in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, Survival, Friendship
