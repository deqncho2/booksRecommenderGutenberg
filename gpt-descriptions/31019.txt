Synopsis: 
'Four Ghost Stories' by Molesworth, Mrs. is a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine. From a haunted house to a mysterious specter, these stories will keep you on the edge of your seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within. Each story presents a unique and eerie encounter with the supernatural, leaving the reader questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead. Prepare to be captivated by these haunting tales that will leave you with a lingering sense of unease.

Genres: 
Gothic fiction, horror, supernatural

Themes: 
The supernatural, the afterlife, fear and suspense
