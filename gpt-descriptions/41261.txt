Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter and the Money Box' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young boy named Dan Carter stumbles upon a mysterious money box hidden in his attic. Little does he know that this discovery will lead him on an exciting adventure filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected twists. As Dan delves deeper into the origins of the money box, he uncovers a hidden world of intrigue and must use his wit and courage to navigate through it. With each clue he uncovers, Dan becomes more entangled in a web of mystery that will test his determination and loyalty. Will he be able to solve the puzzle and unlock the secrets of the money box before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Courage, Loyalty
