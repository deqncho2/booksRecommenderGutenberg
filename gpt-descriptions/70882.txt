Synopsis: In 'The Clue of the New Pin' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious murder takes place in a quiet English village, leaving the local police baffled. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances emerges, leading the reader on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the crime. With each clue discovered, the tension rises, and the stakes become higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
