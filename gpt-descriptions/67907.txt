Synopsis: In "Robinson Crusoe, Told to the Children," John Lang masterfully retells the timeless adventure of Robinson Crusoe, a young man who finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. As Crusoe navigates the challenges of survival, he encounters unexpected companions, battles against nature's forces, and grapples with his own inner demons. Lang's captivating narrative brings to life the thrilling escapades and profound self-discovery that await Crusoe on his solitary island. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Exploration, Self-Discovery
