Synopsis: In 'The Island of Yellow Sands: An Adventure and Mystery Story for Boys', a group of young boys embarks on a thrilling journey to an uncharted island. As they explore the mysterious island, they encounter hidden caves, treacherous cliffs, and a secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and friendship to unravel the island's secrets and make it out alive. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage
