Synopsis: In 'Memory' by Theodore Sturgeon, a man wakes up in a hospital bed with no memory of who he is or how he got there. As he embarks on a journey to uncover his past, he discovers a shocking truth that challenges his perception of reality. With each memory he recovers, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, leading him to question the nature of identity and the power of memory itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Perception, Reality
