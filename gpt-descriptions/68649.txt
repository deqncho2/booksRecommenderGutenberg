Synopsis: 
'The spoil'd child: A farce, in two acts' is a delightful and humorous play that takes place at the Theatre Royal, Drury Lane. The story revolves around a spoiled child and the chaos that ensues when his indulgent behavior is challenged. With a cast of colorful characters and witty dialogue, this farce promises to entertain and keep the audience on their toes until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Spoiled behavior, Consequences of indulgence
