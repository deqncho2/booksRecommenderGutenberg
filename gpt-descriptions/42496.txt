Synopsis: In 'Miracle Gold: A Novel (Vol. 2 of 3)' by Richard Dowling, the captivating story continues as the protagonist, Sarah, finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the enigmatic Miracle Gold, Sarah must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With her life hanging in the balance, she must uncover the truth before it's too late. Will Sarah's determination and resilience be enough to overcome the forces working against her?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Perseverance and survival
