Synopsis: In the heart of Tokyo, a mysterious fire breaks out, engulfing the city in chaos and uncertainty. As the flames spread, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate through the burning streets, uncovering dark secrets and facing unimaginable challenges. With time running out, they must find a way to extinguish the inferno before it consumes everything they hold dear. 

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Sacrifice
