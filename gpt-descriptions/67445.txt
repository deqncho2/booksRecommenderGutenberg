Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Haven, a group of young boys embark on a thrilling adventure as they join their fathers in the dangerous world of ice whaling. Facing treacherous conditions and formidable creatures, these brave boys must prove their worth and uncover the secrets that lie beneath the icy depths. With friendship, courage, and the spirit of exploration, they navigate the challenges of the frozen seas, discovering the true meaning of bravery along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Tradition
