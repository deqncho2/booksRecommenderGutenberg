Synopsis: 
'The Three Brothers' by Eden Phillpotts is a gripping tale that follows the lives of three brothers, each with their own unique personalities and ambitions. As they navigate through love, loss, and the complexities of family dynamics, their paths diverge, leading them on separate journeys filled with unexpected twists and turns. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Dartmoor landscape, this novel explores the bonds of brotherhood and the choices that shape our destinies.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Brotherhood, Individuality, Fate, Love and Loss
