Synopsis: In 'The Kacháris' by Sidney Endle, a young British officer finds himself immersed in the fascinating world of the Kachári tribe in Assam, India. As he navigates the complexities of their culture and customs, he becomes entangled in a web of love, loyalty, and betrayal. With tensions rising between the Kacháris and the British, he must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of both his newfound friends and his own future.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Cultural exploration

Themes: Cultural clash, Identity, Love and loyalty
