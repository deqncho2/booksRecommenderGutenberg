Synopsis: In 'The Real Fairy Folk' by Louise Jamison, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of enchantment and magic right in her own backyard. As she befriends a mischievous fairy named Pippa, Lily is drawn into a thrilling adventure filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. Together, they must navigate the complex relationships between humans and fairies, and uncover the truth about the ancient feud that threatens to tear their worlds apart. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this enchanting tale will transport readers to a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Middle Grade

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Identity, Nature
