Synopsis: In 'Curiosities of Heat' by Lyman Beecher Tefft, a young scientist stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that defies the laws of thermodynamics. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a world of unexplained heat sources and inexplicable energy transfers. With each discovery, he becomes more entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between scientific curiosity and personal obsession blurs. As he races against time to unravel the secrets of heat, he must confront the consequences of his own actions and the ethical dilemmas that arise. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of scientific knowledge and the power of human curiosity.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Scientific exploration, Ethics, Obsession
