Synopsis: In 'Colonel Thomas Blood, Crown-stealer, 1618-1680' by Abbott, Wilbur Cortez, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the audacious life of Colonel Thomas Blood, a notorious figure in history. From his daring attempt to steal the Crown Jewels of England to his involvement in political intrigue and espionage, this captivating biography delves into the mind of a cunning and enigmatic character. As the story unfolds, readers will be left in awe of Blood's audacity and resourcefulness, wondering what motivated him to commit such audacious acts. 

Genres: Biography, History, True Crime

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Power
