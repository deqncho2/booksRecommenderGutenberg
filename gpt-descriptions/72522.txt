Synopsis: In 'Nightmare Tower' by Sam Merwin, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious tower that appears only in her dreams. As she navigates through its treacherous levels, she encounters bizarre creatures, mind-bending puzzles, and a haunting presence that seems to be watching her every move. With each passing level, Emily's grip on reality begins to slip, blurring the lines between her dreams and waking life. As she races against time to escape the Nightmare Tower, she must confront her deepest fears and unravel the secrets that lie within. 

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Dreams and reality, Fear and anxiety, Identity and self-discovery
