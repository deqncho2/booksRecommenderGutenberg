Synopsis: In "Notes of a staff officer of our First New Jersey Brigade on the Seven Day's Battle on the peninsula in 1862," E. Burd Grubb takes readers on a gripping journey through the intense and harrowing events of the Seven Day's Battle during the American Civil War. Through the eyes of a staff officer, the reader is immersed in the chaos, bravery, and sacrifices made by the soldiers on the front lines. As the battle unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, loyalties are tested, and the true nature of war is revealed. This firsthand account offers a unique perspective on one of the most pivotal moments in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The horrors of war
