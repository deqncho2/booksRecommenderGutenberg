Synopsis: In 'A Boy Scout's Courage' by Edward Howard Griggs, a young boy named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that tests his bravery and resourcefulness. When a mysterious treasure map falls into his hands, Jack must navigate through treacherous terrain, encounter dangerous foes, and overcome his own fears to uncover the hidden secrets. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the true meaning of courage.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Self-discovery
