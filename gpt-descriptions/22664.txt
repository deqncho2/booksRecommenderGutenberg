Synopsis: In "The Severed Hand: From "German Tales" Published by the American Publishers' Corporation," Hauff weaves a chilling tale that follows a young man named Friedrich as he embarks on a journey to unravel the mystery behind a severed hand. As Friedrich delves deeper into the dark secrets surrounding the hand, he finds himself entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and sinister forces. With each twist and turn, the story takes on an eerie and suspenseful atmosphere, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the severed hand.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Supernatural, mystery, the power of fate
