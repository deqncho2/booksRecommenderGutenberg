Synopsis: In 'Dolly and Molly and the Farmer Man' by Gordon, Elizabeth, two mischievous sisters embark on an unexpected adventure when they encounter a mysterious farmer man. As they navigate through a series of thrilling and humorous escapades, Dolly and Molly discover secrets that will change their lives forever. With their quick wit and determination, they must unravel the truth and face unexpected challenges along the way.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Resilience
