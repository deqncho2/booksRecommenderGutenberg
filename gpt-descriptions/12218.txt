Synopsis: 

In 'Pastoral Poetry & Pastoral Drama: A Literary Inquiry, with Special Reference to the Pre-Restoration Stage in England', Greg, W. W. delves into the enchanting world of pastoral literature, exploring its origins and evolution during the pre-Restoration era in England. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, Greg uncovers the captivating allure of pastoral poetry and drama, shedding light on the profound influence it had on the literary landscape of the time. From the idyllic countryside settings to the complex relationships between shepherds and nymphs, this inquiry takes readers on a journey through the pastoral realm, uncovering its hidden depths and timeless appeal.

Genres: Literary criticism, Literary history

Themes: Nature and the countryside, Idealized love and romance, Social and political commentary
