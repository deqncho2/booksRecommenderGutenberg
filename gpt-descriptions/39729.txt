Synopsis: In 'Courage, True Hearts: Sailing in Search of Fortune' by Stables, Gordon, embark on a thrilling adventure across treacherous seas as a group of courageous sailors set out on a perilous journey in search of fortune. From battling fierce storms to encountering mysterious islands, the crew faces numerous challenges that test their bravery and determination. As they navigate through uncharted waters, they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected dangers that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Action

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Perseverance
