Synopsis: In 'A Portrait of Old George Town', Peter and Grace Dunlop take readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden stories of a charming town. As they delve into the lives of its inhabitants, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the town's past intertwines with its present. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this book paints a vivid picture of a place where nostalgia and mystery coexist, leaving readers eager to uncover the town's hidden treasures.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: History and memory, Community and belonging, Secrets and revelations
