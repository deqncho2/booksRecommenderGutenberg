Synopsis: In "A Man of the People: A Drama of Abraham Lincoln," Thomas Dixon, Jr. presents a captivating portrayal of the iconic American president, Abraham Lincoln. Set against the backdrop of the Civil War, this gripping drama delves into the complexities of Lincoln's character, his struggles, and the challenges he faced as he navigated the treacherous political landscape of his time. Through vivid storytelling and compelling dialogue, Dixon offers a fresh perspective on one of history's most influential figures, leaving readers eager to uncover the untold aspects of Lincoln's life and legacy.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Politics, Morality, Legacy
