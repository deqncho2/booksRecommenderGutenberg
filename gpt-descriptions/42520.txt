Synopsis: In 'A Marriage Under the Terror' by Patricia Wentworth, a young woman finds herself caught in the midst of the French Revolution, where danger lurks at every corner. As she navigates the treacherous political landscape, she must also confront the secrets and betrayals within her own marriage. With suspenseful twists and unexpected alliances, this gripping historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and what sacrifices must be made to survive.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and loyalty, Betrayal, Survival in times of turmoil
