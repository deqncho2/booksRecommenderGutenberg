Synopsis: In 'The Bondman: A Story of the Times of Wat Tyler' by O'Neill, Mrs., readers are transported to the tumultuous era of Wat Tyler's rebellion in 14th century England. The story revolves around the life of a young bondman named Alwyn, who finds himself caught between his loyalty to his master and his desire for freedom. As Alwyn navigates the treacherous political landscape and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and conspiracies that could change the course of history. With its gripping narrative and vivid historical backdrop, 'The Bondman' offers a captivating tale of adventure, love, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Freedom and oppression, Loyalty and betrayal, Social inequality
