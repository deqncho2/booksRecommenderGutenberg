Synopsis: 
'Last Night Out' by Milton A. Rothman is a gripping mystery thriller that follows a group of friends on a fateful night out in the city. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and a shocking event takes place, leaving the group questioning their own actions and the true nature of their friendship. With twists and turns at every corner, this suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Secrets, Consequences
