Synopsis: In 'The Ragged Edge' by Harold MacGrath, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. Set against the backdrop of the bustling city of New York, the story follows the protagonist's journey as she navigates through a world of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As she delves deeper into the shadows, she discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the truth lies on the ragged edge between darkness and light.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Redemption
