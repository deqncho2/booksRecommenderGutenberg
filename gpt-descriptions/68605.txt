Synopsis: In 'A Corner in Corn; or, How a Chicago Boy Did the Trick' by Self-made man, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the bustling streets of Chicago as a young boy discovers a hidden talent that could change his life forever. Faced with adversity and unexpected challenges, he must navigate the treacherous world of business and learn to outsmart his competitors. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the boy's ultimate triumph.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Business

Themes: Ambition, Resilience, Ingenuity
