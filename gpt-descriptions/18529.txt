Synopsis: In 'August First', a gripping historical fiction, Mary Raymond Shipman Andrews and Roy Irving Murray transport readers to the tense atmosphere of the American Civil War. The story follows a young soldier named Ned, who finds himself torn between his duty to fight for the Union and his personal beliefs. As the fateful day of August 1st approaches, Ned must make a life-altering decision that will test his loyalty, courage, and the true meaning of freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Identity, Freedom
