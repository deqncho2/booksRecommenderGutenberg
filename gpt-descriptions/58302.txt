Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Baroness Orczy' is a collection of works by the renowned author, Baroness Orczy. This compilation includes some of her most famous novels, such as "The Scarlet Pimpernel" and "The Elusive Pimpernel," which revolve around daring adventures, secret identities, and the fight for justice. From the French Revolution to the Napoleonic era, these captivating stories transport readers to a world of intrigue, romance, and suspense. Whether it's the heroic exploits of the enigmatic Scarlet Pimpernel or the thrilling escapades of his elusive counterpart, this collection promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Love, Justice, Deception
