Synopsis: In "The Cowardly Lion of Oz" by Ruth Plumly Thompson, join the beloved characters from the Land of Oz on a thrilling adventure as they encounter a mysterious new creature. When the Cowardly Lion discovers a hidden talent, he must summon his courage to face a series of unexpected challenges. With the help of his friends, the Lion embarks on a quest that will test his bravery and reveal the true meaning of courage. Will he overcome his fears and prove himself worthy of his title? Find out in this enchanting tale set in the magical world of Oz.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
