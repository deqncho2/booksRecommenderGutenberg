Synopsis: 
'The Warden' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that delves into the moral and ethical dilemmas faced by a compassionate warden of a charitable institution. Set in the fictional town of Barchester, the story follows the life of Mr. Harding, a kind-hearted man who finds himself at the center of a controversy surrounding the funds allocated to the institution he oversees. As tensions rise and conflicts emerge, Mr. Harding must navigate the complexities of duty, loyalty, and personal integrity, ultimately questioning the very nature of his role and the institution itself.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Social Commentary, Satire

Themes: 
Morality, Ethics, Duty, Integrity, Social Justice
