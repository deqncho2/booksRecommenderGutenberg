Synopsis: 

'Prairie, Peak, and Plateau: A Guide to the Geology of Colorado' takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through the diverse landscapes of Colorado, uncovering the geological wonders that shape this remarkable state. From the majestic peaks of the Rocky Mountains to the vast prairies and rugged plateaus, this guide provides a comprehensive exploration of Colorado's geological history, formations, and natural phenomena. Through vivid descriptions, captivating photographs, and detailed maps, the authors invite readers to delve into the fascinating world of Colorado's geology, revealing the forces that have shaped its unique landscapes over millions of years. Whether you are a geology enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply curious about the natural wonders of Colorado, this guide is an essential companion for discovering the hidden secrets beneath the surface.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Geology

Themes: Geological history, Natural wonders, Landscape exploration
