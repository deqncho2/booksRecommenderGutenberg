Synopsis: In 'The Happy Man' by Gerald W. Page, a seemingly ordinary man named Mr. Smith moves into a small town and quickly becomes the center of attention. His infectious happiness and positive outlook on life captivate the townspeople, who are drawn to his charismatic personality. However, as they delve deeper into Mr. Smith's life, they begin to uncover a series of mysterious events and dark secrets that challenge their perception of happiness and the true nature of human emotions. As the town becomes consumed by curiosity and obsession, they must confront their own desires and fears, leading to unexpected consequences.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Happiness, Perception, Secrets, Human Nature
