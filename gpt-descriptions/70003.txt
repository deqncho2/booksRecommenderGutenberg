Synopsis: 

In 'Flight 18' by Torak, Paul A., a routine flight takes a terrifying turn when a mysterious force causes Flight 18 to vanish from radar and reappear in a parallel dimension. As the passengers and crew struggle to comprehend their new reality, they must confront their deepest fears and work together to find a way back home. With time running out and tensions rising, they soon discover that their survival depends on unraveling the enigma that brought them to this strange and dangerous world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Parallel Dimensions, Human Nature
