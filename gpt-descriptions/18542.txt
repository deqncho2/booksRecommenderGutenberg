Synopsis: In 'How to Cook Fish' by Reed, Myrtle, a young woman named Mary embarks on a culinary adventure that takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the tranquil shores of a small fishing village. As she learns the art of cooking fish from a mysterious and enigmatic chef, Mary discovers that there is more to this culinary journey than just perfecting a recipe. With each dish she prepares, Mary uncovers hidden secrets, confronts personal demons, and finds unexpected love. This captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of food will leave readers hungry for more.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Culinary

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Food
