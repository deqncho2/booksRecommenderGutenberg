Synopsis: In "Home as Found: Sequel to 'Homeward Bound'", James Fenimore Cooper takes readers on a captivating journey as the characters from the first book navigate the challenges of returning home. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing America, this sequel delves into the complexities of family, love, and the pursuit of happiness. As old friendships are tested and new alliances are formed, the characters must confront their own desires and ambitions, ultimately discovering the true meaning of home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Patriotism, Social Change
