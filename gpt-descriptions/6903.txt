Synopsis: In the small town of Ludington, a mysterious woman arrives claiming to be the long-lost sister of Miss Ludington. As the townspeople become captivated by her enigmatic presence, dark secrets and hidden desires begin to unravel, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of identity and the power of the human psyche.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, Family secrets, Madness, Obsession
