Synopsis: The Mercer Boys on a Treasure Hunt takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as the three Mercer brothers stumble upon a mysterious clue that leads them on a quest for hidden treasure. With danger lurking at every turn, the brothers must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the secrets of the past and uncover the coveted riches. As they delve deeper into the treasure hunt, they encounter unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, making their journey all the more thrilling and unpredictable.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Exploration, Perseverance
