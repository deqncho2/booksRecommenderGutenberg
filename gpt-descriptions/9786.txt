Synopsis: In 'Love's Shadow' by Ada Leverson, a group of witty and fashionable friends navigate the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations in Edwardian London. As secrets are revealed and relationships are tested, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of desire, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With sharp humor and astute observations, Leverson explores the intricacies of human relationships and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy of Manners

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, friendship and loyalty
