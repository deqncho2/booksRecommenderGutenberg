Synopsis: In "Comrades of the Saddle; Or, The Young Rough Riders of the Plains" by Frank V. Webster, three adventurous young friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey across the vast plains of the American West. As they navigate through dangerous encounters, unexpected challenges, and the mysteries of the untamed wilderness, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With their trusty horses as their only companions, these young rough riders must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to overcome the perils that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Survival
