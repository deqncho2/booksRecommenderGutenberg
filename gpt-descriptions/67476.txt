Synopsis: 
In 'The Automaton Ear, and Other Sketches' by Florence McLandburgh, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and thought-provoking sketches. From a mysterious automaton with an uncanny ability to hear secrets to a series of unexpected encounters in a bustling city, each sketch presents a unique and captivating story that will leave readers wanting more. With a blend of mystery, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, this collection of sketches is sure to keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Unexpected encounters, Human nature
