Synopsis: In 'Christmas for Tad: A Story of Mary and Abraham Lincoln', Helen Topping Miller takes readers on a heartwarming journey into the lives of the Lincoln family during the holiday season. Set against the backdrop of the Civil War, this poignant tale follows Mary and Abraham Lincoln as they strive to create a joyful Christmas for their young son, Tad. Amid the challenges of war and personal loss, the Lincolns discover the true meaning of love, hope, and resilience during this special time of year.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Holiday

Themes: Love, Resilience, Family, Hope
