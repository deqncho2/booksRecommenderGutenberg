Synopsis: In 'The Smoker's Year Book', Oliver Herford takes readers on a whimsical journey through the world of smoking, exploring the various rituals, habits, and idiosyncrasies of smokers. From the art of lighting a cigar to the allure of the smoke-filled room, Herford's witty observations and clever illustrations offer a delightful and humorous exploration of the smoker's world. Whether you're a smoker or simply curious about the culture surrounding it, this book is sure to entertain and enlighten.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Illustration

Themes: Smoking culture, Habits and rituals, Social commentary
