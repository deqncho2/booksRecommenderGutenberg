Synopsis: In "The Danes in Lancashire and Yorkshire," S. W. Partington takes readers on a captivating journey through history, uncovering the fascinating tales of the Danish invaders in the regions of Lancashire and Yorkshire. From epic battles to unexpected alliances, this book delves into the rich tapestry of Viking influence and their impact on the local communities. As the author weaves together historical accounts and archaeological discoveries, readers will be enthralled by the gripping stories of conquest, survival, and the clash of cultures that shaped these lands centuries ago.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Cultural studies

Themes: Viking invasions, Cultural assimilation, Historical heritage
