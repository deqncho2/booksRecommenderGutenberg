Synopsis: 
'The Tale of Peter Rabbit' follows the mischievous adventures of a young rabbit named Peter, who constantly finds himself in trouble as he ventures into Mr. McGregor's garden against his mother's warnings. With danger lurking around every corner, Peter must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to outsmart his adversaries and make it back home safely. This timeless children's classic is filled with excitement, humor, and valuable life lessons that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Adventure, disobedience, consequences, family, resourcefulness
