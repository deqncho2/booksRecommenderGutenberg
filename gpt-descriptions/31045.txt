Synopsis: In 'Frondes Agrestes: Readings in 'Modern Painters'', John Ruskin takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of art, exploring the profound impact of nature on the works of renowned painters. Through his eloquent prose and insightful analysis, Ruskin delves into the intricate details of various paintings, unraveling the hidden meanings and emotions they convey. From the vibrant landscapes of Turner to the delicate brushstrokes of the Old Masters, this collection of readings offers a unique perspective on the power of art to capture the essence of the natural world.

Genres: Art criticism, Literary criticism, Non-fiction

Themes: Nature, Aesthetics, Symbolism
