Synopsis: In 'The Sign at Six' by Stewart Edward White, a mysterious sign appears in a small town, bearing a cryptic message that captures the attention of the entire community. As the townspeople try to decipher its meaning, they are drawn into a web of secrets, hidden pasts, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the tension escalates, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Community and interconnectedness
