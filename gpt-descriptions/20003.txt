Synopsis: In 'The Infernal Marriage' by Benjamin Disraeli, a young and ambitious politician finds himself entangled in a web of love, power, and betrayal. As he navigates the treacherous world of high society, he becomes embroiled in a scandalous affair that threatens to destroy his career and personal life. With political intrigue, forbidden romance, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of society and the sacrifices one must make to achieve their ambitions.

Genres: Political fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Power, Love, Society
