Synopsis: 
'The Poetical Works of Thomas Traherne, 1636?-1674, from the original manuscripts' is a captivating collection of poems that delves into the depths of human existence and the wonders of the natural world. With each verse, Traherne invites readers on a journey of self-discovery, exploring the profound beauty and mystery of life. From contemplating the innocence of childhood to pondering the divine nature of the universe, these poems offer a glimpse into the soul of a poet who finds solace and inspiration in the smallest of details. 

Genres: Poetry, Spiritual, Metaphysical

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature, Spirituality
