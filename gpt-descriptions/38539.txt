Synopsis: 

In the eleventh volume of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, readers are taken on a captivating journey from the life and teachings of the enigmatic Johann Gichtel to the concept of glory itself. This volume delves into the fascinating world of mysticism, exploring the spiritual beliefs and practices of various cultures and individuals. From the mystical writings of Gichtel, a German theosophist, to the exploration of glory as a concept that transcends time and culture, this volume offers a profound exploration of the human quest for meaning and transcendence. 

Genres: Reference, Philosophy, Mysticism

Themes: Mysticism, Spirituality, Transcendence
