Synopsis: In the picturesque West Country, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the map, she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger who may hold the key to unlocking the secrets of the past. With danger lurking around every corner, Emily must navigate through treacherous landscapes and unexpected alliances to unravel the truth and secure her future.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Treasure hunting, Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal
