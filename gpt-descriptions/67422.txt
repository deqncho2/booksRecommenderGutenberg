Synopsis: In 'The Black Cat (Vol. I, No. 1, October 1895)', a mysterious black cat becomes the center of a chilling tale that explores the depths of human psychology and the boundaries of sanity. As the story unfolds, the cat's presence takes on an eerie significance, leading the reader through a series of unsettling events that will leave them questioning the nature of reality and the darkness that resides within us all.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Madness, Guilt, Supernatural, Psychological Exploration
