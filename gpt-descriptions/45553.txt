Synopsis: In 'The Lu Lu Alphabet' by Colman, Miss (Pamela Atkins), a mysterious letter arrives at Lu Lu's doorstep, setting off a chain of events that will challenge her perception of reality and lead her on a thrilling adventure. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the alphabet, Lu Lu discovers a world filled with magic, danger, and unexpected friendships. With each letter she unravels, Lu Lu uncovers a truth that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Perception, Friendship
