Synopsis: 

Step into the captivating world of literary legends as James Thomas Fields, a prominent American publisher, takes you on a journey through his cherished memories with renowned authors. In "Yesterdays with Authors," Fields shares intimate anecdotes and personal encounters with literary giants such as Charles Dickens, Nathaniel Hawthorne, and Ralph Waldo Emerson. From lively conversations in cozy parlors to unexpected adventures in far-flung corners of the world, Fields offers a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of these extraordinary individuals. With wit, charm, and a deep appreciation for the written word, Fields paints a vivid portrait of the literary landscape of his time, leaving readers yearning for more tales from the golden age of literature.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Nonfiction

Themes: Literary history, Personal relationships, Cultural exchange
