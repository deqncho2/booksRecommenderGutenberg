Synopsis: In 'The Code of the Mountains' by Charles Neville Buck, a young lawyer named John Graham finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is sent to a remote mountain town to settle a legal dispute. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the mountains, he uncovers a hidden code that holds the key to a long-buried secret. With his life on the line, John must navigate treacherous terrain and face the wrath of those who will stop at nothing to protect the code.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Survival and self-discovery
