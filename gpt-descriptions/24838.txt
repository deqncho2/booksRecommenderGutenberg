Synopsis: In 'Tessa: 1901' by Louis Becke, a young woman named Tessa finds herself caught between two worlds when she is transported back in time to the year 1901. As she navigates the unfamiliar landscape of the past, Tessa must confront the challenges of a bygone era while desperately searching for a way to return to her own time. Along the way, she encounters unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries, all while unraveling the mysteries that surround her sudden time travel. With each passing day, Tessa's determination grows, but will she be able to find her way back home before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Adaptation, Resilience
