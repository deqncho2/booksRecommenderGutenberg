Synopsis: In 'The Voice of the Pack' by Marshall Edison, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden ability to communicate with wolves. As she delves deeper into this newfound power, she becomes entangled in a dangerous world of ancient secrets and hidden agendas. With the help of a mysterious wolf pack, Lily must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves. This thrilling tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power of nature, Identity, Loyalty
