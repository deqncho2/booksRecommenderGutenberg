Synopsis: 
In the Swim takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and exhilarating world of gay New York. Set against the backdrop of the city's bustling nightlife, this novel delves into the lives of a diverse group of characters, each navigating their own personal struggles and desires. From the glamorous drag queens to the closeted businessmen, the story weaves together their interconnected lives, revealing the undercurrents that shape their experiences. As secrets are unveiled and relationships are tested, In the Swim explores the complexities of identity, love, and acceptance in a city that never sleeps.

Genres: LGBTQ+ Fiction, Contemporary Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Acceptance, Self-discovery
