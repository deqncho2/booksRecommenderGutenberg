Synopsis:
"Lady Molly of Scotland Yard" follows the captivating adventures of Lady Molly Robertson-Kirk, a brilliant and unconventional detective working for Scotland Yard. With her sharp intellect and fearless determination, Lady Molly tackles a series of perplexing cases that baffle her male colleagues. From solving mysterious murders to uncovering hidden secrets, Lady Molly's investigations take her into the heart of London's high society and the darkest corners of the criminal underworld. As she navigates the complexities of her profession and challenges societal expectations, Lady Molly proves that she is a force to be reckoned with, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the truth behind each enigma.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Historical Fiction
Themes: Gender roles, Justice, Class divide
