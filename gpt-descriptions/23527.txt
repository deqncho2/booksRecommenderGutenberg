Synopsis: In 'The Twelve Magic Changelings' by Glen, M. A., a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of enchantment when she stumbles upon a group of mysterious beings known as the Changelings. As she becomes entangled in their magical realm, Lily must navigate a dangerous quest to uncover the truth about her own identity and the ancient prophecy that binds her fate. With each encounter, she unravels secrets that challenge her perception of reality and forces her to confront her deepest fears. As the line between fantasy and reality blurs, Lily must harness her own inner magic to save both worlds from impending doom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Magic, Courage
