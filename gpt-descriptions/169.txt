Synopsis: In 'The Well at the World's End: A Tale' by Morris, William, a young prince embarks on a perilous journey to find the legendary Well at the World's End, a fabled source of eternal youth and happiness. Along the way, he encounters treacherous landscapes, encounters with mysterious beings, and tests of his courage and determination. As he delves deeper into this enchanting world, he discovers that the true meaning of his quest may not be what he initially expected. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Quest for meaning, Self-discovery, Love and sacrifice
