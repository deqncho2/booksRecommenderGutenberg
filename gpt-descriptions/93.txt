Synopsis: In "Tom Sawyer, Detective," Mark Twain takes readers on a thrilling adventure as Tom Sawyer and his friend Huck Finn embark on a mysterious case that requires their detective skills. When a crime is committed, Tom and Huck find themselves in the midst of a perplexing puzzle, where they must unravel clues, navigate through danger, and outsmart the cunning culprits. As they delve deeper into the investigation, the boys discover that things are not always as they seem, and their detective abilities are put to the ultimate test. Will Tom Sawyer and Huck Finn be able to crack the case and bring the truth to light?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Morality
