Synopsis: In 'Earth needs a killer' by Walton, Bryce, a mysterious and deadly threat looms over the planet, forcing humanity to seek an unconventional solution. As the world teeters on the brink of destruction, a reluctant hero emerges, armed with a unique set of skills and a dark past. With time running out, this unlikely savior must navigate a treacherous web of secrets, betrayal, and moral dilemmas to save Earth from its impending doom. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Action

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Sacrifice
