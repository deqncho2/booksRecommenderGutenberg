Synopsis: In "The Life of Robert Louis Stevenson for Boys and Girls," Jacqueline Overton takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating life of one of the most beloved authors in history. From Stevenson's adventurous childhood in Scotland to his travels across the globe, this engaging biography unveils the extraordinary experiences that shaped his imagination and inspired his timeless stories. Filled with tales of pirates, hidden treasures, and daring escapades, this book invites young readers to discover the remarkable life of a literary genius whose imagination knew no bounds.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Literary Legacy
