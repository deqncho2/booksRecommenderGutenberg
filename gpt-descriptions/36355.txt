Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious salamander appears, bringing with it a series of inexplicable events that disrupt the lives of the townspeople. As the salamander's presence becomes increasingly ominous, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind its arrival and confront their own deepest fears. Secrets, betrayal, and the supernatural intertwine in this gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and deception, Fear and courage, Supernatural forces
