Synopsis: In 'The Course of Creation' by Anderson, John, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the origins of life. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of secrecy, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect his revolutionary findings from those who would stop at nothing to control them.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Human curiosity, Power struggle
