Synopsis: In 'The Sleeping Beauty' by C.S. Evans, a young woman named Aurora finds herself trapped in a world where dreams and reality intertwine. As she navigates through a mysterious castle filled with enchantments and secrets, she must uncover the truth about her own identity and the hidden powers she possesses. With the help of a charming prince and a group of unlikely allies, Aurora embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and determination. Will she be able to break the spell that has kept her asleep for so long and awaken to her true destiny?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Magic
