Synopsis: In 'Yesterday's Revenge' by Nichols, H. L., a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she discovers a hidden diary that reveals a shocking truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Emily becomes entangled in a dangerous game of revenge, where the line between justice and vengeance becomes blurred. With her life on the line, Emily must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Revenge, Justice
