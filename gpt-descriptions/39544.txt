Synopsis: In 'The Incendiary: A Story of Mystery' by William Augustine Leahy, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious fires that threaten to destroy everything in their path. As the flames continue to spread, a determined detective must unravel the secrets hidden within the community, delving into the dark pasts and hidden motives of its residents. With each new revelation, the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Uncovering the truth
