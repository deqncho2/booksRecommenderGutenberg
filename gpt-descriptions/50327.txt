Synopsis: In 'The River Motor Boat Boys on the Ohio; Or, The Three Blue Lights' by Harry Gordon, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey along the Ohio River. When they encounter a mysterious set of three blue lights, their curiosity is piqued, leading them to uncover a series of unexpected events and dangerous encounters. As they navigate the treacherous waters, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to solve the enigma behind the three blue lights and ensure their own safety.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Mystery-solving
