Synopsis: 

'Area Handbook for Albania' provides a comprehensive and insightful exploration of the fascinating country of Albania. Written by a team of experts, this handbook delves into the historical, cultural, political, and economic aspects of Albania, offering a deep understanding of its unique identity. From the ancient Illyrians to the communist regime and its subsequent transition, the book uncovers the layers of Albania's complex past and examines its present-day challenges and opportunities. With detailed analysis and engaging anecdotes, 'Area Handbook for Albania' is an indispensable guide for anyone seeking to unravel the mysteries of this enigmatic nation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History

Themes: Albanian culture, Politics, Economic development, Historical context
