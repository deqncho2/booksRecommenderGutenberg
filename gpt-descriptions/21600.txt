Synopsis: In "The Flourishing of Romance and the Rise of Allegory: (Periods of European Literature, vol. II)" by George Saintsbury, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of European literature. From the enchanting tales of courtly love to the emergence of allegorical storytelling, this volume explores the evolution of romance and its profound impact on the literary landscape. With meticulous analysis and engaging anecdotes, Saintsbury delves into the works of renowned authors, revealing the intricate web of themes and motifs that shaped this transformative period in European literature.

Genres: Literary criticism, History, Cultural studies

Themes: Romance, Allegory, Love, Transformation, Literary evolution
