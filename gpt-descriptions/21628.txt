Synopsis: In 'Imaginary Conversations and Poems: A Selection' by Walter Savage Landor, readers are transported to a world where historical figures and literary characters engage in captivating dialogues and poetic musings. From the conversations between Julius Caesar and Cicero to the poetic reflections of Sappho and Dante, Landor's collection offers a unique blend of fiction and poetry that explores the depths of human emotions, the complexities of love and friendship, and the timeless themes of power, morality, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Fiction, Poetry, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Friendship, Power, Morality, Truth
