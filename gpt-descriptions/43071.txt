Synopsis: In 'The Alternative' by George Barr McCutcheon, a young man named John Brinton finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret society that holds the power to change the course of history. As he delves deeper into this clandestine world, John must navigate a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life hanging in the balance, John must make a choice that will not only determine his own fate but also the fate of the world as he knows it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secret societies, Power and control, Betrayal, Identity
