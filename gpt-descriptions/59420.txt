Synopsis: 

In 'Use of Mines in Trench Warfare (From the French School of St. Cyr)', the Army War College delves into the fascinating world of trench warfare and the ingenious use of mines. This captivating work explores the strategic and tactical aspects of mining operations, revealing the intricate methods employed by the French School of St. Cyr. From the construction and placement of mines to the devastating impact they had on the enemy, this book offers a gripping account of the deadly cat-and-mouse game played beneath the surface of the trenches. 

Genres: Military history, War tactics, Historical analysis

Themes: Trench warfare, Military strategy, Technological advancements
