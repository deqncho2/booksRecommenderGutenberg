Synopsis: 

In 'The English-American, His Travail by Sea and Land: or, A New Survey of the West-India's', Thomas Gage takes readers on a captivating journey through the West Indies, offering a unique perspective on the region. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Gage explores the diverse landscapes, cultures, and people he encounters during his travels. From the bustling cities to the untamed wilderness, he delves into the complexities of colonial life, shedding light on the social, economic, and political dynamics of the time. As he navigates through various challenges and adventures, Gage's narrative unfolds with a sense of wonder and curiosity, leaving readers eager to discover the wonders and mysteries of the West Indies alongside him.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical Non-fiction
Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Cultural Diversity
