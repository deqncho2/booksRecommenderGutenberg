Synopsis: In 'Secret Bread' by Jesse, F. Tennyson, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden family secret that leads her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and redemption. As she delves deeper into her family's past, she uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and long-held secrets that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew. With each revelation, Lily must confront her own fears and insecurities, ultimately finding the strength to forge her own path and redefine her identity. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
