Synopsis: In 'Jenifer's Prayer' by Crane, Oliver, a young woman named Jenifer finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon an ancient artifact with mysterious powers. As she delves deeper into the artifact's history, Jenifer becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where her life and the fate of those she loves hang in the balance. With each step closer to the truth, Jenifer must confront her own inner demons and make a choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and deception, Power of choice, Inner strength
