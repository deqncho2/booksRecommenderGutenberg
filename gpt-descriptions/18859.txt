Synopsis: In 'Cross Purposes and The Shadows' by George MacDonald, a young man named Hugh Sutherland finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in his ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled with a group of eccentric characters, each with their own hidden agendas. With danger lurking around every corner, Hugh must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of love and loyalty, the battle between good and evil
