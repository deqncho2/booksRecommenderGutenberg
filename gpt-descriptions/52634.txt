Synopsis: In 'Women's Work', two women from different backgrounds find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and lies as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As their lives become increasingly intertwined, they must confront their own desires and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With its gripping narrative and compelling characters, this novel explores the power dynamics between men and women, the pursuit of personal fulfillment, and the sacrifices we make for the ones we love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Ambition, Love and relationships, Societal expectations
