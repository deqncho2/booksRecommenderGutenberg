Synopsis: Milly and Olly is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two young siblings, Milly and Olly, as they navigate the challenges of growing up in a small, idyllic village. Their world is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a series of unexpected events that will test their bond and resilience. As Milly and Olly embark on a thrilling adventure, they uncover secrets that will forever change their lives and the fate of their community.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Discovery
