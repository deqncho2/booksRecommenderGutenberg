Synopsis: In 'The Story of Sonny Sahib' by Sara Jeannette Duncan, readers are transported to colonial India where they follow the life of Sonny Sahib, a young boy caught between two worlds. As Sonny navigates the complexities of his mixed heritage and the expectations placed upon him, he must confront the clash of cultures and the challenges of identity. Set against the backdrop of British imperialism, this captivating tale explores the struggles and triumphs of a young protagonist striving to find his place in a rapidly changing society.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Cultural

Themes: Identity, Colonialism, Cultural assimilation
