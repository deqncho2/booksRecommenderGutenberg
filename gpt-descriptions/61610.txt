Synopsis: Ralph Osborn, Midshipman at Annapolis: A Story of Life at the U.S. Naval Academy is an enthralling coming-of-age tale set in the prestigious Naval Academy. Follow the journey of Ralph Osborn as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of his time at Annapolis, facing rigorous training, intense competition, and the complexities of friendship and loyalty. As Ralph strives to prove himself and fulfill his dreams of becoming a naval officer, he discovers the true meaning of honor, courage, and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Honor, Self-discovery
