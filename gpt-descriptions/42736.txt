Synopsis: 

In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 17, Slice 5 takes readers on an intriguing journey from "Malta" to "Map, Walter." This captivating volume delves into the rich history, culture, and geography of Malta, a Mediterranean gem with a fascinating past. From the Knights of St. John to the strategic importance of its location, readers will uncover the secrets of this enchanting island. Moving on, the volume explores the life and contributions of Walter Map, a medieval scholar and writer whose works have left an indelible mark on literature and history. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this volume offers a captivating exploration of two distinct subjects that will leave readers eager to learn more.

Genres: Reference, History, Biography

Themes: History, Culture, Geography, Literature, Scholarly Contributions
