Synopsis: In 'Secret Societies of the Middle Ages', Keightley delves into the mysterious world of clandestine organizations that thrived during the Middle Ages. From the enigmatic Templars to the elusive Assassins, this captivating exploration uncovers the hidden rituals, beliefs, and power struggles of these secret societies. Keightley's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the intrigue, conspiracy, and mysticism that surrounded these enigmatic groups, revealing a world of hidden knowledge and forbidden secrets.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Mystery, Occult

Themes: Secret societies, Power and influence, Conspiracy, Religion and spirituality
