Synopsis: 
In "Penny Nichols and the Mystery of the Lost Key" by Clark, Joan, Penny Nichols, a curious and adventurous teenager, stumbles upon an old key that holds a mysterious secret. As she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the key's origin, Penny finds herself entangled in a web of hidden clues, unexpected allies, and dangerous adversaries. With each step closer to the truth, Penny realizes that the key's power goes far beyond what she could have ever imagined, and she must race against time to unlock its secrets before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Discovery, Friendship, Danger
