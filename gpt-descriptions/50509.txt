Synopsis: In 'Baby-Land' by Almira Louisa Corey Frink, a young woman finds herself transported to a mysterious world where babies reign supreme. As she navigates this enchanting realm, she discovers the secrets and challenges that come with being a caretaker in Baby-Land. With whimsical characters and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the wonders and complexities of the world of babies.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Parenthood, Imagination, Responsibility
