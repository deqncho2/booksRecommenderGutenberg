Synopsis: In 'The Sorceress', the final installment of the thrilling trilogy by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of a kingdom hangs in the balance. As the sorceress's powers grow stronger, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous world of magic, betrayal, and love to fulfill her destiny. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this captivating conclusion will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and corruption, Redemption, Fate and destiny
