Synopsis: In 'Grand-Daddy Whiskers, M.D.', follow the extraordinary adventures of Dr. Whiskers, a wise and mischievous cat with a knack for healing. From curing ailing animals to solving mysterious medical cases, this feline doctor's unconventional methods and unwavering determination will captivate readers of all ages. As Dr. Whiskers navigates the challenges of his profession, unexpected friendships and heartwarming moments await, making for an enchanting tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Healing, Determination
