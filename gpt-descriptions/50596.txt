Synopsis: 
In the bustling world of antique dealing, Joseph Quinney is a renowned expert with a keen eye for valuable treasures. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he meets the captivating and enigmatic Miss Muriel. As their relationship deepens, Quinney finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and betrayal that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With his reputation and livelihood at stake, Quinney must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he loves.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and betrayal, Trust and deception, Ambition and sacrifice
