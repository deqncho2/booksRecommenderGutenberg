Synopsis: In 'The Honourable Mr. Tawnish' by Farnol, Jeffery, a charming and enigmatic gentleman finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue when he becomes the target of a mysterious organization. As he navigates through a world of danger and deception, he must uncover the truth behind his own identity and the motives of those who seek to destroy him. With unexpected twists and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
