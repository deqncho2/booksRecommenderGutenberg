Synopsis: In 'Diggers in the Earth' by Eva March Tappan, a group of archaeologists embarks on a thrilling expedition to uncover the secrets buried beneath the earth's surface. As they dig deeper into the ancient ruins, they stumble upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. With each discovery, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, racing against time to protect their findings from those who would stop at nothing to possess its power. This gripping tale of adventure and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Power and Greed
