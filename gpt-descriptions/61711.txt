Synopsis: 
In 'Pig Raising: A Manual for Pig Clubs', Greene and Nolan take readers on a captivating journey into the world of pig farming. Through their expert guidance, they delve into the intricacies of raising pigs, from selecting the right breed to managing their health and nutrition. With practical tips and insightful anecdotes, this manual offers a comprehensive and engaging resource for both novice and experienced pig farmers. As readers immerse themselves in the pages, they will discover the joys and challenges of pig raising, gaining a newfound appreciation for these intelligent and fascinating creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Animal Husbandry

Themes: Farming, Animal Welfare, Sustainable Agriculture
