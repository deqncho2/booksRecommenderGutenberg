Synopsis: In 'Mr. Fortescue: An Andean Romance' by William Westall, a young Englishman named Charles Fortescue embarks on a thrilling journey to the Andes Mountains in search of adventure and fortune. As he delves deeper into the mysterious landscapes and encounters a captivating cast of characters, Charles finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and danger. With each twist and turn, the story unravels the hidden treasures of the Andean region, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Forbidden Love
