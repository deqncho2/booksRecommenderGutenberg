Synopsis: In 'The Rubáiyát of a Persian Kitten', Oliver Herford takes readers on a whimsical journey through the eyes of a mischievous Persian kitten. Through a series of charming and humorous quatrains, the kitten shares its unique perspective on life, love, and the pursuit of happiness. From playful adventures to contemplative musings, this delightful collection of poetry offers a delightful glimpse into the feline world.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Children's Literature

Themes: Playfulness, Curiosity, Wisdom, Love of Life
