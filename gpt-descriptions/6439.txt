Synopsis: Nan Sherwood, a spirited and adventurous young woman, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey to Rose Ranch, a place shrouded in mystery and rumored to hold an ancient treasure. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the ranch, Nan uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected friendships. With her determination and resourcefulness, Nan must navigate through the challenges that arise, all while unraveling the enigma of the old Mexican's treasure. Will Nan's courage and wit lead her to the hidden riches, or will she discover something even more valuable along the way?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
