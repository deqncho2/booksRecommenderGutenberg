Synopsis: 
In 'Yachting Party' by Holden, Fox B., a group of wealthy and influential individuals gather on a luxurious yacht for a seemingly extravagant party. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and unexpected alliances are formed. Amidst the opulence and glamour, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power dynamics
