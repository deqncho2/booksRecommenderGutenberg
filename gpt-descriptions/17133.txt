Synopsis: In "Mildred's Inheritance; Just Her Way; Ann's Own Way," three young women navigate the challenges of life, love, and self-discovery in the late 19th century. Mildred, a spirited heiress, must choose between societal expectations and following her heart. Just, a determined artist, struggles to find her place in a world that dismisses her talent. Ann, a compassionate nurse, faces the complexities of love and duty. As their paths intertwine, these women embark on a journey of personal growth and empowerment, defying the conventions of their time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love, Society's Expectations
