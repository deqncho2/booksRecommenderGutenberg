Synopsis: 

'A Desk-Book of Errors in English: Including Notes on Colloquialisms and Slang to be Avoided in Conversation' by Frank H. Vizetelly is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricacies of the English language, highlighting common errors, colloquialisms, and slang that should be avoided in conversation. With a focus on improving communication skills, this book offers valuable insights and practical tips to help readers navigate the complexities of the English language with precision and clarity. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Language and Linguistics

Themes: Language usage, Communication skills, Linguistic accuracy
