Synopsis: In "Syria, the land of Lebanon" by Leary, Lewis Gaston, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey through the ancient ruins of Syria, uncovering hidden secrets and encountering unexpected dangers. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of this captivating land, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal turmoil, forcing him to question his own beliefs and confront the harsh realities of war-torn Syria. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale takes readers on an unforgettable adventure that will leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Archaeology, Political Intrigue, Personal Transformation
