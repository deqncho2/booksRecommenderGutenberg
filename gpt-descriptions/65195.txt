Synopsis: In 'Quicksands' by B.M. Croker, a young woman named Muriel is swept away by the allure of a charming stranger, leading her into a world of mystery and danger. As she navigates through the treacherous quicksands of her own choices, Muriel must confront her deepest fears and make life-altering decisions that will test her resilience and strength. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Love and betrayal, self-discovery, the consequences of one's actions
