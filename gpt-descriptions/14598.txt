Synopsis: 
In 'The Goose Girl' by Harold MacGrath, a young woman named Mary Ogden finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she is mistaken for a princess. As she navigates the treacherous world of royalty and deception, Mary must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Survival
