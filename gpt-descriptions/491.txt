Synopsis: In 'Rezanov' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a captivating historical novel set in the early 19th century, a charismatic Russian diplomat embarks on a perilous journey to the distant shores of Spanish California. As Nikolai Rezanov navigates treacherous waters and encounters a diverse cast of characters, including a beautiful Spanish aristocrat, his mission takes an unexpected turn, leading to a tale of love, ambition, and the clash of cultures. Will Rezanov's determination and charm be enough to overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, adventure

Themes: Cultural clash, ambition, forbidden love
