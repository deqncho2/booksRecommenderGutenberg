Synopsis: In 'The Art of Natural Sleep', Lyman P. Powell delves into the fascinating world of sleeplessness and offers a unique approach to its cure. Through a series of intriguing cases treated in Northampton and beyond, Powell explores the intricate connection between the mind and sleep, revealing the secrets to achieving a wholesome and natural slumber. With definite directions and practical advice, this book promises to captivate readers as they embark on a journey to unlock the art of natural sleep.

Genres: Self-help, Health, Psychology

Themes: Sleep disorders, Mind-body connection, Holistic healing
