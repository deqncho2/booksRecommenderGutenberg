Synopsis: 

In 'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1970 July - December', the Library of Congress Copyright Office takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of intellectual property. This compilation unveils a treasure trove of renewed copyrights, revealing the creative endeavors and cultural touchstones that captivated the American imagination during the latter half of 1970. From literature to music, art to film, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse and ever-evolving landscape of American creativity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Creative expression, Cultural significance
