Synopsis: 
'Present at a Hanging and Other Ghost Stories' by Ambrose Bierce is a collection of chilling and eerie tales that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From encounters with vengeful spirits to inexplicable supernatural occurrences, each story delves into the realm of the unknown, exploring the darker side of human existence. With Bierce's masterful storytelling and unexpected twists, this collection promises to captivate and haunt readers long after they have turned the final page.

Genres: 
Horror, Mystery, Supernatural Fiction

Themes: 
Death, Fear, The Unseen, Human Nature
