Synopsis: In 'Impressions of America' by Oscar Wilde, embark on a captivating journey as the renowned playwright and poet shares his unique observations and experiences during his visit to the United States. Through his witty and insightful prose, Wilde offers a fascinating glimpse into the American society, culture, and people, painting a vivid picture of the land of opportunity. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast landscapes of the Wild West, Wilde's impressions are both humorous and thought-provoking, providing a fresh perspective on the American Dream.

Genres: Travelogue, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Cultural identity, Stereotypes, Social class, Materialism
