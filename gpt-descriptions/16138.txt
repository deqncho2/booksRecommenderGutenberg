Synopsis: In 'The Cromptons' by Mary Jane Holmes, the lives of the Crompton family are turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives in their small town. As secrets are revealed and relationships are tested, the Cromptons must navigate through love, betrayal, and unexpected twists that threaten to tear them apart. With a blend of romance, mystery, and family drama, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Secrets and betrayal, Resilience and family bonds
