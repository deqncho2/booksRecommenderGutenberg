Synopsis: 

In 'Authors and Writers Associated with Morristown: With a Chapter on Historic Morristown', Julia Keese Colles takes readers on a captivating journey through the literary history of Morristown. From renowned authors who found inspiration in this charming town to lesser-known writers who left their mark, Colles uncovers the fascinating stories behind their works. With a special chapter dedicated to the rich history of Morristown itself, this book offers a unique blend of literary exploration and local heritage, making it a must-read for both book lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Literary history, Local heritage, Inspiration, Creativity
