Synopsis: In 'The Sea-Kings of Crete' by James Baikie, embark on a thrilling adventure that takes you back to the ancient world of Crete. Follow the journey of a young prince as he navigates treacherous waters, encounters mythical creatures, and battles against formidable enemies. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this book will transport you to a time of legendary sea-kings and their epic exploits.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Heroism, Mythical Creatures, Ancient Civilizations
