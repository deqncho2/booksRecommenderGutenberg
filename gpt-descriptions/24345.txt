Synopsis: In 'A Boy's Voyage Round the World' by Samuel Smiles, embark on an exhilarating adventure as a young boy sets sail on a remarkable journey that takes him across the globe. From encountering fascinating cultures to facing thrilling challenges, this captivating tale follows the boy's transformation as he navigates the vast oceans and discovers the wonders of the world. With each new destination, he learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the power of exploration. Will he return home forever changed by his extraordinary voyage?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Cultural diversity
