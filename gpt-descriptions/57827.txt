Synopsis: In 'The Story of Rustem, and other Persian hero tales from Firdusi', Elizabeth D. Renninger brings to life the captivating world of Persian mythology and folklore. This collection of heroic tales follows the legendary figure Rustem, a valiant warrior who embarks on epic adventures, battles mythical creatures, and faces moral dilemmas. From his birth to his rise as a renowned hero, Rustem's journey is filled with bravery, love, and the pursuit of justice. Renninger's vivid storytelling transports readers to a realm of ancient Persia, where honor, loyalty, and the triumph of good over evil reign supreme.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Love, Justice
