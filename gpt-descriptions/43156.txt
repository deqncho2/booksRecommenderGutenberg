Synopsis: In 'Unawares: A Story of an Old French Town' by Frances Mary Peard, a mysterious stranger arrives in a quaint French town, stirring up curiosity and suspicion among the locals. As the townspeople delve into the stranger's past, they uncover secrets that have long been buried, leading to unexpected consequences and revelations. With its atmospheric setting and intriguing characters, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery
