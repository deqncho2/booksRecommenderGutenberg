Synopsis: In "A Soldier's Mother in France," Rheta Childe Dorr takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart-wrenching experiences of a mother whose son is serving in World War I. Through her vivid and emotional storytelling, Dorr explores the challenges, sacrifices, and unwavering love that a soldier's mother endures during a time of war. As she navigates the complexities of war and the impact it has on her family, the protagonist's strength and resilience shine through, making this a compelling and poignant tale.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Motherhood, Sacrifice, Love, War, Resilience
