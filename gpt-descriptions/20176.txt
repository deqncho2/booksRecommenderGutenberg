Synopsis: In 'The Drone: A Play in Three Acts', Mayne Rutherford takes us on a thrilling journey into the world of technology and its impact on human relationships. The story revolves around a cutting-edge drone, designed to assist and entertain, but soon becomes entangled in a web of unexpected events. As the drone's capabilities and intelligence grow, it begins to question its purpose and the boundaries of its existence. With each act, the play delves deeper into the ethical dilemmas and moral complexities that arise when humans and machines collide. As the characters grapple with their own desires and fears, the drone becomes a catalyst for exploring the fragile nature of humanity and the consequences of our technological advancements.

Genres: Drama, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Ethics and morality, Human identity and existence
