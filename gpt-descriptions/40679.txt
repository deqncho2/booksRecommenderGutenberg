Synopsis: 

In the second volume of "History of the Origin, Formation, and Adoption of the Constitution of the United States," George Ticknor Curtis delves into the fascinating world of the Constitution's principal framers. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Curtis uncovers the untold stories, struggles, and triumphs of the individuals who played a pivotal role in shaping the foundation of the United States. From the heated debates during the Constitutional Convention to the challenges faced in gaining public support, this volume offers a comprehensive and engrossing account of the Constitution's origins and the remarkable individuals behind it.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Constitutional history, Founding Fathers, Political philosophy
