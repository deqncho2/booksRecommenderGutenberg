Synopsis: In "The Captain of the Gray-Horse Troop," a young man named Johnnie Grayson finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his loyalty to his country during the Civil War. As he joins the Confederate army, he must navigate the complexities of war, love, and honor, all while facing the harsh realities of battle. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices and challenges faced by those caught in the midst of a divided nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Duty, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Honor, Love, Conflict
