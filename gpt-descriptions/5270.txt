Synopsis: In 'The Film Mystery' by Arthur B. Reeve, a renowned detective and his loyal sidekick find themselves entangled in a perplexing case when a famous film actress mysteriously disappears during the production of a highly anticipated movie. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Film Industry
