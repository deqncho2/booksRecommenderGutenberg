Synopsis: 

In 'Garrison's Finish: A Romance of the Race Course', the reader is transported into the thrilling world of horse racing, where the stakes are high and the competition fierce. The story revolves around the enigmatic character of Garrison, a talented jockey with a troubled past, who finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. As the tension builds on the racecourse, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human character is put to the ultimate test. Will Garrison be able to overcome his demons and emerge victorious, both on and off the track?

Genres: Romance, Sports, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Love, Betrayal, Personal growth
