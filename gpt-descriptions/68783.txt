Synopsis: In "The Swamp Was Upside Down" by Leinster Murray, a mysterious event turns the natural order of a swamp completely topsy-turvy. As the protagonist navigates this bizarre and disorienting world, they uncover hidden secrets and encounter strange creatures that challenge their perception of reality. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigma, eagerly anticipating the resolution of this mind-bending puzzle.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Perception, Reality, Exploration
