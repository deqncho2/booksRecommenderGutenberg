Synopsis: 
In 'Sailing' by Knight, E. F., a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure when she joins a sailing crew. As she navigates the challenges of life at sea, she encounters unexpected friendships, tests her limits, and finds herself drawn to the enigmatic captain. However, the voyage takes an unexpected turn when they stumble upon a mysterious island that holds secrets that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking in the shadows, Emily must summon her courage and unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Courage, Secrets
