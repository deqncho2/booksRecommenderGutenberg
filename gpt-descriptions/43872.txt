Synopsis: 
In 'Billy Whiskers' Travels', follow the thrilling adventures of a mischievous and clever goat named Billy Whiskers. After escaping from his farm, Billy embarks on a series of escapades that take him to unexpected places, from the bustling streets of New York City to the wild and untamed wilderness. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, both human and animal, and finds himself in one daring situation after another. Will Billy ever find his way back home, or will his wanderlust lead him on even more extraordinary journeys?

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Animal Fiction

Themes: Freedom, Curiosity, Friendship
