Synopsis: 

In "Raphael: A Collection of Fifteen Pictures and a Portrait of the Painter with Introduction and Interpretation," Estelle M. Hurll takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of one of the greatest Renaissance painters, Raphael. Through a collection of fifteen stunning pictures and a detailed portrait of the artist, Hurll delves into the life, artistry, and legacy of this iconic figure. From his early works to his masterpieces, readers will be enthralled by the beauty and depth of Raphael's creations, as well as the fascinating stories behind each painting. Hurll's insightful interpretation and analysis provide a deeper understanding of the artist's techniques, influences, and the historical context in which he worked. 

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Renaissance art, artistic techniques, historical context, artistic legacy
