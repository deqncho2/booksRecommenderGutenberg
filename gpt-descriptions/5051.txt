Synopsis: In 'The Morals of Marcus Ordeyne', we are introduced to the enigmatic protagonist, Marcus Ordeyne, a man of great intellect and ambition. As he navigates through the complexities of Victorian society, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and moral dilemmas. From the bustling streets of London to the opulent salons of the elite, Marcus's journey takes him on a path of self-discovery and redemption, where he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this captivating novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: Morality, Redemption, Identity, Society and Class
