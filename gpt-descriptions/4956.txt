Synopsis: In 'The Duke's Prize; a Story of Art and Heart in Florence', the reader is transported to the enchanting city of Florence, where a young artist finds herself caught in a web of love, ambition, and intrigue. As she navigates the vibrant art scene and the aristocratic circles of the city, she becomes entangled with a powerful Duke who holds the key to her dreams. With secrets lurking in the shadows and a forbidden romance blossoming, the protagonist must make choices that will shape her future and challenge her perception of love and art.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Ambition and Sacrifice, Art and Creativity
