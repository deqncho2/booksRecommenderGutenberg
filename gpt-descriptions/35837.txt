Synopsis: 
In "Joan of Arc: A Play in Five Acts" by J. A. Sargant, the captivating story of the legendary French heroine unfolds on the stage. Set against the backdrop of the Hundred Years' War, the play follows the extraordinary journey of Joan of Arc, a young peasant girl who claims to have been chosen by God to lead the French army to victory. As she faces skepticism, betrayal, and the challenges of war, Joan's unwavering faith and indomitable spirit inspire those around her and leave an indelible mark on history. This gripping play explores the complexities of faith, courage, and the power of conviction, making it a must-read for anyone fascinated by the enigmatic figure of Joan of Arc.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith, Heroism, Identity, Power
