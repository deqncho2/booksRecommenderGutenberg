Synopsis: In 'The Cottage of Delight: A Novel', Will N. Harben takes readers on a captivating journey to a small Southern town where secrets, love, and redemption intertwine. When a mysterious stranger arrives at the quaint cottage, the lives of its inhabitants are forever changed. As the past is unraveled and hidden desires are revealed, the characters must confront their own demons and make choices that will shape their futures. With its richly drawn characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists, this novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Love, Redemption, Forgiveness
