Synopsis: 
'A Schoolmaster's Diary: Being Extracts from the Journal of Patrick Traherne, M.A., Sometime Assistant Master at Radchester and Marlton' is a captivating and introspective novel that delves into the personal journal of Patrick Traherne, a former assistant master at two prestigious schools. Through Traherne's diary entries, we are taken on a journey through the challenges, triumphs, and inner thoughts of a dedicated educator. As Traherne navigates the complexities of his profession, he also grapples with his own personal growth, relationships, and the impact he has on the lives of his students. This diary offers a unique and intimate perspective on the world of education and the profound influence it can have on both teachers and students.

Genres: Fiction, Diary, Coming-of-age

Themes: Education, Personal growth, Relationships, Mentorship
