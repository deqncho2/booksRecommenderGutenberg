Synopsis: In 'The Night Operator' by Frank L. Packard, a mysterious and enigmatic night operator finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of history. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate a treacherous world of spies, double-crosses, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, the night operator must rely on his wit, resourcefulness, and a few unlikely allies to survive and protect what he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Identity
