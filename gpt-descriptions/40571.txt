Synopsis: 
In 'The Angel' by Thorne, Guy, a renowned art dealer finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a captivating painting with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the origins of the artwork, he becomes entangled in a world of art forgery, international crime, and a secret society that will stop at nothing to protect their secrets. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic painting and save himself from a fate worse than death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Art forgery, Crime, Secrets and deception
