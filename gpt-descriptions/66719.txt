Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Seventieth, or, the Surrey Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the regiment from 1758 to 1848. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, this historical record unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the Surrey Regiment of Foot. As readers delve into the pages of this enthralling account, they will witness the regiment's involvement in pivotal battles, its encounters with formidable foes, and the unwavering dedication of its soldiers. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the triumphs and tribulations of this esteemed regiment, leaving readers eager to uncover the remarkable legacy it left behind.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Military Strategy, Historical Legacy
