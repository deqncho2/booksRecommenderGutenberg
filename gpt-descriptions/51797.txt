Synopsis: 
In 'To the Highest Bidder' by Florence Morse Kingsley, a young woman named Ruth finds herself caught in a web of deceit and manipulation when she becomes the subject of a high-stakes auction. As the bidding escalates, Ruth's life takes an unexpected turn, forcing her to confront her own strength and resilience. With secrets lurking in every corner, she must navigate a treacherous world where trust is a luxury and survival is the ultimate prize.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics, Identity
