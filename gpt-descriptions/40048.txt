Synopsis:
'The Newcastle Song Book; or, Tyne-Side Songster' takes readers on a delightful journey through the vibrant and eccentric world of Newcastle and its surrounding areas. This collection of comic and satirical songs introduces us to a colorful cast of characters, each with their own unique quirks and stories. From the boisterous dockworkers to the lively pub-goers, the songs vividly depict the manners and customs of the labouring population in this bustling port city. With its witty and humorous verses, this book offers a delightful insight into the everyday lives and experiences of the people of Newcastle and the surrounding neighbourhood.

Genres:
Comic, Satirical, Songbook

Themes:
Labouring population, Eccentric characters, Manners and customs
