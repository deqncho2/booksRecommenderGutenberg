Synopsis: 
"The Note-Book of an Attaché: Seven Months in the War Zone" takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of war. Written by Eric Fisher Wood, an attaché stationed in a war zone, this book offers a firsthand account of the chaos, danger, and human resilience witnessed during seven intense months. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Wood provides a unique perspective on the realities of war, shedding light on the sacrifices made by soldiers and civilians alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: War, Human resilience, Sacrifice
