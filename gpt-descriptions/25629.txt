Synopsis: In 'Postmark Ganymede' by Robert Silverberg, a mysterious postcard from the distant moon of Ganymede arrives on Earth, sparking a global frenzy of curiosity and speculation. As scientists and astronomers scramble to decipher the enigmatic message, a young linguist named Richard Strauss becomes obsessed with unraveling the secrets hidden within the postcard. His quest takes him on a thrilling journey across space and time, leading him to confront the profound mysteries of the universe and the limits of human understanding.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Communication, Human curiosity, Existentialism
