Synopsis: 
'Stevensoniana: Being a Reprint of Various Literary and Pictorial Miscellany Associated with Robert Louis Stevenson, the Man and His Work' is a captivating collection that delves into the life and writings of the renowned author, Robert Louis Stevenson. This anthology brings together a diverse range of literary and pictorial pieces that offer unique insights into Stevenson's world, including personal anecdotes, critical essays, and rare illustrations. From his early days as a struggling writer to his adventures in the South Seas, this compilation provides a fascinating glimpse into the mind of a literary genius. 

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Anthology

Themes: Exploration of the author's life and works, Literary analysis, Artistic inspiration
