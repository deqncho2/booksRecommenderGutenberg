Synopsis: In 'The Flying Death' by Samuel Hopkins Adams, a small town is plagued by a mysterious and deadly illness that strikes without warning. As panic spreads, a young doctor must race against time to uncover the truth behind the outbreak and find a cure before it claims more lives. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping medical thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Medical Fiction

Themes: Disease outbreak, Medical ethics, Heroism
