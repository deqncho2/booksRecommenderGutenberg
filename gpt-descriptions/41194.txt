Synopsis: In "The Speeches (In Full) of the Rt. Hon. W. E. Gladstone, M.P., and William O'Brien, M.P., on Home Rule, Delivered in Parliament, Feb. 16 and 17, 1888," two influential politicians engage in a passionate debate over the contentious issue of Home Rule. William Ewart Gladstone and William O'Brien deliver powerful speeches in the British Parliament, presenting their contrasting perspectives on the matter. As they eloquently argue their cases, tensions rise and the fate of Home Rule hangs in the balance.

Genres: Political discourse, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Home Rule, Nationalism, Political ideology, Parliamentary debate
