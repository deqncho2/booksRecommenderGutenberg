Synopsis: In the Saddle follows the thrilling adventures of a young protagonist who finds himself caught up in a dangerous world of horse racing, secret societies, and hidden treasures. As he navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters mysterious characters, he must rely on his wit and courage to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the suspenseful twists and turns that keep them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Secrets, Pursuit of Truth
