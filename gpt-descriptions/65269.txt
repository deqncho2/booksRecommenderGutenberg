Synopsis: 

In "The Ice Crop: How to Harvest, Store, Ship and Use Ice" by Theron L. Hiles, readers are taken on a fascinating journey into the world of ice harvesting. This comprehensive guide explores the intricate process of gathering, preserving, and utilizing ice, revealing the secrets behind this essential resource. From the techniques employed in cutting and storing ice to the innovative methods of transportation and the myriad of ways ice can be employed, this book offers a captivating glimpse into a world that is both practical and enchanting. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Resource management, Innovation, Industrialization
