Synopsis: In "Children of Destiny" by Molly Elliot Seawell, a group of young individuals find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, love, and ambition as they navigate the complexities of society in the late 19th century. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing America, this captivating novel follows the lives of these children of destiny as they strive to carve their own paths and shape their futures. With unexpected twists and turns, the story explores the power of choice, the pursuit of dreams, and the enduring bonds of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Society, Friendship, Love, Identity
