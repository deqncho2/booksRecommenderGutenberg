Synopsis: In "The Curse of Clifton, or The Widowed Bride," Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth weaves a captivating tale of mystery and romance. Set in the enchanting Clifton Manor, the story follows the life of a young woman named Rosalind, who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and curses. As she navigates through the treacherous paths of love and betrayal, Rosalind must uncover the truth behind the curse that haunts her family and threatens her own happiness. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family curses, Secrets and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
