Synopsis: 

In 'Synopsis of Jewish History: From the Return of the Jews from the Babylonish Captivity, to the Days of Herod the Great', Henry Abraham takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of the Jewish people. From their liberation from Babylonian captivity to the reign of the infamous Herod the Great, this book delves into the trials, triumphs, and transformations that shaped the Jewish nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Jewish history, Biography

Themes: Identity and survival, religious and cultural resilience, political power struggles
