Synopsis: "I Was There" with the Yanks on the Western Front, 1917-1919 takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of Cyrus Leroy Baldridge, an American soldier who served on the Western Front during World War I. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Baldridge provides a firsthand account of the harrowing experiences, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by the American troops during this tumultuous period. From the trenches to the battlefields, from the horrors of war to the moments of resilience and humanity, this memoir offers a gripping and intimate portrayal of the American soldiers' role in the Great War.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: War, Patriotism, Brotherhood, Human Resilience
