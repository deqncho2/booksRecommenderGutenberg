Synopsis: Tom Slade, a resourceful and adventurous young boy, finds himself on an exciting journey to Overlook Mountain. As he explores the rugged terrain, encounters mysterious characters, and uncovers hidden secrets, Tom's courage and determination are put to the test. With danger lurking at every turn, Tom must rely on his wit and bravery to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. Will he be able to unravel the mysteries of Overlook Mountain and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Determination, Exploration
