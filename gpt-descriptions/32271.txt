Synopsis: In 'A Struggle for Rome, v. 1' by Felix Dahn, a young Germanic nobleman named Witichis finds himself caught in the midst of a power struggle for control over Rome during the turbulent 6th century. As he navigates treacherous political alliances, battles against invading armies, and grapples with his own personal demons, Witichis must decide where his loyalties truly lie and what sacrifices he is willing to make for the future of Rome.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Ambition, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
