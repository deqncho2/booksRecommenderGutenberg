Synopsis: 
'Ingersollia: Gems of Thought from the Lectures, Speeches, and Conversations of Col. Robert G. Ingersoll, Representative of His Opinions and Beliefs' is a captivating collection of the most profound and thought-provoking ideas from the brilliant mind of Col. Robert G. Ingersoll. Through his lectures, speeches, and conversations, Ingersoll explores a wide range of topics, challenging conventional beliefs and advocating for reason, science, and humanism. This anthology presents a treasure trove of intellectual gems that will inspire readers to question, reflect, and embrace the power of critical thinking.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Essay

Themes: Reason, Science, Humanism, Critical Thinking
