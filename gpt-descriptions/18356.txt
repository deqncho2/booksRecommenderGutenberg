Synopsis: In 'Orange and Green: A Tale of the Boyne and Limerick', G. A. Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Williamite War in Ireland. Set against the backdrop of the late 17th century, this historical adventure follows the lives of two young friends, Ned and Terence, who find themselves on opposite sides of the conflict. As they navigate the treacherous political landscape, their loyalty, courage, and friendship are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Henty weaves a captivating tale of war, honor, and the enduring power of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, War, Political Conflict
