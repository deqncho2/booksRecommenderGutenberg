Synopsis: In 'The Garden God: A Tale of Two Boys' by Reid, Forrest, two young boys stumble upon a hidden garden that holds a mysterious secret. As they delve deeper into the garden's enchanting beauty, they uncover a world filled with magic, adventure, and unexpected challenges. With each step they take, their friendship is tested, and they must learn to navigate the complexities of trust and loyalty. As they unravel the garden's secrets, they discover that their destinies are intertwined with the fate of this magical realm. Will they be able to unlock the power of the garden and fulfill their roles as the chosen ones? Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery Themes: Friendship, Trust, Destiny
