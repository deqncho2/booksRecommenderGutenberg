Synopsis: 
In the remote coastal town of Port Ticonderoga, a mysterious stranger washes ashore, captivating the curiosity of the locals. As the enigmatic beachcomber becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human connection is explored. With each passing day, the beachcomber's presence brings both hope and turmoil, leaving the residents questioning their own desires and the depths of their own souls.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Human Connection
