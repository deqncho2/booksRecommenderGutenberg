Synopsis: In 'Through the Wall' by Moffett Cleveland, a young woman named Lily moves into a new apartment, only to discover a hidden door in her bedroom wall. Curiosity gets the best of her, and she decides to explore what lies beyond. As she delves deeper into the mysterious world on the other side, Lily uncovers secrets that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront her own fears. With each step she takes, the line between the two worlds blurs, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Perception, Exploration
