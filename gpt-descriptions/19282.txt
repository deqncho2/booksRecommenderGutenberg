Synopsis: In 'The Promised Day Is Come' by Shoghi Effendi, a gripping tale unfolds as the destiny of a global community hangs in the balance. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous world, the story follows the struggles and triumphs of a group of individuals who are united by a common purpose. As they navigate through treacherous waters, facing opposition and uncertainty, they must find the strength to persevere and fulfill their sacred mission. With unexpected twists and turns, this thought-provoking narrative explores the power of faith, the resilience of the human spirit, and the transformative potential of unity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Unity, Resilience, Transformation
