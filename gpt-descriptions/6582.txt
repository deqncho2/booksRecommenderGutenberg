Synopsis: In the Court of King Arthur takes readers on a captivating journey to the legendary realm of Camelot, where the fate of the kingdom hangs in the balance. As the knights of the Round Table face treacherous challenges and embark on daring quests, a young squire named Tom finds himself caught in the midst of a grand adventure. With the fate of Camelot resting on his shoulders, Tom must navigate the complexities of loyalty, honor, and love, all while discovering his own true destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Destiny, Love
