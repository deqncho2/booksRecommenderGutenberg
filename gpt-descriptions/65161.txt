Synopsis: In the mysterious and enchanting world of 'The Forest Giant' by Le Corbeau, Adrien, a young artist, stumbles upon a hidden realm where imagination and reality intertwine. As he delves deeper into this ethereal forest, he encounters extraordinary creatures and uncovers a long-lost secret that could change his life forever. With each step, Adrien must navigate the fine line between dreams and truth, leading him on a captivating journey of self-discovery and the power of the human imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Reality, Self-discovery
