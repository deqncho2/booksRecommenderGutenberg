Synopsis: 

In "Ladies-In-Waiting" by Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin, a group of young women find themselves unexpectedly thrust into the world of royalty when they are chosen to serve as ladies-in-waiting to a prominent queen. As they navigate the intricacies of court life, they soon discover that behind the glamour and opulence lies a web of secrets, rivalries, and hidden agendas. Each lady-in-waiting must learn to navigate the treacherous waters of the court, balancing their own desires and ambitions with the demands of their queen and the expectations of society. As they form unlikely alliances and face unexpected challenges, these women must find the strength within themselves to survive and thrive in a world where power and betrayal go hand in hand.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Friendship and Loyalty, Identity and Self-discovery
