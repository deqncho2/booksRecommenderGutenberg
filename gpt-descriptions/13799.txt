Synopsis: In 'Old Fires and Profitable Ghosts: A Book of Stories', Arthur Quiller-Couch takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From mysterious apparitions to forgotten secrets, each story unravels a unique narrative that will leave readers spellbound. With a blend of suspense, supernatural elements, and unexpected twists, Quiller-Couch masterfully crafts a world where old fires continue to burn and profitable ghosts haunt the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Hauntings, Intrigue
