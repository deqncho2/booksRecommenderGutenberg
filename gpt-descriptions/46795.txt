Synopsis: In 'The Pioneer Boys on the Great Lakes; or, On the Trail of the Iroquois' by Rathborne, St. George, three adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey across the vast and treacherous Great Lakes. As they navigate through dangerous waters, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges, they find themselves entangled in a quest to uncover the secrets of the Iroquois tribe. With their courage and resourcefulness tested at every turn, the boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to unravel the ancient mysteries that lie hidden within the heart of the Great Lakes.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Native American Culture
