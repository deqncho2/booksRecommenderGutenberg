Synopsis: In the small village of Socartes, a young girl named Marianela lives a life of hardship and isolation due to her physical appearance. Despite her circumstances, she possesses a remarkable intelligence and a deep love for the world around her. When she meets a blind young man named Pablo, their lives become intertwined in a complex and emotional journey that challenges societal norms and explores the true nature of beauty and love.

Genres: Realism, Romance, Tragedy

Themes: Beauty and ugliness, Love and sacrifice, Social inequality
