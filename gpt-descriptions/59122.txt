Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Lord Byron' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of the renowned poet, George Gordon Byron, Baron Byron. This anthology encompasses a wide range of genres, including poetry, drama, and prose, showcasing the brilliance and versatility of Lord Byron's writing. From his epic narrative poems like "Don Juan" to his thought-provoking dramas such as "Manfred," this index provides a glimpse into the captivating world of one of the most influential figures in English literature. With each work carefully curated and organized, readers can explore the depth of Lord Byron's imagination and immerse themselves in his captivating storytelling.

Genres: Poetry, Drama, Prose
Themes: Love and passion, Nature and the sublime, Individualism and rebellion
