Synopsis: 
Step into the Museum of Antiquity and embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world. In this remarkable book, Haines and Yaggy transport readers to the realms of ancient civilizations, unveiling the mysteries of their daily lives, customs, and beliefs. From the grandeur of Egypt's pyramids to the awe-inspiring architecture of Greece and Rome, this vividly illustrated work brings history to life, offering a glimpse into the fascinating world of our ancestors.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Daily life, Cultural heritage
