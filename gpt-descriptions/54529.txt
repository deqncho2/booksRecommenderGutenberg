Synopsis: 
In 'Phoebe Daring: A Story for Young Folk', follow the adventures of Phoebe, a spirited young girl with a knack for getting into trouble. When her family moves to a new town, Phoebe finds herself caught up in a mysterious secret that leads her on a thrilling journey filled with danger, friendship, and unexpected discoveries. As she unravels the truth behind the town's hidden past, Phoebe must summon all her courage and resourcefulness to save the day. Will she succeed in her daring quest?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
