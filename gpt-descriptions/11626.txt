Synopsis: In 'The Dawn of All' by Robert Hugh Benson, a mysterious event occurs that transforms the world as we know it. As society grapples with the consequences of this extraordinary phenomenon, a group of individuals find themselves drawn together in a quest for truth and meaning. With elements of mystery, adventure, and spiritual exploration, this captivating novel takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through a world on the brink of a profound transformation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual Fiction

Themes: Transformation, Identity, Faith, Human Potential
