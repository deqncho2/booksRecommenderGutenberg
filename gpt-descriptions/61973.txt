Synopsis: In 'The Giant Fish of Florida' by Turner-Turner, J., a small coastal town in Florida becomes captivated by rumors of a legendary giant fish lurking in the depths of the ocean. As the townspeople embark on a thrilling quest to uncover the truth, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected discoveries. With each twist and turn, the story dives deeper into the secrets of the sea, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Mythology, Community
