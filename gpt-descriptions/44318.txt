Synopsis: In 'The History of Rome, Books 37 to the End: with the Epitomes and Fragments of the Lost Books' by Livy, the captivating tale of Rome's rise and fall reaches its climactic conclusion. As the Roman Empire faces internal strife and external threats, the fate of the mighty civilization hangs in the balance. Livy's vivid narrative takes readers on a journey through epic battles, political intrigue, and the complex dynamics of power. With each page, the reader becomes immersed in the grandeur and turmoil of ancient Rome, eagerly following the lives of its legendary figures and witnessing the pivotal moments that shape the destiny of an empire.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epic, Biography

Themes: Power and politics, War and conquest, Rise and fall of civilizations
