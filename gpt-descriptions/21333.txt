Synopsis: In 'Doom Castle' by Neil Munro, a young man named Alan Glen journeys to a remote Scottish island to uncover the secrets of an ancient castle. As he delves deeper into the castle's dark history, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery, romance, and supernatural occurrences. With each twist and turn, Alan must confront his own fears and unravel the truth behind the castle's haunting past.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of the supernatural, personal transformation
