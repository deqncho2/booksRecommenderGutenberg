Synopsis: In 'A Captive of the Roman Eagles' by Felix Dahn, a young Germanic warrior named Arminius finds himself torn between loyalty to his people and his love for a Roman noblewoman. As he navigates the treacherous world of ancient Rome, Arminius must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of his people and determine his own fate. Filled with political intrigue, forbidden romance, and epic battles, this captivating historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the clash of civilizations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
