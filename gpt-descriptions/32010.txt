Synopsis: In 'A Feast of Demons' by Joseph Samachson, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called to investigate a series of bizarre and seemingly supernatural murders. As he delves deeper into the case, he discovers a hidden world of dark magic and ancient rituals, where demons and humans coexist in a delicate balance. With time running out and the body count rising, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and put an end to the feast of demons.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Occultism, Power and Corruption
