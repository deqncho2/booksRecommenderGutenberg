Synopsis: 
'Tales of the Enchanted Islands of the Atlantic' by Thomas Wentworth Higginson is a captivating collection of mythical stories that transport readers to the mysterious and magical islands scattered across the Atlantic Ocean. From the legendary Atlantis to the enchanting Avalon, these tales weave together ancient folklore, heroic quests, and fantastical creatures, captivating readers with their imaginative and spellbinding narratives. Each story unveils a new island with its own unique wonders and challenges, inviting readers to embark on a journey of discovery and adventure. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: 
Exploration, Mythical Creatures, Heroism
