Synopsis: 
In "In Her Own Right" by John Reed Scott, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from her estranged aunt. As she delves into the history of the estate, Emily uncovers a hidden world of power, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate through a treacherous maze of lies and uncover the truth before it's too late. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Power dynamics
