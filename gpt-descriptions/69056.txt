Synopsis: In 'John de Lancaster: a novel; vol. II.', Richard Cumberland takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of the eponymous protagonist. John de Lancaster, a man of mystery and intrigue, finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates through the treacherous world of politics, love, and power, John must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns of John's life, eagerly anticipating the next revelation that will change everything.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Power, Redemption
