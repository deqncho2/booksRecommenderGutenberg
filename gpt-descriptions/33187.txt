Synopsis: In 'By Right of Conquest: A Novel' by Arthur Hornblow, readers are transported to the vibrant world of New York City in the early 1900s. The story follows the life of Richard Duvall, a young lawyer who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he takes on a seemingly routine case. As he delves deeper into the shadows of the city, Richard uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but also the future of the entire nation. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Power, Justice, Betrayal
