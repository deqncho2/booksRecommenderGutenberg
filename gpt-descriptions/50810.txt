Synopsis: In 'Patience Worth: A Psychic Mystery' by Casper S. Yost, a renowned journalist and skeptic finds himself captivated by the enigmatic Patience Worth, a spirit supposedly communicating through a young woman named Pearl Curran. As Yost delves deeper into the mysterious origins of Patience Worth's messages, he becomes entangled in a web of inexplicable events and unexplained phenomena. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Yost questioning his own beliefs and the true nature of Patience Worth's existence.

Genres: Mystery, Paranormal, Historical Fiction

Themes: Spirit communication, Skepticism, Identity
