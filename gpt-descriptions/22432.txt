Synopsis: In 'Parables of the Christ-life', I. Lilias Trotter takes readers on a captivating journey through the parables of Jesus, exploring their deeper meanings and unveiling profound spiritual truths. Through her vivid storytelling and insightful interpretations, Trotter invites readers to delve into the heart of Christ's teachings, discovering timeless wisdom and transformative lessons that resonate with the human experience. With each parable, she paints a vivid picture that engages the imagination and stirs the soul, leaving readers inspired and challenged to live out the Christ-life in their own unique way.

Genres: Christian literature, spiritual growth, inspirational

Themes: The teachings of Jesus, spiritual transformation, living out one's faith
