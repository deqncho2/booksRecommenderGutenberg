Synopsis: In 'Needler' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Tormey invents a revolutionary device that can alter reality itself. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, Tormey finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between what is real and what is illusion becomes increasingly blurred. As he races against time to reclaim his invention, Tormey must confront his own fears and navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Technology's Impact on Society, Moral Responsibility
