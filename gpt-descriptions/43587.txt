Synopsis: 
'Princeton Stories' by Jesse Lynch Williams takes readers on a captivating journey through the prestigious Princeton University, where a diverse cast of characters navigate the challenges and triumphs of college life. From the intense academic pressures to the complexities of love and friendship, these interconnected stories offer a glimpse into the vibrant world of Princeton, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Education, Ambition, Identity, Relationships
