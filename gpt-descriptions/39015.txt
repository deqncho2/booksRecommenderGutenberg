Synopsis: 

'Ophiolatreia: An Account of the Rites and Mysteries Connected with the Origin, Rise, and Development of Serpent Worship in Various Parts of the World' delves into the intriguing world of serpent worship, exploring its origins, growth, and significance across different cultures. Edward Sellon takes readers on a captivating journey, uncovering the mysteries and rituals associated with this ancient practice. From ancient civilizations to modern societies, this book sheds light on the enigmatic allure of serpent worship and its enduring presence throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Ancient rituals, Cultural practices, Symbolism, Religious beliefs
