Synopsis: In 'Rogue psi' by James H. Schmitz, a mysterious and powerful psychic ability emerges, threatening the delicate balance of power in a futuristic society. As the protagonist delves deeper into the origins of this rogue psi, they uncover a web of secrets and conspiracies that could change the course of humanity forever. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating exploration of psychic powers, 'Rogue psi' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Psychic abilities, Power struggles, Conspiracy
