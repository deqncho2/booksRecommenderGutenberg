Synopsis: In "Spears of Destiny: A Story of the First Capture of Constantinople," Arthur D. Howden Smith takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the 4th century AD, where the fate of an empire hangs in the balance. Amidst the chaos of war, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate treacherous waters, face formidable enemies, and uncover ancient secrets in their quest to capture the legendary city of Constantinople. As the tension rises and the stakes grow higher, readers will be captivated by the gripping tale of bravery, betrayal, and the pursuit of power.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Betrayal, Power
