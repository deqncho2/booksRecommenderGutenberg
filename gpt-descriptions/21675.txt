Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the fascinating world of old advertisements in 'The Olden Time Series, Vol. 4: Quaint and Curious Advertisements: Gleanings Chiefly from Old Newspapers of Boston and Salem, Massachusetts' by Henry M. Brooks. This captivating collection takes readers on a journey through the pages of historical newspapers, uncovering a treasure trove of intriguing and often bizarre advertisements from the past. From peculiar remedies and peculiar services to peculiar products and peculiar requests, these advertisements offer a unique glimpse into the daily lives and interests of people in 18th and 19th century Boston and Salem. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Reference, Cultural History

Themes: Advertising, Social History, Cultural Significance
