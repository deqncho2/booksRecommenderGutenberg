Synopsis: In "The Children's Longfellow: Told in Prose," Henry Wadsworth Longfellow's beloved poems are brought to life through captivating storytelling. This enchanting collection takes readers on a journey through the imaginative world of Longfellow's poetry, introducing them to memorable characters and exploring themes of love, loss, and the power of nature. From the stirring verses of "Paul Revere's Ride" to the haunting beauty of "The Song of Hiawatha," this book offers a delightful and accessible way for young readers to discover the timeless magic of Longfellow's words.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Prose

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Historical Events
