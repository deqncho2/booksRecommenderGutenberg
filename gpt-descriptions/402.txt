Synopsis: 
'Penrod' by Booth Tarkington is a delightful coming-of-age story that follows the misadventures of a mischievous young boy named Penrod Schofield. Set in early 20th century America, Penrod navigates the challenges of school, friendships, and family life with his unique blend of curiosity and mischief. From his hilarious escapades to his encounters with eccentric characters, Penrod's journey is filled with laughter, heartwarming moments, and valuable life lessons.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Humor, Fiction

Themes: 
Childhood, Friendship, Family dynamics
