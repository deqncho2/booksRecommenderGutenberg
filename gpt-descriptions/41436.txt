Synopsis: In 'The Sportswoman's Library, Vol. 1 of 2', embark on a thrilling journey through the world of sports as a young woman defies societal expectations and pursues her passion for athleticism. From horseback riding to archery, she challenges the norms of her time and discovers her own strength and resilience. As she navigates the competitive world of sports, she also faces personal obstacles and learns valuable life lessons along the way. This captivating volume is filled with exhilarating adventures, unexpected twists, and inspiring triumphs that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Gender roles, Determination, Self-discovery
