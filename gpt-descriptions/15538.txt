Synopsis: In 'Hetty Gray; or, Nobody's bairn', we follow the captivating journey of Hetty Gray, a young girl with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. Set in the enchanting landscapes of Ireland, Hetty's life takes unexpected turns as she navigates through love, loss, and the search for her true identity. With secrets waiting to be unraveled and a cast of intriguing characters, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover Hetty's destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Family, Love, Resilience
