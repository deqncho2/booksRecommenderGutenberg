Synopsis: In 'Riches have wings; or, A tale for the rich and poor', Arthur T. S. takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two individuals from contrasting backgrounds. As their paths intertwine, secrets, ambitions, and unexpected twists unfold, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. This thought-provoking tale explores the consequences of wealth, the power of compassion, and the true meaning of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Compassion, Happiness, Social inequality
