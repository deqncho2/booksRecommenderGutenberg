Synopsis: 
'From Paris to Pekin over Siberian Snows' takes readers on an extraordinary adventure as Victor Meignan recounts his daring journey by sledge through the treacherous snows of European Russia and Siberia. From there, he embarks on a thrilling caravan expedition through Mongolia, braving the harsh conditions of the Gobi Desert and encountering the awe-inspiring Great Wall. Finally, Meignan continues his expedition by mule palanquin, traversing the vast landscapes of China until he reaches the captivating city of Pekin. This gripping narrative captures the spirit of exploration and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural Encounter
