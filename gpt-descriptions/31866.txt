Synopsis: In "Hansford: A Tale of Bacon's Rebellion" by Tucker, St. George, a young man named Hansford finds himself caught in the midst of a tumultuous rebellion in colonial Virginia. As the rebellion unfolds, Hansford is faced with difficult choices that will test his loyalty, courage, and ultimately, his own sense of justice. With the backdrop of a society on the brink of chaos, this gripping tale explores the complexities of power, honor, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Rebellion, Loyalty, Justice, Power
