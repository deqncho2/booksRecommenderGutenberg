Synopsis: In "The Canterbury Pilgrims: A Comedy," MacKaye takes us on a hilarious and unexpected journey with a diverse group of pilgrims traveling to Canterbury. From a mischievous innkeeper to a mysterious knight, each character brings their own unique personality and secrets to the table. As they embark on their pilgrimage, their interactions and adventures lead to uproarious situations and surprising revelations. This comedic tale is filled with wit, satire, and a touch of romance, making it a delightful and entertaining read.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes: Human nature, Identity, Morality
