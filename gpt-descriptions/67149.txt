Synopsis: 
"A Child's History of the World" by V.M. Hillyer is a captivating journey through time, taking young readers on an adventure that spans from the earliest civilizations to the modern world. With vivid storytelling and engaging illustrations, this book introduces children to the wonders of history, exploring the lives of great leaders, the rise and fall of empires, and the remarkable achievements of humanity. From ancient Egypt and Greece to the Renaissance and beyond, young readers will be enthralled by the fascinating tales of the past, making history come alive before their eyes.

Genres: 
Historical, Educational, Children's Literature

Themes: 
History, Civilization, Cultural Development, Human Achievements
