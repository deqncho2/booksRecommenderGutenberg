Synopsis: 

In 'With Rogers on the Frontier: A Story of 1756' by J. Macdonald Oxley, readers are transported to the perilous and untamed American frontier during the French and Indian War. The story follows the adventures of a young and courageous protagonist who joins the legendary Major Robert Rogers and his Rangers on a dangerous mission. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes, face hostile Native American tribes, and engage in intense battles, the protagonist's loyalty, bravery, and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale immerses readers in a thrilling historical adventure that will keep them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Exploration
