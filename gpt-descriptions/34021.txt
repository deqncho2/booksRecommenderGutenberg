Synopsis: In 'Small Souls' by Louis Couperus, a seemingly idyllic family is torn apart by secrets, desires, and the pursuit of happiness. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows the lives of three siblings and their complex relationships with each other and the world around them. As their individual desires clash with societal expectations, the family's facade begins to crumble, revealing the hidden depths of their small souls.

Genres: Literary fiction, family drama, psychological fiction

Themes: Family dynamics, societal expectations, individual desires, secrets and lies
