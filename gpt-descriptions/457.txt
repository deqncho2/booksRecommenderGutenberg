Synopsis: In 'The Price She Paid' by David Graham Phillips, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself trapped in a loveless marriage with a wealthy and powerful man. As she navigates the complexities of her oppressive life, she discovers a shocking secret that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With her courage and determination, Evelyn must make a choice that will forever change her destiny and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, power and corruption
