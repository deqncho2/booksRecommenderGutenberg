Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of the Civil War," Cora Mitchel takes readers on a captivating journey through her personal experiences during one of the most tumultuous periods in American history. From her unique perspective as a woman living in the midst of the Civil War, Cora shares her encounters with soldiers, her involvement in secret missions, and the challenges she faced as she navigated the complexities of love and loyalty. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt emotions, this memoir offers a compelling glimpse into the lives of those who lived through this defining era.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Courage and Resilience, Gender Roles and Identity
