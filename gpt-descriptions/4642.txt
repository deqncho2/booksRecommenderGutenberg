Synopsis: In the midst of the tumultuous Gold Rush era, a small town in California becomes a hotbed of corruption and lawlessness. When a series of brutal crimes go unpunished, a group of ordinary citizens forms a secret vigilante committee to restore justice and protect their community. As they delve deeper into the dark underbelly of their town, they uncover shocking secrets and face dangerous adversaries, testing their resolve and challenging their own moral compasses. With lives on the line and the fate of their town hanging in the balance, the members of the Vigilance Committee of 1856 must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices in their pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Vigilantism, Corruption, Morality, Justice
