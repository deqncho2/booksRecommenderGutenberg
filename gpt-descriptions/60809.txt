Synopsis: In the near future, a world devastated by economic collapse and overpopulation, a group of desperate individuals embark on a dangerous journey to find a mythical place called Gravy Train. This elusive destination promises a life of abundance and prosperity, but as they venture deeper into the unknown, they soon discover that the price of admission may be higher than they ever imagined.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Adventure

Themes: Economic disparity, Survival, Hope, Human nature
