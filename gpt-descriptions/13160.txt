Synopsis: In 'An Iron Will', Orison Swett Marden and Abner Bayley explore the power of determination and resilience through a collection of inspiring stories and practical advice. From tales of ordinary individuals overcoming extraordinary challenges to insights on how to cultivate a strong will, this book offers a compelling exploration of the human spirit and its ability to triumph over adversity.

Genres: Self-help, Motivational, Inspirational

Themes: Determination, Resilience, Overcoming adversity
