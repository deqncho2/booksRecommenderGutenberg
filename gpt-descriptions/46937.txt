Synopsis: In 'A Book of Cornwall', Baring-Gould takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of Cornwall. From ancient legends and folklore to tales of smugglers and shipwrecks, this book is a treasure trove of fascinating stories that will transport readers to a world of mystery and adventure. With vivid descriptions and a deep love for the region, Baring-Gould paints a vivid picture of Cornwall's unique charm and its enduring allure.

Genres: Travel, History, Folklore

Themes: Mythology, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
