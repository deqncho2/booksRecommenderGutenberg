Synopsis: In 'Folly Corner' by Henry Dudeney, Mrs., a young woman named Mary inherits a mysterious property known as Folly Corner. As she delves into the history of the house, she uncovers a series of secrets and hidden treasures that have been concealed for generations. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Mary embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind Folly Corner and its intriguing past. As she unravels the mysteries, Mary finds herself questioning her own identity and the choices she has made in her life. Will she be able to reconcile the past with the present and find her true path forward?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Heritage, Self-discovery
