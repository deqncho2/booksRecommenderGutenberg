Synopsis: In "The Coming of the White Men: Stories of How Our Country Was Discovered," Mary Hazelton Blanchard Wade takes readers on a captivating journey through the early exploration and discovery of America. Through a collection of fascinating stories, readers will witness the encounters between Native Americans and the first European explorers, the challenges they faced, and the profound impact these encounters had on both cultures. From the arrival of Christopher Columbus to the adventures of Lewis and Clark, this book offers a vivid and engaging account of the pivotal moments that shaped the history of our country.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Cultural exploration

Themes: Exploration, Encounter of cultures, Historical significance
