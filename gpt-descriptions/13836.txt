Synopsis: In 'Wide Courses' by James B. Connolly, a young man named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the American West. As he navigates through various challenges and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Jack discovers hidden truths about himself and the world around him. With danger lurking at every turn, he must summon courage and resilience to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover Jack's fate.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Survival
