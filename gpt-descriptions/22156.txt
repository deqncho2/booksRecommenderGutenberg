Synopsis: In 'The Boy with the U. S. Weather Men' by Rolt-Wheeler, Francis, follow the thrilling journey of a young boy who becomes an integral part of the U.S. Weather Bureau. As he embarks on daring adventures, facing treacherous storms and uncovering hidden secrets, he discovers the power of meteorology and its impact on the world. With his determination and passion, he strives to make a difference in understanding and predicting the weather, all while navigating the challenges of his own personal growth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Weather, Science, Courage, Personal growth
