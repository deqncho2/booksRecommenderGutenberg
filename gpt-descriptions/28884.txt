Synopsis: 

'A History of England from Early Times: A Linked Index to the Project Gutenberg Editions' is a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the rich history of England. Written by a team of esteemed authors including Smollett, Hume, Farr, and Nolan, this work takes readers on a captivating journey through the various periods and events that have shaped the nation.

From the ancient times of the Celts and Romans to the Norman Conquest, the Tudor dynasty, the English Civil War, and beyond, this book delves into the political, social, and cultural developments that have influenced England's trajectory. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the key figures, battles, and transformations that have defined the country.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: English history, political developments, cultural transformations, influential figures, societal changes
