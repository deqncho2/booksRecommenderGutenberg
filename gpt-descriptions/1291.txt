Synopsis: In the ancient kingdom of Judea, a tale of power, desire, and revenge unfolds. 'Herodias' follows the story of Herod Antipas, the ruler of Galilee, and his wife, Herodias, as they navigate the treacherous world of politics and personal ambition. When a prophet named John the Baptist challenges their authority and condemns their actions, the couple finds themselves torn between their own desires and the consequences of their actions. As tensions rise and the kingdom teeters on the brink of chaos, Herodias must make a fateful decision that will forever alter the course of their lives.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Power and corruption, Desire and temptation, Morality and conscience
