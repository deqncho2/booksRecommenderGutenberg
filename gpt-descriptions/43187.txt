Synopsis: In 'The Harlequin Opal: A Romance. Vol. 1 (of 3)' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious opal with a captivating allure becomes the center of a thrilling tale. As the opal changes hands, it brings both fortune and misfortune to those who possess it, leading to unexpected twists and turns. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this enthralling romance explores the power of desire, the consequences of greed, and the lengths people will go to obtain what they desire most.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Desire, Greed, Fate
