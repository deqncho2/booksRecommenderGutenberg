Synopsis: In the final volume of "History of Egypt From 330 B.C. To the Present Time," A. S. Rappoport takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that shaped Egypt from ancient times to the modern era. From the rise and fall of powerful dynasties to the influence of foreign powers, this volume delves into the rich tapestry of Egypt's history, exploring the political, cultural, and social developments that have defined the nation. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Rappoport paints a vivid picture of Egypt's past, offering readers a comprehensive understanding of this fascinating civilization.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural and social developments, Influence of foreign powers
