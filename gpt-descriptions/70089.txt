Synopsis: In 'Drifted Ashore; or, A Child Without a Name' by Evelyn Everett-Green, a mysterious child is found washed up on the shores of a small coastal village. With no memory of who she is or where she came from, the child is taken in by a kind-hearted family who name her Hope. As she grows up, Hope's presence in the village begins to unravel long-held secrets and ignite a series of unexpected events. With the community divided and tensions rising, Hope must navigate her own identity while uncovering the truth about her past and the village's hidden history.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Community
