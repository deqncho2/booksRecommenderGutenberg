Synopsis: In "Jerry of the Islands" by Jack London, a young dog named Jerry finds himself torn between his loyalty to his human family and his instinctual connection to his wild ancestors. When circumstances force Jerry to leave his comfortable home and venture into the untamed world of the South Seas, he must navigate the treacherous waters of survival, love, and self-discovery. As Jerry encounters new challenges and forms unexpected alliances, he must confront the age-old question of where he truly belongs.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Nature vs. Nurture
