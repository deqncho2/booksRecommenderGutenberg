Synopsis: 
In 'The Foolish Lovers' by St. John G. Ervine, two young lovers find themselves entangled in a web of misunderstandings and miscommunications. As their relationship faces numerous obstacles, they must navigate through societal expectations, family pressures, and their own insecurities. With a cast of colorful characters and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love and the consequences of foolish decisions.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, Miscommunication, Society and expectations
