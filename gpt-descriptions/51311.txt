Synopsis: In 'Make Me An Offer' by Blomberg, Con, a struggling artist named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she receives a mysterious offer that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the world of high-stakes art auctions and dangerous alliances, Lily must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems. With her dreams and relationships hanging in the balance, she must decide how far she is willing to go to secure her future.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Identity, Art
