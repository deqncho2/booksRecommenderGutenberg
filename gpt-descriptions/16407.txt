Synopsis: 
'Under the Dragon Flag: My Experiences in the Chino-Japanese War' by James Allan takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous Chino-Japanese War. Through the eyes of the author, we witness the horrors and triumphs of war as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of China and Japan. From intense battles to unexpected alliances, Allan's personal account offers a unique perspective on the conflict that shaped the region. As the war unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of bravery, sacrifice, and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Conflict and its consequences, Loyalty and betrayal, Resilience and survival
