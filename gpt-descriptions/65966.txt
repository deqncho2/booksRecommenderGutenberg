Synopsis: 
'Roundabout to Moscow: An Epicurean Journey' follows the captivating adventures of John Bell Bouton as he embarks on a culinary expedition across Europe and Asia. From the bustling streets of Paris to the enchanting landscapes of Russia, Bouton's journey takes him through a tapestry of cultures, flavors, and encounters with fascinating individuals. As he indulges in delectable dishes and explores the intricacies of each region's cuisine, Bouton discovers that food is not only a means of sustenance but also a gateway to understanding the world and oneself. With vivid descriptions and humorous anecdotes, this travel memoir invites readers to join Bouton on a mouthwatering odyssey that transcends borders and tantalizes the senses.

Genres: Travel memoir, Food writing, Adventure

Themes: Culinary exploration, Cultural immersion, Self-discovery
