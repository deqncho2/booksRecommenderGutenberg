Synopsis: In 'Under a Charm: A Novel. Vol. II' by Werner, E., the captivating story continues as secrets unravel and forbidden desires ignite. Set in a world of opulence and intrigue, the lives of two star-crossed lovers intertwine amidst a backdrop of political turmoil and societal expectations. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love and loyalty, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever alter their destinies. With each page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth and discover the ultimate price of passion.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Power dynamics, Identity
