Synopsis: In 'Stand Fast, Craig-Royston! (Volume III)' by William Black, the reader is transported to the picturesque Scottish Highlands, where the lives of the Craig-Royston family are about to be turned upside down. As the family faces unexpected challenges and secrets from the past resurface, they must navigate through love, betrayal, and the pursuit of their dreams. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking landscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Family Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Secrets and revelations, Pursuit of happiness, Scottish culture and heritage
