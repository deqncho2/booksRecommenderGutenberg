Synopsis: 
In the enchanting world of 18th-century France, 'The Queen Pedauque' takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of a secret society of winegrowers, a mysterious abbey, and a young boy named Gontran. As Gontran becomes entangled in a web of hidden family secrets, forbidden love, and ancient rituals, he discovers that his destiny is intricately linked to the mystical Queen Pedauque. With its rich historical backdrop and a touch of the supernatural, this novel weaves a tale of intrigue, passion, and the pursuit of identity that will keep readers spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Identity, Tradition and rituals
