Synopsis: 
In 'Rejected Addresses; Or, The New Theatrum Poetarum', the Smith brothers take readers on a hilarious journey through the world of poetry and the theater. Through a collection of witty and satirical poems, they cleverly parody the works of famous poets and playwrights, offering a fresh and comical perspective on the literary world. From Shakespeare to Wordsworth, no poet is safe from their humorous pen. With their sharp wit and clever wordplay, the Smith brothers deliver a delightful and entertaining read that will leave readers laughing out loud.

Genres: Satire, Poetry, Humor

Themes: Parody, Literature, Comedy
