Synopsis: 
In 'The Passenger' by Kenneth Harmon, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger boards a train, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of reality and test the limits of human perception. As the passengers find themselves trapped in a surreal and ever-shifting landscape, they must confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately questioning the nature of their own existence. With each twist and turn, the line between dreams and reality blurs, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the truth behind this mind-bending journey.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
