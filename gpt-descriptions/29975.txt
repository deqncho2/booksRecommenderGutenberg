Synopsis: In 'One Martian Afternoon' by Tom Leahy, a group of astronauts embarks on a mission to Mars, but what they encounter on the red planet is beyond their wildest imagination. As they explore the mysterious landscape, they stumble upon a hidden civilization with secrets that could change the course of human history. With danger lurking at every turn, the astronauts must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and make it back home alive.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Survival
