Synopsis: In 'The False Gods' by George Horace Lorimer, a young and ambitious lawyer named John Graham finds himself caught in a web of deceit and corruption as he navigates the treacherous world of high society in New York City. When he is offered a chance to join an exclusive club known as the "False Gods," John must decide how far he is willing to go to achieve success and whether he can maintain his integrity in a world where power and influence reign supreme. As he delves deeper into this secretive society, John uncovers shocking secrets and faces moral dilemmas that will test his character and challenge his beliefs. Will he succumb to the allure of wealth and power, or will he find the strength to resist the temptations that surround him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Corruption, Morality, Ambition, Power, Society
