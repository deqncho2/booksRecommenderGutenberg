Synopsis: 

"The Fortunate Foundlings" is a captivating novel that follows the extraordinary lives of Colonel M——Rs and his sister, Madam Du P——Y. Born into a noble family, they embark on a series of thrilling adventures during their travels across Europe. Along the way, they encounter a diverse cast of characters, including individuals of high social standing, and become entangled in their intriguing stories. This tale is filled with remarkable incidents that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as the siblings navigate through the most elegant courts of Europe.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Social class, Family, Identity, Love and Relationships
