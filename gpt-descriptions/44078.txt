Synopsis: In "The Trail Boys of the Plains; Or, The Hunt for the Big Buffalo" by Jay Winthrop Allen, a group of adventurous young boys embarks on a thrilling journey across the vast plains in search of the legendary Big Buffalo. As they face numerous challenges and dangers, their bond is tested, and they discover the true meaning of courage and friendship. Will they be able to conquer the wild frontier and find the elusive creature they seek?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
