Synopsis: The Outdoor Girls embark on a thrilling adventure as they head to sunny Florida for the winter. Amidst the beautiful landscapes and exotic wildlife, they find themselves caught up in a mysterious series of events that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their friendship and determination, the girls must unravel the secrets of the Sunshine State and overcome unexpected challenges that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Resilience
