Synopsis: 
'Out-of-Doors in the Holy Land: Impressions of Travel in Body and Spirit' takes readers on a captivating journey through the awe-inspiring landscapes and sacred sites of the Holy Land. Renowned author Henry Van Dyke shares his personal experiences and reflections, immersing readers in the vibrant culture, rich history, and profound spirituality of this ancient land. From the bustling streets of Jerusalem to the serene shores of the Sea of Galilee, Van Dyke's vivid descriptions and heartfelt observations paint a vivid picture of the Holy Land's beauty and significance. Through his encounters with locals, encounters with nature, and encounters with his own spirituality, Van Dyke invites readers to embark on a transformative pilgrimage of their own.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Spirituality

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Spirituality, Transformation
