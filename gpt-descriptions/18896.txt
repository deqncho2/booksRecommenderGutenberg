Synopsis:
In 'Faith Gartney's Girlhood', we follow the journey of Faith Gartney, a young girl navigating the challenges and joys of growing up in the mid-19th century. From her idyllic childhood in a small New England town to her experiences in boarding school and beyond, Faith's story is one of self-discovery, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams. As she encounters various trials and tribulations, Faith must learn to navigate societal expectations, find her own voice, and ultimately discover what it means to be a woman in a changing world.

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Women's Literature

Themes:
Self-discovery, Friendship, Gender roles, Society's expectations
