Synopsis: 
"Cornell Nature-Study Leaflets" is a collection of carefully selected and revised educational materials from various publications by the College of Agriculture at Cornell University. Spanning from 1896 to 1904, this compilation offers a wealth of knowledge on nature-study, providing valuable insights into the wonders of the natural world. From lessons on plants and animals to practical advice on gardening and farming, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both teachers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Educational, Nature, Reference

Themes: Nature appreciation, Environmental education, Agricultural practices
