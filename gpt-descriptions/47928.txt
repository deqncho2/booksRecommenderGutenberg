Synopsis: In 'Time Telling through the Ages' by Brearley, Harry Chase, embark on a captivating journey through history as the author unravels the fascinating evolution of timekeeping. From ancient sundials to modern atomic clocks, this book explores the ingenious inventions and cultural significance of time measurement across civilizations. Discover the secrets behind the development of clocks, watches, and other timepieces, and how they have shaped our perception of time. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this book will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the intricate tapestry of time.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Timekeeping, Invention, Cultural Significance
