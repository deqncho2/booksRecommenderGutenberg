Synopsis: In "The Automobile Girls at Palm Beach; Or, Proving Their Mettle Under Southern Skies," Laura Dent Crane takes readers on an exciting journey with the adventurous and spirited Automobile Girls. As they embark on a trip to Palm Beach, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that tests their courage and determination. With their trusty automobiles as their allies, the girls navigate through the glamour and intrigue of Palm Beach society, uncovering secrets and proving their mettle in the face of danger. Will they be able to solve the mystery and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Independence, Female Empowerment
