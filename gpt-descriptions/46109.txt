Synopsis: 
'Gudrun: A Mediaeval Epic' takes readers on a captivating journey through a world of knights, chivalry, and courtly love. Set in the Middle Ages, this epic tale follows the life of Gudrun, a strong-willed and courageous young woman who defies societal expectations to forge her own path. From her humble beginnings to her encounters with powerful rulers and dangerous adversaries, Gudrun's story is one of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of personal freedom. As she navigates the complexities of medieval society, Gudrun's resilience and determination will inspire readers to question the limitations placed upon them by their own time and place.

Genres: Medieval literature, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Love and desire, Individualism, Social expectations
