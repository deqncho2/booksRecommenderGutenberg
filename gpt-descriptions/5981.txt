Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts in Front of Warsaw; Or, In the Wake of War', Durston takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside a group of brave Boy Scouts who find themselves caught in the midst of the chaos and destruction of war. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of Warsaw, their loyalty, resourcefulness, and determination are put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, the Boy Scouts must rely on their training and camaraderie to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they be able to fulfill their mission and make it out alive?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
