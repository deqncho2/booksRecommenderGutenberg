Synopsis: In 'Inventions of the Great War', A. Russell Bond takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable technological advancements that emerged during World War I. From the ingenious inventions that revolutionized warfare to the unexpected innovations that transformed everyday life, this book unveils the incredible ingenuity and resourcefulness of individuals amidst the chaos of war. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Bond explores the fascinating world of wartime inventions, shedding light on the profound impact they had on the course of history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Technological advancements, Innovation, Human ingenuity, Impact of war
