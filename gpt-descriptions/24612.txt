Synopsis: In "The Monitor and the Merrimac: Both Sides of the Story," authors Greene, Ramsay, Watson, and Worden delve into the captivating tale of the historic naval battle between the ironclad warships, the USS Monitor and the CSS Virginia (formerly the USS Merrimac). Through a combination of firsthand accounts, expert analysis, and meticulous research, this book offers a comprehensive and riveting exploration of the events leading up to, during, and after this groundbreaking clash of iron giants. From the innovative design of the ships to the bravery and skill of the sailors involved, the authors bring to life the dramatic and pivotal moments of this legendary battle, shedding light on the impact it had on naval warfare and the course of the American Civil War.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Technological advancements in warfare, Bravery and heroism in battle, Impact of naval warfare on the American Civil War
