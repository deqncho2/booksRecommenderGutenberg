Synopsis: In "The New Christianity; or, The Religion of the New Age" by Bland, Salem Goldworth, a young woman named Emily embarks on a spiritual journey that challenges her beliefs and leads her to question the traditional teachings of Christianity. As she delves into the teachings of the New Age movement, she encounters a charismatic leader who promises a new understanding of spirituality and a path to personal enlightenment. Caught between her upbringing and the allure of this new religion, Emily must navigate the complexities of faith, doubt, and the search for meaning in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Religious exploration, Faith and doubt, Personal enlightenment, Tradition versus modernity
