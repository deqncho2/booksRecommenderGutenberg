Synopsis: In 'A New Astronomy', David P. Todd takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast expanse of the universe, unveiling groundbreaking discoveries and challenging conventional wisdom. From the mysteries of distant galaxies to the enigmatic nature of black holes, Todd's exploration of the cosmos will leave readers in awe of the wonders that lie beyond our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Exploration, Scientific discovery, Wonder of the universe
