Synopsis: In 'Stories From Livy', Alfred John Church takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Rome, bringing to life the legendary tales and historical events chronicled by the renowned historian Livy. From the founding of Rome by Romulus and Remus to the rise and fall of emperors, this collection of stories offers a thrilling glimpse into the rich tapestry of Roman history. Through vivid storytelling and vivid descriptions, Church transports readers to a world of epic battles, political intrigue, and larger-than-life characters, all while shedding light on the values, customs, and beliefs of ancient Rome. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Heroism, Fate and Destiny
