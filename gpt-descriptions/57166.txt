Synopsis: 
In 'Just a Girl' by Charles Garvice, a young woman named Maud is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a large fortune. As she navigates the complexities of her newfound wealth, Maud finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and unexpected danger. With her life hanging in the balance, Maud must uncover the truth and fight for her happiness against all odds.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Love and sacrifice
