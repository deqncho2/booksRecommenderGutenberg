Synopsis: 

'History of the Seventy-Second Regiment of the North Carolina Troops in the War Between the States, 1861-'65' by John Wetmore Hinsdale is a captivating account that delves into the experiences and struggles of a regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Hinsdale brings to life the trials and triumphs of the soldiers who fought under the banner of the Seventy-Second Regiment. From the initial fervor of enlistment to the harrowing battles and the toll of war on both body and spirit, this book offers a compelling narrative that sheds light on the sacrifices made by these brave men.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The human cost of war
