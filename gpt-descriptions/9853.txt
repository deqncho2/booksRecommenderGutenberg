Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Four Fingers' by Fred M. White, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious criminal organization known as the Four Fingers emerges. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, he must race against time to unravel the truth and bring the Four Fingers to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Justice
