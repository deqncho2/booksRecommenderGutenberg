Synopsis: In 'Moon of Treason' by Robert Emmett McDowell, a brilliant scientist finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and betrayal when he stumbles upon a shocking secret hidden within the moon. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate a dangerous world of espionage, double-crossing agents, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must decide who to trust and how far he is willing to go to protect the truth.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Trust, Scientific Discovery
