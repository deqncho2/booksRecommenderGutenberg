Synopsis: 
In 'The Poet' by Nicholson, Meredith, a renowned poet finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic message that leads her on a thrilling journey. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden truths that threaten to unravel her world. With her poetic prowess as her only weapon, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Poetry

Themes: Identity, Artistic Expression, Deception
