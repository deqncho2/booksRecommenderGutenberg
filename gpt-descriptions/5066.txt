Synopsis: In 'The Whole Family: a Novel by Twelve Authors', twelve renowned authors collaborate to create a captivating story that delves into the lives of the members of the Quicksilver family. Each author takes on the perspective of a different character, providing a unique and intimate glimpse into their thoughts, desires, and struggles. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the complexities of family dynamics are explored. With a diverse cast of characters and a rich tapestry of storytelling styles, this novel offers a compelling and immersive reading experience.

Genres: Fiction, Family Drama, Literary Fiction

Themes: Family, Identity, Secrets, Relationships, Social Class, Gender Roles
