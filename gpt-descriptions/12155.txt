Synopsis: In 'Stepping Backwards: Night Watches, Part 5', the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark and mysterious streets of London. As the protagonist, a night watchman, encounters a series of peculiar and unsettling events, the line between reality and imagination becomes blurred. With each passing night, the watchman's perception of the world around him is challenged, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning their own senses.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Identity
