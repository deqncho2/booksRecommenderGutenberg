Synopsis: In 'The Story of Milk' by Frederiksen, Johan Ditlev, a mysterious dairy farmer named Lars embarks on a journey to uncover the secret behind his family's prized milk. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic history of his ancestors, Lars discovers a hidden world filled with ancient rituals, supernatural beings, and a profound connection between milk and the human soul. With each revelation, Lars becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing him to confront his own identity and the true power of milk.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Family Legacy, Supernatural Forces
