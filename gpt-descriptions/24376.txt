Synopsis: 
In "Floyd Grandon's Honor" by Amanda M. Douglas, readers are introduced to Floyd Grandon, a young man who finds himself caught between duty and love. As he navigates the challenges of his personal and professional life, Floyd must make difficult choices that will test his integrity and honor. With secrets, unexpected twists, and a blossoming romance, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth and discover the ultimate fate of Floyd Grandon.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Honor, Duty, Love, Integrity
