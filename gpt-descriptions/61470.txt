Synopsis: In the second volume of "Life of Edwin Forrest, the American Tragedian," the captivating story of the renowned actor continues to unfold. As Edwin Forrest navigates the highs and lows of his career, he faces numerous challenges, both on and off the stage. From his intense rivalry with another prominent actor to his tumultuous love affairs, Forrest's life is filled with drama and passion. This volume delves deeper into the complexities of his personal life, shedding light on the sacrifices and triumphs that shaped him into a legendary figure of the American theater.

Genres: Biography, Drama, Historical

Themes: Ambition, Love and Betrayal, Artistic Expression
