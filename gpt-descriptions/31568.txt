Synopsis: In 'Walladmor, Vol. 2', the captivating story continues as the English translation of Sir Walter Scott's work is now translated back into English from the German version by Alexis, Willibald. Set against the backdrop of political turmoil and war, this volume delves deeper into the lives of the intriguing characters and their intertwining destinies. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties tested, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Political upheaval, Loyalty, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
