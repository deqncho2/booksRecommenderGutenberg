Synopsis: In 'Danger in the Void' by Charles E. Fritch, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to explore the uncharted depths of space. As they venture further into the unknown, they encounter a series of inexplicable phenomena that threaten their very existence. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must unravel the mysteries of the void before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, the Unknown
