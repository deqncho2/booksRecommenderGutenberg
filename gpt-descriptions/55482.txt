Synopsis: In 'Machines at Work' by Mary Elting, readers are taken on an exciting journey through the bustling world of construction sites. From towering cranes to powerful bulldozers, this captivating book introduces young readers to the fascinating world of machines and the important roles they play in building our cities. With vibrant illustrations and engaging text, 'Machines at Work' is sure to captivate the imagination of children and adults alike, as they discover the incredible power and precision of these mighty machines.

Genres: Children's Literature, Non-fiction

Themes: Construction, Machinery, Engineering
