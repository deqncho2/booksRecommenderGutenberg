Synopsis: In 'The Cinder Pond' by Rankin, Carroll Watson, a young boy named Jeremy is haunted by a tragic accident that occurred in his family. Seeking solace and redemption, he discovers an unexpected friendship with a mysterious old man who lives by the cinder pond. As Jeremy delves into the secrets of the pond, he embarks on a transformative journey that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Friendship, Healing, Overcoming tragedy
