Synopsis: 
In 'Winnie Childs, the Shop Girl', we follow the journey of Winnie, a young and ambitious shop girl who dreams of a better life. As she navigates the challenges of working in a bustling department store, she encounters unexpected friendships, romantic entanglements, and a world of secrets. With determination and resilience, Winnie strives to overcome the obstacles that stand in her way, all while discovering her own strength and identity. This captivating tale takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling world of early 20th-century retail, where dreams and aspirations collide with the realities of class and society.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Social Class, Identity, Friendship
