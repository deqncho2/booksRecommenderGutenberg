Synopsis: 
In "The Rubáiyát of a Bachelor," the protagonist takes us on a captivating journey through the highs and lows of his bachelorhood. With wit and charm, he shares his experiences, observations, and reflections on love, relationships, and the pursuit of happiness. From the exhilarating moments of romance to the bitter taste of heartbreak, this poetic narrative explores the complexities of modern love and the eternal quest for fulfillment.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Relationships, Self-discovery, Happiness
