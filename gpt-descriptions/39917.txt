Synopsis: 
In 'Narrative of an Expedition to the Shores of the Arctic Sea in 1846 and 1847', John Rae takes readers on a thrilling journey to the treacherous Arctic region. As he recounts his experiences and encounters with the indigenous Inuit people, Rae uncovers a shocking truth that challenges the prevailing beliefs of his time. With danger lurking at every turn, this gripping narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the secrets hidden within the frozen wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Challenging conventional wisdom
