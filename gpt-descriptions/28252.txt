Synopsis: 

In 'Violin Making: 'The Strad' Library, No. IX.', author Walter H. Mayson takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of crafting violins. Through detailed descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Mayson unveils the secrets behind the creation of these exquisite instruments, delving into the artistry, craftsmanship, and dedication required to produce a truly exceptional violin. From the selection of the finest materials to the delicate process of shaping and varnishing, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the meticulous work of violin makers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Crafts

Themes: Artistry, Craftsmanship, Dedication
