Synopsis: In 'A Diplomat's Memoir of 1870', Frederic Reitlinger takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Franco-Prussian War. As a diplomat stationed in Paris during the siege, Reitlinger finds himself trapped in the city, but with an audacious plan in mind: to escape using a hot air balloon. His daring escape leads him on a political mission to London and Vienna, where he becomes embroiled in high-stakes negotiations and encounters a cast of intriguing characters. This gripping memoir offers a unique perspective on the war and its aftermath, blending adventure, diplomacy, and historical insight.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War and Conflict, Diplomacy, Survival, Political Intrigue
