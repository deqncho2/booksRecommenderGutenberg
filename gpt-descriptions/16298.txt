Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of American history with 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 01 (of 15), American (1)' by Charles Morris. This first volume of the series takes readers on a thrilling journey through the early days of America, exploring the fascinating stories and events that shaped the nation.

From the daring adventures of early explorers to the struggles and triumphs of the American Revolution, this book brings to life the remarkable individuals and pivotal moments that defined the birth of a nation. Discover the untold tales of courageous pioneers, visionary leaders, and ordinary people who played extraordinary roles in shaping the American identity.

With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Charles Morris paints a vivid picture of the challenges and triumphs that marked America's early years. From the founding of Jamestown to the signing of the Declaration of Independence, this volume is a captivating exploration of the people and events that laid the foundation for the United States.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: American history, Exploration, Revolution, Nation-building
