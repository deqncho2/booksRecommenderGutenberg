Synopsis: In "The Moving Picture Boys at Panama; Or, Stirring Adventures Along the Great Canal," the young and adventurous protagonists find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the Panama Canal. Armed with their trusty cameras, the boys capture not only the awe-inspiring engineering marvels of the canal but also stumble upon a series of unexpected and dangerous encounters. As they navigate through treacherous jungles, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover hidden secrets, the boys must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance, Discovery
