Synopsis: In 'What Shall It Profit?' by Anderson, Poul, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John McEvoy makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, his invention falls into the wrong hands, leading to a thrilling chase across continents and a race against time to prevent a catastrophic event. As McEvoy fights to protect his creation, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and question the true nature of progress and its consequences.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ethics, Technology, Power, Morality
