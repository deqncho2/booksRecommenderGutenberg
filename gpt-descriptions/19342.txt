Synopsis: 
In 'Complete Hypnotism, Mesmerism, Mind-Reading and Spiritualism: How to Hypnotize', Alpheus, A. delves into the fascinating world of hypnotism, mesmerism, mind-reading, and spiritualism. This exhaustive and practical guide provides readers with a comprehensive system of methods, applications, and uses for these intriguing practices. From learning how to hypnotize others to exploring the depths of the human mind, this book offers a captivating journey into the realm of the extraordinary. With its detailed instructions and real-life examples, readers will be enthralled by the possibilities that lie within the power of the mind.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Self-help

Themes: Hypnotism, Mind control, Psychic abilities, Spiritual exploration
