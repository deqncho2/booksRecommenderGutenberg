Synopsis: In 'Beauty and the Beast, and Tales of Home' by Taylor, Bayard, a young woman finds herself drawn to a mysterious and enigmatic beast, leading her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and unexpected love. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the beast's enchanted castle, she uncovers a world of magic, danger, and hidden truths that challenge her perceptions of beauty and the true nature of humanity. Alongside this enchanting tale, the collection also features other captivating stories that explore the complexities of home, belonging, and the power of love. 

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Fairy Tale

Themes: Beauty and appearance, Love and acceptance, Self-discovery, Home and belonging
