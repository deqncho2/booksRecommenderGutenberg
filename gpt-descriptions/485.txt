Synopsis:
In "The Road to Oz," young Dorothy finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz. This time, she embarks on a thrilling journey along with her new friends, including the Shaggy Man and Button-Bright, as they encounter peculiar creatures and face unexpected challenges. Together, they must navigate the mysterious road that leads to the Emerald City, where they hope to meet the famous Wizard of Oz. Along the way, Dorothy learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Imagination
