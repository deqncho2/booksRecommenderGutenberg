Synopsis: In "A Modest Proposal," Jonathan Swift presents a unique and controversial solution to the problem of poverty in Ireland. With a satirical tone, he proposes a shocking idea that aims to alleviate the burden of poor families and benefit society as a whole. This thought-provoking work challenges societal norms and forces readers to question the limits of compassion and the consequences of extreme measures.

Genres: Satire, Political commentary

Themes: Poverty, Social inequality, Morality, Irony
