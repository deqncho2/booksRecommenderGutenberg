Synopsis: In 'Ambush: A Terran Empire vignette' by Wilson, Ann, a group of elite soldiers find themselves in a treacherous situation as they navigate a hostile alien planet. With danger lurking at every turn, they must rely on their training and instincts to survive. As the tension rises, unexpected alliances are formed and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Betrayal
