Synopsis: 
"A History of the Japanese People: From the Earliest Times to the End of the Meiji Era" takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and complex history of Japan. From its ancient origins to the transformative Meiji period, this book delves into the fascinating stories, events, and individuals that shaped the nation. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Brinkley and Kikuchi provide a comprehensive account of Japan's cultural, political, and social evolution, offering a captivating exploration of a nation's remarkable past.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Cultural studies, Biography

Themes: 
Japanese history, Cultural identity, Political transformation
