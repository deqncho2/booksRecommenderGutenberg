Synopsis: In 'Elias: An Epic of the Ages' by Orson F. Whitney, embark on a thrilling journey through time and space as Elias, a young and courageous hero, battles against the forces of darkness to protect his loved ones and fulfill his destiny. From ancient civilizations to futuristic realms, this epic tale weaves together elements of adventure, romance, and mysticism, captivating readers with its richly imagined world and captivating characters. As Elias faces formidable challenges and discovers hidden truths, the fate of the universe hangs in the balance, making this an unforgettable epic that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Destiny, Love and Sacrifice
