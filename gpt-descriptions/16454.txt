Synopsis:
In "The Upas Tree: A Christmas Story for all the Year" by Florence L. Barclay, a mysterious tree holds a secret that has the power to change lives forever. When a young woman stumbles upon this enchanted tree, she is granted a wish that will test her faith, love, and the true meaning of Christmas. As the story unfolds, unexpected twists and turns lead to a heartwarming conclusion that will leave readers captivated and inspired.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Faith, Love, Redemption, Christmas Spirit
