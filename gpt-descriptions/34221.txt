Synopsis: In 'Electricity and Magnetism' by Gray, Elisha, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking journey to unravel the mysteries of these two powerful forces. As he delves deeper into his experiments, he discovers astonishing phenomena that challenge everything he thought he knew. From electrifying discoveries to magnetic marvels, this captivating tale takes readers on an electrifying adventure that will leave them in awe of the invisible forces that shape our world.

Genres: Science fiction, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes: Scientific exploration, Discovery, Power of nature
