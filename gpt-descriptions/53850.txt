Synopsis: 
In 'Design and Tradition: A short account of the principles and historic development of architecture and the applied arts', Fenn Amor takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of architecture and the applied arts. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this book explores the principles, techniques, and historical development of these creative disciplines, offering a concise yet comprehensive overview. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Amor delves into the intricate relationship between design and tradition, shedding light on the ways in which cultural, social, and technological factors have shaped the evolution of these artistic practices. Whether you are an architecture enthusiast or simply curious about the history of design, this book will leave you inspired and enlightened.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art History, Architecture

Themes: Design principles, Historic development, Cultural influences, Tradition and innovation
