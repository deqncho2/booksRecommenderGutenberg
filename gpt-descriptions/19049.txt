Synopsis: In 'The Builders: A Story and Study of Masonry', Joseph Fort Newton takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of Freemasonry. Through a compelling narrative, the author explores the secrets, rituals, and symbolism of this ancient fraternity, revealing the hidden truths that lie within its mysterious walls. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigmatic realm of Masonry, he uncovers a web of intrigue, loyalty, and personal growth, ultimately leading to a profound understanding of the power of brotherhood and the pursuit of enlightenment. With its rich historical backdrop and thought-provoking insights, 'The Builders' offers a fascinating exploration of the hidden world of Freemasonry.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Brotherhood, Enlightenment, Symbolism
