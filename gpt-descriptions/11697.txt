Synopsis: In "Mare Nostrum (Our Sea): A Novel," Blasco Ibáñez takes readers on a captivating journey through the Mediterranean, where love, ambition, and sacrifice intertwine against the backdrop of a changing world. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows the life of a charismatic naval officer, Ulysses Ferragut, as he navigates the treacherous waters of war, romance, and personal growth. From the shores of Spain to the battlefields of World War I, Ulysses' experiences shape his character and challenge his beliefs, ultimately leading him to confront the profound complexities of human nature.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Passion, Ambition and Sacrifice, War and Conflict
