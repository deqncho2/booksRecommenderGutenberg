Synopsis: In 'Men of Invention and Industry' by Samuel Smiles, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable individuals who have shaped the world through their ingenuity and determination. From inventors and engineers to entrepreneurs and visionaries, this book unveils the inspiring stories of these unsung heroes who have revolutionized various fields and left an indelible mark on society. Discover the trials and triumphs of these extraordinary men as they overcome obstacles, challenge conventions, and transform their dreams into reality. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Success, Entrepreneurship
