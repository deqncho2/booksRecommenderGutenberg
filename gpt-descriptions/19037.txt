Synopsis: In the Transactions of the American Society of Civil Engineers, Brace and Mason delve into the fascinating world of the New York Tunnel Extension of the Pennsylvania Railroad and the Cross-Town Tunnels. Through meticulous research and detailed analysis, they uncover the challenges, triumphs, and groundbreaking engineering feats behind these monumental projects. From the intricate planning to the awe-inspiring construction, this paper offers a captivating glimpse into the remarkable tunnels that transformed the transportation landscape of New York City.

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, History

Themes: Infrastructure development, Urban transformation, Technological innovation
