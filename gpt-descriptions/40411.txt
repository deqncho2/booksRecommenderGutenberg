Synopsis: 
"The Potter's Craft: A Practical Guide for the Studio and Workshop" by Charles Fergus Binns is a comprehensive and engaging guide that takes readers on a journey into the world of pottery. From the basics of clay preparation to advanced techniques of glazing and firing, this book provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for both beginners and experienced potters. With detailed instructions, beautiful illustrations, and inspiring anecdotes, Binns invites readers to explore their creativity and master the art of pottery.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Craft

Themes: Creativity, Skill development, Artistic expression
