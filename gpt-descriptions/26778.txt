Synopsis: In 'The Ocean Wireless Boys and the Naval Code' by John Henry Goldfrap, a group of young wireless operators find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure when they intercept a mysterious naval code. As they delve deeper into the encrypted messages, they uncover a dangerous plot that could have far-reaching consequences. With their skills and courage put to the test, the Ocean Wireless Boys must race against time to decipher the code and prevent disaster. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Code-breaking, Patriotism
