Synopsis: 

In "Comet Lore: Halley's Comet in History and Astronomy" by Edwin Emerson, embark on a captivating journey through time and space as the author delves into the fascinating history and scientific significance of Halley's Comet. From ancient civilizations to modern astronomers, this book uncovers the myths, legends, and discoveries surrounding one of the most enigmatic celestial phenomena.

Discover how Halley's Comet has captured the imagination of humanity for centuries, inspiring awe, fear, and wonder. Explore the ancient beliefs and cultural interpretations of comets, as well as the groundbreaking scientific advancements that have allowed us to unravel the mysteries of these celestial wanderers. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Emerson paints a vivid picture of the comet's impact on human history and our understanding of the universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Astronomy

Themes: Celestial phenomena, Human fascination, Scientific discovery
