Synopsis: In 'Mr. Pickwick's Christmas', the beloved characters from Charles Dickens' 'The Pickwick Papers' embark on a festive adventure at Manor Farm. As they gather for Christmas celebrations, they encounter a mischievous goblin who steals a sexton, leading to a series of unexpected events. Amidst the merriment, the Pickwickians also partake in thrilling sports on the ice, creating a delightful and heartwarming tale that captures the spirit of the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Christmas literature, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Festivity, Redemption
