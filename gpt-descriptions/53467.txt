Synopsis: In 'Prejudices, second series', H. L. Mencken delves into a collection of thought-provoking essays that challenge societal norms and expose the flaws of human nature. With his signature wit and sharp intellect, Mencken explores a wide range of topics, from politics and religion to literature and culture. Through his incisive observations and fearless critiques, he invites readers to question their own prejudices and confront the uncomfortable truths that lie beneath the surface of society.

Genres: Essay, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Prejudice, Society, Human Nature, Intellectualism
