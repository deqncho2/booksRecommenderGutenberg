Synopsis: In 'Lectures on the true, the beautiful and the good', Victor Cousin takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of philosophy, art, and morality. Through a series of thought-provoking lectures, he explores the profound connections between truth, beauty, and goodness, unraveling the intricate tapestry that binds them together. With eloquence and depth, Cousin delves into the essence of human existence, challenging readers to question their own perceptions and beliefs. As he navigates through the realms of reason, aesthetics, and ethics, he unveils a world of profound insights and timeless wisdom, leaving readers inspired and enlightened.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Truth, Beauty, Goodness, Philosophy, Morality
