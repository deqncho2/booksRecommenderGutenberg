Synopsis: In "The Boy Scouts on Belgian Battlefields" by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young Boy Scouts find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I as they embark on a daring adventure through the war-torn landscapes of Belgium. As they navigate the treacherous battlefields, they encounter unexpected allies, face dangerous challenges, and uncover secrets that will test their courage and loyalty. With their Scout training put to the ultimate test, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and bravery to survive the chaos of war and make a difference in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Coming of Age
