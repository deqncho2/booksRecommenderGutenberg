Synopsis: In "The Laird o' Coul's Ghost" by William Ogilvie, a young man named Jamie finds himself entangled in a mysterious and haunting tale when he inherits an old Scottish estate. As he delves into the history of the property, Jamie uncovers dark secrets and encounters a ghostly presence that seems determined to reveal the truth. With each revelation, Jamie's own life becomes increasingly intertwined with the past, leading him on a thrilling journey of discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ghosts and the supernatural, family secrets, the power of the past, redemption
