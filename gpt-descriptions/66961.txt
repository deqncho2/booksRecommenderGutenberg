Synopsis: In 'Plet: A Christmas Tale of the Wasatch' by Alfred Lambourne, a young boy named Plet embarks on a magical adventure in the snowy mountains of the Wasatch Range. As he encounters mysterious creatures and faces unexpected challenges, Plet learns the true meaning of Christmas and the power of love and friendship. This heartwarming tale will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and enchantment.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Christmas

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Christmas spirit
