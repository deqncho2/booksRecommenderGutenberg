Synopsis: 
In 'Twice Lost' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young sailor named Jack is thrust into a thrilling adventure when his ship is attacked by pirates. Separated from his crewmates, Jack finds himself stranded on a remote island, where he must navigate treacherous terrain, encounter mysterious inhabitants, and uncover the secrets of a hidden treasure. As he battles against the odds to survive and reunite with his comrades, Jack's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, 'Twice Lost' takes readers on a captivating journey of courage, friendship, and the pursuit of redemption.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Redemption
