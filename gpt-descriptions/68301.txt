Synopsis: In "Under the German Shells" by Emmanuel Bourcier, readers are transported to the harrowing and tumultuous period of World War I. Through the eyes of a young soldier, the story unfolds amidst the relentless bombardment of German shells. As the protagonist navigates the horrors of war, the narrative delves into the profound impact of conflict on individuals and the resilience of the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this gripping tale offers a unique perspective on the devastating realities of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Survival, Sacrifice, Resilience, Human Spirit
