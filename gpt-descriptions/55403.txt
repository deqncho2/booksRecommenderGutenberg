Synopsis: In "The Petrol Engine: A Text-book dealing with the Principles of Design and Construction, with a Special Chapter on the Two-stroke Engine," Francis John Kean takes readers on an enlightening journey into the fascinating world of petrol engines. Exploring the intricate principles of design and construction, Kean delves into the inner workings of these powerful machines, providing valuable insights into their operation and maintenance. With a special focus on the two-stroke engine, this comprehensive textbook offers a wealth of knowledge for both aspiring engineers and curious enthusiasts alike. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, Automotive

Themes: Engineering principles, Mechanical design, Automotive technology
