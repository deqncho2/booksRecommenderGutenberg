Synopsis: 
In 'The Wooden Horse' by Hugh Walpole, a young man named David falls under the spell of a mysterious and enigmatic woman named Perdita. As their relationship deepens, David becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and emotional turmoil. Set against the backdrop of a remote English countryside, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, obsession, and the power of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and deception
