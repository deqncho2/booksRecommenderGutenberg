Synopsis: 
In "Sport and Work on the Nepaul Frontier: Twelve Years Sporting Reminiscences of an Indigo Planter," James Inglis takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Nepaul Frontier. Through vivid and captivating storytelling, Inglis recounts his thrilling adventures as an indigo planter, where he not only faces the challenges of cultivating indigo but also encounters dangerous wildlife, treacherous landscapes, and encounters with local tribes. From heart-pounding encounters with tigers and elephants to navigating through dense jungles and crossing raging rivers, Inglis's tales of survival and triumph will leave readers on the edge of their seats. 

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Wildlife, Cultural Encounters
