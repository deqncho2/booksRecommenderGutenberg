Synopsis: In 'The Riddle Club through the holidays', join the clever and curious Riddle Club as they embark on a thrilling adventure during the festive season. As the club members gather to celebrate the holidays, they find themselves entangled in a series of perplexing riddles that need to be solved. With their sharp minds and teamwork, they unravel the mysteries one by one, leading them to an unexpected discovery hidden within a snowman. This heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the riddles.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Problem-solving, Curiosity
