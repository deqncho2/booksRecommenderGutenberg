Synopsis: 

In 'The Farmer's Own Book: A treatise on the numerous diseases of the horse: with an explanation of their symptoms, and the course of treatment to be pursued; also a treatise on the diseases of horned cattle' by J. D. Koogle, readers are taken on a comprehensive journey through the world of equine and bovine health. This invaluable guide provides a detailed exploration of the various diseases that afflict horses and horned cattle, offering a thorough understanding of their symptoms and the appropriate treatment methods to be employed. From common ailments to more obscure conditions, Koogle's expertise shines through as he equips farmers and animal enthusiasts with the knowledge needed to ensure the well-being of their beloved animals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Animal care, Disease prevention, Veterinary treatment
