Synopsis: In 'Canzoni & Ripostes: Whereto are appended the Complete Poetical Works of T.E. Hulme', Ezra Pound presents the poetic works of T.E. Hulme, a remarkable figure in the early 20th-century literary movement. This collection showcases Hulme's unique style and profound insights, exploring themes of modernity, war, and the human condition. Through his evocative verses, Hulme invites readers to delve into the depths of his poetic vision, leaving them captivated by his thought-provoking words.

Genres: Poetry, Modernist Literature

Themes: Modernity, War, Human Condition
