Synopsis: In the heart of 18th century Edinburgh, a respected cabinetmaker named Deacon Brodie leads a double life as a cunning burglar. As his crimes escalate, the city becomes captivated by his audacity and the authorities are determined to bring him to justice. The Trial of Deacon Brodie unveils a thrilling tale of deception, betrayal, and the dark secrets that lie beneath the surface of society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Dualities
