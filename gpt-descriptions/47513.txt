Synopsis: In 'The Flea' by Harold Russell, a seemingly insignificant encounter with a flea sets off a chain of events that will challenge the protagonist's perception of reality. As the story unfolds, the boundaries between the natural and supernatural become blurred, leading to a thrilling exploration of the human psyche and the power of the mind. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, 'The Flea' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what is real and what is imagined.

Genres: Psychological thriller, supernatural fiction, mystery

Themes: Perception, reality vs. illusion, the power of the mind
