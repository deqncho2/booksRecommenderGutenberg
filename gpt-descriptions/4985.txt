Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and ambitious girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure when she moves to the Red Mill. As she settles into her new surroundings, Ruth uncovers a mysterious secret held by the enigmatic Jasper Parloe. Determined to uncover the truth, Ruth delves deeper into the secrets of the Red Mill, leading her on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will Ruth be able to unravel the secret and bring light to the darkness that surrounds the mill?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Courage and determination, Friendship and loyalty
