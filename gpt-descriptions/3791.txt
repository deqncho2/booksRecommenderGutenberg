Synopsis: In 'The Reign of Law; a tale of the Kentucky hemp fields', James Lane Allen takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Kentucky, where the cultivation of hemp serves as the backdrop for a story filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As the lives of the characters intertwine amidst the lush fields, secrets are unveiled, and the consequences of their actions reverberate through the generations. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the enduring power of the law.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Love, Family, Morality
