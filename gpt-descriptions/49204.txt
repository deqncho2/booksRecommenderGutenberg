Synopsis: 

"Buffalo Bill" from Prairie to Palace: An Authentic History of the Wild West takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most legendary figures, Buffalo Bill Cody. From his humble beginnings as a buffalo hunter on the prairies to his rise as a renowned showman, this book delves into the thrilling adventures, triumphs, and challenges that shaped Buffalo Bill's extraordinary career. Filled with vivid descriptions of daring buffalo hunts, epic battles, and captivating performances, this historical account paints a vivid picture of the Wild West and the larger-than-life character who became its symbol.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: American frontier, Wild West culture, Showmanship, Legends and myths
