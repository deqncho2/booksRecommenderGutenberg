Synopsis: 

'Beautiful Shells of New Zealand: An Illustrated Work for Amateur Collectors of New Zealand Marine Shells, with Directions for Collecting and Cleaning them' by Edward George Britton Moss is a captivating guide that takes readers on a visual journey through the stunning world of New Zealand marine shells. Filled with vibrant illustrations and detailed descriptions, this book provides amateur collectors with invaluable knowledge on how to collect and clean these exquisite treasures. From the rugged coastlines to the depths of the ocean, Moss explores the diverse habitats where these beautiful shells can be found, offering readers a glimpse into the fascinating world of marine life. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Guidebook

Themes: Marine life, Collecting, Natural beauty
