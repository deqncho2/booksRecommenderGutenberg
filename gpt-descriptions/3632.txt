Synopsis: In 'Poor Miss Finch' by Wilkie Collins, a young woman named Lucilla Finch is blind, but her world is turned upside down when a revolutionary medical treatment restores her sight. However, the newfound ability to see brings unexpected challenges and dangers into her life, as she becomes entangled in a complex web of love, deception, and mystery. Lucilla must navigate the treacherous waters of her new reality, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the secrets that threaten to destroy her. 

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Perception, Identity, Deception, Love and Relationships
