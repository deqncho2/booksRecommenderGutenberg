Synopsis: In 'The Scranton High Chums on the Cinder Path: Or, The Mystery of the Haunted Quarry' by Ferguson, Donald, a group of adventurous friends from Scranton High School stumble upon a hidden secret while exploring an abandoned quarry. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover a series of strange occurrences and encounter unexpected dangers that test their courage and friendship. With each clue they uncover, the chums find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, leading them to question who they can trust and what lies hidden within the haunted quarry.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Trust, Secrets
