Synopsis: In 'Morag: A Tale of the Highlands of Scotland', we are transported to the rugged and enchanting landscapes of Scotland, where we follow the journey of Morag, a young woman with a mysterious past. As she navigates through the challenges of love, loss, and self-discovery, Morag finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and ancient traditions that threaten to unravel her world. With its vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this tale of adventure and romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Morag's extraordinary destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Fate, Tradition, Love and Loss
