Synopsis: In 'The History of Education: Educational Practice and Progress Considered as a Phase of the Development and Spread of Western Civilization', Cubberley takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of education, exploring its profound impact on the development and spread of Western civilization. From ancient times to the modern era, this comprehensive work delves into the various educational practices, institutions, and philosophies that have shaped societies and transformed the way knowledge is acquired and disseminated. With insightful analysis and engaging anecdotes, Cubberley sheds light on the pivotal role education has played in shaping the world we live in today.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Education

Themes: Evolution of education, Impact of education on civilization, Educational practices and institutions, Knowledge dissemination
