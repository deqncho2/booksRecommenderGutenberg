Synopsis: 
'Tales of a Traveller' by Washington Irving is a collection of captivating and imaginative stories that take readers on a journey through various settings and time periods. From the mysterious and supernatural to the humorous and satirical, these tales introduce a diverse cast of characters and explore the intricacies of human nature. Each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers engaged and eager to uncover the secrets that lie within. Whether it's a haunting ghost story or a lighthearted adventure, 'Tales of a Traveller' offers a delightful escape into the realms of imagination.

Genres: 
Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, satire, adventure

Themes: 
Exploration of human nature, the power of storytelling, the supernatural, satire of societal norms
