Synopsis: In 'Dodo's Daughter: A Sequel to Dodo', we are transported back to the enchanting world of Dodo, where the beloved characters from the original novel return with new adventures and unexpected twists. As Dodo's daughter, the spirited and independent Paula, navigates the complexities of love, society, and her own identity, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and scandals that threaten to unravel her world. With wit, charm, and a touch of mystery, this sequel takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the next generation of Dodo's family.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, societal expectations, family dynamics
