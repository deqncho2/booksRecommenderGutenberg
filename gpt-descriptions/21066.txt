Synopsis: In 'Harry Escombe: A Tale of Adventure in Peru' by Collingwood, readers are transported to the captivating world of Peru, where young Harry Escombe finds himself embarking on a thrilling journey filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected discoveries. As Harry navigates through the treacherous landscapes of the Peruvian wilderness, he encounters ancient ruins, encounters with indigenous tribes, and encounters with ruthless treasure hunters. With each twist and turn, Harry's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, as he unravels the secrets of Peru and uncovers a hidden treasure that could change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Treasure Hunting
