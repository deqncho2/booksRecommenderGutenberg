Synopsis: In "Time for Survival" by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows him to manipulate time. As he delves deeper into the possibilities of this newfound power, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue and must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and make difficult choices that will determine the course of history.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Morality, Consequences of scientific advancements
