Synopsis: 

In 'On Land and Sea at the Dardanelles', T. C. Bridges takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Gallipoli Campaign during World War I. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the horrors and heroism of war as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of the Dardanelles. From the intense battles on land to the perilous naval encounters at sea, this gripping tale immerses readers in the heart-pounding action and the emotional struggles faced by those caught in the midst of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The futility of war
