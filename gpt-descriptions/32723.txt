Synopsis: In 'The Minute Boys of Boston' by James Otis, a group of young boys find themselves caught up in the excitement and danger of the American Revolution. As tensions rise and battles loom, these brave boys must navigate the challenges of loyalty, courage, and sacrifice. With their lives forever changed, they become an integral part of the fight for freedom, proving that even the smallest patriots can make a big difference.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Coming of age
