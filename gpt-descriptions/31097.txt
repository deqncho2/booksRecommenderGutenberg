Synopsis: In 'Froebel's Gifts', two sisters embark on a journey of self-discovery and education as they explore the world through the innovative teaching methods of Friedrich Froebel. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and heartwarming experiences, they uncover the transformative power of play and creativity, forever changing their perception of education and childhood.

Genres: Educational, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Education, Creativity, Childhood, Self-discovery
