Synopsis: 

In "The Young Carthaginian: A Story of The Times of Hannibal," we are transported back to ancient times, where we follow the thrilling adventures of Malchus, a young Carthaginian nobleman. As war looms between Carthage and Rome, Malchus finds himself torn between his loyalty to his city and his desire for justice. With his quick wit, bravery, and unwavering determination, Malchus embarks on a dangerous journey that will test his loyalty, forge unlikely alliances, and ultimately shape the fate of his people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Bravery, Friendship, Conflict
