Synopsis: In 'The Metropolis' by Sinclair, Upton, we follow the gripping journey of a young immigrant who arrives in the bustling city of Chicago, seeking a better life. As he navigates the treacherous world of industrialization, corruption, and social inequality, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and exploitation. With each twist and turn, he must confront the harsh realities of urban life and fight for his own survival. Will he succumb to the darkness that surrounds him, or will he find a way to rise above it all?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Social Commentary, Political Drama

Themes: Immigration, Capitalism, Social Inequality, Corruption
