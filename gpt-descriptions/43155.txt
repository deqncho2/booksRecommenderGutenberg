Synopsis: In 'Thorpe Regis' by Frances Mary Peard, a young woman named Maud finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of high society, Maud must confront her own ambitions and the sacrifices she is willing to make for love. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque English village, this captivating novel explores the power dynamics and secrets that lie beneath the surface of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Love and sacrifice, Ambition and desire
