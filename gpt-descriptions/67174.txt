Synopsis: In 'Samantha on Children's Rights' by Holley, Marietta, a young girl named Samantha embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the mysterious disappearance of children in her town. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Samantha discovers a hidden world where children's rights are violated, and she becomes determined to fight for justice. With the help of her friends, Samantha must navigate through danger and deception to expose the dark secrets that threaten the well-being of children everywhere.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Children's rights, Justice, Friendship, Courage
