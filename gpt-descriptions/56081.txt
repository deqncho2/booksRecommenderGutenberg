Synopsis: In 'A Traitor in London' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious murder takes place in the heart of Victorian London, setting off a thrilling chain of events. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the line between loyalty and betrayal becomes blurred. With a cast of intriguing characters and a web of deceit to untangle, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Loyalty, Deception, Justice
