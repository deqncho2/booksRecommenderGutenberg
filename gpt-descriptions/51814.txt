Synopsis: 

In 'Preferred Risk' by McCann, Edson, we are introduced to a world where the boundaries between reality and virtual reality blur, and the consequences of our actions become increasingly uncertain. The story follows a brilliant computer programmer named Alex, who is on the verge of creating a groundbreaking technology that could revolutionize the gaming industry. However, as Alex delves deeper into his creation, he begins to question the ethical implications of his work and the potential dangers it may unleash. As he navigates a web of corporate intrigue and personal dilemmas, Alex must confront the dark side of his own ambition and make choices that will have far-reaching consequences.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Technological

Themes: Ethics, Virtual Reality, Ambition, Consequences
