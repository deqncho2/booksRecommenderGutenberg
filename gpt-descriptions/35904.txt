Synopsis: In 'The Five Arrows' by Allan Chase, a group of five friends stumble upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a hidden treasure. As they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the past, they find themselves facing dangerous obstacles and unexpected alliances. With each arrow they follow, the stakes get higher, and the friends must rely on their wit and courage to solve the puzzle before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Treasure Hunting, Secrets and Intrigue
