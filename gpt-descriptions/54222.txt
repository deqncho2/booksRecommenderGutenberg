Synopsis: 
In 'Blood and Sand' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente, we are transported to the vibrant world of bullfighting in early 20th century Spain. The story follows the rise of Juan Gallardo, a poor and ambitious young man from Seville, who becomes a renowned matador. As Juan's fame and success grow, he finds himself torn between his love for his devoted wife, his passion for a seductive socialite, and the dangerous allure of fame and fortune. Set against the backdrop of the bullring, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices one must make for glory and the consequences of succumbing to one's desires.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Love and Desire, Sacrifice, Social Class
