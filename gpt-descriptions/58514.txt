Synopsis: In 'The Green Tent Mystery at Sugar Creek' by Hutchens, Paul, the Sugar Creek Gang stumbles upon a mysterious green tent hidden deep in the woods. Intrigued by its presence, the gang embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets that lie within. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they encounter unexpected challenges and must rely on their friendship and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle. With each clue they uncover, the gang becomes more determined to unravel the truth behind the green tent, leading them to a surprising and heartwarming conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
