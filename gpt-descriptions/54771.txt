Synopsis: 
In 'A Prince of Swindlers' by Boothby, Guy, we are introduced to a charming and enigmatic protagonist, who leads a double life as a respected member of society and a masterful con artist. With his quick wit and cunning, he effortlessly navigates the treacherous world of high society, using his skills to manipulate and deceive those around him. As he embarks on his most audacious scheme yet, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving them questioning the true nature of trust and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality
