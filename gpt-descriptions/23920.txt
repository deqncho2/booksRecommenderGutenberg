Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Proportion' by Anne Walker, a renowned architect finds herself entangled in a mysterious affair when she is commissioned to design a grand mansion for a wealthy client. As she delves deeper into the project, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel her professional reputation and personal life. With each twist and turn, the architect must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal, all while trying to maintain her artistic integrity and protect those she holds dear. This gripping tale of ambition, intrigue, and the pursuit of truth will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Betrayal, Artistic Integrity
