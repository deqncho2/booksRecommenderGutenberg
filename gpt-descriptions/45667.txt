Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts Along the Susquehanna; or, The Silver Fox Patrol Caught in a Flood', the Silver Fox Patrol embarks on an exciting adventure along the Susquehanna River. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they find themselves caught in a sudden and dangerous flood. With their survival skills put to the test, the scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and bravery to overcome the perilous situation and save themselves and others in need.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Bravery
