Synopsis: 

In 'Light Science for Leisure Hours', Richard A. Proctor takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of scientific subjects and natural phenomena. Through a series of familiar essays, Proctor explores various intriguing topics, from the mysteries of the night sky to the secrets of light and color. With his engaging writing style and accessible explanations, he invites readers to delve into the fascinating world of science and discover the hidden wonders that surround us every day. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Essays

Themes: Scientific exploration, Natural phenomena, Wonder and curiosity
