Synopsis: In the midst of political turmoil and social unrest, a renowned Roman general named Coriolanus finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his own personal desires. As he rises to power and gains the adoration of the people, Coriolanus must navigate the treacherous world of politics, manipulation, and betrayal. With his fierce temperament and unwavering principles, he becomes a tragic figure caught in a web of conflicting loyalties and the consequences of his own actions.

Genres: Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Reflection
