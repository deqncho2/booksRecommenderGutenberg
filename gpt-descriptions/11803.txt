Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1951 January - June, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. Copyright Office that unveils a treasure trove of forgotten literary gems. Delve into the pages of this remarkable collection and discover the hidden stories, forgotten characters, and lost narratives that have shaped the literary landscape of America. From beloved classics to obscure masterpieces, this anthology offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of creativity that has defined the mid-20th century. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Literature

Themes: Intellectual property, Cultural heritage, Literary history
