Synopsis: 
"The Autobiography of an Indian Princess" is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a remarkable journey through the life of Sunity Devee, the Maharani of Cooch Behar. From her privileged upbringing in a royal family to her experiences as a young bride and a mother, Sunity Devee's story is filled with intrigue, love, and the challenges of navigating a changing world. As she shares her personal struggles and triumphs, readers are transported to a bygone era, where tradition clashes with modernity, and the strength of a woman's spirit shines through.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Cultural

Themes: Identity, Tradition vs. Modernity, Women's Empowerment, Love and Relationships
