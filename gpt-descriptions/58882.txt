Synopsis: In 'Our Family Affairs, 1867-1896' by E. F. Benson, readers are transported to the late 19th century, where they become intimately acquainted with the lives of the eccentric and captivating members of the Benson family. From scandalous love affairs to unexpected inheritances, this engrossing narrative delves into the secrets, desires, and ambitions that shape the lives of these fascinating characters. As the years unfold, the Bensons navigate the ever-changing landscape of Victorian society, facing both triumphs and tribulations that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical fiction, family saga, romance

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, family dynamics, inheritance, secrets and scandals
