Synopsis: In 'A Wife's Duty: A Tale' by Amelia Opie, a young woman finds herself torn between her duty as a wife and her desire for personal fulfillment. As she navigates the complexities of marriage and societal expectations, she must confront the consequences of her choices and the true meaning of love. With unexpected twists and emotional depth, this captivating tale explores the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Duty vs. personal fulfillment, Love and sacrifice, Societal expectations
