Synopsis: In 'The Peace of Roaring River' by Van Schaick, George, a young man named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure when he inherits a mysterious piece of land nestled in the heart of the untamed wilderness. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Roaring River, Jack encounters a cast of intriguing characters, uncovers hidden treasures, and unravels a long-forgotten legend that holds the key to his own destiny. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unexpected challenges that will test his courage and determination. Will he be able to unlock the true power of Roaring River and find the peace he seeks?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Self-discovery
