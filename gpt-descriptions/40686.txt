Synopsis: In 'Demonology and Devil-lore', Moncure Daniel Conway delves into the fascinating world of demons and devils, exploring their origins, characteristics, and the various beliefs surrounding them. From ancient mythology to religious texts and folklore, Conway presents a comprehensive study of these supernatural beings, their influence on human culture, and the enduring fascination they hold for us today. With captivating anecdotes and thought-provoking analysis, this book offers a captivating journey into the realm of the supernatural.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Folklore

Themes: Mythology, Religion, Supernatural, Cultural Beliefs
