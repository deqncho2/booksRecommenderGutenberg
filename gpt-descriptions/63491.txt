Synopsis: In 'The Snow Baby: A true story with true pictures', Josephine Diebitsch Peary takes readers on an extraordinary journey to the Arctic, where she and her husband, renowned explorer Robert E. Peary, encounter a remarkable and heartwarming surprise. Through vivid descriptions and captivating photographs, the author shares the incredible story of a little girl they discover in the frozen wilderness, and the bond that forms between her and the Peary family. As the mystery of the snow baby unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the beauty of the Arctic landscape and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Family, Resilience
