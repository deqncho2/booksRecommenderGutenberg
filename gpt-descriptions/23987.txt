Synopsis: In 'The Torch Bearer: A Camp Fire Girls' Story' by Ida Treadwell Thurston, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Sylvia as she embarks on a life-changing adventure at Camp Fire Girls. As Sylvia navigates the challenges and triumphs of camp life, she discovers the true meaning of friendship, self-discovery, and the power of the flame that ignites her spirit. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this enchanting tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the camp's flickering fire.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Empowerment
