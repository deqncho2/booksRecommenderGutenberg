Synopsis: In "The Problem of the Rupee, Its Origin and Its Solution," B.R. Ambedkar delves into the complex history and challenges surrounding the Indian currency. Through meticulous research and analysis, Ambedkar uncovers the origins of the rupee and explores the economic, political, and social implications of its fluctuating value. As he presents his proposed solutions, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey that sheds light on the intricacies of monetary systems and their impact on a nation's development.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, History

Themes: Currency, Economic policy, Colonialism, Nationalism
