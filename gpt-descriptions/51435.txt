Synopsis: In 'The Business, As Usual' by Sharkey, Jack, a seemingly ordinary office worker finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret within his company. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he must navigate a treacherous world of corporate espionage, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corporate corruption, Identity, Trust
