Synopsis: In "History of the Conflict Between Religion and Science," Draper delves into the fascinating and often contentious relationship between two powerful forces: religion and science. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, he uncovers the historical clashes, intellectual debates, and profound implications that have shaped this enduring conflict. From Galileo's trial to Darwin's theory of evolution, Draper explores the pivotal moments that have defined the struggle between faith and reason, shedding light on the complex interplay between these influential spheres of human understanding.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Philosophy

Themes: Conflict, Intellectualism, Evolution, Faith, Reason
