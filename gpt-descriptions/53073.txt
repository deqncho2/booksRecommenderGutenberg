Synopsis: In 'God's Country: The Trail to Happiness' by James Oliver Curwood, a young woman embarks on a daring adventure to the untamed wilderness of the Canadian Rockies. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected dangers, she discovers the true meaning of love, courage, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart. This captivating tale of self-discovery and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the majestic beauty of God's Country.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Love and sacrifice, Personal growth
