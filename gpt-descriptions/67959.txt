Synopsis: 
In 'On the Preparations of the Indian Hemp, or Gunjah (Cannabis Indica): Their Effects on the Animal System in Health, and Their Utility in the Treatment of Tetanus and Other Convulsive Diseases', O'Shaughnessy takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of cannabis and its potential medicinal uses. Through meticulous research and experimentation, he explores the effects of cannabis on the animal system, shedding light on its therapeutic properties in the treatment of tetanus and other convulsive diseases. This groundbreaking work offers a unique perspective on the potential benefits of cannabis, challenging conventional medical practices and opening up new possibilities for treatment.

Genres: Medical research, Pharmacology, Scientific literature

Themes: Medicinal properties of cannabis, Alternative medicine, Therapeutic potential
