Synopsis: 

In 'Ten Acres Enough: A practical experience, showing how a very small farm may be made to keep a very large family,' Edmund Morris takes us on a captivating journey as he shares his personal experience of transforming a tiny plot of land into a thriving farm capable of sustaining his large family. With vivid descriptions and practical advice, Morris reveals the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected discoveries he encountered along the way. This inspiring tale of self-sufficiency and resourcefulness will leave readers eager to explore the possibilities of their own small-scale farming endeavors.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Self-sufficiency

Themes: Farming, Self-sufficiency, Family, Resourcefulness
