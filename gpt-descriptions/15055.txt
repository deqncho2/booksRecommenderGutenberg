Synopsis: In 'The Free Rangers: A Story of the Early Days Along the Mississippi', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Mississippi River during the early 19th century. The story follows a group of young pioneers who find themselves caught in the midst of a fierce conflict between Native American tribes and the settlers. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face the constant threat of attack, the free rangers must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering bond to survive the perils of the frontier.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival, Friendship, Conflict between Settlers and Native Americans
