Synopsis: In "Emmeline, the Orphan of the Castle," we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Emmeline, who finds herself alone and abandoned in a mysterious castle. As she navigates the secrets and intrigues of her surroundings, Emmeline discovers hidden truths about her past and uncovers a web of deceit that threatens her very existence. With each twist and turn, she must summon her courage and resilience to unravel the enigma of her identity and find her place in the world.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Resilience, Secrets
