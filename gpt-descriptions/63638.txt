Synopsis: In 'Electron Eat Electron' by Noel M. Loomis, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the world forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and a race against time. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the limits of scientific progress and the moral implications of technological advancements.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technological advancement, Corporate greed, Government conspiracies
