Synopsis: 
'Boy Scouts in Northern Wilds; Or, The Signal from the Hills' follows a group of adventurous Boy Scouts as they embark on a thrilling expedition into the untamed wilderness of the Northern Wilds. Led by their resourceful scoutmaster, the boys encounter mysterious signals from the hills that lead them on a quest filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected discoveries. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and face various challenges, the Boy Scouts must rely on their skills, teamwork, and determination to unravel the secrets hidden within the wilds.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Camaraderie, Self-discovery
