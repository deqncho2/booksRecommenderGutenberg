Synopsis: In "The Rat-Trap" by Wyllarde, Dolf, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger, finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when he stumbles upon a secluded village. As he becomes acquainted with the locals, he discovers that the idyllic facade hides a dark underbelly of deceit, betrayal, and hidden desires. With his own past haunting him, Dolf must navigate through a treacherous maze of lies and manipulation, unsure of who to trust and what secrets lie just beyond his reach.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust, Redemption
