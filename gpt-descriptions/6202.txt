Synopsis: In "When Valmond Came to Pontiac: The Story of a Lost Napoleon. Volume 1," Gilbert Parker takes readers on a captivating journey to the small Canadian village of Pontiac. The arrival of a mysterious stranger named Valmond sparks curiosity and intrigue among the townspeople, who are drawn to his charismatic personality and enigmatic past. As Valmond's influence grows, the village becomes divided, with some embracing him as a savior while others view him with suspicion. Amidst political turmoil and personal conflicts, the fate of Pontiac hangs in the balance, and the true nature of Valmond's intentions remains shrouded in mystery.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, political drama

Themes: Identity, power and manipulation, loyalty and betrayal
