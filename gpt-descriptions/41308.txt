Synopsis: In 'The Soul of a Nation' by Philip Gibbs, a renowned journalist is assigned to cover a tumultuous period in history, where political unrest and social upheaval threaten to tear a nation apart. As he delves deeper into the heart of the conflict, he uncovers shocking secrets and hidden agendas that challenge his own beliefs and force him to question the very essence of his nation's soul. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue, where loyalty and betrayal blur the lines between right and wrong. As the stakes rise, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect the soul of his nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Identity, Truth and Deception, Loyalty and Betrayal
