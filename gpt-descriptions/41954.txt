Synopsis: In "The Wheel of Fortune" by Mahatma Gandhi, a young lawyer named Mohan embarks on a transformative journey that takes him from his comfortable life in India to the bustling streets of London. As he navigates the challenges of a foreign land, Mohan encounters a diverse cast of characters who challenge his beliefs and force him to question the very essence of his identity. Through his experiences, Mohan begins to understand the power of compassion, nonviolence, and the pursuit of truth, ultimately leading him to become a prominent figure in the fight for justice and equality. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Cultural Clash, Self-Discovery, Nonviolence, Social Justice
