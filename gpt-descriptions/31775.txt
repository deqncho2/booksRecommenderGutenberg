Synopsis: In 'Si Klegg, Book 5: The Deacon's Adventures at Chattanooga in Caring for the Boys' by John McElroy, follow the humorous and heartwarming journey of Si Klegg, a Union soldier, as he finds himself in the midst of the chaos and camaraderie of the Civil War. Set in Chattanooga, Si Klegg, along with his fellow soldiers, faces various challenges and unexpected adventures while under the care of the compassionate Deacon. From hilarious mishaps to poignant moments, this book takes readers on a rollercoaster of emotions as Si Klegg navigates the realities of war and the bonds formed between soldiers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Humor, War

Themes: Friendship, Sacrifice, Resilience
