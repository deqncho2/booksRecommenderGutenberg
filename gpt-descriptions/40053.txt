Synopsis: In the second volume of "Margaret Capel: A Novel," the captivating story continues as Margaret finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As she navigates the complexities of love and society, her world is turned upside down by unexpected revelations and shocking betrayals. With her heart torn between duty and passion, Margaret must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Conformity, Betrayal and Loyalty
