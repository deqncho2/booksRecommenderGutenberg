Synopsis: In 'Black Ivory', a young Englishman named Ralph Rover embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him across the vast African continent. Captured by Arab slave traders, Ralph finds himself in the midst of a dangerous and treacherous world. With the help of newfound friends and his own determination, he must navigate through perilous encounters, unexpected alliances, and the harsh realities of the slave trade. As Ralph fights for his freedom and the lives of others, he discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Slavery, Friendship
