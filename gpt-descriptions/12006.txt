Synopsis: 
In "A Handbook to Agra and the Taj, Sikandra, Fatehpur-Sikri and the Neighbourhood," E. B. Havell takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical wonders of Agra, India. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Havell paints a vivid picture of the majestic Taj Mahal, the grandeur of Sikandra, the architectural marvel of Fatehpur-Sikri, and the enchanting surroundings. From the intricate details of the monuments to the rich cultural heritage of the region, this handbook serves as a comprehensive guide, transporting readers to a world of awe-inspiring beauty and historical significance.

Genres: Travel, History, Architecture

Themes: Cultural heritage, Architectural marvels, Historical significance
