Synopsis:
In "The French Revolution: A Short History," Robert Matteson Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in history. From the opulent courts of Versailles to the blood-soaked streets of Paris, Johnston vividly portrays the events and personalities that shaped the French Revolution. Through his engaging narrative, readers will witness the rise of the revolutionaries, the fall of the monarchy, and the birth of a new era that forever changed the course of France and the world.

Genres:
Historical Non-fiction, Biography, Political History

Themes:
Revolution, Power and Corruption, Social Inequality, Ideological Conflict
