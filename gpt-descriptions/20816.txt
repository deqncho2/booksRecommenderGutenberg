Synopsis: 
In "Socialism as it is: A Survey of the World-Wide Revolutionary Movement," William English Walling takes readers on a captivating journey through the global landscape of socialism. From the bustling streets of Paris to the remote villages of Russia, Walling explores the diverse manifestations of this revolutionary ideology. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, he delves into the lives of passionate activists, the struggles they face, and the impact they have on society. As Walling uncovers the complexities and contradictions of socialism, readers are left questioning their own beliefs and pondering the potential for a more equitable world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Science, History

Themes: Socialism, Revolution, Activism, Equality, Ideology
