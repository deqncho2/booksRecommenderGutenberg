Synopsis: In 'A Danish Parsonage' by John Fulford Vicary, follow the captivating journey of a young Englishman who finds himself in a remote Danish village, where he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and the haunting presence of the past. As he delves deeper into the lives of the villagers, he uncovers a dark history that threatens to unravel the very fabric of their community. With each revelation, the young man must confront his own demons and make choices that will forever change his life. This atmospheric and suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth that lies hidden within the Danish parsonage.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Forbidden Love, Redemption, Identity
