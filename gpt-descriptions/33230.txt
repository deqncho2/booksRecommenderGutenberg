Synopsis: In 'The Gray Mask' by Camp, Wadsworth, a mysterious figure known as the Gray Mask emerges in London, orchestrating a series of daring crimes that baffle the police and captivate the public's imagination. As the city becomes consumed by fear and intrigue, a young woman named Rosalie strives to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Gray Mask, risking everything in a dangerous game of cat and mouse. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Justice
