Synopsis: 

In 'Domestic Life in Virginia in the Seventeenth Century' by Annie Lash Jester, readers are transported back in time to the captivating world of colonial Virginia. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Jester paints a rich and immersive picture of the daily lives, struggles, and triumphs of the early settlers. From the challenges of building a new society to the complexities of family dynamics and the clash of cultures, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the domestic sphere of seventeenth-century Virginia.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Colonial history, Social history

Themes: Colonial life, Family dynamics, Cultural clash, Social structure
