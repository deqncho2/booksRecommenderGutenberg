Synopsis: When Patty Went to College by Jean Webster is a delightful coming-of-age novel that follows the adventures of Patty Wyatt as she embarks on her journey to higher education. Leaving behind her small town and close-knit family, Patty enters a world of new experiences, friendships, and romantic entanglements. With her wit, charm, and determination, Patty navigates the challenges of college life, discovering her own passions and aspirations along the way. As she embraces the opportunities and pitfalls of independence, Patty's journey becomes a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Independence, Pursuit of dreams
