Synopsis: 
In 'The Song of Roland', a legendary medieval epic, the valiant knight Roland finds himself at the center of a treacherous battle against a formidable enemy. As he faces overwhelming odds, Roland's unwavering loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions of epic battles, political intrigue, and personal sacrifice, this timeless tale explores the complexities of honor, duty, and the consequences of pride.

Genres: Epic poetry, Chivalric romance, Historical fiction

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Bravery, Betrayal, Pride, Sacrifice
