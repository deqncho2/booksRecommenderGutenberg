Synopsis: In 'Children's Classics in Dramatic Form, A Reader for the Fourth Grade', Augusta Stevenson brings to life beloved children's classics through engaging and captivating dramatic adaptations. From the enchanting adventures of Alice in Wonderland to the thrilling escapades of Tom Sawyer, this collection transports young readers into the imaginative worlds of timeless stories. Each play is carefully crafted to retain the essence of the original tales while making them accessible and enjoyable for fourth-grade readers. With lively dialogue and vivid descriptions, Stevenson's adaptations are sure to ignite the imagination and spark a love for literature in young minds.

Genres: Children's Literature, Drama, Adaptations

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Friendship, Coming of Age
