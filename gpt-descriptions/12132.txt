Synopsis: In 'Three at Table: The Lady of the Barge and Others, Part 12,' W.W. Jacobs weaves together three captivating tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From a mysterious dinner party with unexpected guests to a chilling encounter with a supernatural entity, each story is filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate through their eerie and unpredictable circumstances, they must confront their deepest fears and make choices that will forever alter their lives. Prepare to be enthralled by this collection of thrilling and suspenseful tales that will leave you wanting more.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Suspense

Themes: Fear, Choices, Supernatural
