Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the fascinating history of the Bristol Royal Mail in this captivating book by Robert Charles Tombs. Delve into the world of post, telegraph, and telephone as you uncover the secrets and stories behind one of Britain's most iconic institutions. From the humble beginnings of mail delivery to the revolutionary advancements in communication technology, this book takes you on a journey through time, revealing the triumphs, challenges, and innovations that shaped the Bristol Royal Mail. Prepare to be enthralled by tales of daring postal workers, groundbreaking inventions, and the ever-evolving landscape of communication. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Technology, British History

Themes: Communication, Innovation, Postal Service, Technological Advancements
