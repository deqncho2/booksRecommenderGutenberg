Synopsis: In 'Out with Garibaldi: A story of the liberation of Italy', George Alfred Henty takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the tumultuous period of Italy's struggle for independence. Set against the backdrop of the mid-19th century, the story follows the adventures of a young Englishman who finds himself caught up in the fervor of Garibaldi's revolutionary movement. From daring escapades and battles to unexpected alliances and personal growth, this gripping tale offers a vivid portrayal of the fight for freedom and the indomitable spirit of the Italian people.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Identity, Freedom, Loyalty
