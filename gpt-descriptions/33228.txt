Synopsis: In 'A Master Hand: The Story of a Crime' by Dallas, Richard, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of seemingly unrelated crimes occur in quick succession. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that lead him down a dark and twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective realizes that the true mastermind behind the crimes may be closer than he ever imagined. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
