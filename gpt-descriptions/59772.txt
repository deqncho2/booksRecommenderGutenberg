Synopsis: 

In 'Convict B 14: A Novel' by R. K. Weekes, a gripping tale unfolds as the reader is transported to the harsh and unforgiving world of a maximum-security prison. The story follows the life of an enigmatic inmate, known only as Convict B 14, who finds himself caught in a web of secrets, betrayal, and redemption. As the plot unravels, the reader is taken on a rollercoaster ride of suspense and unexpected twists, as Convict B 14's past and present collide in a battle for survival. 

Genres: Crime Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Secrets, Survival
