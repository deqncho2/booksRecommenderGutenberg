Synopsis: In 'The Sepoy' by Edmund Candler, a young Indian soldier finds himself torn between loyalty to his British superiors and his own people during the tumultuous period of the Indian Rebellion of 1857. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, betrayal, and identity, the sepoy must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Colonialism, Rebellion
