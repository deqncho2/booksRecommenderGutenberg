Synopsis: In 'The Eliminator; or, Skeleton Keys to Sacerdotal Secrets' by Richard B. Westbrook, a mysterious figure known as The Eliminator embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover hidden secrets within the world of the clergy. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of religious institutions, he uncovers a web of deceit, corruption, and forbidden knowledge that threatens to shake the very foundations of faith. With danger lurking at every turn, The Eliminator must navigate treacherous paths and face formidable adversaries to bring the truth to light. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of faith and the lengths some will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Religion, Secrecy
