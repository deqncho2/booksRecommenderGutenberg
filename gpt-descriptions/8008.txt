Synopsis: In the eighth book of the King James version of the Bible, we are introduced to Ruth, a young woman who finds herself in a foreign land after a series of tragic events. Determined to support her widowed mother-in-law, Ruth embarks on a journey that will test her faith, resilience, and loyalty. As she navigates the challenges of a new culture and faces unexpected opportunities, Ruth's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, ultimately revealing the power of love, redemption, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Religious fiction, Historical fiction, Drama

Themes: Faith, Loyalty, Redemption, Love, Resilience
