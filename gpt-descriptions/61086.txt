Synopsis: In 'Matthew on Money' by Keister, Lawrence, a young man named Matthew embarks on a thrilling journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the hidden corners of the financial world. As he delves deeper into the realm of money and power, Matthew uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel his own life. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of wealth and the lengths one will go to attain it.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Ambition, Morality
