Synopsis: In "Annie o' the Banks o' Dee" by Gordon Stables, a young girl named Annie finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city of Aberdeen and the tranquil countryside along the banks of the Dee River. As she navigates the challenges of growing up, Annie discovers a hidden secret that will change her life forever. With a touch of mystery and a dash of adventure, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Love
