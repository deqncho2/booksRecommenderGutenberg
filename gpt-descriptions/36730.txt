Synopsis: In 'Occultism and Common-Sense' by Willson, Beckles, a skeptical journalist delves into the mysterious world of occultism, uncovering secrets that challenge his rationality and force him to question the boundaries of reality. As he encounters enigmatic individuals and witnesses inexplicable phenomena, he must confront his own beliefs and confront the possibility that there may be more to the world than meets the eye.

Genres: Mystery, Occult Fiction, Supernatural Thriller

Themes: Skepticism vs. Belief, Exploration of the Unknown, Perception of Reality
