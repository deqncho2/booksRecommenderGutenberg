Synopsis: In 'Frank Before Vicksburg: The Gun-Boat Series' by Castlemon, Harry, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside Frank, a young and courageous protagonist. Set during the American Civil War, Frank finds himself aboard a gun-boat, navigating treacherous waters and facing the perils of battle. As he encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely alliances, Frank's determination and bravery are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover Frank's fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
