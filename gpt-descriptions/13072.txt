Synopsis: In 'Common Sense, How to Exercise It', Yoritomo-Tashi takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of the human mind, exploring the power of common sense and its potential to transform our lives. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and practical advice, the author unveils the secrets to harnessing this often overlooked faculty, guiding readers towards a deeper understanding of themselves and the world around them. As readers delve into the pages of this enlightening work, they will discover a wealth of wisdom that can empower them to navigate life's challenges with clarity and confidence.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Psychology

Themes: Common sense, Self-awareness, Personal growth, Decision-making
