Synopsis: In 'The Slanderers' by Deeping, Warwick, a small English village is shaken by a scandalous rumor that threatens to tear apart the community. As the whispers grow louder and the accusations become more vicious, the lives of the villagers are turned upside down. Amidst the chaos, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and the true nature of human nature is exposed. Will the truth prevail or will the power of slander destroy everything they hold dear?

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Rumor and gossip, betrayal, the consequences of lies, the fragility of reputation
