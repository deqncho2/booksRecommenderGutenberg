Synopsis: In 'A Happy Boy' by Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson, we follow the life of Oyvind, a young boy growing up in a small Norwegian village. From his innocent childhood adventures to his coming of age, the story takes us on a journey filled with love, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams. As Oyvind navigates the challenges of life, he learns valuable lessons about loyalty, perseverance, and the power of forgiveness. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and find true happiness? Genres: Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Romance. Themes: Love, Friendship, Dreams, Loyalty, Perseverance, Forgiveness.
