Synopsis: In 'The Street That Wasn't There', a small town is plagued by a mysterious street that appears and disappears at will, leading its residents on a journey of discovery and wonder. As they delve into the secrets of this enigmatic street, they uncover a world beyond their imagination, filled with strange creatures and hidden truths. With each encounter, the residents must confront their own beliefs and question the nature of reality itself. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Perception of reality, Exploration of the unknown, Existentialism
