Synopsis: 
'By the Sea, and Other Verses' is a captivating collection of poetry by H. Lavinia Baily, also known as Hannah Lavinia. This enchanting compilation takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences, exploring the beauty of nature, the depths of human emotions, and the mysteries of life. From the tranquil shores of the sea to the bustling streets of the city, each poem paints vivid imagery and evokes a range of feelings. With lyrical language and heartfelt expressions, Baily's verses invite readers to reflect on the wonders of the world and the complexities of the human soul.

Genres: Poetry, Nature, Emotion

Themes: Nature's beauty, Human emotions, Life's mysteries
