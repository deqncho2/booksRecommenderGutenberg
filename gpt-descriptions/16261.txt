Synopsis: 
In "Some Chinese Ghosts" Lafcadio Hearn takes us on a captivating journey through the mystical world of Chinese folklore. From haunting spirits and vengeful ghosts to forbidden love and supernatural encounters, this collection of eerie tales will transport readers to a realm where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. With each story, Hearn masterfully weaves together elements of horror, romance, and the supernatural, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the shadows of ancient China.

Genres: 
Horror, Supernatural, Romance

Themes: 
Death and the afterlife, Forbidden love, Cultural beliefs and superstitions
