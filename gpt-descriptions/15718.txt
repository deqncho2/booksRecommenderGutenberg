Synopsis: 
In "How To Write Special Feature Articles," Willard Grosvenor Bleyer offers a comprehensive guide for aspiring writers who dream of contributing to popular magazines and newspaper sections. With practical advice and insightful tips, Bleyer takes readers on a journey through the art of crafting engaging and captivating articles. From understanding the needs of readers to conducting thorough research and mastering the art of storytelling, this handbook equips writers with the tools they need to succeed in the competitive world of journalism. Whether you're a reporter, correspondent, or free-lance writer, this book is an invaluable resource that will inspire and empower you to create special feature articles that leave a lasting impact.

Genres: Non-fiction, Writing, Journalism

Themes: Writing techniques, Journalism skills, Magazine writing
