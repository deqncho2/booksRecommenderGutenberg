Synopsis: In 'Minor Tibetan Texts: 1. The song of the Eastern Snow-mountain', readers are transported to the mystical and enchanting world of Tibet. Through vivid descriptions and poetic verses, the author takes us on a journey to the majestic Eastern Snow-mountain, where secrets and ancient wisdom await. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to a cast of intriguing characters and witness their encounters with the supernatural and the divine. This captivating tale is filled with mystery, spirituality, and the exploration of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Spiritual

Themes: Tibetan culture, Mysticism, Nature, Spirituality
