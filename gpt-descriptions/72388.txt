Synopsis: In 'The House' by Bordeaux, Henry, a young couple moves into a seemingly perfect house in a quiet neighborhood. However, as they settle in, they begin to uncover dark secrets hidden within the walls of their new home. Strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena lead them on a chilling journey to unravel the mysteries of the house, testing their sanity and their relationship along the way.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Haunting, Secrets, Psychological suspense
