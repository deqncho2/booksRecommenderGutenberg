Synopsis: 

In 'The Oyster: Where, How and When to Find, Breed, Cook and Eat It', Eustace Clare Grenville Murray takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of oysters. From the mysterious depths of the ocean to the art of cultivating these delectable creatures, Murray explores every aspect of oyster hunting, breeding, cooking, and savoring. With vivid descriptions and practical advice, he unveils the secrets of finding the finest oysters, shares mouthwatering recipes, and delves into the cultural significance of this beloved delicacy. Whether you're a seasoned oyster enthusiast or a curious food lover, this book will leave you craving for more knowledge about these fascinating bivalves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, Travel

Themes: Culinary exploration, Seafood appreciation, Sustainability
