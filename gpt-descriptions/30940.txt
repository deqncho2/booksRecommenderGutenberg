Synopsis: In the small town of Liberty, Peggy Owen finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Peggy must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a changing America, this captivating novel explores the complexities of self-discovery and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Independence, Tradition, Love and Relationships
