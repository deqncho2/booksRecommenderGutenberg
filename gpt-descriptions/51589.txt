Synopsis: In the post-apocalyptic world of 'The Rag and Bone Men', a group of scavengers known as the Rag and Bone Men roam the desolate landscape, searching for valuable remnants of the past. When they stumble upon a mysterious artifact that could hold the key to humanity's survival, they find themselves caught in a dangerous game of power and deception. As they navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected challenges, the Rag and Bone Men must confront their own pasts and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Morality
