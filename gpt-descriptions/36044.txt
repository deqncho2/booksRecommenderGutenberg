Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of Canada, a young boy named White Otter embarks on a remarkable journey of self-discovery and survival. Raised by his indigenous tribe, White Otter must navigate the challenges of his changing world, facing both the encroachment of settlers and the ancient spirits that dwell in the land. As he grapples with his identity and the secrets of his past, White Otter's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test. This captivating tale of adventure and resilience will transport readers to a vivid and untamed landscape, where the bonds of family, tradition, and the power of nature intertwine.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, Nature, Resilience
