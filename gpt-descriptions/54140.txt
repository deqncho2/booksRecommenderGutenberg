Synopsis: 

In "The Exeter Road: the story of the west of England highway," Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and significance of the famous Exeter Road. This ancient highway, stretching from London to Exeter, holds secrets and tales that have shaped the region for centuries. Harper's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring to life the fascinating characters, events, and landscapes that have unfolded along this historic route. From Roman times to the present day, the Exeter Road has witnessed triumphs, tragedies, and everything in between, making it a captivating subject for anyone interested in the rich tapestry of British history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel literature

Themes: History, Transportation, Cultural heritage
