Synopsis: 
In 'The Country Housewife and Lady's Director in the Management of a House, and the Delights and Profits of a Farm' by Richard Bradley, readers are transported to the enchanting world of rural life. This comprehensive guide offers invaluable advice on everything from household management to the joys and benefits of running a farm. With its practical tips and insightful anecdotes, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to embrace the charms of country living.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Lifestyle

Themes: Rural life, Household management, Farming, Self-sufficiency
