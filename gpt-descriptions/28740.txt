Synopsis: 
In 'The Girls of Central High in Camp; Or, the Old Professor's Secret' by Morrison, Gertrude W., the girls of Central High School embark on an exciting summer adventure at a campsite near a mysterious old professor's house. As they explore the surrounding woods, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. With their friendship and courage tested, the girls must unravel the old professor's secret before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
