Synopsis: 

In 'Sacred and legendary art, volume 1 (of 2)', Mrs. Jameson takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of art, exploring the legends and representations of angels, archangels, evangelists, apostles, doctors of the church, and St. Mary Magdalene. Through vivid descriptions and detailed analysis, the author unveils the rich symbolism and artistic interpretations of these sacred figures as depicted in various forms of fine art. From the ethereal beauty of angelic beings to the profound influence of the apostles, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of the spiritual and artistic significance of these revered subjects.

Genres: Art History, Religious Studies, Cultural Studies
Themes: Spirituality, Symbolism, Artistic Interpretation
