Synopsis:
In "Hands Up; or, Thirty-Five Years of Detective Life in the Mountains and on the Plains: Reminiscences," General D. J. Cook takes us on a thrilling journey through his remarkable career as the Chief of the Rocky Mountains Detective Association. From the treacherous mountains to the vast plains, Cook shares his captivating experiences as a detective, recounting the most daring and dangerous cases he encountered during his thirty-five years on the job. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this memoir offers a unique glimpse into the world of crime-solving in the wild and untamed American West.

Genres: Memoir, Crime, Western

Themes: Adventure, Justice, Survival
