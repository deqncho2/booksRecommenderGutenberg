Synopsis: In "One thousand dollars a day: Studies in practical economics," Adeline Knapp takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of economics, offering a unique perspective on the practical applications of economic principles. Through a series of engaging case studies and real-life examples, Knapp explores the fascinating ways in which economic decisions impact our daily lives, from personal finance to global markets. With a blend of wit, insight, and relatable anecdotes, this book challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to think critically about the economic forces that shape our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Business

Themes: Practical economics, Personal finance, Global markets, Decision-making, Economic impact
