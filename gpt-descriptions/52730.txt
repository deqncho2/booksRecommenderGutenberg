Synopsis: In 'Portraits in Plaster, from the Collection of Laurence Hutton', Laurence Hutton takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of plaster casts, revealing the stories and secrets behind each unique piece. From famous historical figures to renowned artists, Hutton's collection of plaster portraits unveils the lives and legacies of these individuals, shedding light on their triumphs, struggles, and the impact they left on the world. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be transported to different eras and places, immersing themselves in the rich tapestry of human history and creativity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art History, Biography

Themes: History, Art, Legacy, Identity
