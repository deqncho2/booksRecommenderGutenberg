Synopsis: In 'Pirates: A comedy in one act', chaos ensues when a group of bumbling pirates attempt to carry out a daring heist. With their questionable skills and hilarious antics, these pirates find themselves in one absurd situation after another, leaving the audience in stitches. As the plot unfolds, unexpected twists and turns keep everyone on their toes, making for a side-splitting comedy that will leave you wanting more.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Adventure, Humor, Ineptitude
