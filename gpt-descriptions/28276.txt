Synopsis: In 'The End of the Rainbow' by Mary Esther Miller MacGregor, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within her ancestral home, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and long-held grudges. With each revelation, Lily's world is turned upside down, and she must confront the consequences of her family's actions. Will she be able to find redemption and forge her own path, or will the past continue to haunt her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Redemption, Identity
