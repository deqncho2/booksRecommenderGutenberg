Synopsis: In "An Answer to a Question that Nobody thinks of, viz., But what if the Queen should Die?" by Daniel Defoe, the author delves into a hypothetical scenario that explores the consequences of the Queen's death. Through a series of thought-provoking arguments and insightful analysis, Defoe presents a unique perspective on the potential political, social, and economic upheaval that could occur in such a situation. This intriguing work challenges conventional thinking and offers a captivating exploration of the unknown.

Genres: Political fiction, speculative fiction, essay

Themes: Political stability, succession, societal impact
