Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln and the Union: A Chronicle of the Embattled North," Nathaniel W. Stephenson takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the American Civil War. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Stephenson delves into the life and leadership of Abraham Lincoln, exploring the challenges he faced as he fought to preserve the Union amidst a divided nation. From the early days of Lincoln's presidency to the decisive battles that shaped the course of history, this chronicle offers a gripping account of the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of the embattled North.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Leadership, Unity, Conflict, Sacrifice
