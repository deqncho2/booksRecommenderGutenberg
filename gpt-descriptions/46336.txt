Synopsis: In 'Seven Mohave Myths', A. L. Kroeber delves into the rich folklore of the Mohave people, presenting a collection of captivating myths that transport readers to a world of ancient legends and supernatural beings. From tales of heroic warriors to stories of powerful spirits, each myth offers a glimpse into the Mohave culture and their deep connection with the natural world. As readers journey through these enchanting narratives, they will be captivated by the Mohave's unique perspective on creation, the afterlife, and the interplay between humans and the divine. 

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural preservation, Spiritual beliefs, Oral tradition
