Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Jockey Hollow: Arden Blake Mystery Series #2' by Cleo F. Garis, young detective Arden Blake finds herself entangled in a puzzling case when she stumbles upon a hidden message in an old diary. As she delves deeper into the secrets of Jockey Hollow, a historic Revolutionary War site, Arden uncovers a web of intrigue, deception, and long-buried mysteries. With the help of her loyal friends, she must decipher the clues and unravel the truth before it's too late. This thrilling and suspenseful mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within Jockey Hollow.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Friendship and loyalty, Solving puzzles and unraveling mysteries
