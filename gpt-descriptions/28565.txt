Synopsis: 
In 'Good Luck' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Muriel is faced with a series of unexpected events that turn her life upside down. When she receives a mysterious letter, she embarks on a journey that takes her to a remote village in Scotland, where she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden identities. As Muriel delves deeper into the enigma surrounding her, she discovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront her own past. With danger lurking at every corner, Muriel must navigate through treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will determine her fate. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption
