Synopsis: 
'Caruso and Tetrazzini on the Art of Singing' is a captivating guide that brings together the wisdom and expertise of two legendary opera singers, Enrico Caruso and Luisa Tetrazzini. Through a series of insightful conversations, they delve into the secrets of vocal technique, stage presence, and the emotional depth required to truly captivate an audience. With anecdotes from their own illustrious careers, Caruso and Tetrazzini provide invaluable advice and practical exercises for aspiring singers, as well as a fascinating glimpse into the world of opera. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Biography

Themes: Vocal technique, Performance art, Artistic expression
