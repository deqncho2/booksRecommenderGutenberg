Synopsis: In 'The Desert Drum: 1905' by Hichens, Robert, a young Englishwoman named Ethel is drawn into a mysterious and captivating world during her travels in Egypt. As she becomes entangled in the secrets of the desert, she encounters a charismatic and enigmatic figure who holds the key to a hidden treasure. Ethel's journey takes her through the vast and treacherous landscapes of the desert, where danger and desire intertwine, leading her to question her own identity and the true nature of love.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration of identity, Forbidden love, Quest for treasure
