Synopsis: In "Life of Charles T. Walker, D.D.: ("The Black Spurgeon") Pastor Mt. Olivet Baptist Church, New York City," Silas Xavier Floyd delves into the captivating life of Charles T. Walker, a remarkable figure known as "The Black Spurgeon." From his humble beginnings to his rise as a prominent pastor in New York City, this biography unveils the inspiring journey of a man who overcame adversity and made a lasting impact on his community. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Floyd paints a vivid portrait of Walker's struggles, triumphs, and unwavering faith, offering readers a glimpse into the life of a true trailblazer.

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Perseverance, Faith, Social Impact
