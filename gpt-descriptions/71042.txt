Synopsis: 
In 'Poster advertising: $b Being a talk on the subject of posting as an advertising medium, with helpful hints and sensible suggestions to poster advertisers, and with thirty-two pages of full color reproductions of posters used by national advertisers', George Henry Edward Hawkins takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of poster advertising. With a wealth of knowledge and experience, Hawkins provides valuable insights and practical advice to poster advertisers, while showcasing thirty-two pages of stunning full-color reproductions of posters used by national advertisers. This book is a must-read for anyone interested in the art and effectiveness of poster advertising.

Genres: Non-fiction, Advertising, Marketing

Themes: Poster advertising, Advertising medium, Tips and suggestions, National advertisers, Visual communication
