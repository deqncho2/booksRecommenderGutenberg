Synopsis: In 'The Voice' by Margaret Wade Campbell Deland, a young woman named Marcia is haunted by a mysterious voice that only she can hear. As she navigates her way through life, the voice becomes both a source of comfort and a source of turmoil, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. With its blend of mystery, romance, and psychological depth, 'The Voice' is a captivating exploration of the power of inner voices and the impact they can have on our lives.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Inner voices
