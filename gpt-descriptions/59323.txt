Synopsis: In 'Bleedback' by Winston K. Marks, a gripping cyberpunk thriller, the world is on the brink of chaos as a new technology called "bleedback" emerges. This technology allows people to experience the memories and emotions of others, blurring the lines between reality and virtuality. When a renowned scientist mysteriously dies, a former detective turned private investigator is drawn into a dangerous web of conspiracy and deception. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of this futuristic society, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Cyberpunk, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Identity and memory, Corruption and power
