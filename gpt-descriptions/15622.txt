Synopsis: 

In the second edition of 'Handbook on Japanning', author William Norman Brown delves into the intricate art of japanning, providing comprehensive guidance on its application to various materials such as ironware, tinware, and wood. This edition also includes additional sections on tinplating and galvanizing, expanding the reader's knowledge and skills in these related techniques. With detailed instructions, practical tips, and historical insights, this handbook offers a valuable resource for both beginners and experienced craftsmen seeking to master the art of japanning.

Genres: Craftsmanship, Reference, Historical

Themes: Artistic techniques, Material transformation, Historical preservation
