Synopsis: 
'International Cartoons of the War' takes readers on a captivating journey through the power of cartoons during times of conflict. This thought-provoking collection showcases a diverse range of cartoons from around the world, each offering a unique perspective on war and its consequences. From poignant illustrations that depict the human cost of conflict to satirical cartoons that challenge political leaders, this book explores the profound impact of cartoons as a medium for social commentary and protest. Delve into the pages of 'International Cartoons of the War' and discover the extraordinary ability of cartoons to capture the essence of war and inspire change.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, History

Themes: War, Social Commentary, Political Satire
