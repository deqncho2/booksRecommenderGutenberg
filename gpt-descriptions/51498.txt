Synopsis: 
In 'Bad Memory' by Fahy, Patrick, a young woman wakes up in a hospital bed with no recollection of who she is or how she got there. As she embarks on a journey to uncover her past, she discovers shocking secrets and a dangerous conspiracy that threatens her very existence. With each clue she uncovers, she becomes more entangled in a web of deceit and must rely on her instincts to survive. As the truth slowly unravels, she realizes that her forgotten memories hold the key to her future, and she must confront her past in order to reclaim her identity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Memory, Deception, Survival
