Synopsis: In 'The Air Pirate' by Thorne, Guy, a notorious air pirate known as the "Sky Phantom" embarks on a daring adventure across the skies, leaving a trail of mystery and intrigue in his wake. As he evades capture and outwits his pursuers, the true motives behind his audacious acts become increasingly enigmatic. With a cast of captivating characters and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following the Sky Phantom's every move.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Steampunk

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Morality
