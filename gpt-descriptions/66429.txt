Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the Thirteenth, First Somerset, or the Prince Albert's Regiment of Light Infantry," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this esteemed regiment from 1685 to 1848. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the rich history of this regiment, offering a fascinating account of their triumphs and challenges. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he brings to life the bravery, camaraderie, and sacrifices of the soldiers who served in this renowned regiment.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Bravery, Sacrifice
