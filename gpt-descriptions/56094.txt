Synopsis: 

'Allegheny Episodes: Folk Lore and Legends Collected in Northern and Western Pennsylvania' by Henry W. Shoemaker takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and legends of the Allegheny region in Pennsylvania. From mysterious creatures lurking in the dense forests to tales of lost treasures and haunted places, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world where the line between reality and myth blurs. As the author delves into the depths of local folklore, he uncovers hidden truths and uncanny connections that will leave readers both enthralled and intrigued.

Genres: Folklore, Legends, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mystery, Supernatural, Cultural Heritage
