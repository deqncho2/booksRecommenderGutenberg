Synopsis: 
In the Border Country follows the story of a young woman named Mary, who finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city life of New York and the serene countryside of Vermont. When she unexpectedly inherits a farm in the border country, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. As she navigates the challenges of rural life, she encounters a cast of colorful characters who teach her valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the importance of finding one's place in the world. With its vivid descriptions and engaging narrative, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the contrasting landscapes of the border country.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Self-discovery, Rural life
