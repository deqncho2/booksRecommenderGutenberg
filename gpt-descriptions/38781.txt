Synopsis: In 'A Russian Gentleman' by Aksakov, we follow the life of a young nobleman named Ivan, who finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his desire for personal freedom. Set in 19th-century Russia, this captivating novel takes us on a journey through love, betrayal, and the struggle to find one's true identity. As Ivan navigates the complexities of society and grapples with his own inner conflicts, the reader is drawn into a world of secrets, political intrigue, and the timeless quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Duty vs. Personal Freedom, Love and Betrayal
