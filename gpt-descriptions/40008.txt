Synopsis: 
In "The Autobiography of a Cornish Smuggler: (Captain Harry Carter, of Prussia Cove) 1749-1809," Captain Harry Carter takes us on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of smuggling in 18th-century Cornwall. From his humble beginnings as a fisherman's son to becoming one of the most notorious smugglers in the region, Carter's gripping tale is filled with daring escapades, close calls with the law, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates treacherous waters and evades capture, Carter's story sheds light on the hidden world of smuggling and the lengths people will go to for survival and prosperity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Pursuit of Wealth, Justice
