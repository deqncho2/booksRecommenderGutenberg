Synopsis:
In 'The Golden Calf' by M. E. Braddon, a mysterious and captivating stranger arrives in a small English village, bringing with him an air of intrigue and a hidden past. As the villagers become enthralled by his charm and charisma, they soon discover that beneath his polished exterior lies a web of secrets and deceit. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more apparent, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes:
Deception, Identity, Morality
