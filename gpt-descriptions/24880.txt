Synopsis: In 'The Wreck of the Titan: or, Futility' by Robertson, Morgan, a magnificent ocean liner named the Titan embarks on its maiden voyage, touted as unsinkable and luxurious. However, as the story unfolds, the Titan faces a series of eerie and uncanny events that seem to foreshadow its tragic fate. With a cast of intriguing characters and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale explores the limits of human hubris and the unpredictable forces of nature.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Hubris, Fate, Man vs. Nature
