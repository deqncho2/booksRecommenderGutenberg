Synopsis: In "The Camp in the Foot-Hills; or, Oscar on Horseback" by Castlemon, Harry, young Oscar embarks on an exciting adventure in the rugged foot-hills. As he explores the wilderness on horseback, he encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely friendships. With danger lurking in the shadows, Oscar must rely on his courage and resourcefulness to navigate through the untamed landscape. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and prove his mettle in the face of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Western

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
