Synopsis: 
In 'On Horsemanship', Xenophon takes us on a captivating journey into the world of horsemanship, revealing the secrets of training and caring for horses. Through vivid anecdotes and practical advice, he explores the art of selecting the perfect horse, the importance of a strong bond between rider and mount, and the techniques for achieving harmony and grace in equestrian pursuits. As Xenophon shares his wisdom, readers will be enthralled by the timeless lessons that transcend the ages, making this a must-read for both horse enthusiasts and those seeking to understand the profound connection between humans and animals.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Classical literature, Animal care

Themes: 
Horsemanship, Animal training, Human-animal bond
