Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of John Leech' is a collection of works by the renowned British caricaturist and illustrator, John Leech. This compilation showcases Leech's exceptional talent through a diverse range of illustrations, including political cartoons, social satires, and humorous sketches. From his witty commentary on Victorian society to his clever depictions of famous literary characters, Leech's artistry captivates readers and offers a unique glimpse into the cultural and political landscape of his time.

Genres: 
Art, Illustration, Satire

Themes: 
Victorian society, Politics, Humor
