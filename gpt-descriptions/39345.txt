Synopsis: In 'Mitchelhurst Place: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 2)' by Margaret Veley, a mysterious inheritance leads the protagonist, Lucy, to the grand estate of Mitchelhurst Place. As she delves into the secrets of the house and its enigmatic occupants, Lucy finds herself entangled in a web of family drama, hidden pasts, and forbidden love. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Lucy must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty and betrayal to uncover the truth that lies within the walls of Mitchelhurst Place.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Inheritance, Betrayal
