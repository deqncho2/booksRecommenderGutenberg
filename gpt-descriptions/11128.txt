Synopsis:
When a wealthy diamond merchant is found dead in his office with a mysterious red thumb mark imprinted on his desk, the police are baffled. Enter Dr. John Thorndyke, a brilliant forensic scientist, who is determined to solve the perplexing case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Thorndyke uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With the clock ticking, can Thorndyke unravel the truth behind the red thumb mark before it's too late?

Genres:
Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes:
Forensic Science, Deception, Justice
