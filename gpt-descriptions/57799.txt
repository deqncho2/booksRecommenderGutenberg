Synopsis: In 'The House of Arden: A Story for Children', two siblings, Edred and Elfrida, stumble upon a magical secret that transports them back in time to the days of their ancestors. As they navigate through the past, they encounter mysterious characters, embark on thrilling adventures, and uncover hidden treasures. With the power to change their own destinies, the siblings must make difficult choices and face unexpected challenges. This enchanting tale of time travel and discovery will captivate young readers and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Family bonds, Self-discovery
