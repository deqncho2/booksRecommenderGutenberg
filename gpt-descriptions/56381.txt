Synopsis: In 'Guy Harris, the Runaway' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Guy finds himself caught in a web of danger and adventure when he decides to run away from home. As he embarks on a thrilling journey, he encounters unexpected allies and faces numerous challenges that test his courage and resilience. With each twist and turn, Guy's determination to find his place in the world is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles and discover his true identity?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Bravery, Friendship
