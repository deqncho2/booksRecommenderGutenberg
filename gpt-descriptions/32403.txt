Synopsis: In 'Human Error' by Raymond F. Jones, a groundbreaking scientific experiment takes an unexpected turn, leading to a series of catastrophic events that threaten the very fabric of humanity. As the consequences of this error unfold, a group of brilliant scientists must race against time to find a solution and prevent the irreversible destruction of the world as they know it. With high-stakes action, mind-bending twists, and thought-provoking ethical dilemmas, 'Human Error' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology gone wrong, Moral responsibility, Consequences of scientific advancements
