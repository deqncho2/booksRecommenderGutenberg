Synopsis: In the Sphere of Time takes readers on a mind-bending journey through parallel universes, where a group of scientists stumble upon a mysterious artifact that grants them the ability to travel through time. As they navigate the complexities of time travel, they soon discover that their actions have far-reaching consequences, leading to a thrilling race against time to save not only their own lives but the very fabric of reality itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Thriller

Themes: The nature of time, the consequences of our actions, the fragility of reality
