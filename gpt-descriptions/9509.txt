Synopsis: In the small town of Magnolia Springs, secrets run deep and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As dark secrets are unearthed and long-held grudges resurface, the residents of Magnolia Springs must confront their own demons and make choices that will determine their fate. With its gripping plot and complex characters, 'Southern Lights and Shadows' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twisted underbelly of a seemingly idyllic Southern town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Southern Gothic

Themes: Secrets and lies, morality, redemption, the duality of human nature
