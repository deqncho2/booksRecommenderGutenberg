Synopsis: 
In "The Clicking of Cuthbert," a collection of humorous short stories by P.G. Wodehouse, the world of golf becomes the backdrop for a series of entertaining and unexpected events. From the struggles of a young golfer trying to impress his love interest to the hilarious encounters between eccentric characters on the golf course, each story presents a delightful blend of wit, romance, and absurdity. As the characters navigate the challenges of the sport and the complexities of human relationships, readers are taken on a delightful journey filled with laughter and unexpected twists.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love and romance, Sportsmanship, Social class, Satire
