Synopsis: 

'For Gold or Soul? The Story of a Great Department Store' by Sheldon, Lurana is a captivating tale that takes readers on a journey through the rise and fall of a renowned department store. Set in the bustling city of New York, the story follows the ambitious and enigmatic protagonist as she navigates the cutthroat world of retail, facing numerous challenges and making difficult choices along the way. As the store becomes a symbol of both success and corruption, the narrative delves into the intricate web of relationships, secrets, and betrayals that shape the lives of those involved. With its richly developed characters and intricate plot, this novel offers a mesmerizing exploration of ambition, power, and the price one is willing to pay for success.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Power, Corruption, Relationships
