Synopsis: In "Was It Right to Forgive? A Domestic Romance" by Amelia E. Barr, a young woman named Edith finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for personal happiness. When a long-lost secret threatens to unravel her carefully constructed life, Edith must navigate the complexities of forgiveness, love, and sacrifice. As she grapples with the consequences of her choices, Edith discovers the true meaning of forgiveness and the power it holds to heal and transform lives.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Forgiveness, Family, Sacrifice, Secrets
