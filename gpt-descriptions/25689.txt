Synopsis: In 'The Secret Witness' by Gibbs, George, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious witness comes forward with crucial information about a high-profile crime. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and lies to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
