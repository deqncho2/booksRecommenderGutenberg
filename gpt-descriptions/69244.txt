Synopsis: 

'Via Berlin' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the streets of Berlin, where a chance encounter between two strangers sets off a chain of events that will test their courage, loyalty, and the power of human connection. As secrets unravel and danger lurks around every corner, the protagonists find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, forcing them to confront their pasts and make choices that will determine their futures. With the backdrop of a vibrant and ever-changing city, 'Via Berlin' is a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Trust, Resilience
