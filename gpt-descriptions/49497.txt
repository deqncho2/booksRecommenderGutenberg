Synopsis: 
'Tuscan folk-lore and sketches, together with some other papers' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Tuscan folk tales and traditions. Isabella Mary Anderton weaves together a collection of delightful stories, sketches, and papers that offer a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Tuscany. From mythical creatures and magical encounters to heartwarming tales of love and resilience, this book immerses readers in the captivating folklore of the region. With each page, Anderton invites readers to explore the hidden corners of Tuscany and discover the timeless wisdom and beauty that lies within its folklore.

Genres: 
Folklore, Travel, Cultural Studies

Themes: 
Mythology, Tradition, Cultural Heritage
