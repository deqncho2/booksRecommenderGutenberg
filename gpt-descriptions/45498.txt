Synopsis: 

'History of the Union Jack and Flags of the Empire' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and fascinating history of the iconic Union Jack flag and the various flags that represented the British Empire. From the origins of the Union Jack to its evolution over centuries, this book delves into the stories behind the flags that symbolized the might and influence of the British Empire across the globe. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Cumberland Barlow uncovers the hidden meanings, historical events, and cultural significance behind these flags, offering a unique perspective on the power and legacy of the British Empire.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: National identity, Colonialism, Symbolism
