Synopsis: 

Step back in time and delve into the captivating world of gambling in England with John Ashton's enthralling book, 'The History of Gambling in England'. From the bustling streets of London to the opulent gambling houses of the aristocracy, this meticulously researched account takes readers on a thrilling journey through the highs and lows of England's gambling past. Uncover the secrets of notorious gamblers, witness the evolution of popular games, and explore the social and cultural impact of this timeless pastime. Ashton's vivid storytelling and wealth of historical anecdotes will leave readers spellbound, as they discover the fascinating and often surprising history of gambling in England.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Gambling culture, Social impact, Historical anecdotes
