Synopsis: 
'In Secret' by Robert W. Chambers is a gripping tale that follows the life of a young artist named Louis, who becomes entangled in a mysterious and dangerous love affair. Set in the bohemian art scene of 19th-century Paris, the story takes unexpected twists and turns as Louis finds himself torn between passion and morality. As secrets are unveiled and dark desires come to light, the consequences of his actions threaten to consume him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and obsession, Morality and consequences
