Synopsis: In "A Modern Aladdin; or, The Wonderful Adventures of Oliver Munier: An Extravaganza in Four Acts," Howard Pyle takes readers on a thrilling journey through the fantastical world of Oliver Munier. This captivating extravaganza follows the young protagonist as he stumbles upon a mysterious lamp that grants him incredible powers. As Oliver embarks on a series of extraordinary adventures, he encounters magical creatures, treacherous villains, and unexpected allies. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what awaits Oliver in this enchanting tale.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: Power of imagination, Quest for identity, Triumph of good over evil
