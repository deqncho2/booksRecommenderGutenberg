Synopsis: In 'Cat and Mouse' by Ralph Williams, a cunning detective finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of wits with a notorious criminal mastermind. As the detective races against time to solve a series of puzzling crimes, he becomes increasingly aware that the line between hunter and prey is blurred. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling cat-and-mouse chase will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics
