Synopsis: 
'Martin Eden' by Jack London is a captivating novel that follows the journey of a young sailor named Martin Eden, who dreams of becoming a successful writer. Fueled by his love for Ruth, a beautiful and educated young woman from a higher social class, Martin embarks on a relentless pursuit of knowledge and self-improvement. As he navigates the complexities of society, Martin faces numerous challenges and obstacles that test his determination and resilience. Will Martin's pursuit of success and love lead him to the fulfillment of his dreams, or will he discover a different path altogether?

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: 
Social class, Ambition, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
