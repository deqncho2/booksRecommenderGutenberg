Synopsis: In "A Letter to Grover Cleveland," Lysander Spooner delivers a scathing critique of the false promises made by the newly inaugurated president, exposing the usurpations and crimes committed by lawmakers and judges. Spooner delves into the dire consequences of these actions, which have led to poverty, ignorance, and servitude among the people. Through a compelling and thought-provoking analysis, Spooner challenges the status quo and calls for a reevaluation of the government's role in society.

Genres: Political essay, Historical analysis

Themes: Government accountability, Individual liberty, Socioeconomic inequality
