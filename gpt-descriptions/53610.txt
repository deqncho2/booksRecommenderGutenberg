Synopsis: In "A Sister to Evangeline: Being the Story of Yvonne de Lamourie, and how she went into exile with the villagers of Grand Pré," Yvonne de Lamourie finds herself torn between love and loyalty as she navigates the tumultuous events surrounding the expulsion of the Acadians from their homeland. Set in the picturesque village of Grand Pré, Yvonne's journey takes her through heartbreak, sacrifice, and ultimately, a search for identity and belonging. As she joins the villagers in their exile, Yvonne's resilience and determination are tested, leading her to discover the true meaning of family and the power of community.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Identity and Belonging, Resilience and Sacrifice
