Synopsis: In the 18th edition of 'The Laws and Principles of Whist Stated and Explained,' Cavendish presents a captivating exploration of the popular card game, Whist. Through the use of hands played completely through, the author illustrates an original system that sheds light on the intricacies of the game. As players navigate the rules and strategies, they are drawn into a world of suspense and excitement, where every move holds the potential for victory or defeat. With its comprehensive approach and engaging examples, this edition of 'The Laws and Principles of Whist' is sure to captivate both novice and experienced players alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gaming, Strategy

Themes: Card games, Rules and principles, Strategy
