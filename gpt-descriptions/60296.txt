Synopsis: 
'Tillicums of the Trail: Being Klondike Yarns Told to Canadian Soldiers Overseas by a Sourdough Padre' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and untamed landscapes of the Klondike Gold Rush. As a Sourdough Padre shares his tales with Canadian soldiers stationed overseas, the stories of adventure, perseverance, and the indomitable human spirit come alive. From the treacherous trails of the Yukon to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this collection of Klondike yarns offers a glimpse into a bygone era that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Resilience
