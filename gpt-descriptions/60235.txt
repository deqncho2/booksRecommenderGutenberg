Synopsis: In 'The Seventy's Course in Theology, First Year: Outline History of the Seventy and A Survey of the Books of Holy Scripture', author B.H. Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the Seventy and provides a comprehensive overview of the books of Holy Scripture. From the origins of the Seventy to the significance of each book in the Bible, Roberts delves into the rich tapestry of religious history, offering insights and revelations that will leave readers eager to explore further.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Educational

Themes: Religious history, Scriptural analysis, Spiritual growth
