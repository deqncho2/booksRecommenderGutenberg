Synopsis: In 'An Attic Philosopher in Paris - Volume 1', we follow the journey of a young philosopher named Émile who leaves his small village to seek knowledge and adventure in the bustling city of Paris. As he navigates the vibrant streets and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Émile's philosophical musings and observations provide a unique perspective on the social and cultural aspects of Parisian life. From the struggles of the working class to the opulence of the upper echelons, Émile's experiences shed light on the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of happiness in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Fiction, Philosophical novel, Historical fiction

Themes: Social inequality, Human nature, Pursuit of knowledge and happiness
