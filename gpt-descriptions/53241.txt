Synopsis: In "The Scouring of the White Horse; Or, The Long Vacation Ramble of a London Clerk," a London clerk embarks on a summer adventure that takes him to the picturesque countryside of England. As he explores the idyllic landscapes and encounters a variety of intriguing characters, he becomes entangled in a mysterious local legend surrounding a giant white horse carved into a hillside. With each step he takes, the clerk unravels the secrets of the past, leading him on a thrilling journey of discovery and self-reflection.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of nature, Local legends and folklore, Personal growth and self-discovery
