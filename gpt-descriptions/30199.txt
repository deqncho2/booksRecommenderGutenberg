Synopsis: In the small town of Sac Prairie, a mysterious object falls from the sky, captivating the residents and sparking a series of strange events. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the object, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Human curiosity, Small-town dynamics
