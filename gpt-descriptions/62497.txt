Synopsis: In "Goose Creek Folks: A Story of the Kentucky Mountains," the lives of the humble residents of Goose Creek are vividly portrayed, revealing the joys, struggles, and secrets that shape their close-knit community. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of love, loss, and resilience, where the beauty of the Kentucky mountains serves as a backdrop to the characters' intertwined destinies. Through the eyes of the spirited protagonist, Mary, we witness the power of friendship, the complexities of family dynamics, and the enduring spirit of hope that prevails even in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Appalachian Literature

Themes: Community, Family, Resilience, Love, Secrets
