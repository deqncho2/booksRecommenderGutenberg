Synopsis: In 'Bobby Blake on a Plantation; Or, Lost in the Great Swamp' by Frank A. Warner, young Bobby finds himself in a thrilling adventure when he becomes lost in the mysterious Great Swamp. As he navigates through the treacherous terrain, Bobby encounters unexpected dangers and encounters that test his courage and resourcefulness. With each twist and turn, Bobby's determination to find his way back home grows stronger, leading him to uncover secrets that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Coming of Age
