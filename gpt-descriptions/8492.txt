Synopsis: In the mysterious and haunting world of "The King in Yellow," a forbidden play brings madness and despair to those who dare to read it. This collection of interconnected stories explores the dark and twisted consequences that unfold when the boundaries between reality and fiction blur. From the eerie streets of Paris to the depths of the human psyche, Robert W. Chambers weaves a chilling tapestry of psychological horror that will leave readers questioning their own sanity.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Madness, Forbidden Knowledge, Reality vs. Fiction
