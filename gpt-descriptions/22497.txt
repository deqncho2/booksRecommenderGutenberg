Synopsis: In 'Cab and Caboose: The Story of a Railroad Boy' by Munroe, Kirk, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside young Jack, a boy who dreams of becoming a railroad engineer. From his first encounter with a steam locomotive to his adventures on the tracks, Jack's determination and passion for trains lead him on a path filled with excitement, challenges, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates the world of railroads, Jack learns valuable lessons about courage, perseverance, and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Friendship, Overcoming obstacles
