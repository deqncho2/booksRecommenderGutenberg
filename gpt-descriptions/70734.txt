Synopsis: 

In 'The Skeleton Crew: Wildfire Ned' by Anonymous, a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious fires that seem to be connected to a long-forgotten legend. As the fires escalate and the town's residents grow increasingly fearful, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind the fires and put an end to the chaos before it consumes everything they hold dear. With a blend of suspense, folklore, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, Small-town secrets, Courage and resilience
