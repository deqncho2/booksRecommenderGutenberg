Synopsis: 

In this captivating and informative legal guide, the United States Library of Congress Copyright Office delves into the intricacies of the Copyright Law of the United States of America and Related Laws Contained in Title 17 of the United States Code, Circular 92. With a focus on intellectual property rights, this comprehensive work explores the legal framework that governs copyright protection, fair use, licensing, and the digital age.

Genres: Legal, Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Intellectual property, Creative expression, Fair use, Digital rights, Copyright infringement
