Synopsis: In "The Span o' Life: A Tale of Louisbourg & Quebec," McIlwraith and McLennan take readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of 18th-century Canada. Set against the backdrop of the French and Indian War, the story follows the lives of two young men, each with their own dreams and ambitions, as they navigate the treacherous waters of war, love, and loyalty. From the bustling streets of Louisbourg to the battlefields of Quebec, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, testing their resolve and challenging their beliefs. As the fate of nations hangs in the balance, the characters must confront their own demons and make choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: War and Conflict, Identity and Belonging, Love and Sacrifice
