Synopsis: 

In "Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 04," the courtroom becomes a stage for justice as the most notorious figures of the Nazi regime face trial for their crimes against humanity. As the prosecution presents its evidence and the defense fights to protect their clients, a gripping battle of words and ideologies unfolds. With the world watching, the outcome of this historic trial will shape the future of international law and set a precedent for holding individuals accountable for their actions during times of war.

Genres: Historical, Legal, Non-fiction

Themes: Justice, Accountability, Human Rights, International Law
