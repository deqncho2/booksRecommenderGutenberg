Synopsis: In 'History of the English People, Volume VII: The Revolution, 1683-1760; Modern England, 1760-1767', John Richard Green takes readers on a captivating journey through a transformative period in English history. From the political upheavals of the Glorious Revolution to the dawn of the Industrial Revolution, Green delves into the intricate web of events, personalities, and societal changes that shaped the nation. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he explores the struggles for power, the rise of the middle class, the impact of scientific advancements, and the evolving social dynamics that laid the foundation for modern England.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Revolution, Political upheaval, Social change, Industrialization, Scientific advancements
