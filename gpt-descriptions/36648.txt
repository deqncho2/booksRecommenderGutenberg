Synopsis: In 'Home Influence: A Tale for Mothers and Daughters' by Grace Aguilar, a young woman named Edith embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth as she navigates the complexities of family, love, and societal expectations. Set in the 19th century, Edith's story unfolds against the backdrop of a changing world, where traditional values clash with emerging ideas. As she grapples with her own desires and aspirations, Edith must confront the challenges of maintaining her individuality while fulfilling her roles as a daughter, sister, and potential wife. Through her experiences, she learns the importance of finding balance, embracing one's true self, and the power of love and forgiveness. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family, Identity, Love, Societal Expectations
