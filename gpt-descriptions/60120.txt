Synopsis: In 'The House of Baltazar' by William John Locke, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Baltazar arrives in a small French village, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As he settles into the grand house he has purchased, the villagers become increasingly intrigued by his past and the secrets he holds. Through a series of unexpected events and encounters, Baltazar's true identity and the dark history of his house are gradually unveiled, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption
