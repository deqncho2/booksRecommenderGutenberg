Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Fourth, or the King's Own, Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its humble beginnings in 1680 to its remarkable achievements up until 1839, Cannon delves into the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the soldiers who served in this esteemed unit. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he unveils the untold stories of battles fought, victories won, and the indomitable spirit that defined the King's Own Regiment.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
