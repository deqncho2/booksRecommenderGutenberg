Synopsis: In 'The Troublemakers' by George O. Smith, a group of brilliant scientists stumble upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As they navigate the treacherous waters of corporate greed, government interference, and personal vendettas, they must decide whether to use their invention for the betterment of society or succumb to the allure of power and wealth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
