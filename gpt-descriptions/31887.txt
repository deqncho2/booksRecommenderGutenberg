Synopsis: 

In 'Our Battery; Or, The Journal of Company B, 1st O.V.A.', Cutter, O. P. takes us on a captivating journey through the eyes of the soldiers of Company B. Set during the American Civil War, this gripping journal offers a unique perspective on the experiences, challenges, and camaraderie of the soldiers as they navigate the harsh realities of war. From the intense battles to the quiet moments of reflection, the journal provides a vivid and emotional account of the soldiers' lives on the front lines. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in the gripping narrative, eagerly following the soldiers' journey and wondering what fate awaits them.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Diary/Epistolary

Themes: War, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
