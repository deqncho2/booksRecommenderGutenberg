Synopsis: 

"The Shuttle" by Frances Hodgson Burnett is a captivating novel that follows the story of two sisters, Rosy and Bettina Vanderpoel, who embark on a transatlantic journey between England and America. Rosy, a strong-willed and determined woman, travels to England to rescue her younger sister Bettina from a loveless marriage. As she delves into the lives of the aristocratic English family, Rosy uncovers dark secrets and manipulative schemes that threaten to tear her family apart. With her indomitable spirit, Rosy sets out to restore happiness and love to her sister's life, while also finding unexpected love of her own.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, social class and inequality, women's empowerment
