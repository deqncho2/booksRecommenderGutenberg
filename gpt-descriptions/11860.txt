Synopsis: 

In 'Black Beauty, Young Folks' Edition' by Anna Sewell, embark on a captivating journey through the eyes of a magnificent horse named Black Beauty. From his idyllic days as a young foal to his encounters with various owners, Black Beauty's story is filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected friendships, and heart-wrenching trials. As he navigates the harsh realities of life, Black Beauty's resilience and unwavering spirit shine through, teaching us valuable lessons about compassion, empathy, and the enduring bond between humans and animals.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Animal welfare, empathy, human-animal bond, social issues
