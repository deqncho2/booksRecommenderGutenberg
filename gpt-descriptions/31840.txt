Synopsis: 
In 'Ring Once for Death' by Arthur, Robert, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of mysterious deaths occur, each marked by a single ring of a doorbell. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a dark and twisted path. With each ring of the doorbell, the tension rises, and the detective must race against time to unravel the truth before more lives are claimed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Investigation, Suspense
