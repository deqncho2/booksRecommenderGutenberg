Synopsis: In 'Madge Morton's Secret' by Amy D. V. Chalmers, a young girl named Madge finds herself caught up in a mysterious secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must navigate a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each clue she uncovers, Madge becomes more determined to uncover the truth, but she soon realizes that some secrets are better left buried. Will Madge be able to unravel the mystery before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and deception, Coming of age, Courage and perseverance
