Synopsis: In 'The Boy Travellers in Mexico', two adventurous youths embark on a thrilling journey through the vibrant landscapes of Northern and Central Mexico, Campeachey, and Yucatan. Along the way, they encounter fascinating cultures, explore ancient ruins, and witness the awe-inspiring beauty of the region. As they delve deeper into their exploration, they also learn about the Republics of Central America and the ambitious plans for the construction of the Nicaragua Canal. This captivating travelogue takes readers on an immersive and educational adventure, filled with excitement and discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Historical significance
