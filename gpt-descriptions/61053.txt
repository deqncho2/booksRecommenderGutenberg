Synopsis:
In 'Tolliver's Orbit' by H. B. Fyfe, a brilliant scientist named Tolliver embarks on a groundbreaking mission to explore the mysteries of space. However, as he delves deeper into the unknown, he discovers a shocking secret that could change the course of humanity forever. With his life and the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Tolliver must navigate a treacherous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. This gripping science fiction tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Exploration, Scientific Discovery, Morality
