Synopsis: In "My Life and Work," Samuel Crowther takes us on an extraordinary journey through the life and achievements of one of the most influential figures in modern history, Henry Ford. From his humble beginnings to revolutionizing the automotive industry, this captivating memoir unveils the secrets behind Ford's unparalleled success, his innovative mindset, and the challenges he faced along the way. With fascinating anecdotes and personal insights, Crowther paints a vivid portrait of a man who forever changed the world.

Genres: Biography, Business, History

Themes: Innovation, Entrepreneurship, Industrialization
