Synopsis: In the final volume of Oliver Twist, Charles Dickens takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of Victorian London. As Oliver finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances, he must navigate the treacherous streets to uncover the truth about his past. With vivid descriptions and unforgettable characters, this gripping tale of survival and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Social injustice, Poverty, Morality, Identity
