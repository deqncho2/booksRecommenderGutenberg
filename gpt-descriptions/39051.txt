Synopsis: 
In "Ralph in the Switch Tower; Or, Clearing the Track" by Chapman, Allen, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Ralph, a young switchman who finds himself in a precarious situation. As he faces a series of unexpected challenges and dangers, Ralph must rely on his quick thinking and resourcefulness to navigate through the chaos and ensure the safety of the trains and passengers. With heart-pounding suspense and unexpected twists, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Problem-solving, Perseverance
