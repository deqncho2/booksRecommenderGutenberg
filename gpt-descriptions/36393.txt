Synopsis: In 'The Best Policy' by Flower Elliott, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into her family's past, she uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew. With each revelation, Lily must navigate a dangerous path of betrayal and redemption, all while trying to protect her loved ones and find her own place in the world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
