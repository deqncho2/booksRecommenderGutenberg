Synopsis: 
In 'Marjorie Dean, High School Junior' by Josephine Chase, readers are introduced to Marjorie Dean, a bright and ambitious young girl who is about to embark on her junior year of high school. As Marjorie navigates the challenges of school, friendships, and personal growth, she finds herself facing unexpected obstacles and making difficult choices. With her determination and resilience, Marjorie learns valuable lessons about loyalty, self-discovery, and the power of perseverance. This coming-of-age story takes readers on a captivating journey through the ups and downs of adolescence, as Marjorie Dean discovers her true potential and learns to embrace the opportunities that lie ahead.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Life

Themes: Friendship, Personal Growth, Resilience, Self-Discovery
