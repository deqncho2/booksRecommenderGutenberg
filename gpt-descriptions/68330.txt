Synopsis: In 'The Great White Hand; Or, the Tiger of Cawnpore: A story of the Indian Mutiny', J. E. Muddock takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Indian Mutiny. Amidst the chaos and violence, a mysterious figure known as the Tiger of Cawnpore emerges, striking fear into the hearts of both the British and Indian forces. As the story unfolds, a young British officer finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love, all while trying to uncover the true identity of the Tiger. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Rebellion, Identity, Love and Betrayal
