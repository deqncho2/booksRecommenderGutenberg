Synopsis: In 'Captain Lucy in France' by Havard, Aline, follow the thrilling adventures of Captain Lucy as she embarks on a dangerous mission in the heart of France during World War II. With her quick wit, bravery, and a team of loyal comrades, Lucy must navigate through enemy territory, facing treacherous obstacles and unexpected alliances. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, Lucy's determination to complete her mission becomes a race against time. Will she succeed in her mission and make it out alive?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Espionage, Resilience
