Synopsis: 
Princess Sukey is no ordinary pigeon. With her vibrant feathers and captivating personality, she captures the hearts of everyone she encounters. When she befriends a group of kind-hearted humans, including a young girl named Lily, their lives are forever changed. Together, they embark on a remarkable journey filled with adventure, friendship, and unexpected discoveries. As Princess Sukey and her human friends navigate the challenges of their world, they learn valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and the power of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Acceptance, Self-discovery
