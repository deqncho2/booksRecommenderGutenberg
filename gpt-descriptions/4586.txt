Synopsis: In 'Danger; Or, Wounded in the House of a Friend' by Arthur, T. S., a seemingly idyllic friendship takes a dark turn when a young man finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. As secrets unravel and danger lurks around every corner, the protagonist must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Loyalty, Trust
