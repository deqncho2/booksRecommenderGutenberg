Synopsis: In 'The Babe in the Bulrushes' by Amy Steedman, a young girl named Mary finds herself caught up in a mysterious adventure when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's attic. As she unravels the truth behind the secret, Mary is transported to a world filled with magic, danger, and unexpected friendships. With the fate of both worlds hanging in the balance, Mary must summon her courage and embark on a thrilling journey to save those she loves. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
