Synopsis: In "Rambles in an Old City," Susan Swain Madders takes readers on a captivating journey through the winding streets of an ancient city. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for history, Madders uncovers the hidden stories and forgotten secrets that lie within the city's walls. From exploring ancient ruins to delving into the lives of notable figures, she weaves together a tapestry of antiquarian, historical, biographical, and political associations. As she rambles through the city, the author invites readers to join her in unraveling the mysteries of the past, immersing themselves in the rich tapestry of the city's heritage.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: History, Heritage, Exploration, Identity
