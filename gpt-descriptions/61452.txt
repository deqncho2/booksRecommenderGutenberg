Synopsis: 
Embark on an exciting adventure through the captivating Cumberland Island with the Junior Ranger Program Activity Guide for Ages 5-7. Join young explorers as they uncover the island's hidden treasures, encounter fascinating wildlife, and learn about the rich history and natural wonders that make Cumberland Island a truly unique place. Packed with interactive activities, fun facts, and stunning illustrations, this guide is the perfect companion for young nature enthusiasts and aspiring junior rangers.

Genres: Educational, Adventure, Nature

Themes: Environmental conservation, History, Wildlife exploration
