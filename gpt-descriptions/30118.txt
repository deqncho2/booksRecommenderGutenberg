Synopsis: 

In 'Masonic Monitor of the Degrees of Entered Apprentice, Fellow Craft and Master Mason: together with the Ceremony of Installation, Laying Corner Stones, Dedications, Masonic Burial, Etc.', author George Thornburgh delves into the mysterious world of Freemasonry, providing a comprehensive guide to the rituals and ceremonies of the craft. From the initiation of an Entered Apprentice to the advanced degrees of Fellow Craft and Master Mason, Thornburgh offers a detailed exploration of the symbolism, teachings, and practices that define this ancient fraternity. Additionally, the book includes instructions for significant events such as the installation of officers, laying cornerstones, dedications, and even Masonic burials. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Occult

Themes: Freemasonry, Symbolism, Rituals, Brotherhood
