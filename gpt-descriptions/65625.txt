Synopsis: In "The first of May: a new version of a celebrated modern ballad: sixth edition," Drury takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two individuals whose paths unexpectedly cross on the first day of May. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With vivid descriptions and a touch of mystery, Drury's narrative explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of second chances.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Secrets and deception, Redemption
