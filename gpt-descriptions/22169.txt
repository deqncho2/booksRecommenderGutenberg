Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Barbary Corsairs', Kelley and Lane-Poole delve into the captivating history of the notorious Barbary Corsairs, the fearsome pirates who ruled the Mediterranean seas for centuries. From their origins in North Africa to their audacious raids on European shores, this gripping account unveils the hidden world of these seafaring outlaws, their cunning tactics, and the political intrigue that surrounded their activities. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the thrilling adventures and larger-than-life characters that shaped this dark chapter in maritime history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Piracy, Power and Politics, Cultural Exchange
