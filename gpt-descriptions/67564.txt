Synopsis: In 'Jerry Todd and the Talking Frog' by Edwards, Leo, Jerry Todd, a curious and adventurous young boy, stumbles upon a talking frog during a summer vacation at his grandparents' farm. As Jerry and his friends embark on a series of mischievous adventures with the talking frog, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With humor, mystery, and a touch of magic, this delightful tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Imagination
