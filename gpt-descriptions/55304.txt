Synopsis: In 'Azalea's Silver Web' by Elia Wilkinson Peattie, a young woman named Azalea finds herself entangled in a mysterious web of secrets and deceit when she moves to a small town. As she delves deeper into the town's history, she uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Azalea must navigate through a web of lies and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect herself from the town's sinister forces.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town intrigue, Personal identity and self-discovery
