Synopsis: 
In the ancient Greek tragedy 'Alcestis' by Euripides, a devoted wife makes a selfless sacrifice to save her husband's life. As the story unfolds, we witness the emotional turmoil and moral dilemmas faced by the characters, leading to unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations. This gripping tale explores the depths of love, loyalty, and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: 
Tragedy, Drama

Themes: 
Love and sacrifice, Loyalty, Mortality and immortality
