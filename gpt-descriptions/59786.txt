Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Cornelius Tacitus' is a collection of works by the renowned Roman historian Cornelius Tacitus. This compilation includes his influential writings on the history and politics of ancient Rome, covering a wide range of topics such as the reigns of emperors, military campaigns, and the social dynamics of the time. From the gripping accounts of battles to the intricate details of Roman society, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the past, providing valuable insights into the rise and fall of one of the greatest civilizations in history.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political commentary

Themes: 
Power and politics, Historical analysis, Social dynamics
