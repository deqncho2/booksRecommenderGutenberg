Synopsis: In 'Select Conversations with an Uncle (Now Extinct) and Two Other Reminiscences', H.G. Wells takes us on a captivating journey through time and space as he engages in thought-provoking conversations with his eccentric and enigmatic uncle. From discussing the mysteries of the universe to exploring the depths of human nature, these conversations offer a unique perspective on life, love, and the ever-changing world. Alongside these conversations, Wells shares two other intriguing reminiscences that delve into the realms of science and imagination. Prepare to be captivated by the wit, wisdom, and boundless curiosity of this extinct uncle and the profound insights he imparts.

Genres: Science Fiction, Philosophy, Memoir

Themes: Time, Existence, Human Nature, Science and Imagination
