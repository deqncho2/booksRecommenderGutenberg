Synopsis: In 'The Talkative Wig' by Follen, Eliza Lee Cabot, a mysterious wig with a mind of its own becomes the center of attention in a small town. As the wig's secrets unravel, it brings both chaos and unexpected revelations to the lives of those who encounter it. With its witty dialogue and intriguing characters, this tale takes readers on a whimsical journey filled with humor, suspense, and a touch of magic.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power of Objects
