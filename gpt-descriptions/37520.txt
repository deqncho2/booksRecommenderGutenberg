Synopsis: In 'Surnames as a Science' by Robert Ferguson, embark on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of surnames. Delve into the origins, meanings, and hidden stories behind these seemingly ordinary names that hold extraordinary secrets. From ancient civilizations to modern times, uncover the intricate web of connections that surnames weave, revealing surprising insights into our shared human history. As you explore the rich tapestry of surnames, prepare to be enthralled by the unexpected tales they tell and the profound impact they have on our identities.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Linguistics

Themes: Identity, Genealogy, Cultural History
