Synopsis: In 'The Strange Case of Cavendish' by Parrish Randall, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing mystery surrounding the disappearance of a wealthy businessman, Cavendish. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that lead him down a dark and twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective finds himself questioning the true nature of Cavendish's character and the motives of those around him. As the truth slowly unravels, the detective must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
