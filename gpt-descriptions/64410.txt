Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Great Schoolmaster' by H.G. Wells, we are introduced to Mr. Parham, a dedicated and unconventional schoolmaster who embarks on a journey to transform the lives of his students. As he challenges the traditional educational system and encourages his pupils to think critically, he faces numerous obstacles and resistance from both the school administration and the students themselves. Through his unwavering determination and innovative teaching methods, Mr. Parham strives to create an environment where learning becomes a joyous and transformative experience for all involved.

Genres: Fiction, Educational, Coming-of-age

Themes: Education reform, Individuality, Mentorship, Personal growth
