Synopsis: 
Thais is a captivating novel that takes readers on a journey through the decadent and alluring world of ancient Alexandria. The story revolves around the charismatic and hedonistic monk, Paphnutius, who embarks on a mission to save the soul of the beautiful and seductive courtesan, Thais. As Paphnutius becomes increasingly entangled in Thais' world, he is forced to confront his own desires and question the boundaries between sin and salvation. Set against a backdrop of religious fervor, philosophical debates, and passionate love, Thais is a tale that explores the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Philosophical fiction

Themes: Redemption, Temptation, Spirituality, Desire, Morality
