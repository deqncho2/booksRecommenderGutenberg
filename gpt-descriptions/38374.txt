Synopsis: In 'Abraham Lincoln: Was He a Christian?', John E. Remsburg delves into the religious beliefs of one of America's most iconic figures, Abraham Lincoln. Through meticulous research and analysis, Remsburg explores the complex and often debated question of whether Lincoln was truly a Christian. Drawing from Lincoln's own writings, speeches, and the accounts of those who knew him, Remsburg presents a thought-provoking examination of the president's religious journey, shedding light on the enigmatic nature of Lincoln's faith.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Religion and politics, Faith and doubt, Personal beliefs and public image
