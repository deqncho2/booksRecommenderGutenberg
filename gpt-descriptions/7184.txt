Synopsis: In 'A Heart-Song of To-day (Disturbed by Fire from the 'Unruly Member'): A Novel', Annie G. Savigny takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young woman named Emily. Set in the bustling city of London, the story follows Emily as she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. However, her world is turned upside down when a mysterious fire disrupts her life, forcing her to confront her deepest fears and desires. As Emily grapples with the aftermath of the fire, she embarks on a transformative journey of self-discovery, ultimately finding the strength to follow her heart's true song.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and passion, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
