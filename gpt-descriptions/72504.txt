Synopsis: In 'Finders Keepers' by Stephen Marlowe, a down-on-his-luck private investigator stumbles upon a mysterious case that leads him into a dangerous world of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Morality
