Synopsis: In 'Perfect Control' by Richard Stockham, a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking technology that allows him to manipulate the human mind. As he delves deeper into the possibilities of his invention, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and control. With the line between right and wrong blurred, he must confront the consequences of his actions and question the limits of his own morality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Ethics, Power, Morality
