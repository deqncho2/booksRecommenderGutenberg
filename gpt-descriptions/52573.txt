Synopsis: 

In "Samuell Gorton: A Forgotten Founder of our Liberties; First Settler of Warwick, R. I.", Lewis G. Janes delves into the life of an often overlooked figure in American history. This captivating biography uncovers the remarkable journey of Samuell Gorton, a man who played a pivotal role in the establishment of Warwick, Rhode Island. From his early struggles in England to his eventual settlement in the New World, Gorton's story is one of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of religious freedom. Janes skillfully weaves together historical accounts, personal letters, and anecdotes to paint a vivid portrait of this forgotten founder, shedding light on his contributions to the development of our liberties.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Religious freedom, Settling the New World, Overcoming adversity
