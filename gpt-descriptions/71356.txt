Synopsis: In 'Le chevalier de Maison-Rouge', set during the French Revolution, a mysterious knight emerges to challenge the reign of terror. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape, he becomes entangled in a dangerous love affair and finds himself torn between loyalty and justice. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this thrilling historical novel takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in French history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Love and Sacrifice
