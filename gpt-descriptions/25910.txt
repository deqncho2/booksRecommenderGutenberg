Synopsis: In 'The Long Portage' by Harold Bindloss, a young man named Ralph Stannard embarks on a perilous journey through the rugged Canadian wilderness. With his life at stake, he must navigate treacherous rivers, face hostile encounters, and confront his own inner demons. As Ralph battles the unforgiving elements and the secrets of his past, he discovers unexpected allies and a strength he never knew he possessed. This gripping tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
