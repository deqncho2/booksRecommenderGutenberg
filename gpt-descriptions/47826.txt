Synopsis: In 'A Tender Attachment: A Farce' by George M. Baker, a series of hilarious misunderstandings and mistaken identities ensue when a young woman disguises herself as a man to pursue her true love. As the characters navigate through a web of confusion and deception, the stage is set for a delightful comedy of errors that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and deception, Gender roles and identity, Miscommunication and misunderstandings
