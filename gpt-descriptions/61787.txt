Synopsis: In 'Seeing Lincoln' by Anne Longman, a young woman named Emily discovers an extraordinary ability to see and communicate with the ghost of Abraham Lincoln. As she delves deeper into this mysterious connection, Emily uncovers hidden truths about Lincoln's life and the impact he continues to have on the world. With each encounter, she becomes more entangled in a web of secrets and historical significance, leading her on a captivating journey that intertwines the past and the present.

Genres: Historical fiction, Paranormal, Mystery

Themes: History, Legacy, Identity, Communication
