Synopsis: 
"The Escorial: A Historical and Descriptive Account of the Spanish Royal Palace, Monastery and Mausoleum" takes readers on a captivating journey through the grandeur and mystique of one of Spain's most iconic landmarks. Albert Frederick Calvert delves into the rich history and intricate details of the Escorial, unraveling its secrets and shedding light on the lives of the Spanish royals who once inhabited its halls. From the awe-inspiring architecture to the hidden chambers and treasures within, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the palace, monastery, and mausoleum that have witnessed centuries of Spanish history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Travel

Themes: Spanish monarchy, Religious institutions, Cultural heritage
