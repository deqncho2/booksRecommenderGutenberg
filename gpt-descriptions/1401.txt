Synopsis: In "Tarzan the Untamed," Edgar Rice Burroughs takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as Tarzan, the legendary ape-man, finds himself in the midst of World War I. As he navigates the treacherous African jungle, Tarzan encounters a ruthless German officer, a beautiful American girl, and a mysterious secret that could change the course of the war. With his incredible strength, cunning, and primal instincts, Tarzan must fight to protect his jungle home and those he holds dear, while facing the horrors of war and the complexities of love.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Love and Loyalty, Clash of Civilizations
