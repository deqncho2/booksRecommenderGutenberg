Synopsis: In 'The Food of the Gods: A Popular Account of Cocoa' by Head, Brandon, embark on a fascinating journey into the world of cocoa, where the secrets of this delectable treat are unveiled. From its ancient origins to its modern-day significance, this captivating account explores the history, cultivation, and cultural impact of cocoa. Discover the intriguing stories behind the creation of chocolate and its transformation into a global phenomenon. Delve into the tantalizing details of cocoa's production process and its role in various culinary delights. Prepare to be enchanted by the rich and indulgent world of cocoa in this captivating exploration.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, History

Themes: History and Origins of Cocoa, Cultural Significance, Culinary Delights
