Synopsis: In 'A Poor Man's House' by Reynolds, Stephen Sydney, a struggling artist named Jack finds himself drawn into a mysterious world when he discovers an old painting hidden in the attic of his new house. As he delves deeper into the secrets behind the artwork, Jack becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected romance. With each clue he uncovers, the lines between reality and imagination blur, leading him on a thrilling journey that challenges his perceptions of art, love, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Perception
