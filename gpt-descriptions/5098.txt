Synopsis: In "Lives of the English Poets: Waller, Milton, Cowley," Samuel Johnson delves into the lives and works of three influential poets from the 17th century. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Johnson uncovers the fascinating stories behind their creations, shedding light on their struggles, triumphs, and the impact they had on English literature. From the political intrigues of Waller to the epic genius of Milton and the metaphysical complexities of Cowley, this book offers a captivating exploration of the lives and poetic genius of these remarkable individuals.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Poetry, Artistic Expression, Historical Context
