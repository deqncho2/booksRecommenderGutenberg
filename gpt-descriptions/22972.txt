Synopsis: In "Rollo in Holland" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting adventure as he travels to the Netherlands. Alongside his cousin, Jane, Rollo explores the vibrant cities, picturesque countryside, and encounters fascinating Dutch customs and traditions. From windmills to tulips, canals to cheese markets, Rollo's journey is filled with captivating experiences that will transport readers to the enchanting world of Holland.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Travel

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Curiosity
