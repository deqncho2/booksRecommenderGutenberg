Synopsis: 
'Around the world in eighty minutes: Photographic reproductions of the most magnificent edifices, the most interesting remains and the most beautiful scenes on the earth's surface' takes readers on a breathtaking visual journey across the globe. Through stunning photographs, the book captures the essence of the world's most remarkable architectural wonders, captivating historical remains, and awe-inspiring natural landscapes. From ancient ruins to modern marvels, this collection offers a glimpse into the diverse beauty found on Earth's surface, transporting readers to different corners of the world in just eighty minutes.

Genres: Travel, Photography, Coffee Table Book

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
