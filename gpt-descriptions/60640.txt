Synopsis: In 'The Wind People' by Marion Zimmer Bradley, a young woman named Kaela finds herself torn between two worlds when she discovers a hidden community of people who possess the ability to control the wind. As she delves deeper into their society, Kaela must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the power struggles that threaten to tear her newfound family apart. With secrets and betrayals lurking at every turn, Kaela's journey becomes a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and force her to confront her own identity.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Love and Loyalty
