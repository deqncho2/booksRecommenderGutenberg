Synopsis: 
In 'Dr. Heidenhoff's Process', a young man named John Fenton finds himself entangled in a mysterious and sinister medical experiment. Dr. Heidenhoff, a renowned psychiatrist, claims to have developed a revolutionary process that can cure any mental illness. Intrigued by the promise of a better life, John agrees to undergo the treatment. However, as he delves deeper into the process, he begins to question the doctor's true intentions and the ethical implications of his methods. As John's sanity hangs in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of deception, manipulation, and self-discovery to uncover the truth behind Dr. Heidenhoff's process.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Sanity, Manipulation, Ethics
