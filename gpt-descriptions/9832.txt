Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Blind' by Fred M. White, a mysterious and wealthy man named Sir Horace Fewbanks is found dead in his study, with a crimson blind drawn down over the window. As the investigation unfolds, it becomes clear that there is more to Sir Horace's death than meets the eye. Detective John Kendall is determined to uncover the truth, but he soon finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists. With each clue he uncovers, Kendall gets closer to the shocking truth behind Sir Horace's demise, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful climax that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Intrigue
