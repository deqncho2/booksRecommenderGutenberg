Synopsis: In 'Where There's a Will' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a wealthy family gathers at their ancestral home to hear the reading of their late patriarch's will. However, tensions rise as long-held secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances come to light. As the family members navigate through a web of deception and betrayal, they must confront their own desires and face the consequences of their actions. With suspense and intrigue at every turn, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family dynamics, Greed, Deception, Betrayal
