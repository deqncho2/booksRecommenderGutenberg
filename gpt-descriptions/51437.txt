Synopsis: In 'Boy of My Heart' by Marie Connor Leighton, a young woman named Lily finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly reunited with her childhood friend, Ethan. As their friendship deepens into something more, Lily must navigate the complexities of family expectations, societal norms, and her own heart's desires. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal town, this captivating tale explores the power of first love, the bonds of friendship, and the courage to follow one's own path.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Family expectations
