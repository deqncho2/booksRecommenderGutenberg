Synopsis: In 'Five Little Friends' by Adams, Sherred Willcox, a group of childhood friends reunite after many years for a weekend getaway at a secluded cabin in the woods. As they reminisce about their shared past, they soon realize that their bond is not as strong as it once was. Secrets, betrayals, and unresolved conflicts come to the surface, threatening to shatter their friendship forever. Tensions rise as they navigate through a series of unexpected events, forcing them to confront their past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their friendship.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Secrets, Redemption
