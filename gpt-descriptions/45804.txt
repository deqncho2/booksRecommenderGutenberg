Synopsis: In 'Elsie and Her Namesakes' by Martha Finley, a young girl named Elsie discovers a hidden family secret that leads her on a captivating journey of self-discovery. As she unravels the mystery behind her namesakes, Elsie encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely friendships. With each new encounter, Elsie's understanding of herself and her place in the world deepens, ultimately transforming her life in ways she never could have imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Family, Friendship, Secrets
