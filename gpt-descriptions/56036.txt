Synopsis: In 'Inside the Lines' by Biggers, Earl Derr and Ritchie, Robert Welles, a young American diplomat finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue and danger when he is assigned to a secret mission in war-torn Europe. As he navigates through a world of spies, double agents, and forbidden love, he must make difficult choices that could alter the course of history. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Love and Betrayal, Moral Dilemmas
