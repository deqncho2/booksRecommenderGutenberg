Synopsis: 

In 'Learning Theory' by McConnell, James V., a brilliant young psychologist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that challenges the very foundations of human learning. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the mind, he uncovers shocking truths that could revolutionize education and reshape society. But as his research takes a dark turn, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between knowledge and power becomes blurred. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the ultimate truth about the nature of learning.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: The power of knowledge, Ethics in scientific research, The limits of human understanding
