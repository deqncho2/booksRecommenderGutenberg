Synopsis: On a snowy Christmas Eve, Dr. John Westley finds himself caught in a mysterious and heartwarming adventure that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever. As he encounters a series of unexpected events and encounters, he must confront his own past and rediscover the true meaning of love, forgiveness, and the magic of the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Christmas, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Second chances, Love, Forgiveness, Christmas spirit
