Synopsis: In 'Waiting for an Omnibus in the Lowther Arcade on a Rainy Day: A Farce, in One Act', chaos ensues as a group of eccentric characters find themselves trapped in a bustling arcade during a torrential downpour. As they wait for an omnibus that never seems to arrive, their interactions become increasingly absurd and hilarious, leading to unexpected consequences. 

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Absurdism

Themes: Human folly, Miscommunication, Unexpected outcomes
