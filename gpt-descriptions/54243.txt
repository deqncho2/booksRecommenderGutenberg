Synopsis: In 'Demoniality; or, Incubi and Succubi', Sinistrari delves into the mysterious world of demonic entities known as incubi and succubi. Through a collection of chilling accounts and historical references, he explores the seductive and terrifying encounters between these supernatural beings and humans. Sinistrari's meticulous research and vivid descriptions will captivate readers as they uncover the dark secrets and forbidden desires that lie at the heart of these otherworldly encounters.

Genres: Horror, Occult, Non-fiction

Themes: Supernatural entities, Forbidden desires, Seduction, Human vulnerability
