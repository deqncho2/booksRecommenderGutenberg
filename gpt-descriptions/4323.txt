Synopsis: In 'The Heart-Cry of Jesus' by Byron J. Rees, a mysterious encounter with a stranger leads a young woman on a transformative journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As she delves deeper into the teachings of Jesus, she uncovers profound truths that challenge her beliefs and ignite a passionate desire for a more authentic and compassionate life. With each step, she finds herself drawn closer to the heart-cry of Jesus, a call to love, forgiveness, and radical transformation. This captivating novel explores the power of faith, the search for meaning, and the transformative nature of divine love.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Self-discovery, Transformation, Love, Forgiveness
