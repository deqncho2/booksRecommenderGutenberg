Synopsis: In "A Circuit Rider's Wife" by Corra Harris, a young woman named Mary is thrust into the challenging life of a circuit rider's wife in rural Georgia. As she navigates the hardships of isolation, religious fervor, and the constant movement of her husband's ministry, Mary finds herself questioning her own beliefs and desires. With the arrival of a charismatic stranger, Mary's world is turned upside down, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Southern Literature

Themes: Religion and spirituality, gender roles and expectations, forbidden love, self-discovery
