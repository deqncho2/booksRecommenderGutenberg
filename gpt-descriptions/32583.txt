Synopsis: 
In 'Tape Jockey' by Tom Leahy, a mysterious cassette tape arrives at the doorstep of a struggling musician, setting off a chain of events that will challenge his perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the origins of the tape, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, leading him on a thrilling journey through time and space. With each tape he listens to, the boundaries between past and present blur, and he must confront the consequences of his actions. As the mystery unfolds, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape of love, loss, and the power of music, ultimately discovering the true nature of his own existence.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Time Travel, Identity, Music
