Synopsis: In 'Old Crow' by Alice Brown, a mysterious figure arrives in a small New England town, captivating the locals with her enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become entangled in her web, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human desires is revealed. With its atmospheric setting and compelling characters, 'Old Crow' is a gripping tale that explores the complexities of love, betrayal, and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity, Redemption
