Synopsis: In 'The New Forest Spy' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Jack finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious secret hidden within the depths of the New Forest. As he delves deeper into the forest's secrets, Jack must navigate treacherous paths, encounter dangerous characters, and uncover the truth that could change everything he knows. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of Jack's courageous journey.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Secrets, Coming of Age
