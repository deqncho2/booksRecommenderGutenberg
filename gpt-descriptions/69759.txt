Synopsis:
"The North-West Passage by land" is a thrilling narrative that follows the expedition of two explorers as they embark on a daring journey from the Atlantic to the Pacific. Their mission is to explore a route across the continent to British Columbia through British territory, specifically by venturing through one of the northern passes in the Rocky Mountains. As they face treacherous landscapes, encounter indigenous peoples, and navigate the unknown, the explorers' determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. This gripping account will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the challenges and triumphs that await these intrepid adventurers.

Genres:
Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes:
Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Encounter
