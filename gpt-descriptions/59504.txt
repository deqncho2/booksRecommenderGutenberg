Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Order' by Holden, Fox B., a brilliant detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and corruption when he is assigned to investigate a seemingly ordinary murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, the detective must navigate a treacherous maze of lies and betrayal to bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Justice
