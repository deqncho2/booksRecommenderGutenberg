Synopsis:
'Within the Precincts' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret) is a gripping tale set in the atmospheric backdrop of a small English cathedral town. The story follows the lives of two sisters, one of whom becomes embroiled in a scandalous affair that threatens to unravel the fabric of their close-knit community. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, wondering how the characters will navigate the delicate balance between love, duty, and societal expectations.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Scandal, Betrayal, Family, Morality
