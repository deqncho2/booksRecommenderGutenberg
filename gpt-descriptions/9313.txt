Synopsis: 
'Old Greek Folk Stories Told Anew' by Josephine Preston Peabody is a captivating collection of ancient Greek myths that have been reimagined for modern readers. From the epic adventures of Hercules to the tragic love story of Orpheus and Eurydice, these timeless tales are brought to life with vivid storytelling and rich imagery. Delve into the world of gods, heroes, and mythical creatures as you embark on a journey through the ancient Greek civilization.

Genres: 
Mythology, Fantasy, Fiction

Themes: 
Heroism, Love and Loss, Fate and Destiny
