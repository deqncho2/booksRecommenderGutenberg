Synopsis: In 'Engines of the Gods' by Gardner F. Fox, a brilliant archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of a lost civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the artifact, he becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing treacherous enemies and uncovering mind-bending truths that challenge everything he thought he knew about the world. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating blend of science fiction and adventure, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeology, Exploration, Secrets and discoveries
