Synopsis: 
In "Audrey" by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Audrey finds herself caught between duty and desire in the midst of the American Revolution. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and betrayal, Audrey must make choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. With vivid historical detail and a captivating narrative, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous time in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Independence and Freedom
