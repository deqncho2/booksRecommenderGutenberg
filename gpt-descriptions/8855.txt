Synopsis: In 'Astral Worship' by J. H. Hill, a captivating exploration of ancient civilizations and their connection to celestial bodies unfolds. The author delves into the mysterious practices of astral worship, revealing the profound impact it had on the beliefs and rituals of various cultures throughout history. From the awe-inspiring monuments of Egypt to the enigmatic stone circles of Britain, Hill takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through time, unraveling the secrets of celestial reverence and its enduring influence on human civilization.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Religion and spirituality, Cultural practices
