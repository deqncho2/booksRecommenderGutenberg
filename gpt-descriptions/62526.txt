Synopsis: 
In "Let Us Have Peace," Logan H. Roots delivers a powerful speech in the House of Representatives, addressing the assassination of Hon. James Hinds. With eloquence and passion, Roots delves into the tragic event, shedding light on the impact it has had on the nation. Through his words, he explores the importance of unity, justice, and the pursuit of peace in a divided society. As he reflects on the life and legacy of Hinds, Roots inspires his audience to strive for a future where peace prevails over violence and hatred.

Genres: 
Speech, Historical, Political

Themes: 
Unity, Justice, Peace, Legacy
