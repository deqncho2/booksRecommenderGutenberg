Synopsis: 
In "Looking Backward, 2000 to 1887," Edward Bellamy takes readers on a captivating journey through time, transporting them from the late 19th century to the year 2000. The story follows Julian West, a young man who falls into a deep sleep and wakes up over a century later to find himself in a utopian society. As Julian explores this new world, he encounters remarkable advancements in technology, social equality, and a profound transformation in the way society functions. Through thought-provoking conversations and experiences, Julian grapples with the stark contrast between the past and the future, ultimately questioning the very foundations of his former reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Utopian Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Social Equality, Technological Advancement, Critique of Capitalism
