Synopsis: In 'Interference and Other Football Stories', Harold M. Sherman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of football, exploring the highs and lows, the triumphs and tragedies, and the intense rivalries that define the sport. From the gripping tale of a star quarterback facing unexpected challenges to the heartwarming story of a young fan's unwavering dedication, each story in this collection captures the essence of football and the indomitable spirit of those who play and love the game. With its captivating narratives and unexpected twists, 'Interference and Other Football Stories' is a must-read for football enthusiasts and fans of compelling storytelling alike.

Genres: Sports Fiction, Short Stories, Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Redemption, Passion, Sacrifice
