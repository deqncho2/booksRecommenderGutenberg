Synopsis: In "Maid of the Mist" by John Oxenham, a young woman named Joan embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the breathtaking landscapes of the Scottish Highlands. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters intriguing characters, Joan discovers the power of love, the importance of self-discovery, and the resilience of the human spirit. This captivating tale will transport readers to a world filled with adventure, romance, and the beauty of nature.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Resilience
