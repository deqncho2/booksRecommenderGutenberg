Synopsis: 
In "Woman on the American Frontier," William Worthington Fowler takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of the American frontier, shedding light on the remarkable lives of the courageous and resilient pioneer women who played a pivotal role in shaping the nation. From their heroic acts and thrilling adventures to their unwavering determination and sacrifices, this valuable and authentic history unveils the untold stories of the "Pioneer Mothers of the Republic." As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be transported to a time of hardship, danger, and triumph, where the strength and spirit of these extraordinary women shine through.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Resilience, Gender roles, Frontier life
