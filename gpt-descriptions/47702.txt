Synopsis: In 'Through the Gates of Old Romance' by Mills, Weymer Jay, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden portal that transports her to a world of knights, castles, and chivalry. As she navigates this enchanting realm, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ancient secrets. With danger lurking at every turn, she must uncover the truth about her own past and make a choice that could change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Adventure, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
