Synopsis: 

In 'Hawaiian Historical Legends' by W. D. Westervelt, embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Hawaiian history and mythology. From the ancient tales of gods and goddesses to the heroic exploits of legendary figures, this collection of historical legends will transport you to a world of enchantment and wonder. Discover the origins of the islands, witness epic battles, and delve into the fascinating lives of the Hawaiian people. With each story, the reader will be immersed in a vibrant and magical realm that will leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Hawaiian culture, Legends and mythology, History and heritage
