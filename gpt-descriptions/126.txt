Synopsis: In 'The Poison Belt' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a group of friends find themselves facing an unimaginable threat as the world plunges into a deadly cosmic phenomenon. As they struggle to survive, they must confront their deepest fears and grapple with the fragility of human existence. With time running out, they embark on a race against the clock to unravel the mystery behind this cataclysmic event and find a way to save humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Human vulnerability, Exploration of the unknown
