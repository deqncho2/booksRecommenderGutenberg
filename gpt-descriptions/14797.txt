Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Timmy Tiptoes', Beatrix Potter takes readers on a delightful adventure with Timmy, a squirrel who finds himself in a rather peculiar situation. As Timmy and his wife search for a new home, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will Timmy be able to solve the mystery and find a place to call home?

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Home
