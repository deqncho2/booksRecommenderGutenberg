Synopsis: 
In 'A Servant of the Public' by Hope, Anthony, we are introduced to the intriguing character of Richard Vane, a successful playwright who suddenly finds himself thrust into the world of politics. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power and corruption, Vane must confront his own ideals and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his career and personal life. With a captivating blend of suspense, romance, and political intrigue, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of public service and the sacrifices one must make for the greater good.

Genres: Political fiction, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Power and corruption, Idealism and compromise
