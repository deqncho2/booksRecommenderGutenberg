Synopsis: In "Habits that Handicap: The Menace of Opium, Alcohol, and Tobacco, and the Remedy," Charles Barnes Towns delves into the dark world of addiction, exploring the devastating effects of opium, alcohol, and tobacco on individuals and society. Through compelling stories and insightful analysis, Towns sheds light on the gripping struggles faced by those trapped in the clutches of these vices. With a focus on providing a remedy, he offers hope and guidance for those seeking to break free from the chains of addiction.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Addiction, Health, Personal Transformation
