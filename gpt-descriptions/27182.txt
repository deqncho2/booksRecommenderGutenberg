Synopsis: In 'More Peers: Verses' by Hilaire Belloc, readers are taken on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and satirical poems that explore the lives and quirks of various peers in society. From the eccentric Lord Lundy to the enigmatic Lady Jane, Belloc's verses offer a humorous and insightful glimpse into the world of the British aristocracy. With his signature style and clever wordplay, Belloc invites readers to laugh, ponder, and perhaps even question the nature of privilege and power.

Genres: Poetry, Satire, Humor

Themes: Social class, Satire, Critique of aristocracy
