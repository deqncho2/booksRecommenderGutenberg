Synopsis: In 'Gypsies of the Air' by Bess Moyer, a group of daring aviators embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them across continents and into the heart of danger. As they navigate treacherous skies and encounter unexpected obstacles, their bonds are tested and secrets are revealed. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Betrayal
