Synopsis: In 'The Boy's Book of Heroes' by Helena Peake, embark on an extraordinary adventure alongside young protagonist, Oliver, as he discovers a hidden world filled with mythical creatures, ancient legends, and the power of friendship. As Oliver delves deeper into this enchanting realm, he must navigate through challenges and face his fears, all while uncovering the true meaning of heroism. With captivating storytelling and vivid imagery, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey that will leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Heroism, Friendship, Self-discovery
