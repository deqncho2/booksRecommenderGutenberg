Synopsis: In 'The Great War in England in 1897' by William Le Queux, a series of mysterious events unfold in England, leading to a tense atmosphere of impending war. As the country becomes embroiled in a web of political intrigue, espionage, and secret societies, a young journalist finds himself caught in the middle of a dangerous conspiracy. With the fate of the nation hanging in the balance, he must navigate through a treacherous landscape of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and prevent catastrophe.

Genres: Thriller, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: National security, Political conspiracy, Espionage
