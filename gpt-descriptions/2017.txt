Synopsis: 
'Dhammapada, a Collection of Verses; Being One of the Canonical Books of the Buddhists' is a profound and enlightening collection of verses that offers timeless wisdom and guidance for leading a meaningful and fulfilling life. Through a series of poetic verses, this canonical book explores the fundamental teachings of Buddhism, delving into topics such as mindfulness, compassion, self-discipline, and the nature of existence. Each verse presents a thought-provoking insight, inviting readers to reflect on their own actions and attitudes, and encouraging them to cultivate inner peace and spiritual growth. This collection serves as a powerful reminder of the transformative power of the mind and the importance of living in harmony with oneself and the world.

Genres: Philosophy, Religious Text, Poetry
Themes: Buddhism, Mindfulness, Compassion, Self-discipline, Spiritual Growth
