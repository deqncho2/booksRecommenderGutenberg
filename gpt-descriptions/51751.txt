Synopsis: In 'Oh, Rats!' by Miriam Allen De Ford, a small town is plagued by an infestation of highly intelligent and mischievous rats. As the townspeople struggle to find a solution, they soon discover that these rats are not just ordinary pests, but possess a secret that could change everything. With a blend of humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Intelligence, Survival, Community
