Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events from around the world. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this magazine keeps its young audience on the edge of their seats. Packed with fascinating stories, intriguing puzzles, and thought-provoking articles, this issue is sure to captivate the minds of boys and girls alike.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Discovery
