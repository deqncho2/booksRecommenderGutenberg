Synopsis: In 'Spacemen Die at Home' by Ludwig, Edward W., a group of astronauts return from a perilous mission in space, only to face an unexpected and mysterious threat back on Earth. As they struggle to uncover the truth behind this new danger, they find themselves questioning their own sanity and the very nature of reality. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Perception
