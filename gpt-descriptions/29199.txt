Synopsis: In 'The Siege of Boston' by French, Allen, a small town in Massachusetts becomes the unlikely battleground for a fierce conflict during the American Revolution. As the British army tightens its grip on Boston, a group of determined patriots must navigate treacherous alliances, secret missions, and the constant threat of betrayal. Amidst the chaos, a young woman finds herself caught in the crossfire, forced to make impossible choices that will shape the fate of her community and the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Survival
