Synopsis: 

In "Salt mines and castles: The discovery and restitution of looted European art," Thomas Carr Howe takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of stolen European art. From the depths of salt mines to the grandeur of castles, this gripping narrative unveils the shocking truth behind the looting of priceless masterpieces during World War II. As the author delves into the efforts to recover and restore these stolen treasures, readers are immersed in a thrilling tale of intrigue, deception, and the relentless pursuit of justice. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Art history

Themes: World War II, Art theft, Restitution, Cultural heritage
