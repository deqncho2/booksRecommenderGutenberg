Synopsis: In 'Peril Orbit' by Wedlake, C. J., a brilliant astrophysicist discovers a mysterious anomaly in space that threatens the very fabric of the universe. As she embarks on a perilous mission to unravel the truth behind this enigma, she finds herself caught in a web of intrigue, facing unexpected dangers and uncovering shocking secrets that could change everything we know about the cosmos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Consequences of scientific discovery
