Synopsis: In 'Sharing Her Crime: A Novel' by Fleming, May Agnes, a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes an unwitting accomplice to a crime. As she grapples with the consequences of her actions, she must navigate a dangerous world where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones and the haunting power of guilt.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Guilt, Loyalty
