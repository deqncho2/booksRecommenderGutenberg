Synopsis:
In "Fred Fenton on the Crew; Or, The Young Oarsmen of Riverport School" by Chapman, Allen, Fred Fenton, a talented athlete, finds himself facing a new challenge as he joins the crew team at Riverport School. As Fred navigates the world of rowing, he encounters fierce competition, unexpected friendships, and a mystery that threatens to disrupt the team's success. With determination and teamwork, Fred and his fellow oarsmen must overcome obstacles both on and off the water to prove their worth and achieve their goals.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Perseverance
