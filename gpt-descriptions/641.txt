Synopsis: In 'The Violet Fairy Book', Lang, Andrew presents a captivating collection of enchanting tales from various cultures around the world. From magical creatures and brave heroes to treacherous villains and unexpected twists, this anthology takes readers on a thrilling journey through the realms of fantasy and folklore. Each story is filled with wonder, adventure, and the timeless power of imagination, making it impossible to resist the allure of these captivating tales.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Adventure, Magic, Morality, Heroism
