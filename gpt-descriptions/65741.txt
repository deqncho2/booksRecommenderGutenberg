Synopsis: In 'Run, Little Monster!' by Chester S. Geier, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the residents in a state of fear and paranoia. As the tension rises, a young boy named Tommy discovers a hidden world filled with fantastical creatures and dark secrets. With the help of his newfound friends, Tommy embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the disappearances and save his town from an ancient evil. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they join Tommy in a race against time to confront the monsters lurking in the shadows.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Overcoming fear, Good vs. Evil
