Synopsis: 

In 'One Year in Scandinavia: Results of the gospel in Denmark and Sweden; sketches and observations on the country and people; remarkable events; late persecutions and present aspect of affairs', Erastus Fairbanks Snow takes us on a captivating journey through Denmark and Sweden, revealing the transformative power of the gospel and the remarkable events that unfolded during his time there. From his sketches and observations on the country and its people to the late persecutions faced by believers, Snow paints a vivid picture of the present aspect of affairs in Scandinavia. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Religious

Themes: Faith, Transformation, Persecution
