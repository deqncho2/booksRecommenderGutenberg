Synopsis: In 'Love at Second Sight' by Ada Leverson, a chance encounter between two strangers leads to a whirlwind romance that defies societal expectations. As their relationship blossoms, they navigate the complexities of love, desire, and the constraints of their respective social circles. But when a shocking secret threatens to tear them apart, they must confront their own vulnerabilities and make a choice that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Comedy

Themes: Love, Society, Identity, Secrets
