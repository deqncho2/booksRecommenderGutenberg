Synopsis: 
In 'Literary Taste: How to Form It: With Detailed Instructions for Collecting a Complete Library of English Literature,' Arnold Bennett takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of literature. With his insightful guidance, he offers detailed instructions on how to cultivate a refined literary taste and build an impressive library of English literature. From exploring the classics to discovering lesser-known gems, Bennett's book serves as a valuable resource for both avid readers and those looking to expand their literary horizons. Through his engaging prose and thoughtful recommendations, he encourages readers to embark on a lifelong love affair with books and the power of words.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Self-help

Themes: Literature appreciation, Reading habits, Personal growth
