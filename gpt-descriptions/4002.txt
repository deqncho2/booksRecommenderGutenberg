Synopsis: In 'The Honor of the Name' by Emile Gaboriau, a wealthy and respected family finds themselves entangled in a web of mystery and scandal when a series of crimes threaten to tarnish their prestigious name. As the family's secrets are slowly revealed, a relentless detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and restore the honor of the name.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Family honor, Deception, Justice
