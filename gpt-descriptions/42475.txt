Synopsis: In 'The History of Ancient America, Anterior to the Time of Columbus', author George Jones delves into the fascinating and controversial topic of the origins of the indigenous peoples of America. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, Jones presents a compelling argument that the aborigines of America were connected to the ancient Tyrians and Israelites. Furthermore, he explores the intriguing claim that Christianity was introduced to the Western Hemisphere by none other than the Apostle St. Thomas. This thought-provoking work challenges conventional historical narratives and invites readers to reconsider the ancient history of America.

Genres: Historical research, Religious studies, Archaeology

Themes: Cultural identity, Ancient civilizations, Religious influence
