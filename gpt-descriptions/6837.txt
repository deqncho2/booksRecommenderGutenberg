Synopsis: 
In 'The Little Warrior' by P. G. Wodehouse, a young and spirited girl named Joan Valentine finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of adventure and romance. When she becomes entangled in a case of mistaken identity, Joan must navigate a series of hilarious and unpredictable situations, all while trying to maintain her independence and prove her worth. With a cast of eccentric characters and Wodehouse's signature wit, 'The Little Warrior' is a delightful tale of love, mistaken identities, and the triumph of the underdog.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love and Relationships
