Synopsis: In 'White Fire' by John Oxenham, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man named David Warde embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a legendary gemstone known as the White Fire. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding this precious jewel, David finds himself entangled in a web of danger, romance, and ancient folklore. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own fears and make choices that will shape his destiny forever. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma of the White Fire.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Quest for truth, Love and sacrifice
