Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Friend: A Judy Bolton Mystery' by Margaret Sutton, Judy Bolton finds herself caught up in a perplexing case when a mysterious figure starts appearing in her small town. As Judy delves deeper into the enigma, she uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives that lead her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. With danger lurking around every corner, Judy must rely on her wit and determination to solve the puzzle and bring justice to her community.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Friendship and loyalty
