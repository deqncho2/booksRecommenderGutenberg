Synopsis: In 'The Long Vacation' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a group of young friends embarks on a summer adventure that takes them to a picturesque countryside. As they explore the idyllic surroundings, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a thrilling journey of discovery and self-realization. With unexpected twists and turns, the story delves into the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the power of youthful curiosity. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
