Synopsis: 

In 'Chinese pottery and porcelain; vol. 1. Pottery and early wares', Robert Lockhart Hobson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and exquisite craftsmanship of Chinese pottery and porcelain. From the ancient origins of pottery-making to the development of early wares, this volume delves into the fascinating world of Chinese ceramics, exploring the techniques, styles, and cultural significance behind these remarkable creations. With stunning illustrations and insightful analysis, Hobson unveils the secrets of this ancient art form, leaving readers eager to discover more about the intricate beauty and cultural heritage of Chinese pottery and porcelain.

Genres: Art History, Cultural Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Chinese Art, Craftsmanship, Cultural Heritage
