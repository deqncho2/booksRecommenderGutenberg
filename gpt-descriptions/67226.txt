Synopsis: In 'The King's Own Borderers: A Military Romance, Volume 1 (of 3)' by Grant, James, readers are transported to the tumultuous world of 19th-century warfare and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars, this captivating tale follows the lives of two young soldiers, Captain Manners and Lieutenant Lindsay, as they navigate the treacherous battlefields and the complexities of their own hearts. As their loyalty to their regiment is tested and their paths intertwine with a mysterious woman, the boundaries between duty and desire blur, leading to a series of unexpected twists and turns. With vivid descriptions of military campaigns, heartfelt emotions, and a touch of intrigue, this first volume of the trilogy promises an enthralling journey through love, war, and the sacrifices made in the name of honor.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Duty, Honor, Forbidden Love, Loyalty
