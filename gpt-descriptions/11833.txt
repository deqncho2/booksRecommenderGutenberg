Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1966 January - June, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. Copyright Office that unveils a treasure trove of forgotten literary gems. Delve into the pages of this remarkable collection and discover the hidden stories, forgotten characters, and lost narratives that have shaped American literature. From beloved classics to obscure masterpieces, this anthology offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of creativity that has defined the literary landscape of the United States. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Literature

Themes: Intellectual property, Literary history, Cultural heritage
