Synopsis: In 'Double Play: A Story of School and Baseball' by Ralph Henry Barbour, follow the thrilling journey of a young student-athlete as he navigates the challenges of both the classroom and the baseball field. As he strives to excel in both areas, he must confront unexpected obstacles, make difficult choices, and learn valuable life lessons along the way. With a perfect blend of sportsmanship, friendship, and personal growth, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final pitch.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Young adult

Themes: Determination, Balancing priorities, Friendship, Personal growth
