Synopsis: In "Leo the Circus Boy; or, Life under the great white canvas" by Edward Stratemeyer, readers are introduced to Leo, a young boy who finds himself thrust into the captivating world of the circus. As Leo navigates the challenges and adventures of circus life, he discovers hidden talents, forms unexpected friendships, and faces thrilling dangers. From daring acrobatics to heart-stopping performances, Leo's journey under the great white canvas will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Overcoming challenges
