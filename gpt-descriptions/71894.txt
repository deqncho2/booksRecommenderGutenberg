Synopsis:
In "The Retreat of the Ten Thousand," Xenophon takes us on an epic journey through the treacherous landscapes of ancient Persia. When a Greek mercenary army finds themselves stranded deep in enemy territory, they must navigate through hostile forces, harsh terrains, and internal conflicts to find their way back home. As they face unimaginable challenges and encounter unexpected allies, the ten thousand soldiers must summon their courage and resilience to survive against all odds. This gripping tale of survival and camaraderie will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes:
Survival, Loyalty, Leadership, Perseverance
