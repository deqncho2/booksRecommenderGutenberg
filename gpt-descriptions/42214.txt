Synopsis: 
In 'On a Donkey's Hurricane Deck', Robert Pitcher takes readers on an extraordinary adventure across the American continent, covering a staggering distance of four thousand and ninety-six miles. What makes this journey even more remarkable is that Pitcher accomplishes it all on the back of a burro, starting with no money and relying solely on his resourcefulness to earn his way. From encountering wild animals to facing treacherous weather conditions, Pitcher's voyage is filled with thrilling and unexpected twists. This captivating memoir will leave readers in awe of the author's determination and resilience as he navigates through the vast and diverse landscapes of America.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: Perseverance, Resourcefulness, Exploration
