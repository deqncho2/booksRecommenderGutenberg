Synopsis: 

'Area Handbook for Romania' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the rich history, culture, and politics of Romania. From the stunning landscapes of Transylvania to the bustling streets of Bucharest, this handbook offers a captivating exploration of the country's past and present. With detailed information on Romania's economy, government, and social dynamics, it provides a valuable resource for anyone seeking to understand this fascinating nation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel Guide, Cultural Studies

Themes: Romanian history, Cultural heritage, Political landscape, Economic development
