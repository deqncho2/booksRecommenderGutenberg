Synopsis: 
In 'Dr. Jolliffe's Boys' by Hough, Lewis, a group of mischievous boys find themselves under the care of the strict and enigmatic Dr. Jolliffe. As they navigate the challenges of their new environment, they uncover a hidden secret that will test their loyalty and courage. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Discovery
