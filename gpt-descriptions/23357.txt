Synopsis: In 'Miss Mehetabel's Son' by Thomas Bailey Aldrich, a young man named Richard returns to his childhood home after many years, only to find himself entangled in a web of family secrets and hidden desires. As he delves deeper into his mother's mysterious past, Richard uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to shatter everything he thought he knew about his own identity. With each revelation, he becomes more determined to uncover the dark secrets that have haunted his family for generations, leading him on a thrilling and suspenseful journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Family Secrets, Redemption
