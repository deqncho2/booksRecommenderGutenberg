Synopsis: In 'The Truth About Tristrem Varick: A Novel' by Edgar Saltus, readers are introduced to the enigmatic Tristrem Varick, a man whose life is shrouded in mystery and intrigue. As the story unfolds, secrets from Varick's past begin to surface, revealing a complex web of deception, love, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of New York City's high society, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark corners of Varick's past, ultimately leading to a shocking revelation that will leave them questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Love, Secrets
