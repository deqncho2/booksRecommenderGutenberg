Synopsis: In "The Cleveland Era: A Chronicle of the New Order in Politics," Henry Jones Ford takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformative political landscape of Cleveland. Set in the late 19th century, this gripping narrative explores the rise of a new political order and the challenges faced by its leaders. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in a world of power struggles, corruption, and the pursuit of progress. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Ford paints a vivid picture of a city on the brink of change, where political alliances are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Biography

Themes: Political reform, Power dynamics, Corruption, Progress and change
