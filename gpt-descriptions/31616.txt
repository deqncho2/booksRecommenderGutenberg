Synopsis: In 'Health Lessons, Book 1' by Davison, Alvin, a mysterious illness sweeps through a small town, leaving its residents in a state of panic and confusion. As the community struggles to find answers, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind the outbreak. With each passing day, they delve deeper into a web of secrets, encountering unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries along the way. As the stakes rise, they must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their town. 'Health Lessons, Book 1' is a gripping and suspenseful tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Community, Courage, Secrets
