Synopsis: In 'Sons and Daughters' by Mrs. Oliphant, a small Scottish town becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of family secrets, forbidden love, and societal expectations. As the lives of the town's inhabitants intertwine, a web of intrigue and hidden desires begins to unravel, revealing the complex relationships between parents and children, husbands and wives, and the choices they make in the pursuit of happiness. With its richly drawn characters and a plot that keeps you guessing until the very end, 'Sons and Daughters' is a compelling exploration of the human heart and the lengths we go to protect those we love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Love and sacrifice, Social expectations
