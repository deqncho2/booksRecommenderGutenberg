Synopsis: In the Shadow of Death is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of Detective Pieter Kritzinger as he investigates a series of gruesome murders in a small town. As the body count rises, Kritzinger finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception, leading him to question his own sanity and the true nature of evil. With each twist and turn, the detective must confront his darkest fears and confront the shadows that lurk in the depths of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Crime fiction, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. evil, Sanity and madness, Deception and secrets
