Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Barnegat, a young artist named Maurice finds himself torn between his passion for painting and his duty to his family's fishing business. As he navigates the challenges of love, loss, and the changing tides of life, Maurice must confront the secrets that lie beneath the surface of his seemingly idyllic community. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Jersey Shore, 'The Tides of Barnegat' is a captivating tale of self-discovery, resilience, and the enduring power of the sea.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Artistic expression, Family obligations, Secrets and hidden truths, Nature's influence on human lives
