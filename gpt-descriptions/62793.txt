Synopsis: In 'Gray Lady and the Birds: Stories of the Bird Year for Home and School', Mabel Osgood Wright takes readers on a captivating journey through the seasons, exploring the fascinating world of birds. From the arrival of the first migratory birds in spring to the winter's hushed stillness, this collection of stories weaves together the lives of various bird species and their interactions with the natural world. Through vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Wright invites readers to witness the beauty, intelligence, and resilience of these feathered creatures, while also shedding light on the challenges they face in an ever-changing environment. 

Genres: Nature, Non-fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Environmental awareness, Appreciation of nature
