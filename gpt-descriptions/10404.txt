Synopsis: In 'Man-Size' by William MacLeod Raine, a young man named Jim is faced with a life-altering decision when he discovers a hidden secret that could change his future forever. As he grapples with the consequences of his actions, Jim finds himself caught in a web of danger, love, and betrayal. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and ultimately determine his own destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Identity and self-discovery
