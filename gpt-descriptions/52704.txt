Synopsis: In 'As It Was Written: A Jewish Musician's Story' by Harland, Henry, we are taken on a captivating journey through the life of a Jewish musician, filled with passion, resilience, and the power of music. From his humble beginnings in a small village to his rise to fame, the protagonist faces numerous challenges and triumphs, all while navigating his identity as a Jewish artist in a changing world. As he grapples with love, loss, and the pursuit of his dreams, the reader is drawn into a rich tapestry of history, culture, and the universal language of music.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Music

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Love and Loss, Cultural Heritage
