Synopsis: In 'How Mr. Rabbit Lost his Tail: Hollow Tree Stories', follow the misadventures of Mr. Rabbit as he embarks on a quest to find his lost tail. Joined by his friends, including Mr. Crow, Mr. Possum, and Mr. Turtle, they encounter a series of humorous and unexpected challenges along the way. Will Mr. Rabbit ever recover his beloved tail, or will he have to learn to embrace his new tail-less identity?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Acceptance, Resilience
