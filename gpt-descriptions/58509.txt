Synopsis: 

'Brave Belgians' by Buffin, Camille is a gripping historical novel set during World War I. The story follows a group of courageous Belgians who find themselves caught in the midst of the German invasion. As they face unimaginable challenges and witness the horrors of war, their bravery and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With their lives hanging in the balance, these ordinary individuals must summon extraordinary courage to protect their homeland and fight for their freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Resilience, Patriotism, Sacrifice
