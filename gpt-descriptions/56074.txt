Synopsis: 
'Pan-Worship, and Other Poems' by Eleanor Farjeon is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through enchanting landscapes, mythical creatures, and the depths of human emotions. From the whimsical world of Pan to the poignant exploration of love and loss, each poem in this collection is a lyrical masterpiece that will transport readers to a realm of beauty and introspection.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Nature, Mythology, Love, Loss, Imagination
