Synopsis: In the remote Scottish Highlands, a mysterious and enigmatic figure named Lochinvar arrives, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, Lochinvar's true identity and purpose remain shrouded in mystery. With the backdrop of breathtaking landscapes and a cast of unforgettable characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with love, betrayal, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Desire
