Synopsis: 
In the quiet village of King's Abbot, the wealthy and respected Roger Ackroyd is found dead in his study, stabbed with a dagger. As the local police investigate the murder, they are joined by the famous detective Hercule Poirot, who soon discovers that everyone in the household has something to hide. With a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists, Poirot must unravel the truth behind the murder of Roger Ackroyd, in a case that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Trust, Justice
