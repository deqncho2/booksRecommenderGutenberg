Synopsis: In "The Confessions of the Celebrated Countess of Lichtenau, Late Mrs. Rietz: Now Confined in the Fortress of Gloglau as a State-prisoner" by Husen, Heinrich, we are introduced to the intriguing life of the Countess of Lichtenau, a woman of great beauty and charm who finds herself imprisoned in the fortress of Gloglau. Through her captivating confessions, we delve into the scandalous world of courtly affairs, political intrigue, and the pursuit of power. As the Countess reveals her secrets, the reader is left wondering how she ended up in such a predicament and what her ultimate fate will be.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Drama

Themes: Power and manipulation, Love and desire, Betrayal and loyalty
