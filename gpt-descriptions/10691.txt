Synopsis: 
In 'Select Speeches of Kossuth' by Lajos Kossuth, readers are transported to a time of political upheaval and passionate oratory. This collection showcases the powerful speeches of Kossuth, a prominent Hungarian statesman and leader of the 1848 revolution. From his stirring calls for freedom and democracy to his impassioned pleas for national independence, Kossuth's words resonate with the spirit of a nation yearning for change. Through his eloquence and unwavering determination, Kossuth becomes a symbol of hope and inspiration for his people, leaving readers captivated by his unwavering commitment to justice and liberty.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political speeches, Historical

Themes: Freedom, Democracy, Nationalism, Revolution
