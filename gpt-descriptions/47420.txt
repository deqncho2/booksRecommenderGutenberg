Synopsis: 
'Unaddressed Letters' is a captivating mystery novel that follows the life of Emily, a young woman who stumbles upon a hidden collection of letters in her deceased grandmother's attic. As she delves into the letters, she becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind their mysterious origins and the secrets they hold. With each letter she reads, Emily is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue, deception, and long-buried family secrets. As she unravels the truth, she discovers that the past has a way of resurfacing, and that some secrets are better left buried. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Intrigue, Deception, The power of the past
