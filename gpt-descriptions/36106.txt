Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Trevlyn Hold', secrets and mysteries surround the ancestral estate of Trevlyn Hold, where a young woman named Edith finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue. As she delves deeper into the history of the estate and its inhabitants, Edith uncovers dark family secrets, forbidden love affairs, and a haunting presence that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With each revelation, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Trevlyn Hold.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Haunting presence, Intrigue
