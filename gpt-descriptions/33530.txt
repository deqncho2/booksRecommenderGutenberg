Synopsis: 
In "Dorothy Dale at Glenwood School" by Margaret Penrose, readers are introduced to the spirited and adventurous Dorothy Dale as she embarks on a new chapter of her life at Glenwood School. As Dorothy navigates the challenges of making new friends, facing unexpected mysteries, and unraveling secrets, she discovers that her determination and quick thinking are her greatest assets. With her loyal friends by her side, Dorothy's journey at Glenwood School becomes a thrilling and heartwarming tale of friendship, courage, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: 
Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
