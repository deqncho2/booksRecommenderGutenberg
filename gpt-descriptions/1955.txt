Synopsis: In 'The Darrow Enigma' by Melvin L. Severy, a renowned archaeologist, Dr. Jonathan Darrow, stumbles upon a mysterious artifact during an excavation in Egypt. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, he becomes entangled in a web of ancient riddles, hidden codes, and dangerous adversaries. With time running out, Dr. Darrow must decipher the enigma before it falls into the wrong hands, risking not only his own life but also the fate of humanity.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Mysteries, Code-breaking, Survival
