Synopsis: In 'The Poor Plutocrats' by Jókai, Mór, a young and ambitious man named Lajos sets out on a journey to make his fortune in the bustling city of Budapest. However, his dreams of wealth and success are soon challenged by unexpected circumstances and a series of misfortunes. As Lajos navigates the treacherous world of business and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he must confront his own values and priorities. Will he be able to rise above his humble beginnings and achieve his aspirations, or will he be forever trapped in the cycle of poverty?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Social mobility, Ambition, Wealth and poverty
