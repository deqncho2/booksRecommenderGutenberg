Synopsis: 

In 'Tell England: A Study in a Generation' by Raymond, Ernest, we are transported to the early 20th century, amidst the backdrop of the Great War. This gripping novel follows the lives of a group of young British boys as they navigate the challenges and horrors of war, their innocence gradually giving way to the harsh realities of battle. From the idyllic countryside to the trenches of France, their journey is one of camaraderie, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of youth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Youth and Innocence, Friendship and Loyalty, Loss and Sacrifice
