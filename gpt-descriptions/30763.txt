Synopsis: In 'Am I Still There?' by James R. Hall, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own demons and question the very nature of reality. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Conspiracy, Identity, Redemption
