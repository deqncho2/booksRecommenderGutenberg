Synopsis: In "The Sinn Fein Rebellion as I Saw It," Mrs. Hamilton takes us on a captivating journey through her personal experiences during the Sinn Fein Rebellion in Ireland. From the chaos and violence on the streets to the clandestine meetings and political intrigue behind closed doors, she provides a unique and intimate perspective on this pivotal moment in Irish history. As she navigates through the complexities of loyalty, patriotism, and the fight for independence, Mrs. Hamilton's gripping narrative keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the rebellion.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Political Drama

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Independence, Political Activism
