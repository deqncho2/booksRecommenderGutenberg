Synopsis: 
Step into a world of enchantment and wonder with 'The Magic House, and Other Poems' by Scott, Duncan Campbell. This captivating collection of poems takes readers on a whimsical journey through the realms of imagination, where houses come alive, dreams take flight, and ordinary objects reveal extraordinary secrets. From the mystical allure of a hidden garden to the mischievous antics of talking animals, each poem unveils a new and delightful surprise. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Scott, Duncan Campbell invites readers of all ages to explore the magic that lies within the ordinary and discover the extraordinary in the everyday.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Wonder, Transformation, Nature, Adventure
