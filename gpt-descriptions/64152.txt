Synopsis: In 'The Strange Visitation' by Marie Corelli, a young woman named Mavis Clare experiences a series of inexplicable events that shake the very foundation of her beliefs. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her, she finds herself caught between the realms of the living and the dead, questioning the boundaries of reality and the existence of supernatural forces. With each encounter, Mavis's curiosity grows, leading her on a thrilling journey that will challenge her perceptions and force her to confront the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Gothic

Themes: The nature of reality, the power of belief, the thin line between life and death
