Synopsis: 
In 'The Avenger' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows, seeking justice for a crime that has haunted him for years. As he embarks on a relentless pursuit to uncover the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger. With his cunning intellect and unwavering determination, he stops at nothing to bring the perpetrators to justice, even if it means risking everything he holds dear. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of vengeance is explored in this gripping tale of suspense and intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Betrayal
