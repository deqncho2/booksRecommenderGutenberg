Synopsis: In 'The Medici Boots' by Swet, Pearl Norton, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious pair of boots during an excavation in Florence. Little does she know that these boots hold a secret that could change history forever. As Pearl delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the boots, she finds herself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and ancient secrets. With time running out, Pearl must navigate the treacherous world of art theft, historical conspiracies, and unexpected romance to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Art and history, Secrets and conspiracies, Love and betrayal
