Synopsis: 
In 'The Forge in the Forest: Being the Narrative of the Acadian Ranger, Jean de Mer, Seigneur de Briart; and How He Crossed the Black Abbé; and of His Adventures in a Strange Fellowship', readers are transported to the captivating world of Jean de Mer, a brave Acadian Ranger. Jean finds himself entangled in a thrilling journey as he crosses paths with the mysterious Black Abbé and becomes part of an extraordinary fellowship. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns, the secrets and revelations, and the unexpected alliances that Jean encounters along the way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage and Bravery, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
