Synopsis: In 'The York Problem' by Kastle, Herbert D., a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of mysterious disappearances plague the city of York. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that challenge his wit and intuition. With time running out, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own demons to solve the enigma that is the York Problem.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Redemption, the Complexity of Human Nature
