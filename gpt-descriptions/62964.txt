Synopsis: 
In 'The Peacock Feather: A Romance' by LM (Leslie Moore), a young woman named Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love when she inherits a mysterious peacock feather. As she delves deeper into the feather's history, Amelia uncovers a hidden world of passion, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means risking her own heart and happiness. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque countryside, this captivating romance will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Family secrets, Betrayal, Self-discovery
