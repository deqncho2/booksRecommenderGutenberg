Synopsis: In 'A Svmmarie and Trve Discovrse of Sir Frances Drakes VVest Indian Voyage: Wherein were taken, the townes of Saint Iago, Sancto Domingo, Cartagena & Saint Augustine,' Walter Bigges takes us on an exhilarating journey through the daring exploits of Sir Frances Drake in the West Indies. As Drake and his crew embark on their voyage, they encounter thrilling adventures, unexpected challenges, and engage in intense battles with the towns of Saint Iago, Sancto Domingo, Cartagena, and Saint Augustine. Bigges masterfully captures the excitement and danger of Drake's expedition, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of this epic voyage.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Heroism, Conquest
