Synopsis: 

In "Shackleton's Last Voyage: The Story of the Quest," Macklin and Wild take readers on a thrilling and harrowing journey to the Antarctic. This gripping true story follows the legendary explorer Sir Ernest Shackleton and his crew as they embark on their final expedition aboard the ship Quest. Battling treacherous icy waters, extreme weather conditions, and the constant threat of disaster, Shackleton and his men face unimaginable challenges in their quest for discovery and survival. As the gripping narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the resilience, courage, and determination of these extraordinary individuals.

Genres: Adventure, Biography, History

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Leadership, Survival
