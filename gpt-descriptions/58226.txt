Synopsis: In 'The Ghost in the Tower: An Episode in Jacobia', a small town is plagued by a mysterious ghost that haunts the old tower. As the townspeople try to unravel the secrets behind the ghostly presence, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried secrets that threaten to tear the community apart. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth behind the ghost in the tower.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Supernatural phenomena
