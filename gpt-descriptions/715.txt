Synopsis: In "The Moon Endureth: Tales and Fancies," John Buchan takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that blend elements of mystery, adventure, and the supernatural. From the eerie landscapes of Scotland to the exotic settings of Africa, each tale presents a unique and enthralling narrative that will leave readers spellbound. With unexpected twists and turns, Buchan's storytelling prowess shines as he explores themes of fate, the power of the human spirit, and the enduring nature of the moon.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Fate, Human Spirit, Endurance
