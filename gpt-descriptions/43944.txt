Synopsis: In 'The Devil-Tree of El Dorado: A Novel' by Frank Aubrey, a group of adventurers embarks on a treacherous journey deep into the heart of the Amazon rainforest in search of a legendary tree said to possess unimaginable powers. As they navigate through the dense jungle, they encounter mysterious tribes, deadly creatures, and face their own inner demons. The quest for the Devil-Tree of El Dorado becomes a thrilling and perilous adventure that tests their courage, loyalty, and the limits of human desire.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Mythology, Power and Greed
