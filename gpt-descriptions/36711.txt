Synopsis: In 'Hookers' by Mann, Richard F., a young journalist named Alex finds himself immersed in the gritty underworld of prostitution while investigating a series of mysterious disappearances. As he delves deeper into the lives of the women involved, he uncovers a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected connections that challenge his own beliefs and morality. With each encounter, Alex becomes entangled in a complex game of power, desire, and survival, where nothing is as it seems and the line between right and wrong blurs. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Morality, Power dynamics, Identity, Survival
