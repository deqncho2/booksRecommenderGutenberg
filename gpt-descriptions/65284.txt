Synopsis: In 'Massasoit's Town Sowams in Pokanoket, Its History Legends and Traditions' by Baker, Virginia, readers are transported to the fascinating world of the Native American tribe, the Pokanoket. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author unveils the rich history, captivating legends, and cherished traditions of Massasoit's Town Sowams. From the tribe's early beginnings to their encounters with European settlers, this book offers a captivating exploration of a vibrant culture that has often been overlooked. As readers delve into the pages, they will be enthralled by the tales of bravery, spirituality, and resilience that shaped the lives of the Pokanoket people.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Cultural studies, Indigenous history

Themes: Native American culture, Colonial encounters, Oral traditions, Identity and heritage
