Synopsis: In 'Peace in Friendship Village' by Gale, Zona, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in the midst of a war-torn village, where friendships are tested and secrets are unveiled. As she navigates through the chaos, Lily discovers the power of unity and the strength of love, ultimately leading her on a journey of self-discovery and hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Love, Resilience, Identity
