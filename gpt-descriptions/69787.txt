Synopsis: In "The Splendour of Asia: The Story and Teaching of the Buddha," Lily Adams Beck takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and teachings of the Buddha. From his humble beginnings as Prince Siddhartha to his enlightenment and the establishment of Buddhism, this book delves into the profound wisdom and spiritual insights that have shaped the lives of millions. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Beck brings to life the vibrant landscapes, rich cultures, and timeless teachings of Asia, offering readers a profound understanding of the Buddha's transformative journey.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Spiritual Fiction, Biographical Fiction

Themes: Enlightenment, Spiritual Awakening, Self-Discovery, Eastern Philosophy, Cultural Exploration
