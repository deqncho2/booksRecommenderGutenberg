Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Martins Of Cro' Martin', the captivating story continues as the Martin family faces new challenges and unexpected twists. As the family's fortunes fluctuate, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and love is put to the ultimate test. With a mix of adventure, romance, and intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Family Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Social class, Love and sacrifice
