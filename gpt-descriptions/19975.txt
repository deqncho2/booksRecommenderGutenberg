Synopsis: 
In "Base-ball: How to become a player, with the origin, history and explanation of the game," John Montgomery Ward takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the captivating world of baseball. From its humble beginnings to its rise as America's favorite pastime, Ward delves into the origins and evolution of the game, providing a comprehensive understanding of its rules, strategies, and techniques. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, this book offers aspiring players and avid fans alike a fascinating exploration of the sport that has captured the hearts of millions.

Genres: Sports, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Passion for the game, Evolution and growth, Strategy and skill
