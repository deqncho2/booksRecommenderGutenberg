Synopsis: In 'The Little School-Mothers' by L. T. Meade, a group of young women find themselves unexpectedly thrust into the role of school-mothers, responsible for the education and well-being of a group of mischievous children. As they navigate the challenges of teaching and disciplining their young charges, they also discover secrets and mysteries that will test their courage and determination. With a blend of heartwarming moments and thrilling twists, this story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Responsibility, Discovery
