Synopsis: 
In 'The Misses Mallett (The Bridge Dividing)' by E.H. Young, two sisters, Miss Mallett and Miss Harriet, live a quiet and secluded life in a small English village. Their lives are disrupted when a young and charming stranger arrives, stirring up long-buried emotions and secrets. As the sisters navigate their complicated relationships with each other and the newcomer, they are forced to confront their past and make choices that will shape their future. With its intriguing characters and hidden mysteries, this novel explores the complexities of family, love, and the power of secrets.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and their consequences, Love and relationships
