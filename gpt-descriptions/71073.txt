Synopsis: In 'The Starvel Hollow Tragedy: An Inspector French Case', Inspector French is faced with a perplexing mystery when a wealthy businessman is found dead in his locked study, with no apparent cause of death. As French delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives among the residents of Starvel Hollow. With time running out and the pressure mounting, French must unravel the truth behind this baffling tragedy before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Greed, Justice
