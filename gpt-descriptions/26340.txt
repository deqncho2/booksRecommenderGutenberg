Synopsis: 
'Of Natural and Supernatural Things: Also of the first Tincture, Root, and Spirit of Metals and Minerals, how the same are Conceived, Generated, Brought forth, Changed, and Augmented' by Basilius Valentinus is a captivating exploration of the hidden secrets and transformative powers of metals and minerals. Through a combination of ancient wisdom and alchemical knowledge, Valentinus delves into the mysterious processes by which these substances are conceived, generated, and ultimately transformed. From the birth of metals to their augmentation, this work unveils the intricate relationship between the natural and supernatural realms, offering readers a glimpse into the profound mysteries of the universe.

Genres: Alchemy, Philosophy, Occultism

Themes: Transformation, Mysticism, Natural Philosophy
