Synopsis: In 'Eyes That Watch' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the power to unlock the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into its enigmatic origins, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, pursued by shadowy forces determined to possess the artifact at any cost. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must race against time to uncover the truth and protect the world from an unimaginable threat.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the consequences of discovery
