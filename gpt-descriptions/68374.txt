Synopsis: In 'Friends and Enemies' by Leiber, Fritz, a group of friends find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger when they stumble upon a mysterious artifact with incredible powers. As they navigate through a world of shifting alliances and hidden agendas, they must confront their own personal demons and make difficult choices that will test the limits of their loyalty and friendship. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Power
