Synopsis: In 'A Coin of Edward VII: A Detective Story' by Fergus Hume, a seemingly ordinary coin becomes the center of a mysterious and thrilling investigation. When a man is found dead with the coin clutched in his hand, Detective Jack Tarling is determined to uncover the truth behind its significance. As he delves deeper into the case, Tarling finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead him on a captivating journey through the criminal underworld of London. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Tarling must race against time to solve the puzzle before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Deception, Criminal Underworld, Investigation and Deduction
