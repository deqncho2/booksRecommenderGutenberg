Synopsis: In 'Mark Tidd, Manufacturer' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey that takes them from their small town to the bustling city. Led by the charismatic Mark Tidd, they stumble upon an opportunity to start their own business, but soon find themselves caught up in a web of mystery and danger. As they navigate through unexpected challenges, the boys learn valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Entrepreneurship, Problem-solving, Childhood Innocence
