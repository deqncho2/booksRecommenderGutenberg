Synopsis: In 'Marriage in Free Society' by Edward Carpenter, a young woman named Emily finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires for independence and self-discovery. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and personal freedom, Emily's journey takes her on a path of self-discovery that challenges the norms of her time. With thought-provoking twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of personal happiness in a society bound by tradition.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and desire, Individual freedom, Gender roles, Societal expectations
