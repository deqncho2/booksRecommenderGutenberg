Synopsis: In "Sergeant York And His People," Sam K. Cowan takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Alvin C. York, a humble Tennessee farmer who becomes an unlikely hero during World War I. From his early struggles and religious awakening to his remarkable acts of bravery on the battlefield, this gripping biography explores the extraordinary transformation of a man who went on to inspire a nation.

Genres: Biography, History, War

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Transformation
