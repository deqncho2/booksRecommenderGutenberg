Synopsis: In the final volume of the thrilling 'Colville of the Guards' series, secrets are unveiled, loyalties are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. As Captain Colville finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas, he must navigate treacherous waters to protect those he holds dear. With danger lurking at every turn, will Colville's unwavering determination be enough to overcome the forces working against him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Political Intrigue
