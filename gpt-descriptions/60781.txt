Synopsis: 
'Catalogue of Standard Recitations, Numbers 19-34: For School, Lyceum, Parlor and Other Entertainments' by M.J. Ivers & Co. is a captivating collection of recitations that will transport readers to various settings and evoke a range of emotions. From heartfelt stories to humorous anecdotes, this catalogue offers a diverse selection of pieces suitable for different occasions. Whether it's a school event, a gathering in the parlor, or a public performance, these recitations are sure to captivate and entertain audiences of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Drama, Fiction

Themes: Emotion, Entertainment, Performance
