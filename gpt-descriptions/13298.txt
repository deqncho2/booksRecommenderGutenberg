Synopsis: 
The California Birthday Book is a captivating anthology that brings together a collection of prose and poetry from talented living California authors. Each selection is accompanied by a brief biographical sketch, providing readers with a glimpse into the lives and inspirations of these remarkable writers. From heartfelt reflections on love and loss to vivid descriptions of the California landscape, this book offers a diverse range of literary voices that will transport readers to the vibrant world of contemporary California literature.

Genres: Anthology, Poetry, Prose

Themes: California culture, Identity, Nature, Love, Loss
