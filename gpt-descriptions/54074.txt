Synopsis: In 'An Eye for an Eye: Big Blue Book no. B-24' by Clarence Darrow, a gripping tale unfolds as a small-town lawyer takes on a seemingly impossible case. When a young man is accused of a heinous crime, the lawyer must navigate a web of deceit, corruption, and personal vendettas to uncover the truth. As the trial progresses, the lawyer finds himself entangled in a dangerous game where justice hangs in the balance. With unexpected twists and turns, this legal thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final verdict is reached.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Crime

Themes: Justice, Morality, Corruption
