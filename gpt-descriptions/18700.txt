Synopsis: In 'The Mayor of Warwick' by Herbert M. Hopkins, the small town of Warwick is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives and runs for mayor. As the election campaign heats up, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and deception. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Small-town politics, Betrayal, Identity
