Synopsis: 
In 'File No. 113' by Emile Gaboriau, a seemingly ordinary murder case takes a thrilling turn when a detective uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies. When a wealthy and influential man is found dead in his home, the police quickly arrest a suspect. However, as the investigation progresses, the detective begins to suspect that there is more to the case than meets the eye. With each new clue, the detective delves deeper into a complex and twisted plot, where nothing is as it seems. As the truth slowly unravels, the detective must navigate through a maze of deception and betrayal to uncover the shocking truth behind File No. 113.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets
