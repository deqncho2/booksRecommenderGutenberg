Synopsis: In 'The Kingdom of God is Within You; What is Art?' Tolstoy delves into the profound questions of spirituality and the purpose of art. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, he challenges conventional beliefs and explores the true essence of human existence. With his characteristic insight and intellectual prowess, Tolstoy invites readers on a journey of self-discovery and contemplation, urging them to question the foundations of their own beliefs and values.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction, Essay

Themes: Spirituality, Morality, Artistic Expression
