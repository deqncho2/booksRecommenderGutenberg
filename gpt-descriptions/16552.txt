Synopsis: In 'The Next of Kin: Those who Wait and Wonder', Nellie L. McClung takes readers on an emotional journey through the lives of women left behind during World War I. As they anxiously await news of their loved ones at war, these women find strength, resilience, and unexpected friendships in the face of uncertainty. Through their stories, McClung explores the sacrifices, fears, and hopes of those who are left behind, shedding light on the often overlooked experiences of women during wartime.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Women's fiction

Themes: Love and loss, Strength and resilience, Gender roles and expectations
