Synopsis: In 'A Safety Match' by Hay, Ian, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious object. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding this enigmatic item, he becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time, where every decision could mean life or death. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Secrets, Danger, Consequences
