Synopsis: In 'The Misuse of Mind' by Stephen Karin, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could revolutionize the world. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he realizes that his invention has the potential to be used for nefarious purposes. Caught between his desire for scientific progress and the ethical implications of his creation, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and unexpected consequences. As the line between right and wrong blurs, the protagonist must confront the dark side of human nature and make a choice that will determine the fate of his invention and possibly the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Human Nature
