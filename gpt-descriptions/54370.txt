Synopsis: In "What's What in America," Brewster takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse and ever-evolving landscape of America. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the Midwest, this book offers a unique exploration of the people, places, and cultural phenomena that shape the nation. Through vivid storytelling and insightful observations, Brewster uncovers the hidden gems and untold stories that make America truly remarkable. Prepare to be enthralled by the rich tapestry of American life that unfolds within these pages.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: American identity, Diversity, Cultural exploration
