Synopsis: 
In 'Wild Animals of the Rockies: With a List of Mammals found in Rocky Mountain National Park', James P. Gilligan takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of the Rocky Mountains. Through vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, Gilligan introduces us to the diverse and awe-inspiring wildlife that calls this majestic region home. From elusive predators to graceful herbivores, this book provides a captivating glimpse into the lives of the wild animals that roam the Rockies. Whether you are an avid nature enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of the natural world, this book is sure to ignite your sense of adventure and leave you yearning to explore the untamed beauty of Rocky Mountain National Park.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Wildlife

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Biodiversity, Natural beauty
