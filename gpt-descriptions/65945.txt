Synopsis: In 'The Toy' by Kris Neville, a seemingly ordinary toy holds a mysterious power that captivates the imagination of a young boy. As the toy's influence grows, it begins to blur the lines between reality and fantasy, leading to unexpected consequences. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of perception and the power of the human mind, leaving readers questioning the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Perception, Imagination, Reality vs. Fantasy
