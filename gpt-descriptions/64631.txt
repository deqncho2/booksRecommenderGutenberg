Synopsis: In 'Let the Ants Try' by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that could change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his invention, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel everything he has worked for. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his creation and ensure the future of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
