Synopsis: In 'Clutterbuck's Treasure' by Frederick Whishaw, a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the hidden secrets of an ancient treasure. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Amelia encounters unexpected allies and treacherous foes, all while navigating through dangerous landscapes and deciphering cryptic clues. With time running out, she must race against the odds to unlock the truth behind Clutterbuck's Treasure and protect it from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Betrayal, Redemption
