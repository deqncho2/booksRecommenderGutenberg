Synopsis: 
'Four Plays of Gil Vicente' is a collection of four captivating plays written by the renowned Portuguese playwright, Gil Vicente. Each play takes the reader on a journey through different aspects of society, exploring themes of love, deception, and the human condition. From the comedic and satirical 'The Ugly One' to the thought-provoking 'The Inquisition,' Vicente's plays offer a unique blend of wit, social commentary, and dramatic storytelling. Prepare to be enthralled by the colorful characters, clever dialogue, and unexpected twists that unfold in these timeless works.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Love, Deception, Human Condition, Social Commentary
