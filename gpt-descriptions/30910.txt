Synopsis: In 'The Queen Against Owen' by Upward, Allen, a young and ambitious lawyer named Owen finds himself entangled in a high-stakes legal battle against the formidable Queen of a powerful nation. As the case unfolds, Owen must navigate treacherous political waters, face personal dilemmas, and uncover shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With his reputation and life on the line, Owen must summon all his wit and courage to challenge the Queen and fight for justice.

Genres: Legal thriller, political drama, suspense

Themes: Power struggles, morality, personal sacrifice
