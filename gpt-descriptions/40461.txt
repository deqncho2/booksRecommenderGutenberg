Synopsis: In "Indian Home Rule," Mahatma Gandhi presents a compelling vision for the future of India, exploring the path towards self-governance and liberation from British colonial rule. Through thought-provoking insights and impassioned arguments, Gandhi challenges conventional notions of power and advocates for a nonviolent struggle to achieve independence. This influential work offers a profound exploration of the principles of Satyagraha (truth-force) and Swaraj (self-rule), inspiring readers to reflect on the nature of freedom and the potential for transformative change.

Genres: Political philosophy, Nonfiction, Autobiography

Themes: Independence, Nonviolence, Self-governance, Colonialism, Civil disobedience
