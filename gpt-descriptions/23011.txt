Synopsis: In 'Eatin' Crow; and The Best Man in Garotte' by Harris, Frank, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the lives of two intriguing characters. The story follows a man named Crow, who finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger as he navigates the treacherous streets of Garotte. Meanwhile, the enigmatic Best Man, known for his impeccable skills, becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of power and betrayal. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of trust is explored. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power dynamics
