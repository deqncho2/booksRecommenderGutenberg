Synopsis: In "The Betrothed," set in 17th-century Lombardy, two young lovers, Renzo and Lucia, are torn apart by the machinations of a powerful nobleman. As they navigate through a web of political intrigue, religious conflict, and personal hardships, their love is tested and their faith is challenged. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way and find their way back to each other?

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and devotion, Social hierarchy, Religious persecution, Power and corruption
