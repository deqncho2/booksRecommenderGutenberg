Synopsis: In 'The Crime Club' by Holt-White, W., a group of friends forms a secret society dedicated to solving crimes that have baffled the police. When they stumble upon a mysterious case involving a missing person, they find themselves drawn into a dangerous web of deceit and intrigue. As they dig deeper, they uncover shocking secrets that threaten to tear their club apart. With their lives on the line, the members of the Crime Club must use their wits and teamwork to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Deception, Justice
