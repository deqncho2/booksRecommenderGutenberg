Synopsis: 
'Standard Selections: A Collection and Adaptation of Superior Productions from Best Authors for Use in Class Room and on the Platform' is a captivating anthology that brings together a diverse range of exceptional literary works. From thought-provoking plays to poignant poems and engaging prose, this collection offers a rich tapestry of stories that will captivate readers and ignite their imagination. Each selection has been carefully chosen and adapted to be used in classrooms and on platforms, making it an invaluable resource for educators and performers alike. 

Genres: Anthology, Drama, Poetry, Prose

Themes: Education, Performance, Literature, Imagination
