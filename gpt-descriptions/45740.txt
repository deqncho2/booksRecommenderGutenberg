Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Scouts of Lakeville High' by Leslie W. Quirk, a group of misfit high school students find themselves unexpectedly thrown together in a Boy Scouts troop. As they navigate the challenges of scouting, they discover hidden talents, forge unlikely friendships, and uncover a secret that could change their lives forever. With humor, heart, and a touch of mystery, this captivating story takes readers on a thrilling adventure that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: 
Young Adult, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Friendship, Self-discovery, Adventure
