Synopsis: 

'Diccionario Ingles-Español-Tagalog: Con partes de la oracion y pronunciacion figurada' by Calderón, Sofronio G. is a captivating language reference book that takes readers on a linguistic journey through English, Spanish, and Tagalog. This comprehensive dictionary not only provides translations but also includes detailed explanations of grammar and pronunciation, making it an invaluable resource for language learners and enthusiasts. With its user-friendly format and extensive coverage, this book promises to enhance the reader's understanding and mastery of these three languages.

Genres: Language Reference, Education, Linguistics

Themes: Language Learning, Cultural Exchange, Communication
