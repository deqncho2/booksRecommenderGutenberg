Synopsis:
In 'Jewellery' by H. Clifford Smith, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a priceless piece of jewellery goes missing, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of love, loyalty, and trust. As the search for the missing jewel intensifies, secrets are unearthed, alliances are formed, and unexpected connections are revealed. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where appearances can be deceiving and where the true value of a precious gem lies not in its monetary worth, but in the power it holds over those who possess it.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes:
Deception, Betrayal, Greed, Redemption
