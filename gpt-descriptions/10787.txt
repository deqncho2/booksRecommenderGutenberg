Synopsis: In 'The Toll-House: Sailor's Knots, Part 7,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with mystery and suspense. Set in a remote toll-house, the story follows a group of strangers who find themselves trapped together during a stormy night. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the toll-house becomes a hotbed of intrigue and danger. With each passing moment, the characters must confront their pasts and make choices that will determine their fate. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Confronting the past, Survival
