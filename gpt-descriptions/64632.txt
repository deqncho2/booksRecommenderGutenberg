Synopsis: In 'Cargo to Callisto' by Bixby, Jerome, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous journey to deliver a mysterious cargo to the distant moon of Callisto. As they navigate through treacherous space, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the ultimate test of their humanity. With each passing moment, the stakes rise higher, and the fate of not only the crew but also the entire mission hangs in the balance. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Sacrifice, Morality
