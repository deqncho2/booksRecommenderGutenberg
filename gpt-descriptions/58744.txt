Synopsis: In 'Money is the Root of All Good' by Wilkins, Patrick, a struggling artist named Max finds himself caught in a web of greed and temptation when he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that promises unimaginable wealth. As Max's life takes an unexpected turn, he must navigate the dark side of money and confront the consequences of his choices. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale explores the true nature of wealth and its power to corrupt.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Greed, Morality, Consequences
