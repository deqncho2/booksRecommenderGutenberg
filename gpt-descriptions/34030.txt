Synopsis: 

'Turning and Boring' is a comprehensive and specialized treatise that delves into the world of machining, specifically focusing on turning and boring methods. Written for machinists, students in industrial and engineering schools, as well as apprentices, this book explores the modern practices associated with engine lathes, turret lathes, and vertical and horizontal boring machines. With a wealth of knowledge and practical insights, 'Turning and Boring' offers a valuable resource for those seeking to enhance their skills and understanding in the field of machining.

Genres: Technical, Engineering, Industrial

Themes: Machining techniques, Modern practices, Skill development
