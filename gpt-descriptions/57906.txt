Synopsis: 

'Wild Life at the Land's End: Observations of the Habits and Haunts of the Fox, Badger, Otter, Seal, Hare and of Their Pursuers in Cornwall' takes readers on a captivating journey into the untamed wilderness of Cornwall, where the lives of various fascinating creatures unfold. From the cunning fox to the elusive otter, from the playful seal to the swift hare, this book offers a vivid exploration of their habits and habitats. Through detailed observations and vivid descriptions, the author paints a rich tapestry of the natural world, revealing the intricate relationships between these animals and their relentless pursuers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Wildlife Observation

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Animal behavior, Human-animal interactions
