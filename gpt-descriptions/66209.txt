Synopsis: In "To Sup With the Devil" by Myron I. Scholnick, a brilliant and enigmatic detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a mysterious murder. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister plot that reaches the highest echelons of power. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Power, Betrayal
