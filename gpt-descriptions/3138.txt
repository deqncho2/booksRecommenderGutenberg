Synopsis: 
In 'Ballades & Rhymes from Ballades in Blue China and Rhymes a la Mode', Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting ballades and rhymes. From tales of ancient legends to whimsical verses about everyday life, this compilation offers a delightful blend of wit, humor, and imagination. With each poem, Lang weaves a tapestry of emotions, transporting readers to different worlds and inviting them to explore the depths of human experience. Whether it's a ballade about lost love or a rhyme about the mysteries of the universe, this collection is sure to captivate and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Love, Mythology, Human Experience
