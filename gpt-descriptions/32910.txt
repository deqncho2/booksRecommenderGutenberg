Synopsis: 

'With Wolseley to Kumasi: A Tale of the First Ashanti War' follows the thrilling journey of a young British officer, Lieutenant Ronald Clive, as he embarks on a perilous mission deep into the heart of Africa. Tasked with leading a small expeditionary force, Clive must navigate treacherous terrain, face hostile tribes, and confront the formidable Ashanti warriors in their stronghold of Kumasi. As the tension mounts and the odds stack against them, Clive and his men must summon every ounce of courage and resourcefulness to achieve their objective and secure victory.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Cultural Clash, Colonialism
