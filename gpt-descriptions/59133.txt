Synopsis: In 'A Body of Divinity, Vol. 1', Thomas Ridgley delves into the profound doctrines of the Christian religion, offering a comprehensive explanation and defense of these beliefs. Drawing from a series of lectures on the Assembly's Larger Catechism, Ridgley explores the core principles of Christianity, providing a thought-provoking and enlightening exploration of faith. This volume serves as a captivating introduction to the complex and profound teachings of the Christian religion.

Genres: Theology, Religious Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Christian doctrine, Apologetics, Religious education
