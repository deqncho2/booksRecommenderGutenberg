Synopsis: In 'Cressy and Poictiers: The Story of the Black Prince's Page', we are transported to the captivating world of medieval England, where a young page named Cressy finds himself entangled in the tumultuous events surrounding the legendary Black Prince. As Cressy navigates the treacherous paths of loyalty, honor, and love, he becomes an unwitting witness to the epic battles of Cressy and Poictiers, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this historical adventure takes readers on a thrilling journey through the chivalrous world of knights, castles, and courtly intrigue.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Love, Courage, Identity
