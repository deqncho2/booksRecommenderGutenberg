Synopsis: In "By Order of the Company" by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Barbara is thrust into a world of adventure and danger when she becomes entangled in a secret mission for a powerful company. As she navigates treacherous waters, encounters mysterious characters, and faces unexpected challenges, Barbara must rely on her wit, courage, and determination to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Barbara's fate becomes intertwined with the fate of the company itself. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery
