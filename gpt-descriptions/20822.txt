Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls on the March; Or, Bessie King's Test of Friendship" by Jane L. Stewart, Bessie King finds herself facing a challenging test of friendship as she embarks on a thrilling adventure with her Camp Fire Girls. As they march forward, they encounter unexpected obstacles and must rely on their resourcefulness and loyalty to overcome them. With secrets to uncover and bonds to strengthen, Bessie's journey becomes a true test of character and friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Resourcefulness
