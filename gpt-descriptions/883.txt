Synopsis: In the bustling streets of Victorian London, a mysterious death sets off a chain of events that intertwines the lives of a diverse cast of characters. From the murky depths of the Thames to the opulent homes of the wealthy, secrets, love, and deception abound. As the story unfolds, unexpected connections are revealed, and the characters find themselves entangled in a web of greed, ambition, and redemption. With Dickens' signature wit and social commentary, 'Our Mutual Friend' takes readers on a captivating journey through the complexities of human nature and the stark contrasts of society.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Social satire

Themes: Wealth and poverty, Identity and disguise, Love and betrayal, Social class and inequality
