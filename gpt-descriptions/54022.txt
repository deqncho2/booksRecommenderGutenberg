Synopsis: In "Dorothy Dale in the West" by Margaret Penrose, young Dorothy finds herself embarking on an exciting adventure as she travels to the wild and untamed West. Accompanied by her friends, she encounters mysterious cowboys, hidden treasures, and unexpected dangers. As Dorothy navigates the rugged landscapes and encounters the colorful characters of the West, she learns valuable lessons about bravery, friendship, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Self-discovery
