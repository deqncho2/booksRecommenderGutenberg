Synopsis: 
In "Old English Colour Prints," Malcolm C. Salaman takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of 18th-century English prints. From intricate landscapes to charming portraits, this book showcases the exquisite artistry and technical mastery of the era. With detailed descriptions and stunning illustrations, Salaman unravels the stories behind these prints, shedding light on the artists, their techniques, and the cultural significance of their work. Whether you are an art enthusiast or simply curious about the history of printmaking, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a bygone era of artistic expression.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Printmaking, English Art, Cultural History
