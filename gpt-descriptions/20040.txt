Synopsis: In 'The Call of the Beaver Patrol; Or, A Break in the Glacier' by G. Harvey Ralphson, a group of adventurous young scouts embarks on a thrilling expedition to the icy wilderness. As they navigate treacherous terrain and face unexpected challenges, they uncover a mysterious secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the beaver patrol must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
