Synopsis: In "Where Duty Called; or, In Honor Bound" by George Waldo Browne, a young man finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his loyalty to his family. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, this gripping tale follows the protagonist as he navigates the complexities of honor, sacrifice, and the consequences of his choices. As he is faced with impossible decisions, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, love, and the ultimate test of character.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Duty, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Honor, Family
