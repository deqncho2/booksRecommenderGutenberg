Synopsis: 

'Abraham Lincoln and the London Punch: Cartoons, Comments and Poems, Published in the London Charivari, During the American Civil War (1861-1865)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years of the American Civil War. This unique collection showcases the witty and thought-provoking cartoons, comments, and poems published in the renowned London Charivari magazine, offering a fascinating perspective on the conflict from across the Atlantic.

As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will encounter a wide range of satirical and humorous illustrations that depict Abraham Lincoln, the iconic figure at the center of the war, in various situations and scenarios. These cartoons are accompanied by insightful comments and poems that provide additional context and commentary on the events unfolding in America during this critical period.

Genres: Satire, History, Art

Themes: American Civil War, Political Satire, International Perspectives
