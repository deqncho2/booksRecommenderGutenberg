Synopsis: In 'A Mysterious Disappearance: A Farce' by George M. Baker, a small town is thrown into chaos when a prominent citizen suddenly vanishes without a trace. As the bewildered townspeople scramble to uncover the truth, a series of hilarious misadventures and mistaken identities ensue, leading to unexpected revelations and uproarious laughter. With a cast of eccentric characters and witty dialogue, this farcical tale keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Mystery, Farce

Themes: Deception, Identity, Community
