Synopsis: In "1851; Or, The adventures of Mr. and Mrs. Sandboys and family, who came up to London to enjoy themselves, and to see the Great Exhibition," Mayhew takes readers on a delightful journey with the Sandboys, a charming family from the countryside, as they embark on an adventure to London to experience the wonders of the Great Exhibition. From their encounters with eccentric characters to their comical misadventures in the bustling city, the Sandboys' escapades provide a humorous and heartwarming tale that captures the spirit of exploration and discovery during a significant historical event.

Genres: Historical fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Family dynamics, Exploration, Cultural exchange
