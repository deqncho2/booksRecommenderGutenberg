Synopsis: In "The Black Watch at Ticonderoga and Major Duncan Campbell of Inverawe," Frederick B. Richards takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of the French and Indian War. Set against the backdrop of the iconic Battle of Ticonderoga, the story follows the brave soldiers of the Black Watch regiment and their charismatic leader, Major Duncan Campbell of Inverawe. As they face the perils of war and navigate the complexities of loyalty and honor, a gripping tale of sacrifice, courage, and unexpected alliances unfolds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Conflict
