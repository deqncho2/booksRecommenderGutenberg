Synopsis: In 'The Melting of Molly' by Maria Thompson Daviess, Molly is a spirited young woman who finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. When a charming stranger enters her life, Molly's world is turned upside down, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected romance. As she navigates the complexities of love and personal growth, Molly must confront the choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Societal expectations
