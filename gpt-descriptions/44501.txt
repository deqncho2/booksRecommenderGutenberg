Synopsis: In 'Toy Craft' by Baxter, Leon H., a young boy named Max discovers a hidden world of magical toys that come to life when no one is watching. As Max embarks on an extraordinary adventure filled with danger and wonder, he must learn to navigate the intricate politics and rivalries of the toy kingdom. With the fate of both the toy and human worlds hanging in the balance, Max must summon all his courage and ingenuity to save the day.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Bravery
