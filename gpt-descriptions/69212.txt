Synopsis: 
In 'Grounded' by Sambrot, William, a young pilot named Alex embarks on a routine flight that takes an unexpected turn, leaving her stranded in a mysterious and desolate location. As she navigates the challenges of survival and tries to find a way back home, Alex discovers startling secrets about the place she finds herself in, and the true nature of her own existence. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into the realms of science fiction and psychological exploration, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Existentialism, Survival
