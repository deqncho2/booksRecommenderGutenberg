Synopsis: In "The Head Hunters of Northern Luzon," Willcox takes readers on a thrilling journey through the remote and dangerous regions of the Philippines, where he encounters the fierce and enigmatic headhunting tribes. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding encounters, the author delves into the fascinating customs, rituals, and beliefs of these tribes, while also shedding light on the complex dynamics between the indigenous people and the colonial powers. As Willcox immerses himself in this captivating world, he uncovers shocking secrets and experiences unexpected alliances, making this a gripping and eye-opening exploration of a little-known culture.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural exploration, Colonialism, Survival
