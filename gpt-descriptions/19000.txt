Synopsis: In a near-future world where 3D printers have become a ubiquitous technology, a talented hacker named Trent discovers a dangerous secret hidden within the code of these machines. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse with powerful forces determined to keep the truth hidden. With the line between the virtual and physical worlds blurring, Trent must navigate a treacherous landscape of corporate espionage and underground resistance to expose the truth and protect the freedom of information.

Genres: Science Fiction, Cyberpunk, Thriller

Themes: Technology, Surveillance, Freedom of Information
