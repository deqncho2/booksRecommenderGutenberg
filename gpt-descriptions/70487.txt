Synopsis: In "A New Name" by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Lois is faced with a life-altering decision that will challenge her faith, test her resilience, and lead her on a journey of self-discovery. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, Lois must confront her past, confront her fears, and ultimately find the strength to embrace a new identity. This captivating story explores the power of redemption, the importance of forgiveness, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Christian fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Forgiveness, Identity
