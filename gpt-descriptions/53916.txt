Synopsis: 

In 'Ireland under the Stuarts and During the Interregnum, Vol. 2 (of 3), 1642-1660', Richard Bagwell takes us on a captivating journey through a tumultuous period in Irish history. As the second volume of his comprehensive work, Bagwell delves into the intricate details of the Irish Rebellion of 1641, the subsequent Cromwellian conquest, and the complex political landscape that emerged during the Interregnum. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Bagwell brings to life the key figures, battles, and events that shaped Ireland during this transformative era.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Rebellion, Conquest, Political upheaval, Religious conflict
