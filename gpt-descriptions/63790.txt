Synopsis: In 'Rosemary and Pansies' by Effie Waller Smith, a young woman named Rosemary finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires when she meets a charming stranger. As their paths intertwine, Rosemary must navigate the complexities of love, family, and self-discovery, leading to unexpected revelations and life-altering choices.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and passion, societal expectations, self-discovery
