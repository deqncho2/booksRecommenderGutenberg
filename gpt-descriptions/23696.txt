Synopsis: 
In 'A Gentleman's Gentleman: 1909' by Smith, Francis Hopkinson, we are transported to the elegant world of New York City in the early 20th century. The story revolves around the enigmatic character of Mr. John Smith, a gentleman's gentleman who possesses an uncanny ability to anticipate his employer's every need. As we delve deeper into Smith's life, we uncover a web of secrets, hidden desires, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With its captivating narrative and intriguing characters, this tale takes us on a journey through the complexities of human relationships and the blurred lines between servitude and friendship.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Class Divide
