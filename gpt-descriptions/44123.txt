Synopsis: In 'Jessie's Parrot' by Joanna H. Mathews, a young girl named Jessie discovers a mysterious parrot in her backyard. As she forms a bond with the parrot, she uncovers a hidden secret that leads her on an unexpected adventure. With the help of her friends, Jessie must navigate through challenges and unravel the truth behind the parrot's presence. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of trust, and the magic that can be found in unexpected places.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Discovery
