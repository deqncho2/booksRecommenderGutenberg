Synopsis: In the small town of Tylerton, something strange is happening. Every morning, the residents wake up to find that it's June 15th again, and they are forced to relive the same day over and over. As they try to unravel the mystery behind this bizarre phenomenon, they discover a dark secret lurking beneath the surface of their seemingly idyllic lives. With twists and turns at every corner, 'The Tunnel Under the World' is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality manipulation, Corporate control, Identity
