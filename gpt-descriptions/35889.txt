Synopsis: In 'Seed-time and Harvest: A Novel' by Fritz Reuter, a young man named Klaus finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his desire for personal freedom. Set in 19th century Germany, this captivating novel follows Klaus as he navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations. As he grapples with his own ambitions and the weight of tradition, Klaus embarks on a journey that will test his resolve and challenge the very fabric of his existence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Duty vs. Freedom, Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Rebellion
