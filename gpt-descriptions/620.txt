Synopsis: In "Sylvie and Bruno" by Lewis Carroll, a young girl named Sylvie and her friend Bruno embark on a whimsical journey that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. As they navigate through a series of enchanting adventures, they encounter peculiar characters, magical creatures, and thought-provoking situations. With Carroll's signature wit and charm, this captivating tale takes readers on a delightful exploration of imagination, friendship, and the power of belief.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Belief
