Synopsis: In 'The Sporting Dictionary and Rural Repository, Volume 2', author William Taplin takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of sports and rural life. This comprehensive volume provides a wealth of information on various subjects related to field sports, offering readers a fascinating insight into the intricacies of hunting, fishing, horse racing, and more. From detailed descriptions of different game animals and their habits to practical advice on training and caring for horses, Taplin's work is a treasure trove of knowledge for both seasoned sports enthusiasts and those new to the field. With its engaging anecdotes and informative entries, this volume is sure to captivate readers and leave them eager to explore the great outdoors.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Reference

Themes: Field sports, Rural life, Animal behavior, Horse care, Outdoor activities
