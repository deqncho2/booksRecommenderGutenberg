Synopsis: In 'The Limit' by Ada Leverson, a young woman named Sylvia is caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and alluring stranger. As their relationship intensifies, Sylvia finds herself questioning her own desires and the limits she is willing to push in the pursuit of love. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of passion, trust, and the consequences of crossing boundaries.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and betrayal
