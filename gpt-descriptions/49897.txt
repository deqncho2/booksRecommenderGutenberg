Synopsis: In 'The Gravity Business' by James E. Gunn, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Edward Lansing discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows him to manipulate gravity. As he delves deeper into his research, Lansing realizes that his invention has the potential to revolutionize the world, but it also attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess this incredible power. With his life and the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Lansing must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and danger to protect his invention and ensure that it is used for the greater good.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power and its consequences, Ethics and responsibility
