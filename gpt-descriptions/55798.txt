Synopsis:
In 'False Evidence' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and intrigue when he is called upon to solve a seemingly straightforward murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a series of unexpected twists and turns that challenge his every assumption. With the stakes rising and the truth becoming increasingly elusive, the detective must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the real culprit and bring justice to those who have been wronged.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes:
Deception, Betrayal, Justice
