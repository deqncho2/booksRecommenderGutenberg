Synopsis: In 'My Father, the Cat' by Henry Slesar, a young man discovers a mysterious connection between his father and a peculiar feline companion. As he delves deeper into this enigmatic bond, he uncovers a world of secrets and unexpected revelations that challenge his perception of reality. With each revelation, the young man finds himself questioning the true nature of his father and the extraordinary power of love and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and loyalty
