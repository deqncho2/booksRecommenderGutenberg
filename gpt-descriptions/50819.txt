Synopsis: In 'A Bad Day for Sales' by Leiber, Fritz, a down-on-his-luck salesman finds himself in a peculiar situation when he stumbles upon a mysterious shop that seems to hold the key to his success. However, as he delves deeper into the shop's secrets, he realizes that his newfound success comes at a price he never anticipated. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of success and the lengths one is willing to go to achieve it.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Consequences
