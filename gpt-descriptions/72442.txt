Synopsis: In "Gold: $b A play in four acts," Eugene O'Neill takes us on a captivating journey into the world of greed, ambition, and the pursuit of wealth. Set against the backdrop of the California Gold Rush, the story follows a diverse group of characters as they navigate the treacherous path to strike it rich. As their desires clash and their moral compasses are tested, alliances are formed and broken, leading to unexpected consequences. With intense drama, complex relationships, and a touch of dark humor, O'Neill masterfully explores the human condition in the face of materialistic obsession.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Greed, Ambition, Morality, Relationships, Human Nature
