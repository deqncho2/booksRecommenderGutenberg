Synopsis: 
'Lassen Trails: A Brief Guide to the Trails of Lassen Volcanic National Park' takes readers on an immersive journey through the breathtaking trails of Lassen Volcanic National Park. Written by experienced hiker and nature enthusiast, Stephen Halsey Matteson, this guide provides a comprehensive overview of the park's trails, offering detailed descriptions, stunning photographs, and helpful tips for hikers of all levels. From the bubbling mud pots of Bumpass Hell to the majestic peaks of Lassen Peak, this guide is a must-have for anyone seeking adventure and natural beauty in one of California's most awe-inspiring national parks.

Genres: Travel, Outdoor Recreation, Nature Guide

Themes: Exploration, Natural Beauty, Outdoor Adventure
