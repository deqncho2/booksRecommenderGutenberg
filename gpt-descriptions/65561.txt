Synopsis:
'Jungle Tales' by B. M. Croker takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of India. In this collection of short stories, we encounter a diverse cast of characters, from cunning tigers and mischievous monkeys to brave hunters and curious explorers. Each tale is filled with suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As we delve deeper into the heart of the jungle, we witness the clash between man and nature, the struggle for survival, and the enduring power of the wild. 

Genres:
Adventure, Short Stories, Wildlife Fiction

Themes:
Man vs. Nature, Survival, Exploration
