Synopsis: In "How "A Dear Little Couple" Went Abroad," follow the delightful adventures of a charming couple as they embark on a journey to explore foreign lands. From the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland, the couple encounters unexpected encounters, cultural differences, and heartwarming moments that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With their endearing personalities and a touch of humor, this couple's escapades will transport readers to a world filled with excitement, discovery, and the joy of travel.

Genres: Fiction, Travel, Humor

Themes: Adventure, Cultural exploration, Love and companionship
