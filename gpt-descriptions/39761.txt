Synopsis: In 'The Knack of Managing' by Urquhart, Lewis K./ Watson, Herbert, a young and ambitious professional finds himself thrust into a high-pressure management role, where he must navigate the complexities of leading a team and achieving success. As he grapples with the challenges of decision-making, communication, and maintaining morale, he discovers that there is more to effective management than meets the eye. With the help of unexpected mentors and a newfound understanding of human psychology, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and learns the invaluable knack of managing.

Genres: Business, Self-help, Fiction

Themes: Leadership, Communication, Decision-making, Personal growth
