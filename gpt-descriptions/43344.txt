Synopsis: In 'Don Hale with the Flying Squadron' by W. Crispin Sheppard, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside the daring protagonist, Don Hale. As a member of the elite Flying Squadron, Don finds himself embroiled in a dangerous mission that takes him across the globe. With high-stakes aerial battles, secret codes, and unexpected alliances, this action-packed story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Friendship, Perseverance
