Synopsis: 
In "Practical Forging and Art Smithing," Thomas F. Googerty takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of blacksmithing, revealing the secrets and techniques behind this ancient craft. From the basics of forging to the creation of intricate and ornate designs, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both beginners and experienced smiths alike. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Googerty immerses readers in the artistry and practicality of blacksmithing, offering invaluable insights and inspiration for those seeking to master this timeless skill.

Genres: 
Crafts & Hobbies, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Creativity, Skill Development, Tradition
