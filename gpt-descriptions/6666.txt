Synopsis: In 'Hello, Boys!' by Ella Wheeler Wilcox, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught between two charming suitors who both vie for her affection. As she navigates the complexities of love and relationships, Alice must make a difficult decision that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of choice and the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Decision-making, Consequences
