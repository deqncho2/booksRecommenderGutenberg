Synopsis: 
'The Bruce' by John Barbour is an epic poem that follows the life and struggles of Robert the Bruce, a Scottish nobleman who becomes a key figure in the Wars of Scottish Independence. Filled with battles, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices, this gripping narrative takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous times of medieval Scotland. As Robert the Bruce fights for his country's freedom, he must also confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of Scotland.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Sacrifice, Redemption
