Synopsis: In 'The World for Sale, Complete' by Gilbert Parker, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Canadian town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and tales of adventure. As his past begins to unravel, the townspeople find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. With each revelation, the stranger's true identity becomes more elusive, leaving everyone questioning their own desires and the price they are willing to pay for them.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Desire, Morality
