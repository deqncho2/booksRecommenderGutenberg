Synopsis: 

In 'German Atrocities: An Official Investigation', author J. H. Morgan delves into a shocking and deeply disturbing account of the atrocities committed by the German forces during World War II. Drawing from official documents and eyewitness testimonies, Morgan uncovers a dark and harrowing side of the war that has been largely hidden from public knowledge. From the chilling accounts of mass executions to the horrifying tales of torture and cruelty, this investigation exposes the true extent of the German war crimes. As the evidence unfolds, readers are left questioning the depths of human depravity and the consequences of unchecked power.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, War Crime Investigation, Military History

Themes: War crimes, Human rights, Morality, Power and corruption
