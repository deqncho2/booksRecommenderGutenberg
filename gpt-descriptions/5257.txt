Synopsis: 
In 'The Broad Highway' by Farnol, Jeffery, a young man named Peter embarks on a journey that will forever change his life. Faced with unexpected challenges and encounters, Peter finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and danger. As he navigates the treacherous path of the broad highway, he must confront his own desires and make choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Self-discovery
