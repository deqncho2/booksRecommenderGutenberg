Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Charles Dickens as Matz, B. W. takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and mysterious inns and taverns that populate the pages of Dickens' timeless novels. From the bustling streets of London to the quaint countryside, this book delves into the rich history and colorful characters that inhabit these iconic establishments. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, 'Dickensian Inns & Taverns' offers a unique perspective on the social fabric of Victorian England, immersing readers in the atmosphere and allure of these beloved literary settings.

Genres: 
Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction, Travel

Themes: 
Social class, Victorian society, Atmosphere and setting
