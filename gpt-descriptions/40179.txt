Synopsis: 
In 'The Carter Girls' Week-End Camp' by Speed, Nell, the four adventurous Carter sisters embark on a thrilling camping trip filled with mystery and unexpected encounters. As they navigate through the wilderness, they stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a quest to uncover a long-lost secret. With their determination and resourcefulness, the Carter girls must solve riddles, overcome challenges, and outsmart their rivals in order to unravel the mystery and claim the treasure. Will they succeed in their quest or will the secrets of the past remain buried forever?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Sisterhood, Adventure, Discovery, Courage
