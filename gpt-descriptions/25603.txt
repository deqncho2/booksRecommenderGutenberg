Synopsis: 

In "Detailed Minutiae of Soldier life in the Army of Northern Virginia, 1861-1865," McCarthy, Carlton takes readers on an immersive journey through the daily lives of soldiers serving in the renowned Army of Northern Virginia during the American Civil War. Through vivid and meticulous descriptions, the author delves into the minutiae of soldier life, offering a unique perspective on the challenges, camaraderie, and sacrifices experienced by these brave men. From the harsh realities of camp life to the adrenaline-fueled chaos of battle, this book provides a captivating glimpse into the world of Civil War soldiers, shedding light on their triumphs, struggles, and unwavering dedication.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Brotherhood and camaraderie, Daily life during wartime, Sacrifice and resilience
