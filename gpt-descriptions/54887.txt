Synopsis: 
In 'Beaufort Chums' by Edwin L. Sabin, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey that takes them deep into the heart of the untamed wilderness. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected dangers, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, courage, and unwavering friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each passing challenge, they discover the true meaning of loyalty and the power of camaraderie, forging an unbreakable bond that will shape their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
