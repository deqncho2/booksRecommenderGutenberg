Synopsis: In 'From One Generation to Another' by Henry Seton Merriman, a young man named Jack discovers a hidden family secret that takes him on a thrilling journey across Europe. As he delves deeper into his family's past, he uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and love that spans generations. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will shape his own destiny. This gripping tale of mystery and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Love, Identity
