Synopsis: In "My dear Cornelia" by Stuart Pratt Sherman, a young woman named Cornelia finds herself caught between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations. Set in the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the choices Cornelia must make and the consequences that follow, ultimately leading her on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Social Expectations, Self-Identity
