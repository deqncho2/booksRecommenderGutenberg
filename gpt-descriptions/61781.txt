Synopsis: In 'Star Pirate' by Frederic Arnold Kummer, a daring space pirate named Captain Roderick Steele embarks on a thrilling adventure across the galaxy. With his loyal crew, he navigates treacherous asteroid fields, engages in epic space battles, and outwits cunning adversaries. As he seeks to uncover hidden treasures and evade capture, Captain Steele's audacious exploits will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what daring escapade he will undertake next.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Rebellion, Morality
