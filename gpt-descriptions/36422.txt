Synopsis:
In 'Lodges in the Wilderness' by W. C. Scully, a group of adventurous travelers embarks on a journey to the untamed wilderness, seeking refuge in remote lodges that hold secrets and mysteries waiting to be unraveled. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter enigmatic characters, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With each lodge they visit, the stakes rise higher, and the travelers must rely on their wits and courage to survive the wilderness and uncover the truth that lies hidden within. 

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Exploration, Survival, Secrets
