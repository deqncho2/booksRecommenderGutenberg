Synopsis: In 'The War with Mexico, Volume 1 (of 2)', Smith, Justin Harvey takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous conflict between the United States and Mexico. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author delves into the political intrigues, military strategies, and personal stories that shaped this pivotal moment in history. As the narrative unfolds, readers will witness the bravery and sacrifice of soldiers on both sides, the complex dynamics of international relations, and the profound impact of war on individuals and nations. This gripping volume sets the stage for an epic tale of power, ambition, and the human spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Political history

Themes: War and its consequences, National identity and pride, Diplomacy and international relations
