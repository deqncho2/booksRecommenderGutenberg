Synopsis: In 'Bataille de dames', two strong-willed women find themselves entangled in a fierce battle of wits and power. As they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, their rivalry escalates to new heights, leading to unexpected consequences. With secrets, manipulation, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to protect their desires and assert their dominance.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Power struggles, Love and desire, Gender roles
