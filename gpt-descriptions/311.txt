Synopsis: 
In the heart of New York City, two unmarried sisters, Ann Eliza and Evelina Bunner, live a quiet and monotonous life running a small shop. Their days are filled with routine and their dreams are confined within the walls of their shop. However, when a mysterious and charming young man enters their lives, the sisters find themselves caught in a web of desire, longing, and forbidden love. As their world begins to unravel, the sisters must confront their deepest desires and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, societal constraints, sacrifice, the pursuit of happiness
