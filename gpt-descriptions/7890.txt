Synopsis: 
'Blind Love' by Collins, Wilkie is a gripping tale that follows the lives of two individuals, each harboring a secret that threatens to unravel their lives. As their paths intertwine, they find themselves caught in a web of deception, passion, and unexpected twists. With danger lurking around every corner, they must navigate through a world where love can be both a blessing and a curse.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Love, Secrets
