Synopsis: In 'The Airedale' by Haynes Williams, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Emily discovers an abandoned dog on her doorstep. Little does she know that this Airedale Terrier holds the key to a hidden world filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships. As Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the dog's origins, she finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue that will test her courage and resilience. With each clue she uncovers, Emily's life becomes intertwined with a forgotten history that has the power to change everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Discovery
