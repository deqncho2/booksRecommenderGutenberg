Synopsis: 
In "How to know the wild flowers: a guide to the names, haunts, and habits of our common wild flowers," Frances Theodora Parsons takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of wild flowers. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Parsons introduces us to the diverse array of wild flowers found in our surroundings, revealing their names, haunts, and intriguing habits. From the delicate petals of the woodland blooms to the vibrant colors of the meadow flowers, this guide provides a fascinating exploration of the natural beauty that surrounds us. Whether you are a seasoned botanist or simply a nature enthusiast, this book will ignite your curiosity and deepen your appreciation for the wonders of the wild.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Reference

Themes: Botany, Nature appreciation, Environmental awareness
