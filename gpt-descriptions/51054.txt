Synopsis: In 'The Wolf-Leader' by Alexandre Dumas, a young man named Thibault finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous world when he encounters a pack of wolves led by a captivating and enigmatic figure. As Thibault delves deeper into this hidden realm, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face supernatural forces, and confront his own inner demons. With secrets lurking at every turn, Thibault's journey becomes a thrilling and suspenseful exploration of the human psyche and the blurred boundaries between man and beast.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Power dynamics, Good vs. Evil
