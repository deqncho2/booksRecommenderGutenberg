Synopsis: In 'A Lucky Deal; or The 'Cutest Boy in Wall Street' by Self-made man, follow the captivating journey of a young and ambitious protagonist as he navigates the high-stakes world of Wall Street. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the protagonist's relentless pursuit of success, the allure of wealth, and the sacrifices one must make to achieve their dreams. As the story unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of power, greed, and the ultimate price of ambition.

Genres: Drama, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Wealth, Sacrifice
