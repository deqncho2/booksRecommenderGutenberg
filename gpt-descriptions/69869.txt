Synopsis: In "Harriet Beecher Stowe: a biography for girls" by Martha Foote Crow, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most influential writers. Discover the remarkable story of Harriet Beecher Stowe, a woman who defied societal norms and used her words to challenge the injustices of her time. From her humble beginnings to her groundbreaking novel that changed the course of history, this biography offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a courageous and trailblazing author.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Women's Literature

Themes: Social justice, Feminism, Literary activism
