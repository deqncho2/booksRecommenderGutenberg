Synopsis: 

'Facts for the Kind-Hearted of England!: As to the Wretchedness of the Irish Peasantry, and the Means for their Regeneration' by Jasper W. Rogers is a compelling and eye-opening account that delves into the dire conditions faced by the Irish peasantry. Through a series of vivid anecdotes and meticulous research, Rogers exposes the harsh realities of poverty, famine, and social injustice that plague the Irish population. With a passionate plea for change, the author proposes innovative solutions and explores the potential for the regeneration of the Irish people. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty, Social Injustice, Famine, Regeneration, Advocacy
