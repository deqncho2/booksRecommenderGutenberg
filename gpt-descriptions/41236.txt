Synopsis: In 'The Little Washington's Relatives' by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, young Washington embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden family secret. As he delves into his ancestry, he uncovers a world filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected connections. With the help of newfound friends, Washington must navigate through a web of secrets and challenges to uncover the truth about his relatives. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigmatic past of the Little Washingtons.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Ancestry, Identity
