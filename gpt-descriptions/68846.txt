Synopsis: 

'Brook and river trouting: A manual of modern north country methods, with coloured illustrations of flies and fly-dressing materials' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the art of trout fishing in the picturesque landscapes of the North Country. Written by Edmonds and Lee, this manual offers a wealth of knowledge on the latest techniques, equipment, and fly-dressing materials used in the pursuit of these elusive fish. With vividly colored illustrations of flies and detailed instructions on their construction, this book is a must-have for both novice and experienced anglers seeking to enhance their skills and increase their chances of success in the tranquil waters of brooks and rivers.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Outdoor Recreation

Themes: Trout fishing, North Country, Fly-dressing, Angling techniques
