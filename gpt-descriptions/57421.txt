Synopsis: 

In 'Rockets, Missiles, and Spacecraft of the National Air and Space Museum, Smithsonian Institution', author Lynne C. Murphy takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the fascinating world of space exploration. From the early days of rocketry to the cutting-edge technology of modern spacecraft, this book showcases the incredible machines that have propelled humanity beyond the confines of Earth. With stunning photographs and detailed descriptions, Murphy provides a captivating look at the history, design, and significance of these incredible vehicles. Whether you're a space enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of the cosmos, this book will ignite your imagination and leave you in awe of the incredible achievements of human ingenuity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Space exploration, Technological advancements, Human achievement
