Synopsis: In 'It Pays to Smile' by Nina Wilcox Putnam, a young woman named Betty finds herself in a precarious situation when she is unexpectedly thrust into the glamorous world of Hollywood. As she navigates the glitz and glamour, Betty discovers that behind the smiles and laughter lies a web of secrets and deceit. With her wit and determination, she must unravel the truth and find her own path to success, all while maintaining her infectious smile.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Deception
