Synopsis: In 'Fourth Down!' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a talented young football player finds himself facing unexpected challenges as he navigates the pressures of high school, friendship, and the pursuit of his dreams. As he strives to prove himself on and off the field, he discovers that sometimes the greatest victories come from unexpected sources. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and achieve his goals?

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Young adult

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
