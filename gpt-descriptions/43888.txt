Synopsis: 
In 'The Blue-Grass Region of Kentucky, and Other Kentucky Articles', James Lane Allen takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of the Blue-Grass region of Kentucky. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Allen paints a vivid picture of the region's natural beauty, its vibrant culture, and the unique character of its people. From the rolling hills and lush pastures to the charming small towns and bustling cities, this collection of articles offers a fascinating exploration of a place that is both timeless and ever-evolving. 

Genres: 
Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Nature and Landscape, Cultural Identity, History and Heritage
