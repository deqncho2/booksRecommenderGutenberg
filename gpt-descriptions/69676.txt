Synopsis: 
In 'The Italian Alp-bee; or, The gold mine of husbandry', Hermann, H. C. unveils a fascinating and practical guide to breeding genuine prolific Italian queens. With his short and concise instructions, he reveals the secrets to multiplying these queens by the hundreds in just a few months. Furthermore, he provides valuable insights on how to transform German hives into Italian ones. This captivating work is sure to captivate beekeeping enthusiasts and those seeking to enhance their knowledge of bee breeding and hive transformation.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Beekeeping, Instructional

Themes: 
Bee breeding, Hive transformation, Practical knowledge
