Synopsis: 
'Wild Oats; or, The Strolling Gentlemen' by John O'Keeffe is a delightful comedy that follows the misadventures of a charming and witty young man named Harry Thunder. After a series of comical misunderstandings and mistaken identities, Harry finds himself caught up in a whirlwind of hilarious situations, including love affairs, duels, and unexpected reunions. As he navigates through the chaos, Harry's quick thinking and clever wit are put to the test, leading to uproarious laughter and unexpected surprises.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Love and romance, Identity and mistaken identity, Wit and humor
