Synopsis: In "First impressions of the New World on two travellers from the Old, in the autumn of 1858" by Trotter, Isabella Strange, two adventurous travellers embark on a journey to the New World in the autumn of 1858. As they set foot on unfamiliar shores, they are immediately captivated by the sights, sounds, and people they encounter. From bustling cities to vast landscapes, they navigate through a world filled with both wonder and challenges. Through their eyes, readers are transported to a time of exploration and discovery, where the clash of cultures and the pursuit of dreams intertwine.

Genres: Historical fiction, travelogue, adventure

Themes: Exploration, cultural clash, dreams and aspirations
