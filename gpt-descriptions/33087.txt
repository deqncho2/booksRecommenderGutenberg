Synopsis: 
In 'Discoveries: A Volume of Essays', W. B. Yeats takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal reflections and profound insights. From exploring the mysteries of ancient civilizations to delving into the realms of art, literature, and spirituality, Yeats offers a collection of thought-provoking essays that will leave readers pondering the nature of existence and the power of human imagination. With his eloquent prose and poetic sensibility, Yeats invites readers to embark on a voyage of discovery, where the boundaries between reality and myth blur, and the depths of the human soul are revealed.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Mythology, Spirituality, Art, Literature, Human Imagination
