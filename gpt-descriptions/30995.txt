Synopsis: 

'Indian Methodist Hymn-book: Hymns Used on the Fraser River Indian Mission, of the Methodist Church, B. C. Conference, to Which Are Appended Hymns in Chinook, and the Lord's Prayer and Ten Commandments' is a captivating collection of hymns and prayers that offers a unique glimpse into the spiritual world of the Fraser River Indian Mission. This hymn-book not only showcases the rich cultural heritage of the indigenous people but also provides a fascinating exploration of the intersection between Christianity and native traditions. With the inclusion of hymns in Chinook, a trade language used by various indigenous groups, as well as the Lord's Prayer and Ten Commandments, this book serves as a bridge between different cultures and belief systems. 

Genres: Religious, Cultural, Historical

Themes: Indigenous spirituality, Cultural exchange, Religious syncretism
