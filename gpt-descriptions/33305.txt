Synopsis: In 'Lost Man's Lane: A Second Episode in the Life of Amelia Butterworth', the brilliant and eccentric detective Amelia Butterworth finds herself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a young woman mysteriously disappears from a secluded country lane. As Amelia delves deeper into the case, she uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities, leading her to question the motives of everyone involved. With her sharp wit and relentless determination, Amelia must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth and bring justice to the missing woman. 

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Justice
