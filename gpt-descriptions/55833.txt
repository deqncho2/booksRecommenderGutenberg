Synopsis: 
'Letters That Have Helped Me' is a collection of insightful and thought-provoking letters exchanged between Judge William Quan and Julia Wharton Lewis Campbell Ver Planck. Through their correspondence, they explore various aspects of life, including love, friendship, spirituality, and personal growth. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a captivating journey of self-discovery and enlightenment, as the authors share their wisdom and experiences. This collection offers a unique perspective on the human condition and provides valuable guidance for navigating the complexities of life.

Genres: Self-help, Epistolary, Philosophy

Themes: Personal growth, Spirituality, Relationships
