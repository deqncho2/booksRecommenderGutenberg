Synopsis: In 'The Case of the Pocket Diary Found in the Snow' by Auguste Groner, a mysterious pocket diary is discovered in the snow, leading to a captivating investigation that unravels a web of secrets and deceit. As Detective Joseph Müller delves deeper into the diary's contents, he becomes entangled in a complex puzzle that could have far-reaching consequences. With each page turned, the stakes rise, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. Will Müller be able to decipher the diary's cryptic messages and solve the case before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Investigation
