Synopsis: In 'Epidemics Examined and Explained: or, Living Germs Proved by Analogy to be a Source of Disease', Grove, John takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of epidemics and disease. Through a series of thought-provoking analogies, Grove presents a compelling argument that challenges conventional wisdom and sheds light on the true source of infectious diseases. As he delves into the microscopic world of living germs, the author unveils a startling revelation that will forever change our understanding of epidemics. Prepare to be captivated by Grove's groundbreaking exploration of the unseen forces that shape our health and well-being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Medical

Themes: Disease, Microbiology, Public Health
