Synopsis: In 'Betty's Virginia Christmas' by Molly Elliot Seawell, readers are transported to a charming small town in Virginia during the holiday season. Betty, a spirited young girl, finds herself caught up in a series of unexpected events that lead her on a heartwarming adventure. As she navigates through the joys and challenges of the Christmas season, Betty discovers the true meaning of love, friendship, and the importance of family bonds. With delightful surprises and a touch of magic, this enchanting tale captures the essence of the holiday spirit and leaves readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Holiday, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Friendship, Love, Adventure, Christmas Spirit
