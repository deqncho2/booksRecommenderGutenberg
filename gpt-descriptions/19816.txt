Synopsis: 
In 'Six little Bunkers at Cowboy Jack's', the adventurous Bunker siblings find themselves embarking on a thrilling summer vacation at Cowboy Jack's ranch. From horseback riding to exploring hidden caves, the Bunkers encounter unexpected challenges and make new friends along the way. But when a mysterious treasure map comes into their possession, the Bunkers must work together to solve the clues and uncover the secrets of the Wild West. Will they be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Family bonds, Friendship, Exploration
