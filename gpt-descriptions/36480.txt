Synopsis: 
In 'The Sweep Winner' by Gould, Nat, a seemingly ordinary teenager named Max discovers a hidden talent that catapults him into the world of competitive sweeping. As he navigates the cutthroat world of professional sweeping, Max must confront unexpected challenges, rivalries, and personal sacrifices. With the stakes higher than ever, Max's journey becomes a thrilling rollercoaster ride that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: 
Young Adult, Sports, Drama

Themes: 
Ambition, Competition, Self-discovery
