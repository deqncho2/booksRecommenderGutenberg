Synopsis: In 'Regiment of Women' by Dane, Clemence, a young woman named Ursula finds herself in a mysterious and captivating world where women hold all the power. As she navigates through this society, Ursula uncovers hidden secrets and challenges the norms that have been imposed upon her. With unexpected twists and turns, this thought-provoking novel explores the complexities of gender dynamics and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Feminist literature, speculative fiction, dystopian fiction

Themes: Gender roles, power dynamics, societal norms, personal freedom
