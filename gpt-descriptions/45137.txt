Synopsis: 
Step into the ancient world of Babylonia and Assyria, where gods and goddesses, heroes and monsters, and epic battles between good and evil unfold. In "Myths & Legends of Babylonia & Assyria," Lewis Spence takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich mythology and folklore of these ancient civilizations. From the creation of the world to the heroic exploits of legendary figures like Gilgamesh and Ishtar, this book delves into the enchanting tales that have shaped the cultural fabric of Mesopotamia. Prepare to be enthralled by the epic adventures, divine interventions, and profound wisdom that lie within these ancient myths and legends.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, History

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Divine Intervention, Wisdom
