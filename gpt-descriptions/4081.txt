Synopsis: In the bustling streets of Jacobean London, a trio of con artists sets up shop, posing as alchemists with the promise of turning base metals into gold. As their fraudulent operation gains momentum, they attract a colorful cast of characters, each with their own desires and secrets. With wit, deception, and a touch of the supernatural, this comedic masterpiece by Ben Jonson takes readers on a wild ride through the world of alchemy, greed, and the pursuit of transformation.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Deception, Greed, Transformation, Illusion, Desire
