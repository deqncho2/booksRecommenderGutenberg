Synopsis: 
In 'A College Girl' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, readers are introduced to the spirited and independent character of Betty, a young woman who defies societal expectations by pursuing higher education. As she navigates the challenges of college life, Betty encounters a diverse cast of characters, including fellow students, professors, and potential suitors. Through her experiences, she grapples with issues of identity, ambition, and the pursuit of love. Will Betty find her place in a world that is not yet ready to accept her unconventional choices?

Genres: 
Romance, Coming-of-age, Women's Fiction

Themes: 
Gender roles, Education, Self-discovery
