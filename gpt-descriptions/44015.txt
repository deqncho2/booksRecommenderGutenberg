Synopsis: In 'The Fatal Dowry', a young nobleman named Charalois finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his love for a woman from a lower social class. As he navigates the complexities of love, honor, and societal expectations, Charalois becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and tragic consequences. This gripping play explores the consequences of societal conventions and the power of love in the face of adversity.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, Social class, Betrayal, Consequences of actions
