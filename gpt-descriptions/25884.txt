Synopsis: 
'Found in the Philippines: The Story of a Woman's Letters' by King, Charles is a captivating tale that follows the discovery of a collection of letters hidden away for decades. As the protagonist delves into the mysterious correspondence, they uncover a web of secrets, love, and betrayal that spans generations. With each letter, the reader is transported to a different era, unraveling the truth behind the woman's life and the impact she had on those around her. This gripping narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the hidden truths that lie within the pages of these long-lost letters.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Love and Relationships, Family Legacy
