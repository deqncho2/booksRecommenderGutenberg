Synopsis: 
'Out of This World Convention' by Forrest J. Ackerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through a fantastical convention where science fiction and reality collide. As attendees gather to celebrate their shared love for the genre, they soon discover that the line between fiction and truth is blurred, leading to unexpected and mind-bending experiences. With a cast of eccentric characters and a series of mysterious events, this novel promises to transport readers to a world where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Fiction, Imagination, Community
