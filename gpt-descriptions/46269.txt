Synopsis: 
In 'Mark Tidd in Business' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a group of adventurous boys led by the charismatic Mark Tidd find themselves embarking on a thrilling business venture that takes them on a wild journey through their small town. With their unique skills and unwavering determination, they face unexpected challenges and discover the true meaning of friendship and teamwork. As they navigate the world of business, the boys learn valuable lessons about trust, perseverance, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Entrepreneurship, Problem-solving
