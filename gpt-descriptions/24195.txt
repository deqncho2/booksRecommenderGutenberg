Synopsis: In "History of the 159th Regiment, N.Y.S.V." by Edward Duffy, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the experiences of a regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, the author delves into the trials and triumphs of the soldiers, shedding light on the sacrifices made and the bonds formed in the face of adversity. From the chaos of battlefields to the camaraderie in camp, this historical account offers a compelling glimpse into the lives of those who fought for their beliefs.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Resilience, Sacrifice
