Synopsis: 

In 'Lover and Husband: A Novel' by Molesworth, Mrs., we are introduced to the intriguing world of Lady Adela Charteris, a young woman who finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set in the late 19th century, this captivating novel follows Lady Adela's journey as she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations. As she grapples with her own desires and the expectations placed upon her, Lady Adela must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. With its richly drawn characters and compelling narrative, 'Lover and Husband' is a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the ultimate fate of Lady Adela.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Societal Expectations
