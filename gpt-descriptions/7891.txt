Synopsis: 
In 'The Dead Alive' by Wilkie Collins, a young lawyer named Frank Lockwood finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous case. When a man is wrongfully accused of murder, Lockwood is determined to uncover the truth and prove his innocence. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, secrets, and unexpected alliances. With his own life at stake, Lockwood must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the shocking truth behind the murder and bring the real culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Justice and injustice, Deception and betrayal
