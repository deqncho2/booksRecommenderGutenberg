Synopsis:
In 'Marriage' by H. G. Wells, a young couple embarks on a journey of love, passion, and the complexities of married life. As they navigate the challenges and expectations of their union, they are confronted with unexpected twists and turns that test the strength of their relationship. With Wells' signature wit and insight, this novel explores the intricacies of marriage and the profound impact it has on individuals and society.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes:
Love, Commitment, Gender Roles, Societal Expectations
