Synopsis: In 'The Tale of the The Muley Cow: Slumber-Town Tales', Arthur Scott Bailey takes readers on a delightful journey to Slumber-Town, where animals have their own unique adventures. This particular tale follows the misadventures of a muley cow named Mooly, who finds herself in a rather peculiar situation. As Mooly tries to navigate her way through unexpected challenges, she discovers the true meaning of friendship and the importance of embracing one's individuality. With Bailey's signature charm and wit, this heartwarming story is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Individuality, Overcoming Challenges
