Synopsis: In "Three Little Kittens Who Lost Their Mittens," author Laura Rountree Smith takes readers on a delightful journey with three mischievous kittens who find themselves in a bit of a predicament. As they search for their missing mittens, they encounter various obstacles and learn valuable lessons along the way. This heartwarming tale is filled with adventure, humor, and a touch of magic that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book, Fantasy

Themes: Responsibility, Consequences, Problem-Solving
