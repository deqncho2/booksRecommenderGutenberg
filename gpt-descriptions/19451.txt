Synopsis: In 'Double Trouble; Or, Every Hero His Own Villain' by Herbert Quick, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of deception and intrigue when he discovers a shocking secret about his own identity. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between hero and villain becomes blurred. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Morality
