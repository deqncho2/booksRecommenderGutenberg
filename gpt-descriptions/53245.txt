Synopsis: 
'Little Dramas for Primary Grades' is a delightful collection of short plays written specifically for young children. Each play presents a unique and engaging story that captures the imagination and encourages participation. From magical adventures to everyday situations, these little dramas are filled with humor, excitement, and valuable life lessons. Whether it's a mischievous talking animal or a group of friends solving a problem together, these plays are sure to captivate young readers and inspire their creativity.

Genres: Children's Literature, Drama, Education

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Problem-solving, Morals and Values
