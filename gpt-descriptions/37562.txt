Synopsis: 
'Wrestling and Wrestlers: Biographical Sketches of Celebrated Athletes of the Northern Ring; to Which is Added Notes on Bull and Badger Baiting' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of wrestling, introducing them to the lives and accomplishments of renowned athletes of the Northern Ring. Alongside these fascinating biographical sketches, the book also delves into the controversial and often brutal practices of bull and badger baiting. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, the authors shed light on the captivating world of wrestling and the darker side of animal baiting.

Genres: Sports, Biography, History

Themes: Athleticism, Entertainment, Animal Cruelty, Controversy
