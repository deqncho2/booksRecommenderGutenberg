Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of Ancient Egyptian, Assyrian, and Persian costumes and decorations. In this fascinating book, authors Florence S. Hornblower and Mary G. Houston take readers on a visual journey through the rich and intricate designs that adorned the people of these ancient civilizations. From the opulent headdresses of Egyptian pharaohs to the intricately patterned textiles of Persian nobility, this book showcases the stunning artistry and cultural significance of these ancient cultures. Delve into the symbolism behind the vibrant colors, the intricate patterns, and the luxurious materials used in these costumes and decorations, and gain a deeper understanding of the beliefs and values that shaped these civilizations. 

Genres: Art, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Costume and fashion, Cultural symbolism
