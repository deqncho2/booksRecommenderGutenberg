Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion of 'The Curse of Kehama', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the mystical and treacherous world of ancient India. As the protagonist battles against supernatural forces and faces the consequences of his past actions, a web of secrets and betrayals unravels, leading to a climactic showdown that will determine the fate of both mortals and gods. With its richly detailed setting and intricate plot twists, this second volume of 'The Curse of Kehama' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Epic poetry, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Redemption, Mythology
