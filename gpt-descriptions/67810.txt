Synopsis: In 'Friends on the Shelf' by Torrey Bradford, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world within the shelves of her local library. As she befriends a group of magical book characters, they embark on a thrilling adventure to save their beloved library from an impending threat. With danger lurking around every corner, Lily and her newfound friends must rely on their courage, wit, and the power of storytelling to overcome the challenges they face. Will they be able to protect the library and preserve the magic within its walls?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, the Power of Books
