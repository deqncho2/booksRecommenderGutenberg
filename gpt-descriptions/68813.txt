Synopsis: In 'Josiah in New York; or, A coupon from the Fresh Air Fund' by James Otis, young Josiah embarks on an exciting adventure when he receives a coupon from the Fresh Air Fund. Leaving his small town behind, he travels to the bustling city of New York, where he encounters new friends, navigates unfamiliar neighborhoods, and discovers the wonders of the city. As Josiah explores the vibrant streets, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, resilience, and the power of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration, Cultural Exchange
