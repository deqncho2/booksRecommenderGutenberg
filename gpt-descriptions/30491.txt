Synopsis: In 'Vital Ingredient' by De Vet, Charles V., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious substance that has the power to change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into its properties, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between good and evil blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate through a treacherous landscape of power-hungry individuals and moral dilemmas. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of progress and the lengths one would go to obtain the vital ingredient.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Human Nature
