Synopsis: In 'Obil, Keeper of Camels: Being the parable of the man whom the disciples saw casting out devils', Lucia Chase Bell takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world, following the enigmatic character of Obil. As the disciples witness Obil's extraordinary ability to cast out demons, they become intrigued by his mysterious past and the source of his power. Through vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, Bell explores the complexities of faith, the power of belief, and the transformative nature of compassion. This thought-provoking tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Obil's remarkable abilities.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Power of Belief, Compassion
