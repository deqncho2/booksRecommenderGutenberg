Synopsis: 

In 'Apple Growing' by M. C. Burritt, a small-town farmer named John embarks on a journey to save his struggling apple orchard. As he delves into the world of apple growing, he encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the secrets to cultivating the perfect apple. Along the way, he forms unlikely alliances and learns valuable lessons about perseverance and the power of nature. 

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Nature, Agriculture
