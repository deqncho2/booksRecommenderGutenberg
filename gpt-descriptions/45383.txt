Synopsis: 
In 'Drifting' by Thomas Buchanan Read, a young artist named Harold embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the American West. As he navigates through various encounters and experiences, Harold finds himself questioning his purpose and identity, ultimately leading him to a profound realization about the true meaning of life.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Purpose in life
