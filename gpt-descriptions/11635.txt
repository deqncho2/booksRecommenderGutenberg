Synopsis: In "Green Tea; Mr. Justice Harbottle" by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a respected judge finds himself tormented by a mysterious presence that only he can see. As he delves deeper into the supernatural, his sanity and reputation are put at stake, leading to a chilling climax that will leave readers questioning the boundaries between reality and the unseen.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, supernatural

Themes: Madness, the supernatural, psychological torment
