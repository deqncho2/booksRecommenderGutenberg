Synopsis: In 'His Own People' by Booth Tarkington, a young man named Royall is torn between his loyalty to his family and his desire for personal freedom. As he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Royall must confront the consequences of his choices and ultimately discover where he truly belongs.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes: Identity, Duty, Individualism, Social expectations
