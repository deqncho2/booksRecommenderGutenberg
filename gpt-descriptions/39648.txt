Synopsis: 
'Character and Conduct: A Book of Helpful Thoughts by Great Writers of Past and Present Ages' is a captivating collection of wisdom and insights from renowned writers throughout history. This thought-provoking book offers a diverse range of perspectives on character, conduct, and the art of living a meaningful life. From ancient philosophers to modern novelists, each writer's unique voice and profound observations provide valuable guidance and inspiration for readers seeking to navigate the complexities of human nature and make ethical choices. Whether you are looking for practical advice or simply enjoy exploring the depths of human experience, this book is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Literature
Themes: Character development, Ethics, Wisdom, Human nature
