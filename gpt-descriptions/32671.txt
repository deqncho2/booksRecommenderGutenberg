Synopsis: In 'The Doors of Death' by Arthur B. Waltermire, a mysterious series of deaths grips a small town, leaving the residents in a state of fear and confusion. As the body count rises, a young detective named Sarah is determined to uncover the truth behind these seemingly inexplicable deaths. With each new clue, Sarah delves deeper into a world of dark secrets and ancient legends, where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. As she races against time to solve the case, Sarah must confront her own fears and confront the possibility that the doors of death may not be as closed as they seem.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Death, Secrets, Fear, Mythology
