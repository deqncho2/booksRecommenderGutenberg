Synopsis: 

'Forgotten Books of the American Nursery: A History of the Development of the American Story-Book' takes readers on a captivating journey through the forgotten treasures of American children's literature. Delving into the rich history of the development of story-books in America, author Rosalie Vrylina Halsey uncovers a world of enchanting tales that have been lost to time. From whimsical fables to heartwarming adventures, this book unveils the forgotten gems that once captivated the imaginations of young readers across the nation. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: American literature, Childhood, Nostalgia
