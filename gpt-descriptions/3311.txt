Synopsis: In 'The Californiacs' by Gillmore, Inez Haynes, follow the captivating journey of two sisters, Mary and Alice, as they embark on a thrilling adventure to California during the Gold Rush era. Leaving behind their comfortable lives in the East, the sisters find themselves entangled in a web of danger, romance, and unexpected twists. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes and encounter a diverse cast of characters, Mary and Alice must rely on their resilience and determination to survive and find their place in this new and untamed world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival and Resilience, Love and Relationships
