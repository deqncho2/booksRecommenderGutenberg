Synopsis: In "The Last Age of the Church" by John Wycliffe, a renowned theologian and reformer, a young scholar embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about the corrupt practices within the Church. As he delves deeper into his investigation, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy, betrayal, and forbidden knowledge that threatens to shake the very foundations of his faith. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the Church and his own soul.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Religious

Themes: Corruption, Faith, Knowledge, Power
