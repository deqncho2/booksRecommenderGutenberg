Synopsis: In 'The Imitator: A Novel' by Pollard Percival, a mysterious imitator emerges, captivating the attention of the public with their uncanny ability to mimic the lives of others. As the imitator's popularity soars, questions arise about their true identity and motives. The story follows a journalist determined to uncover the imitator's secrets, leading to a thrilling journey filled with deception, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the line between reality and imitation becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of identity and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Perception, Obsession
