Synopsis: In 'The Sword of Damocles: A Story of New York Life' by Anna Katharine Green, a mysterious murder takes place in the bustling city of New York, sending shockwaves through the upper echelons of society. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power and privilege is exposed. With a web of deceit and intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Power and privilege, Deception and betrayal
