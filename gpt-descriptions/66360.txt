Synopsis: In "John's Other Practice" by Winston K. Marks, a renowned surgeon named John finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous world beyond his medical career. As he delves deeper into this hidden realm, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life and the lives of those around him. With each revelation, John must confront his own morality and make choices that could have far-reaching consequences. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Identity, Consequences
