Synopsis: In "Critical Miscellanies (Vol. 3 of 3), Essay 4: The Life of George Eliot," John Morley delves into the fascinating life of one of the most celebrated female authors of the Victorian era. From her humble beginnings to her rise as a literary genius, Morley uncovers the trials and triumphs that shaped George Eliot's extraordinary journey. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Morley paints a vivid portrait of a woman who defied societal norms and left an indelible mark on the world of literature.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, literary influence
