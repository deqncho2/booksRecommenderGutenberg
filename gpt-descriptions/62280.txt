Synopsis: 
In "Thackeray's London: a description of his haunts and the scenes of his novels," William H. Rideing takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant city that inspired one of the greatest Victorian novelists, William Makepeace Thackeray. From the bustling streets of London to the hidden corners of its literary haunts, Rideing unveils the real-life locations that served as the backdrop for Thackeray's iconic novels. As readers delve into the rich history and vivid descriptions of these settings, they are transported to a bygone era, where they can imagine themselves walking alongside the characters that Thackeray brought to life. 

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Travelogue
Themes: Victorian London, Literary Inspiration, Historical Settings
