Synopsis: In 'German Moonlight' by Wilhelm Raabe, a young man named Johannes finds himself entangled in a mysterious and haunting love affair. Drawn to a captivating woman who seems to possess an otherworldly charm, Johannes embarks on a journey that takes him through the depths of his own desires and the complexities of human nature. As he navigates through the shadows of the moonlit German landscape, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the secrets that lie hidden beneath the surface.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romanticism, Psychological thriller

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery, Supernatural elements
