Synopsis: In the wild and rugged landscape of Exmoor, a forbidden love story unfolds between John Ridd, a young farmer, and Lorna Doone, a beautiful and mysterious woman. Set in the 17th century, this captivating romance takes readers on a journey filled with adventure, danger, and the clash between two feuding families. As John becomes entangled in the violent world of the notorious Doone clan, he must navigate treacherous paths to protect his love and seek justice. Will their love conquer all, or will the forces of fate tear them apart?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden Love, Family Feuds, Justice and Revenge
