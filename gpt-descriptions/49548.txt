Synopsis: 
'Walks near Edinburgh' by Margaret Warrender takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes surrounding the enchanting city of Edinburgh. From tranquil coastal paths to breathtaking hilltop vistas, this guidebook unveils hidden gems and secret trails that will leave readers yearning to explore the great outdoors. With vivid descriptions and detailed maps, Warrender invites readers to embark on unforgettable adventures, discovering the natural beauty and rich history that lies just beyond the city's bustling streets.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Guidebook

Themes: Nature exploration, Scottish landscapes, Outdoor activities
