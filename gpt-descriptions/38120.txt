Synopsis: 
In 'Bridge Axioms and Laws', Elwell, J. B. takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of bridge playing. With a unique blend of storytelling and expert analysis, Elwell delves into the fascinating strategies, tactics, and psychological aspects of the game. As the plot unfolds, readers are introduced to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own motivations and secrets, all vying for victory in the high-stakes world of competitive bridge. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Bridge Axioms and Laws' keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate secrets of success in this mind-bending game.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Strategy, Competition, Psychology
