Synopsis:
In "The Rivals of Acadia: An Old Story of the New World," Harriet Vaughan Cheney takes readers on a captivating journey to 17th-century Acadia, a land of beauty, danger, and conflicting loyalties. Amidst the backdrop of the French and English struggle for control, two young women find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and treacherous alliances. As their lives intertwine, they must navigate the complexities of friendship, betrayal, and the enduring power of hope. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Cheney paints a vivid picture of a tumultuous era that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
