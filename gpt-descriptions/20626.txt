Synopsis: 
In 'Torchy' by Ford, Sewell, meet Torchy, a charming and mischievous young woman who finds herself in a series of unexpected adventures. From her encounters with a mysterious stranger to her involvement in a high-stakes poker game, Torchy's quick wit and resourcefulness keep readers on the edge of their seats. As she navigates through a world filled with danger and intrigue, Torchy's determination to uncover the truth leads her down a path she never could have imagined. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Courage, Deception, Redemption
