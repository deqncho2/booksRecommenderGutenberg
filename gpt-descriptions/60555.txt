Synopsis: 

In 'The Parochial History of Cornwall, Volume 1 (of 4)', the reader is transported to the enchanting landscapes of Cornwall, where ancient legends and historical events intertwine. This meticulously researched and beautifully written volume delves into the rich tapestry of Cornwall's past, uncovering forgotten tales of bravery, love, and betrayal. From the mysterious ruins of forgotten castles to the rugged cliffs that guard the coast, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the lives of the people who shaped Cornwall's history. As the pages turn, secrets are unveiled, and the reader becomes immersed in a world where folklore and reality merge, leaving them eager to uncover the hidden truths that lie within the remaining volumes.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Mystery

Themes: History, Mythology, Identity, Heritage
