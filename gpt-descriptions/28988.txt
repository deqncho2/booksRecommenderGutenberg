Synopsis: 
Jennie Gerhardt: A Novel by Theodore Dreiser is a captivating tale that follows the life of a young and innocent girl named Jennie Gerhardt. Set in the early 20th century, the story takes us on a journey through Jennie's tumultuous life as she navigates love, loss, and societal expectations. From her humble beginnings as a maid to her encounters with powerful and influential men, Jennie's story is one of resilience and determination. As she faces numerous challenges and difficult choices, the reader is drawn into her world, eagerly turning the pages to discover what fate has in store for her.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Realism

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Social Class, Morality, Gender Roles
