Synopsis: In 'A Wonder Book and Tanglewood Tales, for Girls and Boys' by Nathaniel Hawthorne, embark on a magical journey through ancient Greek mythology as the author retells classic tales in his own unique and enchanting style. From the adventures of Theseus and the Minotaur to the captivating story of Pandora's Box, Hawthorne brings these timeless myths to life, captivating readers of all ages with his vivid imagination and lyrical prose. Prepare to be transported to a world of gods, heroes, and mythical creatures, where bravery, love, and the power of the human spirit are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Children's Literature

Themes: Heroism, Morality, Fate, Curiosity, Redemption
