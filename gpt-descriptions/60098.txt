Synopsis: In 'Mr. Rabbit at Home: A sequel to Little Mr. Thimblefinger and his Queer Country' by Joel Chandler Harris, we are transported back to the enchanting world of Mr. Thimblefinger and his magical Queer Country. This time, the story revolves around Mr. Rabbit, who finds himself in a series of unexpected adventures and encounters with peculiar creatures. As he navigates through this whimsical realm, Mr. Rabbit learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the importance of embracing one's true self. With its delightful blend of fantasy and folklore, this sequel promises to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Self-discovery
