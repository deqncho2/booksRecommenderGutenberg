Synopsis: In 'Oscar in Africa' by Castlemon, Harry, a young and adventurous protagonist named Oscar embarks on a thrilling journey to the heart of Africa. Faced with the untamed wilderness, Oscar encounters dangerous wildlife, treacherous landscapes, and unexpected allies. As he navigates through the unknown, Oscar's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and fulfill his mission? This captivating tale takes readers on an exhilarating adventure filled with suspense, discovery, and the triumph of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Bravery
