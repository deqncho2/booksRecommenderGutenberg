Synopsis: In 'The Adventure of Two Dutch Dolls and a 'Golliwogg'' by Upton, Bertha, two lively Dutch dolls named Peg and Sarah embark on a thrilling journey with their mischievous friend, the Golliwogg. Together, they encounter a series of unexpected events and face numerous challenges that test their courage and friendship. As they navigate through a world filled with excitement and danger, the dolls and the Golliwogg learn valuable lessons about loyalty, bravery, and the power of unity.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Unity
