Synopsis: 
The Journal of Submarine Commander von Forstner takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the eyes of a German U-boat commander during World War I. As von Forstner navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, he encounters not only the perils of war but also the moral dilemmas that come with his role. Through his vivid and introspective journal entries, we witness the intense battles, the camaraderie among the crew, and the psychological toll of life beneath the surface. With each page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of von Forstner and his crew.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War and its consequences, Morality and ethics in wartime, The human experience in extreme conditions
