Synopsis: In the March 2nd, 1895 edition of Punch, or the London Charivari, readers are treated to a delightful collection of satirical cartoons, humorous articles, and witty commentary on the social and political happenings of the time. From poking fun at the latest fashion trends to cleverly dissecting the absurdities of Victorian society, this volume offers a humorous and insightful glimpse into the world of late 19th-century London. Whether it's the comical illustrations or the clever wordplay, this edition of Punch is sure to entertain and leave readers wanting more.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Cartoons

Themes: Social commentary, Victorian society, Fashion, Politics
