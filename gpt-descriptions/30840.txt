Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Central High on Lake Luna; Or, The Crew That Won', a group of spirited young girls from Central High School embark on a thrilling adventure at Lake Luna. As they form a crew to compete in a rowing race, they face unexpected challenges, forge new friendships, and discover the power of teamwork. With determination and perseverance, these girls prove that they are capable of achieving greatness both on and off the water.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Adventure, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Overcoming Challenges
