Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works in Verse and Prose of William Butler Yeats, Vol. 5 (of 8): The Celtic Twilight and Stories of Red Hanrahan', delve into the mystical world of Irish folklore and enchanting tales. This collection transports readers to a realm where ancient legends come alive, weaving together captivating stories of mythical creatures, supernatural encounters, and the enduring power of love and destiny. From the hauntingly beautiful landscapes of Ireland to the depths of human emotions, this volume showcases Yeats' masterful storytelling and his deep connection to his Irish heritage.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Irish folklore, Mythology, Supernatural, Love, Destiny
