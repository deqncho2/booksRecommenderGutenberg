Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Life of Robert, Lord Clive', author John Malcolm delves deeper into the fascinating and complex life of this renowned British military leader and statesman. Drawing from the intimate and revealing family papers provided by the Earl of Powis, Malcolm uncovers the untold stories and hidden truths behind Lord Clive's rise to power and his pivotal role in shaping the British Empire. From his early struggles and triumphs in India to his political battles and personal relationships, this volume offers a captivating exploration of Lord Clive's life, shedding light on the man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Ambition, Power, Colonialism, Leadership
