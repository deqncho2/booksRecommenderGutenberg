Synopsis: 
In 'Northern Nut Growers Report of the Proceedings at the Twenty-First Annual Meeting: Cedar Rapids, Iowa, September 17, 18, and 19, 1930', readers are transported to a fascinating gathering of nut enthusiasts from all walks of life. As they convene in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, the attendees share their knowledge, experiences, and discoveries about nut cultivation and the potential of various nut species. From engaging discussions on the latest research to practical demonstrations and insightful presentations, this report offers a captivating glimpse into the world of nut growing and the passionate individuals who dedicate themselves to this unique field.

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Agriculture

Themes: Nut cultivation, Horticultural research, Community and knowledge sharing
