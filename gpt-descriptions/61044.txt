Synopsis: In "The Anatomist Dissected: or the man-midwife finely brought to bed," Gulliver, a surgeon, examines the conduct of Mr. St. Andre, a man-midwife, regarding a peculiar case involving a rabbit-bearer. Through St. Andre's own narrative, Gulliver delves into the intriguing circumstances surrounding this event, unraveling a tale that challenges conventional medical practices and raises questions about the boundaries of science and ethics.

Genres: Satire, Medical Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Medical ethics, Scientific inquiry, Satirical critique of medical practices
