Synopsis: In 'My Neighbor Raymond', Paul de Kock takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of the residents of a Parisian neighborhood. The story revolves around the enigmatic character of Raymond, a charming and mysterious neighbor who becomes the center of attention for the other inhabitants. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed, all against the backdrop of the bustling city of Paris. With its intriguing characters and unpredictable twists, this novel offers a delightful exploration of human nature and the complexities of everyday life.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Secrets and mysteries, Community dynamics
