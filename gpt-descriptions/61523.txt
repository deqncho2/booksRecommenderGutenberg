Synopsis: In 'The Moth Decides: A Novel' by Edward Alden Jewell, a mysterious and enigmatic moth takes center stage, weaving its way through the lives of a diverse cast of characters. As the moth flutters from person to person, it becomes a catalyst for unexpected connections, hidden desires, and profound transformations. From a struggling artist seeking inspiration to a disillusioned politician searching for redemption, each character is drawn into the moth's orbit, ultimately facing choices that will shape their lives forever. With lyrical prose and a touch of magical realism, this captivating novel explores the intricate web of human existence and the power of choice.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Magical Realism, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Fate and Free Will, Interconnectedness, Transformation, Redemption
