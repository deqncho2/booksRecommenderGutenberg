Synopsis: In 'Proper Pride: A Novel. Volume 2 (of 3)', the captivating story continues as the lives of the intriguing characters unfold amidst a backdrop of societal expectations and personal desires. Secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected romances blossom, all while the characters navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. As the plot thickens, readers will be drawn deeper into the web of relationships and conflicts, eagerly anticipating the resolution that awaits in the final volume.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Society and Class, Love and Relationships, Ambition and Desire
