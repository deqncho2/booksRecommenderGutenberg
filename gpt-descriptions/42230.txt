Synopsis: In 'Esther's Charge: A Story for Girls' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, readers are introduced to Esther, a young girl who finds herself in a challenging situation that requires her to take on a great responsibility. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, Esther's determination and courage are put to the test. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of loyalty, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Responsibility, Friendship, Courage, Resilience
