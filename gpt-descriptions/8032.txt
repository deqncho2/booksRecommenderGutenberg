Synopsis: 

In the Book of Jonah, a reluctant prophet is called upon by God to deliver a message to the city of Nineveh, a place known for its wickedness. However, Jonah's fear and disobedience lead him to embark on a perilous journey that takes him to the depths of the sea and the belly of a great fish. As he faces his own mortality, Jonah must confront his inner demons and come to terms with the power of redemption and the boundless mercy of God.

Genres: Religious, Biblical, Allegorical

Themes: Redemption, Obedience, Mercy, Divine Intervention
