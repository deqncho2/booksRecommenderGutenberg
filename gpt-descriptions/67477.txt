Synopsis: 
In 'Bee-keeping for the Many; or, The management of the common and Ligurian honey bee: Including the selection of hives and a bee-keeper's calendar', J. H. Payne takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of bee-keeping. With a focus on the common and Ligurian honey bee, this comprehensive guide covers everything from selecting hives to maintaining a bee-keeper's calendar. Through vivid descriptions and practical advice, Payne offers a wealth of knowledge that will enthrall both aspiring and experienced bee-keepers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Nature

Themes: Bee-keeping, Honey bees, Hive management, Seasonal bee-keeping
