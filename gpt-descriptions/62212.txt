Synopsis: 
In 'Prison Planet' by Tucker Wilson, a group of unlikely allies find themselves trapped on a desolate planet, where survival becomes their only goal. As they navigate the treacherous landscape and encounter mysterious creatures, they must confront their own pasts and form unexpected bonds to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With each passing day, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, and the true nature of the prison planet is revealed, leaving them questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Betrayal, Identity
