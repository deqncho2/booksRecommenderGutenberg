Synopsis: 
In 'The Fugitive: A Play in Four Acts' by John Galsworthy, a young man named James Farrant finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies when he returns home after a long absence. As he tries to reconnect with his family and friends, he becomes entangled in a mysterious incident that threatens to unravel the truth and expose hidden motives. With suspense and intrigue building throughout the play, James must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the secrets that have haunted his loved ones for years.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Truth and deception
