Synopsis:
In 'Journal of Landsborough's expedition from Carpentaria, in search of Burke & Wills', William Landsborough takes us on a thrilling journey through the uncharted Australian outback. Faced with treacherous landscapes, hostile indigenous tribes, and the constant threat of starvation, Landsborough's determination to find the missing explorers Burke and Wills becomes a gripping tale of survival and resilience. As he battles against the harsh elements and navigates through unknown territories, the journal offers a firsthand account of the hardships and triumphs encountered during this daring expedition.

Genres:
Adventure, Exploration, Historical

Themes:
Survival, Exploration, Perseverance
