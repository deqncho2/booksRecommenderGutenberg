Synopsis: 
'Grandmother Dear: A Book for Boys and Girls' by Molesworth, Mrs. is a heartwarming tale that follows the adventures of two siblings, Ethel and Harry, as they spend a summer with their beloved grandmother. From exploring the enchanting countryside to encountering new friends and discovering hidden secrets, the children embark on a journey of self-discovery and learn valuable life lessons along the way. With their grandmother's guidance and wisdom, Ethel and Harry navigate the challenges of growing up, forming lasting bonds, and finding joy in the simple pleasures of life.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Coming of age, Nature and exploration
