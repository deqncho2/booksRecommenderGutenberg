Synopsis:
In "Liberalism," L. T. Hobhouse presents a thought-provoking exploration of the principles and evolution of liberalism, offering a fresh perspective on its significance in shaping modern society. Through a comprehensive analysis of historical events and philosophical ideas, Hobhouse delves into the core values of liberalism and its impact on individual freedom, social progress, and the pursuit of a just society. With eloquent prose and compelling arguments, the author challenges conventional notions and invites readers to reconsider their understanding of liberalism and its relevance in contemporary politics and governance.

Genres:
Political philosophy, Social science, History

Themes:
Individual freedom, Social progress, Justice, Democracy
