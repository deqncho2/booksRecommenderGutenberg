Synopsis: In 'My Lady Caprice' by Farnol, Jeffery, a dashing young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance when he encounters a captivating and enigmatic woman. As he delves deeper into her world, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of secrets and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and deception, Identity and disguise
