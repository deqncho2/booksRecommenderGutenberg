Synopsis: In 'A Historic Sketch, Lest We Forget Company "E," 26th Ohio Infantry in the War for the Union, 1861-65' by Kelly Walden, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the experiences of Company "E" of the 26th Ohio Infantry during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and historical accounts, the book delves into the trials, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave soldiers as they navigate the horrors of war and fight for the preservation of the Union.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
