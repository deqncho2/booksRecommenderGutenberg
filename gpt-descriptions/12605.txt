Synopsis: In 'Parish Papers' by Norman Macleod, a young minister finds himself in a small Scottish village, where he uncovers a collection of mysterious papers hidden in the church's attic. As he delves into the secrets of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of scandal, love, and redemption that will challenge his faith and change the lives of those around him forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Faith and doubt, the power of secrets, the complexities of human relationships
