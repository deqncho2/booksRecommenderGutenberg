Synopsis: In 'The Knights of the White Shield: Up-the-Ladder Club Series, Round One Play' by Edward A. Rand, a group of young boys forms a secret club with a noble mission: to protect their neighborhood from a mysterious threat. As they embark on their first mission, they uncover hidden clues, face unexpected challenges, and learn the true meaning of friendship and bravery. With each step up the ladder, their bond grows stronger, and their determination to save their community becomes unwavering. Will they succeed in their quest and become the heroes they aspire to be?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Teamwork, Community
