Synopsis:
In "The Youth of the Great Elector," Luise Mühlbach takes readers on a captivating journey through the formative years of a remarkable historical figure. Set in 17th-century Europe, the story follows the young prince as he navigates the treacherous waters of court politics, battles personal demons, and strives to fulfill his destiny. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Mühlbach paints a vivid portrait of a man who would go on to become one of Prussia's most influential leaders. As secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns that shape the Great Elector's path to greatness.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biographical fiction, Political drama

Themes: Ambition, Power struggles, Personal growth, Loyalty, Betrayal
