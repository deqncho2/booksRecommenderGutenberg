Synopsis: In "The Lost King of Oz," a mysterious visitor arrives in the magical land of Oz, claiming to be the long-lost king. As Dorothy and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth, they encounter enchanting creatures, treacherous villains, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Will they be able to restore the rightful ruler to the throne and bring harmony back to Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Power
