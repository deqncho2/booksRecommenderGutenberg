Synopsis: In 'The Confessions of a Caricaturist, Vol. 1', Harry Furniss takes us on a captivating journey through the world of caricature, revealing the secrets and stories behind his most famous works. From the halls of power to the bustling streets of London, Furniss shares his encounters with influential figures, his humorous observations, and the challenges he faced as a caricaturist. With wit and charm, he invites readers into his creative process, offering a unique glimpse into the art form that both entertains and satirizes society.

Genres: Memoir, Art, Humor

Themes: Artistic expression, Satire, Society and culture
