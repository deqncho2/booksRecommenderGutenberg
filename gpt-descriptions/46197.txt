Synopsis: 
In the small town of Starved Rock, a mysterious murder takes place, leaving the community in shock and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, revealing the dark underbelly of this seemingly idyllic town. With a web of deceit and betrayal, the residents of Starved Rock must confront their own demons and face the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Betrayal, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
