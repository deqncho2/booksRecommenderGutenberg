Synopsis: 

In 'Records of a Family of Engineers' by Robert Louis Stevenson, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of the Stevenson family, a lineage of brilliant engineers spanning several generations. From the construction of lighthouses to the design of steam engines, this memoir-like account delves into the triumphs, challenges, and adventures faced by these remarkable individuals. Through vivid anecdotes and personal reflections, Stevenson paints a vivid picture of the dedication, ingenuity, and passion that runs deep within this extraordinary family. 

Genres: Memoir, Biography, History

Themes: Engineering, Family Legacy, Perseverance, Innovation
