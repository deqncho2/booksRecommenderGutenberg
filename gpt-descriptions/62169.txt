Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Derelict' by John Broome, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to investigate an abandoned spaceship drifting in the vastness of space. As they explore the mysterious vessel, they uncover mind-bending secrets that challenge their understanding of the universe and their place within it. With each revelation, the astronauts find themselves entangled in a web of cosmic intrigue, where danger lurks at every turn and the fate of humanity hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Existentialism, Humanity's place in the universe
