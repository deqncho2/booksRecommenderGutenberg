Synopsis: In 'How to Become Rich: A Treatise on Phrenology, Choice of Professions and Matrimony' by Windsor, William, embark on a captivating journey that explores the secrets to acquiring wealth, success, and happiness. Through the lens of phrenology, the author delves into the intricate connections between one's physical attributes, career choices, and even the choice of a life partner. With a blend of scientific analysis and practical advice, this thought-provoking treatise offers a unique perspective on the pursuit of riches and the art of living a fulfilling life.

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Non-fiction

Themes: Wealth accumulation, Career guidance, Relationship dynamics
