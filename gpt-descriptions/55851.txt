Synopsis: In "The Wishing Horse of Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to save the magical land of Oz from a mischievous sorcerer. When a mysterious horse with the power to grant wishes appears, they must navigate through enchanted forests, encounter peculiar creatures, and outsmart the sorcerer's wicked plans. With the fate of Oz hanging in the balance, will they be able to harness the true power of friendship and overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Magic
