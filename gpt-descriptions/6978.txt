Synopsis: In 'Made-Over Dishes' by S. T. Rorer, embark on a culinary journey filled with innovative recipes and creative twists on classic dishes. From mouthwatering appetizers to delectable desserts, this cookbook offers a fresh take on traditional favorites, providing readers with a delightful and unexpected culinary experience. With easy-to-follow instructions and helpful tips, Rorer invites readers to explore the world of cooking in a whole new way, making every meal a memorable and delicious adventure.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink

Themes: Culinary creativity, Reinventing traditional recipes
