Synopsis: In 'Peregrine's Progress' by Farnol, Jeffery, follow the captivating journey of Peregrine, a young man with a mysterious past, as he navigates through a world filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists. From the bustling streets of London to the treacherous high seas, Peregrine's life takes an exhilarating turn when he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, he must outsmart his adversaries and uncover the truth about his own identity. Will Peregrine's progress lead him to redemption or a darker fate?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Deception
