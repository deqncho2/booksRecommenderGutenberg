Synopsis: In the remote island of Samburan, a man named Axel Heyst seeks solace from the complexities of civilization. However, his peaceful existence is disrupted when he becomes entangled with a captivating woman and a sinister trio of criminals. As the story unfolds, Heyst finds himself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and moral dilemmas, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Psychological Fiction, Romance

Themes: Isolation, Morality, Betrayal, Redemption
