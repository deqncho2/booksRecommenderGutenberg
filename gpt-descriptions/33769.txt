Synopsis: 

In 'The Harwich Naval Forces: Their Part in the Great War', author E. F. Knight takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known but crucial role played by the Harwich Naval Forces during World War I. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Knight unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and strategic brilliance that unfolded in the waters off the coast of Harwich. From daring naval battles to covert operations, readers will be enthralled by the gripping accounts of the men and women who fought valiantly to protect their homeland.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Heroism, Naval warfare, Patriotism, Sacrifice
