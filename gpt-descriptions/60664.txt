Synopsis: In 'Pipe Dream' by Fritz Leiber, a struggling writer named Joe Smith discovers a mysterious pipe that grants him the ability to see into the future. As he becomes addicted to the visions it provides, Joe must navigate the blurred lines between reality and fantasy, leading him down a dangerous path of obsession and self-destruction. With each glimpse into the future, Joe's life becomes increasingly entangled in a web of uncertainty, forcing him to question the true nature of his newfound power and the consequences it may bring.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Addiction, Reality vs. Fantasy, Fate and Free Will
