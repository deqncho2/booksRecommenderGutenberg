Synopsis:
In "The Island of Doctor Moreau," a shipwrecked man finds himself stranded on a mysterious island inhabited by peculiar creatures. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the island, he uncovers the disturbing experiments conducted by the enigmatic Doctor Moreau. With each revelation, the man's sense of horror and fascination grows, leading him to question the boundaries of science, morality, and the nature of humanity itself.

Genres:
Science fiction, Horror, Thriller

Themes:
Ethics, Evolution, Identity, Morality
