Synopsis: 

'Children's Books and Their Illustrators' by White and Gleeson takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of children's literature. This insightful book explores the fascinating relationship between authors and illustrators, delving into the creative process behind some of the most beloved children's books of all time. From the whimsical illustrations of Maurice Sendak to the timeless stories of Dr. Seuss, this book celebrates the magic and artistry that bring children's books to life. Through engaging anecdotes and stunning visuals, 'Children's Books and Their Illustrators' offers a behind-the-scenes look at the imaginative minds and extraordinary talents that have shaped the world of children's literature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Literary Criticism

Themes: Creativity, Imagination, Collaboration
