Synopsis: 
In 'Experimental Mechanics: A Course of Lectures Delivered at the Royal College of Science for Ireland', Robert S. Ball takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of experimental mechanics. Through a series of engaging lectures, Ball explores the fascinating principles and applications of this field, revealing the hidden secrets behind the mechanics of everyday objects. From the intricate workings of machines to the mysteries of motion and energy, this book offers a captivating exploration of the fundamental principles that govern our physical world.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: 
Mechanics, Physics, Scientific Inquiry
