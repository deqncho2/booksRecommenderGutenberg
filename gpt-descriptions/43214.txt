Synopsis: 
In 'Dr. Arne and Rule, Britannia', William Hayman Cummings takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Dr. Thomas Augustine Arne, a renowned composer of the 18th century. Set against the backdrop of Georgian England, this enthralling biography delves into the fascinating world of music, love, and ambition. As we follow Dr. Arne's rise to fame and his tumultuous relationships, we are transported to a time of grandeur and elegance, where the power of music holds the key to both personal and national identity.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Music

Themes: Ambition, Love, Identity, Music
