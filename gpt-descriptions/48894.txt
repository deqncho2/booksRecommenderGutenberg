Synopsis:
In the second volume of "Mademoiselle de Maupin," the captivating story of the enigmatic and alluring Mademoiselle de Maupin continues. As her secret identity is threatened to be revealed, she finds herself entangled in a web of passion, desire, and deception. With her heart torn between two lovers, Mademoiselle de Maupin must navigate the complexities of love, society, and self-discovery. Will she be able to maintain her facade and protect her true identity, or will the truth unravel everything she holds dear?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Love, Gender Roles, Deception
