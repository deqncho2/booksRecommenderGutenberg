Synopsis: In 'The Opal Serpent' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a priceless opal serpent becomes the center of a web of secrets, deceit, and murder. Set in the vibrant city of London, the story follows a determined detective who must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth behind the opal serpent's curse. As the investigation deepens, the line between good and evil blurs, and the characters find themselves entangled in a dangerous game where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Greed, Betrayal, Redemption
