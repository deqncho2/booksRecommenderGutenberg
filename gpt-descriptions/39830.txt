Synopsis: In 'Memoranda on Poisons' by Thomas Hawkes Tanner, a renowned toxicologist, delves into the dark and mysterious world of poisons. Through a collection of intriguing case studies and scientific analysis, Tanner uncovers the hidden secrets behind some of history's most notorious poisonings. From ancient times to the present day, this captivating work explores the motives, methods, and consequences of poisoners, leaving readers spellbound by the deadly art of poisoning.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, History

Themes: Poison, Crime, Forensic Science
