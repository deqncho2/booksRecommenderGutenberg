Synopsis: 

'Among the Sioux: A Story of the Twin Cities and the Two Dakotas' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and cultural tapestry of the Twin Cities and the Dakotas. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this enthralling novel follows the lives of a diverse group of characters as they navigate the complexities of frontier life, Native American relations, and the rapid development of the region. From the bustling streets of Minneapolis and St. Paul to the vast expanses of the Dakotas, the story weaves together tales of love, loss, adventure, and the enduring spirit of the people who call this land home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Cultural diversity, Frontier life, Native American relations, Love and loss, Resilience
