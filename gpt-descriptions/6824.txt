Synopsis: In the picturesque setting of Yorkshire, a young woman named Mary Anerley finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue. As she navigates through the rugged landscapes and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Mary's journey takes unexpected turns, revealing secrets that will challenge her beliefs and test her courage. With its captivating blend of romance, adventure, and suspense, 'Mary Anerley: A Yorkshire Tale' is a tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic events that unfold.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Social Class, Justice
