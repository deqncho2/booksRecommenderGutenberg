Synopsis: In the small town of Clearwater, the sheriff's son, Jake, finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies when a mysterious stranger arrives, stirring up long-buried memories and threatening to expose the town's darkest secrets. As Jake delves deeper into the stranger's past, he uncovers a shocking truth that will test his loyalty, challenge his beliefs, and ultimately force him to confront his own identity. With danger lurking around every corner, Jake must navigate a treacherous path to protect his family and the town he loves, all while unraveling the truth behind the stranger's arrival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Loyalty, Small-town dynamics
