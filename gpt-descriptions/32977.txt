Synopsis: 
'From Sea to Sea; Letters of Travel' by Rudyard Kipling takes readers on a captivating journey across continents, as the renowned author shares his experiences and observations through a series of vivid letters. From the bustling streets of Bombay to the vast landscapes of Australia, Kipling's engaging narrative transports readers to exotic locales, introducing them to a diverse array of cultures, people, and customs. Through his keen observations and insightful reflections, Kipling offers a unique perspective on the world, capturing both the beauty and complexities of the places he encounters. With his signature storytelling prowess, Kipling weaves together a tapestry of travel tales that will leave readers yearning for their own adventures.

Genres: Travel literature, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Human connection, Identity
