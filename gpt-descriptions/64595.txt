Synopsis: 
In 'Animat' by Basil Wells, a renowned scientist creates a groundbreaking invention that blurs the line between man and machine. As the protagonist delves deeper into the capabilities of his creation, he discovers the unforeseen consequences of playing god. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, this gripping tale explores the ethical implications of technological advancements and the boundaries of human nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ethics, Technological Advancements, Human Nature
