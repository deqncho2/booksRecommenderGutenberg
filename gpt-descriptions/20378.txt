Synopsis: In 'Christmas Sunshine', a young woman named Lily finds herself unexpectedly spending the holiday season in a charming seaside town. As she immerses herself in the local festivities, she crosses paths with a mysterious stranger who seems to hold the key to unlocking the true meaning of Christmas. With each passing day, Lily's heart is filled with hope, love, and the magic of the season, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected surprises.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Holiday

Themes: Love, Redemption, Finding Joy
