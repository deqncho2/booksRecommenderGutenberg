Synopsis: In 'The Dream Coach', a mysterious coach appears in the small town of Ashford, offering its passengers the chance to live out their wildest dreams. As the townspeople embark on their fantastical journeys, they soon discover that the coach holds more secrets than they could have ever imagined. With each dream fulfilled, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of those aboard the Dream Coach.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Magical Realism

Themes: Dreams and desires, the power of imagination, the consequences of escapism
