Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the Night' by Gaston Leroux, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a missing person. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a series of dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With danger lurking at every turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and deception, Justice and truth
