Synopsis: 

In 'The Traditional Games of England, Scotland, and Ireland (Vol 2 of 2): With Tunes, Singing-Rhymes, and Methods of Playing etc.', Alice Bertha Gomme takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and vibrant world of traditional games. From the lively streets of England to the enchanting landscapes of Scotland and Ireland, this volume delves into the fascinating history, rules, and variations of beloved games that have been passed down through generations. With detailed descriptions and delightful anecdotes, Gomme invites readers to rediscover the joy and camaraderie of these timeless pastimes.

Genres: Non-fiction, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Heritage, Community, Play
