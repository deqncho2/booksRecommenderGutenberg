Synopsis: In 'Polly and the Princess' by Emma C. Dowd, a young girl named Polly discovers a hidden secret that leads her on an extraordinary adventure. When she stumbles upon a mysterious locket, Polly is transported to a magical kingdom where she encounters a brave princess in need of her help. Together, they embark on a thrilling quest filled with danger, friendship, and unexpected twists. As Polly navigates this enchanting world, she learns valuable lessons about courage, loyalty, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-belief
