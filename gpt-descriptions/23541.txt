Synopsis: In 'Dick, Marjorie and Fidge: A Search for the Wonderful Dodo' by G. E. Farrow, three adventurous siblings embark on a thrilling quest to find a legendary creature that was thought to be extinct. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover secrets that challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever. This captivating tale of exploration and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Belief, Family bonds
