Synopsis: In "I.N.R.I.: A prisoner's Story of the Cross" by Peter Rosegger, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a prisoner who finds himself unexpectedly intertwined with the story of the cross. As the prisoner's life takes an unexpected turn, he is faced with profound questions about faith, redemption, and the power of forgiveness. Through his unique perspective, the reader is invited to explore the depths of human suffering and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Historical fiction, religious fiction, philosophical fiction

Themes: Faith, redemption, forgiveness, human suffering, love
