Synopsis: 
'Practical Taxidermy' is a comprehensive manual that offers step-by-step instructions to amateurs interested in collecting, preserving, and setting up natural history specimens. Written by Montagu Browne, this guide also includes a chapter on the pictorial arrangement of museums and provides additional instructions in modelling and artistic taxidermy. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced taxidermist, this book will captivate your interest and provide valuable insights into the world of preserving and displaying natural specimens.

Genres: Non-fiction, Instructional, Natural History

Themes: Taxidermy, Natural History, Artistic Preservation
