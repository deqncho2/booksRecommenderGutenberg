Synopsis: In 'An Outline of Russian Literature', Maurice Baring takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse world of Russian literature. From the enchanting tales of Pushkin to the profound works of Dostoevsky, Baring delves into the lives and writings of the great Russian authors, exploring the themes and ideas that have shaped this literary tradition. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Baring paints a vivid picture of the cultural and historical context that gave birth to these masterpieces, offering readers a fascinating glimpse into the soul of Russian literature.

Genres: Literary criticism, Biography, History

Themes: Russian culture, Identity, Human nature, Social commentary
