Synopsis: 
'Gleanings in Graveyards: A Collection of Curious Epitaphs' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tombstones of various cemeteries, unearthing a treasure trove of intriguing and often humorous epitaphs. From witty one-liners to poignant reflections on life and death, this collection offers a glimpse into the lives and personalities of those who have passed on. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover a rich tapestry of stories, emotions, and reflections that will both entertain and provoke thought.

Genres: Non-fiction, Anthology, Humor

Themes: Life and death, Human nature, Reflections on mortality
