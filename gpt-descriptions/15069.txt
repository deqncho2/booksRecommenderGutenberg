Synopsis: In 'Diet and Health; With Key to the Calories', Lulu Hunt Peters takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of nutrition and weight management. With a refreshing blend of scientific knowledge and practical advice, Peters explores the secrets behind maintaining a healthy diet and achieving optimal well-being. From debunking common myths to providing insightful tips, this book offers a comprehensive guide to understanding the relationship between diet and health. Prepare to be enlightened and inspired as Peters empowers readers to take control of their own wellness journey.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Self-help

Themes: Nutrition, Weight management, Healthy lifestyle
