Synopsis: In 'The War Romance of the Salvation Army', two remarkable women, Grace Livingston Hill and Evangeline Booth, come together to share their extraordinary experiences during World War I. Through their captivating stories, they reveal the untold tales of love, sacrifice, and unwavering faith amidst the chaos of war. As they navigate the frontlines and the challenges of serving in the Salvation Army, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to profound transformations and unexpected romance. This powerful and inspiring novel takes readers on a journey of courage, resilience, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Faith, Resilience
