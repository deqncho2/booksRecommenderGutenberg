Synopsis: In 'The Goop Directory of Juvenile Offenders Famous for their Misdeeds and Serving as a Salutary Example for all Virtuous Children' by Burgess, Gelett, readers are introduced to a collection of mischievous youngsters who have gained notoriety for their misdeeds. From the cunning trickster to the incorrigible troublemaker, each character's story unfolds, providing a cautionary tale for young readers. With humorous anecdotes and clever illustrations, this book offers an entertaining exploration of the consequences of bad behavior and the importance of virtuous conduct.

Genres: Children's Literature, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Virtue
