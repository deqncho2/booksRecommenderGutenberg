Synopsis: In 'The Fiery Totem: A Tale of Adventure in the Canadian North-West' by Saxby, C. F. Argyll, a group of intrepid explorers embarks on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of the Canadian North-West. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious indigenous tribes, they become entangled in a web of ancient legends and supernatural forces. With danger lurking at every turn, the adventurers must rely on their wits and courage to unravel the secrets of the fiery totem and survive the unforgiving wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Indigenous cultures, Supernatural forces
