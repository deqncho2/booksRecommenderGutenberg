Synopsis: In "A Wonder Book for Girls & Boys" by Nathaniel Hawthorne, embark on a captivating journey through Greek mythology as the author retells six enchanting tales. From the daring adventures of Perseus and the terrifying encounter with Medusa, to the heartwarming story of Pandora and her mysterious box, each myth is brought to life with Hawthorne's imaginative storytelling. Discover the magical world of gods, goddesses, and mythical creatures, where heroes face incredible challenges and learn valuable lessons along the way. This collection of timeless tales is sure to captivate readers of all ages, inviting them to explore the wonders of ancient Greece.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Children's Literature

Themes: Heroism, Morality, Curiosity, Consequences
