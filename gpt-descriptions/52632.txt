Synopsis: In 'The Third Officer: A Present-day Pirate Story' by Percy F. Westerman, a young officer finds himself caught in a thrilling and dangerous adventure on the high seas. When his ship is attacked by modern-day pirates, he must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart his captors, and uncover the truth behind their sinister motives. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, the third officer must rely on his wits and courage to survive in this gripping tale of piracy and intrigue.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Courage
