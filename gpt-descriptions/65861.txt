Synopsis: In 'The Mischievous Typesetter' by Noel M. Loomis, a small-town newspaper is turned upside down when a mischievous typesetter begins inserting scandalous stories into the paper. As the town becomes consumed by gossip and chaos, the editor must uncover the identity of the culprit before the newspaper's reputation is irreparably damaged. With a mix of humor, mystery, and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Deception, Small-town dynamics, Journalism ethics
