Synopsis: In 'The Comrade in White' by William Harvey Leathem, a young doctor finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is assigned to a remote hospital in a war-torn country. As he navigates through the chaos of war, he becomes increasingly drawn to a mysterious patient known only as "The Comrade in White." With each passing day, the doctor uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to question the true nature of loyalty and sacrifice. As the lines between friend and foe blur, he must make a choice that will not only determine his own fate but also the fate of those around him.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Identity, Morality
