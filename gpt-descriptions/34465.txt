Synopsis: In "A Little Book of Christmas," John Kendrick Bangs takes readers on a delightful journey through the holiday season, filled with heartwarming stories, humorous anecdotes, and charming characters. From mischievous elves causing havoc to unexpected encounters with Santa Claus himself, this collection of Christmas tales is sure to bring joy and laughter to readers of all ages. With its enchanting storytelling and timeless charm, "A Little Book of Christmas" captures the true spirit of the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Holiday

Themes: Christmas spirit, Joy and laughter, Holiday traditions
