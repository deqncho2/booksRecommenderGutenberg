Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the captivating history of the Lee Mansion National Memorial in Arlington, Virginia. This meticulously researched and beautifully illustrated book takes readers on a journey through the grandeur and intrigue of one of America's most iconic landmarks. Discover the secrets hidden within the walls of this historic mansion, as well as the fascinating stories of the people who lived and visited there. From the opulent rooms to the lush gardens, immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of the Lee Mansion National Memorial and uncover the untold tales that have shaped its legacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Biography

Themes: American history, Civil War, Preservation of historic sites
