Synopsis: 

'Key and Guide to Native Trees, Shrubs, and Woody Vines of Dallas County' by Norma Stillwell is a comprehensive and captivating guidebook that takes readers on a journey through the diverse and enchanting world of native flora in Dallas County. With detailed descriptions, vivid photographs, and an easy-to-use key, this book serves as an invaluable resource for nature enthusiasts, hikers, and botany lovers alike. From towering trees to delicate shrubs and intricate woody vines, the author unveils the hidden beauty and ecological significance of each species, providing readers with a deeper understanding and appreciation for the natural wonders that surround them. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced naturalist, this guidebook will inspire you to explore and discover the remarkable plant life that thrives in Dallas County.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Botany

Themes: Biodiversity, Environmental Conservation, Nature Appreciation
