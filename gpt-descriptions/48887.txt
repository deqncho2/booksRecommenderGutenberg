Synopsis: 

'The Month of Mary, According to the Spirit of St. Francis of Sales: Thirty-One Considerations With Examples, Prayers, Etc.' takes readers on a spiritual journey through the month of May, dedicated to the Virgin Mary. Written by Saint Francis de Sales and Gaspar Gilli, this book offers thirty-one daily considerations, each filled with examples, prayers, and reflections that delve into the profound devotion and love for Mary. Through these pages, readers will discover the beauty of Mary's role in the life of a Christian, her virtues, and the profound impact she can have on their own spiritual journey.

Genres: Religious, Devotional, Spiritual

Themes: Devotion to Mary, Spiritual Growth, Christian Virtues
