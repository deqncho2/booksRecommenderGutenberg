Synopsis: 

In "China" by Henry Arthur Blake, Sir, a young British diplomat finds himself embroiled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during his assignment in China. As he navigates the complex world of international relations and cultural clashes, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where loyalty and betrayal blur the lines. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and challenge his understanding of love and duty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Cultural clash, Diplomacy, Love and duty
