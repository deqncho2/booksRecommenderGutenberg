Synopsis: In "Chivalry" by James Branch Cabell, a young woman named Melicent embarks on a quest to find the legendary knight, Sir Launcelot, and bring him back to her modern world. As she navigates through time and space, Melicent encounters various characters from Arthurian legends and faces unexpected challenges that test her determination. This enchanting tale weaves together elements of fantasy, romance, and adventure, taking readers on a captivating journey through the realms of myth and imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Quest for identity, Time and reality, Myth and legend
