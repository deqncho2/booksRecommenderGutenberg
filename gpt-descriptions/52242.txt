Synopsis: In "The Life of Tolstoy: First Fifty Years: Fifth Edition," Aylmer Maude delves into the fascinating life of one of the greatest literary figures of all time, Leo Tolstoy. From his privileged upbringing to his tumultuous relationships and his profound spiritual awakening, Maude paints a vivid portrait of Tolstoy's early years. This meticulously researched biography takes readers on a captivating journey through the formative experiences and influences that shaped Tolstoy's remarkable literary career.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic development, Personal transformation, Social and political commentary
