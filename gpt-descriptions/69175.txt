Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious house stands abandoned, its secrets hidden behind seven locked doors. When a group of friends stumble upon the house, they are drawn into a thrilling and dangerous adventure that will test their courage and unravel the dark history of the seven stairs. As they unlock each door, they uncover chilling secrets and face unimaginable horrors, leading them to question their own sanity and the true nature of evil lurking within the house. With each step they take, the stakes grow higher, and they must confront their deepest fears to survive the night.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Fear, Friendship, Secrets, Supernatural, Psychological suspense
