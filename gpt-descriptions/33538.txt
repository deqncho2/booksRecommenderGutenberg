Synopsis: 
In 'A Bed of Roses' by George, Walter Lionel, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves deeper into the history of the estate, Rose uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, betrayal, and tragedy. With each revelation, Rose's own life becomes entangled in the past, forcing her to confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. This gripping tale of love, mystery, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
