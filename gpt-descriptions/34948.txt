Synopsis: 
In the remote wilderness of Maine, a young man named King Spruce finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he becomes entangled with a powerful lumber baron and a mysterious woman. As he navigates the treacherous world of the timber industry, King must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions of the rugged landscape and a cast of compelling characters, 'King Spruce' is a gripping tale of ambition, love, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery, Environmentalism
