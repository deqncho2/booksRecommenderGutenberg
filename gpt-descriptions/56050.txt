Synopsis: 

Step into the rich history of Castillo de San Marcos National Monument in Florida with this comprehensive guide by the United States National Park Service. Discover the captivating stories behind the oldest masonry fort in the continental United States, from its strategic significance to its role in shaping the nation's history. Explore the architectural marvels, immerse yourself in the tales of battles fought and won, and uncover the secrets that lie within the fortress walls. This guide is a must-read for history enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and anyone curious about the remarkable legacy of Castillo de San Marcos.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Historical preservation, Military history, Cultural heritage
