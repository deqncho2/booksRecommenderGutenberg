Synopsis: In 'A Personal Problem' by Bedford-Jones, H., a seemingly ordinary man finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he receives a cryptic message that threatens to unravel his entire life. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers shocking secrets and uncovers a hidden world filled with deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and confront the personal demons that haunt him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
