Synopsis: 

In "Historical sketch of the Fifteenth Regiment, New Jersey Volunteers: First Brigade, First Division, Sixth Corps," Edward Livingston Campbell takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the Fifteenth Regiment during the American Civil War. From the regiment's formation to its involvement in major battles, Campbell provides a vivid and detailed account of the soldiers' bravery, sacrifices, and the challenges they faced on the battlefield. Through personal anecdotes and historical records, the author paints a compelling picture of the regiment's role in shaping the outcome of the war, leaving readers eager to learn more about this often overlooked chapter in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Historical memory
