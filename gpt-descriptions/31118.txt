Synopsis: 
"The Long Day: The Story of a New York Working Girl, as Told by Herself" is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a journey through the life of a young woman in early 20th century New York City. Dorothy Richardson, the protagonist, shares her personal experiences and struggles as she navigates the challenges of being a working girl in a bustling metropolis. From her humble beginnings to her encounters with love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams, Dorothy's story is a poignant and inspiring tale of resilience and determination.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Gender Roles, Social Class, Self-discovery
