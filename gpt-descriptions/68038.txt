Synopsis: In "With our army in Flanders" by Valentine Williams, a young journalist finds himself thrust into the heart of the action during World War I. As he navigates the treacherous trenches and witnesses the horrors of war, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and espionage that could change the course of the conflict. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive and uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
