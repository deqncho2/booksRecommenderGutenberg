Synopsis: 
In "Death Star" by Tom Pace, a gripping and suspenseful tale unfolds as a team of unlikely heroes embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious and powerful weapon. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected betrayals, the fate of the galaxy hangs in the balance. With its intricate plot twists and compelling characters, this thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal
