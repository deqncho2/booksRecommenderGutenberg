Synopsis: 

In 'The North American Slime-Moulds: A Descriptive List of All Species of Myxomycetes Hitherto Reported from the Continent of North America, with Notes on Some Extra-Limital Species', Thomas H. Macbride takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of slime-moulds. This comprehensive guide provides a detailed account of all the known species of myxomycetes found in North America, shedding light on their characteristics, habitats, and distribution. Macbride's meticulous observations and insightful notes on some species beyond the continent make this a valuable resource for both scientists and nature enthusiasts alike. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Biodiversity, Natural History, Scientific Exploration
