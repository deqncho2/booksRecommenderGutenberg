Synopsis: In 'With Axe and Rifle' by William Henry Giles Kingston, embark on an exhilarating adventure set in the untamed wilderness of North America. Follow the journey of a young protagonist who finds himself caught in a web of danger, survival, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters both friend and foe, he must rely on his wit, courage, and the tools at his disposal to overcome the perils that lie ahead. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of our intrepid hero.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Action

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Coming of Age
