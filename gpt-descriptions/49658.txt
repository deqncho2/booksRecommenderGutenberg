Synopsis: 
In 'Writ in Barracks' by Wallace Edgar, a young soldier finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret within the barracks. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of his comrades. With time running out, he must navigate through a maze of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Intrigue
