Synopsis: In "The Cycle of Spring" by Rabindranath Tagore, a young artist named Amulya embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against societal norms. Set in the vibrant city of Calcutta, the story follows Amulya as he navigates the complexities of love, art, and the pursuit of freedom. As he challenges the traditional values and expectations imposed upon him, Amulya finds himself torn between his passion for creativity and the responsibilities that come with his privileged background. Through a series of encounters with diverse characters, Amulya's journey becomes a reflection on the transformative power of art and the struggle to find one's true identity.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Individuality, Artistic expression, Social conformity, Love and relationships
