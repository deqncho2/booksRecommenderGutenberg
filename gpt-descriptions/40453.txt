Synopsis: In 'Tom Ossington's Ghost' by Richard Marsh, a young man named Tom Ossington finds himself haunted by a mysterious ghostly presence. As the apparition becomes increasingly malevolent, Tom becomes determined to uncover the truth behind its existence. With each chilling encounter, he delves deeper into a web of secrets and dark history, leading him to question his own sanity and the nature of the supernatural. As the haunting intensifies, Tom must confront his deepest fears and unravel the enigma before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Ghosts, Psychological suspense, Identity
