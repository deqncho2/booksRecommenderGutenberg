Synopsis: 
'Field Hospital and Flying Column: Being the Journal of an English Nursing Sister in Belgium & Russia' follows the captivating journey of Violetta Thurstan, an English nursing sister, as she embarks on a remarkable adventure in the midst of war. From the chaos of the field hospitals in Belgium to the treacherous landscapes of Russia, Violetta's journal offers a unique perspective on the harrowing experiences faced by nurses during World War I. Through her vivid and heartfelt accounts, the reader is transported to the frontlines, witnessing the courage, resilience, and compassion of those who dedicated their lives to saving others.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, the role of women in wartime, the power of compassion and resilience.
