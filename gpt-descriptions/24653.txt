Synopsis: In "Personal Recollections and Experiences Concerning the Battle of Stone River," Milo S. Hascall takes us on a gripping journey through one of the bloodiest battles of the American Civil War. Through his vivid and personal accounts, Hascall provides a unique perspective on the harrowing events that unfolded during the Battle of Stone River, revealing the true horrors and heroism experienced by soldiers on both sides of the conflict. As he recounts his own experiences and those of his comrades, Hascall paints a vivid picture of the chaos, bravery, and sacrifice that defined this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Military history

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Bravery and sacrifice, Historical perspective
