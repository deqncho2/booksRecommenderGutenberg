Synopsis: 
'A Journey to the Centre of the Earth' follows the adventures of Professor Otto Lidenbrock and his nephew Axel as they embark on a daring expedition to explore the depths of the Earth. After discovering an ancient manuscript that leads them to believe there is a hidden world beneath their feet, the duo sets off on a perilous journey filled with unexpected discoveries, treacherous obstacles, and breathtaking wonders. As they delve deeper into the Earth's core, they encounter strange creatures, geological marvels, and uncover secrets that challenge their understanding of the world. Will they survive the dangers that lie beneath the surface and unravel the mysteries of the Earth's core?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Scientific Discovery
