Synopsis: 
"The Answering Voice: One Hundred Love Lyrics by Women" is a captivating collection of poetic expressions that delves into the depths of love, desire, and longing. Through the voices of various women, this anthology explores the multifaceted nature of love, from its intoxicating beginnings to its bittersweet endings. Each lyric is a window into the hearts and minds of these women, revealing their vulnerabilities, passions, and the complexities of their relationships. With evocative imagery and lyrical prowess, this anthology invites readers on an emotional journey through the intricacies of love.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Women's Literature

Themes: Love, Desire, Longing, Relationships, Femininity
