Synopsis: In 'Of Time and Texas' by William F. Nolan, a mysterious time-traveling device transports a group of unsuspecting tourists to the wild and untamed landscapes of Texas in the 1800s. As they navigate the dangers and challenges of the past, they must confront their own personal demons and make difficult choices that could alter the course of history. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what will happen next.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, personal growth, the consequences of one's actions
