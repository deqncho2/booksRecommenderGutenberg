Synopsis: In 'The Lamplighter' by Maria S. Cummins, a young orphan named Gerty finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and adventure when she is taken in by a kind-hearted lamplighter. As she navigates the bustling streets of Boston and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Gerty's life takes an unexpected turn that will test her courage and resilience. With secrets to uncover and challenges to overcome, Gerty's journey will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Identity, Social class
