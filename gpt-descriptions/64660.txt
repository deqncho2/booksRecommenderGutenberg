Synopsis:
In 'The Serpent's Tooth' by B. M. Croker, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and alluring stranger. As their relationship deepens, Evelyn discovers that there is much more to this man than meets the eye, and she must navigate a dangerous path to uncover the truth. With twists and turns at every corner, 'The Serpent's Tooth' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes:
Deception, Trust, Identity
