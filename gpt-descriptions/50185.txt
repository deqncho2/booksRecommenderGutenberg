Synopsis: 

In 'English Caricature and Satire on Napoleon I. Volume 2', John Ashton delves into the fascinating world of English caricature and satire during the era of Napoleon Bonaparte. This second volume continues to explore the witty and often scathing depictions of the French emperor and his influence on British society. From political cartoons to humorous illustrations, Ashton uncovers the clever and sometimes controversial ways in which Napoleon was portrayed by English artists. 

Genres: History, Art, Satire

Themes: Political satire, National identity, War and conflict
