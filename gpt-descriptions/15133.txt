Synopsis: In 'Campfire Girls in the Allegheny Mountains; or, A Christmas Success against Odds' by Stella M. Francis, a group of adventurous Campfire Girls embark on a winter camping trip in the picturesque Allegheny Mountains. As they face unexpected challenges and encounter mysterious events, the girls must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and the spirit of Christmas to overcome the odds and create a memorable holiday celebration. This heartwarming tale of friendship, resilience, and the magic of the holiday season will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Teamwork, Holiday Spirit
