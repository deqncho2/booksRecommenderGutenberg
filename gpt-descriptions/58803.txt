Synopsis: 
'The Witch-Maid, & Other Verses' by Dorothea Mackellar is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through enchanting tales and evocative verses. From mystical witches to haunting landscapes, each poem transports the reader to a world filled with wonder and imagination. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Mackellar weaves together stories that explore the depths of human emotions and the beauty of the natural world. This collection is sure to captivate poetry lovers and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Nature, Emotions
