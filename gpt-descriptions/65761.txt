Synopsis: In 'A Merchant Fleet at War' by Archibald Hurd, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of World War I. Set against the backdrop of a merchant fleet caught in the crossfire, this gripping tale follows the lives of courageous sailors as they navigate the perils of enemy submarines, deadly attacks, and the constant threat of destruction. As the war rages on, the characters must confront their deepest fears, forge unbreakable bonds, and find strength in the face of adversity. With its vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the untold stories of those who risked their lives on the high seas during wartime.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
