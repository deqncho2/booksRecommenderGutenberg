Synopsis: In the midst of a harsh winter in 1734, Micah Clarke, an elderly man, shares an extraordinary tale with his three grandchildren. As he recounts his adventures as a young soldier during the Monmouth Rebellion, the reader is transported to a tumultuous time in English history. Filled with battles, political intrigue, and personal growth, Micah's story is a captivating journey through a pivotal period in the nation's past.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Identity, Courage
