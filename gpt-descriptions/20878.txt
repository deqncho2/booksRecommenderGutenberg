Synopsis: In 'Critical Miscellanies, Vol. 1, Essay 2: Carlyle', John Morley delves into the life and works of the enigmatic writer Thomas Carlyle. Through an exploration of Carlyle's unique writing style and his influence on the literary world, Morley uncovers the captivating story behind this influential figure. As Morley delves into Carlyle's unconventional ideas and controversial views, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional wisdom and offers a fresh perspective on the power of words.

Genres: Literary criticism, biography, essay

Themes: Influence of literature, unconventional ideas, power of words
