Synopsis: In "What a Young Woman Ought to Know," Mary Wood-Allen explores the essential knowledge that every young woman should possess. Through a series of enlightening and empowering discussions, she delves into topics such as physical health, emotional well-being, and the importance of self-respect. With a compassionate and informative approach, Wood-Allen provides invaluable guidance for young women navigating the challenges of adolescence and adulthood.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Education

Themes: Women's health, Personal development, Empowerment
