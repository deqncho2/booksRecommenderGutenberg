Synopsis: 
In 'Cattle-Ranch to College: The True Tales of a Boy's Adventures in the Far West', Doubleday, Russell takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the rugged landscapes of the American West. This captivating memoir follows the author's remarkable coming-of-age story as he navigates the challenges of growing up on a cattle ranch and eventually pursuing higher education. From thrilling encounters with wild animals to heartwarming friendships forged in the untamed wilderness, this book offers a unique glimpse into a world filled with both danger and beauty. As the protagonist faces numerous trials and triumphs, readers will be captivated by the vivid descriptions and the author's unwavering spirit of adventure.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Western

Themes: Coming-of-age, Nature, Pursuit of Education
