Synopsis: In 'Little Helpers' by Margaret Vandegrift, a group of seemingly ordinary women come together to form an unconventional support group. As they navigate the challenges of their daily lives, they discover a shared secret that binds them together. With their unique abilities, they embark on a mission to help those in need, all while keeping their extraordinary powers hidden from the world. As their bond strengthens, they find themselves facing unexpected dangers and uncovering a hidden world they never knew existed. This thrilling and heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the strength within oneself, and the extraordinary potential that lies within ordinary people.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Women's Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Empowerment, Hidden Potential
