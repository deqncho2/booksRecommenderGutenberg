Synopsis: In 'Mr Punch's Pocket Ibsen - A Collection of Some of the Master's Best Known Dramas', Anstey presents a captivating compilation of Henrik Ibsen's most renowned plays. From the gripping tale of a woman's struggle for independence in "A Doll's House" to the haunting exploration of societal expectations in "Ghosts," this collection offers a diverse range of thought-provoking dramas. Each play delves into the complexities of human relationships, societal norms, and the pursuit of personal freedom, leaving readers captivated by Ibsen's timeless storytelling.

Genres: Drama, Classic Literature

Themes: Independence, Society, Personal Freedom, Relationships
