Synopsis: In 'The Golden Bough: A Study in Magic and Religion', James George Frazer delves into the fascinating world of ancient beliefs and rituals. This third edition, Volume 05 of 12, takes readers on a captivating journey through various cultures and civilizations, exploring the intricate connections between magic, religion, and society. From the mystical practices of ancient priests to the customs surrounding fertility and harvest, Frazer uncovers the hidden threads that bind humanity's spiritual past. With meticulous research and compelling anecdotes, this volume offers a thought-provoking exploration of the universal human quest for meaning and the power of belief.

Genres: Anthropology, History, Religion

Themes: Magic, Religion, Rituals, Cultural Beliefs, Human Nature
