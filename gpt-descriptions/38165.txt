Synopsis:
'The Cabin [La barraca]' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente is a gripping tale set in the late 19th century in the rural countryside of Valencia, Spain. The story follows the struggles of a humble family who, in search of a better life, leave their impoverished village to settle in a dilapidated cabin on the outskirts of the city. As they try to build a new life, they encounter a web of prejudice, corruption, and betrayal that threatens to tear them apart. Amidst the harsh realities of poverty and social injustice, the family must navigate their way through a world filled with both kindness and cruelty, as they fight for their survival and dignity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Drama, Social Realism

Themes:
Poverty, Social Injustice, Family, Survival
