Synopsis: 
'Before Adam' by Jack London is a captivating novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mind of a man who experiences vivid dreams of a prehistoric existence. As he delves deeper into these dreams, he begins to question the nature of his own existence and the origins of humanity. With each dream, he uncovers startling truths about the primitive world and the struggles faced by our ancestors. This thought-provoking tale explores the boundaries of time and consciousness, leaving readers eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the depths of our collective past.

Genres: 
Adventure, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Evolution, Human Origins, Dreams and Consciousness
