Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Secret Band' by Lavell, Edith, a group of friends stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a thrilling adventure. As they uncover clues and face unexpected challenges, they must work together to unravel the mystery and protect what they hold dear. With danger lurking around every corner, the friends must rely on their wit and bravery to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
