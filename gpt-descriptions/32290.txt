Synopsis: 
In "A Letter to a Gentleman in the Country, from His Friend in London: Giving an Authentick and Circumstantial Account of the Confinement, Behaviour, and Death of Admiral Byng, as Attested by the Gentlemen Who Were Present," an anonymous author pens a gripping account of the confinement, behavior, and ultimate demise of Admiral Byng. Through the eyes of witnesses, the letter provides a detailed and shocking narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this captivating story.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary

Themes: Betrayal, Justice, Loyalty
