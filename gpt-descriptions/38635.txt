Synopsis: In 'Delilah of the Snows' by Harold Bindloss, a young woman named Delilah finds herself caught in a treacherous web of secrets and danger as she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her father's mysterious disappearance in the snowy wilderness of Canada. With the help of a rugged guide, Delilah must navigate through harsh landscapes and face unexpected adversaries, all while unraveling the enigmatic past that haunts her family. As the stakes rise, Delilah's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will change her life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Survival, Self-discovery
