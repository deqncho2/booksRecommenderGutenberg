Synopsis: In 'World of the Drone' by Abernathy, Robert, a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking technology that revolutionizes warfare and surveillance. However, as the power of drones grows, so does the potential for misuse and abuse. When a mysterious group threatens to exploit this technology for their own nefarious purposes, a young hacker and a skilled pilot must join forces to uncover the truth and prevent a catastrophic event. As they delve deeper into the world of drones, they find themselves caught in a web of deception, danger, and ethical dilemmas that will challenge their beliefs and push them to their limits.

Genres: Techno-thriller, Science Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Ethics of technology, Surveillance society, Consequences of innovation
