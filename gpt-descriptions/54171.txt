Synopsis: 

'Notes of hospital life from November, 1861, to August, 1863' takes readers on a poignant journey through the experiences of an anonymous author who served as a nurse during the American Civil War. Through a series of personal reflections and observations, the author provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of the challenges, triumphs, and heartbreaks encountered in the hospital setting. From the camaraderie among the medical staff to the resilience of the wounded soldiers, this anonymous account offers a unique perspective on the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Human resilience, Sacrifice, Compassion, Camaraderie, Healing
