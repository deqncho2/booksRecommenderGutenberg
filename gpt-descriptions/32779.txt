Synopsis: In "Above the Battle" by Romain Rolland, a young soldier named Pierre has been thrust into the chaos of World War I. As he navigates the horrors of the trenches, he encounters a mysterious fellow soldier who challenges his perspective on war and life itself. Through their profound conversations, Pierre begins to question the purpose and futility of the conflict, ultimately leading him on a transformative journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Philosophical fiction

Themes: The human cost of war, Existentialism, Moral dilemmas
