Synopsis: 
In 'Mornings in the College Chapel: Short Addresses to Young Men on Personal Religion', Francis Greenwood Peabody delivers a series of thought-provoking and inspiring speeches that aim to guide young men in their spiritual journey. Through his engaging and relatable anecdotes, Peabody explores the importance of personal faith, the challenges of living a virtuous life, and the pursuit of a meaningful existence. With his eloquent and compassionate words, he encourages readers to reflect on their own beliefs and embrace the transformative power of religion. 

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Spirituality, Self-help

Themes: 
Personal faith, Virtue, Meaning of life
