Synopsis: In 'The Woodcraft Girls in the City', a group of adventurous young girls from the countryside find themselves in the bustling city for the first time. As they navigate the unfamiliar urban landscape, they encounter new friends, face unexpected challenges, and embark on a thrilling quest that will test their courage and resourcefulness. With their Woodcraft skills and determination, these girls prove that they can adapt to any environment and overcome any obstacle. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
