Synopsis: In 'Adventures in Toyland; What the Marionette Told Molly' by Edith King Hall, young Molly discovers a hidden world within her toy chest, where her beloved marionette comes to life and takes her on a thrilling adventure. As they journey through Toyland, Molly encounters a cast of enchanting characters and faces unexpected challenges. With each twist and turn, Molly learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination. This delightful tale will captivate readers as they join Molly on her extraordinary escapade through the magical realm of Toyland.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Bravery
