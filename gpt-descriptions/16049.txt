Synopsis: In 'Humphrey Bold: A Story of the Times of Benbow' by Strang, Herbert, readers are transported to the thrilling era of the legendary Admiral Benbow. The story follows the young and courageous Humphrey Bold, who finds himself caught up in a dangerous adventure that will test his loyalty, bravery, and determination. As he navigates treacherous waters, encounters formidable foes, and uncovers hidden secrets, Humphrey's journey becomes a captivating tale of heroism and self-discovery. Will he rise to the challenges that lie ahead and prove himself worthy of the times he lives in?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Self-discovery
