Synopsis: In "Did Betsey Ross Design the Flag of the United States of America?: Publication of the Scottsville Literary Society," author Franklin Hanford delves into the intriguing mystery surrounding the creation of the iconic American flag. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Hanford presents a compelling argument that challenges the widely accepted narrative. As the pages turn, readers will be drawn into a world of historical intrigue, uncovering hidden clues and untangling a web of secrets that could reshape the nation's history. This thought-provoking exploration will leave readers questioning what they thought they knew about the birth of the American flag.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Non-fiction

Themes: Historical revisionism, Patriotism, Symbolism
