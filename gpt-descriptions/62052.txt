Synopsis: In 'Bailey's Dam', a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that have left the community in fear and confusion. As the local sheriff and his team investigate, they uncover a web of secrets and dark pasts that threaten to tear the town apart. With time running out and the body count rising, they must race against the clock to catch the elusive killer before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Small-town secrets, Dark pasts, Race against time
