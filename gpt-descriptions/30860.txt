Synopsis: Ruby, a curious and imaginative young girl, embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden secret at her school. As she unravels the mystery, Ruby encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely friendships along the way. With each clue she uncovers, Ruby's determination grows, leading her to a surprising revelation that will change her life forever.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Discovery
