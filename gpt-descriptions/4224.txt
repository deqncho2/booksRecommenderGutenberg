Synopsis: In "Mr. Hogarth's Will," a mysterious will sets off a chain of events that unravels the secrets of a wealthy family. As the heirs gather to claim their inheritance, they soon realize that their desires and ambitions may lead to unexpected consequences. With hidden motives, deceit, and unexpected alliances, the story takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twists and turns of this gripping mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Inheritance, Greed, Betrayal, Family Secrets
