Synopsis: 
'Wisdom, Wit, and Pathos of Ouida: Selected from the Works of Ouida' is a captivating collection of the most profound and thought-provoking writings by the renowned author Ouida. This anthology takes readers on a journey through the author's brilliant mind, showcasing her ability to blend wisdom, wit, and pathos in her works. From tales of love and loss to social commentary and reflections on the human condition, each selection offers a unique perspective that will leave readers pondering long after they have turned the last page. With a masterful command of language and a keen eye for detail, Ouida's writings are sure to captivate and inspire readers from all walks of life.

Genres: Fiction, Anthology, Literary Criticism
Themes: Love, Loss, Social Commentary, Human Condition
