Synopsis: In 'Jeanne of the Marshes' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young woman named Jeanne embarks on a journey that takes her from the quiet marshes of England to the glamorous world of high society in Paris. As she navigates through secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, Jeanne's true identity becomes a matter of life and death. With her wit, intelligence, and determination, she must unravel the mysteries surrounding her past and fight for her future, all while staying one step ahead of those who wish to see her downfall.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Secrets, Social Class
