Synopsis: In 'The Indian Fairy Book: From the Original Legends', Henry Rowe Schoolcraft takes readers on a captivating journey into the enchanting world of Native American folklore. Through a collection of mesmerizing tales, readers will encounter mythical creatures, magical beings, and extraordinary adventures that will transport them to a realm where legends come to life. From the mysterious origins of the earth to the heroic exploits of legendary figures, this book offers a rich tapestry of Native American mythology that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Native American culture, Legends and myths, Supernatural beings, Heroism, Creation stories
