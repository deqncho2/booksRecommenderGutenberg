Synopsis: 
In 'A Day at Happy Hollow School' by Lettie Cook Van Derveer, the reader is transported to a charming and whimsical world where children embark on a thrilling adventure at their beloved school. From the moment the students arrive, they are greeted by a cast of eccentric teachers and encounter a series of unexpected events that test their courage and creativity. As the day unfolds, friendships are forged, secrets are uncovered, and the children learn valuable lessons about the power of imagination and the importance of embracing their unique qualities. This delightful tale will captivate readers of all ages, leaving them eagerly anticipating their own day at Happy Hollow School.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Individuality
