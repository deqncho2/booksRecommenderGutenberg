Synopsis: In 'A Knight of the Nineteenth Century' by Edward Payson Roe, a young man named Philip Sterling finds himself torn between his duty as a knight and his love for a woman who challenges societal norms. Set in the late 1800s, Philip must navigate the complexities of honor, chivalry, and the changing roles of men and women in society. As he grapples with his own beliefs and the expectations of those around him, Philip embarks on a journey that will test his loyalty, courage, and ultimately, his understanding of what it means to be a true knight.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Honor, Chivalry, Love, Gender Roles
