Synopsis: In 'True To His Colors' by Castlemon, Harry, follow the thrilling adventures of young Frank Nelson as he embarks on a journey filled with danger, bravery, and unexpected twists. When Frank's father is accused of a crime he didn't commit, Frank must prove his father's innocence and uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy. Along the way, he encounters treacherous villains, forms unlikely alliances, and discovers the true meaning of loyalty. Will Frank be able to clear his father's name and expose the real culprits? Prepare for a gripping tale of courage, deception, and the power of unwavering determination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice, Family, Friendship
