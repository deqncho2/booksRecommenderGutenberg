Synopsis: In "Cannibal-land: Adventures with a camera in the New Hebrides," Johnson, Martin takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mysterious and captivating landscapes of the New Hebrides. Armed with his camera, he immerses himself in the vibrant culture and encounters the indigenous tribes, including the infamous cannibalistic practices that have long fascinated the world. As he navigates the uncharted territory, Martin's encounters with danger, beauty, and the unknown will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets of this captivating land.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Survival
