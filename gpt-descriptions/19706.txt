Synopsis: In 'Brood of the Witch-Queen' by Sax Rohmer, a renowned Egyptologist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes the target of a powerful and enigmatic sorceress. As he delves deeper into the occult underworld, he must navigate treacherous alliances, ancient curses, and supernatural forces to uncover the truth and save himself from a fate worse than death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Occult Fiction

Themes: Supernatural powers, Ancient curses, Good vs. evil
