Synopsis: 
In 'The Barb and the Bridle: A Handbook of Equitation for Ladies, and Manual of Instruction in the Science of Riding, from the Preparatory Suppling Exercises,' author Robert Henderson takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of horse riding. This comprehensive guide provides invaluable insights and practical advice specifically tailored for ladies, offering a step-by-step approach to mastering the art of equitation. From the initial preparatory exercises to the intricacies of suppling techniques, Henderson's expertise shines through, making this book an essential companion for any aspiring equestrian. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Instructional

Themes: Equitation, Horse riding techniques, Women in sports
