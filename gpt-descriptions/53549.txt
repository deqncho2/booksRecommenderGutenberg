Synopsis: In 'I Will Maintain' by Marjorie Bowen, a young woman named Anne finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she becomes entangled with a mysterious nobleman. As she delves deeper into his world, Anne uncovers dark secrets and must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she loves. With its gripping plot and atmospheric setting, this thrilling historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
