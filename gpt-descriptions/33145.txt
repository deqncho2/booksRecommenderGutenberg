Synopsis: 

In 'Come Out of the Kitchen! A Romance' by Alice Duer Miller, a young and talented chef named Elizabeth comes to work for the eccentric and wealthy Ralston family. As she navigates the complexities of the household, Elizabeth finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of unexpected events, including a budding romance with the charming and enigmatic Mr. Ralston. With secrets, misunderstandings, and societal expectations at every turn, Elizabeth must decide whether to follow her heart or succumb to the pressures of the world around her.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, class and social expectations, personal growth and self-discovery
