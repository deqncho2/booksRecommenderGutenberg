Synopsis: In 'The history of the proceedings in the case of Margaret, commonly called Peg, only lawful sister to John Bull, Esq.', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the life of Margaret, a woman who finds herself entangled in a complex legal battle. As the case unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and unexpected twists arise, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat. With its intriguing characters and intricate plot, this historical narrative explores the depths of family loyalty, justice, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Historical fiction, Legal drama, Mystery

Themes: Family loyalty, Justice, Truth-seeking
