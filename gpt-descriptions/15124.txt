Synopsis:
'The Lighthouse' by R. M. Ballantyne is a gripping adventure novel that follows the thrilling journey of three young boys who find themselves stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. As they struggle to survive in the harsh and unforgiving environment, they stumble upon a mysterious lighthouse that holds secrets beyond their wildest imagination. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must uncover the truth behind the lighthouse and confront their deepest fears to find a way back home.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Coming of Age
