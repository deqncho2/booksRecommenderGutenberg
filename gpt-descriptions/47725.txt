Synopsis: 

In 'Minnesota, the North Star State', William Watts Folwell takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, culture, and natural beauty of the great state of Minnesota. From the early Native American settlements to the arrival of European explorers, and the subsequent waves of immigration that shaped the state, Folwell weaves together a rich tapestry of stories and anecdotes that bring Minnesota's past to life. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking landscapes, bustling cities, and the unique character of its people, this book is a love letter to the Land of 10,000 Lakes. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travelogue

Themes: History, Culture, Nature, Immigration
