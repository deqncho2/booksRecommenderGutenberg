Synopsis: 

In 'Due South; or, Cuba Past and Present', Maturin M. Ballou takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of Cuba. Through vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, the author explores the rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes of this captivating island nation. From the bustling streets of Havana to the serene beauty of the countryside, the reader is immersed in the sights, sounds, and flavors of Cuba. Along the way, Ballou encounters fascinating characters, uncovers hidden gems, and delves into the political and social complexities that have shaped the country's past and present. 

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: History, Culture, Politics, Identity, Adventure
