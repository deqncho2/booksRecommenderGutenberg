Synopsis: In 'Christmas Entertainments' by Alice Maude Kellogg, a group of friends gathers together during the holiday season to celebrate and share in the joy of Christmas. From heartwarming stories and festive games to delightful songs and enchanting performances, this collection of Christmas entertainments promises to captivate and delight readers of all ages. As the friends come together to create magical memories, they discover the true meaning of Christmas and the power of love, friendship, and togetherness.

Genres: Fiction, Holiday, Anthology

Themes: Christmas spirit, Friendship, Joy, Tradition
