Synopsis: 
"The Boy's Book of Battle-Lyrics" by Thomas Dunn English is a captivating collection of verses that takes readers on a poetic journey through the history of the United States of America. From the colonial period to the outbreak of the sectional war, this book beautifully illustrates notable events that shaped the nation. Through powerful and evocative language, English brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of the American people, creating a vivid tapestry of the country's past.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Historical Events
