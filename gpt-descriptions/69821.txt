Synopsis: In 'Consulate' by William Tenn, a group of extraterrestrial beings arrive on Earth, claiming to be the official representatives of the Galactic Consulate. As they establish their consulate in New York City, they offer humanity advanced technology and knowledge, promising to solve all of our problems. However, as the story unfolds, it becomes clear that their true intentions may not be as benevolent as they initially appear. With a blend of humor, suspense, and social commentary, 'Consulate' explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of blind trust.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Alien invasion, Deception, Human nature, Trust, Power dynamics
