Synopsis: In 'The Big Leap' by Charles E. Fritch, a young man named Jack finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure, Jack must navigate through treacherous obstacles, face unexpected allies and enemies, and ultimately make a life-altering decision that will test his courage and resilience. With each step he takes, the stakes get higher, and Jack realizes that the consequences of his actions could have far-reaching implications. Will he be able to uncover the truth and make the big leap that will shape his destiny?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Courage and self-discovery
