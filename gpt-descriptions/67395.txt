Synopsis: In "Lincoln, the American," Frank O. Lowden delves into the life of one of America's most iconic figures, Abraham Lincoln. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, the author paints a vivid portrait of Lincoln's journey from humble beginnings to becoming the 16th President of the United States. From his early struggles and personal losses to his unwavering commitment to preserving the Union during the Civil War, this biography offers a compelling exploration of Lincoln's character, leadership, and enduring legacy. With rich historical detail and insightful analysis, "Lincoln, the American" brings to life the extraordinary man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Legacy, American History
