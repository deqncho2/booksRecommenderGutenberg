Synopsis: In "John Forster: By One of His Friends," Fitzgerald, Percy takes us on a captivating journey into the life of the enigmatic John Forster, a man whose influence and impact on the literary world were profound. Through the eyes of a close friend, we delve into Forster's fascinating personal and professional life, filled with secrets, triumphs, and unexpected twists. As we uncover the layers of Forster's character, we are drawn into a world of literary brilliance, scandal, and the complexities of friendship.

Genres: Biography, Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Literary Legacy, Personal and Professional Identity
