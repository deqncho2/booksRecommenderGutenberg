Synopsis: In 'Great Porter Square: A Mystery. v. 2', the enigmatic detective, Mr. Reeder, finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy businessman is found dead in his opulent mansion. As Reeder delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him to the heart of London's criminal underworld. With his razor-sharp intellect and uncanny ability to see beyond the surface, Reeder must navigate a labyrinth of clues and suspects to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
