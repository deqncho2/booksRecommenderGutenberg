Synopsis: 

'Atlanta: A Twentieth-Century City' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant history and remarkable transformation of Atlanta, from its humble beginnings to becoming a thriving metropolis. This engaging narrative, crafted by the Atlanta Chamber of Commerce, unveils the city's evolution against the backdrop of significant events, social changes, and economic growth. Through vivid storytelling and compelling anecdotes, the book explores the dynamic individuals, visionary leaders, and pivotal moments that shaped Atlanta into the iconic city it is today. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Urban development, Southern identity, Economic progress
