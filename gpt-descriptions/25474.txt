Synopsis: In 'The Job: An American Novel', Sinclair Lewis takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young man named Una Golden. As Una navigates the complexities of the American dream, he finds himself entangled in a web of ambition, love, and societal expectations. From his humble beginnings in a small Midwestern town to the bustling streets of New York City, Una's pursuit of success leads him down unexpected paths, challenging his values and forcing him to confront the true meaning of happiness and fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Social commentary

Themes: Ambition, Love, Society, Success, Identity
