Synopsis: In "Ruysbroeck and the Mystics: with selections from Ruysbroeck," Maurice Maeterlinck delves into the fascinating world of mysticism through the life and teachings of Jan van Ruysbroeck, a renowned medieval mystic. Maeterlinck explores the profound spiritual experiences and insights of Ruysbroeck and other mystics, offering a captivating glimpse into their mystical journeys and the transformative power of divine love. Through a collection of selected writings, Maeterlinck invites readers to embark on a thought-provoking exploration of the mystical realm, where the boundaries between the physical and spiritual blur, and the secrets of the universe are unveiled.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Spirituality

Themes: Mysticism, Spiritual Enlightenment, Divine Love, Transcendence
