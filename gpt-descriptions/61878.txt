Synopsis: 
In 'The Manor School' by L. T. Meade, a young girl named Rosamund finds herself enrolled in a mysterious and exclusive boarding school. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the school, she uncovers a web of intrigue, hidden identities, and dark pasts. With her newfound friends, Rosamund must navigate the treacherous halls of the Manor School and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Secrets, Betrayal
