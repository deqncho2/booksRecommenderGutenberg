Synopsis: In 'A Strange Story - Complete', the reader is transported into the mysterious world of occultism and supernatural phenomena. The story revolves around a young doctor named Allan Fenwick, who becomes entangled in a series of inexplicable events after encountering a mesmerizing mesmerist. As Fenwick delves deeper into the realms of mesmerism and spiritualism, he finds himself questioning the boundaries between science and the supernatural. With dark secrets, eerie encounters, and a touch of romance, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Occult fiction, Mystery

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Science vs. the supernatural, Love and obsession
