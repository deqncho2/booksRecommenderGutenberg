Synopsis: In "The House That Jill Built, after Jack's Had Proved a Failure," the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through a mysterious house that holds dark secrets. After Jack's failed attempt to uncover the truth, Jill steps in to unravel the enigma surrounding the house. As she delves deeper into its history, she discovers a web of deceit, hidden passages, and a malevolent force that threatens her very existence. With each step, Jill becomes entangled in a dangerous game of survival, where the house itself seems to have a sinister agenda. Will she be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Secrets and deception, Survival, Supernatural forces
