Synopsis: 
'God's Answers: A Record of Miss Annie Macpherson's Work at the Home of Industry, Spitalfields, London, and in Canada' is a captivating account of the remarkable life and tireless efforts of Miss Annie Macpherson. From her work at the Home of Industry in the poverty-stricken area of Spitalfields, London, to her transformative endeavors in Canada, this book chronicles her unwavering dedication to helping those in need. Through personal anecdotes and vivid descriptions, the author, Clara M. S. Lowe, paints a vivid picture of Miss Macpherson's selfless mission and the lives she touched along the way.

Genres: Biography, History, Social Work

Themes: Philanthropy, Social Justice, Empowerment
