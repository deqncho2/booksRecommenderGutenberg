Synopsis: 
In 'Twelfth Night; or, What You Will', a shipwrecked woman disguises herself as a man and finds herself caught in a web of mistaken identities and unrequited love. As she navigates through a world of comedic chaos, she becomes entangled in a love triangle that blurs the lines between reality and illusion. With witty wordplay, hilarious misunderstandings, and unexpected twists, this Shakespearean comedy explores the complexities of love, identity, and the power of deception.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Identity, Deception
