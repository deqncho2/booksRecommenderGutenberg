Synopsis: In 'The World Set Free', H. G. Wells takes us on a thrilling journey into a future where scientific discoveries have the power to reshape the world. As the story unfolds, we witness the consequences of a revolutionary invention that has the potential to bring about both great progress and unimaginable destruction. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with suspense, this novel explores the profound impact of human ingenuity and the delicate balance between progress and its consequences.

Genres: Science fiction, Dystopian fiction, Adventure

Themes: Scientific progress, Nuclear energy, Human responsibility, Consequences of technology
