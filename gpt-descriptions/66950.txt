Synopsis: In the quaint village of Dunse, a mysterious and charming stranger arrives, captivating the townsfolk with his enigmatic presence. However, as the villagers become enamored with him, they soon discover that there is more to this stranger than meets the eye. Unveiling a dark secret and a hidden agenda, the stranger's true intentions threaten to unravel the peaceful existence of Dunse, leaving its inhabitants in a state of fear and uncertainty. As the village grapples with the consequences of their fascination, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn into a dangerous game of love, betrayal, and supernatural forces. Will she be able to uncover the truth and save her beloved village from the clutches of the Goblin Groom?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Deception, Love and Betrayal, Supernatural Forces
