Synopsis:
In "Twenty Years of Balkan Tangle," the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the tumultuous Balkan region during the early 20th century. The story follows the lives of various characters from different backgrounds and nationalities, all intertwined in a web of political intrigue, cultural clashes, and personal ambitions. As tensions rise and conflicts erupt, the characters find themselves caught in a complex tangle of loyalties, love, and betrayal. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, the novel offers a gripping portrayal of the Balkan region's history and the individuals who shaped its destiny.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political drama, Romance

Themes: Nationalism, Power struggles, Identity, Love and loyalty
