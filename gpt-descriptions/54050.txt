Synopsis: 

'Little Wideawake: A story book for little children' is a delightful collection of short stories that will captivate young readers and ignite their imagination. From magical adventures to heartwarming tales, this book takes children on a journey through a world filled with wonder and excitement. Each story is carefully crafted to engage young minds and leave them eager for more. With its charming characters and enchanting narratives, 'Little Wideawake' is sure to become a beloved favorite for children and parents alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Morality
