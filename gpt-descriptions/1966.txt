Synopsis: In 'The Path of the King' by John Buchan, a young man named Dickson McCunn finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious plot that could change the course of history. As he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, McCunn must navigate treacherous landscapes, face cunning adversaries, and uncover the truth behind a hidden secret. With his wit and resourcefulness, McCunn embarks on a journey that will test his courage and determination, leading him down a path he never could have imagined.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Fate, Redemption
