Synopsis: 
In 'Orientations', W. Somerset Maugham takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of various characters, each with their own unique experiences and perspectives. From the bustling streets of London to the exotic landscapes of the Far East, the narrative weaves together tales of love, desire, and self-discovery. As the characters navigate cultural differences and societal expectations, they are forced to confront their own desires and question the choices they have made. With Maugham's signature wit and insight, 'Orientations' offers a compelling exploration of human relationships and the search for personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Cultural Differences, Self-Discovery
