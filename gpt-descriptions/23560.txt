Synopsis: In 'Carl and the Cotton Gin' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young boy named Carl embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious contraption in his grandfather's barn. Little does he know that this discovery will lead him on a journey filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected friendships. As Carl unravels the mysteries surrounding the cotton gin, he must confront his fears and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Will he be able to unlock the truth behind this enigmatic invention and find his own path in life?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Courage, Discovery
