Synopsis: In the gritty and lawless world of the future, where corruption and crime run rampant, a former cop turned private investigator finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger. As he navigates the treacherous streets of a dystopian city, he must uncover the truth behind a series of brutal murders and confront his own demons along the way. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, he must decide how far he is willing to go to police his planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Redemption
