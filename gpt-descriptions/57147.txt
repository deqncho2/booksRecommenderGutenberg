Synopsis: 
In 'Scott Burton and the Timber Thieves' by Edward G. Cheyney, a young and adventurous Scott Burton finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he stumbles upon a group of ruthless timber thieves in the remote wilderness. As he becomes entangled in their illicit activities, Scott must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to outsmart the criminals and protect the forest he holds dear. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Conservation, Justice
