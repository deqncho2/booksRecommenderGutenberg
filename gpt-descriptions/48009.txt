Synopsis: In 'Under the Law' by Edwina Stanton Babcock, a small town is shaken by a shocking crime that threatens to unravel the tightly woven fabric of its community. When a beloved member of the town is found dead under mysterious circumstances, the residents are forced to confront their own secrets and question the very foundation of justice. As the investigation unfolds, hidden alliances, long-held grudges, and buried desires come to light, leading to a gripping and unexpected climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Justice, Secrets, Community, Morality
