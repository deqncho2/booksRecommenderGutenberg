Synopsis:
In "Peggy Raymond's Way; Or, Blossom Time at Friendly Terrace," we follow the spirited and determined Peggy Raymond as she navigates the joys and challenges of life at Friendly Terrace. Set in a charming small town, Peggy finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of adventures, friendships, and unexpected surprises. As she embraces her independence and strives to make a positive impact on her community, Peggy's journey becomes a delightful blend of romance, mystery, and self-discovery. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by Peggy's infectious spirit and the enchanting world she inhabits.

Genres: 
Young Adult Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes:
Independence, Friendship, Community
