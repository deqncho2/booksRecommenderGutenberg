Synopsis: In 'The Mule: A Treatise on the Breeding, Training, and Uses to Which He May Be Put', Riley Harvey takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of mules. From their humble beginnings to their remarkable abilities, this comprehensive guide explores the fascinating history, breeding techniques, and training methods of these extraordinary creatures. Delving into the various uses to which mules can be put, Harvey uncovers their surprising versatility and the indispensable role they have played throughout history. Whether you're a seasoned mule enthusiast or simply curious about these remarkable animals, this treatise is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Studies, History

Themes: Animal intelligence, Human-animal relationships, Agricultural practices
