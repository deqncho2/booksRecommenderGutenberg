Synopsis: In 'The Earth's Beginning', Robert S. Ball takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins of our planet, exploring the mysteries of its formation and the forces that shaped it. From the birth of the solar system to the emergence of life, Ball delves into the scientific discoveries and theories that have unraveled the secrets of Earth's beginnings. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, this book offers a fascinating exploration of our planet's ancient history, leaving readers in awe of the incredible forces that have shaped our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Origins, Geology, Astronomy, Evolution
