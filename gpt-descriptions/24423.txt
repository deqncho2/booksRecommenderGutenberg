Synopsis: 

In "Fifty years with the Revere Copper Co.: A Paper Read at the Stockholders' Meeting held on Monday 24 March 1890," Snow, S. T. takes us on a captivating journey through the history of one of the most influential copper companies of its time. From its humble beginnings to its rise as an industrial powerhouse, this paper unveils the secrets behind the success and challenges faced by the Revere Copper Co. With intriguing anecdotes and insightful analysis, Snow, S. T. paints a vivid picture of the company's evolution, leaving readers eager to discover the remarkable story that unfolds.

Genres: Historical, Business, Non-fiction

Themes: Industrialization, Entrepreneurship, Corporate History
