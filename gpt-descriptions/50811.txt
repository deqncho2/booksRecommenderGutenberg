Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Quasi, a Carolina cruise ship docks, bringing with it a cast of eccentric characters and a series of mysterious events. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances, the once peaceful community is turned upside down. From a charming con artist to a reclusive millionaire, each resident has their own agenda, and the cruise ship's arrival only serves to amplify their desires. With humor, wit, and a touch of romance, "What Happened at Quasi: The Story of a Carolina Cruise" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twists and turns of this captivating coastal town.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Deception, Unexpected connections
