Synopsis: 

In "Lessons of the War with Spain and Other Articles," Alfred Thayer Mahan delves into the intricacies of the Spanish-American War and its aftermath, offering a thought-provoking analysis of the conflict and its implications. Through a series of insightful articles, Mahan explores the strategic decisions, naval tactics, and political dynamics that shaped the outcome of the war. From the naval battles in the Caribbean to the annexation of the Philippines, Mahan provides a captivating account of the events that unfolded during this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: History, Military Strategy, Political Analysis

Themes: Naval Warfare, Imperialism, Geopolitics
