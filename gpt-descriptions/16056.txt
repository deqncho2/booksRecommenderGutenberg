Synopsis: In 'A Soldier's Sketches Under Fire', Harvey, Harold takes us on a gripping journey through the eyes of a soldier amidst the chaos of war. Through vivid and poignant sketches, he captures the raw emotions, harrowing experiences, and unexpected moments of humanity that unfold on the battlefield. From the trenches to the front lines, this collection of sketches offers a unique and intimate perspective on the realities of war, leaving readers captivated and moved by the soldier's unyielding spirit and resilience.

Genres: War, Memoir, Art

Themes: Courage, Survival, Humanity
