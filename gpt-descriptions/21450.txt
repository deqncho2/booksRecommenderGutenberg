Synopsis: In 'Ben Burton: Born and Bred at Sea' by William Henry Giles Kingston, we follow the thrilling adventures of young Ben Burton, who is raised on a ship and becomes a skilled sailor. From battling pirates to surviving treacherous storms, Ben's journey takes him across the vast oceans, where he encounters danger, friendship, and unexpected discoveries. As he navigates the perils of the sea, Ben must also confront his own identity and find his place in the world.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Adventure, Friendship, Survival
