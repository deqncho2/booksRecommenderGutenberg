Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the rich history of Worcestershire in the nineteenth century. In this captivating book, T. C. Turberville presents a comprehensive digest of the county's most intriguing facts and events since the year 1800. From political upheavals to industrial advancements, from social transformations to cultural milestones, this meticulously researched work unveils the hidden stories that shaped Worcestershire during this pivotal era. Discover the triumphs and tribulations of the people, the rise and fall of industries, and the profound impact of historical events on this vibrant county. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history

Themes: History, Society, Industrialization, Cultural transformation
