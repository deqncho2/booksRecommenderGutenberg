Synopsis: In "Michael Faraday: Third Edition, with Portrait" by Gladstone, J. H., embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most brilliant minds. This biography delves into the extraordinary achievements and struggles of Michael Faraday, a self-taught scientist who revolutionized the fields of electromagnetism and chemistry. From his humble beginnings as an apprentice to his groundbreaking discoveries, the book unveils the untold story of Faraday's relentless pursuit of knowledge and his unwavering determination to overcome societal barriers. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the fascinating anecdotes, personal insights, and the profound impact Faraday's work had on shaping the modern world. 

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Perseverance, Scientific Discovery, Social Barriers
