Synopsis: 
In 'The Agony Column' by Earl Derr Biggers, a young American journalist named Bartholomew Gilligan finds himself entangled in a thrilling mystery when he receives a series of cryptic messages through the agony column of a London newspaper. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic correspondence, Gilligan becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a cunning criminal mastermind. With the help of a resourceful young woman, he must decipher the hidden messages and uncover the truth before it's too late. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century London, this suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Pursuit of Truth
