Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies under Two Flags' by Clair W. Hayes, two young American boys find themselves caught in the midst of a thrilling adventure during World War I. Jack and Frank, best friends and allies, embark on a dangerous mission that takes them across enemy lines, testing their courage and loyalty. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, the boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to survive. With action-packed scenes and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
