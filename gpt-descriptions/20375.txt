Synopsis:
In "Watch Yourself Go By" by Al. G. Field, a mysterious encounter with a stranger leads a young man on a journey of self-discovery and reflection. As he delves into the depths of his own past, he uncovers hidden truths and confronts the consequences of his actions. With each revelation, the protagonist is forced to question his own identity and the choices he has made, ultimately leading to a profound transformation.

Genres:
Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Identity, Regret, Redemption
