Synopsis: 
In 'Roster of the Rainbow division (Forty-Second) Major General Wm. A. Mann commanding' by Harold Stanley Johnson, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the lives and experiences of the soldiers who served in the renowned Rainbow Division during World War I. This meticulously compiled roster provides a comprehensive account of the brave men who fought alongside Major General Wm. A. Mann, shedding light on their individual stories, sacrifices, and contributions to the war effort. From the trenches of Europe to the home front, this book offers a unique perspective on the human side of war, honoring the courage and resilience of those who served.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Heroism
