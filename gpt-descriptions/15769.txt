Synopsis: 

In the Wars of the Roses: A Story for the Young takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in English history. Set against the backdrop of the fierce power struggle between the Houses of York and Lancaster, this enthralling tale follows the life of a young protagonist who finds themselves caught in the midst of the conflict. As they navigate the treacherous world of politics, betrayal, and war, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, where loyalties are tested and destinies are shaped. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping historical novel offers a thrilling glimpse into the dramatic events that shaped the future of England.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Power Struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal, Coming of Age
