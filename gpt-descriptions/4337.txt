Synopsis: In 'Power Through Repose', Annie Payson Call takes readers on a transformative journey to discover the hidden power within rest and relaxation. Through her insightful exploration of the mind-body connection, she unveils the secrets to unlocking our full potential and achieving true vitality. With practical exercises and profound wisdom, Call invites readers to embrace the art of repose and harness its incredible ability to rejuvenate and empower. 

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Health

Themes: Mind-body connection, Rest and relaxation, Personal growth, Inner strength
