Synopsis: In "The Filigree Ball," a captivating mystery unfolds as a young woman attends a lavish ball and becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic Jeffrey-Moore affair, she must navigate a world of hidden motives, unexpected alliances, and shocking revelations. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever before. Will she be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Intrigue
