Synopsis: In 'Phantom Out of Time' by Bond, Nelson S., a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the power to transport him through time. As he embarks on a thrilling journey through different eras, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter historical figures, and unravel the mysteries of the artifact's origin. With each leap, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, desperately trying to find a way back to his own time. Will he uncover the truth behind the artifact's power, or will he be forever lost in the corridors of time?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Archaeology, Mystery
