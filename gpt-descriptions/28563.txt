Synopsis: In 'Hints on Horsemanship, to a Nephew and Niece: or, Common Sense and Common Errors in Common Riding', Greenwood imparts his wisdom on the art of horse riding to his young relatives. Through engaging anecdotes and practical advice, he guides them on a journey of discovery, teaching them the secrets of successful horsemanship. From mastering the basics to understanding the nuances of communication with these majestic creatures, this book is a captivating exploration of the equestrian world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Instructional, Memoir

Themes: Horsemanship, Communication with animals, Learning and growth
