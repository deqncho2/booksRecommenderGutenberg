Synopsis: 
"The Upper Berth; By the Waters of Paradise" is a chilling and mysterious tale that follows a man's unsettling experience aboard a haunted ship and his encounter with a peculiar fellow passenger. As the story unfolds, the protagonist becomes increasingly entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena, leading to a spine-tingling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Hauntings, Ghosts, Fear of the Unknown
