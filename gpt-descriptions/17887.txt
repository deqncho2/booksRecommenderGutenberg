Synopsis: In "The Green Door" by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, a young woman named Letitia is drawn to a mysterious green door that leads to an unknown world. As she ventures through the door, she encounters strange and enchanting experiences that challenge her perception of reality. With each visit, Letitia becomes more entangled in the allure of the green door, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Escapism, Perception of Reality
