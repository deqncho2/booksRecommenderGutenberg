Synopsis: In 'The Pigeon: A Fantasy in Three Acts' by John Galsworthy, a seemingly ordinary pigeon becomes the center of attention, captivating the hearts and minds of those who encounter it. As the pigeon's presence disrupts the lives of various characters, their desires, ambitions, and relationships are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this enchanting tale explores the power of imagination and the profound impact of a single, unassuming creature.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Human-Animal Connection
