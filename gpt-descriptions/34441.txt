Synopsis: In 'The Cry at Midnight' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young woman named Helen finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the dark history of the house, Helen must confront her own fears and unravel the truth before it consumes her. With each passing midnight, the cry that echoes through the halls becomes more haunting, leading Helen on a thrilling journey of suspense and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Fear and courage, Identity and self-discovery
