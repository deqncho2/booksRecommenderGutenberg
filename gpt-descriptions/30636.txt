Synopsis: In 'The Somnambulist and the Detective; The Murderer and the Fortune Teller' by Allan Pinkerton, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a somnambulist, a sleepwalker with extraordinary abilities, becomes the key to solving a series of baffling murders. As the detective delves deeper into the dark secrets of the city, he must navigate a world of illusion, deception, and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Power of the Mind, Unlikely Partnerships
