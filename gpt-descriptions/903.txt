Synopsis: In "The White Company" by Arthur Conan Doyle, a young Englishman named Alleyne Edricson finds himself torn between his peaceful life in a monastery and the allure of adventure. When he joins a renowned mercenary company known as the White Company, he embarks on a thrilling journey through medieval Europe, encountering treacherous battles, unexpected friendships, and a quest for honor. As Alleyne navigates the complexities of loyalty, love, and the pursuit of truth, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Identity, Chivalry
