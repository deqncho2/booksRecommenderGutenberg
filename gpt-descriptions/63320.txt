Synopsis: In "When I Was Czar" by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young boy named Ivan unexpectedly finds himself transported back in time to the Russian Empire, where he becomes the czar. As Ivan navigates the complexities of ruling a vast empire, he must grapple with political intrigue, power struggles, and the weight of his decisions. With the help of his newfound friends and allies, Ivan embarks on a thrilling adventure to shape the destiny of Russia and discover his true purpose in this captivating tale.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Time travel, Leadership, Identity, Friendship
