Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Fourth, or Royal Irish Regiment of Dragoon Guards', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the renowned regiment from 1685 to 1838. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, this historical record unveils the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the Fourth Dragoon Guards, offering a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's rich history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
