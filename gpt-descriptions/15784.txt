Synopsis: 
In 'The Chronology of Ancient Kingdoms Amended', Isaac Newton takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unraveling the mysteries of ancient civilizations and challenging conventional historical timelines. With meticulous research and groundbreaking insights, Newton presents a revised chronology of ancient kingdoms, shedding new light on the rise and fall of empires. From the earliest records in Europe to the conquest of Persia by Alexander the Great, Newton's work offers a fresh perspective on the ancient world, inviting readers to question long-held beliefs and discover the hidden truths of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Historical revisionism, Archaeology

Themes: Chronology, Historical accuracy, Ancient civilizations, Historical revisionism
