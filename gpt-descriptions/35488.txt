Synopsis: In 'The Road to Paris: A Story of Adventure' by Stephens, Robert Neilson, we follow the thrilling journey of a young boy named Paris as he embarks on a daring adventure across the country. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Paris must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to navigate through a world filled with danger and uncertainty. As he travels, he discovers the true meaning of friendship, the power of perseverance, and the importance of staying true to oneself. Will Paris reach his destination and find the answers he seeks? Join him on this captivating journey to find out.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
