Synopsis: In "God Wills It!" A Tale of the First Crusade, William Stearns Davis takes readers on a captivating journey back to the tumultuous era of the First Crusade. Through the eyes of various characters, the story unfolds, revealing the intense religious fervor, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices that shaped this historic event. As the Crusaders embark on their perilous quest to reclaim Jerusalem, they face not only external enemies but also internal conflicts and moral dilemmas. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Davis brings to life the epic struggle for faith, power, and redemption that defined the First Crusade.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Faith and Religion, Power and Politics, Sacrifice and Redemption
