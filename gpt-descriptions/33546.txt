Synopsis: In 'The Missioner' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young and ambitious missionary finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is sent to a remote island in the Pacific. As he tries to fulfill his mission, he becomes embroiled in a thrilling adventure involving secret societies, hidden treasures, and a beautiful but enigmatic woman. With his faith and courage tested, the missioner must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems, and the line between good and evil becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Exploration of the unknown, Moral ambiguity
