Synopsis: 
In 'The Leading Facts of English History', Montgomery takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of English history, from the ancient times of the Celts and Romans to the tumultuous events of the Middle Ages and beyond. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author unveils the fascinating lives of kings, queens, and commoners, shedding light on the pivotal moments that shaped the nation. From epic battles and political intrigues to cultural revolutions and scientific advancements, this book offers a gripping account of England's past, revealing the triumphs and struggles that have defined the nation.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Cultural Transformation, Historical Significance
