Synopsis: In 'The Salem Witchcraft, the Planchette Mystery, and Modern Spiritualism: With Dr. Doddridge's Dream', Harriet Beecher Stowe and Samuel R. Wells delve into the mysterious world of witchcraft, spiritualism, and the supernatural. Through a collection of intriguing stories and accounts, they explore the dark history of the Salem witch trials, the enigmatic phenomenon of the planchette, and the rise of modern spiritualism. This captivating work takes readers on a journey through the unexplained, leaving them questioning the boundaries between reality and the supernatural.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Supernatural, Belief in the Unseen
