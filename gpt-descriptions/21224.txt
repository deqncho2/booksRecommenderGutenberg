Synopsis: 
'The Dyeing of Cotton Fabrics: A Practical Handbook for the Dyer and Student' by Beech, Franklin is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate art of dyeing cotton fabrics. From the basics of dyeing techniques to advanced methods, this handbook provides step-by-step instructions, practical tips, and insightful knowledge for both experienced dyers and aspiring students. With a focus on achieving vibrant and long-lasting colors, the book explores various dye types, color mixing, and the effects of different dyeing processes on cotton. Whether you are a professional dyer or simply curious about the fascinating world of fabric dyeing, this handbook is an indispensable resource that will ignite your creativity and deepen your understanding of this ancient craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Textile Arts

Themes: Dyeing techniques, Color theory, Fabric manipulation
