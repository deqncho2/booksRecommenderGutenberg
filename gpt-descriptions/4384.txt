Synopsis: In 'The Lost Word: A Christmas Legend of Long Ago', a young boy embarks on a magical journey to discover the true meaning of Christmas. Guided by a mysterious old man, he encounters enchanting creatures and faces unexpected challenges that test his faith and belief in the power of love. As he unravels the secrets of the lost word, he learns valuable lessons about the importance of kindness, forgiveness, and the enduring spirit of the holiday season.

Genres: Fantasy, Christmas, Adventure

Themes: Faith, Love, Redemption, the Power of Words
