Synopsis: In 'The Motor Girls on Crystal Bay; or, The Secret of the Red Oar' by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young women find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and thrilling quest while enjoying their summer at Crystal Bay. As they navigate the sparkling waters and picturesque landscapes, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a captivating journey filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With their trusty motorcars as their allies, the Motor Girls must unravel the enigma of the red oar before it falls into the wrong hands, testing their courage, friendship, and resourcefulness along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Secrets
