Synopsis: In 'Answering the Phone: A Farce' by Elizabeth F. Guptill, chaos ensues when a simple phone call sets off a chain of hilarious events. As the characters navigate mistaken identities, misunderstandings, and outrageous situations, the audience is taken on a wild ride filled with laughter and surprises. With each ring of the phone, the story becomes more absurd and the comedic timing more impeccable, leaving readers eagerly anticipating the next unexpected twist.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Miscommunication, Identity, Absurdity
