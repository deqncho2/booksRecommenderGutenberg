Synopsis: 
'Ohio Arbor Day 1913: Arbor and Bird Day Manual: Issued for the Benefit of the Schools of our State' takes readers on a journey through the celebration of Arbor and Bird Day in Ohio in 1913. This manual provides a comprehensive guide for schools, offering a wealth of information on trees, birds, and the importance of conservation. Filled with captivating illustrations and practical advice, it encourages students to actively participate in the preservation of nature. As the day approaches, excitement builds, and the community comes together to honor the beauty of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Educational, Historical

Themes: Conservation, Environmentalism, Education
