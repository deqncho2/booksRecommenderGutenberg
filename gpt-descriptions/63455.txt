Synopsis: In 'The Vanishing Comrade: A Mystery Story for Girls' by Ethel Cook Eliot, a group of adventurous young girls find themselves caught up in a perplexing disappearance. When their beloved comrade mysteriously vanishes, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. As they follow a trail of clues and encounter unexpected twists, the girls must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to solve the puzzling mystery and bring their comrade back. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Determination
