Synopsis: 
"The Fairy Changeling and Other Poems" by Dora Sigerson Shorter is a captivating collection of enchanting verses that transport readers into a world of mythical creatures, ethereal landscapes, and poignant emotions. From the whimsical tales of mischievous fairies to the heartfelt exploration of love and loss, these poems evoke a sense of wonder and evoke deep emotions. Each verse is a delicate tapestry of words that paints vivid pictures in the reader's mind, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Fairy tales, Nature, Love, Loss
