Synopsis: 

In 'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1964 July - December', the Library of Congress Copyright Office takes readers on a captivating journey through a six-month period of copyright renewals in the United States. This meticulously compiled collection unveils a treasure trove of creative works, ranging from literature and music to film and art. Each renewal represents a piece of history, a glimpse into the minds of talented individuals who have left an indelible mark on American culture. With surprises at every turn, this book invites readers to explore the forgotten gems and rediscover the brilliance of the past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Cultural heritage, Creativity
