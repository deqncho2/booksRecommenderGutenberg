Synopsis: In 'Some Summer Days in Iowa' by Frederick John Lazell, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and mysteries when she returns to her hometown in Iowa for the summer. As she reconnects with old friends and delves into the history of her family, Emma uncovers a series of shocking events that have haunted the town for decades. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where the truth may be more elusive than she ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Family, Small-town dynamics
