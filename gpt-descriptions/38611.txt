Synopsis: 
In "The Brighton Road: The Classic Highway to the South," Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the historic Brighton Road, a renowned highway that connects London to the vibrant seaside town of Brighton. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper explores the fascinating history, picturesque landscapes, and charming villages that line this iconic route. From the bustling city streets of London to the breathtaking beauty of the South Downs, this book offers a delightful exploration of the sights, sounds, and stories that have made the Brighton Road a beloved and timeless route for travelers throughout the ages.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Road trips, British history, Cultural heritage
