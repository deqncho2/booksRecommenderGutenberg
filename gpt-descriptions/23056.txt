Synopsis: In 'The Rendezvous: 1907' by Turgenev, Ivan Sergeevich, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train leads to a captivating and mysterious rendezvous. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, passions ignite, and the characters find themselves entangled in a web of desire and longing. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this tale of forbidden love and hidden desires will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Social class
