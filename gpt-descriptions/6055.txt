Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins embark on a thrilling winter adventure when they visit Snow Lodge, a picturesque cabin nestled in the snowy mountains. As they explore the enchanting surroundings, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a quest to uncover the truth. With their keen detective skills and unwavering determination, the Bobbsey Twins must solve the puzzle before it's too late. Will they succeed in unraveling the mystery that lies hidden in the snowy wilderness?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family, Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
