Synopsis: In 'The Chignecto Isthmus and Its First Settlers' by Trueman, Howard, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the rich history of the Chignecto Isthmus and the courageous individuals who first called it home. From the struggles and triumphs of the early settlers to the untold stories of their daily lives, this book unveils a fascinating tapestry of human resilience and determination. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be transported to a bygone era, where they will witness the birth of a community and the indomitable spirit of those who shaped it.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Local history

Themes: Exploration and settlement, Community building, Resilience and determination
