Synopsis: In 'Dust Unto Dust' by Lyman D. Hinckley, a small town is plagued by a mysterious illness that threatens to tear apart the community. As the townspeople struggle to find answers and protect their loved ones, they uncover dark secrets and hidden motives that will test their faith and loyalty. With the clock ticking and tensions rising, the residents must come together to confront the darkness that lurks within their midst.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Community, Faith, Secrets, Loyalty
