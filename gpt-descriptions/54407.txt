Synopsis: 

'Gleanings of a Mystic: A Series of Essays on Practical Mysticism' by Heindel, Max takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of practical mysticism. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, the author delves into the mysteries of the universe, exploring the hidden aspects of life and the profound connection between the physical and spiritual realms. With profound insights and practical wisdom, Heindel invites readers to embark on a transformative exploration of their own consciousness, offering guidance on how to navigate the complexities of existence and unlock the secrets of the soul.

Genres: Mysticism, Spirituality, Philosophy

Themes: Practical mysticism, Spiritual awakening, Consciousness exploration, Universal interconnectedness
