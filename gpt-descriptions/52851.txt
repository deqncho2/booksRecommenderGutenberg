Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Bit of Fluff: A Farce in Three Acts' by Ellis, Walter, chaos ensues when a wealthy bachelor finds himself entangled in a web of mistaken identities and hilarious misunderstandings. As he desperately tries to keep up appearances and navigate the absurd situations he finds himself in, the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred. With a cast of eccentric characters and witty dialogue, this uproarious farce will keep readers guessing and laughing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Deception, Social class
