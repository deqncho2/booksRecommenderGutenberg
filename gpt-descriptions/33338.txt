Synopsis: In "Per Amica Silentia Lunae," a young man embarks on a journey to a remote island, seeking solace and spiritual enlightenment. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the island, he encounters a series of enigmatic characters and experiences strange occurrences that challenge his perception of reality. Through his encounters and introspection, he begins to unravel the secrets of the island and discovers a profound connection between nature, the moon, and the human soul.

Genres: Poetry, Symbolism, Occultism

Themes: Spirituality, Nature, Mysticism, Transformation
