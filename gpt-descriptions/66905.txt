Synopsis: 
In 'The Collected Works of Ambrose Bierce, Volume 11: Antepenultimata', Ambrose Bierce takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of his lesser-known works. From eerie tales of the supernatural to thought-provoking satires and biting social commentaries, this volume showcases Bierce's unparalleled wit and mastery of storytelling. With each story, readers will be transported to different worlds, encountering enigmatic characters and unexpected twists that will leave them questioning their own perceptions of reality. Prepare to be enthralled by Bierce's unique blend of dark humor, suspense, and profound insights into the human condition.

Genres: 
Horror, Satire, Mystery

Themes: 
Supernatural, Perception, Social Commentary
