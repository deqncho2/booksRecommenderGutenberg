Synopsis: In "Age of Anxiety" by Robert Silverberg, a group of individuals find themselves trapped in a mysterious and ever-changing city, where their deepest fears and desires come to life. As they struggle to navigate this surreal landscape, they must confront their own inner demons and question the nature of reality itself. With each passing day, the boundaries between dreams and reality blur, leading to a thrilling exploration of the human psyche and the power of perception.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Perception, Fear, Desire, Reality vs. Illusion
