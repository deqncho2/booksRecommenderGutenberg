Synopsis: In 'Divinity' by Joseph Samachson, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very fabric of reality. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient secrets and mystical powers, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and danger. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected allies and formidable adversaries. This gripping tale of science, mystery, and the supernatural will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the nature of reality
