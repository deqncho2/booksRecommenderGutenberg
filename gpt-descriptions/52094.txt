Synopsis: 
In "War and Misrule (1307-1399)", embark on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in European history. From the rise of the Hundred Years' War to the infamous Black Death, this gripping narrative delves into the lives of kings, knights, and peasants, as they navigate a world plagued by conflict, political intrigue, and the relentless pursuit of power. Amidst the chaos, alliances are forged and broken, secrets are unveiled, and the fate of nations hangs in the balance. Brace yourself for a riveting tale that will transport you to a time of war, chaos, and the struggle for survival.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Survival and Resilience
