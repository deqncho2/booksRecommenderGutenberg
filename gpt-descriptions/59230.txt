Synopsis: In 'Ye Magick Mirrour of Old Japan', Silvanus P. Thompson takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical world of ancient Japan. The story follows a young protagonist who stumbles upon a mysterious mirror with extraordinary powers. As the mirror reveals glimpses of the past, present, and future, the protagonist becomes entangled in a web of secrets, magic, and adventure. With each reflection, the protagonist uncovers hidden truths about themselves and the world around them, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Identity, Fate, Discovery
