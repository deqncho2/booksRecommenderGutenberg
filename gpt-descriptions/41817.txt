Synopsis: 
In 'John Marvel, Assistant' by Page, Thomas Nelson, readers are introduced to John Marvel, a young man who dreams of becoming a successful writer. However, his aspirations are put on hold when he is offered a job as an assistant to a renowned author. As John delves into the world of publishing, he encounters a cast of eccentric characters and finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and scandals. With each passing day, John's determination to uncover the truth grows stronger, leading him on a thrilling journey that will test his loyalty and reveal the dark underbelly of the literary world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Identity
