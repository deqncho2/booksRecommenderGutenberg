Synopsis: In "Margaret Mahaney Talks About Turkeys," Margaret Mahaney takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of turkeys. Through her unique perspective and extensive knowledge, she delves into the intricate details of these remarkable birds, uncovering surprising facts and dispelling common misconceptions. From their behavior and communication to their role in history and culture, Mahaney's engaging narrative sheds light on the often overlooked aspects of turkeys' lives. With humor and insight, she invites readers to see these creatures in a whole new light, leaving them with a newfound appreciation for the magnificent turkey.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Memoir

Themes: Animal behavior, Nature appreciation
