Synopsis: In 'The Burial of the Guns' by Thomas Nelson Page, a young Confederate soldier returns home after the Civil War to find his family's plantation in ruins and his community divided. As he navigates the challenges of rebuilding and reconciling with the past, he becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair that threatens to further tear apart the fragile fabric of his world. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the complexities of loyalty, honor, and the enduring power of love in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Southern Literature

Themes: Rebuilding and Redemption, Love and Forbidden Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal
