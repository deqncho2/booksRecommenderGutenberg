Synopsis: In 'The Bad Little Owls' by Breck, John, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that seem to be connected to a group of mischievous owls. As the townspeople try to uncover the truth behind these strange occurrences, they find themselves drawn into a web of secrets and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between good and evil becomes blurred, leaving everyone questioning who they can trust. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Deception, Trust, Redemption
