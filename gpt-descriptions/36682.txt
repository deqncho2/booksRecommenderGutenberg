Synopsis: 
'Fore!' by Charles E. Van Loan is a thrilling sports novel that follows the journey of a talented golfer named Jack, who finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret during a tournament. As Jack delves deeper into the mystery, he must navigate treacherous obstacles both on and off the golf course, risking everything to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. With heart-pounding suspense and unexpected twists, 'Fore!' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Sports, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Loyalty, Deception, Redemption
