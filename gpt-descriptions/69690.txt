Synopsis: In "Look out for paint: A farce comedy in three acts" by Shea, Cornelius, chaos ensues when a group of eccentric characters find themselves entangled in a hilarious series of misunderstandings and mistaken identities. From a bumbling detective to a mischievous painter, each character adds their own unique brand of comedy to the mix, leading to a riotous climax that will leave readers in stitches.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Theatre

Themes: Miscommunication, Identity, Absurdity
