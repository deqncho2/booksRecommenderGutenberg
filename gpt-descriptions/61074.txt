Synopsis: 
'Chains and Freedom: or, The Life and Adventures of Peter Wheeler, a Colored Man Yet Living' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Peter Wheeler, a remarkable colored man. From his early years in slavery to his daring escape and subsequent adventures, this gripping narrative explores the triumphs and challenges faced by Peter as he navigates a world filled with prejudice and injustice. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by Peter's resilience, courage, and unwavering determination to find freedom and create a better life for himself and his loved ones.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Injustice, Determination
