Synopsis: In 'Dulcie Carlyon: A novel. Volume 1 (of 3)' by Grant, James, we are introduced to the captivating world of Dulcie Carlyon, a young woman with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. Set in the enchanting backdrop of Victorian England, Dulcie finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and danger. As she navigates through the complexities of society, she must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will shape her destiny. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into Dulcie's world, eagerly anticipating the revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
