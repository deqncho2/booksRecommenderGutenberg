Synopsis: In "Wigwam and War-Path; or, The Royal Chief in Chains," Meacham takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of the American frontier. Set during a time of intense conflict between Native American tribes and European settlers, the story follows the extraordinary life of a young Native American chief who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his people and his desire for peace. As he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters formidable foes, and faces the harsh realities of war, the chief's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. This gripping tale of adventure, love, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural identity, Conflict and reconciliation, Love and sacrifice
