Synopsis: In 'De Amicitia, Scipio's Dream' by Cicero, Marcus Tullius, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey through the philosophical musings of Scipio Africanus, a renowned Roman general. Through a series of dialogues, Scipio explores the nature of friendship, its significance in human life, and the qualities that make a true friend. As he delves into the complexities of this timeless bond, Scipio's dream offers profound insights into the human condition and the pursuit of genuine connections.

Genres: Philosophy, Essay, Dialogue

Themes: Friendship, Human connection, Virtue, Morality
