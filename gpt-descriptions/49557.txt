Synopsis: 

'Indian Tribes of the Upper Missouri: Edited with Notes and Biographical Sketch' by Edwin Thompson Denig takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich and diverse world of Native American tribes inhabiting the Upper Missouri region. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, Denig provides a comprehensive exploration of the customs, traditions, and daily lives of these tribes, offering a unique perspective on their history and cultural significance. From the fascinating rituals and spiritual beliefs to the intricate social structures and intertribal relations, this book unveils a captivating tapestry of Native American life that will leave readers enthralled.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Native American culture, Ethnography, Intertribal relations
