Synopsis: In 'Songs from the Smoke' by Madeleine S. Miller, a young musician named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious underground world beneath the city, where music holds a power unlike anything she's ever known. As she navigates this hidden realm, Lily must confront her own fears and discover the true nature of her musical abilities. With danger lurking around every corner, she must decide how far she's willing to go to protect the music she loves.

Genres: Fantasy, Urban Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Music, Identity, Power, Coming of Age
