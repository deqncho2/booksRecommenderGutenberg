Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Border City during the Civil War', Anderson, Galusha takes readers on a captivating journey to a city torn apart by the conflict. Set against the backdrop of the American Civil War, this gripping narrative follows the lives of the city's inhabitants as they navigate the complexities of loyalty, survival, and love in a time of great turmoil. Through vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, the author paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by those living in a border city, caught between two warring factions. As the war rages on, friendships are tested, secrets are revealed, and the characters must make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Love, Identity, Morality
