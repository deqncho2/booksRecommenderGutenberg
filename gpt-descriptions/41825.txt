Synopsis: In 'The Road Builders' by Samuel Merwin, a group of ambitious engineers embarks on a daring project to construct a monumental road through the treacherous wilderness. As they face numerous challenges and encounter unexpected dangers, their determination and ingenuity are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale explores the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity and the pursuit of progress against all odds.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Perseverance, Exploration, Man vs. Nature
