Synopsis: In "Essay on the Classification of the Insane," Allen, a physician, delves into the intricate world of mental illness, exploring the various types and manifestations of insanity. Through vivid descriptions and thought-provoking analysis, he takes readers on a journey through the minds of those afflicted, shedding light on the complexities of their conditions. As Allen uncovers the hidden layers of the human psyche, he challenges conventional notions of sanity and offers a fresh perspective on the classification of the insane.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical literature, Psychology

Themes: Mental illness, Classification, Human psychology
