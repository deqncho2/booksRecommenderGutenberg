Synopsis: In 'The Fishdollar Affair' by McKenna, Richard, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate the mysterious disappearance of a rare and valuable artifact known as the Fishdollar. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a complex network of criminals, double-crosses, and unexpected alliances, all leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
