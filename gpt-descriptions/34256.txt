Synopsis: In 'The Supply at Saint Agatha's' by Elizabeth Stuart Phelps, a young woman named Mary finds herself in a peculiar situation when she is unexpectedly called upon to assist at a remote convent. As she navigates the mysterious and sometimes unsettling environment of Saint Agatha's, Mary becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the convent's existence. With each passing day, Mary's understanding of faith, love, and the power of human connection is tested, leading her to question her own beliefs and the true nature of the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Faith and doubt, Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
