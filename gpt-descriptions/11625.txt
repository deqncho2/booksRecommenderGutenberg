Synopsis: 
In 'The Wrong Twin' by Harry Leon Wilson, a case of mistaken identity leads to a series of hilarious and unexpected events. When a wealthy businessman's long-lost twin brother suddenly appears, chaos ensues as the two brothers try to navigate their newfound relationship. With mistaken identities, romantic entanglements, and a touch of mystery, this delightful tale takes readers on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and surprises.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Deception
