Synopsis: In "Running to Waste: The Story of a Tomboy," we follow the captivating journey of a spirited young girl named Tommie, who defies societal expectations and embraces her true self. From her unconventional childhood to her rebellious teenage years, Tommie's story is one of resilience, self-discovery, and the pursuit of freedom. As she navigates the challenges of gender norms and societal pressures, Tommie's determination to live authentically shines through, inspiring readers to question the limitations placed upon them and embrace their own unique identities.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, LGBTQ+

Themes: Gender identity, Self-acceptance, Individuality
