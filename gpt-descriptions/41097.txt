Synopsis: In "Mary Lee the Red Cross Girl" by Helen Hart, follow the captivating journey of Mary Lee as she embarks on a life-changing adventure with the Red Cross. From the bustling streets of New York City to the war-torn battlefields of Europe, Mary Lee's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. As she faces heart-wrenching challenges and forms unexpected friendships, Mary Lee discovers the true meaning of sacrifice and the power of compassion. Will she be able to make a difference in the lives of those affected by war? Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age. Themes: War, Humanitarianism, Resilience.
