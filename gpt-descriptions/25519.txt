Synopsis:
In "Little Wizard Stories of Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a series of enchanting adventures in the magical land of Oz. From encountering mischievous creatures to solving perplexing puzzles, this collection of short stories will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and imagination. As Dorothy and her companions navigate through the whimsical landscapes of Oz, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of believing in oneself. Prepare to be captivated by the charm and magic of the Land of Oz in this delightful collection of tales.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Belief in oneself
