Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Day's Daughters' by Mary E. Mann, the lives of three sisters are forever changed when their father unexpectedly passes away, leaving them to navigate the challenges of life on their own. As they each embark on their own unique paths, they must confront societal expectations, personal desires, and the complexities of love and relationships. Set against the backdrop of a changing world, this captivating novel explores the bonds of sisterhood, the pursuit of independence, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Sisterhood, Independence, Love and Relationships, Resilience
