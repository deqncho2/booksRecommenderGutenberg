Synopsis: In 'My Pretty Maid; or, Liane Lester' by Miller, Alex. McVeigh, Mrs., a young woman named Liane Lester finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, duty, and self-discovery, Liane's life takes unexpected turns that challenge her perception of happiness and fulfillment. Set in a time of strict social conventions, this captivating tale explores the choices we make and the consequences they bring, ultimately revealing the power of personal agency and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Self-Discovery
