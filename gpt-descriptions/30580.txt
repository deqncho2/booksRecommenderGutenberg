Synopsis: 
'The Fairy Books of Andrew Lang: A Project Gutenberg Linked Index to All Stories in the 12 Volumes' is a captivating collection that brings together twelve enchanting volumes of fairy tales from around the world. From the classic tales of Cinderella and Sleeping Beauty to lesser-known stories from Africa, Asia, and Europe, this compilation offers a treasure trove of magical adventures. Each volume is filled with captivating characters, whimsical settings, and timeless lessons that will transport readers to a world of wonder and imagination. Whether you are seeking tales of bravery, love, or the triumph of good over evil, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy tales, folklore, fantasy
Themes: Magic, adventure, morality, cultural diversity
