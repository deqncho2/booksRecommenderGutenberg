Synopsis: In the mystical land of Yamato, a young samurai named Hiroshi embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a series of mysterious disappearances. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of his homeland, Hiroshi finds himself entangled in a web of ancient legends, supernatural beings, and political intrigue. With the help of a wise and enigmatic white kami, Hiroshi must navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable adversaries to save his people from an impending catastrophe. The White Kami is a captivating tale of adventure, honor, and the power of belief that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Identity, Courage, Redemption
