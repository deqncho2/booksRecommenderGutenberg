Synopsis: In "A Jongleur Strayed: Verses on Love and Other Matters Sacred and Profane," Richard Le Gallienne takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of love, passion, and the human experience. Through his lyrical and evocative poetry, Le Gallienne explores the complexities of relationships, the beauty of nature, and the profound emotions that shape our lives. With each verse, he weaves a tapestry of emotions that will leave readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Romance, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Nature, Human emotions, Relationships
