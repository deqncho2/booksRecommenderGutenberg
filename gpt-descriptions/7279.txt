Synopsis: In 'The Shopkeeper Turned Gentleman', a wealthy shopkeeper becomes obsessed with the idea of becoming a gentleman and immerses himself in the world of high society. As he tries to adopt the manners and behaviors of the upper class, hilarity ensues, leading to unexpected consequences and revelations. This comedic play explores the absurdity of social climbing and the clash between appearance and true character.

Genres: Comedy, Satire

Themes: Social class, Identity, Appearance vs. reality
