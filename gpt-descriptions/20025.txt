Synopsis: In 'Pirate Gold' by Stimson, Frederic Jesup, a young sailor named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart cunning pirates, and unravel the mysteries of the past to claim the fabled pirate gold. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the map, Jack discovers that the true treasure may not be what he initially expected. Will he be able to overcome the challenges and find the ultimate prize?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Courage, Betrayal, Redemption
