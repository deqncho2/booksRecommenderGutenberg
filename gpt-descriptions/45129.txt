Synopsis: In this captivating work, Wyatt, M. Digby takes readers on a journey through the fascinating history, theory, and practice of illuminating. Condensed from his renowned book, 'The Art of Illuminating,' this volume delves into the secrets and techniques behind the creation of exquisite illuminated manuscripts. From the ancient origins of this art form to the intricate processes involved in illuminating, the author's expertise shines through every page. With stunning illustrations and insightful commentary, this condensed version offers a glimpse into the world of illumination that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Art, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Illumination, Manuscripts, Artistic Techniques, Historical Significance
