Synopsis: 

In 'Epidemic Respiratory Disease: The pneumonias and other infections of the respiratory tract accompanying influenza and measles', authors Blake, Opie, Rivers, and Small delve into the intricate world of respiratory infections that often accompany influenza and measles outbreaks. Through a comprehensive exploration of the pneumonias and other infections, the authors shed light on the devastating impact these diseases have on the respiratory system. From the historical context to the latest research findings, this book offers a captivating journey into the realm of epidemic respiratory diseases.

Genres: Medical literature, Epidemiology, Infectious diseases

Themes: Respiratory infections, Influenza, Measles, Public health, Disease prevention
