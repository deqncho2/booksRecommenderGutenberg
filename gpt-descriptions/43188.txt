Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Harlequin Opal: A Romance" by Fergus Hume, the captivating tale continues as the mysterious opal continues to weave its spellbinding influence over the lives of those who possess it. As the opal changes hands, it brings both fortune and misfortune, love and betrayal, and secrets that threaten to unravel the lives of its owners. With each new revelation, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and desire, where the power of the opal becomes increasingly irresistible. As the story unfolds, the true nature of the opal and its connection to the past is slowly unveiled, leading to a climactic confrontation that will determine the fate of all involved.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Fate and destiny, Love and obsession, Deception and secrets
