Synopsis:
In the small town of Willow Creek, a series of mysterious fires has the community on edge. As the fires continue to spread, a young detective named Jack Collins is assigned to the case. With each new fire, Jack becomes more determined to catch the elusive culprit known as "The Firebug." As he delves deeper into the investigation, Jack uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives that lead him down a dangerous path. With the clock ticking and the fires escalating, Jack must race against time to stop The Firebug before the town is consumed by flames.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes:
Justice, Obsession, Deception
