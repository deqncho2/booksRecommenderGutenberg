Synopsis:
In the eighth volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt delves into the intriguing world of convent affairs. Set in the 18th century, Casanova's narrative takes us on a journey through the hidden lives of nuns and the forbidden desires that lurk within the walls of the convent. As he encounters a diverse cast of characters, including passionate nuns, cunning abbesses, and even a disguised lover, Casanova's escapades within the cloistered walls unfold with scandalous twists and turns. With his signature charm and wit, Casanova paints a vivid picture of the secret lives and amorous encounters that take place in the most unexpected of places.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Erotic Literature
Themes: Forbidden love, Sexual desire, Religious hypocrisy, Freedom of choice
