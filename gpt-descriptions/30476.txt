Synopsis: In 'Zero Hour' by Edmond Hamilton, a mysterious signal from outer space captivates the world, leading to a thrilling race against time to uncover its origin and purpose. As the clock ticks, a group of scientists and adventurers must navigate treacherous obstacles and confront the unknown, all while facing the imminent threat of global catastrophe. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping sci-fi tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Human resilience, Consequences of scientific discovery
