Synopsis: In 'Plague of Pythons' by Frederik Pohl, a mysterious and deadly force begins to spread across the world, causing chaos and destruction in its wake. As society crumbles and individuals struggle to survive, a small group of people find themselves at the center of a battle against an unimaginable evil. With time running out, they must uncover the truth behind the plague and find a way to stop it before humanity is completely consumed.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Apocalyptic events, Human resilience, Moral dilemmas
