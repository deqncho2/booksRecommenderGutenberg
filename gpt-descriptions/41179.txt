Synopsis: In 'Portage Paths: The Keys of the Continent' by Archer Butler Hulbert, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of North America as the author uncovers the hidden secrets of the continent's historic portage paths. From the treacherous rapids of the Great Lakes to the dense forests of the Appalachian Mountains, Hulbert's vivid storytelling transports readers to a time when these paths were the lifelines connecting civilizations. As he delves into the rich history and legends surrounding these ancient trails, Hulbert uncovers tales of adventure, conflict, and the indomitable spirit of those who traversed them. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the untold stories that lie within the forgotten paths of the continent.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Cultural exchange, Human resilience
