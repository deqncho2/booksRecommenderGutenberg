Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of old-time gardens as Alice Morse Earle takes you on a captivating journey through history, revealing the secrets and beauty of these timeless landscapes. From the charming cottage gardens of New England to the grand estates of the South, Earle's vivid descriptions transport you to a bygone era, where every flower and every stone tells a story. Discover the art of creating a garden that not only delights the senses but also reflects the spirit of its time. With her meticulous research and passion for horticulture, Earle invites you to explore the hidden treasures and forgotten traditions of these newly revived old-time gardens.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Gardening

Themes: Garden design, Horticulture, Historical preservation
