Synopsis: 

In "Bulgaria" by Frank Fox, a young American woman named Emily embarks on a journey to the enchanting country of Bulgaria, seeking solace and a fresh start. As she immerses herself in the rich culture and history of this Eastern European gem, she discovers a hidden world of mystery and intrigue. From the bustling streets of Sofia to the picturesque landscapes of the countryside, Emily becomes entangled in a web of secrets that could change her life forever. With each step she takes, she uncovers the dark past of Bulgaria and finds herself caught in a dangerous game of survival. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous path ahead and find the redemption she seeks?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Travel

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Cultural Exploration
