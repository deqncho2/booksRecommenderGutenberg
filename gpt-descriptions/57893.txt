Synopsis: In "The Country School: An Entertainment in Two Scenes," Martha Russell Orne takes readers on a captivating journey to a quaint country school. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique quirks and personalities. From the strict schoolmaster to the mischievous students, the school becomes a stage for both laughter and heartwarming moments. Amidst the daily routines and unexpected surprises, the country school becomes a microcosm of life itself, where friendships are formed, lessons are learned, and the true essence of education is discovered.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Education, Friendship, Growth, Community
