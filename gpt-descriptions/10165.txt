Synopsis: 

'Across the Zodiac: The Story of a Wrecked Record' takes readers on a thrilling journey through space and time. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a young Englishman named Percy Greg who embarks on an extraordinary adventure to the planet Mars. After a catastrophic event leaves him stranded on the red planet, Percy must navigate a strange and unfamiliar world, encountering bizarre creatures, advanced technology, and a society on the brink of revolution. As he strives to find a way back home, Percy becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal discovery, ultimately leading him to question the very nature of humanity and the boundaries of scientific possibility.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Alienation, Social and Political Commentary
