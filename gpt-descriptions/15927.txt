Synopsis: In 'The Vehement Flame' by Margaret Wade Campbell Deland, a young woman named Marcia is torn between her duty to her family and her desire for personal freedom. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations, Marcia must confront the consequences of her choices and find her own path in a world that seeks to confine her. Set in the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the struggles of a woman striving to break free from the constraints of her time.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, personal freedom
