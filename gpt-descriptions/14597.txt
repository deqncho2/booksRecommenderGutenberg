Synopsis: 
"The Woman Thou Gavest Me; Being the Story of Mary O'Neill" by Hall Caine, Sir, is a captivating novel that delves into the complex life of Mary O'Neill. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows Mary, a young woman who finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires. As she navigates through love, betrayal, and the challenges of her time, Mary's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of personal choice.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Society and expectations, Love and betrayal, Personal freedom and choice
