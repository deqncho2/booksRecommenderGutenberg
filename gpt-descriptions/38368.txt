Synopsis: 
In 'A Knight on Wheels' by Hay, Ian, we are introduced to a mysterious and enigmatic character named Sir Richard. With his unconventional methods and a motorcycle as his trusty steed, Sir Richard embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through the dark underbelly of a corrupt city. As he navigates through dangerous encounters and uncovers shocking secrets, Sir Richard's true identity and purpose become increasingly intriguing. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Corruption, Justice
