Synopsis: 
When Life Was Young: At the Old Farm in Maine takes readers on a nostalgic journey to a simpler time in rural Maine. Through the eyes of the author, Charles Asbury Stephens, we are transported to a charming old farm where he spent his childhood. From the joys of exploring the surrounding woods and fields to the heartwarming interactions with the farm animals, Stephens paints a vivid picture of a bygone era filled with innocence and wonder. As we follow his adventures and encounters with nature, we are reminded of the beauty and magic that can be found in the simplest of moments.

Genres: Memoir, Autobiography, Historical

Themes: Nostalgia, Nature, Childhood, Rural Life
