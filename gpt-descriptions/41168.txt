Synopsis: In 'The Siege of Norwich Castle: A story of the last struggle against the Conqueror', Matilda Maria Blake takes us back to the tumultuous time of the Norman Conquest. Set in the city of Norwich, the story follows a group of brave individuals who find themselves at the heart of a fierce battle for freedom and survival. As the castle becomes a symbol of resistance against the Conqueror's rule, the characters must navigate treacherous alliances, personal sacrifices, and the relentless pursuit of power. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Blake brings to life the harrowing events of the siege, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Resistance, Power, Loyalty, Sacrifice
