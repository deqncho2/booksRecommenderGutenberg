Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: The Cradle of Liberty, Vol. 6, Num. 10, Serial No. 158, July 1, 1918', Albert Bushnell Hart takes readers on a captivating journey through the birthplace of American independence. From the bustling streets of Boston to the hallowed grounds of Lexington and Concord, this volume delves into the rich history and pivotal events that shaped the American Revolution. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Hart brings to life the heroes, the struggles, and the triumphs that forever changed the course of a nation.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Educational

Themes: American Revolution, Independence, Patriotism
