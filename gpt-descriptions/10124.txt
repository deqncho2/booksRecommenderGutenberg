Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces and Uncle John', three young girls find themselves in the care of their mysterious Uncle John after the sudden passing of their beloved Aunt Jane. As they navigate their new life with Uncle John, they uncover hidden family secrets and embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them from their quiet countryside home to the bustling city. With unexpected twists and turns, the girls must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the mysteries surrounding their family and discover the true meaning of love and loyalty.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Coming of age, Discovery
