Synopsis: In 'The High Heart' by Basil King, a mysterious artist named John Strickland arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic personality and extraordinary talent. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, secrets are unveiled, passions ignite, and the boundaries between reality and imagination blur. With each stroke of his brush, Strickland weaves a mesmerizing tapestry that challenges the perceptions of art, love, and the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Love and desire, Perception of reality
