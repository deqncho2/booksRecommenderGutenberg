Synopsis: In 'The Last American: A Fragment from the Journal of Khan-li, Prince of Dimph-yoo-chur and Admiral in the Persian Navy' by John Ames Mitchell, readers are transported to a future world where the United States has vanished, and the last remaining American is discovered in a hidden underground city. As the enigmatic Khan-li recounts his extraordinary journey, filled with adventure, romance, and political intrigue, readers are left questioning the fate of America and the true nature of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Identity, Nationalism, Survival
