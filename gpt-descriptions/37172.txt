Synopsis: In "In a Glass Darkly, v. 1/3" by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a collection of chilling tales is presented through the writings of a mysterious doctor. From a haunted room to a sinister portrait, each story delves into the dark corners of the human psyche, exploring the supernatural and the macabre. As the doctor's accounts unfold, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of suspense and terror, where the line between reality and the supernatural becomes blurred.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: The supernatural, psychological terror, the duality of human nature
