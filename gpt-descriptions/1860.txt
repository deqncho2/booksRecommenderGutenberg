Synopsis: 

'Westward Ho! Or, The Voyages and Adventures of Sir Amyas Leigh, Knight, of Burrough, in the County of Devon, in the Reign of Her Most Glorious Majesty Queen Elizabeth' is an enthralling historical adventure set in the Elizabethan era. The story follows the courageous Sir Amyas Leigh, a knight from Devon, as he embarks on a series of thrilling voyages and encounters numerous adventures. From battles at sea to encounters with pirates and exploration of new lands, Sir Amyas Leigh's journey is filled with danger, romance, and the pursuit of honor. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, he must confront his own personal demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Honor, Love and Sacrifice
