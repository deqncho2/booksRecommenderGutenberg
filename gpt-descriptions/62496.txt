Synopsis: In 'The Rāmāyana, Volume 4. Uttara Kānda' by Valmiki, the epic tale of Prince Rama and his quest to rescue his wife Sita reaches its climactic conclusion. As Rama prepares to ascend the throne, a series of unexpected events unfold, challenging his righteousness and testing the bonds of love and loyalty. With the fate of Ayodhya hanging in the balance, Rama must confront his deepest fears and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of his kingdom and his own soul.

Genres: Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Love and devotion, Dharma (righteousness), Duty and responsibility, Redemption
