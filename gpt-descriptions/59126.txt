Synopsis: In 'Faulkner's Folly' by Wells, Carolyn, a renowned detective is called to a small town to investigate a mysterious disappearance. As she delves deeper into the case, she uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that challenge her every step. With time running out, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
