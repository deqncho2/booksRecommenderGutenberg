Synopsis: 
In the bustling streets of New York City, a chance encounter between a wealthy young man and a mysterious woman sets off a whirlwind romance that unfolds over the course of one unforgettable night. As they navigate the city's vibrant nightlife, their connection deepens, revealing hidden secrets and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Tracy, Louis weaves a captivating tale of love, intrigue, and the power of fate in this enchanting romance set against the backdrop of the city that never sleeps.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love, Destiny, Secrets
