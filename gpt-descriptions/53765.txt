Synopsis: In "Kabumpo in Oz" by Ruth Plumly Thompson, a magical elephant named Kabumpo takes center stage as he embarks on a thrilling adventure in the enchanting land of Oz. Joined by his loyal friends, they encounter a host of whimsical characters and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and wit. As they journey through Oz, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome obstacles and unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. Will Kabumpo and his companions succeed in their quest and bring harmony back to the land of Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving, Imagination
