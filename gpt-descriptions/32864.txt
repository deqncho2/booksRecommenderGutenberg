Synopsis: 
In 'Bedside Manner' by Joseph Samachson, a renowned surgeon finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when a series of bizarre events unfold at his hospital. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets lurking within the medical institution, he must confront his own demons and navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With lives hanging in the balance, the surgeon must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Medical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Morality, Redemption
