Synopsis:
In the small town of Patesville, a young woman named Beulah finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Beulah's journey takes her on a path of self-discovery and resilience. With vivid characters and a captivating narrative, 'Beulah' explores the power of personal choice and the strength it takes to challenge the status quo.

Genres:
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes:
Identity, Independence, Social norms
