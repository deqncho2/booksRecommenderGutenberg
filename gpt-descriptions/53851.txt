Synopsis: In "Deborah: A tale of the times of Judas Maccabaeus," the reader is transported to ancient Israel during a tumultuous period of rebellion against oppressive rulers. Amidst the chaos, Deborah, a young and courageous woman, finds herself caught in the crossfire of political intrigue, religious conflict, and personal struggles. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of power and faith, Deborah's journey becomes a testament to the strength of the human spirit and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Faith, Freedom, Love, Identity
