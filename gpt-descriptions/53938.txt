Synopsis: In "Nothing to Do: A Tilt at Our Best Society," follow the captivating journey of a young man named Harry, who finds himself caught between the allure of wealth and the pursuit of true happiness. As he navigates the glamorous world of high society, Harry discovers that appearances can be deceiving and that the pursuit of material success may not lead to fulfillment. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of social status, the power of personal choices, and the true meaning of a life well-lived.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Social critique

Themes: Social class, Materialism, Authenticity, Self-discovery
