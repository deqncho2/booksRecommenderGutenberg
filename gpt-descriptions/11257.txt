Synopsis: 
In 'Little Folks Astray', May, Sophie takes readers on a captivating journey as a group of children find themselves unexpectedly separated from their parents during a summer vacation. Lost in an unfamiliar place, they must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to navigate through various challenges and obstacles. As they encounter new friends and face unexpected dangers, the children learn valuable lessons about independence, resilience, and the power of friendship. Will they be able to find their way back to their parents and overcome the obstacles that stand in their path?

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Resilience, Friendship
