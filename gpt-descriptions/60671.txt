Synopsis: In the heart of Los Angeles, a city on the brink of collapse, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate the chaos and corruption to uncover the truth behind a series of mysterious events. As the city descends into madness, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of not only themselves, but the entire city. With pulse-pounding action and unexpected twists, 'The Last Days of L.A.' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a dystopian landscape where survival is the ultimate prize.

Genres: Dystopian, Thriller, Action

Themes: Corruption, Survival, Redemption
