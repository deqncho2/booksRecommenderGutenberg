Synopsis: In 'Miss McDonald' by Mary Jane Holmes, a young woman named Miss McDonald finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she takes a position as a governess in a wealthy household. As she navigates the complexities of her new role, Miss McDonald becomes entangled in a forbidden romance that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and betrayal, social class and hierarchy, the power of secrets
