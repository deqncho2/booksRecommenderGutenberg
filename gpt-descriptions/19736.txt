Synopsis: 
When the Bunker family arrives at Aunt Jo's house for a summer vacation, they have no idea what adventures await them. From mysterious noises in the night to unexpected visitors, the six little Bunkers find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of excitement and mystery. As they navigate through the challenges and surprises, the Bunker children learn the importance of family, friendship, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family, Friendship, Imagination
