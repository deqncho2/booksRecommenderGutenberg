Synopsis: 
In "The Chronicles of Aunt Minervy Ann," readers are transported to the post-Civil War South, where they are introduced to the witty and wise Aunt Minervy Ann. Through a series of letters, Aunt Minervy Ann shares her observations and experiences, providing a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of the era. From her humorous encounters with eccentric characters to her poignant reflections on love, loss, and the complexities of race, Aunt Minervy Ann's chronicles offer a captivating glimpse into a bygone era.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel

Themes: Race relations, Southern culture, Resilience, Love and loss
