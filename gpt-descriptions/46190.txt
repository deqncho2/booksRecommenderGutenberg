Synopsis: In 'Stories of Robin Hood' by Bertha Evangeline Bush, embark on a thrilling adventure through the legendary Sherwood Forest. Join Robin Hood and his band of merry outlaws as they outwit the corrupt Sheriff of Nottingham and champion the cause of the oppressed. From daring heists to heartwarming acts of kindness, this collection of tales brings to life the timeless heroics of Robin Hood and his loyal companions. Prepare to be captivated by the excitement, camaraderie, and noble ideals that define this beloved folklore.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Rebellion, Social Inequality
