Synopsis: 
'The Ballads and Songs of Yorkshire' is a captivating collection of traditional songs and ballads that have been transcribed from private manuscripts, rare broadsides, and scarce publications. This anthology takes readers on a journey through the rich cultural heritage of Yorkshire, showcasing the lyrical beauty and storytelling prowess of the region. From tales of love and loss to historical events and local legends, these songs offer a glimpse into the lives and experiences of the people of Yorkshire. With each ballad and song, readers are transported to a bygone era, immersing themselves in the vibrant tapestry of Yorkshire's musical history.

Genres: Folklore, Poetry, Music

Themes: Love, Loss, History, Local Legends
