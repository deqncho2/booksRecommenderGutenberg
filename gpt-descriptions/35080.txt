Synopsis: In 'A Hero of Ticonderoga' by Robinson, Rowland Evans, a young soldier finds himself thrust into the heart of the American Revolution, facing the perils of war and the challenges of leadership. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of Ticonderoga, he must confront his own fears and discover what it truly means to be a hero.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Leadership
