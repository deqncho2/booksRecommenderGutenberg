Synopsis: In 'A New Subspecies of Microtus montanus from Montana and Comments on Microtus canicaudus Miller', Hall and Kelson present a groundbreaking discovery of a new subspecies of Microtus montanus, a small mammal found in Montana. Their research not only sheds light on the taxonomy and distribution of this species but also challenges previous assumptions about Microtus canicaudus Miller. Through detailed analysis and comparison, the authors provide valuable insights into the evolutionary history and ecological significance of these rodents, leaving readers eager to learn more about these fascinating creatures.

Genres: Scientific research, Zoology, Ecology

Themes: Taxonomy, Evolution, Biodiversity
