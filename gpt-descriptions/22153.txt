Synopsis: In "Pagan and Christian Rome," Lanciani takes us on a captivating journey through the ancient city of Rome, revealing the hidden layers of its rich history. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the sacredness of the Vatican, Lanciani uncovers the fascinating clash between pagan and Christian cultures that shaped the destiny of Rome. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he unveils the secrets of forgotten temples, underground catacombs, and the rise of Christianity amidst the remnants of a once-mighty empire. This enthralling exploration of Rome's past will leave readers spellbound, as Lanciani brings to life the vibrant tapestry of a city that has stood the test of time.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Travel

Themes: Clash of cultures, Religious transformation, Urban development
