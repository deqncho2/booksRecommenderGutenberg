Synopsis: 
In 'Happy Jack' by Thornton W. Burgess, readers are introduced to a mischievous little rabbit named Happy Jack. Living in the Green Forest, Happy Jack finds himself in one adventure after another, often getting into trouble due to his curious nature. From encounters with other woodland creatures to unexpected challenges, Happy Jack's escapades will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what he will get up to next.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Nature
