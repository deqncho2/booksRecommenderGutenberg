Synopsis: 
'English Jests and Anecdotes, Collected from Various Sources' is a delightful compilation of humorous tales and witty anecdotes that will leave readers chuckling and entertained. From clever wordplay to hilarious situations, this collection showcases the wit and humor of the English language. Each page offers a new joke or amusing story, ensuring that readers will find themselves eagerly turning the pages to discover the next laugh-out-loud moment. Whether you're in need of a good laugh or simply enjoy the art of storytelling, this book is sure to bring a smile to your face.

Genres: Humor, Comedy, Anthology

Themes: Wit, Humor, Language, Entertainment
