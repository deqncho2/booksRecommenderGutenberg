Synopsis: In "The Orloff Couple, and Malva" by Maksim Gorky, a mysterious couple arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with their enigmatic presence. As their true intentions slowly unravel, the townspeople find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and unexpected consequences. Amidst the backdrop of a changing society, the characters navigate love, betrayal, and the pursuit of personal freedom, leading to a gripping climax that challenges their notions of identity and morality.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Love and Betrayal, Social Change
