Synopsis: In 'The Three Days' Tournament: A Study in Romance and Folk-Lore: Being an Appendix to the Author's 'Legend of Sir Lancelot'', Jessie L. Weston takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of romance and folk-lore. Set against the backdrop of a thrilling tournament, this appendix to the author's 'Legend of Sir Lancelot' explores the intricate web of relationships and the power of love. As the tournament unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists and turns keep readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Weston weaves a tale that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Romance, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Loyalty and betrayal, Fate and destiny
