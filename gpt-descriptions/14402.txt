Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Old Mr. Crow' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the captivating journey of a wise and mysterious crow as he embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of the forest. As he encounters various creatures and faces unexpected challenges, Old Mr. Crow's determination and cunning nature are put to the test. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enchanting world of the forest, where danger and wonder coexist in equal measure.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Wisdom, Nature, Perseverance
