Synopsis: 
'Forest Life and Forest Trees' takes readers on a captivating journey into the winter camp-life of loggers and the thrilling adventures of the wild woods. Set against the backdrop of the rivers of Maine and New Brunswick, the book provides vivid descriptions of lumbering operations and the challenges faced by those who work in the forests. With a blend of adventure and exploration, the story offers a unique perspective on the rugged beauty of the wilderness and the lives of those who inhabit it.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Lumbering operations, Winter camp-life, Nature appreciation
