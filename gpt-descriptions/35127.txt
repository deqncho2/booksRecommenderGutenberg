Synopsis: In "Frank Merriwell's Return to Yale," Frank Merriwell, the beloved college athlete, finds himself back at Yale University for another thrilling adventure. As he navigates the challenges of his sophomore year, Frank must confront a mysterious secret society, face off against formidable rivals, and uncover the truth behind a series of perplexing events. With his unwavering determination, exceptional athletic skills, and a loyal group of friends, Frank Merriwell is ready to take on whatever obstacles come his way in this captivating tale of courage, loyalty, and triumph.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Honor
