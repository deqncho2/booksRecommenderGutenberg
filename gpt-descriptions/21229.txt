Synopsis: In 'Thistle and Rose: A Story for Girls', follow the captivating journey of two young girls, Thistle and Rose, as they navigate the challenges of friendship, family, and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a charming English countryside, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading them on a path of adventure, secrets, and personal growth. As they face trials and tribulations, Thistle and Rose learn the importance of resilience, loyalty, and the power of embracing their true selves. This heartwarming tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the girls' destinies and the lessons they learn along the way.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Family, Self-discovery
