Synopsis: In 'The Girl from Keller's' by Harold Bindloss, a chance encounter between a mysterious young woman and a struggling artist sets off a chain of events that will test their courage, loyalty, and love. As they navigate the treacherous waters of deceit, danger, and hidden secrets, they must confront their own pasts and make difficult choices that will determine their future. With vivid descriptions of the rugged Canadian wilderness and a cast of intriguing characters, this gripping tale of romance and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Secrets and deception
