Synopsis: In 'The Conquistadors Come', Mary Elizabeth Counselman takes readers on a thrilling journey through time, where the clash of civilizations unfolds in the wake of the arrival of the Conquistadors. As the Spanish explorers set foot on the shores of the New World, they encounter a land teeming with mystery, danger, and ancient secrets. Through the eyes of both the conquerors and the conquered, Counselman weaves a tale of exploration, conquest, and the profound impact of cultural collision. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'The Conquistadors Come' immerses readers in a world of adventure, treachery, and the enduring resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Cultural

Themes: Exploration, Conquest, Cultural Collision, Resilience
