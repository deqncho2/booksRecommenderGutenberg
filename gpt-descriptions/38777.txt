Synopsis: 
'Lad: A Dog' by Albert Payson Terhune is a heartwarming tale that follows the adventures of Lad, a noble and courageous collie. From his early days as a mischievous puppy to his remarkable feats as a loyal and intelligent companion, Lad's story is filled with excitement, loyalty, and the unbreakable bond between humans and their four-legged friends. Set in the beautiful countryside, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey through Lad's life, showcasing his unwavering devotion and his ability to overcome challenges with unwavering determination.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Companionship
