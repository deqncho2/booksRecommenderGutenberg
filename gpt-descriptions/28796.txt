Synopsis: In 'The White Doe: The Fate of Virginia Dare', Sallie Southall Cotten weaves a captivating tale that delves into the mystery surrounding the disappearance of Virginia Dare, the first English child born in the New World. Set against the backdrop of the Roanoke Colony in the late 16th century, this historical fiction takes readers on a journey filled with intrigue, love, and the supernatural. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the fate of Virginia Dare becomes entwined with the mythical white doe that haunts the wilderness. Will the truth be uncovered, or will the mystery of Virginia Dare's disappearance remain forever unsolved?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Legends and Folklore
