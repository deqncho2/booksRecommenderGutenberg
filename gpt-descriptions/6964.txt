Synopsis: 

In "Telling Fortunes By Tea Leaves: How to Read Your Fate in a Teacup," Cicely Kent takes readers on a captivating journey into the mystical world of tea leaf reading. Through her expert guidance, readers will learn the ancient art of divination, discovering how to interpret the intricate patterns left behind in their teacups. As the secrets of the tea leaves unfold, a fascinating tapestry of destinies, love, and hidden truths emerges, captivating readers with each turn of the page. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Divination

Themes: Fate, Destiny, Intuition, Symbolism
