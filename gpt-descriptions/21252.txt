Synopsis: In "The Practical Distiller," Samuel McHarry presents a comprehensive guide to the art of distilling, offering readers the opportunity to create high-quality spirits in larger quantities than ever before. With a focus on whiskey, gin, brandy, and other spirits, McHarry shares his expertise on the distilling process, providing valuable insights and techniques that go beyond the conventional methods used at the time. This practical manual is a treasure trove of knowledge for those seeking to enhance their distilling skills and produce exceptional spirits.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Reference

Themes: Distilling techniques, Spirit production, Quality improvement
