Synopsis: In 'Millions from Waste' by Frederick Arthur Ambrose Talbot, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery hidden within the depths of waste. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a world of unimaginable possibilities and potential riches. However, his newfound success attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess his invention. With danger lurking at every turn, the scientist must navigate a treacherous path to protect his creation and secure a future that could change the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Innovation, Greed, Power
