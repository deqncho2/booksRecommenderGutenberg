Synopsis: 
In the Morning Glow: Short Stories by Gilson, Roy Rolfe is a captivating collection of tales that will transport readers to different times and places. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene countryside of New England, each story offers a unique glimpse into the lives of its characters. Whether it's a young woman searching for her identity, a struggling artist finding inspiration, or a family grappling with loss, these stories are filled with rich emotions and unexpected twists. With vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, Gilson weaves together a tapestry of human experiences that will leave readers pondering long after they've turned the last page.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Identity, Inspiration, Loss, Human experiences
