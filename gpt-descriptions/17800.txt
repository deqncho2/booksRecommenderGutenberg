Synopsis: In the enchanting novel 'Wych Hazel', a young woman named Hazel finds herself drawn to a mysterious and secluded estate known as Wychwood. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the estate, she uncovers a hidden world of magic, romance, and dark family legacies. With each discovery, Hazel's own powers begin to awaken, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and danger. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous paths of love and power, or will she succumb to the darkness that lurks within Wychwood?

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Self-discovery, Family legacies
