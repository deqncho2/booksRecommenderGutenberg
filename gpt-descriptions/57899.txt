Synopsis: 

In "Decisive Battles of America," embark on a thrilling journey through the most pivotal moments in American history. From the Revolutionary War to the Civil War and beyond, this captivating book delves into the strategic genius, heroic sacrifices, and unexpected twists that shaped the nation. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author brings to life the intense battles that determined the fate of a young nation, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of each conflict.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American history

Themes: Leadership, Courage, Sacrifice, Strategy, Nation-building
