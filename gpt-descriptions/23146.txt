Synopsis: In 'And All the Earth a Grave' by MacApp, C. C., a mysterious and thought-provoking tale unfolds as a lone survivor navigates a desolate world filled with unexpected challenges. As he encounters various individuals and uncovers the truth behind their motivations, the protagonist is forced to question his own beliefs and confront the consequences of his actions. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into the complexities of human nature and the fragile balance between hope and despair.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Morality, Identity
