Synopsis: In "Franz Liszt: The Story of a Boy Who Became a Great Pianist and Teacher," author Thomas Tapper takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of the most influential composers and pianists in history. From Liszt's humble beginnings as a young boy with a passion for music to his rise to fame and his transformative impact on the world of classical music, this biography offers a fascinating exploration of the trials, triumphs, and personal growth of a musical prodigy. Through vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, Tapper paints a vivid portrait of Liszt's extraordinary talent, his complex relationships, and the profound legacy he left behind. 

Genres: Biography, Music, Historical

Themes: Passion for music, Personal growth, Legacy
