Synopsis: 

'Life and Literature: Over two thousand extracts from ancient and modern writers,; and classified in alphabetical order' by Richardson, John Purver is a captivating anthology that takes readers on a journey through the vast realm of literature. This meticulously curated collection features a diverse range of over two thousand extracts from both ancient and modern writers, all organized in alphabetical order for easy reference. From the profound musings of philosophers to the lyrical verses of poets, and the insightful observations of novelists, this anthology offers a rich tapestry of literary gems that will leave readers inspired and eager to explore the works of these remarkable authors.

Genres: Anthology, Literature, Reference

Themes: Wisdom, Inspiration, Human Experience
