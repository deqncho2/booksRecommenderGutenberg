Synopsis: 
"Lives of the Most Remarkable Criminals Who have been Condemned and Executed for Murder, the Highway, Housebreaking, Street Robberies, Coining or other offences" delves into the gripping and shocking tales of notorious criminals who have committed heinous acts. From murderers to highwaymen, housebreakers to street robbers, this collection of true stories explores the dark underbelly of society and the crimes that have captivated the public's imagination. Each narrative offers a glimpse into the lives and motivations of these criminals, as well as the consequences they faced for their actions. Prepare to be enthralled by the chilling accounts of these remarkable individuals.

Genres: True Crime, Biography, History

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Morality, Society's fascination with criminals
