Synopsis: 

'Account of the Russian Discoveries between Asia and America: To which are added, the conquest of Siberia, and the history of the transactions and commerce between Russia and China' by Coxe, William is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey of exploration and conquest. 

In this fascinating work, Coxe delves into the remarkable Russian discoveries that took place between Asia and America, shedding light on the courageous explorers who ventured into uncharted territories. From the conquest of Siberia to the intricate history of transactions and commerce between Russia and China, the book unveils a world of adventure, diplomacy, and cultural exchange.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Conquest and colonization, Cultural exchange and diplomacy
