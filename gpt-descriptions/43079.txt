Synopsis: In 'The Boy Patrol on Guard' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a group of young boys find themselves thrust into a dangerous adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious secret that puts their lives at risk. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering loyalty to protect themselves and uncover the truth. With thrilling twists and heart-pounding suspense, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Discovery
