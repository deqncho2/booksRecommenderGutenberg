Synopsis: In "History of the United Netherlands from the Death of William the Silent to the Twelve Year's Truce, 1586c," John Lothrop Motley takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period following the assassination of William the Silent, the founding father of the Dutch Republic. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Motley delves into the political intrigues, military conflicts, and religious tensions that shaped the destiny of the United Netherlands. From the rise of the formidable Dutch navy to the epic battles against the Spanish Empire, this gripping historical account unveils the heroic struggles and sacrifices of the Dutch people in their quest for independence and religious freedom.

Genres: History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Independence, Religious Freedom, Political Intrigue, Military Conflict
