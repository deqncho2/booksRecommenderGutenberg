Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders on the Lost River Trail', Grace and her adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey through the rugged wilderness. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected dangers, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the Overland Riders uncover secrets and face perilous situations that test their mettle and determination. Will they be able to unravel the mysteries of the Lost River Trail and emerge victorious from their daring expedition?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
