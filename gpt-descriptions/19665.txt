Synopsis: In 'My Lady of the Chinese Courtyard' by Elizabeth Cooper, a young American woman finds herself immersed in the captivating world of the Chinese imperial court. As she navigates the intricate web of palace politics and cultural traditions, she becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair that challenges her loyalty and tests the boundaries of her own identity. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing China, this mesmerizing tale explores the clash between tradition and modernity, love and duty, and the power of personal choice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Cultural

Themes: Cultural identity, Forbidden love, Tradition vs. Modernity
