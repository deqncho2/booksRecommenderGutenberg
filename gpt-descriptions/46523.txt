Synopsis: In 'The Spruce Street Tragedy; or, Old Spicer Handles a Double Mystery', a small town is shaken by a shocking crime that leaves the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, the enigmatic detective, Old Spicer, is called upon to unravel a web of secrets and deceit, leading him down a path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With the clock ticking, Old Spicer must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth before another tragedy strikes. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
