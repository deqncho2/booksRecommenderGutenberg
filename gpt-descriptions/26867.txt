Synopsis: In 'John Jones's Dollar' by Harry Stephen Keeler, a seemingly ordinary dollar bill sets off a chain of events that leads to a thrilling and mind-bending mystery. As various characters become entangled in the search for the truth behind the dollar, secrets are revealed, identities are questioned, and unexpected connections are made. With each twist and turn, the reader is kept on the edge of their seat, eagerly following the trail of clues that will ultimately uncover the astonishing truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Coincidence
