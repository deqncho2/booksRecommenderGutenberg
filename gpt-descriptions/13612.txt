Synopsis: 

In 'Studies in the Psychology of Sex, Volume 3: Analysis of the Sexual Impulse; Love and Pain; The Sexual Impulse in Women', Havelock Ellis delves into the complex and often misunderstood realm of human sexuality. With a meticulous and scientific approach, Ellis explores the various facets of the sexual impulse, examining its origins, manifestations, and the intricate interplay between love and pain. Through a particular focus on the sexual impulse in women, Ellis sheds light on the unique experiences and challenges faced by women in relation to their sexuality. 

Genres: Psychology, Sexuality, Non-fiction

Themes: Human sexuality, Love, Pain, Gender dynamics
