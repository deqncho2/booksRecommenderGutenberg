Synopsis: 
In 'State Trials, Political and Social. Volume 1 (of 2)', readers are taken on a gripping journey through some of the most notorious trials in history. From high-profile political figures to ordinary citizens, this collection of trials offers a fascinating insight into the complex world of justice and power. With each trial presenting its own unique set of circumstances and legal challenges, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns of these real-life courtroom dramas. From scandalous affairs to shocking betrayals, this volume is a compelling exploration of the human condition under the scrutiny of the law.

Genres: 
True Crime, Historical Non-fiction, Legal Drama

Themes: 
Justice and Power, Betrayal, Morality, Human Nature
