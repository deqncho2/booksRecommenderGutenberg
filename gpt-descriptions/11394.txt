Synopsis: In 'Fighting in Flanders', E. Alexander Powell takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing battlefields of World War I. Through vivid and personal accounts, Powell provides a firsthand perspective of the brutal realities faced by soldiers fighting in the trenches of Flanders. From the relentless artillery bombardments to the heart-stopping moments of hand-to-hand combat, this book offers a compelling and immersive narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: War, History, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Survival
