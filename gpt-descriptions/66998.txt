Synopsis: 

In "Rubber Hand Stamps and the Manipulation of Rubber," T. O'Conor Sloane takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of India rubber manufacturing. This practical treatise delves into the intricate process of creating rubber hand stamps, small rubber articles, and even explores the hektograph, special inks, cements, and related subjects. With detailed instructions and insightful explanations, Sloane unveils the secrets behind the manipulation of rubber, offering a comprehensive guide for both beginners and experts in the field. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Technical Manual, Craftsmanship

Themes: Rubber Manufacturing, Hand Stamp Production, Material Manipulation
