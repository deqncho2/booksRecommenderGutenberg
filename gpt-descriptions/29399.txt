Synopsis: In 'Clairvoyance', C. W. Leadbeater takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of the unseen. Through his personal experiences and extensive research, he explores the fascinating world of clairvoyance, unveiling the secrets of this extraordinary psychic ability. From the exploration of astral planes to encounters with spiritual entities, Leadbeater's accounts will leave readers spellbound, questioning the boundaries of perception and the hidden dimensions that surround us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Spiritual

Themes: Clairvoyance, Psychic Abilities, Spirituality, Metaphysics
