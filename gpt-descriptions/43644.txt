Synopsis:
In the gripping novel "G. H. Q. (Montreuil-Sur-Mer) by "G.S.O." by Frank Fox, readers are transported to the heart of World War I. Set in the quaint town of Montreuil-sur-Mer, the story follows a group of soldiers stationed at the General Headquarters (G.H.Q.) during the war. As they navigate the challenges and horrors of the battlefield, they also find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that could change the course of the war. With unexpected twists and turns, the novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the mysteries that unfold.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes: War, Loyalty, Betrayal, Sacrifice
