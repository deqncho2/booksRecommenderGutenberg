Synopsis: 

In 'Sketches in Prison Camps: A Continuation of Sketches of the War', Charles C. Nott takes us on a captivating journey through the harrowing experiences of prisoners of war during the Civil War. Through vivid and poignant sketches, Nott provides a unique perspective on the daily struggles, camaraderie, and resilience of those confined within the confines of prison camps. From the monotony of daily life to the constant threat of disease and starvation, Nott's sketches offer a powerful glimpse into the human spirit's ability to endure even in the darkest of times. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Art 

Themes: Resilience, Human spirit, Survival, Camaraderie
