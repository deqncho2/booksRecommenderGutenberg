Synopsis: In "Through Connemara in a governess cart," two adventurous women embark on a journey through the stunning landscapes of Connemara, Ireland, in a rather unconventional mode of transportation. As they navigate the rugged terrain, they encounter a series of unexpected and humorous situations, meeting eccentric characters along the way. This delightful tale takes readers on a charming and entertaining escapade through the picturesque Irish countryside.

Genres: Travelogue, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Female friendship, Exploration, Irish culture and landscapes
