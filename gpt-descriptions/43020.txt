Synopsis: 
Join Ernest Ingersoll on an exhilarating summer adventure as he embarks on a ramble through the majestic Rocky Mountains and beyond. From breathtaking landscapes to encounters with fascinating wildlife, Ingersoll's vivid descriptions transport readers to the heart of the untamed wilderness. As he navigates treacherous trails and explores hidden valleys, Ingersoll's journey becomes a captivating exploration of both the natural world and the human spirit. With each step, he uncovers the secrets of the mountains and discovers the profound beauty that lies within. 

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Nature Writing

Themes: Exploration, Connection with Nature, Self-Discovery
