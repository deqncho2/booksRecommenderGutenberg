Synopsis: In 'Sleeping Fires: a Novel', a young woman named Elizabeth inherits a mysterious old house from her late aunt, only to discover that it holds dark secrets and hidden treasures. As she delves deeper into the house's history, Elizabeth becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and a long-lost treasure that could change her life forever. With each revelation, she must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. Will Elizabeth uncover the truth and find her own path, or will the sleeping fires of the past consume her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
