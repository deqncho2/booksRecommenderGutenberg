Synopsis: In 'The Trapper's Son' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young boy named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed wilderness of North America. Raised by his trapper father, Jack learns the ways of survival, tracking, and trapping from a young age. But when tragedy strikes, Jack must rely on his skills and determination to navigate the treacherous terrain and face unexpected dangers. Along the way, he encounters fascinating characters, tests his limits, and discovers the true meaning of courage and resilience. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Family, Resilience, Nature
