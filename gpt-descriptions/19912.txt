Synopsis: In 'The Insurrection in Paris' by Davy, an Englishman finds himself caught in the midst of a tumultuous uprising in the heart of Paris. As chaos ensues and loyalties are tested, he must navigate a treacherous web of political intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love. With the fate of the city hanging in the balance, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where trust is a luxury and survival is uncertain. Will he be able to uncover the truth and make it out alive?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Betrayal, Identity
