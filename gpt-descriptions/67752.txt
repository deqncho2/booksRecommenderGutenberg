Synopsis: 
In 'On building a theatre: Stage construction and equipment for small theatres, schools and community', author Irving Pichel takes readers on an enlightening journey into the world of theatre construction and equipment. With a wealth of practical advice and insider tips, Pichel explores the intricate process of building a theatre from scratch, catering specifically to small theatres, schools, and community spaces. From designing the stage to selecting the right lighting and sound equipment, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable insights for anyone interested in creating a captivating theatrical experience. Whether you're a theatre enthusiast, a teacher, or a community organizer, Pichel's expertise will inspire and empower you to bring your creative vision to life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Theatre, Education

Themes: Theatre construction, Stage design, Equipment selection, Community engagement
