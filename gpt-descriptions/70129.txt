Synopsis: In "General Washington's Spies on Long Island and in New York" by Morton Pennypacker, delve into the thrilling world of espionage during the American Revolution. Follow the courageous and cunning spies who risked their lives to gather crucial information for General George Washington. From secret codes and hidden messages to dangerous undercover missions, this captivating account unveils the untold stories of these unsung heroes who played a pivotal role in the fight for independence.

Genres: Historical Non-Fiction, Military History, Espionage

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Courage, Loyalty
