Synopsis:
'The High Toby: Being further chapters in the life and fortunes of Dick Ryder, otherwise Galloping Dick, sometime gentleman of the road' follows the thrilling adventures of Dick Ryder, a notorious highwayman known as Galloping Dick. Set in 18th century England, this gripping tale takes readers on a journey through the dangerous world of highway robbery, as Dick navigates treacherous encounters, unexpected alliances, and the pursuit of justice. As his escapades unfold, secrets from Dick's past come to light, adding layers of intrigue and suspense to the narrative. With each daring heist and narrow escape, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of this charismatic outlaw.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Justice
