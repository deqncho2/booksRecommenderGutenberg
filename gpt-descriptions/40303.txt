Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys Down East; or, The Struggle for the Stanhope Fortune" by Edward Stratemeyer, the adventurous Rover brothers find themselves embroiled in a thrilling quest for a hidden fortune. When a mysterious letter reveals the existence of a vast inheritance, the Rovers set off on a journey to the rugged coast of Maine, where they encounter treacherous cliffs, secret passages, and a cast of intriguing characters. As they navigate through dangerous situations and unravel the clues left behind by their late uncle, the brothers must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to uncover the truth and claim their rightful inheritance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Inheritance, Family, Adventure, Loyalty
