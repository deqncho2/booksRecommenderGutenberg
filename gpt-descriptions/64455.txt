Synopsis: 
In 'Australia in Palestine', a gripping historical novel, the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds intertwine against the backdrop of the tumultuous events in Palestine during World War I. As an Australian soldier finds himself stationed in the heart of the conflict, he forms an unlikely alliance with a Palestinian woman who is determined to protect her family and preserve her homeland. Together, they navigate the complexities of war, love, and loyalty, facing unimaginable challenges that will test their resilience and reshape their destinies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Romance

Themes: Identity, Resistance, Sacrifice, Cultural Clash
