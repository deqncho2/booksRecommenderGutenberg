Synopsis: 

'A True Account of the Voyage of the Nottingham-Galley of London' takes readers on a thrilling journey from the River Thames to New England. Written by Christopher Langman, Nicholas Mellen, and George White, a sailor on the Nottingham galley, this gripping narrative recounts the perils and adventures faced by the crew as they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges. From fierce storms to encounters with pirates, the Nottingham galley's voyage is filled with danger and excitement. As the crew battles the elements and faces the unknown, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and camaraderie to survive. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Camaraderie
