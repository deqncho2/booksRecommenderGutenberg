Synopsis: In "A Journal of Impressions in Belgium," Sinclair, May takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant cities of Belgium. Through her vivid descriptions and personal reflections, she unveils the hidden gems and cultural nuances of this captivating country. From the bustling streets of Brussels to the picturesque canals of Bruges, Sinclair, May's journal offers a unique perspective on the people, history, and traditions of Belgium. As she immerses herself in the local culture, she encounters unexpected encounters and discovers the profound impact of war on the nation. With each page, readers will be transported to the heart of Belgium, experiencing its beauty, resilience, and the enduring spirit of its people.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, History, Resilience
