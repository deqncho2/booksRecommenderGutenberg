Synopsis: In 'The Jungle Baby' by Farrow, G. E., a young girl finds herself lost in the depths of a mysterious jungle after a plane crash. As she navigates through the dense foliage and encounters various creatures, she must rely on her wits and courage to survive. Along the way, she discovers hidden secrets and forms unlikely friendships that will forever change her life. Will she ever find her way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
