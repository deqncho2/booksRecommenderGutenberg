Synopsis: In 'Melchior's Dream and Other Tales', Juliana Horatia Ewing weaves a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking stories that transport readers to different worlds and times. From a young boy's extraordinary dream to a magical encounter with a mysterious stranger, each tale is filled with wonder, adventure, and unexpected twists. As readers delve into these captivating stories, they will be captivated by the power of imagination and the enduring themes of love, courage, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Short Stories

Themes: Imagination, Love, Courage, Resilience
