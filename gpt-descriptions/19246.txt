Synopsis: In 'The Young Pitcher' by Zane Grey, a talented young baseball player named Ken Ward faces numerous challenges as he strives to make a name for himself in the world of professional baseball. With his unwavering determination and the guidance of a seasoned coach, Ken must navigate the complexities of the game, the pressures of fame, and the personal sacrifices required to achieve his dreams. As he battles both on and off the field, Ken discovers the true meaning of perseverance and the transformative power of pursuing one's passion.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Sacrifice, Mentorship
