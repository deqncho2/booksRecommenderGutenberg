Synopsis: In "Open That Door!" by R. Sturgis Ingersoll, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious house with a dark secret. As she explores the eerie rooms and corridors, she uncovers a series of chilling clues that lead her deeper into a web of suspense and danger. With each door she opens, Emily's curiosity grows, but so does the sense of impending doom. Will she uncover the truth behind the house's haunting secrets, or will she become another victim of its malevolent presence?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Curiosity, Fear, Secrets, Survival
