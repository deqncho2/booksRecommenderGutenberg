Synopsis: 
In "Lions 'n' tigers 'n' everything," Cooper, Courtney Ryley takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of exotic animals and the people who dare to tame them. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this captivating true story follows the lives of courageous animal trainers, their awe-inspiring performances, and the dangers they face in their quest for fame and fortune. From the heart-stopping encounters with ferocious lions and tigers to the behind-the-scenes drama of the circus, this gripping narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for these fearless individuals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Ambition, Danger, Entertainment
