Synopsis: 
'Austral English: A dictionary of Australasian words, phrases and usages with those aboriginal-Australian and Maori words which have become incorporated in the language, and the commoner scientific words that have had their origin in Australasia' is a comprehensive dictionary compiled by Edward Ellis Morris. This fascinating work explores the unique linguistic landscape of Australasia, delving into the rich tapestry of words, phrases, and usages that have shaped the region's language. From indigenous Australian and Maori words to scientific terms originating in Australasia, this dictionary offers a captivating insight into the diverse linguistic heritage of the region.

Genres: Reference, Linguistics, Cultural Studies

Themes: Language and Identity, Indigenous Cultures, Linguistic Evolution
