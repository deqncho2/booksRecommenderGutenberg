Synopsis: In 'The Sword Maker' by Barr, Robert, a renowned swordsmith finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is commissioned to create a legendary weapon. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his craft, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient rivalries and dark forces that will test his skills and courage to the limit. With each swing of the hammer, he must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own demons, all while racing against time to complete the sword before it falls into the wrong hands. This thrilling tale of craftsmanship, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Artistry and Craftsmanship, Loyalty and Betrayal, Redemption
