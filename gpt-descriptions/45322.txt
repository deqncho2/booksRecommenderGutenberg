Synopsis: 
In "From North Carolina to Southern California Without a Ticket and How I Did It: Giving My Exciting Experiences as a 'Hobo'", John Peele takes readers on a thrilling journey across the United States as he shares his daring adventures as a "hobo". From hopping on freight trains to hitchhiking through vast landscapes, Peele recounts his encounters with colorful characters, unexpected challenges, and the sheer exhilaration of living life on the edge. As he navigates the vast expanse of America, readers will be captivated by the suspense and excitement that unfolds with each turn of the page.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travel

Themes: Freedom, Survival, Human Connection
