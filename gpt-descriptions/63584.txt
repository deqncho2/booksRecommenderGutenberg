Synopsis: 

The Nuclear Ship Savannah: First Atomic Merchant Ship, One of the World's Safest Ships takes readers on a captivating journey into the groundbreaking world of nuclear-powered ships. This fascinating account explores the creation and maiden voyage of the SS Savannah, the first atomic-powered merchant ship, which revolutionized the maritime industry. From the ship's innovative design to its historic transatlantic crossing, this book unveils the triumphs and challenges faced by the pioneers of nuclear shipping. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Nuclear Power, Maritime Industry
