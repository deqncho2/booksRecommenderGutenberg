Synopsis: In 'Peggy Stewart, Navy Girl, at Home' by Gabrielle E. Jackson, follow the captivating journey of Peggy Stewart as she returns home after serving in the Navy during World War II. As she navigates the challenges of readjusting to civilian life, Peggy finds herself caught between her desire for independence and the expectations of society. With secrets from her past resurfacing and a mysterious stranger entering her life, Peggy's homecoming takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a path of self-discovery and adventure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Coming of Age, Identity, Love and Relationships, Post-War Adjustments
