Synopsis: In "From Yauco to Las Marias: A recent campaign in Puerto Rico by the Independent Regular Brigade under the command of Brig. General Schwan," Herrman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of Puerto Rico during a significant military campaign. The story follows the Independent Regular Brigade, led by the formidable Brig. General Schwan, as they navigate the challenging terrain and face numerous obstacles in their mission. With vivid descriptions and intense action, Herrman captures the bravery and determination of the soldiers as they strive to achieve their objectives.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Leadership, Resilience, Exploration
