Synopsis: In "The True Story of Christopher Columbus, Called the Great Admiral," Elbridge S. Brooks takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most renowned explorers. From Columbus' humble beginnings to his audacious voyages across the Atlantic, this book delves into the triumphs, challenges, and controversies that shaped his legacy. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Brooks paints a vivid portrait of a man whose determination and vision forever changed the course of history.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Legacy
