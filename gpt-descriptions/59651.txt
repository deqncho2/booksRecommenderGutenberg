Synopsis: In 'The Creation of God' by Hartmann, Jacob, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the origins of life. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden truth that could change the course of humanity forever. With the weight of this knowledge on his shoulders, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas, personal sacrifices, and the pursuit of ultimate truth. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of science, faith, and the power of human curiosity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Religion, Human Nature
