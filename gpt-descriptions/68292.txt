Synopsis: In "Tales from Silver Lands," Charles Joseph Finger takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting folklore of Latin America. From the mystical mountains of Mexico to the vibrant jungles of Brazil, this collection of stories introduces us to a myriad of fascinating characters and their extraordinary adventures. We encounter brave warriors, mischievous spirits, and wise old men, all woven together with a touch of magic and a deep appreciation for the rich cultural heritage of the region. Each tale is a delightful exploration of the human spirit, filled with wonder, courage, and the timeless power of storytelling.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Magic, Courage, Wisdom
