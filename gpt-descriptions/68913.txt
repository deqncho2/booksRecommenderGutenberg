Synopsis: In "The Iroquois; or, the bright side of Indian character," Anna C. Johnson takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of the Iroquois tribe. Through the eyes of a young protagonist, we witness the clash of cultures, the struggles for survival, and the profound beauty of the Native American way of life. As friendships are forged and secrets are unveiled, the story unfolds with a mix of adventure, romance, and thought-provoking insights. This compelling narrative explores the complexities of identity, the power of resilience, and the enduring spirit of humanity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural Identity, Survival, Friendship, Resilience
