Synopsis: In 'Huts in Hell' by Daniel A. Poling, a group of American soldiers find themselves trapped behind enemy lines during World War II. Cut off from their unit and surrounded by the enemy, they must rely on their wits, courage, and the bonds of brotherhood to survive. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn Europe, they face unimaginable challenges and make heart-wrenching sacrifices. Will they be able to escape the clutches of hell and make it back home alive?

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Action

Themes: Survival, Brotherhood, Sacrifice
