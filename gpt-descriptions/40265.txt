Synopsis: In 'The Great Quest: A romance of 1826', we follow the captivating journey of Josiah Woods of Topham and his companions as they set sail for Cuba and the Gulf of Guinea. Filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected twists, this enthralling tale takes readers on a thrilling quest that will test the limits of courage and determination. As Josiah and his fellow travelers navigate treacherous waters and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover secrets that could change their lives forever. Will they succeed in their mission, or will they be consumed by the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance, Discovery
