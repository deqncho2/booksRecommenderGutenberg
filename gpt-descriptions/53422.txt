Synopsis: In 'Free Trapper's Pass; or, the Gold-seeker's Daughter!' by William R. Eyster, a thrilling tale unfolds as a young woman embarks on a treacherous journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. With danger lurking at every turn, she must rely on her wit, courage, and determination to survive. As she navigates the rugged terrain and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the pursuit of gold becomes a catalyst for both adventure and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Independence, Discovery
