Synopsis: In "Miss Lulu Bett" by Zona Gale, we are introduced to Lulu Bett, a young woman living with her oppressive family in a small Midwestern town. Lulu's life takes an unexpected turn when a visitor arrives, bringing with him the possibility of a new beginning. As Lulu navigates the complexities of love, family dynamics, and societal expectations, she discovers her own strength and the power to shape her own destiny.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Self-discovery
