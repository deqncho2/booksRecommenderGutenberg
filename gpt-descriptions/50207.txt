Synopsis: In 'Tea-Blending as a Fine Art' by Joseph M. Walsh, embark on a captivating journey into the world of tea blending, where the author unveils the secrets behind creating the perfect cup of tea. From the lush tea gardens of Asia to the bustling tea markets of Europe, Walsh takes readers on a sensory exploration of the artistry and craftsmanship that goes into blending teas of exceptional quality. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, this book offers a delightful blend of history, culture, and the science of tea blending, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the ancient and intricate craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, History

Themes: Artistry, Craftsmanship, Cultural Appreciation
