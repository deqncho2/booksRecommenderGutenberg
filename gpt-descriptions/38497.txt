Synopsis: 
In 'Nurse and Spy in the Union Army: The Adventures and Experiences of a Woman in Hospitals, Camps, and Battle-Fields', Sarah Emma Evelyn Edmonds takes us on a captivating journey through her remarkable life as a nurse and spy during the American Civil War. Disguised as a man, she infiltrates the Union Army, risking her life to provide care for wounded soldiers and gather crucial intelligence. From the chaos of the battlefield to the secrecy of espionage, Edmonds's gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the war and the extraordinary sacrifices made by women in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Gender roles, Patriotism, Sacrifice
