Synopsis: In 'An Open Verdict: A Novel, Volume 2', the gripping sequel to Mary Elizabeth Braddon's thrilling mystery series, the enigmatic detective, Robert Audley, finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. When a shocking murder takes place in a secluded country estate, Audley must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth. As the investigation deepens, Audley's own past comes back to haunt him, forcing him to confront his own demons. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will Audley be able to solve the case before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Betrayal, Secrets and lies
