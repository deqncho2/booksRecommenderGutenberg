Synopsis: In 'The Sun-Death' by Stanley Whiteside, a renowned astrophysicist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the sun. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that could have catastrophic consequences for humanity. With time running out, he must race against the clock to prevent the sun from becoming our ultimate demise.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Existential threat, Moral responsibility
