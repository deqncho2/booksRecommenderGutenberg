Synopsis: 

'New lands within the Arctic circle: Narrative of the discoveries of the Austrian ship "Tegetthoff" in the years 1872-1874' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through uncharted territories within the Arctic circle. Julius Payer, a member of the Austrian ship's crew, recounts their daring expedition filled with treacherous ice, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with indigenous peoples. As they push the boundaries of exploration, the crew faces numerous challenges that test their resilience and determination. Payer's vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling transport readers to the frozen wilderness, leaving them eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the unexplored Arctic.

Genres: Adventure, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Survival in extreme conditions, Cultural encounters
