Synopsis: In "Florence: A Sketch-Book" by Fred Richards, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Florence. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the author takes readers on a visual exploration of the city's iconic landmarks, hidden corners, and vibrant streets. From the grandeur of the Duomo to the serenity of the Boboli Gardens, immerse yourself in the rich history, art, and culture of this timeless Italian city. As you turn each page, you'll feel as if you're strolling through the cobblestone streets, breathing in the aroma of freshly brewed espresso, and discovering the secrets that Florence holds. 

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Beauty
