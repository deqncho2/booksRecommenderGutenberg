Synopsis: In 'The Book of Good Manners; a Guide to Polite Usage for All Social Functions' by Walter Cox Green, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the intricacies of social etiquette. From formal dinners to casual gatherings, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable advice on how to navigate various social situations with grace and charm. Filled with practical tips, amusing anecdotes, and timeless wisdom, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to master the art of good manners and make a lasting impression in any social setting.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Non-fiction

Themes: Social etiquette, Politeness, Communication
