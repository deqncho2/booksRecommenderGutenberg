Synopsis: 
'Roma beata; letters from the Eternal city' by Maud Howe Elliott takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Rome. Through a series of beautifully written letters, Elliott shares her personal experiences, encounters, and reflections on the vibrant culture, rich history, and timeless beauty of the Eternal City. From exploring ancient ruins to indulging in delicious Italian cuisine, the author's vivid descriptions and heartfelt observations bring Rome to life, making readers feel as if they are strolling through its cobblestone streets themselves. With each letter, Elliott unveils the hidden gems, the captivating stories, and the profound connections she discovers in this remarkable city, leaving readers yearning to experience the magic of Rome for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Cultural exploration, History, Beauty of Rome
