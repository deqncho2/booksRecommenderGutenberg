Synopsis: 

In the Fourth Year: Anticipations of a World Peace takes readers on a thought-provoking journey into the future, where H.G. Wells envisions a world transformed by the horrors of war. Through a series of essays and reflections, Wells explores the potential for a lasting peace and the necessary steps humanity must take to achieve it. From the role of education and science to the power of collective action, this book offers a compelling vision of a world united in peace and progress.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political philosophy, Utopian literature

Themes: World peace, International cooperation, Social progress, Education, Science
