Synopsis: In 'The Twa Miss Dawsons', Margaret M. Robertson weaves a captivating tale of two sisters, Isabella and Marion Dawson, who embark on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in 19th-century Scotland. As they navigate the challenges of societal expectations, family secrets, and unexpected love, the sisters find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue that will test their loyalty and courage. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the Scottish countryside, where the bonds of sisterhood are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Sisterhood, Identity, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Deception
