Synopsis:
In "Ruth Fielding Homeward Bound; Or, A Red Cross Worker's Ocean Perils," Ruth Fielding finds herself embarking on a treacherous journey across the ocean as a Red Cross worker. As she faces the perils of the sea, she encounters unexpected dangers and must summon her courage to navigate through them. With her determination and resourcefulness, Ruth becomes a beacon of hope for those around her, proving that even in the face of adversity, compassion and bravery can prevail.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes:
Courage, Resilience, Humanitarianism
