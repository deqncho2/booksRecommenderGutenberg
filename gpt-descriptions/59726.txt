Synopsis: In 'The Fairy Green' by Rose Fyleman, a young girl named Mary discovers a hidden world of enchantment right in her own backyard. As she ventures into the magical realm of fairies, Mary encounters whimsical creatures, embarks on thrilling adventures, and learns valuable lessons about friendship and bravery. But when an unexpected danger threatens the fairy kingdom, Mary must summon all her courage to save her newfound friends and protect their home. Will she succeed in her mission and prove that even the smallest among us can make a big difference?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Imagination, the Power of Nature
