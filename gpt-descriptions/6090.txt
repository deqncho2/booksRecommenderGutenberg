Synopsis: In 'What Can She Do?' by Edward Payson Roe, a young woman named Grace is faced with a difficult decision that could change her life forever. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Grace discovers hidden talents and strengths she never knew she possessed. With the support of unexpected allies, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment, defying the limitations placed upon her by society. This captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the pursuit of personal fulfillment, and the power of resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Self-discovery, Empowerment, Societal Expectations
