Synopsis: In 'Dream Town' by Henry Slesar, a seemingly idyllic town holds a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the protagonist delves deeper into the town's mysteries, they must confront their own fears and navigate a web of deception and manipulation. With each twist and turn, the line between dreams and reality becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is truly possible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
