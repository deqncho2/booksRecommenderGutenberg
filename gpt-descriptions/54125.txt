Synopsis: 
Beyond the Old Frontier: Adventures of Indian-Fighters, Hunters, and Fur-Traders takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West. Through a collection of captivating stories, author George Bird Grinnell introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, from fearless Indian-fighters to skilled hunters and intrepid fur-traders. As we follow their adventures, we are transported to a time when the frontier was still wild and untamed, and danger lurked around every corner. From thrilling encounters with Native American tribes to heart-pounding hunts for elusive game, this book offers a vivid and immersive experience of the American frontier.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and conquest of the American West, Native American relations, Survival in the wilderness
