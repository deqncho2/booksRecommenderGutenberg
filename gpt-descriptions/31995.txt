Synopsis: In 'The Reluctant Weapon' by Howard L. Myers, a seemingly ordinary teenager named Alex discovers a hidden power within himself that he never knew existed. As he grapples with this newfound ability, he is thrust into a world of danger and intrigue, where he must navigate a web of secrets and betrayal. With the fate of his loved ones hanging in the balance, Alex must decide whether to embrace his role as a weapon or find another way to protect those he cares about.

Genres: Science Fiction, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Identity, Power, Morality, Sacrifice
