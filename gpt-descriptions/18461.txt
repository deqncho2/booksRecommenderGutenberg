Synopsis: 
In 'Six little Bunkers at Mammy June's', the adventurous Bunker children find themselves embarking on a summer vacation at their grandmother's house. Little do they know that this trip will be filled with unexpected surprises, mysterious happenings, and thrilling escapades. From exploring hidden treasures to unraveling family secrets, the Bunkers' summer becomes an unforgettable journey of discovery and excitement.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family bonds, Curiosity and exploration
