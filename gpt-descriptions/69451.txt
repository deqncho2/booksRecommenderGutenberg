Synopsis: 

'Behind the Scenes in a Restaurant: A Study of 1017 Women Restaurant Employees' takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden world of restaurant workers. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, the Consumers' League of New York City unveils the untold stories of these women, shedding light on the challenges they face, the sacrifices they make, and the resilience they embody. From the bustling kitchens to the bustling dining rooms, this eye-opening study offers a rare glimpse into the often overlooked lives of those who work tirelessly to create memorable dining experiences.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Sciences, Labor Studies

Themes: Gender inequality, Labor rights, Workplace dynamics, Social justice
