Synopsis: 

In "John Patrick, Third Marquess of Bute, K.T. (1847-1900), a Memoir," David Oswald Hunter Blair delves into the captivating life of one of Scotland's most enigmatic figures. From his privileged upbringing to his influential role in shaping the architectural landscape of Victorian Britain, this memoir unveils the untold story of John Patrick, Third Marquess of Bute. With a keen eye for detail and a wealth of historical research, Blair paints a vivid portrait of a man whose passions and eccentricities left an indelible mark on his era.

Genres: Biography, History, Architecture

Themes: Legacy, Eccentricity, Cultural Influence
