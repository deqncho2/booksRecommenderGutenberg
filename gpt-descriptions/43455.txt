Synopsis: 
In "Baseball Joe in the World Series; or, Pitching for the Championship," the talented young pitcher, Baseball Joe, finds himself facing the ultimate challenge as he competes in the highly anticipated World Series. With the hopes and dreams of his team and fans resting on his shoulders, Joe must summon all his skill, determination, and courage to navigate the intense pressure and fierce competition that awaits him on the grandest stage of baseball. As he faces formidable opponents and personal obstacles, Joe's journey in the World Series becomes a thrilling and inspiring tale of perseverance, teamwork, and the pursuit of greatness.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Overcoming challenges
