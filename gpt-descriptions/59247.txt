Synopsis: 
In 'Pee-wee Harris in Luck' by Fitzhugh, Percy Keese, young Pee-wee Harris finds himself in the middle of an unexpected adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious object. As he tries to uncover its secrets, Pee-wee's curiosity leads him on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, he encounters new friends, faces challenges, and learns valuable life lessons that will shape his character forever. Will Pee-wee's luck hold out, or will he find himself in over his head?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Self-discovery
