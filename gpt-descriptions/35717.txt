Synopsis: 
In "Luttrell Of Arran" by Charles James Lever, the reader is transported to the rugged and captivating landscapes of Scotland. The story follows the life of Roland Cashel, a young man of mysterious origins who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and adventure. As Roland navigates the treacherous waters of high society, he must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions, unexpected twists, and a cast of intriguing characters, "Luttrell Of Arran" is a thrilling tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love and Betrayal, Social Class
