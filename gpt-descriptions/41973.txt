Synopsis: 

'Yachting, Vol. 2' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the world of yachting, filled with thrilling adventures, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected challenges. This volume, written by a team of experienced authors including Dufferin and Ava, Frederick Temple Blackwood, Marquis of, Pritchett, R. T., Blake, G. L., McFerran, James, and Middleton, T. B., delves deeper into the captivating world of yachting, offering a collection of captivating stories and insights that will leave readers yearning for their own nautical escapades. From daring races to encounters with majestic marine creatures, this book immerses readers in the beauty and excitement of life at sea, providing a unique perspective on the art of yachting.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Sports

Themes: Exploration, Competition, Nature, Camaraderie
