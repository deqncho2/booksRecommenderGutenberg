Synopsis:
In 'Moor Fires' by E.H. Young, a young woman named Mary Barfoot finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of the hauntingly beautiful moors, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes:
Forbidden love, Duty vs. desire, Secrets and betrayal
