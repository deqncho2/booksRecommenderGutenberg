Synopsis: 

In 'Legal Chemistry: A Guide to the Detection of Poisons, Examination of Tea, Stains, Etc., as Applied to Chemical Jurisprudence', Alfred Naquet delves into the fascinating world of forensic chemistry. This comprehensive guide explores the intricate methods and techniques used in the detection of poisons, examination of tea, stains, and other substances, all within the context of chemical jurisprudence. Naquet's meticulous research and detailed explanations provide a captivating insight into the crucial role that chemistry plays in the field of law enforcement and justice. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Crime

Themes: Forensic science, Chemistry, Criminal investigation
