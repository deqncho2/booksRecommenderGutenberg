Synopsis: In the remote village of Gutech, a woodcutter named Hans leads a simple and content life, until one fateful day when he stumbles upon a hidden secret deep within the forest. As he unravels the mystery, Hans finds himself embarking on a perilous journey that will test his courage, challenge his beliefs, and reveal the true power of nature. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, Hans must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable dangers to protect the ancient wisdom that lies within the heart of the woods.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Nature's power, Self-discovery, Courage
