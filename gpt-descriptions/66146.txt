Synopsis: In "The Cream of the Jest: A comedy of evasions," James Branch Cabell takes readers on a whimsical journey through the life of Felix Kennaston, a dreamer and aspiring author. As Kennaston navigates the complexities of love, art, and the pursuit of happiness, he finds himself entangled in a web of illusions and deceptions. With a touch of satire and a dash of romance, this enchanting tale explores the blurred lines between reality and fantasy, challenging readers to question the nature of truth and the power of imagination.

Genres: Satire, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Artistic Expression, Love and Desire
