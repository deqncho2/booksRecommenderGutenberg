Synopsis: In 'Carnac's Folly, Volume 1' by Gilbert Parker, readers are transported to the vibrant world of Quebec in the late 19th century. The story revolves around Carnac, a mysterious and enigmatic man with a dark past, who arrives in the small town of St. Saviour. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, secrets are unveiled, love is tested, and the consequences of past actions come to light. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
