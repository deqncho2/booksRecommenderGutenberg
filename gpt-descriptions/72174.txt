Synopsis: In 'Travelogue' by Roger D. Aycock, embark on a thrilling journey through time and space as a group of explorers stumble upon a mysterious artifact that grants them the ability to travel to different dimensions. As they navigate through perilous landscapes and encounter strange civilizations, they must unravel the secrets of the artifact and confront their own inner demons. With each dimension presenting new challenges and unexpected twists, the explorers must rely on their wits and courage to survive and find their way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Survival
