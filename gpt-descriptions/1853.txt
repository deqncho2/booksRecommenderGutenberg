Synopsis: In "The Ninth Vibration and Other Stories," Lily Adams Beck takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of mystical and enchanting tales. From ancient Egypt to modern-day England, these stories explore the realms of the supernatural, delving into the mysteries of the human mind and the unseen forces that shape our lives. Each story presents a unique and thought-provoking narrative, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will leave readers spellbound. Prepare to be transported to a world where the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, and where the power of the human spirit is tested in extraordinary ways.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Spirituality, Fate, Human Potential
