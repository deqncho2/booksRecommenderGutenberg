Synopsis: 
Marjorie Dean, High School Freshman follows the journey of Marjorie Dean, a young girl who enters high school with dreams of making new friends, excelling academically, and finding her place in the world. As she navigates the challenges of high school life, Marjorie discovers the importance of loyalty, perseverance, and self-discovery. With the support of her friends and mentors, she learns valuable life lessons that shape her character and help her overcome obstacles along the way.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: 
Friendship, Identity, Resilience, Personal Growth
