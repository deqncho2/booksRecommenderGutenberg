Synopsis: 

In 'Demobilization: Our Industrial and Military Demobilization after the Armistice, 1918-1920', authors Crowell and Wilson delve into the tumultuous period following World War I, exploring the challenges and complexities of transitioning from a wartime economy to a peacetime society. Through meticulous research and compelling narratives, they shed light on the immense task of demobilization, examining its impact on both the military and industrial sectors. From the struggles of returning soldiers to the economic repercussions of dismantling war production, this book offers a captivating account of a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Economic history

Themes: Demobilization, Post-war transition, Economic impact, Social challenges
