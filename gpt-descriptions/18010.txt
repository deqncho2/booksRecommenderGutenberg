Synopsis: In "Marie Gourdon: A Romance of the Lower St. Lawrence," we are transported to the enchanting landscapes of rural Quebec, where the young and spirited Marie Gourdon finds herself torn between duty and desire. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Marie's journey unfolds against the backdrop of the breathtaking Lower St. Lawrence region, with its rugged beauty and captivating charm. With secrets to uncover and choices to be made, Marie's story is one of passion, resilience, and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Modernity, Self-Discovery
