Synopsis: In 'This World Must Die!' by H. B. Fyfe, a brilliant scientist makes a shocking discovery that threatens the very existence of humanity. As he races against time to find a solution, he uncovers a web of conspiracy and deceit that reaches the highest levels of power. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of danger and betrayal to save mankind from its own destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Existentialism, Ethics, Power and Corruption
