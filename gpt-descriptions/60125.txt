Synopsis: In 'The Winding Stair' by A. E. W. Mason, a young man named Richard Lane finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a grand estate in the English countryside. As he delves into the secrets of the house and its previous occupants, Richard uncovers a hidden past that threatens to consume him. With each twist and turn, he must navigate through treacherous alliances, unexpected revelations, and a haunting presence that seems to lurk within the very walls of the manor. Will Richard uncover the truth before it's too late, or will he become another victim of the winding stair?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets and Betrayal, Supernatural Elements
