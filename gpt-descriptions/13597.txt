Synopsis: In the small, isolated village of St. Rest, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a sense of unease and curiosity among the townspeople. As the stranger's presence begins to unravel the secrets and hidden desires of the parishioners, the once peaceful community is thrown into turmoil. With its haunting atmosphere and complex characters, "A Tale of a Lonely Parish" delves into the depths of human nature, exploring the consequences of isolation, longing, and the power of secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Isolation, Secrets, Human Nature
