Synopsis: In the year 2071, a groundbreaking discovery is made that challenges everything we know about the nature of reality. As the world grapples with the implications of this revelation, a young linguist named Eva finds herself at the center of a dangerous conspiracy. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, navigating a dystopian future where technology and power collide. As Eva delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape the destiny of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Power and Control
