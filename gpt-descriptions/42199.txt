Synopsis: In 'Winter' by Dallas Lore Sharp, a small New England town is transformed into a winter wonderland, but beneath the picturesque scenery lies a chilling secret. As the townspeople navigate the harsh winter conditions, they uncover a series of mysterious events that threaten to unravel their peaceful existence. With each passing snowfall, the tension rises, and the residents must confront their deepest fears to survive the unforgiving season.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Survival in extreme conditions
