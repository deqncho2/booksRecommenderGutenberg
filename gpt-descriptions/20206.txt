Synopsis: In "The Agony of the Church," Velimirović takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the challenges faced by the Church in the early 20th century. Through a series of poignant and insightful essays, he explores the struggles, conflicts, and triumphs of the Church, shedding light on its role in society and the spiritual battles it faces. With a blend of historical analysis, theological reflections, and personal anecdotes, Velimirović offers a compelling exploration of the Church's agony and its enduring strength.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious literature, Historical analysis

Themes: Church and society, Spiritual warfare, Faith and perseverance
