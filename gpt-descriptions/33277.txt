Synopsis:
In 'John Dene of Toronto: A Comedy of Whitehall', the reader is introduced to John Dene, a wealthy Canadian businessman who arrives in London with a bold plan to revolutionize the British Navy. However, his unconventional methods and brash personality clash with the bureaucratic and traditional Whitehall establishment. As John Dene navigates the complex world of politics, intrigue, and romance, the reader is taken on a humorous and captivating journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres:
Comedy, Political Fiction, Satire

Themes:
Innovation, Clash of Cultures, Power Dynamics
