Synopsis: 
'Zigzag Journeys in the White City. With Visits to the Neighboring Metropolis' takes readers on an exciting adventure through the bustling streets of Chicago during the World's Columbian Exposition of 1893. Join the young protagonist as they navigate the wonders of the White City, encountering fascinating exhibits, meeting historical figures, and uncovering hidden secrets along the way. With each zigzagging journey, the reader is transported to a different part of the fair, immersing themselves in the sights, sounds, and marvels of this extraordinary event.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, History, Discovery
