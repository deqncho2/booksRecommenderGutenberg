Synopsis: In "When We Were Strolling Players in the East," Louise Jordan Miln takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of traveling theater in the East. Set against the backdrop of exotic locales and cultural diversity, the story follows a group of talented actors as they navigate the challenges of their nomadic lifestyle. As they encounter love, betrayal, and unexpected friendships, the characters must confront their own desires and ambitions, ultimately discovering the transformative power of art and the enduring bonds of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Artistic Expression, Cultural Exploration, Friendship, Love
