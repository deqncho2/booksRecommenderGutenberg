Synopsis: In 'The Whole Truth and Nothing But', a renowned defense attorney finds himself caught in a web of lies and deceit when he takes on a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life and reputation. With the clock ticking and the stakes higher than ever, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and save his client, all while questioning his own moral compass.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Truth and deception, Morality and ethics, Justice and injustice
