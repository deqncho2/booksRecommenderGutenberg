Synopsis: 
In 'My Lord Duke' by E. W. Hornung, a young and ambitious journalist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is assigned to cover the scandalous affairs of a powerful and enigmatic Duke. As he delves deeper into the Duke's world, he uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to destroy not only his career but also his life. With each revelation, the journalist becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means risking everything he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Secrets and deception
