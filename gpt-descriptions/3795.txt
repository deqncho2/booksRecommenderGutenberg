Synopsis: Two young girls, Bab and Betty, stumble upon a mysterious circus performer named Ben Brown and his talented dog, Sancho, who are hiding out in a lilac bush. As the girls befriend Ben and Sancho, they embark on a series of adventures together, discovering the power of friendship, the importance of loyalty, and the joy of embracing the unexpected. Set in a quaint New England town, "Under the Lilacs" is a heartwarming tale that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Adventure, Imagination
