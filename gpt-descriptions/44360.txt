Synopsis: In "The Amenities of Book-Collecting and Kindred Affections," A. Edward Newton takes readers on a delightful journey through the world of book collecting, sharing his passion, anecdotes, and insights. From the thrill of discovering rare editions to the joy of building a personal library, Newton's witty and charming narrative explores the fascinating world of bibliophiles and the unique connections they form with their beloved books. With humor and enthusiasm, he invites readers to join him in celebrating the pleasures and quirks of book collecting, offering a glimpse into the captivating world of literary treasures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Book collecting, Bibliophilia, Personal libraries, Literary treasures, Passion for books
