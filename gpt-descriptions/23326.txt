Synopsis: In 'Mère Giraud's Little Daughter' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, readers are transported to the bustling streets of Paris, where a young girl named Rosalie lives with her mother, Mère Giraud. Despite their poverty, Rosalie's life is filled with love and joy, until a tragic event forces her to face the harsh realities of the world. Determined to protect her daughter, Mère Giraud embarks on a journey that will test their strength and resilience, leading them to unexpected places and encounters that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Poverty, Motherhood, Resilience, Love, Sacrifice
