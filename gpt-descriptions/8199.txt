Synopsis: In 'The Moon Metal' by Garrett Putman Serviss, a mysterious substance is discovered on the moon, sparking a race between nations to harness its power. As the world becomes consumed by greed and ambition, a group of scientists and adventurers embark on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the moon metal and its potential consequences. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale explores the lengths humanity will go to control a powerful and enigmatic substance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Power and Greed, Consequences of Scientific Discovery
