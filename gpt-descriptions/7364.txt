Synopsis: In 'The Albany Depot: a Farce' by William Dean Howells, chaos ensues when a group of eccentric characters find themselves stranded at a small train station in Albany. As they wait for their delayed train, their interactions become increasingly absurd and hilarious, leading to a series of unexpected events that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With witty dialogue, mistaken identities, and comical misunderstandings, this farce takes readers on a delightful journey filled with laughter and surprises.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Absurdity, Social Commentary, Human Foibles
