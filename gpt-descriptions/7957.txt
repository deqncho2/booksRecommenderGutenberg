Synopsis: 
In 'Out of the Fog: A Story of the Sea', Charles K. Ober takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the sea. The story follows a group of sailors who find themselves caught in a dense fog, leading to a series of unexpected events and life-or-death situations. As they struggle to navigate through the fog and its mysterious dangers, the crew must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will test their resilience and camaraderie. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Survival, Courage, Camaraderie, Facing the Unknown
