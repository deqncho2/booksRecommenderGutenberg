Synopsis: 

In "A History of Commerce" by Clive Day, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the fascinating evolution of trade and commerce. From the ancient civilizations of Mesopotamia and Egypt to the bustling markets of medieval Europe and the rise of global trade in the modern era, Day skillfully weaves together a tapestry of stories, anecdotes, and historical facts that bring this complex subject to life. Discover the pivotal role played by merchants, explorers, and inventors in shaping the world we know today, and gain a deeper understanding of the economic forces that have shaped societies throughout history. 

Genres: History, Economics, Non-fiction

Themes: Trade and commerce, Economic development, Globalization
