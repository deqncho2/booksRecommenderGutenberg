Synopsis: 

In "Washington in Domestic Life. From Original Letters and Manuscripts" by Richard Rush, readers are transported into the intimate world of the first President of the United States, George Washington. Through a collection of original letters and manuscripts, Rush unveils the lesser-known aspects of Washington's personal life, shedding light on his relationships, daily routines, and private thoughts. From his interactions with family and friends to his experiences as a farmer and a host, this captivating work offers a unique glimpse into the domestic life of one of America's most revered figures. 

Genres: Biography, History, Letters

Themes: Personal relationships, Daily routines, Private thoughts, Historical significance
