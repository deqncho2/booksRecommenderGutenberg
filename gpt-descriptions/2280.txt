Synopsis: 
In the small mining town of Rough-and-Ready, a mysterious stranger arrives with a fortune that could change the lives of its residents forever. As the townspeople try to uncover the source of his wealth, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, greed, and unexpected alliances. With the promise of riches and the threat of betrayal, the millionaire's presence sets off a chain of events that will test the true nature of friendship and loyalty in this captivating tale of ambition and redemption.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Wealth and its impact on society, Friendship and loyalty, Greed and its consequences
