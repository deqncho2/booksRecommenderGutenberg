Synopsis: In 'A Middy of the King: A Romance of the Old British Navy' by Collingwood, Harry, we follow the thrilling adventures of young Jack Rogers as he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and bravery in the treacherous world of the British Navy. From facing enemy ships and battling the elements to navigating complex relationships and uncovering hidden secrets, Jack's resilience and determination are put to the test. Set against the backdrop of the Old British Navy, this captivating tale will transport readers to a time of high seas, honor, and the pursuit of glory.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Identity, Honor
