Synopsis: 
In 'Major Vigoureux' by Arthur Quiller-Couch, a retired military officer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that threatens to shake the foundations of society. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face unexpected adversaries, and confront his own haunted past. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Major Vigoureux must summon all his courage and cunning to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Redemption and personal growth
