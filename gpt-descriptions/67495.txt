Synopsis: 

'From Sea to Sea; Or, Clint Webb's Cruise on the Windjammer' follows the thrilling journey of Clint Webb, a young sailor who embarks on an unforgettable adventure aboard a majestic windjammer. As the ship sails across vast oceans, Clint encounters a series of unexpected challenges and encounters, from treacherous storms to encounters with exotic cultures. Along the way, he forms deep bonds with his fellow crew members and discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of the human spirit. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
