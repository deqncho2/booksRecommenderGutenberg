Synopsis: In 'The Master-Knot of Human Fate', a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as the Master weaves together the lives of various individuals, from a young artist seeking fame to a powerful politician driven by ambition. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and destinies are forever altered. With its intricate plot and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the profound impact of our choices.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Fate and free will, Power and ambition, Identity and self-discovery
