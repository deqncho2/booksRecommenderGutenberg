Synopsis: In 'Are we ruined by the Germans?' by Harold Cox, a gripping tale unfolds as a group of individuals find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. Set against the backdrop of post-World War I Europe, the story follows a diverse cast of characters who must navigate the aftermath of war, political unrest, and personal secrets. As they grapple with their own demons, they soon discover that the Germans may hold the key to their salvation or their ultimate downfall. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the lasting impact of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Post-war trauma, Political unrest, Secrets and betrayal
