Synopsis: 
In 'Vision and Design', Roger Fry takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of art, exploring the profound impact it has on our perception and understanding of the world. From the vibrant colors of the Impressionists to the bold lines of the Cubists, Fry delves into the various artistic movements that have shaped our visual culture. Through his insightful analysis and thought-provoking anecdotes, he invites readers to question their own artistic sensibilities and discover the transformative power of art.

Genres: Art criticism, Art history, Cultural studies

Themes: Aesthetics, Perception, Artistic expression, Cultural significance
