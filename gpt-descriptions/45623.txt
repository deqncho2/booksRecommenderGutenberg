Synopsis: In 'The Old Maids' Club' by Zangwill, Israel, a group of unmarried women in their thirties forms a secret society to support each other through the challenges of singlehood. As they navigate societal expectations, romantic disappointments, and the pressures of spinsterhood, the members of the club find solace, friendship, and unexpected adventures. With wit and charm, Zangwill explores the complexities of love, independence, and the pursuit of happiness in a world that often undervalues unmarried women.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, Female empowerment, Societal expectations
