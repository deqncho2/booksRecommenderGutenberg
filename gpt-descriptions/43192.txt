Synopsis: 

'Afterwards, and Other Stories' by Maclaren, Ian is a collection of captivating short stories that will transport readers to different worlds and leave them pondering the mysteries of life. From tales of love and loss to unexpected twists and turns, each story offers a unique and thought-provoking experience. Whether it's a haunting encounter with the supernatural or a heartwarming exploration of human connections, these stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Love and loss, Human connections, Mystery, Supernatural encounters
