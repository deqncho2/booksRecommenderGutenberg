Synopsis: In "The Last Days of Mary Stuart, and the journal of Bourgoyne her physician," Samuel Cowan takes us on a gripping journey into the final days of the ill-fated Queen of Scotland, Mary Stuart. Through the eyes of her devoted physician, Bourgoyne, we witness the intense political intrigue, personal struggles, and tragic events that surround Mary's imprisonment and impending execution. As Bourgoyne's journal unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of a queen hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biographical fiction, Political thriller

Themes: Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal, Fate and destiny
