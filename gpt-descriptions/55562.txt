Synopsis:
In 'Frank Reade, Jr.'s Search for the Silver Whale: Or, Under the Ocean in the Electric "Dolphin"', the brilliant inventor Frank Reade, Jr. embarks on an extraordinary adventure beneath the ocean's depths. Equipped with his revolutionary electric submarine, the "Dolphin," Frank and his loyal crew set out to uncover the mysteries of the deep sea. As they encounter treacherous creatures, uncharted territories, and unexpected allies, the search for the elusive silver whale becomes a thrilling and perilous quest. Will Frank Reade, Jr. succeed in his mission and unlock the secrets hidden beneath the waves?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Perseverance
