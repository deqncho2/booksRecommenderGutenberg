Synopsis:
'Jones of the 64th: A Tale of the Battles of Assaye and Laswaree' follows the gripping journey of Lieutenant Richard Jones, a young officer in the British East India Company's army, during the tumultuous battles of Assaye and Laswaree in 19th-century India. As Jones faces the horrors of war, he must navigate the treacherous political landscape, forge unlikely alliances, and confront his own inner demons. With vivid descriptions of the battlefield and the complex relationships between soldiers, this thrilling historical novel explores the sacrifices, bravery, and resilience of those caught in the midst of war.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes:
War and its consequences, Loyalty and betrayal, Coming of age
