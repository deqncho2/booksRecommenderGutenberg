Synopsis: In 'The Treasure' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Margaret embarks on a journey of self-discovery when she unexpectedly inherits a mysterious treasure. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Margaret uncovers a web of hidden desires, long-buried secrets, and unexpected connections that will forever change her life. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a thrilling and emotional quest that will test her courage and challenge her perceptions of love, loyalty, and the true meaning of wealth.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Self-discovery, Love and relationships, Wealth and its consequences
