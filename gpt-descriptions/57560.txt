Synopsis: In 'Limitations: A Novel' by Benson, E. F., a young woman named Kitty inherits a mysterious old house from her late aunt. As she begins to explore the house, she discovers a hidden room that holds a collection of diaries written by her aunt. Intrigued by the secrets within the diaries, Kitty embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. Along the way, she encounters unexpected twists and turns that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront the limitations of her own beliefs.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Perception of reality
