Synopsis:
In the bustling streets of London, a group of eccentric and disillusioned intellectuals find themselves entangled in a web of unconventional relationships and hedonistic pursuits. As they navigate the chaotic landscape of post-World War I society, their lives intertwine in a series of comical and often absurd encounters. From wild parties to philosophical debates, their search for meaning and fulfillment takes them on a journey that challenges societal norms and explores the boundaries of human desires.

Genres:
Satire, Comedy, Social Criticism

Themes:
Existentialism, Modernity, Hedonism, Disillusionment
