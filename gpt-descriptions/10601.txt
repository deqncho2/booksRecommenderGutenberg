Synopsis: In 'The Rangeland Avenger' by Max Brand, a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as the Rangeland Avenger emerges from the shadows to seek justice in the lawless land of the Wild West. With his unparalleled skills and relentless determination, he becomes a force to be reckoned with, as he battles against corruption, deceit, and the dark secrets of his own past. As the Avenger's true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, his actions ignite a chain of events that will test his resolve and lead him down a treacherous path of redemption and revenge.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Identity
