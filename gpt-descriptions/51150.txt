Synopsis: In "Venus is a Man's World" by William Tenn, a groundbreaking scientific experiment transports a group of men to a planet where women are the dominant gender. As they navigate this new world, they must confront their own biases and preconceptions, leading to unexpected discoveries and challenges that will forever change their perspectives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Social Commentary, Satire

Themes: Gender Roles, Power Dynamics, Cultural Assumptions
