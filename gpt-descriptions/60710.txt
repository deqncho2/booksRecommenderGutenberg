Synopsis: 

In 'The Geology of Groton State Forest', Robert A. Christman takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through the hidden geological wonders of Groton State Forest. From towering cliffs to mysterious caves, this book unveils the captivating geological history of this enchanting forest, revealing secrets that have remained hidden for centuries. As Christman delves into the intricate details of the forest's geological formations, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of ancient rocks, glacial landscapes, and the fascinating forces that have shaped the land over millions of years. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Geology, Natural History, Environmental Conservation
