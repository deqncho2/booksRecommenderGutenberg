Synopsis: 
In 'Baseball Joe, Home Run King; or, The Greatest Pitcher and Batter on Record' by Chadwick, Lester, follow the thrilling journey of Joe Matson, a talented young baseball player with an extraordinary ability to both pitch and hit home runs. As Joe rises through the ranks of the baseball world, he faces numerous challenges and rivalries, pushing him to his limits both on and off the field. With his unwavering determination and unwavering love for the game, Joe strives to become the ultimate baseball player and secure his place in history as the home run king.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Sportsmanship, Ambition
