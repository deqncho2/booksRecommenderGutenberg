Synopsis: In "The Disowned," a young man named Ernest Maltravers finds himself caught between the allure of high society and the call of his own artistic ambitions. As he navigates the treacherous waters of love, friendship, and betrayal, Ernest must confront the consequences of his choices and discover his true identity. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this gripping tale explores the complexities of personal freedom, societal expectations, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Love and Betrayal, Social Expectations
