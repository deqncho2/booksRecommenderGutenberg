Synopsis: In 'The Last Harvest' by John Burroughs, a small farming community is plagued by a series of mysterious and devastating events that threaten their way of life. As the crops fail and the livestock fall ill, the townspeople are left desperate for answers. When a young farmer uncovers a dark secret hidden within the fields, he must confront the terrifying forces at play to save his community from destruction. With elements of suspense and supernatural intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final harvest.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Community, Survival, Secrets
