Synopsis: In the medieval town of Hirschau, a young prince embarks on a perilous journey to save his beloved from a sinister fate. Along the way, he encounters a host of fascinating characters, including a mysterious monk, a cunning sorceress, and a brave knight. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Golden Legend, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will determine the course of his destiny.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Good versus evil, Redemption, Faith and spirituality
