Synopsis: In 'Gramp' by Charles V. De Vet, a young boy named Timmy discovers a mysterious creature hiding in his grandfather's barn. As Timmy befriends the creature, he soon realizes that it possesses extraordinary abilities and a connection to his family's past. Together, they embark on an adventure filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected twists that will forever change Timmy's life.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Family bonds, Discovery, Coming of age
