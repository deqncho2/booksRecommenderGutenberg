Synopsis: In 'The Wishing Cap' by Mary Martha Sherwood, a young girl named Lucy discovers a magical cap that grants her every wish. As she embarks on a series of adventures, Lucy learns valuable lessons about the consequences of her desires and the true meaning of happiness. With each wish, she must navigate the fine line between fantasy and reality, ultimately discovering the power of selflessness and gratitude. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Consequences of Actions, Gratitude
