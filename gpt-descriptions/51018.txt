Synopsis: 
In 'Three Days in the Village, and Other Sketches', Tolstoy takes us on a captivating journey through a series of vignettes written between September 1909 and July 1910. From the bustling streets of Moscow to the serene countryside, Tolstoy's keen observations and vivid storytelling transport us to a world filled with intriguing characters and thought-provoking situations. Each sketch offers a glimpse into the lives of ordinary people, exploring themes of love, morality, social inequality, and the pursuit of happiness. As we delve into these captivating narratives, we are left pondering the complexities of human nature and the choices that shape our lives.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Morality, Social Inequality, Happiness, Human Nature
