Synopsis: In the heart of a bustling metropolis, a renowned architect unveils his latest creation - a towering structure that defies gravity and challenges the laws of physics. As the city becomes captivated by this architectural marvel, a young journalist begins to uncover a web of secrets and hidden agendas surrounding the building. With each revelation, the journalist is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue, danger, and a conspiracy that threatens to shake the very foundations of society.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, the pursuit of perfection, the consequences of unchecked ambition
