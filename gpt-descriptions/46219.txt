Synopsis: In "An Unsinkable Titanic: Every Ship its own Lifeboat" by John Bernard Walker, a gripping tale unfolds as the author delves into the untold story of the Titanic disaster. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Walker uncovers shocking secrets and reveals a groundbreaking theory that challenges everything we thought we knew about the tragic event. As the pages turn, readers will be taken on a thrilling journey that explores the hidden truths behind the sinking of the "unsinkable" ship. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Disaster, Survival, Conspiracy
