Synopsis: 

In 'Protection from Fire and Thieves', George Hayter Chubb delves into the fascinating world of security and protection. From the construction of locks, safes, and fireproof buildings to the prevention of burglary and the detection and extinction of fires, Chubb provides a comprehensive guide to safeguarding one's possessions and property. With a wealth of knowledge and practical advice, this book offers invaluable insights into the art of protection, ensuring readers can sleep soundly at night knowing their belongings are secure.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Home Improvement

Themes: Security, Protection, Safety
