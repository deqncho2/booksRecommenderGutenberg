Synopsis: In "Cuba in War Time" by Richard Harding Davis, a daring American journalist finds himself in the midst of the Cuban War of Independence, where he witnesses the struggles and sacrifices of the Cuban people fighting for their freedom. As he navigates the dangerous and unpredictable landscape, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, romance, and personal growth. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the war-torn streets of Cuba.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Love and Sacrifice
