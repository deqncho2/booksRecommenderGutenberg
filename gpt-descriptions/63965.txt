Synopsis: In 'Monster' by Joseph Samachson, a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to change the world. However, his creation soon becomes a terrifying force that threatens to unleash chaos and destruction. As the scientist races against time to stop his own creation, he must confront the moral implications of his actions and grapple with the consequences of playing god. This gripping tale of science gone wrong will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of human knowledge and the power of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ethics, Responsibility, Consequences of Scientific Advancement
