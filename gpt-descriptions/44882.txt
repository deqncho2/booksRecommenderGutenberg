Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts Through the Big Timber; Or, The Search for the Lost Tenderfoot', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling journey through the dense and mysterious Big Timber forest. When a fellow scout goes missing, they must rely on their survival skills, teamwork, and determination to find him before it's too late. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the scouts uncover secrets that will test their courage and loyalty. Will they be able to rescue their lost comrade and unravel the mysteries hidden within the Big Timber?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration, Courage
