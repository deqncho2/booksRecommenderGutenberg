Synopsis: In the small town of St. Augustine, Florida, a mysterious stranger named Horace Chase arrives, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and intriguing past. As the townspeople become increasingly fascinated by Horace, they soon discover that his presence brings both joy and turmoil, stirring up long-buried secrets and igniting unexpected passions. Set against the backdrop of the post-Civil War era, 'Horace Chase' is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption, Love and Betrayal
