Synopsis: In 'An original theory or new hypothesis of the universe', Thomas Wright presents a groundbreaking exploration of the laws of nature and mathematical principles to unravel the mysteries of the visible creation. With a particular focus on the enigmatic via lactea, or Milky Way, Wright's theory offers a fresh perspective on the general phenomena of the universe. Through his innovative approach, he captivates readers with a compelling narrative that challenges conventional wisdom and invites them to embark on a journey of discovery.

Genres: Scientific literature, Astronomy, Philosophy

Themes: Nature's laws, Mathematical principles, Cosmic phenomena, Exploration of the universe
