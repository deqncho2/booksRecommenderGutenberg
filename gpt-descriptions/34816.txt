Synopsis: In 'Notice of Runic Inscriptions Discovered during Recent Excavations in the Orkneys' by Farrer, James, a team of archaeologists stumbles upon a series of mysterious runic inscriptions during their excavation in the Orkney Islands. As they delve deeper into the meaning behind these ancient symbols, they uncover a hidden history that could change our understanding of the region forever. With each inscription leading them closer to a long-lost secret, the team must navigate treacherous obstacles and unravel the enigmatic messages before time runs out. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Archaeological Thriller

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Secrets and hidden knowledge, Adventure and exploration
