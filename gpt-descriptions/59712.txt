Synopsis: 
In 'The Floater' by Walton Bryce, a seemingly ordinary office worker finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he discovers a hidden message in a forgotten file. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of secrets and deception, where nothing is as it seems. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
