Synopsis: In 'Billy Whiskers in France' by Frances Trego Montgomery, follow the adventurous journey of Billy Whiskers, a mischievous goat, as he finds himself in the midst of World War I. From the trenches to the battlefields, Billy encounters danger, makes unexpected friends, and becomes an unlikely hero. Will Billy be able to navigate the chaos of war and find his way back home?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Historical fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
