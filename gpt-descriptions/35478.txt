Synopsis: In 'Neighbours' by Robert J. C. Stead, a seemingly ordinary suburban neighborhood becomes the backdrop for a gripping tale of secrets, suspicion, and unexpected connections. When a new family moves in next door, the lives of the residents are forever changed as they become entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the true nature of the neighbors is slowly revealed, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of everyday life.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Suspicion, Community, Identity
