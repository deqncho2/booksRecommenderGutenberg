Synopsis: 
In 'Roy Blakeley's Bee-line Hike', Roy and his friends embark on an exciting adventure that takes them deep into the wilderness. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome obstacles and reach their destination. With humor, suspense, and a touch of mystery, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Resourcefulness, Teamwork
