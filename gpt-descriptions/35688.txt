Synopsis: 
"Alice in Wonderland: A Dramatization of Lewis Carroll's "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" and "Through the Looking Glass" by Carroll, Lewis/ Gerstenberg, Alice" is a captivating adaptation of Lewis Carroll's beloved classics. Follow Alice as she tumbles down the rabbit hole into a whimsical world filled with peculiar characters and nonsensical adventures. From the Mad Hatter's tea party to the Queen of Hearts' croquet game, Alice must navigate through this enchanting realm, encountering both delightful and bizarre encounters along the way. This dramatization brings to life the imaginative and surreal world that has captivated readers for generations.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Identity, Absurdity, Coming of Age
