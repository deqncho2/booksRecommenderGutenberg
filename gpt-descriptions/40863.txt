Synopsis: In Volume 15, Slice 6 of the Encyclopaedia Britannica, 11th Edition, embark on a captivating journey through history as you explore the intriguing life of Justinian II, a controversial Byzantine emperor. Witness the rise and fall of this enigmatic ruler, as his reign is marked by political turmoil, treachery, and a quest for power. Delve into the fascinating world of the Kells, a renowned medieval manuscript that holds secrets and mysteries waiting to be unraveled. Discover the captivating stories behind these historical figures and artifacts, as you delve into the pages of this enthralling volume.

Genres: Historical, Biographical, Reference

Themes: Power and Politics, Historical Figures, Art and Manuscripts
