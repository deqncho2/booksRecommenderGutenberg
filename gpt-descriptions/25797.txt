Synopsis: In 'The School and the World', Gollancz and Somervell take readers on a captivating journey through the intertwined lives of two individuals, exploring the clash between traditional education and the ever-changing world. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their own beliefs and desires. With its richly developed characters and thought-provoking narrative, this novel delves into the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Education, Tradition vs. Modernity, Personal growth, Love and relationships
