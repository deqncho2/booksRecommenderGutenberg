Synopsis: In 'The Green Mouse' by Robert W. Chambers, a young artist named Louis Malcourt finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous world when he becomes the target of a secret society. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers a hidden realm filled with dark secrets, forbidden desires, and a sinister plot that threatens his very existence. With his life on the line, Louis must navigate a treacherous path, where nothing is as it seems, and trust becomes a deadly game.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, the blurred line between reality and illusion
