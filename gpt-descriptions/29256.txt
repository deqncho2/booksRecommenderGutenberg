Synopsis: In 'High Finance' by Otto H. Kahn, a young and ambitious investment banker finds himself entangled in a web of power, greed, and deception as he navigates the cutthroat world of high finance in New York City. With his career on the line and his personal life in turmoil, he must make difficult choices that will test his integrity and challenge his moral compass. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the financial industry, he discovers shocking secrets that could bring down the entire system. Will he succumb to the allure of wealth and power, or will he find the strength to expose the truth and fight for justice?

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Corruption, Power, Truth
