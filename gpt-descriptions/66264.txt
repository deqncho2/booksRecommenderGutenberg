Synopsis: In 'Damned: The Intimate Story of a Girl' by E. S. Dorrance, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the life of a young girl named Lily. From her idyllic childhood to the tumultuous teenage years, Lily's story is one of resilience, heartbreak, and self-discovery. As she navigates the complexities of family, love, and societal expectations, Lily's experiences will leave readers captivated and emotionally invested in her journey.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Family Dynamics, Social Expectations
