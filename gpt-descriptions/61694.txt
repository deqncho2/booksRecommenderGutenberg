Synopsis: 

In 'Expedition to Pluto', Pratt Fletcher and Laurence Manning take readers on a thrilling journey to the far reaches of our solar system. When a group of scientists and explorers embark on a daring mission to Pluto, they encounter unexpected dangers and mysteries that will test their courage and ingenuity. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter strange phenomena, the team must unravel the secrets of this distant planet and confront the unknown forces that lurk within. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
