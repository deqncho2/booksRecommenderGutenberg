Synopsis: 

David Cusick's Sketches of Ancient History of the Six Nations takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and early history of North America. Divided into three parts, the book begins with a mesmerizing tale of the foundation of the Great Island (now North America), the birth of two infants, and the creation of the universe. The second part provides a real account of the early settlers of North America and the conflicts that arose among them. Finally, the third part delves into the origin of the Kingdom of the Five Nations, known as the Long House, exploring the wars, encounters with fierce animals, and more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mythology, Native American literature

Themes: Creation myths, Cultural origins, Conflict and unity, Nature and wildlife
