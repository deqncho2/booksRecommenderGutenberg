Synopsis: 
In 'The Auction Block' by Rex Beach, a young man named John, desperate to save his family's estate from financial ruin, finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of deception and betrayal. As he navigates the treacherous world of auctions and wealthy collectors, John discovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel everything he holds dear. With each bid, the tension rises, and John must decide how far he is willing to go to protect his family's legacy. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Deception, Betrayal
