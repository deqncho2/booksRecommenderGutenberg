Synopsis: 
In 'Agesilaus' by Xenophon, follow the captivating journey of Agesilaus, the Spartan king, as he navigates the treacherous world of politics, warfare, and personal ambition. From his rise to power to his relentless pursuit of glory, this gripping tale explores the complexities of leadership, loyalty, and the eternal struggle between duty and desire. As Agesilaus faces numerous challenges and adversaries, his unwavering determination and cunning strategies will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of this legendary figure.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Military fiction

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Loyalty, Politics, Warfare
