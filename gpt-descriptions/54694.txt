Synopsis: 

'International Language and Science: Considerations on the Introduction of an International Language into Science' is a thought-provoking work co-authored by Couturat, Ostwald, Jespersen, Lorenz, and Pfaundler von Hadermur. This book delves into the fascinating realm of language and science, exploring the potential benefits and challenges of introducing an international language into the scientific community. Through a comprehensive analysis, the authors present compelling arguments and insights that will captivate readers interested in the intersection of language, communication, and scientific progress.

Genres: Non-fiction, Linguistics, Science

Themes: Language and communication, Scientific progress, International cooperation
