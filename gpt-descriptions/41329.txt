Synopsis: 

In 'Mrs. Dorriman: A Novel. Volume 1 of 3', we are introduced to the enigmatic Mrs. Dorriman, a woman of mystery and charm who captivates those around her. Set in the backdrop of 19th-century England, this novel takes us on a journey through the intricacies of society, as Mrs. Dorriman navigates the complexities of her relationships and the secrets she holds close to her heart. As the story unfolds, we are drawn into a web of intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, yearning to uncover the truth behind Mrs. Dorriman's captivating persona.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, societal expectations, the power of perception
