Synopsis: In "Rose of the World" by Castle, Egerton and Castle, Agnes, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a peculiar family heirloom. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestry, Rose discovers a hidden world filled with ancient prophecies, supernatural abilities, and a dangerous quest that could change the fate of humanity. With the help of a charismatic stranger, Rose must navigate treacherous paths and face unimaginable challenges to uncover the truth about her heritage and fulfill her destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family legacy, Destiny, Supernatural abilities, Prophecy, Identity
