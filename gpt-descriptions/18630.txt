Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Frisky Squirrel' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the adventurous journey of Frisky, a curious squirrel who embarks on a quest to discover the secrets of the forest. As Frisky encounters various woodland creatures and faces unexpected challenges, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the importance of embracing one's true nature. This heartwarming tale will captivate readers as they join Frisky on his enchanting adventure through the magical world of the forest.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Self-discovery
