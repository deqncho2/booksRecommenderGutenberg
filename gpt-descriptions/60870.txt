Synopsis: 
'Underneath the Bough: A Book of Verses' takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of human emotions and experiences. With each turn of the page, George Allan England's collection of verses unveils a tapestry of love, loss, longing, and the profound beauty found in the ordinary moments of life. From the enchantment of nature to the complexities of the human heart, these verses paint vivid portraits that resonate with readers, evoking a range of emotions and leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Romance, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Nature, Human Emotions, Transcendence
