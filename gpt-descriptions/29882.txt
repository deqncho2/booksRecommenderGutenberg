Synopsis: In the October 1930 edition of 'Astounding Stories of Super-Science', prepare to be transported to a world where scientific wonders and extraordinary adventures await. From a thrilling tale of a man who discovers a hidden civilization beneath the Earth's surface, to a mind-bending story of a scientist who invents a device that can manipulate time itself, this collection of stories will leave you on the edge of your seat. Brace yourself for mind-boggling inventions, otherworldly encounters, and the boundless possibilities of super-science!

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of unknown realms, the power of scientific discovery, the limits of human imagination
