Synopsis:
In the captivating novel "Susan Proudleigh" by Herbert George De Lisser, readers are transported to the vibrant island of Jamaica during the early 20th century. The story revolves around the life of Susan Proudleigh, a strong-willed and determined young woman who defies societal expectations and challenges the status quo. As Susan navigates her way through a world filled with racial tensions, political upheaval, and personal struggles, she becomes a symbol of resilience and empowerment. With vivid descriptions of the Jamaican landscape and a cast of unforgettable characters, "Susan Proudleigh" is a compelling tale of love, ambition, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Racial identity, Gender roles, Social justice, Resilience
