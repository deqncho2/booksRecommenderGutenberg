Synopsis: In 'The Soul Scar: A Craig Kennedy Scientific Mystery Novel', renowned detective Craig Kennedy finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a young woman is found dead with a mysterious mark on her forehead. As Kennedy delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives that lead him to question the very nature of the human soul. With his scientific expertise and sharp intuition, Kennedy must navigate a world of deceit and intrigue to uncover the truth behind the soul scar.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Science and technology, Identity and consciousness, Deception and manipulation
