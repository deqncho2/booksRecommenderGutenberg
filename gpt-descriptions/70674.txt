Synopsis: 

In "The Glamour of Prospecting," follow the captivating journey of a South African prospector as he embarks on a thrilling quest for precious minerals such as copper, gold, emeralds, and diamonds. Fred C. Cornell takes readers on a mesmerizing adventure through the untamed landscapes of Africa, where danger lurks at every turn and the allure of striking it rich is irresistible. From treacherous encounters with wildlife to encounters with fellow prospectors, this gripping narrative unveils the highs and lows of the prospector's life, filled with excitement, uncertainty, and the constant pursuit of fortune.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Risk-taking, Wealth and Ambition
