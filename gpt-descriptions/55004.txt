Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 18, August 21, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in the heart of a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the line between reality and the supernatural becomes blurred. With each turn of the page, the tension builds, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and deception, the thin line between reality and the supernatural, the power of fear and paranoia
