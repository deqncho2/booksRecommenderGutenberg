Synopsis: 
In 'Travels in England in 1782', Moritz, Karl Philipp takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and bustling cities of England. Through his vivid descriptions and keen observations, Moritz provides a unique perspective on the country's culture, society, and politics during this pivotal period. From encounters with eccentric characters to unexpected adventures, this travelogue offers a fascinating glimpse into the heart of England, leaving readers yearning to explore its rich history and vibrant cities for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Social commentary, Political observations
