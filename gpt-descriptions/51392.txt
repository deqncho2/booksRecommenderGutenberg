Synopsis:
In 'Hafiz in London' by Justin H. McCarthy, a young Persian poet named Hafiz embarks on a journey to London, leaving behind his homeland and everything he knows. As he navigates the bustling city, Hafiz encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own stories and struggles. Through his interactions, Hafiz begins to question his own identity and purpose, ultimately leading him on a path of self-discovery and transformation. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and multicultural London, this novel explores themes of cultural clash, personal growth, and the universal search for meaning.

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Multicultural

Themes:
Cultural Identity, Self-Discovery, Transformation
