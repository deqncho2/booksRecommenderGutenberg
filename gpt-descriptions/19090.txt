Synopsis: In 'Star Hunter' by Andre Norton, a skilled hunter named Ras Hume embarks on a dangerous mission to track down a group of fugitives hiding on an alien planet. As he navigates treacherous terrain and encounters mysterious creatures, Ras soon realizes that he is not the only one hunting the fugitives. With his survival at stake, Ras must uncover the truth behind the fugitives' secrets and confront his own past. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
