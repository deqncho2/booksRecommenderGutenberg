Synopsis: 

In "American Renaissance: A Review of Domestic Architecture," Joy Wheeler Dow takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of American architecture. From the grandeur of the Gilded Age to the simplicity of the Arts and Crafts movement, Dow explores the diverse styles and influences that shaped the nation's architectural landscape. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the book unveils the stories behind iconic buildings and the visionary architects who brought them to life. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover a rich tapestry of architectural marvels that reflect the spirit and aspirations of a nation in constant transformation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, History

Themes: American identity, Architectural evolution, Cultural influences
