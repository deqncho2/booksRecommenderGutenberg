Synopsis: In 'The Runaway Bunny' by Smith, Laura Rountree, a young bunny dreams of exploring the world beyond his mother's watchful eye. Determined to break free, he embarks on a whimsical adventure, encountering various obstacles along the way. As the bunny tries to outsmart his mother's love and protection, their bond is tested in unexpected ways. This heartwarming tale explores the universal theme of a parent's unconditional love and the lengths they will go to keep their child safe.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book

Themes: Love, Family, Adventure
