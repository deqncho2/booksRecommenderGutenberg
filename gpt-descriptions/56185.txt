Synopsis: In 'The Abounding American' by T. W. H. Crosland, a young American man named John embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the enchanting landscapes of Europe. As he navigates through the complexities of love, friendship, and ambition, John finds himself torn between his American roots and the allure of the Old World. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel explores the transformative power of travel and the universal quest for identity and belonging.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Travel

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Love, Ambition
