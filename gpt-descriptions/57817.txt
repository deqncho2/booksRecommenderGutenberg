Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Secret Journal' by Morison, Daniel, a renowned doctor's hidden journal is discovered, revealing shocking secrets that could change everything. As the pages unfold, the reader is drawn into a world of mystery, intrigue, and moral dilemmas. With each entry, the doctor's past and present collide, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Morality and ethics, Redemption
