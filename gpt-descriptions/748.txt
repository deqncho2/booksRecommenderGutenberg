Synopsis: In 'The Brother of Daphne' by Yates, Dornford, a young man named Richard Chandos finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he receives a cryptic message from his estranged sister, Daphne. As Richard embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind his sister's disappearance, he becomes immersed in a world of secret societies, hidden identities, and dangerous alliances. With each clue he uncovers, Richard's own life becomes increasingly endangered, forcing him to confront his own past and make choices that will determine the fate of those he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
