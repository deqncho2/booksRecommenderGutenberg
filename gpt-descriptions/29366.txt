Synopsis: 
In 'The Prisoner' by Alice Brown, a small New England town is captivated by the arrival of a mysterious stranger who takes up residence in an abandoned house. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about the enigmatic figure, they soon discover that his presence holds a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of their community. Told through the eyes of multiple characters, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature, the power of secrets, and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets, Community, Human Nature
