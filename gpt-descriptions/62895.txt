Synopsis: 
'Cartoons by McCutcheon: A selection of one hundred drawings' by John T. McCutcheon is a delightful collection of witty and insightful cartoons that will captivate readers of all ages. From political satire to everyday observations, McCutcheon's drawings offer a humorous and thought-provoking commentary on society. Each cartoon is a window into the artist's unique perspective, inviting readers to laugh, reflect, and appreciate the art of visual storytelling.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Art

Themes: Social commentary, Observational humor
