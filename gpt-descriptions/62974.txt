Synopsis: In "Benjamin Franklin" by McKown, Robin, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most influential figures. From his humble beginnings as a young printer's apprentice to his remarkable achievements as a statesman, inventor, and philosopher, this biography delves into the fascinating world of Benjamin Franklin. Discover the secrets behind his insatiable curiosity, his tireless pursuit of knowledge, and his unwavering dedication to the ideals of freedom and progress. Uncover the untold stories and lesser-known aspects of Franklin's life that shaped the course of history, making him an enduring symbol of the American dream.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Knowledge, Freedom, Progress
