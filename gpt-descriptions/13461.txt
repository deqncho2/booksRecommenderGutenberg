Synopsis: 

In 'Mistress and Maid: A Household Story' by Dinah Maria Mulock Craik, we are introduced to two women from different social classes who find themselves bound together by circumstance. The story follows the lives of Miss Rosamond and her maid, Lucy, as they navigate the complexities of their roles in Victorian society. As their relationship evolves, secrets are revealed, and unexpected alliances are formed, challenging the rigid social norms of the time. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Social class, Female friendship, Power dynamics, Identity, Love and loyalty
