Synopsis: In the remote Pacific island of Melanesia, a young anthropologist named David tries to unravel the mysteries surrounding the Lani people, a tribe with a unique and enigmatic culture. As David immerses himself in their world, he becomes captivated by their customs, rituals, and the profound connection they have with nature. However, as he delves deeper, he uncovers a shocking secret that challenges everything he thought he knew about the Lani people and forces him to question his own beliefs and values.

Genres: Anthropological fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Belief systems, Environmentalism
