Synopsis: In 'A Strange Story - Volume 01', the reader is introduced to a young doctor named Allan Fenwick, who becomes entangled in a mysterious and supernatural world. As he delves deeper into his research on mesmerism, he encounters a mesmerist named Margrave, whose powers seem to surpass anything Fenwick has ever witnessed. Drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, Fenwick must navigate the blurred lines between science and the occult, as he uncovers dark secrets and faces unimaginable consequences.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Science vs. the occult, the power of the mind, the boundaries of reality, the pursuit of knowledge
