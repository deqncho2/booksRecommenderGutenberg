Synopsis: In 'The Girl of the Period, and Other Social Essays, Vol. 2', Elizabeth Lynn Linton delves into the complexities of Victorian society, exploring the lives of women and the challenges they face. Through a collection of thought-provoking essays, Linton examines the role of women in society, the impact of fashion and trends, and the struggles faced by those who dare to defy societal norms. With her sharp wit and insightful observations, Linton offers a captivating exploration of the social dynamics of the era.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social commentary, Feminist literature

Themes: Gender roles, Women's rights, Social expectations, Fashion and trends
