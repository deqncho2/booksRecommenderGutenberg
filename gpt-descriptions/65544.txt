Synopsis: In "The Assassination of Christopher Marlowe (A New View)" by Samuel A. Tannenbaum, a gripping historical mystery unfolds as the renowned playwright Christopher Marlowe is found dead under suspicious circumstances. As the investigation delves into the seedy underbelly of Elizabethan England, a web of political intrigue, espionage, and forbidden love is revealed. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot that keeps readers guessing until the very end, this novel offers a fresh perspective on the untimely demise of one of literature's most enigmatic figures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Political intrigue, Espionage, Forbidden love
