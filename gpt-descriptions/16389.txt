Synopsis: Four dissatisfied women from different walks of life embark on a life-changing journey to an enchanting Italian villa. As they immerse themselves in the beauty of the surroundings, they discover unexpected friendships, newfound passions, and the transformative power of love and self-discovery. With each passing day, the women's lives intertwine in ways they never could have imagined, leading them to question their own desires and the meaning of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Women's Literature

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Transformation, Escapism, Love
