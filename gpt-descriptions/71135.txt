Synopsis: In "An Englishwoman's adventures in the German lines," Ann Gladys Lloyd takes readers on a captivating journey through her extraordinary experiences during World War I. As an Englishwoman living in Germany, she finds herself caught in the midst of the conflict, facing danger, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. From daring escapes to undercover missions, Lloyd's gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the war and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Identity, Conflict
