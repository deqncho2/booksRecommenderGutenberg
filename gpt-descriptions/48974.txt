Synopsis: In 'As Others Saw Him: A Retrospect, A.D. 54', Joseph Jacobs takes us on a captivating journey through time to explore the enigmatic figure of Jesus Christ. Drawing from a wide range of historical sources and ancient texts, Jacobs presents a thought-provoking analysis of how Jesus was perceived by those who lived during his time. From the awe-inspiring to the skeptical, the adoring to the critical, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse perspectives on one of history's most influential figures. As we delve into the minds of those who witnessed Jesus firsthand, we are left pondering the profound impact he had on the world and the enduring questions surrounding his life and teachings.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Biography

Themes: Perception of historical figures, Religious beliefs and interpretations
