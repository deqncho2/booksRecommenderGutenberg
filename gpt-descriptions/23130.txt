Synopsis: In 'Black, White and Gray: A Story of Three Homes' by Amy Walton, three families find their lives unexpectedly intertwined when they are forced to confront their own prejudices and preconceptions. As secrets are revealed and relationships tested, the characters must navigate the complexities of love, forgiveness, and the true meaning of home. This captivating tale explores the power of empathy and the transformative nature of human connections.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family

Themes: Prejudice, Forgiveness, Home
