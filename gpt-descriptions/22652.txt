Synopsis: In "A Campfire Girl's Test of Friendship" by Jane L. Stewart, a group of young girls embark on a thrilling adventure that tests the strength of their friendship. When a mysterious treasure map is discovered, the girls must work together to navigate through challenges, solve riddles, and overcome obstacles in order to uncover the hidden secrets. As they face unexpected dangers and unexpected alliances, their bonds are put to the ultimate test. Will their friendship prevail, or will the quest for treasure tear them apart?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Teamwork, Discovery
