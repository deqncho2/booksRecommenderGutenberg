Synopsis: In 'An Enquiry into the Truth of the Tradition, Concerning the Discovery of America, by Prince Madog ab Owen Gwynedd, about the Year, 1170' by John Williams, a fascinating investigation unfolds as the author delves into the mysterious tale of Prince Madog, a Welsh prince who allegedly discovered America in the 12th century. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Williams explores the evidence, legends, and historical accounts surrounding this enigmatic figure, taking readers on a captivating journey through time and across continents.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Adventure

Themes: Discovery, Legends and folklore, Historical authenticity
