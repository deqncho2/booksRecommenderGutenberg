Synopsis: 
In "Persephone of Eleusis: A Romance of Ancient Greece" by Clare Winger Harris, readers are transported to the enchanting world of ancient Greece. The story follows Persephone, a young woman living in the city of Eleusis, as she embarks on a journey that will forever change her life. Drawn into a web of love, betrayal, and divine intervention, Persephone finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her own desires. As she navigates the treacherous paths of power and passion, Persephone discovers hidden truths about herself and the gods that will challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mythology

Themes: Love and Desire, Fate and Destiny, Power and Betrayal
