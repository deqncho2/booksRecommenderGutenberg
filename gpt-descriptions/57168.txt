Synopsis: 
"The Land Beyond the Forest: Facts, Figures, and Fancies from Transylvania" takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting region of Transylvania. Written by Gerard, E. (Emily), this book delves into the rich history, folklore, and natural beauty of the land, offering a unique blend of facts, personal anecdotes, and imaginative tales. From the haunting legends of Dracula to the breathtaking landscapes of the Carpathian Mountains, this book paints a vivid picture of Transylvania's allure, inviting readers to explore its hidden treasures and unravel its secrets.

Genres: Travel, History, Folklore

Themes: Myth and Legend, Cultural Identity, Natural Beauty
