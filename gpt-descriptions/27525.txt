Synopsis: In 'Bones in London' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious and cunning criminal mastermind known as "The Whisperer" is wreaking havoc in the city. As Detective Inspector Elk and his team race against time to catch the elusive villain, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists. With the fate of London hanging in the balance, Elk must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Deception and manipulation
