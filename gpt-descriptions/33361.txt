Synopsis: In "Ozma of Oz," Dorothy Gale finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz, but this time she is accompanied by a yellow hen named Billina. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with encounters with familiar friends like the Scarecrow, the Tin Woodman, and the Cowardly Lion, as well as new companions like Tiktok and the Hungry Tiger. As they journey through the fantastical landscapes of Oz, Dorothy and her friends face numerous challenges and dangers, all while trying to find a way to rescue the royal family of Ev from the clutches of the wicked Nome King. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Overcoming Challenges
