Synopsis: 

In "Mushroom and Toadstools: How to Distinguish Easily the Differences Between Edible and Poisonous Fungi," Smith, Worthington George takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of mushrooms and toadstools. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, this book serves as a comprehensive guide to help both novice and experienced foragers identify the subtle nuances that distinguish edible fungi from their poisonous counterparts. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover the secrets of mushroom hunting, learn about the various habitats where these fungi thrive, and discover the crucial characteristics that can mean the difference between a delicious meal and a dangerous mistake. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Reference

Themes: Fungal identification, Foraging, Nature appreciation
