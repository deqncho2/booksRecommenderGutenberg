Synopsis: 
When Mother Lets Us Give a Party is a delightful guidebook that offers young readers valuable tips and ideas on how to host the perfect party for their friends. Written by Elsie Duncan Yale, this charming book is filled with creative suggestions and practical advice that will inspire children to become the ultimate party planners. From choosing the right theme and decorations to planning exciting games and activities, this book is a treasure trove of fun-filled ideas that will make any party a memorable event. Whether it's a birthday celebration, a holiday gathering, or just a spontaneous get-together, this book is the go-to resource for young hosts and hostesses looking to entertain and amuse their little friends.

Genres: Children's Literature, Non-fiction, How-to Guide

Themes: Party Planning, Creativity, Friendship, Childhood
