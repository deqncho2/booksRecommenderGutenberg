Synopsis: In 'The Invaders' by Benjamin Ferris, a seemingly idyllic coastal town is disrupted when a mysterious family returns after a long absence. As tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, the community is forced to confront their own dark pasts and question the true nature of loyalty and belonging. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and betrayal, Identity and belonging
