Synopsis: In 'The Case of Summerfield' by W.H. Rhodes, a renowned detective is called upon to investigate a mysterious disappearance in the small town of Summerfield. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that lead him down a path he never could have anticipated. With each clue he uncovers, the detective finds himself drawn further into the dark underbelly of the town, where nothing is as it seems and danger lurks around every corner. Will he be able to solve the case and bring justice to Summerfield, or will the truth remain forever hidden?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Small-town secrets, Uncovering the truth
