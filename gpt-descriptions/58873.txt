Synopsis: In 'Skewbald, the New Forest Pony' by Allen W. Seaby, a young girl named Emily discovers a mysterious pony with a unique coat pattern in the enchanting New Forest. As she forms a deep bond with Skewbald, they embark on a thrilling adventure together, uncovering secrets and facing unexpected challenges. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the beauty of nature, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Nature, Resilience
