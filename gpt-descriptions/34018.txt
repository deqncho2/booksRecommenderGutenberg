Synopsis: 

In the second volume of the 11th edition of the Encyclopaedia Britannica, readers are taken on a captivating journey from "Anjar" to "Apollo." This section delves into a wide range of fascinating topics, exploring the ancient city of Anjar and its historical significance, before delving into the realm of Greek mythology and the god Apollo. From the architectural wonders of Anjar to the mythical tales surrounding Apollo, this volume offers a rich tapestry of knowledge and discovery.

Genres: Reference, History, Mythology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Greek mythology, Architecture
