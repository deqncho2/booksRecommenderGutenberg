Synopsis: In 'The Good Englishwoman' by Williams, Orlo, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy during World War II. As she navigates the treacherous waters of espionage and betrayal, Emily must make difficult choices that will test her loyalty and courage. With the fate of her country hanging in the balance, she becomes a key player in a high-stakes game of secrets and lies. Will Emily be able to outwit her enemies and protect those she holds dear, or will she become another casualty of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Betrayal, Sacrifice
