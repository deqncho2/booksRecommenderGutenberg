Synopsis: 
In "Earthbound" by Guth, Henry, a young woman named Emma discovers a mysterious artifact that transports her to a parallel world filled with captivating wonders and dangerous secrets. As she navigates this unfamiliar realm, Emma must unravel the enigmatic origins of the artifact and confront her own inner demons. With the fate of both worlds hanging in the balance, Emma embarks on a thrilling journey of self-discovery, forging unexpected alliances and facing unimaginable challenges along the way.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: 
Identity, Exploration, Redemption
