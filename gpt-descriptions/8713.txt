Synopsis:
'A Man of Means' is a delightful collection of interconnected short stories that follow the misadventures of a wealthy and eccentric gentleman named James Ramsden. From his encounters with a mysterious stranger to his attempts at matchmaking, Ramsden's life is filled with hilarious escapades and unexpected twists. As the stories unfold, readers are taken on a whimsical journey through the world of high society, where money can't always buy happiness.

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes:
Wealth and its consequences, Love and relationships, Social class
