Synopsis: In the gripping historical novel "Rookwood," secrets, betrayal, and a mysterious curse intertwine to create a thrilling tale of love, revenge, and the pursuit of justice. Set in 18th-century England, the story follows a young man named Luke Rookwood, who finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he becomes the target of a notorious gang of highwaymen. As Luke delves deeper into the dark secrets of his family's past, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change his life forever. With its vivid descriptions, unexpected twists, and a cast of intriguing characters, "Rookwood" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Revenge, Justice
