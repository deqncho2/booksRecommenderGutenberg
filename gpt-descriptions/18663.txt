Synopsis: In the March 10, 1898 issue of "The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It," young readers are taken on an exciting journey through the pages of a weekly magazine filled with captivating stories, intriguing news, and fascinating facts. From thrilling adventures to thought-provoking articles, this issue offers a diverse range of content that will keep boys and girls eagerly turning the pages, discovering new worlds and expanding their knowledge. Delve into this captivating magazine and join the adventure!

Genres: Children's literature, Magazine, Non-fiction

Themes: Adventure, Education, Curiosity, Exploration
