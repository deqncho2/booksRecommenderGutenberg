Synopsis: In the final book of the New Testament, 'The Bible, King James version, Book 64: 3 John' takes readers on a journey of faith, friendship, and the power of truth. Through a series of letters, the book explores the struggles faced by a devoted follower of Christ named Gaius, as he navigates the challenges of maintaining his faith in a world filled with deception and false teachings. As Gaius seeks guidance and support from his mentor, the apostle John, he discovers the importance of standing firm in his beliefs and the transformative power of love and hospitality. This thought-provoking book offers a glimpse into the early Christian community and the timeless lessons it holds for believers today.

Genres: Religious literature, Epistolary fiction

Themes: Faith, Truth, Friendship, Hospitality
