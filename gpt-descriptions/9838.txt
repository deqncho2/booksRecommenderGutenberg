Synopsis: In 'Strong Hearts' by George Washington Cable, a young woman named Claire finds herself caught between two worlds - the vibrant and diverse city of New Orleans and the strict traditions of her family's plantation. As she navigates the complexities of love, race, and societal expectations, Claire must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the status quo. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Cable weaves a captivating tale of identity, resilience, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love, Race, Tradition, Social Expectations
