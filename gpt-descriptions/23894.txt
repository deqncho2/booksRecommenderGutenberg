Synopsis: 
In 'Billie Bradley at Three Towers Hall; Or, Leading a Needed Rebellion' by Janet D. Wheeler, Billie finds herself in a new boarding school, Three Towers Hall, where she quickly realizes that the strict rules and oppressive atmosphere are not what they seem. Determined to uncover the truth, Billie forms an unlikely alliance with a group of rebellious classmates and together they embark on a mission to challenge the authority and bring about much-needed change. As they navigate secret passages, hidden clues, and unexpected friendships, Billie discovers her own strength and courage, leading her to become a leader in a rebellion that will forever change Three Towers Hall.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Rebellion, Friendship, Courage, Coming of Age
