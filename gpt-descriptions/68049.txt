Synopsis: 
'Book of Detective Stories' is a captivating collection of thrilling tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From mysterious disappearances to perplexing murders, each story presents a unique puzzle for the brilliant detectives to solve. As the pages turn, readers will be immersed in a world of suspense, clues, and unexpected twists, where nothing is as it seems. With each story, the tension builds, leading to a satisfying conclusion that will leave readers craving for more.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Investigation, Deception, Justice
