Synopsis: 

In 'Round the year with the stars: The chief beauties of the starry heavens as seen with the naked eye', Garrett Putman Serviss takes readers on a captivating journey through the celestial wonders that can be observed without the aid of a telescope. From the mesmerizing constellations that grace the night sky to the awe-inspiring phenomena such as shooting stars and eclipses, Serviss expertly guides readers in discovering the hidden secrets and enchanting beauty of the stars. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, this book is a delightful exploration of the celestial realm that will leave readers in awe of the wonders that lie just beyond our reach.

Genres: Non-fiction, Astronomy, Science

Themes: Wonder and awe of the universe, Exploration and discovery of the stars, Appreciation of natural beauty
