Synopsis: In 'The professor's experiment: A novel, Vol. 1 (of 3)', Duchess takes readers on a thrilling journey into the mysterious world of a brilliant professor and his groundbreaking experiment. As the professor delves deeper into his research, he uncovers secrets that challenge his own beliefs and push the boundaries of science. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the consequences of playing with the unknown and the power of human curiosity. 

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Scientific exploration, Morality, Consequences of curiosity
