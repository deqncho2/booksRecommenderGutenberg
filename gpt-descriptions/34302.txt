Synopsis: In 'The Operatic Problem' by William Johnson Galloway, a renowned opera singer finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic message from an anonymous admirer. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she discovers a dark secret that threatens not only her career but also her life. With time running out, she must navigate the treacherous world of opera and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Obsession, Deception, Identity
