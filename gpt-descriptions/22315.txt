Synopsis:
In the rugged and treacherous landscape of Exmoor, a young man named John Ridd finds himself entangled in a dangerous feud between his family and the notorious Doone clan. As he navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and revenge, John becomes determined to bring justice to those who have wronged him. With his unwavering determination and the help of unexpected allies, he embarks on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and resilience, leading to a climactic showdown that will forever change his life.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Revenge, Love and Loyalty, Justice and Redemption
