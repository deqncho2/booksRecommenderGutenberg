Synopsis:
In 'The Moving Picture Girls Snowbound: Or, The Proof on the Film' by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous and talented Moving Picture Girls find themselves trapped in a remote cabin during a snowstorm. With no way to escape, they stumble upon a mysterious film reel that holds a shocking secret. As they unravel the truth behind the film, they must confront unexpected dangers and make difficult choices that could change their lives forever. Will they be able to escape the cabin and expose the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
