Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt; or, The King of the Wheel: Motor Stories Thrilling Adventure Motor Fiction No 1,' readers are introduced to the daring and resourceful Motor Matt, a young man with a passion for automobiles and a knack for solving mysteries. When a valuable artifact goes missing, Motor Matt finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure that takes him across the country, testing his skills and courage at every turn. As he races against time to uncover the truth, Motor Matt must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart cunning adversaries, and rely on his quick thinking and mechanical expertise to save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Pursuit of Justice
