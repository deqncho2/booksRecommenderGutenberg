Synopsis: In 'Z' by Charles L. Fontenay, a young scientist named Zachary is thrust into a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden message from an alien civilization. As he deciphers the message, he becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing mysterious enemies and uncovering shocking secrets that could change the fate of humanity forever. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Zachary must navigate a treacherous path filled with intrigue, suspense, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Exploration, Conspiracy, Survival
