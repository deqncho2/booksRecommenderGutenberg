Synopsis: In 'The Conquest of America: A Romance of Disaster and Victory, U.S.A., 1921 A.D.', Moffett, Cleveland takes readers on a thrilling journey through an alternate version of America in the early 20th century. As the country faces a series of catastrophic events, including natural disasters and political upheaval, a diverse group of individuals must navigate the chaos and find a way to save their nation. With a mix of adventure, suspense, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of unity in the face of adversity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Unity, Political Turmoil
