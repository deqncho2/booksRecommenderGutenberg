Synopsis: 
In 'A Hundred Anecdotes of Animals', Percy J. Billinghurst takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of animals. From mischievous monkeys to loyal dogs and cunning cats, this collection of anecdotes offers a delightful glimpse into the lives and behaviors of various creatures. Each anecdote is filled with humor, heartwarming moments, and unexpected twists, making it impossible to put the book down. Whether it's a tale of bravery, friendship, or sheer hilarity, these stories will leave readers both entertained and enlightened about the incredible diversity of the animal kingdom.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Animal Stories, Humor

Themes: 
Animal behavior, Friendship, Humor
