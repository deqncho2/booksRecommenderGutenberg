Synopsis: In 'The Race of the Swift' by Edwin Carlile Litsey, a young and talented jockey named Jack finds himself caught in a high-stakes horse race that will test his skills, courage, and determination. As he faces formidable opponents and unexpected obstacles, Jack must navigate a treacherous path to victory, all while uncovering the secrets and betrayals that surround him. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the thrilling race and the personal journey of a young man striving to prove himself in a world where winning means everything.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Mystery

Themes: Perseverance, Betrayal, Identity
