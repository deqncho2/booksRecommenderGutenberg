Synopsis: 
'Under the Chinese Dragon: A Tale of Mongolia' takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the vast and mysterious landscapes of Mongolia. Set during the early 20th century, the story follows a group of explorers who find themselves entangled in a web of political intrigue, ancient legends, and the pursuit of hidden treasures. As they navigate the treacherous terrain and encounter fascinating characters, they must unravel the secrets that lie beneath the surface of the Chinese Dragon. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of the explorers and the future of Mongolia hang in the balance.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Political Intrigue, Cultural Heritage
