Synopsis: 

In "The Geologic Story of the Great Plains," Donald E. Trimble takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unraveling the mysteries behind the formation and transformation of the vast and awe-inspiring landscape of the Great Plains. With a skillful blend of scientific knowledge and storytelling, Trimble paints a vivid picture of the geological forces that have shaped this remarkable region, from ancient seas and towering mountains to the gradual erosion and deposition processes that have sculpted the land we see today. Through engaging narratives and accessible language, Trimble invites readers to explore the fascinating geologic history of the Great Plains, providing a deeper understanding of the forces that have shaped our planet.

Genres: Nonfiction, Science, Geology

Themes: Geologic history, Landscape formation, Natural forces, Environmental change
