Synopsis: In "Max Havelaar; or, the coffee auctions of the Dutch trading company," we follow the journey of Max Havelaar, a Dutch colonial administrator stationed in the Dutch East Indies. Faced with the corruption and exploitation of the local population by his fellow countrymen, Max Havelaar becomes determined to fight for justice and expose the truth. Through a series of captivating events, the story unfolds, revealing the harsh realities of colonialism and the power of one individual's quest for righteousness.

Genres: Historical fiction, social criticism, political satire

Themes: Colonialism, exploitation, corruption, justice, morality
