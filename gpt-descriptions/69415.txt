Synopsis: In "The Spirit of '76: Some Recollections of the Artist and the Painting," Henry Kelsey Devereux takes readers on a captivating journey through the creation of one of America's most iconic paintings. Through vivid recollections and personal anecdotes, Devereux unveils the untold story behind the famous artwork, shedding light on the artist's inspiration, struggles, and the historical context that shaped this masterpiece. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported to a pivotal moment in American history, where patriotism, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of the nation converge in a single painting.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, American history, Artistic inspiration, National identity
