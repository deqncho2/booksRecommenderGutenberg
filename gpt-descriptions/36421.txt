Synopsis: In 'By Veldt and Kopje' by W. C. Scully, a young Englishman named Jack Raymond embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed African wilderness. Drawn to the allure of the African landscape, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger, romance, and mystery. As he navigates through treacherous terrain and encounters fascinating characters, Jack's journey becomes a test of courage, resilience, and self-discovery. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world where the beauty and brutality of nature collide.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Love and Loss
