Synopsis: 
In "Ancient China Simplified" by Edward Harper Parker, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history of ancient China. From the legendary Yellow Emperor to the powerful dynasties that shaped the nation, this book unveils the fascinating tales of emperors, warriors, philosophers, and inventors. Delve into the mysteries of the Great Wall, the art of calligraphy, and the intricate world of Chinese medicine. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Parker brings to life the vibrant civilization that laid the foundation for modern China. Prepare to be enthralled by the wonders and complexities of Ancient China!

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Chinese civilization, Dynasties, Cultural heritage
