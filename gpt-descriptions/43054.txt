Synopsis: In the final volume of the thrilling Donald Ross of Heimra series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. As Donald Ross navigates the treacherous world of politics and power, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape the future of Heimra. With danger lurking at every turn, will Donald be able to protect those he loves and secure the legacy of his family? Prepare for a gripping conclusion that will leave you on the edge of your seat.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Family Saga

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Redemption and sacrifice
