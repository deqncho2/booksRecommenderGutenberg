Synopsis: In 'The Court of Boyville' by William Allen White, a small town is turned upside down when a group of mischievous boys form their own court to dispense justice. As the boys take on the roles of judge, jury, and executioner, their hilarious antics and unexpected wisdom challenge the adults' notions of authority and power. Amidst the chaos, a young lawyer finds himself caught between the boys' wild imagination and the town's desire for order. This heartwarming and humorous tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of imagination, and the complexities of justice in a small community.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Justice, Authority
