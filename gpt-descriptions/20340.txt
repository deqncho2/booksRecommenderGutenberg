Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid of Old Maine', follow the captivating journey of young Priscilla as she navigates the challenges and adventures of life in colonial Maine. From encounters with Native Americans to unexpected friendships, Priscilla's courage and determination will inspire readers of all ages. Will she be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in her way and find her place in this new world?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Identity, Cultural diversity
