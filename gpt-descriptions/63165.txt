Synopsis: 

In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious crimson clover crop begins to grow, captivating the attention of the townspeople. As the crimson clover spreads its vibrant petals, it brings with it a series of unexplained events that disrupt the peaceful lives of the residents. With each passing day, the town becomes more entangled in the enigma of the crimson clover, leading to unexpected alliances, secrets unveiled, and a race against time to uncover the truth behind this extraordinary phenomenon.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Intrigue, Community, Secrets, Nature's Power
