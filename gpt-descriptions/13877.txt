Synopsis: In 'Poise: How to Attain It' by Starke, D., readers are taken on a captivating journey through the art of cultivating poise in one's life. Through a series of insightful anecdotes, practical exercises, and thought-provoking reflections, the author guides us towards discovering the power of grace, composure, and self-assurance. As we delve into the pages of this book, we uncover the secrets to navigating life's challenges with elegance and confidence, ultimately transforming ourselves into individuals who exude poise in every aspect of our being.

Genres: Self-help, Personal Development, Psychology

Themes: Self-confidence, Emotional Intelligence, Personal Growth
