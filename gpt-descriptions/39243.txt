Synopsis: 
In 'Six One-Act Plays' by Margaret Scott Oliver, readers are taken on a captivating journey through six distinct stories that explore the complexities of human relationships and the unexpected twists and turns of life. From a chance encounter on a train to a mysterious reunion at a funeral, each play presents a unique and thought-provoking narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets and revelations that lie within.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Love and loss, Fate and destiny, Identity and self-discovery
