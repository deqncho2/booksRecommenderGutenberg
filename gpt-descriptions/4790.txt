Synopsis: In 'Half a Rogue' by Harold MacGrath, a mysterious and charming man named John Darrow finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he becomes entangled with a beautiful woman and a secret society. As he navigates through a world of deception and betrayal, Darrow must use his wit and cunning to survive and uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
