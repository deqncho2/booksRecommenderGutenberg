Synopsis: In 'Law of the North', Samuel Alexander White takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and untamed wilderness of Rupert's Land. Set against the backdrop of love and battle, this enthralling tale follows the lives of courageous individuals who must navigate the harsh realities of the North. As they face treacherous landscapes, fierce rivalries, and unexpected alliances, their destinies become intertwined in a gripping narrative that explores the depths of human resilience and the power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Survival, Honor, Love, Conflict
