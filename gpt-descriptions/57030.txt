Synopsis: In 'The Pocket Lavater', two renowned scholars, Porta and Lavater, delve into the fascinating world of physiognomy, the study of facial features and their relation to character traits. As they explore the intricate connections between human and animal physiognomy, they uncover surprising similarities that challenge conventional wisdom. This captivating work takes readers on a journey of discovery, offering insights into the hidden depths of human nature and the intriguing parallels between humans and animals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Physiognomy, Human nature, Animal behavior, Character analysis
