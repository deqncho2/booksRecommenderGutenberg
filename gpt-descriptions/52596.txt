Synopsis: 
'Czech Folk Tales' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to a world filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and mysterious quests. From mischievous forest spirits to cunning witches, each tale is a delightful blend of adventure, folklore, and timeless wisdom. These captivating stories will ignite the imagination and leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the secrets and wonders hidden within the Czech folklore.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Heroism, Wisdom, Supernatural beings
