Synopsis: In 'Social Origins and Primal Law', Lang and Atkinson delve into the fascinating world of ancient societies and their origins. Through a captivating exploration of myths, customs, and rituals, they uncover the hidden threads that connect humanity's earliest social structures. From the enigmatic practices of primitive tribes to the complex dynamics of early civilizations, this book offers a thought-provoking journey into the roots of human society.

Genres: Anthropology, History, Sociology

Themes: Evolution of society, Cultural anthropology, Origins of civilization
