Synopsis: In 'The Rise of Roscoe Paine' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young and ambitious man named Roscoe Paine finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal challenges. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics, Roscoe must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of power, ambition, and the price one is willing to pay for success.

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Power, Personal growth
