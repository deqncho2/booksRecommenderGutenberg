Synopsis: 
In 'The Handbook of Soap Manufacture', Appleton and Simmons take readers on a captivating journey into the world of soap production. From the ancient art of soap making to the modern techniques used in industrial manufacturing, this comprehensive guide offers a wealth of knowledge and practical advice. Delving into the chemistry, ingredients, and processes involved, the authors provide a fascinating exploration of the science behind soap. With detailed instructions, troubleshooting tips, and historical anecdotes, this handbook is a must-read for anyone curious about the secrets behind creating the perfect bar of soap.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Science

Themes: Soap making, Chemistry, Industrial processes
