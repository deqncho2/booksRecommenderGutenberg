Synopsis: In 'Freaks of Fortune; or, Half Round the World' by Optic, Oliver, embark on an exhilarating adventure as a group of young friends set sail on a grand voyage around the world. From encountering treacherous storms to exploring exotic lands, their journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As they navigate through thrilling escapades and face various challenges, the characters discover the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Pursuit of Dreams
