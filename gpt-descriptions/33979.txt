Synopsis: In 'Miscellaneous Aphorisms; The Soul of Man', Oscar Wilde presents a collection of thought-provoking and witty aphorisms that delve into the complexities of human nature and society. Through his sharp observations and clever wordplay, Wilde challenges conventional wisdom and explores the true essence of the human soul. This collection offers a unique perspective on various aspects of life, from art and beauty to morality and individuality, leaving readers captivated by Wilde's profound insights.

Genres: Philosophy, Literature, Essay

Themes: Human nature, Society, Individuality, Morality, Art and beauty
