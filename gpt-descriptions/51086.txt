Synopsis: In "Raymond; or, Life and Death," Sir Oliver Lodge delves into the mysterious realm of the afterlife, exploring the evidence for the survival of memory and affection beyond death. The story follows Raymond, a young man who tragically passes away, but continues to communicate with his loved ones from the other side. Through a series of compelling examples and experiences, Lodge presents a thought-provoking exploration of the possibility of life after death, challenging conventional beliefs and offering a glimpse into the eternal bonds of memory and love.

Genres: Spiritual Fiction, Supernatural Mystery, Philosophical Inquiry

Themes: Life after death, Communication with the deceased, Love and memory, Existential exploration
