Synopsis: In "Shadow and Light: An Autobiography with Reminiscences of the Last and Present Century," Mifflin Wistar Gibbs takes us on a captivating journey through his remarkable life as a prominent African American figure in the 19th century. From his early struggles as a free Black man in pre-Civil War America to his rise as a successful businessman, politician, and diplomat, Gibbs shares his experiences, challenges, and triumphs in a society marked by racial inequality and social upheaval. With vivid storytelling and insightful reflections, this autobiography offers a unique perspective on the historical events and cultural shifts that shaped the nation during this pivotal period.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Racial inequality, Social justice, Personal resilience, Historical events, Cultural shifts
