Synopsis: 
'From Jest to Earnest' by Edward Payson Roe is a captivating novel that follows the life of a young man named Ralph. Starting off as a carefree and mischievous individual, Ralph's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a series of events that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own shortcomings. As he navigates through love, loss, and personal growth, Ralph's journey becomes a compelling exploration of the human spirit and the transformative power of self-reflection.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Personal growth, Self-reflection, Love and loss
