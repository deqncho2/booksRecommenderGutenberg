Synopsis: In the Cards by George O. Smith is a thrilling science fiction novel that follows the story of a brilliant scientist who discovers a mysterious deck of cards that can predict the future. As he delves deeper into the cards' powers, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of manipulation and deceit, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With each card he draws, the stakes rise higher, and he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the cards' origins and their true purpose.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Fate and free will, Perception vs. reality, Power and manipulation
