Synopsis: 
In "Historical sketches of old Charing: The hospital and chapel of Saint Mary Roncevall. Eleanor of Castile, Queen of England, and the monuments erected in her memory," Sir James Galloway takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Charing, uncovering the secrets of the hospital and chapel of Saint Mary Roncevall. Delving into the life of Eleanor of Castile, Queen of England, Galloway explores the fascinating monuments erected in her memory. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the past, shedding light on the forgotten stories and hidden treasures of Charing.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Architecture

Themes: History, Royalty, Architecture, Memory
