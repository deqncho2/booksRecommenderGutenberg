Synopsis: The Outdoor Girls embark on a thrilling adventure when they set out on a road trip in their motor car, only to stumble upon a mysterious haunted mansion in Shadow Valley. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the mansion, the girls find themselves caught up in a web of intrigue and danger. Will they be able to uncover the truth behind the haunting and escape unscathed?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
