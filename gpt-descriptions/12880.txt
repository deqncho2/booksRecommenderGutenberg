Synopsis: 

In 'Three Times and Out: Told by Private Simmons, Written by Nellie L. McClung', we are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of Private Simmons, a soldier who has experienced the horrors of war not once, but three times. Through his vivid and heartfelt storytelling, we witness the physical and emotional toll that war takes on him and his comrades. As we delve deeper into his narrative, we are confronted with the harsh realities of battle, the bonds formed in the face of adversity, and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Memoir

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
