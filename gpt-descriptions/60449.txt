Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Seventy-first Regiment, Highland Light Infantry', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the renowned regiment from 1777 to 1852. This meticulously researched account delves into the regiment's origins, its notable achievements, and the challenges it faced throughout its history. From battles fought on distant shores to the personal stories of the brave men who served, Cannon's narrative paints a vivid picture of the regiment's enduring legacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Military strategy, Historical significance
