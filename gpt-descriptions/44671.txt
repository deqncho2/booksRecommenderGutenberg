Synopsis: 
In 'Jack in the Rockies: A Boy's Adventures with a Pack Train', young Jack embarks on a thrilling journey through the majestic Rockies. Joining a pack train, he encounters breathtaking landscapes, encounters with wildlife, and unexpected challenges that test his courage and resourcefulness. As Jack navigates the rugged terrain, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of nature. Will he conquer the mountains and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Resilience
