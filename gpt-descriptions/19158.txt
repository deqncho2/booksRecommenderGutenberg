Synopsis: In 'The Return', a mysterious phenomenon transports a group of people from different time periods to a desolate future Earth. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they uncover shocking secrets about the world they now inhabit. With danger lurking at every turn, they must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront their own fears in order to unravel the truth and secure their return.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Post-Apocalyptic

Themes: Survival, Identity, Exploration, Human Nature
