Synopsis: In 'Sixpenny Pieces' by A. Neil Lyons, a mysterious package arrives at the doorstep of a struggling artist, setting off a chain of events that will challenge his perception of reality and lead him on a thrilling journey through time and space. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the package, he uncovers a web of intrigue and danger that threatens not only his own life but the very fabric of existence itself. With each twist and turn, the artist must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine the fate of the world as he knows it.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perception of reality, Time travel, Identity, Consequences of choices
