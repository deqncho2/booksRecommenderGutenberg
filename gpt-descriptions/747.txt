Synopsis: 
'Anomalies and Curiosities of Medicine' takes readers on a captivating journey through the bizarre and extraordinary aspects of the medical world. From peculiar medical conditions to unusual surgical procedures, this book delves into the fascinating realm of medical anomalies and curiosities. With a blend of historical accounts, scientific explanations, and captivating anecdotes, Gould and Pyle shed light on the peculiarities that have both perplexed and amazed medical professionals throughout history. Prepare to be astounded by the strange and extraordinary tales that lie within the pages of this captivating work.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, History

Themes: Medical anomalies, Curiosities, Historical accounts, Scientific explanations
