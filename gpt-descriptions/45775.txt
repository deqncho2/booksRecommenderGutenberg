Synopsis: 
'Toy-Making in School and Home' is a captivating guide that takes readers on a journey into the world of toy-making. Written by Mabel Irene Rutherford Polkinghorne and Ruby Kathleen Polkinghorne, this book offers a treasure trove of creative ideas and practical instructions for making toys from everyday materials. From simple paper crafts to more intricate woodworking projects, the authors provide step-by-step guidance that will inspire both children and adults to unleash their imagination and create their own unique toys. With a delightful blend of nostalgia and innovation, 'Toy-Making in School and Home' is a must-read for anyone seeking to bring joy and creativity into their lives.

Genres: 
Crafts, Hobbies, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Creativity, Education, Play
