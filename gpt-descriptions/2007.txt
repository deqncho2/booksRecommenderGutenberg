Synopsis: In 'We Two: A Novel' by Edna Lyall, readers are transported to the late 19th century, where they follow the lives of two remarkable women, Olive and Janet. Olive, a strong-willed and independent woman, finds herself drawn to the world of literature and dreams of becoming a successful writer. Janet, on the other hand, is a gentle and compassionate soul, dedicated to helping others and making a difference in society. As their paths intertwine, they form a deep bond that transcends societal expectations and challenges the norms of their time. Together, they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and personal growth, all while striving to find their own voices in a world that often silences women. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Women's Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Female Empowerment, Friendship, Self-Discovery
