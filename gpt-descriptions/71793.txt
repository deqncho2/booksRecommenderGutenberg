Synopsis: In "Sunny Boy at the Seashore" by Ramy Allison White, readers are transported to a charming coastal town where a young boy named Sunny embarks on a captivating adventure. As Sunny explores the seashore, he encounters mysterious creatures, uncovers hidden treasures, and forms unexpected friendships. With each new discovery, Sunny's imagination soars, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and wonder. This heartwarming tale is filled with enchanting moments that will leave readers longing for their own seaside escapades.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Exploration, Friendship
