Synopsis: 
In 'The Mark of Cain' by Carolyn Wells, a mysterious murder takes place in a quaint New England town, leaving the residents baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the dark underbelly of the seemingly idyllic community is exposed. With a web of deceit and suspicion woven around them, the characters must confront their own demons and grapple with the consequences of their actions. As the tension rises, the truth becomes increasingly elusive, and the reader is left guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Consequences
