Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Bible in Spain," the Englishman continues his extraordinary journey through the captivating and treacherous landscapes of the Iberian Peninsula. Filled with thrilling adventures, unexpected encounters, and perilous imprisonments, this book takes readers on a riveting exploration of the Englishman's relentless pursuit to circulate the Scriptures in a land deeply entrenched in religious and political turmoil. As he navigates through the diverse cultures and landscapes of Spain, the Englishman's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test, leading to unexpected alliances and life-changing revelations.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Religious freedom, Cultural diversity, Perseverance, Political intrigue
