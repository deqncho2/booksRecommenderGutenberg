Synopsis: In 'Lilith: A Novel', Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth weaves a captivating tale of mystery and romance. Set in the enchanting world of 19th-century America, the story follows the life of Lilith, a young woman with a mysterious past and an indomitable spirit. As Lilith navigates through the complexities of society, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Lilith's world, eagerly uncovering the truth behind her enigmatic existence. Will Lilith's resilience and determination be enough to overcome the challenges she faces? Or will her past catch up with her, threatening to unravel everything she holds dear?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal, Resilience
