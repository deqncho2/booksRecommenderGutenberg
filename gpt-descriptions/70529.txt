Synopsis: In "Little Arthur's History of England" by Maria, Lady Callcott, embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of English history. Follow the young protagonist, Little Arthur, as he encounters legendary figures, battles, and pivotal moments that shaped the nation. From the ancient Celts to the Norman Conquest and beyond, this enchanting tale brings history to life, blending adventure, humor, and valuable lessons along the way.

Genres: Historical fiction, Children's literature

Themes: History, Adventure, Education, Identity
