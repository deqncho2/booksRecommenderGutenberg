Synopsis: 
In the gritty and corrupt streets of Farmington, a group of police officers known as the Strike Team walks a fine line between justice and criminality. Led by the cunning and morally ambiguous Detective Vic Mackey, they navigate a treacherous world where loyalty is tested, alliances are formed and broken, and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. As they battle against both external threats and their own demons, the Strike Team must confront the consequences of their actions and the price they are willing to pay to protect their own.

Genres: Crime, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Corruption, Loyalty, Power
