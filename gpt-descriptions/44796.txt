Synopsis: In 'The Prodigal Village: A Christmas Tale' by Irving Bacheller, a young man returns to his childhood village after many years, only to find it transformed into a bustling city. As he navigates the changes and reconnects with old friends, he uncovers a hidden secret that could change the fate of the village forever. This heartwarming Christmas tale explores the power of community, the importance of tradition, and the enduring spirit of homecoming.

Genres: Fiction, Christmas, Historical

Themes: Community, Tradition, Homecoming
