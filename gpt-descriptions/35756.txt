Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Knight Of Gwynne', the captivating story continues as the lives of the Gwynne family become entangled in a web of secrets, love, and political intrigue. As the protagonist, Sir Marmaduke Gwynne, faces new challenges and unexpected alliances, the fate of his family and their ancestral home hangs in the balance. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Family, Honor, Betrayal, Redemption
