Synopsis: In 'Drolls From Shadowland', Joseph Henry Pearce takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of mysterious and enchanting tales. From the depths of the supernatural to the whimsical realms of fantasy, these drolls transport readers to a world where shadows hold secrets and dreams come alive. Each story is a delightful blend of suspense, humor, and unexpected twists, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the next captivating tale. Prepare to be spellbound as Pearce weaves a tapestry of imagination and wonder in this mesmerizing collection.

Genres: Fantasy, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Dreams, Mystery, Imagination
