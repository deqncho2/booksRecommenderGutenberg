Synopsis: In 'The Plague of the Heart' by Prevost, Francis, a young woman named Isabella finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a passionate love affair. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century France, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, betrayal, and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Duty and responsibility, Betrayal and consequences
