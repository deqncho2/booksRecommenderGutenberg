Synopsis: 
In 'A Terrible Coward' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Tom is haunted by a terrible secret that threatens to destroy his life. As he navigates through a web of deceit and betrayal, he must confront his deepest fears and find the courage to face the consequences of his actions. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: 
Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Guilt, Redemption, Bravery
