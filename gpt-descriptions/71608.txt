Synopsis: 
In 'The juvenile forget-me-not: A Christmas and New Year's present', embark on a heartwarming journey filled with enchanting tales and delightful adventures. This collection of stories is sure to captivate readers of all ages, as it weaves together tales of friendship, courage, and the magic of the holiday season. From a mischievous elf's quest to save Christmas to a young girl's encounter with a mysterious snow globe, each story is a delightful reminder of the power of love and the joy of giving. Get ready to be transported to a world where dreams come true and miracles happen, making this book an unforgettable gift for the holiday season.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Holiday Fiction
Themes: Friendship, Courage, Magic, Love, Giving
