Synopsis: In 'The Servant Problem' by Robert F. Young, a seemingly ordinary household is turned upside down when a mysterious servant arrives, bringing with her a series of strange occurrences and unsettling secrets. As the family tries to uncover the truth behind their new servant's enigmatic past, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic home.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
