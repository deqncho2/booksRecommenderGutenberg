Synopsis: 
'The Islands of Magic: Legends, Folk and Fairy Tales from the Azores' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of the Azores, a group of islands in the Atlantic Ocean. This collection of legends, folktales, and fairy tales unveils the rich cultural heritage and mystical traditions of the Azorean people. From magical creatures and mythical beings to tales of love, bravery, and adventure, each story transports readers to a realm where imagination knows no bounds. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover a tapestry of captivating narratives that will leave them spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Cultural Heritage, Adventure
