Synopsis: 
'Jamaican Song and Story: Annancy Stories, Digging Sings, Ring Tunes, and Dancing Tunes' is a captivating collection of traditional Jamaican folktales, songs, and stories that will transport readers to the vibrant and enchanting world of Jamaica. From the mischievous adventures of the cunning spider Anansi to the rhythmic beats of traditional Jamaican music, this book offers a rich tapestry of cultural heritage and oral traditions. Through vivid storytelling and melodic tunes, readers will be immersed in the captivating folklore and musical traditions of Jamaica.

Genres: Folklore, Music, Cultural Heritage

Themes: Oral traditions, Trickery and cunning, Cultural identity
