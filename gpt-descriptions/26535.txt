Synopsis: 
'The Standard Electrical Dictionary: A Popular Dictionary of Words and Terms Used in the Practice of Electrical Engineering' is a comprehensive and accessible guide that demystifies the complex world of electrical engineering. Written by T. O'Conor Sloane, this dictionary provides clear definitions and explanations for the terminology and concepts used in the field. From basic principles to advanced techniques, this book serves as an indispensable resource for both beginners and professionals in the electrical engineering industry.

Genres: 
Reference, Engineering, Technology

Themes: 
Electrical engineering, Terminology, Education
