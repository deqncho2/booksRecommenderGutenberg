Synopsis: In 'Two New Pelycosaurs from the Lower Permian of Oklahoma', Richard C. Fox takes us on a thrilling journey into the world of prehistoric creatures. Set in the Lower Permian period, the story unveils the discovery of two remarkable pelycosaurs in the heart of Oklahoma. As the paleontologists delve deeper into their findings, they uncover surprising secrets that challenge our understanding of these ancient reptiles. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Fox brings these long-extinct creatures to life, captivating readers with a tale of scientific exploration and the wonders of the past.

Genres: Paleontology, Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Prehistoric life, Scientific discovery, Evolution
