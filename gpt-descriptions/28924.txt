Synopsis: In 'Collector's Item' by Robert F. Young, a renowned art collector stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that defies all logic and challenges his perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the origins and purpose of this enigmatic object, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between imagination and truth blurs. With each revelation, the collector's obsession grows, leading him on a thrilling journey that will test the limits of his sanity and reshape his understanding of the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality, Obsession
