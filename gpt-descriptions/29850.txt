Synopsis: 

In "Woodrow Wilson's Administration and Achievements," Bryan and Lord delve into the captivating story of one of America's most influential presidents. This meticulously researched account takes readers on a journey through Wilson's presidency, exploring his remarkable achievements and the challenges he faced during his time in office. From his groundbreaking domestic policies to his transformative foreign policy decisions, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging portrait of Wilson's presidency. Bryan and Lord skillfully weave together historical analysis and personal anecdotes to bring Wilson's era to life, shedding light on the man behind the presidency and the lasting impact of his leadership.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Science

Themes: Leadership, Progressive Era, International Relations, Domestic Policy
