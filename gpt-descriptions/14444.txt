Synopsis: In 'The Complete English Tradesman', Daniel Defoe takes us on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of 18th-century London, immersing us in the daily life and challenges faced by the city's tradesmen. Through a series of vivid anecdotes and practical advice, Defoe explores the trials and triumphs of these hardworking individuals, revealing the secrets to their success and the pitfalls they must navigate. From the struggles of apprenticeship to the intricacies of business dealings, this enlightening work offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of commerce and the pursuit of prosperity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Historical

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Social mobility, Work ethic
