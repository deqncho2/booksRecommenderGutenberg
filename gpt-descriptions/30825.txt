Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls on Ellen's Isle; Or, The Trail of the Seven Cedars', the Camp Fire Girls embark on an exciting adventure to a mysterious island. As they explore the island's secrets and encounter unexpected challenges, they must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and courage to unravel the trail of the seven cedars. With each step, they uncover clues that lead them closer to a hidden treasure, but they soon realize that they are not the only ones searching for it. Will the Camp Fire Girls be able to outwit their rivals and claim the treasure for themselves?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving, Competition
