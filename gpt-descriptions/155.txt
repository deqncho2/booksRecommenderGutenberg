Synopsis: 
The Moonstone by Wilkie Collins is a gripping mystery novel that revolves around the theft of a precious diamond, the Moonstone, and the subsequent investigation to uncover the truth. The story unfolds through multiple narrators, each with their own unique perspective and secrets to hide. As the search for the missing gem intensifies, the plot takes unexpected twists and turns, leading the reader through a labyrinth of deception, suspense, and intrigue. With its complex characters and intricate plot, The Moonstone keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Betrayal, Identity, Fate and Destiny
