Synopsis: 
In 'Jemima Placid; or, The Advantage of Good-Nature' by Mary Ann Kilner, we are introduced to the kind-hearted and virtuous Jemima Placid. Despite facing numerous challenges and temptations, Jemima remains steadfast in her commitment to goodness and compassion. As she navigates through various trials and tribulations, Jemima's unwavering good-nature becomes both her greatest strength and her ultimate advantage. This captivating tale explores the power of kindness and the rewards it brings, as Jemima's character is tested and transformed in unexpected ways.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Moral Tale, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Goodness, Kindness, Virtue, Temptation, Transformation
