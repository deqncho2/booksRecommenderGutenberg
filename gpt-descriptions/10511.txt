Synopsis: In "A Rogue by Compulsion: An Affair of the Secret Service" by Victor Bridges, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of espionage and secret agents. When a renowned British spy mysteriously disappears, his protégé, Richard Larch, is thrust into a treacherous mission to uncover the truth. As Larch delves deeper into a web of deceit and betrayal, he must navigate a world where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury he cannot afford. With his life on the line, Larch must outwit his enemies and uncover the shocking secrets that lie at the heart of the Secret Service.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Loyalty, Espionage
