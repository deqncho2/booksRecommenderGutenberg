Synopsis: In 'An Interpretation of Friends Worship' by Jean Toomer, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery as she becomes immersed in the world of the Quakers. Drawn to their unique form of worship, she finds herself questioning her own beliefs and grappling with the complexities of faith, love, and community. As she delves deeper into the Quaker way of life, she uncovers surprising truths about herself and the power of human connection. 

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Faith, Identity, Community, Self-Discovery
