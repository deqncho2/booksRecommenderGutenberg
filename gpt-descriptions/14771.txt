Synopsis: In 'The False One: A Tragedy', Fletcher and Beaumont transport us to the vibrant world of ancient Egypt, where political intrigue, forbidden love, and the pursuit of power collide. As the charismatic general, Cleopatra, rises to prominence, a web of deception and manipulation begins to unravel, threatening to tear apart the kingdom. With its complex characters and gripping plot, this tragedy explores the consequences of ambition, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: Ambition, Loyalty, Deception, Power
