Synopsis: In "The Spirit of the School" by Ralph Henry Barbour, a group of high school students find themselves caught up in a mysterious and thrilling adventure that tests their loyalty, courage, and determination. As they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their friendship and the spirit of their school to overcome the obstacles in their path. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
