Synopsis: In 'The Mad Lover, a Tragi-Comedy', the talented duo Francis Beaumont and John Fletcher present a captivating tale filled with love, madness, and mistaken identities. As the story unfolds, a young nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and desire, leading to a series of hilarious and heart-wrenching events. With a cast of eccentric characters and unexpected twists, this play explores the complexities of love and the consequences of unchecked passion.

Genres: Tragi-Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Madness and Sanity, Deception and Identity
