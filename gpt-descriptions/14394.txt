Synopsis: In the bustling city of Damascus, a young American journalist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of espionage and international politics, he must navigate the treacherous streets and uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, the journalist's journey down the Street Called Straight becomes a thrilling race against time.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Espionage

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Identity, Power
