Synopsis: In 'A Venetian June' by Anna Fuller, a young woman named Isabella finds herself captivated by the enchanting city of Venice during a summer visit. As she explores the winding canals and hidden alleyways, she becomes entangled in a mysterious love affair that challenges her perception of reality. With each passing day, Isabella is drawn deeper into a world of secrets and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems. Will she uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Deception and secrets, Cultural exploration
