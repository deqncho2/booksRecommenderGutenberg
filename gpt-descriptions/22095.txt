Synopsis: In 'The Red Cross Girls with the Russian Army', follow the thrilling journey of a group of courageous young women who join the Red Cross and find themselves amidst the chaos of war-torn Russia. As they navigate the treacherous landscape, facing danger and uncertainty at every turn, they discover the true meaning of resilience, friendship, and sacrifice. Will their determination and compassion be enough to make a difference in the lives of those they encounter?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Resilience, Sacrifice
