Synopsis: In 'Curious Punishments of Bygone Days', Alice Morse Earle takes us on a captivating journey through history, revealing the bizarre and often cruel punishments inflicted upon individuals in the past. From public humiliation to unusual forms of torture, this book uncovers the dark and fascinating world of justice in bygone eras. Prepare to be shocked and intrigued as Earle delves into the peculiar punishments that were once considered acceptable. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Justice, Cruelty, Social norms
