Synopsis: 

In 'Destiny' by Charles Neville Buck, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates a world of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected love. Set against the backdrop of a sprawling estate in the early 20th century, this captivating novel follows the protagonist's journey as she unravels the mysteries of her family's past and discovers her own true destiny. With vivid descriptions, complex characters, and a web of interconnected relationships, 'Destiny' is a gripping tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Family Secrets, Self-discovery
