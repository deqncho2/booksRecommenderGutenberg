Synopsis: In 'The Wayfarers' by Mary Stewart Cutting, a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure when she joins a group of unconventional travelers. As they traverse through breathtaking landscapes and encounter unexpected challenges, Lucy finds herself drawn to the enigmatic leader of the group, who harbors a mysterious past. With secrets and hidden agendas unraveling, Lucy must navigate the complexities of trust, love, and betrayal, all while discovering her own inner strength and purpose.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Trust, Love, Betrayal
