Synopsis: In 'Guide to Fortune-Telling by Dreams' by Anonymous, a mysterious and ancient practice is unveiled, revealing the power of dreams to predict the future. As the reader delves into the world of dream interpretation, they are taken on a captivating journey through the hidden meanings and symbols that can unlock the secrets of destiny. With each turn of the page, the reader becomes immersed in a realm where dreams hold the key to unraveling the mysteries of life and shaping one's own fortune.

Genres: Occult, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Dream interpretation, Fate and destiny, Personal empowerment
