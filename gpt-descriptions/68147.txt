Synopsis: In 'Please pass the cream: A comedy' by Holmes, Charles Nevers, a quirky group of friends find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious misadventures when they embark on a quest to find the perfect cup of coffee. From mistaken identities to outrageous encounters, this uproarious comedy takes readers on a wild ride filled with laughter, surprises, and a touch of romance. 

Genres: Comedy, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Pursuit of Happiness
