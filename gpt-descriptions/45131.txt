Synopsis: In the second volume of "Charles Bradlaugh: a Record of His Life and Work," the authors delve into the remarkable life and achievements of Charles Bradlaugh, a prominent figure in British politics and a champion of free thought and secularism. This edition focuses on his parliamentary struggle, providing an in-depth account of his political career, teachings, and the challenges he faced. From his controversial speeches to his tireless advocacy for social justice, Bradlaugh's story is one of resilience and determination in the face of opposition. 

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Free thought, Secularism, Political activism, Social justice
