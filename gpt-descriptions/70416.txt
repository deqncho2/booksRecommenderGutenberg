Synopsis: 
'Emmanuel Burden, merchant, of Thames St., in the city of London, exporter of hardware: A record of his lineage, speculations, last days and death' by Hilaire Belloc is a captivating historical novel that delves into the life of Emmanuel Burden, a prominent merchant in London during the 19th century. The story follows Burden's rise to success in the hardware export business, exploring his family history, business ventures, and the challenges he faces in a rapidly changing world. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into the intricate web of Burden's life, witnessing his triumphs and tragedies, and ultimately, his mysterious demise.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Family legacy, Business and commerce, Mortality
