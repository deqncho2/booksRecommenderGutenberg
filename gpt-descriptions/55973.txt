Synopsis: 
In "Shinto: The ancient religion of Japan," Aston delves into the captivating world of Japan's oldest religion, Shinto. From its mysterious origins to its enduring influence on Japanese culture, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the beliefs, rituals, and practices that have shaped the spiritual landscape of Japan for centuries. Aston's meticulous research and engaging storytelling bring to life the enchanting tales of gods and goddesses, sacred shrines, and the profound connection between humans and nature. Discover the essence of Shinto and unravel the secrets of this ancient religion that continues to thrive in modern Japan.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Japanese culture, Spirituality, Nature worship
