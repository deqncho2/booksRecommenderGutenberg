Synopsis: In "Westward Hoboes: Ups and Downs of Frontier Motoring," Winifred Hawkridge takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West. Join the author and her adventurous companions as they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter eccentric characters, and face unexpected challenges on their cross-country road trip. From breathtaking natural wonders to quirky roadside attractions, this captivating travelogue offers a unique perspective on the joys and perils of exploring the frontier by car.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural encounters
