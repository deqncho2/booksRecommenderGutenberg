Synopsis: In 'The Time of Cold' by Mary Carlson, a small town is gripped by a mysterious and bone-chilling cold that seems to have no explanation. As the temperature drops, the residents find themselves facing not only physical challenges but also the unraveling of their relationships and the dark secrets that lie beneath the surface. With each passing day, the cold becomes more than just a weather phenomenon, turning into a metaphor for the frozen emotions and hidden truths that have long been ignored. As the town struggles to survive, the characters must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Human resilience, The power of nature, Redemption
