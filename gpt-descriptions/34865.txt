Synopsis: In 'Great Mysteries and Little Plagues' by John Neal, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that leave the residents baffled and fearful. As the occurrences escalate, a group of unlikely allies must come together to unravel the truth behind these perplexing phenomena. With each revelation, they uncover dark secrets that challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their own inner demons. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and lies, the power of belief, the human capacity for darkness
