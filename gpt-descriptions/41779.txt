Synopsis: In 'The Human Boy and the War' by Eden Phillpotts, a young boy finds himself caught in the midst of a war that threatens to tear his world apart. As he navigates the chaos and uncertainty, he discovers unexpected allies and faces unimaginable challenges that test his courage and resilience. With danger lurking at every turn, the boy must summon all his strength to protect what he holds dear and find hope amidst the darkness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Courage, Resilience, Friendship, Hope
