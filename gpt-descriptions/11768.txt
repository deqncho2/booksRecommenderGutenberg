Synopsis: In Volume 05 of 'The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D.', readers are treated to a collection of Samuel Johnson's captivating and thought-provoking Miscellaneous Pieces. From essays on literature and politics to biographical sketches and critical reviews, this volume showcases Johnson's unparalleled wit, wisdom, and mastery of the English language. Delve into his musings on the nature of poetry, his reflections on the lives of notable individuals, and his astute observations on society and culture. With each piece, Johnson's unique voice shines through, engaging readers in a journey of intellectual exploration and enlightenment.

Genres: Essay, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literature and its significance, Human nature and society, Art and aesthetics
