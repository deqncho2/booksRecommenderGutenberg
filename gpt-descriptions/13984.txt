Synopsis: In the Wrong Paradise, and Other Stories by Lang, Andrew is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking tales that transport readers to various realms of imagination. From a mysterious island where time stands still to a haunted house with a chilling secret, each story presents a unique and unexpected twist. Delve into the lives of intriguing characters as they navigate love, loss, and the complexities of human nature. With its blend of fantasy, mystery, and suspense, this collection promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Love, Loss, Human Nature
