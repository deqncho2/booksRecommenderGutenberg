Synopsis: In the rugged and lawless frontier of the American West, a mysterious gunslinger named Lassiter arrives in a small Mormon settlement called Cottonwoods. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, he discovers a web of secrets, forbidden love, and a powerful religious leader who will stop at nothing to maintain his control. Lassiter's quest for justice and the truth leads him to confront the forces of oppression and corruption, while also finding unexpected allies and a love that defies all odds.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Love, Religion, Freedom
