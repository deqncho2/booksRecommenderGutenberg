Synopsis: On the secluded Rollins Island, a team of scientists embark on a groundbreaking ecological study, only to uncover a series of mysterious and unsettling phenomena that challenge their understanding of the natural world. As they delve deeper into the island's secrets, they must confront their own fears and the ethical dilemmas that arise, leading to a thrilling exploration of the delicate balance between humanity and the environment.

Genres: Science fiction, mystery, thriller

Themes: Environmentalism, human impact on nature, scientific ethics
