Synopsis: In 'The Viking's Skull' by John R. Carling, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious Viking artifact that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the history and mythology surrounding the skull, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of ancient curses, hidden treasures, and a race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late. With each clue leading him closer to the heart of a long-forgotten Viking legend, the protagonist must confront his own fears and confront the malevolent forces that seek to possess the power of the skull.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mythology, Archaeology, Supernatural forces
