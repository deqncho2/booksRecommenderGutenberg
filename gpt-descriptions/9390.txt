Synopsis: 
In "Fifteen Years with the Outcast," we follow the extraordinary journey of Fflorens Roberts, a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. From the bustling streets of London to the rugged landscapes of the Scottish Highlands, Fflorens navigates a world filled with danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As she unravels the mysteries of her past, she discovers the true meaning of resilience and the power of redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Redemption
