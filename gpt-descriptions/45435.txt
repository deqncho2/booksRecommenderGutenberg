Synopsis: In "History of the Cathedral Church of Wells: As Illustrating the History of the Cathedral Churches of the Old Foundation," Edward A. Freeman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Wells Cathedral. From its humble beginnings to its grandeur as a symbol of faith and architectural marvel, Freeman delves into the intricate details of the cathedral's construction, the lives of those who built it, and the significant events that shaped its existence. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the cathedral's past, shedding light on the broader history of cathedral churches of the old foundation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Religious history

Themes: Architecture and design, Religious institutions, Cultural heritage
