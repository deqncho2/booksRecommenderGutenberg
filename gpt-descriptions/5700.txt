Synopsis: In 'Love and Life: An Old Story in Eighteenth Century Costume', Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two young women, Lucy and Marianne, as they navigate the complexities of love, society, and personal growth in eighteenth-century England. As their paths intertwine with those of intriguing suitors and formidable challenges, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of these captivating characters.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Romance, Social Expectations, Personal Growth, Female Empowerment
