Synopsis: 

In 'Cameos from English History, from Rollo to Edward II', Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey through the annals of English history, presenting a series of vivid and engaging vignettes that bring to life some of the most fascinating figures and events from the past. From the legendary Viking leader Rollo to the troubled reign of Edward II, Yonge skillfully weaves together historical facts and imaginative storytelling, offering readers a glimpse into the lives, struggles, and triumphs of these influential individuals. Each cameo provides a unique perspective on the political, social, and cultural landscape of its time, creating a tapestry of English history that is both educational and entertaining.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Short stories

Themes: Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal, Historical significance, Personal struggles, Cultural and social dynamics
