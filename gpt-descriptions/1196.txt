Synopsis: In 'The Purse' by Honoré de Balzac, a young man named Eugène de Rastignac becomes entangled in a mysterious and alluring world when he discovers a hidden purse. As he delves deeper into the secrets behind the purse, Eugène finds himself torn between his desire for wealth and the moral dilemmas that arise. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Wealth and ambition, Morality and ethics, Deception and secrets
