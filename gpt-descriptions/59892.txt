Synopsis: In 'Changeling, and Other Stories' by Donn Byrne, prepare to be transported to a world where reality and fantasy intertwine. From the mysterious disappearance of a young girl to a man's encounter with a mischievous fairy, these captivating tales will keep you on the edge of your seat. Delve into the depths of human nature as characters navigate love, loss, and the supernatural. With each story, Byrne weaves a spellbinding narrative that will leave you questioning what is real and what lies beyond our perception.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Perception, Loss, Love, Folklore
