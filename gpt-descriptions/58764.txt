Synopsis: 
In 'Cycle Rides Round London', Charles G. Harper takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant streets of London, uncovering hidden gems and sharing fascinating historical anecdotes along the way. From the bustling markets of Covent Garden to the tranquil beauty of Richmond Park, Harper's vivid descriptions and detailed maps invite readers to explore the city's diverse neighborhoods and discover the unexpected. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or simply an armchair adventurer, this captivating guide will inspire you to hop on your bike and embark on your own two-wheeled adventure through the heart of London.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Urban exploration, Cycling, London history and culture
