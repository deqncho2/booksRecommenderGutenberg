Synopsis:
In 'Tony Butler' by Charles James Lever, we are introduced to the charming and enigmatic character of Tony Butler, a man who seems to effortlessly navigate the complexities of society. Set in the vibrant world of 19th-century Ireland, this novel takes us on a journey through Tony's life as he encounters love, friendship, and the challenges of social status. As we delve deeper into Tony's world, we are drawn into a web of intrigue and secrets that will keep us guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes:
Social class, Love and relationships, Identity
