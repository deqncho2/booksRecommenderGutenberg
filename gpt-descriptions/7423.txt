Synopsis:
'Mike' by P. G. Wodehouse is a delightful and humorous tale that follows the misadventures of Mike Jackson, a talented but mischievous young man who finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious escapades. From his time at a prestigious boarding school to his encounters with eccentric characters in London, Mike's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Comedy, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes:
Youthful rebellion, Friendship, Identity
