Synopsis: In 'Garrick's Pupil' by Augustin Filon, a young and ambitious actor named Garrick takes on a talented but troubled pupil, leading them both on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of artistic excellence. As their mentorship unfolds, they navigate the complexities of the theater world, facing challenges that test their dedication and passion. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the sacrifices and triumphs of those who dare to follow their dreams.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Literary Fiction

Themes: Artistic pursuit, Mentorship, Self-discovery, Ambition, Sacrifice
