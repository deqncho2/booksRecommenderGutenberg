Synopsis: "Boy" the Wandering Dog: Adventures of a Fox-Terrier follows the captivating journey of Boy, a spirited and curious fox-terrier, as he embarks on a series of thrilling adventures. From his daring escapades in the bustling city streets to his encounters with a diverse cast of characters, Boy's determination and loyalty are put to the test. As he navigates through unexpected challenges and heartwarming moments, Boy's unwavering spirit and unwavering love for his human companions shine through, making for an enchanting and unforgettable tale.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Courage, Exploration
