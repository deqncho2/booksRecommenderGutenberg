Synopsis: In "Puck of Pook's Hill" by Rudyard Kipling, two children, Dan and Una, discover a magical connection to the past when they encounter Puck, a mischievous fairy. Puck takes them on a series of extraordinary adventures, where they meet historical figures from different periods of English history. As they delve into the past, the children learn valuable lessons about loyalty, courage, and the enduring power of storytelling.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Time travel, History, Identity, Mythology
