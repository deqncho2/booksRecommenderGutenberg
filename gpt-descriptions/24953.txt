Synopsis: In "The Flemmings and "Flash Harry" of Savait" from "The Strange Adventure of James Shervinton, and Other Stories" by Louis Becke, readers are transported to the exotic island of Savait, where the lives of the Flemmings, a British family, intertwine with the enigmatic character known as "Flash Harry." As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected events take place, leading to a thrilling and captivating narrative that keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural clash, Betrayal, Survival
