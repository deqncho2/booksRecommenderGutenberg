Synopsis: In 'The World of Ice', R. M. Ballantyne takes readers on a thrilling adventure to the Arctic, where a group of explorers find themselves stranded on an icy island. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous wildlife, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive. But amidst the harsh conditions, they also uncover a mysterious secret that could change their lives forever. Will they be able to unravel the enigma and make it back home?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Discovery
