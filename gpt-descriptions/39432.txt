Synopsis: In 'The House 'Round the Corner' by Tracy, Louis, a young couple moves into a charming old house with a mysterious past. As they settle in, they begin to uncover a series of strange occurrences that lead them down a path of dark secrets and unexpected twists. With each revelation, their lives become entangled in a web of suspense and danger, forcing them to confront their own fears and question the true nature of the house they now call home.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Haunting, Psychological suspense
