Synopsis: In 'Tom Slade with the Flying Corps: A Campfire Tale', Tom Slade finds himself in the midst of thrilling adventures as he joins the Flying Corps during World War I. From daring aerial battles to secret missions behind enemy lines, Tom's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. As he navigates the dangers of war, Tom's loyalty, determination, and unwavering sense of justice shine through, making him a true hero in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Heroism
