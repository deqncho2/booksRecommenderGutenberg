Synopsis: 

'History of the American Clock Business for the Past Sixty Years, and Life of Chauncey Jerome: Barnum's Connection with the Yankee Clock Business' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of the American clock industry. Written by Chauncey Jerome, a prominent figure in the clock business, this book provides a unique perspective on the growth and development of the industry over a span of sixty years. 

From the early days of clockmaking to the rise of mass production, Jerome delves into the challenges, triumphs, and innovations that shaped the American clock business. Alongside the historical account, the book also delves into the life of Chauncey Jerome himself, exploring his personal experiences and his connection with the legendary showman P.T. Barnum.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Industrialization, Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Collaboration, Personal growth
