Synopsis: 

In the tenth volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative delves into the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, which ravaged the kingdom. The book vividly recounts the gripping struggle for power, the possession of Paris and Normandy by the English, and their eventual expulsion from these territories. Enguerrand de Monstrelet also provides a captivating account of other significant events that unfolded not only in France but also in various countries.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes: Power struggles, War and conflict, Political intrigue, Historical events
