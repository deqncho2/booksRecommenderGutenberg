Synopsis: In "Bonnie Prince Fetlar: The Story of a Pony and His Friends" by Saunders Marshall, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Fetlar, a spirited pony with a heart full of adventure. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Scottish countryside, this heartwarming tale follows Fetlar and his diverse group of animal friends as they embark on a remarkable journey filled with danger, friendship, and self-discovery. From thrilling escapades to unexpected encounters, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the fate of Bonnie Prince Fetlar and his extraordinary companions.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
