Synopsis: In "Peru: a land of contrast," Millicent Todd Bingham takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Peru. From the majestic peaks of the Andes to the mysterious ruins of Machu Picchu, Bingham's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes paint a vivid picture of this enchanting country. As she delves into the history, traditions, and natural wonders of Peru, Bingham uncovers hidden gems and uncanny tales that will leave readers yearning to explore this land of contrast for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Historical significance
