Synopsis: 

'The True History of Tom & Jerry: or, The Day and Night Scenes, of Life in London from the Start to the Finish!' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the bustling streets of 19th-century London. This captivating work follows the misadventures of Tom and Jerry, two young men from different backgrounds, as they navigate the vibrant and often dangerous world of the city. From their encounters with eccentric characters to their involvement in various escapades, the story offers a vivid portrayal of the highs and lows of urban life during this time period. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Social class, Friendship, Urban life, Morality
