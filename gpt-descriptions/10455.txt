Synopsis: In 'A Golden Book of Venice' by Turnbull, Lawrence, Mrs., readers are transported to the enchanting city of Venice, where secrets lie hidden in its labyrinthine streets and canals. The story follows a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue as she uncovers the truth behind a long-lost treasure. As she delves deeper into the city's history, she discovers that the past and present are intricately connected, and that her own fate may be tied to the secrets of Venice. With vivid descriptions of the city's beauty and a captivating plot, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Venice.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Fate and destiny, Beauty and allure of Venice
