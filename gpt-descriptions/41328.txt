Synopsis: In 'May Iverson's Career' by Elizabeth Garver Jordan, follow the captivating journey of May Iverson as she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her professional life. From her humble beginnings as a young girl with big dreams, May's determination and resilience propel her forward in a world dominated by men. As she faces societal expectations, workplace discrimination, and personal sacrifices, May's unwavering ambition and passion for her work become her driving force. With each obstacle she overcomes, May's career takes unexpected turns, leading her on a path of self-discovery and empowerment. This compelling story explores the complexities of gender roles, ambition, and the pursuit of success in a changing society.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Gender Roles, Workplace Discrimination, Self-Discovery, Empowerment
