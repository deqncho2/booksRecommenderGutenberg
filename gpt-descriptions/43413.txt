Synopsis: In the final volume of the captivating Life of Mozart series, Otto Jahn delves into the last years of the musical genius, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. As Mozart faces personal and professional challenges, including financial struggles and the pressures of composing for the royal court, Jahn uncovers the untold stories behind some of Mozart's most iconic works. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Jahn paints a vivid portrait of Mozart's final years, revealing the triumphs and tragedies that shaped his legacy.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Genius and creativity, Struggles and triumphs, Legacy and immortality
