Synopsis: In "Bobbins of Belgium: A book of Belgian lace, lace-workers, lace-schools and lace-villages," Charlotte Kellogg takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of Belgian lace. From the bustling lace-schools to the quaint lace-villages, this book delves into the lives of the skilled lace-workers who create these delicate masterpieces. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Kellogg paints a vivid picture of the rich history and cultural significance of Belgian lace. This enchanting exploration will leave readers mesmerized by the beauty and artistry of this timeless craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Artistry, Cultural heritage, Craftsmanship
