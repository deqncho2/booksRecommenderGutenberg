Synopsis: 
'Illuminated Illustrations of Froissart; Selected from the ms. in the British Museum' is a captivating collection of stunning illustrations that bring to life the medieval world chronicled by the renowned French chronicler, Jean Froissart. This beautifully curated selection showcases the most exquisite illuminations from the original manuscript, transporting readers to a time of chivalry, epic battles, and courtly intrigue. From knights in shining armor to noble ladies and grand castles, these illustrations offer a visual feast that complements Froissart's vivid storytelling. 

Genres: 
Art, History, Medieval Literature

Themes: 
Chivalry, Courtly Life, Historical Events
