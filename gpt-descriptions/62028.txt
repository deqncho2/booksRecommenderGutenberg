Synopsis: In "The Soul of Ann Rutledge: Abraham Lincoln's Romance," Bernie Babcock delves into the mysterious and captivating love story between Abraham Lincoln and Ann Rutledge. Set against the backdrop of early 19th-century America, this historical romance unravels the deep connection between the young lawyer and the enchanting Ann Rutledge, exploring the complexities of their relationship and the impact it had on Lincoln's life. As secrets are unveiled and emotions run high, readers are taken on a journey of love, loss, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Biography

Themes: Love and Loss, Fate and Destiny, Personal Growth
