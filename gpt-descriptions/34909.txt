Synopsis: In "Ancient Chinese account of the Grand Canyon, or course of the Colorado," Alexander McAllan uncovers a fascinating historical document that challenges conventional beliefs about the origins of the Grand Canyon. This ancient Chinese account, hidden for centuries, reveals a captivating tale of exploration, adventure, and the mysterious forces that shaped one of the world's most awe-inspiring natural wonders. As McAllan delves deeper into the account, he uncovers secrets that could rewrite history and forever change our understanding of the Grand Canyon's ancient past.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Natural wonders, Cultural exchange
