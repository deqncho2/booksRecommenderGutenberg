Synopsis: 

"The Anti-Slavery Crusade: A Chronicle of the Gathering Storm" by Jesse Macy is a captivating historical account that delves into the tumultuous era of the fight against slavery. Set against the backdrop of a nation divided, this chronicle follows the courageous individuals who dedicated their lives to the abolitionist cause. From the underground railroad to the fiery debates in Congress, Macy's narrative brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of those who fought for freedom.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Social justice

Themes: Slavery, Abolition, Civil rights, Activism
