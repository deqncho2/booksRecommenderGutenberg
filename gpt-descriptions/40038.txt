Synopsis: In "The Lone Ranger Rides" by Fran Striker, a mysterious masked hero emerges from the shadows to fight for justice in the Wild West. As he battles against corrupt outlaws and defends the innocent, the Lone Ranger's true identity remains a secret, adding an air of intrigue to his heroic deeds. With his trusty horse Silver and his loyal Native American companion Tonto by his side, the Lone Ranger embarks on thrilling adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Action, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Heroism, Identity
