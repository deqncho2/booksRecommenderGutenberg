Synopsis: In "The Turning of the Tide; Or, Radcliffe Rich and His Patients" by Elijah Kellogg, readers are introduced to Radcliffe Rich, a young doctor who finds himself facing a series of challenging cases in his medical practice. As he navigates the complexities of his patients' ailments, Radcliffe also grapples with personal struggles and ethical dilemmas that test his dedication to his profession. With each patient he encounters, Radcliffe's journey takes unexpected turns, leading him to question his own beliefs and the true meaning of healing.

Genres: Fiction, Medical Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Medical ethics, Personal growth, Healing and redemption
