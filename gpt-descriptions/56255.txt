Synopsis: In 'All But Lost: A Novel. Vol. 2 of 3' by G. A. Henty, the gripping tale continues as the young protagonist, Frank, finds himself caught in the midst of a treacherous war. As he navigates through dangerous battles, political intrigue, and personal challenges, Frank's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover Frank's fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Loyalty, Coming of Age
