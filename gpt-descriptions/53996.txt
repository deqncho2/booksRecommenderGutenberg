Synopsis: In 'Where Love Is' by William John Locke, a young artist finds himself torn between duty and desire when he falls in love with a captivating woman from a different social class. As their forbidden romance unfolds against the backdrop of a vibrant European city, the protagonist must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations. With secrets to be revealed and choices to be made, this captivating tale explores the power of love and the sacrifices one is willing to make to follow their heart.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Social class, Duty vs Desire
