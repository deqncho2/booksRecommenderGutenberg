Synopsis: On a remote island shrouded in mystery, a group of scientists embark on a perilous expedition to uncover the secrets of the undead. As they delve deeper into the island's dark past, they find themselves facing unimaginable horrors and a battle for survival against an ancient evil that refuses to stay buried. With each step, the line between life and death becomes blurred, and the island's chilling secrets threaten to consume them all.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Supernatural, Exploration
