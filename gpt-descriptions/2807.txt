Synopsis: In 'To Have and to Hold' by Mary Johnston, a young Englishwoman named Jocelyn Leigh finds herself embarking on a treacherous journey to the New World in search of a new life and love. Set in the early 17th century, Jocelyn's arrival in the Virginia colony is met with danger, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates the harsh realities of colonial life, Jocelyn must confront her own desires, face the perils of the wilderness, and make choices that will shape her future. With its vivid historical backdrop and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with romance, suspense, and the quest for personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Survival and Resilience, Exploration and Discovery
