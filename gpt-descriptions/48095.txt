Synopsis: In 'Back o' the Moon, and other stories' by Oliver Onions, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of mysterious and thought-provoking tales. From a haunting encounter with a ghostly figure to a chilling exploration of the human psyche, each story presents a unique and gripping narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. As the characters navigate through the shadows of their own fears and desires, secrets are unveiled and unexpected twists unfold, keeping readers guessing until the very end. 

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Fear, Identity, Perception, Secrets
