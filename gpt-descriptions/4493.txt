Synopsis: In 'The Case of General Ople and Lady Camper' by George Meredith, a mysterious encounter between General Ople and Lady Camper sets off a chain of events that will challenge societal norms and unravel hidden secrets. As the two characters navigate a web of intrigue and deception, their lives become entangled in a thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigmatic world of these captivating individuals, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Deception, Social conventions, Love and relationships
