Synopsis: 
In "Ruth Erskine's Son" by Pansy, we follow the life of a young man named Ruth Erskine, who is faced with a series of unexpected challenges and difficult decisions. As he navigates through the complexities of family, friendship, and faith, Ruth's journey takes him on a path of self-discovery and personal growth. With secrets to uncover and relationships to mend, this captivating story explores the power of love, forgiveness, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Drama, Coming-of-age, Christian Fiction

Themes: 
Family, Friendship, Faith, Redemption
