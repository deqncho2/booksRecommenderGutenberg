Synopsis: 
'Pleasures of the Telescope' takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the heavens, providing an illustrated guide for amateur astronomers and a delightful description of the celestial marvels for general readers. From the mesmerizing beauty of the moon's craters to the awe-inspiring majesty of distant galaxies, Garrett Putman Serviss invites us to explore the mysteries of the universe through the lens of a telescope. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the celestial wonders that await those who dare to look up.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Wonder and awe of the universe, Exploration and discovery, Amateur astronomy
