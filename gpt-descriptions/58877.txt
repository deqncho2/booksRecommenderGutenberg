Synopsis: In "Washington and the Riddle of Peace," H. G. Wells takes readers on a thrilling journey through the corridors of power and international diplomacy. Set in the near future, the story follows the enigmatic character of Washington, a man with a mysterious past and an uncanny ability to navigate the complex world of politics. As tensions rise between nations and the threat of war looms, Washington becomes the key player in a high-stakes game of intrigue, where the fate of peace hangs in the balance. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of power and the pursuit of peace.

Genres: Political thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power and politics, Diplomacy, War and peace
