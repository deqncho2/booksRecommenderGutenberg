Synopsis: In "Think Before You Speak; Or, The Three Wishes," a young woman named Rosalind finds herself in possession of three magical wishes. However, she soon discovers that her wishes have unintended consequences, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and understanding. As Rosalind navigates the challenges brought about by her wishes, she learns valuable lessons about the power of words and the importance of thinking before speaking.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Moral tale

Themes: Consequences of actions, Power of words, Self-discovery
