Synopsis: 

In 'Desk and Debit; or, The Catastrophes of a Clerk' by Optic, Oliver, we are introduced to the life of a young clerk named Frank. As he navigates the challenges of his job, Frank finds himself entangled in a series of unexpected and humorous mishaps. From misplaced documents to comical misunderstandings, Frank's journey takes us on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and suspense. As the story unfolds, we witness Frank's determination to overcome these catastrophes and prove himself in the world of business.

Genres: Comedy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Humor
