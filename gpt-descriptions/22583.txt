Synopsis: In the thrilling adventure novel 'The Highgrader' by Raine, William MacLeod, a young prospector named Jim Galloway finds himself caught in a dangerous game of deception and greed in the wilds of the Canadian Rockies. As he uncovers a hidden gold mine, Jim must navigate treacherous alliances, face deadly adversaries, and confront his own moral compass. With the stakes higher than ever, he must decide how far he is willing to go to protect what he has discovered.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Greed, Morality, Survival
