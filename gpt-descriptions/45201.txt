Synopsis: In 'Fallen Fortunes' by Evelyn Everett-Green, a young woman named Maud finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she unexpectedly inherits a fortune. As she navigates the complexities of her newfound wealth, Maud becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deceit, where nothing is as it seems. With the help of a charming stranger, she must unravel the truth behind her inheritance and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy her newfound happiness.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery
