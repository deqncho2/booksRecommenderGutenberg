Synopsis: In 'The Dare Boys in Vincennes' by Cox, Stephen Angus Douglas, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling and dangerous mission during the American Revolution. When they stumble upon a hidden message, they embark on a journey to Vincennes, where they must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart enemy spies, and face unexpected challenges. As they uncover secrets and face life-threatening situations, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to complete their mission and help secure victory for their country.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism
