Synopsis:
In 'All Roads Lead to Calvary', we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Jenny, who finds herself caught in the whirlwind of early 20th-century London. From her humble beginnings as a governess to her unexpected rise in society, Jenny encounters a colorful cast of characters and experiences a series of thrilling and often humorous adventures. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Jenny's path ultimately leads her to a profound realization about the true meaning of life.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes:
Social mobility, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
