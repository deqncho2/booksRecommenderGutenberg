Synopsis: 
'Old Rome: A Handbook to the Ruins of the City and the Campagna' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient ruins of Rome, providing a comprehensive guide to the city's historical sites and the surrounding countryside. Written by Robert Burn, this handbook offers a vivid exploration of the remnants of the once-mighty Roman Empire, delving into the fascinating stories and legends that have shaped the city's rich history. From the iconic Colosseum to the majestic Roman Forum, Burn's meticulous descriptions and insightful commentary bring these ancient sites to life, transporting readers back in time to experience the grandeur and splendor of Old Rome.

Genres: Travel, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Historical preservation, Cultural heritage
