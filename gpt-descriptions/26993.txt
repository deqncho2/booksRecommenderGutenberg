Synopsis: In 'The Copper Princess: A Story of Lake Superior Mines', Munroe takes readers on a thrilling journey to the rugged and treacherous world of Lake Superior mines. The story follows the life of a young woman named Kate, who finds herself caught between the allure of wealth and the call of her heart. As she navigates the dangerous world of mining, Kate must confront her own desires and the challenges that come with them. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, Munroe paints a captivating portrait of a woman's struggle for independence and love in a male-dominated industry.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Pursuit of wealth, Love and sacrifice
