Synopsis: In 'The Unseen Hand; or, James Renfew and His Boy Helpers' by Elijah Kellogg, a young boy named James Renfew finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he discovers a mysterious handprint on his bedroom window. With the help of his loyal group of boy helpers, James embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind the unseen hand and the secrets it holds. As they delve deeper into the unknown, they encounter unexpected challenges, uncover hidden treasures, and learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of determination. Will James and his boy helpers be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Determination
