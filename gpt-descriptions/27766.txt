Synopsis: In 'Romance of Roman Villas (The Renaissance)', Elizabeth W. Champney takes readers on a captivating journey through the opulent villas of ancient Rome, intertwining history, art, and romance. Through the eyes of a young artist, we are transported to a world of grandeur, where love and passion flourish amidst the ruins of the past. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of these magnificent villas, she uncovers hidden stories of forbidden love, political intrigue, and artistic brilliance. With vivid descriptions and a touch of mystery, Champney invites readers to explore the allure of Roman villas and the timeless power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and passion, Art and creativity, History and heritage
