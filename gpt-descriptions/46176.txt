Synopsis: In "The Knights of the Round Table: Stories of King Arthur and the Holy Grail," William Henry Frost takes readers on a captivating journey into the legendary world of King Arthur and his noble knights. From the enchanting tales of chivalry and bravery to the quest for the Holy Grail, this collection of stories brings to life the epic adventures and timeless characters that have fascinated readers for centuries. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a realm of honor, loyalty, and the pursuit of truth, where the fate of the kingdom hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Quest for Truth
