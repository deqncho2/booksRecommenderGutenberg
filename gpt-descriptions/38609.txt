Synopsis: In "The Corner House Girls on a Houseboat: How they sailed away, what happened on the voyage, and what was discovered," the adventurous Corner House Girls embark on a thrilling journey aboard a houseboat. As they set sail, unexpected events unfold, leading to surprising discoveries that will change their lives forever. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets hidden within the houseboat's voyage.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
