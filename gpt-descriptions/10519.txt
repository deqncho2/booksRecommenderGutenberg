Synopsis: In "Mercy Philbrick's Choice" by Helen Hunt Jackson, a young woman named Mercy finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a complex love triangle. As she navigates the challenges of societal expectations and her own heart's desires, Mercy must make a choice that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, personal freedom
