Synopsis: In 'The Living Link: A Novel' by De Mille, James, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Grant embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between life and death. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers a mysterious connection that challenges everything he thought he knew about the human mind and the nature of existence. With each revelation, Dr. Grant finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the boundaries of science and morality become increasingly blurred. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must confront his own beliefs and confront the consequences of playing with the very fabric of life itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics of scientific experimentation, the nature of consciousness, the pursuit of knowledge
