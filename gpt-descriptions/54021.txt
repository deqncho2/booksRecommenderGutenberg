Synopsis: In 'Dorothy Dale's Promise' by Margaret Penrose, Dorothy finds herself caught up in a mysterious adventure when she makes a promise to a stranger. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her new acquaintance, Dorothy must navigate a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists. With her loyal friends by her side, she embarks on a thrilling journey that will test her courage and determination. Will Dorothy be able to keep her promise and uncover the truth? Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult. Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Secrets.
