Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Twelfth, or the Prince of Wales's Royal Regiment of Lancers', Richard Cannon takes us on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this esteemed regiment from 1715 to 1848. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the rich history of the regiment, offering a fascinating account of their triumphs and challenges. This meticulously researched work provides a comprehensive and engaging narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the remarkable stories and contributions of the Prince of Wales's Royal Regiment of Lancers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
