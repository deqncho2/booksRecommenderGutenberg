Synopsis: In "The Forged Coupon, and Other Stories," Tolstoy presents a collection of gripping tales that delve into the complexities of human nature and morality. From a seemingly innocent act of forgery to a tragic love triangle, these stories explore the consequences of our actions and the power of redemption. As the characters navigate through themes of deceit, justice, and the search for meaning, Tolstoy masterfully weaves together narratives that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Moral Fiction

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Redemption, Justice, Human Nature
