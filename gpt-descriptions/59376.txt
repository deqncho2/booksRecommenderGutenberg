Synopsis: In 'The Patriot' by Charles L. Fontenay, a young man named John is thrust into a world of political intrigue and danger when he discovers a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and betrayal, John must navigate the treacherous landscape of the American Revolution, risking everything for the ideals he holds dear. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, John's journey will test his courage, loyalty, and determination, ultimately shaping his destiny and the fate of a nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Identity
