Synopsis: In 'The Woman's Way' by Charles Garvice, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage with a wealthy but cold-hearted man. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Muriel discovers unexpected allies and embarks on a journey of self-discovery, challenging societal norms and fighting for her own happiness. With secrets, romance, and a touch of mystery, this captivating novel explores the power of love and the strength of a woman determined to forge her own path.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, female empowerment
