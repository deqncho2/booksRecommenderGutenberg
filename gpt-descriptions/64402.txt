Synopsis: In 'The Reign of Gilt' by Phillips, David Graham, a mysterious artifact is discovered, setting off a thrilling race against time and a battle for power. As a group of unlikely allies embarks on a perilous journey, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, face ancient secrets, and confront their own inner demons. With each step, the stakes rise higher, and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. Will they be able to unlock the artifact's true power before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Power and corruption, Redemption, Identity
