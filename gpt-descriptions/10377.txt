Synopsis: In the chilling Gothic tale of 'The Evil Guest' by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a mysterious stranger arrives at a remote country house, bringing with him an air of malevolence and a dark secret. As the tension mounts and the guests become entangled in a web of deceit and danger, they must confront their own fears and confront the evil that lurks within. With its eerie atmosphere and spine-tingling suspense, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking climax.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Deception, Supernatural, Fear
