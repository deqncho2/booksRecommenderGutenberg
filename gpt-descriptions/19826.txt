Synopsis: In 'The White Christmas and other Merry Christmas Plays' by Walter Ben Hare, readers are transported to a collection of heartwarming and festive plays that capture the magic and joy of the holiday season. From a mischievous elf causing havoc in Santa's workshop to a group of children discovering the true meaning of Christmas, each play is filled with laughter, love, and the spirit of giving. With delightful characters and enchanting stories, this collection is sure to bring smiles and warmth to readers of all ages.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Holiday

Themes: Christmas spirit, Family, Generosity, Joy
