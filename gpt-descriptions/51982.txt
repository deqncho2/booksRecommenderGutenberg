Synopsis: In 'Simeon Tetlow's Shadow' by Lee Jennette, a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious diary hidden in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the pages, she becomes captivated by the life of Simeon Tetlow, a renowned artist from the past. Drawn into a world of secrets, passion, and betrayal, Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind Simeon's enigmatic past. As she unravels the mysteries of the diary, Lily finds herself questioning her own identity and the choices she has made. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of love, loss, and redemption that spans generations.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Artistic Expression, Family Dynamics
