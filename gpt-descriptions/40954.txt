Synopsis: In 'Potential Enemy' by Reynolds, Mack, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Technology, Ethics, Trust, Power
