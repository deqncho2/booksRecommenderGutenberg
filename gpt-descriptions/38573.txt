Synopsis:
In "Christina" by L. G. Moberly, a young woman named Christina finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the late 19th century, Christina embarks on a journey of self-discovery as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal fulfillment. As she grapples with the constraints placed upon her by society, Christina must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Gender Roles, Individuality, Love and Sacrifice
