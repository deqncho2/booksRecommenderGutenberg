Synopsis:
In "Cousin Lucy at Study: By the Author of the Rollo Books," we follow the adventures of Lucy, a young girl who is determined to excel in her studies. As she navigates the challenges of school and the expectations of her family, Lucy discovers the power of perseverance and the importance of finding joy in learning. With the help of her supportive cousin, Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres:
Children's Literature, Educational Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Perseverance, Education, Self-Discovery
