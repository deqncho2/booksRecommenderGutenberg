Synopsis: In 'The Worshippers' by Damon Knight, a small town is plagued by a mysterious cult that worships an ancient deity. As the townspeople become increasingly entangled in the cult's web, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind the worshippers and their dark intentions. With suspenseful twists and a chilling atmosphere, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to in the name of faith and the power of belief.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Cults, Religion, Faith, Power of Belief
