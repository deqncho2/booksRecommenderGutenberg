Synopsis: In 'Harold's Bride: A Tale' by A. L. O. E., a young man named Harold finds himself torn between duty and love when he is unexpectedly betrothed to a mysterious bride. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about his bride's past, Harold is faced with a series of challenges that test his loyalty, courage, and the strength of his convictions. With secrets, intrigue, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Duty and Responsibility, Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-Discovery
