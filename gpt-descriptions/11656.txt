Synopsis: In "The Great Shadow and Other Napoleonic Tales," Arthur Conan Doyle takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the Napoleonic Wars. From the battlefields of Waterloo to the treacherous streets of Paris, this collection of tales immerses readers in the gripping adventures and intrigues of the time. As the shadow of Napoleon looms large over Europe, heroes and villains emerge, each driven by their own ambitions and desires. From daring escapades to heart-pounding espionage, these stories offer a thrilling glimpse into the lives of those caught in the wake of one of history's most iconic figures.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: War and conflict, Ambition and power, Loyalty and betrayal
