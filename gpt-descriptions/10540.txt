Synopsis: In the quaint coastal village of Scarborough, four spirited siblings navigate the trials and triumphs of life under the loving care of their widowed mother, Mrs. Carey. As they face financial hardships, unexpected visitors, and the challenges of growing up, the Carey children learn the importance of resilience, family bonds, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Resilience, Family, Love, Growth
