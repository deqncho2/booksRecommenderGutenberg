Synopsis: In 'The Storm. An Essay.' by Daniel Defoe, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the devastating power of nature. Set against the backdrop of a ferocious storm, the essay explores the harrowing experiences of individuals caught in its path. Through vivid descriptions and personal accounts, Defoe paints a vivid picture of the chaos and destruction unleashed by the tempest. As the storm rages on, the essay delves into the profound impact it has on human lives, revealing the resilience and vulnerability of mankind in the face of nature's fury.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Natural Disaster

Themes: Power of Nature, Human Resilience, Vulnerability
