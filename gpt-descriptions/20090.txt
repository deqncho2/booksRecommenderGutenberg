Synopsis: In 'The Uncollected Writings of Thomas de Quincey, Vol. 2: With a Preface and Annotations by James Hogg', readers are invited into the captivating world of Thomas de Quincey, a brilliant and enigmatic writer of the 19th century. This volume presents a collection of de Quincey's lesser-known works, carefully curated and annotated by the esteemed James Hogg. From mesmerizing tales of opium-induced dreams to insightful literary criticism, this compilation offers a glimpse into the mind of a literary genius whose words continue to resonate with readers today.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essays, Literary Criticism

Themes: Addiction, Dreams, Imagination, Literature, Philosophy
