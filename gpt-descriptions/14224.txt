Synopsis: In "Samoa, A Hundred Years Ago And Long Before" by George Turner, embark on a captivating journey to the enchanting islands of Samoa, where the author vividly recounts his experiences and encounters with the indigenous people, their customs, and the breathtaking natural beauty that surrounded him. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming friendships, this memoir offers a unique glimpse into a bygone era, transporting readers to a time when Samoa was untouched by modern influences. As Turner immerses himself in the local culture, he uncovers fascinating stories and traditions that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Adventure, Nostalgia
