Synopsis: 

In 'Glories of Spain', Charles W. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and vibrant culture of Spain. From the majestic architecture of the Alhambra to the passionate rhythms of flamenco, this book unveils the hidden treasures and captivating stories that make Spain a truly remarkable country. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Wood paints a vivid picture of Spain's diverse landscapes, its influential artists and writers, and the enduring spirit of its people. Whether exploring the bustling streets of Madrid or the serene beauty of Andalusia, readers will be transported to a world of enchantment and discovery. 

Genres: Travel, History, Cultural

Themes: Spanish culture, History, Artistic heritage, National identity
