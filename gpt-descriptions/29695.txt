Synopsis: In 'The Hound From The North' by Cullum, Ridgwell, a mysterious and enigmatic figure arrives in a small Canadian town, bringing with him a sense of danger and intrigue. As the townspeople become captivated by his presence, they soon discover that he is not what he seems, and his true intentions may have dire consequences for them all. With secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Consequences
