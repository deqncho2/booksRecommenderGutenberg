Synopsis: 
In 'The Bride of the Mistletoe' by James Lane Allen, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, she must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of happiness. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting holiday season, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of love and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, personal fulfillment
