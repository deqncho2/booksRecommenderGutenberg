Synopsis: 

"The Influence and Development of English Gilds: As Illustrated by the History of the Craft Gilds of Shrewsbury" by Francis Aiden Hibbert takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of craft gilds in Shrewsbury. This meticulously researched work delves into the historical significance and impact of these gilds, shedding light on their influence on English society. From the origins of these organizations to their evolution over time, Hibbert explores the intricate web of relationships, traditions, and power dynamics that shaped the craft gilds of Shrewsbury. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, the author paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, offering readers a unique glimpse into the lives of craftsmen and the communities they belonged to.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Social history, Cultural studies

Themes: Guilds and associations, Social hierarchy, Economic history, Community and identity
