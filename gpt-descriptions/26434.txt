Synopsis: 
In 'The Boys of Crawford's Basin: The Story of a Mountain Ranch in the Early Days of Colorado', author Sidford F. Hamp takes readers on a thrilling journey to the rugged landscapes of Colorado during its early settlement days. The story revolves around a group of young boys who find themselves at the heart of a mysterious and dangerous adventure. As they navigate the treacherous terrain of Crawford's Basin, they uncover secrets that could change their lives forever. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking wilderness and a cast of intriguing characters, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration, Coming of Age
