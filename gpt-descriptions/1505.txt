Synopsis: 
In "The Rape of Lucrece," Shakespeare delves into the tragic tale of a virtuous woman whose life is forever altered by a heinous act. Set in ancient Rome, the narrative follows the beautiful and chaste Lucrece, whose purity becomes the target of a powerful and influential man. As the story unfolds, Shakespeare explores the devastating consequences of this assault, delving into the depths of human nature and the complexities of honor, shame, and revenge.

Genres: Tragedy, Poetry

Themes: Sexual violence, Honor, Shame, Revenge
