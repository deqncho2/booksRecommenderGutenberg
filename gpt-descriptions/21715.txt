Synopsis: In 'Away in the Wilderness' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young boy named Ralph finds himself stranded in the Canadian wilderness after a shipwreck. Alone and facing numerous challenges, Ralph must rely on his resourcefulness and courage to survive. As he navigates through the untamed wilderness, he encounters wild animals, harsh weather conditions, and unexpected dangers. Along the way, Ralph discovers the true meaning of resilience and the power of the human spirit. Will he be able to find his way back home or will he be forever lost in the vast wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Self-discovery
