Synopsis: In "Hira Singh: When India Came to Fight in Flanders" by Talbot Mundy, a young Indian soldier named Hira Singh finds himself torn between his loyalty to his homeland and his duty to fight for the British Empire during World War I. As he navigates the treacherous trenches of Flanders, Hira Singh's experiences challenge his beliefs, test his courage, and force him to confront the complexities of identity, patriotism, and the true meaning of honor.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Identity, Patriotism, Loyalty, Honor, Cultural Conflict
