Synopsis: In 'Oh! Susannah!: A Farcical Comedy in Three Acts', chaos ensues when Susannah, a quirky and free-spirited woman, finds herself caught in a web of hilarious misunderstandings and mistaken identities. As she navigates through a series of comical situations, Susannah's unconventional approach to life challenges the norms of society and leaves everyone around her questioning their own sanity. With a cast of eccentric characters and a plot filled with witty dialogue and unexpected twists, this uproarious comedy will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Identity, Conformity, Individuality
