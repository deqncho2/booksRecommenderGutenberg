Synopsis: In 'At the Seaside' by Warner-Sleigh, Mrs., a group of strangers find themselves drawn together by the enchanting allure of the seaside. As they navigate the sun-soaked beaches and bustling promenades, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing hidden secrets and igniting passionate desires. With each passing day, the idyllic setting becomes a backdrop for love, betrayal, and the pursuit of personal liberation. Will they find solace in the soothing waves or will the crashing tides bring forth unforeseen consequences?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and revelations, Freedom and self-discovery
