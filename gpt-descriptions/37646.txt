Synopsis: 

'Forest Trees of Illinois: How to Know Them' takes readers on an immersive journey through the diverse and captivating world of Illinois' forest trees. Written by a team of knowledgeable authors, this comprehensive guide provides a wealth of information on identifying and understanding the various tree species found in the state. From the majestic oaks to the delicate dogwoods, the book offers detailed descriptions, vivid illustrations, and practical tips to help readers develop a deeper appreciation for the natural beauty that surrounds them. Whether you are a nature enthusiast, a hiker, or simply curious about the trees in your backyard, this book is an invaluable resource that will ignite your curiosity and inspire you to explore the enchanting world of Illinois' forest trees.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Reference

Themes: Nature appreciation, Tree identification, Environmental conservation
