Synopsis: 
Don Quixote is a captivating novel that follows the adventures of an eccentric gentleman named Alonso Quixano, who becomes convinced that he is a knight-errant named Don Quixote. With his loyal squire, Sancho Panza, by his side, Don Quixote embarks on a series of misadventures, battling windmills he believes to be giants and rescuing damsels in distress. As the line between reality and fantasy blurs, Don Quixote's chivalrous pursuits bring both laughter and reflection, challenging the reader to question the nature of truth and the power of imagination.

Genres: 
Satire, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: 
Reality vs. Illusion, Madness, Idealism, The Power of Imagination
