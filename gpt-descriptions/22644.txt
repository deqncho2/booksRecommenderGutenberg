Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scout Treasure Hunters; Or, The Lost Treasure of Buffalo Hollow' by Charles Henry Lerrigo, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embarks on a thrilling quest to uncover the long-lost treasure hidden deep within Buffalo Hollow. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter mysterious clues, and face unexpected challenges, the young scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and determination to unravel the secrets of the past and claim the coveted treasure. Will they succeed in their quest, or will the secrets of Buffalo Hollow remain forever buried?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
