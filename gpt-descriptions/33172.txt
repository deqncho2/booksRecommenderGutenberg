Synopsis: 

'Down Town Brooklyn: A Report to the Comptroller of the City of New York on Sites for Public Buildings and the Relocation of the Elevated Railroad Tracks now in Lower Fulton Street, Borough of Brooklyn' is a comprehensive report compiled by the Committee of Ten in Brooklyn, New York. This report explores potential sites for public buildings and proposes the relocation of the elevated railroad tracks in Lower Fulton Street. It delves into the historical, architectural, and urban planning aspects of the area, providing valuable insights and recommendations for the city's development.

Genres: Non-fiction, Urban Planning, Architecture

Themes: Urban development, Infrastructure improvement, Historical preservation
