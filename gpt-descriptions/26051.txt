Synopsis: In "Bolshevism: A Curse & Danger to the Workers," H. W. Lee delves into the heart of a political movement that has captivated the world. Through a thought-provoking exploration, Lee uncovers the hidden dangers and unforeseen consequences that Bolshevism poses to the very people it claims to champion. With meticulous research and compelling arguments, this book challenges the prevailing narrative and sheds light on the true nature of this ideology.

Genres: Political analysis, historical critique, social commentary

Themes: Political ideologies, worker's rights, societal impact
