Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls on the Farm; Or, Bessie King's New Chum" by Jane L. Stewart, Bessie King finds herself in a new and exciting adventure when she visits her aunt's farm. As she immerses herself in farm life, Bessie encounters unexpected challenges and forms new friendships with the Camp Fire Girls. Together, they navigate the joys and hardships of farm work, discovering the importance of teamwork, resilience, and the beauty of nature along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Teamwork, Resilience, Nature
