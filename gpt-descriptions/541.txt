Synopsis: In the high society of 1870s New York, Newland Archer finds himself torn between duty and desire when he becomes engaged to the perfect woman, May Welland, while secretly yearning for the unconventional and passionate Countess Ellen Olenska. As Archer navigates the intricacies of societal expectations and the constraints of his own moral compass, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the sacrifices required to maintain the facade of innocence.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Social commentary

Themes: Conformity vs. individuality, Love and desire, Society and its constraints, Class and privilege
