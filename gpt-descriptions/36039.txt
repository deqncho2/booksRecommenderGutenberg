Synopsis: In 'The Giant Crab, and Other Tales from Old India', W.H.D. Rouse takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient India, where mythical creatures, cunning tricksters, and magical beings come to life. From the enchanting story of a brave prince who must outwit a giant crab to the tale of a clever monkey who outsmarts a group of greedy merchants, each story is filled with adventure, suspense, and unexpected twists. With vivid descriptions and rich cultural details, Rouse transports readers to a world of wonder and imagination, where heroes and villains clash in epic battles and where the power of wit and courage can triumph over any challenge.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Trickery, Supernatural beings, Cultural traditions
