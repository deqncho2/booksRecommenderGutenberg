Synopsis: In 'A Boy's Fortune; Or, The Strange Adventures of Ben Baker' by Horatio Alger Jr., readers are introduced to Ben Baker, a young boy who finds himself thrust into a series of unexpected and thrilling adventures. From encounters with mysterious strangers to unexpected inheritances, Ben's life takes a dramatic turn as he navigates through a world filled with secrets and surprises. As he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Ben must confront challenges and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that come his way and find his true fortune?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Fate
