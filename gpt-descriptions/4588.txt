Synopsis:
In 'The Allen House; Or, Twenty Years Ago and Now', readers are transported to a mysterious and haunting mansion that holds secrets from the past. As the story unfolds, the lives of the present inhabitants become intertwined with the tragic events that occurred two decades earlier. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the eerie occurrences within the Allen House.

Genres:
Gothic fiction, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes:
Family secrets, Redemption, Supernatural elements
