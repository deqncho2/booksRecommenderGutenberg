Synopsis: 
In 'The Sunny South: An Autumn in Spain and Majorca', John William Clayton takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant landscapes and rich culture of Spain and Majorca. Through his vivid descriptions and personal experiences, Clayton immerses readers in the beauty and charm of these Mediterranean destinations. From the bustling streets of Barcelona to the tranquil shores of Majorca, the author's encounters with locals, historical sites, and culinary delights paint a captivating picture of the region. As Clayton delves deeper into the heart of Spain and Majorca, he uncovers hidden gems and uncovers the secrets that make these places truly special. 

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Beauty of nature, Appreciation of local customs and traditions
