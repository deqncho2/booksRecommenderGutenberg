Synopsis: Emma Bovary, a young and passionate woman, finds herself trapped in a stifling marriage in a small provincial town. Seeking escape from her mundane existence, she embarks on a series of illicit affairs and indulges in extravagant fantasies. As her desires grow insatiable, Emma spirals into a world of deception, disillusionment, and despair. Flaubert's masterful portrayal of Emma's inner turmoil and the consequences of her actions offers a captivating exploration of human desires and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Literary fiction, Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Romanticism, Dissatisfaction, Illusion vs Reality, Conformity, Gender Roles
