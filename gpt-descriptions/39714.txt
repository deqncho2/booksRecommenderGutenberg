Synopsis: 

In the D.C. Powered Timing Light Model 161.2158 for 12 Volt Ignition Systems, Sears, Roebuck and Company takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of automotive mechanics. This meticulously crafted owner's manual unveils the secrets behind the powerful timing light, providing step-by-step instructions and expert tips to optimize the performance of 12 volt ignition systems. With its comprehensive approach and user-friendly language, this manual becomes an indispensable tool for both seasoned mechanics and enthusiastic DIYers.
