Synopsis: 
In 'The Mountain Spring, and Other Poems' by Nancy Rebecca Campbell Glass, readers are transported to a world where nature's beauty intertwines with the depths of human emotions. Through a collection of evocative and lyrical poems, Glass explores the delicate balance between love and loss, hope and despair, and the eternal quest for meaning. From the enchanting imagery of a mountain spring to the poignant reflections on the passage of time, each poem invites readers to delve into the complexities of the human experience and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Nature, Emotion

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Time, Reflection
