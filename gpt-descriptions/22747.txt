Synopsis: In 'Financial Crime and Corruption' by Samuel Vaknin, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of the global financial system. This thought-provoking book delves into the intricate web of deceit, manipulation, and illicit activities that plague the world of finance. With real-life examples and expert analysis, Vaknin exposes the shocking truth behind financial crime and corruption, leaving readers questioning the very foundations of our economic systems.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Economics

Themes: Financial fraud, Corruption, Ethics in finance, Global economy
