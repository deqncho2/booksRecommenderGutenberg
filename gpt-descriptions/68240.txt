Synopsis:
Betty Wales, B. A. is a captivating story that follows the journey of Betty Wales, a young and ambitious college student. As she navigates the challenges of academia, friendships, and societal expectations, Betty discovers her true passions and strengths. With her determination and resilience, she strives to make a difference in the world and find her place in society. Filled with relatable characters and unexpected twists, this coming-of-age tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes:
Identity, Ambition, Friendship, Self-discovery
