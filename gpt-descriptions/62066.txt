Synopsis: In the year 1874, the Parish of St. Mary Abbotts in Kensington experienced a series of mysterious events that left the community in shock. From unexplained illnesses to strange occurrences within the church walls, the annual report by Dudfield, T. Orme delves into the dark secrets and hidden truths that plagued the parish throughout the year. As the investigation unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue, uncovering the unsettling history of the parish and the individuals who call it home.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Supernatural Phenomena, Community and Identity
