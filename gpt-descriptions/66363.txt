Synopsis: 
'A Soldier's Diary' by Scott, Ralph is a gripping and emotional tale that follows the journey of a young soldier as he navigates the horrors of war and the complexities of human nature. Through the pages of his diary, we witness his struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact that war has on his psyche. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and the true cost of war becomes painfully clear. This poignant narrative explores the depths of human resilience, the bonds of brotherhood, and the profound impact of war on the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Friendship, Loss, Resilience
