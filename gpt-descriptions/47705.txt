Synopsis: 
In "A Sister of the Red Cross: A Tale of the South African War," the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of the South African War. The story follows the courageous and compassionate Mary O'Neill, a young woman who joins the Red Cross as a nurse to aid the wounded soldiers on the front lines. As Mary navigates the horrors of war, she encounters unexpected friendships, faces personal challenges, and finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue. With danger lurking at every turn, Mary's determination to make a difference is put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to save lives and find her own path amidst the chaos of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Love, Identity
