Synopsis: 
'John Call Dalton, M.D., U.S.V.' is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a remarkable journey through the life of Dr. John Call Dalton, a renowned physician who served in the United States Volunteers during the Civil War. From his early days as a medical student to his experiences on the battlefield, Dalton's memoir offers a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of a doctor during one of the most tumultuous periods in American history. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this memoir provides a fascinating glimpse into the life of a dedicated physician and the impact of war on both individuals and society.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Medicine and war, personal sacrifice, resilience
