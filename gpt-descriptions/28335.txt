Synopsis: In 'How Two Boys Made Their Own Electrical Apparatus', two young boys embark on an exciting journey of discovery as they learn to create their own electrical devices. With step-by-step instructions, they explore the world of elementary electricity, making various simple apparatus along the way. From basic circuits to electromagnets, the boys' enthusiasm and ingenuity shine through as they uncover the wonders of this fascinating field. This engaging and educational book is sure to captivate readers of all ages, inspiring them to explore the world of science and electricity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Curiosity, Ingenuity, Science Education
