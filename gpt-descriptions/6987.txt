Synopsis: In 'Five Little Peppers Abroad', the lovable Pepper family embarks on an exciting adventure across the Atlantic Ocean. Join Polly, Ben, Joel, Davie, and Phronsie as they explore new cultures, encounter unexpected challenges, and make lifelong memories. From the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland, the Peppers' journey is filled with laughter, friendship, and heartwarming moments. Will their travels bring them closer together as a family? Genres: Adventure, Family, Coming-of-Age. Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Family Bonds.
