Synopsis: 
In 'The Clue' by Wells, Carolyn, a mysterious murder takes place in a luxurious mansion, leaving everyone baffled. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the list of suspects grows longer. With each clue, the tension rises, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, trying to piece together the puzzle before the thrilling climax.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue
