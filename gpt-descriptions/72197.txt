Synopsis: In "The Akkra Case" by Miriam Allen De Ford, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing murder mystery in the futuristic city of Akkra. As the detective delves deeper into the case, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Crime and Justice, Corruption, Technology and Society
