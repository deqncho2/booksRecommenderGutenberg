Synopsis: 
'Celtic Tales, Told to the Children' by Louey Chisholm is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the mystical world of Celtic folklore. From brave warriors and cunning fairies to magical creatures and ancient legends, this book weaves together a tapestry of captivating tales that will leave readers spellbound. Each story is filled with adventure, mystery, and a touch of whimsy, making it a perfect read for both children and adults alike. 

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Bravery, Magic, Tradition, Mythology
