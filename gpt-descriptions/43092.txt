Synopsis: In 'The Dead Secret: A Novel' by Wilkie Collins, a young woman named Rosamund Treverton discovers a mysterious secret hidden within the walls of her ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and family secrets that threaten to unravel her world. With the help of an unlikely ally, Rosamund must navigate through a labyrinth of hidden clues and unexpected revelations to uncover the truth before it consumes her. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Family dynamics, Betrayal, Redemption
