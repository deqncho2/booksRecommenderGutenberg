Synopsis: In 'The Lost Naval Papers' by Copplestone, Bennet, a retired naval officer stumbles upon a mysterious set of documents that could potentially change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the papers, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of espionage, betrayal, and international intrigue. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect the secrets that could alter the balance of power forever.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Power Struggles
