Synopsis:
In 'Dora Deane; Or, The East India Uncle' by Mary Jane Holmes, readers are introduced to the young and spirited Dora Deane, who finds herself caught between the allure of wealth and the importance of love. When her long-lost uncle returns from the East Indies, Dora's life takes an unexpected turn as she is thrust into a world of opulence and luxury. However, as she navigates this new lifestyle, Dora must grapple with the true meaning of happiness and the sacrifices she is willing to make for it. With secrets, romance, and a touch of mystery, 'Dora Deane' is a captivating tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wealth and happiness, Love and sacrifice
