Synopsis: In 'The Dreadnought Boys on Battle Practice' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey aboard a battleship. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they encounter unexpected challenges and uncover a mysterious secret that could change the course of their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their courage, teamwork, and quick thinking to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Discovery
