Synopsis: In 'The Discovery of America by the Northmen, 985-1015', Edmund F. Slafter delves into the fascinating historical accounts of the Norse exploration of North America long before Columbus. Through meticulous research and compelling narratives, Slafter uncovers the remarkable journeys of these intrepid seafarers, shedding light on their encounters with indigenous peoples and the impact they had on the New World. From the mysterious Vinland to the legendary Leif Erikson, this book unveils a lesser-known chapter in the history of America, captivating readers with tales of exploration, adventure, and cultural exchange.

Genres: History, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Cultural Exchange, Indigenous Peoples, Pre-Columbian History
