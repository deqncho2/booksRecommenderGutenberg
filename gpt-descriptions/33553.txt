Synopsis: 
'In a Belgian Garden, and Other Poems' by Frank Oliver Call is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking poems that take readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences. From the enchanting beauty of a Belgian garden to the depths of human emotions, these poems explore themes of love, loss, nature, and the complexities of the human condition. Each poem is a window into the poet's soul, inviting readers to reflect on their own lives and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Human emotions, Reflection
