Synopsis: In 'Army Boys on the Firing Line; or, Holding Back the German Drive' by Randall, Homer, a group of young soldiers find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I, facing the relentless onslaught of the German army. As they navigate the treacherous trenches and battlefields, these brave soldiers must rely on their courage, camaraderie, and resourcefulness to hold back the enemy's advance. With danger lurking at every turn, the Army Boys must prove their mettle and fight for their lives in the face of overwhelming odds.

Genres: War fiction, Adventure, Historical fiction

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Survival
