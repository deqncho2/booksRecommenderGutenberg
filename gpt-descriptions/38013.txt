Synopsis: In 'Animals of the Past', Lucas takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating world of prehistoric creatures. From the towering dinosaurs to the mysterious marine reptiles, Lucas unveils the secrets of these ancient animals, shedding light on their behavior, habitats, and the incredible adaptations that allowed them to thrive. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book brings the past to life, offering a glimpse into a world that existed millions of years ago.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paleontology, Natural History

Themes: Prehistoric life, Evolution, Extinction, Adaptation
