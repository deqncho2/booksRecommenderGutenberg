Synopsis: In "A Kiss for Cinderella: A Comedy," Barrie takes us on a whimsical journey into the world of dreams and imagination. The story revolves around a young woman named Cinderella, who finds solace in her dreams amidst the harsh realities of her life. As she navigates through her daily struggles, she discovers the power of love, hope, and the magic that lies within her own heart. With a touch of humor and enchantment, Barrie invites readers to join Cinderella on her extraordinary adventure, where dreams come true in the most unexpected ways.

Genres: Comedy, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Dreams and imagination, Love and hope, Overcoming adversity
