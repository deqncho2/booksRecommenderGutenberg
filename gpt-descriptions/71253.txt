Synopsis: In "Cleopatra's Needle: With Brief Notes on Egypt and Egyptian Obelisks," Sir Erasmus Wilson takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Egypt, exploring the fascinating history and significance of Egyptian obelisks. From the majestic Cleopatra's Needle, a towering obelisk that now stands in London, to the mysterious rituals and beliefs surrounding these ancient monuments, Wilson delves into the secrets and wonders of Egypt's rich cultural heritage. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the world of obelisks and their enduring legacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Travelogue

Themes: Ancient Egypt, Cultural heritage, Symbolism, Archaeological exploration
