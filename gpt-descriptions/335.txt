Synopsis: 
Frances Waldeaux, a captivating and enigmatic woman, finds herself at the center of a scandalous love triangle in this gripping novel. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows Frances as she navigates the complexities of her relationships with two very different men: the wealthy and powerful Mr. Waldeaux, and the charming and mysterious Dr. Kirby. As her heart becomes entangled in a web of desire and deceit, Frances must make choices that will shape her future and challenge societal norms. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the depths of passion, the constraints of society, and the pursuit of true love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Morality, Power Dynamics
