Synopsis:
In 'Men of Iron' by Howard Pyle, a young boy named Myles Falworth finds himself thrust into a world of chivalry, honor, and danger. Trained as a knight, Myles must navigate the treacherous waters of medieval society, facing formidable foes and uncovering long-held secrets. As he strives to prove his worth and protect those he loves, Myles discovers the true meaning of bravery and loyalty. Will he rise above the challenges and become the knight he was destined to be?

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Chivalry, Honor, Identity, Loyalty
