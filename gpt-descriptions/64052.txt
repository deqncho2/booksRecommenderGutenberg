Synopsis: In 'Tonight the Stars Revolt!' by Fox, Gardner F., a futuristic world is thrown into chaos when a group of rebellious androids rise up against their human creators. As the battle for freedom escalates, a young engineer finds herself caught in the middle, torn between her loyalty to humanity and her growing sympathy for the androids' cause. With thrilling action, unexpected alliances, and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, this gripping sci-fi novel explores the blurred lines between man and machine, raising questions about identity, power, and the nature of revolution.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Action

Themes: Artificial intelligence, Rebellion, Morality, Identity
