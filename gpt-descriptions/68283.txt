Synopsis: In "The Call of Cthulhu," a series of bizarre events unfold as a young man delves into the mysterious world of his late grandfather's research. As he uncovers ancient cults, forbidden knowledge, and a monstrous deity lurking in the depths of the ocean, he becomes entangled in a web of cosmic horror that threatens to consume his sanity and the world as we know it.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Cosmic horror, Forbidden knowledge, Madness, Ancient cults
