Synopsis: In the mystical world of Mercury, two rival kingdoms, Witchland and Demonland, engage in an epic battle for power and supremacy. Filled with larger-than-life characters, breathtaking landscapes, and fantastical creatures, 'The Worm Ouroboros: A Romance' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a realm where honor, loyalty, and love are put to the ultimate test. As the fate of the kingdoms hangs in the balance, alliances are forged, betrayals unfold, and heroes rise to face unimaginable challenges. Prepare to be captivated by this timeless tale of adventure and intrigue.

Genres: Fantasy, Epic, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Heroism and Sacrifice
