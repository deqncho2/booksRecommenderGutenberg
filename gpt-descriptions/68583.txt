Synopsis: In "Elastic and non-elastic narrow fabrics: and a chapter on narrow fabrics made on knitting machines," Samuel Brown delves into the fascinating world of narrow fabrics, exploring their various types and manufacturing processes. From elastic bands to non-elastic ribbons, this comprehensive guide provides an in-depth analysis of the materials, techniques, and applications of these versatile textiles. With a special focus on narrow fabrics made on knitting machines, Brown uncovers the intricate artistry and engineering behind their creation, revealing the secrets of this often overlooked industry. Whether you're a textile enthusiast or simply curious about the hidden world of narrow fabrics, this book will captivate your imagination and expand your understanding of this essential component of our everyday lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Textile Engineering, Manufacturing

Themes: Textile production, Fabric technology, Industrial processes
