Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Legend of Ulenspiegel', the mischievous and witty Ulenspiegel, along with his loyal friend Lamme Goedzak, continue their thrilling adventures in the enchanting Land of Flanders and beyond. From their encounters with powerful rulers and cunning spies to their involvement in daring rebellions and secret missions, Ulenspiegel and Lamme find themselves embroiled in a web of danger, intrigue, and heroism. As they navigate through the tumultuous times of war and political unrest, their bond is tested, and they must rely on their wit and courage to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Rebellion, Friendship, Heroism, Political Intrigue
