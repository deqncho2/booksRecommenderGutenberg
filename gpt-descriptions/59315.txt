Synopsis: 

In "The Periodical Cicada, 'the 17-year Locust'" by the United States Department of Agriculture, a fascinating and mysterious creature takes center stage. This captivating book delves into the life cycle and behavior of the periodical cicada, also known as the 17-year locust. Through vivid descriptions and scientific insights, readers are transported into the world of these enigmatic insects, uncovering their remarkable survival strategies and the awe-inspiring phenomenon of their synchronized emergence. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Nature's wonders, Adaptation and survival strategies
