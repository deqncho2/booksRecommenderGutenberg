Synopsis: 
'The Story of the Great War, Volume 2: History of the European War from Official Sources' takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of the European War. Drawing from official sources, this volume provides a comprehensive account of the war's progression, from the early battles to the evolving strategies and the impact on nations and individuals. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, the book delves into the political, military, and social aspects of the conflict, offering a captivating narrative that keeps readers engrossed until the very end.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War literature

Themes: War and its consequences, Nationalism, Leadership and strategy, Human resilience and sacrifice
