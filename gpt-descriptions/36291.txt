Synopsis: In the final volume of "Ormond; Or, The Secret Witness," the gripping tale reaches its climax as the secrets and mysteries surrounding the enigmatic Ormond are finally revealed. As the characters navigate through treacherous plots, dangerous alliances, and unexpected betrayals, the truth behind Ormond's past and his connection to the mysterious Constantia is unveiled. With lives hanging in the balance, the characters must confront their deepest fears and make impossible choices that will forever alter their destinies. Will justice prevail, or will the secrets of the past consume them all?

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, deception, betrayal, justice, the power of secrets
