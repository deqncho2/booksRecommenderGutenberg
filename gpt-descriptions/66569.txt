Synopsis: 
In 'Flight Perilous!' by Ray C. Noll, a group of strangers find themselves aboard a seemingly ordinary flight, only to discover that they are trapped in a high-stakes game of survival. As the plane hurtles through the sky, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the passengers must confront their deepest fears in order to make it out alive. With each passing moment, the danger intensifies, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind this perilous flight.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Trust, Deception
