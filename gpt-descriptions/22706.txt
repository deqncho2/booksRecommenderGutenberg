Synopsis: In 'Two Days' Solitary Imprisonment: 1898', Bellamy, Edward takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing experience of a man confined to solitary confinement for two days. As the protagonist battles with his own thoughts and fears, the reader is drawn into a suspenseful narrative that explores the depths of human resilience and the fragility of the human mind. With each passing hour, the boundaries between reality and imagination blur, leaving the reader questioning what is truly happening within the confines of the prison cell. This captivating tale will keep you on the edge of your seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Suspense, Historical fiction

Themes: Isolation, Mental resilience, Perception of reality
