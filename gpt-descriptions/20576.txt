Synopsis: In 'Two Suffolk Friends' by Francis Hindes Groome, two childhood friends, Tom and Dick, find themselves on opposite sides of a conflict that threatens to tear their friendship apart. Set against the backdrop of the beautiful Suffolk countryside, their loyalty and love for each other are put to the ultimate test. As they navigate through personal struggles and societal pressures, the reader is taken on a journey filled with heart-wrenching choices, unexpected twists, and the enduring power of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Conflict, Identity, Social Change
