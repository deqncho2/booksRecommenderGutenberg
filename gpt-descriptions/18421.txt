Synopsis: Bunny Brown and His Sister Sue Keeping Store follows the adventures of Bunny and Sue as they embark on a new venture - running their very own store. With their parents' guidance, the siblings learn the ins and outs of managing a business, facing challenges and making new friends along the way. From stocking shelves to serving customers, Bunny and Sue discover the joys and responsibilities of entrepreneurship, all while navigating the excitement and surprises that come with their new endeavor.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Responsibility, Entrepreneurship, Friendship
