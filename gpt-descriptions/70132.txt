Synopsis: 
In 'Mezzotints in modern music', James Huneker takes us on a captivating journey through the lives and works of some of the greatest composers in history. From the passionate melodies of Brahms to the dramatic symphonies of Wagner, Huneker delves into the intricate details of their compositions, revealing the hidden stories and emotions behind each piece. With his insightful analysis and vivid descriptions, he paints a vivid picture of the musical genius and the world that inspired them. 

Genres: Music criticism, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Music appreciation, Artistic inspiration, Creative process
