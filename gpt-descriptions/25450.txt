Synopsis: In 'The Rogue Elephant: The Boys' Big Game Series' by Whitney Elliott, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling expedition deep into the heart of the African wilderness. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter dangerous wildlife, they stumble upon a mysterious rogue elephant with a dark secret. With their courage and resourcefulness put to the test, the boys must unravel the enigma surrounding the elephant while facing unexpected challenges that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
