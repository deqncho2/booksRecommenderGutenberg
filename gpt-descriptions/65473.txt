Synopsis: In this captivating satirical novel, Gulliver's Travels takes readers on an extraordinary journey through strange lands and encounters with peculiar beings. Join Lemuel Gulliver, a ship's surgeon, as he embarks on four remarkable voyages that will challenge his beliefs and expose the absurdities of human nature. From a land of tiny people to a kingdom of giants, Gulliver's adventures will both amaze and entertain, while offering a sharp critique of society and its flaws.

Genres: Satire, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Human nature, Society and its flaws, Critique of politics and government
