Synopsis: In the small, secluded town of Rayton, a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that unravels the dark secrets hidden within the community. As the investigation unfolds, the protagonist, a determined outsider, finds themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried truths. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the search for the truth becomes a thrilling journey through the backwoods of Rayton, where danger lurks around every corner and the line between friend and foe blurs. Will the protagonist uncover the truth before it's too late, or will they become the next victim of this enigmatic backwoods mystery?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Betrayal, Deception, Survival
