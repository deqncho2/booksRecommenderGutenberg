Synopsis: 
In 'Olive: A Novel', we follow the life of Olive Rothesay, a young woman who faces numerous challenges and heartbreaks as she navigates through the complexities of love, family, and society. From her humble beginnings in a small village to her encounters with wealth and privilege, Olive's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers captivated until the very end. As she grapples with her own desires and the expectations placed upon her, Olive must make difficult choices that will shape her future and test the strength of her character.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Love and relationships, Social class and societal expectations, Personal growth and self-discovery
