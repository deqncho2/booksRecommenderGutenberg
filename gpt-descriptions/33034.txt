Synopsis: 
Step back in time to 1914 and discover the captivating world of Premo Cameras, a historical masterpiece by Canadian Kodak Company. This enthralling tale takes readers on a journey through the early days of photography, as a young photographer named James embarks on a quest to capture the essence of life through his lens. From the bustling streets of Toronto to the serene landscapes of the Canadian wilderness, James encounters unexpected challenges and encounters that test his passion for his craft. As he navigates the rapidly evolving world of photography, James must confront his own fears and insecurities, ultimately discovering the true power of capturing moments that last a lifetime.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Photography, Pursuit of passion, Self-discovery
