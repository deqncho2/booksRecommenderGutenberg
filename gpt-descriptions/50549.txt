Synopsis: 
In "Three Wisconsin Cushings," Theron Wilber Haight delves into the captivating lives of Howard B., Alonzo H., and William B. Cushing, three remarkable individuals born into a pioneering family in Waukesha County. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Haight paints a vivid portrait of these brothers, their unique journeys, and the impact they had on their community. From their early days on the family farm to their remarkable achievements in various fields, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these unsung heroes of Wisconsin history.

Genres: Biography, History, Local Interest

Themes: Family legacy, Pioneering spirit, Community impact
