Synopsis:
In 'Keeping Tryst: A Tale of King Arthur's Time' by Annie F. Johnston, readers are transported to the enchanting world of King Arthur and his knights. The story follows the adventures of a young girl named Tryst, who finds herself caught up in the legendary realm of Camelot. As Tryst navigates the treacherous waters of loyalty, love, and honor, she discovers secrets that could change the fate of the kingdom. With the fate of Camelot hanging in the balance, Tryst must make difficult choices that will test her courage and determination. Will she be able to keep her tryst and fulfill her destiny?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Honor, Destiny
