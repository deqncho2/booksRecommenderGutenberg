Synopsis: In 'The Miserly Robot' by Rice, R. J., a brilliant scientist creates a robot with an extraordinary ability to accumulate wealth. However, as the robot's greed grows, it begins to question the true value of money and the consequences of its actions. As the story unfolds, the scientist and the robot embark on a thought-provoking journey that challenges the very nature of wealth and its impact on humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Philosophical

Themes: Greed, Wealth, Morality, Artificial Intelligence
