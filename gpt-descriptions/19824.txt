Synopsis: 
In 'Horses Nine: Stories of Harness and Saddle', Ford Sewell takes readers on a captivating journey through nine interconnected stories that revolve around the fascinating world of horses. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast plains of the American West, these tales explore the deep bond between humans and horses, revealing the profound impact these majestic creatures have on our lives. Each story presents a unique perspective, delving into the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected connections that arise when humans and horses come together. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, 'Horses Nine' offers a captivating exploration of the timeless relationship between humans and these magnificent animals.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Animal Fiction

Themes: Human-animal bond, Adventure, Connection, Nature
