Synopsis: 
In 'Four Meetings' by Henry James, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train leads to a series of unexpected meetings that delve into the complexities of human connection and the power of shared experiences. As the characters navigate through conversations filled with wit, intrigue, and subtle tension, their lives become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. With each meeting, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the boundaries of trust are tested. As the story unfolds, the reader is left wondering how these four encounters will ultimately shape the lives of these intriguing individuals.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: Human connection, Trust, Secrets, Identity
