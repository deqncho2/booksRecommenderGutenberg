Synopsis:
In 'A Poor Gentleman' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we follow the life of a struggling artist named Austin Wentworth. Despite his immense talent, Austin finds himself trapped in a cycle of poverty and obscurity. However, a chance encounter with a mysterious benefactor offers him a glimmer of hope and the possibility of a brighter future. As Austin navigates the complexities of his newfound circumstances, he must grapple with the moral dilemmas that arise and question the true nature of success and happiness.

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes:
Poverty, Artistic Pursuit, Morality, Social Inequality
