Synopsis: 
In "The Ceramic Art: A Compendium of The History and Manufacture of Pottery and Porcelain," Jennie J. Young takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of ceramics. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this comprehensive guide explores the rich history, intricate techniques, and exquisite beauty of pottery and porcelain. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Young unveils the secrets behind the creation of these timeless art forms, revealing the remarkable craftsmanship and artistic vision that have shaped cultures throughout the ages. Whether you are a seasoned collector or simply curious about the art of ceramics, this book is sure to ignite your passion and deepen your appreciation for this enduring craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, History

Themes: Craftsmanship, Cultural heritage, Artistic expression
