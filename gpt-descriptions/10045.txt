Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Darrin's Second Year at Annapolis: Or, Two Midshipmen as Naval Academy "Youngsters"', we follow the thrilling journey of Dave Darrin and his friend Dan Dalzell as they navigate their second year at the prestigious Naval Academy. As they face new challenges and adventures, the young midshipmen must prove their mettle and determination in the face of adversity. From intense academic pressures to demanding physical training, Dave and Dan must rely on their friendship and resilience to overcome the obstacles that come their way. With their futures as naval officers hanging in the balance, the stakes have never been higher for these two young men.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Military

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Honor, Duty
