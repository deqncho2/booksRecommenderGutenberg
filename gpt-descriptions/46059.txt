Synopsis: In "The Adopting of Rosa Marie: (A Sequel to Dandelion Cottage)" by Rankin, Carroll Watson, the beloved characters from Dandelion Cottage return in a heartwarming tale of love, family, and unexpected surprises. When the four friends discover a mysterious baby left on their doorstep, they embark on a journey to uncover the truth behind Rosa Marie's origins and find her a loving home. As they navigate the challenges of adoption and face unforeseen obstacles, their bond is tested, and they learn the true meaning of friendship and the power of unconditional love.

Genres: Fiction, Children's Literature, Family Drama

Themes: Friendship, Adoption, Love, Family Dynamics
