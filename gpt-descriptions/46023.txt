Synopsis: In 'The Garden of Swords' by Pemberton, Max, a young and ambitious artist named Julian finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is commissioned to paint a portrait of a captivating woman. As he delves deeper into her world, Julian discovers a hidden society of artists, each with their own dark secrets and desires. With his own life at stake, Julian must navigate a treacherous path filled with deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic woman and the garden of swords that holds the key to their destinies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Artistic passion, Secrets and deception, Forbidden love
