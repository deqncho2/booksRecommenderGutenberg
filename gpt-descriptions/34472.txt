Synopsis: In 'Origin and Early History of the Fashion Plate', Nevinson takes readers on a captivating journey through time to explore the fascinating origins and evolution of the fashion plate. From its humble beginnings as a simple illustration to its rise as a powerful tool of influence, this book unveils the secrets behind the creation and dissemination of these iconic images. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Nevinson delves into the lives of the artists, publishers, and fashion enthusiasts who shaped the fashion plate's history, revealing the profound impact it had on society and the fashion industry. Prepare to be enthralled by this captivating exploration of a cultural phenomenon that continues to shape our perception of style.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Cultural studies

Themes: Fashion and society, Visual culture, Historical influence
