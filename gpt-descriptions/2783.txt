Synopsis: In "The Trampling of the Lilies" by Sabatini, Rafael, a young French nobleman finds himself caught in the midst of the tumultuous French Revolution. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape, he must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty, love, and honor. With danger lurking at every turn, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal, where survival becomes a matter of cunning and courage. Will he be able to protect his loved ones and preserve his own ideals in a world consumed by chaos?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Honor
