Synopsis: In 'The Financier: A Novel', Theodore Dreiser takes us into the cutthroat world of finance and power in late 19th century America. We follow the ambitious and cunning Frank Cowperwood as he rises from humble beginnings to become a prominent financier, navigating through scandals, political corruption, and personal relationships. As he amasses wealth and influence, Frank's insatiable desire for more threatens to consume him and everyone around him. Will he be able to maintain his grip on power, or will his relentless pursuit of success lead to his downfall?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Business

Themes: Ambition, Power, Corruption, Wealth, Morality
