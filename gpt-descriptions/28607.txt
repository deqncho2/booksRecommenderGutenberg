Synopsis: In 'The Road and the Roadside' by Potter, Burton Willis, a mysterious traveler embarks on a journey along a desolate road, encountering a series of enigmatic characters and strange occurrences along the way. As the traveler delves deeper into the unknown, the line between reality and imagination becomes blurred, leading to a captivating exploration of the human psyche and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Magical Realism

Themes: Identity, Perception, Existentialism
