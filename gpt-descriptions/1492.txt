Synopsis: 

"The Life of Columbus: From His Own Letters and Journals and Other Documents of His Time" by Edward Everett Hale takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Christopher Columbus. Drawing from Columbus's own letters and journals, as well as other historical documents, Hale provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of the renowned explorer. From his humble beginnings to his audacious voyages across the Atlantic, readers will be enthralled by the triumphs, challenges, and discoveries that shaped Columbus's extraordinary life. 

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Legacy
