Synopsis: In "Daedalus; or, Science and the Future," J.B.S. Haldane takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of science and its potential impact on the future. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Haldane explores various scientific advancements and their implications, from genetic engineering to space travel, and delves into the ethical and philosophical questions that arise. With a blend of scientific expertise and imaginative storytelling, Haldane paints a vivid picture of a world where scientific progress holds both promise and peril.

Genres: Science fiction, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Scientific progress, Ethics, Future possibilities
