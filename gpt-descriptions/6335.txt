Synopsis: In "The Antiquity of Man," Sir Charles Lyell takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating evidence that challenges traditional beliefs about the age of humanity. From ancient fossils to geological formations, Lyell presents a compelling case for the existence of prehistoric civilizations and the profound impact they had on shaping our world. As he delves into the mysteries of the past, the reader is left questioning their own understanding of human history and pondering the implications of these groundbreaking discoveries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Archaeology, Geology, Human Origins
