Synopsis: In "The New Sunday Liquor Law Vindicated," author J. Ewing Ritchie takes readers on a thrilling journey through the controversial world of the Sunday liquor law. Set in a small town, the story follows a group of individuals who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the town becomes a battleground for justice and personal freedom. With each twist and turn, the truth behind the Sunday liquor law is revealed, leaving readers questioning their own beliefs and the consequences of societal norms.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Legal Thriller

Themes: Morality, Freedom, Justice, Social Norms
