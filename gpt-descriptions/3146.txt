Synopsis: 
In 'Two on a Tower' by Thomas Hardy, the lives of two individuals from different social backgrounds intertwine in a captivating tale of forbidden love and astronomical discovery. Lady Constantine, a young and beautiful aristocrat, finds herself drawn to the enigmatic Swithin St. Cleeve, a passionate astronomer. As their relationship develops against the backdrop of societal expectations and personal ambitions, they face numerous obstacles that test the limits of their love and challenge their individual desires. Set in the picturesque landscape of rural England, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the pursuit of personal happiness amidst societal constraints.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Social class, Individual desires, Society's expectations
