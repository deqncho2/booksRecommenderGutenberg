Synopsis: In 'Valperga Volume 2: or, The life and adventures of Castruccio, prince of Lucca', Mary Shelley takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of Castruccio, a charismatic and ambitious prince. Set against the backdrop of medieval Italy, this gripping tale delves into the complexities of power, love, and loyalty as Castruccio navigates treacherous political landscapes and faces personal dilemmas that threaten to unravel his carefully constructed world.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Power, Love, Loyalty, Political intrigue
