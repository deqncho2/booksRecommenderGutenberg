Synopsis: In 'Coming to Grips with White Knuckles' by Paul Cameron Brown, a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious artifact. As she delves deeper into the truth behind the artifact, she must confront her own past and make difficult choices that could change her life forever. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Redemption
