Synopsis: In 'The Man from Jericho' by Edwin Carlile Litsey, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Jericho, capturing the attention and curiosity of its residents. As the townspeople try to uncover the secrets behind his arrival, they soon realize that he holds the key to their deepest desires and darkest fears. With his enigmatic presence, the stranger sets off a chain of events that will challenge the town's beliefs and force them to confront their own demons. As the truth unfolds, the residents of Jericho find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and redemption that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
