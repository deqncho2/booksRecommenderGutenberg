Synopsis: In 'The Solitary Farm' by Fergus Hume, a young woman named Emily finds herself inheriting a remote farm in the countryside. Eager for a fresh start, she moves to the farm, only to discover that it holds dark secrets and a mysterious past. As Emily delves deeper into the history of the farm, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences. With each revelation, Emily's life is put at risk, and she must uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Supernatural forces, Redemption
