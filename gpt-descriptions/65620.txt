Synopsis: 
In 'A Cruel Enigma' by Paul Bourget, a renowned French novelist, a young and talented artist named André Mariolle finds himself captivated by the enigmatic and alluring Madame de Bonnivet. As he becomes entangled in her web of secrets and desires, André's obsession with Madame de Bonnivet takes him on a thrilling journey through the complexities of love, passion, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of Parisian society, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of pursuing forbidden desires.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Betrayal, Secrets and Deception
