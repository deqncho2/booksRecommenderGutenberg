Synopsis: In "He Fell in Love with His Wife" by Edward Payson Roe, a man finds himself in a peculiar situation when he wakes up from a coma to discover that he has no memory of the past five years. As he tries to piece together his life, he realizes that he is married to a woman he doesn't remember and must navigate the challenges of rebuilding their relationship. With unexpected twists and turns, this heartwarming tale explores the power of love, forgiveness, and second chances.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Amnesia, Marriage, Redemption
