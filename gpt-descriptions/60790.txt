Synopsis: In 'The Spanish Royal Tapestries' by Albert Frederick Calvert, a mysterious tapestry collection holds the key to a hidden secret that spans centuries. When a young art historian stumbles upon these exquisite works of art, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. As she delves deeper into the history behind the tapestries, she uncovers a shocking truth that could change the course of history forever. With each thread she unravels, the stakes become higher, and she must race against time to protect the truth and her own life.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Art and its significance, Secrets and hidden histories, Love and sacrifice
