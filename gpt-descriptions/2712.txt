Synopsis: 
In 'A Drift from Redwood Camp' by Bret Harte, a group of travelers find themselves stranded in the wilderness after a sudden storm. As they struggle to survive and find their way back to civilization, they encounter unexpected dangers and form unlikely alliances. With each passing day, their true characters are revealed, and they must confront their own fears and desires. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that nature and their own flaws present? This gripping tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Fiction

Themes: Survival, Human nature, Redemption
