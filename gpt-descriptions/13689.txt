Synopsis: 

In "Sea-Wolves of the Mediterranean: The grand period of the Moslem corsairs," E. Hamilton Currey takes readers on a thrilling journey through the captivating era of the Moslem corsairs in the Mediterranean. This meticulously researched historical account unveils the hidden world of these daring pirates who ruled the seas during a time of great political and cultural upheaval. From their audacious raids on unsuspecting ships to their audacious escapes from powerful navies, the corsairs' exploits will leave readers on the edge of their seats. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Piracy, Power struggles, Cultural clashes
