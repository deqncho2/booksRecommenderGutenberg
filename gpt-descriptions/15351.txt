Synopsis:
In "H. G. Wells" by J. D. Beresford, delve into the fascinating life of one of the most influential science fiction writers of all time. This captivating biography takes readers on a journey through the extraordinary mind of H. G. Wells, exploring his groundbreaking ideas, his tumultuous personal life, and the lasting impact he had on literature and society. From his early struggles to his rise to fame, this book offers a compelling portrait of a visionary writer who dared to imagine the future.

Genres:
Biography, Science Fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes:
Imagination, Social Commentary, Time and Space Exploration
