Synopsis: In the Dead of Night: A Novel. Volume 1 (of 3) by Speight, T. W. (Thomas Wilkinson) takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark and mysterious streets of Victorian London. When a young woman named Alice finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger, she must navigate the treacherous underworld of the city to uncover the truth. As the night grows darker, Alice's courage is tested, and she discovers that the line between good and evil is not always clear. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie in the dead of night.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, the darkness within, the power of courage and resilience
