Synopsis: In 'A Practical Treatise on Gas-light', Friedrich Christian Accum presents a fascinating exploration of the revolutionary technology of gas lighting. With a focus on the apparatus and machinery used for illuminating streets, houses, and manufactories with carburetted hydrogen or coal-gas, Accum delves into the utility, safety, and general nature of this new branch of civil economy. Through his insightful remarks, the author sheds light on the potential of gas lighting to transform the way we illuminate our surroundings. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Industrialization, Energy
