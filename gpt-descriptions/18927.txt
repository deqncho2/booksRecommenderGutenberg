Synopsis: In 'The Uttermost Farthing' by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. When a stranger arrives in her small village, he brings with him a dark past and a hidden agenda that threatens to unravel the lives of those around him. As the truth slowly unravels, the protagonist must navigate through a labyrinth of lies and betrayal, questioning her own beliefs and loyalties. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
