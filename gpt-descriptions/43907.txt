Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Gordon at Bramble Farm; Or, The Mystery of a Nobody', Betty Gordon finds herself in a quaint countryside setting, surrounded by mysterious happenings and a cast of intriguing characters. As she delves deeper into the secrets of Bramble Farm, Betty uncovers a hidden world of unexpected twists and turns. With her determination and quick thinking, she sets out to solve the enigma that lies at the heart of this captivating mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Independence, Friendship
