Synopsis: In 'Precipitations' by Scott Evelyn, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and devastating weather events that seem to be connected to the arrival of a peculiar newcomer. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these inexplicable occurrences, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and the unyielding power of nature. With each passing storm, the stakes rise higher, and the line between reality and the supernatural becomes increasingly blurred. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma that lies within the storm.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Nature's Power
