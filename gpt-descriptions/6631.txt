Synopsis: 
"The Sculpture and Mural Decorations of the Exposition" takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the stunning artistry of the Panama-Pacific International Exposition. Through vivid descriptions and breathtaking illustrations, Stella G. S. Perry unveils the intricate sculptures and mesmerizing murals that adorned this grand event. From the delicate details of the sculptures to the vibrant colors of the murals, this book offers a glimpse into the artistic marvels that captivated visitors from around the world. 

Genres: Art, History, Travel

Themes: Beauty of art, Cultural exchange, Celebration of human creativity
