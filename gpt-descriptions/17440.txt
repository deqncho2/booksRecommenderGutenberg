Synopsis: In "Amadis of Gaul, an opera: As it is perform'd at the King's Theatre in the Hay-market," we are transported to a world of chivalry, romance, and enchantment. The story revolves around the valiant knight Amadis, who embarks on a perilous journey to win the heart of his beloved Oriana. Along the way, he faces treacherous foes, encounters magical beings, and must navigate the complexities of love and honor. This captivating opera takes us on a thrilling adventure filled with passion, bravery, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Opera, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Love, Honor, Magic
