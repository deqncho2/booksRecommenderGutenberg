Synopsis: 
When twelve-year-old Jonathan arrives at his aunt's house on Hemlock Mountain, he is warned about the dangers that lurk in the woods. Determined to prove his bravery, Jonathan sets off on an adventure to uncover the truth behind the mysterious law that governs the mountain. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Hemlock Mountain, Jonathan must rely on his wit and courage to navigate the treacherous terrain and face the unknown. Will he uncover the truth or become another victim of the mountain's law?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Middle Grade

Themes: Bravery, Courage, Exploration, Overcoming Fear
