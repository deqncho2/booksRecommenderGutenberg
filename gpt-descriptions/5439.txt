Synopsis: In 'Uarda: a Romance of Ancient Egypt - Volume 01', Georg Ebers takes readers on a captivating journey to ancient Egypt, where the lives of two young individuals, Uarda and Seti, become intertwined amidst the backdrop of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the search for identity. As Uarda, a young Egyptian girl, discovers her true heritage, she must navigate the complexities of her society and face the challenges that arise from her forbidden love for Seti, an Egyptian prince. With vivid descriptions of ancient Egyptian life and a compelling narrative, Ebers weaves a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Forbidden Love, Political Intrigue
