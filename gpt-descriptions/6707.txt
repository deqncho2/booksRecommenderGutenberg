Synopsis: In 'St. Patrick's Day; Or, The Scheming Lieutenant: A Farce in One Act', the mischievous Lieutenant O'Connor finds himself entangled in a web of hilarious schemes and mistaken identities during the lively celebrations of St. Patrick's Day. As the chaos unfolds, secrets are revealed, love is tested, and the characters must navigate a series of comical misunderstandings to find their happy endings.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Theatre

Themes: Deception, Love, Identity, Celebration
