Synopsis: In 'The Rival Submarines' by Percy F. Westerman, two brilliant young engineers find themselves caught in a dangerous race to develop the most advanced submarines. As they strive to outdo each other, their rivalry escalates into a thrilling battle of wits and technology, with high stakes and unexpected twists. With the fate of naval warfare hanging in the balance, the tension builds as the submarines dive into uncharted depths, testing the limits of human ingenuity and courage.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Rivalry, Innovation, Technological Advancement, Risk-taking
