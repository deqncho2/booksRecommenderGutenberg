Synopsis: In 'An Interloper' by Frances Mary Peard, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, disrupting the peaceful lives of its inhabitants. As the villagers try to uncover the stranger's true identity and intentions, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the community is forced to confront their own prejudices and fears. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of judgment and suspicion.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Prejudice, Secrets, Community Dynamics
