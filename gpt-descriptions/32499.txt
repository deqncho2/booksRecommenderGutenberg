Synopsis: In 'To a Skull on My Bookshelf' by Elizabeth Virginia Raplee, a young woman discovers a mysterious skull on her bookshelf, setting off a chain of events that lead her on a thrilling journey through time and space. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the skull, she uncovers hidden truths about her own identity and the power of knowledge. With each turn of the page, the protagonist must confront her fears and make choices that will shape her destiny. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma of the skull and its significance.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Knowledge, Fate
