Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Surrey as Eric Parker takes you on an enchanting exploration of its hidden treasures. From quaint villages nestled amidst rolling hills to majestic castles steeped in history, 'Highways and Byways in Surrey' unveils the secrets and wonders of this idyllic English county. As Parker delves into the rich tapestry of Surrey's past, he uncovers fascinating anecdotes and encounters intriguing characters along the way. With vivid descriptions and charming storytelling, this book invites you to immerse yourself in the beauty and allure of Surrey's countryside, leaving you yearning to discover its hidden gems for yourself.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Exploration, Local history, Natural beauty
