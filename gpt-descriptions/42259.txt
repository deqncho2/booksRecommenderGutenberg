Synopsis: In "The People of the Black Circle," Conan the Cimmerian finds himself entangled in a treacherous web of sorcery, political intrigue, and forbidden love. When a powerful sorceress kidnaps the sister of a tribal chieftain, Conan must navigate a dangerous landscape filled with deadly assassins, ancient curses, and a mysterious cult known as the Black Circle. As he battles against formidable enemies and uncovers dark secrets, Conan's strength, cunning, and indomitable will are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes: Power and its corrupting influence, Loyalty and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
