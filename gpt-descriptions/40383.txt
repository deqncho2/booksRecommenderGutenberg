Synopsis: In 'Musical Myths and Facts, Volume 1 (of 2)', Carl Engel takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of music. From uncovering the truth behind famous musical legends to exploring the origins of beloved compositions, this book is a treasure trove of intriguing anecdotes and surprising revelations. Engel's meticulous research and engaging storytelling make this volume a must-read for music enthusiasts and history buffs alike. Prepare to have your musical knowledge challenged and expanded as you delve into the captivating myths and facts that surround the world of music.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Music history, Mythology, Cultural significance
