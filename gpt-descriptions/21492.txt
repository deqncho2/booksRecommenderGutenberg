Synopsis: In 'A True Hero: A Story of the Days of William Penn' by William Henry Giles Kingston, follow the captivating journey of a young man named Ralph, who finds himself caught in the midst of the tumultuous times of William Penn's Pennsylvania. As Ralph navigates through the challenges of colonial life, he encounters unexpected friendships, dangerous adversaries, and a quest for justice that will test his courage and determination. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will transport readers to a time of exploration, conflict, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Justice, Freedom
