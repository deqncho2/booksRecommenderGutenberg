Synopsis: In 'Making the House a Home' by Edgar A. Guest, a young couple embarks on a journey to transform their new house into a warm and inviting home. As they face various challenges and unexpected surprises, they discover the true meaning of family, love, and the power of creating a space that reflects their unique personalities. With heartwarming anecdotes and insightful reflections, Guest takes readers on a delightful exploration of the joys and struggles of turning a house into a home.

Genres: Fiction, Inspirational, Self-help

Themes: Family, Love, Home, Personal growth
