Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the year 1860 and embark on a fascinating journey through the vast expanse of Canada's post offices. In this meticulously compiled list, the Canada Post Office Department unveils the names of the postmasters across the country, offering a unique glimpse into the postal system of the era. From bustling cities to remote towns, this comprehensive record paints a vivid picture of the individuals entrusted with the important task of delivering mail and connecting communities. Discover the stories behind these post offices and the dedicated postmasters who played a vital role in shaping Canada's communication network.

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Canadian history, Postal system, Community development
