Synopsis:
In the second volume of "Aurora Floyd," the captivating story of Aurora Floyd continues to unfold with even more twists and turns. As secrets from the past are revealed and new mysteries arise, Aurora finds herself caught in a web of deceit and danger. With her reputation at stake and her heart torn between two men, she must navigate through a world of betrayal and intrigue to uncover the truth and secure her future. Will Aurora be able to overcome the obstacles in her path and find happiness, or will she be forever haunted by the shadows of her past?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Victorian Literature

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Love and Desire
