Synopsis: In 'A Chinese Wonder Book' by Norman Hinsdale Pitman, embark on a captivating journey through ancient China, where mythical creatures, magical beings, and extraordinary adventures await. From the mischievous Monkey King to the courageous Dragon Princess, this collection of enchanting tales will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, bravery, and the power of imagination. Discover the secrets of the celestial realm, witness epic battles between good and evil, and unravel the mysteries of ancient Chinese folklore in this captivating and timeless collection.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Heroism, Transformation, Morality
