Synopsis: In "Boy Wanted: A Book of Cheerful Counsel" by Waterman, Nixon, a young boy named Tom embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Faced with various challenges and obstacles, Tom learns valuable life lessons that shape his character and help him navigate the complexities of adolescence. Through heartwarming anecdotes and relatable experiences, this book offers cheerful counsel to young readers, inspiring them to embrace their individuality, overcome adversity, and find joy in the journey of growing up.

Genres: Self-help, Coming-of-age, Inspirational

Themes: Self-discovery, Personal growth, Resilience, Individuality
