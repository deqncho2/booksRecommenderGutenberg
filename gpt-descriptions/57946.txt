Synopsis: In the Dead of Night: A Novel. Volume 2 (of 3) takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark and mysterious world of Victorian London. As the city grapples with a series of gruesome murders, Detective Inspector Thornton finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. With each step closer to the truth, he uncovers shocking revelations that challenge his beliefs and push him to the edge of his sanity. As the clock ticks and the body count rises, Thornton must navigate the treacherous streets and shadowy corners of the city to catch a killer who seems to strike in the dead of night.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Crime and Investigation, Deception and Betrayal, Psychological Tension
