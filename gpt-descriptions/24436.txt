Synopsis: In 'Anything You Can Do ...' by Garrett Randall, a brilliant scientist and a cunning detective find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of wits and deception. As they race against time to solve a series of perplexing crimes, they must confront their own personal demons and navigate a treacherous web of secrets and lies. With each twist and turn, the stakes escalate, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Psychological warfare
