Synopsis: 
In 'The Daffodil Mystery' by Wallace Edgar, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy man is found dead in his locked study, surrounded by daffodils. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the mystery becomes more intricate, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
