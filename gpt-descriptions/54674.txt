Synopsis: In the small town of White Motley, a mysterious newcomer named Max Pemberton arrives, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm. As the townspeople become entangled in Max's web, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of White Motley is revealed. With its atmospheric setting and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel explores the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic community, leaving readers questioning the boundaries of trust and the consequences of hidden desires.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Identity
