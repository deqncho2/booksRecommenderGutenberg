Synopsis: 
In 'The Jade Story Book; Stories from the Orient', Coussens, Penrhyn Wingfield takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of the Orient. This collection of tales weaves together a tapestry of ancient myths, legends, and fables, transporting readers to far-off lands filled with magic, adventure, and wisdom. From the mystical realms of China to the vibrant streets of Japan, each story unfolds with vivid imagery and rich cultural details, immersing readers in a world of dragons, warriors, and timeless wisdom. Whether it's a tale of love and sacrifice or a lesson in the power of compassion, each story in this collection is sure to captivate and inspire readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Fiction, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: 
Cultural diversity, Wisdom, Morality, Adventure
