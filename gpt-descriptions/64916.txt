Synopsis: 
'Riverside London: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the enchanting streets and iconic landmarks of London. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated sketches, the book unveils the hidden gems and lesser-known corners of the city, capturing the essence of its vibrant atmosphere and rich history. From the bustling markets to the tranquil riverside, each sketch tells a unique story, inviting readers to explore and discover the magic that lies within the heart of London.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City exploration, Cultural heritage, Urban beauty
