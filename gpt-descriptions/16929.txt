Synopsis: 
'Treat 'em Rough: Letters from Jack the Kaiser Killer' by Lardner, Ring is a gripping collection of letters that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mind of a mysterious and deadly figure known as Jack the Kaiser Killer. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue, suspense, and unexpected twists, leaving them eager to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic character.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, War, Morality
