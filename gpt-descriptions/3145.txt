Synopsis: In "The Author of Beltraffio," a young American artist named Mark Ambient moves to Europe with his wife and becomes friends with a renowned author, the titular Beltraffio. However, as Mark delves deeper into Beltraffio's world, he begins to uncover dark secrets and hidden desires that challenge his own artistic ambitions and personal relationships. This gripping tale explores the complexities of art, identity, and the blurred lines between reality and fiction.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological thriller, Literary fiction

Themes: Artistic ambition, Identity, Deception, Morality
