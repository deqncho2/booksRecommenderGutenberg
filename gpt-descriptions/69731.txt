Synopsis: In "The Cosmic Jackpot" by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the universe, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between scientific advancement and ethical boundaries blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to unlock the secrets of the cosmos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific exploration, Ethical dilemmas, Consequences of discovery
