Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Sanctuary' by Edith Wharton, a young woman named Kate Orme finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a mysterious secret that threatens to unravel her world. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Kate must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, ultimately facing a choice that could have far-reaching consequences. With its compelling narrative and intricate web of secrets, 'Sanctuary' is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Secrets and Betrayal, Societal Expectations
