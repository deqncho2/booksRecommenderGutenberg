Synopsis: In 'The BYU Solar Cooker/Cooler' by Steven E. Jones, a brilliant inventor creates a revolutionary device that harnesses the power of the sun to cook and cool food. As he navigates the challenges of perfecting his invention, he discovers unexpected applications and faces opposition from powerful forces. This gripping tale explores the potential of renewable energy and the lengths one man will go to make a difference.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Renewable Energy, Innovation, Environmentalism
