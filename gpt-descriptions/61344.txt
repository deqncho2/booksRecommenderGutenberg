Synopsis: In 'The Happy Isles' by Basil King, a young artist named John is drawn to a mysterious island off the coast of Maine, where he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires. As he delves deeper into the island's history and its enigmatic inhabitants, John's perception of reality begins to blur, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and forbidden love.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Forbidden Love, Secrets and Betrayal
