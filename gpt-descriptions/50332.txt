Synopsis: In "The Diamond Lens" by O'Brien, Fitz James, a brilliant young scientist becomes obsessed with a microscopic world that exists within a single drop of water. As he delves deeper into this hidden realm, he discovers a breathtaking secret that could change the course of science forever. However, his pursuit of knowledge comes at a great cost, leading him down a dark and dangerous path that challenges his sanity and morality.

Genres: Science fiction, horror, psychological thriller

Themes: Obsession, the pursuit of knowledge, the blurred line between genius and madness
