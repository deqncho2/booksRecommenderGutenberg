Synopsis: 
In 'The Time Mirror' by South, Clark, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Hartley invents a groundbreaking device that allows her to peer into the past. However, as she delves deeper into the mysteries of time, she discovers that her invention has unintended consequences, leading her on a thrilling journey through different eras and dimensions. With each leap, Amelia must confront her own past and make difficult choices that could alter the course of history forever. As she races against time itself, she uncovers a shocking truth that will challenge everything she thought she knew about the nature of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Mystery

Themes: The nature of time, the consequences of one's actions, the power of choice
