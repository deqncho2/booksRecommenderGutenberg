Synopsis: In 'The Saint of the Dragon's Dale: A Fantastical Tale' by Davis, William Stearns, a young boy named Tommy embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden world filled with mythical creatures and ancient magic. As he navigates through the enchanting Dragon's Dale, Tommy must confront his fears, unravel the secrets of the land, and ultimately find the courage to become the hero he never knew he could be.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Self-discovery, Mythical creatures, Magic
