Synopsis: In 'Ovington's Bank' by Stanley John Weyman, a young man named Philip Ovington finds himself thrust into the world of banking when he inherits his father's prestigious bank. As he navigates the treacherous waters of finance and power, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With his reputation and the future of the bank at stake, Philip must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those around him.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Loyalty, Deception, Love, Power
