Synopsis: In 'My Lady of the Chimney Corner' by Alexander Irvine, a young boy named Larry grows up in poverty-stricken Ireland, surrounded by hardship and despair. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious woman known as "My Lady" who lives in the chimney corner. Through her wisdom, love, and unwavering faith, My Lady becomes a guiding light in Larry's life, teaching him valuable lessons about hope, resilience, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Inspirational

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Faith, Love, Mentorship
