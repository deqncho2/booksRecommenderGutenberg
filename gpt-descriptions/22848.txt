Synopsis: In 'Sandhya: Songs of Twilight' by Dhan Gopal Mukerji, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Sandhya, a young Indian girl with a deep connection to nature and a remarkable gift for music. As Sandhya navigates the challenges of her traditional upbringing and the allure of modernity, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and cultural exploration. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century India, this captivating tale weaves together themes of tradition, identity, and the power of music, taking readers on a lyrical and poignant exploration of one girl's pursuit of her dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Cultural Fiction
Themes: Tradition, Identity, Music, Self-Discovery
