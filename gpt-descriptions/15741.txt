Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel's House Party," the lovable and spirited Little Colonel, Lloyd Sherman, finds herself in the midst of a thrilling adventure when she attends a grand house party at her cousin's mansion. As the young guests gather, unexpected events and mysterious occurrences unfold, leading Lloyd to uncover secrets and embark on a quest to solve a puzzling mystery. With her quick wit and determination, Lloyd navigates through the twists and turns of the house party, forming new friendships and discovering the true meaning of loyalty and bravery along the way.

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Bravery, Coming of Age
