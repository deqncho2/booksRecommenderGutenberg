Synopsis: In 'My Friend Bobby' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young boy named Tim discovers a mysterious creature named Bobby hiding in his backyard. As Tim befriends Bobby, he soon realizes that his new friend possesses extraordinary abilities that could change the world. However, as they embark on a thrilling adventure together, they also face dangerous forces that seek to exploit Bobby's powers for their own gain. Tim must protect Bobby and uncover the truth behind his origins before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Power, Morality
