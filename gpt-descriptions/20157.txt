Synopsis: In 'The Call of the Blood' by Hichens, Robert, a young Englishman named Maurice Mainwaring embarks on a journey to Egypt, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. Drawn into the enigmatic world of a captivating Egyptian woman, Maurice finds himself torn between his duty and his desires. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the ancient land, he uncovers shocking revelations that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own identity. With its gripping plot and atmospheric setting, 'The Call of the Blood' takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Cultural Exploration, Forbidden Love
