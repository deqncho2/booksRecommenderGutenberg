Synopsis: In 'Desert Gold' by Zane Grey, a young woman named Ellen Jorth embarks on a perilous journey through the treacherous Arizona desert to save her father's ranch from a ruthless gang of outlaws. Along the way, she encounters a mysterious gunslinger named Glenn Kilbourne, who becomes her unlikely ally in the fight against the lawless band. As they navigate the harsh desert landscape, Ellen and Glenn must confront their own personal demons and find the strength to overcome the dangers that lie ahead.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Redemption, Survival
