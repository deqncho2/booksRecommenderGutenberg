Synopsis: 

In 'Fiat Money Inflation in France: How it Came, What it Brought, and How it Ended', Andrew Dickson White takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of France's experiment with fiat money during the French Revolution. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, White unveils the shocking consequences of this economic experiment, revealing how it transformed the lives of ordinary citizens and shaped the course of history. From the initial optimism to the devastating aftermath, this gripping account explores the rise and fall of a financial system that had far-reaching implications for France and the world.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Economics, Political Science

Themes: Economic instability, Financial crises, Government intervention, Societal impact
