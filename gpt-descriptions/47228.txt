Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Indian mythology and legend, as Donald A. Mackenzie weaves together a collection of enchanting tales that will transport readers to a world of gods, goddesses, heroes, and mythical creatures. From the epic battles of the Mahabharata to the divine love stories of Radha and Krishna, this book delves into the depths of Indian folklore, exploring the origins of the universe, the creation of mankind, and the timeless struggles between good and evil. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'Indian Myth and Legend' offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the ancient myths that have shaped the cultural fabric of India for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Love and Devotion, Good vs. Evil, Divine Intervention
