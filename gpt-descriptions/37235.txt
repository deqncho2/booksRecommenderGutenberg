Synopsis: In 'The Black Poodle, and Other Tales' by Anstey, F., readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and mysterious stories. From a peculiar black poodle with an uncanny ability to predict the future, to a man who finds himself in a perplexing predicament after a chance encounter, each tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With a blend of humor, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, this collection promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what surprises lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Humor

Themes: Fate and destiny, Unexpected consequences
