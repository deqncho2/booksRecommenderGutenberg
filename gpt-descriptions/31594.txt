Synopsis: In 'The Song of Lancaster, Kentucky: To the statesmen, soldiers, and citizens of Garrard County,' Eugenia Dunlap Potts takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart of Kentucky during the Civil War. Set in the small town of Lancaster, the story follows the lives of its resilient inhabitants as they navigate the challenges and sacrifices brought about by the war. Through vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, Potts weaves a tale of love, loss, and the indomitable spirit of a community united in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Community, Sacrifice
