Synopsis: 
In 'Laboratory Manual of Glass-Blowing', Francis C. Frary takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate art of glass-blowing. Through detailed instructions and vivid illustrations, Frary unveils the secrets of this ancient craft, revealing the delicate balance between technique and creativity. From the basics of glass manipulation to the creation of intricate glassware, this manual offers a comprehensive guide for both beginners and experienced glass-blowers alike. As readers delve into the world of glass-blowing, they will discover the beauty and precision required to transform molten glass into stunning works of art.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Craft and Hobbies, Art

Themes: 
Creativity, Skill Development, Artistic Expression
