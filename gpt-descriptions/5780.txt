Synopsis: The Rover Boys at School; Or, The Cadets of Putnam Hall follows the thrilling adventures of three brothers, Tom, Sam, and Dick Rover, as they navigate their way through the challenges and mysteries of their new boarding school, Putnam Hall. From uncovering a secret plot to facing off against a notorious bully, the Rover boys must rely on their wit, courage, and brotherly bond to overcome the obstacles that come their way. With their loyal friends by their side, the Rover boys embark on a series of daring escapades that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Brotherhood, Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
