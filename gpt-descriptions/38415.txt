Synopsis: 

In 'Gas-Engines and Producer-Gas Plants', Mathot, Rodolphe Edgard presents a comprehensive and practical treatise on the principles of gas-engines and producer design. This informative guide covers a wide range of topics, including the selection and installation of an engine, conditions for perfect operation, the potential of producer-gas engines, and the proper care of gas-engines and producer-gas plants. Additionally, it delves into the fascinating realm of volatile hydrocarbon and oil engines. 

Genres: Technical, Engineering, Reference

Themes: Gas-engine principles, Producer-gas plants, Engine selection and installation, Perfect operation conditions, Care of gas-engines and producer-gas plants, Volatile hydrocarbon and oil engines.
