Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Trevor' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, the reader is introduced to the charming and spirited Betty Trevor. As she navigates the challenges of her everyday life, Betty finds herself caught between duty and desire, torn between societal expectations and her own dreams. With a cast of intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Identity, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
