Synopsis: 
'Inside the Russian Revolution' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that unfolded during one of the most significant periods in Russian history. Through the eyes of the author, Rheta Childe Dorr, readers are immersed in the chaos, passion, and intrigue that characterized the revolution. From the streets of Petrograd to the halls of power, Dorr provides a firsthand account of the revolution's key players, their motivations, and the impact of their actions on the Russian people. As the revolution unfolds, readers are left on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of a nation hanging in the balance.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political thriller, Biography

Themes: Revolution, Power dynamics, Social change, Ideology, Personal sacrifice
