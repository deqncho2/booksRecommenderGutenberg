Synopsis: In 'Sunfire!' by Hamilton, Edmond, a daring astronaut embarks on a perilous mission to explore the mysteries of the sun, only to find himself caught in a web of unexpected dangers and mind-bending discoveries. As he delves deeper into the heart of our solar system, he must confront the unimaginable and make a choice that could alter the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Sacrifice, Humanity's Place in the Universe
