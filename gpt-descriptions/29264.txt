Synopsis: 
In 'Diary from November 12, 1862, to October 18, 1863', Adam Gurowski takes us on an intimate journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Through his personal diary entries, we witness the unfolding events, political intrigues, and the profound impact of war on both the nation and the individual. Gurowski's sharp observations and unfiltered opinions provide a unique perspective on the war, its key players, and the struggles faced by a divided nation.

Genres: Historical, Memoir, Political

Themes: American Civil War, Politics, National Identity, Individual Experience
