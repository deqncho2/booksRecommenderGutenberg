Synopsis: 

"The Border and the Buffalo: An Untold Story of the Southwest Plains: The Bloody Border of Missouri and Kansas. The Story of the Slaughter of the Buffalo. Westward among the Big Game and Wild Tribes. A Story of Mountain and Plain" takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed Southwest Plains. Set against the backdrop of the violent border between Missouri and Kansas, the story delves into the little-known history of the buffalo slaughter and the clash between settlers and Native American tribes. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported to a world of adventure, danger, and the vastness of the American frontier.

Genres: Historical fiction, Western, Adventure

Themes: Frontier life, Conflict and violence, Native American culture, Manifest Destiny
