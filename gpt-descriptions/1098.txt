Synopsis: In the small Midwestern town of Monarch, a family's fortunes rise and fall amidst the backdrop of societal changes and personal struggles. As the town undergoes rapid industrialization and the old ways of life are challenged, the Sheridan family finds themselves caught in a web of ambition, love, and betrayal. With the arrival of a charismatic stranger, the town's delicate balance is further disrupted, leading to a series of events that will forever alter the lives of its inhabitants.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social change, Ambition, Love and relationships, Family dynamics
