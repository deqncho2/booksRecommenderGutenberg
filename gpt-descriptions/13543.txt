Synopsis: 

In "People You Know" by Ade George, a seemingly ordinary group of friends find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deception when a mysterious newcomer disrupts their lives. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the friends must confront their own hidden desires and face the consequences of their actions. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Morality, Manipulation
