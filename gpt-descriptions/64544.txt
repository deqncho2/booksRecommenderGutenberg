Synopsis: In 'A Wayfarer's Faith: Aspects of the common basis of religious life', T. Edmund Harvey takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes of religious belief and practice. Drawing from his extensive travels and encounters with various faith traditions, Harvey explores the fundamental threads that unite humanity's spiritual quests. From ancient rituals to modern expressions of devotion, this thought-provoking exploration delves into the shared experiences and universal truths that underpin religious life, offering a fresh perspective on the tapestry of human faith.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Philosophy

Themes: Religious diversity, Shared human experiences, Universal truths
