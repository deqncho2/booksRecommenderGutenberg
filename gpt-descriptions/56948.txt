Synopsis: In 'An Autumn Sowing' by E. F. Benson, a young woman named Mary grows up in a small English village, surrounded by the beauty of nature and the secrets of her family's past. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, Mary discovers that her idyllic world is not as innocent as it seems. With a mysterious stranger arriving in town and a series of unexpected events, Mary's life takes a thrilling turn, leading her to question everything she thought she knew about herself and those around her.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
