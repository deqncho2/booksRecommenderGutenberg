Synopsis: 
In 'The Cooking Manual of Practical Directions for Economical Every-Day Cookery', Juliet Corson takes readers on a culinary journey filled with practical tips and delicious recipes. From budget-friendly ingredients to time-saving techniques, this comprehensive manual offers a wealth of knowledge for those seeking to master the art of economical cooking. With a focus on simplicity and efficiency, Corson's guide is sure to inspire both novice and experienced cooks alike, providing them with the tools they need to create flavorful and satisfying meals without breaking the bank.

Genres: Cookbook, Non-fiction

Themes: Economical cooking, Culinary techniques, Budget-friendly ingredients
