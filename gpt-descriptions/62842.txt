Synopsis: 
In "In Fashions: for Him, for Her, in Knits, in Crochets" by American Thread Company, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the world of fashion, exploring the latest trends and designs for both men and women. From stylish knits to intricate crochets, this book showcases a wide range of innovative and eye-catching garments that are sure to inspire any fashion enthusiast. With stunning visuals and detailed instructions, readers will be able to create their own unique pieces and stay ahead of the fashion curve. Get ready to be immersed in a world of creativity and style!

Genres: Fashion, Crafts, Design

Themes: Fashion trends, DIY fashion, Creativity
