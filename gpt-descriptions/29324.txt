Synopsis: In 'A Poetical Review of the Literary and Moral Character of the late Samuel Johnson', Courtenay presents a captivating exploration of the renowned writer and moralist, Samuel Johnson. Through poetic verses, Courtenay delves into Johnson's life, his literary achievements, and his moral character, shedding light on the complexities and brilliance of this influential figure. The poem offers a unique perspective on Johnson's impact on the literary world and his contributions to society, leaving readers intrigued to discover more about this remarkable man.

Genres: Poetry, Biography

Themes: Literary criticism, Morality, Legacy
