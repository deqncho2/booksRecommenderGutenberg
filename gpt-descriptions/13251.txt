Synopsis: In 'The Chums of Scranton High on the Cinder Path: Or, The Mystery of the Haunted Quarry' by Ferguson, Donald, a group of adventurous friends from Scranton High School stumble upon a puzzling secret while exploring an abandoned quarry. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they find themselves facing unexpected dangers and uncovering a haunting past that threatens to unravel their friendship. With their courage and determination, the chums must navigate the treacherous cinder path and solve the enigma before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
