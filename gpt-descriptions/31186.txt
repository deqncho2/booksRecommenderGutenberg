Synopsis: 
'What Shall We Do Now?: Five Hundred Games and Pastimes' by Dorothy Canfield Fisher is a delightful collection of games and activities that will captivate readers of all ages. From classic board games to outdoor adventures, this book offers a wide range of options to keep boredom at bay. Whether you're looking for a quiet evening indoors or an exciting day in the great outdoors, Fisher's book has something for everyone. With clear instructions and helpful tips, this comprehensive guide is sure to inspire hours of fun and laughter. Get ready to discover new games, challenge your friends and family, and create lasting memories with 'What Shall We Do Now?: Five Hundred Games and Pastimes'.

Genres: Non-fiction, Entertainment, Hobbies

Themes: Recreation, Family bonding, Creativity
