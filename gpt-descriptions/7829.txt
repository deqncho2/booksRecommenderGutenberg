Synopsis: In 'American Prisoners of the Revolution', author Danske Dandridge takes readers on a captivating journey through the untold stories of American prisoners during the Revolutionary War. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Dandridge unveils the harrowing experiences, resilience, and sacrifices of these forgotten heroes. From the infamous British prison ships to the brutal conditions endured by captives, this book sheds light on the often overlooked aspect of the war. With a blend of historical facts and personal narratives, 'American Prisoners of the Revolution' offers a compelling and eye-opening perspective on the human cost of the fight for independence.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War history, Biography

Themes: Resilience, Sacrifice, Forgotten history, Human cost of war
