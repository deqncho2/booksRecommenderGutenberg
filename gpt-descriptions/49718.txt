Synopsis: In 'The Desultory Man: Collection of Ancient and Modern British Novels and Romances. Vol. CXLVII.', James, G. P. R. takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of ancient and modern British novels and romances. From tales of adventure and mystery to stories of love and betrayal, this volume offers a diverse range of narratives that will keep readers hooked from beginning to end. As the pages turn, readers will be transported to different eras and settings, encountering fascinating characters and experiencing the highs and lows of their lives. With each story offering its own unique blend of excitement, suspense, and emotion, 'The Desultory Man' promises to be a literary treasure trove that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and betrayal, Mystery and suspense, Human nature and morality
