Synopsis: 
'Without a Home' by Roe, Edward Payson is a captivating novel that follows the journey of a young woman named Helen who finds herself without a place to call home. After a series of unfortunate events, Helen is forced to navigate the harsh realities of life on the streets, encountering both kindness and cruelty along the way. As she struggles to survive and find a sense of belonging, Helen's resilience and determination are put to the test. Will she be able to overcome the challenges that come her way and ultimately find a place to call home?

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Homelessness, Resilience, Belonging
