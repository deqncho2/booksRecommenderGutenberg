Synopsis: In 'The House of Defence v. 2' by E. F. Benson, a mysterious and opulent mansion becomes the setting for a series of inexplicable events. As the new occupants of the house uncover its dark secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With each room holding its own enigma, the house becomes a labyrinth of suspense, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to unravel the mysteries within.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Supernatural elements, Psychological suspense
