Synopsis: 
'Knots, Bends, Splices: With tables of strengths of ropes, etc. and wire rigging' by Jutsum, Captain is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of knots, bends, and splices. From the basics to the most complex techniques, this book offers a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for sailors, adventurers, and knot enthusiasts alike. With detailed illustrations and tables of strengths, readers will discover the art of securing ropes and wire rigging with precision and skill. As they delve into the pages, they will uncover the secrets behind the strength and reliability of various knots, bends, and splices, unlocking a world of possibilities in the realm of knotting.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Adventure

Themes: Seamanship, Practical skills, Problem-solving
