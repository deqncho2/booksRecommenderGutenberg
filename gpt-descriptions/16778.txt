Synopsis: In 'Pulpit and Press' by Mary Baker Eddy, a renowned religious leader finds herself entangled in a web of controversy and intrigue when her teachings clash with the powerful forces of the media and established religious institutions. As she fights to defend her beliefs and protect her followers, she uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to shake the foundations of society. With her reputation on the line, she must navigate the treacherous waters of public opinion and confront the dark forces that seek to silence her voice.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Religion, Power, Truth
