Synopsis:
In 'Personal Friendships of Jesus', J.R. Miller delves into the intimate relationships Jesus formed during his time on Earth. From his encounters with the outcasts of society to his deep connections with his disciples, this book uncovers the profound impact these friendships had on Jesus' ministry and the lives of those he touched. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Miller invites readers to explore the transformative power of genuine friendship and the lessons we can learn from Jesus' example.

Genres:
Biography, Christian Literature, Inspirational

Themes:
Friendship, Compassion, Redemption
