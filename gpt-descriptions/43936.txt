Synopsis: 
When a tornado sweeps young Dorothy away from her Kansas home, she finds herself in the magical Land of Oz. Desperate to return home, she embarks on a journey to seek the help of the powerful Wizard of Oz. Along the way, she encounters peculiar characters, including a scarecrow, a tin woodman, and a cowardly lion, who join her quest in hopes of fulfilling their own desires. Together, they face numerous challenges and confront the wicked Wicked Witch of the West. Will Dorothy and her newfound friends succeed in their mission and find what they truly seek?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
