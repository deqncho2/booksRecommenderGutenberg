Synopsis: 
In 'My Book of Favourite Fairy Tales' by Vredenburg, Edric, embark on a magical journey through a collection of enchanting and timeless stories. From brave princesses and cunning witches to talking animals and mischievous fairies, this book is filled with captivating tales that will transport readers to fantastical realms. Each story is beautifully illustrated, bringing the characters and settings to life, and will leave readers spellbound with their imaginative and heartwarming narratives. Whether you are rediscovering beloved classics or discovering new tales, 'My Book of Favourite Fairy Tales' is a delightful read that will ignite the imagination and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Fairy tales, Children's literature, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Magic, Morality, Transformation
