Synopsis: In 'The Adventure Girls in the Air' by Blank, Clair, a group of fearless young women embark on a thrilling journey that takes them high above the clouds. When an unexpected opportunity arises to join a daring aviation expedition, the Adventure Girls eagerly seize the chance to prove their skills and push the boundaries of what is considered possible for women in the early 20th century. As they navigate the challenges of flying, they encounter breathtaking landscapes, encounter unexpected dangers, and form unbreakable bonds of friendship. With each twist and turn, the Adventure Girls demonstrate their resilience, courage, and determination to conquer the skies.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Female empowerment, Breaking societal norms, Friendship, Courage
