Synopsis: In 'We Run From the Hunted!' by Marlowe, Stephen, a group of friends find themselves caught in a deadly game of cat and mouse when they stumble upon a dark secret. As they race against time to uncover the truth, they must outsmart their pursuers and confront their own fears. With heart-pounding suspense and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Courage
