Synopsis: In this thought-provoking letter, Irons challenges the proposed surrender of the Prayer-Book and Articles of the Church of England, addressing the controversial views of Professor Stanley on clerical and university "subscription." With eloquence and conviction, Irons delves into the implications of such surrender, raising important questions about the future of the Church and its core beliefs. This letter serves as a powerful call to action, urging readers to critically examine the potential consequences of compromising their faith.

Genres: Religious literature, theological discourse, epistolary writing

Themes: Faith and belief, religious authority, theological controversy, the role of tradition in the Church
