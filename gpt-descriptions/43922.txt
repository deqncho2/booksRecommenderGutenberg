Synopsis: 
'Original Photographs Taken on the Battlefields during the Civil War of the United States' by Miller, Francis Trevelyan is a captivating collection of photographs that offers a unique glimpse into the harrowing realities of the American Civil War. Through the lens of the camera, the author takes readers on a visual journey, showcasing the raw emotions, devastation, and heroism experienced by soldiers and civilians alike. From haunting landscapes to intimate portraits, these original photographs provide a powerful and unfiltered perspective on one of the most significant conflicts in American history.

Genres: Historical, Photography, War

Themes: Conflict, Human resilience, Historical documentation
