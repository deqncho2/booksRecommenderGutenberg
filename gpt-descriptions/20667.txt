Synopsis: 

In 'A Canyon Voyage: The Narrative of the Second Powell Expedition down the Green-Colorado River from Wyoming, and the Explorations on Land, in the Years 1871 and 1872', Frederick Samuel Dellenbaugh takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. Joining the second Powell Expedition, Dellenbaugh recounts the harrowing adventures and breathtaking landscapes encountered as they navigate the treacherous Green and Colorado Rivers. From thrilling encounters with Native American tribes to heart-stopping encounters with rapids and cliffs, this gripping narrative captures the spirit of exploration and the indomitable human will to conquer the unknown.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Wilderness, Survival, Native American Culture
