Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Atlantic Cable', Sir Charles Bright takes us on a captivating journey through the remarkable history of the transatlantic telegraph cable. From the audacious dreams of visionaries to the perilous challenges faced by engineers, this book unveils the gripping tale of how a single cable forever transformed global communication. With tales of daring expeditions, technological breakthroughs, and unexpected setbacks, Bright's narrative paints a vivid picture of the immense human effort and ingenuity that went into connecting two continents. As the story unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the triumphs and tribulations that ultimately led to the successful laying of the Atlantic cable, forever changing the world as we know it.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Technology, Adventure

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Global connectivity
