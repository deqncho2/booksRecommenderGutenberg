Synopsis: In 'A laugh a day keeps the doctor away' by Irvin S. Cobb, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives with a peculiar talent for making people laugh. As the townsfolk become enthralled by his infectious humor, they soon discover that laughter has the power to heal not only their bodies but also their souls. But as the stranger's true identity is revealed, the town must confront the deeper meaning behind their newfound laughter and the consequences it may bring.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Fiction

Themes: Healing power of laughter, Community, Identity
