Synopsis: 

In 'The Expositor's Bible: The Books of Chronicles', W. H. Bennett delves into the rich historical accounts found in the biblical books of Chronicles. With meticulous attention to detail, Bennett uncovers the hidden gems within these often overlooked texts, shedding light on the lives of kings, prophets, and the people of Israel. Through his insightful analysis, he brings to life the stories of triumph and tragedy, faith and rebellion, and the enduring legacy of a nation. 

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical commentary, Historical analysis

Themes: History, Faith, Leadership, Divine Providence
