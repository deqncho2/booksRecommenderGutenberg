Synopsis: In 'Malignant Self Love [Excerpts]', Vaknin, Samuel delves into the dark and complex world of narcissism, offering a captivating exploration of the mind of a narcissist. Through a series of thought-provoking excerpts, the author unveils the intricate web of manipulation, grandiosity, and self-obsession that characterizes this personality disorder. As the reader delves deeper into the psyche of the narcissist, they are confronted with shocking revelations and unsettling truths that challenge their understanding of human behavior. This gripping work provides a unique and insightful perspective on the enigmatic nature of narcissism, leaving readers both fascinated and disturbed.

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Memoir

Themes: Narcissism, Manipulation, Self-identity, Psychological disorders
