Synopsis: 
In the heart of Washington D.C., where power and secrets intertwine, a young journalist stumbles upon a scandal that could shake the foundations of the nation. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of politics, he finds himself entangled in a web of corruption, betrayal, and danger. With his life on the line, he must navigate the treacherous world of power players and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power, Betrayal, Journalism
