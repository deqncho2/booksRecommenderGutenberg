Synopsis: In the midst of the tranquil French countryside, a young woman finds herself caught in the whirlwind of World War I. As the conflict unfolds, she must navigate love, loss, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart. Told in a French Garden: August, 1914 is a captivating tale that transports readers to a time of both beauty and devastation, where the resilience of the human spirit shines brightest.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Resilience in the face of adversity, The impact of war on ordinary lives
