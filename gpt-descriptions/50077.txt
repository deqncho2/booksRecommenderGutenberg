Synopsis: 
'Maternity: Letters from Working-Women' is a captivating collection of letters that offers a unique and intimate glimpse into the lives of working women navigating the challenges of motherhood. Through heartfelt and honest correspondence, these women share their joys, struggles, and the profound impact that motherhood has on their careers and personal lives. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a world of resilience, sacrifice, and the pursuit of balance, as these women strive to redefine societal expectations and find their own paths to fulfillment. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Epistolary, Women's Studies

Themes: Motherhood, Work-life balance, Gender roles, Identity, Empowerment
