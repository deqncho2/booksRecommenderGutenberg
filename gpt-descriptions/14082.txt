Synopsis: In 'Le Corbeau = The Raven', a mysterious visitor arrives at the narrator's door one night, bringing with it a sense of foreboding and unease. As the raven perches upon a bust of Pallas, it begins to speak a single word, driving the narrator to the brink of madness. With each repetition of the word, the narrator's sanity unravels further, leading to a haunting exploration of the human psyche and the power of obsession.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, poetry

Themes: Madness, obsession, the supernatural
