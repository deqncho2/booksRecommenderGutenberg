Synopsis: In 'The Demi-gods' by James Stephens, a group of misfit children stumble upon a hidden world filled with magical creatures and ancient secrets. As they navigate through this enchanting realm, they must confront their own fears and insecurities, discovering the true power that lies within them. With danger lurking at every turn, the children must band together and harness their newfound abilities to save both the magical world and their own. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Courage
