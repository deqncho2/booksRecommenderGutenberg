Synopsis: In 'The Broken Font: A Story of the Civil War, Vol. 1 (of 2)', Sherer Moyle takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the divided nation, this captivating tale follows the lives of two individuals from opposite sides of the conflict. As they navigate the horrors of war, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, forcing them to question their loyalties and confront the true cost of their actions. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this first volume of the series promises an immersive and thought-provoking exploration of the human experience during one of America's darkest periods.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Identity, Sacrifice
