Synopsis:
In 'Ombra' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of her family's ancestral home, Ombra. As she delves deeper into the history of the house, she becomes entangled in a web of dark mysteries and supernatural occurrences. With each revelation, Lucy's understanding of her own identity and the true nature of Ombra is challenged, leading her to question the boundaries between reality and the supernatural.

Genres:
Gothic Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes:
Identity, Family Secrets, Supernatural Phenomena
