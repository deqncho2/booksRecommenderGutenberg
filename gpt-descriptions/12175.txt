Synopsis: In 'The Nest of the Sparrowhawk: A Romance of the XVIIth Century', the reader is transported to the tumultuous world of 17th-century France, where political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets intertwine. As the young and beautiful Marquise de Mortaine finds herself caught in a web of treachery, she must navigate the treacherous court of Louis XIV, where loyalty is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. With her life hanging in the balance, she must rely on her wit, courage, and the unexpected help of a mysterious stranger to unravel the truth and reclaim her destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue, Identity and Self-Discovery
