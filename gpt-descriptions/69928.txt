Synopsis: In "My Fight for Irish Freedom," Dan Breen takes us on a gripping journey through his personal experiences as a key figure in the Irish struggle for independence. From his early involvement in the Irish Republican Brotherhood to his daring escapades as a member of the Irish Volunteers, Breen's account is filled with heart-pounding action, political intrigue, and moments of profound sacrifice. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of the Irish War of Independence, Breen's unwavering determination and unwavering commitment to his cause will inspire readers and shed light on a pivotal chapter in Irish history.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction, Political Memoir

Themes: Nationalism, Patriotism, Revolution, Sacrifice, Identity
