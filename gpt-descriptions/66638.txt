Synopsis: 

In 'Ireland in Fiction: A Guide to Irish Novels, Tales, Romances, and Folk-lore', Stephen J. M. Brown takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Irish literature. From enchanting tales of mythical creatures to gripping historical novels, this comprehensive guide explores the diverse range of Irish fiction. Brown delves into the works of renowned authors such as James Joyce, Oscar Wilde, and Bram Stoker, while also shedding light on lesser-known gems of Irish literature. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, this guide offers a tantalizing glimpse into the captivating world of Irish storytelling.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Irish Literature, Folklore

Themes: Irish identity, Mythology, Historical context
