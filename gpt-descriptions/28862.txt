Synopsis: In 'The Time of Roses' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Rosamund finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious estate. As she delves deeper into the history of the house and its previous occupants, she uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets. With each discovery, Rosamund becomes more entangled in the past, and her own life begins to mirror the tragic events that took place within the walls of the estate. As she races against time to uncover the truth, Rosamund must confront her own fears and desires, ultimately leading her to a shocking revelation that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Forbidden love, Family legacy
