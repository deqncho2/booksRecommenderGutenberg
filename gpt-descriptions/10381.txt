Synopsis: In "The History of a Crime: The Testimony of an Eye-Witness," Victor Hugo takes us on a gripping journey through a notorious event that shook the nation. Through the eyes of an eyewitness, we are immersed in a tale of intrigue, conspiracy, and the dark underbelly of society. As the truth unfolds, the reader is left questioning the nature of justice and the power of truth in a world filled with corruption and deceit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Crime thriller, Political drama

Themes: Justice and truth, Corruption and deceit, Power and manipulation
