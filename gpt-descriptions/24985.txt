Synopsis: 
In 'Comedies of Courtship' by Hope, Anthony, readers are transported to the vibrant world of London's high society, where love, wit, and social conventions collide. The story follows the lives of several young men and women as they navigate the treacherous waters of courtship, facing unexpected twists and turns along the way. From secret rendezvous to mistaken identities, this delightful tale of romance and humor will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and attraction, Social class and etiquette, Deception and mistaken identity
