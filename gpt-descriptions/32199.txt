Synopsis: In 'A House-Party, Don Gesualdo, and A Rainy June' by Ouida, a group of diverse characters find themselves entangled in a series of unexpected events during a lavish house-party. As secrets are revealed and passions ignite, the atmosphere becomes increasingly tense, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Love, Social Class
