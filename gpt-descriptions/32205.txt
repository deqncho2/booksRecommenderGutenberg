Synopsis: 
In 'The Woodlands Orchids, Described and Illustrated: With Stories of Orchid-Collecting' by Frederick Boyle, readers are transported into the captivating world of orchids and the adventures of orchid-collecting. Set in the lush woodlands, this beautifully illustrated book takes us on a journey through the enchanting realm of these exquisite flowers. Through vivid descriptions and captivating stories, Boyle unveils the secrets of orchids, their diverse species, and the thrill of seeking them out in their natural habitats. From the treacherous expeditions to the heartwarming encounters with local communities, this book offers a unique blend of botanical knowledge and captivating narratives that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Non-fiction, Botany, Adventure

Themes: Nature exploration, Beauty of orchids, Adventure and discovery
