Synopsis: Adele Doring, a spirited and curious young girl, finds herself thrust into the unfamiliar world of boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of making new friends, facing strict rules, and discovering hidden secrets, Adele's journey becomes a captivating tale of self-discovery and resilience. With each passing day, she unravels the mysteries that surround her, leading her to unexpected adventures and life-changing lessons. Will Adele find her place in this new environment, or will she succumb to the pressures that come with it?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Resilience, Secrets
