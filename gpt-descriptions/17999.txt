Synopsis: 
In 'The Chief Legatee' by Anna Katharine Green, a wealthy and influential man passes away, leaving behind a mysterious will that sets off a chain of unexpected events. As the deceased's family and friends gather to hear the will's contents, tensions rise and secrets are revealed. The protagonist, a clever and determined young woman, finds herself caught in the middle of a web of deceit and must navigate through a series of puzzling clues to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a thrilling tale of inheritance, betrayal, and hidden motives.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Inheritance, Deception, Family Dynamics
