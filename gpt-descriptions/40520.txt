Synopsis: In 'The Soul Stealer' by Thorne, Guy, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and paranoia. As the townspeople search for answers, they uncover a dark secret that threatens to consume them all. With each passing day, the line between reality and nightmare blurs, and the protagonist must confront their own inner demons to save themselves and their loved ones. This gripping tale of suspense and supernatural forces will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Identity, Redemption
