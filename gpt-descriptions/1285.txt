Synopsis: In 'The Water Goats, and Other Troubles' by Butler, Ellis Parker, follow the misadventures of a peculiar group of characters as they navigate a series of bizarre and hilarious situations. From encounters with mischievous water goats to unexpected encounters with eccentric individuals, this collection of stories will keep readers entertained and guessing what could possibly happen next.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Short Stories

Themes: Absurdity, Irony, Human Foibles
