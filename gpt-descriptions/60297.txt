Synopsis: In 'The World Above: A Duologue' by Crow, Martha Foote, two strangers find themselves trapped in an abandoned subway station during a city-wide blackout. As they navigate the darkness and their own fears, they begin to unravel each other's secrets, leading to a surprising connection that challenges their perceptions of reality. With tension building and time running out, they must confront their pasts and make a choice that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Human Connection
