Synopsis: 
In 'The Car That Went Abroad: Motoring Through the Golden Age', Albert Bigelow Paine takes readers on a thrilling journey across Europe in the early 1900s. Join the author and his trusty automobile as they navigate through picturesque landscapes, encounter fascinating characters, and face unexpected challenges. From the bustling streets of Paris to the serene countryside of England, this travelogue captures the essence of a bygone era and the spirit of adventure that comes with exploring the unknown.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Adventure, Cultural Exchange
