Synopsis: In 'A Chariot of Fire' by Elizabeth Stuart Phelps, a young woman named Helen embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment in the face of societal expectations and limitations. Set in the late 19th century, Helen's story unfolds against the backdrop of a rapidly changing world, where she must navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of personal freedom. As she grapples with the constraints placed upon her by society, Helen finds inspiration in unexpected places, ultimately challenging the status quo and redefining her own path. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender Roles, Individualism, Social Expectations
