Synopsis: 

In 'Criminal Negligence' by J. Francis McComas, a seemingly ordinary day takes a dark turn when a young woman is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As the investigation unfolds, Detective Markham is faced with a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that lead him down a dangerous path. With each twist and turn, the line between justice and negligence becomes blurred, leaving Markham questioning his own moral compass. As the truth slowly unravels, he must confront the devastating consequences of criminal negligence.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Justice, Morality, Consequences
