Synopsis: 
In the final volume of 'The Poems of Philip Freneau, Poet of the American Revolution,' readers are transported to a tumultuous era of American history through the powerful verses of Philip Morin Freneau. This collection showcases Freneau's poetic prowess as he delves into the themes of patriotism, freedom, and the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity. From stirring odes to the heroes of the Revolution to poignant reflections on the cost of war, Freneau's words paint a vivid picture of the struggles and triumphs of the American people during this pivotal time. With each poem, readers are invited to experience the raw emotions and unwavering determination that shaped the birth of a nation.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Literature

Themes: Patriotism, Freedom, Resilience
