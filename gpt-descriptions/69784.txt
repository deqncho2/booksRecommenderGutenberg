Synopsis: In 'Shadows and Sunbeams: Being a Second Series of Fern Leaves from Fanny's Portfolio', Fern takes us on another captivating journey through her collection of essays and musings. With her unique perspective and eloquent writing style, she delves into various aspects of life, exploring the shadows that haunt us and the sunbeams that bring us joy. From reflections on love and loss to observations on society and nature, Fern's words paint a vivid picture of the human experience, leaving readers eager to uncover the hidden depths within her prose.

Genres: Essay, Memoir, Literary Nonfiction

Themes: Life's complexities, Love and loss, Society and culture, Nature and beauty
