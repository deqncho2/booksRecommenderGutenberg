Synopsis: 

'Stories by English Authors: Africa (Selected by Scribners)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and cultures of Africa. This anthology brings together a collection of gripping short stories written by English authors, each offering a unique perspective on the continent. From the bustling streets of colonial cities to the untamed wilderness, these tales explore the complexities of human relationships, the clash of cultures, and the enduring spirit of Africa. Through vivid storytelling and rich imagery, this anthology paints a vivid picture of a continent that is both enchanting and mysterious.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Cultural diversity, Colonialism, Human relationships, Adventure, Identity
