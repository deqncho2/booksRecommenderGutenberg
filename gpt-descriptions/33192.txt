Synopsis: In 'John Marsh's Millions', a mysterious millionaire named John Marsh leaves behind a puzzling will that sets off a thrilling treasure hunt. As a group of strangers race against time to uncover the hidden fortune, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each clue leading them deeper into the unknown, the stakes rise higher, and the true nature of John Marsh's millions becomes increasingly elusive.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Trust and betrayal, The pursuit of happiness
