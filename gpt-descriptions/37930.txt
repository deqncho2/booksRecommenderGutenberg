Synopsis:
"The Legendary and Poetical Remains of John Roby: author of 'Traditions of Lancashire', with a sketch of his literary life and character" delves into the fascinating life and works of John Roby, the renowned author of "Traditions of Lancashire." This collection showcases Roby's legendary and poetic writings, offering a glimpse into his literary genius and the captivating stories he weaves. Through a sketch of his literary life and character, readers are invited to explore the mind of this talented writer and gain a deeper understanding of his contributions to the literary world.

Genres:
Biography, Poetry, Literary Criticism

Themes:
Literary genius, Folklore, Regional traditions
