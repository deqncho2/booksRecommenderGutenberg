Synopsis: In 'The Revellers' by Tracy Louis, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes them deep into the heart of a mysterious carnival. As they navigate through the enchanting yet treacherous world of the carnival, they uncover dark secrets and face unexpected challenges that test their friendships and their own identities. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must rely on their wit, courage, and trust in one another to survive the night and unravel the carnival's enigmatic past.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Courage
