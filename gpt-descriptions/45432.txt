Synopsis: In 'Monica's Choice' by Flora E. Berry, Monica, a young woman with a promising future, finds herself at a crossroads when she is faced with a life-altering decision. As she navigates through the complexities of love, family, and personal growth, Monica must confront her deepest desires and make a choice that will shape her destiny. With unexpected twists and emotional depth, this captivating novel explores the power of self-discovery and the consequences of our decisions.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Family, Decision-making
