Synopsis: In 'Drusilla with a Million' by Elizabeth Cooper, a young woman named Drusilla unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. Thrust into a world of wealth and privilege, she must navigate the complexities of high society while staying true to herself. As Drusilla's life takes a dramatic turn, she encounters unexpected challenges, surprising friendships, and the possibility of love. Will she be able to find happiness amidst the glittering facade of her newfound wealth?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Comedy

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Identity and self-discovery, Love and relationships
