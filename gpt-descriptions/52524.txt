Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of Clara Barton National Historic Site, Maryland, where the remarkable life of a pioneering woman unfolds before your eyes. This immersive experience takes you on a journey through the historic home of Clara Barton, the founder of the American Red Cross. Explore the rooms where she lived, worked, and made history, as you uncover the secrets and stories that shaped her extraordinary legacy. From her tireless efforts during the Civil War to her groundbreaking humanitarian work, this site offers a glimpse into the indomitable spirit of a true trailblazer.

Genres: 
Historical, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Women's History, Humanitarianism, American Civil War
