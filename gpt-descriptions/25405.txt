Synopsis: In 'Honey-Bee: 1911' by Anatole France, a young woman named Honey-Bee embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against societal norms in early 20th century France. As she navigates through the complexities of love, art, and politics, Honey-Bee's unconventional spirit challenges the established order and ignites a spark of change in the world around her. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a captivating adventure through the tumultuous era of 1911.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Individualism, Feminism, Social Change
