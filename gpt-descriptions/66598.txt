Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Thirty-sixth, or the Herefordshire Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1701 to 1852. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, shedding light on the battles fought, the heroes who emerged, and the challenges they faced. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the triumphs and tribulations of the Herefordshire Regiment of Foot.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
