Synopsis: In 'The Changeling' by Walter Besant, a young woman named Muriel is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she discovers a hidden secret about her own identity. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding her birth, Muriel finds herself entangled in a web of deception, danger, and forbidden love. With each revelation, she must navigate treacherous waters and make choices that will forever alter her life. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
