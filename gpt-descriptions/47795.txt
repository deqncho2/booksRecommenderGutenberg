Synopsis:
In 'Recollections with the Third Iowa Regiment', Seymour D. Thompson takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a soldier during the American Civil War. From the chaos of the battlefield to the camaraderie among comrades, Thompson's vivid recollections provide a unique and personal perspective on the trials and triumphs of war. As he recounts the harrowing moments and the bonds formed in the face of adversity, readers will be drawn into the gripping narrative, eager to discover the fate of the Third Iowa Regiment.

Genres:
Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes:
War and its impact, Brotherhood and camaraderie, Personal resilience
