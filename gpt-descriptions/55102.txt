Synopsis: In 'The Wooden Hand: A Detective Story' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case involving a mysterious wooden hand. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping detective story will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Investigation
