Synopsis: 
In 'The House of Orchids, and Other Poems' by Sterling, George, readers are transported to a world where the beauty of nature intertwines with the complexities of human emotions. Through vivid imagery and lyrical language, the poet explores themes of love, longing, and the transient nature of life. From the delicate petals of orchids to the depths of the human soul, this collection of poems invites readers to delve into the profound and mysterious aspects of existence.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Symbolism

Themes: Nature, Love, Transience, Human emotions
