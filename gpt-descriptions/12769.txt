Synopsis: 

In 'How to Teach', Norsworthy and Strayer take readers on an enlightening journey into the art of teaching, offering invaluable insights and practical strategies for educators of all levels. Through a combination of personal anecdotes, research-based evidence, and thought-provoking exercises, the authors explore the fundamental principles of effective teaching and provide guidance on how to create engaging and inclusive learning environments. From fostering student motivation to utilizing innovative teaching techniques, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both new and experienced educators, inspiring them to transform their classrooms and empower their students to reach their full potential.

Genres: Education, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Teaching strategies, Classroom management, Student engagement, Inclusive education, Motivation, Professional development
