Synopsis: In 'Instigations: Together with An Essay on the Chinese Written Character', Ezra Pound takes readers on a captivating journey through his thoughts and observations on various topics, ranging from literature and art to economics and politics. With his unique perspective and sharp wit, Pound challenges conventional ideas and offers fresh insights into the world around us. Through a collection of essays and his exploration of the Chinese written character, he invites readers to question their own assumptions and encourages them to see the world in a new light.

Genres: Essay, Literary Criticism, Philosophy

Themes: Language and communication, Art and aesthetics, Cultural exchange, Social and political commentary
