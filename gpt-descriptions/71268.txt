Synopsis: In 'Skull-face' by Robert E. Howard, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of a forgotten temple, he becomes entangled in a web of dark magic and sinister forces. With his life hanging in the balance, he must confront the terrifying Skull-face, a malevolent entity that will stop at nothing to protect its secrets. This thrilling tale of adventure and supernatural horror will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Horror, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Good vs. Evil
