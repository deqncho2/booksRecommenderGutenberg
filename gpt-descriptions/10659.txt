Synopsis: In 'Three More John Silence Stories' by Algernon Blackwood, the renowned psychic detective, John Silence, returns to solve three perplexing cases that delve into the realms of the supernatural and the unknown. From a haunted house with a chilling secret to a mysterious curse that plagues a family, Silence must use his extraordinary abilities to unravel the enigmas and confront the malevolent forces at play. With each story, the stakes rise higher, and Silence's courage and intellect are put to the ultimate test. Prepare to be captivated by these thrilling tales of suspense and the supernatural.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Psychic abilities, Supernatural phenomena, Confronting the unknown
