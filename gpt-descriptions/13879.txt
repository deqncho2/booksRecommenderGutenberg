Synopsis: In 'The Death of Balder' by Johannes Ewald, a tragic event unfolds as the beloved Norse god Balder meets his untimely demise. As the gods and goddesses of Asgard grapple with grief and seek justice, a web of deceit, betrayal, and divine intervention is woven. Amidst the turmoil, a lone figure emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind Balder's death and restore balance to the realm of gods. This gripping tale of love, loss, and the struggle between good and evil will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Tragedy

Themes: Fate and destiny, Betrayal, Redemption, Mortality
