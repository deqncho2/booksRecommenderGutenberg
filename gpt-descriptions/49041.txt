Synopsis: 

In "Kate Greenaway" by M.H. Spielmann and George Somes Layard, readers are transported into the enchanting world of one of the most beloved children's book illustrators of all time. This captivating biography delves into the life and artistry of Kate Greenaway, revealing the inspirations behind her iconic illustrations and the impact she had on the world of children's literature. From her humble beginnings to her rise to fame, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the creative mind of a true visionary.

Genres: Biography, Art, Children's Literature

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Childhood imagination, Legacy
