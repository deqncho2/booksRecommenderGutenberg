Synopsis: In 'Fairy Prince and Other Stories', Eleanor Hallowell Abbott weaves a collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world where magic and reality intertwine. From a young girl's encounter with a mischievous fairy prince to a mysterious love affair that defies time, each story captivates with its whimsical charm and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate through the realms of fantasy and the complexities of human emotions, they discover the power of love, the importance of self-discovery, and the beauty of embracing the extraordinary in everyday life.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Short Stories

Themes: Magic, Love, Self-discovery, Imagination
