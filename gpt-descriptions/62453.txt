Synopsis: 
In 'Achilles' by Karl Friedrich Becker, readers are transported to the ancient world of Greek mythology, where the legendary hero Achilles takes center stage. This gripping tale follows Achilles' journey from his humble beginnings to becoming one of the greatest warriors of all time. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in a world filled with epic battles, tragic love, and the pursuit of immortality. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'Achilles' explores the complex nature of heroism, the price of glory, and the eternal struggle between fate and free will.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: 
Heroism, Fate vs. Free Will, Love and Loss
