Synopsis: In 'The Cultivation and Manufacture of Tea', Edward Money takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of tea, revealing the secrets behind its cultivation and the intricate process of its manufacture. From the lush tea gardens of China to the bustling tea markets of India, Money's meticulous research and vivid descriptions transport readers to the heart of this ancient and cherished beverage. Delving into the history, traditions, and cultural significance of tea, this book offers a captivating exploration of the plant that has captivated the world for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Tea culture, Agriculture, Global trade
