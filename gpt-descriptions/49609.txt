Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works in Verse and Prose of William Butler Yeats, Vol. 2 (of 8): The King's Threshold. On Baile's Strand. Deirdre. Shadowy Waters', delve into a collection of captivating plays by the renowned poet and playwright, William Butler Yeats. Explore the emotional turmoil of a king who takes a stand for justice, witness the tragic consequences of love and betrayal, and embark on a mystical journey through the depths of the human soul. With rich language and vivid imagery, these plays transport readers to ancient Ireland, where honor, passion, and destiny collide.

Genres: Drama, Poetry, Irish Literature

Themes: Justice, Love and Betrayal, Fate and Destiny
