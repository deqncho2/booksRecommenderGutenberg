Synopsis: In 'The Boys' Life of Lafayette' by Helen Nicolay, embark on an extraordinary journey through the life of a remarkable historical figure. Follow the captivating story of Lafayette, a young French nobleman who defied convention and risked everything to fight for freedom and justice. From his daring adventures on the battlefield to his enduring friendship with George Washington, this gripping biography brings to life the incredible exploits of a true hero.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism
