Synopsis:
In "Ozma of Oz," Dorothy Gale finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz, but this time with a new set of companions including the Yellow Hen, the Scarecrow, the Tin Woodman, Tiktok, the Cowardly Lion, and the Hungry Tiger. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected encounters with both friends and foes. As they navigate through the enchanting landscapes of Oz, Dorothy and her companions must rely on their wit, courage, and loyalty to overcome the challenges they face and protect the land they hold dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Good vs. Evil
