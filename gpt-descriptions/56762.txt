Synopsis: 

'Wild Life in New Zealand. Part I. Mammalia.' takes readers on an enthralling journey through the diverse and captivating world of New Zealand's mammalian species. Written by George Malcolm Thomson, this manual offers a comprehensive exploration of the unique wildlife found in this enchanting country. From the elusive kiwi bird to the playful dolphins and majestic whales, Thomson's vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes bring these creatures to life. Discover the fascinating behaviors, habitats, and conservation efforts surrounding New Zealand's mammalian inhabitants in this captivating guide.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Wildlife

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Ecological Balance
