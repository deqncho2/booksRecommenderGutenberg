Synopsis: 
In the small town of Saumur, Eugenie Grandet, a young and innocent girl, lives with her miserly father, Felix Grandet. Despite their immense wealth, Felix refuses to spend a single penny, hoarding his fortune and subjecting his family to a life of extreme frugality. When Eugenie's cousin, Charles, arrives from Paris, she finds herself drawn to him, but their budding romance is threatened by her father's greed and the secrets that lie within their family. As Eugenie navigates the complexities of love, money, and societal expectations, she must confront the harsh realities of her father's world and make choices that will shape her future.

Genres: 
Realism, Historical Fiction, Psychological Fiction

Themes: 
Wealth and Greed, Love and Relationships, Social Class, Family Dynamics
