Synopsis: In 'Maggie Miller: The Story of Old Hagar's Secret' by Mary Jane Holmes, a young woman named Maggie Miller finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she discovers an old letter hidden in her deceased mother's belongings. As she delves deeper into the past, Maggie uncovers a long-buried secret that could change her life forever. With the help of a charming stranger, she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, facing unexpected challenges and uncovering shocking revelations along the way.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, the power of family bonds, the search for identity
