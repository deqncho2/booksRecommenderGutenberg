Synopsis: In 'Hampton Court' by Walter Jerrold, a mysterious and enchanting tale unfolds within the walls of the historic Hampton Court Palace. As the protagonist delves into the secrets of the palace's past, they become entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and supernatural occurrences. With each discovery, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the truth behind the palace's haunting mysteries.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of the past, the thin veil between reality and the supernatural
