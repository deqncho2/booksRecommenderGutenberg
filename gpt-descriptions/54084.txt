Synopsis: 

'List of Post Offices in Canada, with the Names of the Postmasters ... 1865' is a comprehensive compilation of post offices across Canada during the year 1865. This historical document provides a fascinating glimpse into the postal system of the time, revealing the vast network of post offices and the individuals responsible for their operation. From bustling urban centers to remote rural areas, this list showcases the diverse locations and dedicated postmasters who played a crucial role in connecting communities through mail services. 

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Communication, Community Development
