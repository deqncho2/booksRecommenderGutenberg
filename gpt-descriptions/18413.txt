Synopsis: In 'Prudence of the Parsonage' by Ethel Hueston, readers are introduced to Prudence, a young woman who finds herself in a small town parsonage, surrounded by a cast of eccentric characters. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, Prudence discovers unexpected friendships, faces moral dilemmas, and experiences the joys and heartaches of love. With wit, humor, and a touch of romance, this delightful tale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Morality
