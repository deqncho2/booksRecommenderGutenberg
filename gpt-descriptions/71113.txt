Synopsis: In the second volume of the captivating novel "Far above rubies," the story continues to follow the life of the resilient protagonist as she navigates through a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, where the protagonist's strength and determination are put to the ultimate test. As she unravels the mysteries surrounding her family and confronts the demons of her past, she discovers that the choices she makes will have far-reaching consequences. With each page, the tension builds, leaving the reader eagerly anticipating the final installment of this gripping tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Resilience, Redemption
