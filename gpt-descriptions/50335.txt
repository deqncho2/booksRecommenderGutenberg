Synopsis: 
'American War Ballads and Lyrics, Volume 1' is a captivating collection of songs and ballads that chronicles the tumultuous history of America from the Colonial Wars to the Civil War. This volume delves into the stirring tales of bravery, sacrifice, and patriotism that have shaped the nation. Through powerful and evocative lyrics, the book transports readers to pivotal moments in American history, capturing the spirit and emotions of those who fought and lived through these conflicts.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Music

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Sacrifice, National Identity
