Synopsis: In 'On Guard: Mark Mallory's Celebration' by Sinclair, Upton, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he investigates a seemingly innocent celebration. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the guests, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With his wit and intuition, Mallory must navigate a treacherous game of cat and mouse to protect those he loves and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
