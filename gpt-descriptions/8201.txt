Synopsis: In "Mary Marston" by George MacDonald, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between the expectations of society and her own desires for independence and self-discovery. As she navigates the complexities of love, faith, and personal growth, Mary's journey takes unexpected turns, challenging her to question the conventional norms of her time. With a cast of intriguing characters and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, this captivating novel explores the depths of human nature and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Spiritual

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Love, Faith, Social Expectations
