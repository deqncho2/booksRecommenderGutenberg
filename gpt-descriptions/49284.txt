Synopsis: In 'A Maid of Brittany: A Romance' by Mabel Winifred Knowles, the reader is transported to the enchanting world of medieval Brittany. The story follows the life of a young and spirited maid named Yvonne, who finds herself caught in the midst of political turmoil and a forbidden love affair. As Yvonne navigates the treacherous landscape of power struggles and secrets, she must summon her courage and resilience to protect her family and fight for her own destiny. With its captivating blend of romance, adventure, and intrigue, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the captivating tapestry of medieval Brittany.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics, Identity and Independence
