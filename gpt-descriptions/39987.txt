Synopsis: In 'The Flower of Forgiveness' by Flora Annie Steel, a young woman named Meera finds herself torn between tradition and her own desires when she falls in love with a man from a different caste. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Meera must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of forgiveness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Tradition and modernity, Forgiveness and redemption
