Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Spain: founded on the Historia de España y de la civilización española of Rafael Altamira', Rafael Altamira takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and complex history of Spain. From its ancient origins to the modern era, Altamira weaves together a compelling narrative that explores the nation's political, cultural, and social developments.

Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Altamira delves into the rise and fall of empires, the influence of religion, the impact of colonization, and the struggles for independence. From the Roman conquest to the Islamic rule, from the Spanish Inquisition to the Golden Age of exploration, this book offers a comprehensive and enlightening account of Spain's past.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Spanish history, Politics, Culture, Religion, Colonization, Independence
