Synopsis: In 'QRM-Interplanetary' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could revolutionize space travel. As he delves deeper into his invention, he becomes entangled in a web of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and interplanetary politics. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention and ensure a future among the stars.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Technology, Power Struggles, Ethics
