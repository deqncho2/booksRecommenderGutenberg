Synopsis: 

'Good Stories for Great Holidays' is a delightful collection of stories curated by Frances Jenkins Olcott, perfect for both storytelling and reading aloud. This enchanting book offers a diverse selection of tales that are sure to captivate children's imaginations and transport them to different times and places. From heartwarming holiday adventures to thrilling escapades, each story is carefully arranged to engage young readers and listeners alike. Whether it's a tale of bravery, friendship, or the magic of the holiday season, this collection is bound to create cherished memories for children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Storytelling, Anthology

Themes: Holidays, Adventure, Friendship, Bravery, Magic
