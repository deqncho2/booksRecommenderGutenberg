Synopsis: In 'The Lost Million' by William Le Queux, a mysterious disappearance of a vast fortune sets off a thrilling chain of events. When a wealthy man vanishes without a trace, leaving behind a fortune worth millions, a determined detective is called upon to unravel the enigma. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden identities. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the detective must navigate a treacherous world of secrets and lies to uncover the truth behind the lost million.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Wealth and greed, Identity and deception
