Synopsis: In 'Lumen' by Camille Flammarion, a renowned astronomer stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that challenges his understanding of the universe. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he embarks on a thrilling journey that takes him to uncharted territories, where he encounters extraordinary beings and uncovers secrets that could alter the course of humanity. With each revelation, the boundaries between science and spirituality blur, leaving the reader captivated by the wonders of the cosmos and the profound questions they raise.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the intersection of science and spirituality, the nature of existence
