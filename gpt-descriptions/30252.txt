Synopsis: In 'The Four-Faced Visitors of Ezekiel' by Arthur W. Orton, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious group of visitors with four faces each suddenly appear. As the townspeople grapple with the implications of their arrival, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of these enigmatic beings is slowly revealed. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of reality and the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Existentialism
