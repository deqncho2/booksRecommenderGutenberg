Synopsis: 

"The Empire of Russia: From the Remotest Periods to the Present Time" takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and tumultuous history of Russia. From its mysterious origins to its rise as a global power, this book delves into the fascinating stories of the country's rulers, wars, and cultural developments. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, John S. C. Abbott paints a vivid picture of the Russian Empire, revealing its triumphs, struggles, and enduring legacy. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural development, Historical legacy
