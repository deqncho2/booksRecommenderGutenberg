Synopsis: The Outdoor Girls at Ocean View; Or, The Box That Was Found in the Sand is an exciting adventure novel that follows a group of young girls as they embark on a thrilling seaside vacation. When the girls stumble upon a mysterious box buried in the sand, they become entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected events. As they work together to unravel the mystery, they encounter danger, friendship, and unexpected surprises that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Curiosity
