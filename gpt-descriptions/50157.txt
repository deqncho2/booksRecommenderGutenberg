Synopsis: In 'Billie Bradley and the School Mystery; Or, The Girl From Oklahoma' by Janet D. Wheeler, a new girl from Oklahoma arrives at Billie's school, bringing with her a mysterious past and a secret that could change everything. As Billie and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth, they find themselves caught up in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists. Will they be able to solve the school mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Coming of Age
