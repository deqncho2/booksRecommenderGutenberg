Synopsis: In 'Confession and Absolution' by T. J. Capel, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when a mysterious patient confesses to a crime that shakes the foundations of his own beliefs. As he delves deeper into the truth, he must confront his own demons and question the boundaries of forgiveness and redemption. With each revelation, the lines between right and wrong blur, leaving the reader questioning their own moral compass.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Guilt and redemption, Morality and ethics, Deception and truth
