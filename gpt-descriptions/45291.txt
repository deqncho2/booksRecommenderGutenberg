Synopsis: In 'The Missing Prince' by G. E. Farrow, a young prince mysteriously disappears from the royal palace, leaving the kingdom in turmoil. As the search for the missing prince intensifies, a brave and resourceful young girl named Lily embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind his disappearance. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must navigate treacherous paths, encounter magical creatures, and unravel a web of secrets that could change the fate of the kingdom forever.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Identity, Power
