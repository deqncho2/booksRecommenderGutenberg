Synopsis: In 'The Council of Seven' by J. C. Snaith, a mysterious group of individuals known as the Council of Seven holds immense power and influence over the fate of nations. As the story unfolds, a young protagonist finds themselves entangled in a web of secrets, political intrigue, and ancient prophecies. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape of alliances and betrayals, all while uncovering the true nature and intentions of the enigmatic Council. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate truth behind the Council of Seven.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and Influence, Secrets and Betrayal, Fate and Prophecy
