Synopsis: In 'The Art of Music, Vol. 02 (of 14), A Narrative History of Music. Book 2, Classicism and Romanticism', embark on a captivating journey through the evolution of music during the Classical and Romantic periods. Delve into the lives of renowned composers, witness the birth of new musical forms, and explore the profound emotions that shaped these transformative eras. From the elegance and balance of Classicism to the passionate and expressive nature of Romanticism, this comprehensive volume offers a rich tapestry of musical history that will captivate both music lovers and musicians alike.

Genres: Music History, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Evolution of Music, Classical Period, Romantic Period, Composers, Musical Forms, Emotion in Music
