Synopsis: In 'The Night-Born' by Jack London, a collection of thrilling short stories takes readers on a journey through the dark and mysterious corners of the human experience. From the treacherous Alaskan wilderness to the gritty streets of San Francisco, these tales explore the depths of human nature, delving into themes of survival, love, and the pursuit of adventure. With vivid storytelling and unexpected twists, 'The Night-Born' captivates readers with its gripping narratives and leaves them yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Survival, Love, Human Nature
