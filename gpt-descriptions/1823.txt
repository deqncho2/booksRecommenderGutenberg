Synopsis: In 'The Make-Believe Man' by Davis, Richard Harding, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his charm and enigmatic persona. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with him, they begin to question the reality of his existence. Is he a figment of their imagination or a genuine person with a hidden agenda? This gripping tale explores the power of perception, the allure of illusion, and the consequences of living in a world of make-believe.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Identity, Deception
