Synopsis: In 'Beggars on Horseback: A riding tour in North Wales', Ross Martin takes readers on a captivating journey through the stunning landscapes of North Wales. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, the author invites us to join him on an unforgettable riding tour, where unexpected encounters, hidden treasures, and personal reflections intertwine. As the narrative unfolds, we are immersed in the beauty of the Welsh countryside, encountering a diverse cast of characters and experiencing the transformative power of nature. Through this travelogue, Ross Martin offers a unique perspective on the joys and challenges of exploring a new land on horseback, leaving readers yearning for their own adventure amidst the breathtaking scenery of North Wales.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Personal Growth
