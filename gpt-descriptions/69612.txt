Synopsis: 
"The Sea Scouts of the Kestrel" takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the English Channel as a group of young sea scouts embark on a daring adventure aboard a small yacht. Filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected challenges, this thrilling tale follows the scouts as they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and test their courage and resourcefulness. As they face the perils of the sea, the scouts learn valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and the power of friendship. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead and prove their mettle as true sea scouts?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Teamwork, Perseverance
