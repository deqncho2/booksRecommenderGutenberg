Synopsis: In 'Gleanings by the Way', John A. Clark takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal experiences and encounters with various individuals, offering profound insights and thought-provoking reflections. From chance encounters with strangers to deep conversations with friends, Clark's collection of anecdotes and observations provides a unique perspective on life's complexities and the human condition. With each story, the reader is invited to delve into the depths of human emotions, contemplate the meaning of existence, and explore the interconnectedness of all beings.

Genres: Memoir, Philosophy, Inspirational

Themes: Human connection, Existentialism, Wisdom, Reflection
