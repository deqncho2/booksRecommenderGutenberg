Synopsis: 
In 'Transient' by William Harris, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small, isolated town, bringing with him an air of enigma and a dark secret. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by his presence, they soon discover that his arrival is not a mere coincidence. With each passing day, the stranger's true nature is slowly unveiled, leading to a series of unexpected events that will forever change the lives of the townsfolk. Filled with suspense and unexpected twists, 'Transient' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the depths of human nature and the consequences of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Consequences
