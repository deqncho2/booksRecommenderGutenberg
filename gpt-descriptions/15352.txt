Synopsis: 

'Venereal Diseases in New Zealand (1922): Report of the Special Committee of the Board of Health appointed by the Hon. Minister of Health' delves into the hidden world of venereal diseases in early 20th century New Zealand. This groundbreaking report uncovers the shocking prevalence and devastating impact of these diseases on individuals and society as a whole. Through meticulous research and eye-opening testimonies, the committee exposes the harsh realities and challenges faced by those affected, while also shedding light on the efforts made to combat the spread of these diseases. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Historical

Themes: Public health, Stigma, Social impact
