Synopsis: In 'Boys and Girls from Thackeray' by Kate Dickinson Sweetser, readers are transported to the charming world of Thackeray's novels, where they meet a delightful cast of characters. From the mischievous boys who embark on adventurous escapades to the spirited girls who navigate the complexities of society, this collection of stories offers a captivating glimpse into the lives of the young protagonists. With humor, wit, and a touch of nostalgia, Sweetser brings Thackeray's beloved characters to life, weaving together tales of friendship, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Classic Literature, Short Stories

Themes: Coming of age, Social class, Gender roles, Friendship, Love
