Synopsis: In 'Copper Coleson's Ghost' by Edward P. Hendrick, a small town is plagued by a mysterious ghostly presence that haunts the residents, leaving them terrified and questioning their sanity. As the ghostly encounters become more frequent and intense, a group of unlikely allies must come together to uncover the truth behind the haunting and put an end to the ghost's torment. With each revelation, they delve deeper into the town's dark history, unearthing secrets that have long been buried. As the line between the living and the dead blurs, they must confront their own fears and confront the malevolent force that threatens to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Ghosts, Small-town secrets, Overcoming fear
