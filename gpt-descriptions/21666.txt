Synopsis: 
'Uncle Rutherford's Nieces: A Story for Girls' follows the lives of three young sisters, Lucy, Grace, and Marion, who are sent to live with their eccentric Uncle Rutherford after the death of their parents. As they adjust to their new surroundings, they discover a hidden family secret that leads them on a thrilling adventure. Along the way, they encounter mysterious characters, solve puzzles, and learn valuable life lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of family bonds. This heartwarming tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
