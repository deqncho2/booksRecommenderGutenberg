Synopsis: 
In 'Miss Leslie's Lady's New Receipt-Book, 3rd ed.', Eliza Leslie presents a comprehensive guide for both large and small families, offering a wealth of knowledge on cooking, preserving, pickling, and more. With detailed directions and helpful tips, this book is a must-have for anyone seeking to enhance their culinary skills and create delicious meals. From traditional recipes to innovative techniques, Miss Leslie's expertise shines through, making this book an invaluable resource for all aspiring home cooks.

Genres: Cookbook, Non-fiction

Themes: Culinary skills, Home cooking, Food preservation
