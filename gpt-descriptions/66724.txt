Synopsis: In 'The Four-Fingered Glove; Or, The Cost of a Lie' by Carter, Nicholas, a mysterious glove with a missing finger sets off a chain of events that unravels a web of secrets and deceit. As the protagonist delves deeper into the glove's origins, they discover a dark and dangerous world lurking beneath the surface of their seemingly ordinary life. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the cost of keeping secrets becomes increasingly perilous. Will the truth be uncovered, or will the consequences of a lie prove too great to bear?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Consequences of Secrets, Uncovering the Truth
