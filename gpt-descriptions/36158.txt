Synopsis: In 'Daisy Burns (Volume 2)' by Julia Kavanagh, the captivating story of Daisy continues as she navigates through the complexities of love, loss, and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, Daisy's journey takes unexpected turns, leading her to confront her past and make difficult choices that will shape her future. As secrets unravel and new relationships form, Daisy must find the strength to follow her heart and find her true path in life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Relationships, Identity and Self-Discovery, Resilience and Redemption
