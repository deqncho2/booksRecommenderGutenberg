Synopsis: In the small town of Pinos Altos, secrets lie buried beneath the surface, waiting to be unearthed. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As the past collides with the present, a web of intrigue, love, and betrayal begins to unravel, revealing the true nature of the town and its inhabitants. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of Pinos Altos, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
