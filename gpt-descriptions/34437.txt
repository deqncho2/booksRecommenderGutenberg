Synopsis: In 'Methods of Destroying Rats' by David E. Lantz, a small town is plagued by an infestation of rats, causing chaos and fear among its residents. As the situation escalates, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to find a solution and restore peace to their community. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to in order to protect their loved ones and overcome adversity.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Survival, Community, Courage
