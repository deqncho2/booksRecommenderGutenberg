Synopsis: In Hostile Red takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American frontier during the French and Indian War. As tensions rise between the British and French forces, young Robert Lennox finds himself caught in the middle of a dangerous conflict. With his loyal friends by his side, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face ruthless enemies, and uncover a secret that could change the course of the war. Will Robert's courage and determination be enough to survive in this hostile red wilderness?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Coming of Age
