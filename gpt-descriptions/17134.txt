Synopsis: In 'Taboo: A Legend Retold from the Dirghic of Sævius Nicanor, with; Prolegomena, Notes, and a Preliminary Memoir' by Cabell, James Branch, a mysterious and forbidden legend is brought to life through the ancient writings of Sævius Nicanor. As the story unfolds, readers are transported to a world where societal norms are challenged, taboos are broken, and the consequences of such actions are explored. With captivating prose and intricate details, Cabell weaves a tale that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and the boundaries they have set for themselves.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Taboo, Morality, Identity, Conformity
