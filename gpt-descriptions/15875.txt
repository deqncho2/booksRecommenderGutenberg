Synopsis:
In "The Unseen Bridegroom; Or, Wedded For a Week," a young woman finds herself in a peculiar situation when she agrees to marry a man she has never met. However, there is a catch - her new husband is invisible! As she navigates the challenges of being married to an unseen partner, secrets and mysteries begin to unravel, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and unexpected romance. Will she be able to uncover the truth behind her husband's invisibility and find true happiness?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Love and marriage, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and hidden truths
