Synopsis: In "Maida's Little House" by Inez Haynes Gillmore, a young girl named Maida receives a unique gift from her father - a miniature house that is an exact replica of their own home. As Maida explores the tiny house, she discovers that it holds magical secrets and becomes a gateway to a world of adventure and imagination. With the help of her friends, Maida embarks on a series of enchanting escapades that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Magic
