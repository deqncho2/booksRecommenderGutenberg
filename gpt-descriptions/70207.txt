Synopsis: In "The Man with the Iron Mask" by Topin, Marius, a mysterious prisoner is held captive in the depths of the Bastille, concealed behind an iron mask that hides his true identity. As rumors spread and intrigue deepens, a group of loyal friends embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth and free the enigmatic prisoner. Their quest takes them through a web of political conspiracies, secret societies, and unexpected alliances, leading to a shocking revelation that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Betrayal, Power
