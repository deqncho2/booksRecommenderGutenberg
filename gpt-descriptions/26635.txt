Synopsis: In "The Rose-Garden Husband," a young woman named Mary finds herself in a peculiar situation when she marries a man who seems to be more interested in his beloved rose garden than in her. As Mary navigates the challenges of her unconventional marriage, she discovers unexpected secrets and embarks on a journey of self-discovery. This captivating story explores the complexities of love, marriage, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Marriage, Self-discovery
