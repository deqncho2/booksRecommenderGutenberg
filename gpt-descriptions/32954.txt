Synopsis: In the midst of the Wars of the Roses, young Dick Shelton finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue, secret identities, and forbidden love. As he embarks on a quest for justice and revenge, he must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover hidden truths, and confront his own inner demons. With the fate of England hanging in the balance, Dick must choose between loyalty and betrayal, honor and survival, in a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Justice
