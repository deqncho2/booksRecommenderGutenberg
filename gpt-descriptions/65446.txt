Synopsis: In 'Six Frightened Men' by Robert Silverberg, a group of six strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious underground bunker with no memory of how they got there. As they struggle to uncover the truth behind their confinement, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling battle for survival. With each passing moment, the men must confront their deepest fears and confront the darkness that lurks within themselves. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Fear, Survival
