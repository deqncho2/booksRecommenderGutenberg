Synopsis: 
In 'More Conjuring: Simple Tricks for Social Gatherings' by Hercat, prepare to be amazed as the author takes you on a captivating journey through a collection of mind-boggling magic tricks that are perfect for entertaining friends and family at social gatherings. From mind reading to vanishing acts, Hercat reveals the secrets behind these astonishing illusions, providing step-by-step instructions that will have you wowing your audience in no time. But be warned, as the tricks become more complex, the line between reality and illusion begins to blur, leaving you questioning what is truly possible. Get ready to unlock the secrets of the magician's world and become the life of the party with 'More Conjuring: Simple Tricks for Social Gatherings'.

Genres: Magic, Entertainment, How-to

Themes: Illusion, Mystery, Performance
