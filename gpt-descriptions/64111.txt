Synopsis: 

In "Pugilistica: The History of British Boxing, Volume 3," author Henry Downes Miles takes readers on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of British boxing. This volume, the final installment of the series, delves into the lives of the most celebrated pugilists, providing full reports of their battles as documented in contemporary newspapers. With the inclusion of authentic portraits, personal anecdotes, and sketches of the principal patrons of the prize ring, Miles presents a comprehensive history of the ring from its early days in the 18th century to the monumental championship battle between King and Heenan in December 1863.

Genres: Sports, History, Biography

Themes: Boxing, Courage, Determination, Social History
