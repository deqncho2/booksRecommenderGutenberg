Synopsis: "War to the Knife;" or, Tangata Maori by Boldrewood, Rolf is a gripping historical adventure set in 19th century New Zealand. The story follows a young Englishman who finds himself caught in the midst of the conflict between the indigenous Maori people and the European settlers. As tensions rise and battles ensue, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, forge unlikely alliances, and confront his own beliefs and loyalties. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a turbulent era in New Zealand's history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Cultural clash, Loyalty, Survival
