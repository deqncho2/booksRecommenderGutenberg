Synopsis: Nan Sherwood, a spirited and adventurous young woman, finds herself embroiled in a thrilling mystery when she arrives at Lakeview Hall. As she settles into her new boarding school, strange occurrences begin to unfold, particularly centered around the haunted boathouse. Determined to uncover the truth, Nan and her friends embark on a daring investigation, uncovering secrets and facing unexpected dangers along the way. Will they be able to solve the mystery of the haunted boathouse before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Curiosity, Coming of Age
