Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Central High at Basketball; Or, The Great Gymnasium Mystery', the girls of Central High School find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery surrounding their beloved gymnasium. As they prepare for an important basketball game, strange occurrences and unexplained events begin to unfold, leading the girls on a suspenseful journey to uncover the truth. With their teamwork, determination, and love for the sport, the girls of Central High must navigate through unexpected twists and turns to solve the great gymnasium mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Sports, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Determination, Mystery-solving
