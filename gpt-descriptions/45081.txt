Synopsis: In 'The Apostles', Renan, Ernest takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the twelve apostles of Jesus Christ. From their humble beginnings as fishermen and tax collectors to their pivotal roles in spreading the teachings of Christianity, this book delves into the personal struggles, doubts, and triumphs of these influential figures. As Renan weaves together historical facts and imaginative storytelling, he offers a fresh perspective on the lives of the apostles, shedding light on their individual personalities and the challenges they faced in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Religious literature

Themes: Faith and doubt, Transformation, Leadership, Friendship, Sacrifice
