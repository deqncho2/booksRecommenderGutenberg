Synopsis: In 'The Evil Genius: A Domestic Story' by Collins, Wilkie, a seemingly ordinary family is torn apart by a mysterious and malevolent force. As secrets are unveiled and tensions rise, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and psychological manipulation. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who the true evil genius behind it all really is.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Deception, Family Dynamics, Psychological Manipulation
