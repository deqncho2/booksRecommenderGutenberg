Synopsis: 
In 'Diplomatic Days' by Edith O'Shaughnessy, readers are transported to the glamorous world of international diplomacy. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows the adventures of the author herself as she navigates the intricate web of political intrigue, social events, and personal relationships. From lavish parties to secret negotiations, O'Shaughnessy provides a captivating glimpse into the hidden world of diplomats and their enthralling lives. As she encounters fascinating characters and experiences unexpected twists, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where appearances can be deceiving and loyalty is constantly tested. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Political Thriller

Themes: Diplomacy, Intrigue, Identity, Loyalty
