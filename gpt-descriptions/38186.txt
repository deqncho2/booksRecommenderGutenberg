Synopsis: 
In 'The Sailor' by J. C. Snaith, a young sailor named Tom embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas, facing treacherous storms, mysterious islands, and encounters with fascinating characters. As he navigates through the unknown, Tom discovers secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to question his own identity. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds into a captivating adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Exploration, Self-discovery
