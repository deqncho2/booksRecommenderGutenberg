Synopsis: In 'Left Behind; Or, Ten Days a Newsboy' by James Otis, follow the gripping tale of Johnny, a young newsboy who finds himself caught up in a mysterious and dangerous adventure. When Johnny's life takes an unexpected turn, he must navigate the treacherous streets of New York City, encountering a cast of intriguing characters and facing numerous challenges along the way. As he strives to uncover the truth behind a perplexing secret, Johnny's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the odds and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Coming of Age
