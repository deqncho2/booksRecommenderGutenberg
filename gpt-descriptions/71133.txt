Synopsis: 
In 'Won at last : $b or, Mrs. Briscoe's nephews' by Agnes Giberne, a mysterious inheritance sets the stage for a thrilling tale of family secrets, unexpected twists, and a race against time. When Mrs. Briscoe's nephews discover they have been left a substantial fortune, they are overjoyed. However, they soon realize that their newfound wealth comes with a series of challenges and dangers they never could have anticipated. As they navigate through a web of deceit and betrayal, the nephews must uncover the truth behind their inheritance before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Inheritance, Family Secrets, Deception
