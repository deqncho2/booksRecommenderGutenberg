Synopsis: In 'The Curse of the Reckaviles' by Walter S. Masterman, a small village is plagued by a mysterious curse that has haunted the Reckaviles family for generations. When a young historian arrives to investigate the curse, he uncovers a web of dark secrets and supernatural occurrences that threaten to consume him. As he delves deeper into the history of the Reckaviles, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where the line between reality and the supernatural blurs. With time running out, he must unravel the truth behind the curse before it claims another victim.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Family curses, Secrets and lies, Supernatural forces, Betrayal
