Synopsis: 

In 'ASCE 1193: The Water-Works and Sewerage of Monterrey, N. L., Mexico', author G. R. G. Conway takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate water and sewerage systems of Monterrey, N. L., Mexico. Through detailed descriptions and insightful analysis, Conway unveils the challenges faced and innovative solutions implemented in this bustling city. From the engineering marvels to the social impact, this article offers a fascinating glimpse into the infrastructure that supports the daily lives of Monterrey's residents.

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, History

Themes: Infrastructure development, Urban planning, Technological advancements
