Synopsis: 
In 'Caleb Conover, Railroader' by Albert Payson Terhune, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of Caleb Conover, a man whose passion for railroading knows no bounds. From his humble beginnings as a young boy fascinated by trains to his rise as a prominent figure in the railroad industry, Caleb's determination and unwavering spirit propel him forward. As he faces numerous challenges, both personal and professional, Caleb must navigate the treacherous world of business, confront his own limitations, and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of the railroad landscape and a cast of compelling characters, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Caleb's adventures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Sacrifice
