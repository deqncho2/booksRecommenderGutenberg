Synopsis: In 'In exitu Israel: an historical novel, volume 2', the captivating sequel to the first volume, author S. Baring-Gould delves deeper into the lives of the characters as they navigate through the tumultuous events of ancient Israel. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, religious conflicts, and the struggle for power, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the fate of the nation hangs in the balance. Will the characters find redemption and freedom, or will they succumb to the forces that seek to destroy them?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Religious Fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Faith and Religion, Loyalty and Betrayal
