Synopsis: In 'The Duplicate Death' by Arthur Charles Fox-Davies, a seemingly ordinary murder investigation takes a thrilling turn when a second identical murder occurs. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden identities that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With a cleverly crafted plot and a cast of intriguing characters, this gripping mystery will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
