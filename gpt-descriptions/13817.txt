Synopsis: In "With Marlborough to Malplaquet: A Story of the Reign of Queen Anne," Richard Stead and Herbert Strang take readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous reign of Queen Anne. Set against the backdrop of the War of the Spanish Succession, the story follows the adventures of a young protagonist who finds himself caught up in the epic battles and political intrigues of the time. From the grandeur of the royal court to the chaos of the battlefield, the narrative unfolds with gripping action, unexpected twists, and a cast of memorable characters. As the protagonist navigates the treacherous landscape of war and politics, he must confront his own personal challenges and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Power, Coming of Age
