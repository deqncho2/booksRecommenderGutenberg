Synopsis: 
In "Queen Mary; and, Harold" by Alfred Tennyson, Baron Tennyson, the reader is transported to a tumultuous period in English history. Set during the reign of Queen Mary I, the story follows the intertwined lives of the titular queen and Harold, a nobleman torn between loyalty and love. As political and religious tensions rise, the characters are faced with difficult choices that will shape the destiny of the nation. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this work delves into the struggles of power, love, and faith in a time of great upheaval.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: 
Power struggles, Loyalty, Love, Religion, Identity
