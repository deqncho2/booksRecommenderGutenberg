Synopsis:
In the small coastal town of Ballycastle, Ireland, a group of young fishermen embark on a dangerous adventure that will test their courage, loyalty, and resilience. As they set out to prove themselves as skilled seafarers, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the harsh realities of the unpredictable sea. With their livelihoods and the future of their community at stake, the seiners must navigate treacherous waters both on and off the boat, forging unbreakable bonds and discovering the true meaning of sacrifice along the way.

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes:
Courage, Loyalty, Community, Survival
