Synopsis:
'Aurelian; or, Rome in the Third Century' by William Ware is a gripping historical novel set in ancient Rome during the tumultuous third century. The story follows the life of Aurelian, a young Roman soldier who rises through the ranks to become Emperor. As Aurelian navigates the treacherous world of politics, power, and war, he must confront personal challenges and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of Rome. Filled with vivid descriptions of the grandeur and decadence of the Roman Empire, this captivating tale explores the complexities of leadership, loyalty, and the enduring spirit of Rome.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Drama

Themes:
Leadership, Loyalty, Power, Fate, War
