Synopsis: 

In 'The English and Scottish Popular Ballads, Volume 5', readers are transported to a world of enchanting tales and captivating folklore. This final volume of the collection delves into the rich tapestry of traditional ballads from England and Scotland, bringing to life stories of love, betrayal, heroism, and the supernatural. From hauntingly beautiful ballads of lost love to thrilling tales of adventure and mythical creatures, this volume offers a mesmerizing journey through the oral tradition of these two nations. 

Genres: Folklore, Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Love, Betrayal, Heroism, Supernatural, Mythology
