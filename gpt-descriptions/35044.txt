Synopsis: In 'The Boys of the Wireless; Or, A Stirring Rescue from the Deep' by Frank V. Webster, a group of young friends find themselves caught in a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious distress signal while experimenting with their wireless telegraph. As they embark on a daring mission to save lives, they must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected dangers, and rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the odds. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of the boys' courageous rescue mission.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
