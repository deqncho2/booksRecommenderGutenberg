Synopsis: 
In "A World of Green Hills: Observations of Nature and Human Nature in the Blue Ridge," author Bradford Torrey takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Through his keen observations, Torrey delves into the intricate relationship between nature and human nature, painting a vivid portrait of the region's flora, fauna, and the people who call it home. From the enchanting melodies of songbirds to the hidden secrets of the forest, Torrey's lyrical prose invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and wonder of the Blue Ridge.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Nature appreciation, Human connection with the environment, Environmental conservation
