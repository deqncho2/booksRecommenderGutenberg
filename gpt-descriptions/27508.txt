Synopsis: In 'The King's Warrant: A Story of Old and New France' by Alfred H. Engelbach, readers are transported to the captivating world of 17th-century France. The story follows the life of a young nobleman who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of the French court, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those around him. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the contrasting worlds of old and new France.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
