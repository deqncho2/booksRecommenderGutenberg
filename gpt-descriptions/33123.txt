Synopsis: In 'A Transient Guest, and Other Episodes' by Edgar Saltus, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and thought-provoking episodes. From the mysterious arrival of a transient guest to the unexpected encounters of various characters, each episode presents a unique and captivating story that will leave readers wanting more. With unexpected twists and turns, this collection explores the depths of human nature and the complexities of life, offering a glimpse into the extraordinary moments that shape our existence.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Human nature, Fate, Unexpected encounters
