Synopsis: 
In 'Ned, Bob and Jerry at Boxwood Hall; Or, The Motor Boys as Freshmen', three adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling new chapter of their lives as they enter Boxwood Hall as freshmen. Amidst the excitement of college life, they quickly discover that their passion for motor vehicles and their knack for solving mysteries will lead them on an unexpected journey. As they navigate the challenges of academia, make new friends, and encounter a series of puzzling events, Ned, Bob, and Jerry must rely on their wit, courage, and their beloved motor machines to unravel the mysteries that unfold before them.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Problem-solving, Coming of Age
