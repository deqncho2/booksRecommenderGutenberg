Synopsis: In 'The Return Of The Soul: 1896' by Hichens, Robert, a renowned Egyptologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient Egyptian tomb. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own beliefs and question the boundaries of life and death. This gripping tale of adventure and the supernatural will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma that lies within the tomb.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of the afterlife, Ancient Egyptian mythology, Quest for knowledge
