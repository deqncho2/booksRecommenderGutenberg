Synopsis: In "Sadhana: The Realisation of Life," Rabindranath Tagore takes readers on a profound journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Through a collection of essays, he explores the essence of human existence, delving into the depths of love, beauty, and the interconnectedness of all living beings. Tagore's insightful reflections and poetic prose invite readers to question their own perceptions of reality and embrace the transformative power of inner growth.

Genres: Philosophy, Spiritual, Essay

Themes: Self-discovery, Spirituality, Love, Beauty, Interconnectedness
