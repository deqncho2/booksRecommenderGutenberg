Synopsis: In "Runnymede and Lincoln Fair: A Story of the Great Charter," author John G. Edgar takes readers on a captivating journey through medieval England, where two young friends find themselves caught up in a dangerous quest for justice and freedom. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, their loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of the historic signing of the Magna Carta, this thrilling tale explores the power of friendship, the pursuit of liberty, and the enduring legacy of one of history's most significant documents.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Justice, Freedom, Historical Significance
