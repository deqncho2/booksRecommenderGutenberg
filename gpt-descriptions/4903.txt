Synopsis:
In 'Hilda Wade, a Woman with Tenacity of Purpose' by Allen Grant, readers are introduced to the enigmatic and determined Hilda Wade, a young nurse with a mysterious past. As she navigates the world of medicine, Hilda finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected romance. With her sharp intellect and unwavering determination, Hilda becomes a force to be reckoned with, uncovering hidden truths and fighting for justice. This thrilling tale takes readers on a captivating journey filled with suspense, intrigue, and the resilience of a remarkable woman.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Determination, Secrets, Justice
