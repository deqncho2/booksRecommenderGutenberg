Synopsis: In 'Equality' by Charles Dudley Warner, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small New England town, challenging the residents' deeply ingrained beliefs and societal norms. As the stranger's presence disrupts the town's harmony, tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, leading to a thought-provoking exploration of human nature and the pursuit of true equality. 

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Equality, Individualism, Hypocrisy, Social Justice
