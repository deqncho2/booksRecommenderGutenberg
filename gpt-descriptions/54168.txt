Synopsis: 

'English Lands, Letters and Kings, vol. 1: From Celt to Tudor' by Mitchell, Donald Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of England, from the ancient Celts to the Tudor dynasty. This meticulously researched volume delves into the lives of kings, queens, and notable figures, exploring their triumphs, struggles, and the impact they had on shaping the English nation. From battles and political intrigue to cultural and literary achievements, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging account of England's past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Cultural history

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural identity, Literary heritage
