Synopsis: 

'Philosophic Nights in Paris: Being selections from Promenades Philosophiques' takes readers on a captivating journey through the streets of Paris, as seen through the eyes of the author, Remy de Gourmont. In this collection of philosophical musings, Gourmont explores the hidden depths of the city, delving into the mysteries of life, love, art, and the human condition. From encounters with intriguing characters to contemplations on the nature of existence, each night in Paris brings new insights and revelations. 

Genres: Philosophy, Travelogue, Literary Fiction

Themes: Existentialism, Beauty, Human Nature, Art, Love
