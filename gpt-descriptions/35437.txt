Synopsis: 
'Six Prize Hawaiian Stories of the Kilohana Art League' is a captivating collection of six short stories set in the enchanting landscapes of Hawaii. From tales of forbidden love and mysterious legends to thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the rich cultural tapestry of the islands. Each story offers a unique perspective on the beauty, traditions, and complexities of Hawaiian life, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Cultural

Themes: Love and Relationships, Tradition and Culture, Mystery and Intrigue
