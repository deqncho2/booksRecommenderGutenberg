Synopsis: In the untamed wilderness of the Canadian Rockies, a young man named Unaga finds himself caught between two worlds - the world of his Native American heritage and the world of the white settlers encroaching upon his land. As tensions rise and conflicts escalate, Unaga must navigate the treacherous path of loyalty, love, and self-discovery, ultimately facing a choice that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Cultural identity, Conflict between tradition and progress, Love and sacrifice
