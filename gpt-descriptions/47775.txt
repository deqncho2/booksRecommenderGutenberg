Synopsis: 
In 'Kitty Carter, Canteen Girl' by Ruby Lorraine Radford, we follow the captivating journey of Kitty Carter, a young woman who finds herself working in a bustling canteen during World War II. As she navigates the challenges of war, Kitty discovers unexpected friendships, faces personal hardships, and experiences the transformative power of love. With vivid descriptions and a compelling narrative, this novel takes readers on an emotional rollercoaster, filled with heartwarming moments and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, resilience in the face of adversity, the power of friendship, personal growth and self-discovery
