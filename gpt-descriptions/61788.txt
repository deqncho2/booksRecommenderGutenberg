Synopsis: In 'Swiss Heroes: An Historical Romance of the Time of Charles the Bold', the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of Charles the Bold, where political intrigue, forbidden love, and epic battles intertwine. Amidst the backdrop of a divided Switzerland, two young heroes, Rudolph and Arnold, find themselves caught in a web of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice. As they navigate treacherous alliances and face formidable enemies, their courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to protect their homeland and the ones they love, or will they succumb to the perils of war and betrayal?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Love, Sacrifice, Political Intrigue
