Synopsis: In 'The Unknown Sea' by Housman, Clemence, a young woman named Sylvia embarks on a journey that takes her to the mysterious and enchanting world of the sea. As she delves deeper into its secrets, she discovers a hidden realm filled with captivating creatures and ancient legends. Along the way, Sylvia encounters unexpected allies and faces formidable challenges that test her courage and determination. With each step closer to uncovering the truth, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and must confront her own fears and desires. Will Sylvia unlock the secrets of the unknown sea, or will she be forever lost in its depths?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Coming of Age
