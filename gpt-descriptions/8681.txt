Synopsis: In 'The Face and the Mask' by Robert Barr, a mysterious man named Felix Marl approaches a struggling artist, offering him a deal that could change his life forever. As the artist becomes entangled in Marl's web of secrets and deception, he must navigate a world where appearances can be deceiving and trust is a dangerous game. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale explores the lengths people will go to protect their true identities and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Trust
