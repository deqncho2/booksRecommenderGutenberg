Synopsis: 
In 'A Visit to the Monastery of La Trappe in 1817', the author takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Le Perche, Normandy, Bretagne, Poitou, Anjou, Le Bocage, Touraine, Orleanois, and the environs of Paris. Along the way, the narrative focuses on a particular visit to the renowned Monastery of La Trappe, offering a unique glimpse into the secluded and mysterious world of the Trappist monks. Through vivid descriptions and numerous colored engravings, the author brings to life the serene beauty of the monastery and its surroundings, while also delving into the spiritual practices and daily routines of the monks. This immersive travelogue provides an intriguing exploration of a hidden world, inviting readers to discover the secrets and wonders of the Monastery of La Trappe.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Spirituality, Monasticism, Exploration of Religious Practices
