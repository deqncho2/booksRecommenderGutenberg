Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the heart of the Congo as John H. Weeks delves into the rich tapestry of Congolese life and folklore. From the bustling streets of Kinshasa to the remote villages nestled deep within the rainforest, this immersive exploration offers a glimpse into the vibrant traditions, enchanting myths, and extraordinary customs that shape the lives of the Congolese people. As Weeks uncovers the hidden stories and legends passed down through generations, he uncovers a world teeming with magic, mystery, and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Cultural anthropology, Travelogue, Folklore

Themes: Cultural identity, Oral tradition, Supernatural beliefs, Resilience
