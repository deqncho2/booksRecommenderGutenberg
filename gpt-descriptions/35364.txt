Synopsis: 
In 'Ethel Morton at Sweetbrier Lodge', Ethel and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure when they are invited to spend the summer at a picturesque lodge. As they settle into their new surroundings, they soon discover that Sweetbrier Lodge holds secrets and mysteries waiting to be unraveled. With their curiosity piqued, Ethel and her friends find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and friendship. Will they be able to uncover the truth behind the lodge's enigmatic past?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Courage
