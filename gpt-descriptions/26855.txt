Synopsis: 
In 'Hard Guy' by Browne, Howard, we are introduced to Jack, a tough and enigmatic character with a dark past. When a mysterious woman enters his life, Jack finds himself caught in a web of danger and deceit. As he navigates through a treacherous underworld, Jack must confront his own demons and make choices that could determine his fate. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, 'Hard Guy' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Identity
