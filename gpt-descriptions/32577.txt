Synopsis:
In the small town of Dorothy Page, a mysterious newcomer arrives, stirring up curiosity and suspicion among the locals. As secrets from the past begin to unravel, the lives of the townspeople become intertwined in unexpected ways. With each revelation, the true nature of Dorothy Page is slowly revealed, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the town's hidden secrets.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Suspense

Themes:
Identity, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
