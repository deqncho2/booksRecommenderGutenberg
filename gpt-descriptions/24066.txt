Synopsis: In "Dr. Johnson and His Circle" by John Cann Bailey, readers are transported to the vibrant world of 18th-century London, where the charismatic and enigmatic figure of Dr. Samuel Johnson reigns supreme. Through a captivating narrative, the book delves into the lives and relationships of Johnson and his eclectic circle of friends, including renowned writers, artists, and intellectuals. As the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and intellectual debates intertwine, painting a vivid portrait of a fascinating era and the extraordinary individuals who shaped it.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Friendship, Intellectualism, Society and Culture, Literary Legacy
