Synopsis: In 'The Blind Man's Eyes', a mysterious blind man arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his uncanny ability to see into their souls. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the blind man's powers, they soon discover that his visions hold the key to their deepest secrets and desires. But as the blind man's influence grows, the line between truth and deception becomes blurred, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Secrets and Deception, Power and Manipulation
