Synopsis: 

In "Two Men on a Mill: The Story of the Restoration of Baxter's Mill," author A. Harold Castonguay takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of historical preservation. The story revolves around two men, each with their own unique backgrounds and motivations, who come together to restore the dilapidated Baxter's Mill. As they delve into the mill's rich history and face numerous challenges along the way, their determination and passion for preserving the past becomes a driving force. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Historical preservation, Friendship, Overcoming obstacles
