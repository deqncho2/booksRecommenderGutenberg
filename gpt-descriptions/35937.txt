Synopsis: In 'Are the Planets Inhabited?' E. Walter Maunder takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysteries of the universe, exploring the possibility of life on other planets. With a blend of scientific knowledge and imaginative speculation, Maunder delves into the latest astronomical discoveries and presents compelling evidence that challenges our understanding of the cosmos. From Mars to Venus, and even beyond our solar system, this thought-provoking book will leave readers questioning the boundaries of life in the universe.

Genres: Science, Astronomy, Speculative Non-Fiction

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Astrobiology, Scientific exploration
