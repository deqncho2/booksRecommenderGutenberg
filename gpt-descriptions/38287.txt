Synopsis: In 'We're Civilized!', Clifton and Apostolides take readers on a thrilling journey through a dystopian world where societal norms are turned upside down. As the protagonist navigates this new reality, they uncover shocking secrets that challenge everything they thought they knew. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Dystopian, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Societal norms, Identity, Power dynamics
