Synopsis: In 'The Crock of Gold: A Rural Novel', Tupper, Martin Farquhar takes readers on a whimsical journey through the Irish countryside, where two mischievous leprechauns, a philosopher and a poet, embark on a quest to find the legendary Crock of Gold. Along the way, they encounter a colorful cast of characters, including a wise old goat and a mischievous fairy, as they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of happiness. This enchanting tale weaves together elements of fantasy, folklore, and philosophy, creating a captivating narrative that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Friendship, Happiness, Nature, Irish folklore
