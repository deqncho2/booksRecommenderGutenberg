Synopsis: In the aftermath of the Civil War, a small Southern town becomes a battleground for racial tensions, political corruption, and personal vendettas. As the lives of both black and white residents intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of justice is put to the ultimate test. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, 'The Marrow of Tradition' explores the deep-rooted legacy of slavery and the struggle for equality in post-war America.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Southern Gothic, Social Commentary

Themes: Racism, Power and Corruption, Identity, Justice
