Synopsis: In "History of Biology," L. C. Miall takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of the scientific study of life. From ancient civilizations' observations of nature to groundbreaking discoveries in genetics and evolution, this book delves into the fascinating stories of the individuals who shaped the field of biology. Miall's engaging narrative explores the triumphs and challenges faced by these pioneers, revealing the intricate web of ideas and theories that have shaped our understanding of life on Earth. With vivid descriptions and thought-provoking insights, "History of Biology" offers a captivating exploration of the ever-evolving quest to unravel the mysteries of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Scientific Discovery, Intellectual Progress
