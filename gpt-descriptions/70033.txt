Synopsis: 
"The History of Magic" by Éliphas Lévi is a captivating exploration of the world of magic, delving into its intricate procedures, enigmatic rituals, and mysterious secrets. From ancient civilizations to modern practices, Lévi takes readers on a journey through the evolution of magic, shedding light on its various forms and the profound impact it has had on human culture. With a blend of historical analysis and personal anecdotes, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the hidden realms of the occult, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover the depths of magical knowledge.

Genres: Occult, History, Philosophy

Themes: Magic, Mysticism, Esotericism, Human spirituality
