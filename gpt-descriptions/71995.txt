Synopsis: In the quaint town of Dunmow, a centuries-old tradition takes place every four years, where married couples are challenged to prove their unwavering love and devotion to one another in order to win a flitch of bacon. As the latest competition approaches, a young couple, burdened by secrets and doubts, decides to participate, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of their relationship and the true meaning of love.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and devotion, Secrets and lies, Tradition and customs
