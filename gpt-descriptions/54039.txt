Synopsis: 
In 'Fast-Day Cookery; or, Meals without Meat', Grace Johnson presents a tantalizing collection of recipes that will revolutionize the way you approach meatless meals. From hearty soups and stews to delectable desserts, Johnson's innovative and mouthwatering creations will leave you wondering why you ever thought vegetarian cooking was boring. With her clever use of ingredients and imaginative flavor combinations, she proves that meat-free dishes can be just as satisfying and delicious as their carnivorous counterparts. Whether you're a seasoned vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, this cookbook is a must-have for anyone seeking culinary inspiration and a new perspective on fast-day cooking.

Genres: Cookbook, Vegetarian Cuisine, Culinary Arts

Themes: Vegetarianism, Healthy Eating, Creative Cooking
