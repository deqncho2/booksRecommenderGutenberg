Synopsis: In "Religion and Theology: A Sermon for the Times," Tulloch delivers a thought-provoking sermon that challenges conventional beliefs and explores the intersection of religion and theology. Preached in two different churches, the sermon captivates the audience with its insightful and timely message. As Tulloch delves into the complexities of faith, he offers a fresh perspective that encourages introspection and sparks a deeper understanding of religious principles. 

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Theology, Religious Interpretation
