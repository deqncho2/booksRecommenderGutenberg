Synopsis: 
'Poetry for children' is a delightful collection of poems written by Charles and Mary Lamb, specifically crafted to captivate the hearts and minds of young readers. From whimsical tales of talking animals to enchanting verses about nature and imagination, this anthology offers a treasure trove of lyrical wonders that will transport children to magical worlds and ignite their love for poetry. With its charming illustrations and playful language, 'Poetry for children' is a must-read for both children and adults alike, promising to spark joy and ignite the imagination.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Poetry, Anthology

Themes: 
Imagination, Nature, Animals, Childhood
