Synopsis: 
'Paradisi in sole paradisus terrestris' is a comprehensive guide written by John Parkinson that explores the world of gardening and horticulture. It provides detailed information on various types of flowers, herbs, roots, fruits, and trees that can be grown in England. The book covers everything from the proper planting and care of these plants to their uses and virtues. It serves as a valuable resource for anyone interested in creating and maintaining a beautiful garden or orchard.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Horticulture

Themes: Gardening, Nature, Plant cultivation
