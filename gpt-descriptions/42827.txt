Synopsis: In 'Bannertail: The Story of a Graysquirrel' by Ernest Thompson Seton, follow the captivating journey of Bannertail, a young gray squirrel, as he navigates the challenges of the wild. From daring escapes to unexpected friendships, Bannertail's determination and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Set against the backdrop of the lush forest, this heartwarming tale explores the beauty of nature and the power of loyalty.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Nature's Majesty
