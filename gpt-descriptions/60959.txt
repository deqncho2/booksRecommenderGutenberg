Synopsis: 
'John Chinaman on the Rand' by Anonymous is a gripping and mysterious tale set in the gold mines of South Africa. The story follows John Chinaman, a Chinese immigrant who finds himself caught in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates the treacherous world of the gold mines, John must confront his own past and make choices that will determine his future. With its richly detailed setting and complex characters, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Survival, Betrayal
