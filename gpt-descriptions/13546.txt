Synopsis: In 'The Dark House' by I. A. R. Wylie, a young woman named Mary inherits a mysterious old house from her late aunt. As she settles into her new home, Mary becomes increasingly aware of strange occurrences and eerie secrets that surround the house. With each passing day, the house seems to hold a dark and haunting presence, leading Mary to question her own sanity and the true nature of the house's past. As she delves deeper into the mysteries, Mary must confront her deepest fears and uncover the chilling truth that lies within the dark house.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Haunting, Secrets, Sanity, Inheritance
