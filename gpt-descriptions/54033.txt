Synopsis: 
When She Came Home from College follows the story of a young woman named Emily who returns home after completing her college education. However, her homecoming is far from what she expected, as she finds herself caught in a web of family secrets, hidden desires, and unexpected betrayals. As Emily navigates through the complexities of her relationships with her family and friends, she must confront the truth about herself and the choices she has made. With each revelation, Emily's world is turned upside down, forcing her to question everything she thought she knew about love, loyalty, and the meaning of home.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity and self-discovery, Family dynamics
