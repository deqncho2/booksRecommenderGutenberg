Synopsis: In 'Idols' by William John Locke, a renowned sculptor finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he receives a peculiar commission from an enigmatic client. As he delves deeper into the project, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected connections that challenge his perception of art and reality. With each sculpture he creates, the artist unknowingly unravels a complex puzzle that could have far-reaching consequences. As the lines between truth and illusion blur, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Art and creativity, Perception and reality, Betrayal and trust
