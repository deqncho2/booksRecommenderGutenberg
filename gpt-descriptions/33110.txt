Synopsis: 

In 'Corporal 'Lige's Recruit: A Story of Crown Point and Ticonderoga', young Lige is thrust into the heart of the American Revolution as he joins the ranks of the Continental Army. From the treacherous wilderness of Crown Point to the legendary fortress of Ticonderoga, Lige's journey is filled with danger, camaraderie, and unexpected discoveries. As he navigates the perils of war, Lige must confront his own fears and find the strength to fight for his beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Coming of Age
