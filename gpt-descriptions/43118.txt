Synopsis: In 'A Bevy of Girls' by L. T. Meade, a group of young women find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when they stumble upon a hidden secret. As they navigate through the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, they must uncover the truth before it tears their lives apart. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Female friendship, Love and relationships
