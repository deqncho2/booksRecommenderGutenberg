Synopsis: 
Set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars, 'War and Peace' follows the lives of several aristocratic families in Russia as they navigate love, war, and the pursuit of happiness. Through the intertwining stories of these characters, Tolstoy explores the complexities of human nature, the impact of historical events on individuals, and the eternal struggle between fate and free will. From grand balls to battlefields, from passionate romances to philosophical musings, this epic novel takes readers on a journey through the triumphs and tragedies of life, offering a profound reflection on the human condition.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Epic

Themes: War, Love, Fate, Free will, Philosophy
