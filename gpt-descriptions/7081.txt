Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls on Cedar Lake; Or, the Hermit of Fern Island," the adventurous and resourceful Motor Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling summer vacation at Cedar Lake. Little do they know that their idyllic getaway will lead them to uncover a mysterious hermit living on Fern Island. As they navigate the lake's treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, the Motor Girls must rely on their wit and determination to solve the enigma of the hermit and protect their newfound friend. Will they succeed in unraveling the secrets of Cedar Lake?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
