Synopsis: In 'The Rival Campers Ashore; or, The Mystery of the Mill' by Smith, Ruel Perley, a group of young campers find themselves embroiled in a perplexing mystery when they stumble upon an abandoned mill. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the mill, they uncover a web of intrigue and danger that tests their courage and resourcefulness. With each clue they uncover, the campers become more determined to solve the mystery, but they soon realize that they are not the only ones interested in the mill's secrets. Will they be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
