Synopsis: In 'The Hero of Ticonderoga; or, Ethan Allen and His Green Mountain Boys' by John De Morgan, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. This gripping historical novel follows the daring exploits of Ethan Allen and his band of fearless Green Mountain Boys as they embark on a perilous mission to capture the strategic fort of Ticonderoga from the British. Filled with action, intrigue, and the spirit of patriotism, this captivating tale brings to life the bravery and determination of these unsung heroes of the American Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Leadership, Freedom
