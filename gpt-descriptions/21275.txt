Synopsis: In 'The Goat and Her Kid' by Myrtle, Harriet, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious goat and her kid in the woods near her home. As she befriends the animals, she uncovers a hidden world filled with magic and adventure. Together, they embark on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and friendship, leading them to uncover secrets that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Courage, Magic
