Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Culm Rock, a year unfolds with unexpected events and life-altering lessons. As the townspeople navigate through personal triumphs and tragedies, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human resilience is explored. From the enigmatic arrival of a mysterious stranger to the haunting echoes of the past, Culm Rock becomes a microcosm of the human experience, where the power of community and the fragility of life intertwine in unexpected ways.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Community, Resilience, Secrets, Redemption
