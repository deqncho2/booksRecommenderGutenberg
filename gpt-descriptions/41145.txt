Synopsis: In "An Account of the Death of Philip Jolin," the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the dark and mysterious events surrounding the execution of Philip Jolin for the murder of his own father. Set in the Island of Jersey in 1829, this true crime account delves into the intricate web of secrets, motives, and betrayals that led to this shocking crime. As the story unfolds, the reader is left questioning the true nature of guilt and innocence, and the lengths one would go to protect their own secrets. 

Genres: True Crime, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Justice and punishment
