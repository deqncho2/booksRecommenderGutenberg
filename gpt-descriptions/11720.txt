Synopsis:
In 'Fenton's Quest' by M. E. Braddon, a young man named Gilbert Fenton finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind a tragic event. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the past, he discovers shocking revelations that threaten to shatter his world and those around him. With danger lurking at every turn, Fenton must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth, even if it means putting his own life on the line.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes:
Obsession, Secrets and Betrayal, Redemption
