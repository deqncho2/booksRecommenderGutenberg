Synopsis:
David Copperfield is a captivating coming-of-age tale that follows the life of a young boy named David as he navigates through the trials and tribulations of Victorian England. From his idyllic childhood to his tumultuous journey into adulthood, David encounters a colorful cast of characters, including the eccentric Mr. Micawber, the enigmatic Uriah Heep, and the kind-hearted Agnes Wickfield. As he faces numerous challenges and heartbreaks, David must find the strength to overcome adversity and discover his true identity in a world filled with both cruelty and compassion.

Genres:
Bildungsroman, Victorian Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Love and Relationships, Social Class, Resilience
