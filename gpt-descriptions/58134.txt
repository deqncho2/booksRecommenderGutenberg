Synopsis:
In "The Manchester and Glasgow Road, Volume 1: This Way to Gretna Green," Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the historic road connecting Manchester and Glasgow. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper explores the fascinating stories and hidden gems that lie along this iconic route. From picturesque landscapes to charming villages, the author paints a vivid picture of the road's rich history and the characters who have traversed it. As the journey unfolds, readers are transported back in time, discovering the secrets and scandals that have unfolded on this famous road. With each turn of the page, the allure of the Manchester and Glasgow Road becomes irresistible, leaving readers eager to uncover more of its captivating tales.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Road trips, Historical landmarks, Cultural heritage
