Synopsis: In 'The Rim of the Desert' by Ada Woodruff Anderson, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure through the rugged desert landscape, Mary must navigate treacherous obstacles and confront her own fears. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, she discovers that the true treasure may not be what she initially sought. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Self-discovery
