Synopsis: In 'The Enormous Word' by William Oberfield, a young boy named Sam discovers a mysterious word that has the power to change the world. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the word's origin and meaning, Sam encounters unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries. With each step, he unravels the secrets behind the enormous word and learns the true power of language. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic word.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Power of language, Discovery, Friendship
