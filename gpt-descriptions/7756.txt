Synopsis: 
In 'Godolphin, Complete' by Edward Bulwer Lytton, Baron Lytton, a young and ambitious politician named Godolphin finds himself torn between his political aspirations and his passionate love for a captivating woman. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the sacrifices he is willing to make for power and love. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this gripping tale explores the complexities of ambition, desire, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Political drama

Themes: Ambition, Love, Power, Sacrifice
