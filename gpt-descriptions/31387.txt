Synopsis: 
Betty Wales, Freshman follows the journey of Betty Wales as she enters college, leaving behind her sheltered life and embarking on a new chapter filled with challenges, friendships, and self-discovery. As Betty navigates the complexities of college life, she encounters a diverse group of classmates, each with their own ambitions and secrets. With determination and resilience, Betty strives to find her place in this new world, facing unexpected obstacles and learning valuable lessons along the way. Will Betty be able to overcome the hurdles that come her way and emerge stronger than ever?

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Friendship, Ambition, Resilience
