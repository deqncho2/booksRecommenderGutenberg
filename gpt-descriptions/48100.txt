Synopsis: 
"The Sabbath-School Index: Pointing out the history and progress of Sunday-schools, with approved modes of instruction" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history and development of Sunday-schools. Written by Richard Gay Pardee, this enlightening work explores the various methods of instruction that have been employed in these schools over time. From the early beginnings to the present day, the author delves into the evolution of Sunday-schools, providing valuable insights into their impact on education and society.

Genres: 
Historical, Educational, Non-fiction

Themes: 
History of Education, Progress, Instruction Methods
