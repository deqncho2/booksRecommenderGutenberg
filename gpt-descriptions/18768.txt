Synopsis: In 'The Sky Is Falling' by Del Rey, Lester, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of conspiracy and betrayal to save mankind from impending doom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Conspiracy, Survival
