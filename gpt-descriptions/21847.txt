Synopsis: 
In 'Dr. Wortle's School' by Anthony Trollope, the esteemed headmaster of a small English school finds himself embroiled in a scandal when a mysterious woman arrives, claiming to be the lawful wife of one of his teachers. As the truth becomes increasingly elusive, the headmaster must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and reputation, all while trying to maintain the integrity of his beloved school.

Genres: 
Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Love and marriage, Reputation and social standing, Loyalty and betrayal
