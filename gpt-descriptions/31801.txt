Synopsis: In 'The Story of Old Fort Loudon', Charles Egbert Craddock takes readers on a captivating journey to the rugged frontier of Tennessee during the French and Indian War. Set against the backdrop of the historical Fort Loudon, the story follows the lives of a diverse group of settlers as they navigate the perils of war, forge unlikely alliances, and confront the harsh realities of survival. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the fate of the fort and its inhabitants hangs in the balance, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: War and Conflict, Survival, Loyalty, Community
