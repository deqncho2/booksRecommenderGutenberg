Synopsis: In 'Meg's Friend: A Story for Girls' by Alice Corkran, readers are introduced to Meg, a young girl who finds herself in a new town, feeling lonely and out of place. However, her life takes an unexpected turn when she meets a mysterious girl named Grace. As their friendship blossoms, Meg discovers that Grace has a secret that could change everything. With themes of friendship, self-discovery, and the power of secrets, this captivating story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Self-Discovery, Secrets
