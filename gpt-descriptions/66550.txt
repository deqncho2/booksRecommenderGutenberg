Synopsis:
In 'Farewell' by F. W. Harvey, a young soldier finds himself torn between duty and love during the chaos of World War I. As he navigates the treacherous trenches and faces the harsh realities of war, he becomes entangled in a forbidden romance that challenges his loyalty and tests his resolve. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices made in the name of love and the devastating consequences of war.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, The Brutality of War
