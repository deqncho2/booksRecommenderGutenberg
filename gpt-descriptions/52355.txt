Synopsis: In 'Battle of New Orleans: Its Real Meaning', Folk, Reau E. delves into the historical events surrounding the famous Battle of New Orleans, uncovering a hidden narrative that challenges conventional understanding. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, the author sheds light on the untold aspects of this pivotal moment in American history, revealing surprising truths that have long been overlooked. As the layers of the past are peeled back, readers are taken on a captivating journey that will forever change their perception of this iconic battle.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American history

Themes: Historical revisionism, Patriotism, Heroism, National identity
