Synopsis:
In 'Trent's Last Case' by Bentley, E. C., the renowned detective Philip Trent is called upon to solve a perplexing murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Trent finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, secrets, and unexpected twists. With each clue he uncovers, the truth becomes more elusive, leading him down a path he never could have anticipated. As the case reaches its climax, Trent must confront his own limitations and make a decision that will forever change his life.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes:
Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
