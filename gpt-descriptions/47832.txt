Synopsis: In 'Perpetua. A Tale of Nimes in A.D. 213', the reader is transported to the ancient city of Nimes, where a young woman named Perpetua finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and religious persecution. As she navigates the treacherous world of Roman society, Perpetua must make choices that will not only determine her own fate but also challenge the very foundations of the society she lives in. With its gripping narrative and rich historical backdrop, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the walls of Nimes.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Religious persecution, Forbidden love, Personal freedom, Societal norms
