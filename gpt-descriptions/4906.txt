Synopsis: In 'The Lucky Man' by Baron, Monsieur (Michel), a down-on-his-luck gambler stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that grants him an extraordinary power. As he navigates the treacherous world of high-stakes gambling, he must decide whether his newfound luck is a blessing or a curse. With danger lurking around every corner, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal, where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Luck, Fate, Morality
