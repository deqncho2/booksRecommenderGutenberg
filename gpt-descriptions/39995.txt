Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'For the Soul of Rafael' by Marah Ellis Ryan, a young artist named Rafael finds himself caught between his passion for his art and the love he feels for a captivating woman. As he navigates the challenges of his personal and professional life, Rafael must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant art scene in early 20th century Paris, this tale of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Artistic passion, Love and sacrifice, Self-discovery
