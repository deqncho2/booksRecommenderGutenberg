Synopsis: 
In "Mountaineering in the Sierra Nevada," Clarence King takes readers on a thrilling journey through the majestic peaks of the Sierra Nevada mountain range. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding adventures, King recounts his daring expeditions, from scaling treacherous cliffs to navigating treacherous glaciers. As he explores the untamed wilderness, encountering breathtaking landscapes and encountering unexpected challenges, readers will be captivated by the sheer beauty and danger of the Sierra Nevada. 

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Perseverance
