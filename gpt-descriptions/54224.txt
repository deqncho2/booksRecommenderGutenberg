Synopsis: 

'Venice: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on an enchanting journey through the captivating city of Venice. Through the eyes of the author, Fred Richards, we are transported to the winding canals, majestic palaces, and bustling squares that make up this unique and timeless city. With vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, Richards captures the essence of Venice, revealing its hidden corners and secret stories. From the vibrant festivals to the quiet moments of reflection, this sketch-book offers a glimpse into the soul of Venice, inviting readers to immerse themselves in its beauty and mystery.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Beauty, Cultural Heritage
