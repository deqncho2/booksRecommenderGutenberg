Synopsis: 
In "Hebrew Humor and Other Essays," J. Chotzner takes readers on a delightful journey through the world of Jewish humor. From witty anecdotes to clever wordplay, this collection of essays explores the rich tradition of humor within the Hebrew culture. Chotzner's insightful analysis and humorous storytelling will leave readers laughing out loud and gaining a deeper appreciation for the power of laughter. Whether you're familiar with Jewish humor or new to the genre, this book is sure to entertain and enlighten.

Genres: 
Humor, Cultural Studies, Essay Collection

Themes: 
Jewish culture, Humor as a coping mechanism, Language and wordplay
