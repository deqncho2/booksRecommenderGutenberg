Synopsis: In the remote English countryside, a mysterious and ancient evil awakens from its slumber, terrorizing the local community. As a group of brave individuals uncover the dark secrets hidden within the lair of the white worm, they find themselves entangled in a battle against an unimaginable force that threatens to consume them all.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural thriller

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Ancient mythology, Power of fear and belief
