Synopsis: 

'Investigation of Communist activities in Seattle, Wash., area. Hearings, Part 3' delves into the gripping hearings conducted by the United States Congress' House Committee on Un-American Activities. This installment focuses on the Seattle, Washington area, where the committee uncovers a web of clandestine Communist activities. As witnesses testify and evidence is presented, the true extent of the Communist infiltration in the region begins to unravel, leading to shocking revelations and unexpected twists.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political, Historical

Themes: Cold War, Government Surveillance, Ideological Conflict
