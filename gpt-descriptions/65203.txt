Synopsis: 

"The Book of the West" takes readers on a captivating journey through the birth and early adventures of western Canada. From its youthful combats to its peaceful settlement, the book explores the region's rich history, highlighting the great transformation it has undergone and shedding light on its present ways. Through vivid storytelling and engaging anecdotes, Howard Angus Kennedy paints a vivid picture of the challenges, triumphs, and remarkable individuals who shaped the West. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Frontier life, Cultural transformation, Resilience and perseverance
