Synopsis: In 'The Fleet: Its Rivers, Prison, and Marriages' by John Ashton, embark on a captivating journey through the hidden world of London's infamous Fleet Prison. Uncover the secrets and scandals that unfold within its walls, as well as the surprising connections that are forged through unexpected marriages. From tales of forbidden love to daring escapes, this riveting account offers a glimpse into the lives of those who found themselves entangled in the web of the Fleet. 

Genres: Historical Non-Fiction, True Crime, Social History

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Injustice, Survival
