Synopsis: 
Silas X. Floyd's Short Stories for Colored People Both Old and Young: Entertaining, Uplifting, Interesting is a collection of captivating tales that will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, humor, and thought-provoking moments. From the heartwarming adventures of a mischievous young boy to the poignant reflections of an elderly woman, these stories celebrate the resilience, strength, and beauty of the African American experience. With each turn of the page, readers will be enthralled by the vivid characters, rich settings, and unexpected twists that make this collection a true gem.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, African American Literature

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Community, Heritage
