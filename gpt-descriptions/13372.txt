Synopsis: In 'The Gloved Hand' by Stevenson, Burton Egbert, a mysterious glove with a dark secret falls into the hands of a young woman, setting off a chain of events that will test her courage and unravel a web of deceit. As she delves deeper into the glove's history, she discovers that it holds the power to change lives, but at a great cost. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it consumes her. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the consequences of curiosity
