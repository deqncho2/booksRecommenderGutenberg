Synopsis: In "Polite Conversation in Three Dialogues," Swift takes us on a witty and satirical journey through the world of polite society. Through three engaging dialogues, he explores the absurdity and pretentiousness of social interactions, shedding light on the hypocrisy and shallow nature of polite conversation. With his sharp and biting humor, Swift exposes the ridiculousness of societal norms and challenges the reader to question the true value of politeness.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Social Criticism

Themes: Hypocrisy, Social Conventions, Satire of Manners
