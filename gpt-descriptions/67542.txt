Synopsis: In 'The Mouthpiece of Zitu' by J. U. Giesy, a young lawyer named John Dene is thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he is tasked with defending a notorious criminal. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, secret societies, and ancient prophecies that could change the course of history. With his life on the line, John must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Redemption, Power
