Synopsis: In "The Fall River Tragedy: A History of the Borden Murders," Edwin H. Porter delves into the infamous case of the Borden murders that shocked the small town of Fall River, Massachusetts, in the late 19th century. Through meticulous research and gripping storytelling, Porter unravels the mysterious events surrounding the brutal deaths of Andrew and Abby Borden, leaving readers captivated by the twists and turns of this real-life crime.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Non-fiction, Mystery

Themes: Murder, Family Dynamics, Justice and Innocence
