Synopsis: In 'Dennison Grant: A Novel of To-day', the enigmatic protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue as he navigates the dark underbelly of society. With his sharp intellect and relentless determination, Dennison Grant uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of his world. As he delves deeper into the shadows, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will test his morality and loyalty. This gripping tale of suspense and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Morality and ethics, Self-discovery
