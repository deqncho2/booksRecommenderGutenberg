Synopsis: In "The Measurement of Intelligence," Lewis M. Terman presents a comprehensive guide to the Standard revision and extension of the Binet-Simon intelligence scale. This groundbreaking work delves into the intricacies of measuring intelligence, providing an explanation that is both enlightening and practical. Terman explores the various factors that contribute to intelligence and offers valuable insights into how to effectively assess and understand this complex trait. With a focus on the Binet-Simon scale, Terman's work serves as an indispensable resource for psychologists, educators, and anyone interested in unraveling the mysteries of human intelligence.

Genres: Psychology, Education, Research

Themes: Intelligence assessment, Cognitive abilities, Psychological testing
