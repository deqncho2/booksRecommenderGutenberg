Synopsis: In 'The Great Horse; or, The War Horse: from the time of the Roman Invasion till its development into the Shire Horse,' Sir Walter Gilbey takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the magnificent horse. From its role as a powerful warhorse during the Roman invasion to its transformation into the iconic Shire Horse, this book delves into the fascinating evolution of this majestic creature. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Gilbey paints a vivid picture of the horse's significance in various cultures and its impact on human civilization. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Animal studies, Equestrian literature

Themes: Evolution of horse breeds, Human-animal relationship, Historical significance of horses
