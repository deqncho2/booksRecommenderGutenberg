Synopsis: 

In the 'New National Fourth Reader', Barnes and Hawkes take readers on an exhilarating literary journey filled with captivating stories, thought-provoking essays, and inspiring poems. From tales of adventure and mystery to heartwarming narratives of friendship and perseverance, this reader offers a diverse collection of engaging texts that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With each story carefully selected to both entertain and educate, readers will be transported to different worlds, encountering fascinating characters and exploring a wide range of emotions. Whether it's unraveling a thrilling mystery or delving into the depths of human nature, the 'New National Fourth Reader' promises to captivate readers of all ages and ignite a love for literature.

Genres: Fiction, Non-fiction, Poetry

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Perseverance, Mystery, Human nature
