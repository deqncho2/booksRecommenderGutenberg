Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious carpenter arrives, offering his unique skills to the townspeople. As he crafts beautiful rustic furniture, he unknowingly weaves together the lives of the community, revealing hidden secrets and igniting unexpected romances. But as his work progresses, a dark presence from his past threatens to unravel everything he has built, forcing him to confront his own demons and protect those he has come to care for. With its blend of heartwarming moments and gripping suspense, 'Rustic Carpentry' takes readers on a captivating journey through love, redemption, and the power of craftsmanship.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Community, Secrets, Redemption, Personal growth
