Synopsis: 
When the Bunker family arrives at Grandma Bell's house for a summer vacation, they have no idea what adventures await them. From mysterious noises in the attic to secret passageways, the six Bunker siblings find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected surprises. As they uncover hidden treasures and unravel family secrets, the Bunkers learn the importance of teamwork, bravery, and the power of family bonds.

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family, Adventure, Discovery
