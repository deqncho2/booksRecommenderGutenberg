Synopsis:
In the small town of Evesham, a mysterious event takes place that leaves the residents bewildered and questioning their reality. As the townspeople grapple with the strange occurrences, a young woman named Alice finds herself at the center of it all, uncovering secrets that have long been buried. With each revelation, the line between truth and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leading Alice on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and the search for answers. Evesham is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma that lies within the town's dark past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Secrets and Hidden Truths, Self-Discovery
