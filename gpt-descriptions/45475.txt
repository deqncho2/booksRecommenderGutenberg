Synopsis: In 'The Bible Unveiled', Mangasarian fearlessly delves into the origins and contradictions of the Bible, challenging long-held beliefs and shedding light on its historical context. With meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, he uncovers the human hands behind the sacred text, unraveling the myths and legends that have shaped religious traditions for centuries. This eye-opening exploration invites readers to question and reevaluate their understanding of one of the most influential books in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Criticism, Historical Analysis

Themes: Religious skepticism, Historical context, Mythology, Human authorship
