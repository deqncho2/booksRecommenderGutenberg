Synopsis:
In the remote hills of West Virginia, a young lawyer named John Sherwood finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous feud between two families. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the hills, he uncovers a web of dark pasts, hidden motives, and long-held grudges. With his own life at stake, Sherwood must navigate the treacherous terrain of the hills and confront the sinister forces that dwell within.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Family feuds, Secrets and hidden motives, Survival
