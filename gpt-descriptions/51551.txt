Synopsis: 

In "The Russian Campaign, April to August, 1915: Being the Second Volume of 'Field Notes from the Russian Front'," author Stanley Washburn takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of the Russian campaign during World War I. Through vivid and detailed field notes, Washburn provides a firsthand account of the intense battles, strategic maneuvers, and the resilience of the soldiers amidst the harsh conditions of war. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the relentless pursuit of victory, the complexities of military strategy, and the human stories that emerge from the front lines. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, resilience and determination in the face of adversity
