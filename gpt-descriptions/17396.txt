Synopsis: 
When young Mary Lennox is sent to live with her uncle in a gloomy mansion on the Yorkshire moors, she discovers a hidden garden that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of her own past. As Mary begins to tend to the neglected garden, she not only transforms the once barren land but also the lives of those around her. With the help of newfound friends, Mary embarks on a journey of healing, friendship, and the power of nature's rejuvenation.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
The transformative power of nature, Healing and growth, Friendship and connection
