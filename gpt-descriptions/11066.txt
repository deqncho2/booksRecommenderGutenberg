Synopsis: In "Graf von Loeben and the Legend of Lorelei," Porterfield takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Germany. Set in the early 19th century, the story follows the adventures of Graf von Loeben, a young nobleman who becomes entangled in a mysterious legend surrounding the mythical Lorelei. As he delves deeper into the legend, Graf von Loeben finds himself caught between love, duty, and the supernatural forces that threaten to unravel his world. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this tale of romance, intrigue, and folklore will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Myth and Legend, Fate and Destiny
