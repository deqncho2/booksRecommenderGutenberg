Synopsis: "Downright Fighting: The Story of Cowpens" by Fleming, Thomas J. is a gripping historical account that takes readers back to the American Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the pivotal Battle of Cowpens, the book follows the courageous soldiers who fought for freedom and the strategic maneuvers that shaped the outcome of the war. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Fleming brings to life the intense battles, the personal sacrifices, and the triumphs that ultimately led to American independence.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American Revolution

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Strategy, Independence
