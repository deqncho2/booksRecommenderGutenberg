Synopsis: 

Step into the captivating world of Randolph Caldecott, a renowned artist whose early art career is explored in this personal memoir by Henry Blackburn. Delve into the fascinating journey of a talented and innovative illustrator as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of his artistic pursuits. From his humble beginnings to his rise to fame, this memoir offers a unique glimpse into the life and work of a true artistic genius. 

Genres: Biography, Art, Memoir

Themes: Artistic development, Creativity, Success and recognition
