Synopsis: 
'Across China on Foot' is an exhilarating travel memoir that takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the vast and diverse landscapes of China. Edwin John Dingle, a British adventurer, embarks on an audacious mission to traverse the entire country on foot, immersing himself in the rich culture, encountering fascinating characters, and facing numerous challenges along the way. From bustling cities to remote villages, from breathtaking mountains to treacherous terrains, Dingle's captivating narrative paints a vivid picture of his awe-inspiring expedition, offering a unique perspective on China's history, people, and natural wonders.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Perseverance, Human connection
