Synopsis: In 'Practical School Discipline: Applied Methods, Part 2', author Ray Coppock delves deeper into the realm of effective discipline strategies for educators. This comprehensive guide offers practical techniques and real-life examples to help teachers create a positive and productive learning environment. From addressing disruptive behavior to fostering student engagement, Coppock provides invaluable insights and step-by-step instructions that empower educators to handle disciplinary challenges with confidence. With a focus on empathy, communication, and proactive approaches, this book equips teachers with the tools they need to promote a harmonious classroom atmosphere and support students' personal growth.

Genres: Education, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Classroom management, Discipline strategies, Student engagement, Teacher-student relationships
