Synopsis: In 'Cecilia of the Pink Roses' by Haviland-Taylor, Katharine, a young woman named Cecilia finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love when she stumbles upon a hidden garden filled with pink roses. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the garden, Cecilia must navigate the complexities of her own heart and the consequences of her choices. With each petal of the pink roses, the story unfolds, revealing a captivating tale of passion, sacrifice, and the power of love.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets, Self-discovery
