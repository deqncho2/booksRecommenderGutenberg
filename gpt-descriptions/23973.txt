Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Christy's Bridge Party' by Sara Ware Bassett, a seemingly innocent bridge party turns into a thrilling mystery when a valuable necklace goes missing. As the guests try to solve the puzzle, secrets are revealed, suspicions arise, and unexpected alliances are formed. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating story will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Deception, Trust, Friendship, Greed
