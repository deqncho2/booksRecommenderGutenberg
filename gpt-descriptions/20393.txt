Synopsis: 
'A Short History of English Printing, 1476-1898' takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of printing in England, from its humble beginnings to its significant impact on society. Henry R. Plomer delves into the fascinating stories of the pioneers, the challenges they faced, and the innovations that shaped the printing industry. From the invention of the printing press to the rise of newspapers and the spread of literacy, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the transformative power of printing in English history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Innovation, Communication, Cultural Transformation
