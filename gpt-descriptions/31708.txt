Synopsis: In 'The Pond' by Carl Ewald, a seemingly ordinary pond becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of mystery and enchantment. As the story unfolds, a young boy discovers a hidden world beneath the water's surface, filled with extraordinary creatures and unexpected adventures. With each visit to the pond, the boy's imagination is ignited, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the unknown. This enchanting tale will transport readers to a world where the ordinary becomes extraordinary, and where the power of imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Discovery, Self-discovery, Wonder
