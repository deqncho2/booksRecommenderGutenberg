Synopsis: In 'The Fairy-Faith in Celtic Countries', Evans-Wentz delves into the enchanting world of Celtic folklore and explores the beliefs and experiences surrounding fairies. Through interviews with locals and extensive research, he uncovers a captivating realm where humans and fairies coexist, and where encounters with these mystical beings can have profound effects on individuals and communities. From ancient legends to modern encounters, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the mysterious and magical world of the fairy-faith.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: Belief systems, Supernatural encounters, Cultural traditions
