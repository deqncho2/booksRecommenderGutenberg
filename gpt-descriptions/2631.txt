Synopsis: In "Mr. Gladstone and Genesis: Essay #5 from 'Science and Hebrew Tradition'," Thomas Henry Huxley engages in a thought-provoking exploration of the relationship between science and religion. Through a captivating analysis of the biblical story of Genesis, Huxley challenges conventional beliefs and presents a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning their own understanding of the world. 

Genres: Essay, Philosophy, Science, Religion

Themes: Science and religion, Evolution, Biblical interpretation, Skepticism
