Synopsis: In 'The Red Mouse: A Mystery Romance' by Osborne, William Hamilton, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and passion when he is hired to investigate the mysterious disappearance of a wealthy socialite. As he delves deeper into the case, he discovers a hidden world of betrayal, forbidden love, and dangerous alliances. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, the detective must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems, and where the line between love and deception becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Deception
