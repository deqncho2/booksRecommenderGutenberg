Synopsis: In a world where words are strictly regulated, a young rebel discovers a hidden underground society that celebrates the power of nonsense. As she delves deeper into this mysterious world, she uncovers a shocking truth that could change everything she thought she knew. With danger lurking at every corner, she must decide whether to conform to society's rules or embrace the liberating chaos of nonsense.

Genres: Dystopian, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Freedom of expression, Rebellion against authority, Power of language
