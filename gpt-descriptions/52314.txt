Synopsis: 
'Nooks and Corners: being the companion volume to 'From Kitchen to Garret'' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden corners and secret spaces of everyday life. From the bustling streets of London to the quiet solitude of country cottages, this collection of stories delves into the mysteries and intrigues that lie within the nooks and corners of our world. With each tale, the author weaves a web of suspense, romance, and unexpected twists, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within these hidden spaces.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden spaces, Intrigue, Human connections
