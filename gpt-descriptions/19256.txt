Synopsis: 
In 'Georgie' by Abbott, Jacob, we follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Georgie, who finds himself in a series of unexpected and thrilling adventures. From encounters with mysterious strangers to uncovering hidden secrets, Georgie's life takes an exhilarating turn as he navigates through a world filled with danger and intrigue. As he faces numerous challenges and tests his courage, Georgie's resilience and determination shine through, making for a truly captivating and heartwarming tale.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Discovery
