Synopsis: In 'The Root of All Evil' by Fletcher, J.S., a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a wealthy family and a mysterious death. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that threaten to tear the family apart. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit to uncover the truth and bring the culprit to justice. This gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma that lies at the root of all evil.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Family dynamics, Greed, Justice
