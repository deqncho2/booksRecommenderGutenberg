Synopsis: 

In "Ashton-Kirk, Criminologist" by John T. McIntyre, readers are introduced to the brilliant and enigmatic detective, Ashton-Kirk. Known for his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, Ashton-Kirk is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a mysterious disappearance and a series of baffling crimes. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, Ashton-Kirk's deductive prowess is put to the test, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers eager for more.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Intrigue, Uncovering the Truth
