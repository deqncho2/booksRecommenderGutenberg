Synopsis:
In 'Abroad at Home: American Ramblings, Observations, and Adventures of Julian Street', Julian Street takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and vibrant cities of America. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene beauty of the Grand Canyon, Street's vivid descriptions and humorous anecdotes bring each destination to life. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, immerses himself in local customs, and uncovers hidden gems that even seasoned travelers may have overlooked. With his keen eye for detail and knack for storytelling, Street invites readers to experience the thrill of exploration and the joy of discovering the extraordinary in the ordinary.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Humor

Themes: Adventure, Cultural exploration, Appreciation of the everyday
