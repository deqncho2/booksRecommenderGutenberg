Synopsis: 
In 'A Fortunate Term' by Angela Brazil, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Daphne as she embarks on a new chapter of her life at a prestigious boarding school. As Daphne navigates the challenges of making friends, facing academic pressures, and discovering her own strengths and passions, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that could change everything. With unexpected twists and turns, this coming-of-age tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the school's hidden past.

Genres: 
Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, School Story

Themes: 
Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Resilience
