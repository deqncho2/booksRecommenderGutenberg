Synopsis: In 'Circle of Confusion' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking device that has the potential to change the world. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, it sets off a dangerous chain of events that threatens to unravel society as we know it. As the protagonist races against time to reclaim his invention, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Trust and betrayal
