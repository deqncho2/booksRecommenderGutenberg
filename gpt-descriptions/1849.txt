Synopsis: In 'The Yellow Crayon' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious yellow crayon becomes the catalyst for a thrilling adventure. When a young artist discovers that anything he draws with the yellow crayon comes to life, he embarks on a journey filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected consequences. As he grapples with the power of his creation, he must navigate a world where imagination and reality blur, and where the line between good and evil becomes increasingly blurred. With each stroke of the crayon, the artist's life is forever changed, leading to a climactic showdown that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Power of imagination, Consequences of actions, Blurring of reality and fiction
