Synopsis: 

'The Art of Glass-Blowing: Plain Instruction for the Making of Chemical and Philosophical Instruments Which are Formed of Glass' by Danger, T.-P. is a captivating guide that delves into the intricate world of glass-blowing. This book takes readers on a fascinating journey through the process of creating various chemical and philosophical instruments using glass. From the basic techniques to the advanced methods, Danger, T.-P. provides clear and concise instructions, making it accessible to both beginners and experienced glass-blowers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Crafts and Hobbies, Science

Themes: Artistry, Scientific Inquiry, Skill Development
