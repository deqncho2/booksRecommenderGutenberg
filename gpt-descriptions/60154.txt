Synopsis: In 'The Red Cross Girls in the British Trenches', follow the courageous journey of a group of young American women who volunteer as nurses during World War I. As they navigate the treacherous trenches of war-torn Europe, they encounter danger, heartbreak, and unexpected friendships. With their unwavering dedication to saving lives, these brave women must confront the harsh realities of war while finding strength in each other.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Resilience
