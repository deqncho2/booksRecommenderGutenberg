Synopsis:
In 'London in the Jacobite times, Volume II', Dr. Doran takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant and tumultuous city of London during the Jacobite era. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, he unveils the hidden secrets, political intrigues, and scandalous affairs that shaped the lives of both the common people and the aristocracy. From the bustling streets to the opulent ballrooms, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the inhabitants of London during this pivotal period in history.

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Biography, Social history

Themes:
Political intrigue, Social hierarchy, Cultural transformation
