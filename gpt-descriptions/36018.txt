Synopsis: 
When twelve-year-old Lucy arrives at the prestigious boarding school, she never expects to be roommates with the mischievous Tucker twins, Max and Mia. As they navigate the challenges of school life together, Lucy finds herself caught up in their whirlwind of pranks, secret adventures, and unexpected friendships. From sneaking out after curfew to unraveling the mysteries of the school's hidden past, Lucy's time at boarding school becomes an unforgettable journey of self-discovery and the power of true friendship.

Genres: Middle-grade fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Coming-of-age, Identity, Adventure
