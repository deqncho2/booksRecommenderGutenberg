Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's New Comedian; Or, The Rise of a Star' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the captivating journey of Frank Merriwell as he discovers a hidden talent for comedy. When a chance encounter leads him to a struggling vaudeville theater, Frank seizes the opportunity to showcase his comedic skills and quickly becomes the talk of the town. As his fame rises, Frank must navigate the challenges of show business, including jealous rivals, unexpected romances, and the pressure to maintain his newfound success. Will Frank's rise to stardom be everything he dreamed of, or will the harsh realities of the entertainment industry threaten to bring him down?

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Pursuing one's passion, Overcoming adversity, The price of fame
