Synopsis: 
In 'Bird Neighbors', Neltje Blanchan takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of birds commonly found in our own backyards. With vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, she introduces us to one hundred and fifty different species, revealing their unique characteristics, behaviors, and habitats. From the melodious songs of the warblers to the graceful flight of the hummingbirds, this book offers a delightful exploration of the avian wonders that surround us every day.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Field Guide

Themes: Wildlife appreciation, Environmental awareness, Biodiversity
