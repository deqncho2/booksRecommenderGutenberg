Synopsis: 
In 'Practical Basketry' by Gill, Anna A., readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of basket weaving. Through vivid descriptions and step-by-step instructions, the author introduces a variety of techniques and materials, from traditional to contemporary, that will inspire both beginners and experienced weavers alike. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in the beauty and intricacy of this ancient craft, discovering the endless possibilities of creating functional and artistic baskets. From the humblest of materials to the most intricate designs, 'Practical Basketry' is a testament to the power of creativity and the joy of working with one's hands.

Genres: Crafts, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Tradition, Artistry
