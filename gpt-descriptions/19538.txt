Synopsis: In 'The Broncho Rider Boys with Funston at Vera Cruz: Or, Upholding the Honor of the Stars and Stripes', the adventurous trio of Larry, Walt, and Frank find themselves caught up in the midst of the Mexican Revolution. When they witness a daring act of bravery by General Funston, they are inspired to join the fight and uphold the honor of the Stars and Stripes. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes and face dangerous enemies, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty to protect their country and make a difference in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Friendship, Loyalty
