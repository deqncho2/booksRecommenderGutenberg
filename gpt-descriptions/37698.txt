Synopsis: In 'Dawn of the Morning' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Dawn Rensselaer finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of high society, Dawn must confront her own values and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With secrets, romance, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social class, Identity, Morality
