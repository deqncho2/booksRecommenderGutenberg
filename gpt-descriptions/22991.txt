Synopsis: In 'Boy Scouts Mysterious Signal; Or, Perils of the Black Bear Patrol' by G. Harvey Ralphson, a group of Boy Scouts embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious signal that leads them into the heart of danger. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected obstacles, the Black Bear Patrol must rely on their skills, teamwork, and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets behind the signal and save the day. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth behind the enigmatic signal.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Problem-solving, Exploration
