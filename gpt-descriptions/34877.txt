Synopsis: In "Chats on Old Furniture: A Practical Guide for Collectors," Arthur Hayden takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of antique furniture. From the opulent halls of European castles to the humble abodes of everyday people, Hayden unveils the hidden stories and secrets behind each piece of furniture, shedding light on their historical significance and craftsmanship. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, this practical guide offers invaluable tips for collectors, helping them navigate the intricate world of antique furniture and make informed decisions. Whether you're a seasoned collector or simply intrigued by the allure of old furniture, this book will transport you to a bygone era, where each piece holds a tale waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Antiques

Themes: Historical significance, Craftsmanship, Collecting
