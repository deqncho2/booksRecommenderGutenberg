Synopsis:
In the vibrant world of 17th-century France, Cyrano de Bergerac, a brilliant poet and swordsman, finds himself caught in a web of unrequited love. Blessed with a razor-sharp wit and an extraordinary gift for words, Cyrano is tragically burdened by his own physical appearance. When he falls for the beautiful Roxane, he is faced with a dilemma: should he reveal his true feelings, or help his handsome but dim-witted friend win her heart? As Cyrano navigates the complexities of love, honor, and self-sacrifice, he becomes a symbol of courage and poetic brilliance, captivating readers with his unforgettable journey.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Unrequited love, Appearance vs. inner beauty, Honor and self-sacrifice
