Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Mary' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious estate. As she delves deeper into the history of the property, Mary uncovers a hidden past that holds the key to her own identity and the fate of those she holds dear. With danger lurking around every corner, Mary must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and unexpected alliances to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption
