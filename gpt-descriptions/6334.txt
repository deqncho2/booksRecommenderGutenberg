Synopsis: In 'Sara, a Princess: The Story of a Noble Girl' by Fannie E. Newberry, readers are transported to a world of royalty, secrets, and unexpected twists. Sara, a young princess, finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as she uncovers hidden truths about her family and the kingdom she is destined to rule. With the help of a mysterious stranger, Sara must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Sara's noble lineage.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Power, Betrayal
