Synopsis: In 'The Story of Brussels' by Gilliat-Smith, Ernest, a young artist named Victor embarks on a journey to the vibrant city of Brussels, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the city's secrets, Victor discovers a hidden world of art, love, and betrayal that will challenge his perceptions and change his life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Art and creativity, Love and relationships, Betrayal and deception
