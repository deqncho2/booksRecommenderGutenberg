Synopsis: In 'Mavericks' by Raine, William MacLeod, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes them to the heart of a hidden world. When a mysterious artifact is discovered, they must unravel its secrets and confront their own inner demons. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous creatures, they discover that their destinies are intertwined in ways they never imagined. With high-stakes action and unexpected twists, 'Mavericks' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Destiny, Friendship, Redemption
