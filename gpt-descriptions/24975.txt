Synopsis: In 'The Gift Bearer' by Charles L. Fontenay, a young archaeologist named David discovers an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and betrayal. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, David must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth behind the artifact before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Betrayal, Quest for truth
