Synopsis: 
In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 04: Little Journeys to the Homes of Eminent Painters', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and homes of some of the most renowned painters in history. From the vibrant studios of Michelangelo and Rembrandt to the serene landscapes that inspired Monet and Turner, this volume offers a unique glimpse into the private worlds of these artistic geniuses. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Hubbard paints a vivid picture of the environments that shaped these great painters and the profound impact they had on the art world. 

Genres: Biography, Art, History
Themes: Artistic inspiration, Creativity, Influence of environment
