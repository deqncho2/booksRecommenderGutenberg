Synopsis: In 'The Silver Bullet' by Fergus Hume, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths, all seemingly connected to a legendary silver bullet. As the local detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of legends and folklore
