Synopsis: 

'Within the Maze: A Novel, Vol. 1 (of 2)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate web of secrets and mysteries that surround the lives of its intriguing characters. Set in the enchanting backdrop of Victorian England, this gripping tale follows the lives of two young women, Amelia and Charlotte, as they navigate the labyrinthine maze of society's expectations, forbidden desires, and hidden pasts. As their paths intertwine, they find themselves drawn deeper into a world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth that lies within the maze.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Society and Expectations, Forbidden Love
