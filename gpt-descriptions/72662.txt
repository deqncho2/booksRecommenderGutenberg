Synopsis: In 'A Time to Die' by Calin, Harold, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a series of inexplicable events that disrupt the lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople grapple with the stranger's presence, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and secrets, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity, Redemption
