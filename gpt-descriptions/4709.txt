Synopsis: 
Montgomery Brewster, a young and carefree man, unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune from his deceased uncle. However, there is a catch: in order to receive his full inheritance of seven million dollars, Brewster must spend one million dollars within a year, with nothing to show for it except the clothes on his back. As he embarks on this seemingly impossible task, Brewster finds himself caught in a whirlwind of extravagant spending, outrageous schemes, and unexpected challenges. Will he succeed in squandering his fortune or will he discover that money isn't everything?

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Wealth and materialism, the value of money, personal growth and self-discovery
