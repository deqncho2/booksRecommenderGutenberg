Synopsis: In "The Sirdar's Oath: A Tale of the North-West Frontier," Bertram Mitford takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous landscapes of the North-West Frontier. Set during the late 19th century, the story follows Captain Ronald Standish, a British officer stationed in India, as he navigates the complexities of tribal politics, espionage, and personal vendettas. When a mysterious oath binds him to a local Sirdar, Captain Standish finds himself torn between duty, honor, and the pursuit of justice. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the captain must make difficult choices that will shape the fate of the region and his own destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Cultural Conflict, Duty, Justice
