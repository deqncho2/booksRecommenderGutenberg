Synopsis: In "The extraordinary confessions of Diana Please" by Capes, Bernard, we are introduced to Diana Please, a seemingly ordinary woman with an extraordinary secret. As she navigates through her mundane life, she becomes entangled in a web of lies, deceit, and unexpected twists. With each confession, Diana's true nature is slowly revealed, leaving readers questioning their own perceptions of reality. This gripping tale will keep you on the edge of your seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the shocking truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Perception
