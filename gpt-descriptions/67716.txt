Synopsis: In 'First Theater in America: When was the drama first introduced in America? An inquiry, including a consideration of the objections that have been made to the stage,' Charles P. Daly delves into the fascinating history of the first theater in America and explores the objections that were raised against this new form of entertainment. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Daly uncovers the origins of drama in America and the challenges it faced in its early days. From the first performances to the controversies surrounding the stage, this inquiry offers a captivating exploration of the birth of theater in America.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Cultural history

Themes: The origins of theater, Cultural resistance to new forms of entertainment
