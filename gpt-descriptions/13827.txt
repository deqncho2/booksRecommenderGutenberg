Synopsis: 

In 'Fields of Victory' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous war. As she navigates the challenges and dangers of the battlefield, she discovers a strength within herself that she never knew existed. Alongside her comrades, Emily must face not only the physical perils of war but also the emotional and moral dilemmas that arise in such a chaotic environment. With unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching sacrifices, 'Fields of Victory' takes readers on a gripping journey through the trials and triumphs of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Identity, Sacrifice, Resilience
