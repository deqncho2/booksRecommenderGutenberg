Synopsis: In 'The Book of Saints and Friendly Beasts' by Abbie Farwell Brown, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of saints and their extraordinary encounters with animals. From the courageous St. Francis of Assisi to the humble St. Martin of Tours, each saint's story is beautifully intertwined with the loyalty, wisdom, and compassion of the friendly beasts they encounter along the way. Through these enchanting tales, readers will discover the profound connections between humans and animals, and the transformative power of faith and love.

Genres: Children's Literature, Folklore, Christian Literature

Themes: Faith, Compassion, Animal-Human Connection
