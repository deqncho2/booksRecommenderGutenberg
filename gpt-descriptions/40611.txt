Synopsis: 

In 'Prince Charlie' by Delannoy, Burford, a young prince finds himself thrust into a world of danger and intrigue as he navigates a treacherous path to reclaim his rightful throne. With his life hanging in the balance, he must rely on his wit, courage, and unexpected allies to outsmart his enemies and restore peace to his kingdom. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, the prince's journey becomes a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Intrigue

Themes: Power and Leadership, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery
