Synopsis: In 'The Most Extraordinary Trial of William Palmer, for the Rugeley Poisonings, which lasted Twelve Days', the reader is taken on a gripping journey through a sensational trial that captivated Victorian England. As the trial unfolds over twelve intense days, the shocking details of the Rugeley Poisonings are revealed, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning the true nature of justice and the depths of human depravity.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Non-Fiction, Legal Drama

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Morality and Ethics, Manipulation and Deception
