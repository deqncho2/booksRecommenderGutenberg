Synopsis: In 'Rivals for the Team: A Story of School Life and Football' by Ralph Henry Barbour, two talented athletes find themselves competing for a spot on the school football team. As they navigate the challenges of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth, their passion for the game is put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and intense competition, this gripping story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover who will emerge victorious.

Genres: Sports, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
