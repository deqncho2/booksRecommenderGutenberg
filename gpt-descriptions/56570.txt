Synopsis: 

In "Breaking into the Movies," Emerson and Loos take readers on a captivating journey into the glamorous and cutthroat world of Hollywood. Through a series of anecdotes and insider tips, they reveal the secrets to making it big in the film industry. From auditions to screenwriting, from navigating studio politics to dealing with eccentric directors, this book provides a fascinating behind-the-scenes look at the challenges and triumphs of pursuing a career in movies. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Entertainment

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Hollywood industry, Success and failure
