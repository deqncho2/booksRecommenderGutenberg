Synopsis: 
In the midst of the French Revolution, three men find themselves entangled in a web of loyalty, sacrifice, and redemption. As the country is torn apart by violence and chaos, a young royalist soldier, a revolutionary leader, and a devoted priest embark on separate journeys that will test their beliefs and challenge their understanding of humanity. Their paths converge in a small coastal town, where their fates become intertwined in a gripping tale of love, honor, and the pursuit of justice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'Ninety-Three' explores the depths of human nature and the complexities of moral choices in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Redemption, Morality, Human nature
