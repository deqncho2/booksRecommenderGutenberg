Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Joan of Arc', Ida M. Tarbell takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most iconic figures. This issue, Serial No. 98, delves into the fascinating story of Joan of Arc, a young peasant girl who defied societal norms and led armies to fight for her country. Through vivid storytelling and historical analysis, Tarbell explores the trials and triumphs of Joan's life, shedding light on her unwavering faith, her strategic military tactics, and the impact she had on the course of history. Discover the extraordinary tale of a young woman who defied all odds and became a symbol of courage and inspiration.

Genres: Historical biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Faith, Gender roles, Historical significance
