Synopsis: In 'Facing the World' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., a young man named Harry Vane finds himself thrust into the bustling streets of New York City after a series of unfortunate events. With determination and a strong work ethic, Harry navigates the challenges of the city, encountering both friends and foes along the way. As he strives to make a name for himself, Harry discovers the true meaning of perseverance and the power of self-belief.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Hard work, Resilience, Self-discovery
