Synopsis: 
In 'Bashful Fifteen' by L. T. Meade, a young girl named Mabel finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires. As she navigates the challenges of adolescence, Mabel discovers a hidden talent that could change her life forever. With the help of unexpected friendships and a newfound confidence, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. But will Mabel have the courage to break free from societal constraints and embrace her true self?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Society's expectations
