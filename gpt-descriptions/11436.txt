Synopsis: 

In 'Stories by American Authors, Volume 1', readers will be captivated by a collection of diverse and compelling tales penned by some of America's most renowned authors. From the hauntingly mysterious to the heartwarming and humorous, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of American storytelling. Taylor, Bayard, Davis, Rebecca Harding, Bunner, H. C., Matthews, Brander, and Webster, Albert each contribute their unique voices, offering a range of narratives that explore the complexities of human nature, the power of love and friendship, and the ever-present struggle between tradition and progress. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Human nature, Love and friendship, Tradition and progress
