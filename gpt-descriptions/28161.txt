Synopsis: 
In 'The Master Mummer' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a renowned actor finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is mistaken for a notorious criminal. As he delves deeper into the dangerous world of espionage and deception, he must use his acting skills to outwit his enemies and uncover the truth. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Espionage
