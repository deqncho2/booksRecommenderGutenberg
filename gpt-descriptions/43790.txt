Synopsis: 
'The Book of Cats: A Chit-chat Chronicle of Feline Facts and Fancies, Legendary, Lyrical, Medical, Mirthful and Miscellaneous' is a delightful collection of stories, anecdotes, and facts about our beloved feline friends. From ancient legends to modern-day medical marvels, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of cats. With its lyrical prose and humorous tales, it offers a unique blend of entertainment and education that will leave cat lovers purring with delight.

Genres: Non-fiction, Anthology, Humor

Themes: Cats, Legends, Medicine, Humor, History
