Synopsis: In the bustling streets of New York City, a young boot-black named Dick strives to rise above his impoverished circumstances. With determination and resourcefulness, he navigates the challenges of street life, encountering both friends and foes along the way. As Dick's story unfolds, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the trials and triumphs of a young boy determined to make a better life for himself.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Realistic fiction, Adventure

Themes: Social mobility, Perseverance, Friendship, Self-improvement
