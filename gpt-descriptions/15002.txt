Synopsis: 
In 'All Aboard; or, Life on the Lake: A Sequel to "The Boat Club"', the adventurous Boat Club boys are back, ready to embark on another thrilling summer on the lake. This time, they find themselves facing new challenges and unexpected dangers that test their courage and friendship. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter mysterious characters, the boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they be able to conquer the perils of the lake and emerge victorious once again?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience
