Synopsis:
In 'Titian' by S. L. Bensusan, a mysterious painting holds the power to unlock a hidden secret, leading a young art enthusiast on a thrilling journey through the art world of Renaissance Italy. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic life of the renowned artist Titian, he uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and passion that spans centuries. With each clue he uncovers, the protagonist becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and art blurs. Will he be able to solve the puzzle before it consumes him?

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Art

Themes:
Artistic obsession, Secrets and hidden truths, The power of art
