Synopsis: In "Abe Lincoln Gets His Chance" by Cavanah, Frances, a young Abraham Lincoln finds himself in a small town, yearning for a chance to prove himself. When an unexpected opportunity arises, he must navigate the challenges and obstacles that come his way, testing his determination and resilience. Will Abe Lincoln seize his chance and make a lasting impact on the world?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Self-discovery
