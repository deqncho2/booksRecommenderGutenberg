Synopsis: In 'Madame Midas' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious woman named Madame Midas arrives in the goldfields of Australia, captivating the attention of the locals with her beauty and enigmatic past. As she becomes entangled in the world of gold mining, Madame Midas must navigate treacherous alliances, deceit, and the allure of wealth. With her cunning and determination, she sets out on a thrilling journey that will test her resilience and challenge societal norms. Will Madame Midas find fortune or fall victim to the dangers lurking in the shadows of the goldfields?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Power, Identity, Gender Roles
