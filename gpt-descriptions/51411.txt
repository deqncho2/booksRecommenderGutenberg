Synopsis: In 'The Campaign in Russian Poland' by Percy Cross Standing, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Polish-Russian conflict. The story follows a group of brave soldiers as they navigate the treacherous landscapes and face the horrors of war. Amidst the chaos, personal relationships are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the characters must confront their own inner demons. As the campaign unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, secrets are revealed, and the true cost of war becomes painfully clear. This gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Identity, Conflict
