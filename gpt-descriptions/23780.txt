Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old New York', we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Emily as she navigates the bustling streets of 19th century New York City. From encounters with street vendors and visits to the grand Central Park, to unexpected friendships and heartwarming moments, Emily's adventures offer a delightful glimpse into the vibrant tapestry of old New York. As she immerses herself in the city's rich history and diverse culture, Emily discovers the power of resilience, the importance of family, and the beauty of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Family, Cultural Diversity
