Synopsis:
In the remote jungles of Borneo, a brilliant scientist named Dr. von Horn conducts a secret experiment to create the perfect human being. However, his creation, known as Number Thirteen, turns out to be far from perfect. As Number Thirteen struggles to understand his own existence and grapples with his monstrous nature, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and deadly danger. With the fate of those around him hanging in the balance, Number Thirteen must navigate a treacherous world where humanity and monstrosity blur together.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes:
Identity, Morality, Nature vs. Nurture
