Synopsis: In 'The Beasts of Tarzan', the legendary jungle hero, Tarzan, finds himself facing a new and dangerous adversary. Kidnapped by a group of bloodthirsty pirates, Tarzan's wife and son are taken to a remote island where they become pawns in a deadly game. Determined to rescue his family, Tarzan embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with treacherous landscapes, savage beasts, and unexpected allies. As he navigates through the perils of the jungle, Tarzan must tap into his primal instincts and unleash his inner beast to save those he loves.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Family, Heroism
