Synopsis: In "Old Put" the patriot, Frederick A. Ober takes us on a captivating journey through the American Revolution, following the life of a courageous and enigmatic figure known as "Old Put." From the battlefields of Lexington and Concord to the treacherous winter at Valley Forge, this historical novel immerses readers in the thrilling adventures and sacrifices of a true patriot. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. Will "Old Put" be able to rally his fellow soldiers and lead them to victory against the British forces? Prepare to be swept away by this gripping tale of bravery, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of the American Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice, Leadership
