Synopsis: 

"The History of the First United States Flag: and the Patriotism of Betsy Ross, the Immortal Heroine That Originated the First Flag of the Union" takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins of the iconic American flag. Written by J. Franklin Reigart, this historical account delves into the life of Betsy Ross, the legendary figure credited with creating the first flag of the United States. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Reigart uncovers the untold story behind the creation of this enduring symbol of American patriotism, shedding light on the remarkable life and contributions of Betsy Ross.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, American history, Women's contributions to society
