Synopsis: In 'The Vailan or annular theory', author Stephen Bowers presents an intriguing argument put forth by Prof. I. N. Vail, suggesting that our Earth once possessed a system of rings similar to Saturn. Bowers delves into the evidence and reasoning behind this theory, captivating readers with the possibility of a forgotten celestial phenomenon that once adorned our planet. As the plot unfolds, readers are taken on a journey of scientific exploration and discovery, uncovering the mysteries of our planet's past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Scientific exploration, Earth's history, Celestial phenomena
