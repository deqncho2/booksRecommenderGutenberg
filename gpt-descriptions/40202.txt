Synopsis: In 'The Annals of Ann' by Kate Trimble Sharber, readers are introduced to Ann, a young woman with a unique ability to see into the future. As Ann navigates her way through life, her visions become increasingly vivid and unsettling, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the search for her true purpose. Along the way, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces numerous challenges that test her strength and resilience. With each vision, Ann must grapple with the consequences of her choices and the impact they have on those around her. This captivating tale explores the power of foresight, the complexities of fate, and the indomitable spirit of one woman determined to shape her own destiny.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Fate and free will, Identity and self-discovery, Power of choice
