Synopsis: 
'Poems: Patriotic, Religious, Miscellaneous' by Ryan, Abram Joseph is a captivating collection of poems that explores various aspects of patriotism, religion, and other miscellaneous themes. From heartfelt tributes to the nation to profound reflections on faith and spirituality, this anthology takes readers on a poetic journey that evokes a range of emotions and leaves them pondering the deeper meaning of life. With its lyrical language and thought-provoking verses, this collection is sure to captivate poetry enthusiasts and ignite a sense of pride, introspection, and wonder.

Genres: Poetry, Patriotic Literature, Religious Literature, Miscellaneous Literature

Themes: Patriotism, Religion, Faith, Spirituality, Life's Meaning
