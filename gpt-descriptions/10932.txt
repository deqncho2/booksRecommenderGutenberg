Synopsis: In 'Over the Pass' by Frederick Palmer, a group of strangers find themselves thrown together on a treacherous journey through the rugged mountains. As they navigate the dangers of the wilderness, they must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true strength of the human spirit. With each step, the characters are tested physically and emotionally, leading to unexpected revelations and a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Courage, Redemption
