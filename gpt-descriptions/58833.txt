Synopsis: 

In 'Battery D First Rhode Island Light Artillery in the Civil War', author George C. Sumner takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a remarkable artillery unit during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Sumner brings to life the trials and triumphs of Battery D, offering a unique perspective on the war and the individuals who fought in it. From the chaos of battlefields to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this book provides a gripping account of the sacrifices made by these brave soldiers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, War's impact on individuals and society
