Synopsis: 
In 'Ginseng and Other Medicinal Plants', A. R. Harding takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of valuable medicinal plants. This comprehensive guide provides valuable information for both growers and collectors, offering insights into the cultivation, harvesting, and uses of various medicinal roots, barks, leaves, and more. From the elusive ginseng to lesser-known plants, Harding's book unveils the secrets of these natural wonders, captivating readers with fascinating anecdotes and practical advice. Whether you are a seasoned grower or simply curious about the healing power of plants, this book is sure to pique your interest and leave you eager to explore the world of medicinal plants.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Natural Medicine

Themes: Medicinal plants, Cultivation, Healing properties, Traditional medicine
