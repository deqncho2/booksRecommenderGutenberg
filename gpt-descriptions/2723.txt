Synopsis: In the small town of Tasajara, a mysterious family arrives, bringing with them a sense of intrigue and excitement. As the townspeople become enamored with the newcomers, secrets from the past begin to unravel, leading to unexpected consequences and a clash of cultures. With its richly drawn characters and a captivating storyline, 'A First Family of Tasajara' takes readers on a journey filled with love, betrayal, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Cultural clashes, Secrets, Love and betrayal
