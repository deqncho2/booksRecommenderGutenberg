Synopsis: In 'Next Door, Next World' by Robert Donald Locke, a seemingly ordinary suburban neighborhood becomes the backdrop for a series of mysterious and unsettling events. When a new family moves in next door, the protagonist's life takes an unexpected turn as they uncover dark secrets and hidden connections that transcend the boundaries of their reality. As the tension builds, the line between what is real and what is imagined becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of their own existence.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
