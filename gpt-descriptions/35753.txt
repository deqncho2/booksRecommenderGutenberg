Synopsis: 
'War Posters Issued by Belligerent and Neutral Nations 1914-1919' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the powerful propaganda posters of World War I. This meticulously curated collection showcases the artistic and persuasive techniques employed by both belligerent and neutral nations during the war. From stirring patriotic imagery to emotional appeals, these posters offer a glimpse into the wartime mindset and the efforts made to rally support and shape public opinion. With stunning visuals and insightful commentary, this book provides a unique perspective on the role of propaganda in shaping history.

Genres: Art, History, War

Themes: Propaganda, Patriotism, Public Opinion, Artistic Expression
