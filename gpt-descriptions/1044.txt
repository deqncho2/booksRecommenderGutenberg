Synopsis: In 'Extract from Captain Stormfield's Visit to Heaven', Mark Twain takes readers on a captivating journey as Captain Stormfield finds himself in an unexpected destination after his death. As he navigates the celestial realm, encountering peculiar characters and witnessing astonishing events, Stormfield's perceptions of heaven are challenged, leading to a thought-provoking exploration of the afterlife and the nature of human existence.

Genres: Satire, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Afterlife, Perception, Human Nature
