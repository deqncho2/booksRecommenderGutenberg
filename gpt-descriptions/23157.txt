Synopsis: In 'Other People's Business: The Romantic Career of the Practical Miss Dale', Harriet L. Smith takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Miss Dale, a practical and independent woman who finds herself entangled in the affairs of others. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, Miss Dale's own romantic career takes unexpected turns, leading her to question her own desires and priorities. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this novel explores the intricacies of relationships and the choices we make in matters of the heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Independence, Societal expectations
