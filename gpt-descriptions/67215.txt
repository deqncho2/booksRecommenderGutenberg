Synopsis: In "A United States Midshipman Afloat" by Stirling Yates, follow the thrilling journey of a young midshipman as he embarks on a perilous voyage across the high seas. From facing treacherous storms to encountering enemy ships, the protagonist must navigate the challenges of life at sea while also grappling with personal growth and the complexities of loyalty. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the gripping adventures and unexpected twists that await this courageous midshipman.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Personal Growth
