Synopsis: 
'Landseer: A collection of fifteen pictures and a portrait of the painter with introduction and interpretation' by Estelle M. Hurll is a captivating exploration of the renowned painter, Sir Edwin Landseer. Through a collection of fifteen exquisite pictures and a portrait of the artist, Hurll delves into the life and artistry of Landseer, providing insightful interpretations and analysis. From his iconic animal portraits to his stunning landscapes, this book offers a glimpse into the genius and imagination of one of the most celebrated painters of the 19th century.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Criticism

Themes: Artistic interpretation, Animal symbolism, Romanticism
