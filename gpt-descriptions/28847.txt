Synopsis: Max and Maurice: A Juvenile History in Seven Tricks is a delightful and mischievous tale that follows the adventures of two young boys, Max and Maurice, as they navigate their mischievous pranks and tricks in their small village. From their clever pranks on their teacher to their misadventures with the local baker, Max and Maurice's escapades will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what hilarious antics they will get up to next. This classic children's book is filled with humor, wit, and a touch of naughtiness that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Humor, Poetry

Themes: Mischief, Friendship, Consequences
