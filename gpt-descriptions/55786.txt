Synopsis: 
'World Stories Retold for Modern Boys and Girls' is a captivating collection of one hundred and eighty-seven classic stories, carefully curated by William James Sly. These timeless tales have been adapted for retelling in various settings, including homes, Sunday schools, children's services, public school grades, and public libraries' story hours. From enchanting fairy tales to thrilling adventures, this book offers a diverse range of stories that will captivate the imagination of both young and old readers alike.

Genres: 
Fiction, Children's Literature, Anthology

Themes: 
Imagination, Morality, Adventure, Cultural Diversity
