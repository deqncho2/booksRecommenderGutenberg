Synopsis: In 'The Lion's Masquerade: A Sequel to The Peacock at Home', Catherine Ann Turner Dorset takes readers on a captivating journey through the animal kingdom once again. Set in the enchanting world of animals, this sequel follows the adventures of a courageous lion who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As the lion navigates through a grand masquerade ball, he must uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges. With vivid descriptions and a cast of charming characters, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the lion's fate.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Identity, Deception
