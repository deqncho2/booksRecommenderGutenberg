Synopsis: In 'The Land of Afternoon: A Satire' by Knox, Gilbert, readers are transported to a whimsical world where time stands still and absurdity reigns supreme. Follow the adventures of a group of eccentric characters as they navigate through a land filled with peculiar customs, nonsensical laws, and outlandish traditions. From the absurd to the hilarious, this satirical tale will keep readers guessing and laughing until the very end.

Genres: Satire, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Critique of societal norms, Absurdity of bureaucracy, Exploration of human folly
