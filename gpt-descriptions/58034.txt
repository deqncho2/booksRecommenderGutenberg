Synopsis: In 'Hawaiian Flowers', Kuck and Tongg take readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and enchanting world of Hawaiian flora. Through stunning photographs and insightful descriptions, they unveil the hidden beauty and unique characteristics of these exotic flowers, showcasing the rich cultural significance they hold in Hawaiian traditions. From the delicate plumeria to the majestic hibiscus, this book is a visual feast that will transport readers to the lush landscapes of the Hawaiian islands.

Genres: Non-fiction, Photography, Travel

Themes: Nature, Culture, Beauty
