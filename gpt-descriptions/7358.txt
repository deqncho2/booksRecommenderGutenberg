Synopsis: In 'Brought Home' by Hesba Stretton, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption after a series of tragic events. As she navigates through the challenges of her past and present, Ruth finds solace in unexpected places and learns the power of forgiveness and second chances. This gripping tale explores the depths of human resilience and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Redemption, Forgiveness, Self-discovery
