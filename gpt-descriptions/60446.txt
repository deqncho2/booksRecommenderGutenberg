Synopsis: In 'Norah of Billabong' by Mary Grant Bruce, a spirited young girl named Norah finds herself thrust into a world of adventure and self-discovery when she moves to the Australian outback. As she navigates the challenges of her new surroundings, Norah forms deep connections with the land, its people, and the captivating wildlife that surrounds her. With determination and resilience, Norah embarks on a journey that will test her courage and reveal her true strength.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Australian literature

Themes: Exploration of nature, Identity and self-discovery, Resilience and bravery
