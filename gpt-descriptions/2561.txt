Synopsis: In "Robert Falconer" by George MacDonald, follow the journey of a young Scottish boy named Robert as he navigates the complexities of life, love, and faith. Raised by his strict grandmother, Robert's thirst for knowledge and his encounters with mysterious characters lead him on a path of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As he grapples with the challenges of poverty, loss, and societal expectations, Robert's unwavering determination and unwavering faith propel him towards a destiny that will test his beliefs and transform his life forever.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Self-discovery, Social justice
