Synopsis: In "Old Glass and How to Collect it," Lewis, J. Sydney takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of antique glass collecting. From the elegant beauty of delicate crystal to the rustic charm of vintage stained glass, this book explores the fascinating history, craftsmanship, and value of old glass. With insightful tips on identifying, evaluating, and caring for glassware, this guide is a must-read for both novice and seasoned collectors. Discover the hidden stories behind each piece and unlock the secrets of this timeless art form.

Genres: Non-fiction, Antiques, Collectibles

Themes: History, Artistry, Valuation, Preservation
