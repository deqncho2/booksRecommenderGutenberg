Synopsis:
In the gripping novel 'Carità' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Carità finds herself caught between duty and desire in the picturesque Italian countryside. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Carità must make choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating tale explores the power of passion and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles, Social Expectations
