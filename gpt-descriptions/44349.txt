Synopsis: 

'Hours with the Ghosts or, Nineteenth Century Witchcraft: Illustrated Investigations into the Phenomena of Spiritualism and Theosophy' takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious realms of spiritualism and theosophy during the 19th century. Through a series of illustrated investigations, author Henry Ridgely Evans delves into the enigmatic phenomena surrounding witchcraft, ghosts, and the supernatural. With a blend of historical accounts, personal experiences, and in-depth research, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the unseen forces that have captivated human curiosity for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Paranormal

Themes: Spiritualism, Theosophy, Supernatural, Witchcraft, Investigation
