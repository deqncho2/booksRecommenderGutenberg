Synopsis: In 'Ullr Uprising' by H. Beam Piper, a group of colonists on a distant planet find themselves caught in the middle of a rebellion against the oppressive Earth government. As tensions rise and the fight for freedom intensifies, the colonists must navigate treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals to secure their independence. With a blend of political intrigue, action-packed battles, and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, 'Ullr Uprising' is a thrilling science fiction adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Political Thriller

Themes: Rebellion, Oppression, Freedom, Loyalty
