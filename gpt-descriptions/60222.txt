Synopsis: In 'The Raid of Dover: A Romance of the Reign of Woman, A.D. 1940', Douglas Morey Ford takes readers on a thrilling journey to a dystopian future where women rule with an iron fist. Set in the year 1940, the story follows a group of rebels who embark on a daring mission to liberate the city of Dover from the oppressive regime. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies, the fate of the rebellion hangs in the balance. With its gripping narrative and suspenseful plot twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Dystopian fiction, Science fiction, Adventure

Themes: Oppression, Rebellion, Gender roles
