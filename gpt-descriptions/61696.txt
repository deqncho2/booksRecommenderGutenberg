Synopsis: In 'Martian Terror' by Ed Earl Repp, a small town is thrown into chaos when strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena begin to plague its residents. As the mystery deepens, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind these otherworldly events and confront a terrifying force from beyond our planet. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding action, 'Martian Terror' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Alien invasion, Survival, Courage
