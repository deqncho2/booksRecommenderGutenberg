Synopsis: 
'The Works of John Galsworthy: An Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Galsworthy' is a comprehensive collection of the renowned author's works, providing readers with a diverse range of stories and characters. From poignant dramas to thought-provoking social commentaries, this index offers a glimpse into Galsworthy's literary genius. Whether it's exploring the complexities of human relationships or delving into the injustices of society, each work promises to captivate readers with its compelling narratives and profound insights.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Human relationships, Social injustice, Morality, Class divide
