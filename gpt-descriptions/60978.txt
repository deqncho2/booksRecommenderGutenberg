Synopsis: In 'Will Somers, the Boy Detective' by Morris Charles, readers are introduced to a young and brilliant detective named Will Somers. When a mysterious crime occurs in his small town, Will takes it upon himself to unravel the truth behind the puzzling events. With his sharp intellect and keen observation skills, Will embarks on a thrilling adventure, uncovering hidden secrets and unmasking the culprit. As the suspense builds, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns of this gripping mystery, eagerly following Will's every move to see if he can crack the case.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Detective work, Problem-solving, Coming of age
