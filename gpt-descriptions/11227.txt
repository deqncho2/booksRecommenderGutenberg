Synopsis: In "Ten Boys from Dickens" by Kate Dickinson Sweetser, readers are introduced to ten captivating characters from the beloved novels of Charles Dickens. From the mischievous Artful Dodger to the kind-hearted Tiny Tim, this collection brings to life the unforgettable boys who have captured the hearts of readers for generations. Through their trials and triumphs, these young protagonists navigate the gritty streets of Victorian London, facing adversity, friendship, and the pursuit of justice. Each chapter delves into the unique experiences and personalities of these boys, offering a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Dickens' literary world.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Anthology

Themes: Coming of age, Social injustice, Friendship, Resilience
