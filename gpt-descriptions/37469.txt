Synopsis: 
'Some Imagist Poets, 1916: An Annual Anthology' brings together the works of renowned poets such as D. H. Lawrence, Amy Lowell, John Gould Fletcher, H. D., Richard Aldington, and F. S. Flint. This anthology showcases a collection of vivid and evocative poems that capture the essence of the imagist movement. From the exploration of nature's beauty to the introspection of human emotions, these poets paint a tapestry of imagery that will transport readers to new realms of thought and feeling.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Nature, Emotions, Imagery
