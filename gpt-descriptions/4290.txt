Synopsis: In 'The Dominion in 1983' by Centennius Ralph, a mysterious event in the year 1983 alters the course of history, leading to a world where power and control are concentrated in the hands of a secretive organization known as The Dominion. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of this shadowy group, they uncover a web of deception, manipulation, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the fabric of society. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape where trust is scarce and the truth is a precious commodity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Power, Control, Deception
