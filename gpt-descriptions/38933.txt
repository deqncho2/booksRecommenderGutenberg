Synopsis: 

'By Canadian Streams' takes readers on a captivating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Canada, as seen through the eyes of Lawrence J. Burpee. From the majestic Rocky Mountains to the serene shores of the Atlantic, this travelogue paints a vivid picture of the country's natural wonders, while also delving into the rich history and diverse cultures that shape Canada. As Burpee explores the hidden gems and lesser-known regions, he encounters fascinating characters and shares heartwarming anecdotes that will leave readers yearning to embark on their own Canadian adventure.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Nature, Exploration, Cultural diversity
