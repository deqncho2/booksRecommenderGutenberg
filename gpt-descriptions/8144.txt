Synopsis: 
'Acetylene, the Principles of Its Generation and Use: A Practical Handbook on the Production, Purification, and Subsequent Treatment of Acetylene for the Development of Light, Heat, and Power' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the fascinating world of acetylene. Written by W. J. Atkinson Butterfield and F. H. Leeds, this practical handbook explores the generation, purification, and treatment of acetylene for various applications such as lighting, heating, and power generation. From the historical background to the latest advancements, this book provides a wealth of knowledge and techniques for harnessing the potential of acetylene.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Energy production, Chemical processes, Practical applications
