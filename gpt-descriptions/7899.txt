Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys' First Wireless; Or, Winning the Ferberton Prize' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey into the world of wireless communication. When they stumble upon a mysterious message, they become determined to uncover its meaning and find themselves caught up in a dangerous race against time. With their ingenuity and resourcefulness, the boys must navigate through unexpected obstacles and outsmart their rivals to claim the coveted Ferberton Prize. Will their passion for radio technology lead them to triumph or put them in grave danger?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Ingenuity, Competition
