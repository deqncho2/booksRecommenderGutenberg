Synopsis: In "Polly of the Hospital Staff" by Emma C. Dowd, we follow the journey of Polly, a young woman who finds herself thrust into the challenging world of hospital work. As she navigates the demanding and often heartbreaking realities of caring for the sick and injured, Polly's determination and compassion shine through. Along the way, she forms deep connections with her fellow staff members and patients, discovering the true meaning of resilience and the power of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Compassion, Growth, Human connection
