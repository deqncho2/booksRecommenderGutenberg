Synopsis: 
When Sir Charles Baskerville is found dead on the moors surrounding his ancestral home, the legend of a monstrous hound haunting the Baskerville family resurfaces. Fearing for the life of the last remaining Baskerville heir, Sir Henry, Dr. John Watson and the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes are called upon to solve the mystery. As they delve into the dark secrets of the Baskerville family and the eerie landscape of the moors, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and supernatural occurrences. With time running out, Holmes and Watson must uncover the truth behind the curse of the Baskervilles before it claims another victim.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: The supernatural, the power of legends and myths, the conflict between reason and superstition, the nature of evil, the importance of logic and deduction.
