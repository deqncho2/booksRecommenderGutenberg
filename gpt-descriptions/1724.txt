Synopsis: In 'Finished' by Haggard, H. Rider, a young man named Alan Vernon embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind his father's mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into a world of ancient secrets and hidden treasures, Alan finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own fears and make choices that will determine his fate. Will Alan uncover the truth and find closure, or will he become another victim of the treacherous path he treads?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family, Identity, Courage, Betrayal
