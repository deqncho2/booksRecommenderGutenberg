Synopsis: In 'On the Origin of Clockwork, Perpetual Motion Devices, and the Compass', Derek J. de Solla Price takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and evolution of mechanical devices. From ancient civilizations to the Renaissance, Price explores the ingenuity and creativity behind the development of clockwork, perpetual motion devices, and the compass. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he unveils the fascinating stories of inventors, their groundbreaking discoveries, and the impact these inventions had on society. This thought-provoking exploration of human ingenuity will leave readers marveling at the wonders of mechanical engineering throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Invention and innovation, Technological advancements, Historical impact
