Synopsis: In "A Jar of Jelly Beans" by Gregory Franklin, a seemingly ordinary jar of jelly beans holds a secret that will change the lives of those who possess it. As the jar passes from one person to another, it becomes a catalyst for unexpected events, revealing the hidden desires and fears of its owners. With each new encounter, the jar's power grows, leading to a thrilling and unpredictable chain of events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and destiny, Power of objects, Human desires and fears
