Synopsis: In the ancient city of Xuchotl, Conan the Cimmerian finds himself entangled in a deadly web of treachery, sorcery, and forbidden desires. As he navigates the perilous streets and encounters a seductive and enigmatic woman, Conan must confront the dark secrets that lie within the city's blood-soaked walls. With danger lurking at every turn, Conan's survival depends on his cunning, strength, and the edge of his trusty blade.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes: Betrayal, Forbidden Desires, Survival
