Synopsis: In 'The King Who Went on Strike', a powerful and revered king suddenly decides to go on strike, leaving his kingdom in chaos and his subjects bewildered. As the kingdom's economy crumbles and the people's lives are turned upside down, a young and determined commoner takes it upon himself to uncover the truth behind the king's unexpected actions. With the help of a mysterious group of rebels, he embarks on a thrilling journey that will challenge his beliefs and reveal shocking secrets about the kingdom's history. Will he be able to restore order and justice, or will the kingdom be forever changed?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and authority, Rebellion and revolution, Truth and deception
