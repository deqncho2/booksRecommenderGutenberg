Synopsis: In "Allison Bain; Or, By a Way She Knew Not," Margaret M. Robertson weaves a captivating tale of a young woman named Allison Bain who embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Set in the picturesque Scottish Highlands, Allison finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires, as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. As she delves deeper into her own past, Allison uncovers secrets that will challenge everything she thought she knew about herself and her place in the world. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rugged landscapes of Scotland and the depths of the human heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Love and Relationships, Tradition and Modernity
