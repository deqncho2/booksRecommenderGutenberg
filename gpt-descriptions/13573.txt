Synopsis: In 'Elizabeth's Campaign' by Mrs. Humphry Ward, a young woman named Elizabeth finds herself caught in the midst of a political campaign that challenges her beliefs and forces her to confront her own identity. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Elizabeth becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that could change the course of her life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Political Intrigue
