Synopsis: In 'More Jataka Tales' by Ellen C. Babbitt, embark on a captivating journey through ancient India as you encounter a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking tales. Delve into the lives of animals, gods, and humans as they navigate through moral dilemmas, learn valuable life lessons, and discover the true essence of compassion and wisdom. From clever monkeys to wise elephants, each story unveils a unique perspective on the human condition, leaving readers captivated by the timeless wisdom and universal truths found within these ancient fables.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Moral Tales

Themes: Compassion, Wisdom, Morality, Human Nature
