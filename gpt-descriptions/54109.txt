Synopsis: In "Round the Fire Stories" by Arthur Conan Doyle, readers are invited to gather around the hearth and immerse themselves in a collection of captivating tales. From mysterious disappearances to eerie encounters with the supernatural, each story takes the reader on a thrilling journey filled with suspense and intrigue. As the characters share their chilling experiences, the reader is left questioning the boundaries between reality and the unknown. With each turn of the page, the stories become increasingly enthralling, leaving the reader eager to uncover the secrets that lie "round the fire."

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural, Human Nature
