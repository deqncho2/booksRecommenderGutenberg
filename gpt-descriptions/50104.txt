Synopsis: In 'Jessica's First Prayer; and, Jessica's Mother' by Hesba Stretton, a young girl named Jessica finds herself in a desperate situation, leading her to seek solace in prayer. As she navigates the challenges of her life, Jessica's faith and determination are put to the test, ultimately leading to unexpected outcomes for both her and her mother.

Genres: Christian fiction, Victorian literature, coming-of-age

Themes: Faith, redemption, the power of prayer, family dynamics, social inequality
