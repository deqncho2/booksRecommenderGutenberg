Synopsis: In 'The Kingdom Round the Corner: A Novel' by Dawson, Coningsby, a young artist named Richard Carey stumbles upon a hidden world that exists parallel to our own. As he delves deeper into this mysterious realm, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, magic, and ancient prophecies. With the help of a captivating woman named Elara, Richard must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable challenges to uncover the truth about his own identity and the fate of both worlds. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Parallel worlds, Identity, Destiny, Magic
