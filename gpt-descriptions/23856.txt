Synopsis: In 'The Heart of Arethusa' by Frances Barton Fox, a young woman named Amelia finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she discovers an ancient artifact that holds a secret power. As she delves deeper into the artifact's history, Amelia becomes entangled in a web of danger, romance, and supernatural forces. With her life at stake, she must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices to uncover the truth and protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Secrets and hidden histories, Love and sacrifice
