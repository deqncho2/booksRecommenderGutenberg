Synopsis: In 'Springfield in the Spanish American War' by Walter W. Ward, the small town of Springfield is unexpectedly thrust into the midst of the Spanish American War. As the conflict unfolds, the residents of Springfield find themselves facing unexpected challenges, forming unlikely alliances, and discovering the true strength of their community. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping historical novel takes readers on a journey through the trials and triumphs of a town caught up in the chaos of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Community drama

Themes: Patriotism, Community resilience, Unexpected heroism
