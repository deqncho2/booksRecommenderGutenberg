Synopsis: 
Step into the whimsical world of Charles Dana Gibson's latest drawings in "Gibson: New Cartoons." This captivating book showcases a collection of Gibson's most recent illustrations, filled with wit, charm, and a touch of satire. From elegant socialites to mischievous children, Gibson's artistry brings to life a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique story to tell. With his signature style and keen eye for detail, Gibson's drawings will transport readers to a bygone era, where humor and elegance collide in delightful harmony. 

Genres: Art, Humor, Satire

Themes: Social commentary, Everyday life, Wit
