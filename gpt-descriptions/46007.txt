Synopsis: In 'Our Young Aeroplane Scouts in Russia; or, Lost on the Frozen Steppes' by Horace Porter, a group of adventurous young scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey through the vast and treacherous landscapes of Russia. As they navigate the frozen steppes, they encounter unexpected challenges, mysterious characters, and must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to survive. With their trusty aeroplanes as their only means of transportation, the scouts embark on a mission that will test their skills and determination to the limit. Will they find their way back home or remain lost in the unforgiving wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Resilience
