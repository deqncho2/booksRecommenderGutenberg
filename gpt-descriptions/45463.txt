Synopsis: 
'Letters of John Calvin, Volume II: Compiled from the Original Manuscripts and Edited with Historical Notes' offers a captivating collection of personal letters written by the influential theologian, John Calvin. This volume delves into the intimate correspondence of Calvin, revealing his thoughts, struggles, and insights as he navigates the challenges of his time. From theological debates to personal reflections, these letters provide a unique window into the mind of one of the most significant figures of the Protestant Reformation.

Genres: 
Historical, Biography, Theology

Themes: 
Religious reform, Intellectual discourse, Personal growth
