Synopsis: In 'Guilt of the Brass Thieves' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young and ambitious journalist, Penny Parker, finds herself entangled in a dangerous web of mystery and deceit when she stumbles upon a series of brass thefts in her small town. Determined to uncover the truth, Penny embarks on a thrilling investigation, risking her own safety as she uncovers shocking secrets and unexpected alliances. As the stakes rise, Penny must navigate a treacherous path to expose the guilt of the brass thieves and bring justice to her community.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Crime and Justice, Courage and Determination, Secrets and Betrayal
