Synopsis: In 'Loaves and Fishes' by Bernard Capes, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small village, bringing with him a captivating charm and an uncanny ability to perform miracles. As the villagers become enthralled by his presence, they soon discover that his powers may not be as benevolent as they initially seemed. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the stranger's true intentions and the consequences of his actions become a source of both fascination and fear for the villagers.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Power, Morality
