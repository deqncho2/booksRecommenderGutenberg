Synopsis: 
In 'Irish Ned: The Winnipeg Newsy' by Fea, Samuel, follow the captivating journey of Irish Ned, a young newspaper boy in the bustling city of Winnipeg. Ned's life takes an unexpected turn when he stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads him into a world of secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships. As he delves deeper into the hidden truths of the city, Ned must navigate through a web of deceit and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic letter. With each twist and turn, Ned's determination and courage are put to the test, making this an exhilarating and heartwarming tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets, Betrayal
