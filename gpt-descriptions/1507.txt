Synopsis: In the dark and twisted world of ancient Rome, a renowned general returns from war only to be plunged into a web of revenge, betrayal, and unspeakable horrors. As the body count rises and the boundaries of morality are pushed to their limits, Titus Andronicus must navigate a treacherous path to seek justice and redemption. With its shocking violence and relentless suspense, this tragic tale will leave readers breathless until the very end.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Revenge

Themes: Revenge, Violence, Power, Justice, Morality
