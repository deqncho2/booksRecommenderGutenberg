Synopsis: In 'Out and About London' by Burke, Thomas, a young woman named Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure through the bustling streets of London. As she navigates the city's hidden corners and encounters a cast of eccentric characters, Emily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries. With each step she takes, the stakes rise higher, and she must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma that lies within the heart of London.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of urban landscapes, Secrets and hidden truths, Personal growth and self-discovery
