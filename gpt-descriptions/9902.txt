Synopsis:
In "The Middle of Things" by J.S. Fletcher, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a perplexing crime scene. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a series of unexpected twists and turns that lead him to question everything he thought he knew. With danger lurking around every corner, he must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and deceit to uncover the truth before it's too late. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma that lies at "The Middle of Things."

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Uncovering the Truth
