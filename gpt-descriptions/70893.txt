Synopsis: In "The Abandoned Farm, and Connie's Mistake" by Mary Jane Holmes, a young woman named Connie stumbles upon an eerie abandoned farm while on a leisurely stroll. Intrigued by the mystery surrounding the property, she decides to investigate further, unaware of the grave mistake she is about to make. As Connie delves deeper into the secrets of the farm, she uncovers a dark and haunting past that threatens to consume her. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of danger and deceit, forcing her to confront her own fears and make a life-altering decision.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Consequences of curiosity
