Synopsis: In 'A Thought For Tomorrow' by Gilbert, Robert E., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing him to confront the ethical implications of his creation. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while grappling with the profound question of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Identity, Technological Advancement
