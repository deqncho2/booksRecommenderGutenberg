Synopsis:
In 'It Is Never Too Late to Mend' by Charles Reade, a young man named George Fielding finds himself unjustly accused of a crime he did not commit. As he is sent to a brutal prison, he becomes determined to seek justice and prove his innocence. Along the way, he encounters love, betrayal, and a relentless pursuit of truth that will test his resilience and challenge the corrupt forces that have conspired against him.

Genres:
Drama, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Injustice, Redemption, Love and Betrayal
