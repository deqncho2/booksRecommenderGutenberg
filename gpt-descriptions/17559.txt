Synopsis: In 'On the Church Steps' by Sarah C. Hallowell, a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that occurs on the steps of their beloved church. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of forgiveness is explored. This gripping tale delves into the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption, leaving readers questioning the boundaries of faith and the lengths we go to protect those we love.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Faith, Forgiveness, Secrets, Redemption
