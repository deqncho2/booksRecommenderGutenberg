Synopsis: In 'The Restless Sex' by Robert W. Chambers, a young woman named Mary Wayne finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and independence, Mary must confront the consequences of her choices in a world that is not always kind to women who dare to defy convention.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, love and marriage, self-discovery
