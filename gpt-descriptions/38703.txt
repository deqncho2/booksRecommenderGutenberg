Synopsis: In 'The Black Moth: A Romance of the XVIIIth Century', the dashing and enigmatic Jack Carstares finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, this captivating tale follows Jack as he navigates the treacherous world of highwaymen, aristocracy, and secret identities. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must confront his past and make choices that will shape his future. Will he be able to outwit his enemies and find redemption, or will he succumb to the darkness that threatens to consume him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Love and Sacrifice
