Synopsis: 

'Epitome of the Pharmacopeia of the United States and the National Formulary: With Comments' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the world of pharmacopeias and formularies, providing valuable insights and commentary. Written by Hewlett, Albion Walter, Puckner, William August, Sollmann, Torald Hermann, and Wilbert, Martin I., this book offers a condensed version of the official pharmacopeias and formularies, accompanied by expert analysis and explanations.

Genres: Reference, Medicine, Pharmacy

Themes: Pharmaceutical standards, Drug formulation, Medical practice
