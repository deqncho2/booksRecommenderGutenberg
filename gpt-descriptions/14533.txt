Synopsis: In 'Hocken and Hunken: A Tale of Troy' by Quiller-Couch, Arthur, readers are transported to the legendary city of Troy, where two brothers, Hocken and Hunken, find themselves caught in the midst of a great war. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of ancient Greece, they encounter heroes, gods, and mythical creatures, all while facing their own personal trials and tribulations. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this tale of adventure and bravery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Hocken and Hunken.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: War and Conflict, Brotherhood, Heroism
