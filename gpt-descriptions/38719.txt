Synopsis: In 'A Romance of Wastdale', a young woman named Mary Briscoe finds herself torn between two suitors, each with their own secrets and hidden pasts. Set against the backdrop of the rugged and captivating Wastdale landscape, this gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Deception and secrets, Redemption and forgiveness
