Synopsis: 

'Hands Around [Reigen]: A Cycle of Ten Dialogues' by Arthur Schnitzler is a captivating collection of interconnected dialogues that explores the complexities of human relationships and desires. Each dialogue features two characters engaging in intimate conversations, revealing their hidden desires, secrets, and vulnerabilities. As the cycle progresses, the reader is taken on a journey through various social classes and walks of life, witnessing the intricate web of connections that bind them all together. With each dialogue, the reader becomes more entangled in the characters' lives, eagerly anticipating the next encounter and the revelations it may bring.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love, Desire, Morality, Social Class, Human Nature
