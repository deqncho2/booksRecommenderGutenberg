Synopsis: In "The Cock, the Mouse, and the Little Red Hen: An Old Tale Retold," Félicité Lefèvre presents a captivating retelling of a classic tale. The story follows a determined little red hen who embarks on a journey with her unlikely companions, a cock and a mouse. Together, they face numerous challenges and encounter various characters, each with their own motives and desires. As they navigate through a world filled with danger and adventure, the little red hen's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. This enchanting tale takes readers on a thrilling and heartwarming adventure, showcasing the power of teamwork, perseverance, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Teamwork, Perseverance, Self-discovery
