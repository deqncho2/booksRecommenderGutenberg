Synopsis: 
'Cinderella, and Other Stories' by Davis, Richard Harding is a captivating collection of short stories that takes readers on a journey through various settings and characters. From the enchanting tale of Cinderella to other intriguing stories, this book offers a delightful blend of romance, adventure, and mystery. Each story presents unique and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Transformation, Fate and destiny
