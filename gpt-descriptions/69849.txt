Synopsis: In "Mr. Arnold: A romance of the Revolution" by Lynde, Francis, the reader is transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. The story follows the life of Mr. Arnold, a man torn between his loyalty to his country and his love for a woman. As the Revolution unfolds, Mr. Arnold finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden passion. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Patriotism, Sacrifice
