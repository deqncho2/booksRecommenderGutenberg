Synopsis: In 'The Hosts of the Lord' by Flora Annie Webster Steel, a young British woman named Mary Lennox finds herself in the midst of a tumultuous journey as she travels to India to join her father, who is stationed there as a British officer. As Mary navigates the complexities of colonial India, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. With the backdrop of the Indian Rebellion of 1857, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn, forcing her to question her loyalties and confront the harsh realities of the world around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue
