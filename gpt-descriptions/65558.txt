Synopsis: In 'By Honour Bound: A School Story for Girls' by Bessie Marchant, readers are transported to a prestigious all-girls boarding school where friendships are tested, secrets are unveiled, and loyalty is put to the ultimate test. As a group of spirited young girls navigate the challenges of school life, they find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of integrity, and the lengths one will go to protect those they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, School

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Integrity
