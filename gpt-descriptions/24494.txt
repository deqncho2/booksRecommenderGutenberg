Synopsis: In 'The Spirit of Lafayette' by James Mott Hallowell, a young American soldier finds himself transported back in time to the American Revolution, where he becomes entangled in the life of the legendary Marquis de Lafayette. As he navigates the perils of war and the complexities of friendship, he discovers the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. This captivating historical fiction takes readers on a thrilling journey through time, blending adventure, romance, and the enduring spirit of patriotism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism
