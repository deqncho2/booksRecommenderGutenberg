Synopsis: In 'God and My Neighbour' by Robert Blatchford, a chance encounter between two strangers leads to a profound exploration of faith, morality, and the nature of humanity. As their lives intertwine, they are forced to confront their own beliefs and question the existence of a higher power. With thought-provoking dialogue and unexpected twists, this gripping novel delves into the complexities of religion and the impact it has on our relationships and society.

Genres: Fiction, Philosophical, Drama

Themes: Faith and doubt, Morality, Human nature
