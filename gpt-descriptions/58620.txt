Synopsis: In 'The Power of a Lie' by Johan Bojer, a small Norwegian village is shaken by a scandalous lie that spreads like wildfire, threatening to destroy the lives of its inhabitants. As the lie takes hold, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. With each passing day, the consequences of the lie become more devastating, forcing the villagers to confront their own secrets and question the nature of truth. This gripping tale explores the power of deception and the fragile nature of trust, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Morality, Truth
