Synopsis: In 'Parallel Paths: A Study in Biology, Ethics, and Art', Rolleston delves into the fascinating intersection of science, morality, and creativity. Through captivating anecdotes and thought-provoking analysis, he explores the intricate connections between biology, ethics, and art, revealing surprising parallels that challenge our conventional understanding of these disciplines. As the pages unfold, readers will embark on a captivating journey that unveils the intricate tapestry of life, morality, and human expression, leaving them with a renewed appreciation for the interconnectedness of the world around us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Interdisciplinary connections, Morality, Creativity
