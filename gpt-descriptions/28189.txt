Synopsis: 

In 'My Friends the Savages: Notes and Observations of a Perak settler (Malay Peninsula)', Giovanni Battista Cerruti takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed landscapes of the Malay Peninsula. As a settler in Perak, Cerruti immerses himself in the vibrant culture and customs of the indigenous people, forming deep connections with the local tribes. Through his keen observations and vivid storytelling, Cerruti unveils a world filled with fascinating rituals, ancient traditions, and the delicate balance between man and nature. From encounters with fearsome warriors to heartwarming friendships, Cerruti's memoir offers a unique perspective on the beauty and complexity of life in the Malay Peninsula.

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural exploration, Indigenous traditions, Human-nature relationship
