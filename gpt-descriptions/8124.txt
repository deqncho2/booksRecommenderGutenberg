Synopsis: In "Woodrow Wilson as I Know Him," Joseph P. Tumulty offers a captivating and intimate portrayal of one of America's most enigmatic presidents. Drawing from his personal experiences as Wilson's close aide and confidant, Tumulty provides a behind-the-scenes look at the man behind the public persona. From Wilson's early political career to his transformative presidency and the challenges he faced, Tumulty's account sheds light on the complexities and contradictions of this influential leader. With vivid anecdotes and insightful observations, "Woodrow Wilson as I Know Him" offers a unique perspective on a president who shaped the course of American history.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Politics, Personal Relationships
