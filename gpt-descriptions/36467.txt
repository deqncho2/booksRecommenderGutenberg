Synopsis: 

In 'A Butterfly on the Wheel: A Novel', a gripping psychological thriller, a seemingly ordinary family's life is turned upside down when they become the target of a ruthless and cunning kidnapper. As the kidnapper's demands escalate, the family is forced to confront their darkest secrets and make unimaginable choices to save themselves. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning who can be trusted and what lengths one would go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Psychological thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, secrets and lies, survival
