Synopsis: 

In 'Coca and its Therapeutic Application, Third Edition', Angelo Mariani takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of coca, exploring its fascinating history, cultural significance, and its potential therapeutic benefits. From ancient civilizations to modern medicine, Mariani delves into the various uses of coca and its impact on human health. With meticulous research and compelling anecdotes, this book sheds light on the controversial plant that has both captivated and divided societies for centuries. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medicine

Themes: Cultural significance, Therapeutic potential, Controversy
