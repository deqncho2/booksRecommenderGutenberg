Synopsis: In 'The Eureka Stockade' by Carboni, Raffaello, a young Italian immigrant finds himself caught up in the turbulent events of the Australian gold rush. As he becomes entangled in the struggle for miners' rights and the fight against injustice, Raffaello must navigate a dangerous world of corruption, betrayal, and rebellion. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, he must decide where his loyalties lie and what he is willing to sacrifice for the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Drama

Themes: Freedom and Independence, Social Justice, Identity and Belonging
