Synopsis: In 'A Fair Jewess' by B.L. Farjeon, a young Jewish woman named Esther finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she navigates the complexities of her heritage and the prejudices of society, Esther must uncover the truth behind her family's past and confront the challenges that lie ahead. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel explores themes of identity, love, and the power of resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Prejudice, Family, Love, Resilience
