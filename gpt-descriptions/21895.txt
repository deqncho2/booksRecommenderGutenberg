Synopsis: 
In "The Old Coast Road: From Boston to Plymouth" by Agnes Rothery, embark on a captivating journey through the historic and picturesque landscapes of Massachusetts. Follow the author as she takes you along the winding path of the old coast road, revealing hidden tales of the region's rich history, charming villages, and breathtaking natural beauty. From quaint colonial towns to majestic lighthouses, this enchanting travelogue paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, inviting readers to explore the timeless allure of New England's coastal treasures.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Historical preservation, Cultural heritage, Natural beauty
