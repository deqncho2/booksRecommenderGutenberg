Synopsis: 
Embark on an exhilarating adventure through the enchanting South Seas as George S. Chappell takes you on a captivating journey aboard the Kawa. From the vibrant shores of Tahiti to the remote islands of Fiji, Chappell's vivid descriptions and thrilling encounters with the local inhabitants will transport you to a world of exotic beauty and untamed wilderness. As the Kawa sails through treacherous waters and encounters unexpected challenges, the crew's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. Join Chappell as he immerses himself in the rich cultures, explores hidden gems, and unravels the mysteries of the South Seas in this enthralling travel memoir.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Survival
