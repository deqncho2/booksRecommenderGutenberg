Synopsis: In 'The Amateur' by Richard Harding Davis, a young American journalist finds himself caught up in a dangerous web of international intrigue while on assignment in Europe. As he navigates through a world of spies, secret societies, and political conspiracies, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Journalism, Survival
