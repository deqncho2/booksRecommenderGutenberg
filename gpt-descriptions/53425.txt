Synopsis: In 'The Dearest Things in Boots' by Edna I. MacKenzie, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her deceased grandmother's old boots. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily uncovers a world of family secrets, lost treasures, and unexpected connections that will change her life forever. With each step she takes, Emily unravels the past and discovers the true meaning of love, loyalty, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Hidden treasures, Love and forgiveness
