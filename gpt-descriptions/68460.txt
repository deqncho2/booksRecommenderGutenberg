Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 121, January 2, 1915: The call of death; or, Nick Carter's clever assistant', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious call leads him to a series of puzzling deaths. With the help of his resourceful assistant, Carter must navigate a web of deceit and danger to uncover the truth behind the call of death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Danger
