Synopsis: 

In "Description of the Process of Manufacturing Coal Gas, for the Lighting of Streets Houses, and Public Buildings," Friedrich Christian Accum takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of gas manufacturing. Through detailed descriptions, elevations, sections, and plans, Accum explores the most advanced apparatus used in the gas works of London and other major towns in Great Britain. Accompanied by comparative estimates, this work unveils the most economical methods of procuring this innovative form of lighting. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Industrial Revolution, Innovation, Energy
