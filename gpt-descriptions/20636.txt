Synopsis: 

"The Military Journals of Two Private Soldiers, 1758-1775: With Numerous Illustrative Notes" by Tomlinson, Abraham takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of two private soldiers during a crucial period in American history. Through their detailed journals, the reader is transported to the battlefields and encampments of the mid-18th century, witnessing the hardships, triumphs, and camaraderie experienced by these unsung heroes. As the soldiers navigate the challenges of war, their personal stories intertwine with the larger narrative of the American Revolution, providing a unique perspective on this pivotal time in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Historical events and their impact
