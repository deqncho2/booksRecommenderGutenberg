Synopsis: In 'The Country Christmas' by Frances Margaret Fox, a young woman named Emma finds herself unexpectedly transported back in time to a charming country village during the holiday season. As she navigates this unfamiliar world, she encounters a cast of endearing characters, including a handsome local farmer who captures her heart. With the magic of Christmas guiding her, Emma must decide whether to embrace her new reality or find a way back to her own time. This heartwarming tale of love, self-discovery, and the enchantment of the holiday season will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Romance, Time Travel, Holiday

Themes: Love, Belonging, Embracing the Present
