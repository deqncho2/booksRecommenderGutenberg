Synopsis: 

'English Monasteries: From Saxon Days to Their Dissolution' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of English monastic life. From the early days of the Saxons to the tumultuous period of their dissolution, author J. Charles Cox delves into the fascinating world of these religious institutions. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Cox unveils the daily routines, spiritual practices, and architectural wonders that characterized these monasteries. From tales of devotion and enlightenment to stories of power struggles and political intrigue, this book offers a captivating exploration of a pivotal era in English history.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Religion, Architecture, Power dynamics, Cultural heritage
