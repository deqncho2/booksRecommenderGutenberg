Synopsis: 
In 'Piccadilly Jim', the charming and mischievous James Crocker, known as Piccadilly Jim, finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and absurd situations. When his eccentric father decides to marry an American millionaire's daughter, Jim's life takes an unexpected turn. With mistaken identities, secret romances, and a touch of blackmail, Jim must navigate the chaos to save his family's reputation and find true love. Set against the backdrop of 1920s London, this delightful comedy of errors will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Satire

Themes: Love and relationships, Social class, Identity and mistaken identity
