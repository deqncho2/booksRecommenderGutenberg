Synopsis: In "The Abbot's Ghost, or Maurice Treherne's Temptation: A Christmas Story," a young man named Maurice finds himself drawn to a mysterious and haunted abbey during the holiday season. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the abbey, Maurice becomes entangled in a web of temptation, love, and the supernatural. With Christmas approaching, he must navigate the treacherous path between desire and duty, all while uncovering the truth behind the ghostly presence that haunts the abbey.

Genres: Gothic fiction, romance, mystery

Themes: Temptation, love, duty, supernatural, redemption
