Synopsis: In 'Secret History of To-day: Being Revelations of a Diplomatic Spy', the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the shadowy world of international espionage. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows a seasoned diplomat who uncovers a web of intrigue and conspiracy that threatens to destabilize the delicate balance of power between nations. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the world's most powerful figures, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception, danger, and hidden agendas. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the protagonist must make difficult choices to protect his country and his own life. This gripping tale of espionage will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the mysteries and uncover the truth behind the secret history of today.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Power and Politics, Deception, Loyalty, National Security
