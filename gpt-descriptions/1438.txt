Synopsis: 
No Name by Wilkie Collins is a gripping Victorian mystery that follows the lives of two sisters, Magdalen and Norah, who are unexpectedly disinherited by their wealthy uncle. Determined to reclaim their rightful inheritance, the sisters embark on a thrilling journey filled with deception, revenge, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate through a web of secrets and hidden identities, they must confront the consequences of their choices and the true nature of justice.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Victorian literature

Themes: Inheritance, identity, revenge, justice, deception
