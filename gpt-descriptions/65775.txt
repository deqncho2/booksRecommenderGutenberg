Synopsis: 
'The Cry for Justice: An Anthology of the Literature of Social Protest' is a captivating collection of writings that spans across five thousand years, featuring the voices of philosophers, poets, novelists, social reformers, and others who have passionately expressed their struggle against social injustice. This anthology brings together a diverse range of works from twenty-five languages, offering a profound exploration of the human quest for equality and fairness throughout history.

Genres: 
Anthology, Literature, Social Justice

Themes: 
Social Injustice, Equality, Human Rights, Activism
