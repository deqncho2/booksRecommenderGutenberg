Synopsis: In 'The Heir Presumptive and the Heir Apparent' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), two young women find themselves unexpectedly thrust into positions of power and privilege. As they navigate the complexities of their new roles, they must confront the challenges of societal expectations, personal ambition, and the ever-present specter of scandal. With their futures hanging in the balance, these two heirs must make difficult choices that will shape not only their own lives but also the fate of those around them.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and privilege, societal expectations, personal ambition, scandal
