Synopsis: In 'The Broken Thread' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Muriel Chetwynd finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic message from her estranged father. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her family's past, Muriel becomes the target of a sinister plot that threatens not only her own life but also the security of her country. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with espionage, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will Muriel uncover the truth and unravel the broken thread that connects her to a world she never knew existed?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, National security
