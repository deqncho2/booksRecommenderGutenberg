Synopsis: In 'Clare Avery: A Story of the Spanish Armada' by Emily Sarah Holt, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Spanish Armada. The story follows Clare Avery, a young Englishwoman whose life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself caught in the midst of the epic battle between England and Spain. As Clare navigates the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and survival, she must summon her inner strength and make choices that will shape her destiny. With vivid historical detail and a captivating narrative, this tale of adventure and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Love, Historical Events
