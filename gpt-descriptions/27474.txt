Synopsis: In "Queen Berngerd, The Bard and the Dreams, and Other Ballads," a captivating collection of ballads, readers are transported to a world of enchantment and adventure. From the courageous Queen Berngerd who defies societal norms to the mysterious Bard who weaves tales of love and loss, each ballad unveils a unique story filled with passion, betrayal, and the power of dreams. As readers delve into the lyrical verses, they will be captivated by the vivid imagery and the emotional depth of the characters, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Fantasy, Poetry, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Love, Dreams, Betrayal
