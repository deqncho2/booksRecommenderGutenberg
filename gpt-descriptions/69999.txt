Synopsis: 

In 'Jigsaw' by Tom McMorrow, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a complex web of clues and puzzles as he investigates a series of mysterious murders. With each victim, a new piece of the puzzle is revealed, leading him closer to the truth. As the stakes rise and the body count increases, the detective must race against time to solve the ultimate jigsaw before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Puzzle-solving, Crime investigation, Psychological suspense
