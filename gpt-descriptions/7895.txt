Synopsis: In 'A Terrible Temptation: A Story of To-Day', Charles Reade weaves a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. The story follows the lives of two individuals from different social backgrounds who find themselves irresistibly drawn to each other. As their forbidden attraction intensifies, they are faced with a series of choices that will test their loyalty, integrity, and the very fabric of their lives. Set against the backdrop of Victorian society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human desires and the consequences of succumbing to temptation.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Morality, Social class, Temptation
