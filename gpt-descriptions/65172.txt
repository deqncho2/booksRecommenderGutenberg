Synopsis: 
'A Gentleman of Leisure' follows the misadventures of Jimmy Pitt, a charming and resourceful young man who finds himself entangled in a web of hilarious and unexpected circumstances. When Jimmy's financial situation takes a turn for the worse, he concocts a brilliant plan to live a life of luxury without having to work. However, his scheme soon spirals out of control, leading to a series of comical escapades involving mistaken identities, romantic entanglements, and a cast of eccentric characters. As Jimmy navigates the chaotic world he has created, he must rely on his wit and quick thinking to avoid the consequences of his actions.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Wealth and class, Identity and deception, Love and romance
