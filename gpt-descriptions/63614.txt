Synopsis: In 'With the Guns' by Cecil J. C. Street, a young soldier finds himself thrust into the chaos of World War I, where he must navigate the horrors of the battlefield and the bonds of camaraderie. As he faces the relentless onslaught of enemy forces, he discovers the true meaning of sacrifice and the indomitable spirit of those who fight alongside him. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable journey through the trenches of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
