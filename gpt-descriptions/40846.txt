Synopsis: 
In "Voltaire" by John Morley, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most influential thinkers. This enthralling biography delves into the fascinating world of Voltaire, a brilliant philosopher, writer, and advocate for freedom of thought. From his early years as a rebellious young man to his encounters with powerful figures of his time, Morley paints a vivid portrait of a man who challenged the status quo and fearlessly spoke his mind. With its blend of intellectual exploration, political intrigue, and personal triumphs, "Voltaire" offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a true iconoclast.

Genres: Biography, History, Philosophy

Themes: Freedom of thought, Enlightenment ideals, Intellectual rebellion
