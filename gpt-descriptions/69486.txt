Synopsis: In 'Through Bolshevik Russia', Ethel Snowden takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous landscape of post-revolutionary Russia. As an eyewitness to history, Snowden provides a vivid and intimate account of her encounters with prominent Bolshevik leaders, the struggles of the Russian people, and the complex political climate of the time. From the grandeur of the Kremlin to the hardships faced by ordinary citizens, this gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Political

Themes: Revolution, Power, Social Justice
