Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Haven, a mysterious stranger arrives to take on the role of Harbor Master. As he settles into his new position, strange occurrences begin to unfold, revealing a hidden world of secrets and danger lurking beneath the tranquil surface of the harbor. With the help of a curious young local, the Harbor Master embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, leading them both into a web of intrigue and peril that will test their courage and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town intrigue, Courage and loyalty
