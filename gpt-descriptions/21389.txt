Synopsis: In "Ronald Morton; or, the Fire Ships: A Story of the Last Naval War," William Henry Giles Kingston takes readers on a thrilling journey through the final naval conflict. Set in the backdrop of a war-torn world, the story follows the courageous Ronald Morton as he embarks on a perilous mission to protect his country's shores. As he faces treacherous enemies, dangerous waters, and unexpected alliances, Ronald's determination and bravery are put to the ultimate test. With high-stakes battles, unexpected twists, and a captivating narrative, this novel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
