Synopsis: In 'Men of Affairs' by Roland Pertwee, a group of influential men find themselves entangled in a web of scandal, power struggles, and hidden agendas. As they navigate the treacherous world of politics, business, and personal relationships, their lives become increasingly intertwined, leading to unexpected consequences and shocking revelations. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this thrilling tale explores the dark underbelly of society and the lengths people will go to protect their interests.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power, Corruption, Betrayal, Ambition
