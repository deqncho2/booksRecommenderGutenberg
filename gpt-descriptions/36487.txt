Synopsis: In the heart of Kentucky, a tale of love, hate, and adventure unfolds as the tobacco uprising takes center stage. 'The Night Riders' delves into the gripping story of a community torn apart by conflicting loyalties and the lengths people will go to protect what they hold dear. As tensions rise and danger lurks in the shadows, a young couple finds themselves caught in the midst of the turmoil, their lives forever changed by the events that unfold. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this thrilling narrative explores the dark underbelly of a society on the brink of chaos.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Conflict, Sacrifice
