Synopsis: In 'Madcap' by Gibbs, George, a brilliant but eccentric scientist invents a device that can alter reality itself. When the device falls into the wrong hands, chaos ensues as the boundaries between imagination and reality blur. As the world spirals into madness, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate a surreal landscape filled with bizarre creatures, mind-bending puzzles, and unexpected twists. With time running out, they must unravel the secrets of the device and find a way to restore order before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Reality manipulation, Imagination, Heroism
