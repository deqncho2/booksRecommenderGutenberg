Synopsis: In 'The Leopard Woman' by Stewart Edward White, a young American engineer finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure while working on a railroad project in Africa. As he navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters dangerous wildlife, he becomes captivated by the mysterious allure of a woman known as the Leopard Woman. With her enigmatic presence and a secret that could change everything, he must decide between loyalty and desire, ultimately facing the consequences of his choices.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Forbidden love, Moral dilemmas
