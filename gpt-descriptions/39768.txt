Synopsis: In 'Vassall Morton: A Novel' by Francis Parkman, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the American Revolution. Set in the backdrop of colonial Massachusetts, the story follows the life of Vassall Morton, a young man torn between his loyalty to his family and his desire for independence. As the Revolution unfolds, Vassall finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Parkman weaves a tale of passion, courage, and the struggle for freedom that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Independence, Political Intrigue, Sacrifice
