Synopsis: In 'The Jewel City' by Ben Macomber, a mysterious artifact is discovered in the heart of a bustling metropolis, setting off a thrilling adventure that intertwines history, mythology, and the pursuit of power. As a group of unlikely heroes races against time to unlock the secrets of the artifact, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and ancient prophecies. With each step closer to the truth, they uncover a hidden world filled with hidden agendas, hidden treasures, and hidden dangers. Will they be able to unravel the mysteries of the Jewel City before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Power and corruption, Mythology and history, Redemption and sacrifice
