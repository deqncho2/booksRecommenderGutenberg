Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Don Marquis' is a comprehensive collection of the works of Don Marquis, a prolific American writer known for his wit and humor. This index includes a wide range of Marquis' works, including poetry, essays, plays, and fiction. From the misadventures of Archy, a poetic cockroach, to the satirical tales of the Old Soak, a lovable drunkard, this collection offers a delightful journey through Marquis' imaginative and humorous world. Whether you are a fan of his famous characters or discovering his works for the first time, this index provides a treasure trove of Marquis' literary gems.

Genres: Poetry, Essays, Plays, Fiction
Themes: Humor, Satire, Social Commentary
