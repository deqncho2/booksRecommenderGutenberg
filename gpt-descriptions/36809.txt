Synopsis: In 'Crying for the Light; Or, Fifty Years Ago. Vol. 2', the captivating sequel to the first volume, the reader is transported back in time to a tumultuous era filled with secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected twists. As the story unfolds, the lives of the intriguing characters become increasingly entangled, leading to a series of shocking revelations that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Set against the backdrop of a bygone era, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Redemption, Resilience
