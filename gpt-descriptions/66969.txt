Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Greensea Island, strange occurrences have been happening for years. When a young journalist named Emily arrives to investigate the mysterious disappearances and eerie legends surrounding the island, she uncovers a web of secrets that the townspeople have long kept hidden. As she delves deeper into the island's dark history, Emily finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With each clue she uncovers, the line between reality and myth becomes blurred, and Emily must race against time to uncover the truth before she becomes the next victim of Greensea Island's haunting mysteries.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Supernatural elements, Small-town intrigue
