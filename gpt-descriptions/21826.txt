Synopsis: 
'My Pet Recipes, Tried and True: Contributed by the Ladies and Friends of St. Andrew's Church, Quebec' is a delightful collection of recipes that takes readers on a culinary journey through the kitchens of the women of St. Andrew's Church. From traditional family favorites to unique and innovative dishes, this cookbook offers a wide range of delectable recipes that are sure to tantalize the taste buds. Each recipe is lovingly shared by the talented cooks of the church, making this book a treasure trove of culinary expertise and community spirit.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Community

Themes: Culinary traditions, Community collaboration, Sharing and generosity
