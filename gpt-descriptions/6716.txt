Synopsis: In "The Spirit of 1906" by George W. Brooks, a mysterious artifact from the past is discovered, leading a group of unlikely heroes on a thrilling adventure through time. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the artifact, they uncover a hidden world filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected connections to their own lives. With each twist and turn, the characters must confront their fears and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine the fate of not only their present but also the future.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Legacy, Courage
