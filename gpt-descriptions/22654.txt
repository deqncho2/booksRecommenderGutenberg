Synopsis: In 'The Doctor of Pimlico: Being the Disclosure of a Great Crime', a renowned doctor finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a shocking secret. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of Pimlico, he must navigate treacherous alliances, unexpected betrayals, and a sinister plot that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With his reputation and life on the line, the doctor must race against time to uncover the truth and bring the perpetrators to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
