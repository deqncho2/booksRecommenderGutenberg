Synopsis: In "Mary Liddiard; Or, The Missionary's Daughter," follow the captivating journey of Mary, a young woman torn between her duty to her family and her desire for adventure. Set against the backdrop of the exotic and mysterious East, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself caught in a web of danger, romance, and self-discovery. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters fascinating characters, Mary must confront her deepest fears and make choices that will shape her destiny. This enthralling tale will transport readers to distant lands and keep them on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Duty and responsibility, Self-discovery, Cultural exploration
