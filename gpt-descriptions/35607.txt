Synopsis: In 'The Spell' by William Dana Orcutt, a young woman named Margaret discovers an ancient book of spells that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into the world of magic, Margaret becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where every choice she makes has unforeseen consequences. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves. This captivating tale of magic and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Magic, Secrets, Consequences
