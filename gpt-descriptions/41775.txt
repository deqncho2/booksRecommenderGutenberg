Synopsis: 
In 'Cornish Characters and Strange Events', Baring-Gould takes us on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes of Cornwall, where he introduces us to a colorful cast of characters and recounts a series of bizarre and mysterious events. From tales of ghostly apparitions haunting ancient ruins to encounters with mischievous pixies and supernatural creatures, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world where the line between reality and the supernatural is blurred. With his vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Baring-Gould invites us to explore the rich folklore and legends of Cornwall, leaving us both enthralled and intrigued.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: 
Cornish folklore, Supernatural encounters, Mystery and intrigue
