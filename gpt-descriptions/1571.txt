Synopsis: In 'Critias' by Plato, a renowned philosopher, a group of ancient Athenians embark on a captivating journey to explore the lost city of Atlantis. As they delve into the history and culture of this mythical civilization, they uncover astonishing secrets that challenge their understanding of power, morality, and the nature of humanity. Through vivid storytelling and philosophical discourse, 'Critias' invites readers to ponder the mysteries of the past and contemplate the implications they hold for the present and future.

Genres: Philosophy, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Power, Morality, History, Human Nature
