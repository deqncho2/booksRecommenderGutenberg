Synopsis: In "The Works of Henry Fielding, vol. 11: A Journey From This World to the Next; and A Voyage to Lisbon," embark on a captivating journey through the afterlife and a perilous voyage to Lisbon. This volume combines two of Fielding's most intriguing works, taking readers on a thought-provoking exploration of life, death, and the human condition. Through vivid storytelling and witty observations, Fielding invites us to reflect on the nature of existence and the choices we make in our earthly lives. Prepare to be enthralled by this imaginative and philosophical adventure.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Life and Death, Morality, Human Nature, Redemption
