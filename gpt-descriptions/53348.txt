Synopsis: In 'Some Irish Yesterdays', Ross and Somerville take readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Irish history, weaving together tales of love, loss, and resilience. From the ancient legends of Ireland's mythical past to the turbulent times of rebellion and revolution, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the lives of ordinary people caught up in extraordinary circumstances. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, the authors transport readers to a world where past and present intertwine, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets hidden within Ireland's yesterdays.

Genres: Historical fiction, Short stories, Irish literature

Themes: History, Identity, Resilience, Love and loss
