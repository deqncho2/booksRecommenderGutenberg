Synopsis: In 'Nordenholt's Million', a brilliant scientist named Nordenholt devises a plan to solve the world's overpopulation crisis. However, his solution is met with controversy and resistance as it challenges the very fabric of society. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with intrigue, moral dilemmas, and unexpected twists that will keep them on the edge of their seat.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Overpopulation, Ethics, Social Order
