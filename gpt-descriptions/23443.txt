Synopsis: In 'Unspecialist' by Yaco, Murray F., a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself thrust into a world of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, John discovers hidden abilities within himself that he never knew existed. With the help of a group of unlikely allies, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the artifact's origins and its true purpose. As the stakes rise and the lines between friend and foe blur, John must embrace his newfound powers and confront his own inner demons to save not only himself but also the fate of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Redemption
