Synopsis: In 'Motor Boat Boys Mississippi Cruise; or, The Dash for Dixie' by Arundel, Louis, three adventurous friends embark on an exhilarating journey down the mighty Mississippi River. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With danger lurking around every bend, the Motor Boat Boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they reach their destination unscathed, or will their daring expedition take an unexpected turn? 

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
