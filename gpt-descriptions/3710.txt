Synopsis: 
'An Unprotected Female at the Pyramids' follows the journey of a young woman named Lady Julia, who embarks on a trip to Egypt with her aunt and cousin. Amidst the breathtaking backdrop of the Pyramids, Lady Julia finds herself caught in a web of romantic entanglements and unexpected encounters. As she navigates the complexities of love and society, Lady Julia's adventure takes an unexpected turn, leading her to question her own desires and the conventions of her time.

Genres: 
Romance, Travelogue, Victorian Literature

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Gender Roles, Cultural Exploration
