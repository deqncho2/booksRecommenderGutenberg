Synopsis: In 'Fee of the Frontier' by H. B. Fyfe, a young and ambitious explorer named Fee embarks on a perilous journey to the uncharted regions of the galaxy. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious beings, Fee's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. With each step closer to the unknown, he unravels secrets that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
