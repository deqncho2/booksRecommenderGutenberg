Synopsis: 

'Key-Notes of American Liberty' is a captivating collection that brings together the most significant speeches, proclamations, and acts of Congress from the birth of the United States to the present day. This anthology offers a unique glimpse into the evolution of American democracy, showcasing the powerful words and actions that have shaped the nation's history. From the stirring speeches of founding fathers to the landmark legislation that has defined civil rights, this book provides a comprehensive overview of the key moments that have shaped American liberty. 

Genres: Historical, Political, Anthology

Themes: Democracy, Freedom, Civil Rights, American History
