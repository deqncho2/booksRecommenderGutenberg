Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Yellow Flag: A Novel', the gripping tale continues as the protagonist, John, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Yellow Flag, a notorious criminal organization, John must navigate treacherous alliances, unexpected betrayals, and a race against time to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the final volume.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime and justice, Loyalty and betrayal, Deception and secrets
