Synopsis: In 'For God and Gold' by Julian Stafford Corbett, a young sailor finds himself caught in a web of treachery and adventure as he embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas. With his loyalty tested and his life on the line, he must navigate through dangerous waters, encountering ruthless pirates, hidden treasures, and a secret that could change the course of history. As he fights for survival and searches for answers, he discovers that the true meaning of honor and loyalty may not be what he once believed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Discovery
