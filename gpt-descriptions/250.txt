Synopsis: 

In 'A Brief History of the Internet: The Bright Side: The Dark Side', authors Hart and Fuller take readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of the internet, exploring both its positive and negative aspects. From its humble beginnings as a military project to its widespread impact on society, this book delves into the fascinating stories and key moments that shaped the internet as we know it today. With a careful balance of historical facts and thought-provoking analysis, the authors shed light on the bright side and the dark side of this revolutionary technology, leaving readers with a deeper understanding of its profound influence on our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, History

Themes: Technological advancement, Social impact, Ethical considerations
