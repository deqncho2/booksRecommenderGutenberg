Synopsis: In 'The Story of Gombi' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, a young boy named Gombi embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to a mysterious island. As he explores the island's enchanting landscapes and encounters its peculiar inhabitants, Gombi discovers secrets that will change his life forever. With danger lurking at every turn, Gombi must summon his courage and resourcefulness to navigate through the unknown and uncover the truth that lies hidden within the island's depths.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Courage
