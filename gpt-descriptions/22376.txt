Synopsis: In 'Love to the Uttermost: Expositions of John XIII.-XXI.', F.B. Meyer takes readers on a captivating journey through the latter half of the Gospel of John. With his insightful and thought-provoking expositions, Meyer delves into the profound teachings and events surrounding Jesus' final days on earth. From the Last Supper to the resurrection, this book offers a fresh perspective on the love, sacrifice, and ultimate victory of Jesus Christ.

Genres: Christian literature, Biblical commentary, Spiritual inspiration

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Redemption, Faith, Victory
