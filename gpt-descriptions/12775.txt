Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Darrin's Fourth Year at Annapolis: Headed for Graduation and the Big Cruise', Dave Darrin, a determined and ambitious midshipman, faces his final year at the United States Naval Academy. As he prepares for graduation and the highly anticipated Big Cruise, Dave finds himself confronted with unexpected challenges and tests of character. With his friends by his side, he must navigate the complexities of leadership, loyalty, and honor, all while striving to achieve his dreams of becoming a successful naval officer. Will Dave's unwavering determination be enough to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-Age, Military

Themes: Leadership, Loyalty, Honor, Perseverance
