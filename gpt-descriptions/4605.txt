Synopsis: 
Basil, a gripping novel by Wilkie Collins, follows the life of a young man named Basil Hallward, who becomes infatuated with a mysterious and captivating woman named Margaret Sherwin. As their paths intertwine, Basil finds himself caught in a web of secrets, deceit, and forbidden desires. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of hidden identities, scandalous affairs, and the consequences of obsession. Will Basil's love for Margaret lead him to salvation or destruction?

Genres: 
Gothic Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Obsession, Identity, Deception
