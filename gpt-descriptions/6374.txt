Synopsis: In "Princess Maritza" by Percy James Brebner, a captivating tale unfolds as a young princess finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. Maritza, a spirited and independent princess, must navigate the treacherous waters of courtly life while concealing her true identity. As she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of secrets and deception, Maritza's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to protect her kingdom and follow her heart, or will the forces working against her prove too powerful?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics
