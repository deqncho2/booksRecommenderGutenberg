Synopsis: In "Remarks on the Present System of Road Making," John Loudon McAdam presents a comprehensive analysis of the existing laws and methods of road construction, repair, and preservation. Drawing from his extensive practical experience, McAdam offers insightful observations and proposes improvements to the current system. This seventh edition includes a carefully revised text, an appendix, and a report from the Select Committee of the House of Commons, providing valuable insights into the state of road infrastructure in 19th-century England. With extracts from the evidence presented, McAdam's work sheds light on the challenges faced in maintaining roads and offers potential solutions to ensure the efficient use of road funds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Transportation, Infrastructure

Themes: Road construction, Road maintenance, Government policy, Efficiency, Infrastructure development
