Synopsis: In "About My Father's Business: Work Amidst the Sick, the Sad, and the Sorrowing," Thomas Archer takes readers on a captivating journey through the challenging yet rewarding world of his father's work. From the bustling hospital corridors to the quiet rooms of grieving families, Archer shares poignant and heartwarming stories that shed light on the profound impact his father's profession had on both patients and their loved ones. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the often overlooked aspects of healthcare and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Memoir, Non-fiction, Medical

Themes: Compassion, Healing, Resilience, Family, Human connection
