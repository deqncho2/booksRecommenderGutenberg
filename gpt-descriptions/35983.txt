Synopsis: In "Little Nettie; or, Home Sunshine," the lives of two young sisters, Nettie and Maggie, take an unexpected turn when their father's business fails, leaving them in dire circumstances. As they navigate the challenges of poverty and loss, the sisters find solace in their unwavering love for each other and their determination to create their own sunshine amidst the darkness. This heartwarming tale explores the power of family bonds, resilience, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Family, Resilience, Love, Poverty, Hope
