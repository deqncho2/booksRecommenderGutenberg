Synopsis: In the small town of Silverton, secrets run deep and the past refuses to stay buried. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the town's residents find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and deception. As the truth slowly unravels, long-held grudges and hidden desires come to light, threatening to tear the community apart. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the residents of Silverton must confront their own demons in order to survive.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
