Synopsis:
'The King Country; or, Explorations in New Zealand: A Narrative of 600 Miles of Travel Through Maoriland' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the uncharted and mysterious landscapes of New Zealand. Join the intrepid explorer, J.H. Kerry-Nicholls, as he embarks on a daring expedition, encountering breathtaking natural wonders, indigenous Maori tribes, and unexpected challenges along the way. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this narrative offers a thrilling exploration of a land untouched by modern civilization.

Genres:
Adventure, Travelogue, Historical

Themes:
Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Natural Beauty
