Synopsis: 
"The Golden Threshold" is a collection of enchanting poems that transport readers to the vibrant world of India, capturing the essence of its rich culture, history, and mythology. Sarojini Naidu's lyrical verses paint vivid pictures of love, longing, and the beauty of nature, while also delving into themes of freedom, feminism, and the power of the human spirit. Through her evocative words, Naidu invites readers on a poetic journey that celebrates the resilience and indomitable spirit of the Indian people. 

Genres: Poetry, Literature, Cultural

Themes: Love, Nature, Freedom, Feminism, Indian culture
