Synopsis: 

'Songs of the West: Folk Songs of Devon & Cornwall Collected from the Mouths of the People' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich musical heritage of the West of England. Sabine Baring-Gould, Frederick William Bussell, and Henry Fleetwood Sheppard have meticulously collected and preserved a treasure trove of traditional folk songs, capturing the essence of the region's culture and history. From haunting ballads to lively sea shanties, this collection immerses readers in the captivating melodies and poignant lyrics that have been passed down through generations. 

Genres: Folklore, Music, History

Themes: Cultural preservation, Oral tradition, Regional identity
