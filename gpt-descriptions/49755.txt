Synopsis: 
'Brief Diversions: Being Tales, Travesties and Epigrams' by J. B. Priestley is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking stories that will transport readers to various settings and time periods. From humorous tales that will leave you chuckling to poignant stories that will tug at your heartstrings, this collection offers a diverse range of narratives that are sure to captivate and entertain. Whether it's a whimsical adventure through the streets of Paris or a thought-provoking exploration of human nature, each story in this collection is a delightful diversion that will leave readers wanting more.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor, Drama

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Social commentary
