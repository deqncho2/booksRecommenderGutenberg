Synopsis: 

In 'Five Children and It' by E. Nesbit, five siblings stumble upon a magical creature with the power to grant them one wish each day. However, they soon discover that their wishes don't always turn out as expected, leading to a series of misadventures and unexpected consequences. As the children navigate their newfound powers, they learn valuable lessons about the importance of responsibility, contentment, and the true meaning of happiness.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Wishes, Responsibility, Contentment, Happiness
