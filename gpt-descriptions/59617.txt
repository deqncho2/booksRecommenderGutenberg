Synopsis: 
When Wilderness was King: A Tale of the Illinois Country takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Illinois Country in the early 18th century. The story follows the adventures of a young frontiersman named Pierre, who finds himself caught in the midst of a fierce struggle for power and survival. As he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters Native American tribes, and faces off against ruthless traders, Pierre must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale immerses readers in a world where danger lurks at every turn and the wilderness reigns supreme.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Conflict, Cultural Encounter
