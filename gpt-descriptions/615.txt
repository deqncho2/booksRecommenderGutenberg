Synopsis: In the epic poem 'Orlando Furioso' by Lodovico Ariosto, readers are transported to a world of chivalry, love, and adventure. The story revolves around the brave knight Orlando, who embarks on a quest to win the heart of his beloved Angelica. However, as Orlando's journey unfolds, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, magical enchantments, and fierce battles. With a cast of colorful characters, including other knights, sorcerers, and mythical creatures, the poem takes readers on a thrilling and unpredictable ride through a fantastical realm.

Genres: Epic poetry, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and desire, Heroism and chivalry, Fate and destiny, Madness and obsession
