Synopsis: In 'Obiter Dicta: Second Series', Augustine Birrell takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and insightful essays. With his signature charm and wit, Birrell explores a wide range of topics, from literature and politics to everyday observations and personal anecdotes. Through his keen observations and clever musings, Birrell invites readers to ponder the complexities of life and society, all while being entertained by his delightful prose.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Humor

Themes: Literature, Politics, Society, Observations, Personal Reflections
