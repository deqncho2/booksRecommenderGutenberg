Synopsis: In 'The Divine Fire' by Sinclair, May, a talented young pianist named Gabriel Stanton finds himself drawn into a world of passion, ambition, and forbidden love when he meets the enigmatic and captivating Rosamund Everard. As Gabriel's musical abilities soar to new heights under Rosamund's guidance, their intense connection becomes a catalyst for both artistic brilliance and personal turmoil. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant and decadent Parisian art scene of the late 19th century, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices one must make for their art and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Artistic passion, Forbidden love, Ambition, Sacrifice
