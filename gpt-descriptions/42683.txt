Synopsis: In 'God: Outlines of the new theology, based on facts, science, nature, reason, intuition, revelation and common sense', author T. D. Curtis presents a thought-provoking exploration of theology that challenges traditional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the concept of God. Through a combination of scientific evidence, philosophical reasoning, and personal insights, Curtis invites readers on a journey to reconsider their understanding of the divine. This book delves into the mysteries of existence, the nature of consciousness, and the interconnectedness of all things, ultimately aiming to inspire a deeper connection with the divine and a more inclusive understanding of spirituality.

Genres: Theology, Philosophy, Science

Themes: Reimagining God, Science and Spirituality, Consciousness and Existence
