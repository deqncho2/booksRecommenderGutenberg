Synopsis: In 'The Curse of Pocahontas' by Gilman, Wenona, a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious curse that has haunted her family for generations. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestry, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of ancient legends, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate through treacherous landscapes and confront her own fears to break the curse before it consumes her and those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Identity, Courage, Supernatural forces
