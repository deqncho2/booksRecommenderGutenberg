Synopsis: In 'The Works of Robert G. Ingersoll, Volume VIII.: Interviews', Robert Green Ingersoll engages in a series of captivating and thought-provoking interviews. From discussing religion and morality to exploring the nature of happiness and the pursuit of truth, Ingersoll fearlessly challenges conventional beliefs and offers refreshing perspectives on various topics. Through his eloquent and persuasive arguments, he invites readers to question their own beliefs and embrace the power of reason and critical thinking. This collection of interviews is a testament to Ingersoll's intellectual prowess and his unwavering commitment to championing freedom of thought and individual autonomy.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Interviews

Themes: Religion, Morality, Reason, Freedom of Thought, Individual Autonomy
