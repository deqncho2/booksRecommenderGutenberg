Synopsis: 
In "Mussolini as revealed in his political speeches (November 1914-August 1923)" by Benito Mussolini, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the mind of one of history's most controversial figures. Through a collection of his powerful speeches, Mussolini unveils his charismatic and persuasive personality, providing a unique insight into his rise to power and the ideologies that shaped his political career. From his early days as a socialist to his transformation into a fascist leader, this book offers a compelling exploration of Mussolini's rhetoric and the impact it had on the Italian people during a tumultuous period in history.

Genres: 
Biography, Political Science, History

Themes: 
Leadership, Nationalism, Ideology, Propaganda
