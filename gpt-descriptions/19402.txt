Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Reward' by Burt L. Standish, the beloved character Frank Merriwell finds himself facing a new set of challenges as he navigates the world of college athletics. With his reputation on the line, Frank must prove his worth both on and off the field, all while uncovering a mysterious secret that threatens to unravel everything he has worked for. As the stakes rise, Frank's determination and integrity are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Integrity, Perseverance, Loyalty
