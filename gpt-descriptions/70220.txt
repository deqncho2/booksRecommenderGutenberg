Synopsis: In "Mary Derwent: A Tale of Wyoming and Mohawk Valleys in 1778," Ann S. Stephens takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed landscapes of Wyoming and Mohawk Valleys during the tumultuous year of 1778. Mary Derwent, a young and spirited woman, finds herself caught in the midst of the American Revolution, facing danger, love, and betrayal. As she navigates the treacherous paths of war, Mary's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to survive the chaos and find her true purpose in this turbulent time?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: War and Revolution, Love and Betrayal, Resilience and Determination
