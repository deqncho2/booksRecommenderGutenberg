Synopsis: In "The White Morning: A Novel of the Power of the German Women in Wartime," Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of German women during a time of war. Set against the backdrop of World War I, the story follows a group of resilient women who find themselves facing extraordinary challenges and discovering their own strength in the face of adversity. As they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and sacrifice, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing the indomitable spirit of the German women during this tumultuous period. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Female Empowerment, Love and Loss, Sacrifice, The Impact of War
