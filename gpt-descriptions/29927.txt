Synopsis:
'With Manchesters in the East' by Gerald B. Hurst takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Set in the Eastern theater of the war, the story follows a group of brave soldiers from the Manchester Regiment as they navigate the treacherous landscapes and face the horrors of battle. Amidst the chaos and danger, friendships are forged, love is found, and the true strength of the human spirit is tested. As the soldiers fight for their lives and their beliefs, they must confront their own fears and discover the meaning of sacrifice.

Genres:
War fiction, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Courage, Brotherhood, Love, Sacrifice
