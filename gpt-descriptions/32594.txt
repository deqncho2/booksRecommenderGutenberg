Synopsis: In 'Stalemate' by Basil Wells, a small town is suddenly cut off from the rest of the world by an impenetrable force field. As the townspeople struggle to survive and find a way out, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling battle for survival. With each passing day, the mystery deepens, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind this inexplicable phenomenon.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Isolation, Survival, Human Nature
