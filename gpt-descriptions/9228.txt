Synopsis: In "The Christmas Banquet," Hawthorne takes readers on a mysterious journey to a grand holiday feast, where a group of individuals with unique backgrounds and secrets gather. As the night unfolds, tensions rise, and unexpected revelations come to light, leaving the guests questioning their own identities and the true nature of the gathering. With Hawthorne's signature blend of suspense and psychological exploration, this story delves into the complexities of human nature and the power of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Deception, Human nature
