Synopsis: In 'The Little Green Goblin', a mischievous creature wreaks havoc in a small town, leaving the residents puzzled and frightened. As the townspeople band together to uncover the truth behind the little green goblin's antics, they discover a surprising connection between the creature and their own lives. With a blend of mystery, humor, and heartwarming moments, this enchanting tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Acceptance, Self-discovery
