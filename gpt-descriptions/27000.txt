Synopsis: In "What the Moon Saw: and Other Tales," Hans Christian Andersen takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking stories. From a magical moonlit encounter to a heartwarming tale of friendship, each story offers a unique glimpse into the human experience. With Andersen's signature blend of whimsy and depth, these tales will transport readers to a world where dreams come alive and the ordinary becomes extraordinary.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Transformation, Human nature
