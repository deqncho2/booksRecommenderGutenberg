Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies with Marshal Foch; or, The Closing Days of the Great World War', young allies Jack and Frank find themselves thrust into the heart of the action as they join forces with the legendary Marshal Foch. As the Great World War nears its end, the boys must navigate treacherous battlefields, outsmart cunning enemies, and face the ultimate test of their courage. With danger lurking at every turn, their loyalty and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test. Will they emerge victorious and help bring an end to the devastating conflict?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Loyalty, Coming of Age
