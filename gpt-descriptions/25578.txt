Synopsis: 
In 'The Sunbridge Girls at Six Star Ranch', four adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling summer adventure at a remote ranch. As they navigate the challenges of ranch life, they uncover a hidden mystery that puts their courage and friendship to the test. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
