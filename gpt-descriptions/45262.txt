Synopsis: 
In 'The Bungalow Boys in the Great Northwest' by Goldfrap, John Henry, two adventurous brothers find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the untamed wilderness of the Great Northwest. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the brothers must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Brotherhood, Courage
