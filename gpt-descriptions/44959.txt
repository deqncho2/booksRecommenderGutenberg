Synopsis: In 'Tales of My Time, Vol. 2 (of 3): Who Is She? [concluded]; The Young Reformers' by Scargill, William Pitt, a mysterious woman enters the lives of the characters, leaving them questioning her true identity and intentions. As the story unfolds, the young reformers find themselves caught in a web of secrets and intrigue, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With each revelation, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigma surrounding this enigmatic woman, eagerly anticipating the final resolution.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Change
