Synopsis: In 'John Smith, U.S.A.', Field, Eugene takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of an ordinary American man named John Smith. From his humble beginnings to his unexpected encounters with fame and fortune, this novel explores the triumphs and tribulations of a man who embodies the American dream. As John navigates through various challenges and experiences, readers are drawn into a tale that is both heartwarming and thought-provoking. With its richly developed characters and engaging narrative, 'John Smith, U.S.A.' offers a unique perspective on the complexities of life and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: The American dream, Identity, Success and failure
