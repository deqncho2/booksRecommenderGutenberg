Synopsis: In 'The Aeroplane Speaks' by H. Barber, a young engineer stumbles upon a mysterious invention that has the power to revolutionize the world of aviation. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where his life and the fate of the invention hang in the balance. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Invention, Exploration, Danger, Intrigue
