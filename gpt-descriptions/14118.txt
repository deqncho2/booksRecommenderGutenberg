Synopsis: In 'Legend of Moulin Huet' by Lizzie A. Freeth, a young artist named Emily embarks on a journey to the picturesque cliffs of Guernsey, where she discovers a hidden world of enchantment and mystery. As she delves deeper into the secrets of Moulin Huet, Emily becomes entangled in a captivating tale that intertwines past and present, love and loss, and the power of art to transcend time. With each stroke of her paintbrush, Emily uncovers a haunting legend that will forever change her perception of reality.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Timelessness, Love and loss
