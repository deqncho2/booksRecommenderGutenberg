Synopsis: In 'Way of the Lawless' by Max Brand, a mysterious gunslinger named Anthony Bard arrives in a small western town, stirring up trouble and leaving a trail of unanswered questions in his wake. As the townspeople become entangled in Bard's enigmatic past, they must confront their own secrets and face the consequences of their actions. With a gripping blend of suspense, action, and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Consequences
