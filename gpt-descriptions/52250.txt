Synopsis: 
In 'A Nurse's Life in War and Peace', Eleanor Constance takes us on a captivating journey through her experiences as a nurse during times of both conflict and tranquility. From the chaos of the battlefield to the serenity of the hospital ward, she shares the triumphs, heartbreaks, and moments of profound humanity that shaped her career. With vivid descriptions and poignant anecdotes, this memoir offers a unique perspective on the sacrifices and resilience of those who dedicate their lives to caring for others.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: Courage, Compassion, Resilience, Humanitarianism
