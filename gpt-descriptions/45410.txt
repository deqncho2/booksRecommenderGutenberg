Synopsis: In 'Composition: A series of exercises in art structure for the use of students and teachers', Arthur W. Dow presents a captivating exploration of the principles of art composition. Through a series of exercises and examples, Dow guides readers on a journey to discover the secrets behind creating visually harmonious and impactful artworks. From the arrangement of lines and shapes to the use of color and texture, this book offers invaluable insights into the art of composition, inspiring both students and teachers to unlock their creative potential.

Genres: Art, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Art composition, Visual harmony, Creative expression
