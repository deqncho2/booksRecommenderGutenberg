Synopsis: In 'Joe Leslie's Wife; or, a Skeleton in the Closet' by Robertson, Alexander, M.D., a seemingly ordinary marriage takes a dark turn when a shocking secret threatens to unravel their lives. As the truth slowly emerges, the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will forever change their futures. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Consequences of one's actions, Identity and self-discovery
