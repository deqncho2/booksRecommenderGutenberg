Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, find themselves in the heart of the nation's capital, Washington D.C., where they stumble upon a mysterious secret that could have far-reaching consequences. As they navigate through the city's iconic landmarks and encounter intriguing characters, the twins must use their wit and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and protect those they care about. With thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, "The Bobbsey Twins in Washington" takes readers on a captivating journey through the nation's political center.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family, Friendship, Patriotism
