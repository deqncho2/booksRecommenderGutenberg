Synopsis: 

In 'Readings in Money and Banking: Selected and Adapted' by Phillips, Chester Arthur, embark on a captivating journey through the intricate world of finance and economics. This thought-provoking collection of readings delves into the complexities of money, banking, and the global financial system, offering a comprehensive exploration of the subject. From historical perspectives to contemporary issues, the book presents a diverse range of perspectives that will challenge and enlighten readers. With engaging narratives and insightful analysis, 'Readings in Money and Banking' is a must-read for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the forces that shape our economic landscape.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Finance

Themes: Money, Banking, Global Financial System, Economic Perspectives
