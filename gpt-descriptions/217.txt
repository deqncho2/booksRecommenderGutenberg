Synopsis:
'Sons and Lovers' follows the life of Paul Morel, a young man torn between his love for his overbearing mother and his desire for independence. Set in a coal-mining town in England, the novel explores the complexities of family relationships, the struggles of self-discovery, and the power of passion and longing. As Paul navigates his way through love, art, and the constraints of society, he must confront the conflicting forces that shape his identity and ultimately determine his fate.

Genres:
Psychological fiction, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes:
Oedipal complex, Love and desire, Individuality and conformity, Class and social expectations
