Synopsis: 
In "Black Bass: Where to catch them in quantity within an hour's ride from New York," Charles Barker Bradford takes us on an exciting journey through the hidden fishing spots just a stone's throw away from the bustling city. With his expert knowledge and captivating storytelling, Bradford unveils the secret locations where black bass thrive, promising an unforgettable angling adventure. As he shares his personal experiences and tips, readers will be drawn into the thrill of the chase, eagerly anticipating the next cast and the possibility of reeling in a trophy-sized bass. Whether you're an avid angler or simply seeking an escape from the urban jungle, this guide will leave you yearning to explore the untamed waters that lie just beyond the city limits.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor Recreation, Travel

Themes: Fishing, Nature exploration, Urban escapism
