Synopsis: In 'The Study of Elementary Electricity and Magnetism by Experiment', Thomas M. St. John takes readers on an electrifying journey through two hundred captivating experiments. With simple, home-made apparatus, St. John explores the fascinating world of electricity and magnetism, unraveling their mysteries and showcasing their practical applications. From static electricity to electromagnetism, readers will be enthralled by the hands-on experiments and the profound insights they provide. This book is a must-read for anyone curious about the fundamental forces that shape our modern world.

Genres: Science, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration of electricity and magnetism, Practical applications of scientific principles
