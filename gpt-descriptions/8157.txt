Synopsis: 

Esther Waters, a young and innocent servant girl, finds herself caught in a whirlwind of love, betrayal, and societal expectations in the bustling streets of Victorian London. As she navigates through the trials and tribulations of her life, Esther's resilience and determination are put to the test, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, George Moore paints a vivid portrait of the struggles faced by women in a society bound by rigid conventions and class divisions.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Realism

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Class, Gender Roles, Morality, Self-Identity
