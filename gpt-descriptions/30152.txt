Synopsis: In 'Narcissistic and Psychopathic Leaders', Samuel Vaknin delves into the dark and captivating world of power-hungry individuals who possess an insatiable need for admiration and control. Through a thought-provoking exploration of their manipulative tactics and destructive behaviors, Vaknin unveils the chilling reality of these leaders and the havoc they wreak on society. Drawing from extensive research and real-life examples, this book offers a fascinating and unsettling glimpse into the minds of narcissistic and psychopathic leaders, leaving readers questioning the true nature of power and its consequences.

Genres: Psychology, Political Science, Sociology

Themes: Narcissism, Psychopathy, Leadership, Manipulation, Power Dynamics
