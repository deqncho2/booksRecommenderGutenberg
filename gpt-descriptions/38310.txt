Synopsis: In 'The Wolves of God, and Other Fey Stories', Algernon Blackwood and Wilfred Wilson take readers on a captivating journey into the realm of the fey, where ancient forces and supernatural beings dwell. From encounters with mischievous woodland creatures to eerie encounters in haunted houses, these stories explore the mysterious and often unsettling relationship between humans and the otherworldly. Prepare to be enthralled by tales that blur the line between reality and the supernatural, leaving readers questioning what lies beyond the veil of our everyday existence.

Genres: Fantasy, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: The supernatural, the power of nature, the thin veil between reality and the otherworldly
