Synopsis: In "Me and Nobbles" by Amy Le Feuvre, a young girl named Dolly finds solace in her imaginary friend, Nobbles, after facing a series of disappointments and challenges. As Dolly's world becomes increasingly difficult, Nobbles becomes her confidant and source of strength. Together, they embark on a heartwarming journey that explores the power of imagination, friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Resilience
