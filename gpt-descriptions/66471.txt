Synopsis: 
In 'Like Another Helen' by Sydney C. Grier, a young woman named Helen finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden diary that reveals a dark secret from the past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Helen becomes entangled in a thrilling journey that takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the picturesque landscapes of Europe. With each clue she uncovers, Helen realizes that the truth she seeks may not only change her own life but also rewrite history as we know it.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
