Synopsis: 

In "Remarks on the management, or rather, the mis-management of woods, plantations, and hedge-row timber," author J. West delves into the fascinating world of forestry and unveils the shocking truth behind the mismanagement of these precious resources. Through a series of eye-opening anecdotes and insightful observations, West exposes the detrimental practices that have led to the decline of woods, plantations, and hedge-row timber. From the devastating impact of improper harvesting techniques to the neglect of essential maintenance, this thought-provoking work sheds light on the urgent need for better management strategies. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Environmental Science, Conservation

Themes: Forestry management, Environmental sustainability, Conservation awareness
