Synopsis: 

In "Heroes of the Great Conflict; Life and Services of William Farrar: Smith, Major General, United States Volunteer in the Civil War," author James Harrison Wilson delves into the remarkable life and military career of General William Farrar Smith. From his early days as a West Point cadet to his crucial role in the American Civil War, Smith's journey is one of courage, leadership, and unwavering dedication to his country. Wilson's vivid storytelling brings to life the epic battles, strategic maneuvers, and personal sacrifices that shaped Smith's legacy as a true hero of the Great Conflict.

Genres: Biography, History, Military

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Sacrifice
