Synopsis: In "The Putnam Hall Cadets; or, Good Times in School and Out," follow the thrilling adventures of a group of young cadets as they navigate the challenges of school life and the excitement that awaits them beyond the classroom walls. From daring escapades to unexpected friendships, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for these spirited cadets.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, School

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Growth, Camaraderie
