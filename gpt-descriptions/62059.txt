Synopsis: 
'The Irish Peasant; Or, The History of Peter Lacy and His Wife Susan' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of Peter and Susan, an Irish couple facing numerous challenges and hardships. Set against the backdrop of rural Ireland, this gripping tale explores their struggles, triumphs, and the enduring power of love and resilience. As Peter and Susan navigate the complexities of their world, readers will be drawn into a story filled with heartache, hope, and the indomitable spirit of the human soul.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Resilience, Social Injustice, Class Divide
