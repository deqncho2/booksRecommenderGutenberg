Synopsis: 
In 'Test Pilot' by Collins, James, readers are taken on a thrilling journey into the high-stakes world of aviation. The story follows a talented and daring test pilot who pushes the limits of both man and machine, facing exhilarating challenges and heart-stopping moments in the sky. As he navigates through dangerous missions and personal struggles, the protagonist must confront his own fears and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With its gripping narrative and intense aerial sequences, 'Test Pilot' offers an adrenaline-fueled adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Risk-taking, Confronting fears
