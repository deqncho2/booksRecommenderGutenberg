Synopsis: In 'Mr. Punch's History of the Great War', Charles L. Graves takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years of World War I. Through the lens of the iconic Mr. Punch, a mischievous puppet character, Graves presents a unique and satirical perspective on the events that shaped the war. From the trenches to the home front, this book offers a blend of humor, tragedy, and poignant observations that will keep readers engrossed from start to finish.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Satire, War Literature

Themes: War, Humor, Irony, Social Commentary
