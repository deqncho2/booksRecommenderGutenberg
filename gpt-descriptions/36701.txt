Synopsis: 
In 'Graceful Riding: A Pocket Manual for Equestrians', Samuel C. Wayte takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of horseback riding. With a wealth of knowledge and experience, Wayte delves into the intricacies of mastering the art of riding, offering invaluable tips and techniques to enhance both skill and style. From perfecting posture and balance to understanding the subtle communication between horse and rider, this pocket manual is a treasure trove of wisdom for equestrians of all levels. Whether you're a novice rider or a seasoned equestrian, 'Graceful Riding' is sure to inspire and empower you to reach new heights in your equestrian pursuits.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Self-help

Themes: Equestrianism, Skill development, Communication with animals
