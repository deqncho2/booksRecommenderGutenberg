Synopsis:
In 'K' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a mysterious letter arrives at the doorstep of a wealthy family, setting off a chain of events that will test their loyalty, unravel dark secrets, and ultimately lead to a shocking revelation. As the family members grapple with their own suspicions and hidden agendas, they must confront the enigmatic figure known only as 'K' who seems to hold the key to their fate. With its gripping suspense and intricate web of deceit, 'K' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Family secrets, Loyalty, Deception
