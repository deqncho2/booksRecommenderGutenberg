Synopsis: In "In Honour Bound: An Original Play, in One Act," a chain of unexpected events unfolds as a group of characters find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, misunderstandings, and comedic mishaps. Set against the backdrop of a lively comedy, this play explores the complexities of human relationships and the lengths people will go to protect their honor. As the plot unfolds, the characters must navigate their way through a series of hilarious and unpredictable situations, all while trying to maintain their dignity and uphold their sense of duty.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Farce

Themes: Honor, Deception, Miscommunication, Relationships
