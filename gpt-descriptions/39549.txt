Synopsis: In 'The Carved Lions' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Gwendoline discovers a hidden secret within the walls of her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she uncovers a world of enchantment and danger, where the past intertwines with the present in unexpected ways. With the help of a peculiar new friend, Gwendoline must navigate a treacherous path to unlock the truth and protect her family's legacy. This captivating tale of adventure and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Coming of age, Magic and enchantment
