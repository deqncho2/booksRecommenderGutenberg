Synopsis: In 'Dave Dashaway and His Giant Airship; or, A Marvellous Trip Across the Atlantic', young Dave Dashaway finds himself embarking on an extraordinary adventure when he is given the opportunity to join a daring crew on a journey across the Atlantic in a massive airship. As they soar through the skies, facing unexpected challenges and encountering new friends and foes, Dave's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles and fulfill his dreams of flying high?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Dreams and Aspirations
