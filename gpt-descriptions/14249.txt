Synopsis: In 'Half A Chance' by Frederic Stewart Isham, a young man named Jack finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a series of clues that lead him on a thrilling adventure across continents, where he must outsmart cunning adversaries and confront his own fears. With time running out, Jack must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Redemption
