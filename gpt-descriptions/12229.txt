Synopsis: 

In 'Who Goes There?: The Story of a Spy in the Civil War', author B. K. Benson takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of espionage during the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of a divided nation, this gripping tale follows the life of a spy as they navigate treacherous landscapes, infiltrate enemy lines, and risk everything for the cause they believe in. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who can be trusted and what secrets lie beneath the surface. As the war rages on, the spy's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Spy Thriller, War Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Sacrifice, Identity
