Synopsis: In 'The Scandalized Martians' by Marmor, Arnold, a group of Martians stumble upon a shocking secret that threatens to unravel their entire society. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they must confront their own beliefs and face the consequences of their actions. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Society and conformity, Morality and ethics, Identity and self-discovery
