Synopsis: 

In 'True Detective Stories from the Archives of the Pinkertons', Moffett Cleveland takes readers on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of crime-solving in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Drawing from the real-life cases of the renowned Pinkerton Detective Agency, this collection of gripping tales introduces us to a diverse cast of detectives and criminals, each with their own secrets and motivations. From high-stakes heists to mysterious disappearances, these stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats as they unravel the intricate web of clues and follow the relentless pursuit of justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Crime-solving, Justice, Morality, Intrigue
