Synopsis: In 'A Little Rebel: A Novel' by Duchess, we are introduced to a young woman named Amelia, who finds herself trapped in a world of strict societal expectations and suffocating traditions. Fueled by her fiery spirit and determination, Amelia embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion, challenging the norms of her time. As she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her own happiness, Amelia's courage and resilience inspire those around her, ultimately leading to a transformative revolution that will change her life and the lives of those she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Individuality, Freedom, Love, Rebellion
