Synopsis: 

'Asparagus, its culture for home use and for market: A practical treatise on the planting, cultivation, harvesting, marketing, and preserving of asparagus, with notes on its history' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the fascinating world of asparagus. Written by F. M. Hexamer, this practical treatise offers valuable insights into the planting, cultivation, harvesting, marketing, and preserving of asparagus, while also providing intriguing historical notes about this versatile vegetable. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Culinary

Themes: Horticulture, Sustainable farming, Culinary history
