Synopsis: In 'Mr. Marx's Secret' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is tasked with uncovering the truth behind a secretive man named Mr. Marx. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic life of Mr. Marx, the detective discovers a series of unexpected connections and hidden secrets that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Uncovering the truth, Intrigue and suspense
