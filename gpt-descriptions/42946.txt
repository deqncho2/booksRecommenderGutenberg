Synopsis: In 'Live Toys; Or, Anecdotes of Our Four-Legged and Other Pets', Emma Davenport invites readers into a captivating world where animals come to life and share their extraordinary tales. From mischievous cats to loyal dogs, and even talking parrots, this collection of anecdotes will transport you into a realm where animals possess unique personalities and unexpected adventures unfold. With heartwarming and humorous stories, 'Live Toys' offers a delightful glimpse into the secret lives of our beloved pets.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Stories, Short Stories

Themes: Animal intelligence, Human-animal bond, Adventure, Humor
