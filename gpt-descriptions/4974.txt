Synopsis: 

In 'Australia Twice Traversed: The Romance of Exploration', Ernest Giles takes readers on a thrilling journey through the uncharted territories of Central South Australia and Western Australia. Through the compilation of journals from five exploring expeditions conducted between 1872 and 1876, Giles paints a vivid picture of the challenges, triumphs, and dangers faced by these intrepid explorers. From treacherous terrains to encounters with indigenous peoples, the narrative captures the spirit of adventure and the relentless pursuit of discovery that defined this era of exploration.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Indigenous cultures, Perseverance
