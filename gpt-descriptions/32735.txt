Synopsis: In 'Forsyte's Retreat' by Winston K. Marks, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a daring expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With each discovery, the stakes rise higher, and he must navigate treacherous paths to protect his findings and his own life. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth hidden within the ruins.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
