Synopsis: In "The Mother's Recompense" by Edith Wharton, we are introduced to Kate Clephane, a woman who has spent her life sacrificing her own desires for the sake of her son. However, when her son grows up and becomes a successful playwright, Kate finds herself at a crossroads. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Kate must confront the consequences of her choices and discover if there is still a chance for her own happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Sacrifice, Motherhood, Identity, Ambition, Love
