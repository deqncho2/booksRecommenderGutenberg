Synopsis: In "Lady Betty Across the Water," a young woman named Lady Betty embarks on a thrilling journey across the Atlantic Ocean, leaving behind her comfortable life in England. As she navigates the challenges of a new country and encounters unexpected adventures, Lady Betty's courage and resilience are put to the test. With secrets to uncover and a mysterious past to unravel, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Exploration, Resilience
