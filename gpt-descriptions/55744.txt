Synopsis: In 'The Exclusives (vol. 1 of 3)' by Charlotte Campbell, Lady Bury, a group of young women at a prestigious boarding school form a secret society known as The Exclusives. As they navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and power, their secrets and ambitions threaten to unravel their carefully constructed world. With scandalous secrets, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of privilege and the lengths people will go to protect their status.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Power dynamics, Friendship, Secrets, Betrayal
