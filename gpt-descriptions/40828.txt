Synopsis: In "The Closed Book: Concerning the Secret of the Borgias," Le Queux takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark secrets of the infamous Borgia family. When a mysterious book resurfaces, it holds the key to a hidden secret that could shake the foundations of history. As a determined protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they become entangled in a web of danger, deceit, and treachery. With each turn of the page, the truth becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever before. Will they uncover the secret before it consumes them?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Historical intrigue, Betrayal, Power struggle
