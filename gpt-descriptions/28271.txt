Synopsis: In 'Seven Miles to Arden' by Ruth Sawyer, a young girl named Eileen embarks on a journey to find her missing father, who mysteriously disappeared years ago. Determined and resourceful, Eileen sets off on a perilous adventure through the enchanting Irish countryside, encountering magical creatures, ancient legends, and unexpected allies along the way. As she unravels the secrets of her father's disappearance, Eileen discovers her own strength and resilience, ultimately leading her to a revelation that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family bonds, Self-discovery, Irish folklore
