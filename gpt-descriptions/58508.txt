Synopsis:
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Pelham Grenville Wodehouse' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works by the renowned author, P. G. Wodehouse. This compilation includes a wide range of humorous and witty stories, showcasing Wodehouse's unparalleled talent for creating memorable characters and entertaining plots. From the misadventures of the lovable Bertie Wooster and his resourceful valet Jeeves, to the escapades of the eccentric Blandings Castle residents, this index offers a delightful journey through the whimsical world of Wodehouse's imagination.

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes:
Social class, Love and romance, Miscommunication, British society
