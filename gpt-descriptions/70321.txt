Synopsis: In "The Riddle Club at Sunrise Beach: How they toured to the shore, what happened on the sand and how they solved the mystery of Rattlesnake Island" by Alice Dale Hardy, a group of young friends embarks on an exciting adventure to the beach. As they explore the sandy shores, they stumble upon a mysterious island that holds a secret waiting to be unraveled. With their clever minds and teamwork, the Riddle Club sets out to solve the enigma of Rattlesnake Island, encountering unexpected twists and turns along the way.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Problem-solving, Exploration
