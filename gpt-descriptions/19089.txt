Synopsis: In 'A Pagan of the Hills' by Charles Neville Buck, a young man named David finds himself torn between his love for a woman and his loyalty to his family's traditions. Set in the picturesque hills of rural America, David embarks on a journey of self-discovery, encountering a mysterious group of pagans who challenge his beliefs and force him to question the values he holds dear. As he delves deeper into their world, David must confront his own desires and make a choice that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Love and Sacrifice, Self-Discovery
