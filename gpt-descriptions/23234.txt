Synopsis: 
'Semiramis, and Other Plays' by Olive Tilford Dargan is a captivating collection of plays that takes readers on a journey through various historical and mythical settings. From the powerful and enigmatic Queen Semiramis to the tragic tale of a young woman caught in the midst of the Salem witch trials, these plays delve into the complexities of human nature, love, power, and the pursuit of justice. Each play offers a unique and thought-provoking exploration of the human condition, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets and revelations that lie within the pages.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Power and ambition, Love and betrayal, Justice and morality
