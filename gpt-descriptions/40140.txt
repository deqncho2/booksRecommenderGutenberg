Synopsis: 
"On the Face of the Waters: A Tale of the Mutiny" by Flora Annie Webster Steel is a gripping historical novel set during the Indian Mutiny of 1857. The story follows the lives of two British sisters, Helen and Kate, who find themselves caught in the midst of the rebellion. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn India, they encounter love, betrayal, and the struggle for survival. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this tale of adventure and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: War and Conflict, Colonialism, Resilience, Love and Betrayal
