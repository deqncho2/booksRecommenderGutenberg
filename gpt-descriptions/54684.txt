Synopsis: In the small Sicilian village of Aci Trezza, a mysterious house stands by a medlar tree, harboring secrets that have been passed down through generations. As the lives of the villagers intertwine, love, betrayal, and tragedy unfold within the walls of this enigmatic dwelling. The House by the Medlar-Tree is a captivating tale that explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of hidden desires.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Historical

Themes: Love and passion, Family dynamics, Secrets and their consequences
