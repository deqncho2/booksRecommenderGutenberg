Synopsis: In "The Old Church Clock" by Richard Parkinson, a small town is haunted by the mysterious ticking of an ancient church clock. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the clock's eerie presence, they uncover a dark secret that threatens to unravel their lives. With suspense and intrigue, this gripping tale explores the power of time and the hidden depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Time, Obsession, Secrets
