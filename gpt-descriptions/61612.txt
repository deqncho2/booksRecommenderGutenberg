Synopsis: In "The Temples and Ritual of Asklepios at Epidauros and Athens: Two Lectures Delivered at the Royal Institution of Great Britain," Richard Caton takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient temples and rituals dedicated to the Greek god of healing, Asklepios. From the renowned sanctuary of Epidauros to the bustling city of Athens, Caton explores the fascinating history, architecture, and religious practices surrounding these sacred sites. Through vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, he unveils the secrets and significance of these temples, shedding light on the profound impact they had on the lives of the ancient Greeks.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient Greek religion, Healing practices, Sacred architecture
