Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 141, May 22, 1915: The duplicate night', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious duplicate night. As the city plunges into chaos and confusion, Carter must unravel the truth behind this inexplicable phenomenon before it consumes everything in its path. With time running out, he must rely on his keen intellect and unparalleled investigative skills to solve the enigma and restore order. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Chaos
