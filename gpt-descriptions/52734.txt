Synopsis: In "The Scourge of God: A Romance of Religious Persecution," Bloundelle-Burton takes readers on a captivating journey through a tumultuous period of history. Set against the backdrop of religious persecution, this gripping tale follows the lives of two individuals from different backgrounds who find themselves entangled in a web of danger, love, and sacrifice. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of religious intolerance, their paths converge, leading to unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching choices. With its rich historical detail and compelling characters, this novel explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of faith in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Religious Persecution, Love and Sacrifice, Resilience, Faith
