Synopsis: In "The Excavations of Roman Baths at Bath," Charles Edward Davis takes readers on a captivating journey through time as he uncovers the secrets of the ancient Roman baths in the city of Bath. With meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Davis delves into the fascinating history of these remarkable structures, revealing the stories of the people who once walked their halls and the mysteries that lie beneath their surface. As the excavation progresses, unexpected discoveries and unexpected challenges arise, leading to a thrilling exploration of the past that will leave readers eager to uncover more.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Travel

Themes: Ancient history, Roman civilization, Archaeological exploration, Cultural heritage
