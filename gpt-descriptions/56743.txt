Synopsis: In 'Larry Dexter and the Stolen Boy; or, A Young Reporter on the Lakes' by Howard Roger Garis, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside Larry Dexter, a young reporter with a knack for uncovering mysteries. When a young boy mysteriously disappears from a lakeside town, Larry is determined to solve the case and bring the missing child back to safety. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Larry uncovers a web of secrets, unexpected twists, and dangerous encounters that test his courage and journalistic skills. With each clue he uncovers, Larry gets closer to the truth, but also finds himself in increasing danger. Will Larry be able to rescue the stolen boy and expose the culprits behind the kidnapping?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Determination, Journalism, Friendship
