Synopsis: In the vibrant Latin Quarter of 19th-century Paris, a group of young artists and writers embark on a journey of self-discovery, love, and artistic pursuit. Through their bohemian lifestyle, they navigate the challenges of poverty, societal expectations, and the pursuit of their dreams. As they form deep friendships and experience the highs and lows of their unconventional lives, they find solace and inspiration in their shared passion for art and the pursuit of beauty. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Artistic expression, Friendship, Love, Poverty, Society's expectations
