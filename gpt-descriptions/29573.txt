Synopsis: In 'The O'Ruddy: A Romance', the reader is transported to the vibrant streets of New York City in the late 19th century, where the charismatic and audacious O'Ruddy becomes the center of attention. With his quick wit and daring escapades, O'Ruddy captures the hearts of both men and women alike, leaving a trail of chaos and intrigue in his wake. As his adventures unfold, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of society, encountering love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will O'Ruddy's charm be his downfall or his salvation?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Social Class
