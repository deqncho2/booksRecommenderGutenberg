Synopsis: 
In Polyeucte, a gripping tragedy by Pierre Corneille, the story revolves around a nobleman named Polyeucte who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his country and his newfound religious beliefs. As he grapples with this internal conflict, Polyeucte's actions set off a chain of events that will test the limits of love, faith, and sacrifice. With its intense emotional depth and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, Polyeucte takes readers on a journey of passion, conviction, and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Religion, Love, Sacrifice
