Synopsis: In "My Path to Atheism," Annie Besant takes readers on a captivating journey through her personal exploration of faith, challenging societal norms and religious dogmas along the way. From her early encounters with religious institutions to her intellectual awakening, Besant's narrative delves into the profound questions that led her to embrace atheism. With thought-provoking anecdotes and philosophical reflections, she invites readers to question their own beliefs and embark on a path of self-discovery. 

Genres: Autobiography, Philosophy, Religion

Themes: Faith and doubt, Religious skepticism, Personal transformation
