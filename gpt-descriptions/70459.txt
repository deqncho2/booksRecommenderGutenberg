Synopsis: 
"To Let" by B.M. Croker is a captivating novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the vibrant streets of India. The story revolves around a young Englishwoman named Ethel who finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she moves into a new house. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her new home, Ethel becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic "To Let" sign.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Cultural clashes, Power dynamics
