Synopsis: In 'An Embroidery Book' by Arthur, Anne Knox, a young woman named Emily discovers an ancient embroidery book hidden in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the intricate patterns and designs, she becomes captivated by the stories they hold and the secrets they unveil. With each stitch, Emily unravels a tale of love, loss, and redemption, connecting her own life to the lives of those who came before her. As she navigates the challenges of her present, Emily finds solace and inspiration in the art of embroidery, discovering that sometimes the most beautiful stories are woven with a needle and thread.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family legacy, Artistic expression, Identity, Connection between past and present
