Synopsis: In 'A Caution to the Directors of the East-India Company', an anonymous author delivers a compelling warning to the powerful directors of the renowned trading company. With a sense of urgency, the author highlights the potential consequences of neglecting a recent Act of Parliament and a crucial by-law. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of intrigue and corporate politics, where the stakes are high and the repercussions could be disastrous. This cautionary tale serves as a gripping reminder that even the most influential institutions must adhere to the laws that govern them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Political

Themes: Corporate governance, Legal compliance, Accountability
