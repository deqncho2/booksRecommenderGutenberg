Synopsis: In "The War of Independence," John Fiske takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that led to the birth of a nation. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Fiske delves into the political, social, and military aspects of the American Revolution, shedding light on the extraordinary individuals who shaped the course of history. From the Boston Tea Party to the final battle at Yorktown, this gripping account brings to life the struggles, sacrifices, and triumphs of those who fought for freedom.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Revolution, Freedom, Patriotism
