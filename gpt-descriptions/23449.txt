Synopsis: 
'Behind the Beyond, and Other Contributions to Human Knowledge' by Stephen Leacock is a collection of witty and satirical short stories that take readers on a hilarious journey through absurd situations and unconventional ideas. From a man who invents a machine to predict the past to a professor who discovers a new emotion, these tales will leave readers laughing and questioning the limits of human knowledge.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity, Intellectualism, Human Limitations
