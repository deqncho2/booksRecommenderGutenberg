Synopsis: In "Lincoln's Last Hours," Charles A. Leale takes us on a gripping journey through the final moments of one of America's most iconic figures. As the nation mourns the loss of President Abraham Lincoln, Leale, a young doctor thrust into an unexpected role, finds himself at the center of a tragic and historic event. Through his vivid and detailed account, we witness the intense emotions, the race against time, and the profound impact that Lincoln's assassination had on the nation. Leale's narrative offers a unique perspective on this pivotal moment in American history, leaving readers captivated and deeply moved.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Memoir

Themes: Assassination, Grief, National tragedy, Historical significance
