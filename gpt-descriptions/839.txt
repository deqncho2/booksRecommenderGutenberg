Synopsis: In 'New Arabian Nights' by Robert Louis Stevenson, embark on a thrilling journey through the streets of London as you encounter a cast of intriguing characters and witness their extraordinary adventures. From a daring heist to a mysterious murder, each story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping you on the edge of your seat. Set against the backdrop of the bustling city, this collection of tales will transport you to a world where danger lurks around every corner and where the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Crime and punishment, Identity and disguise, Morality and ethics
