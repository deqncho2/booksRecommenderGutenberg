Synopsis: In 'Unmanned' by Stephen Oliver, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emma Lawson finds herself at the center of a groundbreaking experiment that pushes the boundaries of artificial intelligence. As she delves deeper into her creation, she uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only her own life but the future of humanity. With time running out, Emma must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to save herself and those she holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, Betrayal, Survival
