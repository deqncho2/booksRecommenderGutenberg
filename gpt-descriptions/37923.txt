Synopsis: 
In "The Old Debauchees. A Comedy" by Henry Fielding, we are introduced to a group of aging gentlemen who refuse to let go of their wild and reckless youth. Despite their advanced years, these debauchees continue to indulge in all manner of vices, from gambling to womanizing, much to the chagrin of their concerned families. However, when a young and virtuous woman enters their lives, their debauched world is turned upside down, forcing them to confront their own mortality and question the choices they have made. 

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Aging, Morality, Redemption
