Synopsis: In 'The Drama of Glass' by Kate Field, a mysterious glass artifact is discovered, setting off a chain of events that intertwines the lives of a diverse group of characters. As they delve into the history and secrets behind the glass, they find themselves caught in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of objects, the complexities of human relationships
