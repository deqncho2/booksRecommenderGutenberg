Synopsis: In 'King o' the Beach: A Tropic Tale' by George Manville Fenn, follow the thrilling adventures of a young boy named Dick, who finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. As he navigates the challenges of survival, Dick encounters mysterious creatures, uncovers hidden treasures, and forms unlikely friendships. With danger lurking around every corner, will Dick be able to find a way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Exploration
