Synopsis: In "Once Upon a Time in Connecticut," Caroline Clifford Newton takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Connecticut. Through a collection of interconnected stories, Newton weaves together tales of love, loss, and the resilience of the human spirit. From the bustling streets of Hartford to the serene shores of Long Island Sound, this book delves into the lives of ordinary people who find themselves caught in extraordinary circumstances. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into the intricate web of secrets, mysteries, and unexpected connections that bind these characters together. 

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love, Loss, Resilience, Secrets, Connections
