Synopsis: In 'Danforth Plays the Game: Stories for Boys Little and Big' by Ralph Henry Barbour, follow the adventures of Danforth, a young boy with a knack for getting into mischief. From his misadventures on the baseball field to his encounters with a mysterious stranger, Danforth's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each story, Danforth learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of honesty. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Honesty
