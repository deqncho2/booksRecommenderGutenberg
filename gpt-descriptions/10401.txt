Synopsis: 

In "Personal Memoir of Daniel Drayton, for Four Years and Four Months a Prisoner (for Charity's Sake) in Washington Jail: Including a Narrative of the Voyage and Capture of the Schooner Pearl," Daniel Drayton shares his gripping personal account of being imprisoned for his involvement in a daring escape mission. Drayton takes the reader on a harrowing journey, recounting the events leading up to his capture and the subsequent years spent behind bars. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt reflections, he sheds light on the challenges faced by those fighting for freedom and justice during a tumultuous time in American history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Freedom and oppression, Social justice, Resilience
