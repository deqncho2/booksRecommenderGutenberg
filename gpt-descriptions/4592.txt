Synopsis: In 'Cast Adrift' by Arthur, T. S. (Timothy Shay), a young woman finds herself caught in a web of deceit and danger when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she navigates through a world of secrets and betrayal, she must uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Deception, Trust, Redemption
