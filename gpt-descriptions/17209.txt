Synopsis: In 'A Treatise of Witchcraft' by Roberts, Alexander, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that unveils a dark and mysterious world of witchcraft. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this ancient practice, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems. With each revelation, he uncovers shocking truths that challenge his beliefs and force him to question the very nature of good and evil. As he races against time to decipher the cryptic messages within the manuscript, he must confront his own fears and confront the malevolent forces that seek to control him. This gripping tale of witchcraft will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Witchcraft, Good vs. Evil, Knowledge and Power
