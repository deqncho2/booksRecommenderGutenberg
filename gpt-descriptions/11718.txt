Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls at School; Or, The Wohelo Weavers" by Hildegard G. Frey, a group of adventurous Camp Fire Girls find themselves embarking on a new and exciting journey as they enter school. As they navigate the challenges of academic life, they discover the power of friendship, teamwork, and the importance of staying true to themselves. With their Camp Fire training, the girls bring their unique skills and values to the school community, making a lasting impact on those around them.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Empowerment, Education, Identity, Teamwork
