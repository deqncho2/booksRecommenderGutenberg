Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Seventh, or the Queen's Own Regiment of Hussars', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and remarkable achievements of this renowned regiment. From its establishment in 1690 to its valiant services up until 1842, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, shedding light on its pivotal role in various conflicts and campaigns. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the brave men who served in the Queen's Own Regiment of Hussars, showcasing their unwavering dedication and unwavering commitment to duty.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, War and conflict
