Synopsis: 
"The logs of the Serapis--Alliance--Ariel, under the command of John Paul Jones, 1779-1780" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the high seas during the American Revolutionary War. Through the detailed logs of the ships, we witness the daring exploits, intense battles, and strategic maneuvers led by the legendary naval commander, John Paul Jones. As the crew faces treacherous waters, enemy encounters, and internal conflicts, the logs provide a firsthand account of the challenges and triumphs experienced during this pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Leadership, Sacrifice
