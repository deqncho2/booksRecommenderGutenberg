Synopsis: 
"The Monastery of Petschenga: Sketches of Russian Lapland" takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and remote region of Lapland. Drawing from historical and legendary sources, author J.A. Friis weaves together a collection of intriguing stories set in and around the enigmatic Petschenga Monastery. From tales of ancient rituals and mythical creatures to encounters with eccentric monks and the secrets they guard, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich folklore and history of Russian Lapland.

Genres: Historical fiction, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Mystery, Exploration
