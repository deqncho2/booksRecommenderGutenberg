Synopsis: In 'The Magic and Science of Jewels and Stones', Kozminsky, Isidore takes readers on a captivating journey into the mystical world of gemstones and their hidden powers. Through a combination of ancient wisdom, scientific knowledge, and personal anecdotes, the author unveils the enchanting secrets behind these precious stones. From the mesmerizing allure of diamonds to the protective properties of amethysts, this book explores the magical and transformative qualities that lie within each gem. Delving into the history, symbolism, and healing properties of various stones, Kozminsky, Isidore offers a fascinating exploration of the profound connection between humans and these shimmering treasures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Esoteric, New Age

Themes: Mysticism, Symbolism, Healing, Personal Transformation
