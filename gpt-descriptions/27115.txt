Synopsis: In 'The Cattle-Baron's Daughter' by Harold Bindloss, a spirited young woman named Muriel is caught between her father's expectations and her own desires for independence. Set against the backdrop of the rugged Canadian wilderness, Muriel must navigate the challenges of love, family loyalty, and societal expectations as she strives to forge her own path. As she encounters a charismatic stranger who challenges her beliefs, Muriel's journey takes unexpected twists and turns, ultimately leading her to discover her true strength and identity.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Independence, Love and Duty, Gender Roles
