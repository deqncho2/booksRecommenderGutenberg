Synopsis: 

In 'Report on the Migration of Birds in the Spring and Autumn of 1880. [Second Report]', Cordeaux, Harvie-Brown, and Kermode delve into the fascinating world of bird migration during the year 1880. Through meticulous observations and scientific analysis, they uncover remarkable patterns and behaviors exhibited by various bird species during their journeys. From the awe-inspiring spectacle of thousands of birds taking flight to the challenges they face along the way, this report offers a captivating exploration of the mysteries of avian migration.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Ornithology

Themes: Migration, Animal Behavior, Scientific Observation
