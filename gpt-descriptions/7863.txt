Synopsis: In 'The Avalanche: A Mystery Story' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a remote mountain lodge after an unexpected avalanche. As they wait for rescue, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a series of mysterious events that keep the reader guessing until the very end. With each character harboring their own dark past, the avalanche becomes more than just a natural disaster, but a catalyst for uncovering hidden truths and exploring the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Survival, Human nature
