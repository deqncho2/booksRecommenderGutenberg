Synopsis: In 'The Trail to Yesterday' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, stirring up memories and secrets from the past. As the townspeople grapple with their own demons, they are forced to confront the choices they made and the consequences that follow. With danger lurking around every corner, the trail to yesterday becomes a treacherous journey of redemption, forgiveness, and the search for true identity.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Consequences
