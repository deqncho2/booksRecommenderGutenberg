Synopsis: In 'Children of the Night' by Robinson, Edwin Arlington, a mysterious and haunting atmosphere envelops a small town as its inhabitants are plagued by a series of unexplained events. As the darkness deepens, the townspeople are forced to confront their deepest fears and secrets, leading to a gripping exploration of the human psyche and the power of the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Human Nature, Supernatural Phenomena
