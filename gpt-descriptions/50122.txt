Synopsis: In 'The Glorious Return: A Story of the Vaudois in 1689' by Temple Crona, a small community of Vaudois Protestants in the French Alps faces persecution and exile due to their religious beliefs. Forced to leave their homes, they embark on a treacherous journey to find safety and freedom. Along the way, they encounter unexpected allies, face dangerous obstacles, and must summon their courage and resilience to overcome the odds. This gripping historical novel explores the power of faith, the strength of community, and the indomitable human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Religious Fiction

Themes: Persecution, Faith, Resilience, Community
