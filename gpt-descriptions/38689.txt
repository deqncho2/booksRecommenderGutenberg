Synopsis: In 'The Turnstile' by A. E. W. Mason, a young man named Dick Harleston finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes entangled with a mysterious woman. As he navigates through a world of secrets and deception, Dick must uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
