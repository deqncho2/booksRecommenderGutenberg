Synopsis: In 'W. A. G.'s Tale' by Turnbull, Margaret, a mysterious and enigmatic character named W. A. G. takes center stage as the protagonist embarks on a thrilling journey through time and space. As W. A. G. navigates through a series of mind-bending adventures, the reader is drawn into a world where reality and imagination blur, leaving them questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of the imagination. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind W. A. G.'s extraordinary existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Imagination, Time Travel, Identity
