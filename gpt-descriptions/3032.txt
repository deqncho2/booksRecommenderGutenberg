Synopsis: 

"The Fathers of the Constitution: A Chronicle of the Establishment of the Union" by Max Farrand is a captivating historical account that delves into the fascinating journey of the founding fathers of the United States as they come together to shape the nation's future. From the heated debates and compromises at the Constitutional Convention to the intricate drafting of the Constitution, this chronicle offers a riveting exploration of the minds and motivations behind one of the most significant documents in history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Farrand brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and complexities of these visionary leaders, shedding light on the birth of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political history, Biography

Themes: Founding of the United States, Constitutional Convention, Leadership, Political philosophy
