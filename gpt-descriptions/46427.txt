Synopsis: 
In 'The Great War in Verse and Prose', readers are transported to the tumultuous era of World War I through a captivating blend of poetry and prose. This powerful collection delves into the lives of soldiers and civilians, exploring the profound impact of war on individuals and society as a whole. From the trenches to the home front, the book offers a poignant and thought-provoking portrayal of the human experience during one of history's most devastating conflicts.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, War Literature

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Humanity, Loss, Resilience
