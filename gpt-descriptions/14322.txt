Synopsis: In this captivating translation of Euripides' Electra, the reader is transported to ancient Greece, where a tale of revenge, betrayal, and justice unfolds. Electra, a woman consumed by her desire for vengeance, finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and the pursuit of her own happiness. As the plot thickens, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the consequences of past actions come to light. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this timeless tragedy explores the depths of human emotions and the complexities of morality.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Poetry

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Family, Loyalty, Morality
