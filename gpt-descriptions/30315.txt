Synopsis:
In the gripping novel 'Raeburn' by J. L. Caw, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy socialite goes missing under mysterious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a treacherous world of high society, where appearances can be deceiving and danger lurks around every corner. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Power, Class divide
