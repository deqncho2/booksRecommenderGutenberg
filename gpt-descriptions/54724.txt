Synopsis: 
'Folk Tales of Breffny' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Irish folklore. Set in the mystical region of Breffny, this collection of tales weaves together mythical creatures, brave heroes, and ancient legends. From the hauntingly beautiful landscapes to the thrilling adventures that unfold, each story is a testament to the power of storytelling and the enduring magic of Ireland's rich cultural heritage.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: 
The power of storytelling, Irish mythology, Heroism, Magic
