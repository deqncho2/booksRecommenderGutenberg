Synopsis: In "Jack the Hunchback: A Story of Adventure on the Coast of Maine" by James Otis, readers are transported to the rugged and mysterious coast of Maine, where a young boy named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure. With his unique physical condition and a heart full of determination, Jack finds himself caught up in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates treacherous cliffs, hidden caves, and encounters with smugglers, Jack's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface of this coastal town? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction. Themes: Courage, Friendship, Overcoming Adversity.
