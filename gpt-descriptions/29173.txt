Synopsis: In 'The White Lie' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Muriel Chetwynd finds herself caught in a web of deception and danger when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into his world, Muriel discovers that nothing is as it seems, and she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who can be trusted and what secrets lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Identity
