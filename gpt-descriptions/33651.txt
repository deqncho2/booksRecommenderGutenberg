Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of a Man' by E. W. Hornung, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued by his past and his true intentions, they soon discover that he is harboring a dark secret that threatens to unravel their peaceful existence. With suspense and intrigue at every turn, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Morality
