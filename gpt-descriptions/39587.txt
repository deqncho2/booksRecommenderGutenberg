Synopsis: In 'The Graftons: A Novel' by Marshall Archibald, a wealthy and influential family finds themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and betrayal. As the patriarch of the family, Charles Grafton, battles a terminal illness, his children and grandchildren must confront their own demons and navigate the treacherous world of power, wealth, and privilege. With each family member harboring their own hidden agenda, the Graftons' once picture-perfect facade begins to crumble, revealing the dark underbelly of their seemingly idyllic lives. As the family's secrets unravel, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving them questioning the true nature of loyalty and the lengths one will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Power, Betrayal, Family Dynamics, Secrets
