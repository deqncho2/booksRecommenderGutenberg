Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Computer' by H. Beam Piper, Conn Maxwell returns to his hometown on the planet Poictesme, where he discovers a hidden treasure trove of information about an ancient supercomputer that could potentially change the fate of the entire planet. As Conn delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the cosmic computer, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue, corporate greed, and the desperate struggle for power. With the fate of Poictesme hanging in the balance, Conn must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth behind the cosmic computer before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Power and corruption, Redemption and personal growth
