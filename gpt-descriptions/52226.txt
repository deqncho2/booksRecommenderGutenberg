Synopsis: 
In "A Prisoner of the Khaleefa: Twelve Years Captivity at Omdurman," Charles Neufeld takes readers on a gripping journey through his harrowing experience as a captive in Omdurman, Sudan. Neufeld vividly recounts the brutal conditions, the constant threat of violence, and the resilience he and his fellow prisoners displayed during their twelve-year ordeal. As he navigates the treacherous world of the Khaleefa's palace, Neufeld's story becomes a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the power of hope in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Human Spirit, Hope
