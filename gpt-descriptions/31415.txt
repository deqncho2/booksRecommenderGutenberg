Synopsis: In "Stained Glass Work: A text-book for students and workers in glass," Christopher Whall takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of stained glass artistry. Through detailed instructions, insightful anecdotes, and stunning visual examples, Whall unveils the secrets behind this ancient craft, revealing the delicate balance between technical skill and artistic expression. From the historical significance of stained glass to the practical techniques used in its creation, this book offers a comprehensive guide for both aspiring artists and seasoned professionals. With its blend of practical advice and artistic inspiration, "Stained Glass Work" is a must-read for anyone fascinated by the mesmerizing beauty of this timeless art form.

Genres: Art, Craft, Non-fiction

Themes: Stained glass art, Artistic expression, Technical skill, Historical significance
