Synopsis: In "Socialism and Modern Science (Darwin, Spencer, Marx)" by Enrico Ferri, embark on a captivating exploration of the intersection between socialism and the groundbreaking theories of Darwin, Spencer, and Marx. Delve into the fascinating connections between these influential thinkers as Ferri delves into the implications of their ideas on society, politics, and the human condition. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Ferri sheds light on the profound impact of these theories on the development of socialism and modern science, offering a fresh perspective on the complex relationship between ideology and scientific thought.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Political Science

Themes: Socialism, Evolution, Marxism, Scientific Theory, Ideology
