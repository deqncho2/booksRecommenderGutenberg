Synopsis: 
In 'Robert's Rules of Order: Pocket Manual of Rules of Order for Deliberative Assemblies,' Henry Martyn Robert presents a concise and practical guide to conducting meetings and making decisions in a fair and efficient manner. This indispensable manual provides clear instructions on how to establish order, handle debates, and ensure that every voice is heard. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a community leader, this pocket-sized book will equip you with the essential tools to navigate the complexities of group decision-making and facilitate productive discussions. Discover the secrets to running effective meetings and fostering a democratic environment with Robert's Rules of Order.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Self-help

Themes: Decision-making, Leadership, Democracy
