Synopsis: 

"Iberia Won" is a captivating poem that takes readers on a vivid journey through the Peninsular War, combining historical events with the author's personal experiences visiting the battlefields. Terence McMahon Hughes skillfully weaves together descriptive verses and insightful notes, creating a powerful narrative that immerses readers in the tumultuous era. As the poem unfolds, the reader is transported to the heart of the conflict, witnessing the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of those involved. With its rich imagery and historical context, "Iberia Won" offers a unique perspective on the Peninsular War, leaving readers eager to delve deeper into this captivating piece of literature.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, War

Themes: Heroism, Resilience, Historical events
