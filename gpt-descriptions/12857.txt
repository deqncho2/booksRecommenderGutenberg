Synopsis: 
In the small town of Cambridge, a series of mysterious events begins to unfold, leaving the residents bewildered and on edge. As the secrets of the town's dark past slowly come to light, a group of unlikely allies must band together to uncover the truth and protect their community from an ancient evil that threatens to consume them all. With its atmospheric setting and gripping narrative, 'Cambridge' takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, supernatural elements, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Community and unity, Good vs. evil
