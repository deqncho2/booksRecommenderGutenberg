Synopsis: In 'The Two Sides of the Shield' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young woman named Ethel May is caught between two worlds - the glamorous and exciting life of the theater and the quiet, respectable existence of a governess. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Ethel May must make a choice that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of Victorian society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Social Expectations, Love and Sacrifice
