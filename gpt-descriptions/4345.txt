Synopsis: 
In 'Sparrows: The Story of an Unprotected Girl' by Newte, Horace W. C., readers are introduced to the captivating life of a young girl named Sparrow. Set in the bustling streets of Victorian London, Sparrow's journey takes her from the grimy slums to the opulent homes of the wealthy, as she navigates a world filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected alliances. As she strives to protect herself and those she loves, Sparrow's resilience and determination shine through, making her an unforgettable protagonist in this gripping tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Social Inequality, Identity
