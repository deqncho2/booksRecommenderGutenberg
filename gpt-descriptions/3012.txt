Synopsis: In 'The Acharnians', Aristophanes takes us on a hilarious journey through the absurdities of war and politics. The story revolves around a man named Dikaiopolis, who is tired of the ongoing Peloponnesian War and its devastating effects on his daily life. Determined to find peace and prosperity, he embarks on a daring plan that challenges the status quo and brings him face-to-face with a host of eccentric characters. As the plot unfolds, the audience is treated to a satirical exploration of war, diplomacy, and the power of individual action. 

Genres: Comedy, Satire

Themes: War, Politics, Individualism
