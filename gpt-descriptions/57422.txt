Synopsis: 
'Cliff Dwellings of the Mesa Verde: A Study in Pictures' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the ancient cliff dwellings of Mesa Verde. Through a stunning collection of photographs, author Don Watson unveils the intricate details and architectural marvels of these remarkable structures. From the awe-inspiring cliffside locations to the intricate stone masonry, this book offers a unique glimpse into the lives of the Ancestral Puebloans who once called these dwellings home. As readers explore the pages, they will be transported back in time, marveling at the ingenuity and resilience of these ancient civilizations.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Photography, History

Themes: 
Archaeology, Native American culture, Architecture
