Synopsis: 
"The Story of Cole Younger, by Himself" is a gripping autobiography that delves into the life of Cole Younger, a notorious American outlaw and member of the infamous James-Younger Gang. From his early days as a Confederate soldier to his involvement in numerous bank robberies and shootouts, Younger recounts his thrilling adventures with vivid detail and unapologetic honesty. Through his own words, readers are taken on a wild journey through the lawless and dangerous world of the Old West, where loyalty, betrayal, and the pursuit of freedom collide.

Genres: 
Autobiography, Western, Historical

Themes: 
Outlaw life, Loyalty, Redemption
