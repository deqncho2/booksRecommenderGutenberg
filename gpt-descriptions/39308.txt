Synopsis: In "Walks in Rome," Augustus J. C. Hare takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of Rome. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Hare invites us to explore the hidden corners, ancient ruins, and magnificent landmarks of this timeless city. From the bustling piazzas to the serene gardens, each walk reveals the rich tapestry of Rome's past and present, offering a unique perspective on its art, architecture, and culture. As Hare guides us through the labyrinthine streets, we are transported to a world where history comes alive, and the spirit of Rome captivates our imagination.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: History, Art and Architecture, Cultural Heritage
