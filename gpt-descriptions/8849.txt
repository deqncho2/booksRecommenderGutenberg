Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 2 Peter', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the biblical book of 2 Peter. This powerful epistle, written by the apostle Peter, delves into the themes of faith, false teachings, and the importance of living a godly life. Through his modern speech translation, Weymouth brings the timeless message of 2 Peter to life, captivating readers with its relevance and wisdom.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation

Themes: Faith, False teachings, Godly living
