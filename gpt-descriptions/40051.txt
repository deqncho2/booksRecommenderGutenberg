Synopsis: 

In 'Found at Last: the Veritable Garden of Eden', author Van Slyke takes readers on a thrilling adventure as he uncovers a hidden location that may just be the legendary Garden of Eden. Filled with captivating discoveries and thought-provoking revelations, this book delves into the mysteries of biblical history and offers a compelling argument for the existence of this mythical paradise. As Van Slyke's journey unfolds, readers will be left questioning everything they thought they knew about the Garden of Eden and its significance in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, History

Themes: Biblical history, Mythology, Exploration
