Synopsis: In 'Paddington: Past and Present' by Robins, William, the beloved bear from Peru embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through time, uncovering secrets from his past and encountering unexpected challenges in the present. As Paddington navigates the bustling streets of London, he must rely on his wit, charm, and the help of new friends to unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. With heartwarming moments and delightful surprises, this enchanting tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Time Travel
