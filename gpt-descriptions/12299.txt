Synopsis: 

In 'The Mechanical Properties of Wood: Including a Discussion of the Factors Affecting the Mechanical Properties, and Methods of Timber Testing', Samuel J. Record delves into the fascinating world of wood and its mechanical properties. This comprehensive study explores the factors that influence the strength and durability of timber, as well as the various methods used to test its properties. From the microscopic structure of wood to the macroscopic behavior of timber, Record's work offers a captivating exploration of this essential material.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Engineering

Themes: Material science, Structural engineering, Timber technology
