Synopsis: In 'Long Odds' by Harold Bindloss, a young man named Ralph discovers a hidden treasure map that leads him on a thrilling adventure through the rugged Canadian wilderness. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Ralph must navigate treacherous landscapes and make difficult choices that will test his courage and determination. With danger lurking at every turn, will Ralph be able to uncover the long-lost treasure, or will the odds be stacked against him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
