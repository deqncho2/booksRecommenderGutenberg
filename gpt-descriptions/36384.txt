Synopsis: In 'The Squire's Daughter' by Silas K. Hocking, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between duty and love when she falls for a man beneath her social standing. As she navigates the complexities of class and societal expectations, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the norms of her time.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and social class, Duty and sacrifice
