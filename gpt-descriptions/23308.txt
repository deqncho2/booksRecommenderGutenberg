Synopsis: In 'The White Feather Hex' by Don Peterson, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events after a white feather is found on the doorstep of every resident. As the townspeople try to unravel the meaning behind this strange occurrence, they uncover a web of secrets, lies, and hidden connections that threaten to tear their community apart. With each feather, tensions rise, suspicions grow, and the line between reality and superstition becomes blurred. As the town becomes consumed by fear and paranoia, the residents must confront their own demons and face the consequences of their past actions. Will they be able to break the hex and restore peace, or will the white feathers continue to haunt them forever?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and lies, Community dynamics, Superstition and belief
