Synopsis: In 'A Night in Avignon' by Rice, Cale Young, a young American artist finds himself immersed in the vibrant and mysterious world of Avignon, France. As he explores the city's enchanting streets and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Cale becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires that will forever change his perception of art, love, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Forbidden love, Redemption
