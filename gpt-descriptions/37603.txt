Synopsis: 
"The History of the Nineteenth Century in Caricature" takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the tumultuous events and influential figures of the 1800s. Through a collection of vivid and often humorous caricatures, Cooper and Maurice provide a unique perspective on the political, social, and cultural landscape of the time. From the rise and fall of empires to the struggles for equality and the birth of new technologies, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the past, told through the lens of satire and art.

Genres: Art, History, Satire

Themes: Political upheaval, Social change, Cultural transformation
