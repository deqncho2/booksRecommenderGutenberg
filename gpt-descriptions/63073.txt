Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Limehouse, London, a collection of captivating tales unfolds. From the opium dens to the bustling markets, Limehouse Nights takes readers on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of the city. Meet the cunning criminals, the desperate lovers, and the eccentric characters who inhabit this vibrant and dangerous world. Each story is a glimpse into the lives of those who reside in Limehouse, revealing their secrets, desires, and the unexpected twists that fate has in store for them. Prepare to be enthralled by the haunting atmosphere and the unforgettable characters that populate this mesmerizing collection.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and desire, Fate and destiny
