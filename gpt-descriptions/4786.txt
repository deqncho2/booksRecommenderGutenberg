Synopsis: 
In "Zone Policeman 88: A Close Range Study of the Panama Canal and Its Workers," Harry Alverson Franck takes readers on a captivating journey through the Panama Canal Zone. As a zone policeman, Franck provides a unique perspective on the inner workings of this engineering marvel and the diverse group of workers who make it all possible. From encounters with dangerous wildlife to navigating the complex social dynamics of the zone, Franck's firsthand account offers a fascinating glimpse into a world few have experienced. 

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Engineering, Cultural diversity, Social dynamics
