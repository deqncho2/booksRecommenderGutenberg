Synopsis: In 'The Issahar Artifacts' by Jesse F. Bone, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Issahar artifacts, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each discovery, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe blurs. Will he unlock the truth hidden within these artifacts, or will the consequences of his quest prove too great?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of ancient civilizations, the pursuit of knowledge, the consequences of curiosity
