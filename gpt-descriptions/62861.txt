Synopsis: In 'The Gun Alley Tragedy: Record of the Trial of Colin Campbell Ross', author T. C. Brennan delves into a gripping true crime story that captivated Australia in the early 20th century. The book follows the shocking murder of a young girl and the subsequent trial of the accused, Colin Campbell Ross. As the trial unfolds, the reader is taken on a suspenseful journey through the evidence, witness testimonies, and legal proceedings, all while questioning the true identity of the killer. With unexpected twists and turns, this compelling account keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Justice and injustice, the power of evidence, the impact of media on public opinion
