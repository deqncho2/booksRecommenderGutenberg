Synopsis: In 'The Old Maid (The 'Fifties)' by Edith Wharton, we are transported to the vibrant and changing society of mid-19th century New York. The story revolves around two cousins, Delia and Charlotte, whose lives take dramatically different paths. As secrets, betrayals, and societal expectations intertwine, the reader is left wondering how these women will navigate the complexities of love, family, and their own desires.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Social expectations, Family dynamics, Love and sacrifice
