Synopsis: 
In the coastal town of Le Havre, two brothers, Pierre and Jean, lead seemingly ordinary lives. However, their world is turned upside down when a family friend leaves them an unexpected inheritance. As they delve into the mystery surrounding their newfound wealth, long-buried secrets and hidden desires begin to surface, threatening to tear their family apart. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, 'Pierre and Jean' explores the complexities of family dynamics, jealousy, and the pursuit of identity.

Genres: 
Realism, Psychological Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: 
Family relationships, Inheritance, Jealousy, Identity
