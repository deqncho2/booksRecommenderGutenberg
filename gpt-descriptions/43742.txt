Synopsis: 
In 'To Tell You the Truth' by Leonard Merrick, a renowned author finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when a mysterious woman enters his life. As he delves deeper into her enigmatic past, he becomes consumed by the need to uncover the truth, even if it means risking everything he holds dear. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Truth and deception, Identity, Obsession
