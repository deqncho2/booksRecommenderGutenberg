Synopsis: In 'Decision' by Robinson, Frank M., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. As he grapples with the ethical implications of his invention, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where every decision he makes could have catastrophic consequences. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and unexpected twists, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ethics, Consequences, Morality
