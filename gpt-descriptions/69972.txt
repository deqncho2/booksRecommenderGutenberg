Synopsis: In 'The Changed Brides' by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, a mysterious event takes place on the day of a double wedding, altering the lives of two young brides forever. As they navigate the consequences of this unexpected twist, secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances come to light, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey of self-discovery and redemption. Will these changed brides find happiness amidst the chaos that surrounds them?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
