Synopsis: In 'When the Owl Cries' by Bartlett, Paul Alexander, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that leave the community in fear and confusion. As the body count rises, a young detective must unravel the dark secrets hidden within the town's history to uncover the truth behind the killings. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume him. As the clock ticks, he must race against time to stop the killer before he becomes the next victim.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town darkness, Supernatural forces
