Synopsis: In "Turgenev: A Study," Edward Garnett delves into the life and works of the renowned Russian writer Ivan Turgenev. Through a captivating exploration of Turgenev's personal experiences, relationships, and literary achievements, Garnett offers readers a fascinating glimpse into the mind of this influential figure. From his tumultuous love affairs to his profound reflections on the human condition, Turgenev's life unfolds in a way that both captivates and enlightens. Garnett's study serves as a compelling tribute to Turgenev's enduring legacy, shedding light on the man behind the words and the profound impact he had on Russian literature.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Love and relationships, Artistic expression, Social and political commentary
