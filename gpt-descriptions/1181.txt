Synopsis: 
In 'The Symposium' by Xenophon, a group of friends gather together for a lively and thought-provoking discussion on the nature of love. Each participant shares their unique perspective, recounting tales of love's power, its challenges, and its impact on their lives. As the evening unfolds, the conversation delves into the realms of philosophy, psychology, and human nature, revealing surprising insights and raising profound questions about the complexities of love and desire.

Genres: Philosophy, Dialogue, Ancient Literature

Themes: Love, Desire, Friendship, Philosophy, Human Nature
