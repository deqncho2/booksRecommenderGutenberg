Synopsis: In the 21st issue of "The Great Round World And What Is Going On In It," readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and discoveries of the world in April 1897. From mysterious disappearances to groundbreaking scientific advancements, this weekly magazine for boys and girls captivates with its engaging stories and fascinating articles. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover a world filled with adventure, intrigue, and the wonders of the past, present, and future.

Genres: Historical fiction, adventure, educational

Themes: Exploration, curiosity, knowledge
