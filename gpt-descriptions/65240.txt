Synopsis: In 'A Madman on Board' by Robert Silverberg, a seemingly ordinary spaceship journey takes a chilling turn when a passenger begins to exhibit increasingly erratic and dangerous behavior. As the crew and fellow travelers grapple with the escalating madness, tensions rise and the true nature of the threat becomes uncertain. With suspense and psychological intensity, this gripping tale explores the fragile boundaries between sanity and insanity, and the terrifying consequences that can arise when they are breached.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Madness, Sanity, Psychological Exploration, Human Nature
