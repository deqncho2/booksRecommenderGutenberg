Synopsis: In 'The Passport' by Richard Bagot, a mysterious and coveted document becomes the center of a gripping tale set in the early 20th century. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the intricate web of international politics, espionage, and personal ambitions. With unexpected twists and turns, the narrative explores the lengths people will go to protect their secrets and the power that lies within a single piece of paper.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception, Loyalty
