Synopsis: In "The Thing in the Attic" by James Blish, a group of scientists and engineers are sent on a mission to investigate a mysterious object that has crash-landed on Earth. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the enigmatic artifact, they uncover a mind-bending truth that challenges their understanding of reality and the nature of humanity itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Existentialism, Human nature
