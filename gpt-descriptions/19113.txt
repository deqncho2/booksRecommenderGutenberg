Synopsis: In 'The Emigrant Trail' by Geraldine Bonner, a young woman named Mary embarks on a treacherous journey across the American frontier in search of a better life. Facing countless hardships and dangers, she must navigate through rugged landscapes, encounter diverse characters, and confront her own inner demons. As Mary's resilience is tested, she discovers the true meaning of courage, love, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Perseverance, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
