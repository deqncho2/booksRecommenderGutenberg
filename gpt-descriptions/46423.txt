Synopsis: In 'A Contribution to the Critique of Political Economy', Karl Marx delves into the intricate web of economic systems, unraveling the hidden forces that shape society. Through a meticulous analysis of capitalism, Marx uncovers the contradictions and exploitative nature of the prevailing economic order. With sharp insights and thought-provoking arguments, he challenges conventional wisdom and offers a radical perspective on the relationship between labor, capital, and power. As Marx dissects the inner workings of the economy, he presents a compelling case for a more equitable and just society.

Genres: Political philosophy, Economic theory, Social criticism

Themes: Capitalism, Labor exploitation, Class struggle, Economic inequality
