Synopsis: In 'A Trooper Galahad' by Charles King, a young soldier named Ned Wayne finds himself torn between duty and love when he is stationed at a remote outpost in the American West. As he navigates the challenges of military life and the complexities of a forbidden romance, Ned must confront his own beliefs and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions of the rugged frontier and a captivating love story, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Western

Themes: Duty and Honor, Forbidden Love, Self-Discovery
