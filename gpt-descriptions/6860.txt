Synopsis: In 'Keineth' by Jane Abbott, a young girl named Keineth Randolph finds herself torn between her privileged upbringing and her desire for adventure. When her family's fortune takes a turn for the worse, Keineth is forced to leave her comfortable life behind and embark on a journey that will test her resilience and challenge her perceptions of the world. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, faces difficult choices, and discovers the true meaning of courage. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, 'Keineth' is a captivating coming-of-age tale that explores themes of identity, resilience, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Pursuit of Dreams
