Synopsis: 
In 'Wife in Name Only' by Charlotte M. Brame, a young woman named Edith finds herself trapped in a loveless marriage to a wealthy and powerful man. Desperate to escape her miserable existence, she devises a daring plan that involves assuming a false identity and embarking on a dangerous journey. As Edith navigates through a web of deceit and intrigue, she discovers unexpected allies and faces unforeseen challenges that test her courage and determination. Will she be able to break free from her husband's control and find true happiness?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Power and control
