Synopsis: 
In 'The Three Midshipmen' by William Henry Giles Kingston, three young friends embark on a thrilling adventure as they join the Royal Navy and set sail on a perilous journey across the seas. From facing treacherous storms to encountering pirates, the midshipmen must prove their courage and loyalty while navigating the challenges of life at sea. As they strive to fulfill their dreams of becoming naval officers, the trio forms an unbreakable bond that will be tested in the face of danger and adversity. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
