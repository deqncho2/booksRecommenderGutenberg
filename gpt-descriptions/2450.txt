Synopsis: 
'Boyhood' by Leo Tolstoy is a captivating coming-of-age story that follows the life of a young boy named Nikolai. Set in 19th-century Russia, the novel takes readers on a journey through Nikolai's formative years, exploring his relationships, experiences, and the challenges he faces as he navigates the complexities of adolescence. From his encounters with love and loss to his search for meaning and identity, 'Boyhood' offers a poignant and introspective portrayal of youth and the universal struggles that accompany it.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Love and relationships, Moral development
