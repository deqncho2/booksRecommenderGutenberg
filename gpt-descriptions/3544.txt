Synopsis: In "How He Lied to Her Husband," a love triangle unfolds as a passionate poet, his adoring wife, and her dashing admirer find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As the characters navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the power of words, their relationships are put to the ultimate test. With wit and charm, Shaw explores the consequences of lies and the fragile nature of trust in this captivating tale.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Deception and manipulation, Artistic expression
