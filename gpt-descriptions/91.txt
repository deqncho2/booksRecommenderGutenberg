Synopsis: 
In "Tom Sawyer Abroad," Tom, Huck, and Jim embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them far beyond the familiar Mississippi River. Joined by a mysterious companion, they find themselves in the midst of extraordinary encounters and unexpected challenges. As they navigate through uncharted territories, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading to a series of captivating and humorous escapades. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles they face and make it back home safely?

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Humor

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Coming of Age
