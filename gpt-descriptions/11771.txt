Synopsis: 
'Occasional Papers: Selected from the Guardian, the Times, and the Saturday Review, 1846-1890, Vol. 2' is a captivating collection of essays and articles written by R.W. Church. This volume delves into a wide range of topics, from literature and history to politics and religion. Church's insightful and thought-provoking writings offer a unique perspective on the social and intellectual climate of the Victorian era. With each page, readers will be transported back in time, immersing themselves in the intellectual debates and cultural discussions that shaped the period.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essays, Historical

Themes: Victorian society, Intellectual discourse, Cultural criticism
