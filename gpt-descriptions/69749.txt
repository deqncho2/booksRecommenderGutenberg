Synopsis: In 'On to Pekin; or, Old Glory in China' by Edward Stratemeyer, a group of adventurous American boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the heart of China. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, their loyalty to their country and their determination to protect their flag is put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering patriotism to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Exploration
