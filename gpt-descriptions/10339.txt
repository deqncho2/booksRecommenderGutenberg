Synopsis: In 'An Antarctic Mystery' by Jules Verne, a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey to the South Pole, only to find themselves caught in a mysterious and treacherous web of events. As they navigate through the icy landscapes and encounter strange phenomena, they must unravel the enigma surrounding a missing ship and its crew. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonists must rely on their wits and courage to survive the unforgiving Antarctic wilderness and uncover the truth that lies beneath the frozen surface.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
