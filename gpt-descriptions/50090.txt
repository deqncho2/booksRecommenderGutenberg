Synopsis: In 'The Red Lady' by Katharine Newlin Burt, a mysterious woman with fiery red hair arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with her enigmatic charm. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with her, they soon discover that her presence brings both joy and tragedy, leading to a series of unexpected events that will forever change their lives. With secrets lurking beneath her captivating exterior, 'The Red Lady' is a gripping tale of love, desire, and the consequences of obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Obsession, Secrets and deception
