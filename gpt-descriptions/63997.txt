Synopsis: In 'Martian Nightmare' by Walton, Bryce, a team of astronauts embarks on a groundbreaking mission to explore the mysterious red planet. However, as they delve deeper into the Martian terrain, they encounter inexplicable phenomena that defy all scientific understanding. Haunting visions, unexplained disappearances, and a growing sense of dread plague the crew, pushing them to the brink of their sanity. As they struggle to unravel the enigma that Mars holds, they must confront their own inner demons and face the terrifying truth that awaits them on the desolate planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Exploration, Isolation, Psychological terror
