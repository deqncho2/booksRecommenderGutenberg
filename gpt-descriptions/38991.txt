Synopsis: 
'Roumanian Stories, Translated from the Original Roumanian' is a captivating collection of short stories that takes readers on a journey through the rich and enchanting folklore of Romania. From mysterious legends to tales of love and betrayal, each story is a window into the vibrant culture and traditions of this Eastern European country. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, these stories explore themes of identity, destiny, and the power of storytelling itself. Whether it's a haunting ghost story or a heartwarming tale of resilience, this collection is sure to captivate readers with its timeless charm and universal themes.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Storytelling
