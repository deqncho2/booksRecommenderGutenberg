Synopsis: In "The Boy from the Ranch; Or, Roy Bradner's City Experiences" by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Roy Bradner, a young boy who leaves his rural ranch life behind to embark on a thrilling adventure in the city. As Roy navigates the bustling streets, encounters new friends, and faces unexpected challenges, he must find a way to adapt to his new surroundings and prove himself in this unfamiliar urban landscape. With a mix of excitement, mystery, and heartwarming moments, this story takes readers on a captivating journey alongside Roy as he discovers the true meaning of friendship, courage, and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Transition, Friendship, Self-discovery
