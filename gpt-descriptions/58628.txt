Synopsis: In 'Watermelon Mystery at Sugar Creek' by Hutchens, Paul, the Sugar Creek Gang finds themselves in the middle of a puzzling and juicy mystery when a valuable watermelon crop goes missing. As they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth, they encounter unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With their friendship and detective skills put to the test, the gang must unravel the secrets behind the watermelon mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Problem-solving
