Synopsis: In 'The Literary Sense' by Edith Nesbit, a young woman named Lucy discovers a hidden talent for writing that takes her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected success. As she navigates the challenges of the literary world, Lucy must confront her own insecurities and learn to trust her unique voice. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating plot, this novel explores the power of words and the transformative nature of creativity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Literary

Themes: Self-discovery, Creativity, Identity
