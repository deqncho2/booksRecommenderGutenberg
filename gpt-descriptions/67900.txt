Synopsis: In the Dead of Night takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a small town, where secrets and danger lurk around every corner. When a mysterious murder shakes the community, a determined detective must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As the clock ticks and tensions rise, the line between right and wrong becomes blurred, leaving the detective questioning their own morality. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Morality, Betrayal
