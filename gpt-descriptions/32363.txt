Synopsis: In 'Ten Girls from History', Kate Dickinson Sweetser introduces us to ten remarkable women who defied societal expectations and left an indelible mark on history. From the courageous Joan of Arc to the brilliant mathematician Hypatia, each chapter unveils the extraordinary lives and achievements of these trailblazing women. Through captivating storytelling, Sweetser transports readers to different eras and cultures, shedding light on the challenges these women faced and the legacies they left behind. This inspiring collection of biographies celebrates the power of determination, resilience, and the pursuit of one's passions.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, Courage, Resilience, Historical significance
