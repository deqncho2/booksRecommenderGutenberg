Synopsis: In 'The Silver Domino; Or, Side Whispers, Social and Literary' by Corelli, Marie, readers are transported to the glamorous world of high society, where secrets, scandals, and whispers abound. The story follows a group of intriguing characters as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As the silver domino becomes a symbol of mystery and intrigue, the reader is drawn into a web of hidden desires and unexpected alliances. With its captivating blend of romance, suspense, and social commentary, this novel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Desire, Ambition, Society and Class
