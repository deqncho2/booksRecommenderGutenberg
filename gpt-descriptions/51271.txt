Synopsis: In 'The History of the Highland Clearances: Second Edition, Altered and Revised', Alexander Mackenzie delves into the tumultuous period of the Highland Clearances, a dark chapter in Scottish history. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Mackenzie uncovers the harrowing tales of the forced evictions and mass emigration that forever changed the lives of the Highlanders. From the struggles of the displaced clans to the political and economic motivations behind the Clearances, this revised edition offers a comprehensive and thought-provoking exploration of a tragic era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Scottish history, Social history

Themes: Displacement, Cultural heritage, Socio-economic inequality
