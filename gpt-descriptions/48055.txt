Synopsis: In 'The Haunts of Old Cockaigne' by Alexander M. Thompson, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to the mysterious town of Old Cockaigne, a place shrouded in secrets and haunted by a dark past. As she delves deeper into the town's history, Emily uncovers a web of supernatural occurrences and encounters a cast of enigmatic characters who may hold the key to unlocking the town's mysteries. With each revelation, Emily's own life becomes intertwined with the haunting secrets of Old Cockaigne, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and danger.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Supernatural forces, Self-discovery
