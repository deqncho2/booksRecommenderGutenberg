Synopsis: In 'The Athelings; or, the Three Gifts', the lives of three siblings, Ethel, Ralph, and Oliver, take an unexpected turn when they receive mysterious gifts on their eighteenth birthdays. As they navigate the challenges of adulthood, their unique abilities bring them both joy and danger, leading them on a thrilling journey filled with secrets, romance, and the pursuit of their true destinies.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Fate, Family bonds
