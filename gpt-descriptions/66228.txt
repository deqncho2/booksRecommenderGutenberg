Synopsis: In 'Unnoticed London' by Montizambert, Elizabeth, a young woman named Alice finds herself drawn into a hidden world beneath the bustling streets of London. As she navigates through secret societies, mysterious artifacts, and unexpected alliances, Alice uncovers a truth that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every corner, she must unravel the enigma surrounding Unnoticed London before it consumes her. This thrilling tale of adventure and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface of the city.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Urban Fantasy

Themes: Hidden worlds, Secrets, History, Identity
