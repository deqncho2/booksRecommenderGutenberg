Synopsis: In "Washington's Masonic Correspondence: As Found among the Washington Papers in the Library of Congress," delve into the secret world of George Washington's Masonic connections. Unearthed from the archives, this collection of letters reveals the intriguing and mysterious relationships Washington had with fellow Freemasons during his time as a prominent figure in American history. As you explore the hidden depths of this correspondence, you will uncover the hidden symbols, rituals, and ideals that shaped Washington's life and his vision for the nation.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, Secret Societies

Themes: Freemasonry, Brotherhood, Enlightenment ideals, American Revolution
