Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts' Badge of Courage' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them deep into the heart of the wilderness. As they face numerous challenges and unexpected dangers, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each step they take, the boys discover the true meaning of bravery and the transformative power of friendship. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
