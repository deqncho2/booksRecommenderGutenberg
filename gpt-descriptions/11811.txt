Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1955 January - June, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. This enthralling collection unveils a treasure trove of forgotten literary gems, offering a glimpse into the creative landscape of mid-20th century America. From novels to poetry, plays to music, this anthology showcases the diverse range of works that sought to captivate and inspire readers during this transformative era. Delve into the pages of history and discover the forgotten voices that shaped the cultural tapestry of the time.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Reference

Themes: Creativity, Intellectual Property, Cultural Heritage
