Synopsis: In 'What and Where is God? A Human Answer to the Deep Religious Cry of the Modern Soul' by Swain, Richard La Rue, the author embarks on a thought-provoking exploration of the nature and existence of God. Through a captivating blend of personal anecdotes, philosophical musings, and theological insights, Swain delves into the profound questions that have troubled humanity for centuries. With a refreshing perspective, he challenges conventional beliefs and invites readers on a transformative journey to discover their own understanding of God's presence in the world. 

Genres: Philosophy, Spirituality, Religious Studies

Themes: Existence of God, Personal Belief, Spiritual Awakening
