Synopsis: 

'About Algeria: Algiers, Tlemçen, Constantine, Biskra, Timgad' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and rich history of Algeria. Written by Charles Thomas-Stanford, this travelogue explores the enchanting cities of Algiers, Tlemçen, Constantine, Biskra, and Timgad, offering a vivid portrayal of their unique cultural heritage, architectural wonders, and breathtaking natural beauty. From the bustling streets of Algiers to the ancient ruins of Timgad, the author's immersive descriptions and personal anecdotes paint a vibrant picture of Algeria's past and present. Through encounters with locals, exploration of hidden gems, and encounters with the country's complex history, readers will be transported to a land of captivating contrasts and untold stories.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural diversity, Architectural heritage, Historical significance, Natural beauty
