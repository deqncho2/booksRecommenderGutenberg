Synopsis: In 'The Ghost of Jerry Bundler', a young man named Tom receives an unexpected visit from the ghost of a notorious thief named Jerry Bundler. As Tom becomes entangled in Jerry's mischievous plans, he must navigate a world of secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Tom finds himself questioning his own beliefs and discovering the true nature of friendship and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Ghost story, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Redemption
