Synopsis: In 'Trapped by Malays: A Tale of Bayonet and Kris', a group of British sailors find themselves stranded on a remote island in the Malay Archipelago. As they struggle to survive in the harsh jungle environment, they soon discover that they are not alone. With danger lurking at every turn, the sailors must navigate treacherous encounters with pirates, native tribes, and the unforgiving elements. Will they be able to escape the clutches of their captors and make it back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Survival
