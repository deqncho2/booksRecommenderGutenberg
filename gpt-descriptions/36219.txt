Synopsis: 
'Sawdust & Spangles: Stories & Secrets of the Circus' takes readers on a captivating journey into the enchanting world of the circus. Through a collection of intriguing stories and hidden secrets, author W. C. Coup unveils the behind-the-scenes magic that unfolds under the big top. From daring acrobats to mysterious fortune tellers, from mischievous clowns to awe-inspiring animal acts, this book offers a glimpse into the lives of those who make the circus their home. As the stories unfold, readers will be drawn into a world filled with wonder, danger, and the indomitable spirit of the circus.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Memoir, Entertainment

Themes: 
Magic and wonder, Behind-the-scenes secrets, The allure of the circus
