Synopsis: 
In 'The Millionaire Baby' by Anna Katharine Green, a wealthy family is thrown into turmoil when their beloved baby mysteriously disappears. As the investigation unfolds, dark secrets and hidden motives come to light, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Deception
