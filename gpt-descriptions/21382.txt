Synopsis: In 'Son Philip' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Philip finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Philip must navigate treacherous paths, face unexpected adversaries, and uncover the truth that could change his life forever. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Coming of age, Courage, Perseverance
