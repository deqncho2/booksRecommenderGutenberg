Synopsis: In the heart of Dublin, a deadly power struggle unfolds between rival triad gangs, each vying for control over the city's criminal underworld. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, an unlikely alliance forms between a young, ambitious gang member and a seasoned detective. Together, they must navigate the treacherous streets, uncovering dark secrets and facing dangerous adversaries, in a race against time to bring justice to a city on the brink of chaos.

Genres: Crime, Thriller, Action

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal, Justice
