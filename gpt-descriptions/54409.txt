Synopsis: 
In 'New England Joke Lore: The Tonic of Yankee Humor', Arthur George Crandall takes readers on a delightful journey through the rich tradition of New England humor. From witty one-liners to hilarious anecdotes, this collection showcases the unique wit and cleverness of the Yankee spirit. With a blend of classic and contemporary jokes, Crandall captures the essence of New England humor, offering readers a hearty dose of laughter and a glimpse into the region's cultural heritage.

Genres: Humor, Cultural Studies, Folklore

Themes: Regional identity, Tradition, Wit and cleverness
