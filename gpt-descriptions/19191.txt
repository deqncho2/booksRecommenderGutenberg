Synopsis: In the small New England town of Hanaford, a young woman named Justine Brent finds herself caught in a web of love, ambition, and social justice. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships with two very different men, she is faced with difficult choices that will test her integrity and challenge the societal norms of the time. Set against the backdrop of a progressive industrial community, 'The Fruit of the Tree' explores the moral dilemmas and sacrifices one must make in the pursuit of happiness and justice.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Social inequality, Ethics, Love and sacrifice
