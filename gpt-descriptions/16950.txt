Synopsis: In "Goblin Market, The Prince's Progress, and Other Poems," Christina Georgina Rossetti weaves a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking poems that explore the depths of desire, temptation, and the power of love. From the mysterious allure of the Goblin Market to the captivating journey of the Prince's Progress, Rossetti's words transport readers to a world where fantasy and reality intertwine, leaving them captivated by the beauty and complexity of her storytelling.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Victorian Literature

Themes: Desire, Temptation, Love, Femininity, Morality
