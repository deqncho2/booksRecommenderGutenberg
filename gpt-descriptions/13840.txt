Synopsis: In 'The Sign of the Red Cross: A Tale of Old London' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, readers are transported to the bustling streets of Old London, where a mysterious symbol begins to appear, capturing the attention of the city's inhabitants. As the symbol's significance deepens, a young protagonist embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover its secrets, encountering danger, intrigue, and unexpected allies along the way. With each step closer to the truth, the protagonist becomes entangled in a web of ancient mysteries and hidden treasures, leading to a climactic showdown that will forever change the fate of Old London.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and Symbols, Loyalty and Betrayal, Redemption and Discovery
