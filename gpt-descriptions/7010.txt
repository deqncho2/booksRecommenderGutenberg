Synopsis: 

'Graded Lessons in English' is an elementary English grammar book that offers one hundred practical lessons, carefully graded and adapted for classroom use. Written by Kellogg, Brainerd, and Reed, this comprehensive guide aims to provide students with a solid foundation in English grammar. Each lesson is designed to be engaging and interactive, allowing learners to grasp the fundamental concepts of the language in a systematic manner. From basic sentence structure to more complex grammar rules, this book covers a wide range of topics, ensuring that students develop a strong understanding of English grammar. 

Genres: Educational, Language Learning, Grammar Guide

Themes: Language acquisition, Classroom instruction, Language proficiency
