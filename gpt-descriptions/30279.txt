Synopsis: In 'The One Hoss Shay: With its Companion Poems How the Old Horse Won the Bet & The Broomstick Train', Oliver Wendell Holmes takes readers on a whimsical journey through three delightful poems. From the tale of a sturdy carriage that withstands the test of time, to the thrilling account of a horse race that defies all odds, and finally, a fantastical adventure aboard a magical broomstick train, Holmes weaves together a collection of charming and imaginative stories that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Fantasy

Themes: Timelessness, Resilience, Imagination
