Synopsis: In 'The Hills of Refuge: A Novel', Will N. Harben takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes of the Appalachian Mountains. Set in a small, close-knit community, the story follows the lives of two families whose destinies become intertwined in unexpected ways. As secrets are unveiled and long-held grudges resurface, the characters are forced to confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With its richly drawn characters and vivid descriptions, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the enduring power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Southern Literature

Themes: Family, Redemption, Community, Secrets
