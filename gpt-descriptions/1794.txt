Synopsis: 
In the kingdom of Britain, an aging king named Lear decides to divide his kingdom among his three daughters based on their declarations of love for him. However, the consequences of this decision lead to a tragic chain of events that unravel the very fabric of the kingdom. As Lear's power diminishes, his family and kingdom descend into chaos, betrayal, and madness. Amidst the turmoil, the play explores the themes of power, loyalty, family, and the consequences of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Power, Loyalty, Family, Madness, Betrayal
