Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boys in Strange Waters; or, Lost in a Floating Forest' by Clarence Young, the adventurous trio finds themselves embarking on a thrilling journey that takes them to uncharted territories. When the boys stumble upon a mysterious floating forest, they are drawn into a series of unexpected events and encounters that test their courage and resourcefulness. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter strange characters, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit and determination to unravel the secrets hidden within the floating forest and find their way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
