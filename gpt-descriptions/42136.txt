Synopsis: In 'Rebel Women' by Evelyn Sharp, follow the captivating journey of three extraordinary women who defy societal norms and expectations in the early 20th century. From a suffragette fighting for women's rights, to a daring journalist uncovering corruption, and a talented artist challenging artistic conventions, these women navigate a world that seeks to confine them. As their paths intertwine, they find strength, resilience, and a shared determination to break free from the shackles of their time. With secrets, passion, and a touch of rebellion, this novel takes readers on a thrilling ride through the lives of these inspiring women.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction, Drama

Themes: Feminism, Independence, Social Change, Artistic Expression
