Synopsis: 

In 'Preliminary Report of the Commission Appointed by the University of Pennsylvania to Investigate Modern Spiritualism: In Accordance with the Request of the Late Henry Seybert', the University of Pennsylvania delves into the mysterious world of modern spiritualism. Tasked with investigating the claims of supernatural phenomena, the Seybert Commission embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind these otherworldly experiences. Through rigorous scientific methods and meticulous research, the commission aims to shed light on the enigmatic realm of spiritualism and its impact on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Investigation

Themes: Supernatural, Science vs. Belief, Skepticism
