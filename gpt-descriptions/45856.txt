Synopsis: In 'The Smugglers: Picturesque Chapters in the Story of an Ancient Craft', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden world of smugglers. From the rugged coastlines of England to the bustling streets of London, Harper uncovers the secrets and adventures of those who engage in this ancient craft. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, he reveals the ingenious methods used by smugglers to outsmart authorities and transport their illicit goods. From tales of daring escapes to encounters with notorious smugglers, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world shrouded in mystery and danger.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Adventure

Themes: Smuggling, Crime, Intrigue, History
