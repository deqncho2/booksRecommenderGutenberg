Synopsis: In 'Demetrius: A Play' by Friedrich Schiller, a mysterious young man claiming to be the long-lost heir to the Russian throne emerges, sparking a power struggle and igniting the passions of those around him. As the truth becomes increasingly elusive, loyalties are tested, secrets are revealed, and the fate of an empire hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical drama, political intrigue, psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, power, deception, loyalty, ambition
