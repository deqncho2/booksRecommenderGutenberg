Synopsis: In the Border Country is a captivating novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged and mysterious Scottish borderlands. Set in the 16th century, the story follows the life of young Wat Scott, a courageous and enigmatic protagonist who finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous feud between rival clans. As Wat navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With its rich historical backdrop and gripping narrative, this tale of adventure, loyalty, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Conflict
