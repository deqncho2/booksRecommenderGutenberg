Synopsis: 
In 'The Young Alaskans on the Trail' by Hough Emerson, a group of adventurous young friends embarks on a thrilling journey through the rugged wilderness of Alaska. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter wild animals, and face unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With danger lurking at every turn, the young Alaskans must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to survive and achieve their ultimate goal. Will they conquer the trail and emerge victorious, or will the unforgiving wilderness prove too much for them?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
