Synopsis: In 'The Beautiful and Damned', F. Scott Fitzgerald takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of Anthony and Gloria Patch, a young couple living in the vibrant and decadent world of 1920s New York. As they navigate the highs and lows of their tumultuous relationship, we witness their pursuit of wealth, pleasure, and the elusive American Dream. However, as their lives become consumed by excess and self-indulgence, they soon find themselves facing the consequences of their choices. Will they be able to find redemption or will they be forever trapped in their own self-destructive desires?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Wealth and materialism, Love and relationships, The pursuit of happiness, The decline of the American Dream
