Synopsis: In 'The Power and the Glory' by Grace MacGowan Cooke, a young woman named Judith finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a valuable necklace from her late aunt. As she delves into the secrets surrounding the necklace, Judith becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With each clue she uncovers, the stakes grow higher, and Judith must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Identity
