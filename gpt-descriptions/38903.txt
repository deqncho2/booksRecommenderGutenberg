Synopsis: 

"The Business of Mining" is a captivating non-fiction book that delves into the fascinating world of mining operations, offering a concise and accessible exploration of the principles behind running profitable mines. Written by Arthur J. Hoskin, this book takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of the mining industry, revealing the secrets to success in this challenging field. From the extraction of valuable resources to the management of labor and finances, Hoskin provides valuable insights and practical advice for anyone interested in the business side of mining. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Mining

Themes: Mining operations, Profitability, Resource extraction, Labor management, Financial management
