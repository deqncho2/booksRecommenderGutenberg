Synopsis: In 'That Last Waif; or, Social Quarantine' by Fletcher, Horace, a mysterious waif appears in a small town, captivating the residents with her enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with uncovering her secrets, they are drawn into a web of intrigue and deception that threatens to unravel their own lives. With each passing day, the waif's influence grows stronger, blurring the lines between reality and illusion, and forcing the townspeople to confront their deepest fears and desires. In this gripping tale of suspense and psychological exploration, Fletcher, Horace masterfully weaves a narrative that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Obsession, Fear
