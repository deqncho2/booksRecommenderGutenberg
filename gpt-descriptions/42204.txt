Synopsis: In 'Our Little Turkish Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Turkish girl named Ayisha, who finds herself torn between tradition and her desire for education and independence. As Ayisha navigates the vibrant streets of Istanbul and encounters new experiences, she discovers the power of friendship, the importance of cultural heritage, and the strength to challenge societal norms. Will Ayisha be able to forge her own path in a world that often restricts her? Join her on this enchanting adventure through the rich tapestry of Turkish culture and traditions.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Education, Independence, Friendship, Cultural Identity
