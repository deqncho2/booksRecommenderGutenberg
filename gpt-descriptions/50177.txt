Synopsis: In 'The Fourth Generation' by Walter Besant, a mysterious family secret is unearthed, leading to a thrilling journey through time and generations. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of deceit, love, and betrayal that spans four generations. With each revelation, the stakes rise, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the family's dark past.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Time, Deception, Betrayal
