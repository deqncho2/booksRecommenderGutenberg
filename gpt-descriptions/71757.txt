Synopsis: 
In 'Waves and ripples in water, air, and æther', Sir John Ambrose Fleming takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of waves and ripples. Through a series of Christmas lectures delivered at the Royal Institution of Great Britain, Fleming explores the intricate patterns and behaviors of waves in various mediums, from water to air and even the mysterious æther. With vivid descriptions and engaging examples, the author unveils the hidden secrets behind these natural phenomena, leaving readers in awe of the beauty and complexity of the world around us.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Waves, Ripples, Natural phenomena, Scientific exploration
