Synopsis: In "The Long Hillside: A Christmas Hare-Hunt In Old Virginia; 1908," Thomas Nelson Page takes us on a captivating journey to the picturesque countryside of Virginia during the holiday season. Amidst the joyous festivities, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling hare-hunt, weaving together a tale of adventure, camaraderie, and unexpected twists. As they navigate the rugged terrain and face various challenges, the bonds of friendship are tested, secrets are revealed, and the true spirit of Christmas shines through. Set against the backdrop of a bygone era, this heartwarming story captures the essence of tradition, love, and the enduring power of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Christmas

Themes: Friendship, Tradition, Christmas spirit
