Synopsis: In "A Russian Proprietor, and Other Stories," Tolstoy takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that delve into the complexities of human nature and the moral dilemmas faced by individuals in Russian society. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of redemption and self-discovery, each narrative offers a unique glimpse into the lives of diverse characters and their struggles to find meaning and purpose in a rapidly changing world. With his masterful storytelling and profound insights, Tolstoy invites readers to reflect on the universal themes of love, morality, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Morality, Redemption, Self-discovery
