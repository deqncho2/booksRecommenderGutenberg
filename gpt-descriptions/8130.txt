Synopsis: 
In 'Glimpses of Unfamiliar Japan: First Series', Lafcadio Hearn takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting and mysterious landscapes of Japan. Through his vivid descriptions and keen observations, Hearn unveils the hidden aspects of Japanese culture, from ancient traditions and folklore to the everyday lives of its people. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene beauty of rural villages, Hearn's encounters with the people and places of Japan offer a unique and intimate glimpse into a world that is both familiar and unfamiliar. 

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Japanese culture, Tradition, Folklore, Exploration
