Synopsis: In "The Rocket: The Story of the Stephensons, Father and Son," Helen C. Knight takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of George and Robert Stephenson, two remarkable figures who revolutionized the world of transportation. From their humble beginnings to their groundbreaking inventions, this biographical account delves into the triumphs, challenges, and complex relationship between these pioneering engineers. As the story unfolds, readers will witness the birth of the steam locomotive and the impact it had on society, as well as the personal sacrifices and determination that drove the Stephensons to achieve greatness. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Knight brings to life the fascinating tale of these visionary men and their lasting legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Family, Industrial Revolution
