Synopsis: In "The Lowest Rung: Together with The Hand on the Latch, St. Luke's Summer and The Understudy," Mary Cholmondeley weaves together four captivating stories that delve into the lives of individuals from different social classes. From the struggles of a young governess to the secrets hidden behind the doors of a grand estate, these interconnected tales offer a compelling exploration of human relationships, societal expectations, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Social class, Love and relationships, Identity, Gender roles
