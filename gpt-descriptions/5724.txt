Synopsis: 
In 'A Book of Operas: Their Histories, Their Plots, and Their Music', Henry Edward Krehbiel takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of opera. From the enchanting tales of love and tragedy to the grandeur of the music, Krehbiel delves into the histories and plots of some of the most beloved operas of all time. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, he brings to life the stories behind the music, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the passion and drama of each opera. Whether you are a seasoned opera enthusiast or a newcomer to the art form, this book will leave you captivated and eager to explore the magical world of opera.

Genres: Music, History, Performing Arts

Themes: Love, Tragedy, Music Appreciation
