Synopsis: In the captivating novel "The Highland Glen: or, Plenty and Famine" by Matilda Wrench, a remote Scottish village becomes the backdrop for a tale of mystery, love, and survival. As the glen experiences both abundance and scarcity, the lives of its inhabitants are forever changed. Secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed, all against the backdrop of the breathtaking Highland landscape. Will the glen's residents find a way to overcome the challenges that fate has thrown their way?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Resilience, Community, Secrets, Survival
