Synopsis: In 'Waring's Peril' by Charles King, a young and ambitious journalist named Jack Waring finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious manuscript. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes the target of powerful forces determined to keep the truth hidden. With his life on the line, Waring must navigate treacherous waters and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, the pursuit of truth, the power of knowledge
