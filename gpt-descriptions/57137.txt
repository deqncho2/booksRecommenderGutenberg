Synopsis: In "Barry Wynn; Or, The Adventures of a Page Boy in the United States Congress," young Barry Wynn finds himself thrust into the heart of American politics as a page boy in the United States Congress. As he navigates the corridors of power, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, secrets, and unexpected alliances. With his wit, determination, and a touch of naivety, Barry must navigate the treacherous world of politics while uncovering the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy that threatens to shake the very foundations of democracy.

Genres: Political thriller, coming-of-age, mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, loyalty and betrayal, the pursuit of truth
