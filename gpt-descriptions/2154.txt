Synopsis: 
Join Phileas Fogg, a wealthy and eccentric Englishman, on an extraordinary adventure that will take him around the world in just eighty days. Fogg, accompanied by his loyal French valet Passepartout, embarks on a race against time, facing numerous obstacles and unexpected encounters in their quest to complete this seemingly impossible journey. From the bustling streets of London to the exotic landscapes of India, the treacherous waters of the Pacific Ocean to the wild American West, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering if Fogg can truly conquer the challenge and make it back in time. 

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Differences
