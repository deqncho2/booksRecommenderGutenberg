Synopsis: In "Sparky Ames of the Ferry Command" by Roy J. Snell, follow the thrilling adventures of Sparky Ames, a young pilot who joins the Ferry Command during World War II. As she navigates dangerous skies, faces unexpected challenges, and encounters mysterious characters, Sparky's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. With each mission, she learns the true meaning of bravery and discovers the power of friendship. Will Sparky be able to overcome the obstacles that come her way and fulfill her duty to her country?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism
