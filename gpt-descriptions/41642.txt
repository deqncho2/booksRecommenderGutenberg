Synopsis: In "The Court of Cacus; Or, The Story of Burke and Hare" by Alexander Leighton, a dark and gripping tale unfolds as the notorious duo, Burke and Hare, embark on a chilling journey through the underbelly of 19th-century Edinburgh. Driven by desperation and greed, they delve into the macabre world of grave-robbing and murder, selling their victims' bodies to medical schools. As their crimes escalate, the line between predator and prey becomes blurred, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Greed, Desperation, Manipulation
