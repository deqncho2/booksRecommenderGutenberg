Synopsis: In 'The Ten Pleasures of Marriage: and the Second Part, The Confession of the New Married Couple', Marsh takes readers on a captivating journey through the joys and challenges of married life. Through a series of humorous and insightful anecdotes, the author explores the various pleasures that can be found within the institution of marriage, while also delving into the complexities and compromises that come with it. From the excitement of the honeymoon phase to the realities of everyday life, this book offers a candid and entertaining exploration of the intricacies of marriage.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Marriage, Relationships, Humor
