Synopsis:
In "The History of Duelling. Vol. 1 (of 2)" by J. G. Millingen, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the intriguing world of duelling. From ancient civilizations to the Renaissance and beyond, this volume explores the origins, evolution, and cultural significance of this controversial practice. Through vivid anecdotes and meticulous research, Millingen uncovers the hidden stories behind famous duels, shedding light on the motivations, codes of honor, and societal implications that surrounded these deadly encounters. With a blend of historical analysis and captivating storytelling, this volume sets the stage for an enthralling exploration of a practice that both fascinated and horrified generations.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Honor, Violence, Society, Cultural Practices
