Synopsis: In 'Facts and Assertions', George Stanley Faber presents a compelling and thought-provoking examination of the doctrines of the Church of Rome. Through a meticulous analysis of the Sacred Scriptures and the writings of early Christian Church Catholic, Faber reveals the striking incongruity between these doctrines and the foundations of the faith. With a clear and concise style, he challenges the reader to question the teachings of the Church of Rome and consider the implications for their own beliefs.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Theological

Themes: Religious doctrine, Scriptural interpretation, Early Christian Church, Catholicism, Faith and belief
