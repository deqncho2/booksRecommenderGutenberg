Synopsis: 
In 'Bessie on Her Travels' by Joanna H. Mathews, readers are taken on a captivating journey alongside the adventurous and spirited Bessie. As she embarks on a series of travels, Bessie encounters a myriad of fascinating characters, navigates through unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of self-discovery. With each new destination, Bessie's courage and resilience are put to the test, leading her to uncover secrets that will forever change her perspective on life. This enchanting tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, making it impossible to put down.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Resilience
