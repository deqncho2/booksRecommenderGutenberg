Synopsis: 

In the second volume of the Woman's Institute Library of Cookery, readers are taken on a culinary journey exploring the delightful world of milk, butter, cheese, eggs, and vegetables. This comprehensive guide offers a treasure trove of recipes, techniques, and tips that will inspire both novice and experienced cooks alike. From creamy and indulgent dairy-based dishes to the vibrant and nutritious wonders of vegetables, this volume is a must-have for anyone looking to elevate their culinary skills and create delicious meals for themselves and their loved ones.

Genres: Cookbook, Culinary Reference, Food and Beverage

Themes: Culinary arts, Nutrition, Farm-to-table cooking, Healthy eating, Food preparation techniques
