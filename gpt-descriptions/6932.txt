Synopsis: 

'Pictures Every Child Should Know: A Selection of the World's Art Masterpieces for Young People' by Mary Schell Hoke Bacon is a captivating and educational journey through the world of art. This beautifully illustrated book introduces young readers to a diverse range of art masterpieces from different cultures and time periods. From the enigmatic smile of the Mona Lisa to the vibrant colors of Van Gogh's Starry Night, each artwork is accompanied by engaging descriptions that bring the paintings to life. Through this collection, children will not only develop an appreciation for art but also gain a deeper understanding of the stories, emotions, and ideas behind each masterpiece.

Genres: Art, Education, Children's Literature
Themes: Art appreciation, Cultural diversity, Historical context
