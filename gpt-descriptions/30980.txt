Synopsis: 
In 'Kidnapped at the Altar; Or, The Romance of that Saucy Jessie Bain', the spirited and independent Jessie Bain finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger on her wedding day. As she is whisked away by a mysterious stranger, she embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and challenge her notions of love and loyalty. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and redemption
