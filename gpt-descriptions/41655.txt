Synopsis: In 'Stephen: A Soldier of the Cross' by Florence Morse Kingsley, readers are transported to ancient Rome where they follow the captivating journey of Stephen, a young Christian who faces persecution and trials in his unwavering faith. As Stephen navigates the treacherous world of gladiators, political intrigue, and forbidden love, he must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and commitment to his beliefs. This gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Stephen and the impact of his unwavering faith.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith, Persecution, Redemption
