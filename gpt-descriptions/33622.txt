Synopsis: In 'The Day of Wrath: A Story of 1914' by Tracy, Louis, the world is on the brink of war as tensions rise in Europe in the year 1914. Amidst the political turmoil and impending conflict, a group of individuals from different walks of life find themselves intertwined in a web of secrets, betrayal, and love. As the clock ticks towards the fateful day, their lives become entangled in a race against time, where the choices they make will have far-reaching consequences. Will they be able to navigate the treacherous path ahead and find redemption amidst the chaos?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Thriller

Themes: War, Love, Betrayal, Redemption
