Synopsis: In 'Time and Tide: A Romance of the Moon', Robert S. Ball takes readers on a thrilling journey to the moon, where a group of intrepid explorers uncover a mysterious secret that could change the course of human history. As they navigate the treacherous lunar landscape and encounter unexpected challenges, they must confront their own fears and desires. With its blend of adventure, suspense, and scientific speculation, this captivating novel will leave readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the truth that lies hidden among the stars.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Human curiosity, Scientific discovery
