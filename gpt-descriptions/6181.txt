Synopsis: In the second volume of "A Romany of the Snows," we delve deeper into the captivating lives of Pierre and his people. As the last existing records of the enigmatic Pretty Pierre are revealed, we are taken on a journey through the rugged landscapes of the Canadian North, where love, betrayal, and redemption intertwine. With the stakes higher than ever, the characters face their pasts and confront the consequences of their actions, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Survival, Love and Betrayal
