Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'A Noble Queen: A Romance of Indian History', Taylor Meadows takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and tumultuous world of ancient India. As the story unfolds, we follow the remarkable life of a noble queen, whose strength and determination defy the constraints of her time. Amidst political intrigue, forbidden love, and epic battles, the queen must navigate treacherous waters to protect her kingdom and secure her legacy. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, Meadows paints a vivid portrait of a woman who defies societal norms and rises above adversity to become a true symbol of power and resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles and Empowerment
