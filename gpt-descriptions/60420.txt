Synopsis: In 'Observations on the Automaton Chess Player Now Exhibited in London, at 4 Spring Gardens', an Oxford graduate delves into the mysterious world of a chess-playing automaton that has captivated audiences in London. As he investigates the inner workings of this mechanical marvel, he uncovers a web of secrets and surprises that challenge his understanding of human ingenuity and deception. With each revelation, the boundaries between man and machine blur, leaving the reader questioning what it truly means to be alive. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Science Fiction

Themes: Deception, Artificial Intelligence, Identity
