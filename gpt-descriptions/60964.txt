Synopsis: In 'A Righted Wrong: A Novel. Volume 1 (of 3)' by Edmund Yates, a mysterious murder sets the stage for a gripping tale of deception, revenge, and redemption. As the protagonist delves into the dark underbelly of Victorian London, he uncovers a web of secrets that threaten to unravel the lives of those involved. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and suspense, where the line between justice and vengeance becomes blurred. Will the truth prevail, or will the past continue to haunt the present?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Redemption
