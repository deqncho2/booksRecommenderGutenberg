Synopsis: In 'Stories for the Young; Or, Cheap Repository Tracts: Entertaining, Moral, and Religious. Vol. VI.', Hannah More presents a collection of captivating tales that are both entertaining and thought-provoking. From the heartwarming story of a young girl's determination to help her family, to the unexpected adventures of a mischievous boy, these stories will captivate readers of all ages. Each narrative is carefully crafted to convey important moral and religious lessons, making this volume a valuable resource for both education and enjoyment.

Genres: Children's Literature, Moral Fiction, Religious Fiction

Themes: Morality, Family, Determination, Faith
