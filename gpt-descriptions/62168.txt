Synopsis: In 'Asteroid of the Damned', a team of scientists embarks on a dangerous mission to investigate a mysterious asteroid hurtling towards Earth. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the asteroid, they uncover shocking discoveries that challenge their understanding of the universe and the very nature of humanity. With time running out, they must confront their own fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to save the world from impending doom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Existentialism, Human nature, Survival
