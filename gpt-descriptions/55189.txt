Synopsis:
In 'The Summit House Mystery; Or, The Earthly Purgatory' by Lily Dougall, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a remote mountain lodge during a snowstorm. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must unravel the mysteries of the house and confront their own pasts before it's too late. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the eerie happenings in the Summit House.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Redemption, Confronting the past
