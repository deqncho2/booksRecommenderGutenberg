Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies on the Firing Line; Or, Twelve Days Battle Along the Marne', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside two young American boys who find themselves caught in the midst of the intense Battle of the Marne during World War I. As they navigate the chaos and danger of the front lines, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to survive. With each passing day, the stakes grow higher, and the boys must face unimaginable challenges that will test their mettle and shape their destinies. Will they emerge victorious or succumb to the horrors of war?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
