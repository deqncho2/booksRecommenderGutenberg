Synopsis: 
In the play "Philoktetes" by Sophocles, a renowned Greek warrior named Philoktetes is abandoned on a deserted island due to a mysterious and debilitating injury. Years later, a group of Greek soldiers, led by the cunning Odysseus, embark on a mission to retrieve Philoktetes and his powerful bow. However, their plans are complicated by the ethical dilemmas they face and the deep emotional wounds that Philoktetes carries. As the story unfolds, the characters are forced to confront their own flaws and question the true nature of heroism.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Morality, Heroism
