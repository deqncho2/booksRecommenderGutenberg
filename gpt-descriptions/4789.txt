Synopsis: In "Black Caesar's Clan: A Florida Mystery Story," Terhune takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mysterious swamps of Florida. When a young man inherits a vast fortune from his late uncle, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. As he delves deeper into his family's dark past, he uncovers a hidden treasure and a sinister clan determined to protect their legacy at any cost. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Betrayal, Treasure hunting
