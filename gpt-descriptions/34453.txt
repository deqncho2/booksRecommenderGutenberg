Synopsis: 
In 'More Celtic Fairy Tales', prepare to be transported to a mystical world filled with enchanting creatures, magical landscapes, and captivating adventures. This collection of traditional Celtic tales will immerse you in a realm where brave heroes, cunning fairies, and mischievous leprechauns roam. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of bravery and redemption, each narrative will leave you spellbound and yearning for more. Discover the secrets of ancient Celtic folklore and embark on a journey that will ignite your imagination and awaken your sense of wonder.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Magic, Heroism, Love and Betrayal, Redemption
