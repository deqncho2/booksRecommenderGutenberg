Synopsis: In 'Possessed' by Moffett Cleveland, a young woman named Emma discovers an ancient artifact that unleashes a mysterious power within her. As she grapples with this newfound ability, she becomes entangled in a dangerous world of supernatural forces and dark secrets. With her life hanging in the balance, Emma must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and confront the malevolent forces that seek to control her.

Genres: Supernatural, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and control, Identity and self-discovery
