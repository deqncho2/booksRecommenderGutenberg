Synopsis: In 'Historic Paris' by Wolff, Jetta Sophia, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of Paris as the protagonist, Jetta, uncovers the hidden secrets and rich history of the city. From the grandeur of the Louvre to the mysterious catacombs beneath the bustling streets, Jetta's exploration takes her on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she delves deeper into the past, Jetta discovers that the history of Paris holds not only beauty and romance but also dark and haunting tales that have shaped the city's identity. Will Jetta be able to unravel the mysteries that lie within the historic heart of Paris?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History, Secrets, Exploration, Identity
