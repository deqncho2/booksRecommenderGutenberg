Synopsis: In 'Dave Dawson on Convoy Patrol' by Robert Sidney Bowen, Dave Dawson finds himself on a thrilling mission as he joins a convoy patrol during World War II. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, Dave encounters enemy submarines, dangerous weather conditions, and unexpected allies. With his courage and determination tested to the limit, Dave must rely on his wits and resourcefulness to protect the convoy and ensure its safe passage. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
