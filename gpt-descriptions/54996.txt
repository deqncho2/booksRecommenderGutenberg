Synopsis: In 'The Yale Cup' by Albertus T. Dudley, a mysterious and coveted trophy goes missing from the prestigious Yale University. As the search for the cup unfolds, a group of determined students find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances. With each clue they uncover, the stakes rise higher, leading them on a thrilling journey that will test their loyalty and courage. Will they be able to solve the mystery and recover the Yale Cup before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Loyalty, Secrets
