Synopsis:
In 'Witness for the Defence' by A. E. W. Mason, a renowned barrister finds himself defending a man accused of a heinous crime. As the trial unfolds, shocking revelations and unexpected twists emerge, challenging the barrister's beliefs and forcing him to question the nature of truth and justice. With the stakes high and the evidence murky, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the complexities of the legal system and the depths of human nature.

Genres:
Mystery, Legal Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes:
Truth and deception, Justice and morality, The fallibility of memory
