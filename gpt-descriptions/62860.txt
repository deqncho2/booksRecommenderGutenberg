Synopsis: In 'Hidden Foes; Or, A Fatal Miscalculation' by Carter, Nicholas, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers hidden foes and a fatal miscalculation that could have dire consequences. With time running out, the detective must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Consequences
