Synopsis: In 'Monica: A Novel, Volume 1 (of 3)', we are introduced to the captivating Monica, a young woman with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. Set in the enchanting English countryside, Monica's life takes an unexpected turn when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. As she navigates through the complexities of her relationships and the challenges of her own identity, Monica's journey becomes a thrilling and emotional rollercoaster that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next volumes.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
