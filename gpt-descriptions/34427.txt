Synopsis: In 'Dr. Lavendar's People' by Margaret Wade Campbell Deland, a small New England town is forever changed when a charismatic and unconventional doctor arrives. Dr. Lavendar's unorthodox methods and genuine care for his patients quickly endear him to the community, but his arrival also stirs up long-buried secrets and hidden desires. As the townspeople navigate their own personal struggles and relationships, they find themselves drawn to Dr. Lavendar's wisdom and guidance, leading to unexpected transformations and a deeper understanding of what it means to truly live.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Community, Healing, Redemption, Secrets, Transformation
