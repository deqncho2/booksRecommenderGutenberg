Synopsis: 
In 'The Willing Horse: A Novel' by Hay, Ian, a mysterious horse arrives at a small village, captivating the attention of its inhabitants. As the horse's presence begins to unravel the secrets and desires of the villagers, a web of intrigue and passion is woven, leading to unexpected consequences. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this captivating novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of hidden desires.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Desires, Intrigue, Human Nature
