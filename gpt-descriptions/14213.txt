Synopsis: 
In 'Three Months of My Life', we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Emily as she embarks on a life-changing adventure. After receiving a mysterious letter, she finds herself drawn into a world of secrets, intrigue, and unexpected romance. As Emily delves deeper into the enigmatic circumstances surrounding her, she must confront her own fears and make choices that will shape her future. With each passing day, the stakes grow higher, and Emily's determination to uncover the truth becomes unwavering. Will she find the answers she seeks, or will she be forever changed by the events of these three extraordinary months?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Secrets and deception, Love and relationships
