Synopsis: In the heart of a war-torn city, a group of brave individuals form Ambulance Company Number 139, dedicated to saving lives amidst chaos and destruction. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war, they face unimaginable challenges, forge unbreakable bonds, and discover the true meaning of heroism. This gripping tale takes readers on a rollercoaster of emotions, as the ambulance crew's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Survival
