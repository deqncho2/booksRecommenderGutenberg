Synopsis: In 'Max' by Katherine Cecil Thurston, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Max enters the lives of a wealthy family, bringing with him a captivating charm and a hidden past. As Max becomes entangled in their lives, secrets are unveiled, loyalties are tested, and the family's world is turned upside down. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, 'Max' takes readers on a thrilling journey of love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Love and sacrifice
