Synopsis: In 'Honest Money' by Fonda, Arthur Isaac, a down-on-his-luck accountant stumbles upon a mysterious briefcase filled with cash. As he grapples with the moral dilemma of keeping the money or returning it, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deceit and betrayal. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he loves. This gripping thriller will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Crime

Themes: Morality, Deception, Redemption
