Synopsis: In 'Turquoise and Ruby' by L. T. Meade, two young women from vastly different backgrounds find themselves thrown together in a thrilling adventure that takes them from the bustling streets of London to the mysterious depths of the Scottish Highlands. Turquoise, a spirited and independent artist, and Ruby, a sheltered and curious heiress, form an unlikely friendship as they navigate through secrets, danger, and unexpected romance. As they uncover hidden truths and face unforeseen challenges, Turquoise and Ruby must rely on their wits and resilience to unravel a web of intrigue that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Resilience
