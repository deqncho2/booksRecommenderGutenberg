Synopsis: 
In 'A Guide to Mythology', Helen A. Clarke takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of myths and legends from around the world. From the ancient Greek gods and goddesses to the mystical creatures of Norse mythology, this comprehensive guide offers a fascinating exploration of the stories that have shaped cultures throughout history. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Clarke brings these timeless tales to life, unraveling their hidden meanings and shedding light on the universal themes they embody. Whether you are a mythology enthusiast or simply curious about the origins of these captivating stories, 'A Guide to Mythology' is an essential companion that will transport you to a world of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Reference

Themes: Creation and Origin Myths, Heroic Journeys, Symbolism and Allegory
