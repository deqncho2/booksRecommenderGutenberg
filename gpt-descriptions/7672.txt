Synopsis: 

In "Harold: the Last of the Saxon Kings - Volume 01," Edward Bulwer Lytton takes readers on a captivating journey back to 11th century England. Set during the tumultuous reign of King Edward the Confessor, the story follows the life of Harold, a noble Saxon warrior who finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the looming threat of invasion. As Harold navigates the treacherous landscape of power struggles and personal sacrifices, he must confront his own destiny and the fate of his people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Destiny
