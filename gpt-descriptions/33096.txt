Synopsis: In 'Years of Plenty' by Brown, Ivor John Carnegie, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in the whirlwind of the Roaring Twenties, a time of excess and indulgence. As she navigates the glamorous world of high society, Emma becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. With each passing year, she must confront the consequences of her choices and the true cost of living in a time of plenty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Social Class, Love and Betrayal
