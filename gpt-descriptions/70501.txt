Synopsis: In "Moon of Memory" by Bryce Walton, a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that allows him to relive his memories. However, as he delves deeper into his past, he uncovers a shocking secret that threatens to unravel his entire existence. With each memory he revisits, the lines between reality and illusion blur, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Memory, Identity, Perception, Truth
