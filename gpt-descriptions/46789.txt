Synopsis: In 'Rubber' by Edith A. Browne, a seemingly ordinary woman named Alice discovers a mysterious rubber object that possesses an uncanny power. As she delves deeper into its origins and capabilities, Alice becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the true nature of the rubber object is revealed, leading Alice on a thrilling journey that challenges her perception of reality and tests the limits of her courage.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Power, Perception
