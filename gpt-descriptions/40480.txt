Synopsis: In 'The Quest of the Four: A Story of the Comanches and Buena Vista', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed American West. Set during the Mexican-American War, the story follows four young friends who find themselves caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict between the Comanche tribe and the American army. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable foes, the friends must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering bond to survive. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Altsheler paints a captivating tale of adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival, Conflict
