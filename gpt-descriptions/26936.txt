Synopsis: In 'The Gallery' by Phillips, Rog, a mysterious art gallery opens in a small town, captivating the residents with its enigmatic collection. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the gallery, they find themselves drawn into a world where reality and imagination blur, and secrets from the past resurface. With each visit to the gallery, the characters' lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the power of art and the boundaries of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Art and its influence, Perception and reality, Secrets and hidden truths
