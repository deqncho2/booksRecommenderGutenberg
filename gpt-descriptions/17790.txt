Synopsis: In the small New England town of Wharton, a young woman named Jane Field finds herself caught in a web of secrets and scandal. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Jane must confront the consequences of her choices and the true nature of those around her. With its richly drawn characters and gripping plot, 'Jane Field: A Novel' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of 19th-century life.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, societal expectations, the role of women in society
