Synopsis: In 'Sam, This Is You' by Murray Leinster, a man named Sam wakes up in a world that is eerily familiar yet completely different. As he navigates through this perplexing reality, he discovers shocking truths about his own identity and the nature of the world around him. With each revelation, Sam's perception of reality is shattered, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and existential questioning. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality
