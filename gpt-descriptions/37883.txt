Synopsis: 
In 'Steve P. Holcombe, the Converted Gambler: His Life and Work', Alexander Gross takes us on a captivating journey into the life of Steve P. Holcombe, a notorious gambler who undergoes a remarkable transformation. From his early days as a high-stakes gambler to his unexpected conversion, Holcombe's story is filled with thrilling twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As we delve deeper into his life, we witness the profound impact his newfound faith has on his relationships, career, and the gambling world he once dominated. With vivid storytelling and a compelling narrative, Gross explores the complexities of addiction, redemption, and the power of second chances.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Inspirational

Themes: Addiction, Redemption, Faith, Transformation
