Synopsis: In "Is the Bible Indictable?: Being an Enquiry whether the Bible Comes within the Ruling of the Lord Chief Justice as to Obscene Literature," Annie Besant delves into a controversial examination of the Bible's content and its potential classification as obscene literature. With meticulous research and thought-provoking arguments, Besant challenges societal norms and raises important questions about the interpretation and impact of religious texts. This thought-provoking work invites readers to question their own beliefs and consider the boundaries of freedom of expression.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Philosophy

Themes: Freedom of expression, Religious interpretation, Social norms
