Synopsis: In 'The Captain's Story; or, The Disobedient Son', a young man's rebellious nature leads him down a path of adventure and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of the high seas, this captivating tale follows the captain's son as he defies his father's wishes and embarks on a journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and ultimately, his understanding of family bonds. As he encounters treacherous waters, encounters with pirates, and unexpected alliances, the young man must confront his own desires and confront the consequences of his actions. Will he find redemption and reconcile with his father, or will his defiance lead him to a fate he never anticipated?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Rebellion, Family, Identity, Redemption
