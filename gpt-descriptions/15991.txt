Synopsis: 
In "Japhet, in Search of a Father" by Frederick Marryat, a young boy named Japhet embarks on a captivating journey to find his long-lost father. Left in the care of a neglectful guardian, Japhet sets off on a series of adventures, encountering eccentric characters and facing numerous challenges along the way. As he navigates through the highs and lows of his quest, Japhet learns valuable lessons about family, identity, and the true meaning of belonging.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Family, Identity, Belonging
