Synopsis: In "Music as a Language: Lectures to Music Students," Ethel Home takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of music, exploring its profound connection to language. Through a series of enlightening lectures, Home delves into the intricate similarities between music and spoken language, unraveling the secrets behind their shared expressive power. With vivid examples and thought-provoking insights, she unveils the transformative potential of understanding music as a language, inviting readers to embark on a fascinating exploration of this universal art form.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Education

Themes: Language of music, Expressive power, Transformative understanding
