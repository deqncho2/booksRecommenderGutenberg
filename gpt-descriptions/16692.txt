Synopsis: 
'Beyond The Rocks: A Love Story' follows the lives of Theodora Fitzgerald and Lord Bracondale, two individuals from different social classes who find themselves irresistibly drawn to each other. As they navigate the complexities of their forbidden love, they must confront societal expectations, family obligations, and personal sacrifices. Their journey takes them from the glamorous world of high society to the breathtaking landscapes of Europe, where their love is put to the ultimate test. Will their passion withstand the trials and tribulations that fate throws their way?

Genres: Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Social class, Sacrifice
