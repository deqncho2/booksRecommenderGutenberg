Synopsis: In 'Wounded Souls' by Gibbs, Philip, a small town is rocked by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected to a dark secret from the past. As the community becomes consumed by fear and suspicion, a young detective must unravel the truth before more lives are lost. With each twist and turn, the protagonist finds themselves drawn deeper into a web of deceit and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As the stakes rise, the detective must confront their own demons and make a choice that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Redemption, Trust and betrayal
