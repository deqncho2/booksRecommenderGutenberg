Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Curly-Tail' by Smith, Laura Rountree, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Lily as she embarks on a magical adventure to save her beloved pet, Curly-Tail. Filled with mystery, friendship, and unexpected twists, this enchanting tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Love for Animals
