Synopsis: 
'A Day with the Poet Burns' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of the renowned Scottish poet, Robert Burns. Set in the enchanting landscapes of 18th-century Scotland, the story follows a young journalist who is granted a rare opportunity to spend a day with Burns, delving into his creative process, exploring his inspirations, and unraveling the mysteries behind his timeless verses. As the day unfolds, the journalist discovers the profound impact Burns has had on society, the struggles he faced, and the enduring legacy he left behind. This immersive tale offers a glimpse into the mind of a literary genius and the power of words to transcend time and touch the hearts of generations to come.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Literary Fiction

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Legacy, Identity, Love and relationships
