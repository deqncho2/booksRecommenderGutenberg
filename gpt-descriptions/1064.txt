Synopsis: In "The Masque of the Red Death," a wealthy prince and his courtiers lock themselves away in a secluded abbey to escape a deadly plague ravaging the land. As they indulge in extravagant festivities, a mysterious figure clad in a blood-red mask infiltrates their sanctuary, leading to a chilling confrontation that challenges their false sense of security.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, allegory

Themes: Death, the inevitability of mortality, the illusion of safety, the consequences of hedonism
