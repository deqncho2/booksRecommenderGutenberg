Synopsis: In "Joel: A Boy of Galilee," Annie F. Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey to ancient Galilee, where they meet Joel, a young boy with a heart full of curiosity and a spirit eager for adventure. As Joel navigates the bustling streets of his village, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own stories and struggles. From a mysterious healer to a Roman soldier, Joel's encounters shape his understanding of the world and his place in it. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, Johnston paints a vivid picture of life in Galilee during the time of Jesus, capturing the essence of hope, faith, and the power of compassion.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Christian Fiction

Themes: Faith, Compassion, Identity, Cultural Diversity
