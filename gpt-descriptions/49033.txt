Synopsis: In 'A Draught of the Blue, together with An Essence of the Dusk', F. W. Bain takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the mystical realms of love, desire, and the pursuit of eternal beauty. Set in a world where dreams and reality intertwine, the story follows a young artist who becomes captivated by a mysterious woman with an otherworldly allure. As he delves deeper into her enigmatic existence, he discovers a hidden world of passion and secrets that will challenge his perception of love and the boundaries of his own imagination.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Beauty and aesthetics, Illusion and reality
