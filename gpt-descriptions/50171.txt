Synopsis: In "The Art of Aubrey Beardsley," Arthur Symons takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and work of the enigmatic artist, Aubrey Beardsley. From his scandalous illustrations to his unique artistic style, Symons delves into the fascinating world of this influential figure of the late 19th century. Through vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, the book explores Beardsley's artistic evolution, his controversial subjects, and the impact he had on the art world. As readers delve into the pages, they will be captivated by the intricate details of Beardsley's art and the intriguing stories behind each piece, ultimately gaining a deeper understanding of this extraordinary artist.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic rebellion, Aestheticism, Social criticism
