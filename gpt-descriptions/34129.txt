Synopsis: In 'The Trail of Conflict' by Emilie Baker Loring, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a remote mountain lodge. As she delves into the secrets of the lodge's past, she becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and determination. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Courage
