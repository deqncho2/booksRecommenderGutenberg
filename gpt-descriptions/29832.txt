Synopsis: In 'Second Sight' by Basil Wells, a renowned scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows him to see into the future. As he delves deeper into this extraordinary ability, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With each glimpse into the future, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of humanity. As the line between reality and prophecy blurs, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his newfound power and those he holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Time travel, Fate vs. free will, Ethical implications of advanced technology
