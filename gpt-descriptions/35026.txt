Synopsis: 

"The Court Houses of a Century: A Brief Historical Sketch of the Court Houses of London District, the County of Middlesex, and County of Elgin" by Kenneth W. McKay takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of court houses in London District, Middlesex County, and Elgin County. From the early days of settlement to the present, McKay delves into the fascinating stories behind these iconic buildings, exploring their architectural significance, the trials and triumphs that took place within their walls, and the impact they had on the communities they served. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, McKay paints a vivid picture of the evolution of justice and the role these court houses played in shaping the region's legal system. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Local history

Themes: Legal history, Architectural heritage, Community development
