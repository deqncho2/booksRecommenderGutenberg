Synopsis: In 'The Horse in History' by Basil Tozer, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author explores the profound impact that horses have had on human civilization. From their early domestication to their role in warfare, transportation, and leisure, this book delves into the fascinating history of our equine companions. Discover the remarkable stories of legendary horses and the pivotal moments in history where they played a crucial role. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Tozer paints a vivid picture of the horse's enduring significance throughout the ages.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Human-animal relationship, Evolution of transportation, Cultural significance
