Synopsis: 
In 'Billy Whiskers, Jr.', follow the adventures of a mischievous little goat named Billy Whiskers as he embarks on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. From escaping a circus to exploring the bustling city streets, Billy's curious nature leads him into all sorts of exciting escapades. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, both human and animal, who help shape his understanding of the world. Will Billy Whiskers, Jr. find his way back home or will he continue to seek out new adventures?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Independence, Friendship
