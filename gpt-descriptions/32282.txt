Synopsis: In 'Elevator Systems of the Eiffel Tower, 1889' by Vogel, Robert M., readers are taken on a captivating journey through the engineering marvel that is the Eiffel Tower. Delving into the intricate details of the tower's elevator systems, the author unveils the fascinating story behind their creation and the challenges faced by the engineers of the time. From the audacious vision of Gustave Eiffel to the groundbreaking innovations that revolutionized vertical transportation, this book offers a captivating exploration of the technological marvel that forever changed the Parisian skyline.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Engineering marvels, Architectural history
