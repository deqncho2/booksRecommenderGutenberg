Synopsis: In 'The Children's Book of Celebrated Pictures', Lorinda Munson Bryant takes young readers on a captivating journey through the world of art. Filled with stunning illustrations and fascinating stories behind each masterpiece, this book introduces children to renowned paintings from various periods and cultures. From the enigmatic smile of the Mona Lisa to the vibrant colors of Van Gogh's Starry Night, this collection sparks curiosity and imagination, inviting children to explore the beauty and meaning within each picture. Through engaging narratives, Bryant brings these celebrated works to life, encouraging young readers to appreciate art and discover the stories that lie within each brushstroke.

Genres: Children's Literature, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Art appreciation, Creativity, Cultural diversity
