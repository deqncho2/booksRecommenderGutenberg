Synopsis: 

'Tales From Scottish Ballads' by Elizabeth W. Grierson is a captivating collection of traditional Scottish ballads that have been beautifully retold for modern readers. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of heroic battles and supernatural encounters, this book transports readers to the enchanting world of Scottish folklore. Each ballad is filled with vivid imagery, memorable characters, and unexpected twists, making it impossible to put down. Grierson's skillful storytelling brings these ancient tales to life, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of Scottish culture and history.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Heroism, Supernatural Encounters, Scottish Culture and History
