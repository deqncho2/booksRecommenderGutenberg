Synopsis: In 'The Lost Continent' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a young adventurer embarks on a perilous journey to a mysterious and uncharted land. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters strange creatures, he unravels the secrets of this forgotten world, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth hidden within the lost continent.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
