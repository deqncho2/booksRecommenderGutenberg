Synopsis: In 'Odd Numbers: Being Further Chronicles of Shorty McCabe', the lovable and quick-witted Shorty McCabe finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and unexpected adventures. From navigating the treacherous world of high-stakes gambling to outsmarting cunning con artists, Shorty's wit and charm are put to the test. As he navigates the underbelly of society, Shorty's loyalty and resourcefulness are pushed to their limits, leading to a thrilling and unpredictable climax.

Genres: Humor, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Resourcefulness, Wit
