Synopsis: 

'Japanese Literature: Including Selections from Genji Monogatari and Classical Poetry and Drama of Japan' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse world of Japanese literature. This anthology features a selection of works from the renowned 'Genji Monogatari' as well as classical poetry and drama, offering a glimpse into the beauty and complexity of Japanese literary traditions. From the intricate tales of courtly romance to the evocative verses of ancient poetry, this collection showcases the timeless themes and vivid imagery that have captivated readers for centuries. 

Genres: Anthology, Fiction, Poetry, Drama

Themes: Love and romance, societal roles, nature, beauty, tradition
