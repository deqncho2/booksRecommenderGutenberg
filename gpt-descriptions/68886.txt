Synopsis: In "King Edward's Realm: Story of the making of the Empire," C. S. Dawe takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of King Edward, exploring the intricate web of political intrigue, power struggles, and personal sacrifices that shaped the British Empire. From the opulent halls of Buckingham Palace to the farthest corners of the globe, this gripping historical narrative unveils the untold stories of the individuals who played pivotal roles in the expansion of the empire. As the empire grows, so do the challenges faced by its leaders, testing their resolve and forcing them to make difficult decisions that will forever alter the course of history. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Dawe paints a vivid picture of an era defined by ambition, conquest, and the pursuit of greatness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Adventure

Themes: Power and Ambition, Loyalty and Betrayal, Colonialism and Imperialism
