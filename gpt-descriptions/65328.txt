Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Arthur', we are introduced to the enigmatic and captivating character of Mrs. Arthur, a woman of mystery and intrigue. As the story unfolds, we follow her journey through the complexities of society, where secrets and scandals lurk behind every corner. With her wit and charm, Mrs. Arthur navigates the treacherous waters of high society, leaving a trail of curiosity and fascination in her wake. As the first volume of a thrilling trilogy, this book will leave readers eager to uncover the truth behind Mrs. Arthur's captivating persona.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Society, Secrets
