Synopsis: 
In 'Bahá'u'lláh and the New Era', J. E. Esslemont takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and teachings of Bahá'u'lláh, the founder of the Bahá'í Faith. From his early years in Persia to his exile and imprisonment, Bahá'u'lláh's extraordinary life unfolds, revealing his profound spiritual insights and his vision for a united and peaceful world. Esslemont's engaging narrative explores the impact of Bahá'u'lláh's teachings on individuals and societies, offering a glimpse into a new era of spiritual awakening and global transformation.

Genres: Biography, Religion, History

Themes: Spiritual enlightenment, Unity, Social transformation
