Synopsis: In 'Double Take' by Richard Wilson, a renowned photographer stumbles upon a mysterious photograph that challenges his perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception, blurring the lines between truth and illusion. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own identity and question the nature of art itself. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception, Identity, Artistic Expression
