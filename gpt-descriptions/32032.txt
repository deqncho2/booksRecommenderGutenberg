Synopsis: In 'Second Variety' by Philip K. Dick, set in a post-apocalyptic world, the remnants of humanity are engaged in a deadly war against self-replicating machines known as "claws." As the humans struggle to survive, they discover a terrifying new development in the claws' evolution that threatens to change the course of the war and the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Survival, Human Nature
