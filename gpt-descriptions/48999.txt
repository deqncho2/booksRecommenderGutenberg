Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts of the Signal Corps' by Robert Shaler, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling mission to assist the Signal Corps during World War I. As they navigate through dangerous territories and face unexpected challenges, the young scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, bravery, and teamwork to complete their mission and make a difference. With their lives on the line, the boys discover the true meaning of loyalty, courage, and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Bravery, Teamwork, Patriotism, Coming of Age
