Synopsis: 
In 'The Second Fiddle' by Phyllis Bottome, a talented musician finds herself trapped in a life of unfulfilled dreams and unrequited love. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and struggles to find her own voice, she must confront the sacrifices she has made for others and decide if it's finally time to take center stage.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Sacrifice, Ambition, Love, Self-discovery
