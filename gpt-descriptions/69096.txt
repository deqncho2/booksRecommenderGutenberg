Synopsis: In the secluded and mysterious Ashcliffe Hall, secrets lie hidden within its grand walls. As the last century unfolds, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn into the enigmatic world of the Hall, uncovering a web of dark family history and forbidden love. With each revelation, Emily's own life becomes entangled in the haunting legacy of Ashcliffe Hall, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Self-discovery
