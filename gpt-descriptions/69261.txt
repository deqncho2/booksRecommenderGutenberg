Synopsis: In 'A Vagrant Wife' by Florence Warden, a seemingly ordinary woman's life takes a dramatic turn when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she navigates through a world of mystery and danger, she must confront her own past and make choices that will determine her future. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
