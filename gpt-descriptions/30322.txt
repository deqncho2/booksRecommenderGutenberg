Synopsis: In 'The Helpful Hand of God' by Tom Godwin, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that leave the residents bewildered and fearful. As the occurrences escalate, a young woman named Sarah discovers an ancient artifact that holds the key to unlocking the truth behind these inexplicable happenings. With the help of a skeptical journalist, Sarah embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the origins of the artifact and the dark forces at play. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they soon realize that their lives and the fate of the town are intertwined in ways they could never have imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Faith and doubt, the power of belief, the battle between good and evil
