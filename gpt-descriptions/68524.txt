Synopsis: In 'The Optimist' by E. M. Delafield, we follow the life of a young woman named Lydia, who is determined to find happiness and success in a world that seems determined to thwart her at every turn. From her unconventional upbringing to her struggles in love and career, Lydia's unwavering optimism and resilience make for a captivating and inspiring journey.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: Optimism, Resilience, Society's expectations, Gender roles
