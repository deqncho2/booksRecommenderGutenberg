Synopsis: In 'In the Cards' by Alan Cogan, a mysterious deck of tarot cards falls into the hands of a young woman named Lily, changing her life forever. As she delves into the enigmatic world of the cards, she discovers that they hold a power beyond her wildest imagination. With each draw, Lily's reality becomes intertwined with the ancient secrets and hidden forces of the tarot, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and danger. As she navigates the treacherous path laid out by the cards, Lily must confront her deepest fears and make choices that will shape her destiny. Will she unlock the true power of the cards, or will they consume her?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Fate and destiny, Self-discovery, Supernatural forces
