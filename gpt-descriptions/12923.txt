Synopsis: 
In 'The Laird's Luck and Other Fireside Tales', Arthur Quiller-Couch weaves a collection of captivating stories that transport readers to the enchanting landscapes of Cornwall. From tales of love and loss to eerie encounters with the supernatural, each story is filled with vivid imagery and unexpected twists. Whether it's a mysterious shipwreck, a haunted house, or a fateful game of cards, these fireside tales will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Love and Loss, Supernatural Encounters, Fate and Destiny
