Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Landlord at Lion's Head," the story continues to follow the lives of the eclectic group of residents at the boarding house in Lion's Head, a small New England town. As the characters navigate their personal and professional lives, they find themselves entangled in a web of love, ambition, and societal expectations. With the arrival of new tenants and the ongoing dramas of the existing ones, the boarding house becomes a microcosm of the larger world, where dreams clash with reality and secrets threaten to unravel. Will the bonds formed within these walls be strong enough to withstand the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Ambition, Social dynamics, Identity, Secrets
