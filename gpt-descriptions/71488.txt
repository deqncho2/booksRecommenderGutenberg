Synopsis: 
In 'Broadcast' by Will H. Grattan, a renowned radio host finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he receives a cryptic message during his live broadcast. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of those around him. With time running out, he must race against the clock to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Danger and survival
