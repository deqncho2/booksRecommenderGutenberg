Synopsis: In 'Playing With Fire' by Barr, Amelia E., a talented pianist named Lily finds herself entangled in a dangerous game when she discovers a mysterious music box that holds a dark secret. As Lily delves deeper into the box's origins, she becomes entwined with a seductive stranger who seems to know more about her past than she does. With her life and sanity at stake, Lily must navigate a treacherous path of passion, betrayal, and hidden desires, all while trying to unravel the truth behind the music box before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Obsession and desire, Identity and self-discovery
