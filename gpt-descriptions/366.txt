Synopsis: 
In 'Bab: A Sub-Deb' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, we follow the misadventures of Bab, a spirited and unconventional young girl on the cusp of womanhood. With her quick wit and rebellious nature, Bab finds herself entangled in a series of humorous and unexpected situations, from crashing a society ball to getting caught up in a scandalous love affair. As she navigates the complexities of adolescence and the expectations of society, Bab's journey is filled with laughter, romance, and a touch of mystery.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Comedy, Romance

Themes: 
Identity, Rebellion, Society's Expectations
