Synopsis: 
"In the Cage" by Henry James is a captivating novella that follows the life of a young telegraphist working in a post office in London. As she transmits messages between wealthy clients, she becomes privy to their secrets, desires, and scandals. Through her observations and interactions, she navigates the complexities of social class, love, and personal identity, all while being confined to her small cage-like workspace. With each message she sends, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that challenges her own aspirations and forces her to question the boundaries of her own existence.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological, Social Commentary

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and relationships
