Synopsis: In "When Sarah Went to School" by Elsie Singmaster, a young girl named Sarah embarks on a new adventure as she enters school for the first time. From the moment she steps foot in the classroom, Sarah encounters a world filled with new faces, exciting lessons, and unexpected challenges. As she navigates through the ups and downs of school life, Sarah's journey becomes a heartwarming tale of growth, friendship, and the power of education.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, School Story

Themes: Education, Friendship, Personal Growth
