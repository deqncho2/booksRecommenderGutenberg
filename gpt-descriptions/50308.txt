Synopsis: In 'Romney' by Davies, Randall, a young woman named Emma finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she stumbles upon an old diary belonging to a woman named Romney. As Emma delves deeper into Romney's past, she uncovers a web of secrets, forbidden love, and a haunting connection that transcends time. With each page she turns, Emma becomes more entangled in the enigmatic life of Romney, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and a quest for truth that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery, Love and Fate
