Synopsis: 
In this captivating address, Hon. Henry H. Crapo, Governor of Michigan, takes the audience on a thought-provoking journey through the world of agriculture and sheep-shearing. Delivered at the Central Michigan Agricultural Society's Sheep-shearing Exhibition, the speech explores the importance of agriculture in society and the advancements made in the field. Governor Crapo's words inspire and educate, shedding light on the significance of the Agricultural College Farm and its role in shaping the future of Michigan. 

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Speech, Agriculture

Themes: 
Agriculture, Education, Progress
