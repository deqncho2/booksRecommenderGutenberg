Synopsis: 
In 'Living Alone' by Stella Benson, we are introduced to the eccentric and independent Miss Brown, who finds herself unexpectedly living alone in a small English village. As she navigates her new surroundings, she encounters a cast of quirky characters, each with their own secrets and desires. From a mysterious stranger to a mischievous ghost, Miss Brown's life takes an unexpected turn as she becomes entangled in a series of peculiar events that challenge her perception of reality. With wit and charm, Benson takes us on a whimsical journey that explores the joys and complexities of solitude, friendship, and the power of the imagination.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Solitude, Friendship, Imagination
