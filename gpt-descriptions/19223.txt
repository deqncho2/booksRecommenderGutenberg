Synopsis: In 'At War with Pontiac; Or, The Totem of the Bear: A Tale of Redcoat and Redskin', Munroe Kirk takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of North America during the French and Indian War. As tensions rise between the British Redcoats and the Native American tribes, a young British officer finds himself caught between duty and compassion. With vivid descriptions of battles, treacherous landscapes, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale explores the complexities of loyalty, honor, and the clash of cultures in a time of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Cultural Conflict, Survival
