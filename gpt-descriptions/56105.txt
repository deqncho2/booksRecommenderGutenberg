Synopsis: In 'The Dickens Country' by Frederic George Kitton, embark on a captivating journey through the landscapes that inspired Charles Dickens' timeless novels. From the bustling streets of London to the quaint villages of Kent, this book unveils the real-life settings that brought Dickens' vivid characters and gripping stories to life. Through stunning illustrations and insightful anecdotes, discover the hidden gems and historical landmarks that shaped the literary genius of Dickens, immersing readers in a world of mystery, social commentary, and unforgettable tales.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literary inspiration, Historical settings, Cultural heritage
