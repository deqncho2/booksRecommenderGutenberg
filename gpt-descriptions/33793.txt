Synopsis: In 'The Seven-Branched Candlestick: The Schooldays of Young American Jew', we follow the captivating journey of a young American Jew as he navigates the challenges of growing up in a diverse society. Set against the backdrop of his school days, the protagonist grapples with questions of identity, faith, and belonging, while also facing the universal struggles of adolescence. As he encounters a range of characters and experiences, the story delves into the complexities of cultural assimilation, the search for personal meaning, and the power of friendship. With its richly drawn characters and thought-provoking narrative, this coming-of-age tale offers a poignant exploration of the human spirit and the quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Cultural

Themes: Identity, Faith, Belonging, Cultural assimilation, Friendship, Self-discovery
