Synopsis: 
In 'Storyology: Essays in Folk-Lore, Sea-Lore, and Plant-Lore', Benjamin Taylor takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of folklore, sea legends, and plant mythology. Delving into the rich tapestry of human storytelling, Taylor explores the origins and significance of ancient tales, the mysteries of the deep sea, and the enchanting lore surrounding plants. From mythical creatures to maritime adventures and the magical properties of plants, this collection of essays offers a fascinating exploration of the captivating stories that have shaped our cultural heritage.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Non-fiction

Themes: Storytelling, Cultural Heritage, Nature
