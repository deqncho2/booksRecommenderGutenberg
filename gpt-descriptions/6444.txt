Synopsis: 
In 'The Boys of Bellwood School; Or, Frank Jordan's Triumph' by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Frank Jordan, a young and determined student at Bellwood School. Frank faces numerous challenges and obstacles as he navigates the ups and downs of school life, but he refuses to let anything deter him from his goals. With the support of his friends and the guidance of his teachers, Frank embarks on a journey of self-discovery, personal growth, and ultimately, triumph. This captivating story takes readers on a thrilling ride filled with friendship, perseverance, and the power of determination.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: 
Friendship, Perseverance, Personal growth
