Synopsis: 

In "Forty Years a Gambler on the Mississippi," George H. Devol takes readers on a thrilling journey through his life as a professional gambler on the iconic Mississippi River. From his early days as a young card shark to his encounters with notorious outlaws and high-stakes gambling, Devol's memoir is filled with captivating anecdotes and heart-pounding adventures. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, he paints a vivid picture of the vibrant gambling scene that once thrived along the riverbanks. As Devol navigates the treacherous waters of the Mississippi, readers will be enthralled by his cunning strategies, unexpected twists, and the colorful characters he encounters along the way.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Gambling

Themes: Risk-taking, Survival, Life on the Mississippi
