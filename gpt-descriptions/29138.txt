Synopsis: In 'The Doorway' by Evelyn E. Smith, a mysterious doorway appears in the middle of a bustling city, captivating the attention of curious onlookers. As people begin to enter the doorway, they find themselves transported to a world unlike anything they have ever known. With each person's journey through the doorway, secrets are unveiled, and unexpected connections are made. As the protagonist embarks on their own adventure, they must navigate the challenges and revelations that lie beyond the threshold, ultimately questioning the nature of reality and the power of human imagination.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of alternate realities, the power of perception, the boundaries of human understanding
