Synopsis: 
In the heart of Hampstead, a quiet and affluent neighborhood in London, a mysterious crime takes place that leaves the local community in shock. When a prominent resident is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Inspector Chippenfield is called in to investigate. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the mystery only deepens, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will Inspector Chippenfield be able to unravel the enigma of the Hampstead Mystery before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Intrigue
