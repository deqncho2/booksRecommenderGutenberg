Synopsis: In the small town of Cloisterham, the young and charming Edwin Drood suddenly disappears, leaving behind a trail of unanswered questions and a community desperate for answers. As the townspeople grapple with the mystery, suspicions arise, secrets are revealed, and hidden desires come to light. With a cast of eccentric characters, including the enigmatic choirmaster John Jasper and the vivacious Rosa Bud, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Fate, Love and betrayal
