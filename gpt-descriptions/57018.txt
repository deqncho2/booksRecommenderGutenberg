Synopsis: 
'Pieces People Ask For: Serious, Humorous, Pathetic, Patriotic, and Dramatic Selections in Prose and Poetry for Reading and Recitations' is a captivating collection of diverse literary works that will leave readers enthralled. From heart-wrenching tales to lighthearted anecdotes, this anthology offers a wide range of emotions and experiences. Whether it's a serious reflection on life's complexities, a humorous take on everyday situations, or a patriotic tribute to one's homeland, these selections are sure to captivate and resonate with readers. With a perfect blend of prose and poetry, this collection promises to take readers on an unforgettable journey through the depths of human emotions and experiences.

Genres: Poetry, Prose, Anthology
Themes: Emotion, Life, Patriotism, Human Experience
