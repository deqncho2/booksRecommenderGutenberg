Synopsis: 

'Games for the Playground, Home, School and Gymnasium' by Jessie Hubbell Bancroft is a comprehensive guidebook that offers a wide range of engaging and interactive games for children. From classic playground games to creative indoor activities, this book provides a treasure trove of ideas to keep kids entertained and active. With detailed instructions and illustrations, Bancroft presents a collection of games that promote physical fitness, social interaction, and imaginative play. Whether it's a game of tag, a relay race, or a group activity, this book is a valuable resource for parents, teachers, and anyone looking to make playtime more enjoyable and educational for children.

Genres: Educational, Parenting, Recreation

Themes: Physical fitness, Social interaction, Imagination
