Synopsis: 
'Landed Gentry: A Comedy in Four Acts' by W. Somerset Maugham is a delightful tale that follows the lives of the eccentric and aristocratic residents of a small English village. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to a colorful cast of characters, each with their own secrets, desires, and ambitions. From the charming but scheming Lady Muriel to the enigmatic and brooding Sir John, the interactions and relationships between these characters create a web of intrigue, romance, and hilarity. With witty dialogue and unexpected twists, this comedy takes us on a journey through the ups and downs of the landed gentry, revealing the complexities and absurdities of their privileged lives.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Social class, Love and relationships, Deception and secrets
