Synopsis: In 'The River Boss' by Stewart Edward White, a young man named Jack discovers a hidden world of adventure and danger as he embarks on a journey down the treacherous river. With his life at stake, Jack must navigate through unexpected challenges, encounter mysterious characters, and confront his own inner demons. As he delves deeper into the heart of the river, Jack's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Self-discovery
