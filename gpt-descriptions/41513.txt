Synopsis: In the small town of Oakdale, a group of mysterious new boys arrive, turning the lives of the residents upside down. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the town becomes a hotbed of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each passing day, the residents of Oakdale find themselves drawn deeper into a web of mystery that threatens to shatter their peaceful existence. Who are these new boys, and what secrets do they hold? Brace yourself for a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of Oakdale, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Intrigue, Identity
