Synopsis: In "The Best Man" by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Phoebe finds herself torn between two suitors who couldn't be more different. As she navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, Phoebe must make a choice that will not only impact her own happiness but also the lives of those around her. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this captivating novel explores the power of forgiveness, the importance of faith, and the enduring strength of true love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Inspirational

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Faith and spirituality
