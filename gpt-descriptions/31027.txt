Synopsis: In "Walt Whitman, Yesterday & Today" by Henry Eduard Legler, embark on a captivating journey through the life and legacy of one of America's most influential poets. Through a combination of historical analysis and personal anecdotes, Legler paints a vivid portrait of Walt Whitman, exploring his revolutionary ideas, his impact on American literature, and his enduring relevance in today's world. Discover the man behind the words, as Legler delves into Whitman's personal struggles, his poetic genius, and the profound connection he forged with his readers. This insightful exploration of Whitman's life and work will leave readers inspired and eager to delve deeper into the mind of this literary icon.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Poetry

Themes: Identity, Individualism, Literary Legacy
