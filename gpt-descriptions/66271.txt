Synopsis: 
'Bulkies [Sweaters to Knit and Crochet]' by American Thread Company is a delightful collection of patterns that will inspire both novice and experienced knitters and crocheters alike. This book showcases a wide range of stylish and cozy sweaters, designed to keep you warm and fashionable during the colder months. From classic cable knits to trendy oversized cardigans, each pattern is accompanied by clear instructions and beautiful photographs, making it easy to create your own unique and comfortable sweaters. Whether you prefer knitting or crocheting, this book offers something for everyone, ensuring hours of creative enjoyment and the satisfaction of completing your own handmade wardrobe.

Genres: Crafts, Hobbies, Fashion

Themes: Creativity, Self-expression, Fashion
