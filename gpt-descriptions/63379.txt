Synopsis: In 'From Monkey to Man, or, Society in the Tertiary Age', author Austin Bierbower takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating transition from primitive beings to the complex society we know today. Set in the Tertiary Age, the story follows a group of curious individuals who embark on a great expedition from Cocoanut Hill, encountering thrilling adventures and facing the challenges of survival in the treacherous Alligator Swamp. Along the way, they witness the emergence of industry, commerce, government, religion, and the arts, providing a unique perspective on the origins of these fundamental aspects of human civilization. With its blend of adventure, exploration, and thought-provoking insights, this book offers a captivating exploration of humanity's evolution.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Science Fiction

Themes: Evolution, Civilization, Exploration, Survival
