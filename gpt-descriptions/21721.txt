Synopsis: Three young boys find themselves stranded on a deserted island after their ship is wrecked during a storm. As they struggle to survive in this unfamiliar environment, they encounter various challenges and dangers, including hostile natives and treacherous wildlife. With their resourcefulness and determination, the boys must learn to adapt and work together to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they be able to find a way back home or will they be forever marooned on the coral island?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration, Nature
