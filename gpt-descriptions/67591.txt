Synopsis: 

In 'Famous Colonial Houses' by Hollister, Paul M., embark on a captivating journey through time and explore the hidden stories behind some of the most iconic colonial houses in America. From the grandeur of Mount Vernon to the mysterious whispers of the House of the Seven Gables, this book unveils the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that have shaped these remarkable architectural treasures. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, Hollister takes readers on a captivating tour, revealing the fascinating lives of the people who once called these houses home. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: American history, Colonial era, Architectural heritage, Cultural significance
