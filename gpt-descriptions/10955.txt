Synopsis: In "The Gospels in the Second Century: An Examination of the Critical Part of a Work Entitled 'Supernatural Religion'," Sanday delves into the fascinating world of early Christian literature and the controversies surrounding the formation of the Gospels. Through meticulous research and analysis, he uncovers the historical context and challenges faced by the early Christian community, shedding light on the development of the Gospels and their reception in the second century. This thought-provoking work offers a captivating exploration of the origins and evolution of the Gospels, providing valuable insights into the early Christian movement.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Historical Analysis

Themes: Early Christianity, Gospel Formation, Historical Context, Religious Controversy
