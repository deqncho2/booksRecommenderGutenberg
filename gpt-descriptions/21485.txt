Synopsis: In "The Young Rajah" by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young English boy named Dick Hargrave finds himself in an unexpected adventure when he is taken captive by a group of pirates. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Indian Ocean, Dick's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. Along the way, he encounters exotic landscapes, encounters with dangerous wildlife, and unexpected allies. Will Dick be able to escape the clutches of the pirates and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Coming of Age, Cultural Exploration
