Synopsis: 
In "Lucian the Dreamer" by Fletcher, J.S., we are introduced to Lucian, a young man with an extraordinary ability to dream. His dreams are vivid, immersive, and often prophetic, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. As Lucian's dreams become more intense and unpredictable, he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind his unique gift. Along the way, he encounters mysterious characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and must confront his deepest fears. Will Lucian's dreams lead him to enlightenment or plunge him into darkness?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Dreams and reality, Identity and self-discovery, Fate and destiny
