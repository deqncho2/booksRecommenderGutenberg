Synopsis: 
In 'Poor, Dear Margaret Kirby' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Margaret Kirby finds herself caught between two worlds - the glamorous upper class and the struggling working class. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Margaret must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid characters and a captivating storyline, this novel explores the timeless themes of social class, identity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and relationships, Ambition
