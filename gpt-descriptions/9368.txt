Synopsis: 

In 'Welsh Fairy Tales' by William Elliot Griffis, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Welsh folklore. This collection of timeless tales introduces readers to a host of magical creatures, from mischievous fairies to fearsome dragons, as they navigate the rugged landscapes of Wales. From the mysterious depths of ancient lakes to the hidden corners of mystical forests, these stories are filled with adventure, wonder, and the enduring power of Welsh mythology. Delve into the rich tapestry of Welsh folklore and discover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Mythology, Magic, Adventure
