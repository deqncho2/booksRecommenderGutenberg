Synopsis: In 'Idonia: A Romance of Old London' by Arthur Frederick Wallis, readers are transported to the enchanting streets of Old London, where a captivating tale of love, mystery, and adventure unfolds. The story revolves around the enigmatic Idonia, a young woman with a mysterious past and an extraordinary gift. As she navigates the bustling city, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and forbidden love. With vivid descriptions of the vibrant cityscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this historical romance takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Old London, where the past and present collide in unexpected ways.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Secrets, Fate and Destiny
