Synopsis: In 'The Raid of the Guerilla: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a small Appalachian town becomes the battleground for a thrilling clash between a notorious guerilla leader and a group of determined townspeople. As tensions rise and danger looms, the fate of the town hangs in the balance, leading to a gripping and unexpected climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resistance, Community
