Synopsis: 
'Over the Rocky Mountains to Alaska' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of North America. Join the intrepid explorer, Charles Warren Stoddard, as he embarks on a daring expedition, traversing treacherous terrains, encountering fascinating wildlife, and immersing himself in the awe-inspiring beauty of the Alaskan landscape. From breathtaking mountain ranges to icy glaciers, Stoddard's vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes will transport readers to a world of adventure and discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Survival
