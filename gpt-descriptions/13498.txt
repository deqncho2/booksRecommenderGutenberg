Synopsis: In 'The Fortieth Door' by Mary Hastings Bradley, a young American woman named Mary embarks on a thrilling adventure in Egypt during the early 20th century. Drawn into a world of ancient secrets and hidden treasures, Mary finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of espionage and intrigue. As she navigates the labyrinthine streets of Cairo and uncovers the mysteries of the fabled Fortieth Door, Mary must rely on her wit and courage to survive. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies at every turn, the stakes are high as she races against time to unlock the door's secrets and protect what she holds dear.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Identity, Betrayal, Courage
