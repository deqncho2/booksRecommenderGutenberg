Synopsis: In 'The Wings of the Morning' by Tracy, Louis, a young woman named Mary embarks on a thrilling adventure across the Atlantic Ocean in search of her missing father. As she navigates the treacherous waters and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Mary's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. With secrets, romance, and danger lurking at every turn, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family bonds, Perseverance, Self-discovery
