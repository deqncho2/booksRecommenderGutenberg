Synopsis: In 'The Ranger Boys and the Border Smugglers' by Claude A. LaBelle, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves caught up in a dangerous game of cat and mouse along the border. When they stumble upon a smuggling operation, they must use their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to outsmart the cunning criminals and bring them to justice. As they navigate treacherous terrain and face unexpected challenges, the Ranger Boys learn the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and the importance of doing what is right.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Justice, Loyalty
