Synopsis: 
In 'That Printer of Udell's: A Story of the Middle West', we follow the journey of Dick Falkner, a troubled young man who finds himself in the small town of Udell. Through a chance encounter with a printer, Dick's life takes an unexpected turn as he becomes involved in the town's political and social issues. As he navigates the complexities of love, faith, and personal redemption, Dick's transformation becomes intertwined with the fate of Udell itself.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Redemption, Faith, Social and Political Change
