Synopsis: In 'The Mabinogion Vol. 2', embark on a captivating journey through ancient Welsh mythology and folklore. This collection of enchanting tales introduces us to a world filled with heroic quests, magical creatures, and complex relationships. From the mysterious origins of a legendary king to the trials faced by a brave warrior, each story weaves together elements of love, honor, and destiny. As the characters navigate through a realm where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, they must confront their own desires and confront the consequences of their actions. Prepare to be transported to a realm of wonder and adventure in this mesmerizing volume of 'The Mabinogion'.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love and Desire
