Synopsis: 

In 'Practical Stair Building and Handrailing: By the square section and falling line system', Wood, W. H. takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of stair building and handrailing. With a unique approach that combines the square section and falling line system, this book offers a fresh perspective on the art of constructing stairs. From the intricacies of measuring and cutting to the precise calculations required for a flawless finish, Wood, W. H. provides a comprehensive guide that will leave readers eager to embark on their own stair building projects. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Home Improvement, Crafts

Themes: Precision craftsmanship, Architectural design, Practicality
