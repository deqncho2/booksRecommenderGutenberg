Synopsis: In "The Story of Magellan and The Discovery of the Philippines" by Butterworth, Hezekiah, embark on a thrilling adventure as you follow the incredible journey of Ferdinand Magellan and his crew. From their departure from Spain to their encounters with indigenous tribes and the challenges they face along the way, this captivating tale will transport you to the uncharted waters of the 16th century. With vivid descriptions and historical accuracy, the story unveils the untold secrets of Magellan's quest to find a new route to the Spice Islands, ultimately leading to the discovery of the Philippines. Prepare to be enthralled by the bravery, determination, and resilience of these explorers as they navigate through treacherous seas and unknown territories.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Perseverance
