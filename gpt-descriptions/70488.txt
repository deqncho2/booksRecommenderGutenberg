Synopsis: In 'The Keeper of Red Horse Pass' by W. C. Tuttle, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Red Horse Pass, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the peaceful community. As tensions rise and the townspeople become suspicious of one another, a young rancher must uncover the truth behind the stranger's arrival and protect his loved ones from the impending danger that looms over Red Horse Pass.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Community and trust
