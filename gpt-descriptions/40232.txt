Synopsis: In 'Post-Impressions: An Irresponsible Chronicle' by Simeon Strunsky, embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of art, literature, and society in early 20th century New York. Follow the witty and observant narrator as he encounters eccentric characters, navigates the complexities of love and friendship, and delves into the profound impact of artistic expression. With a delightful blend of humor, introspection, and cultural commentary, this chronicle offers a unique perspective on the post-impressionist movement and its influence on the human experience.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Satire

Themes: Artistic expression, Society and culture, Love and friendship
