Synopsis: In "The Fair God; or, The Last of the 'Tzins: A Tale of the Conquest of Mexico," Wallace, Lew takes readers on a captivating journey to the ancient land of Mexico during the time of the Spanish conquest. The story follows the life of a young Aztec nobleman named Guatemozin, who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his people and his love for a Spanish maiden. As the clash between the Aztec empire and the Spanish conquistadors intensifies, Guatemozin must navigate treacherous alliances, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices in a battle for survival and love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural clash, Loyalty, Love and sacrifice
