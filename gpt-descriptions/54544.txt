Synopsis: In 'The Silent Battle' by Gibbs, George, a renowned psychologist finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a patient with a mysterious past arrives at his clinic. As he delves deeper into the patient's troubled mind, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to consume them both. With each session, the psychologist becomes more determined to unravel the truth, but the line between sanity and madness begins to blur, leading to a gripping psychological battle that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Mental health
