Synopsis: 
In 'More Russian Picture Tales' by Karrik, Valerian Viliamovich, prepare to embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Russian folklore. This collection of tales takes readers on a series of whimsical adventures, filled with magical creatures, clever protagonists, and unexpected twists. From mischievous forest spirits to talking animals, each story is a delightful blend of fantasy and moral lessons that will leave readers both entertained and enlightened. Whether it's a clever fox outwitting its foes or a brave young girl facing supernatural challenges, these tales are sure to captivate readers of all ages with their timeless charm and rich cultural heritage.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Children's Literature
Themes: Morality, Wisdom, Courage, Supernatural
