Synopsis: In "Ozoplaning with the Wizard of Oz," follow Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure through the magical land of Oz. With the help of the Wizard, they must face new challenges, encounter peculiar creatures, and navigate treacherous landscapes to save the day. As they journey through Oz, they discover the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Belief in oneself
