Synopsis: 
In 'The White Wolf and Other Fireside Tales', Arthur Quiller-Couch weaves a collection of captivating and mysterious stories that will transport readers to different realms and times. From the haunting tale of a spectral white wolf that prowls the moors, to the eerie encounter with a ghostly apparition in an ancient castle, each story is filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As readers delve into these fireside tales, they will be drawn into a world where the line between reality and the supernatural becomes blurred, and where the power of storytelling holds the key to unlocking hidden truths.

Genres: 
Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, mystery

Themes: 
The power of storytelling, the supernatural, the blurred line between reality and fantasy
