Synopsis: 
'Poems' by Nora May French is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the depths of human emotions and experiences. From love and heartbreak to joy and self-discovery, these poems beautifully capture the essence of life's most profound moments. With each verse, French weaves a tapestry of words that will leave readers spellbound, contemplating the intricacies of the human soul and the power of language itself.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Self-Reflection
