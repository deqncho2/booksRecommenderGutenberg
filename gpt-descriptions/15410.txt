Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Pilgrim in the Unseen' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young girl embarks on a mysterious journey to a hidden realm beyond the physical world. Guided by a wise and enigmatic companion, she encounters a series of captivating and thought-provoking adventures that challenge her perceptions of reality and unveil the secrets of the unseen. As she navigates through this ethereal realm, she discovers profound truths about life, love, and the power of faith. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Spiritual Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Exploration of the supernatural, Quest for knowledge, Spiritual awakening
