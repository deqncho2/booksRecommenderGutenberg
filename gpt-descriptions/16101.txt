Synopsis: 
In the small town of Mercer, a mysterious woman named Diane arrives in her green van, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As she settles into her new life, Diane's enigmatic past and unconventional lifestyle begin to unravel, revealing a captivating tale of love, adventure, and unexpected twists. Through her interactions with the townspeople, Diane's presence brings about profound changes, challenging societal norms and inspiring personal growth. As the secrets of the green van slowly unfold, readers are taken on a journey filled with intrigue, romance, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Freedom, Community, Transformation
