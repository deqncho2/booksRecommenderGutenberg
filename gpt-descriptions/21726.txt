Synopsis:
In 'Deep Down, a Tale of the Cornish Mines', R. M. Ballantyne takes readers on a thrilling journey into the treacherous world of the Cornish mines. The story follows the life of young Jack Trevanion, who finds himself caught up in a series of dangerous adventures as he navigates the dark and mysterious depths of the mines. From unexpected encounters with smugglers to life-threatening cave-ins, Jack's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the mines, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change everything he thought he knew.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes:
Perseverance, Courage, Exploration, Industrial Revolution
