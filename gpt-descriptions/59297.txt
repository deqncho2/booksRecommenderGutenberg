Synopsis: In 'Your Time is Up' by Sheldon, Walter J., a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when a mysterious patient with a dark secret seeks his help. As the psychiatrist delves deeper into the patient's troubled past, he becomes a target himself, racing against time to uncover the truth before it's too late. This gripping psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of trust and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Deception, Consequences
