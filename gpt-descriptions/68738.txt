Synopsis: In 'The Lanchester Tradition' by G. F. Bradby, a mysterious family secret threatens to unravel the lives of the Lanchester family. As the story unfolds, dark secrets, hidden desires, and unexpected alliances come to light, leading to a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of the Lanchester tradition, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
