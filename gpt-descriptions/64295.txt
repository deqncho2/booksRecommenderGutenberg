Synopsis: In 'Young Men; In Business', Guest and Wells take readers on a captivating journey through the lives of ambitious young men striving to make their mark in the business world. From the bustling streets of New York City to the boardrooms of powerful corporations, the story follows the intertwining paths of these driven individuals as they navigate the challenges and opportunities that come their way. As they face personal and professional obstacles, the characters must confront their own ambitions, relationships, and the sacrifices required to achieve success. With a blend of drama, suspense, and unexpected twists, this novel offers a compelling exploration of the complexities of ambition and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Drama, Suspense, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Success, Relationships, Sacrifice
