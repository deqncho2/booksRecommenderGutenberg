Synopsis: 
In "Steel Traps: Describes the Various Makes and Tells How to Use Them, Also Chapters on Care of Pelts, Etc.", author A.R. Harding delves into the fascinating world of trapping, providing a comprehensive guide on the different types of traps and their effective usage. From the art of setting traps to the proper care of pelts, this book offers invaluable knowledge for both novice and experienced trappers. With detailed descriptions and practical advice, Harding takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of trapping, revealing the secrets and techniques that have been passed down through generations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Survival, Hunting

Themes: Trapping techniques, Wildlife conservation, Outdoor skills
