Synopsis: In "Kirsteen: The Story of a Scotch Family Seventy Years Ago," we are transported back in time to a small Scottish village, where we follow the life of Kirsteen, a spirited young woman with dreams and ambitions that defy the expectations of her time. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal norms, Kirsteen's determination and resilience shine through, making her a captivating protagonist. With vivid descriptions of the Scottish landscape and a cast of memorable characters, this novel offers a glimpse into a bygone era and the struggles and triumphs of one remarkable woman.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Gender roles, Social expectations, Love and relationships, Individuality, Resilience
