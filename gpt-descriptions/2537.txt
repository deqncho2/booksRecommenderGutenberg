Synopsis: 
'The Pocket R.L.S.: Being Favourite Passages from the Works of Stevenson' is a collection of selected passages from the renowned author Robert Louis Stevenson's works. This compilation offers readers a glimpse into the captivating world of Stevenson's imagination, showcasing his mastery of storytelling and his ability to transport readers to different times and places. From thrilling adventures to poignant reflections on life, love, and human nature, this collection is a treasure trove of literary gems that will leave readers eager to explore more of Stevenson's works.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality, Human Nature
