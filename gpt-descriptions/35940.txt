Synopsis: In "The Golden Galleon," we follow the thrilling adventures of Master Gilbert Oglander, a young man who finds himself embarking on a perilous journey in the year 1591. Joining the renowned Sir Richard Grenville, Oglander becomes part of a great sea-fight off the coast of Flores, where he serves aboard her Majesty's Ship, the Revenge. As the battle unfolds, Oglander's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Naval Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Survival
