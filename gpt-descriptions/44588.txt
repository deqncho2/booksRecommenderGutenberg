Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'John Inglesant: A Romance', the eponymous protagonist finds himself embroiled in a web of political intrigue and religious conflict in 17th century England. As a devout Anglican, John is torn between his loyalty to the Church of England and his growing fascination with the mystical teachings of the Roman Catholic Church. Amidst the backdrop of the English Civil War, John becomes entangled in a dangerous plot that could have far-reaching consequences for his faith and his country. With his life hanging in the balance, John must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own inner demons in order to find redemption and peace.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Religious conflict, Loyalty, Redemption, Political intrigue
