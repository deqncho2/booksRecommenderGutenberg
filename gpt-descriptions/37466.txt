Synopsis: 
In 'Lost in the Cañon: The Story of Sam Willett's Adventures on the Great Colorado of the West' by Calhoun, A. R., readers are taken on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. When young Sam Willett finds himself stranded in the treacherous canyons of the Colorado River, he must rely on his wits and resourcefulness to survive. As he encounters dangerous rapids, encounters with Native American tribes, and unexpected allies, Sam's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he find his way back home or be forever lost in the unforgiving cañon?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Coming of Age
