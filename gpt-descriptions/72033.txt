Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they decide to explore the mysterious woods that surround their community. As they delve deeper into the dense forest, they uncover secrets that have been hidden for generations, leading them to question everything they thought they knew about their town and themselves. With each step they take, the woods reveal a dark and enchanting world filled with unexpected dangers and extraordinary wonders. As their journey unfolds, the friends must confront their deepest fears and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Self-discovery, Coming of Age
