Synopsis: In "An Introduction to Mythology," Lewis Spence takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient myths and legends of various cultures. From the gods and goddesses of ancient Greece to the heroic tales of Norse mythology, Spence delves into the rich tapestry of stories that have shaped human civilization. With his engaging storytelling style, he explores the origins, symbolism, and significance of these myths, shedding light on the universal themes and timeless wisdom they contain. Whether you are a mythology enthusiast or simply curious about the power of storytelling, this book will transport you to a world of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mythology, History

Themes: Mythology, Symbolism, Human Nature
