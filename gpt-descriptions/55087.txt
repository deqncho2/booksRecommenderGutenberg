Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Forty-sixth or South Devonshire Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this esteemed regiment from 1741 to 1851. From the regiment's humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the soldiers who served under its banner. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of those who fought for their country, shedding light on the regiment's pivotal role in various conflicts and campaigns. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Warfare, Military camaraderie, Historical legacy
