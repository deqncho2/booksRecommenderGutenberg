Synopsis:
In "Woman in Science: With an Introductory Chapter on Woman's Long Struggle for Things of the Mind," Zahm delves into the fascinating world of women in science, exploring their contributions, challenges, and triumphs throughout history. From ancient times to the modern era, this book uncovers the remarkable stories of women who defied societal norms and made groundbreaking discoveries in various scientific fields. With captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Zahm sheds light on the often overlooked role of women in shaping the scientific landscape. This thought-provoking work celebrates the resilience and brilliance of women in science, inspiring readers to reconsider the traditional narratives of scientific achievement.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Women's Studies

Themes: Gender equality, Scientific achievement, Overcoming societal barriers
