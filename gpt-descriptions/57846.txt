Synopsis: 

'Byways in British Archaeology' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known corners of British history, unearthing fascinating archaeological discoveries that have shaped the nation's past. From mysterious burial sites to ancient ruins, renowned archaeologist Walter Johnson delves into the hidden stories behind these remarkable finds, shedding light on the lives and cultures of those who came before us. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Johnson brings to life the forgotten chapters of British history, offering a fresh perspective on the rich tapestry of the land. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: British history, Archaeological discoveries, Cultural heritage
