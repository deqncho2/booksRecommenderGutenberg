Synopsis: In "Richard Wagner, Composer of Operas" by John F. Runciman, delve into the captivating life and artistic genius of one of the most influential composers in history. From his tumultuous personal relationships to his groundbreaking musical innovations, this biography offers a fascinating exploration of Wagner's complex character and the enduring impact of his operatic masterpieces. Discover the man behind the music and the profound cultural and artistic legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Artistic genius, Love and relationships, Cultural impact
