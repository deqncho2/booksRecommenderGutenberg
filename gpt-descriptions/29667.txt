Synopsis:
In "Adrift on the Pacific: A Boy's Story of the Sea and its Perils," we follow the thrilling journey of a young boy named Ben who finds himself stranded at sea after a catastrophic event. With only his wits and determination, Ben must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected dangers, and confront his deepest fears in order to survive. As he battles the elements and encounters both friend and foe, Ben's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he find his way back home or be forever lost at sea?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Courage, Self-discovery
