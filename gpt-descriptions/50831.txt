Synopsis: In 'The River Motor Boat Boys on the Yukon: The Lost Mine of Rainbow Bend', the adventurous River Motor Boat Boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey along the treacherous Yukon River. As they navigate through dangerous rapids and encounter mysterious characters, they stumble upon a clue that leads them to the fabled Lost Mine of Rainbow Bend. With their courage and resourcefulness, the boys must overcome numerous obstacles and outsmart their rivals in a race against time to uncover the hidden treasure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
