Synopsis: In 'The Revolt of the Angels' by Anatole France, a group of angels, disillusioned with their heavenly existence, stage a rebellion against God. Led by the charismatic Seraphitus, they embark on a journey to explore the human world and challenge the divine order. As they navigate the complexities of mortal life, they encounter love, temptation, and the pursuit of knowledge, all while questioning the nature of good and evil. This thought-provoking and imaginative novel delves into the realms of spirituality, philosophy, and the eternal struggle between freedom and obedience.

Genres: Fantasy, Philosophical Fiction, Satire

Themes: Rebellion, Freedom, Religion, Morality, Knowledge
