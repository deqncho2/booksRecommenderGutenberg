Synopsis: In the second volume of "What Will He Do with It?" the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of the protagonist's life as he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists and turns keep the reader on the edge of their seat. With a cast of compelling characters and a richly detailed setting, this volume delves into the protagonist's choices and the consequences they bring, leaving the reader eagerly anticipating the next installment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Relationships, Ambition, Society and Class
