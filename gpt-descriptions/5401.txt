Synopsis: In 'Old Rose and Silver' by Reed, Myrtle, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught between two worlds when she inherits a mysterious estate. As she delves into the secrets of the past, she uncovers a tale of love, betrayal, and redemption that spans generations. With the help of a charming stranger, Rose must navigate the complexities of her newfound inheritance and discover the true meaning of family and home.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and loss, Family bonds, Secrets and revelations
