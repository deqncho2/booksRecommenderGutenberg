Synopsis: In 'The Faces Outside' by McAllister, Bruce, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, a young detective uncovers a web of secrets and hidden identities, leading to a shocking revelation that will change everything. With each page, the tension builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the faces outside.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Fear, Community
