Synopsis: 
In 'The School for Husbands' by Molière, two brothers, Sganarelle and Ariste, have contrasting views on how to raise their young ward, Isabella. While Sganarelle believes in strict control and surveillance, Ariste advocates for freedom and trust. As Isabella blossoms into a beautiful young woman, the brothers' conflicting approaches to her upbringing become increasingly apparent. However, when a charming stranger named Valère enters the picture, the dynamics between the characters are thrown into disarray, leading to a series of hilarious and unexpected events. Will Isabella find her own path amidst the chaos, or will she succumb to the whims of her overprotective guardians?

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Love and marriage, Gender roles, Freedom and independence
