Synopsis: In 'The Time Snatcher' by Garrett Randall, a brilliant scientist discovers a way to manipulate time, but his invention falls into the wrong hands. As time begins to unravel, a group of unlikely heroes must embark on a thrilling adventure to save the world from the clutches of the enigmatic Time Snatcher. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of time itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Heroism, Consequences of power
