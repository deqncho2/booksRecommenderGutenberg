Synopsis: 

In 'Seven Wives and Seven Prisons: Or, Experiences in the Life of a Matrimonial Monomaniac. A True Story', the author L. A. Abbott takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a man consumed by an insatiable desire for marriage. This gripping memoir recounts the astonishing experiences of a man who, driven by an unrelenting compulsion, finds himself entangled in a series of seven marriages and the subsequent prisons he is confined to. With each new wife, the protagonist's life takes unexpected turns, leading him down a path of both love and despair. As the story unfolds, readers are left questioning the limits of human desire and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, True Crime

Themes: Obsession, Love, Consequences
