Synopsis: In "Betty's Bright Idea; Deacon Pitkin's Farm; and the First Christmas of New England," Harriet Beecher Stowe weaves together three heartwarming tales set in New England. Follow Betty and her family as they navigate the challenges of life on Deacon Pitkin's farm and experience the magic of the first Christmas in their new home. Through their trials and triumphs, Stowe explores the power of community, the importance of kindness, and the joy of finding hope in unexpected places.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Christmas Literature

Themes: Community, Kindness, Hope
