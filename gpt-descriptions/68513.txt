Synopsis: In "Old Bear-Paw, the trapper king; or, The love of a Blackfoot queen," Henry M. Avery takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. Set against the backdrop of the fur trade era, this captivating tale follows the extraordinary life of Old Bear-Paw, a legendary trapper known for his unmatched skills and indomitable spirit. When he encounters a Blackfoot queen, their fates become intertwined in a tale of love, adventure, and the clash of cultures. As Old Bear-Paw navigates treacherous landscapes and faces formidable foes, he must also confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Avery's novel immerses readers in a world of danger, passion, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Wilderness survival, Cultural clash, Love and sacrifice
