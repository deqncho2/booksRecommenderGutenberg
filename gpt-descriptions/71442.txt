Synopsis: In "An English woman-sergeant in the Serbian Army," Flora Sandes takes us on a captivating journey through her extraordinary experiences as the only British woman to serve as a soldier in the Serbian Army during World War I. From her initial decision to join the war effort to her remarkable rise through the ranks, Sandes provides a gripping firsthand account of the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices she faced on the frontlines. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, she offers a unique perspective on the horrors of war, the bonds of camaraderie, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Memoir, War, History

Themes: Gender roles, Patriotism, Resilience, Identity, Sacrifice
