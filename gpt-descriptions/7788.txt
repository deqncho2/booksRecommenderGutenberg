Synopsis: In 'Blindfolded' by Walcott, Earle Ashley, a young woman wakes up in a mysterious room, blindfolded and disoriented. As she struggles to uncover the truth about her situation, she must confront her deepest fears and navigate a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, desperate to uncover the secrets hidden within the darkness.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Trust, Deception, Survival
