Synopsis: In the heart of New York City, a mysterious and captivating jewel known as "The Flaming Jewel" holds the power to change the lives of those who possess it. As a group of individuals from different walks of life become entangled in its allure, they are drawn into a web of love, betrayal, and danger. From the glittering world of high society to the gritty underbelly of the city, this thrilling tale explores the lengths people will go to obtain their desires and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Obsession, Power, Deception
