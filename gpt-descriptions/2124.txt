Synopsis: 
'A Record of Buddhistic Kingdoms' follows the incredible journey of the Chinese monk Fa-hsien as he embarks on a daring expedition to India and Ceylon in search of the sacred Buddhist books of discipline. Facing treacherous landscapes, encountering diverse cultures, and overcoming numerous obstacles, Fa-hsien's quest unveils a captivating tale of adventure, spirituality, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical, Religious

Themes: Exploration, Spiritual Enlightenment, Cultural Exchange
