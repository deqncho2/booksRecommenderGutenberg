Synopsis: 

"The War Upon Religion: Being an Account of the Rise and Progress of Anti-Christianism in Europe" by Francis A. Cunningham takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical rise of anti-Christian sentiment in Europe. From the shadows of ancient civilizations to the tumultuous events of the Renaissance and Enlightenment, this book unveils the forces that have shaped the battle between religion and secularism. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Cunningham explores the complex interplay of politics, philosophy, and religious beliefs that have fueled this ongoing conflict. 

Genres: History, Religious Studies, Cultural Studies

Themes: Religious persecution, Secularism, Intellectual movements, Historical conflicts
