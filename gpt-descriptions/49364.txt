Synopsis: In 'Inmates of My House and Garden' by Brightwen, Mrs. (Eliza Elder), embark on a captivating journey through the author's personal encounters with the fascinating creatures that inhabit her home and garden. From unexpected visitors to peculiar inhabitants, this delightful narrative unveils a world of enchantment and wonder, where the ordinary becomes extraordinary. As the author's interactions with these creatures unfold, readers are invited to explore the intricate web of life that surrounds us, offering a fresh perspective on the beauty and complexity of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Memoir

Themes: Nature, Wildlife, Interconnectedness, Curiosity
