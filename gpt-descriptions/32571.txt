Synopsis: 
Step into a world of enchantment and wonder with Hans Andersen's Fairy Tales. First Series. This collection of timeless tales will transport you to far-off lands, where you'll meet brave princesses, mischievous trolls, and talking animals. From the heartwarming story of a little mermaid longing for love to the thrilling adventures of a steadfast tin soldier, each tale is filled with captivating characters and unexpected twists. Lose yourself in the magic of Andersen's imagination as you embark on a journey that will leave you spellbound.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Courage and resilience, The power of imagination
