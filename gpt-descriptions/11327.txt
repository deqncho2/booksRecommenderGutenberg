Synopsis: In 'English Literature: Modern', George Herbert Mair takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of modern English literature. From the works of renowned authors to lesser-known gems, Mair explores the evolution of literature in the 20th century, delving into the complexities of themes, styles, and cultural influences. With insightful analysis and engaging anecdotes, this book offers a fresh perspective on the modern literary landscape, leaving readers eager to dive into the pages of these remarkable works.

Genres: Literary criticism, Non-fiction

Themes: Modernism, Cultural influences, Evolution of literature
