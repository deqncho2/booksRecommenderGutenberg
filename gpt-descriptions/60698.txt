Synopsis: 
In 'Bargain Basement' by Charles L. Fontenay, a struggling writer stumbles upon a mysterious store tucked away in a forgotten corner of the city. Intrigued by the peculiar items on display, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires. As he delves deeper into the store's enigmatic history, he discovers that every purchase comes with a price, and the consequences of his choices may be more than he bargained for.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Temptation, Consequences, Redemption
