Synopsis: In 'Forty-one Thieves: A Tale of California' by Hall, Angelo, a group of notorious outlaws embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed wilderness of California during the Gold Rush era. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter a diverse cast of characters, their loyalty and cunning are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who will emerge victorious in this high-stakes game of survival.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Morality
