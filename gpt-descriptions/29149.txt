Synopsis: 
In 'Cogito, Ergo Sum' by John Foster West, a brilliant neuroscientist named Dr. Elizabeth Hartley embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that challenges the very nature of consciousness. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the human mind, she uncovers a shocking truth that could change the course of humanity forever. With her own sanity at stake, Dr. Hartley must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas and existential questions, all while being pursued by powerful forces who will stop at nothing to control her discovery.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Consciousness, Identity, Ethics, Power
