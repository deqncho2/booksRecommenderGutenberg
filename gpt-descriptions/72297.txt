Synopsis: In the heart-pounding thriller "Cobra," a highly skilled assassin named Paul Devereaux is forced out of retirement to confront a deadly conspiracy that threatens to unravel the world order. As he navigates a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal, Devereaux must rely on his cunning and lethal abilities to outsmart his enemies and protect those he holds dear. With each twist and turn, the stakes escalate, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Thriller, Action, Suspense

Themes: Espionage, Conspiracy, Redemption
