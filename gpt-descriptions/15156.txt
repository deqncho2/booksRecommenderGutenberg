Synopsis: 
In 'Balloons' by Elizabeth Bibesco, a young woman named Louise finds herself captivated by the enchanting world of hot air balloons. Drawn to their whimsical beauty and the sense of freedom they represent, Louise embarks on a journey that will not only change her life but also challenge societal norms and expectations. As she becomes more involved in the world of balloons, Louise discovers a newfound passion and a group of like-minded individuals who share her love for adventure. However, as her fascination grows, she must confront the consequences of pursuing her dreams in a world that seeks to confine her. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Freedom, Self-discovery, Breaking societal norms
