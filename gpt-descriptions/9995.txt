Synopsis: In "Little Journey to Puerto Rico: For Intermediate and Upper Grades," George and Marian Minnie take readers on an exciting adventure to the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. Join them as they explore the vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and fascinating history of this Caribbean gem. From the bustling streets of San Juan to the lush rainforests and pristine beaches, the Minnie siblings encounter friendly locals, encounter unique wildlife, and immerse themselves in the rich traditions of Puerto Rican life. Through their journey, readers will discover the enchanting beauty and diversity of this tropical paradise, making them eager to embark on their own little journey to Puerto Rico.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Cultural exploration, Discovery, Appreciation of diversity
