Synopsis: In 'The Dollar Hen' by Milo Hastings, a young farmer named John discovers a mysterious hen that possesses an extraordinary ability. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this remarkable bird, John finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With the fate of his farm hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his newfound treasure. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the dollar hen.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Greed, Discovery
