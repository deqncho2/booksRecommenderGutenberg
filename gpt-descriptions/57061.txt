Synopsis: In "Moonlight Schools for the Emancipation of Adult Illiterates," Cora Wilson Stewart takes readers on a captivating journey through the hills of Kentucky, where she pioneers a revolutionary approach to education. Faced with the challenge of eradicating illiteracy among adults, Stewart embarks on a mission to establish moonlight schools, unconventional classrooms that operate after dark. As she encounters resistance and skepticism, she must navigate societal norms and prejudices to bring education to those who have been denied it. This inspiring true story sheds light on the transformative power of education and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Education

Themes: Literacy, Empowerment, Social Change
