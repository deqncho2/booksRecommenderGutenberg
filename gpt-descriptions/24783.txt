Synopsis: In 'The Pirate's Pocket Book' by Calthrop, Dion Clayton, embark on a thrilling adventure across the high seas as a young sailor finds himself entangled with a notorious pirate crew. Filled with hidden treasures, daring escapes, and unexpected alliances, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the pirate's pocket book.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Redemption
