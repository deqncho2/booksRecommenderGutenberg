Synopsis: 

In Dio's Rome, Volume 4, Cassius Dio Cocceianus takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reigns of Septimius Severus, Geta and Caracalla, Macrinus, Elagabalus, and Alexander Severus. Originally written in Greek, this historical narrative has been masterfully presented in English, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the fascinating world of ancient Rome. From political intrigue and military conquests to the rise and fall of emperors, Dio's vivid storytelling brings to life the complex dynamics of power and the consequences of ambition in this gripping account of Rome's history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Ambition, Rise and Fall of Empires
