Synopsis: In 'The Ball and the Cross', two passionate and stubborn men, a Catholic and an atheist, find themselves engaged in a heated debate about the existence of God. Their disagreement escalates to the point where they challenge each other to a duel, but their plans are constantly thwarted by a series of absurd and comical events. As they navigate through a topsy-turvy world, they encounter a cast of eccentric characters and face unexpected challenges that test their beliefs and convictions. This thought-provoking and humorous novel explores the clash between faith and reason, and the power of friendship and understanding.

Genres: Satire, Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Faith vs. Reason, Friendship, Belief, Absurdity
