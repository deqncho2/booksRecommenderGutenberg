Synopsis: On a distant planet, a team of scientists and explorers find themselves trapped in a nightmarish world where reality and illusion blend together. As they struggle to survive and escape, they uncover dark secrets that challenge their perception of what is real and what is not. With each passing moment, the line between dreams and reality becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a thrilling and mind-bending journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Survival
