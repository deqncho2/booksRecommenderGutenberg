Synopsis: In 'Dream Life: A Fable of the Seasons' by Mitchell, Donald Grant, a young artist named John Hazard embarks on a journey through the four seasons, each with its own enchanting and mysterious realm. As he navigates through dreamlike landscapes and encounters captivating characters, John must confront his own desires, fears, and the ever-changing nature of life itself. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, this fable takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the human experience and the transformative power of dreams.

Genres: Fantasy, Magical Realism, Literary Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature, Time, Transformation
