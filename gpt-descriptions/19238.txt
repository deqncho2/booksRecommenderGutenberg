Synopsis: 
In 'Foundations of World Unity', ʻAbdu'l-Bahá takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional notions of unity and explores the interconnectedness of humanity. Through a series of profound insights and captivating anecdotes, he unveils a vision of a harmonious global society, where diversity is celebrated and cooperation is the key to progress. This enlightening work offers a fresh perspective on the potential of humanity and the power of unity to shape a better world.

Genres: Philosophy, Religion, Non-fiction

Themes: Unity, Diversity, Global Harmony, Cooperation, Progress
