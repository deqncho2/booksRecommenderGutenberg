Synopsis: In 'H. R.' by Lefevre, Edwin, a young and ambitious lawyer named Henry Rutherford finds himself entangled in a high-stakes legal case that challenges his moral compass and forces him to confront the dark underbelly of the justice system. As he delves deeper into the case, Henry uncovers shocking secrets and faces dangerous adversaries who will stop at nothing to protect their interests. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, Henry must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and corruption to seek justice and redemption.

Genres: Legal thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Morality, corruption, justice
