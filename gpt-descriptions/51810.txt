Synopsis: In 'The Undetected' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of secrecy and danger, where the line between good and evil blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands. This gripping tale of suspense and moral dilemmas will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
