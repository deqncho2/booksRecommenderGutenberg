Synopsis: In 'Windy McPherson's Son' by Sherwood Anderson, we follow the life of Sam McPherson, a young man who grows up in a small town and struggles to find his place in the world. From his tumultuous relationship with his father to his encounters with various eccentric characters, Sam's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Literary Fiction, Small-town Drama

Themes: Family dynamics, Identity, Social expectations, Individuality
