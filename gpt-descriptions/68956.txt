Synopsis: In 'The Companions of Pickle: Being a Sequel to 'Pickle the Spy' by Lang, Andrew, we are once again thrust into the thrilling world of espionage and adventure. This time, our beloved spy, Pickle, finds himself surrounded by a group of intriguing companions who join him on a dangerous mission. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, the stakes are raised higher than ever before. With twists and turns at every corner, this sequel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of Pickle and his companions.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Loyalty, Betrayal
