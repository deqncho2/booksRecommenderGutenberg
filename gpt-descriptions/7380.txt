Synopsis: 
In 'Alone' by Douglas, Norman, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a remote cabin during a snowstorm, with no means of communication or escape. As the days pass, she begins to experience strange occurrences that make her question her sanity and the true nature of her isolation. With each passing moment, Emily's grip on reality becomes more tenuous, and she must confront the terrifying secrets that lie hidden within the cabin's walls.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Isolation, Sanity, Secrets
