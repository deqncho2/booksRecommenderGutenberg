Synopsis:
"The Little Duke: Richard the Fearless" by Charlotte M. Yonge is a captivating historical novel set in medieval Normandy. The story follows the young Duke Richard, who, despite his tender age, must navigate the treacherous world of politics, power, and warfare. As Richard faces numerous challenges and battles, he must prove himself as a leader and protector of his people. With his unwavering courage and determination, Richard embarks on a journey that will shape his destiny and leave a lasting impact on the kingdom.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Leadership, Courage, Loyalty, Honor
