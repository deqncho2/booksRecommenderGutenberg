Synopsis: 
"The Great Speeches and Orations of Daniel Webster" is a collection of powerful speeches and orations delivered by the renowned American statesman, Daniel Webster. This compilation includes some of Webster's most influential speeches, such as his famous address on the importance of the Constitution and his impassioned defense of the Union. Alongside these remarkable speeches, the book also features an insightful essay by Edwin Percy Whipple, exploring Webster's mastery of the English language and his impact as a public speaker. Through these speeches and Whipple's analysis, readers are invited to delve into the eloquence and persuasive power of one of America's greatest orators.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political speeches, Historical literature

Themes: Patriotism, Constitutional law, Rhetoric and public speaking
