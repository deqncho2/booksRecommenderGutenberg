Synopsis: In 'The Day of Wrath' by Jókai, Mór, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Hungarian village, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the stranger's true intentions become clear, the village is plunged into chaos and turmoil, forcing its residents to confront their deepest fears and desires. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling tale of suspense and betrayal will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Fear, Desire
