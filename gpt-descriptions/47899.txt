Synopsis: In 'The Forbidden Way' by Gibbs, George, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue and betrayal. With time running out, he must navigate treacherous paths and make impossible choices to uncover the truth and protect those he loves. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of power and the lengths one will go to protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Power, Betrayal, Sacrifice
