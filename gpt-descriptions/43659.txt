Synopsis: In "The Old Woman Who Lived in a Shoe; Or, There's No Place Like Home," Douglas, Amanda M. takes readers on a whimsical journey into the life of the famous nursery rhyme character. Set in a charming village, the story follows the old woman and her numerous children as they navigate the challenges of living in a shoe-shaped house. As the family faces unexpected events and encounters colorful characters, they learn valuable lessons about love, resilience, and the true meaning of home.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Family, Home, Resilience, Love
