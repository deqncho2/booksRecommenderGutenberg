Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Purple Emperor', a mysterious and elusive criminal known as the Purple Emperor is wreaking havoc in New York City, leaving the police baffled. Detective Cleek, renowned for his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, is called upon to solve the case. As Cleek delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, Cleek must race against time to unravel the riddle and bring the Purple Emperor to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Deception and disguise, Uncovering the truth
