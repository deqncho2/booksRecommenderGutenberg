Synopsis: In the remote and rugged landscape of Dartmoor, a young man named Mark Brendon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his family's past, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient rituals, forbidden love, and dark forces that threaten to consume him. With the help of an enigmatic woman, Mark must navigate treacherous paths and confront his own demons to uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Love and sacrifice, Supernatural elements
