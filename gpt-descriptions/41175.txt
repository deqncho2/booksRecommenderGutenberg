Synopsis: 

'Woodland Gleanings: Being an Account of British Forest-Trees' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of British forest-trees. Written by Tyas, Robert, this book is a delightful exploration of the diverse and fascinating species that inhabit the woodlands of Britain. From the majestic oak to the delicate birch, each tree is brought to life through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will uncover the hidden secrets, historical significance, and ecological importance of these magnificent trees. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Botany

Themes: Nature appreciation, Environmental conservation, British flora and fauna
