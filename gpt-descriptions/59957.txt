Synopsis: In 'The Shetland Pony', a young girl named Emily discovers a mysterious pony on her family's farm. As she forms a bond with the pony, she uncovers a hidden secret that will change her life forever. With the help of her friends, Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure to protect the pony and unravel the truth behind its origins. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of family, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Family, Discovery
