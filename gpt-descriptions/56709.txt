Synopsis: 

In 'Texas in the Civil War: A Résumé History', author Allan Coleman Ashcraft takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Civil War in Texas. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Ashcraft unveils the lesser-known aspects of Texas's involvement in the war, shedding light on the state's unique contributions and challenges during this pivotal time. From the battles fought on Texan soil to the political and social dynamics that shaped the state's role, this book offers a comprehensive and enthralling account of Texas's Civil War experience.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Texan History

Themes: Texas's role in the Civil War, Texan identity and loyalty, Political and social dynamics during the war
