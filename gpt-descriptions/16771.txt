Synopsis: In "Jacqueline of Golden River" by Egbert, H. M., a young girl named Jacqueline embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden secret in her small town. As she unravels the mystery, Jacqueline finds herself facing unexpected challenges and making unlikely alliances. With danger lurking around every corner, she must summon her courage and wit to protect what she holds dear. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Jacqueline.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
