Synopsis: In "The Roadmender" by Michael Fairless, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Leaving behind the comforts of her privileged life, she finds solace and wisdom in the company of a humble roadmender. Through their profound conversations and encounters with nature, she learns valuable lessons about love, faith, and the true meaning of life. As she navigates the challenges of her own existence, she discovers a path towards inner peace and fulfillment.

Genres: Spiritual fiction, Inspirational literature, Philosophical fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Spiritual awakening, Nature, Love, Faith
