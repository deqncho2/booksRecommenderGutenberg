Synopsis: 

'New-England's rarities discovered' takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of early New England, as seen through the eyes of John Josselyn. In this enthralling account, Josselyn unveils a world teeming with extraordinary creatures, from peculiar birds and beasts to mysterious serpents and unique plants. As he explores the natural wonders of the region, Josselyn's vivid descriptions and keen observations transport readers to a time when the New World was still a land of endless discovery and wonder.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Natural beauty, Curiosity, Wonder
