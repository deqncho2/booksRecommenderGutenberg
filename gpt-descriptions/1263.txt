Synopsis: 
In 'The Glimpses of the Moon', we follow the enchanting story of Nick and Susy, two penniless but glamorous friends who devise a clever plan to live off the generosity of their wealthy acquaintances. As they embark on a whirlwind journey through the dazzling social scene of the early 20th century, they find themselves torn between their growing affection for each other and the allure of their luxurious lifestyle. Will their love survive the temptations and compromises of high society?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Social Satire

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Wealth and social status, Morality and ethics
