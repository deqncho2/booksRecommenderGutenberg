Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Henry Wadsworth Longfellow' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of the renowned American poet, Henry Wadsworth Longfellow. This index provides a treasure trove of Longfellow's poems, essays, and translations, showcasing his mastery of language and his ability to evoke powerful emotions through his words. From the iconic "The Song of Hiawatha" to the poignant "Paul Revere's Ride," this collection offers a glimpse into the rich and diverse world of Longfellow's literary genius.

Genres: Poetry, Essays, Translations

Themes: Love, Nature, History, Patriotism
