Synopsis: 
'As I Remember: Recollections of American Society during the Nineteenth Century' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of Gouverneur, Marian, a witness to the transformative events that shaped American society in the 1800s. From the bustling streets of New York City to the battlefields of the Civil War, Gouverneur's vivid recollections offer a unique perspective on the triumphs and struggles of a nation in flux. Through personal anecdotes and insightful observations, the author paints a rich tapestry of a bygone era, immersing readers in the social, political, and cultural fabric of nineteenth-century America.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: American history, societal change, personal perspective, cultural transformation
