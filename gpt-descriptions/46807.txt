Synopsis: 

In 'Lyman's History of old Walla Walla County, Vol. 1: Embracing Walla Walla, Columbia, Garfield and Asotin counties', author William Denison Lyman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the Walla Walla region. From the early days of exploration and settlement to the challenges faced by pioneers, this comprehensive volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the past. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Lyman paints a vivid picture of the people, places, and events that shaped the county's development. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Biography

Themes: Pioneer life, Exploration and settlement, Regional development
