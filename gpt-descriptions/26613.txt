Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a determined and intelligent young woman, finds herself embarking on a new adventure as she enters college. However, her excitement quickly turns into a mystery when a set of important examination papers mysteriously disappear. With her sharp wit and resourcefulness, Ruth sets out to uncover the truth behind the missing papers, facing unexpected challenges and forming new friendships along the way. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Ruth discovers that there is more at stake than just her academic success, and she must use her skills to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Determination, Friendship, Academic integrity
