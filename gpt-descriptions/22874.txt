Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Pursuit; Or, How to Win' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the thrilling adventures of Frank Merriwell, a talented athlete and all-around exceptional young man. When Frank's friend is accused of a crime he didn't commit, Frank takes it upon himself to uncover the truth and clear his friend's name. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Frank finds himself facing dangerous obstacles and formidable adversaries. With his unwavering determination and remarkable skills, Frank must navigate a treacherous path to justice, all while staying true to his values and proving that sometimes winning means more than just coming out on top.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Justice, Perseverance
