Synopsis: 
'Later Poems' by Alice Meynell is a collection of beautifully crafted verses that explore the depths of human emotions and the wonders of nature. From poignant reflections on love and loss to vivid descriptions of the natural world, Meynell's poems captivate readers with their lyrical language and profound insights. Each poem is a window into the poet's soul, inviting readers to contemplate life's complexities and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Nature Writing

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Spirituality
