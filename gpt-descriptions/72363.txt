Synopsis: In "Launch the lifeboat!" by Walton, O. F., Mrs., a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a deserted island after a tragic shipwreck. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and question their own humanity. With each passing day, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, and the true nature of human behavior is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Human nature, Survival, Trust, Morality
