Synopsis: 
In 'All Adrift; Or, The Goldwing Club' by Optic, Oliver, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves in a thrilling and unexpected situation when they embark on a sailing trip. As they set sail on their beloved boat, the Goldwing, they soon encounter a series of perilous challenges that test their courage, resourcefulness, and friendship. From treacherous storms to encounters with mysterious strangers, the boys must navigate through uncharted waters, both literally and metaphorically, in order to survive and find their way back home. This captivating tale of adventure and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for the Goldwing Club.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, Survival
