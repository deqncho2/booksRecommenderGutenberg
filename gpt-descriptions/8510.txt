Synopsis: In 'Lucky Pehr' by August Strindberg, we follow the life of Pehr, a young man who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. Set in 19th-century Sweden, Pehr encounters a series of unexpected events and encounters that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own identity. As he navigates through love, betrayal, and societal expectations, Pehr's journey becomes a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Psychological

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Love and relationships, Society and class
