Synopsis: In 'Far Past the Frontier' by James A. Braden, a group of explorers embarks on a perilous journey to a distant planet, where they encounter mysterious creatures, uncharted territories, and unexpected dangers. As they push the boundaries of human exploration, they must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of both their mission and the planet they have come to explore.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
