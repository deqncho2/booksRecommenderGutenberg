Synopsis: In 'The art of music, Vol. 03 (of 14), A narrative history of music. Book 3, modern music', embark on a captivating journey through the evolution of music in the modern era. From the birth of jazz to the rise of rock and roll, this comprehensive volume explores the diverse and groundbreaking genres that have shaped the musical landscape. Delve into the lives and works of influential musicians, witness the fusion of different cultures and styles, and uncover the societal impact of music throughout history. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, this book offers a rich exploration of the vibrant tapestry of modern music.

Genres: Music History, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Evolution of Music, Cultural Fusion, Societal Impact
