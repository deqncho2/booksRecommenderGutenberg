Synopsis: In 'Off the Beam' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where his own sanity and the fate of the world hang in the balance. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this gripping science fiction thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Consequences of knowledge, Psychological suspense
