Synopsis: In 'What is Coming? A Forecast of Things after the War', H.G. Wells takes readers on a captivating journey into the future, exploring the potential consequences of the ongoing war. Through a series of vivid and thought-provoking predictions, Wells paints a compelling picture of what the world may look like once the conflict has ended. From political shifts to technological advancements, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the possibilities that lie ahead.

Genres: Science Fiction, Speculative Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Future Society, War and its Aftermath, Technological Progress, Political Change
