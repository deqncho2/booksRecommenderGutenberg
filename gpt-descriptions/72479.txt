Synopsis: 
In "Hand-book of punctuation: with instructions for capitalization, letter-writing, and proof-reading," William Johnson Cocker presents a comprehensive guide to the often overlooked but essential aspects of written communication. This invaluable resource covers everything from the correct use of punctuation marks to the art of crafting effective letters and the meticulous process of proofreading. With clear explanations and practical examples, Cocker equips readers with the tools to enhance their writing skills and ensure their messages are conveyed accurately and professionally. Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply someone who values the power of words, this handbook will revolutionize the way you approach written communication.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Language and Writing

Themes: Writing skills, Communication, Language proficiency
