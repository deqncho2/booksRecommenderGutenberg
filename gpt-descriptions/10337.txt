Synopsis: In "Lady into Fox" by David Garnett, a seemingly ordinary couple's lives are turned upside down when the wife suddenly transforms into a fox. As they navigate the challenges of this extraordinary situation, their love and commitment are put to the ultimate test. This enchanting and thought-provoking tale explores the boundaries of human-animal relationships and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Fable

Themes: Transformation, Love, Acceptance
