Synopsis: In 'Wednesday the Tenth, A Tale of the South Pacific', a mysterious and captivating story unfolds as a group of travelers find themselves stranded on a remote island in the South Pacific. As they navigate the island's secrets and encounter its enigmatic inhabitants, they become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With each passing day, the island reveals more of its hidden treasures and dark past, leading the characters on a thrilling and suspenseful journey of discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Secrets, Cultural encounters
