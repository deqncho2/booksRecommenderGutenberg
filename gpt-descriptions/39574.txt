Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys on Secret Service Duty' by Gerald Breckenridge, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mission that takes them deep into the world of espionage. When a mysterious message is intercepted, the Radio Boys must use their skills and knowledge to decipher the code and uncover a dangerous plot that could have far-reaching consequences. As they navigate through hidden clues and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and bravery to protect their country and solve the mystery. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Espionage, Code-breaking, Patriotism
