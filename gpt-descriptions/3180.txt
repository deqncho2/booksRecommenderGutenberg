Synopsis: In 'A Double Barrelled Detective Story', Mark Twain takes readers on a thrilling journey through a small town where a mysterious crime unfolds. As the plot thickens, a young boy and girl team up to solve the perplexing case, using their unique skills and wit. Twain's masterful storytelling keeps readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the secrets hidden within the town's dark underbelly.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Crime and Justice, Innocence and Experience
