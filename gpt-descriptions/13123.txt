Synopsis: 
In "The Great Prince Shan," a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows of the East, captivating the attention of both the political elite and the criminal underworld. As the Great Prince Shan navigates the treacherous waters of international diplomacy and espionage, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love. With his life hanging in the balance, he must rely on his wit, charm, and cunning to outmaneuver his adversaries and protect his true identity. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing world, this thrilling tale of intrigue and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Love and Betrayal
