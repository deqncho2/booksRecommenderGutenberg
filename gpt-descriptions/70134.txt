Synopsis: In "The Curse of Gold" by Ann S. Stephens, a mysterious treasure with a dark past is discovered, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of greed, loyalty, and the power of love. As the characters delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the cursed gold, they must confront their own desires and confront the consequences of their actions. With danger lurking at every turn, the race to break the curse becomes a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Greed, Loyalty, Love, Consequences
