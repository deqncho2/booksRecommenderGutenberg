Synopsis: 
In 'The Bigamist' by F. E. Mills Young, a seemingly ordinary woman's life takes a dramatic turn when she discovers a shocking secret about her husband. As she delves deeper into his mysterious past, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the protagonist must confront her own desires, question her notions of love and loyalty, and ultimately make a life-altering decision that will forever change her future.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality, Love and Relationships
