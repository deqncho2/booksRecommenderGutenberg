Synopsis: 
"The Art of Perfumery, and Methods of Obtaining the Odors of Plants" is a captivating guide that delves into the enchanting world of perfumes and scents. Written by G.W. Septimus Piesse, this book takes readers on a journey through the intricate process of extracting fragrances from various plants and creating exquisite perfumes. From scented powders and odorous vinegars to dentifrices and perfumed soap, Piesse provides detailed instructions on manufacturing a wide range of fragrant products. Additionally, an intriguing appendix explores the fascinating realm of preparing artificial fruit-essences. This book is a treasure trove of knowledge for anyone intrigued by the art of perfumery and the allure of captivating scents.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Crafts and Hobbies

Themes: Perfumery, Fragrance extraction, Manufacturing techniques, Artistic expression
