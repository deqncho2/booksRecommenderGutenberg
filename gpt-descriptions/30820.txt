Synopsis: 
'If Your Baby Must Travel in Wartime' by United States. Children's Bureau is a captivating and informative guide that offers essential advice to parents during times of war. Filled with practical tips and insights, this book explores the unique challenges faced by families when traveling with infants amidst the chaos of war. From ensuring the safety and well-being of the baby to navigating through uncertain circumstances, this guide provides invaluable guidance for parents during wartime.

Genres: Non-fiction, Parenting, Historical

Themes: Parental guidance, Child safety, War-time challenges
