Synopsis: 
In 'Frances of the Ranges; Or, The Old Ranchman's Treasure' by Amy Bell Marlowe, follow the captivating journey of Frances, a spirited young woman who finds herself entangled in a thrilling adventure on the rugged ranchlands. When she stumbles upon a hidden treasure, Frances must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected alliances. As she unravels the secrets of the past, Frances discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of friendship. Will she be able to protect what she holds dear, or will the allure of the treasure prove too tempting to resist?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Friendship, Treasure, Secrets
