Synopsis: In 'The Return', a man mysteriously disappears for five years and then suddenly reappears, claiming to have traveled to another world. As he struggles to convince others of his extraordinary journey, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth behind his disappearance and the secrets of the parallel universe he claims to have visited.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Identity, Conspiracy
