Synopsis: In 'Monica: A Novel, Volume 3 (of 3)', the captivating conclusion to the Monica series, secrets are unveiled, love is tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. As Monica navigates the complexities of her relationships and confronts the ghosts of her past, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, redemption, the power of secrets
