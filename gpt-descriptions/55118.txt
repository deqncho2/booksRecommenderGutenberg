Synopsis: 
In 'Porcelain' by Edward Dillon, a young artist named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she discovers an ancient porcelain doll hidden in her grandmother's attic. As Lily delves deeper into the doll's history, she uncovers a dark secret that connects her family to a forgotten era. With each revelation, Lily's own life becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, forcing her to confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Artistic expression
