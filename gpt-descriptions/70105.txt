Synopsis: In "Gilead Balm, knight errant: His adventures in search of the truth" by Capes, Bernard, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the enigmatic knight, Gilead Balm. As he embarks on a quest to uncover the truth, Balm finds himself entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step, he unravels secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own past. As the truth becomes more elusive, Balm's determination intensifies, leading him to face unimaginable trials and make difficult choices. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Truth and deception, Identity and self-discovery
