Synopsis: 
In 'Rollo in Society: A Guide for Youth', George S. Chappell takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of Rollo, a young man navigating the complexities of society. As Rollo encounters various social situations, he learns valuable lessons about etiquette, manners, and the importance of personal character. From elegant balls to formal dinners, Rollo's experiences provide a delightful blend of humor, wit, and insightful observations. This engaging guide offers readers a chance to explore the intricacies of social interactions while following Rollo's entertaining adventures.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: Etiquette, Manners, Personal character
