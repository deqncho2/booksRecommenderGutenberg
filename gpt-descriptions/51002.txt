Synopsis: 

'Korean Folk Tales: Imps, Ghosts, and Fairies' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Korean folklore. Im, Pang and Yi, Yuk skillfully weave together a collection of intriguing tales filled with mischievous imps, haunting ghosts, and ethereal fairies. From encounters with shape-shifting creatures to encounters with vengeful spirits, each story is a delightful blend of fantasy, adventure, and cultural richness. As readers delve into these captivating narratives, they will be transported to a realm where the supernatural intertwines with everyday life, leaving them spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural beings, Korean culture, Moral lessons
