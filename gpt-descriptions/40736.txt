Synopsis: In "The Second Mrs. Tanqueray: A Play in Four Acts," the lives of a diverse group of characters become intertwined when a wealthy widower marries a woman with a scandalous past. As secrets are revealed and societal expectations clash with personal desires, the characters are forced to confront their own prejudices and question the nature of love, forgiveness, and redemption.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Social Realism

Themes: Society and morality, love and marriage, redemption and forgiveness
