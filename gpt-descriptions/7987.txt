Synopsis:
In the bustling town of Perth, a young and beautiful maiden named Catherine Glover finds herself caught in a web of love, intrigue, and danger. As the town prepares for the annual St. Valentine's Day festivities, Catherine becomes the object of desire for two very different suitors: the dashing knight Sir Patrick Charteris and the mysterious and enigmatic Henry Gow. As their paths intertwine, Catherine must navigate the complexities of her heart and the secrets that threaten to tear her world apart. Set against the backdrop of medieval Scotland, this captivating tale explores the power of love, the consequences of deception, and the strength of a woman's spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Deception and Betrayal, Female Empowerment
