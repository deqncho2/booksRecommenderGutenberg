Synopsis: 
'Colonial facts and fictions: Humorous sketches' by Milne, John takes readers on a delightful journey through the colonial era, filled with amusing anecdotes and witty observations. From the eccentric characters encountered in far-flung colonies to the comical misunderstandings that arise from cultural clashes, this collection of sketches offers a lighthearted and entertaining exploration of colonial life. With a keen eye for humor and a knack for storytelling, Milne captures the essence of the colonial experience, inviting readers to laugh along with the absurdities and idiosyncrasies of the time.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural Misunderstandings, Eccentricity
