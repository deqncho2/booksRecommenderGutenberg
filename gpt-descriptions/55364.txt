Synopsis: In "Under King Henry's Banners: A story of the days of Agincourt," Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the tumultuous days of the famous Battle of Agincourt. Set against the backdrop of the Hundred Years' War, the story follows young protagonist Richard Baldock as he embarks on a perilous adventure, facing treacherous enemies, forging unlikely alliances, and discovering the true meaning of courage and loyalty. As Richard finds himself caught in the midst of the epic clash between England and France, the fate of nations hangs in the balance, and he must summon all his strength and determination to survive the brutal realities of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Coming of Age, Conflict
