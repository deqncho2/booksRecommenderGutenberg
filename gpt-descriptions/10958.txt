Synopsis: In "An Unwilling Maid: Being the History of Certain Episodes during the American Revolution in the Early Life of Mistress Betty Yorke, born Wolcott," we follow the captivating journey of Betty Yorke, a young woman caught in the midst of the American Revolution. As the daughter of a loyalist family, Betty finds herself torn between her loyalty to her family and her growing sympathy for the revolutionary cause. Forced into a role she never imagined, Betty must navigate treacherous waters, facing danger, love, and betrayal, as she discovers her own strength and fights for her beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Independence, Love and Betrayal
