Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the First, or the Royal Regiment of Dragoons," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this esteemed regiment. From its origins in the reign of King Charles the Second to its remarkable exploits up until 1839, Cannon delves into the regiment's triumphs, challenges, and the courageous individuals who shaped its history. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of the Royal Regiment of Dragoons.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
