Synopsis: In 'The Russian Opera' by Newmarch, Rosa, a young and talented opera singer, finds herself entangled in a web of passion, intrigue, and danger when she is invited to perform in the prestigious Russian opera house. As Rosa delves deeper into the world of Russian opera, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten not only her career but also her very life. With her voice as her only weapon, Rosa must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and ambition to survive in this captivating tale of music and mystery.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Love, Secrets, Survival
