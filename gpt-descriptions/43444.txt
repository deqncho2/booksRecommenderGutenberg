Synopsis: In 'White Heather: A Novel (Volume 1 of 3)' by William Black, a young artist named Archie Gilchrist finds himself captivated by the enigmatic beauty of a woman named White Heather. As he becomes entangled in her world, Archie discovers a web of secrets, hidden desires, and unexpected connections that will challenge his perception of love, loyalty, and the power of art. Set against the backdrop of Victorian Scotland, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Art and Creativity, Secrets and Betrayal
