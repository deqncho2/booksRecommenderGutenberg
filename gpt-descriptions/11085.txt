Synopsis: In "M. or N. "Similia similibus curantur."," the protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue as he investigates a series of unexplained deaths. Set against the backdrop of the English countryside, he must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the case, he realizes that nothing is as it seems, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
