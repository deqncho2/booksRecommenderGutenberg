Synopsis: In "The Farmer's Boy: One of R. Caldecott's picture books," join a young boy named Tom as he embarks on a delightful adventure in the countryside. Tom's days are filled with hard work, but he finds joy in the simple pleasures of nature and the animals that surround him. Through beautiful illustrations and charming storytelling, this picture book captures the essence of rural life and the resilience of a young boy's spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book, Adventure

Themes: Rural life, Hard work, Appreciation of nature, Resilience
