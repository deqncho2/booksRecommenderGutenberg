Synopsis: In 'Charity Case' by Jim Harmon, a struggling journalist, Jack, stumbles upon a mysterious charity organization that seems too good to be true. As he delves deeper into the organization's activities, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that puts his life in danger. With the help of an enigmatic woman, Jack must navigate a treacherous path to expose the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Corruption, Redemption
