Synopsis: 

In 'Railroad Accidents, Their Cause and Prevention', author R. C. Richards delves into the gripping world of railway disasters, uncovering the hidden truths behind these catastrophic events. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Richards explores the causes of these accidents, shedding light on the often overlooked factors that contribute to their occurrence. From human error to mechanical failures, this book uncovers the shocking realities that lie beneath the surface of these tragic incidents. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for uncovering the truth, Richards takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of railroad accidents, leaving them with a newfound understanding of the importance of prevention.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Transportation

Themes: Safety, Engineering, Human Error, Disaster Prevention
