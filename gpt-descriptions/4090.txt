Synopsis: In "From Ritual to Romance," Jessie L. Weston delves into the fascinating world of ancient rituals and their connection to the Arthurian legends. Exploring the parallels between ancient fertility rites and the quest for the Holy Grail, Weston uncovers hidden symbols and archetypes that have shaped our understanding of love, sacrifice, and redemption. As she unravels the threads of myth and ritual, a captivating narrative unfolds, revealing the profound impact of ancient ceremonies on our collective consciousness.

Genres: Mythology, Literary Criticism, Historical Analysis

Themes: Rituals and Symbolism, Arthurian Legends, Love and Redemption
