Synopsis: 
In 'How to Be a Detective' by Brady James, readers are introduced to the thrilling world of detective work through the eyes of a young and curious protagonist. When a mysterious crime takes place in their neighborhood, our aspiring detective embarks on a captivating journey to uncover the truth. With each clue they uncover, the stakes rise higher, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As our young detective navigates through a web of secrets and deception, they must rely on their wit, observation skills, and determination to solve the case and bring justice to their community.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Problem-solving, Justice
