Synopsis: In 'The Athelings; or, the Three Gifts', Mrs. Oliphant weaves a captivating tale of three siblings who possess extraordinary talents. As they navigate the complexities of society, their unique gifts become both a blessing and a curse, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of their true destinies. With elements of mystery, romance, and family dynamics, this enchanting novel explores the power of individuality and the choices we make in shaping our own lives.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Individuality, Self-discovery, Family dynamics
