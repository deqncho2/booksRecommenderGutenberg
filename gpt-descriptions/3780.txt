Synopsis:
In 'The King's Highway' by James, G. P. R., a young and ambitious lawyer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he takes on a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the secrets of a powerful family, he uncovers a dark and treacherous world filled with deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his own life on the line, he must navigate the treacherous King's Highway, a path fraught with peril and hidden agendas, to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Deceit, Betrayal, Justice
