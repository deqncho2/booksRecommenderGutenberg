Synopsis: 

In "Reminiscences of a Liverpool Shipowner, 1850-1920," Sir William Bower Forwood takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of shipowning during a pivotal period in history. Through his vivid recollections, Forwood provides a unique insider's perspective on the rise and fall of Liverpool as a prominent maritime hub, as well as the challenges and triumphs faced by shipowners during this era. From the bustling docks to the treacherous seas, Forwood's narrative is filled with captivating anecdotes, personal reflections, and insightful observations that transport readers back in time to experience the excitement, risks, and rewards of the shipowning industry.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Maritime history, Entrepreneurship, Industrialization, Economic development
