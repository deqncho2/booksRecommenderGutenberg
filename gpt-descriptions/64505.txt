Synopsis: In 'Give Back a World' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a renowned scientist discovers a remarkable invention that allows him to travel to parallel universes. As he explores these alternate worlds, he encounters astonishing civilizations, dangerous adversaries, and unexpected allies. With each journey, he becomes more entangled in a web of intrigue and must confront the ethical implications of his actions. As the stakes rise, he must decide whether to use his newfound power for personal gain or to protect the fragile balance of the multiverse.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Ethical dilemmas, Exploration
