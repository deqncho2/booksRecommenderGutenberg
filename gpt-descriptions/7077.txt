Synopsis: 
In 'We Can't Have Everything: A Novel' by Rupert Hughes, we are introduced to the captivating world of young and ambitious Vance Weston. Born into a humble family, Vance dreams of achieving greatness and making a name for himself. As he navigates through the challenges of life, love, and success, Vance finds himself torn between his desire for personal fulfillment and the sacrifices he must make along the way. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this gripping novel takes readers on a journey filled with passion, ambition, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Love, Sacrifice, Social Mobility
