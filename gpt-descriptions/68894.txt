Synopsis: 
In 'Twenty tales by twenty women: From real life in Chicago', Anonymous takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of twenty diverse women living in the vibrant city of Chicago. From the bustling streets of the Loop to the quiet neighborhoods of the South Side, these women share their personal stories, struggles, triumphs, and everything in between. Each tale offers a unique perspective, delving into themes of love, loss, friendship, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves engrossed in the rich tapestry of Chicago life, eagerly awaiting the next captivating story.

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Anthology

Themes: Women's experiences, Urban life, Identity, Relationships, Resilience
