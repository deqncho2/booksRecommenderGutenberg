Synopsis: In 'The Khaki Boys Fighting to Win; or, Smashing the German Lines' by Josephine Chase, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside a group of brave young men who find themselves at the heart of World War I. As they face the horrors of the battlefield, the Khaki Boys must summon their courage, resilience, and unwavering determination to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of their comrades and the success of their mission hang in the balance. Will they be able to smash through the German lines and emerge victorious?

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Heroism
