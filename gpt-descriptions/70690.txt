Synopsis: 
In 'Trial of John Jasper, lay precentor of Cloisterham Cathedral in the County of Kent, for the murder of Edwin Drood, engineer', the Dickens Fellowship takes on the task of unraveling a gripping murder mystery set in the atmospheric town of Cloisterham. John Jasper, a respected lay precentor, finds himself at the center of a scandalous trial accused of the murder of Edwin Drood, a talented engineer. As the trial unfolds, secrets are revealed, motives are questioned, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. With its intricate plot and complex characters, this captivating novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly awaiting the final verdict.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Justice and injustice, Secrets and deception, Identity and perception
