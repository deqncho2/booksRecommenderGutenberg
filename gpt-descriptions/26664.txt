Synopsis:
In "The Live Corpse," Tolstoy takes us on a gripping journey through the complexities of love, marriage, and the human psyche. The story revolves around a man named Ivan Ilyich Praskovya, who finds himself trapped in a loveless marriage and a life devoid of purpose. As he grapples with his own existential crisis, Ivan embarks on a quest for self-discovery, seeking answers to the profound questions that haunt him. With its intricate plot and deeply introspective narrative, "The Live Corpse" delves into the depths of human emotions, exploring the consequences of societal expectations and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love, Marriage, Existentialism, Self-discovery
