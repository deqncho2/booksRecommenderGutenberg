Synopsis: 
In 'The Declaration of Independence of the United States of America', Thomas Jefferson presents a powerful and impassioned argument for the separation of the American colonies from British rule. Through eloquent language and persuasive reasoning, Jefferson outlines the grievances of the colonists and asserts their inherent rights to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness. This seminal document serves as a rallying cry for freedom and independence, igniting a revolution that would shape the course of history.

Genres: Political, Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Freedom, Independence, Human Rights
