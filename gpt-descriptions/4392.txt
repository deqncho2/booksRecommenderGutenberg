Synopsis: In 'Martie, the Unconquered' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, readers are introduced to Martie, a young woman who defies societal expectations and embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Set in the early 20th century, Martie faces numerous challenges and obstacles as she strives to find her own path in a world dominated by tradition and conformity. As she navigates through love, loss, and personal growth, Martie's resilience and determination shine through, inspiring readers to question societal norms and embrace their true selves.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Individuality, Self-discovery, Gender roles, Societal expectations
