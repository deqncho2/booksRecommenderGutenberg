Synopsis: In "Structure and Functions of the Body: A Hand-Book of Anatomy and Physiology for Nurses and Others Desiring a Practical Knowledge of the Subject," author Annette Fiske takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of the human body. With a focus on practical knowledge, this comprehensive guide provides nurses and other interested individuals with a deep understanding of anatomy and physiology. From the complex systems that keep our bodies functioning to the interplay between different organs, Fiske's book offers a fascinating exploration of the inner workings of the human body. Through clear explanations and detailed illustrations, readers will gain a practical knowledge of the subject that can be applied in various healthcare settings. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Educational

Themes: Anatomy, Physiology, Healthcare Education
