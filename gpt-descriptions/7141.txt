Synopsis: In 'Suburban Sketches', William Dean Howells takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of ordinary people in a small New England town. From the eccentric characters who populate the local boarding house to the struggles of a young artist trying to make a name for himself, Howells paints a vivid picture of suburban life in the late 19th century. With his keen observations and sharp wit, he delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring themes of social class, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Realism

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Happiness
