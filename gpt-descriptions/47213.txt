Synopsis: 
'As the Crow Flies: From Corsica to Charing Cross' takes readers on a thrilling journey across continents, as a young man finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue. From the picturesque landscapes of Corsica to the bustling streets of Charing Cross, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous path filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his own identity, he discovers that the choices he makes will have far-reaching consequences. With its gripping narrative and unpredictable twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Secrets
