Synopsis: 
In 'A Black Adonis' by Porter, Linn Boyd, a young African American man named Adonis is thrust into the glamorous world of high society when he becomes the object of desire for wealthy white women. As Adonis navigates this new world, he must confront the complexities of race, identity, and power, all while trying to maintain his own sense of self. With its captivating characters and thought-provoking exploration of societal norms, 'A Black Adonis' is a gripping tale that will keep readers hooked until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Race, Identity, Power, Societal Norms
