Synopsis: In "An Amiable Charlatan" by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a charming and enigmatic man named Felix is drawn into a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes entangled with a notorious criminal organization. As Felix navigates a world of deception and double-crossing, he must use his wit and cunning to outsmart his adversaries and protect those he cares about. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and who is truly the master manipulator.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Loyalty, Betrayal
