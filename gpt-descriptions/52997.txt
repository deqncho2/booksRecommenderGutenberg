Synopsis: 

In 'Frijoles: A Hidden Valley in the New World', author J.W. Hendron takes readers on a captivating journey to a mysterious valley tucked away in the heart of the New World. When a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that spans centuries. As she delves deeper into the history of the valley, she uncovers a hidden civilization and a forgotten treasure that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unexpected challenges to unravel the truth behind the valley's enigmatic past.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Archaeology, Legacy, Secrets
