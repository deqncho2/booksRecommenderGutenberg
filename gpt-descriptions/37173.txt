Synopsis: In 'In a Glass Darkly, v. 2/3' by Le Fanu, Joseph Sheridan, a series of chilling and mysterious tales unfold, each more haunting than the last. From a cursed portrait that brings death to those who gaze upon it, to a young girl tormented by a malevolent spirit, these stories will keep you on the edge of your seat, questioning the boundaries between the supernatural and reality. As the characters grapple with their own fears and the darkness that lurks within, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the macabre and the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: The supernatural, psychological terror, the duality of human nature
