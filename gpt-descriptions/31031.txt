Synopsis: In "Guy Fawkes; Or, A Complete History Of The Gunpowder Treason, A.D. 1605," Thomas Lathbury delves into the captivating tale of the infamous Gunpowder Plot. This historical account takes readers on a thrilling journey through the events leading up to the conspiracy, the daring plot itself, and its shocking aftermath. Lathbury's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the characters involved, their motivations, and the political and religious tensions of the time. As the plot unfolds, readers will be gripped by the suspense and intrigue surrounding this audacious act of rebellion against the English monarchy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political thriller

Themes: Conspiracy, Rebellion, Religious conflict, Loyalty, Power struggle
