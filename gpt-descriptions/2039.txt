Synopsis: In the enchanting land of Acadie, two young lovers, Evangeline and Gabriel, are torn apart by a tragic event. Evangeline embarks on a relentless search to find her lost love, enduring countless hardships and encountering diverse characters along the way. As she traverses through the stunning landscapes of North America, Evangeline's unwavering determination and undying love become a testament to the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Epic poem, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and loss, Fate and destiny, Resilience and perseverance
