Synopsis: In the heart of the Massachusetts Bay Colony, a young girl named Ruth embarks on a journey that will test her courage, resilience, and determination. Set against the backdrop of the early American settlement, Ruth of Boston follows her as she navigates the challenges of a new land, confronts the prejudices of the time, and discovers the true meaning of family and friendship. As Ruth's story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and unexpected twists and turns keep the reader captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Perseverance, Community, Prejudice
