Synopsis: In 'The Century World's Fair Book for Boys and Girls', follow the thrilling adventures of Harry and Philip as they explore the wonders of the World's Columbian Exposition with their tutor, Mr. Douglass. From the breathtaking exhibits to unexpected encounters, the trio embarks on a journey filled with excitement, mystery, and valuable life lessons. As they navigate through the grand fairgrounds, they uncover hidden secrets and forge unforgettable friendships, all while immersing themselves in the marvels of the fair. This captivating tale will transport readers back in time to experience the magic and wonder of one of the most iconic world's fairs in history.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Curiosity, Education
