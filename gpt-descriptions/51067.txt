Synopsis: 
In 'Living Too Fast; Or, The Confessions of a Bank Officer' by Optic, Oliver, we are introduced to the intriguing world of a bank officer who finds himself caught in a web of deception and danger. As he navigates through the treacherous corridors of power and greed, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and make choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the bank officer's confessions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Deception, Power, Consequences
