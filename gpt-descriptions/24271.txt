Synopsis: 

'Children's Rhymes, Children's Games, Children's Songs, Children's Stories: A Book for Bairns and Big Folk' by Robert Ford is a delightful collection of timeless tales and playful rhymes that will captivate both children and adults alike. From whimsical stories of talking animals to catchy songs and interactive games, this book is a treasure trove of entertainment for the young and the young at heart. With its colorful illustrations and engaging storytelling, Ford takes readers on a journey through the magical world of childhood imagination, where laughter, wonder, and joy abound.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Folklore

Themes: Imagination, Playfulness, Nostalgia
