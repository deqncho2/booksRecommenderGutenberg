Synopsis: 
In 'The Pit-Prop Syndicate' by Freeman Wills Crofts, a seemingly ordinary business deal takes a sinister turn when a man mysteriously disappears. As Inspector French delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, greed, and betrayal that reaches far beyond the initial disappearance. With time running out, French must navigate through a complex network of suspects and motives to uncover the truth before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Greed, Betrayal, Investigation
