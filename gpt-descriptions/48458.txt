Synopsis: 
'Within the Capes' by Howard Pyle is a thrilling adventure set in the early 18th century. The story follows a young sailor named Tom, who finds himself caught in a treacherous web of pirates, hidden treasure, and a mysterious curse. As Tom embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas, he must navigate through dangerous encounters, face his deepest fears, and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic legends that haunt the Cape Cod coast. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, 'Within the Capes' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Redemption, Legends and Folklore
