Synopsis: In 'The Truants' by A.E.W. Mason, a young man named Richard Chandos finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes entangled with a group of enigmatic individuals at a remote English country house. As secrets are revealed and tensions rise, Richard must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth, all while questioning his own identity and the motives of those around him. With its atmospheric setting and gripping plot, 'The Truants' will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
