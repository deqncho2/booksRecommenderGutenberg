Synopsis: In 'Counterfeit Money: The "green goods" business exposed for the benefit of all who have dishonest inclinations,' an anonymous author delves into the shadowy world of counterfeit money, revealing the intricate web of deception and danger that surrounds it. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and real-life examples, the author uncovers the methods used by counterfeiters and the devastating consequences their actions have on individuals and society. As the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of crime, they will be left questioning their own perceptions of trust and the true value of money.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Investigative Journalism

Themes: Deception, Crime, Trust, Morality
