Synopsis: 
In the small village of Knaresborough, a mysterious and enigmatic figure named Eugene Aram resides. Known for his intelligence and reclusive nature, Aram becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deceit when a murder takes place. As suspicion falls upon him, Aram must navigate the treacherous path of love, betrayal, and redemption to uncover the truth and clear his name. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, this gripping tale of mystery and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Love and Betrayal, Redemption
