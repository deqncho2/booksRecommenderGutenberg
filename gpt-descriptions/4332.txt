Synopsis: 
'The Ghetto, and Other Poems' by Lola Ridge is a collection of powerful and evocative poems that delve into the lives and struggles of the marginalized and oppressed. Through vivid imagery and poignant language, Ridge explores the harsh realities of life in the urban slums, the resilience of the human spirit, and the pursuit of social justice. From the depths of poverty to the heights of hope, these poems offer a raw and unflinching portrayal of the human experience.

Genres: Poetry, Social Realism, Modernism

Themes: Poverty, Social Inequality, Resilience, Empathy, Social Justice
