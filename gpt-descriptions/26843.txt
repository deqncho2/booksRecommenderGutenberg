Synopsis: In 'The Dope on Mars' by Sharkey, Jack, a group of misfit astronauts embark on a daring mission to Mars, but soon find themselves caught in a web of mystery and danger. As they uncover the secrets of the red planet, they must confront their own inner demons and make choices that will determine the fate of humanity. With unexpected twists and a thrilling narrative, this sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
