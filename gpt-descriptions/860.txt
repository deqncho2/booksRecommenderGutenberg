Synopsis: In 'Baby Mine' by Margaret Mayo, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she discovers a mysterious baby abandoned on her doorstep. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the baby's identity, Lily becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With each clue she uncovers, Lily's life becomes increasingly endangered, forcing her to confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the innocent child.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal
