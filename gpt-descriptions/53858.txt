Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the rich history of Lowestoft, a quaint coastal town that has witnessed centuries of fascinating tales. In "Lowestoft in olden times," Francis Davy Longe takes us on a captivating journey through the past, unveiling the secrets and mysteries that lie within the town's ancient streets. From legendary shipwrecks to tales of smugglers and pirates, this book paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, where adventure and danger were never far away. Discover the hidden treasures of Lowestoft and let your imagination run wild as you delve into the captivating stories that shaped this enchanting town.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Maritime history

Themes: History, Adventure, Mystery, Local culture
