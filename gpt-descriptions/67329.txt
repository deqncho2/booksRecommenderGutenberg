Synopsis: 

'Charles Robert Maturin: His Life and Works' by Idman, Niilo delves into the captivating life and literary contributions of the enigmatic Irish author, Charles Robert Maturin. From his early struggles and personal tragedies to his rise as a prominent figure in the Romantic era, this biography offers a fascinating exploration of Maturin's complex character and the profound impact he had on the literary world. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, the book unveils the hidden depths of Maturin's works, revealing the dark and haunting themes that permeate his writing. As readers embark on this journey through Maturin's life, they will be captivated by the mysteries and complexities that shaped his literary legacy.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Romanticism, Tragedy, Gothic Literature
