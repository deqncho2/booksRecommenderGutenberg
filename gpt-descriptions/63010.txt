Synopsis: 
In 'Verse' by Adelaide Crapsey, a young poet named Adelaide embarks on a journey of self-discovery and artistic exploration. As she navigates the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of her creative voice, Adelaide's experiences shape her understanding of the world and her place within it. Through her poignant and evocative verses, she invites readers to join her on a profound and introspective exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Poetry, Coming-of-Age, Literary Fiction

Themes: Self-expression, Love, Loss, Identity, Artistic Pursuit
