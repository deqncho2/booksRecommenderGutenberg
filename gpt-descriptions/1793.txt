Synopsis: In the gripping tragedy of 'Othello' by William Shakespeare, a respected military general finds himself entangled in a web of jealousy, manipulation, and deceit. As Othello's world unravels, the play explores the destructive power of suspicion and the consequences of unchecked emotions. With its complex characters and intense drama, 'Othello' delves into the dark corners of the human psyche, leaving readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Jealousy, Manipulation, Betrayal, Racism
