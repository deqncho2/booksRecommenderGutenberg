Synopsis:
In 'Castes and Tribes of Southern India. Vol. 7 of 7', Edgar Thurston takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse castes and tribes of Southern India. From the ancient traditions and customs to the intricate social structures, this volume delves deep into the rich tapestry of the region's cultural heritage. Thurston's meticulous research and vivid descriptions bring to life the fascinating stories and unique characteristics of each caste and tribe, shedding light on their history, beliefs, and way of life. As readers explore the pages of this comprehensive work, they will be enthralled by the intricate web of identities and relationships that shape the social fabric of Southern India.

Genres: Anthropology, Cultural Studies, History

Themes: Cultural diversity, Social structures, Identity, Tradition
