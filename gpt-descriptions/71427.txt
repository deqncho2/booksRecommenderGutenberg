Synopsis: Two young lumbermen, hailing from the rugged state of Maine, embark on a thrilling journey across the vast expanse of America to seek their fortune in the untamed wilderness of Oregon. Facing treacherous landscapes, dangerous encounters, and unexpected challenges, their determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. As they navigate through the uncharted territories, the young men discover the true meaning of friendship, resilience, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
