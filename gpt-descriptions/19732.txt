Synopsis: In 'The Eternal City' by Caine, Hall, Sir, a renowned archaeologist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he uncovers a hidden artifact in the heart of Rome. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the ancient city, he becomes the target of a powerful organization willing to do anything to protect their dark agenda. With the help of a brilliant historian and a fearless journalist, he must race against time to unravel the truth and save not only himself but also the future of Rome itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Conspiracy, Betrayal, Redemption
