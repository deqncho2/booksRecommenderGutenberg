Synopsis: In 'Materfamilias' by Cambridge, Ada, a young woman named Lily finds herself entangled in a web of family secrets and hidden desires when she returns to her childhood home after her mother's sudden death. As she delves deeper into her mother's past, Lily uncovers a series of shocking revelations that challenge her perception of her own identity and the meaning of family. With each discovery, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means confronting the darkest corners of her family's history.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Loss, Redemption
