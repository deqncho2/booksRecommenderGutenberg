Synopsis: 
In "Patty in Paris" by Wells, Carolyn, young Patty Fairfield embarks on a thrilling adventure in the enchanting city of Paris. As she explores the vibrant streets, encounters intriguing characters, and becomes entangled in a mysterious plot, Patty's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With a mix of romance, suspense, and a touch of humor, this captivating tale will transport readers to the magical world of Paris and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Cultural exploration, Identity
