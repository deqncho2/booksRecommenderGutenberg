Synopsis: 
"The Curious Lore of Precious Stones" delves into the captivating world of gemstones, exploring their rich history, folklore, superstitions, symbolism, and mystical properties. George Frederick Kunz takes readers on a fascinating journey through the various uses of gemstones in medicine, protection, prevention, religion, and divination. From crystal gazing to birthstones, lucky stones, and talismans, the book uncovers the significance of these precious stones in different cultures and their connections to astral, zodiacal, and planetary influences. With a wealth of intriguing information, this book offers a captivating exploration of the hidden lore and enchanting qualities of precious stones.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Gemology, Symbolism, Mysticism, Cultural Significance
