Synopsis: In 'The Dogs of Boytown' by Walter A. Dyer, a small town is plagued by a mysterious series of dog disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and confusion. As the local children band together to solve the enigma, they uncover a web of secrets that will test their courage and loyalty. With each clue they uncover, the children find themselves drawn deeper into a thrilling adventure that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Community, Secrets
