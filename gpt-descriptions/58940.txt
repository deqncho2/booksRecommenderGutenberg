Synopsis: In "The Wolf Demon; or, The Queen of the Kanawha" by Albert W. Aiken, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds in the heart of the Kanawha Valley. As the townspeople are plagued by a series of unexplained events, a young woman named Eliza finds herself caught in the midst of a supernatural power struggle. With the arrival of a charismatic stranger, Eliza's life takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the wolf demon that haunts the valley. Filled with suspense, romance, and the allure of the supernatural, this gripping story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural thriller, romance

Themes: Power struggles, the supernatural, identity, love and sacrifice
