Synopsis: In 'The Atomic Fingerprint: Neutron Activation Analysis' by Keisch, Bernard, a groundbreaking scientific technique takes center stage as a mysterious artifact leads a team of researchers on a thrilling journey of discovery. As they delve into the world of neutron activation analysis, they uncover hidden secrets and unexpected connections that could change the course of history. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the race to unlock the truth becomes a battle against time. This gripping tale of scientific exploration and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific discovery, Historical mysteries, Consequences of knowledge
