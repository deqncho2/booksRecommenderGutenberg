Synopsis: In 'The Lost Army' by Knox, Thomas Wallace, a group of archaeologists embarks on a daring expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization hidden deep within the Amazon rainforest. As they delve into the mysteries of this lost civilization, they soon find themselves facing unimaginable dangers and unexpected adversaries. With time running out and their lives hanging in the balance, the team must navigate treacherous jungles, decipher cryptic clues, and confront their own inner demons in a race against time to unlock the truth that could change history forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Ancient Civilizations, Human Nature
