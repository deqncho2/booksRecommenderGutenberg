Synopsis: In the small town of Wastralls, secrets lie buried beneath the surface, waiting to be unearthed. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As the past collides with the present, dark secrets are revealed, and the residents of Wastralls must confront their own demons. With its gripping plot and complex characters, 'Wastralls: A Novel' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the shadows of a seemingly idyllic town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Redemption, Identity
