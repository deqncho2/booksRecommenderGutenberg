Synopsis: In 'The World's Illusion, Volume 1: Eva', Wassermann takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Eva, a young woman who finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the early 20th century, this gripping tale explores Eva's struggle to break free from the confines of her traditional upbringing and the challenges she faces as she seeks to forge her own path. As Eva navigates love, loss, and self-discovery, readers are drawn into a world filled with complex characters, unexpected twists, and thought-provoking dilemmas. Will Eva find the courage to defy convention and embrace her true self? Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance. Themes: Identity, Freedom, Societal Expectations.
