Synopsis: In 'The Babes in the Basket; or, Daph and Her Charge', a young woman named Daphne finds herself unexpectedly entrusted with the care of a mysterious baby. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the child's origins, Daphne becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step she takes, the stakes grow higher, and Daphne must rely on her wit and courage to protect the baby and unravel the truth. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate fate of Daphne and her charge.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Motherhood, Trust, Redemption
