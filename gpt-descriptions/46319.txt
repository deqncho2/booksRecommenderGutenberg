Synopsis: 

In 'The Vale of Lyvennet: Its Picturesque Peeps and Legendary Lore', author John Salkeld Bland takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting Vale of Lyvennet. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Bland unveils the hidden wonders and mysterious legends that have shaped this picturesque landscape. From ancient ruins and mystical creatures to tales of love and betrayal, the Vale of Lyvennet holds secrets that will leave readers spellbound. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Travelogue

Themes: Myth and Legend, Nature and Landscape, Human Connection
