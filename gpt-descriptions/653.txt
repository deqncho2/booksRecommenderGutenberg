Synopsis: In 'The Chimes: A Goblin Story of Some Bells That Rang an Old Year out and a New Year In', Dickens takes readers on a haunting journey through the streets of London on New Year's Eve. The story follows Toby Veck, a humble porter, who encounters a group of goblins that show him visions of a bleak future. As the chimes of the bells ring out, Toby must confront his own fears and find hope amidst the darkness.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, social commentary

Themes: Redemption, the power of memory, social inequality
