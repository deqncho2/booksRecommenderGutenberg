Synopsis: In 'The Son of My Friend' by Arthur, T. S., a chance encounter between two old friends leads to a shocking revelation that will test their loyalty and challenge their moral compass. As secrets from the past resurface, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, love, and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Morality, Betrayal
