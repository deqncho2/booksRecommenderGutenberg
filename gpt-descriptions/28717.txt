Synopsis: In 'Wee Wifie' by Rosa Nouchette Carey, a young woman named Winifred is thrust into a world of intrigue and secrets when she marries a wealthy older man. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Winifred must confront the challenges of societal expectations, personal identity, and the true nature of love. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and marriage, societal expectations, personal identity, secrets and deception
