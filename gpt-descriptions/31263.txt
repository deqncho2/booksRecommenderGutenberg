Synopsis: In "Bark Kathleen Sunk By A Whale," author Thomas H. Jenkins takes us on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the sea. This gripping account tells the harrowing tale of the ill-fated ship, Bark Kathleen, as it encounters a massive whale that leads to its tragic demise. As if one tragedy wasn't enough, Jenkins also recounts two similar incidents involving the ships Ann Alexander and Essex. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding suspense, this book explores the dangers faced by sailors and the awe-inspiring power of nature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Survival, Man vs. Nature, Perseverance
