Synopsis: 
In 'Ethel Morton at Rose House', Ethel and her family embark on a summer adventure to a charming country house. Little do they know that this seemingly idyllic retreat will be filled with unexpected surprises, mysterious happenings, and a cast of intriguing characters. As Ethel explores the secrets of Rose House, she discovers hidden treasures, uncovers long-held family secrets, and learns valuable lessons about friendship and loyalty. With each twist and turn, Ethel's curiosity and determination lead her on a captivating journey that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Family, Friendship, Discovery
