Synopsis: In 'Mollentrave on Women: A comedy in three acts' by Alfred Sutro, we are introduced to the eccentric and witty character of Mollentrave, a man who believes he has discovered the secret to understanding women. With his unconventional theories and humorous observations, Mollentrave embarks on a series of comical encounters and misadventures, challenging societal norms and shedding light on the complexities of human relationships. As the story unfolds, we are taken on a delightful journey filled with laughter, unexpected twists, and thought-provoking insights into the enigmatic world of women.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Theatre

Themes: Gender dynamics, Miscommunication, Social expectations
