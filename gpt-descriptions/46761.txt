Synopsis: In 'The Wheat Princess' by Jean Webster, a young woman named Priscilla is thrust into a world of wealth and privilege when she unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. As she navigates the complexities of high society, Priscilla must grapple with the true meaning of happiness and the importance of staying true to oneself. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of love and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Identity and self-discovery, Love and happiness
