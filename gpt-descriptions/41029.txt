Synopsis: In 'The Moon Destroyers' by Ruch, Monroe K., a team of brilliant scientists and daring astronauts embark on a perilous mission to save Earth from an impending catastrophe. As they journey to the moon, they uncover shocking secrets and face unimaginable challenges that threaten not only their mission but also the fate of humanity. With time running out, they must push the boundaries of science and courage to the limit in order to prevent the moon from becoming a weapon of mass destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sacrifice, Human Ingenuity
