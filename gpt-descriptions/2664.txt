Synopsis: In the mystical world of 18th-century Italy, a mysterious and enigmatic figure named Zanoni captivates the attention of all who encounter him. Possessing extraordinary powers and immortality, Zanoni becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair with a beautiful woman, leading him on a perilous journey through the realms of love, ambition, and the pursuit of eternal knowledge. As the forces of good and evil clash, Zanoni must confront his own inner demons and make a fateful choice that will determine his ultimate destiny.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Occult fiction

Themes: Immortality, Forbidden love, Pursuit of knowledge, Good vs. evil
