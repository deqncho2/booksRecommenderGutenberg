Synopsis: In "The Law of Psychic Phenomena," Thomson Jay Hudson presents a captivating exploration of hypnotism, spiritism, mental therapeutics, and other intriguing phenomena. Through a systematic study, Hudson delves into the mysterious world of the mind, uncovering the hidden powers and potentials that lie within. With thought-provoking examples and compelling evidence, he challenges conventional beliefs and offers a groundbreaking hypothesis that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of human consciousness. This thought-provoking work is a fascinating journey into the uncharted territories of the mind.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Occult

Themes: Psychic phenomena, Hypnotism, Spirituality, Mental power, Human consciousness
