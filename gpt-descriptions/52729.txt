Synopsis: 
In 'Round the Corner: Being the Life and Death of Francis Christopher Folyat, Bachelor of Divinity, and Father of a Large Family', we follow the intriguing journey of Francis Christopher Folyat, a respected Bachelor of Divinity and devoted father. As we delve into his life, we uncover a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From his humble beginnings to his rise in the church, Folyat's story is filled with love, betrayal, and the consequences of his choices. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into Folyat's complex world, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie just around the corner.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Family, Morality, Redemption
