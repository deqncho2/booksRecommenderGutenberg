Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Jack Sheppard: A Romance', the thrilling adventures of the notorious 18th-century English thief continue. As Jack Sheppard's fame grows, so does the danger surrounding him. Pursued by the relentless Jonathan Wild, a corrupt thief-taker, Jack must navigate a treacherous world of deception, betrayal, and hidden identities. With his life hanging in the balance, Jack's cunning and audacity are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart his enemies and secure his freedom, or will he succumb to the forces working against him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Survival
