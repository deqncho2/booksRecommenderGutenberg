Synopsis: In "Moonbeams from the Larger Lunacy," Stephen Leacock takes readers on a hilarious and satirical journey through the absurdities of modern life. Through a collection of witty and clever essays, Leacock explores the eccentricities of human behavior, the follies of society, and the comical aspects of everyday situations. From the peculiarities of fashion to the absurdities of bureaucracy, Leacock's sharp observations and humorous anecdotes will leave readers laughing out loud and questioning the sanity of the world around them.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Essay Collection

Themes: Absurdity of modern life, Human follies, Society's idiosyncrasies
