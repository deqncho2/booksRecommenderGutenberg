Synopsis: In 'The Princess Pourquoi' by Margaret Pollock Sherwood, a young princess finds herself trapped in a world where her every decision is predetermined. As she embarks on a journey to discover her true identity and break free from the constraints of her royal life, she encounters unexpected allies and uncovers long-hidden secrets that could change everything. With danger lurking at every turn, the princess must summon her courage and determination to challenge the status quo and forge her own path.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Self-discovery
