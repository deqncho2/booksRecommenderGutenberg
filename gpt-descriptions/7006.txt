Synopsis: In "Bonnie Prince Charlie: a Tale of Fontenoy and Culloden," G. A. Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Jacobite Rebellion in 18th century Scotland. The story follows the adventures of young Archie Forbes, a loyal supporter of the exiled Stuart dynasty, as he becomes entangled in the dangerous world of political intrigue, military campaigns, and forbidden love. From the bloody Battle of Fontenoy to the fateful Battle of Culloden, Archie's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test as he fights for his beliefs and the survival of his beloved Bonnie Prince Charlie.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Political Intrigue, Love and Sacrifice
