Synopsis: 
'Byliny Book: Hero Tales of Russia' by Harrison, Marion Chilton is a captivating collection of ancient Russian folk tales that transport readers to a world of legendary heroes, mythical creatures, and epic battles. From the courageous warrior Ilya Muromets to the cunning trickster Ivan Tsarevich, these tales are filled with adventure, magic, and the timeless struggle between good and evil. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the rich tapestry of Russian folklore, where bravery, honor, and the power of love prevail against all odds.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Good vs. Evil, Love and Sacrifice
