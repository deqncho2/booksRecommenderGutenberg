Synopsis: In 'The 56th Division (1st London Territorial Division)', author Dudley Ward takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the renowned 56th Division during World War I. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Ward delves into the division's formation, training, and deployment, offering a gripping account of their heroic battles and the sacrifices made by these brave soldiers. From the trenches of the Western Front to the intense struggles of the Battle of the Somme, this book provides a compelling narrative that sheds light on the untold stories of the men who fought in this remarkable division.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and conflict, Heroism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie
