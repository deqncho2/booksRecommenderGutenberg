Synopsis:
In the small coastal town of Stavanger, Norway, a young and ambitious shipowner named Skipper Worse finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and financial ruin. As he navigates the treacherous waters of business and personal relationships, Skipper Worse must confront his own flaws and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Norway, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes:
Ambition, Love and Betrayal, Morality, Social Class
