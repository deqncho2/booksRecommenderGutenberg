Synopsis: 
In "Border Guard: The Story of the United States Customs Service" by Don Whitehead, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of the men and women who protect America's borders. From the bustling ports of entry to the treacherous smuggling routes, this gripping narrative unveils the untold stories of the dedicated individuals who risk their lives to safeguard the nation. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding accounts, the book sheds light on the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices of these unsung heroes, offering a captivating exploration of the complex world of border security.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, True Crime

Themes: Border security, Law enforcement, National defense, Patriotism
