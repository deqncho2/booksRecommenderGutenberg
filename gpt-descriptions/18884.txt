Synopsis: 
In "Ducks at a Distance: A Waterfowl Identification Guide," Bob Hines takes readers on an immersive journey into the world of waterfowl, providing a comprehensive guide to identifying different species of ducks. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Hines invites readers to explore the intricate details of each duck's appearance, behavior, and habitat. From the majestic Mallard to the elusive Canvasback, this guide is a must-have for bird enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Field Guide, Nature

Themes: Wildlife identification, Birdwatching, Conservation
