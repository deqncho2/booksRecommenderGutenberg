Synopsis: 

'Food Habits of the Thrushes of the United States' takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of thrushes and their dietary preferences. In this meticulously researched work, author F. E. L. Beal delves into the intricate details of what these birds eat, uncovering surprising insights and shedding light on their unique feeding habits. From the secretive Wood Thrush to the melodious Hermit Thrush, each species is examined, revealing a rich tapestry of culinary preferences that will leave readers in awe of these avian creatures. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Ornithology

Themes: Avian behavior, Wildlife conservation, Biodiversity
