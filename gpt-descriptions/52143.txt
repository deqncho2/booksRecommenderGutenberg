Synopsis:
In 'Fairview Boys at Lighthouse Cove; or, Carried out to Sea' by Frederick Gordon, a group of adventurous boys find themselves in a thrilling and dangerous situation during their summer vacation. When a sudden storm hits Lighthouse Cove, the boys are unexpectedly carried out to sea, facing treacherous waves and unknown perils. As they struggle to survive and find their way back to shore, they uncover a mysterious secret that could change their lives forever. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and make it back home safely?

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Survival
