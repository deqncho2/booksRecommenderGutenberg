Synopsis: In 'The Skilful Cook: A Practical Manual of Modern Experience', Mary Harrison takes readers on a culinary journey filled with tantalizing recipes, expert techniques, and invaluable tips. From mastering the art of sauces to creating delectable desserts, this comprehensive guide offers a wealth of knowledge for both novice and experienced cooks alike. With its emphasis on modern cooking methods and innovative flavors, this book is sure to inspire and elevate your culinary skills to new heights.

Genres: Cookbook, Culinary Arts, Non-fiction

Themes: Culinary expertise, Modern cooking techniques, Flavor exploration
