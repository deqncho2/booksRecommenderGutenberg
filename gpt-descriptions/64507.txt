Synopsis: In 'Ricardo's Virus' by Tenn, William, a brilliant scientist named Ricardo creates a groundbreaking virus that has the potential to change the world. However, as the virus spreads, it becomes clear that its effects are far from what Ricardo had anticipated. As chaos ensues, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate the consequences of Ricardo's creation and find a way to save humanity from the brink of destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Ethics of scientific advancements, Unintended consequences, Survival
