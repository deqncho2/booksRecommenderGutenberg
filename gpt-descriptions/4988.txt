Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls at Wild Rose Lodge; Or, The Hermit of Moonlight Falls" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls embark on a thrilling journey to Wild Rose Lodge, a mysterious and secluded location. As they uncover the secrets of Moonlight Falls and encounter a reclusive hermit, the girls find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and friendship. With their resourcefulness and determination, the Outdoor Girls must navigate through the unknown to solve the mysteries surrounding Wild Rose Lodge.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Secrets
