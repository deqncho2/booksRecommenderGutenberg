Synopsis: In 'On the Tree Top' by Clara Doty Bates, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to a mysterious tree in her backyard that seems to hold a secret. As she delves deeper into the tree's enchanting world, she uncovers a hidden realm filled with magical creatures and unexpected challenges. With the help of newfound friends, Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and reveal the true power of friendship.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Friendship, Courage
