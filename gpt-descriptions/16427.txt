Synopsis: In 'The Splendid Folly' by Margaret Pedler, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden desires when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and alluring stranger. As their paths intertwine, Evelyn must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and self-discovery, all while trying to uncover the truth behind her new companion's enigmatic past. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Self-discovery
