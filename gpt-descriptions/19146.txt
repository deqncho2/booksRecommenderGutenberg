Synopsis: In 'The Entailed Hat; Or, Patty Cannon's Times' by Townsend, George Alfred, readers are transported to the early 19th century, where they follow the gripping tale of Patty Cannon, a notorious criminal and slave trader. Set against the backdrop of the Chesapeake Bay region, this historical novel delves into the dark underbelly of society, exploring the intricate web of crime, corruption, and the pursuit of justice. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their own moral compasses. With its vivid descriptions and compelling narrative, 'The Entailed Hat' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous era in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Crime fiction, Mystery

Themes: Crime and punishment, Morality, Power dynamics
