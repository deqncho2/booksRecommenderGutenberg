Synopsis:
In the small town of Cedarville, a once-thriving community has fallen victim to the destructive power of alcohol. When a new tavern opens its doors, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As the bar becomes a hub of vice and despair, a group of friends must confront their own demons and fight to save their loved ones from the clutches of addiction. Through ten gripping nights, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true cost of indulgence is laid bare. 

Genres:
Drama, Social Commentary, Moral Fiction

Themes:
Alcoholism, Redemption, Community, Family
