Synopsis: In 'Hawk's Nest; or, The Last of the Cahoonshees', James M. Allerton takes readers on a captivating journey to the Delaware Valley in 1690. The story follows the Cahoonshee family, the last of their kind, as they navigate the treacherous landscape of colonial America. Amidst the backdrop of historical events, a tale of romance unfolds, intertwining the lives of the Cahoonshees with the fate of the Delaware Valley. As secrets are revealed and loyalties are tested, readers will be drawn into a world of adventure, love, and the struggle for survival.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Love, Survival
