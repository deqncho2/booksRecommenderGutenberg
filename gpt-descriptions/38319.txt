Synopsis: 
'By Desert Ways to Baghdad' takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the vast and treacherous deserts of the Middle East. Louisa Jebb Wilkins, a courageous and adventurous woman, recounts her thrilling experiences as she travels from England to Baghdad, encountering danger, intrigue, and unexpected friendships along the way. From camel caravans to ancient ruins, this captivating memoir offers a unique glimpse into a world few have ever seen.

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural Exchange
