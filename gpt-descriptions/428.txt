Synopsis: In 'Frivolous Cupid' by Hope, Anthony, a mischievous cupid named Eros finds himself in a predicament when he accidentally shoots an arrow at the wrong person, causing chaos and confusion in the lives of unsuspecting individuals. As Eros desperately tries to fix his mistake, he discovers that love can be both unpredictable and transformative, leading to unexpected consequences for everyone involved.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Love, Fate, Redemption
