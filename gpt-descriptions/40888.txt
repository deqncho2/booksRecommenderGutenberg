Synopsis: 
'Jethro Wood, Inventor of the Modern Plow: A Brief Account of His Life, Services, and Trials; Together with Facts Subsequent to his Death, and Incident to His Great Invention' delves into the fascinating life of Jethro Wood, a man whose invention revolutionized agriculture. This captivating account explores Wood's journey as he overcomes numerous challenges and trials to bring his groundbreaking invention to the world. From his humble beginnings to the aftermath of his invention, this book uncovers the untold story of a remarkable individual whose contribution changed the course of farming forever.

Genres: Biography, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Agricultural Revolution
