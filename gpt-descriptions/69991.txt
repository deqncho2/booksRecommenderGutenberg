Synopsis: 
In 'Stock and Stalks: $b A Book for the Dairy Farmer', author James Russel Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of dairy farming. Through vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, Roberts delves into the challenges, triumphs, and secrets of this often overlooked profession. From the delicate art of breeding and raising cows to the intricate science of cultivating nutritious crops, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the daily life of a dairy farmer. As readers follow the protagonist's journey, they will uncover the hidden complexities and unexpected joys that come with tending to the land and its bovine inhabitants. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Agriculture

Themes: Farming, Livestock, Sustainability, Rural Life
