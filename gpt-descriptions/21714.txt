Synopsis: In 'The Red Eric', a young sailor named Eric finds himself on a thrilling adventure when he joins the crew of a mysterious ship. As they set sail across treacherous seas, Eric encounters dangerous pirates, encounters strange creatures, and uncovers a hidden secret that could change his life forever. With action-packed scenes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Courage, Exploration, Friendship
