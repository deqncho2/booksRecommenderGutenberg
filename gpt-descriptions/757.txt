Synopsis: 
In 'Fifty "Bab" Ballads: Much Sound and Little Sense', Gilbert, W. S. takes readers on a whimsical journey through a collection of witty and satirical poems. From tales of love and courtship to humorous observations on society, these ballads are filled with clever wordplay and unexpected twists. Each poem presents a unique story, often with a moral or social commentary hidden beneath the surface. With its delightful blend of humor and intelligence, this collection is sure to captivate readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Satire, Poetry, Humor

Themes: 
Love, Society, Morality
