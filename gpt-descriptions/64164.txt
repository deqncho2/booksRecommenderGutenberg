Synopsis: 

'Excursions in North Wales: A Complete Guide to the Tourist Through That Romantic Country' by Hicklin, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of North Wales. This comprehensive guidebook offers a wealth of information, from historical landmarks and picturesque villages to breathtaking natural wonders. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover hidden gems, discover fascinating legends, and encounter the vibrant culture that permeates this romantic country. Whether planning a trip or simply seeking an armchair adventure, this guidebook is a must-read for anyone captivated by the allure of North Wales.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
