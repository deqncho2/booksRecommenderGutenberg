Synopsis: 
'English Book-Illustration of To-day: Appreciations of the Work of Living English Illustrators, with Lists of Their Books' is a captivating exploration of the remarkable talent and creativity of contemporary English illustrators. Written by R. E. D. Sketchley, this book delves into the world of book illustration, providing insightful appreciations of the work of various living English illustrators. Through vivid descriptions and analysis, Sketchley introduces readers to the diverse styles, techniques, and themes employed by these talented artists. From whimsical children's illustrations to intricate and thought-provoking designs, this book showcases the breadth and depth of English book illustration in the present day.

Genres: Art, Non-fiction, Criticism

Themes: Creativity, Visual storytelling, Contemporary art
