Synopsis: 
'Baseball ABC' is a captivating children's book that takes young readers on an exciting journey through the world of baseball. With vibrant illustrations and clever rhymes, this book introduces the alphabet in a fun and engaging way, using baseball-related terms for each letter. From A for "at bat" to Z for "zone," children will learn about the game while expanding their vocabulary. Whether they are avid baseball fans or new to the sport, this delightful book is sure to entertain and educate young readers.

Genres: Children's Literature, Sports, Education

Themes: Alphabet, Sportsmanship, Learning
