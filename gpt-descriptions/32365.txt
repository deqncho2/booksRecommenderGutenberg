Synopsis: In 'A Boy Knight' by Martin J. Scott, a young boy named Thomas finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he is chosen to become a knight. As he trains and faces numerous challenges, Thomas must navigate the complexities of loyalty, honor, and the true meaning of bravery. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover Thomas's fate.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Honor, Self-discovery
