Synopsis: In 'The Lilac Fairy Book' by Lang, Andrew, readers will embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting fairy tales from various cultures around the world. From magical creatures and brave heroes to treacherous villains and unexpected twists, this anthology offers a delightful blend of adventure, romance, and mystery. Each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and timeless themes, ensuring that readers of all ages will be captivated by the wonders of the fairy tale realm.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Heroism, Love and Sacrifice
