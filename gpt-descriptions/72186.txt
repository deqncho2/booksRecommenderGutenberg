Synopsis: In 'Westy Martin on the Santa Fe Trail' by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, young Westy Martin embarks on an adventurous journey along the historic Santa Fe Trail. As he faces various challenges and encounters fascinating characters, Westy's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With danger lurking at every turn, will Westy be able to navigate the treacherous trail and fulfill his mission?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Friendship, Survival
