Synopsis: In 'Viewpoint' by Randall Garrett, a renowned scientist invents a groundbreaking device that allows people to experience the world through the eyes of others. As the technology gains popularity, it becomes a tool for both good and evil, blurring the lines between empathy and manipulation. When a series of mysterious incidents occur, a young journalist embarks on a thrilling investigation to uncover the truth behind the device and its unexpected consequences. With twists and turns at every corner, 'Viewpoint' explores the power of perception and the ethical dilemmas that arise when boundaries are pushed.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perception, Ethics, Manipulation
