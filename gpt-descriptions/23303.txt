Synopsis: In this enchanting tale, a young girl named Cinderella finds herself trapped in a life of servitude under her wicked stepmother and stepsisters. However, when an invitation to a royal ball arrives, her life takes an unexpected turn. With the help of a magical fairy godmother, Cinderella embarks on a journey that will change her destiny forever. Will she find her happily ever after?

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Transformation, Love, Perseverance
