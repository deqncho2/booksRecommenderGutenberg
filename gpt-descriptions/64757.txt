Synopsis: In 'Paid Off' by Coburn Walt, a down-on-his-luck accountant named Jack finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious ledger that could change his life forever. As he races against time to uncover the truth, Jack becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life hanging in the balance, Jack must navigate a treacherous world of corruption and betrayal, where the price of redemption may be higher than he ever imagined.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Betrayal
