Synopsis: In 'The Tower Rooms' by Mary Grant Bruce, a young girl named Jean finds herself drawn to the mysterious tower rooms of her family's ancestral home. As she explores the hidden secrets within, she uncovers a forgotten past that intertwines with her own present. With each discovery, Jean becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and a haunting legacy that refuses to be forgotten. As she delves deeper into the tower rooms, Jean must confront her own fears and make choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Identity, Coming of age
