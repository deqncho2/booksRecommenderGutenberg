Synopsis:
In 'Reynolds' by Bensusan, S. L., a mysterious and enigmatic man named Reynolds arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that Reynolds possesses an extraordinary ability that challenges their perceptions of reality. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes:
Identity, Perception, Power
