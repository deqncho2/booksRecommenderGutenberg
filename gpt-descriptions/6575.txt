Synopsis: In 'The Purple Parasol' by George Barr McCutcheon, a young woman named Betty is unexpectedly swept away on a thrilling adventure when she receives a mysterious purple parasol. As she embarks on a journey filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists, Betty discovers hidden secrets and uncovers her own strength and resilience. Will she be able to navigate the challenges that lie ahead and find her way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Love, Secrets
