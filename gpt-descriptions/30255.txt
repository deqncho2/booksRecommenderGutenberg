Synopsis: In 'The Skull' by Philip K. Dick, a man named Joe Fernwright wakes up one day to find a mysterious and powerful object implanted in his head. As he tries to uncover the truth behind this strange occurrence, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy, danger, and mind-bending revelations. With his sanity at stake, Joe must navigate a world where reality and perception blur, and where the true nature of existence hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
