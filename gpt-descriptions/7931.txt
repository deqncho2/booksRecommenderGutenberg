Synopsis: In 'All-Wool Morrison: Time -- Today, Place -- the United States, Period of Action -- Twenty-four Hours' by Day, Holman, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the events of a single day in the United States. The story revolves around the enigmatic character of All-Wool Morrison, whose actions and decisions have far-reaching consequences. As the clock ticks, Morrison finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected encounters. With each passing hour, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Morrison's actions and the ultimate outcome of this fast-paced narrative.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Morality, Consequences of Actions
