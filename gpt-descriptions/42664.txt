Synopsis: In "Gods of the North," Conan the Cimmerian finds himself embroiled in a treacherous journey through frozen lands and ancient ruins. As he battles against formidable foes and uncovers long-lost secrets, Conan's strength, cunning, and indomitable spirit are put to the ultimate test. With his life hanging in the balance, Conan must confront the gods of the north and face the consequences of his actions.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes: Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Power and Corruption
