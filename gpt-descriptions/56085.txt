Synopsis: In "The Silver Princess in Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to help a young princess reclaim her stolen kingdom. Along the way, they encounter enchanting creatures, magical lands, and face dangerous challenges that test their courage and loyalty. Will they succeed in their quest and restore peace to the land of Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Good vs. Evil
