Synopsis: In 'A Husband by Proxy' by Jack Steele, a young woman named Emily finds herself in a desperate situation when her father arranges a marriage for her with a man she has never met. Determined to take control of her own destiny, Emily devises a plan to hire a proxy husband to stand in for her at the wedding. As the charade unfolds, unexpected emotions and complications arise, leading Emily on a journey of self-discovery and true love.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and marriage, Independence and self-determination
