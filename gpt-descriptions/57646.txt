Synopsis: 
In 'Through Unknown Tibet', M. S. Wellby takes readers on a thrilling and perilous journey through the mysterious and unexplored lands of Tibet. As an intrepid explorer, Wellby recounts his extraordinary adventures, encountering treacherous terrains, hostile tribes, and unexpected encounters with the supernatural. From traversing treacherous mountain passes to delving into the secrets of ancient monasteries, this gripping travelogue offers a captivating glimpse into the hidden wonders and dangers of Tibet.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Survival
