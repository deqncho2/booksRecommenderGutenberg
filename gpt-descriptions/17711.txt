Synopsis: 
'Hindustani Lyrics' is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich tapestry of Indian culture, history, and emotions. From the bustling streets of Mumbai to the serene banks of the Ganges, each poem paints a vivid picture of the diverse landscapes and vibrant characters that inhabit the Indian subcontinent. With lyrical verses that evoke a range of emotions, this collection explores themes of love, longing, spirituality, and the timeless beauty of India. 

Genres: Poetry, Cultural, Historical

Themes: Love, Longing, Spirituality, Indian Culture
