Synopsis: In "Carlyon Sahib: A Drama in Four Acts" by Murray, Gilbert, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Indian village, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of Carlyon Sahib's intentions becomes increasingly unclear. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this drama takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of human nature and the clash of cultures.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Colonialism, Betrayal
