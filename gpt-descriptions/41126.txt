Synopsis: In 'The Wolf Cub: A Novel of Spain', the Casey brothers take readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous landscape of Spain during the Spanish Civil War. Amidst the chaos and danger, a young boy named Miguel finds himself caught up in a web of secrets and espionage. As he navigates through the treacherous world of spies and revolutionaries, Miguel must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping novel explores the power of loyalty, the complexities of war, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, War, Espionage
