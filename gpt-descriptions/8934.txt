Synopsis: In the enchanting world of medieval England, two young lovers find themselves entangled in a web of forbidden love and treacherous secrets. As they navigate the lush forests and perilous courtly intrigues, their passion and loyalty are put to the ultimate test. Will their love conquer all, or will they be torn apart by the forces that seek to keep them apart?

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden love, Loyalty, Betrayal, Nature, Courtly intrigue
