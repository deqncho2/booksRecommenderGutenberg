Synopsis: In "Bethink Yourselves!", Tolstoy delivers a thought-provoking and introspective essay that challenges the reader to reflect on the consequences of their actions. Through a series of powerful anecdotes and philosophical musings, Tolstoy explores the destructive nature of war and the importance of personal responsibility. This compelling work urges readers to reconsider their role in society and the impact they have on the world around them.

Genres: Essay, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Personal responsibility, Consequences of actions, War, Society
