Synopsis: In 'A King's Comrade: A Story of Old Hereford', Charles W. Whistler takes us on a captivating journey through medieval England, where a young knight finds himself entangled in a web of treachery, loyalty, and forbidden love. As he navigates the dangerous political landscape of Hereford, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those he holds dear. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel immerses readers in a world of chivalry, intrigue, and the enduring bonds of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Love, Power
