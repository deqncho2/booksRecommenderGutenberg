Synopsis: 
In 'The School of Recreation', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the world of 17th-century leisure activities. From the thrill of hunting and racing to the art of hawking and the elegance of riding, this book serves as a comprehensive guide to the most ingenious exercises of the time. With detailed instructions and anecdotes, the author immerses the reader in the exhilarating world of cock-fighting, fowling, and fishing, revealing the secrets and techniques behind each pursuit. Whether you are a seasoned sportsman or simply curious about the pastimes of the era, this book is sure to captivate and entertain.

Genres: 
Sports, Instructional, Historical

Themes: 
Leisure, Skill, Tradition
