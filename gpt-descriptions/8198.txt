Synopsis: In 'The Fourth Watch' by Cody, H. A., a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and uncertainty. As the local sheriff investigates the unsettling events, he uncovers a web of secrets and dark forces that threaten to consume the town. With time running out, he must confront his own demons and make a choice that could determine the fate of the entire community.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Redemption
