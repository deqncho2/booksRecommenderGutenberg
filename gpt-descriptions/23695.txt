Synopsis: In 'The Little Gray Lady: 1909' by Smith, Francis Hopkinson, a mysterious woman with a captivating presence enters the lives of a group of friends in a small New England town. As they become enthralled by her enigmatic charm, they soon discover that she holds a secret that could change their lives forever. With elements of suspense and intrigue, this story takes readers on a journey of unexpected twists and turns, leaving them questioning the true nature of the little gray lady.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Intrigue
