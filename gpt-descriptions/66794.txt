Synopsis: In 'The Heritage' by Sydney C. Grier, a young woman named Amelia discovers a hidden family secret that takes her on a thrilling journey through time and across continents. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Amelia uncovers a legacy filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets that could change her life forever. As Amelia races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Legacy, Time travel
