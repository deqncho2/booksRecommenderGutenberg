Synopsis: In the small town of Zenith, young and ambitious protagonist, Clark, finds himself torn between his desire for success and his longing for true love. As he navigates the complexities of early 20th-century American society, Clark becomes entangled in a web of relationships, secrets, and moral dilemmas. From the allure of a captivating femme fatale to the allure of societal expectations, Clark's journey explores the depths of human desires and the consequences of pursuing them.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, moral dilemmas
