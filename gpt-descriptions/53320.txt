Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boys in the Army; or, Ned, Bob and Jerry as Volunteers' by Clarence Young, three adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey as they join the army. Ned, Bob, and Jerry, known for their love of motor vehicles, are eager to put their skills to use in service to their country. Little do they know that their mechanical expertise will prove invaluable in a series of unexpected and dangerous situations. As they navigate through the challenges of military life, the Motor Boys must rely on their resourcefulness, bravery, and unwavering friendship to overcome the obstacles that come their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Resourcefulness
