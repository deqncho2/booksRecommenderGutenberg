Synopsis: In 'The Grey Wave' by A. Hamilton Gibbs, a retired professor embarks on a journey that takes him from the comfort of his home to the unexplored depths of his own mind. As he grapples with the challenges of aging and the loss of his beloved wife, he discovers a hidden world of memories, regrets, and forgotten dreams. With each step, he unravels the mysteries of his past and finds unexpected connections that transcend time and space. This poignant and introspective tale explores the profound impact of aging on identity, the power of memory, and the enduring human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological

Themes: Aging, Memory, Loss, Identity, Reflection
