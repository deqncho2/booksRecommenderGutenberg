Synopsis: In 'The Cake and Biscuit Book' by Elizabeth Douglas, a talented baker named Emma embarks on a mouthwatering journey to create the most delectable cakes and biscuits. As she experiments with unique flavors and innovative techniques, Emma discovers that her creations have the power to bring joy, heal wounds, and even mend broken relationships. Along the way, she encounters unexpected challenges and finds inspiration in the most unlikely places. With each recipe, Emma's passion for baking grows, and she learns that the true magic lies not only in the taste of her treats but also in the love and care she puts into every bite.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Culinary

Themes: Love, Healing, Self-discovery
