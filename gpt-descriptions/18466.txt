Synopsis:
In "The Æneid of Virgil, Translated into English Verse," the legendary poet Virgil takes readers on an epic journey through the trials and triumphs of the Trojan hero, Æneas. From the ruins of Troy to the shores of Italy, Æneas faces formidable foes, encounters captivating characters, and navigates the unpredictable realm of the gods. This masterful translation brings to life the timeless tale of love, loss, destiny, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres:
Epic Poetry, Classical Literature, Mythology

Themes:
Fate and Destiny, Heroism, Love and Loss, Divine Intervention
