Synopsis: In 'Snow-shoes and Sledges: A Sequel to "The Fur-Seal's Tooth,"' Munroe, Kirk takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the frozen wilderness of the Arctic. Following the thrilling events of the previous book, this sequel delves deeper into the lives of the courageous characters as they navigate treacherous terrains, encounter indigenous cultures, and face unexpected challenges. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonists must rely on their wit, resilience, and the bonds they have formed to survive the harsh conditions of the Arctic. Will they conquer the unforgiving landscape and emerge victorious, or will the Arctic claim them as its own?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience, Cultural Encounters
