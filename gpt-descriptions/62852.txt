Synopsis: In 'How to Select Cows: or, The Guenon system simplified, explained, and practically applied', Willis P. Hazard takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of cattle selection. With a unique and simplified approach, Hazard unveils the secrets behind the Guenon system, providing practical advice and insights for choosing the perfect cows. From understanding the intricacies of breed characteristics to evaluating physical attributes, this book offers a comprehensive guide for both novice and experienced farmers. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover a wealth of knowledge that will revolutionize their understanding of cattle selection and ultimately lead to more successful farming practices.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Animal Husbandry

Themes: Cattle selection, Farming techniques, Livestock management
