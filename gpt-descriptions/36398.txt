Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a spirited and adventurous schoolgirl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey to Silver Ranch, a remote and rugged cowboy territory. Leaving behind the comforts of her school life, Ruth must navigate the challenges of the wild west, where danger lurks at every turn. As she immerses herself in the world of cowboys and ranch life, Ruth discovers unexpected friendships, uncovers hidden secrets, and faces unexpected obstacles that test her courage and resilience. Will Ruth be able to adapt to this new way of life and prove herself among the cowboys?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Adaptation
