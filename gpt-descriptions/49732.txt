Synopsis: 
In "Florence Nightingale to Her Nurses," the renowned nurse and social reformer, Florence Nightingale, shares her wisdom and guidance with the nurses and probationers of the Nightingale School at St. Thomas's Hospital. Through a selection of her addresses, Nightingale imparts invaluable lessons on nursing, patient care, and the importance of compassion. With her eloquent and passionate words, she inspires her audience to embrace their calling and strive for excellence in their profession. This collection serves as a testament to Nightingale's dedication to nursing and her unwavering commitment to improving healthcare practices.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Historical

Themes: Nursing, Patient Care, Compassion, Professionalism
