Synopsis: In 'Quest's End' by Basil Wells, a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey to uncover a long-lost treasure hidden deep within a mysterious and treacherous land. As they face numerous challenges and encounter strange creatures, their loyalty and determination are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Perseverance
