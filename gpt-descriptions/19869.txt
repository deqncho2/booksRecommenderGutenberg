Synopsis: 
'While Caroline Was Growing' follows the journey of Caroline, a young girl who navigates the complexities of adolescence and the challenges of growing up in a changing world. Set in the early 20th century, the story takes readers on a captivating exploration of Caroline's experiences, friendships, and the lessons she learns along the way. As she grapples with societal expectations, family dynamics, and her own personal growth, Caroline's coming-of-age tale is filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected twists, and poignant reflections on the joys and struggles of youth.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Adolescence, Identity, Family dynamics, Social expectations
