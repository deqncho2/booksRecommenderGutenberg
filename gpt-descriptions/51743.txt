Synopsis: In 'Is Spiritualism Based on Fraud?: The Evidence Given by Sir A.C. Doyle and Others Drastically Examined', Joseph McCabe delves into the world of spiritualism, challenging the claims made by renowned figures such as Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. With meticulous research and a critical eye, McCabe investigates the alleged supernatural phenomena, exposing the potential fraud behind it all. As he presents his findings, the reader is taken on a captivating journey that questions the authenticity of spiritualist practices and the credibility of those who advocate for them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Investigative journalism, Skepticism

Themes: Spiritualism, Fraud, Skepticism, Paranormal phenomena
