Synopsis: 
In 'Three Lancashire Plays: The Game; The Northerners; Zack', Brighouse takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of ordinary people in Lancashire. In "The Game," we witness the intense rivalry between two football teams and the unexpected consequences it brings. "The Northerners" explores the clash between tradition and progress as a family grapples with the changing times. Lastly, "Zack" delves into the complexities of love and loyalty as a young man finds himself torn between his heart and his duty. Each play presents a unique and compelling story that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the fate of these intriguing characters.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Social Realism
Themes: Rivalry, Tradition vs. Progress, Love and Loyalty
