Synopsis: In 'Captain Lucy in the Home Sector' by Havard, Aline, follow the thrilling journey of Captain Lucy as she navigates through a mysterious and dangerous sector of space. With her crew by her side, she must uncover the secrets hidden within the Home Sector and confront the challenges that lie ahead. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, Captain Lucy's determination and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test. Prepare for a gripping adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Loyalty
