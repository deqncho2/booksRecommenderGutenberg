Synopsis: 
In 'Seeing the West: Suggestions for the Westbound Traveller', author K. E. M. Dumbell takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vast and captivating landscapes of the American West. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Dumbell offers invaluable advice and recommendations for those seeking to explore the wonders of the region. From the majestic Rocky Mountains to the awe-inspiring canyons of Arizona, this guidebook is a treasure trove of hidden gems and off-the-beaten-path destinations. Whether you're an adventurous hiker, a history enthusiast, or simply a lover of natural beauty, 'Seeing the West' is the ultimate companion for discovering the untamed wilderness and rich cultural heritage of the American West.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural Heritage
