Synopsis: In 'The Men in the Walls' by William Tenn, a seemingly ordinary family living in a suburban neighborhood discovers a shocking secret hidden within the walls of their home. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover a world of hidden passages, strange creatures, and dark secrets that threaten to consume them. With each revelation, the family's sanity and relationships are put to the ultimate test, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and question the very nature of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Perception, Family Dynamics, Secrets
