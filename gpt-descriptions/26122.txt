Synopsis: In "Five Little Peppers at School" by Margaret Sidney, the lovable Pepper family embarks on a new adventure as the children start attending school. Follow their journey as they navigate the challenges of education, make new friends, and encounter unexpected surprises along the way. With their trademark charm and resilience, the Peppers prove that learning extends far beyond the classroom walls. 

Genres: Children's literature, Family, Adventure

Themes: Education, Friendship, Resilience
