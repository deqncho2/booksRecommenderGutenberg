Synopsis: In "Paul Rundel: A Novel," Will N. Harben takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Paul Rundel, a young man with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. Set against the backdrop of a small Southern town, the story unfolds as Paul navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal identity. As secrets are revealed and unexpected alliances are formed, Paul's journey becomes a gripping exploration of self-discovery and the power of redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Southern Gothic, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption, Love, Family
