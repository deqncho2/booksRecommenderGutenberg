Synopsis: 
In 'A Canadian Calendar: XII Lyrics' by Francis Sherman, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and seasons of Canada. Through twelve beautifully crafted lyrics, the author paints vivid images of the country's natural beauty, from the icy landscapes of winter to the vibrant colors of autumn. Each lyric captures a unique moment in time, evoking a sense of wonder and appreciation for the Canadian landscape. With lyrical prose and a keen eye for detail, Sherman invites readers to experience the changing seasons and discover the hidden treasures of Canada.

Genres: Poetry, Nature Writing

Themes: Canadian identity, Nature appreciation, Seasons
