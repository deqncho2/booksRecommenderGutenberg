Synopsis: 
In 'The Young Dragoon: Every Day Life of a Soldier', we follow the gripping journey of a young soldier as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of military life. From the excitement of his first battle to the heart-wrenching moments of loss and camaraderie, this captivating narrative takes readers on an immersive exploration of the soldier's world. With vivid descriptions and a compelling narrative voice, Drayson paints a vivid picture of the soldier's daily life, revealing the sacrifices, the bonds formed, and the resilience required to survive in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Duty and Honor, Friendship and Brotherhood, Resilience and Survival
