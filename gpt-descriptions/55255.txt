Synopsis: In 'Anecdotes of Big Cats and Other Beasts' by Wilson, David Alec, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness as the author shares captivating stories of encounters with majestic big cats and other fascinating creatures. From heart-pounding encounters with lions and tigers to heartwarming tales of animal resilience, this collection of anecdotes will transport readers to the wild and leave them in awe of the beauty and power of these magnificent beasts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Nature

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Human-animal interaction, Natural beauty
