Synopsis: In 'The Thread of Gold' by Arthur Christopher Benson, a young man named Richard embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Drawn to a mysterious ancient artifact, Richard finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and hidden truths that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the thread of gold, Richard must confront his own fears and confront the ultimate question of what truly matters in life.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Spiritual Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Spirituality, Quest for Truth
