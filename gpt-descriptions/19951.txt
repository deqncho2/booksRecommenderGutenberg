Synopsis: In 'Ted Strong in Montana: Or, With Lariat and Spur', follow the thrilling adventures of young Ted Strong as he embarks on a journey to the untamed wilderness of Montana. Faced with dangerous outlaws, treacherous landscapes, and unexpected friendships, Ted must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to navigate the challenges that lie ahead. With lariat and spur in hand, he must prove himself in the face of adversity and discover the true meaning of bravery.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
