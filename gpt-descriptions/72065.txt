Synopsis: In "The Little Merchant," follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Tommy as he embarks on a remarkable adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. Tommy's entrepreneurial spirit leads him to discover a hidden talent for business, and he soon finds himself caught up in a world of trade and commerce. As he navigates through various challenges and encounters colorful characters, Tommy learns valuable lessons about determination, friendship, and the true meaning of success. Will his small-town dreams lead him to triumph or teach him a different kind of victory altogether?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Friendship, Self-discovery
