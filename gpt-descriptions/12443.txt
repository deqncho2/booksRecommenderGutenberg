Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of the jute industry, where the journey from seed to finished cloth unveils a captivating tale of innovation, perseverance, and global impact. In this meticulously researched book, Kilgour and Woodhouse take readers on a riveting exploration of the jute industry, tracing its origins, delving into the intricate processes involved in its production, and uncovering the profound social and economic implications it has had throughout history. From the humble jute seed to the luxurious finished cloth, this book unveils the hidden stories behind every thread, weaving together a narrative that will leave readers in awe of the remarkable journey that transforms a simple plant into a vital global commodity.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Industry

Themes: Innovation, Globalization, Economic Impact, Social History
