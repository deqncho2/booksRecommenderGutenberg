Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious newcomer arrives. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of peculiar events, they must uncover the truth behind the newcomer's arrival and the bizarre happenings that follow. With a mix of humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, "Decidedly Odd" takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Comedy

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Supernatural elements
