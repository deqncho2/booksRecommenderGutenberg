Synopsis: In the secluded castle of Greifenstein, a dark secret lies hidden within its ancient walls. As the young Countess Hilda returns to her ancestral home, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, the secrets of Greifenstein begin to unravel, leading Hilda on a perilous journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. As the truth behind the castle's dark past is revealed, Hilda must confront her own desires and make a choice that will forever change her life.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
