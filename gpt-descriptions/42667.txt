Synopsis: In 'The Deserted City' by Francis Sherman, a mysterious and eerie atmosphere surrounds a once-thriving city that now lies abandoned. As a group of explorers ventures into the heart of the city, they uncover dark secrets and encounter unexplainable phenomena that challenge their understanding of reality. With each step, the tension builds, and the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. As they delve deeper into the city's past, they must confront their own fears and confront the haunting presence that lingers within its empty streets.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Fear of the supernatural, Confronting the past
