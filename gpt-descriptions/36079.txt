Synopsis: In 'Love and Hatred' by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a seemingly idyllic marriage is shattered when a mysterious stranger enters the lives of a young couple. As secrets are unveiled and tensions rise, the boundaries between love and hatred become blurred, leading to a gripping tale of betrayal and revenge. With unexpected twists and a haunting atmosphere, this psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Love and betrayal, Secrets and deception
