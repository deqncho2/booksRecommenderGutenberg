Synopsis: In "Free and Impartial Thoughts," Richard Finch fearlessly challenges the widely accepted doctrines of election, reprobation, and original sin. With a humble and thought-provoking approach, Finch addresses all those who believe and profess these doctrines, inviting them to question and explore their beliefs. Through a series of insightful arguments and reflections, he delves into the sovereignty of God and its implications on human salvation. This thought-provoking work is sure to captivate readers and encourage them to reevaluate their understanding of these fundamental theological concepts.

Genres: Theology, Philosophy, Religious Studies

Themes: Sovereignty of God, Election, Reprobation, Original Sin, Salvation, Religious Beliefs
