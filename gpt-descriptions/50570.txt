Synopsis: In "Custis-Lee Mansion: The Robert E. Lee Memorial, Virginia," Nelligan, Murray H. takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical significance and hidden stories of the iconic Custis-Lee Mansion. From its grand architecture to its role in the Civil War, this book unveils the secrets and untold tales that have shaped this remarkable landmark. As readers delve into the mansion's past, they will be enthralled by the intriguing narratives and surprising revelations that lie within its walls.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Biography

Themes: History, Legacy, Preservation
