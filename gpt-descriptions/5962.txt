Synopsis: 
In 'Oh, Money! Money! A Novel' by Eleanor H. Porter, we follow the life of young Rosemary, a talented and ambitious writer who dreams of making it big in the literary world. However, her dreams are shattered when she finds herself penniless and alone after her father's sudden death. Determined to overcome her circumstances, Rosemary embarks on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, as she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of wealth. Will Rosemary be able to find true happiness and success, or will she be forever trapped in the clutches of money?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Wealth, Love, Friendship
