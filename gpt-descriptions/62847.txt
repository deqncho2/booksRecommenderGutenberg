Synopsis: 
'Beautiful, Beautiful Afghans' by American Thread Company is a captivating collection of crochet patterns that will transport you to the vibrant and rich culture of Afghanistan. From intricate geometric designs to delicate floral motifs, this book showcases a stunning array of afghans that will inspire both beginners and experienced crocheters alike. Each pattern is accompanied by detailed instructions and beautiful full-color photographs, making it easy to create your own masterpiece. Whether you're looking to add a touch of elegance to your home or seeking a meaningful gift for a loved one, this book is a must-have for anyone with a passion for crochet and a love for Afghan craftsmanship.

Genres: Crafts, Hobbies, Non-fiction

Themes: Afghan culture, Crochet, Home decor
