Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Wilkie Collins' is a comprehensive collection of the works of renowned author Wilkie Collins. This index provides a convenient and organized way to explore the vast literary contributions of Collins, including his famous novels such as "The Woman in White" and "The Moonstone." From gripping mysteries to intricate character studies, this index offers a glimpse into the captivating world of one of the most influential writers of the Victorian era.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Sensation fiction, Historical fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Identity and deception, Love and betrayal, Social class and inequality, Madness and obsession
