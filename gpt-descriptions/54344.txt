Synopsis: 
'Bristol: A Sketch Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant city of Bristol, England. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of artist Dorothy Woollard, the book showcases the city's iconic landmarks, bustling streets, and hidden corners. From the historic Clifton Suspension Bridge to the colorful street art of Stokes Croft, each sketch captures the essence and charm of Bristol, inviting readers to explore its rich history and diverse culture. With accompanying anecdotes and insights, this sketch book offers a unique perspective on one of England's most captivating cities.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City exploration, Cultural diversity, Architectural beauty
