Synopsis: In 'Kalevala: the Epic Poem of Finland - Volume 02', the captivating saga of ancient Finland continues with tales of heroes, sorcery, and mythical creatures. Join the brave Väinämöinen, the skilled Lemminkäinen, and the fearless Ilmarinen as they embark on extraordinary quests, facing treacherous challenges and encountering enchanting beings. From epic battles to forbidden love, this volume delves deeper into the rich folklore and legends of Finland, weaving a tapestry of adventure and magic that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Epic poetry, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Magic, Love and Romance, Mythical Creatures, Finnish Folklore
