Synopsis: 
In 'Just Patty' by Jean Webster, readers are introduced to Patty Wyatt, a spirited and mischievous young girl who finds herself in a boarding school filled with rules and expectations. As Patty navigates the ups and downs of school life, she forms friendships, gets into amusing scrapes, and discovers the power of her own individuality. With her quick wit and infectious energy, Patty's adventures will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what she will do next.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Humor

Themes: 
Individuality, Friendship, Rebellion
