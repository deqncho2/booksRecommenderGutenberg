Synopsis: In 'Captain Midas' by Alfred Coppel, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Midas discovers a groundbreaking formula that can turn any material into gold. As news of his invention spreads, Midas becomes the target of both admiration and greed. With the power to transform the world's economy, he must navigate the treacherous waters of wealth, power, and the consequences of his creation. This thrilling tale explores the moral dilemmas and unforeseen consequences that arise when one man possesses the ability to change the very fabric of society.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Greed, Morality, Power
