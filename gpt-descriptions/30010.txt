Synopsis: In 'Trees Are Where You Find Them' by Arthur Dekker Savage, a young botanist embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of a mysterious tree species hidden deep within the Amazon rainforest. As she delves deeper into the lush wilderness, she encounters unexpected dangers, ancient legends, and a hidden world teeming with both beauty and peril. With time running out, she must navigate treacherous terrain and confront her own fears to unlock the truth that lies within the trees.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Nature's Secrets, Overcoming Challenges
