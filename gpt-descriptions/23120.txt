Synopsis: In 'The King's Daughters' by Emily Sarah Holt, a young woman named Eleanor finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in the court of King Charles II. As she navigates the treacherous world of royalty, Eleanor must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. With secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, this captivating historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Sacrifice
