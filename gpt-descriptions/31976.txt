Synopsis: In 'Derelict' by Alan Edward Nourse, a group of astronauts embarks on a dangerous mission to investigate a mysterious derelict spaceship floating in space. As they explore the eerie vessel, they uncover shocking secrets that challenge their understanding of the universe and the very nature of humanity. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable choices to survive.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality, Survival
