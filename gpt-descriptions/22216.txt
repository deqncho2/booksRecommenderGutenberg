Synopsis: In 'Project Mastodon' by Clifford D. Simak, a group of scientists accidentally stumble upon a time machine and decide to embark on an extraordinary adventure. They travel back in time to the prehistoric era, where they encounter ancient creatures and face unexpected challenges. As they navigate this unfamiliar world, they must grapple with the ethical implications of their actions and confront the limits of human knowledge and understanding. With each passing moment, the scientists find themselves questioning the very nature of existence and the consequences of tampering with the fabric of time.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Ethics, Human Curiosity, Existentialism
