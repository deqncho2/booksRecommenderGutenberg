Synopsis: In 'Our War with Spain for Cuba's Freedom', White, Trumbull takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous conflict that unfolded between the United States and Spain over the fate of Cuba. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors delve into the political intrigue, military strategies, and personal sacrifices that shaped this pivotal moment in history. As the narrative unfolds, readers will witness the bravery and resilience of soldiers, the struggles of civilians caught in the crossfire, and the complex dynamics of international relations. This captivating account sheds light on a war that not only transformed the destiny of Cuba but also had far-reaching implications for the United States and the world.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Political history

Themes: Freedom and independence, International conflict, Human resilience
