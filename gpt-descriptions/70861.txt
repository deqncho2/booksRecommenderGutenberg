Synopsis: In "The Doings of Doris" by Agnes Giberne, readers are introduced to the adventurous and spirited Doris, a young girl who finds herself caught up in a series of unexpected events. From mysterious encounters to thrilling escapades, Doris embarks on a journey that will test her courage and resilience. As she navigates through challenges and uncovers hidden secrets, Doris's determination and quick thinking become her greatest assets. With each twist and turn, the story keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Doris's captivating exploits.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Discovery
