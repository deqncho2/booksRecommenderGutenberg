Synopsis: In 'Laughing Last' by Jane Abbott, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the tension builds and the truth slowly emerges, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a gripping tale of suspense and psychological intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Identity, Redemption
