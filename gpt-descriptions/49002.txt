Synopsis: In "The Border Boys with the Texas Rangers" by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey alongside the legendary Texas Rangers. As they navigate the treacherous borderlands, they encounter dangerous outlaws, uncover hidden secrets, and face life-or-death situations that test their courage and loyalty. With their lives on the line, the Border Boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to bring justice to the lawless frontier.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Justice, Coming of Age
