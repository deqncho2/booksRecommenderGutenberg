Synopsis: In 'The Tour: A Story of Ancient Egypt' by Couperus, Louis, embark on a captivating journey through the mystical land of ancient Egypt. Follow a group of tourists as they explore the awe-inspiring pyramids, encounter enigmatic pharaohs, and unravel the secrets of this ancient civilization. As the tourists delve deeper into the mysteries of Egypt, they find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the timeless struggle between power and morality. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, 'The Tour' immerses readers in a world of intrigue and adventure, where the past comes alive and the present is forever changed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and morality, Love and betrayal
