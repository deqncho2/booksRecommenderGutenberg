Synopsis: In 'The Freelands' by John Galsworthy, a family's idyllic country life is disrupted when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a secret that threatens to unravel their peaceful existence. As the family grapples with the consequences of this revelation, they are forced to confront their own desires, loyalties, and the true meaning of freedom.

Genres: Drama, Family Saga, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Family Dynamics, Secrets, Social Class
