Synopsis: In 'A Dialogue Between Dean Swift and Tho. Prior, Esq.: In the Isles of St. Patrick's Church, Dublin, On that; Memorable Day, October 9th, 1753', two prominent figures engage in a captivating conversation that takes place in the intriguing setting of St. Patrick's Church. As the dialogue unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and unexpected revelations come to light. This thought-provoking exchange between Dean Swift and Tho. Prior, Esq. will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind their encounter.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Secrets and revelations, Power dynamics, Historical context
