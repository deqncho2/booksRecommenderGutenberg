Synopsis: 
In 'Assassin' by Bone, Jesse F., a skilled and enigmatic assassin finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he is assigned a seemingly impossible target. As he delves deeper into the mission, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and secrets that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous world of shadows and assassins, where trust is a luxury he cannot afford. Will he be able to outwit his enemies and complete his mission, or will he become the hunted instead of the hunter?

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Survival
