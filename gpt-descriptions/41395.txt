Synopsis: 
In 'Elementary Zoology, Second Edition', author Vernon L. Kellogg takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of animals. From the smallest insects to the largest mammals, this comprehensive guide provides a wealth of knowledge about the diverse creatures that inhabit our planet. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Kellogg explores the unique characteristics, behaviors, and habitats of various animal species, offering readers a deeper understanding and appreciation for the natural world around them. Whether you're a curious student, an aspiring biologist, or simply an animal lover, this book is sure to ignite your curiosity and leave you with a newfound admiration for the wonders of the animal kingdom.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Biodiversity, Animal Behavior, Nature Exploration
