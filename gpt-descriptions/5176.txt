Synopsis: In 'Corpus of a Siam Mosquito' by Sills, Steven David Justin, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young scientist embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of a rare species of mosquito found in the depths of the Thai rainforest. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected discoveries that challenge his understanding of the world and his own existence. With each revelation, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leaving the reader questioning what is truly possible.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Identity and self-discovery, Nature and its mysteries
