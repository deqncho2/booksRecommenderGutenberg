Synopsis: 
'Twenty Six Choice Poetical Extracts' is a captivating collection of carefully selected poems from renowned authors, each accompanied by a stunning vignette that enhances the essence of the subject. This beautifully engraved work offers readers a delightful journey through a diverse range of poetic expressions, inviting them to explore the depths of human emotions, nature's wonders, and the power of imagination. From heartfelt verses to vivid descriptions, this anthology promises to captivate and inspire readers with its exquisite blend of words and art.

Genres: Poetry, Art, Anthology
Themes: Emotions, Nature, Imagination
