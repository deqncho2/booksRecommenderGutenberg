Synopsis: 
In 'Sabbath-Breaking on the Canal: A Poem' by Clutton, John, a seemingly ordinary day on the canal takes an unexpected turn when a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious event. As they delve deeper into the unfolding mystery, they find themselves questioning their beliefs and facing the consequences of their actions. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, this poem takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that explores the complexities of human nature and the power of choice.

Genres: Poetry, Mystery, Philosophical

Themes: Morality, Religion, Consequences
