Synopsis: 
In "The American Postal Service: History of the Postal Service from the Earliest Times," Louis Melius takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating evolution of the American postal system. From its humble beginnings to its pivotal role in shaping the nation, this book unveils the untold stories of the men and women who dedicated their lives to delivering mail across vast distances and challenging terrains. Through vivid anecdotes and historical accounts, Melius paints a vivid picture of the triumphs, struggles, and innovations that have shaped the American Postal Service into what it is today.

Genres: Non-fiction, History

Themes: Communication, Infrastructure, Progress
