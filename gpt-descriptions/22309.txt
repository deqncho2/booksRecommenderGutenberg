Synopsis: In "An American Robinson Crusoe for American Boys and Girls," young readers are taken on an exciting adventure as they follow the journey of a courageous young boy named Jack. When Jack finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck, he must rely on his resourcefulness, ingenuity, and determination to survive. From building shelter and finding food to encountering wild animals and exploring the mysteries of the island, Jack's resilience is put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges and find a way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Children's Literature

Themes: Resilience, Resourcefulness, Exploration
