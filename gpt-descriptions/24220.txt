Synopsis: In 'The Tyranny of the Dark' by Garland, Hamlin, a brilliant scientist embarks on a daring experiment to conquer darkness itself. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes consumed by an insatiable desire for knowledge, leading him down a treacherous path that challenges his sanity and morality. With each step, he uncovers the hidden truths of the universe, but at a great cost. Will he triumph over the darkness or succumb to its overwhelming power?

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: The pursuit of knowledge, the fine line between genius and madness, the consequences of tampering with nature
