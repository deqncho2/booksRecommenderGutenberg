Synopsis: In 'The River Motor Boat Boys on the Amazon; Or, The Secret of Cloud Island' by Gordon, Harry, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey down the Amazon River. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter mysterious creatures, they stumble upon a hidden island with a secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma of Cloud Island.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
