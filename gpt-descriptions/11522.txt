Synopsis: In 'Deadly Pollen' by Oliver Stephen, a small town is struck by a mysterious illness that leaves its residents fighting for their lives. As the death toll rises, a young scientist and a determined journalist join forces to uncover the truth behind the deadly pollen that seems to be the cause. Racing against time, they must navigate through a web of secrets and deceit, risking their own lives to save their community from an impending catastrophe.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Survival, Conspiracy, Scientific Ethics
