Synopsis: In 'The Wayfarers' by J. C. Snaith, a chance encounter on a train sets in motion a series of events that will forever change the lives of two strangers. As they embark on a journey together, they find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected romance. With each step they take, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of their connection becomes increasingly clear. Will they be able to navigate the treacherous path ahead, or will their pasts catch up with them, threatening everything they hold dear?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Fate and destiny, Identity and self-discovery, Trust and betrayal
