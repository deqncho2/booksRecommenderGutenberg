Synopsis: In 'Trusia: A Princess of Krovitch' by Brinton Davis, readers are transported to the enchanting kingdom of Krovitch, where a young princess named Trusia embarks on a thrilling adventure to save her kingdom from an ancient curse. As she navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mythical creatures, Trusia discovers hidden powers within herself and forms unlikely alliances. With the fate of Krovitch hanging in the balance, Trusia must summon courage and resilience to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Heroism, Self-discovery, Friendship, Sacrifice
