Synopsis: 

'Winning a Cause: World War Stories' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of soldiers during World War I. Through a collection of captivating narratives, Inez Bigwood and John G. Thompson shed light on the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that unfolded on the battlefields. From the trenches of France to the desolate landscapes of Gallipoli, this book offers a poignant and immersive exploration of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable adversity. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Anthology

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Human Spirit
