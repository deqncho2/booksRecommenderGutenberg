Synopsis: In 'The Dalehouse Murder' by Everton Francis, a small, picturesque village is shaken by a brutal crime that sends shockwaves through the community. When a prominent resident is found dead in his own home, the local police are baffled by the lack of evidence and motive. As the investigation unfolds, secrets and hidden agendas begin to surface, revealing a web of deceit and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark underbelly of this seemingly idyllic village, where everyone is a suspect and no one can be trusted.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
