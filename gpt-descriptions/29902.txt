Synopsis: 

In 'Changing Winds: A Novel' by St. John G. Ervine, a small coastal town becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. As the winds of change sweep through the lives of its inhabitants, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of forgiveness. 

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and loss, Betrayal, Redemption, Secrets and lies
