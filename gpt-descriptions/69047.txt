Synopsis: In 'The Tiddly Winks' by Laura Rountree Smith, a seemingly ordinary game of tiddly winks takes an unexpected turn when a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious set of antique winks. As they delve deeper into the game, they uncover a hidden world filled with secrets, magic, and danger. With each flick of the winks, they are transported to different realms, facing thrilling challenges and encountering enigmatic characters. As they navigate this extraordinary journey, the friends must unravel the mysteries of the game and find a way back home before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Magic
