Synopsis: In the small town of Branton, Dr. Guy Brannon is faced with a moral dilemma when a young woman arrives seeking an illegal abortion. As he grapples with his personal beliefs and the consequences of his actions, Dr. Brannon finds himself caught in a web of secrets, lies, and the complexities of human nature. Set against the backdrop of a conservative community in the 1950s, 'Bramble Bush' explores the ethical dilemmas faced by a dedicated doctor and the impact of his choices on his own life and the lives of those around him.

Genres: Medical fiction, Drama, Psychological thriller

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Personal responsibility, Consequences of actions
