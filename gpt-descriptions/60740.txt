Synopsis: In "The Steam-Shovel Man" by Ralph Delahaye Paine, a small town is forever changed when a mysterious stranger arrives with a powerful steam shovel. As the townspeople watch in awe, the steam-shovel man begins to dig a massive hole, but his true intentions remain a mystery. With each passing day, the hole grows deeper, and the townspeople become increasingly curious and fearful. As tensions rise, the steam-shovel man's presence becomes a catalyst for both unity and division within the community. Will the truth behind the steam shovel be revealed, or will the town be forever haunted by its enigmatic presence?

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Fear, Community, Transformation
