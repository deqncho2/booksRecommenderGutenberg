Synopsis: In 'The Lonely Unicorn: A Novel' by Alec Waugh, readers are transported to a mystical world where a solitary unicorn embarks on a captivating journey of self-discovery. As the unicorn navigates through enchanting landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns. With each step, the unicorn's quest for companionship and purpose becomes increasingly poignant, leaving readers spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loneliness, Identity, Friendship, Self-Exploration
