Synopsis:
In 'The Mystery of M. Felix' by B. L. Farjeon, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious man named M. Felix seeks his help. As the detective delves deeper into the enigma surrounding M. Felix, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. Will the detective be able to unravel the mystery and uncover the truth behind M. Felix's enigmatic persona?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Intrigue
