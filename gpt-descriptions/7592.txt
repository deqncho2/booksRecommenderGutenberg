Synopsis: In the seventh volume of "The Caxtons: A Family Picture," the Caxton family finds themselves embroiled in a web of secrets and intrigue. As the patriarch Austin Caxton delves deeper into his research on ancient manuscripts, he uncovers a shocking revelation that threatens to upend everything they thought they knew about their family history. Meanwhile, his daughter, Blanche, finds herself torn between two suitors, each with their own hidden motives. With tensions rising and the truth lurking just beneath the surface, the Caxtons must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the secrets that bind them together.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
