Synopsis: 
'Poor Jack' by Frederick Marryat is a captivating coming-of-age tale set in the early 19th century. The story follows the adventures of Jack Easy, a young and spirited protagonist who finds himself torn between his desire for adventure and the expectations of his family. From joining the Royal Navy to facing perilous situations at sea, Jack's journey is filled with excitement, humor, and unexpected twists. As he navigates the challenges of life, Jack must confront his own identity and make difficult choices that will shape his future.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Family, Adventure, Duty
