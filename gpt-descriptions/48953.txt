Synopsis: 

In 'The Invasions of England', Home, Gordon and Foord, Edward take readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous history of England, exploring the numerous invasions that have shaped the nation. From the Viking raids to the Norman Conquest, and from the Spanish Armada to the Napoleonic Wars, this captivating book delves into the epic battles, political intrigues, and cultural clashes that have defined England's past. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the heroes, villains, and ordinary people caught in the crossfire of these invasions, painting a vivid picture of the resilience and determination of the English people throughout history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Cultural history

Themes: Invasion and conquest, National identity, Resilience and determination
