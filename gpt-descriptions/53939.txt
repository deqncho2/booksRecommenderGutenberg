Synopsis: 

Join Charles Lanman on an exhilarating journey as he embarks on a series of angling adventures across Canada, Nova Scotia, and the United States. From the pristine rivers of Canada to the rugged coastlines of Nova Scotia and the picturesque landscapes of the United States, Lanman's captivating tales will transport you to some of the most breathtaking fishing spots in North America. With vivid descriptions of the natural beauty surrounding him and the thrill of the chase, Lanman's encounters with various fish species and the challenges he faces along the way will keep you hooked from start to finish. 

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Nature exploration, Fishing, Outdoor adventure
