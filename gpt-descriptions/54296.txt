Synopsis: 

'The German Army in Belgium, the White Book of May 1915' is a historical document that provides a detailed account of Germany's military operations in Belgium during World War I. Compiled by the German Foreign Office, this book offers a comprehensive analysis of the events, strategies, and challenges faced by the German Army during their occupation of Belgium. Through official reports, testimonies, and photographs, the White Book sheds light on the German perspective of the conflict, presenting a captivating narrative of the military campaign.

Genres: Historical, Military, Non-fiction

Themes: War, Occupation, Strategy, Perspective
