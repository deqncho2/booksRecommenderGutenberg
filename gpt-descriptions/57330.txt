Synopsis: In 'The Opening Heavens', Joseph Bates takes readers on a captivating journey that intertwines the testimonies of prophets and apostles with astronomical observations. Through a thought-provoking exploration, Bates delves into the present and future location of the New Jerusalem, the Paradise of God. This intriguing work offers a unique perspective on the connection between heavenly revelations and scientific discoveries, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets of the heavens.

Genres: Religious, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Divine Revelation, Cosmology, Eschatology
