Synopsis: In "The Popish Plot: A study in the history of the reign of Charles II," John Pollock delves into a captivating historical period filled with intrigue, conspiracy, and religious tensions. Set during the reign of Charles II, the book uncovers a plot that threatens to shake the very foundations of the English monarchy. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the political and religious landscape of 17th-century England, where loyalties are tested, secrets are unveiled, and the fate of the nation hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political thriller, Religious history

Themes: Conspiracy, Religious conflict, Loyalty, Power struggle
