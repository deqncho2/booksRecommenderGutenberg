Synopsis: 
In 'Twenty' by Stella Benson, a young woman named Stella finds herself transported to a parallel world where she must navigate a society filled with eccentric characters and peculiar customs. As she embarks on a journey to find her way back home, Stella encounters unexpected friendships, magical creatures, and a mysterious prophecy that holds the key to her return. With each twist and turn, she discovers the power of self-discovery and the importance of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Adventure, Parallel worlds
