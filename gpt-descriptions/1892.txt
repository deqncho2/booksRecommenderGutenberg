Synopsis: 
'Extracts from Adam's Diary, translated from the original ms.' by Mark Twain is a humorous and insightful account of Adam's experiences in the Garden of Eden. Through a series of diary entries, Adam shares his observations, frustrations, and discoveries as he navigates life alongside Eve. From his initial confusion and curiosity to his growing affection for Eve, Adam's diary provides a unique perspective on the human condition and the complexities of relationships.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Relationships, Curiosity, Gender dynamics
