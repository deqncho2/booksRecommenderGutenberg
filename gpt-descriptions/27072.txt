Synopsis:
In 'The Flying Doctor: (Le Médecin Volant)' by Molière, a clever young man named Sganarelle finds himself caught in a web of deception when he pretends to be a doctor. As he navigates the challenges of his new role, Sganarelle's quick wit and charm are put to the test, leading to hilarious and unexpected consequences. This delightful comedy explores the lengths people will go to in order to maintain appearances and the power of wit in the face of absurdity.

Genres:
Comedy, Farce

Themes:
Deception, Appearance vs Reality, Wit
