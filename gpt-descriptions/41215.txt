Synopsis: 
In 'Selected Poems of Francis Thompson', embark on a poetic journey through the profound and introspective verses of one of the most enigmatic poets of the Victorian era. Thompson's words weave a tapestry of emotions, exploring the depths of love, faith, and the human experience. From the hauntingly beautiful "The Hound of Heaven" to the ethereal "The Kingdom of God," each poem invites readers to delve into the complexities of life and contemplate the mysteries of existence. With his lyrical and evocative language, Thompson's poetry captures the essence of the human soul, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of those who dare to explore his profound verses.

Genres: Poetry, Victorian Literature, Spiritual

Themes: Love, Faith, Redemption, Human Experience
