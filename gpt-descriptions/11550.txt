Synopsis: In 'Squash Tennis' by Richard C. Squires, a talented young athlete named Alex finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of squash tennis, a unique and intense sport that combines the best elements of both games. As he navigates the competitive world of professional sports, Alex must confront personal challenges, unexpected alliances, and the pressure to succeed. With each match, the stakes get higher, and Alex's determination is put to the ultimate test. Will he rise to the occasion and become a champion, or will the weight of his ambitions prove too much to bear?

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Identity, Competition
