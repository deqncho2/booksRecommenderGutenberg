Synopsis: In 'The Merchants of Venus' by Phelps, A. H., a group of ambitious entrepreneurs embark on a daring mission to colonize the planet Venus, a world filled with mystery and untapped resources. As they face numerous challenges and unexpected discoveries, they must navigate the treacherous landscape of interplanetary commerce, political intrigue, and personal rivalries. With the fate of their venture hanging in the balance, the merchants of Venus must confront their own desires and ambitions, ultimately questioning the true cost of success in the vastness of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Capitalism, Ambition, Morality
