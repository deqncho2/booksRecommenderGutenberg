Synopsis: In 'Fire in the Woods: Illustrated' by De Mille, James, a young photographer named Jess uncovers a mysterious secret while capturing the beauty of nature in the woods. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she finds herself entangled in a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and challenge everything she thought she knew. With stunning illustrations that bring the story to life, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Discovery, Courage, Identity
