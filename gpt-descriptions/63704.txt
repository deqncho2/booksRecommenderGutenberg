Synopsis: In "Some Impressions of My Elders" by St. John G. Ervine, a young protagonist embarks on a journey of self-discovery as he encounters a diverse range of intriguing and enigmatic elders. Through their unique perspectives and life experiences, the protagonist gains valuable insights into the complexities of human nature and the ever-evolving world around him. As he navigates through conversations and encounters with these captivating individuals, the protagonist's own beliefs and values are challenged, leading him to question his own place in society and the legacy he wishes to leave behind. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Psychological

Themes: Identity, Wisdom, Intergenerational relationships, Self-reflection
