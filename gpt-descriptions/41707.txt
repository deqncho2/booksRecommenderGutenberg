Synopsis: In "The Development of Metaphysics in Persia: A Contribution to the History of Muslim Philosophy," Iqbal takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich intellectual heritage of Persia. Exploring the profound influence of Persian thinkers on the development of metaphysics, Iqbal delves into the intricate interplay between philosophy, religion, and culture. Through his insightful analysis, he unveils the hidden gems of Persian thought, shedding light on the profound ideas that have shaped Muslim philosophy. With a blend of historical narrative and philosophical exploration, Iqbal's work offers a fascinating exploration of the intellectual landscape of Persia.

Genres: Philosophy, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Metaphysics, Islamic Philosophy, Persian Intellectual Heritage
