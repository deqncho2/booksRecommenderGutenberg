Synopsis: In 'Country Luck' by John Habberton, follow the captivating journey of a young man named Tom, who finds himself thrust into the unpredictable world of the Wild West. From unexpected encounters with outlaws to heartwarming friendships, Tom's resilience and determination are put to the test as he navigates the challenges of life on the frontier. As he learns to adapt to his new surroundings, Tom discovers that sometimes, luck can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Adaptation
