Synopsis: In "Tom Swift in Captivity, Or, A Daring Escape By Airship," Tom Swift finds himself in a perilous situation when he is captured and held captive against his will. With his inventive mind and fearless spirit, Tom must devise a daring plan to escape using an unconventional method - an airship. As he navigates through dangerous obstacles and faces unexpected adversaries, Tom's resourcefulness and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart his captors and secure his freedom?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Ingenuity, Freedom
