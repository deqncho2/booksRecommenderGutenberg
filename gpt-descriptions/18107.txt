Synopsis: In "American Lutheranism Vindicated," author S.S. Schmucker delves into the heart of the Lutheran faith, examining its symbols and addressing controversial topics. With a compelling response to Rev. W.J. Mann's plea, Schmucker offers a thought-provoking exploration that challenges preconceived notions and sheds light on the true essence of American Lutheranism.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Theological

Themes: Lutheran theology, Religious symbols, Controversial topics, Faith exploration
