Synopsis: In 'Survivors' by Savage, Arthur Dekker, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, they must confront their deepest fears, forge unlikely alliances, and unravel the mysteries of the island. With danger lurking at every turn, the survivors must navigate treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and confront their own inner demons. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must come together to overcome the odds and find a way back home.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Human Nature, Redemption
