Synopsis: In 'Boy Scouts on the Trail' by Garth, John, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling expedition into the wilderness. As they navigate through treacherous terrains, encounter mysterious creatures, and face unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their survival skills, teamwork, and determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each step they take, they not only learn valuable lessons about nature and self-reliance but also discover the true meaning of friendship and the importance of perseverance. 

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Friendship, Self-Reliance
