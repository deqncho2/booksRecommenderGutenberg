Synopsis: In 'The Invisible Censor' by Francis Hackett, a renowned author finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he discovers that his latest manuscript has been tampered with. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets, deception, and a shadowy figure known only as the Invisible Censor. With his reputation and sanity on the line, the author must race against time to uncover the truth before it consumes him completely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Manipulation
