Synopsis: 
'The Daisy, or, Cautionary Stories in Verse: Adapted to the Ideas of Children from Four to Eight Years Old' is a delightful collection of cautionary tales written by Mrs. Turner. Through engaging and entertaining stories in verse, the book aims to captivate young readers while imparting important life lessons. Each story follows the adventures of various characters, teaching children about the consequences of their actions and the importance of making wise choices. From mischievous animals to curious children, these cautionary tales provide valuable insights into the world of morality and decision-making. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Moral Tales

Themes: Morality, Consequences, Decision-making
