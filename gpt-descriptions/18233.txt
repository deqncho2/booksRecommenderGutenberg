Synopsis: 
'Animal Ghosts; Or, Animal Hauntings and the Hereafter' by O'Donnell, Elliott delves into the mysterious realm of animal spirits and their presence in the afterlife. Through a collection of chilling and heartwarming anecdotes, the author explores encounters with ghostly animals, from phantom dogs and cats to spectral horses and birds. These haunting tales offer a glimpse into the unexplained connections between humans and animals, blurring the boundaries between the living and the dead. Prepare to be captivated by the eerie and enchanting world of animal ghosts.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Paranormal, Supernatural

Themes: 
Animal spirituality, Life after death, Interconnectedness of humans and animals
