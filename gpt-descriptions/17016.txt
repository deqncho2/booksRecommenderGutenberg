Synopsis: In 'Division of Words', Frederick W. Hamilton delves into the intricate art of dividing words at the ends of lines, offering valuable insights into spelling, syllabication, and pronunciation. With a keen eye for detail, Hamilton explores the rules and techniques that govern the division of words, providing practical guidance for writers, editors, and anyone interested in the nuances of language. This comprehensive guide is a treasure trove of knowledge, shedding light on the often overlooked aspects of word division and offering a deeper understanding of the mechanics behind effective writing.

Genres: Linguistics, Reference, Writing

Themes: Language mechanics, Spelling, Syllabication, Pronunciation
