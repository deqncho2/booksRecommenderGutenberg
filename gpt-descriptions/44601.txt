Synopsis: In 'The Little Maid of Israel', follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Ruth as she navigates the tumultuous times of ancient Israel. From her humble beginnings as a servant to her unexpected encounters with royalty, Ruth's unwavering faith and courage will inspire readers of all ages. As she faces challenges and discovers her true purpose, Ruth's story unfolds with heartwarming moments and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Faith, Courage, Identity, Redemption
