Synopsis: 
Inspector French, renowned for his brilliant detective skills, is faced with his most perplexing case yet. A wealthy businessman is found dead in his locked study, with no signs of forced entry or any apparent motive. As French delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden agendas that lead him down a twisted path of deception and betrayal. With time running out and the pressure mounting, Inspector French must use all his cunning and intuition to unravel the truth and bring the elusive killer to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue, Justice
