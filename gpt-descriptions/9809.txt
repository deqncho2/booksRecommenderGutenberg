Synopsis: In 'The Price of Things' by Elinor Glyn, a young woman named Amelia finds herself caught in a web of secrets and desires when she inherits a mysterious antique shop. As she delves deeper into the shop's history, Amelia uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, betrayal, and the true cost of fulfilling one's deepest desires. With each item she sells, Amelia discovers that the price of things goes far beyond their monetary value, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the human heart.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Desire, Betrayal, Self-discovery
