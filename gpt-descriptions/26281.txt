Synopsis: 
In 'Happy-Thought Hall' by F. C. Burnand, a mysterious invitation leads a group of strangers to a secluded mansion, where they are promised a life-changing experience. As they navigate the eccentricities of their host and the peculiar happenings within the hall, secrets are unveiled, alliances are formed, and unexpected connections are made. With each passing day, the guests find themselves drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and suspense, unsure of who they can trust and what lies behind the enigmatic facade of Happy-Thought Hall.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Trust and betrayal, Unexpected connections
