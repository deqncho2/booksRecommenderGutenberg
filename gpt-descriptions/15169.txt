Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, find themselves in the bustling and exciting city for a summer adventure. As they explore the streets, they stumble upon a mysterious case that needs solving. With their keen detective skills, the twins embark on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, they encounter new friends, encounter dangerous situations, and uncover secrets that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-Solving
