Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 17, August 14, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human behavior is revealed. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the suspense and intrigue that permeate this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Human nature, Small-town dynamics
