Synopsis: In 'What Price Peace?' by Libby and the National Council for Prevention of War, a gripping tale unfolds as a young journalist embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth behind a global conspiracy threatening world peace. As she delves deeper into the shadows, she uncovers shocking secrets and faces life-threatening challenges that force her to question her own beliefs and the sacrifices she is willing to make for the greater good. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn into a thrilling narrative that explores the complexities of power, morality, and the true cost of achieving peace.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Morality, Power, Sacrifice
