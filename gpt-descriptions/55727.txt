Synopsis: 
In 'The Early Oxford Press: A Bibliography of Printing and Publishing at Oxford, '1468'-1640; With Notes, Appendixes and Illustrations', Madan and Falconer delve into the fascinating world of printing and publishing in Oxford during the period from 1468 to 1640. This comprehensive bibliography provides a detailed account of the early Oxford Press, exploring the various printers, publishers, and their works, shedding light on the development of the printing industry in this renowned academic city. With extensive notes, appendixes, and illustrations, the authors offer a captivating glimpse into the rich history of printing at Oxford, uncovering hidden gems and shedding new light on the influential role of the early Oxford Press.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Bibliography

Themes: Printing history, Publishing industry, Academic institutions
