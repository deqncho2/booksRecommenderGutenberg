Synopsis: In "The First Christmas Tree: A Story of the Forest," Van Dyke takes readers on a captivating journey through time and nature. Set in the enchanting Black Forest of Germany, the story follows a young man named Winfried who embarks on a quest to find the perfect tree for a special purpose. Along the way, he encounters mystical creatures, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of Christmas. This heartwarming tale beautifully weaves together elements of adventure, folklore, and spirituality, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of the first Christmas tree.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Historical

Themes: Christmas, Nature, Faith, Tradition
