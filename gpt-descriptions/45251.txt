Synopsis: In 'How to Know the Ferns' by S. Leonard Bastin, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of ferns. This beautifully illustrated guide takes readers on an exploration of the diverse species of ferns, revealing their hidden secrets and unique characteristics. From the delicate fronds of the maidenhair fern to the towering presence of the tree fern, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind these ancient plants. As you delve deeper into the intricate details of ferns, you will discover a hidden world of beauty and resilience that will leave you in awe of nature's wonders.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Botany

Themes: Exploration, Beauty of Nature, Biodiversity
