Synopsis: In "Breathes there a man" by Charles E. Fritch, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by his peculiar abilities, they soon discover that his arrival is not a mere coincidence. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the stranger's true intentions and the town's fate hang in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Power, Fate
