Synopsis: In "A Bird of Passage" by B.M. Croker, a young woman named Violet is swept away on a thrilling adventure to India, where she encounters a mysterious stranger who ignites her curiosity and passion. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery, Violet finds herself torn between the allure of a new world and the ties that bind her to her past. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will transport readers to exotic lands and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-Discovery, Cultural Exploration
