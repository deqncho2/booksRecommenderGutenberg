Synopsis: In 'Kent Knowles: Quahaug' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, readers are introduced to Kent Knowles, a young lawyer who finds himself entangled in a mysterious case involving a small coastal town. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Quahaug, Knowles uncovers a web of intrigue, deceit, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel the fabric of the community. With his sharp intellect and unwavering determination, Knowles must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront the dark forces at play, all while trying to protect his own life and those he holds dear. This gripping tale of suspense and mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Community
