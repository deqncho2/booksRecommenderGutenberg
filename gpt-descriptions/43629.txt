Synopsis: 
'Strange Stories from a Chinese Studio' is a captivating collection of supernatural tales that will transport readers to a world of enchantment and mystery. Set in ancient China, these two volumes are filled with intriguing stories of ghosts, demons, and otherworldly beings, each with its own unique twist. From forbidden love affairs to vengeful spirits, the narratives explore the depths of human desires and the consequences that arise when one delves into the supernatural realm. With its rich cultural backdrop and imaginative storytelling, this collection is sure to leave readers spellbound.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Horror, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Supernatural Encounters, Moral Consequences
