Synopsis:
"The Real Charlotte" is a gripping and atmospheric novel that follows the lives of two cousins, Charlotte and Francie, as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and family secrets in late 19th-century Ireland. When Francie visits her wealthy cousin Charlotte at her grand estate, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit and manipulation that threatens to unravel their lives. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of hidden desires, forbidden love, and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests. With its richly drawn characters and hauntingly beautiful setting, "The Real Charlotte" is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the dark side of human nature.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Love and desire, Family secrets, Manipulation and deceit, Social class, Female empowerment
