Synopsis: In 'Mabel's Mistake' by Ann S. Stephens, a young woman named Mabel finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she makes a seemingly innocent decision. As the consequences of her mistake unfold, Mabel must navigate through a world of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With her life on the line, she must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
