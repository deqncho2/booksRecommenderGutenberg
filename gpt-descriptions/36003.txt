Synopsis: 

In 'Degeneracy: Its Causes, Signs and Results' by Eugene S. Talbot, readers are taken on a captivating exploration of the dark underbelly of society. Through a series of thought-provoking case studies and meticulous research, Talbot delves into the intricate web of factors that contribute to the degeneration of individuals and communities. From the hidden vices of the elite to the struggles of the working class, this book uncovers the shocking truths that lie beneath the surface of society. As the author uncovers the causes, signs, and results of degeneracy, readers will find themselves questioning their own assumptions and gaining a deeper understanding of the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Sociology, Psychology, Non-fiction

Themes: Degeneration, Social decay, Morality, Human behavior
