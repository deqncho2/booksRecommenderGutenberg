Synopsis: 
'Tales from a Dugout' by Arthur Guy Empey takes readers on a captivating journey through the trenches of World War I. Through a series of vivid and poignant anecdotes, Empey shares his personal experiences as a soldier, offering a unique and intimate perspective on the realities of war. From the camaraderie among fellow soldiers to the horrors of battle, Empey's storytelling transports readers to the front lines, immersing them in the triumphs and tragedies of the Great War.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: War experiences, Camaraderie, Sacrifice, Survival
