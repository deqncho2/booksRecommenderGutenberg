Synopsis: In 'The Border Boys Along the St. Lawrence' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey along the majestic St. Lawrence River. As they navigate through treacherous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover hidden secrets, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limits. With danger lurking at every turn, the Border Boys must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Resilience
