Synopsis: In 'Miss Civilization: A Comedy in One Act', a young American woman finds herself in a foreign land, where her unconventional ways clash with the strict societal norms of the locals. As she navigates through cultural misunderstandings and comedic situations, she inadvertently becomes a catalyst for change, challenging the status quo and redefining what it means to be civilized. 

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Cultural clash, Individualism, Social norms
