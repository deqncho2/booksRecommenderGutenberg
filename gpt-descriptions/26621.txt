Synopsis: In 'The Story of Madras' by Barlow, Glyn, a young journalist named Emma embarks on a journey to uncover the hidden secrets of the vibrant city of Madras. As she delves deeper into its rich history and cultural tapestry, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each revelation, Emma's perception of the city and her own identity is challenged, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Cultural Exploration, Secrets and Intrigue
