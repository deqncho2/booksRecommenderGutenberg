Synopsis: 
In 'Junior' by Abernathy, Robert, we follow the captivating journey of a young prodigy named Junior, who possesses an extraordinary talent that sets him apart from his peers. As he navigates the challenges of growing up in a small town, Junior's unique abilities attract both admiration and envy, leading him down a path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With secrets to uncover and a mysterious past to unravel, Junior's story is a thrilling exploration of identity, ambition, and the power of embracing one's true self.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Ambition, Self-discovery
