Synopsis: In "The White Cipher" by Leverage, Henry, a brilliant cryptographer, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he receives an encrypted message that holds the key to a long-lost treasure. As he delves deeper into the intricate code, Henry discovers that he is not the only one searching for the treasure, and he must race against time to decipher the message before it falls into the wrong hands. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Henry's quest to unlock the secrets hidden within the white cipher.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Cryptography, Treasure Hunt, Betrayal
