Synopsis: In 'A Series of Letters, in Defence of Divine Revelation', Reverend Hosea Ballou engages in a thought-provoking exchange with Reverend Abner Kneeland, who questions the authenticity of divine revelation. Through a series of letters, Ballou passionately defends the existence and significance of divine revelation, presenting compelling arguments and counterarguments. Additionally, the book includes a religious correspondence between Ballou and Reverend Dr. Joseph Buckminster and Reverend Joseph Walton, pastors of Congregational Churches in Portsmouth, N.H. This correspondence delves into theological discussions and explores various perspectives on religious matters.

Genres: Religious literature, theological discourse, epistolary writing

Themes: Divine revelation, authenticity of religious texts, theological debates, religious perspectives
