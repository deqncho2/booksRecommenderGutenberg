Synopsis: In "Remember the Alamo" by Amelia E. Barr, a young woman named Lucinda is thrust into the chaos of the Texas Revolution. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of war, Lucinda finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and her growing love for a dashing Texan soldier. With the Alamo as the backdrop, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices made in the pursuit of freedom and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and loyalty, War and sacrifice, Resilience and determination
