Synopsis: In 'Only an Irish Boy; Or, Andy Burke's Fortunes' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Andy Burke, a young Irish immigrant who dreams of a better life in America. Facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Andy must navigate the bustling streets of New York City, where he encounters both friends and foes. As he strives to overcome adversity and make a name for himself, Andy's determination and resilience are put to the test. Will he be able to rise above his circumstances and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Immigration, Social mobility, Perseverance, Friendship
