Synopsis: 

In 'Copper Work: A Text Book for Teachers and Students in the Manual Arts', Augustus F. Rose takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of copper craftsmanship. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of knowledge and practical techniques for both teachers and students interested in mastering the art of working with copper. From basic tools and materials to intricate designs and finishes, Rose's expertise shines through as he shares his passion for this versatile metal. With step-by-step instructions and detailed illustrations, readers will be inspired to explore their creativity and create stunning copper pieces of their own.

Genres: Educational, Craftsmanship, Art

Themes: Craftsmanship, Creativity, Skill Development
