Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the Twenty-second, or the Cheshire Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes us on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1689 to 1849. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the rich history of the Cheshire Regiment, offering a fascinating account of their triumphs and challenges. This meticulously researched work provides a comprehensive and engaging narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the remarkable stories and contributions of this esteemed regiment.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events, War and conflict
