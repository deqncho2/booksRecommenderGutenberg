Synopsis:
In 'The Rector' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a small English village is turned upside down when a new rector arrives with a mysterious past. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued by his enigmatic personality, they uncover a web of secrets that threaten to unravel the fabric of their community. With tensions rising and loyalties tested, the rector's presence becomes a catalyst for both personal and societal transformation, leading to unexpected consequences for all involved.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Community, Morality, Redemption
