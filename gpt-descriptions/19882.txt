Synopsis: 
Embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting island of Corsica as C. B. Black takes you on an unforgettable itinerary. From the bustling cities to the serene countryside, this travelogue explores the hidden gems of Corsica, revealing its rich history, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant culture. Whether traversing the island by rail, carriage, or venturing off the beaten path on forest roads, Black's vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes will transport you to this captivating Mediterranean paradise. Discover the charm of Corsica through its picturesque villages, ancient ruins, and stunning coastal vistas, all while immersing yourself in the unique traditions and flavors that make this island a true gem of the Mediterranean.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
