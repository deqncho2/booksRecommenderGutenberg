Synopsis: In 'Fifteen Hundred Miles an Hour' by Charles Dixon, a young prodigy finds himself caught in a thrilling race against time and a mysterious enemy. As he embarks on a high-stakes adventure, he must rely on his intellect, courage, and a secret power to uncover the truth and save the world from impending disaster. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Power of the Mind, Overcoming Challenges
