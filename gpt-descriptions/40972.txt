Synopsis: In 'Mythical Monsters' by Charles Gould, embark on a captivating journey through the realms of folklore and legend as the author delves into the mysterious and awe-inspiring world of mythical creatures. From the ferocious dragons of ancient tales to the elusive Loch Ness Monster, Gould explores the origins, characteristics, and cultural significance of these legendary beings. With vivid descriptions and captivating illustrations, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the enduring fascination with mythical monsters that has captivated humanity for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mythology, Folklore

Themes: Legends and folklore, Cultural significance, Human fascination with the unknown
