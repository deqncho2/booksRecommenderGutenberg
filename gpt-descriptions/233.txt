Synopsis: In the bustling city of Chicago, a young and ambitious country girl named Carrie Meeber arrives with dreams of a better life. Through a series of chance encounters and unexpected opportunities, Carrie's journey takes her from poverty to success, from innocence to experience, and from love to betrayal. As she navigates the complexities of urban life, Carrie must confront the choices she makes and the consequences they bring, ultimately discovering the true nature of ambition and the price of desire.

Genres: Realism, Naturalism, Social Commentary

Themes: Ambition, Desire, Social mobility, Morality, Gender roles
