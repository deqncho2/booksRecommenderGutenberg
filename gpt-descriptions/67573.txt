Synopsis: 
In 'Antimachus of Colophon and the Position of Women in Greek Poetry: A fragment printed for the use of scholars', Benecke delves into the fascinating world of ancient Greek poetry and explores the role of women within it. Through an analysis of the fragments of Antimachus of Colophon's work, the author uncovers the complex and often overlooked portrayal of women in Greek literature. As the reader delves into this scholarly exploration, they will be captivated by the revelations and insights that shed light on the position of women in ancient Greek society.

Genres: 
Academic, Literary Criticism, Gender Studies

Themes: 
Gender roles, Women in literature, Ancient Greek society
