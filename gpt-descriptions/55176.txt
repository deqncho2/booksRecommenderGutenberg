Synopsis: 
'Cartoons and Caricatures of Seattle Citizens' takes readers on a whimsical journey through the vibrant city of Seattle, capturing the essence of its diverse inhabitants through clever and humorous illustrations. From quirky coffee shop regulars to eccentric street performers, this collection of cartoons offers a delightful and satirical portrayal of the city's unique characters. As readers flip through the pages, they will find themselves chuckling at the exaggerated yet relatable depictions of everyday Seattleites, while also gaining a deeper appreciation for the city's rich cultural tapestry.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Art

Themes: City life, Cultural diversity, Satirical commentary
