Synopsis: 
'Ravenshoe' by Henry Kingsley is a captivating Victorian novel that follows the lives of the Ravenshoe family, their secrets, and the trials they face. Set in the backdrop of the English countryside, the story revolves around the young protagonist, Charles Ravenshoe, who finds himself entangled in a web of love, loyalty, and political intrigue. As Charles navigates through the complexities of his family's history and the challenges of his own fate, he must make difficult choices that will shape his future and the destiny of Ravenshoe.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Family, Love, Loyalty, Political Intrigue
