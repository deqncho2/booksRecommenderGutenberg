Synopsis: In "Philosophy in Sport Made Science in Earnest," John Ayrton Paris takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of natural philosophy using popular toys and sports as his guide. Through a series of engaging experiments and demonstrations, Paris explores the fundamental principles of science, unraveling the mysteries of the physical world in a way that is both accessible and entertaining. From the physics of a spinning top to the mechanics of a bouncing ball, this book offers a unique perspective on the wonders of the natural world, making science come alive through the lens of play and discovery.

Genres: Science, Popular Science, Education

Themes: Natural philosophy, Science education, Play and learning
