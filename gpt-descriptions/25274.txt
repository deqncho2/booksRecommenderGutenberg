Synopsis: In 'Rollo at Work' by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on a series of exciting adventures as he explores the world of work. From helping his father in the garden to assisting a blacksmith, Rollo learns valuable lessons about responsibility, perseverance, and the importance of hard work. Along the way, he encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the satisfaction that comes from a job well done. This engaging tale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and growth, as Rollo navigates the complexities of the adult world and discovers his own unique talents and passions.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Responsibility, Perseverance, Hard Work, Self-Discovery
