Synopsis: In "The Father of British Canada: a Chronicle of Carleton," William Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Sir Guy Carleton, a pivotal figure in the history of Canada. From his early military career to his role in shaping the destiny of British North America, Carleton's story is one of courage, diplomacy, and unwavering dedication. As readers delve into the pages of this historical chronicle, they will witness the challenges and triumphs that Carleton faced, as well as the profound impact he had on the development of Canada. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Canadian literature

Themes: Leadership, Nation-building, Colonial history
