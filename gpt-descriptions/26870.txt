Synopsis: In 'On the uncertainty of the signs of murder in the case of bastard children' by William Hunter, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving the mysterious deaths of illegitimate children. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that challenge his own beliefs and force him to question the very nature of justice. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth, all while battling his own personal demons.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice and morality, Secrets and deception, Identity and societal norms
