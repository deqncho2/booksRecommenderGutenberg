Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion of the 'A Righted Wrong' series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and justice hangs in the balance. As the protagonist races against time to uncover the truth, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and danger, where every decision could have dire consequences. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Redemption
