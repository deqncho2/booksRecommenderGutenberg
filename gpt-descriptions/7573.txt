Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotes and Images from Fromont and Risler by Alphonse Daudet: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' offers a captivating collection of quotes and images from the renowned novel 'Fromont and Risler' by Alphonse Daudet. This compilation takes readers on a journey through the lives of the two main characters, Fromont and Risler, as they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and ambition in 19th-century Paris. Through vivid descriptions and thought-provoking quotes, the book provides a glimpse into the intertwining destinies of these characters, their triumphs, and their struggles. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Ambition and Success, Friendship and Loyalty
