Synopsis: 

In "Richard Wagner His Life and His Dramas: A Biographical Study of the Man and an Explanation of His Work," author W. J. Henderson delves into the fascinating life and artistic genius of one of the most influential composers in history. From Wagner's humble beginnings to his rise as a revolutionary figure in the world of opera, this biographical study offers a captivating exploration of the man behind the music. Henderson skillfully weaves together anecdotes, historical context, and analysis of Wagner's works, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the complexities and controversies surrounding his life and creations. 

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Artistic genius, Controversy, Influence
