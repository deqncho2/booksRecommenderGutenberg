Synopsis: 
'Behind the News: Voices from Goa's Press' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of journalism in the vibrant state of Goa, India. Through a series of compelling interviews with journalists, editors, and photographers, this book uncovers the untold stories, challenges, and triumphs that lie behind the headlines. From uncovering corruption to reporting on cultural events, the journalists featured in this book provide a unique perspective on the complex tapestry of Goan society. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into the thrilling world of investigative reporting, the power of the press, and the delicate balance between truth and sensationalism. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Journalism, Biography

Themes: Journalism ethics, Freedom of the press, Cultural identity, Corruption, Truth-seeking
