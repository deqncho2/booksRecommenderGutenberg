Synopsis: In 'Restricted Tool' by Malcolm B. Morehart, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious device that has the power to change the world. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and intrigue. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the restricted tool and its potential consequences. This gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the lengths people will go to control it.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power and control, Ethics in science and technology
