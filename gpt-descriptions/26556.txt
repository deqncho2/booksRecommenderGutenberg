Synopsis: In "Myths and Marvels of Astronomy," Richard A. Proctor takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the universe. From ancient myths and legends to the latest scientific discoveries, Proctor explores the fascinating stories and mysteries that have surrounded astronomy throughout history. With a blend of scientific knowledge and imaginative storytelling, this book unveils the awe-inspiring beauty and complexity of the cosmos, leaving readers spellbound and eager to explore the stars themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Astronomy, Mythology, Scientific Discovery
