Synopsis: In 'The Schemes of the Kaiser' by Adam, Juliette, a young and ambitious journalist, stumbles upon a hidden conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the Kaiser, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception, betrayal, and political intrigue. With her life on the line, Juliette must navigate a treacherous web of alliances and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Power, Corruption, Truth
