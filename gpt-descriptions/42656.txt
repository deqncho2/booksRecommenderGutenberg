Synopsis: In 'The Cross and Crown' by Curtis, T. D., a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into the artifact's history, Emily becomes entangled in a centuries-old battle between good and evil, where she must uncover the truth and make a choice that could change the course of history.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Power of Choice, Ancient Secrets
