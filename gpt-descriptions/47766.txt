Synopsis: 

In 'The Propaganda for Reform in Proprietary Medicines, Vol. 1 of 2', the Council on Pharmacy and Chemistry, a branch of the American Medical Association, delves into the murky world of proprietary medicines. Through a meticulous investigation, they expose the deceptive practices and questionable ingredients used by manufacturers to promote their products. This eye-opening volume uncovers the hidden truths behind the marketing strategies employed by the pharmaceutical industry, leaving readers astounded and eager to learn more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Investigative

Themes: Deception, Pharmaceutical industry, Consumer awareness
