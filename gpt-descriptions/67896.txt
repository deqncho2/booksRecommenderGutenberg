Synopsis: In 'Sun Hunting: Adventures and Observations Among the Native and Migratory Tribes of Florida,' Kenneth Lewis Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse tribes of Florida. From the stoical time-killers of Palm Beach to the gentle and gregarious tin-canners of the remote interior, and the vivacious and semi-violent peoples of Miami and its surroundings, Roberts provides a fascinating exploration of the unique cultures and lifestyles found in the Sunshine State. Through his vivid observations and thrilling adventures, the author unveils the hidden treasures and untold stories of Florida's native and migratory tribes, painting a vibrant picture of a land teeming with life and diversity.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural diversity, Exploration, Human connection
