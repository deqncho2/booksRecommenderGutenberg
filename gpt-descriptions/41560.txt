Synopsis: In the small, idyllic town of Willowbrook, a mysterious carnival arrives, bringing with it an air of enchantment and excitement. But as the townspeople indulge in the carnival's attractions, they soon discover that there is something sinister lurking beneath the surface. Secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred. As the carnival's dark influence spreads, the residents of Willowbrook find themselves trapped in a web of temptation and danger, where the playground becomes a battleground for their souls.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Temptation, Illusion and Reality
