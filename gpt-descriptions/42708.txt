Synopsis: 

In "Woman under Monasticism: Chapters on Saint-Lore and Convent Life between A.D. 500 and A.D. 1500," Lina Eckenstein delves into the fascinating world of women in monasticism throughout the medieval period. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Eckenstein uncovers the hidden lives of these women, exploring their motivations, struggles, and triumphs within the confines of the convent walls. From the early days of female monasticism to the height of its influence, this book offers a unique perspective on the lives of women who chose a path of devotion and spirituality.

Genres: History, Religious Studies, Women's Studies

Themes: Monasticism, Gender and Religion, Female Empowerment, Spirituality
