Synopsis: In 'A Difficult Problem: 1900' by Anna Katharine Green, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case that seems to defy all logic. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the detective must use his keen intellect and intuition to solve this seemingly unsolvable mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Deception, Intrigue
