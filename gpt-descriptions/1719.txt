Synopsis: In 'The Ballad of the White Horse', G.K. Chesterton weaves a captivating tale of adventure, courage, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. Set in the backdrop of the ninth-century Viking invasion of England, the story follows the legendary figure of King Alfred the Great as he rallies his people to defend their land and their faith. Through vivid imagery and poetic storytelling, Chesterton takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with battles, chivalry, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Good vs. Evil, National identity
