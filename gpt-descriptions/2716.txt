Synopsis: In 'Sir Dominick Ferrand' by Henry James, a renowned English novelist is invited to a grand country estate to meet the enigmatic Sir Dominick Ferrand. As the novelist delves into the secrets and mysteries surrounding Sir Dominick, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and deception. With each revelation, the boundaries between reality and fiction blur, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and identity.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Truth, Perception
