Synopsis: 
In 'Authors of Greece', T. W. Lumb takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of some of the most influential Greek authors in history. From the epic poems of Homer to the philosophical musings of Plato, Lumb delves into the rich literary heritage of Greece, exploring the struggles, triumphs, and profound insights of these remarkable writers. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Lumb paints a vivid picture of the cultural and intellectual landscape that shaped these authors, offering a fascinating glimpse into the minds behind the timeless classics that continue to captivate readers to this day.

Genres: 
Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: 
Literary legacy, Cultural influence, Intellectual exploration
