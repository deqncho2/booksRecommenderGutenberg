Synopsis: In 'The Toys of Peace, and Other Papers', Saki takes us on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and satirical short stories. From a mischievous child's obsession with war toys to the absurdity of a society obsessed with etiquette, Saki's tales are filled with clever twists and unexpected outcomes. With his signature blend of humor and social commentary, he invites readers to question the norms and conventions of their own world.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Short Stories

Themes: Social commentary, Irony, Critique of societal norms
