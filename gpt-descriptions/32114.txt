Synopsis: 
In 'Command' by William McFee, a gripping tale of adventure and suspense unfolds as Captain John Hanson finds himself at the helm of a massive ocean liner, facing a series of unexpected challenges. As the ship embarks on a perilous journey across treacherous waters, Captain Hanson must navigate through storms, mutiny, and a mysterious stowaway, all while battling his own personal demons. With the lives of thousands of passengers and crew members hanging in the balance, the captain's leadership and decision-making skills are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to command the ship to safety, or will disaster strike? 

Genres: Adventure, Suspense, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Leadership, Survival, Moral Dilemmas
