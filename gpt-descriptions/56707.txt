Synopsis: In 'Bertrand of Brittany' by Deeping, Warwick, a young nobleman named Bertrand finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas in medieval Brittany. As he navigates through treacherous alliances and dangerous secrets, Bertrand must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of medieval life and a gripping narrative, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world of knights, castles, and courtly love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Honor
