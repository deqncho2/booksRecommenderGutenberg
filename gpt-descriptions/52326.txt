Synopsis: In 'The Radio Planet' by Ralph Milne Farley, a brilliant scientist invents a revolutionary device that allows him to communicate with an extraterrestrial civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of this newfound connection, he uncovers a world of danger, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of both Earth and the radio planet hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to save humanity from an impending catastrophe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Communication across dimensions, Survival against all odds
