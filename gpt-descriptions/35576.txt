Synopsis: In the Days of Queen Victoria takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable reign of Queen Victoria, exploring the political, social, and cultural landscape of 19th-century Britain. From the opulence of the royal court to the struggles of the working class, this book offers a vivid portrayal of the era, filled with fascinating anecdotes and historical details. Through the eyes of various characters, readers witness the sweeping changes that shaped Victorian society, from the Industrial Revolution to the rise of the British Empire. As the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances emerge, painting a rich tapestry of a bygone era that continues to captivate our imagination.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Monarchy, Social Class, Industrialization, British Empire, Women's Rights
