Synopsis:
In 'The Obstacle Race' by Ethel M. Dell, a young woman named Joan Carthew finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she inherits a mysterious estate. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house, she becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time, facing unexpected obstacles and uncovering shocking truths. With her life on the line, Joan must navigate through treacherous paths and unexpected twists, all while trying to unravel the mysteries that surround her inheritance.

Genres:
Romantic suspense, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Inheritance, Secrets and deception, Perseverance
