Synopsis: In the heart-pounding sci-fi thriller 'Doomsday on Ajiat' by Neil R. Jones, a team of intrepid explorers embarks on a perilous mission to a distant planet, only to discover a shocking secret that could spell the end of humanity. As they race against time to unravel the mysteries of Ajiat, they must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to save the world from impending doom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sacrifice, Humanity's Fate
