Synopsis:
In "Pearl-Maiden: A Tale of the Fall of Jerusalem," H. Rider Haggard takes readers on a captivating journey to ancient Jerusalem during a time of great turmoil. The story follows the life of Miriam, a young Christian girl who finds herself caught in the midst of a clash between different religious beliefs and political powers. As Miriam navigates the treacherous landscape of love, faith, and war, she discovers her own strength and resilience in the face of adversity. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Haggard weaves a tale of passion, sacrifice, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Religious conflict, Love and sacrifice, Coming of age
