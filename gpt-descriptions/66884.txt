Synopsis: In 'Joseph and His Brethren' by W. K. Tweedie, a young man named Joseph finds himself in a world filled with jealousy, betrayal, and unexpected twists of fate. As he navigates through the challenges of his life, Joseph's resilience and unwavering faith are put to the test. From his humble beginnings to his rise to power, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey of love, loss, and redemption that will keep them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biblical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Betrayal, Forgiveness, Faith, Redemption
