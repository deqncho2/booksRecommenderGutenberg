Synopsis: 
'Coal and Candlelight, and Other Verses' by Helen Parry Eden is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the beauty and complexities of life. From the flickering glow of candlelight to the gritty world of coal mining, each verse paints vivid images and evokes powerful emotions. With a delicate balance of light and darkness, the poems explore themes of love, loss, resilience, and the enduring human spirit. This collection is a lyrical celebration of the ordinary and extraordinary moments that shape our lives.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Resilience, Human Spirit
