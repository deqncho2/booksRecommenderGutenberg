Synopsis: In 'Working in the Shade: Lowly Sowing brings Glorious Reaping' by Wilson, Theodore P., a young and ambitious protagonist finds themselves thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when they stumble upon a hidden society. As they navigate through a web of secrets and unexpected alliances, they must uncover the truth behind their own identity and the purpose of their newfound abilities. With each step closer to the truth, they realize that their actions have far-reaching consequences, and the line between good and evil becomes increasingly blurred. Will they be able to make the right choices and fulfill their destiny, or will they succumb to the darkness that threatens to consume them?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Morality
