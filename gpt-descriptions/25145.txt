Synopsis: In 'A Patriotic Schoolgirl' by Brazil, Angela, a young and determined schoolgirl named Angela finds herself caught in the midst of a political turmoil that threatens to tear her country apart. As she navigates through the challenges of adolescence, Angela becomes an unexpected symbol of hope and resilience, inspiring those around her to stand up for what they believe in. With her unwavering spirit and unwavering love for her country, Angela's journey will captivate readers as they witness her transformation from an ordinary schoolgirl to an extraordinary force for change.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Political Drama

Themes: Patriotism, Resilience, Youth Empowerment, Political Activism
