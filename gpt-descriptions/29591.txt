Synopsis: 
'Getting to know Spain' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse country of Spain. Join the adventurous protagonist, Day, as she immerses herself in the rich culture, history, and traditions of this enchanting land. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the sun-soaked beaches of Barcelona, Day encounters fascinating characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and experiences the true essence of Spain. With each new discovery, Day's perspective on life and herself undergoes a profound transformation, making this an unforgettable tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural exploration, Self-discovery, Adventure
