Synopsis: In "The Daft Days" by Neil Munro, a small Scottish town is preparing for the festive season, but the arrival of a mysterious stranger disrupts the peaceful atmosphere. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected events, they must confront their own fears and desires. With its intriguing characters and atmospheric setting, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Redemption, Community dynamics
