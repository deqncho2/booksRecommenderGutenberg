Synopsis: 
In 'Mathematical Recreations and Essays', W. W. Rouse Ball takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of mathematics, filled with intriguing puzzles, mind-bending problems, and fascinating historical anecdotes. From exploring the mysteries of number theory to unraveling the secrets of geometric shapes, this collection of mathematical recreations will challenge and delight both novice and expert mathematicians alike. Prepare to be enthralled by the beauty and elegance of mathematical concepts as Ball invites you to join him in unraveling the mysteries of the mathematical universe.

Genres: 
Mathematics, Puzzle, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Mathematical puzzles, Number theory, Geometry, History of mathematics
