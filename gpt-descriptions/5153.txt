Synopsis: 
'Rung Ho! A Novel' by Mundy, Talbot takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the dangerous world of espionage and revolution in early 20th century China. The story follows the protagonist, Captain Athelstan King, a British officer who finds himself caught in the midst of a secret society's plot to overthrow the ruling powers. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Captain King must decide where his loyalties truly lie and face the consequences of his choices. With its gripping narrative and rich historical backdrop, 'Rung Ho!' promises an exhilarating journey into a world of intrigue, danger, and moral dilemmas.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Revolution, Identity
