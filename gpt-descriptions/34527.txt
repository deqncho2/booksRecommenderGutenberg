Synopsis: In 'Makers' by Cory Doctorow, a group of inventors and entrepreneurs embark on a journey to revolutionize the world through their innovative creations. As they navigate the challenges of corporate greed, government interference, and the ever-changing landscape of technology, they must confront the consequences of their own success. With a blend of humor, imagination, and thought-provoking ideas, 'Makers' takes readers on a thrilling ride through a future where anyone can be a maker and change the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Techno-thriller, Satire

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Corporate Power, Collaboration, Consequences of Technology
