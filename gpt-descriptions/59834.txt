Synopsis: 
In "My Young Master: A Novel" by Read, Opie Percival, we are introduced to a young and ambitious protagonist, Oliver, who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when he becomes the personal assistant to a wealthy and enigmatic young master. As Oliver delves deeper into his master's world, he uncovers a hidden society filled with intrigue, forbidden desires, and dangerous alliances. With each revelation, Oliver's loyalty and determination are put to the test, forcing him to question his own identity and the true nature of those around him. As the plot unfolds, readers will be captivated by the unpredictable twists and turns, and the complex relationships that develop between the characters.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Secrets, Power dynamics
