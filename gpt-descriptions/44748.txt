Synopsis: In 'The Witch Hypnotizer' by Maher, Zena A., a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden power within herself that allows her to hypnotize others. As she delves deeper into her abilities, she becomes entangled in a dangerous world of magic, secrets, and manipulation. With her newfound skills, Lily must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems. Will she be able to control her powers and uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and control, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and manipulation
