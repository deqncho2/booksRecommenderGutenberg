Synopsis: 
In 'Cinderella Jane' by Marjorie Benton Cooke, we are introduced to Jane, a young woman living in a small town who dreams of a life beyond her current circumstances. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Jane finds herself embarking on a journey that will challenge her beliefs, test her resilience, and ultimately lead her to discover her true worth. As she navigates through the ups and downs of her new life, Jane must confront societal expectations, face her fears, and find the strength to follow her heart. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity, Pursuit of dreams
