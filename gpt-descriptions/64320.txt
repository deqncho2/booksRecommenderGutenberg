Synopsis:
'The Manchester Man' by Mrs. G. Linnaeus Banks is a captivating historical novel set in the bustling city of Manchester during the early 19th century. The story follows the life of Jabez Clegg, a resilient and ambitious young man who rises from poverty to become a prominent figure in the industrial revolution. As Jabez navigates the challenges of love, family, and social class, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to unravel everything he has worked for. With vivid descriptions of the era's social and economic landscape, this gripping tale explores the triumphs and tribulations of one man's journey through a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social mobility, Industrial revolution, Love and betrayal
