Synopsis: In 'War in Heaven' by Pratt, Fletcher, a young scholar stumbles upon a mysterious manuscript that reveals a hidden history of supernatural beings and their ongoing battle for control over the fate of humanity. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the manuscript, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue, where ancient prophecies, forbidden knowledge, and divine intervention collide. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own beliefs to uncover the truth and prevent an apocalyptic war from consuming everything he holds dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Fate and Free Will, Mythology and Religion
