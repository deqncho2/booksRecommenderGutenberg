Synopsis: In 'Royal Children of English History', E. Nesbit takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the young royals who shaped English history. From the courageous and enigmatic Richard the Lionheart to the brilliant and tragic Lady Jane Grey, Nesbit brings these historical figures to life, revealing their triumphs, struggles, and the extraordinary circumstances they faced. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of royalty and the impact it had on the lives of these remarkable children.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Children's literature

Themes: Royalty, Power and politics, Identity and self-discovery
