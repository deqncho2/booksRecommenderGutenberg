Synopsis: In 'A Daughter of Raasay: A Tale of the '45' by Raine, William MacLeod, a young woman named Fiona finds herself caught in the midst of the Jacobite uprising of 1745. As she navigates the treacherous political landscape and faces personal challenges, Fiona's loyalty and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Scottish Highlands, this captivating tale of love, betrayal, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Resilience, Political Intrigue
