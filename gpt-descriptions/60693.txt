Synopsis: 

In 'Growing Season' by Wallace, F. L., a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that alters the lives of its residents forever. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the community must come together to confront their shared past and uncertain future. With vivid characters and a gripping narrative, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Community, Secrets, Redemption, Forgiveness
