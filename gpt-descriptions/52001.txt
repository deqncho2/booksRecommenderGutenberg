Synopsis: In 'The Little Child's Book of Divinity: or Grandmamma's Stories about Bible Doctrines', author John R. Macduff weaves a collection of captivating stories that introduce young readers to important biblical concepts. Through the eyes of Grandmamma, children embark on a journey filled with enchanting tales that explore the wonders of God's creation, the power of prayer, the meaning of faith, and the importance of love and forgiveness. Each story is beautifully crafted to engage young minds and ignite their curiosity about the divine mysteries that shape our world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Fiction

Themes: Bible Doctrines, Faith, Love, Forgiveness, Prayer
