Synopsis: 
'35 Sonnets' by Fernando Pessoa is a captivating collection of poetic musings that delve into the depths of human emotions and the complexities of existence. Through the lens of 35 beautifully crafted sonnets, Pessoa explores themes of love, longing, identity, and the enigmatic nature of the self. Each sonnet is a window into the poet's soul, inviting readers to embark on a profound journey of self-reflection and introspection. With lyrical language and poignant imagery, Pessoa's '35 Sonnets' is a mesmerizing exploration of the human experience.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Identity, Existence, Self-reflection
