Synopsis: 

In 'Half-Hours with the Stars: A Plain and Easy Guide to the Knowledge of the Constellations', Richard A. Proctor takes readers on a captivating journey through the night sky, unraveling the secrets of the constellations. With his engaging storytelling and accessible explanations, Proctor invites readers to explore the wonders of the universe and discover the hidden stories behind the stars. From the ancient myths that inspired the naming of the constellations to the scientific discoveries that have shaped our understanding of the cosmos, this book offers a fascinating blend of history, mythology, and astronomy. Whether you are a seasoned stargazer or a curious beginner, 'Half-Hours with the Stars' will ignite your imagination and deepen your appreciation for the celestial wonders above.

Genres: Non-fiction, Astronomy, Science

Themes: Astronomy, Mythology, Education
