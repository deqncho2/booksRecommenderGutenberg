Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old Philadelphia', we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Lucy as she navigates the bustling streets of 18th-century Philadelphia. From her encounters with historical figures to her adventures in the city's vibrant markets, Lucy's story is filled with charm, mystery, and unexpected friendships. As she immerses herself in the rich history and culture of her surroundings, Lucy discovers the power of resilience and the importance of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, History, Friendship, Resilience
