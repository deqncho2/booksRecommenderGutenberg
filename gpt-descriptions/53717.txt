Synopsis: 

In "Through the Sikh War: A Tale of the Conquest of the Punjaub," George Alfred Henty takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the tumultuous Sikh War in 19th-century India. The story follows the adventures of young Charlie Marryat, an English lad who finds himself caught up in the conflict between the British East India Company and the powerful Sikh Empire. As Charlie navigates the treacherous landscape of war, he must rely on his courage, resourcefulness, and newfound friendships to survive and make a difference in this gripping historical tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival, Conflict
