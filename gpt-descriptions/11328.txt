Synopsis: In 'The Hunted Woman' by James Oliver Curwood, a young woman finds herself caught in a dangerous game of love, betrayal, and survival in the untamed wilderness of the Canadian North. As she flees from a sinister past, she encounters a mysterious man who becomes both her protector and her greatest threat. With breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Forbidden Love
