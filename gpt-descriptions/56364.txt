Synopsis: In the year 1746, amidst the political turmoil and Jacobite uprising in England, a young woman named Georgiana finds herself caught in a dangerous web of love and peril. As she embarks on a daring flight to escape her oppressive circumstances, she must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious allies, and face unexpected betrayals. With her life hanging in the balance, Georgiana's journey becomes a thrilling tale of survival, resilience, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue, Resilience and Survival
