Synopsis: In 'The Awakening: (The Resurrection)' by Leo Tolstoy, a young nobleman finds himself caught in a web of passion, guilt, and societal expectations. As he navigates through the complexities of love and morality, he is forced to confront the consequences of his actions and question the very foundations of his existence. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Russia, this gripping tale explores the depths of human desires and the search for redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Philosophical

Themes: Love and desire, Morality and ethics, Social class and societal expectations
