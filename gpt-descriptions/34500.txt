Synopsis: In 'The Gold Brick' by Ann S. Stephens, a mysterious gold brick becomes the catalyst for a thrilling tale of greed, deception, and unexpected alliances. Set in the bustling city of New York during the mid-19th century, the story follows a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the treacherous world of wealth and power. As the gold brick changes hands, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and lives are forever altered. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, 'The Gold Brick' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Deception, Power, Loyalty
