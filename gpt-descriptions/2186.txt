Synopsis: 
"Captains Courageous: A Story of the Grand Banks" follows the journey of Harvey Cheyne, a spoiled and arrogant young boy who falls overboard from a luxury ocean liner and is rescued by a fishing schooner. Forced to adapt to the harsh realities of life at sea, Harvey learns valuable lessons about hard work, humility, and the importance of human connection. As he navigates the challenges of the fishing industry and forms unexpected friendships, Harvey's transformation becomes a captivating tale of personal growth and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Maritime fiction

Themes: Transformation, Hard work, Humility, Friendship, Self-discovery
