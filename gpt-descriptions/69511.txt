Synopsis: In "Ben Hardy's Flying Machine; or, Making a Record for Himself" by Frank V. Webster, young Ben Hardy dreams of soaring through the skies and sets out to build his own flying machine. With determination and ingenuity, Ben faces numerous challenges and setbacks, but his unwavering spirit propels him forward. As he navigates the complexities of flight, he discovers the true meaning of perseverance and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Determination, Pursuit of Dreams, Overcoming Challenges
