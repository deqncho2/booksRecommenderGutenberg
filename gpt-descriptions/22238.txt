Synopsis: 

In "Charles Carleton Coffin: War Correspondent, Traveller, Author, and Statesman" by William Elliot Griffis, embark on a captivating journey through the life of an extraordinary individual. Charles Carleton Coffin, a renowned war correspondent, takes readers on a thrilling adventure as he travels to the frontlines of some of the most significant conflicts in history. From the American Civil War to the Franco-Prussian War and beyond, Coffin's firsthand accounts provide a gripping narrative that brings the chaos and heroism of war to life. As the story unfolds, readers will witness Coffin's transformation from a fearless journalist to a respected author and influential statesman. 

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: War, Journalism, Personal Growth
