Synopsis: In 'Drawings of Rossetti' by T. Martin Wood, a mysterious collection of drawings by the renowned artist Dante Gabriel Rossetti is discovered, hidden away for decades. As art historian Emma delves into the enigmatic world of Rossetti's sketches, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, passion, and betrayal. With each drawing she uncovers, Emma's own life begins to mirror the tumultuous relationships depicted on the pages, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and artistic awakening. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Art

Themes: Secrets, Passion, Betrayal, Self-discovery, Artistic expression
