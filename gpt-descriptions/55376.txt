Synopsis: In 'The Piccadilly Puzzle: A Mysterious Story' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances in his opulent London mansion. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances, leading him on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of Victorian society. With each twist and turn, the puzzle becomes more intricate, challenging the detective's wit and determination to solve the enigma before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue, Class Divide
