Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, April 26 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian London. Through a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentary, this volume offers a delightful glimpse into the absurdities and eccentricities of the era. From political satire to social observations, 'Punch' presents a captivating snapshot of Victorian society, leaving readers entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Social Commentary

Themes: Victorian society, Politics, Social norms
