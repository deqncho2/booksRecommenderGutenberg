Synopsis: In 'Captain Boldheart & the Latin-Grammar Master: A Holiday Romance from the Pen of Lieut-Col. Robin Redforth, aged 9', Charles Dickens takes us on a thrilling adventure with young Captain Boldheart. When the Latin-Grammar Master threatens to ruin his holiday, Captain Boldheart must summon all his courage and wit to outsmart his formidable foe. With pirates, treasure, and daring escapades, this holiday romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Satire

Themes: Courage, Imagination, Education
