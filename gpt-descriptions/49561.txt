Synopsis: 
'From Peking to Mandalay: A Journey from North China to Burma through Tibetan Ssuch'uan and Yunnan' takes readers on an extraordinary adventure through the heart of Asia. Sir Reginald Fleming Johnston recounts his captivating journey, filled with breathtaking landscapes, encounters with diverse cultures, and unexpected challenges. From the bustling streets of Peking to the mystical mountains of Tibet, and finally to the enchanting city of Mandalay in Burma, Johnston's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes transport readers to a world of wonder and discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Personal Growth
