Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys on the Mexican Border' by Gerald Breckenridge, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves embroiled in a dangerous mystery while on a trip to the Mexican border. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter suspicious characters, the boys must use their wit and radio skills to unravel the secrets that lie within the border town. With thrilling twists and unexpected turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
