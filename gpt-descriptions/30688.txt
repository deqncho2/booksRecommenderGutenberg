Synopsis: 
'Letters of a Radio-Engineer to His Son' is a captivating collection of letters written by a radio-engineer to his son, chronicling their unique bond and the wisdom imparted through their correspondence. Through these heartfelt letters, the reader is taken on a journey of self-discovery, technological marvels, and the power of human connection. As the son navigates his own path in life, he learns invaluable lessons about love, ambition, and the importance of embracing one's true passions. 

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes: 
Father-son relationship, Pursuit of knowledge, Technological advancements, Self-discovery
