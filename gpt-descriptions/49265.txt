Synopsis: In "The Sisters of Lady Jane Grey and Their Wicked Grandfather," author Richard Davey delves into the captivating true stories of Charles Brandon, Duke of Suffolk, and the Ladies Katherine and Mary Grey. Set against the backdrop of Tudor England, this book uncovers the intriguing lives of these historical figures, exploring their relationships, ambitions, and the scandals that surrounded them. From political intrigue to forbidden love, the lives of the Grey sisters and their notorious grandfather are brought to life in this gripping narrative.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Power and ambition, Family dynamics, Forbidden love, Political intrigue
