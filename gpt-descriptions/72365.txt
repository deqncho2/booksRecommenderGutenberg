Synopsis: 

'At home in the Smokies: A History Handbook for Great Smoky Mountains National Park, North Carolina and Tennessee' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and natural wonders of the Great Smoky Mountains National Park. From the early Native American inhabitants to the pioneers and settlers who shaped the region, this book delves into the fascinating stories and events that have shaped this iconic national park. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, the authors provide a comprehensive guide that will leave readers eager to explore the park's breathtaking landscapes and discover the hidden treasures within.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Nature conservation, Cultural heritage, Exploration and discovery
