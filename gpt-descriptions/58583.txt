Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of John Stuart Mill' is a comprehensive collection of the works of the influential philosopher and political economist, John Stuart Mill. This compilation includes a wide range of Mill's writings, covering topics such as ethics, political philosophy, economics, and social reform. From his groundbreaking treatise on utilitarianism to his insightful analysis of individual liberty and women's rights, this index provides a valuable resource for anyone interested in exploring the profound ideas of John Stuart Mill.

Genres: Philosophy, Political Science, Economics

Themes: Utilitarianism, Individual Liberty, Social Reform
