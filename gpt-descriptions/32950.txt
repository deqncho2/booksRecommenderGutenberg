Synopsis: 
In 'Camp and Trail', join the adventurous Stewart Edward White as he embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. From encountering majestic wildlife to facing the challenges of survival, White's captivating tales of his outdoor escapades will transport readers to a world of breathtaking beauty and heart-pounding excitement. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, this book is a must-read for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Nature appreciation, Survival skills
