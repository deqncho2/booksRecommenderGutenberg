Synopsis: In "The Hero of Manila: Dewey on the Mississippi and the Pacific," Rossiter Johnson takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the life of Admiral George Dewey. From his humble beginnings on the Mississippi River to his pivotal role in the Pacific during the Spanish-American War, Dewey's remarkable story unfolds with gripping detail and captivating anecdotes. As readers delve into Dewey's adventures, they witness his unwavering determination, strategic brilliance, and unwavering commitment to duty. From naval battles to political maneuvering, this book offers a thrilling account of one man's rise to become a national hero.

Genres: Biography, History, Military

Themes: Heroism, Leadership, Patriotism
