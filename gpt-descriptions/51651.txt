Synopsis: In 'Conditionally Human' by Walter M. Miller, a brilliant scientist named Dr. James Morgan is faced with a moral dilemma when he discovers a groundbreaking technology that could change the course of humanity. As he grapples with the implications of his invention, he must navigate a world filled with ethical questions, personal sacrifices, and the consequences of playing god. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Dr. Morgan must make a decision that will challenge his own humanity and the very essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Moral Dilemma

Themes: Ethics, Technological Advancement, Human Nature
