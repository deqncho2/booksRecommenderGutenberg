Synopsis: In 'Experiments on the Spoilage of Tomato Ketchup', Bitting takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of food preservation. Through meticulous research and intriguing experiments, he uncovers the secrets behind the spoilage of tomato ketchup, revealing surprising insights that will forever change the way we view this beloved condiment. As Bitting delves deeper into the science behind food preservation, readers will be captivated by the unexpected twists and turns that unfold, leaving them hungry for more knowledge about the mysteries lurking in their own pantries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Food Studies

Themes: Food preservation, Microbiology, Culinary science
