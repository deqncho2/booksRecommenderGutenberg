Synopsis: In 'Sturdy and Strong; Or, How George Andrews Made His Way', follow the remarkable journey of George Andrews as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of life. From his humble beginnings, George's determination and resilience propel him forward, leading him to unexpected opportunities and encounters. As he faces adversity and learns valuable life lessons, George's character is tested, and he must summon his inner strength to overcome the obstacles in his path. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following George's inspiring journey towards success and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Self-improvement, Social mobility
