Synopsis: 

In 'British Butterflies: Figures and Descriptions of Every Native Species', author W. S. Coleman takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of British butterflies. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this comprehensive guide introduces us to the diverse array of native species found in the British Isles. From the delicate and elusive Swallowtail to the vibrant and common Red Admiral, each butterfly is brought to life through Coleman's meticulous observations and detailed depictions. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will not only gain a deeper understanding of these fascinating creatures but also discover the intricate connections between butterflies and their natural habitats. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Field Guide

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural Beauty
