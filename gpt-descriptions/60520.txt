Synopsis: 
In 'The Marrying Man' by Joseph Farrell, a charismatic and enigmatic man named Jack finds himself entangled in a web of love, passion, and danger. Set against the backdrop of 1920s New York City, Jack's life takes an unexpected turn when he meets the captivating and mysterious Evelyn. As their relationship intensifies, Jack becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of secrets and betrayal, where the line between love and obsession becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of these captivating characters.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and obsession, Betrayal, Secrets and deception
