Synopsis: In 'Whoso Findeth a Wife' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Muriel Chetwynd finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes entangled with a charming but enigmatic stranger. As their paths intertwine, Muriel discovers that there is much more to her new acquaintance than meets the eye, and she must navigate a treacherous world of secrets and deceit to uncover the truth. With her life hanging in the balance, Muriel must decide who she can trust and what she is willing to sacrifice to protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Deception, Trust, Love, Loyalty
