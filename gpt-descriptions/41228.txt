Synopsis: In "Guy Deverell, v. 1 of 2" by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a young and ambitious lawyer named Guy Deverell finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous web of secrets and deceit. As he delves deeper into the case of a murder trial, he uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to unravel his own life. With his reputation and future at stake, Guy must navigate through a labyrinth of intrigue, romance, and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Justice, Love and obsession
