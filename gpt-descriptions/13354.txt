Synopsis: In 'The Boy Knight: A Tale of the Crusades' by G. A. Henty, young Cuthbert, a brave and adventurous English boy, finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Crusades. Determined to prove himself on the battlefield, he embarks on a journey filled with danger, friendship, and unexpected twists. As Cuthbert faces the challenges of war and the clash of cultures, he must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. Will he emerge as a true knight and fulfill his quest for honor and glory?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Cultural Conflict, Personal Growth
