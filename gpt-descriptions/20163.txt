Synopsis: In 'The Jolliest School of All' by Angela Brazil, follow the adventures of a group of lively and mischievous schoolgirls as they navigate their way through a new term at a boarding school. From secret midnight feasts to daring escapades, the girls find themselves in a whirlwind of excitement and friendship. But as they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges, they must learn to rely on each other and discover the true meaning of loyalty and courage. 

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, School story

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage
