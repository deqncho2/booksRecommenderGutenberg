Synopsis: 
In 'Fate Knocks at the Door: A Novel' by Comfort, Will Levington, a young artist named John finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he receives a peculiar invitation to a secluded mansion. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house and its enigmatic inhabitants, John's perception of reality begins to blur, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and unexpected revelations. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a world where fate and destiny intertwine, leaving them eagerly anticipating the next twist in this captivating tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Fate, Identity, Perception, Secrets
