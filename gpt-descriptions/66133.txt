Synopsis: In 'Wampum and Old Gold' by Allen, Hervey, a young man named David embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious family heirloom. As he delves into the history of his ancestors, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the American Revolution, David's quest takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the frontier. With each step, he uncovers hidden connections that challenge his perception of his own identity and the world around him.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Love, Betrayal
