Synopsis: In 'Key Out of Time' by Andre Norton, a team of time-traveling archaeologists embarks on a perilous mission to a distant planet. As they explore the mysterious ruins of an ancient civilization, they uncover secrets that could alter the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn and the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, they must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront enigmatic beings to unlock the key to their survival.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Time Travel

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Survival
