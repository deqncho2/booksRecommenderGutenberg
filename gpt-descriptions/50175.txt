Synopsis: In 'Bird Watching' by Edmund Selous, a young woman embarks on a journey to a remote island, where she becomes captivated by the enchanting world of birds. As she delves deeper into her passion, she uncovers hidden secrets and forms unexpected connections with both the avian creatures and the island's inhabitants. With each bird she encounters, she unravels a new layer of her own identity, leading her on a transformative path of self-discovery and understanding.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Nature, Identity, Connection, Self-discovery
