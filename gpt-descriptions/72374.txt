Synopsis: In "The Emperor's Candlesticks" by Baroness Orczy, a captivating tale unfolds as two strangers become entangled in a web of espionage, romance, and intrigue. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century Europe, the story follows a mysterious woman and a charming gentleman who find themselves connected by a pair of exquisite candlesticks. As they navigate a world filled with secrets and danger, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Espionage, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
