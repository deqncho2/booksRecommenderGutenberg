Synopsis: 
In "Weird Tales from Northern Seas," Jonas Lie takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical landscapes of Norway. From haunted islands to enchanted forests, Lie weaves together a collection of eerie and atmospheric tales that will leave readers spellbound. Encounter vengeful spirits, mythical creatures, and supernatural occurrences as Lie delves into the dark corners of Norwegian folklore. With each story, the author masterfully blends elements of horror, mystery, and fantasy, creating a chilling and unforgettable reading experience.

Genres: 
Horror, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: 
Norwegian folklore, Supernatural occurrences, Mythical creatures
