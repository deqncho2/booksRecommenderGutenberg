Synopsis: 
'Goat-Feathers' by Butler, Ellis Parker is a hilarious and whimsical tale that follows the misadventures of a young man named George Helton. When George inherits a fortune, he decides to leave his small town and pursue a life of luxury in the big city. However, his plans quickly unravel as he becomes entangled in a series of absurd and comical situations involving eccentric characters, mistaken identities, and a mischievous goat. As George navigates through these outrageous predicaments, he learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of success and happiness.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Materialism, Identity, Happiness
