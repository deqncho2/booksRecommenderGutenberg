Synopsis: 

'Fighting the Traffic in Young Girls; Or, War on the White Slave Trade' takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of society, exposing the shocking reality of the white slave trade. Set in the early 20th century, this eye-opening narrative follows the courageous efforts of a group of individuals determined to dismantle the network of human trafficking that preys upon young girls. As they navigate treacherous paths and encounter unexpected allies and adversaries, the protagonists find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and the fight becomes more personal. Will they succeed in their mission to rescue the innocent victims and bring the perpetrators to justice?

Genres: Crime fiction, Historical fiction, Thriller

Themes: Human trafficking, Social justice, Courage, Morality
