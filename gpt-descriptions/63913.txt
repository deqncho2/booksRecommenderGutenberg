Synopsis: 

In the eighth volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative delves into the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, as well as the English occupation of Paris and Normandy. Enguerrand de Monstrelet provides a gripping account of the conflicts, the power struggles, and the memorable events that unfolded not only in France but also in other countries. From political intrigue to military campaigns, this volume offers a captivating glimpse into a pivotal era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes: Civil war, Power struggles, Political intrigue, Military campaigns
