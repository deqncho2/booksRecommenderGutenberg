Synopsis: 

'Notable Women Authors of the Day: Biographical Sketches' by Helen C. Black is a captivating collection of biographical sketches that introduces readers to the lives and works of some of the most influential women authors of the time. From the struggles they faced to the triumphs they achieved, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind these remarkable women and their literary contributions. 

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Women's empowerment, Literature, Gender roles
