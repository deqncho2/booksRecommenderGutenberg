Synopsis: 

In "Things a Boy Should Know About Electricity: Second Edition," Thomas M. St. John takes readers on an electrifying journey through the fascinating world of electricity. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice, covering everything from the basics of electrical circuits to advanced concepts like magnetism and electromagnetism. With engaging explanations and hands-on experiments, St. John encourages young readers to explore the wonders of electricity and sparks their curiosity to discover its endless possibilities. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Electricity, Science education, Curiosity, Exploration
