Synopsis: 
In 'The Herapath Property' by Fletcher, J.S., a young lawyer named Ronald Standish finds himself entangled in a mysterious case involving a valuable property and a series of unexplained deaths. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the Herapath estate, Standish uncovers a web of deceit, hidden identities, and long-buried family secrets. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront the sinister forces determined to keep the truth hidden. Will Standish be able to unravel the enigma of the Herapath Property before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Inheritance, Family Secrets
