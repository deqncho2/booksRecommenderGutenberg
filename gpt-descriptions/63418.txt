Synopsis: 
In 'Poor Blossom: The Story of a Horse' by Edith Carrington, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable horse named Blossom. From her humble beginnings as a foal, Blossom faces numerous challenges and hardships, but her indomitable spirit and unwavering determination propel her forward. As she navigates the world of horse racing, Blossom encounters both cruelty and kindness, forging unexpected friendships along the way. This heartwarming tale explores the bond between humans and animals, the resilience of the human spirit, and the power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: 
Fiction, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Resilience, Friendship, Animal Welfare
