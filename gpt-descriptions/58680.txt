Synopsis: In 'The Land of Content' by Edith Barnard Delano, a young woman named Alice embarks on a journey to a mysterious land where happiness and fulfillment are said to be guaranteed. As she navigates through enchanting landscapes and encounters intriguing characters, Alice discovers that the true meaning of contentment lies in unexpected places. With each step, she unravels the secrets of this extraordinary realm, leading her to question her own perceptions of happiness and the choices she has made in her own life.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: The pursuit of happiness, self-discovery, the power of perception
