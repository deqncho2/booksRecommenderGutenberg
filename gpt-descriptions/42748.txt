Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boys Overland; Or, A Long Trip for Fun and Fortune', three adventurous friends embark on an exhilarating journey across the country in their trusty motorcar. As they navigate through treacherous terrains, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, the boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure that tests their courage and resourcefulness. With their unwavering determination and quick thinking, they must overcome obstacles and unravel secrets to uncover the ultimate prize that awaits them. Will they succeed in their quest for both fun and fortune?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
