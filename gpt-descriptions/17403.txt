Synopsis: In 'The Cornet of Horse: A Tale of Marlborough's Wars', follow the thrilling journey of Ned Harley, a young Englishman who joins the army during the tumultuous times of the War of the Spanish Succession. As he rises through the ranks, Ned finds himself embroiled in epic battles, facing danger, and forging lifelong friendships. With his unwavering courage and determination, Ned must navigate the treacherous world of war, politics, and love, all while striving to prove himself on the battlefield. Will he emerge as a hero or succumb to the chaos of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Coming of Age, Friendship, Love
