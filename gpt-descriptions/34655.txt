Synopsis: 
'Folk Stories from Southern Nigeria, West Africa' by Dayrell, Elphinstone is a captivating collection of traditional tales that transport readers to the vibrant world of Southern Nigeria. From cunning animals outsmarting humans to mythical creatures and supernatural beings, these enchanting stories are filled with adventure, wisdom, and a touch of magic. Each tale offers a unique glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region, showcasing the power of storytelling to entertain, educate, and inspire.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Wisdom, Cultural Heritage, Supernatural Beings
