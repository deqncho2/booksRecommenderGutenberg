Synopsis: 
In 'T. Tembarom', Frances Hodgson Burnett takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young boy named Tembarom, who unexpectedly finds himself thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. Raised in poverty, Tembarom's life takes a dramatic turn when he is sent to live with his wealthy and eccentric English relatives. As he navigates this new world, Tembarom encounters a cast of colorful characters, including a beautiful young woman who captures his heart. With determination and resilience, Tembarom must learn to adapt to his new surroundings and face the challenges that come with his newfound status.

Genres: 
Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Social class, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
