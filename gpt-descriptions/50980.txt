Synopsis: 
In 'The Freelancer' by Robert Zacks, a talented and enigmatic freelancer finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he is hired for a seemingly routine job. As he delves deeper into the assignment, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of those he holds dear. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception and betrayal, using his unique skills to outsmart his enemies and uncover the truth. 

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Identity, Trust, Survival
