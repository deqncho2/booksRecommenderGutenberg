Synopsis: 
In 'Old Picture Books, With Other Essays on Bookish Subjects', Alfred W. Pollard takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of old picture books. With his keen eye for detail and deep appreciation for the artistry of these books, Pollard explores their historical significance, the stories they tell, and the impact they have had on generations of readers. From the intricate illustrations to the fascinating anecdotes behind their creation, this collection of essays offers a delightful exploration of the magic and charm of old picture books.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Art History

Themes: Nostalgia, Visual storytelling, Historical significance
