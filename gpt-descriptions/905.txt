Synopsis: 
'Within the Law: From the Play of Bayard Veiller' is a captivating and suspenseful story that follows Mary Turner, a young woman who has been wronged by society. Determined to seek justice, Mary takes matters into her own hands and embarks on a thrilling journey within the confines of the law. As she navigates the complexities of the legal system, Mary's resilience and cunning nature are put to the test, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Crime, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Morality
