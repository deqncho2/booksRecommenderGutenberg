Synopsis: In 'The Ethical Engineer' by Harry Harrison, a brilliant engineer named Conn Maxwell finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and ethical dilemmas when he is sent to a distant planet to oversee a crucial construction project. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of power struggles and corruption, Conn must grapple with his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the clash between personal ethics and societal expectations, leaving readers questioning the true nature of right and wrong.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Ethics, Power, Corruption, Morality
