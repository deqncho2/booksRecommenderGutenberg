Synopsis: 
In 'Quisisana; or, Rest at Last' by Friedrich Spielhagen, a mysterious and enchanting health resort nestled in the Italian Alps becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. As a diverse group of guests arrive seeking solace and healing, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to profound transformations and unforeseen consequences. Amidst the breathtaking beauty of the surroundings, secrets are unveiled, passions ignite, and the true nature of human desires is laid bare. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, 'Quisisana; or, Rest at Last' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey that explores the complexities of the human heart and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Betrayal and redemption, Self-discovery
