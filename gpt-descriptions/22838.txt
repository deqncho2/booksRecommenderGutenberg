Synopsis: 
In 'Country Lodgings' by Mary Russell Mitford, a young woman named Lucy finds herself in a charming country village, seeking solace and a fresh start. As she settles into her new lodgings, she becomes entangled in the lives of the eccentric locals, each with their own secrets and desires. From the enigmatic artist to the mysterious recluse, Lucy discovers that the idyllic countryside is not as peaceful as it seems. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Country Lodgings' takes readers on a captivating journey through love, betrayal, and the power of community.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Secrets, Community, Redemption
