Synopsis: In the midst of the American Civil War, a small town in Illinois becomes a battleground for conflicting loyalties and moral dilemmas. The Copperhead follows the story of Abner Beech, a farmer and fervent opponent of the war, as he navigates the treacherous waters of divided allegiances and the consequences of his beliefs. As tensions rise and violence erupts, Abner must confront the true cost of his principles and the impact they have on his family and community.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Consequences of Beliefs
