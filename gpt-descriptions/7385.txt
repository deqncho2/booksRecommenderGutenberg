Synopsis: 
'Old Calabria' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting region of Calabria in southern Italy. Douglas, Norman, an intrepid traveler and writer, immerses himself in the rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes of this lesser-known part of Italy. From exploring ancient ruins and picturesque villages to encountering fascinating locals and indulging in mouthwatering cuisine, Douglas paints a vivid and evocative portrait of Calabria that will leave readers yearning to experience it for themselves.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, History, Local traditions, Culinary delights
