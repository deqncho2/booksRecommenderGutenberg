Synopsis: 

In the enchanting setting of the New Forest, a young and spirited Cradock Nowell finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. As he navigates the complexities of his relationships, Cradock's journey takes him through the lush landscapes of the forest, where secrets are buried deep within its ancient trees. With his fate hanging in the balance, Cradock must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Revenge, Nature and the Environment
