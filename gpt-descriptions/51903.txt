Synopsis: 
'The Life, Times, and Scientific Labours of the Second Marquis of Worcester: To which is added a reprint of his Century of Inventions, 1663, with a Commentary thereon' delves into the fascinating life and groundbreaking scientific achievements of the Second Marquis of Worcester. This book explores the remarkable inventions and experiments conducted by the Marquis, providing a captivating glimpse into the mind of a visionary thinker. With a reprint of his 'Century of Inventions' accompanied by insightful commentary, readers are taken on a journey through the innovative mind of a historical figure who pushed the boundaries of scientific knowledge.

Genres: Biography, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Scientific Advancement, Historical Exploration
