Synopsis: In 'Camp Lenape on the Long Trail' by Carl Saxon, a group of teenagers embarks on a thrilling adventure in the wilderness of the Appalachian Mountains. As they navigate the rugged terrain, they uncover a mysterious secret that puts their survival skills to the test. With danger lurking at every turn, the campers must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to unravel the enigma and make it out alive. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies hidden in the heart of the mountains.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Discovery
