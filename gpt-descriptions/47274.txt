Synopsis: 

In 'History of Company K. 1st (Inft,) Penn'a Reserves', author H. N. Minnigh takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a remarkable group of soldiers during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Minnigh brings to life the trials and triumphs of Company K, offering a unique perspective on the war that forever changed the nation. From the chaos of the battlefield to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this gripping account sheds light on the untold stories of these brave men and the impact they had on history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Historical significance
