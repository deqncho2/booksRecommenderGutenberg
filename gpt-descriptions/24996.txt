Synopsis: In 'The Tapu of Banderah: 1901' by Louis Becke, a young sailor named Jack is shipwrecked on a remote island in the South Pacific. As he tries to survive and find a way back home, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger. Jack soon discovers that the island is governed by an ancient tapu, a sacred taboo that holds the islanders in fear and superstition. With the help of a local girl named Tessa, Jack must navigate the treacherous waters of tribal customs and forbidden love, all while uncovering the dark secrets that lie beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic paradise.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Cultural clash, Forbidden love, Survival
