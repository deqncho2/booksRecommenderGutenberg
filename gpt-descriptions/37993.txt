Synopsis: 

In 'Climbing in The British Isles. Vol. 1 - England', W. P. Haskett Smith takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the stunning landscapes of England, revealing the hidden world of rock climbing. From the rugged peaks of the Lake District to the dramatic cliffs of Cornwall, Smith's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes transport readers to the heart of the action. As he recounts his daring ascents and encounters with treacherous terrain, readers will be captivated by the thrill and danger of this extreme sport. 

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Sports

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Risk-taking
