Synopsis: In "Cora and The Doctor; or, Revelations of A Physician's Wife" by Madeline Leslie, readers are introduced to Cora, a young woman who marries a respected doctor. As she settles into her new life, Cora begins to uncover secrets and revelations about her husband's profession that challenge her understanding of medicine, morality, and the role of women in society. With each revelation, Cora's world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront her own beliefs and make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating novel delves into the complexities of marriage, societal expectations, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Marriage, Gender Roles, Morality, Truth
