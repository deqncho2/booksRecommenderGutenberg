Synopsis: In 'Samantha on the Race Problem' by Marietta Holley, Samantha, a witty and outspoken woman, finds herself embroiled in a heated debate on the race problem. As she navigates through various encounters and conversations, Samantha's sharp observations and clever insights shed light on the complexities of racial tensions in society. With her unique perspective and unwavering determination, Samantha challenges the status quo and sparks a thought-provoking discussion that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and biases.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Racial tensions, Social justice, Equality, Prejudice
