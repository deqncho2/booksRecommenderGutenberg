Synopsis: In the small village of Roden's Corner, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the peaceful lives of its inhabitants. As the stranger's presence becomes increasingly unsettling, the villagers find themselves caught in a web of suspicion, betrayal, and hidden desires. With tensions rising and secrets being exposed, the once idyllic community is forced to confront the darkness that lies beneath its surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Human nature
