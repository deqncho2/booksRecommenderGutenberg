Synopsis: In 'The Princess And The Jewel Doctor: 1905' by Hichens, Robert, a mysterious princess seeks the help of a renowned jewel doctor to solve a perplexing problem. As they delve into the world of precious gems and hidden secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each step closer to the truth, the princess and the jewel doctor must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of love and sacrifice
