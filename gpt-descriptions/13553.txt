Synopsis: In 'The Tidal Wave and Other Stories', Ethel M. Dell takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that explore the depths of human emotions and the unexpected twists of fate. From tales of love and loss to stories of redemption and second chances, each narrative presents a unique and gripping narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Whether it's a young woman's quest for independence, a mysterious stranger's arrival in a small village, or a life-changing decision made in a moment of crisis, Dell's stories are filled with suspense, romance, and the complexities of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Fate and destiny, Redemption, Self-discovery
