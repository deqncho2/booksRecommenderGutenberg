Synopsis: 
In 'The Mother's Dream, and Other Poems' by Hannah Flagg Gould, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of heartfelt and thought-provoking poems. From the tender musings of a mother's dreams for her child to the exploration of nature's beauty and the complexities of human emotions, Gould's words paint vivid pictures and evoke deep emotions. Each poem is a window into the human experience, offering glimpses of love, loss, hope, and resilience. With lyrical language and a keen eye for detail, 'The Mother's Dream, and Other Poems' is a captivating exploration of life's joys and sorrows, leaving readers with a renewed appreciation for the power of poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Motherhood, Nature, Love, Loss, Hope, Resilience
