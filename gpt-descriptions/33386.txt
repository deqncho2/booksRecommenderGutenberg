Synopsis: In 'The Tremendous Event' by Maurice Leblanc, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious letter arrives, foretelling a shocking event that will occur in just a few days. As the clock ticks down, he must navigate a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth behind this extraordinary event. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the climactic revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime and investigation, Deception and hidden motives
