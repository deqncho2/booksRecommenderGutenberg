Synopsis: In 'Against the Tide' by Bedford-Jones, H., a young and ambitious sailor finds himself entangled in a treacherous web of mystery and danger. When he stumbles upon a hidden island, he becomes embroiled in a battle against a notorious pirate captain and his ruthless crew. As he fights for survival and seeks to uncover the island's secrets, he must navigate through treacherous waters, face deadly adversaries, and confront his own inner demons. With every twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the sailor must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to stay afloat in this thrilling maritime adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Courage, Exploration, Redemption
