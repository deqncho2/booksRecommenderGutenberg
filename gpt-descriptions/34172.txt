Synopsis: In "Peter's Rock in Mohammed's Flood, from St. Gregory the Great to St. Leo III," T. W. Allies takes readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the fascinating encounters between Christianity and Islam during the early medieval period. From the rise of Islam to the reigns of two influential popes, Allies delves into the complex dynamics between these two major religions, shedding light on the challenges faced by the Church and the profound impact these encounters had on the development of Christianity. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Allies unveils a compelling narrative that unveils the struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacies of this pivotal era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Cultural studies

Themes: Interreligious dialogue, Clash of civilizations, Historical impact of religious encounters
