Synopsis: In 'Copper Streak Trail' by Eugene Manlove Rhodes, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Copper Streak, bringing with him a dark past and a hidden agenda. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of secrets, loyalties are tested, alliances are formed, and the line between right and wrong becomes blurred. With danger lurking around every corner, the fate of Copper Streak hangs in the balance, and only the most courageous will survive.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
