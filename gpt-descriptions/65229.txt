Synopsis: In 'Hold Back Tomorrow' by Kris Neville, a mysterious phenomenon known as the "Time Slip" has caused the world to become divided into two distinct time periods. As a group of survivors navigate this fractured reality, they must confront the consequences of their actions and the moral dilemmas that arise when faced with the opportunity to change the past. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking scenarios, this gripping novel explores the complexities of time travel and the profound impact it can have on individuals and society.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Time travel, Morality, Consequences, Survival
