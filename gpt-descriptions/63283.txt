Synopsis: 

'New Mexico Magazine's A Taste of New Mexico Kitchens' takes readers on a mouthwatering journey through the vibrant and diverse culinary landscape of New Mexico. From traditional family recipes passed down through generations to innovative creations by renowned chefs, this book offers a tantalizing glimpse into the heart and soul of New Mexican cuisine. With stunning photography and captivating stories, it explores the rich cultural heritage and unique flavors that make this region's kitchens truly special. Whether you're a seasoned foodie or simply curious about the flavors of the Southwest, this book will leave you hungry for more.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Travel

Themes: Culinary traditions, Cultural heritage, Regional cuisine
