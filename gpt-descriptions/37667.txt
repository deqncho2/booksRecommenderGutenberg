Synopsis: In "Three Hours after Marriage," three friends embark on a hilarious and unpredictable journey filled with mistaken identities, romantic entanglements, and witty banter. As they navigate through a series of comical situations, their lives become intertwined with a cast of eccentric characters, leading to unexpected revelations and uproarious consequences. This delightful comedy of errors will keep readers guessing and laughing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Love and marriage, Identity and deception, Social satire
