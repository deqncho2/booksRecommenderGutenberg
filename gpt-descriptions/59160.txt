Synopsis: In 'The 3rd Party' by Holum, Lee B., a seemingly ordinary couple's lives are turned upside down when they discover they are being watched by a mysterious third party. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding this enigmatic figure, they find themselves caught in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With their relationship and sanity at stake, they must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Surveillance, Trust, Identity
