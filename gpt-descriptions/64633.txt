Synopsis: In 'The Ways of War' by Tom Kettle, a young soldier finds himself caught in the midst of a brutal war, where he must navigate the treacherous landscapes of love, loyalty, and sacrifice. As he grapples with the horrors of battle and the complexities of human nature, he discovers that the true enemy may not always be found on the battlefield. With unexpected twists and heart-wrenching moments, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: The human cost of war, the complexities of morality, the power of love and loyalty
