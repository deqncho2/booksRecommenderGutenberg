Synopsis: 

In 'Hours with the Mystics: A Contribution to the History of Religious Opinion', Robert Alfred Vaughan takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and teachings of some of the most enigmatic figures in religious history. From the ancient mystics of the East to the Christian mystics of the Middle Ages, Vaughan delves into their profound experiences, mystical visions, and spiritual insights. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he explores the depths of their devotion, the struggles they faced, and the profound impact they had on religious thought. As readers immerse themselves in the lives of these mystics, they will be inspired to contemplate the mysteries of faith and the boundless possibilities of the human spirit.

Genres: Religious literature, Biography, History

Themes: Mysticism, Spiritual enlightenment, Religious devotion
