Synopsis: 
In 'Peter Parley's Visit to London, During the Coronation of Queen Victoria', young Peter Parley embarks on an exciting journey to London during the historic coronation of Queen Victoria. As he explores the bustling city, Peter encounters a myriad of fascinating characters, witnesses grand ceremonies, and experiences the vibrant atmosphere of the capital. From the Tower of London to the majestic Westminster Abbey, Peter's adventures offer a captivating glimpse into the heart of Victorian England.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Children's literature

Themes: Exploration, History, Monarchy, Cultural immersion
