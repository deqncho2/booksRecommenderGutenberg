Synopsis: In "The Eldest Son" by John Galsworthy, the lives of the Forsyte family are explored through the eyes of the eldest son, Joseph. As he navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations, Joseph finds himself torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this gripping tale delves into the intricate dynamics of family relationships and the struggle for personal freedom.

Genres: Drama, Family Saga, Social Commentary

Themes: Family dynamics, Love and marriage, Social class, Personal freedom
