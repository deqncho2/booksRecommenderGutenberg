Synopsis: In 'Puppet Government' by Revelle, George, a young journalist stumbles upon a shocking secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, manipulation, and political intrigue. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where nothing is as it seems, and the truth is a dangerous commodity. Will he uncover the truth and expose the puppeteers pulling the strings, or will he become just another pawn in their game?

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Power, Truth
