Synopsis: In 'The Hunted Outlaw, or, Donald Morrison, the Canadian Rob Roy', we follow the thrilling adventures of Donald Morrison, a notorious outlaw in Canada. Falsely accused of a crime he did not commit, Morrison finds himself on the run from the law, navigating treacherous landscapes and encountering a cast of colorful characters along the way. As he fights for his freedom and seeks justice, Morrison's cunning and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to clear his name and find redemption, or will he forever be known as the Canadian Rob Roy?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Crime

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Survival
