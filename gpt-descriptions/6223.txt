Synopsis: In 'The March of the White Guard' by Parker Gilbert, a young soldier finds himself torn between duty and love as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war-torn Europe. Set against the backdrop of World War I, this gripping tale follows the protagonist's journey through the chaos and uncertainty of battle, where he must confront his own beliefs and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. As the war rages on, the young soldier's loyalty, courage, and resilience are put to the ultimate test, leading to unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching sacrifices.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Duty and Honor, Love and Sacrifice, Morality in Times of War
