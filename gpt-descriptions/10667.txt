Synopsis: In 'Snake and Sword: A Novel' by Wren, Percival Christopher, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time. With each step closer to the truth, she must confront her own demons and make choices that will determine the fate of not only the artifact but also her own life. This gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of ancient civilizations, Betrayal, Self-discovery
