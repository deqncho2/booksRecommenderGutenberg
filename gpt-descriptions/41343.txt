Synopsis: 

In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 15, Slice 1 takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating world of Italy to the intriguing Jacobite Church. This volume delves into the rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse landscapes of Italy, exploring its ancient ruins, magnificent art, and influential figures. From the enchanting cities of Rome, Florence, and Venice to the picturesque countryside, readers will uncover the secrets and wonders of this remarkable country. Moving beyond Italy, the volume also sheds light on the fascinating Jacobite Church, a religious movement with a complex history and a unique perspective on faith. 

Genres: Reference, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Italian history, art and culture, religious movements, faith and spirituality
