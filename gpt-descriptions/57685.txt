Synopsis:
In "Griffith Gaunt; or, Jealousy," Charles Reade weaves a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the destructive power of jealousy. Set in the idyllic English countryside, the story follows the lives of two childhood friends, Griffith Gaunt and George Fielding, who find themselves entangled in a web of passion and envy. As their paths diverge, secrets are revealed, and their once unbreakable bond is put to the ultimate test. With its intricate plot and complex characters, this three-volume novel explores the dark depths of human emotions and the consequences of unchecked jealousy.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Jealousy, Betrayal, Love, Friendship
