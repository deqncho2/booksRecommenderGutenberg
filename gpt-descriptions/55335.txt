Synopsis: 
In 'The Mercer Boys' Cruise in the Lassie', the adventurous Mercer brothers embark on a thrilling journey aboard the Lassie, a magnificent yacht. As they set sail, they encounter mysterious islands, treacherous storms, and a series of unexpected events that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their loyal dog, Rex, by their side, the Mercer boys must navigate through dangerous waters and unravel the secrets that lie within the depths of the sea. Will they be able to overcome the challenges and uncover the truth that awaits them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Exploration, Resilience
