Synopsis: 
'The Junior Classics, Volume 2: Folk Tales and Myths' is a captivating collection of timeless stories that will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with mythical creatures, brave heroes, and magical adventures. From ancient Greek myths to traditional folk tales from around the world, this volume offers a rich tapestry of narratives that will ignite the imagination and leave readers yearning for more. Discover the origins of legendary figures like Hercules and King Arthur, and delve into the fascinating folklore of different cultures. Each story is beautifully crafted, brimming with wonder, and sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: 
Heroism, Mythical creatures, Cultural diversity
