Synopsis: 
In 'Connie Morgan in the Lumber Camps', young Connie Morgan finds herself in the midst of an exciting adventure when she joins her father in the rugged lumber camps of the Pacific Northwest. As she navigates the challenges of the wilderness, Connie discovers a hidden world of danger, mystery, and unexpected friendships. With her determination and resourcefulness, she becomes an integral part of the camp, proving that she is more than capable of holding her own in this male-dominated environment. But as tensions rise and secrets unravel, Connie must rely on her wits and courage to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, Wilderness survival, Friendship, Courage
