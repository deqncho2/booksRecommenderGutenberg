Synopsis: 
"The Star-Treader, and other poems" by Clark Ashton Smith is a collection of enchanting and evocative poems that transport readers to otherworldly realms. From celestial landscapes to ancient civilizations, these verses explore the depths of imagination and the mysteries of the universe. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Smith weaves together tales of love, loss, and the eternal quest for meaning. Each poem is a glimpse into a fantastical world, inviting readers to embark on a poetic journey like no other.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Mythology, Transcendence, Beauty, Mortality
