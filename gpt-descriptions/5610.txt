Synopsis: In 'The Cardinal's Snuff-Box' by Henry Harland, a young American artist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance when he stumbles upon a centuries-old secret hidden within the walls of a Parisian mansion. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes torn between his love for a captivating French woman and his duty to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the artist's life becomes increasingly perilous, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and sacrifice, Art and creativity
