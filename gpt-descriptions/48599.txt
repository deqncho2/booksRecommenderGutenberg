Synopsis: In "Norma Kent of the WACS," follow the captivating journey of Norma Kent, a young woman who joins the Women's Army Corps during World War II. As she navigates the challenges of military life, Norma finds herself facing unexpected dangers, forging deep friendships, and discovering her own strength and resilience. With a mix of adventure, romance, and the realities of war, this story takes readers on a thrilling and emotional ride through one woman's wartime experience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Women's empowerment, Friendship, Courage, Identity, Sacrifice
