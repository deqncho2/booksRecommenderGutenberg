Synopsis: 
'Under the Deodars' is a collection of short stories by Rudyard Kipling that takes readers on a journey through the lives of the residents of a small Indian hill station. From scandalous secrets to unexpected romances, these tales offer a glimpse into the complex relationships and hidden desires of the characters living under the shadow of the majestic deodar trees.

Genres: 
Fiction, Short Stories, Drama

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Secrets and Scandals
