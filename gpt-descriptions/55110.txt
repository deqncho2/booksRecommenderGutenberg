Synopsis: 
In 'Vacation Camping for Girls' by Jeannette Augustus Marks, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling summer camping trip. As they navigate the challenges of the great outdoors, they discover the true meaning of friendship, resilience, and self-discovery. From building campfires to exploring hidden trails, the girls form an unbreakable bond that will last a lifetime. But when an unexpected twist puts their camping skills to the ultimate test, they must rely on their newfound strength and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Self-discovery
