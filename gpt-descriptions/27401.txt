Synopsis: 

'Poems & Ballads (Second Series): Swinburne's Poems Volume III' by Algernon Charles Swinburne is a collection of captivating and evocative poems that delve into the depths of human emotions and desires. From passionate love to political rebellion, Swinburne's verses explore the complexities of the human experience, leaving readers spellbound by his lyrical mastery and provocative imagery. This volume showcases Swinburne's ability to intertwine sensuality and intellect, creating a poetic journey that is both sensual and thought-provoking.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Political Rebellion, Sensuality, Beauty, Nature, Death and Mortality
