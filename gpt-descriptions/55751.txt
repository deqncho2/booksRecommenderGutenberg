Synopsis: 

'Lucy Larcom: Life, Letters, and Diary' delves into the captivating life of Lucy Larcom, a remarkable woman whose journey from a humble upbringing to becoming a renowned poet and advocate for social change is nothing short of inspiring. Through her personal letters and diary entries, we are transported to the vibrant world of 19th-century America, where Larcom's indomitable spirit and unwavering determination shine through. From her early struggles as a factory worker to her encounters with influential figures of her time, this biography offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a trailblazing woman who left an indelible mark on literature and society.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social Change, Women's Rights, Literary Legacy
