Synopsis: 
In 'To Him That Hath' by Leroy Scott, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him an enigmatic past and a hidden agenda. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of secrets, they are forced to confront their own desires, fears, and the consequences of their actions. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a suspenseful tale of deception, redemption, and the power of choice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Morality, Redemption, Consequences
