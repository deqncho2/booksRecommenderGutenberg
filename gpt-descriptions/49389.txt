Synopsis: In "The Forbidden Room; Or, 'Mine Answer was My Deed'" by Phoebe Allen, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to a mysterious room in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within, she uncovers a dark and haunting history that threatens to consume her. With each step she takes, Emily becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden desires, leading her to question her own sanity and the true nature of her family's legacy.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of obsession, the blurred line between reality and illusion
