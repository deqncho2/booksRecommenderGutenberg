Synopsis: In the small and mysterious Duchy of Cornwall, strange events unfold as a young journalist uncovers a web of secrets and scandals. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic lives of the Duchy's inhabitants, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception. With each revelation, the journalist finds himself questioning the very fabric of truth and loyalty, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Power, Deception
