Synopsis: In "The White Road to Verdun" by Kathleen Burke, a young woman named Kate embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her great-grandfather's experiences in World War I. As she delves into his past, she uncovers a web of secrets, love, and sacrifice that will forever change her perception of war and its impact on the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this historical fiction novel takes readers on a gripping and emotional exploration of one family's connection to the devastating events of the Great War.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family legacy, the human cost of war, the search for truth and understanding
