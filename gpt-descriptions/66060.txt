Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works of Henrik Ibsen, Vol. 01 (of 11)', delve into the captivating world of one of the greatest playwrights of all time. This volume presents a collection of Ibsen's early works, showcasing his masterful storytelling and exploration of complex human relationships. From the struggles of individuals against societal norms to the consequences of suppressed desires, each play offers a thought-provoking journey into the depths of the human psyche. With themes ranging from the pursuit of personal freedom to the consequences of deceit, this collection is a must-read for anyone seeking profound insights into the human condition.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Realism

Themes: Individualism, Social norms, Deception, Personal freedom
