Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Maine, a mysterious group of individuals known as the Deficient Saints emerges, each harboring a unique and extraordinary ability. As their powers intertwine with the lives of the townspeople, a thrilling tale of secrets, love, and redemption unfolds. With the fate of the town hanging in the balance, the Deficient Saints must confront their own inner demons and make sacrifices that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption, Community
