Synopsis: In 'Dave Dawson at Casablanca' by Robert Sidney Bowen, young Dave Dawson finds himself in the heart of World War II, stationed in the exotic city of Casablanca. As he navigates the dangerous streets and encounters mysterious characters, Dave becomes entangled in a web of espionage and treachery. With his quick thinking and bravery, he must uncover the truth and protect his country from a sinister plot that could change the course of the war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Espionage
