Synopsis: In 'Curiosities of Literature, Vol. 1', Isaac Disraeli takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known corners of literary history. From forgotten authors to curious anecdotes, this collection of essays delves into the intriguing and often bizarre aspects of the written word. Discover the secrets behind famous works, explore the lives of obscure writers, and unravel the mysteries that lie within the pages of literature. With wit and charm, Disraeli invites readers to explore the hidden treasures of the literary world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Literary exploration, Forgotten authors, Anecdotes, Curiosity, Uncovering hidden gems
