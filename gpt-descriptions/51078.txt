Synopsis: 
In 'Venoms: Venomous Animals and Antivenomous Serum-therapeutics', Albert Calmette delves into the captivating world of venomous animals and the development of antivenomous serum-therapeutics. From the deadly bites of snakes to the venomous stings of scorpions, Calmette explores the intricate mechanisms behind these toxins and the remarkable efforts made to counteract their effects. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of venom and the groundbreaking science that has emerged to combat its dangers.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Science, Medicine

Themes: 
Venomous animals, Toxins, Medical advancements, Scientific research
