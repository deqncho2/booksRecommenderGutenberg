Synopsis: 
In the small town of Craneycrow, a mysterious castle stands as a haunting reminder of a dark past. When a young artist named Billy Wopson arrives to paint the castle, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and danger. As he delves deeper into the castle's history, Billy uncovers a shocking truth that could change everything he thought he knew. With each twist and turn, Castle Craneycrow keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the castle's secrets and the fate of its inhabitants.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Love and betrayal, The power of art
