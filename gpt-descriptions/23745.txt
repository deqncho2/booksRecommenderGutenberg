Synopsis: In 'Aladdin & Co.: A Romance of Yankee Magic', the reader is transported into a world where a mysterious stranger named Aladdin arrives in a small New England town, bringing with him a sense of wonder and enchantment. As the townspeople become captivated by Aladdin's magical abilities, they are drawn into a web of intrigue and desire, where wishes come true but at a price. With elements of fantasy, romance, and mystery, this captivating tale explores the consequences of unchecked power and the allure of escaping reality.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, Escapism, Desire
