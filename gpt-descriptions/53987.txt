Synopsis: In 'Marjorie Dean Macy's Hamilton Colony' by Josephine Chase, Marjorie Dean embarks on a thrilling adventure as she joins a mysterious colony in Hamilton. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the colony, Marjorie discovers hidden treasures, encounters unexpected dangers, and uncovers a shocking truth that will change her life forever. With suspenseful twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the mysteries of Hamilton Colony.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Secrets, Self-discovery
