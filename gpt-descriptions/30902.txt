Synopsis: In 'Expediter' by Mack Reynolds, follow the thrilling journey of a skilled negotiator named Jerry Norcross, who finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous political conspiracy. As he navigates through a web of intrigue and deception, Norcross must use his wit and cunning to outsmart his adversaries and protect his own life. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Political conspiracy, Negotiation, Survival
