Synopsis: Step back in time and embark on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of stage-coaches and taverns. Alice Morse Earle takes us on a captivating exploration of the bustling highways and lively inns that once served as the lifeline of early America. From the daring adventures of stagecoach drivers to the colorful characters who frequented the taverns, this book offers a vivid and immersive experience of a bygone era. Discover the secrets, anecdotes, and untold stories that lie hidden within the walls of these historical landmarks, and get ready to be transported to a time when the stage-coach and tavern were at the heart of American life.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Cultural history

Themes: Transportation, Hospitality, Social life, American history
