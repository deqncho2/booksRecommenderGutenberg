Synopsis: In "The Idiot" by John Kendrick Bangs, we are introduced to a peculiar character named Smith, who is mistakenly believed to be an idiot due to his unconventional behavior. As the story unfolds, Smith's unique perspective on life challenges the societal norms and exposes the absurdities of the world around him. Through a series of humorous and thought-provoking encounters, Smith's innocence and wisdom become a catalyst for change, leading to unexpected consequences for those who underestimate him.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Social norms and conventions, Perception and misjudgment, Wisdom in simplicity
