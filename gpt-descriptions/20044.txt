Synopsis: 
'Riders of the Silences' by Max Brand is a thrilling Western novel that follows the story of Anthony Bard, a young man who finds himself caught in a web of danger and deception. When Bard is mistaken for a notorious outlaw, he must navigate a treacherous landscape filled with gunfights, horse chases, and unexpected alliances. As he fights to clear his name and uncover the truth, Bard's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Justice, Redemption
