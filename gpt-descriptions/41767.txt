Synopsis: In 'The Hero of Panama: A Tale of the Great Canal', we follow the thrilling adventures of a young engineer who finds himself at the heart of one of the most ambitious engineering projects of the 20th century - the construction of the Panama Canal. As he navigates the treacherous jungles, battles against deadly diseases, and faces the challenges of political intrigue, our hero must summon all his courage and ingenuity to overcome the obstacles that stand in the way of completing this monumental feat of human achievement.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Engineering

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Exploration, Political intrigue
