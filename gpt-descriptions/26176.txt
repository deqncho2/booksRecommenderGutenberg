Synopsis: 
In 'The Secret House' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious house becomes the center of a thrilling investigation when a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the house's walls, he uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each revelation, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, all while racing against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Intrigue and suspense
