Synopsis: In 'Ashes (Cenere): A Sardinian Story' by Grazia Deledda, a young woman named Marianna finds herself torn between tradition and her own desires in the rugged and enchanting landscape of Sardinia. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Marianna's journey unveils the hidden secrets and deep-rooted conflicts of her community. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the timeless themes of sacrifice, self-discovery, and the power of individuality.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Love and Desire, Family Dynamics, Societal Expectations
