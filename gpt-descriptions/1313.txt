Synopsis: 
'Over the Sliprails' by Henry Lawson is a captivating short story that follows the life of an Australian bushman named Dave Regan. As he navigates the challenges and adventures of living in the outback, Dave finds himself entangled in a series of unexpected events that test his resilience and character. From encounters with swagmen and drovers to the complexities of love and loyalty, this tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of Australia's bush country.

Genres:
Short story, Adventure, Australian literature

Themes:
Life in the Australian outback, Survival, Loyalty, Love, Social class
