Synopsis: In 'Running Free' by James B. Connolly, a young man named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the American West. Along the way, he encounters danger, love, and unexpected friendships that will forever change his life. As Jack navigates through the challenges of the wild, he discovers the true meaning of freedom and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Freedom, Self-discovery, Friendship
