Synopsis: In "Alien Offer" by Al Sevcik, a brilliant scientist receives an unexpected visit from an extraterrestrial being who offers him a proposition that could change the course of humanity. As the scientist delves deeper into the alien's offer, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and grapple with the consequences of his decisions. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with intrigue, danger, and the ultimate question of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Existentialism, Morality, Human Nature
