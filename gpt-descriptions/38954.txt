Synopsis: 
In 'Eye Spy: Afield with Nature Among Flowers and Animate Things', W. Hamilton Gibson takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the natural world. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Gibson explores the intricate beauty of flowers and the fascinating behavior of various creatures. From the delicate dance of butterflies to the hidden secrets of plants, this book offers a unique perspective on the hidden wonders that surround us every day.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Illustration

Themes: Nature appreciation, Environmentalism, Curiosity
