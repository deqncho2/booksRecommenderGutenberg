Synopsis: In 'The Clue of the Twisted Candle' by Wallace Edgar, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a mysterious murder, a twisted candle, and a web of deceit. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a series of shocking secrets and unexpected connections that lead him down a dangerous path. With time running out, the detective must unravel the twisted clues and outsmart the cunning culprits before they strike again.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
