Synopsis: 
In 'Sequel' by Smith, Ben, a renowned author finds himself trapped in a perplexing web of reality and fiction when the characters from his previous novels start coming to life. As he delves deeper into this mysterious phenomenon, he must confront the consequences of his own creations and navigate a world where the boundaries between imagination and reality blur. With each turn of the page, the lines between the author's own life and the stories he has written become increasingly intertwined, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Fiction, Identity, Consequences of Creation
