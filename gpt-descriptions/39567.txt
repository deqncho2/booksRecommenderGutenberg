Synopsis: In 'Two Little Waifs' by Molesworth, Mrs., two young siblings find themselves alone in the world after a tragic event. With no one to turn to, they embark on a journey filled with unexpected encounters, heartwarming friendships, and a quest for a place to call home. As they navigate through the challenges of their circumstances, the siblings discover the power of resilience, the importance of family, and the beauty of finding solace in unexpected places.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Home, Family
