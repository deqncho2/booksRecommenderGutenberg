Synopsis: In 'The Puritan Twins' by Lucy Fitch Perkins, follow the captivating journey of twin siblings, Priscilla and John, as they navigate the challenges and adventures of life in 17th century New England. From encounters with Native Americans to the trials of the Salem witch trials, the twins must rely on their wit, courage, and strong Puritan values to overcome the obstacles that come their way. Will they be able to stay true to their beliefs and find their place in this new world?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Puritanism, Family, Identity, Resilience
