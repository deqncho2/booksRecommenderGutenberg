Synopsis: 
'Mingo, and Other Sketches in Black and White' is a collection of captivating stories that delve into the lives and experiences of African Americans in the post-Civil War South. Through vivid and poignant sketches, Joel Chandler Harris introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, from the wise and enigmatic Mingo to the spirited and resilient Aunt Minervy Ann. These tales offer a unique glimpse into the complexities of race, identity, and the enduring human spirit in a time of great social change.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Historical Fiction

Themes: Race, Identity, Resilience, Social Change
