Synopsis: In 'Bacteria in Daily Life' by Frankland, Grace C., embark on a fascinating journey into the microscopic world that surrounds us every day. From the unseen organisms that inhabit our bodies to the surprising roles they play in our environment, this captivating exploration unveils the hidden power of bacteria. Through captivating anecdotes and scientific discoveries, the author reveals the intricate relationship between humans and these tiny creatures, challenging our preconceived notions and shedding light on the remarkable ways bacteria shape our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Microbiology

Themes: Microbial ecology, Human health, Environmental impact
