Synopsis: In 'Poor Man's Rock' by Sinclair, Bertrand W., a struggling musician named Jack finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the map, Jack must navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter eccentric characters, and confront his own inner demons. With each step closer to the truth, Jack's life becomes increasingly entangled with the enigmatic power of the rock, leading him to question the true value of wealth and the sacrifices one must make to attain it.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Pursuit of wealth, Redemption, Self-discovery
