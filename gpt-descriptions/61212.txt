Synopsis: 

'English as She is Taught: Being Genuine Answers to Examination Questions in Our Public Schools' takes readers on a hilarious journey through the minds of students as they attempt to answer English examination questions. From baffling interpretations of classic literature to creative grammar mishaps, this collection of genuine answers will leave readers in stitches. With each page turn, the book offers a delightful glimpse into the unintentional humor and unique perspectives of young minds grappling with the English language.

Genres: Humor, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Language and communication, Education system, Humor in everyday life
