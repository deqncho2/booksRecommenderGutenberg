Synopsis: In 'Indian Legends Retold', Elaine Goodale Eastman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Native American folklore. From the enchanting tales of shape-shifting animals to the haunting legends of powerful spirits, this collection breathes new life into age-old stories. With her lyrical prose and deep respect for Native American culture, Eastman invites readers to immerse themselves in a world where nature and spirituality intertwine, where wisdom is passed down through generations, and where the power of storytelling holds the key to understanding the human experience.

Genres: Folklore, Native American literature, Mythology

Themes: Nature, Spirituality, Oral tradition, Cultural preservation
