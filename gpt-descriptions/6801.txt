Synopsis: 
In the enchanting kingdom of Graustark, a young American woman named Beverly finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. As she navigates the opulent courts and treacherous landscapes, Beverly's life becomes intertwined with the fate of the kingdom itself. With danger lurking at every turn, she must summon her courage and wit to protect those she holds dear and uncover the truth that could change everything.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Political intrigue, Cultural clashes
