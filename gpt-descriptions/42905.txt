Synopsis: In 'Great Porter Square: A Mystery. v. 1', the bustling streets of London become the backdrop for a perplexing crime that leaves the residents of Great Porter Square in a state of fear and suspicion. When a prominent member of the community is found dead under mysterious circumstances, it is up to the astute Inspector Burnley to unravel the web of secrets and deceit that shrouds the square. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a labyrinth of hidden motives, unexpected alliances, and shocking revelations that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Community dynamics
