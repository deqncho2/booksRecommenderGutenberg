Synopsis: In 'A Rebellion in Dixie' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Frank is caught in the midst of a thrilling adventure during the American Civil War. As he navigates the dangerous and divided world of the South, Frank finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his own sense of justice. With unexpected alliances and heart-pounding encounters, Frank's journey becomes a test of courage and resilience. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous waters of war and emerge with his integrity intact?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Courage, Identity
