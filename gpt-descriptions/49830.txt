Synopsis: In 'Treasures in Heaven', follow the captivating journey of a young Latter-day Saint as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of his faith. From unexpected encounters to life-altering decisions, this fifteenth book in the Faith Promoting Series will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the treasures that await in the heavenly realm. With each turn of the page, the protagonist's unwavering devotion and the power of divine intervention will leave readers inspired and uplifted.

Genres: Religious fiction, Coming-of-age, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Divine intervention, Spiritual growth
