Synopsis: 
Jemmy Stubbins, or the Nailer Boy: Illustrations of the Law of Kindness is a heartwarming tale that follows the extraordinary journey of a young boy named Jemmy. Born into poverty and facing numerous challenges, Jemmy's life takes an unexpected turn when he discovers a hidden talent that sets him on a path of self-discovery and resilience. As he navigates through the trials and tribulations of his circumstances, Jemmy encounters a cast of diverse characters who teach him valuable lessons about the power of kindness, compassion, and the importance of never giving up. This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, rooting for Jemmy as he strives to overcome adversity and find his place in the world.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Kindness, Resilience, Social Inequality
