Synopsis: In 'The Peril Finders' by George Manville Fenn, a group of adventurous young men embark on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of a hidden treasure. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous adversaries, they must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive. With each step closer to their goal, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will they be able to outsmart their enemies and claim the treasure, or will they succumb to the perils that lie in wait?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
