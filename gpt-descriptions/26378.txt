Synopsis: In 'The Care of Books' by John Willis Clark, embark on a fascinating journey through the world of book preservation and restoration. From ancient manuscripts to modern volumes, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable insights into the delicate art of maintaining and protecting books. Discover the secrets of bookbinding, learn how to handle fragile pages with care, and explore the history of libraries and their collections. With captivating anecdotes and practical advice, this book is a must-read for bibliophiles and anyone curious about the intricate world of book care.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Book preservation, Restoration techniques, Library history, Manuscript conservation
