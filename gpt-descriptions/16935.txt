Synopsis: 

In "Thomas Henry Huxley: A Sketch Of His Life And Work," Peter Chalmers Mitchell delves into the fascinating life of one of the most influential scientists of the 19th century. From his humble beginnings to his groundbreaking contributions to the fields of biology and evolution, Huxley's journey is one of relentless curiosity and intellectual courage. Mitchell's vivid portrayal of Huxley's struggles and triumphs will captivate readers, as they witness the birth of modern scientific thought and the clash between religion and science. 

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Evolution, Clash between religion and science
