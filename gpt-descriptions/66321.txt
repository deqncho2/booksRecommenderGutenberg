Synopsis: In 'Rachel and the Seven Wonders' by Netta Syrett, Rachel, a young and curious girl, embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her to extraordinary places. When she discovers a hidden map, Rachel sets out to uncover the secrets of the seven wonders, encountering magical creatures, ancient civilizations, and unexpected challenges along the way. As she delves deeper into this enchanting world, Rachel learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of imagination. Will she be able to unlock the mysteries of the seven wonders and find her own place in this extraordinary realm?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
