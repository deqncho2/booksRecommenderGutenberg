Synopsis: In 'Sarah's School Friend' by Baldwin, May, a young girl named Sarah befriends a mysterious new student at her school. As their friendship grows, Sarah discovers that her new friend has a secret that could change everything. With secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Friendship, Coming-of-age

Themes: Secrets, Trust, Loyalty
