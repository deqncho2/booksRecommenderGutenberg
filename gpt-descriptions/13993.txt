Synopsis: 
'Dere Mable: Love Letters of a Rookie' is a collection of humorous and heartfelt letters written by a young soldier named Bill to his sweetheart Mable during World War I. Through his letters, Bill shares his experiences, struggles, and adventures as a rookie soldier, providing a unique and entertaining perspective on the realities of war. As the letters unfold, readers are taken on a journey filled with laughter, love, and unexpected surprises, making it impossible to resist the charm and wit of Bill's correspondence.

Genres: Humor, Epistolary, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, War, Coming of Age
