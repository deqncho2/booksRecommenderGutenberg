Synopsis: In 'Frank on the Prairie' by Castlemon, Harry, a young adventurous boy named Frank embarks on a thrilling journey to the untamed prairie. Leaving behind the comforts of his home, he faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected dangers. As Frank navigates through the vast wilderness, he discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the importance of friendship. Will he be able to conquer the perils of the prairie and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Friendship
