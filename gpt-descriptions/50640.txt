Synopsis: In 'Elements of Surgery' by Robert Liston, prepare to be captivated by the gripping accounts of surgical procedures that push the boundaries of medical knowledge. From the operating room to the lecture hall, Liston's vivid descriptions will transport you into the world of 19th-century surgery, where life and death hang in the balance. With each turn of the page, you'll witness the remarkable skill and innovation of Liston as he confronts the most challenging cases, leaving you on the edge of your seat, eager to discover the outcome. Brace yourself for a journey through the highs and lows of surgical practice, where every incision holds the potential for both triumph and tragedy.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical History, Biography

Themes: Medical innovation, Life and death, Surgical techniques
