Synopsis:
In Bernard Shaw's "Candida," a seemingly idyllic marriage is put to the test when a young poet enters the picture, stirring up a whirlwind of emotions and challenging the status quo. As the characters navigate their desires, loyalties, and societal expectations, a captivating battle of wits and hearts unfolds, leaving readers questioning the true nature of love and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres:
Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes:
Love and desire, Marriage and fidelity, Gender roles and societal expectations
