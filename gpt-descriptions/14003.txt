Synopsis: 

In 'Disputed Handwriting', Jerome Buell Lavay presents an exhaustive and comprehensive exploration of one of the most crucial subjects of our time: the detection and study of forgery by handwriting. Lavay's work is filled with intriguing illustrations and insightful expositions that will captivate readers as they delve into the intricate world of handwriting analysis. From analyzing the subtle nuances of pen strokes to deciphering hidden messages, Lavay provides valuable tools and techniques for uncovering the truth behind disputed handwriting. As readers navigate through this fascinating journey, they will be drawn into a world of mystery, deception, and the quest for justice.

Genres: Non-fiction, Crime, Forensic Science

Themes: Forgery, Deception, Justice
