Synopsis: In the bustling city of New York, a young man named Oliver embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure. As he navigates the vibrant streets and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Oliver becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and ambition. From the opulent mansions of the elite to the gritty underworld of crime, Oliver's experiences in New York will forever change his perception of the world and his place in it.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Social class, Love and relationships, Ambition
