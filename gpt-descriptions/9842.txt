Synopsis: 

'Y Gododin: A Poem of the Battle of Cattraeth' by Aneirin is an ancient Welsh epic poem that tells the tale of a heroic battle fought by the warriors of Gododdin. Set in the 6th century, the poem follows the brave warriors as they prepare for battle, showcasing their valor, loyalty, and the tragic fate that awaits them. Through vivid descriptions and powerful imagery, the poem captures the essence of honor, sacrifice, and the fleeting nature of glory.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical literature

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Fate, Honor, Transience of life
