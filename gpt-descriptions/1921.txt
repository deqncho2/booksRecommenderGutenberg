Synopsis: In the midst of the French Revolution, a young nobleman finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his love for a beautiful Chouan woman. As he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue and forbidden romance, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of war and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the Chouannerie, a royalist uprising in Brittany, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, honor, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Political thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Betrayal, Political upheaval, Class conflict
