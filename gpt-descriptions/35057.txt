Synopsis: In 'Dilemmas of Pride, (Vol 2 of 3)' by Loudon, Mrs. (Margracia), the captivating story continues as the protagonist finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden desires, and societal expectations. As she navigates through the complexities of love, loyalty, and personal growth, she must confront the consequences of her choices and face the ultimate dilemma that threatens to shatter her world. With unexpected twists and turns, this volume delves deeper into the characters' lives, unraveling their hidden motivations and testing the limits of their pride.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Expectations, Personal Growth
