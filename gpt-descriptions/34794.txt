Synopsis: 

'Englefield Grange; or, Mary Armstrong's Troubles' by Paull, H. B., Mrs. is a captivating novel that follows the life of Mary Armstrong, a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and challenges at Englefield Grange. As Mary navigates through the complexities of her new surroundings, she uncovers hidden truths and faces unexpected obstacles that threaten to unravel her world. With each twist and turn, Mary's strength and resilience are put to the test, as she strives to overcome her troubles and find her place in the grand estate.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, resilience and personal growth
