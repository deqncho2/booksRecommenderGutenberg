Synopsis: In 'Healthful Sports for Young Ladies' by St. Sernin, Mademoiselle, a group of young ladies embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment through the world of sports. As they navigate societal expectations and personal challenges, they find strength, friendship, and a newfound sense of identity. From horseback riding to archery, these young women break barriers and challenge the norms of their time, proving that sports can be a powerful tool for personal growth and liberation.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Female empowerment, Breaking societal norms, Self-discovery
