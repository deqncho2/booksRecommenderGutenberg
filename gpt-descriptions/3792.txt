Synopsis: In 'Capitola the Madcap', we follow the adventures of the spirited and unconventional Capitola Black, a young woman with a mysterious past who finds herself in a series of thrilling and unexpected situations. From her daring escape from a burning building to her encounters with a charming but enigmatic stranger, Capitola's life is filled with danger, romance, and intrigue. As she navigates through a world of secrets and deception, Capitola's quick wit and fearless nature prove to be her greatest assets. Will she uncover the truth about her past and find the happiness she seeks, or will her wild and unpredictable nature lead her down a different path?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love and Deception
