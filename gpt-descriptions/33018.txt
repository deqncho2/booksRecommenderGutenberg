Synopsis: 

'Legends of the Skyline Drive and the Great Valley of Virginia' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting folklore of the Skyline Drive and the Great Valley of Virginia. From tales of Native American legends to stories of Civil War heroes, this book weaves together a tapestry of captivating narratives that will transport readers to a world of mystery and wonder. As the pages turn, readers will uncover the hidden secrets and timeless myths that have shaped the landscapes and communities of this breathtaking region. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Travel Guide

Themes: History, Mythology, Cultural Heritage
