Synopsis: 
In 'The High History of the Holy Graal', a group of knights embarks on a perilous quest to find the legendary Holy Grail, a sacred relic with the power to bestow eternal life. As they journey through treacherous lands and encounter mystical beings, they must confront their own inner demons and make difficult choices that will test their faith and loyalty. With its rich tapestry of adventure, mystery, and spiritual exploration, this epic tale will captivate readers and leave them yearning for the ultimate truth behind the Holy Grail.

Genres: 
Arthurian legend, historical fiction, fantasy

Themes: 
Quest for enlightenment, faith and spirituality, the power of myth and legend
