Synopsis: 

'Autographs for Freedom' is a captivating collection of writings by Mrs. Harriet Beecher Stowe and thirty-five other renowned writers. This anthology takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through various perspectives on the theme of freedom. Each writer contributes a unique piece, showcasing their literary prowess and shedding light on the complexities of human existence. From poignant poetry to powerful essays, this compilation offers a diverse range of voices that will leave readers inspired and deeply moved.

Genres: Anthology, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Freedom, Human Rights, Social Justice
