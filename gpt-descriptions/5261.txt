Synopsis:
In 'Constance Dunlap' by Arthur B. Reeve, we are introduced to the captivating character of Constance Dunlap, a young and intelligent woman who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. When a wealthy businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Constance becomes determined to uncover the truth behind his death. As she delves deeper into the case, she discovers a world of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With her quick wit and resourcefulness, Constance navigates through a labyrinth of clues, risking her own safety to bring justice to those responsible. Will she be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Deception, Female Empowerment
