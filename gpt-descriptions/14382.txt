Synopsis: In 'The Missing Bride' by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, a young woman named Rosamond is set to marry the man of her dreams. However, on the day of the wedding, she mysteriously disappears without a trace. As her family and friends search for answers, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the truth becomes more elusive, and the search for the missing bride takes a thrilling and unexpected turn.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Love and betrayal, Family secrets, Identity and deception
