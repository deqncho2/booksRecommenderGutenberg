Synopsis: In 'Happy Pollyooly: The Rich Little Poor Girl' by Jepson, Edgar, we follow the captivating journey of Pollyooly, a young girl who unexpectedly finds herself in a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates this new life, Pollyooly encounters a series of intriguing characters and faces unexpected challenges that test her resilience and wit. With a delightful blend of humor, mystery, and heartwarming moments, this story takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with surprises at every turn.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social class, Resilience, Identity
