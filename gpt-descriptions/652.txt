Synopsis: 
'Rasselas, Prince of Abyssinia' follows the story of a young prince who embarks on a journey to discover the true meaning of happiness. Raised in a secluded palace, Rasselas becomes disillusioned with his luxurious but confined life and yearns for a deeper understanding of the world. Alongside his companions, Rasselas encounters various individuals and explores different aspects of life, including love, wealth, and knowledge. As they navigate through the challenges and temptations of the outside world, the prince and his companions grapple with the eternal question of what truly brings fulfillment and contentment.

Genres: 
Philosophical fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes: 
The pursuit of happiness, The nature of human desires, The limitations of wealth and power
