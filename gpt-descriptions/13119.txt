Synopsis: 
'Jean François Millet: A Collection of Fifteen Pictures and a Portrait of the Painter, with Introduction and Interpretation' by Estelle M. Hurll is a captivating exploration of the life and art of the renowned French painter, Jean François Millet. Through a collection of fifteen carefully selected pictures and a portrait of the artist, Hurll delves into the profound themes and emotions that Millet's works evoke. From the rustic beauty of rural landscapes to the poignant portrayal of laborers and peasants, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the artistic genius of Millet and the profound impact of his art.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Interpretation

Themes: Rural life, Social realism, Human connection, Beauty in simplicity
