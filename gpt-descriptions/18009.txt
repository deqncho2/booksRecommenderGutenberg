Synopsis: 
'Sagittulae, Random Verses' by Bowling, Edward Woodley is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the depths of human emotions and experiences. From love and heartbreak to joy and introspection, these verses explore the complexities of life with profound insight and lyrical beauty. Each poem is a window into the poet's soul, inviting readers to reflect on their own lives and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Self-reflection, Human emotions
