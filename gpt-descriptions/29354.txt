Synopsis: In 'This One Problem' by Pease, M. C., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a perplexing mystery that could change the course of humanity. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a web of secrets and hidden agendas, forcing her to question everything she thought she knew. With time running out, she must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal to find the truth and save the world from impending disaster.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Conspiracy, Morality
