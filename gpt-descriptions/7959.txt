Synopsis: 
"The Reign of Tiberius, Out of the First Six Annals of Tacitus;: With His Account of Germany, and Life of Agricola" by Tacitus, Cornelius takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of Emperor Tiberius. Through Tacitus' meticulous accounts, we witness the political intrigue, power struggles, and corruption that defined this era. Alongside this gripping narrative, Tacitus also provides a fascinating exploration of Germany and the life of the renowned general Agricola. This work offers a riveting glimpse into the complex world of ancient Rome, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets and revelations that lie within its pages.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political commentary

Themes: Power and corruption, Political intrigue, Exploration and conquest, Historical analysis
