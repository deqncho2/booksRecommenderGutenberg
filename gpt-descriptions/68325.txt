Synopsis: 
In 'Nomad' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Sayre stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing him to confront his own beliefs and make difficult choices. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Dr. Sayre must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Ethics, Consequences of technology
