Synopsis: In 'Peaks of Shala' by Lane, Rose Wilder, a young woman named Lila embarks on a journey to the remote and mystical Shala Mountains. Drawn by the allure of hidden treasures and ancient secrets, she finds herself facing unexpected challenges and encountering enigmatic characters along the way. As Lila delves deeper into the mysteries of the mountains, she discovers that the true treasures lie not in material wealth, but in self-discovery and the power of human connection.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Human connection
