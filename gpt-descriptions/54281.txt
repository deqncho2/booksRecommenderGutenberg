Synopsis: 
'The Voiage and Travayle of Sir John Maundeville Knight: Which treateth of the way towards Hierusalem and of marvayles of Inde with other ilands and countreys' follows the incredible journey of Sir John Maundeville, a knight, as he embarks on a voyage towards Jerusalem. Along the way, he encounters numerous marvels and wonders in the lands of India and other islands and countries. From mythical creatures to extraordinary landscapes, Maundeville's account is filled with captivating tales that will leave readers in awe.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration of unknown lands, Cultural encounters, Myth and legend
