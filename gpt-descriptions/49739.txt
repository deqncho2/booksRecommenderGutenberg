Synopsis: 
In 'Gems of Reminiscence: Seventeenth Book of the Faith Promoting Series, Designed for the Instruction and Encouragement of Young Latter-day Saints', readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of true stories that will both inspire and challenge their faith. From miraculous healings to unexpected blessings, these tales of ordinary individuals facing extraordinary circumstances will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the power of faith and the miracles that can unfold in their own lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Inspirational, Religious

Themes: Faith, Miracles, Personal Growth
