Synopsis: 
'Frank Fairlegh: Scenes from the Life of a Private Pupil' follows the adventures of Frank Fairlegh, a young and spirited protagonist who finds himself in a series of unexpected and thrilling situations. From his time as a private pupil at a boarding school to his encounters with eccentric characters and his involvement in a secret society, Frank's journey is filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected twists. As he navigates through the challenges of his unconventional education, Frank learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the true meaning of courage.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Education, Courage
