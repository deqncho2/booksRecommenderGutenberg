Synopsis: In "The Story of Lewis Carroll: Told for Young People by the Real Alice in Wonderland," the real-life Alice Liddell, who inspired Lewis Carroll's beloved Alice's Adventures in Wonderland, shares her enchanting journey through the whimsical world of the famous author. From their first meeting in Oxford to the creation of the iconic characters and imaginative landscapes, Alice takes readers on a captivating exploration of the man behind the pen name. Filled with anecdotes, illustrations, and the magic of Carroll's storytelling, this book offers a delightful glimpse into the extraordinary friendship that sparked one of the most beloved children's books of all time.

Genres: Biography, Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Creativity, Literary Inspiration
