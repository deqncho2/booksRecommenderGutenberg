Synopsis: In the small Irish village of Garradrimna, secrets and scandals lurk behind every squinting window. When a stranger arrives, the town's dark underbelly is exposed, revealing the lengths some will go to protect their reputation. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the residents of Garradrimna must confront the consequences of their actions in a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the power of community.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Morality, Social conformity
