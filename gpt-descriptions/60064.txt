Synopsis: In "Dorothea Beale of Cheltenham" by Elizabeth Raikes, we are introduced to a remarkable woman who defies societal expectations and challenges the norms of her time. Dorothea Beale, a passionate and determined educator, embarks on a journey to transform the lives of young women through education. As she faces numerous obstacles and prejudices, Dorothea's unwavering dedication to her cause becomes an inspiration to all those around her. This captivating story takes us through the trials and triumphs of a woman who dares to dream big and make a lasting impact on the world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Women's Literature

Themes: Education, Gender Equality, Perseverance, Social Change
