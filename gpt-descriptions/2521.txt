Synopsis: In the small town of Milham, a young woman named Lizzie Leigh finds herself at the center of a scandal that threatens to tear her family apart. As secrets are revealed and judgments are passed, Lizzie must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and forgiveness in a society that is quick to condemn. With the support of unexpected allies, she must find the strength to face the consequences of her actions and fight for her place in a world that often seems determined to keep her down.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Social commentary

Themes: Family, Morality, Social class, Redemption
