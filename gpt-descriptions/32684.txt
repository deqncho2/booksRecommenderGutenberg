Synopsis: In 'The Invader' by Alfred Coppel, a mysterious and powerful alien force threatens the existence of humanity. As the invaders launch their attack, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to uncover the truth behind the invasion and find a way to save the world. With high-stakes action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, 'The Invader' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Alien invasion, Survival, Heroism
