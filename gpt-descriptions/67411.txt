Synopsis: 
In 'Blotted Out' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a young woman named Laura embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind her husband's mysterious disappearance. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Laura finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the dark truth, even if it means putting her own life at risk. This gripping psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the motives and loyalties of every character until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Identity, Trust
