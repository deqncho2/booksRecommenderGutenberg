Synopsis: In 'Night Fall in the Ti-Tree', a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that occur only during the night. As the community becomes increasingly fearful, a young woman named Emily embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she delves into the town's dark secrets, unearthing a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Betrayal, Supernatural forces
