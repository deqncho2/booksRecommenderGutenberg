Synopsis: 
'Old Wonder-Eyes, and Other Stories for Children' is a collection of enchanting tales that will transport readers to a world of imagination and wonder. From the magical adventures of a young girl who discovers a hidden realm to the heartwarming story of a mischievous squirrel who learns the importance of friendship, each story is filled with delightful characters and captivating plots. With its whimsical storytelling and timeless charm, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Discovery
