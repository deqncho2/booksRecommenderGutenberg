Synopsis: In 'The Raid from Beausejour; and How the Carter Boys Lifted the Mortgage: Two Stories of Acadie', Charles G. D. Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and vibrant culture of Acadie. The first story delves into the dramatic events surrounding the infamous raid on Beausejour, where the fate of a community hangs in the balance. The second story follows the Carter boys as they embark on a daring mission to save their family's home from foreclosure. With vivid storytelling and compelling characters, Roberts weaves together two tales of resilience, courage, and the enduring spirit of Acadie.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Family, Resilience
