Synopsis: In 'The Brownies and Other Tales', Juliana Horatia Ewing takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and whimsical tales. From mischievous brownies causing havoc to a magical wishing carpet that transports children to far-off lands, each story is filled with delightful adventures and unexpected twists. Ewing's vivid storytelling and imaginative characters will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what magical surprises await them next.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Fairy tales

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Morality, Magic
