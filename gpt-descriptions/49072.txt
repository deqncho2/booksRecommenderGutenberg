Synopsis: 

Discover the captivating history and breathtaking beauty of the Yser River and the Belgian Coast in this meticulously illustrated guide by Pneu Michelin. From the ancient battles fought along the Yser to the charming coastal towns that have withstood the test of time, this book takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of this remarkable region. With stunning visuals and engaging storytelling, explore the hidden gems, iconic landmarks, and untold stories that make the Yser and the Belgian Coast a truly enchanting destination.

Genres: Travel, History, Art

Themes: War and Conflict, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
