Synopsis: In 'The Wagner Story Book: Firelight Tales of the Great Music Dramas', William Henry Frost takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Richard Wagner's music dramas. Through a collection of vivid and imaginative tales, Frost brings to life the iconic characters and epic stories found in Wagner's operas, such as "Tristan and Isolde," "The Flying Dutchman," and "The Ring of the Nibelung." From mythical realms to passionate love affairs, from heroic quests to tragic destinies, this book offers a delightful exploration of the timeless themes and mesmerizing melodies that have made Wagner's works so enduring.

Genres: Music, Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Love and passion, Mythology and folklore, Heroism and destiny
