Synopsis: In 'Saboteurs on the River' by Mildred A. Wirt, a thrilling mystery unfolds as Nancy Drew finds herself caught in a dangerous web of sabotage and intrigue. When a series of suspicious incidents occur along the river, Nancy must use her detective skills to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Nancy discovers that the river holds secrets that could have far-reaching consequences. With time running out, Nancy must race against the clock to stop the saboteurs before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Loyalty
