Synopsis: 

'Norwich: A Sketch Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic city of Norwich, England. Through the intricate and beautifully detailed sketches of Elsie Vera Cole, the book showcases the city's stunning architecture, charming streets, and hidden corners. From the grandeur of Norwich Cathedral to the quaintness of Elm Hill, each sketch captures the essence and character of this vibrant city. As readers flip through the pages, they will be transported to Norwich, immersing themselves in its rich history and unique atmosphere.

Genres: Art, Travel, History

Themes: Cityscape, Architecture, Cultural Heritage
