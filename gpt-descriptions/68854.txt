Synopsis: 
In "Three years of war in East Africa" by Angus Buchanan, embark on a gripping journey through the tumultuous landscapes of East Africa during a time of intense conflict. Follow the experiences of a diverse group of individuals as they navigate the horrors and complexities of war, facing unimaginable challenges and forging unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching accounts, this book offers a unique perspective on the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Camaraderie, Identity
