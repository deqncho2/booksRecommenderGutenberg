Synopsis: 

In 'For Name and Fame; Or, Through Afghan Passes' by G. A. Henty, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure through the treacherous Afghan Passes. The story follows the courageous and resourceful young protagonist, Ned, as he embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his father from captivity. Along the way, Ned encounters fierce battles, cunning enemies, and unexpected allies, all while navigating the rugged terrain of Afghanistan. With every twist and turn, Ned's determination and bravery are put to the test, making this an enthralling tale of courage, loyalty, and the pursuit of honor.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Survival
