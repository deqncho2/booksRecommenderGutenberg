Synopsis: 
In "Life and Lillian Gish," Albert Bigelow Paine takes readers on an enchanting journey through the life of one of the most iconic actresses of the silent film era. From her humble beginnings on the stage to her rise to stardom in Hollywood, this captivating biography delves into the personal and professional triumphs and challenges faced by the legendary Lillian Gish. Paine's vivid storytelling and intimate portrayal of Gish's life offer a fascinating glimpse into the world of early cinema and the indomitable spirit of a true pioneer.

Genres: Biography, Film History, Memoir

Themes: Perseverance, Artistic Legacy, Women in Film
