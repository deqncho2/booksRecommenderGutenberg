Synopsis: In the 20th issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events of 1897. From mysterious disappearances to scientific breakthroughs, this weekly magazine for boys and girls offers a captivating blend of adventure, knowledge, and curiosity. Delve into the pages of this edition to uncover the secrets of the world and discover what is truly going on in it.

Genres: Historical, Adventure, Educational

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Current Events
