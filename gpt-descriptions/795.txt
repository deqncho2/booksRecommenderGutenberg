Synopsis:
'Ballads and Lyrics of Old France, with Other Poems' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of French history and culture. This collection of poems, written by Andrew Lang, transports us to a bygone era, where we encounter tales of chivalry, romance, and tragedy. From the courtly love of troubadours to the haunting legends of medieval France, each poem paints a vivid picture of a world long past. Through the lyrical verses, we witness the beauty of the French countryside, the passion of forbidden love, and the courage of heroes. With its enchanting melodies and evocative imagery, this collection invites readers to immerse themselves in the captivating world of Old France.

Genres:
Poetry, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes:
Love and Romance, Heroism, French History and Culture
