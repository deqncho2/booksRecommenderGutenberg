Synopsis: In 'The Five Knots' by Fred M. White, a group of strangers find themselves aboard a luxurious yacht, sailing towards an unknown destination. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must unravel the mystery of their shared pasts and confront the dark forces that threaten their lives. With each knot they untangle, the stakes become higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
