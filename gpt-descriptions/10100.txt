Synopsis:
'Byron' by John Nichol is a gripping and thought-provoking novel that follows the life of a mysterious and enigmatic character named Byron. Set in a small coastal town, the story unravels as Byron's presence begins to disrupt the lives of the townspeople, leading to unexpected consequences. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of Byron's identity becomes a captivating puzzle to solve. With its intriguing plot twists and complex characters, 'Byron' keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic figure.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Intrigue
