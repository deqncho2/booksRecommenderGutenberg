Synopsis: In "Ferdinand De Soto, The Discoverer of the Mississippi: American Pioneers and Patriots," John S. C. Abbott takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of early America. This captivating biography follows the life of the intrepid explorer, Ferdinand De Soto, as he embarks on a daring quest to uncover the secrets of the mighty Mississippi River. From thrilling encounters with Native American tribes to treacherous battles and unexpected alliances, De Soto's extraordinary adventures will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Abbott's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the courage, determination, and resilience of one of America's most remarkable pioneers.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Native American relations, Courage
