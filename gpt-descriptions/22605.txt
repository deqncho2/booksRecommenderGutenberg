Synopsis: 
'Book-Lovers, Bibliomaniacs and Book Clubs' by Henry Howard Harper is a captivating exploration of the fascinating world of book lovers, bibliomaniacs, and book clubs. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and historical accounts, Harper delves into the eccentricities, obsessions, and camaraderie that surround the love of books. From the passionate collectors who will stop at nothing to acquire rare editions, to the lively discussions and debates that take place within book clubs, this book offers a delightful glimpse into the enchanting realm of bibliophiles.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literary Criticism

Themes: Love of books, Obsession, Community
