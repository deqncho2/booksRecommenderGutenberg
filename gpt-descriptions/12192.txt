Synopsis: In 'The Long Shadow' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Mary inherits a ranch in the rugged Montana wilderness. Determined to prove herself in a male-dominated world, she faces numerous challenges and unexpected dangers. As Mary navigates the treacherous landscape, she discovers secrets about her family's past that could change everything. With the help of a mysterious stranger, she must unravel the truth and confront her own inner demons. This gripping tale of adventure, love, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Family Secrets
