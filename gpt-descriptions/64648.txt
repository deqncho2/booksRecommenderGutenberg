Synopsis: In 'The Green Dream' by Walton, Bryce, a young botanist named Lily discovers a mysterious plant with extraordinary properties that could revolutionize the world. As she delves deeper into her research, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, where the line between science and ethics blurs. With her life and the fate of the plant hanging in the balance, Lily must navigate a dangerous path filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ethical dilemmas, Scientific discovery, Consequences of ambition
