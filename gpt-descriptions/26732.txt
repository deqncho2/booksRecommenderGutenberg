Synopsis: 

In 'Free Air' by Sinclair Lewis, a spirited young woman named Claire Boltwood embarks on a thrilling cross-country road trip in her brand-new automobile. As she navigates the open roads, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, including a charming mechanic named Milt Daggett. Together, they embark on an adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns, testing their resilience and challenging their preconceived notions. 

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Satire

Themes: Freedom, Independence, Social Commentary
