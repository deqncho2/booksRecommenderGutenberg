Synopsis: In 'To the Last Man' by Zane Grey, a bitter and long-standing feud between two families in the wild and lawless Arizona territory takes center stage. As the tension escalates, a young cowboy finds himself caught in the middle, torn between loyalty and love. With danger lurking at every turn, the characters must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face the consequences of their actions, ultimately leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Family feuds, Loyalty, Love and sacrifice
