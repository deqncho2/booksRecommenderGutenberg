Synopsis: 
In 'The History of Samuel Titmarsh and the Great Hoggarty Diamond', we follow the misadventures of Samuel Titmarsh, a young artist who finds himself entangled in the extravagant and often absurd world of the Hoggarty family. As he becomes acquainted with the eccentric characters and their lavish lifestyle, Samuel's own ambitions and desires are put to the test. With humor and wit, Thackeray takes us on a journey filled with love, deception, and unexpected twists, as Samuel navigates the complexities of society and learns valuable lessons along the way.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Bildungsroman

Themes: Social class, Materialism, Morality
