Synopsis: 

In 'Solid Geometry with Problems and Applications (Revised edition)', authors Lennes and Slaught take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of solid geometry. This revised edition presents a comprehensive exploration of three-dimensional shapes, their properties, and their practical applications, all while challenging readers with intriguing problems to solve along the way. From the intricacies of polyhedra to the elegance of geometric transformations, this book offers a rich and engaging learning experience that will captivate both students and enthusiasts of mathematics.

Genres: Mathematics, Educational, Problem-solving

Themes: Geometry, Three-dimensional shapes, Practical applications
