Synopsis:
In the small town of Oakwood, a mysterious house stands abandoned, its walls holding secrets that have been whispered for generations. When a young girl named Lily moves to town, she becomes fascinated by the rumors surrounding the house and its haunting past. Determined to uncover the truth, Lily embarks on a journey that will lead her to unexpected discoveries and a deeper understanding of her own family history. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the whispering walls, Lily must confront her fears and confront the ghosts of the past.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Family secrets, Identity, Courage, History
