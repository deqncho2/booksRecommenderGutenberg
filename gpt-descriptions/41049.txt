Synopsis: In 'The Onslaught from Rigel' by Pratt, Fletcher, a group of intrepid explorers embarks on a perilous journey to the distant planet of Rigel. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter strange beings, they uncover a sinister plot that threatens not only their own lives but the fate of the entire galaxy. With time running out, they must summon all their courage and resourcefulness to confront the relentless onslaught from Rigel.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Heroism, Interstellar Conflict
