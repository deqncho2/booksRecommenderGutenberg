Synopsis: In the small town of Percherville, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious object crash-lands nearby. As the townspeople become increasingly paranoid and fearful, a group of teenagers embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the extraterrestrial presence. With their courage and resourcefulness tested, they must navigate a web of secrets and confront the unknown to protect their town from an otherworldly threat.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Fear and paranoia, Coming of age, Exploration of the unknown
