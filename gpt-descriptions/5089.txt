Synopsis:
"Boris Godunov: a drama in verse" by Aleksandr Sergeevich Pushkin is a gripping historical tragedy set in 16th-century Russia. The story revolves around the rise and fall of Boris Godunov, a powerful nobleman who becomes the Tsar of Russia. As Boris navigates the treacherous world of politics and power, he must confront his own inner demons and face the consequences of his actions. With its intense character development and intricate plot, this drama in verse explores themes of ambition, guilt, and the corrupting nature of power.

Genres:
Drama, Historical Fiction, Poetry

Themes:
Ambition, Guilt, Power, Politics
