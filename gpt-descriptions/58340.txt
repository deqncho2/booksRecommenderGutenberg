Synopsis: In the bustling city of Budapest, Tarr, a charismatic and enigmatic figure, navigates through a world filled with political intrigue, artistic rebellion, and passionate love affairs. As the story unfolds, Tarr's unconventional and unpredictable actions challenge societal norms and ignite a chain of events that will leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Modernist fiction, Satire, Psychological drama

Themes: Individualism, Social criticism, Artistic rebellion
