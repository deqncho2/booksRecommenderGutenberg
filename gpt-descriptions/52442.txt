Synopsis: In this captivating volume, Captains Lewis and Clark continue their extraordinary expedition, venturing into uncharted territories and facing countless challenges as they make their way to the Pacific Ocean. From navigating treacherous rivers to encountering unfamiliar Native American tribes, the explorers' determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. As they delve deeper into the unknown, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with breathtaking landscapes, unexpected encounters, and the constant pursuit of knowledge.

Genres: Adventure, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Native American Relations, Survival and Resourcefulness
