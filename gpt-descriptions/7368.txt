Synopsis: 
In "Lifted Masks; stories" by Susan Glaspell, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that delve into the complexities of human nature and the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface. From the secrets of a small town to the struggles of individuals trapped in societal expectations, each story unravels a different facet of the human experience. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking narratives, Glaspell's stories will leave readers questioning the masks we all wear and the true nature of the world around us.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Deception, Society, Truth
