Synopsis:
In "Kipps: The Story of a Simple Soul," we follow the journey of Arthur Kipps, a young orphan who unexpectedly inherits a fortune and finds himself thrust into the upper echelons of society. As he navigates this new world, Kipps must grapple with the challenges of social class, love, and the pursuit of true happiness. With a delightful blend of humor and heart, H.G. Wells takes us on a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the complexities of human nature.

Genres:
Fiction, Comedy, Social Satire

Themes:
Social class, Identity, Love and relationships
