Synopsis: In "Tennyson's Life and Poetry: And Mistakes Concerning Tennyson," Eugene Parsons delves into the fascinating life and enigmatic poetry of Alfred Lord Tennyson. Uncovering lesser-known aspects of Tennyson's personal journey, Parsons explores the poet's struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact of his work on Victorian society. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, Parsons challenges common misconceptions about Tennyson, shedding light on the true essence of his poetry and the man behind it.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Poetry

Themes: Identity, Artistic Expression, Society and Culture
