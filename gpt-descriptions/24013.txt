Synopsis: In 'The Telegraph Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of a young and ambitious telegraph messenger named Harry Vane. Determined to rise above his humble beginnings, Harry faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected opportunities that test his character and resilience. As he navigates the bustling streets of New York City, Harry's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, leading him on a path towards self-discovery and success. Will Harry's dreams become a reality, or will the obstacles he faces prove too difficult to overcome?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility
