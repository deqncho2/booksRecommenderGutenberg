Synopsis: In 'May; vol. II' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the captivating sequel to the first volume, we follow the continued journey of May, a young woman navigating the complexities of love, society, and personal growth. As May's world expands, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, unexpected alliances, and heart-wrenching decisions. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into May's world, eagerly anticipating the resolution of her trials and tribulations.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and relationships, Social expectations, Self-discovery
