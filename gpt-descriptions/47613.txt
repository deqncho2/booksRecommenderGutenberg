Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Hunters at the Haunted Lodge' by Wyckoff Capwell, a group of young friends embark on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon an eerie lodge with a dark secret. As they uncover clues and face spine-chilling encounters, they must unravel the mystery before it's too late. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
