Synopsis: 

In 'Thirty Years From Home; or, a Voice From the Main Deck: Being the Experience of Samuel Leech', Samuel Leech takes readers on an extraordinary journey through his life as a sailor in the British Navy during the early 19th century. From his humble beginnings as a young boy pressed into service, to his harrowing experiences in battles and storms, Leech's gripping narrative offers a firsthand account of the trials and triumphs of life at sea. As he navigates the treacherous waters of war and the challenges of daily life aboard a ship, Leech's story is filled with moments of danger, camaraderie, and personal growth. 

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Coming of Age
