Synopsis: In 'The Fifteen Comforts of Matrimony: Responses From Women', a collection of letters from women of various backgrounds and experiences, the true nature of marriage is explored. From the exhilarating highs to the devastating lows, these women candidly share their thoughts, hopes, and fears, offering a unique glimpse into the complexities of married life. As the letters unfold, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true meaning of love is questioned. With each letter, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of emotions, leaving them eager to discover what lies ahead for these women.

Genres: Epistolary fiction, Women's fiction, Romance

Themes: Marriage, Love, Identity, Communication, Gender roles
