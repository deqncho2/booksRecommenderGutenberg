Synopsis: In 'The Grain of Dust: A Novel' by David Graham Phillips, a young man named John Hemingway finds himself caught in a web of ambition, love, and betrayal in the bustling city of New York. As he navigates the cutthroat world of politics and journalism, John becomes entangled with a mysterious woman who holds a secret that could shatter his carefully constructed life. With his career and personal relationships hanging in the balance, John must confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this gripping novel explores the dark underbelly of power and the sacrifices one must make to achieve success.

Genres: Drama, Political Fiction, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Love, Power, Morality
