Synopsis: In "And That's How It Was, Officer" by Sholto, Ralph, a seemingly ordinary police interview takes an unexpected turn when a suspect's account of events becomes increasingly bizarre and surreal. As the officer delves deeper into the suspect's story, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leaving both the reader and the officer questioning what is true and what is fiction. With each twist and turn, the narrative becomes a mind-bending exploration of perception, memory, and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Surreal fiction

Themes: Perception, Memory, Truth vs. Fiction
