Synopsis: In 'The Absurd ABC' by Crane, Walter, embark on a whimsical journey through the alphabet as you encounter a delightful assortment of peculiar creatures and absurd scenarios. From the adventurous antics of an acrobatic alligator to the mischievous mischief of a zany zebra, each page is filled with vibrant illustrations and clever rhymes that will captivate readers of all ages. Prepare to be enchanted by this imaginative and humorous alphabet book that will leave you smiling from A to Z.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Creativity, Imagination, Humor
