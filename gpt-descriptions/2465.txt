Synopsis: 
Carmen, a captivating and enigmatic gypsy woman, enters the life of Don José, a soldier stationed in Seville. As their paths intertwine, Don José becomes infatuated with Carmen, leading him down a dangerous path of obsession, jealousy, and ultimately, tragedy. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Spain, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, desire, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Tragedy

Themes: 
Love and passion, Fate and free will, Jealousy and obsession
