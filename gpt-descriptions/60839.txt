Synopsis: In 'A Pride of Islands' by MacApp, C. C., a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they soon discover that the island holds dark secrets and hidden dangers. With tensions rising and trust being tested, the group must navigate through treacherous terrain and face their own inner demons. Will they be able to escape the island's clutches, or will they become trapped forever?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Survival

Themes: Survival, Trust, Betrayal, Redemption
