Synopsis: In 'Firebrands', Davis and Martin take readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of extraordinary individuals who challenge the status quo and ignite change. From fearless activists to revolutionary thinkers, this captivating collection of biographical sketches explores the untold stories of those who dared to challenge the norms of their time. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the struggles, triumphs, and lasting legacies of these firebrands who shaped history.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Activism, Rebellion, Social Change
