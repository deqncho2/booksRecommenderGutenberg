Synopsis:
In 'The Three Fates' by Crawford, F. Marion, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as three women, each with their own unique powers, come together to shape the destiny of those around them. Set against the backdrop of ancient Greece, their fates intertwine with those of mortals, gods, and mythical creatures, leading to unexpected twists and turns. As the story unravels, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the power of fate itself is questioned. With its richly detailed world and complex characters, 'The Three Fates' is a mesmerizing journey that explores the intricate web of destiny and the choices that can alter its course forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Fate and Destiny, Power and Control, Identity and Self-Discovery
