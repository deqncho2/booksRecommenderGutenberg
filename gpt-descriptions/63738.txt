Synopsis: In 'Subscription the disgrace of the English Church', Charles Nourse Wodehouse takes readers on a captivating journey through the underbelly of the English Church, exposing scandalous secrets and hidden agendas. As the protagonist delves deeper into the dark corners of the institution, he uncovers a web of deceit, corruption, and betrayal that threatens to shake the very foundations of the Church. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of faith and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Religion, Power
