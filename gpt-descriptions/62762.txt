Synopsis: 

'American Beer: Glimpses of Its History and Description of Its Manufacture' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of American beer. From its humble beginnings to its rise as a cultural icon, this book delves into the rich history and intricate process behind the creation of this beloved beverage. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Thomann paints a vivid picture of the people, places, and innovations that have shaped American beer into what it is today. Whether you're a beer enthusiast or simply curious about the origins of this iconic drink, this book is sure to leave you thirsty for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Culinary

Themes: American culture, Brewing techniques, Historical development
