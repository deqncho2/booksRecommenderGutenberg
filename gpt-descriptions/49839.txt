Synopsis: 

In "The Normans in England (1066-1154)", embark on a captivating journey through one of the most transformative periods in English history. From the moment William the Conqueror set foot on English soil in 1066, a new era began, forever altering the course of the nation. This enthralling account delves into the intricate web of political intrigue, epic battles, and cultural clashes that shaped the Norman dynasty's reign over England for nearly a century. Discover the triumphs and tribulations of the Norman kings, the struggles for power, and the profound impact they had on the English people and their way of life. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Medieval history

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural assimilation, Conquest and its consequences
