Synopsis: 
"The Romance of Modern Invention" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of technological advancements. In this non-technical book, Archibald Williams presents intriguing descriptions of various inventions, including wireless telegraphy, liquid air, modern artillery, submarines, dirigible torpedoes, solar motors, and airships. Each chapter delves into the wonders and possibilities of these groundbreaking creations, captivating the reader's imagination and leaving them eager to learn more about the marvels of modern science.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Technological advancements, Innovation, Scientific curiosity
