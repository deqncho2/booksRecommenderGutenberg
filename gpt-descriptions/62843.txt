Synopsis: In 'Georgia's Stone Mountain' by Neal Willard, a young woman named Georgia finds herself drawn to the mysterious Stone Mountain, a place rumored to hold ancient secrets and hidden treasures. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, Georgia encounters unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries, all while unraveling the enigmatic history of the mountain. With each step closer to the truth, Georgia discovers that the secrets of Stone Mountain may be more personal and life-changing than she ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of personal identity, Uncovering hidden truths, The power of ancient history
