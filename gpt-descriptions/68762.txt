Synopsis: 

In 'Chinese pottery and porcelain; vol. 2. Ming and Ch'ing Porcelain', author R. L. Hobson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and exquisite craftsmanship of Chinese porcelain during the Ming and Ch'ing dynasties. From the vibrant colors and intricate designs to the fascinating techniques employed by skilled artisans, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of the beauty and significance of these remarkable works of art. Delve into the world of Ming and Ch'ing porcelain and uncover the secrets behind its enduring allure.

Genres: Art History, Cultural Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Chinese Art, Craftsmanship, Cultural Heritage
