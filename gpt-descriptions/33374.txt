Synopsis: In 'Wake (First 25,000 words)' by Robert J. Sawyer, Caitlin Decter, a blind teenage math genius, is given the opportunity to see for the first time through an experimental implant. As she explores the world of sight, she discovers a hidden layer of reality that only she can perceive. With the help of an artificial intelligence named Webmind, Caitlin embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind this mysterious new dimension. As she delves deeper, she realizes that her newfound sight may hold the key to humanity's future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Perception, Identity, Human Potential
