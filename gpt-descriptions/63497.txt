Synopsis: 

In 'Medicina Gymnastica: or, A treatise concerning the power of exercise, with respect to the animal oeconomy; and the great necessity of it in the cure of several distempers', Francis Fuller explores the transformative power of exercise on the human body and its crucial role in healing various ailments. Through a comprehensive examination of the relationship between exercise and the body's functioning, Fuller reveals the immense potential of physical activity in promoting overall well-being and combating diseases. This enlightening treatise delves into the importance of exercise in the treatment of different disorders, offering valuable insights into the healing properties of movement.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical literature, Health and wellness

Themes: The power of exercise, Physical well-being, Disease treatment and prevention
