Synopsis: In 'The Hands' by Richard A. Sternbach, a renowned surgeon finds himself haunted by a mysterious and unsettling phenomenon involving his own hands. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel his sanity and everything he holds dear. With each passing day, the surgeon's grip on reality weakens, leading him on a gripping journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Redemption
