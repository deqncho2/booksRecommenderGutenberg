Synopsis: 

Embassy to the Eastern Courts of Cochin-China, Siam, and Muscat: In the U. S. Sloop-of-war Peacock, David Geisinger, Commander, During the Years 1832-3-4 takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic and mysterious lands of Cochin-China, Siam, and Muscat. Join Commander David Geisinger and his crew aboard the U.S. Sloop-of-war Peacock as they navigate treacherous waters, encounter fascinating cultures, and face unexpected challenges. From the opulent courts of Cochin-China to the vibrant streets of Siam and the enigmatic realm of Muscat, this gripping account offers a firsthand glimpse into the diplomatic missions and adventures of the early 19th century.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Diplomacy, Cultural Exchange
