Synopsis: In 'Humour of the North', Lawrence J. Burpee takes readers on a delightful journey through the humorous side of life in the North. From hilarious anecdotes of Arctic explorers to amusing tales of life in remote Canadian communities, this collection of witty stories and amusing observations will leave readers chuckling and craving for more. With a keen eye for the absurd and a knack for storytelling, Burpee captures the unique humor that thrives in the harsh and unforgiving landscapes of the North.

Genres: Humor, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Arctic exploration, Canadian culture, Resilience
