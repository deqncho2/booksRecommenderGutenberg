Synopsis: In 'Mother's Little Rhyme Book', a young woman discovers an old, mysterious book filled with enchanting rhymes that seem to hold a hidden power. As she delves deeper into the verses, she becomes entangled in a world of magic and secrets, where the lines between reality and fantasy blur. With each rhyme she unravels, she uncovers a dark and haunting truth that could change her life forever. As she races against time to decipher the book's secrets, she must confront her own fears and make a choice that will determine the fate of those she loves. 

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Magic, Secrets, Identity, Sacrifice
