Synopsis: In 'The Helpmate' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Emily finds herself in a peculiar situation when she agrees to become a helpmate for a wealthy family. As she navigates the complexities of her new role, Emily uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the very fabric of her existence. With each twist and turn, she must decide whether to conform to societal expectations or forge her own path, ultimately discovering the power of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Social class, Gender roles, Power dynamics
