Synopsis: In 'The Penang Pirate: and, The Lost Pinnace', John C. Hutcheson takes readers on a thrilling adventure set in the exotic world of pirates and hidden treasures. When Captain Dugald Cameron and his crew stumble upon a mysterious map, they embark on a perilous journey to uncover a long-lost shipwreck and its valuable cargo. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter ruthless pirates, the crew must rely on their wit, courage, and camaraderie to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this tale of high-seas adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Treasure Hunting
