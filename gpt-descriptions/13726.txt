Synopsis: In 'Apology, Crito, and Phaedo of Socrates' by Plato, follow the captivating journey of the renowned philosopher Socrates as he faces his trial, confronts his impending execution, and delves into the mysteries of life and death. Through thought-provoking dialogues and profound philosophical arguments, Socrates challenges societal norms, explores the nature of justice, and contemplates the immortality of the soul. As his loyal friends and disciples stand by his side, witness the intellectual brilliance and unwavering integrity of Socrates in the face of adversity.

Genres: Philosophy, Drama, Historical

Themes: Justice, Morality, Wisdom, Immortality of the Soul
