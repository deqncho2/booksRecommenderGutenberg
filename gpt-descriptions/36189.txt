Synopsis: In 'In Search of a Son' by William S. Walsh, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about his son's mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of ancient artifacts and hidden secrets, he must confront his own past and make unimaginable sacrifices to find the answers he seeks. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Family, Identity, Redemption
