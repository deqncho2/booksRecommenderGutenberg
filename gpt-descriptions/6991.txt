Synopsis: 
'Across the Years' by Eleanor H. Porter is a heartwarming and captivating story that follows the life of young Polly Milton. After a tragic accident leaves her orphaned and alone, Polly is sent to live with her wealthy but distant aunt. Despite the challenges she faces, Polly's unwavering optimism and determination lead her on a remarkable journey of self-discovery, friendship, and love. As she navigates through the ups and downs of life, Polly learns valuable lessons about resilience, forgiveness, and the power of kindness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Love, Self-discovery, Kindness
