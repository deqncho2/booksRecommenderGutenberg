Synopsis: In 'The Rushton Boys at Treasure Cove; Or, The Missing Chest of Gold' by Davenport Spencer, a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a mysterious map that leads them to a hidden treasure. As they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the missing chest of gold, they encounter unexpected challenges, dangerous obstacles, and a secret society determined to keep the treasure hidden. With their friendship and resourcefulness tested, the Rushton Boys must rely on their wits and courage to solve the puzzle and claim the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
