Synopsis: 
'Duty, and Other Irish Comedies' by Seumas O'Brien is a collection of hilarious and heartwarming tales set in the charming Irish countryside. From a mischievous leprechaun causing havoc to a bumbling priest trying to keep his parish in order, these comedic stories will have you laughing out loud and falling in love with the quirky characters. With each tale filled with wit, charm, and unexpected twists, this collection is a delightful journey into the lighter side of Irish life.

Genres: Comedy, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Irish culture, Humor, Community
