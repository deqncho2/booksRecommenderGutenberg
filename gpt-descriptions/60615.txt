Synopsis: 
In 'A Ride through Syria to Damascus and Baalbec, and ascent of Mount Hermon', Abram, Edward takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the captivating landscapes of Syria. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the author recounts his thrilling adventures, from traversing ancient ruins to ascending the majestic Mount Hermon. As he delves into the rich history and cultural tapestry of the region, readers will be transported to a world of wonder and discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
