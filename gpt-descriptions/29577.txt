Synopsis:
'Mayflower (Flor de mayo): A Tale of the Valencian Seashore' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente is a captivating novel set in the picturesque Valencian seashore. The story follows the lives of three young women from different social backgrounds who find themselves entangled in a web of love, ambition, and betrayal. As they navigate the complexities of their relationships and the challenges of their time, their destinies become intertwined in unexpected ways. With vivid descriptions of the stunning coastal landscape and a cast of compelling characters, 'Mayflower' takes readers on a journey of passion, secrets, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Social class and inequality, Ambition and self-discovery
