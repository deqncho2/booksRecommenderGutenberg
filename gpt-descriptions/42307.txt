Synopsis: 
In 'Frank in the Woods' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Frank embarks on an exciting adventure when he finds himself lost in the mysterious woods. As he navigates through the dense forest, Frank encounters unexpected challenges and encounters with both friendly and dangerous creatures. With his determination and resourcefulness, Frank must rely on his wits to survive and find his way back home. This thrilling tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the woods.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Self-reliance
