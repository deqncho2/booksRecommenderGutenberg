Synopsis: In 'Is it I? A book for every man.', Horatio Robinson Storer takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a man grappling with his own identity and purpose. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, the protagonist embarks on a quest for self-discovery, navigating the complexities of love, ambition, and personal growth. As secrets unravel and unexpected twists arise, readers are left questioning their own perceptions of truth and the nature of human existence. This thought-provoking novel delves into the depths of the human psyche, challenging readers to confront their own inner demons and ultimately find redemption.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Love, Ambition, Truth
