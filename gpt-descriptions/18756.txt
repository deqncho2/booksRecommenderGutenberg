Synopsis: In 'The Heart's Kingdom' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman named Betty finds herself torn between two suitors who represent two very different worlds. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, Betty must make a choice that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. Set against the backdrop of a small Southern town, this captivating novel explores the power of love, the pursuit of happiness, and the courage to follow one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Self-Discovery
