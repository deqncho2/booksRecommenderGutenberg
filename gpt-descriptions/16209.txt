Synopsis: In 'Lectures on the English Poets; Delivered at the Surrey Institution,' the renowned essayist and critic William Hazlitt takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of some of the greatest English poets. From the enchanting verses of Shakespeare to the profound musings of Milton, Hazlitt's lectures offer a unique insight into the minds and creative processes of these literary giants. With his eloquent and passionate analysis, Hazlitt brings these poets to life, exploring their genius, their struggles, and the enduring impact of their words. 

Genres: Literary criticism, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Poetry, Artistic inspiration, Genius, Literary analysis
