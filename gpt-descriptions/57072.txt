Synopsis: 
In 'Autobiography of a Child' by Hannah Lynch, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a young protagonist as they navigate the complexities of childhood. Through vivid and heartfelt storytelling, the author delves into the inner world of this child, exploring their dreams, fears, and the profound impact of their experiences. As the narrative unfolds, readers are invited to reflect on the universal themes of innocence, resilience, and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: 
Autobiography, Coming-of-Age, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Innocence, Resilience, Self-Discovery
