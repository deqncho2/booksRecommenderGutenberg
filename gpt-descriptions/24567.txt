Synopsis: In 'Blind Man's Lantern' by Lang, Allen Kim, a mysterious lantern falls into the possession of a blind man, granting him the ability to see the world in a way he never thought possible. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the lantern's origins, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden truths that challenge his perception of reality. With each revelation, the blind man's world is turned upside down, leading him to question the nature of truth, identity, and the power of perception itself.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Identity, Truth, Mystery
