Synopsis: 

In 'Presidential Candidates: Containing Sketches, Biographical, Personal and Political, of Prominent Candidates for the Presidency in 1860', author David W. Bartlett delves into the lives and political careers of the most influential figures vying for the presidency during a pivotal moment in American history. Through detailed sketches, Bartlett offers a captivating glimpse into the personal and political lives of these candidates, shedding light on their motivations, ambitions, and the challenges they faced. As the nation stands on the brink of change, readers will be enthralled by the intricate web of personal and political dynamics that shaped the race for the presidency in 1860.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Presidential campaigns, American politics, Leadership
