Synopsis: In 'Wulfric the Weapon Thane: A Story of the Danish Conquest of East Anglia', Charles W. Whistler takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous period of the Danish conquest of East Anglia. The story follows the courageous and enigmatic Wulfric, a skilled weapon thane, as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, betrayal, and love. As the Danish invaders tighten their grip on the region, Wulfric finds himself torn between loyalty to his people and his own desires. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Whistler paints a vivid picture of a time when honor and survival clashed in the face of overwhelming odds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Survival
