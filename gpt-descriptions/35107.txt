Synopsis: In "Cruise and Captures of the Alabama," Albert M. Goodrich takes readers on a thrilling journey through the high seas during the American Civil War. This gripping narrative follows the daring exploits of the Confederate raider, the CSS Alabama, as it wreaks havoc on Union merchant ships. Goodrich's firsthand account provides a vivid portrayal of the intense naval battles, the relentless pursuit by Union forces, and the dramatic captures that unfold on the open ocean. With each page, readers will be captivated by the suspense, bravery, and strategic maneuvers that shape this remarkable tale.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Military

Themes: Naval warfare, Patriotism, Survival
