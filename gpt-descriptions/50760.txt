Synopsis: In "Hassan; or, The Child of the Pyramid: An Egyptian Tale," readers are transported to the mystical land of Egypt, where they follow the captivating journey of a young protagonist named Hassan. Born amidst the ancient pyramids, Hassan's life takes an unexpected turn when he discovers a hidden secret that sets him on a path filled with adventure, danger, and self-discovery. As he navigates through the vibrant streets of Cairo and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Hassan's courage and determination are put to the test. Will he be able to unravel the mysteries of his past and fulfill his destiny?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Fate, Exploration of Ancient Egypt, Coming-of-Age
