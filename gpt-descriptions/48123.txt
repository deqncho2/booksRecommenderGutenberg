Synopsis: In 'Echoes of Old Lancashire', William E. A. Axon takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and folklore of Lancashire, England. From ancient legends and ghostly tales to the customs and traditions of the region, this book unveils the hidden stories that have shaped Lancashire's identity. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Axon brings to life the fascinating characters and events that have left their echoes in the Lancashire landscape. Prepare to be enthralled by the enchanting tales and mysteries that lie within the pages of this captivating work.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Folklore, Identity, Tradition
