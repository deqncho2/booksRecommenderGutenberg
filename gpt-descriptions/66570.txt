Synopsis: In 'Hunting License' by James V. McConnell, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he stumbles upon a mysterious hunting license. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets behind the license, he becomes the target of a relentless pursuit, forcing him to confront his own survival instincts and question the true nature of humanity.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Morality, Survival
