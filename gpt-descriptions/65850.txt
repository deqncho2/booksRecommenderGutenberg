Synopsis: In 'The House of Spies' by Deeping, Warwick, a young and ambitious journalist, Robert Drayton, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society operating within the walls of an old mansion. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the security of the nation. With the help of an enigmatic woman, Drayton must navigate a treacherous world of espionage and deception, where nothing is as it seems, and trust is a luxury he cannot afford.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Intrigue and suspense
