Synopsis: In 'Fairview Boys at Camp Mystery; or, the Old Hermit and His Secret' by Frederick Gordon, a group of adventurous boys from Fairview embark on a thrilling summer camp adventure. As they settle into their new surroundings, they stumble upon a mysterious old hermit living deep in the woods. Intrigued by his reclusive nature, the boys become determined to uncover the hermit's secret, leading them on a suspenseful journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. With their friendship and resourcefulness put to the test, the Fairview Boys must unravel the enigma surrounding the old hermit before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Exploration
