Synopsis: 
'The Cynic's Breviary: Maxims and Anecdotes from Nicolas de Chamfort' is a collection of thought-provoking and witty reflections by the renowned French writer, Nicolas de Chamfort. Through a series of concise maxims and entertaining anecdotes, Chamfort explores the complexities of human nature, society, and the pursuit of happiness. With his sharp observations and biting humor, he delves into the contradictions and follies of the world, challenging readers to question their own beliefs and assumptions. This collection offers a unique glimpse into the mind of a brilliant cynic, whose words continue to resonate with readers today.

Genres: Philosophy, Satire, Aphorisms

Themes: Human nature, Society, Happiness, Irony, Critique
