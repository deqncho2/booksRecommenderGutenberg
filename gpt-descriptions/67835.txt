Synopsis: In 'Better days; or, A Millionaire of To-morrow', the lives of two young orphans, Jack and Jill, take an unexpected turn when they stumble upon a mysterious treasure map. With their newfound hope, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, friendship, and the pursuit of a brighter future. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and encounter eccentric characters, Jack and Jill's determination and resilience are put to the test. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles in their path and find the key to a better life?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Hope, Resilience, Friendship, Pursuit of a better life
