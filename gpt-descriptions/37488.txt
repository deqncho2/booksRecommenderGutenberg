Synopsis: 
'Asgard Stories: Tales from Norse Mythology' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of Norse mythology. From the creation of the cosmos to the epic battles of gods and giants, this collection of tales immerses readers in the thrilling adventures of Odin, Thor, Loki, and other legendary figures. As the stories unfold, readers will witness the power struggles, quests for wisdom, and the complex relationships between gods and mortals. With vivid storytelling and vivid illustrations, this book brings to life the timeless tales of Asgard, captivating readers of all ages.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Power and destiny, Heroism and sacrifice, The nature of gods and mortals
