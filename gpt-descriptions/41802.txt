Synopsis: In 'Frank in the Mountains' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Frank embarks on an exciting adventure in the mountains. When Frank's family decides to spend their summer vacation in a remote cabin, he eagerly anticipates the opportunity to explore the wilderness. However, what starts as a simple hiking trip quickly turns into a thrilling journey filled with unexpected challenges and encounters with nature. As Frank navigates through treacherous terrains and encounters various wildlife, he learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the power of the human spirit. Will Frank be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and emerge victorious from his mountain adventure?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Exploration, Nature appreciation
