Synopsis: 
In "Bessie in the City" by Joanna H. Mathews, readers are introduced to Bessie, a young woman who leaves her small town behind to pursue her dreams in the bustling city. As she navigates the challenges and adventures of urban life, Bessie discovers a world filled with unexpected friendships, heartwarming moments, and life-changing experiences. With each step she takes, Bessie learns valuable lessons about love, self-discovery, and the power of following one's passions. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Contemporary

Themes: Self-discovery, Pursuit of dreams, Friendship
