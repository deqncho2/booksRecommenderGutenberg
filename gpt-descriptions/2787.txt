Synopsis: 

Polly Milton, a simple and old-fashioned country girl, ventures into the bustling city of Boston to visit her wealthy and fashionable friends, the Shaws. As she navigates the world of high society, Polly's genuine and kind-hearted nature clashes with the superficiality and materialism of her new acquaintances. Despite the challenges she faces, Polly's unwavering authenticity and strong moral compass inspire those around her, ultimately leading to unexpected transformations and heartfelt connections.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Authenticity, Morality, Social class, Gender roles
