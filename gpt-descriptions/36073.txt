Synopsis: In 'The Poll for an Assistant Minister for the Parish of St. Peter Mancroft, Norwich' by Anonymous, the quiet town of Norwich is thrown into chaos as the election for a new assistant minister takes an unexpected turn. As the candidates vie for the position, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the community is left questioning their faith and values. With scandalous rumors and hidden agendas, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to in order to secure power and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power struggles, Betrayal, Morality, Religion
