Synopsis: 
In 'Overland: A Novel' by John William De Forest, a group of diverse and intriguing characters embark on a perilous journey across the American West during the mid-19th century. As they face the challenges of the untamed wilderness, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing the complexities of human nature and the transformative power of the frontier. With vivid descriptions and a gripping narrative, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable adventure that explores the depths of human resilience and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival, Identity, Human Nature
