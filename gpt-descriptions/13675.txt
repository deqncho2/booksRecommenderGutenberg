Synopsis: 

'Goody Two-Shoes: A Facsimile Reproduction of the Edition of 1766' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young orphan girl named Margery Meanwell. Left with only one shoe, Margery faces numerous hardships and challenges, but her unwavering determination and kind heart propel her towards a brighter future. As she navigates the complexities of society, Margery's resilience and optimism inspire those around her, ultimately leading to unexpected opportunities and a chance at happiness. This facsimile reproduction of the original 1766 edition allows readers to immerse themselves in the charming and timeless tale of Goody Two-Shoes.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Moral Tale

Themes: Perseverance, Kindness, Social Mobility
