Synopsis:
In the small Scottish town of Drumtochty, a mysterious figure known as the Red Axe has been terrorizing the community, leaving a trail of fear and destruction in their wake. As the townspeople live in constant fear, a young and determined hero emerges, vowing to put an end to the Red Axe's reign of terror. With the help of a loyal group of friends, they embark on a thrilling and dangerous journey to uncover the identity of the Red Axe and bring justice to their town. Filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and a touch of romance, 'The Red Axe' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice, Courage, Identity
