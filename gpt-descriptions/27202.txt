Synopsis:
In 'Wagner, the Wehr-Wolf' by George W. M. Reynolds, a young man named Wagner finds himself entangled in a dark and mysterious world after a fateful encounter with a supernatural creature. As he grapples with his newfound powers and struggles to control his inner beast, Wagner becomes embroiled in a web of treachery, romance, and revenge. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate through a shadowy underworld filled with werewolves, witches, and other supernatural beings. Will Wagner succumb to his primal instincts or find a way to harness his powers for good?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Horror, Fantasy

Themes: Transformation, Identity, Good vs. Evil
