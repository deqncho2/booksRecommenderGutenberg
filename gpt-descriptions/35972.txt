Synopsis: In 'For the School Colours' by Angela Brazil, a spirited young girl named Daphne finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she joins a prestigious boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of school life, Daphne discovers hidden secrets, forms unexpected friendships, and uncovers a mystery that could change everything. With courage and determination, she must unravel the truth and prove her loyalty to her newfound companions. This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the thrilling twists and turns that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Discovery
