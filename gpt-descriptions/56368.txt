Synopsis: In 'Alhalla, or the Lord of Talladega: A Tale of the Creek War', Henry Rowe Schoolcraft takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous Creek War. Set in the early 19th century, the story follows the life of Alhalla, a young Creek warrior torn between his loyalty to his people and his love for a young woman from a rival tribe. As the war escalates, Alhalla finds himself facing difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his people.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Identity, Conflict
