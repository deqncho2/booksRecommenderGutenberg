Synopsis: 
The 'Fornander collection of Hawaiian antiquities and folk-lore, Volume 1' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich history and mythology of the Hawaiian islands. Through the accounts of the Hawaiians themselves, this collection explores the formation of the islands, the origins of their race, and the fascinating traditions of their migrations. Drawing from original sources, this volume offers a unique and immersive glimpse into the ancient tales and legends that have shaped the Hawaiian culture.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Folklore, History

Themes: 
Mythology, Cultural Heritage, Migration
