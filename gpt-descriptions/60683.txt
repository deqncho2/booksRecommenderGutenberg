Synopsis: 
In 'Baker's Dozens' by Jim Harmon, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious disappearances that seem to be connected to the local bakery. As the townspeople become increasingly suspicious, a young journalist sets out to uncover the truth behind the vanishing acts. With each clue she uncovers, the mystery deepens, leading her down a dark and unexpected path. As the tension rises, she must confront her own fears and confront the sinister forces at play before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town paranoia, The power of truth
