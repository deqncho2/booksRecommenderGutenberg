Synopsis: In 'Peter and Jane; Or, The Missing Heir', a gripping tale of mystery and intrigue, Sarah Macnaughtan weaves a captivating story that follows the lives of two siblings, Peter and Jane. When their wealthy uncle suddenly disappears, leaving behind a perplexing riddle, the siblings embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth and find the missing heir. As they delve deeper into the secrets of their family's past, they encounter unexpected allies and face dangerous adversaries, all while unraveling a web of deception that spans generations. With each clue they uncover, Peter and Jane find themselves drawn into a world of hidden treasures, hidden identities, and hidden dangers. Will they be able to solve the mystery and discover the missing heir before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Identity, Loyalty
