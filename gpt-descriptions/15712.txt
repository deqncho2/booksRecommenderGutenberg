Synopsis: In 'Hugo: A Fantasia on Modern Themes' by Arnold Bennett, we are introduced to Hugo, a young man with a unique perspective on life. As he navigates the challenges of the modern world, Hugo's unconventional ideas and actions lead him on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Social commentary

Themes: Individualism, Identity, Society and its expectations
