Synopsis: In "Examination of the Rev. Mr. Harris's scriptural researches on the licitness of the slave trade," James Ramsay delves into a thought-provoking analysis of the controversial topic of the slave trade. Through meticulous scriptural research, Ramsay challenges the prevailing beliefs of his time, offering a fresh perspective that will captivate readers and ignite their curiosity. As he presents his findings, Ramsay raises important questions about morality, justice, and the interpretation of religious texts, leaving readers eager to explore the depths of his argument.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Religious

Themes: Morality, Justice, Religion, Slavery
