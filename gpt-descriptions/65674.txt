Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of a Marmotte: Sold for the Distressed Irish' by Grey, Eliza, follow the captivating journey of a courageous marmotte named Mimi as she embarks on a daring quest to save her fellow creatures from a dire fate. From the enchanting forests of France to the bustling streets of Dublin, Mimi encounters a host of colorful characters and faces numerous challenges that test her resilience and determination. This heartwarming tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, as Mimi's bravery and resourcefulness inspire those around her to join in the fight for justice and compassion.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Animal Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Social Justice
