Synopsis: 
'The Young Lady's Mentor: A Guide to the Formation of Character. In a Series of Letters to Her Unknown Friends' is a captivating epistolary novel that follows the journey of a young lady as she navigates the complexities of life through a series of heartfelt letters. Written by an anonymous author, these letters serve as a mentorship guide, offering advice, wisdom, and encouragement to the protagonist and her unknown friends. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a world of self-discovery, personal growth, and the pursuit of a virtuous character. With each letter, the young lady's mentor imparts valuable lessons on love, friendship, morality, and the importance of cultivating a strong sense of self. 

Genres: Epistolary fiction, Self-help, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Personal growth, Morality, Friendship, Love
