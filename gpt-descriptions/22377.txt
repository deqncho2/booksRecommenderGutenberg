Synopsis: In "Rollo in Switzerland" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting journey to Switzerland with his family. As they explore the stunning landscapes, encounter local customs, and learn about the country's history, Rollo's curiosity and sense of adventure are ignited. Along the way, he encounters unexpected challenges and makes new friends, all while discovering the beauty and wonders of Switzerland.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Immersion, Family Bonds
