Synopsis: In 'The Great Court Scandal' by William Le Queux, a mysterious and scandalous event unfolds within the prestigious walls of the Great Court. As the story unravels, secrets are exposed, alliances are tested, and the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and danger. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the scandal that threatens to shake the foundations of the Great Court.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power dynamics
