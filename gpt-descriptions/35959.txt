Synopsis: 
'The Widow's Vow: A Farce, in Two Acts' follows the misadventures of a recently widowed woman who finds herself entangled in a web of comedic chaos. As she navigates through a series of absurd situations and encounters with eccentric characters, the widow must make a vow that will determine her future. With mistaken identities, hilarious misunderstandings, and unexpected twists, this farce promises to keep readers entertained from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Love and marriage, Identity and deception
