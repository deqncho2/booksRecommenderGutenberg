Synopsis: In 'Mother-Meg; or, The Story of Dickie's Attic' by Catharine Shaw, readers are introduced to the mysterious attic of Dickie, a young boy who discovers a hidden world within its walls. As he explores this secret realm, he encounters a captivating character named Mother-Meg, who holds the key to unlocking the attic's secrets. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that will challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Self-discovery
