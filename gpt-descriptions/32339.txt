Synopsis: In 'Brink of Madness' by Walter J. Sheldon, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a patient with a mysterious past is admitted to his care. As he delves deeper into the patient's troubled mind, the doctor uncovers a web of secrets and dark memories that threaten to consume them both. With each session, the boundaries between sanity and madness blur, leading to a gripping psychological battle that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Mental illness, Identity, Memory, Perception
