Synopsis: 
In Volume 04 of 'The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D.', readers are introduced to two captivating collections of essays: 'The Adventurer' and 'The Idler'. 'The Adventurer' takes readers on a journey through various moral and social issues, exploring the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of happiness. Meanwhile, 'The Idler' offers a delightful blend of wit and wisdom, as the author reflects on the idleness and follies of society. Both collections provide a thought-provoking and entertaining exploration of the human condition.

Genres: 
Essay, Satire, Literary Criticism

Themes: 
Human nature, Morality, Society, Happiness, Folly
