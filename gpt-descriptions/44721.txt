Synopsis: In 'The Pillars of the House; Or, Under Wode, Under Rode, Vol. 2 (of 2)', the captivating sequel to the first volume, the lives of the May family continue to unfold amidst the backdrop of the English countryside. As the children grow older, they face new challenges and embark on their own journeys of self-discovery. Secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed, all while the family's steadfast love and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family bonds, Identity, Resilience, Secrets and revelations
