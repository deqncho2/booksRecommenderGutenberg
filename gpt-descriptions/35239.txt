Synopsis: In "Oldfield: A Kentucky Tale of the Last Century" by Nancy Huston Banks, readers are transported to the enchanting world of 19th-century Kentucky. The story revolves around the Oldfield family, who find themselves caught in a web of secrets, love, and tragedy. As the family's fortunes rise and fall, they must navigate the complexities of societal expectations, personal desires, and the ever-changing landscape of their beloved Kentucky. Through vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Banks weaves a tale that explores the timeless themes of family, identity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Saga, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Social expectations, Love and loss, Identity, Pursuit of happiness
