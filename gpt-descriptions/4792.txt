Synopsis: In Freedom's Cause: A Story of Wallace and Bruce is a thrilling historical novel that takes readers on a captivating journey through the Scottish Wars of Independence. Set in the 14th century, the story follows the adventures of young Archie Forbes, who finds himself caught up in the struggle for freedom against English oppression. As Archie becomes entangled with legendary figures such as William Wallace and Robert the Bruce, he must navigate treacherous battles, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices. With its vivid descriptions and gripping narrative, this novel brings to life the heroic struggle of the Scottish people for independence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Freedom, Patriotism, Courage, Loyalty
