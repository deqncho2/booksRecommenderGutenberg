Synopsis: In "Newer Ideals of Peace," Jane Addams explores the complexities of achieving peace in a rapidly changing world. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, she challenges conventional notions of peace and offers fresh perspectives on how to foster harmony among nations. From the impact of industrialization to the role of women in peacebuilding, Addams delves into various aspects of society to uncover the true meaning of peace and its potential for transformation. This captivating work will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and inspired to seek new paths towards a more peaceful world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Social Sciences

Themes: Peace, Social justice, Gender equality, Global cooperation
