Synopsis:
In the quaint town of Tutors' Lane, a mysterious newcomer arrives, turning the lives of its residents upside down. As secrets unravel and hidden connections are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and deception. With each passing day, the stakes grow higher, and the true nature of Tutors' Lane is exposed, leaving everyone questioning who they can trust and what they are willing to sacrifice to protect their own interests.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes:
Deception, Trust, Betrayal
