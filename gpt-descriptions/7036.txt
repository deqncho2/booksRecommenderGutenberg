Synopsis: In 'The Poorhouse Waif and His Divine Teacher: A True Story', Isabel C. Byrum takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a young boy who finds himself in the most unlikely of circumstances. Abandoned and left to the mercy of a harsh world, he discovers an unexpected source of guidance and inspiration that transforms his life forever. This heartwarming true story explores the power of faith, resilience, and the profound impact that one person can have on another.

Genres: Biography, Inspirational, Memoir

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Faith, Redemption, Mentorship
