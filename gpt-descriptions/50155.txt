Synopsis: 
In "Cathay," Li Bai's timeless poetry is masterfully translated by Ezra Pound, transporting readers to the enchanting world of ancient China. Through vivid imagery and lyrical verses, the collection explores themes of love, nature, and the human condition. From majestic landscapes to intimate emotions, "Cathay" offers a captivating glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Chinese literature.

Genres: Poetry, Translation, Classic Literature

Themes: Love, Nature, Human Condition
