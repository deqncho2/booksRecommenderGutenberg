Synopsis: In 'A Victorious Union' by Optic, Oliver, a young soldier named Frank faces the challenges of war and the complexities of love during the American Civil War. As he navigates the treacherous battlefield, he finds himself torn between duty and personal desires. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices made in the pursuit of victory and the power of resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Duty, Resilience and Perseverance
