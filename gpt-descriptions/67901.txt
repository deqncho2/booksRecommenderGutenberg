Synopsis: 

In 'Frank Merriwell in Europe; or, Working His Way Upward' by Standish, Burt L., follow the thrilling adventures of Frank Merriwell as he embarks on a journey across Europe. Faced with numerous challenges and obstacles, Frank must rely on his wit, determination, and athletic prowess to overcome the odds and achieve his goals. From daring escapades in the bustling streets of Paris to unexpected encounters in the picturesque Swiss Alps, Frank's journey takes him through a whirlwind of excitement, danger, and self-discovery. As he navigates the unfamiliar territories of Europe, Frank learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the importance of staying true to oneself. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
