Synopsis:
In "Mr. Justice Raffles," the notorious gentleman thief A.J. Raffles finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes embroiled in a high-profile murder trial. As the trial unfolds, Raffles must navigate the treacherous waters of the courtroom while maintaining his own secret identity. With his quick wit, cunning, and audacious charm, Raffles must outsmart both the law and the true culprits to ensure justice is served.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes:
Identity, Justice, Morality
