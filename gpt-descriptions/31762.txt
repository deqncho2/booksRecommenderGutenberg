Synopsis: In 'The Record of Currupira' by Abernathy, Robert, a young anthropologist embarks on a thrilling journey deep into the heart of the Amazon rainforest. Drawn by the legends of a mythical creature known as Currupira, he becomes entangled in a web of ancient folklore, supernatural encounters, and a race against time to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the rainforest, he discovers that the line between myth and reality is blurred, and that the secrets hidden within the jungle may hold the key to his own destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Mythology, Exploration, Identity
