Synopsis: In the grip of the Hawk: A story of the Maori wars takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous era of the Maori wars in New Zealand. Set against the backdrop of a land torn apart by conflict, the story follows a young soldier named James as he navigates the treacherous terrain of war, facing not only the physical dangers but also the moral dilemmas that arise in the midst of battle. As James becomes entangled in the complex web of alliances and betrayals, he must confront his own beliefs and question the true cost of victory.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Morality, Conflict
