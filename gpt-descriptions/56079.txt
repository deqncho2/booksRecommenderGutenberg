Synopsis: In 'Handy Mandy in Oz', join Mandy, a clever and resourceful girl, as she embarks on a magical adventure to the enchanting land of Oz. With her trusty toolbox and quick thinking, Mandy encounters whimsical creatures, solves puzzling riddles, and faces thrilling challenges, all while trying to find her way back home. Will Mandy's ingenuity and determination be enough to navigate the wonders and dangers of Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Problem-solving
