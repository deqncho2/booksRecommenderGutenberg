Synopsis: 
In 'Alcatraz' by Max Brand, a notorious outlaw named Alcatraz is on the run, pursued by lawmen and bounty hunters alike. With a reputation for being unbeatable, Alcatraz finds himself in a small town where he unexpectedly becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and hidden agendas. As he navigates through a dangerous game of cat and mouse, Alcatraz must rely on his cunning and survival skills to outwit his enemies and uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the stakes get higher, and Alcatraz's past threatens to catch up with him, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Action, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Justice
