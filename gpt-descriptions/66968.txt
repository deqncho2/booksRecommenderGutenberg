Synopsis: In "The Cruise of the "Scandal", and other stories" by Victor Bridges, embark on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, adventure, and unexpected twists. Follow the captivating tales of a stolen yacht, a missing diamond necklace, a secret society, and more. Each story takes readers on a unique and suspenseful ride, leaving them eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind each enigma. With vivid descriptions and intriguing characters, this collection of stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Crime, Deception, Intrigue
