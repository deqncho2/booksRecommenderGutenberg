Synopsis: 

'Under Drake's Flag: A Tale of the Spanish Main' by G. A. Henty is an exhilarating historical adventure set in the 16th century. The story follows the young protagonist, Ned Hearne, as he embarks on a thrilling journey across the treacherous seas of the Spanish Main. Joining Sir Francis Drake's crew, Ned faces perilous battles, encounters with pirates, and the pursuit of hidden treasure. As he navigates through dangerous waters and faces numerous challenges, Ned's courage and loyalty are put to the ultimate test. Will he emerge victorious and fulfill his destiny?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Exploration, Treasure Hunting
