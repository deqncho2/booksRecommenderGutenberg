Synopsis: In 'Uncle Wiggily's Automobile' by Howard Roger Garis, join the lovable Uncle Wiggily Longears on a thrilling adventure as he embarks on a journey in his brand-new automobile. From unexpected detours to encountering peculiar characters, Uncle Wiggily's road trip takes an exciting turn, filled with mystery and excitement. Will Uncle Wiggily reach his destination unscathed? Find out in this delightful tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Exploration
