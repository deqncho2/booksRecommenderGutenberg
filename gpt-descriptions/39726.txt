Synopsis: 
'The Rolliad, in Two Parts: Probationary Odes for the Laureatship & Political Eclogues' is a satirical work that takes aim at the political and cultural landscape of late 18th-century Britain. Through witty and humorous poetry, the authors mock and critique the actions and personalities of prominent figures of the time, including politicians, poets, and even the King himself. From scathing odes to hilarious eclogues, this collection offers a sharp and entertaining commentary on the state of the nation.

Genres: Satire, Poetry

Themes: Political satire, Social commentary, Critique of power and authority
