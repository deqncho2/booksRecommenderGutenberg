Synopsis: In 'The Long Night' by Stanley John Weyman, a young man named Philip Hartsinck finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a dilapidated estate in the French countryside. As he delves into the history of the property, he uncovers a dark secret that has haunted the inhabitants for generations. With the help of an enigmatic woman, Philip must navigate treacherous alliances and face his own demons to unravel the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Redemption and Self-discovery
