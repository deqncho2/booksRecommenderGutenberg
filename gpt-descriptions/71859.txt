Synopsis: In "Accept no substitutes" by Robert Sheckley, a man named Tom wakes up one day to find that his entire life has been replaced by an exact duplicate. As he navigates this strange new world, he begins to question his own identity and the nature of reality itself. With each encounter, Tom is forced to confront the unsettling truth about his existence and the sinister forces at play. This gripping tale takes readers on a mind-bending journey filled with suspense and existential questions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Reality, Existentialism
