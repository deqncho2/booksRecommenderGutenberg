Synopsis: In 'The Builders' by Holden, Fox B., a group of unlikely heroes, each with their own unique skills and backgrounds, come together to seek revenge against a powerful and ruthless enemy. As they embark on a dangerous mission filled with twists and turns, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through a world of betrayal, loyalty, and redemption. With unexpected alliances and heart-pounding action, 'The Builders' is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Action

Themes: Revenge, Loyalty, Redemption
