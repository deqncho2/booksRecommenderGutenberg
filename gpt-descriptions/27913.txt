Synopsis: In 'The Quadroon: Adventures in the Far West' by Reid, Mayne, a young quadroon girl named Zillah finds herself caught between two worlds as she embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed American frontier. With danger lurking at every turn, Zillah must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious characters, and confront her own identity in order to survive. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Survival, Exploration
