Synopsis: In "My First Visit to New England, and Others," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences in the enchanting region of New England. From his encounters with eccentric characters to his exploration of the picturesque landscapes, Howells paints a vivid picture of his adventures. Through his keen observations and witty storytelling, he offers a unique perspective on the people, culture, and traditions of this captivating corner of America.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Exploration of New England, Cultural observations, Personal experiences, Literary analysis
