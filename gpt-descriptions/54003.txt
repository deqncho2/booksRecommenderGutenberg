Synopsis: In 'Farm Legends' by Carleton, Will, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of rural America, where ordinary farm life intertwines with extraordinary tales of mystery, love, and the supernatural. From the haunting legend of the ghostly plowman to the heartwarming story of a farmer's unbreakable bond with his loyal dog, these timeless tales will transport you to a realm where the ordinary becomes extraordinary, and where the land holds secrets waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Folklore

Themes: Rural life, Mystery, Love, Supernatural, Loyalty
