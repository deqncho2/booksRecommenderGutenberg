Synopsis: In the enchanting village of Bess, nestled deep within the woods, a young girl named Bess discovers a hidden world filled with magic and mystery. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, she uncovers ancient secrets and encounters extraordinary creatures that challenge her perception of reality. With danger lurking in every shadow, Bess must summon her courage and unravel the truth behind the village's dark past. Will she be able to protect her newfound world and the people she loves, or will the forces of darkness prevail?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, the power of belief, the balance between light and darkness
