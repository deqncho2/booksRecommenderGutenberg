Synopsis: In 'The Royal Guide to Wax Flower Modelling' by Emma Peachey, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Queen Victoria's court, where a young woman named Amelia finds herself unexpectedly drawn into the secretive art of wax flower modelling. As Amelia navigates the intricacies of palace life and the demands of her newfound talent, she uncovers a hidden world of passion, betrayal, and the power of artistic expression. With each delicate petal she molds, Amelia discovers that the art of wax flower modelling holds the key to unlocking her own desires and finding her true place in society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Art

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Society and class
