Synopsis: In 'Buried Alive: A Tale of These Days' by Arnold Bennett, we are introduced to a young man named Harold, who finds himself trapped in a monotonous and unfulfilling life. Frustrated by the constraints of society and yearning for something more, Harold embarks on a journey of self-discovery that takes him to unexpected places and introduces him to a cast of intriguing characters. As he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Harold must confront the choices that will ultimately shape his future. This gripping tale explores the depths of human desires and the struggle to break free from the confines of a conventional existence.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Individuality, Social conformity, Self-discovery
