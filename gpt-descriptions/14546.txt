Synopsis:
In "Betty Gordon at Mountain Camp; Or, The Mystery of Ida Bellethorne," Betty Gordon finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure as she joins a mountain camp. Little does she know that her time at camp will be filled with unexpected mysteries and secrets waiting to be unraveled. As Betty and her friends delve deeper into the enigma surrounding Ida Bellethorne, they must navigate through treacherous paths, encounter suspicious characters, and uncover hidden clues. With each twist and turn, Betty's determination to solve the mystery grows stronger, leading her on a captivating journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Curiosity, Problem-solving
