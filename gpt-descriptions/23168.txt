Synopsis: In 'A Ride With A Mad Horse In A Freight-Car: 1898', Murray takes readers on a thrilling and unpredictable journey through the American West. The story follows the author's harrowing experience of hitching a ride on a freight train, only to find himself face-to-face with a wild and uncontrollable horse. As the train hurtles through the rugged landscape, Murray must confront his fears and navigate the treacherous terrain to survive. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of this extraordinary ride.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travelogue

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Man vs. Nature
