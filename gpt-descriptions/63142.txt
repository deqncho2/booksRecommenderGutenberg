Synopsis: In 'Harry Joscelyn; vol. 1 of 3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we are introduced to the enigmatic and captivating character of Harry Joscelyn. Set in the vibrant and bustling city of London, the story follows Harry's journey as he navigates through the complexities of society, love, and ambition. With his charm and wit, Harry becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires, drawing the reader into a world of intrigue and suspense. As the plot unfolds, the true nature of Harry's character is gradually revealed, leaving the reader eager to uncover the mysteries that lie ahead in the subsequent volumes.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Betrayal
