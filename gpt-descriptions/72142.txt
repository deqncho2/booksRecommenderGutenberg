Synopsis: In 'Knights and their Days' by Doran, Dr. (John), a group of modern-day knights find themselves thrust into a world of ancient chivalry and honor. As they navigate through treacherous quests and face formidable adversaries, they must confront their own inner demons and discover the true meaning of bravery. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Chivalry, Bravery, Identity
