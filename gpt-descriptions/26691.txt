Synopsis: 
In 'Sanctification' by J. W. Byers, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and gruesome murders that leave the community in a state of fear and paranoia. As the body count rises, a young detective is assigned to the case, determined to uncover the truth behind these horrifying crimes. However, as he delves deeper into the investigation, he begins to question his own sanity and the boundaries between good and evil. With each twist and turn, the detective finds himself entangled in a web of dark secrets and supernatural forces that threaten to consume him. Will he be able to solve the case before it's too late, or will he become the next victim?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Sanity vs. Madness, Secrets and Betrayal
