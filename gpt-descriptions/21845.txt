Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Peter Mink: Sleepy-Time Tales', follow the mischievous adventures of Peter Mink, a clever and resourceful little creature who lives by the river. From daring escapes to unexpected friendships, Peter's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what he will get up to next. With vivid descriptions and charming illustrations, this delightful tale is sure to captivate both young and old alike.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resourcefulness
