Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Golden Summer', Grace and her friends embark on an exciting summer adventure filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected twists. As they explore a picturesque island, they stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling treasure hunt. Along the way, they encounter suspicious characters, face perilous challenges, and uncover long-buried secrets. Will Grace and her friends be able to solve the mystery and find the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Treasure Hunting
