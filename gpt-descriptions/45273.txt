Synopsis: 

"The Cabots and the Discovery of America: With a Brief Description and History of Brandon Hill, the Site of the Cabot Memorial Tower" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of the Cabot family and their significant role in the discovery of America. Elizabeth Hodges skillfully weaves together a narrative that explores the adventures, triumphs, and challenges faced by the Cabots as they embarked on their daring voyages. Alongside this historical account, Hodges provides a vivid description and history of Brandon Hill, the site where the Cabot Memorial Tower stands, adding an extra layer of intrigue to the story. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Family legacy, Historical landmarks
