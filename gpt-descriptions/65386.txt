Synopsis: In 'The Little French Girl' by Anne Douglas Sedgwick, a young Englishwoman named Joan finds herself captivated by the enigmatic charm of a mysterious French girl named Jacqueline. As their paths intertwine, Joan becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected consequences. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century Europe, this captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the power of desire, and the enduring bonds of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Friendship, Betrayal
