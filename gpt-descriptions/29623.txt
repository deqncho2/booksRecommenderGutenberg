Synopsis: In 'The Cuckoo Clock' by Barefoot, Wesley, a young girl named Lucy discovers a mysterious cuckoo clock in her grandmother's attic. Little does she know that this seemingly ordinary clock holds a secret that will transport her to a magical world filled with enchantment and danger. As Lucy embarks on a thrilling adventure, she must navigate through a realm where time is distorted and where the fate of both worlds hangs in the balance. With unexpected allies and treacherous foes, Lucy must unlock the secrets of the cuckoo clock before it's too late. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time, Magic, Courage, Friendship
