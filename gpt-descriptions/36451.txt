Synopsis: In "Richard III: His Life & Character, Reviewed in the Light of Recent Research," author Clements R. Markham delves into the intriguing life and enigmatic character of one of England's most controversial kings. Drawing on the latest research, Markham presents a captivating exploration of Richard III's rise to power, his tumultuous reign, and the mysteries surrounding his legacy. From political intrigue to personal ambition, this book offers a fresh perspective on a historical figure who continues to fascinate and divide opinions.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and ambition, Historical interpretation, Legacy and reputation
