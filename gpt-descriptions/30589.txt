Synopsis: In 'The Continental Dragoon: A Love Story of Philipse Manor-House in 1778', the reader is transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Amidst the backdrop of war, a forbidden love blossoms between a young patriot and a loyalist daughter, setting the stage for a captivating tale of passion, sacrifice, and the struggle for freedom. As the characters navigate the complexities of their divided loyalties, they must confront the consequences of their choices and the power of love in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Patriotism, Conflict and Consequences
