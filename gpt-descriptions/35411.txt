Synopsis: In 'Myra's Well: A Tale of All-Hallow-E'en' by George Francis Dawson, a young woman named Myra finds herself drawn to a mysterious well on the eve of All-Hallow-E'en. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the well, she uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient rituals, supernatural beings, and a dark power that threatens to consume her. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Myra must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and save herself from the clutches of darkness.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural thriller, mystery

Themes: The power of folklore and superstition, the battle between light and darkness, the exploration of hidden realms and ancient rituals
