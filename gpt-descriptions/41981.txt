Synopsis: In a post-apocalyptic world, a young poet embarks on a perilous journey to find the mythical Jewels of Aptor, said to possess incredible powers. Along the way, he encounters a mysterious group of women, a treacherous sorcerer, and a society on the brink of collapse. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Aptor, he must confront his own beliefs and discover the true nature of power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Quest for knowledge, Power and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery
