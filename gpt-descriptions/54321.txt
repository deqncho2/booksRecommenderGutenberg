Synopsis: 

'Newcastle-Upon-Tyne: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant city of Newcastle-upon-Tyne. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of renowned artist Robert J. S. Bertram, this book offers a unique perspective on the city's architecture, landmarks, and hidden gems. From the iconic Tyne Bridge to the bustling Quayside, each sketch captures the essence and charm of Newcastle, inviting readers to explore its rich history and cultural heritage. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for his subject, Bertram's sketches beautifully depict the city's character, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and allure of Newcastle-upon-Tyne.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City exploration, Architectural beauty, Cultural heritage
