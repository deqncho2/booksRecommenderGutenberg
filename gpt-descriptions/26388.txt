Synopsis: In 'A Humorous History of England', Charles Harrison takes readers on a delightful journey through the eccentricities and absurdities of English history. From the bumbling antics of ancient Britons to the comical mishaps of medieval monarchs, Harrison's witty narrative brings to life the hilarious moments that have shaped the nation. With a blend of historical facts and humorous anecdotes, this book offers a refreshing and entertaining perspective on England's past.

Genres: History, Humor

Themes: English culture, Historical events, Satire
