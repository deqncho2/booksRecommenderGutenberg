Synopsis: In 'Army Boys on German Soil: Our Doughboys Quelling the Mobs' by Randall, Homer, a group of young American soldiers find themselves in the heart of Germany after World War I. As they navigate the post-war chaos, they become entangled in a dangerous web of political unrest and secret societies. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Army Boys must uncover the truth and restore order, all while facing unexpected challenges and thrilling adventures.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Post-war turmoil, Courage and resilience, Political intrigue
