Synopsis: In 'The Royal Game of the Ombre', an anonymous author takes us on a thrilling journey into the world of a captivating card game that holds the power to change lives. Set in the year 1665, this intriguing tale follows a group of honorable individuals who become entangled in a high-stakes game of Ombre, a popular card game of the time. As the game progresses, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and alliances are tested, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and manipulation, Loyalty and betrayal, Gambling and risk
