Synopsis: In 'A Lost Cause' by Thorne, Guy, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her late grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the letter, Emily becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With each revelation, she uncovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront her own past. As Emily races against time to uncover the truth, she must navigate a treacherous world of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances, all while trying to protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity, Betrayal, Family bonds
