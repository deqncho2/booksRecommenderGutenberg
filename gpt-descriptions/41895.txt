Synopsis: In 'The Green Bough' by E. Temple Thurston, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love amidst the backdrop of the enchanting English countryside. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and grapples with her own desires, Mary finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own yearning for freedom. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a captivating exploration of love, identity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Self-discovery, Societal expectations, Freedom
