Synopsis: Captain Whalley, a seasoned sailor, finds himself facing the twilight of his career and the challenges that come with it. As he navigates the treacherous waters of his own personal life, he must also confront the moral dilemmas that arise when duty clashes with desire. With his reputation and livelihood hanging in the balance, Captain Whalley must make difficult choices that will ultimately determine his fate. Set against the backdrop of the sea, 'The End of the Tether' is a gripping tale of sacrifice, redemption, and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Psychological

Themes: Morality, Duty, Aging, Redemption
