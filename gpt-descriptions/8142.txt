Synopsis: 

In 'The Riot Act' by Great Britain Parliament, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious event triggers a series of unexpected and dangerous events. As tensions rise and the community becomes divided, the authorities are forced to take drastic measures to restore order. With lives hanging in the balance, the town's residents must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will shape their future.

Genres: Drama, Thriller, Political

Themes: Power and authority, social unrest, individual responsibility
