Synopsis: 

'Lord Milner's Work in South Africa: From its Commencement in 1897 to the Peace of Vereeniging in 1902' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of Lord Milner's involvement in South Africa. From the moment he arrived in 1897 until the historic Peace of Vereeniging in 1902, the book delves into the challenges, triumphs, and controversies that shaped Milner's work and the fate of the nation. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, W. Basil Worsfold paints a vivid picture of the political, social, and economic landscape of South Africa during this critical time. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Colonialism, Imperialism, Nation-building, Conflict and reconciliation
