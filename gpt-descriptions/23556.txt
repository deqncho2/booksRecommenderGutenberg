Synopsis: In 'His Unquiet Ghost: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a young man named John finds himself haunted by a mysterious ghostly presence that seems to be connected to his family's past. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his ancestors, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and tragedy that spans generations. With each revelation, John becomes more entangled in the ghost's grip, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and confront the restless spirit that refuses to be silenced.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Ghostly hauntings, Redemption, Fate
