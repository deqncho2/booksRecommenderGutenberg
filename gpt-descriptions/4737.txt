Synopsis: In 'A Tale of a Tub', Jonathan Swift takes readers on a satirical journey through the lives of three brothers who inherit a peculiar legacy from their late father. As they navigate the absurdities of society, religion, and literature, the brothers find themselves entangled in a series of comical and thought-provoking misadventures. Swift's witty and sharp observations shed light on the follies of human nature, leaving readers both amused and introspective.

Genres: Satire, Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Critique of society, Religion, Human nature
