Synopsis:
'Legend Land, Vol. 2: Being a Collection of Some of the Old Tales Told in Those Western Parts of Britain Served by the Great Western Railway' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting tales of Western Britain. This collection of old stories, passed down through generations, unveils the hidden wonders and magical creatures that inhabit the land. From ancient legends of knights and fairies to tales of love, betrayal, and redemption, each story transports readers to a world where imagination knows no bounds. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be captivated by the rich tapestry of folklore and the timeless lessons that lie within.

Genres:
Folklore, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Magic, Adventure, Love, Redemption, Tradition
