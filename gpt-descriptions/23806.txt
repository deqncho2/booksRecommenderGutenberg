Synopsis: In 'A Temporary Dead-Lock: 1891', Thomas A. Janvier takes us on a thrilling journey through the streets of New York City, where a mysterious crime has left the police baffled. As the investigation unfolds, a web of deceit, secrets, and unexpected alliances emerges, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and clever plot twists, Janvier paints a vivid picture of the city's underbelly, where danger lurks around every corner. Will the truth be uncovered before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Urban Life
