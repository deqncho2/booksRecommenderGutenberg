Synopsis: In "Lincoln, the Politician" by Levy, T. Aaron, delve into the fascinating world of Abraham Lincoln's political career, as he navigates the treacherous waters of American politics. From his humble beginnings to his rise as one of the most influential figures in American history, this book offers a captivating exploration of Lincoln's journey as a politician. With gripping anecdotes and insightful analysis, the author sheds light on the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices that shaped Lincoln's political legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Democracy
