Synopsis: In 'Armageddon, 1970' by Robert W. Krepps, a gripping tale unfolds as the world teeters on the brink of destruction. Set against the backdrop of the Cold War, this thrilling novel follows a group of unlikely heroes who must race against time to prevent a catastrophic event that could change the course of history. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cold War, Apocalypse, Heroism
