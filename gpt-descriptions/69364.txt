Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl's Cookery Book', Benton and Hodge take young readers on a delightful culinary adventure filled with easy-to-follow recipes and charming illustrations. From baking delicious cookies to creating mouthwatering desserts, this book encourages children to explore the joys of cooking while learning valuable skills in the kitchen. With its engaging storytelling and delectable recipes, this book is sure to inspire young chefs to whip up their own culinary masterpieces.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cookbook, Educational

Themes: Cooking, Creativity, Independence, Learning
