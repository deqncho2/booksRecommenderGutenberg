Synopsis: 

'Lost in the Forest: Wandering Will's Adventures in South America' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of South America. When young Will finds himself separated from his expedition group, he must rely on his wits and resourcefulness to survive in a land filled with danger and mystery. From encounters with exotic wildlife to unexpected alliances with indigenous tribes, Will's quest for survival becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural diversity
