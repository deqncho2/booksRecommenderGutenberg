Synopsis: In "The Floors of the Ocean: 1. The North Atlantic," Ewing, Heezen, and Tharp take readers on an extraordinary journey beneath the surface of the North Atlantic. Through a captivating physiographic diagram, they unveil the hidden wonders and mysteries of the ocean's depths, revealing a world that is both awe-inspiring and enigmatic. From the towering underwater mountains to the intricate network of canyons, this exploration of the North Atlantic's floor will leave readers spellbound, eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the waves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Exploration

Themes: Oceanography, Geology, Natural wonders, Discovery
