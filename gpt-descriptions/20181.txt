Synopsis: In 'A Defective Santa Claus' by James Whitcomb Riley, a seemingly ordinary Santa Claus embarks on a whimsical adventure that takes an unexpected turn. As he encounters various challenges and peculiar characters, the true spirit of Christmas is put to the test. With a touch of humor and a dash of mystery, this enchanting tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Holiday

Themes: Christmas spirit, Unexpected journeys
