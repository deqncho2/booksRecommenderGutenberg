Synopsis: In 'Land at Last: A Novel' by Edmund Yates, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the untamed wilderness of a distant land. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Emily must navigate through treacherous landscapes and confront her own inner demons. As she strives to find her place in this new world, she discovers the power of resilience, the importance of human connection, and the transformative nature of self-discovery. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Resilience, Transformation
