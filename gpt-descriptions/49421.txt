Synopsis: In 'The Big Fight (Gallipoli to the Somme)' by David Fallon, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the harrowing battles of World War I. Through the eyes of a diverse group of soldiers, the story unfolds, revealing the brutal realities of war and the unbreakable bonds formed amidst the chaos. As the conflict escalates from the ill-fated Gallipoli campaign to the infamous Battle of the Somme, the characters are tested both physically and emotionally, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and question the meaning of sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this novel captures the true essence of the Great War and the indomitable spirit of those who fought.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Loss, Survival, Sacrifice
