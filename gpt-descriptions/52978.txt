Synopsis: In 'Stem to Stern; or, building the boat' by Optic, Oliver, embark on a thrilling journey as a group of young friends come together to build a boat from scratch. As they navigate the challenges of construction, they also discover the power of teamwork, perseverance, and the joy of creating something extraordinary. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Teamwork, Perseverance, Creativity
