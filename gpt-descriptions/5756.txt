Synopsis: 
In 'The Guest of Quesnay' by Booth Tarkington, a young American artist named John Forsythe finds himself unexpectedly invited to stay at the grand estate of the eccentric Quesnay family in France. As John becomes entangled in the lives of the Quesnays, he discovers a world filled with secrets, hidden desires, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, John's perception of reality becomes blurred, and he must navigate a web of deception and intrigue to uncover the truth. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque French countryside, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of unspoken desires.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Deception, Identity, Forbidden love, Social class
