Synopsis: 

'Life of Liszt' by Ludwig Nohl is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of the renowned composer and pianist, Franz Liszt. From his humble beginnings as a child prodigy to his meteoric rise to fame and his tumultuous relationships, this book offers a fascinating exploration of Liszt's personal and professional journey. Nohl's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the vibrant world of 19th-century Europe, where Liszt's music revolutionized the classical music scene. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the highs and lows of Liszt's life, his relentless pursuit of artistic excellence, and the profound impact he had on the world of music.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Artistic genius, Passion, Love and relationships, Fame and celebrity
