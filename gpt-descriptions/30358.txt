Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Horncastle, from the earliest period to the present time,' Walter J. Conway takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and mysterious past of the charming town of Horncastle. From its humble beginnings to its present-day allure, Conway delves into the fascinating tales and hidden secrets that have shaped this historic place. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he uncovers the forgotten legends, notable figures, and pivotal events that have left an indelible mark on Horncastle's identity. As readers immerse themselves in the pages of this enthralling history, they will uncover a tapestry of triumphs, tragedies, and the enduring spirit of a community that has stood the test of time.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History

Themes: Community resilience, Local heritage, Historical significance
