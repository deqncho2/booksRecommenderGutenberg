Synopsis: In 'Memories of a Hostess: A Chronicle of Eminent Friendships', Annie Fields invites readers into the captivating world of 19th-century Boston's literary elite through the diaries of Mrs. James T. Fields. As a prominent hostess, Mrs. Fields shares her intimate encounters with renowned authors, poets, and artists, offering a unique glimpse into their lives and the vibrant cultural scene of the era. From the lively discussions at her salon to the personal anecdotes and secrets shared behind closed doors, this chronicle paints a vivid portrait of the intellectual and social circles that shaped American literature. 

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary History

Themes: Friendship, Intellectualism, Cultural Exchange
