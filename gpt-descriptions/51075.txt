Synopsis: In 'A Stone and a Spear' by Raymond F. Jones, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing treacherous adversaries and uncovering secrets that could change the course of history. With thrilling action and unexpected twists, this gripping adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Quest for Knowledge
