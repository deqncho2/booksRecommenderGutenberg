Synopsis: In 'Love of the Wild' by McKishnie, Archie P., a young adventurer named Jack embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Canadian Rockies. Drawn by his love for nature and a desire to escape the confines of society, Jack faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters along the way. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and tests the limits of his own resilience, Jack discovers the true meaning of freedom and the transformative power of the wild.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Connection with nature
