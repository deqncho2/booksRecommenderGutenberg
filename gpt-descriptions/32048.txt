Synopsis: 

In "Cottage Building in Cob, Pisé, Chalk and Clay: A Renaissance (2nd edition)" by Williams-Ellis and Clough, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the art of traditional cottage building. This comprehensive guide explores the techniques and materials used in constructing cottages made from cob, pisé, chalk, and clay, showcasing the beauty and sustainability of these ancient building methods. With stunning photographs and detailed instructions, the authors inspire readers to embrace the renaissance of these traditional building practices and discover the timeless charm of cottage architecture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Home Improvement

Themes: Traditional building methods, Sustainability, Cottage architecture
