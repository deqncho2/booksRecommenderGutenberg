Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the Northern Nut Growers Association as they gather for their 13th Annual Meeting in Rochester, N.Y. in 1922. Delve into the fascinating discussions and presentations on various nut-growing topics, from the cultivation of new varieties to the challenges faced by nut growers. Discover the innovative techniques and scientific advancements shared by experts in the field, as well as the camaraderie and passion that unites this unique community. This report offers a captivating glimpse into the world of nut cultivation and the dedicated individuals who strive to expand our knowledge and appreciation of these remarkable plants.

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Agriculture

Themes: Nut cultivation, Scientific advancements, Community and camaraderie
