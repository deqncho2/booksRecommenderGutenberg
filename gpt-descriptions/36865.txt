Synopsis: 
In 'Responsibilities, and other poems', W.B. Yeats takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and introspective poems. From exploring the complexities of love and relationships to delving into the depths of human nature and the passage of time, Yeats weaves together a tapestry of emotions and experiences that will leave readers pondering the mysteries of life.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Relationships, Human Nature, Time, Existentialism
