Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 159, September 25, 1915: Driven from cover; or, Nick Carter's double ruse,' Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of deception and danger as he assumes a new identity to uncover a sinister plot. With his quick thinking and cunning, Carter must navigate a treacherous world of hidden motives and unexpected twists to bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Detective

Themes: Deception, Identity, Intrigue
