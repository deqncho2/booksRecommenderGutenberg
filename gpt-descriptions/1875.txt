Synopsis: In 'Painted Windows' by Elia Wilkinson Peattie, a young woman named Mary finds herself drawn to the mysterious world of art and artists in the bustling city of Chicago. As she navigates the vibrant art scene, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected connections. With each painted window she encounters, Mary's perception of herself and the world around her begins to shift, leading her on a transformative journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Art and creativity, Identity and self-discovery, Society and its expectations
