Synopsis: In "Ben Bruce: Scenes in the Life of a Bowery Newsboy," follow the gripping journey of young Ben Bruce as he navigates the gritty streets of New York City in the late 19th century. From his humble beginnings as a newsboy, Ben encounters a series of challenges and opportunities that will shape his character and test his resilience. As he faces poverty, injustice, and the allure of a life of crime, Ben must make difficult choices that will determine his future. Will he succumb to the harsh realities of his surroundings, or will he find a way to rise above and create a better life for himself?

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, adventure

Themes: Poverty and social inequality, resilience and determination, moral choices and consequences
