Synopsis: In "Moses, not Darwin," Reverend Bennett George Johns delivers a thought-provoking sermon that challenges the prevailing scientific theories of his time. Drawing from biblical teachings, he presents a compelling argument that challenges the notion of evolution and instead emphasizes the importance of faith and divine creation. With eloquence and conviction, Johns explores the profound implications of his message, urging his congregation to reconsider their beliefs and embrace a different perspective on the origins of life.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Creationism, Evolution, Biblical Interpretation
