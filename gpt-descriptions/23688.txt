Synopsis: In 'The Indulgence of Negu Mah' by Arthur, Robert, a mysterious artifact is discovered in the depths of an ancient temple, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of reality and test the limits of human desire. As the protagonist delves deeper into the artifact's power, they are forced to confront their own inner demons and make choices that will have far-reaching consequences. With elements of suspense, mystery, and the supernatural, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Suspense

Themes: Power and temptation, Identity and self-discovery
