Synopsis: In the sixth volume of the Warren Commission Hearings, the investigation into the assassination of President John F. Kennedy intensifies as key witnesses take the stand. This gripping installment delves into the testimonies of individuals who were present at the scene of the crime, shedding light on the events leading up to that fateful day. As the commission seeks to uncover the truth, tensions rise and shocking revelations emerge, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Assassination, Conspiracy, Government Investigation
