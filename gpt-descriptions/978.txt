Synopsis: In 'The Yates Pride: A Romance', Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman weaves a captivating tale of love, family, and societal expectations. Set in a small New England town, the story follows the Yates family, whose pride and determination shape their lives in unexpected ways. As secrets are revealed and relationships tested, the characters must navigate the complexities of their desires and the constraints of their society. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the timeless themes of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Family Dynamics, Social Expectations
