Synopsis:
In 'Jack Heaton, wireless operator' by A. Frederick Collins, readers are transported to the thrilling world of early 20th-century wireless communication. Jack Heaton, a young and ambitious wireless operator, finds himself embarking on a perilous journey aboard a luxurious ocean liner. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, Jack becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With his skills as a wireless operator put to the ultimate test, Jack must unravel the secrets hidden within the ship's walls before it's too late. This gripping tale of adventure and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, the pursuit of knowledge and discovery, the dangers of secrecy and deception
