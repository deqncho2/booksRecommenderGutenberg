Synopsis: In 'Two in a Zoo', join a mischievous monkey and a curious giraffe on a thrilling adventure through the zoo. As they explore the different exhibits, they encounter a variety of animals, each with their own unique personalities and secrets. With humor and heart, this delightful children's book takes readers on a wild journey filled with surprises and valuable life lessons.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Acceptance, Discovery
