Synopsis:
In 'Midnight' by Montgomery, Rutherford G., a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that occur only after dark. As the residents struggle to uncover the truth behind these occurrences, they find themselves drawn into a web of secrets and hidden desires. With each passing night, the tension rises, and the line between reality and nightmare becomes increasingly blurred. As the clock strikes midnight, the true nature of the town's darkness is revealed, leaving the reader breathless and questioning their own perception of reality.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes:
Secrets, Perception, Darkness
