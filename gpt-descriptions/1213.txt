Synopsis: In the small, seemingly virtuous town of Hadleyburg, a stranger arrives with a mysterious proposition that challenges the integrity of its residents. As the stranger's plan unfolds, the town's moral fabric begins to unravel, revealing the true nature of its inhabitants and the consequences of their actions. Twain's masterful storytelling takes readers on a journey of deception, corruption, and the power of human nature.

Genres: Satire, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Morality, Corruption, Hypocrisy, Truth, Consequences
