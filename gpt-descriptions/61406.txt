Synopsis: In "Hannibal Crosses the Alps" by Cecil Torr, embark on a thrilling journey through treacherous terrain as the legendary Carthaginian general, Hannibal, leads his army on an audacious expedition. Torr vividly recounts the challenges faced by Hannibal and his troops as they navigate the unforgiving Alps, battling not only the harsh elements but also their own doubts and fears. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this gripping historical account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Hannibal's every move.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Military History

Themes: Perseverance, Leadership, Strategy, Exploration
