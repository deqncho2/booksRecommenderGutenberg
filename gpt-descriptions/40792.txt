Synopsis: 

'Behind the Scenes in Warring Germany' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous landscape of Germany during a time of war. Edward Lyell Fox, an intrepid journalist, provides a unique perspective as he delves into the hidden corners of this conflicted nation. From the opulent palaces of the elite to the war-torn streets of Berlin, Fox uncovers the secrets, scandals, and sacrifices that lie behind the grand facade of wartime Germany. As he navigates through the chaos and danger, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own hidden agendas and motivations. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Fox paints a vivid picture of a nation on the brink of collapse, where loyalty and betrayal intertwine in unexpected ways. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Mystery

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Survival, Power
