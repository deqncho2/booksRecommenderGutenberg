Synopsis: In 'The Mirrors of Downing Street: Some Political Reflections by a Gentleman with a Duster', Harold Begbie takes readers on a captivating journey through the corridors of power in British politics. With his unique perspective as an observer and participant, Begbie unveils the hidden truths and intriguing anecdotes behind the scenes of Downing Street. From the charismatic leaders to the cunning strategists, he delves into the personalities and motivations that shape the political landscape. As the duster-wielding gentleman shares his reflections, readers will be enthralled by the captivating tales and revelations that lie within the mirrors of power.

Genres: Political non-fiction, memoir, historical

Themes: Politics, power, leadership, behind-the-scenes, personal reflections
