Synopsis: In "Menotah: A Tale of the Riel Rebellion" by John Trevena, a young Englishman named Hugh is sent to Canada to work on a farm, unaware of the political turmoil that awaits him. As he becomes entangled in the Riel Rebellion, Hugh must navigate the complexities of loyalty, love, and the fight for justice in a land on the brink of change. With vivid descriptions of the Canadian landscape and a cast of compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a pivotal moment in Canadian history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Social justice, Love and sacrifice
