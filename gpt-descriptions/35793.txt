Synopsis: In 'Seeing Things at Night' by Heywood Broun, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious woman seeks his help to uncover the truth behind her husband's death. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that challenge his own beliefs and force him to confront his own demons. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems, and the line between truth and illusion becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Perception, Identity, Redemption
