Synopsis: In 'Murder Beneath the Polar Ice' by Howard Hayden, a team of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking expedition to the Arctic, only to find themselves trapped in a deadly game of cat and mouse. As tensions rise and secrets unravel, the team must race against time to uncover the truth before they become the next victims of a chilling conspiracy lurking beneath the frozen surface.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Exploration, Conspiracy
