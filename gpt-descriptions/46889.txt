Synopsis: 

In "Herbals, Their Origin and Evolution: A Chapter in the History of Botany 1470-1670," Agnes Robertson Arber takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of botanical knowledge during the Renaissance and early modern period. Delving into the origins and development of herbals, this book unveils the intricate relationship between science, art, and culture that shaped the botanical discoveries of the time. From the meticulous illustrations to the exploration of medicinal properties, Arber's meticulous research sheds light on the evolution of botanical knowledge and its impact on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Botany

Themes: History of Science, Renaissance, Botanical Illustration, Medicinal Plants
