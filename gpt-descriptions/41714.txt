Synopsis: In a future where organized crime has taken over the government, a young member of the Syndic, the ruling criminal organization, finds himself torn between loyalty and justice. As he delves deeper into the corrupt world he is a part of, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change everything. With danger lurking at every corner, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect what he holds dear and challenge the very foundation of the Syndic's power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Power, Loyalty, Justice
