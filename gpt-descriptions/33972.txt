Synopsis: 

'Florida: An Ideal Cattle State' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lush landscapes and thriving cattle industry of Florida. Written by the Florida State Live Stock Association, this book unveils the untold story of how Florida became a haven for cattle ranching, showcasing the challenges, triumphs, and unique characteristics that make it an ideal destination for this industry. From the pioneers who first recognized the state's potential to the modern-day ranchers who continue to shape its future, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the rich history and vibrant present of Florida's cattle industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Agriculture

Themes: Agriculture and industry, Environmental impact, Economic development
