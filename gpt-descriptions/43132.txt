Synopsis: In 'The Little Old Portrait' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Dolly discovers a mysterious portrait hidden away in her family's attic. As she becomes captivated by the enigmatic figure in the painting, Dolly embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets behind the little old portrait. With each clue she uncovers, Dolly finds herself drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and adventure, where the past intertwines with the present in unexpected ways. Will Dolly be able to unravel the mysteries surrounding the portrait and unlock the truth that lies within?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Curiosity, Identity, Family Secrets
