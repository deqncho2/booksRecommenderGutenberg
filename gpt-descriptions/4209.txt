Synopsis: In 'At the Mercy of Tiberius' by Augusta J. Evans, a young woman named Edna Earl finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes the ward of the enigmatic and powerful Tiberius. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society and uncovers the truth about her own past, Edna must make difficult choices that will determine her future and the lives of those she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and manipulation, Identity and self-discovery, Love and sacrifice
