Synopsis:
In 'Ukridge' by P. G. Wodehouse, the reader is introduced to the eccentric and perpetually optimistic Stanley Featherstonehaugh Ukridge. With his wild schemes and knack for getting into trouble, Ukridge embarks on a series of hilarious misadventures that involve everything from chicken farming to amateur boxing. Alongside his loyal friend and narrator, Corky Corcoran, Ukridge's outrageous plans and larger-than-life personality lead them both into a whirlwind of chaos and laughter.

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Comedy

Themes:
Optimism, Friendship, Misadventure
