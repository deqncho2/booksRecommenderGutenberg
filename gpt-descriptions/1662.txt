Synopsis: 

Step into the world of global intelligence with 'The 1997 CIA World Factbook' by the United States Central Intelligence Agency. This meticulously compiled reference book takes readers on a captivating journey through the geopolitical landscape of the late 20th century. From the smallest island nations to the largest superpowers, this Factbook provides a wealth of information on each country's geography, population, economy, and more. Delve into the intricate web of international relations, uncover hidden cultural gems, and gain a deeper understanding of the world we live in. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Geopolitics

Themes: Global intelligence, International relations, Cultural diversity
