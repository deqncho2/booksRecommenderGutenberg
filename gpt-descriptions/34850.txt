Synopsis: In 'Signal in the Dark' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young and adventurous reporter named Penny Parker finds herself caught up in a mysterious case when she stumbles upon a hidden signal that leads her to a dangerous secret. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Penny must navigate a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about. With each clue she uncovers, the stakes become higher, and Penny's determination to solve the puzzle grows stronger. Will she be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Truth-seeking
