Synopsis: In 'Plain Talks on Avoided Subjects', Henry N. Guernsey fearlessly delves into taboo topics that are often shrouded in silence and secrecy. With a candid and compassionate approach, he explores subjects such as sexuality, mental health, addiction, and death, offering insightful perspectives and practical advice. Through his engaging storytelling and thought-provoking anecdotes, Guernsey challenges societal norms and encourages readers to confront these avoided subjects head-on, fostering understanding and promoting personal growth.

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Non-fiction

Themes: Taboo subjects, Communication, Personal growth
