Synopsis: In the isolated town of Chicken Farm, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious newcomer arrives. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas, they must confront their darkest fears and make choices that will determine the fate of their community. With suspense and intrigue at every turn, 'Chicken Farm' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Community, Identity
