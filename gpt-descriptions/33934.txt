Synopsis: In 'She Knew He Was Coming' by Neville, Kris, a young woman named Emma starts experiencing vivid dreams that seem to predict the arrival of a mysterious stranger in her life. As the dreams become more intense and frequent, Emma becomes convinced that this person is real and will soon enter her world. With a mix of anticipation and trepidation, she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind these premonitions and the identity of the enigmatic figure who seems to be approaching. As the lines between dreams and reality blur, Emma finds herself questioning her own sanity and the nature of fate itself.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Premonition, Destiny, Identity, Perception
