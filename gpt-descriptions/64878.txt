Synopsis: In 'The Truth about Church Extension', Anonymous delves into the intriguing world of religious worship and education, uncovering a web of fallacies and misstatements lurking within the census reports. With meticulous research and a critical eye, the author exposes the hidden truths behind the numbers, challenging conventional wisdom and shedding light on the complex dynamics of religious institutions. As the layers of deception are peeled away, readers are left questioning the reliability of official statistics and pondering the true state of church extension. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Sociology, Religious Studies

Themes: Deception, Statistical Analysis, Religious Institutions
