Synopsis: 

In "Ancient Scottish Lake-Dwellings or Crannogs: With a supplementary chapter on remains of lake-dwellings in England," Robert Munro takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of ancient Scottish lake-dwellings. Delving into the depths of history, Munro uncovers the secrets and wonders of these fascinating structures that were once home to our ancestors. From the construction techniques to the daily lives of the inhabitants, Munro's meticulous research and vivid descriptions bring these ancient dwellings to life. As he explores the remains of lake-dwellings in England as well, Munro paints a comprehensive picture of this unique aspect of human history. 

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Architecture, Daily life, Cultural heritage
