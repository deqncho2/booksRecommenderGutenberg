Synopsis: 
In 'The Stranger's Handbook to Chester and Its Environs', Thomas Hughes takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical city of Chester. With a blend of history, antiquities, and vivid descriptions, Hughes guides the reader on a walk around the city walls, a visit to the magnificent cathedral, castle, and the grand Eaton Hall. As the reader explores the rich heritage and architectural wonders of Chester, they will uncover hidden stories and secrets that make this city truly unique.

Genres: Travel Guide, History, Architecture

Themes: History and Heritage, Architectural Marvels, Exploration
