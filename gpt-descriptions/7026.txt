Synopsis: 

In the Children's Edition of Touching Incidents and Remarkable Answers to Prayer, author Solomon Benjamin Shaw presents a collection of captivating stories that will leave young readers in awe. From heartwarming tales of unexpected miracles to extraordinary encounters with divine intervention, this book takes children on a journey filled with wonder and inspiration. Each story is carefully crafted to engage young minds and ignite their imagination, leaving them eager to discover the power of prayer and the incredible ways in which it can shape their lives.

Genres: Children's Literature, Inspirational, Non-fiction

Themes: Faith, Miracles, Prayer, Divine Intervention
