Synopsis: In the sequel to "Madame Midas," Fergus Hume takes readers on a thrilling journey with the enigmatic character, Miss Mephistopheles. Set in the vibrant city of Melbourne, this novel follows Miss Mephistopheles as she navigates a world of secrets, deception, and hidden desires. As her past catches up with her, she must confront her own demons and make choices that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the dark side of human nature and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
