Synopsis: In 'Spun-yarn and Spindrift' by Norah M. Holland, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the past, Mary uncovers a series of long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel her present and change her future forever. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As Mary races against time to uncover the truth, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect those she holds dear. Will she be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Deception, Identity
