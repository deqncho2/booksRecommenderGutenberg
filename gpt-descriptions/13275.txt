Synopsis: 
In 'The Gibson Upright', we follow the life of Anthony Gibson, a young man who is determined to make a name for himself in the bustling city of New York. As he navigates the challenges of love, career, and societal expectations, Anthony finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected encounters that will test his resilience and force him to confront his deepest desires.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Love, Identity, Social expectations
