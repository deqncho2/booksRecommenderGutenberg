Synopsis: In 'The Forest Beyond the Woodlands: A Fairy Tale' by Kennedy, Mildred, a young girl named Lily embarks on a magical journey through an enchanted forest. As she navigates through mysterious creatures and hidden secrets, Lily discovers her own inner strength and the power of friendship. With each step she takes, the forest reveals its secrets, leading Lily to a destiny she never could have imagined.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Magic
