Synopsis: In "Collecting as a Pastime" by Charles Rowed, embark on a captivating journey into the world of collecting, where the author shares his personal experiences and insights. From rare stamps to antique coins, vintage toys to exquisite art, Rowed delves into the passion, thrill, and obsession that drives collectors. Discover the hidden stories behind these cherished objects and the fascinating individuals who dedicate their lives to amassing these treasures. With vivid anecdotes and a contagious enthusiasm, Rowed invites readers to explore the captivating world of collecting and the profound joy it brings.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Hobbies

Themes: Passion, Obsession, Personal fulfillment
