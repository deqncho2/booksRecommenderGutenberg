Synopsis: In "Under the Ensign of the Rising Sun: A Story of the Russo-Japanese War," Harry Collingwood takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intense conflict between Russia and Japan. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this gripping tale follows the adventures of a young British naval officer who finds himself caught in the midst of the war. As he navigates the treacherous waters of battle, he must confront not only the physical dangers but also the moral dilemmas that arise in times of war. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Collingwood paints a vivid picture of the Russo-Japanese War and the individuals who fought in it.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Conflict and Confrontation, Loyalty and Duty, Morality in War
