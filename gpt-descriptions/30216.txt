Synopsis: 
"The Buddhist Catechism" by Henry Steel Olcott is a captivating exploration of the fundamental teachings and principles of Buddhism. Through a series of thought-provoking questions and enlightening answers, Olcott takes readers on a journey to understand the essence of Buddhism, its core beliefs, and the path to enlightenment. From the nature of existence to the concept of karma, from the role of meditation to the importance of compassion, this book offers a comprehensive and accessible introduction to the profound wisdom of Buddhism.

Genres: Philosophy, Religion, Non-fiction

Themes: Buddhism, Enlightenment, Karma, Meditation, Compassion
