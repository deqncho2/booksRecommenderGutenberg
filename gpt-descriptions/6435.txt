Synopsis: In 'The Principles of Scientific Management', Frederick Winslow Taylor revolutionizes the way we think about work and productivity. Through his meticulous observations and experiments, Taylor unveils a groundbreaking system that promises to increase efficiency and eliminate waste in any industry. As he challenges traditional methods and introduces new techniques, readers will be captivated by the potential of his ideas and the impact they could have on the world of work.

Genres: Business, Management, Non-fiction

Themes: Efficiency, Productivity, Industrialization
