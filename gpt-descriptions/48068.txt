Synopsis: In 'Cursed' by George Allan England, a small town is plagued by a mysterious curse that brings chaos and despair to its inhabitants. As the curse intensifies, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the dark secrets of their town's past and find a way to break the curse before it consumes them all. With each page, the tension builds, and the reader is drawn deeper into a world of supernatural forces and gripping suspense. Will they succeed in their quest for salvation, or will they succumb to the curse's malevolent power?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and destiny, Power of belief, Redemption
