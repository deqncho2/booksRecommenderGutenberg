Synopsis: 
Embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of Paris with Grant Allen's Historical Guides. This meticulously crafted guidebook takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the city's rich history, unveiling hidden stories and secrets that lie beneath its iconic landmarks. From the majestic Notre-Dame Cathedral to the opulent Palace of Versailles, Allen's vivid descriptions transport readers back in time, immersing them in the grandeur and allure of Paris throughout the ages. With a perfect blend of historical facts, intriguing anecdotes, and captivating storytelling, this guidebook is an essential companion for anyone seeking to unravel the mysteries of the City of Light.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Parisian history, Architecture, Cultural heritage
