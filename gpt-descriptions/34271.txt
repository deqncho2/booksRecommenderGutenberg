Synopsis: 
'Is Polite Society Polite? and Other Essays' by Julia Ward Howe is a collection of thought-provoking essays that delve into the complexities of societal norms and expectations. Through her sharp observations and insightful analysis, Howe challenges the notion of politeness and explores the consequences of conformity. From dissecting the role of women in society to questioning the true meaning of politeness, these essays offer a fresh perspective on the intricacies of human interaction and the impact it has on individuals and communities.

Genres: Essay, Social Commentary, Feminist Literature

Themes: Societal norms, Conformity, Gender roles, Politeness, Individuality
