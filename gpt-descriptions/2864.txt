Synopsis: In the midst of the Napoleonic Wars, a young woman finds herself torn between two brothers, both vying for her affection. As the trumpet-major of a local militia, one brother embodies the excitement and danger of war, while the other offers stability and familiarity. Set against the backdrop of a small English village, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the choices we make in times of uncertainty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Conflict and Decision-Making
