Synopsis: 
'Pen Pal' by Stephen Marlowe is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of a troubled writer, Jack, who becomes entangled in a mysterious correspondence with a stranger. As their letters become increasingly intense and personal, Jack finds himself questioning his own sanity and the true identity of his pen pal. With each letter, the lines between reality and fiction blur, leading Jack down a dark and dangerous path that will challenge everything he thought he knew.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception
