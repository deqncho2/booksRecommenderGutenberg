Synopsis: 
In 'Gilbert Weather Bureau (Meteorology) for Boys', Alfred Carlton Gilbert takes young readers on an exciting journey into the world of meteorology. Follow the adventures of a group of boys as they learn about weather patterns, storm tracking, and the science behind forecasting. With hands-on experiments, thrilling anecdotes, and captivating illustrations, this book will ignite a passion for meteorology and inspire young minds to explore the mysteries of the atmosphere.

Genres: Educational, Adventure, Science

Themes: Curiosity, Exploration, Science Education
