Synopsis: Trilbyana: The Rise and Progress of a Popular Novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the creation and impact of a beloved literary masterpiece. Written by Jeannette L. Gilder and Joseph Benson Gilder, this enthralling work delves into the fascinating story behind the creation of the novel Trilby, exploring its rise to popularity and the profound influence it had on society. From the mysterious origins of the novel's characters to the scandalous controversies it sparked, Trilbyana uncovers the secrets and intrigues that surrounded this iconic piece of literature. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the world of Trilby, captivating readers with its enduring legacy and the power of storytelling itself.

Genres: Literary criticism, Biography, History

Themes: The power of literature, Cultural impact, Artistic inspiration
