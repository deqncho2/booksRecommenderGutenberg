Synopsis: In "The Christmas City: Bethlehem Across the Ages," Lewis Gaston Leary takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enduring significance of Bethlehem, the birthplace of Jesus Christ. From its humble beginnings as a small village to its transformation into a renowned pilgrimage site, Leary delves into the fascinating stories, legends, and traditions that have shaped Bethlehem over the centuries. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of the city's vibrant past, exploring its religious, cultural, and political significance. From the biblical accounts of the Nativity to the modern-day challenges faced by the city, "The Christmas City" offers a comprehensive and enlightening exploration of Bethlehem's enduring legacy.

Genres: History, Religion, Cultural Studies

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Cultural heritage, Historical significance, Identity and tradition
