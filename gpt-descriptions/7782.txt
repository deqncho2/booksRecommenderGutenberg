Synopsis: In "The Last Tournament" by Alfred Tennyson, Baron Tennyson, a grand tournament is held in Camelot, where knights from all over gather to compete for glory and honor. However, beneath the surface of chivalry and valor, a web of secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love begins to unravel, threatening to shatter the kingdom's unity. As the tournament progresses, the true nature of the knights and their motivations are revealed, leading to a climactic showdown that will test their loyalties and the very essence of knighthood.

Genres: Poetry, Arthurian literature, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Betrayal, Love, Loyalty
