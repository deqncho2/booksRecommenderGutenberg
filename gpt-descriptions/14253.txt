Synopsis: 
In 'Flames' by Robert Hichens, a captivating tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant city of Cairo, the story follows the protagonist's journey of self-discovery and the unexpected twists and turns that fate has in store for her. As secrets are revealed and passions ignite, the reader is taken on a thrilling and emotional rollercoaster ride, where love and sacrifice collide in the most unexpected ways.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Duty and Sacrifice, Self-Discovery
