Synopsis: 
'Poker Jim, Gentleman, and Other Tales and Sketches' is a captivating collection of short stories and sketches by G. Frank Lydston. From the mysterious world of underground poker games to the adventures of a charming gentleman, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through various intriguing narratives. Each tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and well-crafted characters, Lydston's stories explore the depths of human nature and the complexities of life, leaving readers eager to uncover what lies beyond each page.

Genres: 
Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: 
Human nature, Intrigue, Unexpected twists
