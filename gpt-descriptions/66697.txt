Synopsis: In 'The Fighter' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young man named Bruce is determined to prove himself in the world of boxing. Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Bruce's unwavering determination and resilience push him to overcome his limitations and become a formidable fighter. As he navigates the treacherous world of professional boxing, Bruce must confront his own fears and insecurities, while also discovering the true meaning of courage and perseverance.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
