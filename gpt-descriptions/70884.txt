Synopsis: In 'French without a master: A farce in one act', Bernard, Tristan takes readers on a hilarious journey through the absurdities of language learning. The story revolves around a group of eccentric characters who find themselves in a French language class, each with their own unique quirks and motivations. As the class progresses, chaos ensues, leading to uproarious situations and misunderstandings that will leave readers in stitches. With witty dialogue and clever wordplay, this farce explores the comical side of language barriers and the lengths people will go to master a foreign tongue.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Language learning, Miscommunication, Cultural differences
