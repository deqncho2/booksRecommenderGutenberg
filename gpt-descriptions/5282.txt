Synopsis: In 'The Tales and Novels, v9: Belphegor and Others', La Fontaine weaves together a collection of captivating tales that explore the complexities of human nature and the consequences of our actions. From a mischievous demon wreaking havoc in a small village to a cunning fox outsmarting a group of hunters, each story is filled with wit, humor, and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate through love, greed, and moral dilemmas, readers are taken on a journey that challenges their perceptions and leaves them pondering the intricacies of life.

Genres: Fiction, Fables, Satire

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Consequences of actions
