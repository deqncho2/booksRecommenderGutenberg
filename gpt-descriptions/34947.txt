Synopsis: In 'The House of Strange Secrets: A Detective Story' by Bayly, A. Eric, a renowned detective is called to investigate a mysterious mansion where strange occurrences have been taking place. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the house, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and dark pasts that threaten to consume him. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Deception, Redemption
