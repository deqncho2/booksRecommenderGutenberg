Synopsis: In the midst of the English Civil War, the Beverley children find themselves orphaned and forced to flee their ancestral home. Seeking refuge in the New Forest, they must adapt to a life of survival and secrecy. As they navigate the challenges of their new surroundings, they encounter unexpected friendships, dangerous encounters, and the constant threat of discovery. With their resilience and resourcefulness, the children embark on a journey of self-discovery and resilience, as they strive to protect their family and find a place to call home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Family, Survival, Loyalty
