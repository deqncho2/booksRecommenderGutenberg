Synopsis: In "The Pilot's Daughter: an account of Elizabeth Cullingham," Francis Cunningham takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Elizabeth Cullingham, a young woman who defies societal expectations and embarks on a thrilling adventure. From her childhood in a small coastal town to her daring exploits as a pilot, Elizabeth's story is one of courage, determination, and the pursuit of dreams. As she navigates the challenges of love, loss, and self-discovery, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns that shape her extraordinary life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Independence, Pursuit of Dreams
