Synopsis: In 'The House of Martha' by Frank R. Stockton, a young man named Richard is invited to stay at the mysterious House of Martha, a place known for its peculiar rules and customs. As Richard navigates the peculiarities of the house, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the House of Martha reveals its true nature, leaving Richard questioning his own sanity and the motives of those around him. Will he uncover the truth behind the enigmatic House of Martha, or will he become lost in its labyrinthine corridors forever?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Perception vs. reality
