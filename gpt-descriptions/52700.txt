Synopsis: In 'Happy Island: A New "Uncle William" Story' by Lee, Jennette, embark on a captivating journey to a mysterious island where adventure, friendship, and unexpected discoveries await. Follow the charming protagonist, Uncle William, as he sets foot on this enchanting paradise and encounters a cast of intriguing characters. As secrets unravel and challenges arise, Uncle William must navigate through the island's hidden depths, uncovering the true meaning of happiness along the way. With its delightful blend of mystery, heartwarming moments, and a touch of magic, this story will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the island's secrets.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Happiness
