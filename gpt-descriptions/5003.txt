Synopsis: The Rover Boys in New York; Or, Saving Their Father's Honor follows the thrilling adventures of three brothers, Tom, Sam, and Dick Rover, as they embark on a mission to clear their father's name. When their father is accused of a crime he did not commit, the Rover boys find themselves in the heart of New York City, facing danger, deception, and unexpected allies. As they navigate the bustling streets and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, the brothers must rely on their wit, courage, and brotherly bond to uncover the truth and restore their father's honor.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Family bonds
