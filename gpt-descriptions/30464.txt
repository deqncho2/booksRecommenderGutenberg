Synopsis: In 'A Manifest Destiny' by Julia Magruder, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the American West. Drawn by a mysterious letter and a desire for adventure, Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected romance. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of the frontier, she must confront her own fears and discover the true meaning of courage and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
