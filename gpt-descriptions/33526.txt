Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, June 28 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. Through a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and poems, this volume offers a delightful glimpse into the social, political, and cultural landscape of the time. From clever commentaries on current events to playful caricatures of notable figures, this edition of Punch promises to entertain and enlighten readers with its sharp observations and clever wordplay.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Social commentary, Victorian society, Political satire
