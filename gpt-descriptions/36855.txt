Synopsis: In the enchanting city of Prague, a young Jewish boy named Gabriel embarks on a journey that will test his faith, courage, and resilience. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous time in history, Gabriel finds himself caught between his love for his family and his desire for freedom. As he navigates the intricate maze of secrets and betrayals, Gabriel discovers the power of hope and the strength of the human spirit. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and find his place in a world filled with uncertainty?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Identity, Perseverance, Family, Faith, Love, Freedom
