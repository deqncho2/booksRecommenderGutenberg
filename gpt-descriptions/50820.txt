Synopsis: 
In 'Mrs. Dot: A Farce' by W. Somerset Maugham, the eccentric Mrs. Dot finds herself in a series of hilarious and absurd situations as she tries to navigate the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations. From mistaken identities to comical misunderstandings, this farcical tale takes readers on a delightful journey filled with wit, charm, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Love and Marriage, Social Expectations, Identity
