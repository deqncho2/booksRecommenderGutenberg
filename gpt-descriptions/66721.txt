Synopsis: In 'At Odds with the Regent: A Story of the Cellamare Conspiracy', the reader is transported to 18th-century France, where political intrigue and betrayal lurk in the shadows. As the Regent of France faces a dangerous plot to overthrow the monarchy, a young nobleman finds himself torn between loyalty and his own desires. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous web of secrets and alliances, where one wrong move could cost him everything. This gripping tale of power, deception, and forbidden love will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power Struggles, Forbidden Love
