Synopsis: In 'The K-Factor' by Harry Harrison, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where powerful forces will stop at nothing to possess his invention. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect his creation from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Power struggles, Ethical dilemmas
