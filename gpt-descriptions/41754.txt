Synopsis: 
In 'Jimmy Quixote: A Novel' by Tom Gallon, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the mind of Jimmy, a young man who finds solace in the pages of classic literature. As he navigates the challenges of his own life, Jimmy becomes increasingly convinced that he is a modern-day Don Quixote, embarking on a quest to bring justice and chivalry to the world around him. With a touch of whimsy and a dash of humor, this novel explores the blurred lines between reality and imagination, and the power of literature to shape our perceptions of the world.

Genres: Fiction, Literary Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Imagination, Identity, Literature, Perception
