Synopsis: 
'Fighting Joe; Or, The Fortunes of a Staff Officer. A Story of the Great Rebellion' follows the journey of Joe Percy, a young and ambitious staff officer during the American Civil War. As Joe rises through the ranks, he faces numerous challenges and dangers on the battlefield, while also navigating complex relationships and personal sacrifices. From the blood-soaked battlefields to the corridors of power, Joe's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will he emerge as a hero or succumb to the harsh realities of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Ambition, Loyalty, Love, Power
