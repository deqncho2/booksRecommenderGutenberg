Synopsis: In Volume 10 of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova finds himself imprisoned in the notorious Leads prison in Venice. As he navigates the treacherous world of the prison, Casanova encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including a mysterious woman who captivates his heart. With his wit, charm, and resourcefulness, Casanova must devise a daring plan to escape the clutches of the Leads and regain his freedom. Will he succeed, or will he be forever trapped within its walls?

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Freedom and Imprisonment, Survival and Resourcefulness
