Synopsis: In "Margaret Tudor: A Romance of Old St. Augustine," Annie T. Colcock takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting city of St. Augustine, where the past and present intertwine. Set in the 16th century, the story follows Margaret Tudor, a young Englishwoman who finds herself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. As she navigates the treacherous waters of colonial Florida, Margaret's fate becomes entangled with the fate of the city itself. With vivid descriptions and a cast of compelling characters, this historical romance transports readers to a time of adventure, passion, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue, Identity and Self-Discovery
