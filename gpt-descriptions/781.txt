Synopsis: 
'Sinking of the Titanic and Great Sea Disasters' takes readers on a gripping journey through the tragic events surrounding the sinking of the Titanic, as well as other harrowing sea disasters. From the awe-inspiring luxury of the Titanic's maiden voyage to the heart-wrenching stories of survival and loss, this book delves into the untold details and personal accounts that will leave readers captivated. With vivid descriptions and a meticulous attention to historical accuracy, this compelling narrative sheds light on the human drama that unfolded amidst the icy waters of the North Atlantic. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Disaster, Adventure

Themes: Tragedy, Survival, Human resilience
