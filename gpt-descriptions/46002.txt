Synopsis: 
In 'Motor tours in Yorkshire', Mrs. Stawell takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Yorkshire. With her vivid descriptions and charming anecdotes, she invites us to join her on a series of thrilling motor tours, exploring the hidden gems and historical landmarks of this enchanting region. From the rugged beauty of the Yorkshire Dales to the quaint villages nestled in the Yorkshire Moors, this travelogue is a delightful blend of adventure, discovery, and the joy of the open road.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Nature, History, Adventure
