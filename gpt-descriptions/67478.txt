Synopsis: In "A United States Midshipman in China" by Stirling Yates, follow the thrilling journey of a young midshipman as he embarks on an unexpected adventure in the exotic land of China. From navigating treacherous waters to encountering mysterious cultures, the protagonist's courage and resilience are put to the test. As he delves deeper into the heart of China, he uncovers secrets that could change the course of history. Will he be able to fulfill his duty and protect his country's interests, or will he succumb to the allure of this captivating land?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Duty and Loyalty
