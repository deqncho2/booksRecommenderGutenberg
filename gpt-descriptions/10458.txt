Synopsis: In "Three Short Works: The Dance of Death, The Legend of Saint Julian the Hospitaller, A Simple Soul," Gustave Flaubert presents a collection of captivating tales that explore the depths of human nature and the complexities of life. 

In "The Dance of Death," a young artist finds himself entangled in a mysterious and haunting dance that challenges his perception of reality. "The Legend of Saint Julian the Hospitaller" follows the life of a nobleman whose actions lead him on a path of redemption and self-discovery. Lastly, "A Simple Soul" delves into the life of a devoted servant, Félicité, whose unwavering love and innocence provide solace in a world filled with hardship.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Death, Redemption, Morality, Love, Innocence
