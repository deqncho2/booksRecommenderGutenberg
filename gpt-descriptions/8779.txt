Synopsis: 
In 'The Divine Comedy by Dante, Illustrated, Hell, Volume 01', Dante Alighieri takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the depths of Hell. As Dante finds himself lost in a dark forest, he encounters the spirit of the ancient Roman poet Virgil, who becomes his guide through the nine circles of Hell. Along the way, Dante encounters a multitude of sinners, each suffering their own unique torment that reflects the gravity of their transgressions. Through vivid descriptions and haunting imagery, Dante explores the consequences of human actions and the nature of sin, ultimately leading readers to contemplate the eternal consequences of their own choices.

Genres: Epic poetry, allegory, religious literature

Themes: Sin and punishment, redemption, divine justice, the power of love and faith
