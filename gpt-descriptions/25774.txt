Synopsis: In 'The Rapids' by Alan Sullivan, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious disappearances that seem to be connected to the local river. As the community becomes consumed by fear and suspicion, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind the dark secrets lurking beneath the surface of their seemingly idyllic town. With each twist and turn, the tension rises, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Fear and suspicion, Unlikely alliances
