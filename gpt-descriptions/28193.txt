Synopsis: In 'Mr. Rabbit's Wedding: Hollow Tree Stories', follow the charming adventures of Mr. Rabbit as he prepares for his upcoming wedding. With the help of his friends, including Grandfather Frog and Mr. Crow, Mr. Rabbit navigates through various challenges and unexpected surprises that arise during the wedding preparations. This heartwarming tale is filled with delightful characters, humorous moments, and a touch of romance that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Love, Overcoming obstacles
