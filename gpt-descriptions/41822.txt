Synopsis: 
In the small village of Bryngelly, a mysterious and enigmatic figure named Phroso arrives, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. However, beneath his charismatic facade lies a tragic past that he is desperate to keep hidden. As the villagers become more intrigued by Phroso, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. With each passing day, the true nature of Phroso's identity becomes more elusive, leaving the villagers questioning their own perceptions of truth and reality. 

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love, Betrayal
