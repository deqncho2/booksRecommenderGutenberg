Synopsis: 
In 'A Woman-Hater' by Charles Reade, the reader is introduced to a peculiar and enigmatic character named Henry Little. Known for his disdain towards women, Henry's life takes an unexpected turn when he finds himself entangled in a series of events involving a mysterious woman. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and Henry's beliefs and perceptions are put to the test. With a blend of romance, mystery, and intrigue, 'A Woman-Hater' takes readers on a captivating journey that explores the complexities of human relationships and challenges societal norms.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Gender roles, Love and relationships, Identity
