Synopsis: In the final volume of "The Maid of Honour: A Tale of the Dark Days of France," the gripping story reaches its climax as the fate of the protagonist, Marie, hangs in the balance. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous French Revolution, Marie finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and her love for a man on the opposing side. As the revolutionaries tighten their grip on power, Marie's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous political landscape and find a way to survive?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Revolution, Survival
