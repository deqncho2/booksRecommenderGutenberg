Synopsis: 

'Into the Unknown: A Romance of South Africa' by Fletcher, Lawrence is a captivating tale set in the heart of the African continent. The story follows the journey of a young adventurer who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he explores the uncharted territories of South Africa. With each step into the unknown, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, from indigenous tribes to ruthless treasure hunters, all vying for power and wealth. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this untamed land, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny forever. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Power and Greed
