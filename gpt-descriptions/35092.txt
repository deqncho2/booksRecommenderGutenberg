Synopsis: In this captivating volume of the Encyclopaedia Britannica, 11th Edition, we delve into a wide range of fascinating topics, from the biblical figure Ehud to the intricate workings of the electroscope. Discover the heroic tales of Ehud and his cunning strategies, as well as the scientific marvels of the electroscope and its ability to detect electric charges. With detailed explanations and engaging narratives, this volume offers a captivating journey through history, mythology, and scientific discovery.

Genres: Reference, History, Science

Themes: Heroism, Biblical figures, Scientific exploration, Ancient civilizations
