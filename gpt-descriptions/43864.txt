Synopsis: In 'Running Fox' by Gregor, Elmer Russell, a young Native American boy named Running Fox embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. Faced with numerous challenges and dangers, Running Fox must rely on his courage, resourcefulness, and deep connection with nature to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. As he navigates through breathtaking landscapes and encounters fascinating characters, Running Fox's quest becomes not only a physical adventure but also a profound exploration of identity, resilience, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Connection with Nature
