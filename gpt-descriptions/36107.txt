Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Trevethlan: A Cornish Story', the captivating tale continues as the lives of the residents of Trevethlan Manor become entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. As the plot thickens, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate relationships and hidden agendas that shape the destiny of this Cornish community. With each turn of the page, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader eagerly anticipating the final volume to uncover the ultimate resolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Family Secrets, Social Class, Redemption
