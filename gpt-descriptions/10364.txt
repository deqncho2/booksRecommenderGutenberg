Synopsis: In 'Yeast: a Problem' by Charles Kingsley, a young woman named Lancelot Smith embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the tranquil countryside of Devonshire. As she navigates through the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, Lancelot finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires. With the discovery of a mysterious yeast that holds the power to change lives, Lancelot's world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront her own inner demons and make choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, personal growth
