Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Mr. Edward Arnold's New and Popular Books, December, 1901, where mystery, adventure, and romance intertwine to captivate readers. From the depths of the Amazon rainforest to the bustling streets of London, this collection of stories will transport you to thrilling and unexpected places. Uncover the secrets of a hidden society, witness a daring escape from a notorious prison, and follow the journey of a forbidden love that defies all odds. With each turn of the page, you'll be drawn deeper into the gripping narratives that await you in this remarkable compilation.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden societies, Escapades and daring pursuits, Forbidden love
