Synopsis: 

'Friends of France: The Field Service of the American Ambulance Described by its Members' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of American volunteers serving in the field during World War I. Through vivid firsthand accounts, this book provides a unique and intimate perspective on the bravery, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by these dedicated individuals. From the chaos of the front lines to the heartwarming moments of compassion and resilience, the narrative captures the indomitable spirit of these unsung heroes.

Genres: Non-fiction, War, History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Humanitarianism
