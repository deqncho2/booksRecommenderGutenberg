Synopsis: 
In "Bicycling for Ladies: The Common Sense of Bicycling; with Hints as to the Art of Wheeling—Advice to Beginners—Dress—Care of the Bicycle—Mechanics—Training—Exercise, etc., etc.", Maria E. Ward takes readers on an exciting journey into the world of bicycling. With a focus on women, this comprehensive guide offers practical advice, tips, and insights on everything from proper attire and bicycle maintenance to training and exercise. Ward's engaging writing style and wealth of knowledge make this book a must-read for any lady looking to embrace the liberating and empowering experience of cycling.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Self-help

Themes: Women's empowerment, Physical fitness, Self-improvement
