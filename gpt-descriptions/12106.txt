Synopsis: 
'Old Gorgon Graham: More Letters from a Self-Made Merchant to His Son' is a collection of insightful and humorous letters written by a successful businessman to his son. In this sequel to the original 'Letters from a Self-Made Merchant to His Son,' Old Gorgon Graham imparts valuable life lessons, business advice, and witty anecdotes to guide his son through the challenges of life and the pursuit of success. Through his unique perspective and practical wisdom, Graham offers a captivating glimpse into the world of entrepreneurship and the importance of character, perseverance, and integrity.

Genres: Business, Self-help, Epistolary

Themes: Success, Business, Family, Personal Development
