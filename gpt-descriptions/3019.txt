Synopsis:
In 'Pointed Roofs: Pilgrimage, Volume 1', we follow the captivating journey of Miriam Henderson, a young woman who leaves her small town to become a governess in Germany. Through her vivid and introspective letters, we witness Miriam's struggles with cultural differences, her search for independence, and her quest for self-discovery in a foreign land. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Miriam's letters offer a poignant and intimate glimpse into her evolving thoughts, desires, and aspirations. 

Genres:
Autobiographical fiction, Epistolary novel, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Identity, Feminism, Cultural exploration
