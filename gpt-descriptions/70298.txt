Synopsis: In "The Deformities of the Fingers and Toes" by William Anderson, a renowned surgeon finds himself entangled in a mysterious case involving a series of inexplicable deformities affecting his patients. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark secret that challenges his medical expertise and forces him to confront his own fears. With each revelation, the boundaries between science and the supernatural blur, leading to a gripping and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Medical ethics, Human vulnerability, Supernatural phenomena
