Synopsis: In 'Out of Mind' by William W. Stuart, a renowned neuroscientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between reality and imagination. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the human mind, he discovers a hidden world filled with unexpected dangers and unimaginable possibilities. With his sanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous journey to uncover the truth before it consumes him completely.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Exploration of the Human Mind, Ethical Dilemmas
