Synopsis: In 'Quaint Korea' by Louise Jordan Miln, a young American woman finds herself immersed in the enchanting world of Korea during the early 20th century. As she navigates the unfamiliar customs and traditions, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. With vivid descriptions of the Korean landscape and its people, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey of discovery, self-reflection, and unexpected connections.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural identity, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
