Synopsis: In "Gulf and Glacier; or, The Percivals in Alaska" by Willis Boyd Allen, the Percival family embarks on a thrilling adventure to the untamed wilderness of Alaska. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter indigenous cultures, and face the perils of nature, they discover secrets that will change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the Percivals must rely on their resilience, resourcefulness, and the bonds of family to survive in this captivating tale of exploration and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter, Family Bonds
