Synopsis: 
In 'A Journey to Ohio in 1810, as Recorded in the Journal of Margaret Van Horn Dwight,' readers are transported back in time to join Margaret Van Horn Dwight on her remarkable journey to the untamed wilderness of Ohio. Through her vivid and captivating journal entries, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected encounters she experiences along the way. As Margaret navigates the rugged terrain, encounters Native American tribes, and forms unlikely friendships, her journey becomes a testament to the resilience and spirit of the pioneers who ventured westward during this transformative period in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
