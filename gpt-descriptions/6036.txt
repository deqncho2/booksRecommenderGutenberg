Synopsis: 
"The Kasîdah of Hâjî Abdû El-Yezdî" is a thought-provoking and philosophical work that takes readers on a poetic journey through the realms of faith, reason, and the meaning of life. Written in the form of a long poem, it follows the musings of Hâjî Abdû El-Yezdî, a wise and enigmatic traveler, as he contemplates the nature of existence, the role of religion, and the pursuit of knowledge. Through his eloquent verses, the reader is invited to question their own beliefs and ponder the mysteries of the universe.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Reason, Existentialism, Knowledge, Religion
