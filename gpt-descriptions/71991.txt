Synopsis: 
In "A Boy's Text Book on Gas Engines," author Fay Leone Faurote takes young readers on an exciting journey into the world of automobile gas engines. Through simple language and clear explanations, this book provides boys with a comprehensive understanding of how these engines work. From the basic principles to the intricate mechanisms, readers will discover the inner workings of these powerful machines. With engaging illustrations and practical examples, Faurote's book is sure to captivate young minds and ignite their curiosity about the fascinating world of gas engines.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Science and Technology, Curiosity and Learning
