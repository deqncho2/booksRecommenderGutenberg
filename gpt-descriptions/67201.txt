Synopsis: In 'More Beetles' by Jean-Henri Fabre, embark on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of beetles. Join the renowned naturalist as he uncovers the hidden secrets and peculiar behaviors of these extraordinary creatures. From the intricate mating rituals of the stag beetle to the ingenious survival strategies of the dung beetle, Fabre's vivid descriptions and keen observations will leave you in awe of the remarkable diversity and ingenuity of beetles. Prepare to be enthralled by this entomological adventure that will forever change the way you perceive these tiny yet remarkable insects.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Animal Behavior, Natural Curiosity
