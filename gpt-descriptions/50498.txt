Synopsis: In 'The Raft' by Dawson, Coningsby, a group of friends embark on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious raft floating in the middle of a secluded lake. As they explore its secrets, they soon realize that the raft holds more than just a few hidden treasures. With each discovery, their lives become intertwined with a dark and dangerous past, leading them to question their own friendships and the true nature of the raft itself. As they race against time to uncover the truth, they must confront their deepest fears and make choices that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Consequences
