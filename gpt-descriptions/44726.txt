Synopsis: In 'An Old New Zealander; or, Te Rauparaha, the Napoleon of the South,' Thomas Lindsay Buick takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Te Rauparaha, a legendary figure in New Zealand's history. From his early struggles to his rise as a powerful leader, this book delves into the fascinating world of Maori culture, warfare, and the indomitable spirit of a man who became known as the Napoleon of the South.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Cultural identity, Conflict and war
