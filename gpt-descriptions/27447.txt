Synopsis: In the remote Appalachian Mountains, a young man named Lance is caught in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and a haunting past that threatens to consume him. As he navigates the treacherous terrain of his mountain home, Lance must confront the dark forces that have shaped his family for generations. With vivid prose and a gripping narrative, 'Mountain Blood' takes readers on a journey through the rugged beauty of the mountains, where the line between love and betrayal becomes blurred, and the past refuses to stay buried.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Southern Gothic, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Identity, Nature vs. civilization, Fate and destiny
