Synopsis: 
'Stories by English Authors: The Orient' is a captivating collection of tales that transport readers to the enchanting and mysterious world of the East. From the bustling streets of India to the serene landscapes of Japan, this anthology presents a diverse range of narratives that delve into the complexities of human nature and the clash of cultures. Each story offers a unique perspective on the Orient, exploring themes of love, betrayal, honor, and the pursuit of truth. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this anthology promises to immerse readers in a captivating journey through the exotic and alluring Orient.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Cultural clash, Love and relationships, Honor and duty, Quest for truth
