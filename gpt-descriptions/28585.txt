Synopsis: In 'With Hoops of Steel' by Florence Finch Kelly, a young girl named Florence dreams of becoming a professional basketball player, but faces numerous challenges and obstacles along the way. As she navigates through the competitive world of sports, Florence must confront her own self-doubt, societal expectations, and the pressures of success. With determination and resilience, she learns valuable lessons about perseverance, friendship, and the true meaning of victory.

Genres: Young Adult, Sports, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Overcoming obstacles, Self-discovery, Friendship, Resilience
