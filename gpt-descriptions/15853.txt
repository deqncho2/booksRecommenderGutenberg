Synopsis: In 'One of Life's Slaves' by Lie, Jonas, a young man named Erik finds himself trapped in a monotonous and unfulfilling life. Struggling to break free from the chains of societal expectations, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery that takes him to unexpected places and introduces him to a cast of intriguing characters. As Erik navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and personal identity, he must confront the harsh realities of the world around him and make difficult choices that will shape his future. This captivating novel explores the universal themes of individuality, the search for meaning, and the power of personal agency.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Psychological

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Self-discovery, Society, Love
