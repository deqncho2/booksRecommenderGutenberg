Synopsis: 
"The Student's Mythology: A Compendium of Greek, Roman, Egyptian, Assyrian, Persian, Hindoo, Chinese, Thibetian, Scandinavian, Celtic, Aztec, and Peruvian Mythologies" by Catherine Ann White is a captivating exploration of ancient mythologies from around the world. This comprehensive compendium takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of myths and legends, delving into the fascinating stories of gods, goddesses, heroes, and creatures from various cultures. From the mighty Zeus of Greek mythology to the enigmatic Anubis of Egyptian lore, and from the epic battles of Norse gods to the mystical creatures of Aztec and Peruvian mythologies, this book offers a treasure trove of knowledge and enchantment. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, White brings these ancient tales to life, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the diverse mythological traditions that have shaped human civilization.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Reference

Themes: Gods and deities, Heroes and legends, Cultural diversity, Mythological creatures, Ancient civilizations
