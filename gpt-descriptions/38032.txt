Synopsis: 
In 'Game Birds and Game Fishes of the Pacific Coast', Harry Thom Payne takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the diverse wildlife of the Pacific Coast. From the majestic California condor to the elusive steelhead trout, Payne's vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes bring these remarkable creatures to life. With his extensive knowledge and passion for the subject, he explores the challenges faced by these game birds and game fishes, shedding light on their habitats, behaviors, and conservation efforts. Whether you are an avid angler, a bird enthusiast, or simply a lover of nature, this book is sure to ignite your curiosity and leave you yearning for your own adventure along the Pacific Coast.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Outdoor Recreation

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Biodiversity, Outdoor exploration
