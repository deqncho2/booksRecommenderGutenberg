Synopsis: In 'The Putnam Hall Encampment; or, The Secret of the Old Mill', a group of adventurous boys from Putnam Hall School embark on a thrilling summer camp experience. As they set up camp near an old mill, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a quest for answers. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must use their wits and teamwork to uncover the truth and protect themselves. Will they be able to solve the secret of the old mill before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Problem-solving
