Synopsis:
'List of Post Offices in Canada, with the Names of the Postmasters ... 1872' is a comprehensive compilation of post offices across Canada during the year 1872. This invaluable resource provides a detailed account of the postmasters and their respective locations, offering a fascinating glimpse into the postal system of the time. From bustling cities to remote rural areas, this list showcases the vast network of communication that connected Canadians in the 19th century. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a genealogist, or simply curious about the development of Canada's postal service, this book is sure to captivate your interest.

Genres: Historical reference, Genealogy, Postal history

Themes: Communication, Infrastructure development, Historical documentation
