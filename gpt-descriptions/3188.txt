Synopsis: 
In "Mark Twain's Speeches," the legendary American author and humorist, Mark Twain, takes center stage as he delivers a collection of captivating and thought-provoking speeches. From his witty anecdotes to his sharp social commentary, Twain's words resonate with both humor and wisdom, offering a unique insight into the issues of his time. Through his engaging storytelling and clever observations, Twain invites readers to join him on a journey filled with laughter, satire, and profound reflections on the human condition.

Genres: 
Humor, Satire, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Social commentary, American society, Human nature
