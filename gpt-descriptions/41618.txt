Synopsis: 

In 'Chronological Retrospect of the History of Yarmouth and Neighbourhood: from A.D. 46 to 1884', author William Finch-Crisp takes readers on a captivating journey through time, uncovering the rich and often surprising history of Yarmouth and its surrounding areas. From ancient Roman settlements to medieval battles, and from the bustling maritime trade to the rise of industry, this meticulously researched account offers a fascinating glimpse into the past of this vibrant coastal town. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Finch-Crisp brings to life the characters and events that have shaped Yarmouth's unique identity, making this book a must-read for history enthusiasts and anyone curious about the hidden stories of a place.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history

Themes: Local identity, Historical events and figures
