Synopsis: In "Where I Wasn't Going" by Richmond, Leigh and Richmond, Walt, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery and healing after a life-altering event. As she travels through different cities and encounters a diverse cast of characters, she learns valuable lessons about love, forgiveness, and the power of human connection. With each step she takes, she unravels the layers of her past and finds the courage to embrace an uncertain future.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Travel

Themes: Self-discovery, Healing, Love, Forgiveness, Human Connection
