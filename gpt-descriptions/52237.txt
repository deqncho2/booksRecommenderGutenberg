Synopsis: In "His Most Gracious Majesty King Edward VII," Marie Belloc Lowndes takes readers on a captivating journey into the life and reign of one of Britain's most intriguing monarchs. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing world, this historical biography delves into the personal struggles, political challenges, and scandalous affairs that shaped King Edward VII's reign. From his early years as the Prince of Wales to his ascension to the throne, Lowndes paints a vivid portrait of a charismatic and complex ruler, revealing the untold stories behind the public image. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the secrets, romances, and power struggles that unfolded within the royal court, offering a fascinating glimpse into the life of a king who defied convention and left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Royal Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Scandal, Personal Identity and Public Image
