Synopsis: 

In 'Betty Wales, Junior: A Story for Girls', we follow the journey of Betty Wales, a spirited and determined young woman who enters her junior year at college with high hopes and dreams. As she navigates the challenges of academics, friendships, and societal expectations, Betty finds herself caught between tradition and her own desire for independence. With the support of her loyal friends and the guidance of a wise mentor, Betty must learn to balance her ambitions with the realities of the world around her. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Self-discovery, Gender roles
