Synopsis: In 'Dear Enemy' by Jean Webster, a young woman named Sallie McBride unexpectedly finds herself in charge of a rundown orphanage. Determined to transform the institution, she clashes with the stern and enigmatic Dr. Robin MacRae. As they navigate their differences and face unexpected challenges, Sallie begins to question her own beliefs and finds herself drawn to the mysterious doctor. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this captivating novel explores the power of love, redemption, and the transformative nature of compassion.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love, Redemption, Social Responsibility
