Synopsis: 
'Love, Worship and Death: Some Renderings from the Greek Anthology' by Rodd, Rennell is a captivating collection of poetic translations that delve into the realms of love, devotion, and mortality. From passionate romances to profound expressions of faith, these renderings from the Greek Anthology transport readers to ancient Greece, where emotions run deep and life's fragility is ever-present. Through the evocative power of language, Rodd, Rennell brings to life the timeless themes that have resonated with humanity for centuries.

Genres: Poetry, Translation, Classical Literature

Themes: Love, Devotion, Mortality, Human Emotions
