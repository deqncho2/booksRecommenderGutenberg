Synopsis: In 'The Wishing Carpet' by Ruth Comfort Mitchell, a young girl named Mary discovers a magical carpet that grants her every wish. As she embarks on exciting adventures, she learns valuable lessons about the consequences of her desires and the importance of selflessness. With each wish, Mary's world expands, taking her to far-off lands and introducing her to fascinating characters. However, she soon realizes that the power of the carpet comes with a price. Will Mary be able to navigate the complexities of her newfound abilities and find true happiness?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Magic, Self-discovery, Consequences of actions
