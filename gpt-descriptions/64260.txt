Synopsis: 
'Lyra Celtica: An Anthology of Representative Celtic Poetry' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of Celtic poetry. This anthology brings together a collection of beautifully crafted verses that explore the depths of Celtic mythology, folklore, and the human experience. From ancient legends to poignant love songs, each poem offers a glimpse into the Celtic soul, evoking a sense of wonder, mystery, and longing. With its lyrical language and evocative imagery, 'Lyra Celtica' invites readers to immerse themselves in the timeless beauty of Celtic poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Celtic Literature

Themes: Celtic mythology, Folklore, Love, Nature, Identity, Spirituality
