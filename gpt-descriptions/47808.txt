Synopsis: 

In 'Dog Breaking: The Most Expeditious, Certain, and Easy Method, Whether Great Excellence or Only Mediocrity Be Required, With Odds and Ends for Those Who Love the Dog and Gun', author W. N. Hutchinson presents a comprehensive guide to training dogs for hunting purposes. This book offers invaluable insights and techniques for both experienced hunters and those new to the world of dog training. With a focus on efficiency and effectiveness, Hutchinson shares his expertise on breaking dogs, ensuring they become skilled and obedient hunting companions. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Hunting, Dog Training

Themes: Hunting, Dog Training, Obedience, Partnership
