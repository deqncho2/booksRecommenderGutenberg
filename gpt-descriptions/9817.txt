Synopsis: Peter Ibbetson is a captivating novel that follows the extraordinary life of Peter, a man who discovers a unique ability to travel through time and space in his dreams. Through these dreams, Peter is able to reunite with his childhood love, Mary, and together they embark on a fantastical journey that transcends the boundaries of reality. As their dreams intertwine with their waking lives, Peter and Mary must navigate the complexities of love, fate, and the power of the human imagination.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love, Dreams, Imagination, Fate, Time
