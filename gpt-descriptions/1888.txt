Synopsis: In the quaint village of Bittermeads, a seemingly idyllic community is shaken by a mysterious death. When a wealthy landowner is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Inspector Bobby Owen is called in to unravel the truth behind the tragedy. As he delves deeper into the secrets and hidden motives of the villagers, Owen uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried secrets that threaten to tear the community apart. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and Owen must race against time to solve the Bittermeads Mystery before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town Secrets, Justice
