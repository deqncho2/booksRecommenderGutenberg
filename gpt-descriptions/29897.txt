Synopsis: 
In 'Runaway' by Joseph Samachson, a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to change the world. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, he must embark on a thrilling journey to reclaim it before it falls into the wrong hands. As he races against time, he encounters unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Technology, Morality, Redemption
