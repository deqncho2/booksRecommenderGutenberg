Synopsis: In the remote jungles of Malaita, a mysterious and powerful force known as "The Red One" captivates the minds of a group of explorers. As they delve deeper into the heart of the jungle, they encounter a phenomenon that challenges their understanding of the world and ignites a primal desire within them. With each encounter, the allure of The Red One grows stronger, leading to a thrilling exploration of the human psyche and the boundaries of scientific knowledge.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Power of the Unknown, Human Nature
