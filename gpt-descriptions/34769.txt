Synopsis: In 'A Child of the Sea; and Life Among the Mormons' by Elizabeth Whitney Williams, we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Elizabeth as she navigates the treacherous waters of the sea and finds herself immersed in the intriguing world of the Mormons. From her perilous adventures at sea to her encounters with a unique religious community, Elizabeth's story is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction

Themes: Identity, Faith, Survival
