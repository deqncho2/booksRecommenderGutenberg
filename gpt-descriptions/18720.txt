Synopsis: In the Yule-Log Glow, Book I: Christmas Tales from 'Round the World' takes readers on a captivating journey through various cultures and traditions, exploring heartwarming and magical Christmas tales from different corners of the globe. From the snowy landscapes of Scandinavia to the bustling streets of Mexico City, this collection of stories will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with love, hope, and the spirit of Christmas. Each tale is beautifully woven with cultural nuances and unique holiday customs, making it a delightful read for both children and adults alike.

Genres: Fiction, Anthology, Holiday

Themes: Christmas traditions, Cultural diversity, Love and compassion, Magic and wonder
