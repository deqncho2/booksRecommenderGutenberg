Synopsis: In 'The Supposed Autographa of John the Scot' by Edward Kennard Rand, a mysterious manuscript surfaces, claiming to be the lost writings of a renowned medieval philosopher. As scholars and collectors scramble to authenticate the document, a young historian finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, uncovering secrets that could change the course of history. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Authenticity, Knowledge and Power, Deception
