Synopsis: In 'The Push of a Finger' by Alfred Bester, a seemingly ordinary man discovers a hidden power that allows him to manipulate reality with a simple touch. As he grapples with the consequences of his newfound ability, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where every decision he makes has far-reaching consequences. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and unexpected twists, all while trying to uncover the truth behind his extraordinary gift.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Identity and Self-Discovery, Consequences of Actions
