Synopsis: In 'Citation and Examination of William Shakspeare, Euseby Treen, Joseph Carnaby, and Silas Gough, Clerk' by Walter Savage Landor, a group of individuals are brought together to testify about the true identity of the renowned playwright William Shakespeare. As they are questioned, secrets are unveiled, and the truth becomes more elusive than ever. This gripping and thought-provoking work delves into the mysteries surrounding Shakespeare's life and challenges the conventional understanding of his genius.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Truth, Literary legacy
