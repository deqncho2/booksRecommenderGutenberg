Synopsis: In 'Obedience to the Articles and Rubrics of the Church of England: a Bond of Union between the Established Clergy', Edwin Proctor Denniss delves into the intricate world of religious doctrine and its impact on the clergy. Through a thought-provoking exploration of the Church of England's articles and rubrics, Denniss uncovers the challenges faced by the established clergy in maintaining unity and adherence to these principles. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of the complexities of faith, Denniss invites readers on a journey that delves into the heart of religious obedience and its role in fostering a sense of unity within the Church.

Genres: Religious Studies, Theology, Non-fiction

Themes: Obedience, Unity, Religious Doctrine, Clergy, Faith
