Synopsis: 
In "The House by the River," the seemingly idyllic lives of the Amberson brothers take a dark turn when a mysterious event occurs in their family home. As the brothers struggle to keep their secret hidden, they become entangled in a web of deceit, suspicion, and unexpected consequences. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the brothers find themselves questioning their own sanity and the loyalty of those around them. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque riverside town, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the house by the river.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Family dynamics, Psychological suspense
