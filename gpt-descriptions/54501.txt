Synopsis: In "Two Dianas in Somaliland: The Record of a Shooting Trip," Herbert and Agnes embark on an extraordinary adventure in the untamed landscapes of Somaliland. As they navigate through the rugged terrain, encountering wild animals and facing unexpected challenges, their journey becomes a thrilling test of courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors transport readers into a world of danger and excitement, where the pursuit of adventure intertwines with the exploration of a fascinating and unfamiliar culture.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife, Cultural Encounter
