Synopsis: 
In 'Mother' by Owen Wister, a young woman named Molly Dunn finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As Molly delves deeper into the stranger's past, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel her own life. With danger lurking around every corner, Molly must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she loves. 

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Identity
