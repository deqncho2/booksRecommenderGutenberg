Synopsis: 
In 'That's Not Love' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a young woman named Carol is swept off her feet by the charming and wealthy Paul. As their whirlwind romance unfolds, Carol becomes increasingly entangled in a web of secrets and manipulation. With her life hanging in the balance, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and escape the clutches of a dangerous love.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Love and obsession, Deception and manipulation
