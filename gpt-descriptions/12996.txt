Synopsis: In "True Version of the Philippine Revolution," Emilio Aguinaldo, the first President of the Philippines, offers a gripping firsthand account of the country's struggle for independence. From the early stages of resistance against Spanish colonization to the eventual clash with American forces, Aguinaldo's narrative unveils the untold stories, political intrigues, and personal sacrifices that shaped the Philippine Revolution. With vivid descriptions and intimate details, this historical memoir takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events that defined a nation's fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical memoir, Political history, Biography

Themes: Independence, Nationalism, Colonialism, Leadership, Resistance
