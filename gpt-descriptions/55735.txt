Synopsis: In 'Perfumes and Their Preparation', George William Askinson takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of fragrances and beauty. This comprehensive guide provides complete directions for creating handkerchief perfumes, smelling-salts, sachets, fumigating pastils, and various preparations for skincare, oral care, and hair care. Additionally, the book delves into the realm of cosmetics, hair dyes, and other essential toiletries. With its detailed instructions and insightful tips, 'Perfumes and Their Preparation' offers a fascinating exploration of the art of perfumery and personal grooming.

Genres: Non-fiction, Beauty and Personal Care, Crafts and Hobbies

Themes: Perfumery, Personal grooming, Self-care
