Synopsis: 
In the enchanting world of Once Upon A Planet, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden portal that transports her to a magical planet filled with extraordinary creatures and breathtaking landscapes. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, Lily must navigate through unexpected challenges and unravel the mysteries of this captivating realm. With the help of newfound friends and her own courage, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: 
Friendship, Bravery, Imagination
