Synopsis: 
In 'Bell's Cathedrals: The Cathedral Church of Manchester', Rev. Thomas Perkins takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and description of the magnificent Cathedral Church of Manchester, as well as the intriguing Collegiate Buildings now known as Chetham's Hospital. From its humble beginnings to its grandeur, Perkins unveils the secrets and stories hidden within the walls of this architectural marvel. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, he paints a vivid picture of the church's significance and the unique role it played in the community. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Religious heritage, Community significance, Architectural marvels
