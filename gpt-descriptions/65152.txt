Synopsis: 
In 'Practical School Discipline: Applied Methods, Part 1', author Ray Coppock presents a comprehensive guide to effective discipline strategies in the educational setting. Drawing from his extensive experience as an educator, Coppock offers practical advice and real-life examples to help teachers and administrators navigate the challenges of maintaining discipline while fostering a positive learning environment. From proactive approaches to handling disruptive behavior to promoting student accountability, this book equips educators with the tools they need to create a harmonious and productive classroom. 

Genres: Education, Self-help

Themes: Classroom management, Discipline strategies, Positive learning environment
