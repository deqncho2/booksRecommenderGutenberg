Synopsis: In 'The Killer' by Stewart Edward White, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm. As his true intentions slowly unravel, the community becomes entangled in a web of suspense and danger. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of the killer's identity and motives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality
