Synopsis: 
'The Dreamers: And Other Poems' by Garrison, Theodosia Pickering is a captivating collection of poetry that delves into the depths of the human imagination and explores the power of dreams. From ethereal landscapes to introspective musings, these poems transport readers to a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Garrison invites readers to embark on a journey of self-discovery and contemplation, where dreams become a gateway to understanding the complexities of life and the human experience.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Philosophy

Themes: Dreams, Imagination, Self-discovery, Contemplation
