Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of George Alfred Henty, a prolific writer known for his thrilling historical adventures. In "Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of George Alfred Henty," readers will embark on a journey through various tales that transport them to different time periods and locations. From ancient Rome to medieval England, and from the American Civil War to the Crimean War, Henty's vivid storytelling will immerse readers in action-packed adventures filled with bravery, loyalty, and the pursuit of justice. This comprehensive index serves as a gateway to explore Henty's vast collection of works, each offering a unique blend of history, adventure, and moral lessons.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young adult literature
Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Justice, History, Morality
