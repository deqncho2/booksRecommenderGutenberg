Synopsis: In 'On Strike, or, Where do the Girls come in?' by Glenfield, Edric, a group of women in a small town decide to take a stand against the unfair treatment they face in their daily lives. Faced with discrimination and limited opportunities, they come together to challenge the status quo and fight for their rights. As tensions rise and the community becomes divided, the women must navigate the complexities of their personal lives while striving for equality and justice. This captivating story explores the power of unity, the strength of women, and the importance of standing up for what is right.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender inequality, Activism, Empowerment, Community, Social justice
