Synopsis: In 'By Reef and Palm' by Louis Becke, embark on a thrilling adventure through the exotic South Pacific islands. Follow the journey of a young sailor who finds himself entangled in a web of danger, love, and betrayal. From treacherous reefs to lush palm-fringed shores, this gripping tale will transport you to a world where survival and desire collide, leaving you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Forbidden Love
