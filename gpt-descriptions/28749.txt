Synopsis: In "The Madcap of the School" by Angela Brazil, follow the misadventures of a spirited and unconventional schoolgirl named Joan. With her quick wit and rebellious nature, Joan constantly finds herself in amusing and unpredictable situations, much to the chagrin of her teachers and the delight of her classmates. From daring escapades to unexpected friendships, this delightful coming-of-age story captures the essence of youthful exuberance and the power of individuality.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, School Stories, Adventure

Themes: Individuality, Friendship, Rebellion
