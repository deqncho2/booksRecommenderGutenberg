Synopsis: In 'Billie Bradley and Her Classmates; Or, The Secret of the Locked Tower' by Janet D. Wheeler, a group of adventurous classmates stumble upon a mysterious locked tower in their school. Intrigued by the secrets it holds, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind its hidden past. As they delve deeper into the tower's history, they encounter unexpected challenges and must rely on their friendship and resourcefulness to solve the enigma. With each clue they uncover, the classmates find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, leading them to a shocking revelation that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Discovery
