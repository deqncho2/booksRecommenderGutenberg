Synopsis: 

In "The Trial of Captain John Kimber, for the Murder of Two Female Negro Slaves, on Board the Recovery, African Slave Ship," the reader is transported back to the year 1792, where a gripping courtroom drama unfolds. The trial centers around the shocking murder of two female slaves on board a notorious African slave ship, the Recovery. As the evidence is presented and witnesses take the stand, the true extent of the horrors endured by these enslaved women is revealed, shedding light on the dark underbelly of the transatlantic slave trade. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Legal Drama, Crime

Themes: Slavery, Justice, Morality, Human Rights
