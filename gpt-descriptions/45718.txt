Synopsis: In 'The Blocking of Zeebrugge', Alfred Francis Blakeney Carpenter takes readers on a thrilling journey through one of the most daring naval operations of World War I. Set against the backdrop of the treacherous North Sea, this gripping account follows a group of courageous sailors as they embark on a perilous mission to block the strategic port of Zeebrugge. With high stakes, intense action, and unexpected twists, this true story of bravery and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Naval warfare, World War I
