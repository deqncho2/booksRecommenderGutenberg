Synopsis: 
'Little Novels' by Collins, Wilkie is a collection of intriguing and captivating short stories that delve into the complexities of human nature. Each story presents a unique and compelling narrative, exploring themes of love, betrayal, mystery, and the consequences of one's actions. From tales of forbidden romance to shocking revelations, Collins weaves together a series of gripping narratives that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Prepare to be enthralled by the masterful storytelling of one of the greatest Victorian authors.

Genres: 
Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Betrayal, Consequences of Actions, Human Nature
