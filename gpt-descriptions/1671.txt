Synopsis: 
In 'When a Man Marries' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and intrigue when he marries a woman with a mysterious past. As he delves deeper into his wife's secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and loyalty, Identity and self-discovery
