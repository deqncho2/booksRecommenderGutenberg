Synopsis: In the fifth volume of "What Will He Do with It?" by Edward Bulwer Lytton, the story continues to follow the lives of the characters as they navigate through love, ambition, and the complexities of society. Secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected events unfold, leaving the reader eagerly anticipating the outcome of their intertwined destinies.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and relationships, social class, ambition, secrets and revelations
