Synopsis: In 'The Son of His Father; vol. 1/3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young man named Arthur is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when he discovers a hidden secret about his father's past. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Arthur finds himself entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and dangerous alliances. With each revelation, Arthur's perception of his father is shattered, forcing him to question his own identity and the true nature of his lineage. Set against the backdrop of Victorian society, this captivating tale explores the complexities of family dynamics, the power of secrets, and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and loyalty
