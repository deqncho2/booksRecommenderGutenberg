Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys in Camp; or, The Rivals of Pine Island," the adventurous Rover brothers find themselves embarking on a thrilling summer camp experience on Pine Island. However, their peaceful retreat takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a group of rival campers who are determined to make their stay anything but enjoyable. As the competition between the two groups intensifies, the Rovers must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and quick thinking to outwit their rivals and ensure a memorable camp experience.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Resourcefulness
