Synopsis: In the final volume of "Only an Ensign: A Tale of the Retreat from Cabul," the young and courageous Ensign Edmund Atherstone finds himself facing the ultimate test of his loyalty and bravery. As the British forces retreat from the treacherous mountains of Afghanistan, Edmund must navigate through a web of political intrigue, personal vendettas, and the harsh realities of war. With his comrades falling one by one, Edmund's determination to survive and protect those he cares for is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the enemy and make it back to safety?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Survival
