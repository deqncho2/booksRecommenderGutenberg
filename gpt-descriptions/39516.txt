Synopsis: In 'A Captain of Industry: Being the Story of a Civilized Man' by Sinclair, Upton, we follow the captivating journey of a man who rises from humble beginnings to become a powerful figure in the business world. As he navigates the cutthroat world of industry, he must confront the moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices that come with his success. This gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of capitalism, the pursuit of power, and the price one must pay for civilization's progress.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Capitalism, Morality, Ambition, Power, Social Inequality
