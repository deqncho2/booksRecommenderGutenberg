Synopsis: In Camp on the Big Sunflower takes readers on an exciting adventure as a group of young boys embark on a camping trip in the wilderness. As they navigate through the challenges of nature, encounter mysterious occurrences, and uncover hidden secrets, their bond is tested and their courage is put to the ultimate test. With each twist and turn, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
