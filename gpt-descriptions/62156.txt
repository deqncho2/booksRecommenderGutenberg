Synopsis: 

In 'International Bridge Authority, Sault Ste. Marie' by Anonymous, a mysterious organization known as the International Bridge Authority operates in the shadows, overseeing the construction and maintenance of a crucial bridge connecting two nations. As tensions rise and secrets begin to unravel, a young engineer finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the true purpose of the bridge becomes a matter of life and death. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous world of power struggles, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic International Bridge Authority.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Conspiracy, Identity and deception
