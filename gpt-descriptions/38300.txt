Synopsis: 
In 'Boy Scouts: Tenderfoot Squad; or, Camping at Raccoon Lodge', a group of young scouts embark on an exciting camping adventure at the mysterious Raccoon Lodge. As they navigate through the wilderness, they encounter unexpected challenges and uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With their scout skills put to the test, the Tenderfoot Squad must work together to overcome obstacles and unravel the mysteries of Raccoon Lodge.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Exploration, Self-discovery
