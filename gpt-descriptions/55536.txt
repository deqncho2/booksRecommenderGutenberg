Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Sixth, or Inniskilling Regiment of Dragoons," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its inception in 1689 to its remarkable achievements up until 1846, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, shedding light on its pivotal role in various conflicts and campaigns. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the triumphs and challenges faced by the Inniskilling Regiment of Dragoons.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: War and conflict, Military strategy, Regimental history
