Synopsis: In 'Solid Solution' by Stamers, James, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the world. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and personal vendettas. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention and uncover the truth behind the forces working against him. This gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the lengths people will go to control it.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Corporate greed, Government conspiracy
