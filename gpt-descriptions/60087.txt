Synopsis: 

In 'Influenza' by the Provincial Board of Health of Ontario, a mysterious and deadly virus sweeps across the province, leaving chaos and devastation in its wake. As the epidemic spreads rapidly, the lives of ordinary citizens are turned upside down, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices. Amidst the panic and despair, a group of courageous individuals emerges, determined to find a cure and restore hope to their shattered community. With time running out and the virus mutating, they must navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected challenges, all while battling their own inner demons. 

Genres: Medical thriller, dystopian fiction, suspense

Themes: Survival, sacrifice, resilience, the human spirit, the consequences of unchecked disease.
