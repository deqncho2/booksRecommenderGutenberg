Synopsis: In 'The Lore of the Honey-Bee', Edwardes, Tickner takes us on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of bees. Through vivid storytelling and fascinating anecdotes, the authors delve into the intricate lives of these remarkable creatures, exploring their complex social structures, ingenious communication methods, and extraordinary abilities. From the ancient myths and folklore surrounding bees to their vital role in pollination and honey production, this book offers a captivating exploration of the captivating world of bees.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Nature, Ecology, Animal Behavior, Folklore
