Synopsis: In the heart of Australia, a mischievous and spirited family of seven children, led by their strict military father and their warm-hearted stepmother, embark on a series of unforgettable adventures. From pranks and escapades to secrets and unexpected twists, the lives of the Woolcot children are filled with laughter, tears, and the bittersweet realities of growing up. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence and the complexities of family dynamics, their bond is tested, and they learn valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Childhood, Family, Rebellion, Love, Loss, Resilience
