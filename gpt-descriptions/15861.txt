Synopsis: In 'The Things Which Remain: An Address To Young Ministers', Daniel A. Goodsell delivers a thought-provoking and inspiring message to young ministers, guiding them on their journey of faith and service. Through personal anecdotes, biblical references, and practical advice, Goodsell explores the challenges and joys of ministry, urging young ministers to stay true to their calling and to embrace the transformative power of God's love. This heartfelt address serves as a beacon of hope and encouragement for those embarking on a lifelong commitment to serving others.

Genres: Non-fiction, Christian literature, Self-help

Themes: Ministry, Faith, Service, Calling, Guidance
