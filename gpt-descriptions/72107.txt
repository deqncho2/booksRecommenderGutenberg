Synopsis:
In 'Married or single?, Vol. 2 (of 3)' by B. M. Croker, the lives of two sisters, Evelyn and Mabel, take unexpected turns as they navigate the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations. As Evelyn finds herself torn between duty and desire, Mabel embarks on a journey of self-discovery that challenges the conventions of her time. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century England, this captivating novel delves into the intricacies of relationships, the pursuit of happiness, and the power of personal choice.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes:
Love and Desire, Societal Expectations, Personal Freedom
