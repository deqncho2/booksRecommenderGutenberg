Synopsis: In 'General Brock' by Edgar, Matilda Ridout, Lady, follow the captivating journey of a remarkable military leader who defied all odds. Set against the backdrop of the War of 1812, this historical novel delves into the life of General Isaac Brock, a man driven by duty, honor, and an unwavering commitment to his country. From his early days in the British Army to his pivotal role in defending Upper Canada against American invasion, General Brock's story is one of courage, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of a true hero. As the war intensifies and the stakes grow higher, readers will be enthralled by the strategic brilliance and unwavering determination of this legendary figure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Sacrifice
