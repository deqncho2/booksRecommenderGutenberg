Synopsis: 

'Le Morvan, [A District of France,] Its Wild Sports, Vineyards and Forests; with Legends, Antiquities, Rural and Local Sketches' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting district of Le Morvan in France. Written by Henri de Crignelle, this book delves into the untamed wilderness, picturesque vineyards, and mysterious forests of the region. Along the way, the author weaves in intriguing legends, explores ancient artifacts, and paints vivid portraits of the rural and local life in Le Morvan. 

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Nature and wilderness, Cultural heritage, Local traditions and customs
