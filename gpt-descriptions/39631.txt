Synopsis: In 'The Runaways: A New and Original Story' by Gould, Nat, a group of teenagers find themselves on the run after uncovering a dangerous secret that puts their lives in jeopardy. As they navigate through a world filled with mystery and peril, they must rely on their wits, courage, and the bonds of friendship to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Secrets
