Synopsis: 
'With Axe and Rope in the New Zealand Alps' takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the rugged and treacherous landscapes of the New Zealand Alps. George Edward Mannering, an experienced mountaineer, recounts his daring expeditions, facing perilous cliffs, icy crevasses, and unpredictable weather conditions. From heart-stopping encounters with avalanches to the triumph of reaching the summit, this gripping narrative captures the essence of the exhilarating and dangerous world of mountaineering.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Nature's Power
