Synopsis: 
'Poems' by W.B. Yeats is a captivating collection of lyrical verses that transport readers into a world of enchantment, exploring themes of love, loss, and the complexities of the human experience. From ethereal landscapes to poignant reflections on the passage of time, these poems offer a profound glimpse into the depths of the human soul. With each line, Yeats weaves a tapestry of emotions, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Symbolism

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Spirituality, Mythology
