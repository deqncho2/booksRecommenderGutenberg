Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Strong Arm; Or, Saving an Enemy' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell finds himself in a precarious situation when he discovers that his arch-nemesis, Bart Hodge, is in grave danger. Despite their bitter rivalry, Frank must put aside his personal feelings and use his exceptional strength to save Bart from a perilous predicament. As Frank embarks on this unexpected mission, he learns valuable lessons about compassion, forgiveness, and the true meaning of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Redemption, Overcoming Rivalry
