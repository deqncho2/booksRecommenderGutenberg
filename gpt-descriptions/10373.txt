Synopsis:
In "The Middle Temple Murder" by J.S. Fletcher, a mysterious murder takes place within the prestigious Middle Temple, one of London's renowned Inns of Court. When a body is discovered in a locked room, seemingly impossible to access, the brilliant detective, Mr. Ronald Breton, is called upon to unravel the perplexing puzzle. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected connections that lead him on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of the legal world. With each twist and turn, the tension mounts, and the reader is left guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Murder, Intrigue, Legal System, Secrets, Investigation
