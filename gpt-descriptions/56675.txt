Synopsis: In 'The Attic Guest: A Novel', a young couple moves into their dream home, only to discover a mysterious presence lurking in the attic. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the house, they uncover a haunting tale of love, betrayal, and revenge that spans generations. With each revelation, the couple becomes entangled in a web of dark secrets and must confront their own demons to survive. As the tension builds, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving the reader questioning what is truly lurking in the shadows of the attic.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Love and betrayal, Revenge and redemption
