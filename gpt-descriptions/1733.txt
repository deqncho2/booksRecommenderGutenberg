Synopsis: In "The Red Cross Girl" by Richard Harding Davis, a young woman finds herself caught in the midst of war, as she joins the Red Cross to provide aid and support to soldiers on the front lines. As she navigates the chaos and danger of the battlefield, she discovers love, courage, and the true meaning of sacrifice. Will she be able to save lives while also protecting her own heart?

Genres: War fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Bravery in the face of danger, The impact of war on individuals and society
