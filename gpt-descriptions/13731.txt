Synopsis: 
In 'Romance Island' by Gale, Zona, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to a secluded island in search of adventure and romance. As she arrives, she finds herself surrounded by a diverse group of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires. As the days pass, Lily becomes entangled in a web of passion, mystery, and unexpected twists that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and deception
