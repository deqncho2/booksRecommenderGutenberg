Synopsis: In 'Lectures on Language, as Particularly Connected with English Grammar,' Balch delves into the fascinating intricacies of language, unraveling its mysteries and shedding light on the nuances of English grammar. Through engaging lectures, he explores the evolution of language, the power of words, and the impact of grammar on communication. Balch's insightful analysis and thought-provoking examples will captivate readers, leaving them with a newfound appreciation for the beauty and complexity of language.

Genres: Linguistics, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Language evolution, Power of words, Grammar and communication
