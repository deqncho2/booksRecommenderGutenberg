Synopsis: In "The Hanging Stranger" by Philip K. Dick, a small town is suddenly thrown into chaos when a mysterious event takes place. Ed Loyce, an ordinary citizen, stumbles upon a shocking sight that defies all logic and reason. As he tries to uncover the truth behind this inexplicable occurrence, Loyce finds himself questioning his own sanity and the very fabric of reality. With time running out, he must confront the terrifying implications of what he has discovered before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Paranoia, Existentialism, Perception of Reality
