Synopsis: 
In "Pee-wee Harris in Camp" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, young Pee-wee Harris embarks on an exciting adventure as he joins his friends at a summer camp. From navigating treacherous trails to solving mysterious riddles, Pee-wee's boundless energy and resourcefulness are put to the test. As the campers face unexpected challenges and forge new friendships, Pee-wee's infectious enthusiasm and determination shine through, making this summer one they will never forget.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
