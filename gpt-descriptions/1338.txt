Synopsis: 

In 'Selected Prose of Oscar Wilde', the brilliant and witty Oscar Wilde showcases his unparalleled talent for prose through a collection of his most captivating works. From his humorous and satirical essays to his profound and thought-provoking literary criticism, Wilde's words leap off the page, captivating readers with his unique perspective on art, society, and the human condition. Delve into the mind of one of the greatest literary figures of all time as he explores the complexities of life, love, and the pursuit of beauty in his inimitable style.

Genres: Essay, Literary Criticism, Satire

Themes: Art and Aesthetics, Society and Morality, Love and Relationships
