Synopsis: 
In "Jack Tier; Or, The Florida Reef" by James Fenimore Cooper, a young woman named Rose Budd embarks on a perilous journey to find her missing father, who disappeared while searching for sunken treasure. Along the treacherous Florida Reef, Rose encounters a mysterious sailor named Jack Tier, who becomes her steadfast companion and protector. As they navigate the dangers of the sea and face unexpected challenges, Rose begins to question Jack's true identity and motives. With its gripping adventure, unexpected twists, and a touch of romance, this novel takes readers on a thrilling voyage through the unpredictable waters of the Florida Reef.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Survival
