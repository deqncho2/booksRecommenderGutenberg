Synopsis: 
In "On the Border with Andrew Jackson," John T. McIntyre takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous era of the War of 1812. As a young soldier, McIntyre finds himself on the front lines, fighting alongside the legendary General Andrew Jackson. From intense battles to treacherous encounters with Native American tribes, McIntyre's firsthand account offers a gripping and vivid portrayal of the hardships and triumphs experienced during this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Survival
