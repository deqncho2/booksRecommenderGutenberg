Synopsis: In 'Giphantia: Or a View of What Has Passed, What Is Now Passing, and, During the Present Century, What Will Pass, in the World,' Tiphaigne de La Roche takes readers on a captivating journey through time and space. The story follows a young man named Giphant, who discovers a mysterious substance that allows him to witness events from the past, present, and future. As Giphant explores this newfound ability, he encounters astonishing sights and encounters that challenge his understanding of the world. This thought-provoking tale delves into the realms of science, philosophy, and human curiosity, leaving readers pondering the nature of time and the possibilities that lie beyond our perception.

Genres: Science fiction, philosophical fiction

Themes: Time travel, human curiosity, the nature of reality
