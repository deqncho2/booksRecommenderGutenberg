Synopsis: In "The Philippines a Century Hence," Jose Rizal takes readers on a thought-provoking journey into the future of his beloved country. Through a series of insightful essays, Rizal explores the potential outcomes and challenges that the Philippines may face in the next century. From political and economic predictions to cultural and social transformations, Rizal's vision offers a compelling glimpse into the nation's destiny. As he delves into the complexities of colonialism, nationalism, and identity, Rizal's words resonate with a sense of urgency and hope, urging readers to reflect on the path that lies ahead for the Philippines.

Genres: Essay, Political commentary, Social critique

Themes: Nationalism, Colonialism, Identity, Progress, Cultural transformation
