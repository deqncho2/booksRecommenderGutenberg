Synopsis: In 'Stories from Wagner', J. Walker McSpadden brings to life the captivating tales of Richard Wagner's renowned operas. From the mythical world of gods and heroes in 'The Ring of the Nibelung' to the tragic love story of 'Tristan and Isolde', this collection delves into the dramatic and enchanting narratives that have made Wagner's works timeless. Through vivid storytelling, McSpadden transports readers into a world of passion, betrayal, and redemption, where music and emotion intertwine to create unforgettable experiences. 

Genres: Fiction, Music, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Mythology, Redemption, Betrayal
