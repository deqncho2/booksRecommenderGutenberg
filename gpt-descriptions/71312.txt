Synopsis: In "Leprosy: $b in its clinical & pathological aspects," Hansen and Looft delve into the mysterious world of leprosy, exploring its clinical and pathological aspects. Through a captivating narrative, they uncover the enigmatic nature of this ancient disease, shedding light on its symptoms, progression, and impact on individuals and communities. With a blend of scientific rigor and storytelling, the authors take readers on a journey that challenges preconceived notions and reveals the complex reality of leprosy. 

Genres: Medical non-fiction, Historical exploration, Scientific investigation

Themes: Disease and its impact, Stigma and discrimination, Medical advancements
