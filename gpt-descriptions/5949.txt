Synopsis: In 'Beasley's Christmas Party' by Booth Tarkington, a mysterious and eccentric millionaire named Beasley decides to throw an extravagant Christmas party that captures the attention of the entire town. As the preparations for the party unfold, the reader is introduced to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. The anticipation builds as the guests arrive, and the party takes an unexpected turn, leading to a night filled with surprises, revelations, and unexpected connections. This heartwarming and humorous tale explores the power of community, the magic of the holiday season, and the transformative nature of human connections.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Holiday

Themes: Community, Generosity, Redemption, Human Connections
