Synopsis: 
When Wilderness Was King: A Tale of the Illinois Country takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Illinois territory in the 18th century. Amidst the backdrop of the French and Indian War, the story follows the adventures of a young frontiersman named Robert Lennox. From treacherous encounters with Native American tribes to daring battles against rival European powers, Lennox must navigate a world where survival is uncertain and danger lurks at every turn. As he strives to protect his loved ones and forge his own destiny, Lennox discovers the true meaning of courage, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of the wilderness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Action

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Conflict, Identity, Loyalty
