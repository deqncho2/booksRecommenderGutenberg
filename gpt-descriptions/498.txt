Synopsis: 
Rebecca of Sunnybrook Farm follows the spirited and imaginative Rebecca Rowena Randall as she leaves her rural home to live with her two aunts in the bustling town of Riverboro. Despite facing numerous challenges and encountering eccentric characters, Rebecca's indomitable spirit and infectious charm leave an indelible mark on the community. As she navigates the ups and downs of her new life, Rebecca's determination and resilience shine through, captivating readers with her delightful adventures and heartwarming relationships.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: 
Independence, Imagination, Family, Resilience
