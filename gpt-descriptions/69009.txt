Synopsis: In 'Definition' by Damon Knight, a renowned linguist is tasked with deciphering an ancient text that holds the key to unlocking a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic language, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where every word has the potential to change the course of history. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous world of secrets and deception to uncover the true meaning behind the cryptic words before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Language and communication, Power and manipulation
