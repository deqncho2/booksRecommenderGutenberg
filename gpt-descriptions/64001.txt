Synopsis: In 'J. Comyns Carr: Stray Memories, by His Wife', Alice Vansittart Strettel takes us on a captivating journey through the life and career of her husband, J. Comyns Carr. From their early days in London's vibrant theater scene to their encounters with renowned artists and writers, Strettel paints a vivid picture of a man whose passion for the arts knew no bounds. With anecdotes and personal reflections, she offers a unique perspective on the inner workings of the theater world and the challenges faced by artists striving for recognition. This intimate memoir is a tribute to a remarkable man and a celebration of the enduring power of creativity.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Theater

Themes: Artistic passion, Relationships, The theater world
