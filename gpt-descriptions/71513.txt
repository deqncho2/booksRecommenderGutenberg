Synopsis: 
'Book of Needs of the Holy Orthodox Church' by Russkaia pravoslavnaia tserkov is a captivating and essential guide that delves into the rituals and practices of the Orthodox Church. This comprehensive book not only provides a detailed account of the various needs and services within the Church, but it also includes an intriguing appendix containing offices for the laying on of hands. From the sacraments to the blessings, this book offers a profound insight into the spiritual life of the Orthodox Church, making it an indispensable resource for both clergy and laity.

Genres: Religious, Liturgical, Reference

Themes: Orthodox Christianity, Rituals and Sacraments, Spiritual Guidance
