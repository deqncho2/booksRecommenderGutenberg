Synopsis: In "The Story of Rome from the Earliest Times to the End of the Republic," Arthur Gilman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rise and fall of one of the greatest civilizations in history. From the legendary founding of Rome to the dramatic events that led to the downfall of the Roman Republic, this book delves into the fascinating stories of emperors, generals, and ordinary citizens who shaped the destiny of Rome. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Gilman brings to life the triumphs, struggles, and political intrigues that defined the Roman Empire, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and anyone curious about the origins of Western civilization.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Ancient History

Themes: Power and politics, Rise and fall of civilizations, Leadership and governance
