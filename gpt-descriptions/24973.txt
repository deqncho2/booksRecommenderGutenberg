Synopsis: In 'The Sun King' by Derreaux, Gaston, a mysterious and enigmatic figure rises to power, captivating the hearts and minds of his subjects. As his reign unfolds, the reader is taken on a journey through opulent palaces, political intrigue, and forbidden love. With each turn of the page, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power is explored. This gripping tale will leave readers questioning the lengths one will go to maintain their kingdom and the consequences that come with such ambition.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Ambition, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Intrigue
