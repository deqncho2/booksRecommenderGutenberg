Synopsis: In 'The Chief Engineer' by Abbott, Henry, a brilliant engineer finds himself caught in a web of corporate intrigue and personal vendettas when he is assigned to oversee a groundbreaking project. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the company, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of power struggles, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With his reputation and career on the line, he must uncover the truth before it consumes him completely.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corporate corruption, Ambition, Betrayal
