Synopsis: In "The Honorable Peter Stirling and What People Thought of Him," the charismatic and enigmatic Peter Stirling rises through the ranks of society, captivating everyone he encounters. As his reputation grows, people from all walks of life form their own opinions about him, ranging from admiration to suspicion. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century New York, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human perception and the power of reputation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Perception, Reputation, Social class, Ambition
