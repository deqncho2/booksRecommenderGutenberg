Synopsis: In 'The Belly of Gor Jeetl' by Charles A. Stearns, a renowned archaeologist, Dr. Amelia Hartley, embarks on a perilous expedition to uncover the secrets hidden within the ancient temple of Gor Jeetl. As she delves deeper into the mysterious belly of the temple, she encounters treacherous traps, enigmatic puzzles, and a malevolent force that will stop at nothing to protect its secrets. With time running out and her own sanity at stake, Dr. Hartley must unravel the truth behind Gor Jeetl before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration of ancient civilizations, the pursuit of knowledge, the battle between good and evil
