Synopsis: 
"The Safety Curtain, and Other Stories" is a captivating collection of short stories by Ethel M. Dell that will transport readers into a world of mystery, romance, and unexpected twists. From the haunting tale of a cursed painting to the thrilling adventures of a young woman caught in a web of secrets, each story will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth. With vivid descriptions, complex characters, and a touch of the supernatural, this collection promises to captivate and surprise readers at every turn.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Love and sacrifice
