Synopsis: 

'Notes on Stable Management in India and the Colonies' takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of horse care and management in the vibrant settings of India and the colonies. Written by Joshua A. Nunn, an experienced horseman with a deep understanding of the equestrian world, this book offers a unique perspective on the challenges and intricacies of maintaining stables in diverse and exotic locations. From the scorching deserts of India to the lush landscapes of the colonies, Nunn's insightful observations and practical advice provide a fascinating glimpse into the fascinating world of stable management in these captivating settings.

Genres: Non-fiction, Equestrian, Travel

Themes: Horse care, Stable management, Cultural diversity
