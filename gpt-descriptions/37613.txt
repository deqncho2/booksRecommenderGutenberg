Synopsis: In 'Search-Light Letters' by Robert Grant, a mysterious series of letters begins to arrive at the doorstep of a small New England town, captivating the residents and sparking a chain of events that will uncover long-held secrets and challenge the very fabric of their community. As the letters continue to arrive, the townspeople find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected connections, leading them on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption. With each letter, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Community dynamics, Redemption
