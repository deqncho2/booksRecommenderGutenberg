Synopsis: In 'Gold' by Stewart Edward White, a young man named John Ballard embarks on a thrilling adventure to strike it rich in the gold rush of California. As he faces the harsh realities of the wild and unpredictable landscape, John encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own motivations and secrets. From treacherous river crossings to dangerous encounters with outlaws, John's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Will he find the fortune he seeks, or will he discover something even more valuable along the way?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Pursuit of wealth, Survival in the wilderness, Human nature and morality
