Synopsis: 

The Annual Register 1914: A Review of Public Events at Home and Abroad for the Year 1914 takes readers on a captivating journey through the significant events that unfolded during this pivotal year. From political upheavals and international conflicts to scientific breakthroughs and cultural milestones, this comprehensive review offers a compelling snapshot of the world in 1914. Written by an anonymous author, this meticulously researched work provides a detailed account of the events that shaped history, leaving readers eager to delve into the pages and uncover the fascinating stories that lie within.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Reference

Themes: World events, Politics, Culture, Science
