Synopsis: 
In 'The Golden Asse' by Apuleius, a young man named Lucius embarks on a journey that takes him on a wild and enchanting adventure. After a fateful encounter with a witch, Lucius finds himself transformed into a donkey, unable to communicate with humans. As he navigates through a world filled with magic, mystery, and danger, Lucius encounters a cast of eccentric characters, each with their own secrets and desires. Along the way, he becomes entangled in a series of captivating and often humorous misadventures, all while desperately seeking a way to regain his human form. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: 
Transformation, Magic, Desire, Identity
