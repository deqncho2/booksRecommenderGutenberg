Synopsis: In 'Acid Bath' by Bill Garson, a renowned chemist stumbles upon a groundbreaking formula that has the potential to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his discovery, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and personal vendettas. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention and uncover the truth behind the sinister forces that seek to control it.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific ethics, Power and corruption
