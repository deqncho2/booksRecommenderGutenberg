Synopsis: In 'Last Call for Doomsday!' by Hamilton, Edmond, a brilliant scientist discovers a shocking secret that could bring about the end of the world. As he races against time to prevent the impending catastrophe, he must navigate a treacherous web of conspiracy, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the protagonist must confront his own demons and make unimaginable sacrifices to save the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Apocalyptic, Scientific Discovery, Morality, Redemption
