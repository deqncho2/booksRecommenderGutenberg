Synopsis: In "God Passes By" by Shoghi Effendi, embark on a captivating journey through the history of the Baha'i Faith, as the author masterfully weaves together the stories of its early beginnings and the challenges faced by its followers. From the birth of the Faith in Persia to its spread across the globe, this book unveils the remarkable events and individuals who shaped the Baha'i community. With vivid descriptions and profound insights, "God Passes By" offers a compelling narrative that will leave readers inspired and enlightened.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Biography

Themes: Faith, Persecution, Spiritual Transformation
