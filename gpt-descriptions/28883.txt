Synopsis: In 'The Copper-Clad World' by Vincent Harl, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a hidden world beneath our own. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she uncovers a secret society, long-forgotten legends, and a power that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate a treacherous path to protect the truth and prevent it from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of hidden worlds, the power of ancient artifacts, the battle between good and evil
