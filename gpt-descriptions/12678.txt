Synopsis: 
In 'The House of Mystery: An Episode in the Career of Rosalie Le Grange, Clairvoyant', readers are introduced to the enigmatic world of Rosalie Le Grange, a renowned clairvoyant with a mysterious past. When she is invited to a secluded mansion to investigate a series of unexplained occurrences, Rosalie finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and supernatural phenomena. As she delves deeper into the house's dark history, she must confront her own abilities and confront the malevolent forces that threaten to consume her. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and the supernatural becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Clairvoyance, Secrets, Deception, Supernatural Phenomena
