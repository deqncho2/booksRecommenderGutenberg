Synopsis: In 'The Hippodrome' by Rachel Hayward, a young woman named Lily finds herself drawn into the captivating world of a mysterious circus that appears in her small town. As she becomes entangled in the secrets and enchantments of the performers, Lily discovers that the circus holds more than just thrilling spectacles. With each performance, she uncovers a hidden realm of magic, love, and danger, where nothing is as it seems. As Lily delves deeper into the circus's mysteries, she must navigate a web of illusions and make choices that will forever change her life. 

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Magic, Identity, Love, Perception
