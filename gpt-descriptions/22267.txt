Synopsis: In "A Woman for Mayor: A Novel of To-day," Helen M. Winslow takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of politics, power, and gender dynamics. Set in a small town, the story follows the ambitious and determined Laura Curtis as she challenges the traditional norms by running for mayor. As Laura faces numerous obstacles and confronts deep-rooted prejudices, she must navigate the complexities of her personal life while striving to make a lasting impact on her community. This thought-provoking novel explores the struggles and triumphs of a woman breaking barriers in a male-dominated society, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover if Laura can overcome the odds and become the first female mayor.

Genres: Fiction, Political Drama, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender Equality, Ambition, Power Dynamics, Social Change
