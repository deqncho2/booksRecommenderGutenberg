Synopsis: 

Step into the world of creativity and intellectual property as the Library of Congress Copyright Office presents 'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1958 July - December.' This captivating compilation unveils a treasure trove of renewed copyrights, showcasing the diverse range of artistic endeavors that captivated the American imagination during the latter half of 1958. From literature to music, from film to photography, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the creative landscape of the time. Delve into the pages and discover the hidden stories behind the copyrights that shaped the cultural fabric of the United States.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Creativity, Cultural history
