Synopsis: 
In 'More Portmanteau Plays' by Stuart Walker, prepare to be transported into a world where imagination knows no bounds. This collection of plays takes readers on a rollercoaster ride of emotions, blending comedy, drama, and mystery into a captivating theatrical experience. From a whimsical tale set in a magical forest to a gripping courtroom drama, each play offers a unique and thought-provoking narrative that will leave you wanting more. With unexpected twists and unforgettable characters, 'More Portmanteau Plays' is a testament to the power of storytelling and the limitless possibilities of the stage.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Human nature, Justice
