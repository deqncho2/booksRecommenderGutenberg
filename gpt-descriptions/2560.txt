Synopsis: 
In the small mining town of Red Gulch, three unlikely partners find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of the Wild West, their lives become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. With danger lurking around every corner, they must rely on their wits and each other to survive. Will their partnership withstand the challenges they face, or will it crumble under the weight of their pasts?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Redemption, Survival
