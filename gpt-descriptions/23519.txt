Synopsis: In "The Mormon Menace: The Confessions of John Doyle Lee, Danite," readers are taken on a gripping journey through the life of John Doyle Lee, a member of the secretive Mormon group known as the Danites. Lee's confessions reveal a world of religious fanaticism, violence, and manipulation, as he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and power struggles within the Mormon community. As the story unfolds, the reader is left questioning the true nature of faith, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect their beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Religious Fiction

Themes: Religious fanaticism, Power struggles, Loyalty, Manipulation
