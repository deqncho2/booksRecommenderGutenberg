Synopsis: In "The Mountain School-Teacher" by Melville Davisson Post, a young schoolteacher finds herself in a remote mountain community, where she must navigate the mysterious and often dangerous world of the locals. As she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden motives, she must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth and protect herself from the lurking dangers that surround her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Isolation, Deception, Survival
