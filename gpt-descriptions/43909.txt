Synopsis: Join Sometub on an exhilarating motorboat adventure along the historic C. & O. Canal, as author John Pryor Cowan takes you on a captivating journey through the heart of Maryland. From picturesque landscapes to unexpected encounters, this narrative unveils the hidden gems and untold stories that lie along the canal's meandering waters. As Sometub cruises through locks and tunnels, encountering both natural wonders and man-made marvels, the reader is transported to a bygone era, where the past intertwines with the present, and the beauty of the Maryland countryside unfolds before their eyes.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, History, Nature, Preservation
