Synopsis: In 'Taking the Census: A Farce' by Elizabeth F. Guptill, chaos ensues when a small town is tasked with conducting the census. As the eccentric residents attempt to navigate the process, hilarious misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and unexpected revelations come to light. With a colorful cast of characters and a series of comical mishaps, this farcical tale will keep readers laughing from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Identity, Miscommunication, Community
