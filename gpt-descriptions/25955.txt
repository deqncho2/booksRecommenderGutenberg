Synopsis: In 'The Bronze Eagle: A Story of the Hundred Days', the Baroness Orczy takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous period of the Hundred Days, when Napoleon Bonaparte returned to power in France. Amidst the chaos and uncertainty, a young Englishman finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of espionage and intrigue. As he navigates the treacherous world of spies and double agents, he must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and courage. With its gripping plot and rich historical backdrop, 'The Bronze Eagle' is a captivating tale of adventure and suspense.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Political Intrigue
