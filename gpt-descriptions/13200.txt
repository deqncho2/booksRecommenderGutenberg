Synopsis: 

'Act, Declaration, & Testimony for the Whole of our Covenanted Reformation, as Attained to, and Established in Britain and Ireland; Particularly Betwixt the Years 1638 and 1649, Inclusive' is a powerful and thought-provoking historical document that delves into the tumultuous period of the Covenanted Reformation in Britain and Ireland. 

This work takes readers on a journey through the years 1638 to 1649, exploring the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices made by those who fought for religious freedom and the establishment of a reformed church. It provides a comprehensive account of the events, people, and ideas that shaped this pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Political

Themes: Religious freedom, Reformation, Covenant, Resistance, Sacrifice
