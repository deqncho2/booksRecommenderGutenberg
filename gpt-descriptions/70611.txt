Synopsis: 

Step into the world of artistic creations from 1965 to 1977 with 'U.S. Copyright Renewals: Artwork 1965-1977' by the Library of Congress. This captivating compilation unveils a treasure trove of forgotten and overlooked artwork that once graced the pages of books, magazines, and newspapers. From stunning illustrations to thought-provoking designs, this collection offers a glimpse into the creative minds of the era. Delve into the pages and discover a visual feast that will transport you back in time, igniting your imagination and leaving you yearning for more.

Genres: Art, History, Reference

Themes: Artistic expression, Cultural significance, Historical preservation
