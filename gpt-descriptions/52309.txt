Synopsis: 
In 'Twenty-Two Goblins. Translated from the Sanskrit', embark on a captivating journey through ancient India as you encounter a collection of enchanting and mischievous goblins. These cunning creatures, with their supernatural powers and clever tricks, weave together a tapestry of captivating tales that will leave you spellbound. From humorous escapades to poignant lessons, each goblin's story unveils a unique facet of human nature, exploring the depths of desire, ambition, love, and the consequences of our actions. Prepare to be transported to a world where magic and morality intertwine, and where the line between good and evil becomes delightfully blurred.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: 
Morality, Human Nature, Magic, Consequences
