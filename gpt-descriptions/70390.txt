Synopsis: In 'The Monster-Hunters' by Rolt-Wheeler, Francis, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind a series of mysterious disappearances. As they delve deeper into the dark secrets of their small town, they encounter terrifying creatures and face unimaginable dangers. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their own fears and discover the power of friendship and bravery. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Overcoming fears
