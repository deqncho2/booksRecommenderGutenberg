Synopsis: In 'A Master of Deception' by Richard Marsh, a renowned illusionist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is hired to perform at a secluded manor. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house, he discovers that not everything is as it seems, and his own skills of deception may be put to the ultimate test. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Identity
