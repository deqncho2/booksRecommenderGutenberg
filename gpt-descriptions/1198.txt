Synopsis: 
'Robbery under Arms: A Story of Life and Adventure in the Bush and in the Australian Goldfields' by Boldrewood, Rolf is a thrilling tale set in the rugged Australian outback during the 19th century. The story follows the exploits of Dick Marston, a young man who finds himself caught up in a life of crime and adventure alongside his brother Jim. From cattle rustling to bushranging, the Marston brothers navigate a dangerous world filled with outlaws, lawmen, and the lure of gold. As they face numerous challenges and make difficult choices, their loyalty and resilience are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Crime

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Morality, Outlaw Culture
