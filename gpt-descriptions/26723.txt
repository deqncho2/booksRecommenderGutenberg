Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid of Ticonderoga' by Alice Turner Curtis, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Ruth as she finds herself caught up in the midst of the American Revolution. Set in the historic town of Ticonderoga, Ruth's bravery and resourcefulness are put to the test as she becomes an unexpected hero in the fight for freedom. With danger lurking at every turn, Ruth's determination and loyalty to her country will inspire readers of all ages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Coming of Age
