Synopsis: In 'The Fugitive Slave Law and Its Victims: Anti-Slavery Tracts No. 18' by Samuel May, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of those affected by the Fugitive Slave Law. This powerful work sheds light on the struggles faced by fugitive slaves and the injustices perpetuated by this law, leaving readers on the edge of their seats as they witness the courage and resilience of those fighting for freedom.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Social Justice

Themes: Slavery, Injustice, Resistance, Freedom
