Synopsis: 

In 'Cape of Storms: A Novel' by Pollard Percival, a mysterious shipwreck off the treacherous coast of Cape Point, South Africa, sets in motion a thrilling tale of adventure, secrets, and the power of the human spirit. As the protagonist, a young archaeologist, delves into the enigmatic history of the ship and its crew, she becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue that spans centuries. With each discovery, she uncovers a hidden world of hidden treasures, lost love, and a haunting curse that threatens to consume her. As she races against time to unravel the truth, she must confront her own fears and confront the stormy forces that have shaped her destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Fate and Destiny, Redemption, Secrets and Betrayal
