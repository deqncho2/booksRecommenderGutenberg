Synopsis: In "The Man Whom the Trees Loved," a renowned artist and his wife move to a secluded cottage in the countryside, surrounded by a magnificent forest. As the artist becomes increasingly obsessed with the trees, his relationship with his wife begins to deteriorate, and he finds himself drawn deeper into the mysterious power of nature. Strange occurrences and eerie encounters unfold, blurring the line between reality and imagination, ultimately leading to a shocking revelation.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, supernatural

Themes: Nature's power and influence, obsession, the thin line between sanity and madness
