Synopsis: In 'The Chinese Dragon' by Hayes, L. Newton, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to an ancient secret. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic world of Chinese mythology and history, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue and deception. With time running out, she must decipher the clues and face her own fears to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mythology, History, Secrets and Betrayal
