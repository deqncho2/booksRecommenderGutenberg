Synopsis:
In 'Lorimer of the Northwest' by Harold Bindloss, we follow the gripping journey of Lorimer, a young man who finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of deceit and treachery in the rugged Canadian wilderness. As he navigates through the untamed landscape, Lorimer must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine his future. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Survival, Redemption, Betrayal
