Synopsis: In 'The Tithe' by Stewart, Elmer Bryan, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that have left the community in fear. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events, they soon realize that there is a dark secret lurking beneath the surface. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who can be trusted and what lies hidden in the shadows.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Fear and paranoia
