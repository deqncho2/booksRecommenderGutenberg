Synopsis: In 'The Passing of Mars: A Modern Morality Play' by Wilkinson, Marguerite, a mysterious visitor arrives in a small town, bringing with him a series of thought-provoking challenges for the townspeople. As they grapple with their own desires, fears, and moral dilemmas, the visitor's presence forces them to confront the consequences of their actions. With each passing day, the town becomes a stage for a gripping exploration of human nature and the choices we make.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Human Nature
