Synopsis: In 'The Relations between the Laws of Babylonia and the Laws of the Hebrew Peoples: The Schweich Lectures', C. H. W. Johns delves into the fascinating connection between the legal systems of ancient Babylonia and the Hebrew people. Through meticulous research and analysis, Johns uncovers surprising similarities and intriguing influences that shaped the laws of both civilizations. From the intricate codes of Hammurabi to the commandments of Moses, this captivating exploration sheds light on the historical and cultural interplay between these two ancient societies.

Genres: Historical, Legal, Comparative Studies

Themes: Legal systems, Cultural exchange, Ancient civilizations
