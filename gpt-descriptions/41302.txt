Synopsis: 
"The Letters of Her Mother to Elizabeth" is a captivating epistolary novel that delves into the intimate correspondence between a mother and her daughter. As Elizabeth navigates the challenges of her own life, she discovers a hidden world within her mother's letters, filled with secrets, love, and unexpected revelations. Through these letters, Elizabeth embarks on a journey of self-discovery, uncovering the truth about her family's past and the profound impact it has on her present. With each letter, the bond between mother and daughter grows stronger, leading Elizabeth to question her own identity and the choices she must make. 

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, family drama, mystery

Themes: 
Family secrets, self-discovery, mother-daughter relationships
