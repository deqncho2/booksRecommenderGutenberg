Synopsis: In 'The Rosery Folk' by George Manville Fenn, a young botanist named Tom is drawn into a mysterious world when he discovers a hidden garden filled with enchanted roses. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the garden, Tom uncovers a web of ancient legends, magical powers, and a dangerous quest that could change his life forever. With the help of a group of eccentric and endearing characters known as the Rosery Folk, Tom must navigate through treacherous obstacles and face his own fears to unravel the truth behind the enchanted roses and fulfill his destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Self-discovery
