Synopsis: In 'A Monk of Fife', Norman Leslie of Pitcullo chronicles his extraordinary experiences in the realm of France during the years 1429-1431. As a young Scottish monk, Norman finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Hundred Years' War, witnessing the rise of Joan of Arc and the battles that shaped the destiny of nations. Through his eyes, we are transported to a world of political intrigue, religious fervor, and epic battles, where Norman's own fate becomes intertwined with the fate of nations.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Identity, Loyalty
