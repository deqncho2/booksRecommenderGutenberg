Synopsis: In "The Civil War Battles of Chickamauga and Chattanooga," Jesse Littleton Rogers takes readers on a gripping journey through two pivotal battles that shaped the outcome of the American Civil War. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Rogers brings to life the harrowing experiences of soldiers on both sides, as they navigate the treacherous terrain and engage in intense combat. As the battles unfold, the reader is immersed in the strategic decisions, the bravery of individuals, and the profound impact these conflicts had on the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American history

Themes: War and its consequences, Leadership and strategy, Sacrifice and bravery
