Synopsis: 
'The Bores: A Comedy in Three Acts' by Molière follows the misadventures of a group of eccentric and self-absorbed individuals who are oblivious to their own tediousness. As they gather together, their interactions become increasingly absurd and comical, leading to hilarious consequences. With witty dialogue and clever satire, Molière explores the quirks and foibles of human nature, creating a delightful and entertaining comedy.

Genres: Comedy, Satire

Themes: Social pretension, Self-importance, Absurdity of human behavior
