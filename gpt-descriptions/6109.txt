Synopsis: In "How to Study and Teaching How to Study," Frank M. McMurry presents a comprehensive guide to effective studying techniques and strategies. Through practical advice and insightful tips, McMurry explores various methods to enhance learning, improve memory retention, and develop critical thinking skills. This engaging and informative book offers valuable insights into the art of studying, providing readers with the tools they need to succeed academically and beyond.

Genres: Educational, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Study skills, Learning strategies, Critical thinking, Memory enhancement
