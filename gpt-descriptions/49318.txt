Synopsis: In "Afloat (Sur l'eau)" by Guy de Maupassant, the story revolves around a group of friends who embark on a boating trip along the Seine River. As they navigate the tranquil waters, unexpected events and encounters test their friendships and reveal hidden desires. With each passing day, tensions rise, secrets unravel, and the idyllic journey takes an unforeseen turn. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the unpredictable nature of life.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Desire, Human Nature
