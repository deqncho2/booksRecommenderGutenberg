Synopsis: In 'Condensed Novels: New Burlesques', Bret Harte takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of condensed and satirical versions of popular novels. From the wild west to the high society of New York, Harte's witty and humorous retellings offer a fresh and entertaining perspective on well-known literary works. With clever twists and unexpected turns, this collection is sure to keep readers engaged and amused.

Genres: Satire, Parody, Humor

Themes: Literary criticism, Social commentary
