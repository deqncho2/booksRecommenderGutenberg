Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Victorian London, a young boy named Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure as a lamplighter. As he navigates through the shadows, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each flicker of his lamplight, Tom unravels a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Social inequality, Redemption
