Synopsis: In "For the White Christ: A Story of the Days of Charlemagne," Robert Ames Bennet takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of Charlemagne's reign. Set in the 9th century, the story follows the life of a young warrior named Roland, who finds himself torn between loyalty to his king and his own desires. As Roland navigates the treacherous world of politics, power, and love, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the destiny of an empire.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Power, Love, Identity, Honor
