Synopsis: In "Speaking of Operations--" by Irvin S. Cobb, the narrator takes us on a humorous and insightful journey through the world of medical operations. From the peculiar patients to the eccentric doctors, Cobb shares his experiences and observations with wit and charm. Through a series of entertaining anecdotes, he explores the quirks and idiosyncrasies of the medical profession, shedding light on the fascinating and sometimes bizarre world of surgeries and procedures.

Genres: Humor, Non-fiction, Medical

Themes: Medical practices, Humor in healthcare, Human nature
