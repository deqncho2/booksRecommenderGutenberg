Synopsis: 
"The Old Glade (Forbes's) Road" takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical Pennsylvania State Road, uncovering the secrets and stories hidden within its ancient trails. Archer Butler Hulbert delves into the rich history of this road, exploring the lives of the pioneers, soldiers, and settlers who traversed its path. From the struggles and triumphs of the early American frontier to the tales of adventure and hardship, this book paints a vivid picture of the road's significance in shaping the region's history. As readers follow the road's winding path, they will be transported back in time, discovering the untold stories that lie within its forgotten glades.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Frontier life, Historical significance
