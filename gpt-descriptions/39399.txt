Synopsis: In 'Treasure of Kings: Being the Story of the Discovery of the "Big Fish," or the Quest of the Greater Treasure of the Incas of Peru,' Charles Gilson takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the heart of Peru. When a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a cryptic map, he becomes obsessed with uncovering the legendary "Big Fish," a hidden treasure of immense value left behind by the Incas. With danger lurking at every turn, the archaeologist and his team embark on a perilous journey, facing treacherous landscapes, ancient curses, and rival treasure hunters. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the Incas, they discover that the true treasure may not be material wealth, but rather the knowledge and wisdom of a lost civilization.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration and discovery, the allure of treasure, the power of ancient civilizations, the value of knowledge and wisdom
