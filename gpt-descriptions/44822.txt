Synopsis: 
"Ask Mamma"; or, The Richest Commoner In England by Surtees, Robert Smith is a delightful and humorous novel set in 19th century England. The story follows the adventures of a young man named Peregrine who finds himself entangled in a series of comical misadventures as he navigates the world of high society, love, and horse racing. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Peregrine's journey is filled with wit, charm, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Comedy, Satire, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Social class, Love and romance, Identity
