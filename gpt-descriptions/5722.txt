Synopsis: In 'The Shewing-up of Blanco Posnet', a small town is thrown into turmoil when a young cowboy is accused of a heinous crime. As the trial unfolds, unexpected revelations and moral dilemmas arise, challenging the beliefs and values of the community. With wit and sharp social commentary, Shaw explores the complexities of justice, religion, and the nature of good and evil.

Genres: Drama, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Justice, Morality, Religion, Hypocrisy
