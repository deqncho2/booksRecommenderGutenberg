Synopsis: 
In "History of the Seventh Ohio Volunteer Cavalry," author R.C. Rankin takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Rankin delves into the experiences of the brave men who served in the Seventh Ohio Volunteer Cavalry, offering a unique perspective on the war's impact on both the soldiers and the nation. From the thrilling battles and daring escapades to the personal sacrifices and camaraderie forged on the battlefield, this book paints a vivid picture of the realities faced by these courageous soldiers. As the narrative unfolds, readers will find themselves immersed in the gripping tales of heroism, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of the Seventh Ohio Volunteer Cavalry.

Genres: 
Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: 
War and Conflict, Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice
