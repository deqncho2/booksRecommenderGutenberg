Synopsis:
In 'The Angel of Terror' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious and alluring woman named Lydia Beale captivates the attention of many men, but little do they know the dark secrets she hides. As the story unfolds, a series of shocking events and unexpected twists take place, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat. With a thrilling combination of romance, suspense, and crime, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes:
Deception, Revenge, Love and Obsession
