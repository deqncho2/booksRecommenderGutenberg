Synopsis: In 'Pygmalion's Spectacles' by Stanley G. Weinbaum, a brilliant inventor creates a pair of revolutionary spectacles that transport the wearer into a vivid and immersive virtual reality. As the protagonist embarks on a breathtaking journey through this new world, they encounter extraordinary landscapes, captivating characters, and unexpected dangers. However, the line between reality and illusion begins to blur, leading to a thrilling exploration of the power of perception and the consequences of escaping into a perfect fantasy realm.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Virtual reality, Perception, Escapism
