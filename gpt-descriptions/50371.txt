Synopsis: 
In "When Gretel Was Fifteen" by Nina Rhoades, we are transported to a small, seemingly idyllic town where Gretel, a young girl on the cusp of womanhood, finds herself caught in a web of secrets and mysteries. As she navigates the complexities of family, friendship, and first love, Gretel discovers that the town she thought she knew holds dark secrets that threaten to unravel her world. With each twist and turn, Gretel must confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Coming-of-age
