Synopsis: 

In 'John Pettie, R.A., H.R.S.A.: Sixteen examples in colour of the artist's work', Martin Hardie takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant and dynamic paintings of renowned artist John Pettie. With sixteen stunning examples in full color, this book showcases Pettie's mastery in capturing dramatic historical and literary scenes, as well as his ability to infuse his subjects with emotion and energy. From epic battle scenes to intimate character portraits, each artwork tells a story that will leave readers captivated and eager to explore more of Pettie's remarkable talent.

Genres: Art, Biography, History

Themes: Historical events, Literary scenes, Emotion and energy in art
