Synopsis: 
'Frank and Andy Afloat; Or, The Cave on the Island' follows the thrilling adventures of two young boys, Frank and Andy, as they find themselves stranded on a mysterious island. As they explore their surroundings, they stumble upon a hidden cave that holds secrets beyond their wildest imagination. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must rely on their wits and bravery to uncover the island's mysteries and find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
