Synopsis: In 'At Last: A Novel' by Marion Harland, a young woman named Emily finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. Set in the late 19th century, this captivating tale follows Emily's journey as she grapples with her own ambitions and the pressures of society, ultimately discovering the true meaning of happiness and fulfillment. With richly drawn characters and a compelling narrative, 'At Last' explores the timeless themes of self-discovery, sacrifice, and the pursuit of true love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Duty, Self-Discovery, Societal Expectations
