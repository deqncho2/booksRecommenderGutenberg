Synopsis: In 'Vandyke Brown: A Farce in One Act', the eccentric artist Vandyke Brown finds himself in a series of hilarious misadventures as he tries to navigate the world of art and romance. From mistaken identities to comical misunderstandings, this fast-paced farce will keep readers laughing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Artistic ambition, Love and romance, Identity and mistaken identity
