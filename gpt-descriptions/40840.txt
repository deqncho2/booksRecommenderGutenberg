Synopsis: 
In 'Ten Years Among the Mail Bags: Or, Notes from the Diary of a Special Agent of the Post-Office Department', James Holbrook takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden world of postal espionage. As a special agent for the Post-Office Department, Holbrook shares his captivating experiences, from infiltrating criminal networks to uncovering secret codes and decoding mysterious messages. With each chapter, the stakes rise higher, and the reader becomes engrossed in the dangerous and unpredictable world of mail bag investigations. This gripping memoir offers a unique perspective on the unsung heroes who protect the integrity of the postal system, making it a must-read for fans of true crime and espionage.

Genres: Memoir, True Crime, Espionage

Themes: Postal espionage, Criminal investigations, Code-breaking, Heroism
