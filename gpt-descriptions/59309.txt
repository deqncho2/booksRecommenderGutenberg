Synopsis: 
In 'Forced Move' by Lee, Henry, a young chess prodigy finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of strategy and deception. When his mentor mysteriously disappears, he must navigate a treacherous world of high-stakes chess tournaments, secret societies, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he discovers that the chessboard is not the only battleground, and the consequences of his moves extend far beyond the game. With his own life on the line, he must outwit his opponents and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Strategy, Loyalty, Power
