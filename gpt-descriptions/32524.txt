Synopsis: In 'A Fourth Form Friendship: A School Story' by Angela Brazil, a new girl named Mabel joins the fourth form at Briarcroft School and quickly befriends the lively and adventurous Jeanne. Together, they navigate the challenges of school life, forming a deep bond that is tested by unexpected events and the complexities of friendship. As they face various trials and triumphs, Mabel and Jeanne learn valuable lessons about loyalty, trust, and the power of true friendship.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, School Story, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Trust, Adolescence, Identity
