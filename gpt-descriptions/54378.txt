Synopsis: 
In 'Through the Telescope' by Baikie, James, a young astronomer stumbles upon a mysterious celestial event that defies all scientific explanation. As he delves deeper into the phenomenon, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the boundaries between reality and the unknown blur. With each discovery, he uncovers secrets that challenge his understanding of the universe and his place within it. As he races against time to unravel the truth, he must confront his own fears and confront the consequences of his actions. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the nature of reality, the power of curiosity, the consequences of discovery
