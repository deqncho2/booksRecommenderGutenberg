Synopsis:
In the quiet village of Knaresborough, a mysterious and enigmatic figure named Eugene Aram resides. Highly intelligent and well-read, Aram is respected by the townsfolk, but his past remains shrouded in secrecy. As the story unfolds, a series of events begins to unravel the truth behind Aram's past, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning the nature of guilt and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes:
Guilt, Secrets, Identity, Morality
