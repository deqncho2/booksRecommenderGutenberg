Synopsis:
In Plato's Symposium, a group of prominent Athenians gather for a lively evening of drinking and philosophical discourse. Each guest takes turns delivering a speech on the nature of love, sharing their unique perspectives and experiences. From passionate declarations to humorous anecdotes, the symposium becomes a captivating exploration of the complexities and mysteries of love. As the night unfolds, the guests delve deeper into the essence of desire, beauty, and the pursuit of wisdom, ultimately revealing profound insights about the human condition.

Genres:
Philosophical dialogue, Drama, Literature

Themes:
Love, Desire, Beauty, Wisdom, Human nature
