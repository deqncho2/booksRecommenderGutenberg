Synopsis: 
In 'Flight Into the Unknown' by Tom W. Harris, a young pilot named Alex embarks on a routine flight that takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious phenomenon in the sky. As he navigates through uncharted territory, Alex must confront his deepest fears and make life-altering decisions that will test his courage and resilience. With each passing moment, the stakes rise higher, and the line between reality and imagination becomes blurred. Will Alex find a way back home, or will he be forever lost in the unknown?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Facing fears, Identity, Survival
