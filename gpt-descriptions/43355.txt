Synopsis: 
In 'Mr. Punch's Book of Sport: The Humour of Cricket, Football, Tennis, Polo, Croquet, Hockey, Racing, &c', prepare to be entertained by a delightful collection of humorous anecdotes and illustrations that revolve around various sports. From the comical mishaps on the cricket field to the absurdities of football matches, this book offers a lighthearted and witty exploration of the world of sports. With its clever observations and amusing illustrations, it is sure to bring a smile to the face of any sports enthusiast.

Genres: Humor, Sports, Illustration

Themes: Comedy, Sportsmanship, Absurdity
