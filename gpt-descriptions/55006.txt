Synopsis: In the eerie and atmospheric world of "The Sundial," the Halloran family finds themselves trapped in their ancestral home, where a mysterious prophecy predicts the end of the world. As tensions rise and paranoia takes hold, the family members must navigate their own fears and desires, leading to unexpected alliances and shocking revelations. With its blend of gothic horror and dark humor, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, dark comedy

Themes: Prophecy, family dynamics, paranoia, the supernatural
