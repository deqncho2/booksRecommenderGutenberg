Synopsis: In 'Wappin' Wharf: A Frightful Comedy of Pirates', Charles S. Brooks takes readers on a thrilling and hilarious adventure on the high seas. Set in the 18th century, the story follows the misadventures of a young man named Tom, who finds himself unexpectedly thrust into the world of pirates. From swashbuckling sword fights to treacherous betrayals, Tom must navigate a dangerous world filled with eccentric characters and unexpected twists. As he tries to survive and find his way back home, Tom discovers hidden secrets and uncovers the true meaning of friendship and loyalty.

Genres: Adventure, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Adventure, Identity
