Synopsis: In 'Moral' by Ludwig Thoma, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of moral dilemmas that challenge his values and force him to question the very essence of right and wrong. As he navigates through a series of unexpected events, he is confronted with choices that test his integrity and push him to the edge of his conscience. With each decision he makes, the consequences become increasingly complex, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers pondering the true nature of morality.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Consequences of Actions
