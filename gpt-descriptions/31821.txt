Synopsis: In "Sónnica" by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente, the reader is transported to ancient Carthage, where the captivating and enigmatic Sónnica becomes the center of attention. As the daughter of a wealthy merchant, Sónnica's beauty and intelligence attract the attention of powerful men, leading her on a journey filled with love, betrayal, and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and tumultuous society, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping the reader eagerly turning the pages to uncover Sónnica's fate.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and desire, power and manipulation, societal expectations, personal growth
