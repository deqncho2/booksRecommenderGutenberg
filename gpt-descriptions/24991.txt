Synopsis: In 'Aunt Amy; or, How Minnie Brown learned to be a Sunbeam', young Minnie Brown finds herself in the care of her strict and seemingly joyless Aunt Amy. However, as Minnie navigates the challenges of her new home, she discovers that there is more to Aunt Amy than meets the eye. Through a series of heartwarming and sometimes humorous events, Minnie learns valuable lessons about love, kindness, and the power of a positive attitude.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family, Transformation, Optimism, Self-discovery
