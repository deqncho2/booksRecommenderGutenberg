Synopsis: 
'You Know Me Al: A Busher's Letters' is a captivating collection of humorous and insightful letters written by Jack Keefe, a talented but naive baseball player, as he navigates the world of professional sports in the early 20th century. Through his letters, Jack shares his triumphs, failures, and hilarious misadventures, providing a unique and entertaining glimpse into the life of a bush league player. As Jack's letters unfold, readers will find themselves immersed in his comical anecdotes, colorful descriptions of his teammates and opponents, and his endearing attempts to navigate the complexities of love and fame. 

Genres: 
Humor, Sports Fiction, Epistolary Fiction

Themes: 
Ambition, Naivety, Friendship, Love, Pursuit of Dreams
