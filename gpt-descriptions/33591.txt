Synopsis: In 'Wait and Hope; Or, A Plucky Boy's Luck' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Frank, who finds himself facing numerous challenges and setbacks. From unexpected twists of fate to encounters with both kind-hearted individuals and cunning adversaries, Frank's determination and resilience are put to the test. As he navigates through a world filled with opportunities and obstacles, readers will be drawn into the suspenseful narrative, eagerly awaiting the outcome of Frank's remarkable journey.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Self-reliance
