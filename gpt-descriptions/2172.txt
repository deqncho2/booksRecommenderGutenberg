Synopsis: In 'That Mainwaring Affair' by A. Maynard Barbour, a seemingly ordinary small-town girl finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when she becomes the unexpected heiress to a vast fortune. As she navigates the unfamiliar world of wealth and privilege, she must uncover the secrets of her newfound inheritance while facing unexpected dangers and romantic entanglements. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Social class
