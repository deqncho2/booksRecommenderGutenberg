Synopsis: 
In 'On the King's Service: Inward Glimpses of Men at Arms' by Logan, Innes, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of soldiers as they navigate the complexities of war and duty. Through a series of intimate and revealing glimpses, the book delves into the inner thoughts, struggles, and triumphs of these brave men, shedding light on the sacrifices they make and the bonds they form. From the battlefields to the barracks, this poignant exploration offers a unique perspective on the human experience within the realm of military service.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Duty, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Identity
