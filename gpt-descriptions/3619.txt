Synopsis: In "Cousin Maude" by Mary Jane Holmes, a young woman named Maude is invited to stay with her wealthy cousin, Julia, and her husband. However, Maude soon discovers that her cousin's seemingly perfect life hides dark secrets and hidden motives. As Maude navigates through a web of deceit and betrayal, she must uncover the truth and protect herself from the dangers that lurk within her own family.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Deception, Identity
