Synopsis: 

In 'Mortomley's Estate: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 3)', the reader is transported to the mysterious and captivating world of Mortomley's Estate. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and hidden agendas. The estate itself holds a dark and enigmatic past, with whispers of long-forgotten tragedies and unexplained occurrences. As the protagonist delves deeper into the history of Mortomley's Estate, they become entangled in a web of suspense and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems. With each turn of the page, the reader is left questioning the true nature of the estate and the secrets it holds.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden agendas, the power of the past, the supernatural, the blurred line between reality and illusion.
