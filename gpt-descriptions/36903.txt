Synopsis: 
'Marvels of Pond-life: Or, A Year's Microscopic Recreations Among the Polyps, Infusoria, Rotifers, Water-bears and Polyzoa' takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden world of pond-life. Through the eyes of the author, Henry James Slack, we are introduced to a mesmerizing array of microscopic creatures that inhabit the waters. From the delicate polyps to the elusive water-bears, Slack's observations and recreations bring these tiny organisms to life, revealing their intricate beauty and astonishing behaviors. As the seasons change, so does the pond-life, offering a year-long exploration of this fascinating microcosm. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Wonder, Biodiversity
