Synopsis: In "The day will come," a gripping novel by M. E. Braddon, a young woman named Edith is thrust into a world of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic letter from a long-lost relative. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's dark secrets, Edith finds herself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to unravel the truth, even if it means putting her own life at risk. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Forbidden love, Identity
