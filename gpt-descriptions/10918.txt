Synopsis: In 'The Log of a Noncombatant' by Horace Green, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a noncombatant during the American Civil War. This gripping memoir follows the author's experiences as he navigates the chaos and uncertainty of war, providing a unique perspective on the conflict. From the front lines to the home front, Green's vivid storytelling transports the reader to a time of immense turmoil and challenges their preconceived notions about war and heroism.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, the blurred lines between heroism and cowardice, the resilience of the human spirit
