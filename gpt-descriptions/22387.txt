Synopsis: 
In 'A Tour in Ireland. 1776-1779', Arthur Young takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of Ireland during the late 18th century. Through his vivid descriptions and insightful observations, Young paints a vivid picture of the country's rich history, its people, and their way of life. From bustling cities to serene countryside, he explores the diverse regions of Ireland, encountering fascinating characters and uncovering hidden gems along the way. As he delves deeper into the heart of the Emerald Isle, Young's narrative becomes a captivating exploration of the complexities of Irish society, its struggles, and its resilience. 

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Irish culture, History, Society, Identity
