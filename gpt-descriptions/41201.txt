Synopsis: 
"The Diary of a Superfluous Man, and Other Stories" is a collection of poignant and introspective tales by Ivan Turgenev. From the diary entries of a man who feels out of place in society to the story of a young woman torn between duty and love, these stories delve into the complexities of human emotions and the struggles of individuals trying to find their place in the world. With Turgenev's masterful storytelling, readers are taken on a journey through the depths of the human soul, exploring themes of love, loneliness, societal expectations, and the search for meaning.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: 
Love, Loneliness, Society, Identity, Existentialism
