Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of old London, a young man named Martin finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. As he delves deeper into the city's underworld, he uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to shatter everything he thought he knew. With his life on the line, Martin must navigate treacherous alliances and face his own demons in order to survive. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Identity
