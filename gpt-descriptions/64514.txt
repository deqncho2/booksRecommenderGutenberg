Synopsis: In "Goya, an account of his life and works" by Albert Frederick Calvert, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life and artistic genius of Francisco Goya. From his humble beginnings to becoming one of the most influential painters in history, this book delves into the fascinating world of Goya's art, his relationships, and the turbulent times in which he lived. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Calvert paints a vivid portrait of Goya's life, allowing readers to truly understand the man behind the masterpieces.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Political turmoil, Personal struggles, Human condition
