Synopsis: In 'The Brentons' by Ray, Anna Chapin, a seemingly ordinary family is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious stranger arrives on their doorstep, claiming to be a long-lost relative. As the family grapples with the stranger's presence, secrets from the past begin to unravel, leading to shocking revelations and unexpected consequences. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, 'The Brentons' explores the depths of family bonds and the power of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Trust
