Synopsis: In "Charlotte Temple" by Mrs. Rowson, a young and innocent girl named Charlotte Temple embarks on a journey that takes her from the comforts of her home to the bustling streets of New York City. There, she encounters a charming soldier who sweeps her off her feet and promises her a life of love and happiness. However, as Charlotte soon discovers, appearances can be deceiving, and her newfound romance quickly turns into a nightmare. Will Charlotte be able to navigate the treacherous path she finds herself on, or will she succumb to the perils that await her?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Morality and Virtue, Society's Expectations
