Synopsis: In "The Hand of Fu-Manchu: Being a New Phase in the Activities of Fu-Manchu, the Devil Doctor," the infamous criminal mastermind, Fu-Manchu, returns with a diabolical plan that threatens the world. As the brilliant detective Nayland Smith races against time to stop him, he must navigate a treacherous web of deception, danger, and ancient secrets. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Smith must outwit Fu-Manchu and his deadly henchmen in a thrilling battle of wits and wills.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Power and Control, Cultural Clash
