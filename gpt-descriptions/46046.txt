Synopsis: In "Sonia: Between Two Worlds" by Stephen McKenna, a young woman named Sonia finds herself torn between two vastly different worlds. As she navigates the complexities of her personal and professional life, Sonia must confront her deepest desires and make choices that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the power of self-discovery and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love, Ambition
