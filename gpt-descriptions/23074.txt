Synopsis: Embark on a thrilling adventure as young Jack sets sail on a voyage around the world, encountering treacherous seas, exotic lands, and unexpected dangers. From battling pirates to exploring uncharted territories, Jack's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he overcome the perils that lie ahead and return home safely? Join Jack on this unforgettable journey that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Resilience
