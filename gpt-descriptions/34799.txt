Synopsis: In 'A Runaway Brig; Or, An Accidental Cruise' by James Otis, a group of young friends find themselves embarking on an unexpected adventure when they accidentally set sail on a runaway ship. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter various challenges, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must unravel the mysteries of the ship and its crew before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Courage
