Synopsis: In 'The Motor Maids by Rose, Shamrock and Thistle' by Katherine Stokes, three adventurous young women embark on a thrilling journey across Europe in their trusty motorcar. As they navigate through picturesque landscapes and encounter unexpected challenges, they find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. With their wit, courage, and determination, the Motor Maids must unravel the secrets that lie before them, all while forging lifelong friendships and discovering the true meaning of independence.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Female empowerment, Friendship, Exploration
