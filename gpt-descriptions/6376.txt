Synopsis: 
In 'Self-Raised; Or, From the Depths', we follow the remarkable journey of a young girl named Rosalie, who rises above her humble beginnings to become a force to be reckoned with. From the depths of poverty and despair, Rosalie's indomitable spirit and unwavering determination propel her forward, as she navigates through a world filled with treachery, deceit, and unexpected love. As she strives to overcome the obstacles that stand in her way, Rosalie's resilience and courage inspire those around her, leaving an indelible mark on the lives she touches.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Perseverance, Social Class, Redemption
