Synopsis: In the remote and mystical Great Smoky Mountains, a young boy named Peter is captivated by the enigmatic Prophet, a man who possesses an uncanny ability to communicate with nature and animals. As Peter grows older, he becomes determined to uncover the secrets behind the Prophet's powers and the ancient wisdom he imparts. Along his journey, Peter encounters a cast of colorful characters, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of faith, love, and the interconnectedness of all living things.

Genres: Fiction, Southern Gothic, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Nature, Spirituality, Wisdom, Identity, Community
