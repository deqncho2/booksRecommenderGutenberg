Synopsis: In 'The War of the Worlds', H.G. Wells takes readers on a thrilling journey as mysterious and deadly alien invaders descend upon Earth. As humanity faces an unprecedented threat, the protagonist must navigate through chaos and destruction, witnessing the collapse of society and the resilience of the human spirit. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of the unknown, this classic science fiction novel will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Alien invasion, Survival, Human nature, Societal collapse
