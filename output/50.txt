Synopsis: In 'Pi' by Scott Hemphill, a brilliant mathematician named Max discovers a mysterious and seemingly random number that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Max becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, pursued by both a powerful corporation and a clandestine group with their own hidden agenda. With his life on the line, Max must navigate a treacherous path of deception, betrayal, and mind-bending revelations to protect the truth he has uncovered.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Mathematics, Conspiracy, Identity
