Synopsis: In Abraham Lincoln's First Inaugural Address, the newly elected President delivers a powerful speech that addresses the nation's deep divisions and the impending threat of civil war. With eloquence and conviction, Lincoln appeals to the American people to find common ground and preserve the Union, emphasizing the importance of unity and the rule of law. Through his words, he offers a glimpse into the challenges and uncertainties of a nation on the brink of a historic conflict.

Genres: Historical, Political, Speech

Themes: Unity, Division, Leadership, Preservation of the Union
