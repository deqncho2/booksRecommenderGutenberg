Synopsis:
When young Jim Hawkins discovers a mysterious map leading to buried treasure, he embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to a remote island filled with treacherous pirates. As Jim navigates the dangerous waters of betrayal and greed, he must rely on his wits and courage to survive. With hidden identities, secret alliances, and a legendary pirate captain, Treasure Island is a gripping tale of high-stakes adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Betrayal, Greed, Loyalty, Courage
