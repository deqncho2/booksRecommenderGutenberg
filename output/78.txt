Synopsis: In "Tarzan of the Apes," we follow the extraordinary journey of a young boy named Tarzan, who is raised by apes in the African jungle after his parents are marooned and killed. As Tarzan grows up, he discovers his unique abilities and struggles to find his place in both the animal and human worlds. Along the way, he encounters danger, love, and a profound sense of identity that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Identity, Nature vs. Nurture, Survival, Love and Belonging
