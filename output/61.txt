Synopsis: In this groundbreaking manifesto, Engels and Marx delve into the complexities of society, exposing the inherent contradictions and injustices that plague the capitalist system. Through a powerful analysis of history, economics, and class struggle, they present a compelling argument for the overthrow of the ruling bourgeoisie and the establishment of a classless society. With a call to arms, they ignite a revolutionary spirit that challenges the status quo and envisions a future where the working class rises to reclaim their rightful place in society.

Genres: Political philosophy, Social criticism, Historical analysis

Themes: Class struggle, Capitalism, Revolution, Social inequality, Historical materialism
