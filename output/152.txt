Synopsis: In 'Wild Justice' by Ruth M. Sprague, a small town is shaken by a brutal crime that leaves the community in shock. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the line between justice and revenge becomes blurred. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to protect their loved ones and seek retribution. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Family, Secrets
