Synopsis: In the Plays of Sophocles, we are introduced to the tragic tale of Oedipus, a man whose life is plagued by a prophecy that he will commit unspeakable acts. Oedipus the King follows his relentless pursuit of the truth, Oedipus at Colonus explores his journey towards redemption, and Antigone delves into the consequences of his actions on his family. These timeless plays delve into the depths of human nature, fate, and the power of truth, captivating readers with their gripping narratives and thought-provoking themes.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Fate and free will, the search for truth, the consequences of one's actions, family and loyalty
