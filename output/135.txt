Synopsis: 
Les Misérables by Victor Hugo is a captivating tale set in 19th-century France, following the lives of several characters whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. The story revolves around Jean Valjean, a former convict seeking redemption, and his relentless pursuit by the relentless Inspector Javert. As Valjean navigates the harsh realities of poverty, injustice, and revolution, he encounters a cast of diverse and unforgettable characters, each with their own struggles and desires. From the streets of Paris to the barricades of revolution, Les Misérables is a sweeping epic that explores the depths of human compassion, the pursuit of justice, and the power of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Social injustice, Love and sacrifice, Revolution, Morality
