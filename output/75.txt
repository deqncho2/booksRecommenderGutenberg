Synopsis: 
In 'E-mail 101' by Goodwin, John E., a young professional named Alex finds himself caught in a web of intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious email that leads him down a dangerous path. As he delves deeper into the world of cybercrime, Alex must navigate a treacherous landscape of hackers, corporate espionage, and personal betrayal. With his career and life on the line, he must uncover the truth behind the email before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Cybercrime, Corporate Espionage, Betrayal
