Synopsis: In "The Number "e"", authors Bonnell and Nemiroff take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of mathematics, exploring the mysterious and enigmatic number "e". From its origins in compound interest to its unexpected appearances in nature and the universe, this book unravels the secrets and significance of "e" in a way that will leave readers in awe of its beauty and power. Through engaging storytelling and thought-provoking examples, the authors bring to life the profound impact that "e" has had on fields as diverse as finance, physics, and biology. Prepare to be amazed as you delve into the captivating world of "e" and discover the hidden wonders of this extraordinary number.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mathematics, Science

Themes: Mathematics, Number theory, Applications of mathematics, Natural phenomena
