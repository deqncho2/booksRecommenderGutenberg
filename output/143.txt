Synopsis: In the small town of Casterbridge, a young man named Michael Henchard makes a fateful decision that sets off a chain of events that will shape his life and the lives of those around him. Driven by pride and a desire for redemption, Henchard rises from poverty to become the respected mayor of the town. However, his past mistakes and the consequences of his actions continue to haunt him, threatening to unravel everything he has worked so hard to achieve. As secrets are revealed and relationships are tested, Henchard must confront his own flaws and face the consequences of his choices.

Genres: Classic literature, tragedy, drama

Themes: Fate and destiny, the consequences of one's actions, the nature of redemption, the power of secrets, the complexities of human relationships
