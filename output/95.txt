Synopsis: 
In "The Prisoner of Zenda" by Anthony Hope, a young Englishman named Rudolf Rassendyll finds himself unexpectedly thrust into a dangerous adventure when he is mistaken for the soon-to-be-crowned King of Ruritania. As Rudolf assumes the role of the king, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and treacherous plots. With his life constantly at stake, Rudolf must navigate the complexities of the royal court and face numerous challenges to protect the kingdom and its rightful ruler. Filled with suspense, romance, and unexpected twists, "The Prisoner of Zenda" takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world of deception and loyalty.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Love and Sacrifice
