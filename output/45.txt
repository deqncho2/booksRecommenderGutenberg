Synopsis: 
When Matthew Cuthbert decides to adopt a boy to help him and his sister Marilla on their farm, he is in for a surprise. Instead of a boy, a spirited and imaginative red-haired girl named Anne Shirley arrives at Green Gables. Anne's vibrant personality and vivid imagination quickly captivate the hearts of the Cuthberts and the entire community of Avonlea. As Anne navigates her way through school, friendships, and the challenges of growing up, she brings joy, laughter, and a touch of mischief to everyone she meets. Follow Anne's journey as she discovers the true meaning of family, love, and finding a place to call home.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Identity, Family, Love
