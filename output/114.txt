Synopsis: 
Step into the whimsical world of Lewis Carroll's Alice in Wonderland through the enchanting illustrations of John Tenniel. This captivating book showcases Tenniel's iconic artwork, bringing to life the curious and imaginative characters that Alice encounters on her extraordinary journey. From the mischievous Cheshire Cat to the eccentric Mad Hatter, each illustration is a visual feast that will transport readers into a realm of wonder and delight. Lose yourself in the intricate details and vibrant colors of Tenniel's illustrations, and rediscover the magic of Alice's adventures in a whole new way.

Genres: Art, Children's Literature, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Curiosity, Surrealism
