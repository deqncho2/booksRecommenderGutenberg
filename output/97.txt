Synopsis: In the world of Flatland, where everything exists in only two dimensions, a humble Square finds himself on a mind-bending journey that challenges his perception of reality. As he navigates through this geometric society, encountering beings of different shapes and dimensions, he begins to question the limitations of his own existence. With the arrival of a mysterious visitor from the third dimension, the Square's world is turned upside down, leading him to uncover profound truths about the nature of reality and the limitations of perception.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Mathematical Fiction

Themes: Perception, Reality, Social Hierarchy, Dimensions, Satire of Victorian Society
