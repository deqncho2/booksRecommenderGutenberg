Synopsis: 

In "Anne of the Island," the beloved character Anne Shirley embarks on a new chapter of her life as she leaves her beloved Green Gables behind to attend college. As she navigates the challenges of higher education, Anne finds herself facing new friendships, unexpected romances, and the bittersweet realities of growing up. With her trademark charm, wit, and imagination, Anne discovers that the journey to self-discovery is filled with both joy and heartache.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Love, Self-discovery
