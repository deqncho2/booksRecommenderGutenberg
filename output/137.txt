Synopsis: 
Sara Crewe, a young girl with a vivid imagination, finds herself at Miss Minchin's Boarding School after being separated from her father. Despite her initial wealth and privilege, Sara's life takes a drastic turn when her circumstances change. As she faces adversity and hardship, Sara's resilience and kindness shine through, captivating those around her. With unexpected twists and turns, this heartwarming tale explores the power of imagination, the importance of friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Resilience, Friendship, Adversity
