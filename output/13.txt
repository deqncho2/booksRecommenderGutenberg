Synopsis: 
In "The Hunting of the Snark: An Agony in Eight Fits," Lewis Carroll takes readers on a whimsical and nonsensical journey as a group of adventurers set out to hunt a mysterious creature known as the Snark. As they encounter peculiar characters and face numerous challenges, the quest becomes a thrilling and unpredictable adventure filled with wordplay, humor, and unexpected twists. Will they succeed in their quest to find the elusive Snark, or will they be left with more questions than answers?

Genres: 
Fantasy, Poetry, Nonsense literature

Themes: 
Exploration of the unknown, The nature of reality, The power of imagination
