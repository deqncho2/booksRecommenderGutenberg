Synopsis: In 'A Christmas Carol in Prose; Being a Ghost Story of Christmas', Charles Dickens takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Ebenezer Scrooge, a miserly and cold-hearted man. On Christmas Eve, Scrooge is visited by the ghost of his former business partner, Jacob Marley, who warns him of the consequences of his selfish ways. Throughout the night, Scrooge encounters three more spirits who show him glimpses of his past, present, and future. As the spirits guide him, Scrooge is forced to confront his own choices and the impact they have had on those around him. Will this haunting experience be enough to transform Scrooge into a kinder and more compassionate person?

Genres: Fiction, Ghost story, Christmas literature

Themes: Redemption, Generosity, The power of self-reflection, The importance of human connection
