Synopsis: 
In 'Take Me for a Ride: Coming of Age in a Destructive Cult' by Mark E. Laxer, a young protagonist finds himself drawn into the captivating world of a mysterious cult. As he becomes more deeply involved, he begins to question the true nature of the group and the charismatic leader who holds its members under his spell. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate the dangerous path of self-discovery and escape from the clutches of the cult before it's too late.

Genres: 
Psychological thriller, coming-of-age, suspense

Themes: 
Manipulation, identity, power dynamics
