Synopsis: In 'Thuvia, Maid of Mars' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, the reader is transported to the captivating world of Barsoom, where adventure and romance intertwine. The story follows the courageous hero, Carthoris, as he embarks on a perilous journey to rescue the beautiful Thuvia, who has been kidnapped by a mysterious force. Along the way, Carthoris encounters strange creatures, treacherous enemies, and uncovers a hidden power that could change the fate of Mars forever. Will he be able to save Thuvia and unravel the secrets that lie beneath the surface of this ancient planet?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Heroism, Love and Sacrifice, Exploration of Other Worlds
