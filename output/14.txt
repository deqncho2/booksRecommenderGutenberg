Synopsis: 

The 1990 CIA World Factbook takes readers on a captivating journey through the geopolitical landscape of the world at the dawn of a new decade. This meticulously researched and comprehensive guide offers a wealth of information about the countries and territories of the world, providing a fascinating glimpse into their history, geography, economy, and culture. From the bustling metropolises to the remote corners of the globe, this Factbook unveils a tapestry of diverse nations, each with its own unique story to tell. Delve into the pages of this remarkable resource and discover a world of intrigue, complexity, and endless possibilities.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Geography

Themes: Global politics, Cultural diversity, Economic development
