Synopsis: In 'The Online World' by De Presno, Odd, a young computer programmer, stumbles upon a hidden online community that promises to fulfill people's deepest desires. As he delves deeper into this mysterious world, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, power struggles, and unexpected consequences. With each click, Odd's perception of reality blurs, and he must navigate the treacherous landscape of the online realm to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Technology's impact on society, the allure of escapism, the dangers of unchecked power
