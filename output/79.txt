Synopsis: In 'Terminal Compromise' by Schwartau and Winn, a brilliant computer hacker finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a sinister plot that could have catastrophic consequences. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate through a world of high-stakes cyber warfare, government secrets, and personal vendettas. With his skills pushed to the limit, he must make difficult choices that could determine the fate of nations. 

Genres: Techno-thriller, Suspense, Cyberpunk

Themes: Cybersecurity, Government surveillance, Ethical dilemmas
