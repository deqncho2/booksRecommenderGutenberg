Synopsis: In 'Violists' by McGowan, Richard, a group of talented violists find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and danger when they stumble upon a hidden secret within the world of classical music. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that could change their lives forever. With its captivating blend of music, suspense, and unexpected twists, 'Violists' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of the classical music scene.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Music

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Redemption and personal growth
